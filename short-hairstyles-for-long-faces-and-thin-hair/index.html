Short Hairstyles For Long Faces And Thin Hair

Written by Robby Jun 05, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a long face and thin hair, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. However, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and add volume to your hair. In this article, we’ll go over some of the best short hairstyles for long faces and thin hair, as well as tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for a long face and thin hair, it’s important to consider the overall shape of your face. Ideally, you want a hairstyle that will add width and volume to your hair, while also balancing out the length of your face. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Look for hairstyles with layers or textures, which can add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as these can make your face appear even longer.
  • Consider a side-swept fringe or bangs, which can help to shorten the appearance of your forehead.
  • Choose a hairstyle that works with your natural hair texture, whether it’s straight, wavy, or curly.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Once you’ve chosen your new short hairstyle, it’s important to know how to style it properly. Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling short hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for thin hair. This will help to add volume and thickness to your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair, then apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting your roots as you go to create volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to add texture and shape to your hair.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hair can be low-maintenance, but it still requires some upkeep to stay looking its best. Here are a few tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to help absorb oil and add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can cause damage and make your hair look limp.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.

FAQs

What are the best short hairstyles for long faces and thin hair?

Some of the best short hairstyles for long faces and thin hair include a pixie cut, a bob, and a layered shag. These hairstyles can add volume and texture to your hair, while also balancing out the length of your face.

How can I add volume to my thin hair?

There are a few things you can do to add volume to your thin hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow-dry your hair with a round brush, and consider using a volumizing mousse or spray. You can also try teasing your roots or adding clip-in extensions for extra volume.

Is it hard to maintain short hair?

Short hair can be low-maintenance, but it still requires some upkeep to stay looking its best. You’ll need to get regular trims, use the right hair products, and avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair

If you have thin hair, it’s important to take good care of it to prevent breakage and promote healthy growth. Here are a few hair care tips for thin hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for thin hair.
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can cause damage and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect short hairstyle for your long face and thin hair, and keep it looking great for weeks to come.
