How To Cut Your Hair Shoulder Length By Yourself

Written by Bardi Aug 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune to get your hair cut at the salon? With a little patience and some basic tools, cutting your hair at home can be an easy and cost-effective way to achieve a shoulder-length hairstyle. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to cut your hair shoulder length by yourself.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Shoulder-length hair is a versatile and popular style that can be worn by anyone. It is a perfect length for those who want a medium-length haircut that is not too short or too long. Cutting your hair shoulder length by yourself can save you a lot of money and time, but it does require some practice and patience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut in the comfort of your own home.

How to Cut Your Hair Shoulder Length by Yourself

Step by Step:

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You will need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Water sprayer
  • Mirror

Once you have all of your tools ready, follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair and allow it to dry naturally or blow-dry it to your desired style.
  2. Part your hair down the middle and tie each side into a ponytail at the height you want your hair to be cut.
  3. Comb each section of hair straight and make sure they are even in length.
  4. Use your scissors to cut each ponytail, making sure to cut straight across and not at an angle.
  5. Release your hair and check that both sides are even in length. If not, trim any excess hair until both sides are even.
  6. Use your comb to blend any harsh lines and create a natural-looking haircut.
  7. If you want to add layers or texture, use your scissors to cut small sections of hair at an angle. Make sure to blend them in with the rest of your hair.
  8. Use your clippers (if you have them) to clean up any stray hairs around your ears and the nape of your neck.
  9. Spray your hair with water and comb it again to check for any unevenness. If you find any, trim the excess hair until it is even.
  10. Style your hair as desired and enjoy your new shoulder-length haircut!

Tips:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to the desired length.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or frustrated.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help you if you are unsure.

FAQ:

Q: Can I cut my hair shoulder length with any type of scissors?
A: No, it is recommended to use sharp hair cutting scissors as they are designed to cut hair evenly and smoothly.

Q: Can I cut my hair wet or dry?
A: It is recommended to cut your hair when it is dry and styled to your desired length.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?
A: It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. On average, it is recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.

Hair Care:

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it properly to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Cutting your hair shoulder length by yourself can be a fun and rewarding experience. By following these simple steps and tips, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut without leaving your home. Just remember to be patient and take your time, and soon you will have a stylish and trendy shoulder-length hairstyle!
