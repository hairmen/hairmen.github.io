How To Determine Your Face Shape App A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Oct 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying to figure out your face shape on your own? Look no further than a face shape app! With just a few clicks, you can determine your face shape and get personalized recommendations for hairstyles, makeup, and more. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of using a face shape app to determine your face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the app, let's first discuss how to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

To determine your face shape, you'll need to take measurements of your face. You can do this using a tape measure or ruler. Here's how:

Step 1: Measure Your Forehead

Place the tape measure at the widest point of your forehead (usually just above your eyebrows) and measure straight across to the other side.

Step 2: Measure Your Cheekbones

Place the tape measure at the widest point of your cheekbones (usually just below the outer corner of your eyes) and measure straight across to the other side.

Step 3: Measure Your Jawline

Place the tape measure at the base of your jawline (just below your earlobe) and measure straight across to the other side.

Step 4: Measure Your Face Length

Place the tape measure at the center of your hairline and measure straight down to the bottom of your chin.

Once you have these measurements, you can determine your face shape using a face shape app.

Using a Face Shape App

There are many face shape apps available for download on both iOS and Android devices. Here are some popular options:

  • Face Shape Meter
  • My Face Shape
  • FaceShape

Simply download the app of your choice and follow the on-screen instructions to take a selfie. The app will then analyze your face and determine your face shape.

Tips for Using a Face Shape App

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using a face shape app:

  • Make sure your lighting is good. Poor lighting can affect the accuracy of the app's analysis.
  • Take a clear, straight-on selfie. This will give the app the best view of your face.
  • Use a plain background. A busy background can distract from your face.

FAQ

What if I'm in between face shapes?

It's possible to have a combination of two face shapes. In this case, you can choose the face shape that you feel represents your face the most.

Can a face shape change over time?

Your face shape can change slightly over time due to factors such as aging and weight gain/loss. However, your overall face shape will remain relatively consistent.

What if I don't want to use a face shape app?

If you don't want to use a face shape app, you can determine your face shape using a mirror and a dry erase marker. Simply trace the outline of your face onto the mirror and compare it to the six main face shapes.

Hair Care Based on Your Face Shape

Once you've determined your face shape, you can use this information to choose the best hairstyles for you. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Round face: hairstyles that add height and lengthen the face, such as long layers and side-swept bangs.
  • Oval face: most hairstyles work well with this face shape, but try to avoid hairstyles that cover your forehead.
  • Square face: hairstyles that soften the angles of the face, such as soft waves and side parts.
  • Heart face: hairstyles that add volume to the chin area, such as chin-length bobs and side-swept bangs.
  • Diamond face: hairstyles that add width to the forehead and jawline, such as layered bobs and side parts.
  • Triangle face: hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head, such as pixie cuts and side-swept bangs.

With the help of a face shape app, you can determine your face shape and choose the best hairstyles for you. Happy hairstyling!
