What Length Is A Size 3 Clipper

Written by Luffy Jun 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to keep your hair trimmed and tidy, then you might be considering investing in a set of clippers. However, with so many different sizes and styles available, it can be tough to know where to start. In this post, we're going to take a closer look at what length is a size 3 clipper, and how you can use this tool to achieve your desired hairstyle.

Description

Before we dive into the specifics of what length is a size 3 clipper, let's take a moment to talk about what clippers actually are. Clippers are a type of electric hair-cutting tool that are commonly used by barbers and hairstylists. They consist of a set of blades that oscillate back and forth, cutting the hair as they move. Clippers come in a variety of sizes, each of which is designed to cut hair to a specific length.

How to Use a Size 3 Clipper

Using a size 3 clipper is relatively straightforward, even if you're new to hair cutting. Here are the basic steps you'll need to follow:

Step 1: Choose Your Guard Size

The guard is the attachment that fits over the blades of the clipper and determines the length of the hair that will be cut. In the case of a size 3 clipper, the guard will typically cut hair to a length of around 3/8 inch. However, it's worth noting that different brands of clippers may have slightly different guard sizes, so be sure to check the specifications before you start cutting.

Step 2: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure that the hair you're working with is clean and dry. This will help the clipper blades to move smoothly through the hair, without getting bogged down or tangled.

Step 3: Cut Across the Head

When using a clipper, it's generally best to start at the back of the head and work your way forward. Hold the clipper with the blades facing downwards, and move it across the head in a straight line. You can use your other hand to help guide the clippers, making sure that you're cutting the hair to the desired length.

Tips for Using a Size 3 Clipper

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when using a size 3 clipper:

  • Make sure that the clipper blades are well-oiled before you start cutting. This will help to keep them moving smoothly and prevent them from getting stuck in the hair.
  • Always work in small sections, rather than trying to cut large chunks of hair at once. This will help you to maintain better control over the clipper, and ensure that you're cutting the hair to the desired length.
  • If you're new to hair cutting, it's a good idea to start with a longer guard size and work your way down gradually. This will help you to get a feel for the clipper and avoid making any major mistakes.

FAQ

What length is a size 3 clipper?

A size 3 clipper will typically cut hair to a length of around 3/8 inch.

What is the difference between a clipper and a trimmer?

Clippers are designed to cut larger sections of hair, while trimmers are used for more detailed work, such as shaping beards or trimming around the hairline.

Hair Care

While clippers can be a great way to maintain your hair at home, it's important to take good care of your hair in between cuts. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly, using a gentle shampoo that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and make it more difficult to cut.
  • Use a conditioner to help keep your hair soft and manageable, and consider using a leave-in treatment to help prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and using a size 3 clipper to keep your hair neatly trimmed, you can achieve a stylish, low-maintenance look that's perfect for any occasion.
