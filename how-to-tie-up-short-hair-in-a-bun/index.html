How To Tie Up Short Hair In A Bun A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hair can be a challenge when it comes to styling, but one of the easiest and most versatile styles is the bun. Whether you're looking for a chic updo for a special occasion or just want to keep your hair out of your face on a hot day, a bun is a great option. In this guide, we'll show you how to tie up short hair in a bun in just a few simple steps.

How To

Before we dive into the steps, here are a few things you'll need:

  • Hair elastics
  • Bobby pins
  • Hair spray (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head. Use a hair elastic to secure it.
  3. Twist the ponytail until it starts to coil around itself.
  4. Wrap the twisted ponytail around the base of the hair elastic, forming a bun.
  5. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. Insert the bobby pins vertically, so they grip both the bun and your hair.
  6. Use a hair elastic to secure any loose strands around the bun.
  7. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

And that's it! You now have a stylish bun that will stay put all day.

Tips

Here are a few tips to make sure your bun looks its best:

  • If you have thin or fine hair, add volume to your bun by teasing your hair before creating the ponytail.
  • For a more polished look, smooth down any flyaways with a small amount of hair gel or pomade before starting.
  • If your hair is too short to create a high ponytail, try a low bun instead. Start by gathering your hair at the nape of your neck, then follow the same steps as above.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about tying up short hair in a bun:

Can I wear a bun if my hair is too short?

Yes! Even if your hair is too short to create a high ponytail, you can still create a bun at the nape of your neck. Follow the same steps as above, but start by gathering your hair at the base of your neck instead of the crown of your head.

How do I keep my bun from falling out?

Make sure to use enough bobby pins to secure your bun in place. Insert them vertically, so they grip both the bun and your hair. You can also use a hair elastic to secure any loose strands around the bun.

Can I wear a bun if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! In fact, a bun can be a great way to show off your curls. Follow the same steps as above, but don't worry too much about making it perfect. A slightly messy bun can look great with curly hair.

Hair Care

Remember to always take good care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

Now that you know how to tie up short hair in a bun, you can enjoy this versatile style whenever you like. Whether you're heading to work or a special event, a bun is a great way to keep your hair out of your face and looking stylish.
