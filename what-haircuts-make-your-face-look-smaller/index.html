What Haircuts Make Your Face Look Smaller

Written by Bardi Jun 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a round or chubby face, you may be wondering what types of haircuts can help make your face appear slimmer. While there are no hard and fast rules when it comes to haircuts for different face shapes, certain styles can help create the illusion of a smaller, more defined face. Read on for tips and tricks on how to choose the right haircut for your face shape.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a haircut that will make your face look smaller is to determine your face shape. There are four main face shapes: round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. If you're not sure which category you fall into, use a measuring tape to measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, and compare the ratios to those of the different face shapes.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking at different haircut options. Here are some general guidelines:

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to choose a haircut that creates the illusion of length and definition. Layered cuts that frame the face can help elongate it, while side-swept bangs can help create the illusion of a narrower forehead. Avoid blunt cuts that end at chin level, as these can make your face appear wider.

Oval Face Shape

Oval face shapes are considered the most versatile, as they can pull off a variety of haircuts. However, if you're looking to create the illusion of a smaller face, try a shoulder-length cut with layers that start at the cheekbones. This will help create a more defined jawline.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you'll want to soften your features with a layered cut that adds movement and texture. Side-swept bangs can also help create a more feminine look. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline, as these can make your face appear more angular.

Heart-Shaped Face Shape

Heart-shaped faces are widest at the forehead and narrowest at the chin. To balance out your features, try a chin-length bob with side-swept bangs. This will help create the illusion of a more proportional face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Haircut that Makes Your Face Look Smaller

Now that you know what types of haircuts can help make your face look smaller, it's time to head to the salon. Follow these steps to ensure you get the perfect cut:

  1. Bring photos of the haircut you want, as well as any specific concerns you have about your face shape.
  2. Communicate clearly with your stylist about what you want and what you don't want.
  3. Trust your stylist's professional opinion, but also speak up if you're not comfortable with something.
  4. Ask for tips on how to style and maintain your new haircut.
  5. Tip your stylist generously if you're happy with the results!

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut and Keeping Your Face Looking Slim

Getting the right haircut is just the first step in creating the illusion of a smaller face. Here are some tips for maintaining your new look:

  • Use styling products that add volume and texture, such as mousse or texturizing spray.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create lift at the roots.
  • Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly, as this can emphasize the roundness of your face.
  • Consider experimenting with different hair colors and highlights to add dimension to your hair and create the illusion of a more defined face.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best haircut for a round face?

A layered haircut that frames the face and adds volume at the crown is a great option for a round face. Side-swept bangs can also help create the illusion of a narrower forehead.

Can bangs make your face look smaller?

Yes, side-swept bangs can help create the illusion of a smaller face by drawing attention away from the forehead and toward the eyes.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid blunt cuts that end at chin level, as these can make your face appear wider. Also avoid hairstyles that are slicked back or tightly pulled back from the face, as these can emphasize the roundness of your face.

Hair Care Tips for Keeping Your Hair Healthy and Shiny

Regardless of your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and helps create the illusion of a smaller, more defined face. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
