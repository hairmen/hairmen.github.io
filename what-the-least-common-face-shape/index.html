What Is The Least Common Face Shape

Written by Eveline Jul 01, 2023 · 3 min read
What is the Least Common Face Shape? | Hair Care Tips | FAQ

If you've ever wondered what the least common face shape is, you're not alone. While there are many different face shapes out there, some are more common than others. In this article, we'll explore what the least common face shape is, how to identify it, and some hair care tips for those who have this unique face shape.

Table of Contents

  • What is the Least Common Face Shape?
  • Identifying the Least Common Face Shape
  • Hair Care Tips for the Least Common Face Shape
  • FAQs

Identifying the Least Common Face Shape

The least common face shape is the diamond face shape. This face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead and jawline, with wider cheekbones. The chin is also pointed, similar to the heart-shaped face, but the diamond face shape has a more angular appearance overall.

To identify if you have a diamond face shape, look for these features:

  • A narrow hairline and forehead
  • Wider cheekbones
  • A pointed chin
  • A narrow jawline

Hair Care Tips for the Least Common Face Shape

While the diamond face shape is quite unique, it can also be a bit challenging when it comes to choosing the right hairstyles and haircuts. Here are some tips to help you make the most of your diamond face shape:

  • Choose hairstyles that add width to your forehead and crown area, such as layered bangs, side-swept bangs, or a textured pixie cut.
  • Stay away from hairstyles that add volume to your cheek area, such as blunt bangs or a chin-length bob.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to soften the angles of your face shape.
  • Try a middle or off-center part to balance out the width of your cheekbones.

Haircuts for Diamond Face Shape

Some of the best haircuts for diamond face shapes include:

  • Layered bob
  • Textured pixie cut
  • Long layers with side-swept bangs
  • Curly bob

FAQs

What other face shapes are there?

Other common face shapes include oval, round, square, heart-shaped, and oblong.

Can I still wear any hairstyle I want with a diamond face shape?

While there are some hairstyles that may not be as flattering on a diamond face shape, you should still wear whatever hairstyle makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Are there any celebrities with a diamond face shape?

Yes, some celebrities with a diamond face shape include Rihanna, Tyra Banks, and Elizabeth Hurley.


Now that you know what the least common face shape is and how to identify it, you can choose hairstyles and haircuts that flatter your unique features. Remember to always wear what makes you feel confident and beautiful, regardless of your face shape!
