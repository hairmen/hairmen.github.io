Best Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Face Female

Written by Robby Jun 19, 2023 · 3 min read

Heart shaped faces are characterized by a wider forehead, a pointed chin, and a narrower jawline. If you’re a woman with a heart shaped face, you may find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that flatters your features. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll share with you the best hairstyles for heart shaped face female.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Heart shaped faces are versatile and can pull off various hairstyles. However, the key is to balance the proportions of your face. Hairstyles that add volume to the lower half of your face while minimizing the width of your forehead are ideal for heart shaped faces.

How To

When choosing a hairstyle for heart shaped face female, consider the following:

  • Length: Medium to long hair looks great on heart-shaped faces. It adds volume to the lower half of your face and balances your features.
  • Layers: Layers add texture and volume to your hair. They also help soften the angles of your face.
  • Bangs: Bangs can help shorten the appearance of your forehead. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are great for heart-shaped faces.

Step by Step

Here are some step by step instructions to achieve the best hairstyles for heart shaped face female:

  • Long Layers: Ask your stylist for long layers that frame your face. This will add volume and movement to your hair while softening your features.
  • Side-Swept Bangs: Ask your stylist for long, side-swept bangs that start from the center of your forehead. This will draw attention away from your forehead and towards your eyes.
  • Beach Waves: Use a curling wand to create loose waves in your hair. This will add volume and texture to the lower half of your face.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as it will make your forehead appear larger.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for heart-shaped faces:

What length of hair is best for a heart-shaped face?

Medium to long hair is best for heart-shaped faces. It adds volume to the lower half of your face and balances your features.

What type of bangs are best for a heart-shaped face?

Side-swept or curtain bangs are best for heart-shaped faces. They help shorten the appearance of your forehead while drawing attention to your eyes.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as it can damage your hair.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for heart shaped face female, it’s time to experiment and find your perfect look. Remember to balance your features and have fun with your hair!
