How To Style Women s Undercut Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Sep 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read
If you're looking for a bold and edgy new hairstyle, the women's undercut is a great option to consider. This trendy haircut features shaved or buzzed sides and a longer top, creating a modern and daring look. But how do you style an undercut once you've got it? In this guide, we'll walk you through the process step by step, including tips and advice for maintaining your new 'do.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The women's undercut hairstyle is a bold and daring choice that involves shaving or buzzing the hair on the sides of the head, leaving the longer hair on top. This look has become increasingly popular in recent years, as more and more women seek out edgier and more unconventional hairstyles. The undercut can be tailored to suit a variety of personal styles, from punk rock to minimalist chic.

How to

Styling a women's undercut is relatively simple, but it does require some basic tools and techniques. Here's what you'll need: - A hairbrush or comb - Hair styling product (such as gel or pomade) - Hair dryer (optional)

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair. Apply a small amount of styling product to the longer hair on top of your head, using your fingers to distribute it evenly. 2. Using a hairbrush or comb, brush the longer hair on top of your head forward, towards your forehead. This will create volume and texture. 3. Next, use your fingers to create a side part in the longer hair. Experiment with different parting positions to find the look that works best for you. 4. Now it's time to tackle the shaved or buzzed sides. Apply a small amount of styling product to the sides of your head, using your fingers to distribute it evenly. 5. Use your fingers to smooth down the shaved or buzzed sides, creating a sleek and polished look. You can also experiment with different textures and finishes, depending on your personal style. 6. If desired, use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to set your style in place. This can help to create a more polished and professional look.

Tips

- Experiment with different styling products to find the perfect one for your hair type and personal style. - If you're new to the undercut hairstyle, start with a less drastic cut and work your way up to a more daring look over time. - Consider consulting with a professional hairstylist to get the perfect cut and style for your face shape and hair type.

FAQ

Q: Will an undercut work for my hair type? A: The undercut can work for a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, it may be more difficult to style for very fine or very thick hair. Q: Can I still wear my hair up with an undercut? A: Yes! The longer hair on top of your head can be styled into a variety of updos and braids, making this a versatile hairstyle choice. Q: How often will I need to get my undercut touched up? A: This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how defined you want your undercut to be. Generally, you'll want to schedule a touch-up appointment every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

To keep your undercut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes: - Washing your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner - Using styling products sparingly to avoid buildup - Protecting your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray - Avoiding excessive brushing or combing, as this can cause damage to the hair follicles With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to style your women's undercut like a pro. Whether you're going for a bold and edgy look, or a more subtle and sophisticated style, the undercut is a versatile and trendy choice that's sure to turn heads.
