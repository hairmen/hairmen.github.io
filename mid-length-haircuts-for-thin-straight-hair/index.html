Mid Length Haircuts For Thin Straight Hair

Written by Bardi Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thin straight hair, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. Mid length haircuts are a great option as they add volume and fullness to thin hair. In this article, we will discuss the best mid length haircuts for thin straight hair, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair after getting a new haircut.

How to Choose the Right Mid Length Haircut for Thin Straight Hair

Before getting a mid length haircut, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. If you have a round face, a layered mid length haircut can help elongate your face. If you have an oval face, you can go for a blunt mid length haircut to accentuate your face features. For hair texture, a graduated bob can add volume to thin hair, while a shaggy mid length haircut can create texture and movement.

Best Mid Length Haircuts for Thin Straight Hair

The following mid length haircuts are perfect for thin straight hair:

  • The Lob: A long bob that sits just above the shoulders. This haircut adds volume to thin hair and is easy to maintain.
  • The Shag: A layered haircut with choppy ends that adds texture and movement to thin hair. This haircut is perfect for those who want a low maintenance style.
  • The Blunt Cut: A straight, one-length cut that adds fullness to thin hair. This haircut is perfect for those with oval face shapes.
  • The Graduated Bob: A stacked bob that is shorter in the back and longer in the front. This haircut adds volume to thin hair and creates a modern, edgy look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Mid Length Haircuts for Thin Straight Hair

Styling mid length haircuts for thin straight hair is easy and can be done in a few simple steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to the roots of your hair. This will add volume and fullness to thin hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends to create volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a straightening iron to smooth out any kinks or flyaways.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mid Length Haircut for Thin Straight Hair

To maintain your mid length haircut for thin straight hair, follow these tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add fullness to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQs

Can I still get a mid length haircut if I have very thin hair?

Yes, you can still get a mid length haircut if you have very thin hair. The key is to choose a haircut that adds volume and fullness to your hair.

How often do I need to get my hair trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Straight Hair

In addition to getting the right haircut and styling your hair correctly, taking care of your hair is also important. Here are some hair care tips for thin straight hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh down your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this will prevent breakage.

In conclusion, mid length haircuts are a great option for those with thin straight hair. By choosing the right haircut and following these styling and hair care tips, you can achieve a beautiful and healthy head of hair.
