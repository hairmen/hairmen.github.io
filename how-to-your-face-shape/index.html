How To Determine Your Face Shape The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that suits you? Well, the first step to achieving that perfect look is by determining your face shape. Understanding your face shape is essential as it helps you choose the right hairstyle, makeup, and accessories. In this article, we will discuss how to determine your face shape and give you tips on how to enhance your features.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Determining your face shape can be a bit tricky, but it is not impossible. There are different methods to determine your face shape, and we will discuss the most common ones below:

Method 1: The Mirror Test

Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back, so your face is fully visible. Take a close look at your face and compare it to the different face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. If you are having trouble identifying your face shape, take a picture of yourself and trace the outline of your face on paper.

Method 2: The Measurement Test

Measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline using a tape measure. Write down the measurements and compare them to the different face shapes. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are of similar width, you have a square or round face. If your forehead is wider than your jawline, you have a heart-shaped face. If your jawline is wider than your forehead, you have a diamond-shaped face. If your forehead and jawline are of similar width, and your face is longer than it is wide, you have an oblong face. If your forehead and jawline are of similar width, and your face is slightly longer than it is wide, you have an oval face.

Step by Step Guide on How to Determine Your Face Shape

Now that you know the different methods of determining your face shape, let us break it down step by step:

Step 1: Pull Your Hair Back

Pull your hair back using a hair tie or clip, so your face is fully visible.

Step 2: Identify Your Forehead

Take a look at your forehead and determine its width. Is it wider than your cheekbones and jawline, or is it of similar width?

Step 3: Identify Your Cheekbones

Take a look at your cheekbones and determine their width. Are they wider than your forehead and jawline, or are they of similar width?

Step 4: Identify Your Jawline

Take a look at your jawline and determine its width. Is it wider than your forehead and cheekbones, or is it of similar width?

Step 5: Compare Your Measurements

Compare your measurements to the different face shapes and determine which one closely resembles your face.

Tips on How to Enhance Your Features

Now that you have determined your face shape, here are some tips on how to enhance your features:

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you can rock any hairstyle. Focus on enhancing your best features, such as your cheekbones or eyes, using makeup.

Round Face

If you have a round face, go for hairstyles that add height and length to your face, such as a high ponytail or an updo. Use contouring makeup techniques to create definition on your cheeks and jawline.

Square Face

If you have a square face, go for hairstyles that soften your features, such as a wavy bob or a layered haircut. Use makeup to create a shadow effect on your jawline.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, go for hairstyles that add volume to your chin area, such as a side-swept fringe or a bob. Use blush to enhance your cheekbones.

Diamond-Shaped Face

If you have a diamond-shaped face, go for hairstyles that add width to your forehead, such as a side-parted bob or a layered haircut. Use contouring makeup techniques to create definition on your cheeks and jawline.

Oblong Face

If you have an oblong face, go for hairstyles that add width to your face, such as a curly bob or a pixie cut. Use makeup to create a shadow effect on your forehead and jawline.

FAQ

Q: Can I have a combination of face shapes?

A: Yes, it is possible to have a combination of face shapes. In that case, focus on the features that are dominant in your face.

Q: Can I change my face shape?

A: No, you cannot change your face shape. However, you can use hairstyles and makeup to enhance your features.

Q: How often should I change my hairstyle?

A: You can change your hairstyle as often as you want. However, it is recommended to change it every six months to a year.

Hair Care Tips for Different Face Shapes

Aside from choosing the right hairstyle, it is also essential to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for different face shapes:

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, go for hairstyles that show off your face shape. Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Round Face

If you have a round face, go for hairstyles that add volume to your crown area. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair from becoming too oily.

Square Face

If you have a square face, go for hairstyles that soften your features. Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, go for hairstyles that add volume to your chin area. Use a hair serum to prevent frizz.

Diamond-Shaped Face

If you have a diamond-shaped face, go for hairstyles that add width to your forehead. Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.

Oblong Face

If you have an oblong face, go for hairstyles that add width to your face. Use a dry shampoo to keep your hair fresh between washes.

Now that you know how to determine your face shape, go ahead and try out different hairstyles and makeup looks. Remember, the key to looking your best is by enhancing your natural features. Happy styling!
