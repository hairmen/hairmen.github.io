Men s Haircut Fade Long On Top Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this year 2023, men's hairstyles are becoming more diverse and creative. One of the most popular hairstyles for men with curly hair is the fade long on top. This style is versatile, low-maintenance, and perfect for men who want to look stylish without putting in too much effort. In this blog post, we will discuss how to get the perfect men's haircut fade long on top curly hair, step by step, tips, FAQ and Hair Care.

Description

The men's haircut fade long on top curly hair is a combination of a fade and long curly hair on top. The fade is a gradual tapering of hair length from the sides and back of the head towards the hairline. This style is perfect for men who want to showcase their curly hair on top while keeping the sides and back clean and neat.

How To

If you are considering getting a men's haircut fade long on top curly hair, here are the steps you can follow:

Step 1: Choose Your Style

There are many variations of the men's haircut fade long on top curly hair. You can choose a high or low fade, a skin fade, or a mid-fade. You can also opt for a textured or slicked-back look. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing your look.

Step 2: Find a Good Barber

Getting a men's haircut fade long on top curly hair requires skill and expertise. Look for a barber who specializes in men's curly hair and has experience with fade haircuts. Ask for recommendations from friends or check online reviews to find a good barber near you.

Step 3: Communicate with Your Barber

Once you have found a good barber, communicate your desired style and preferences. Show them pictures of the hairstyle you want and discuss any concerns or questions you may have. It's crucial to have clear communication with your barber to achieve the desired look.

Step 4: Get the Haircut

During the haircut, your barber will start with the fade and gradually blend the hair length towards the top. They will then trim and shape the curly hair on top to your desired length and style. The process can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the complexity of the style.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your men's haircut fade long on top curly hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your curly hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to define and enhance your curls.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

FAQ

Q: Can I get a men's haircut fade long on top curly hair if my hair is not naturally curly?

A: Yes, you can still get this style even if your hair is not naturally curly. Your barber can use a curling iron or texturizing technique to create the desired curl pattern on top.

Q: How often should I get a men's haircut fade long on top curly hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the style. On average, you should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the shape and length of your haircut.

Q: Is the men's haircut fade long on top curly hair suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this style is versatile and can be customized to suit different face shapes. Your barber can adjust the length and shape of the fade and top to complement your face shape.

Hair Care

To keep your men's haircut fade long on top curly hair looking its best, it's essential to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner at least twice a week.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to define and enhance your curls.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

In conclusion, the men's haircut fade long on top curly hair is a stylish and low-maintenance hairstyle that is perfect for men with curly hair. By following the steps and tips mentioned in this blog post, you can achieve the perfect look and keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Remember to communicate clearly with your barber and maintain a good hair care routine to keep your hairstyle looking its best.
