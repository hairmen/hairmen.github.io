Shoulder Length Hair For Oval Shaped Faces The Ultimate Guide

Are you an oval shaped beauty looking for the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape? Look no further than shoulder length hair! In this ultimate guide, we'll cover everything from how to style it to hair care tips. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Shoulder Length Hair for Oval Shaped Faces
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair
  • FAQs

Description

Shoulder length hair is the perfect length for oval shaped faces. It's not too short to make your face appear wider, and not too long to drag your face down. This length frames your face beautifully and accentuates your natural features. The best part? Shoulder length hair is versatile and can be styled in so many different ways!

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair for Oval Shaped Faces

There are so many different ways to style shoulder length hair for oval shaped faces. Some of our favorites include:

1. Straight and Sleek

Straightening your shoulder length hair will give you a sleek and sophisticated look. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair, and finish off with a shine spray to give your hair a glossy finish.

2. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are perfect for a more casual and carefree look. Use a curling iron to create loose waves in your hair, and finish off with a sea salt spray to give your hair that beachy texture.

3. Half Up, Half Down

The half up, half down style is perfect for those days when you want your hair out of your face but still want it to look cute. Simply tie the top half of your hair into a ponytail or bun, and leave the rest of your hair down.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to styling your shoulder length hair:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start styling. If you're using heat tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Decide on your desired style

Choose one of the above styles or come up with your own!

3. Style your hair

Use a flat iron, curling iron, or hair tie to style your hair according to your desired style.

4. Finish with hairspray or other products

Use hairspray or other styling products to keep your hair in place and looking great all day long.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your shoulder length hair:

1. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

2. Use a heat protectant spray

If you're using heat tools on your hair, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair soft and shiny.

4. Avoid over-washing your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every three days.

FAQs

Q: Is shoulder length hair suitable for all face shapes?

A: While shoulder length hair looks great on oval shaped faces, it can also work on other face shapes. It's best to consult with a hairstylist to find out what length and style works best for your face shape.

Q: How often should I wash my shoulder length hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day or every three days to avoid over-washing.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with shoulder length hair?

A: Absolutely! There are plenty of cute updos that work great with shoulder length hair, like the half up, half down style mentioned earlier.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your shoulder length hair looking healthy and beautiful:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb

When combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage and damage.

2. Protect your hair from the sun

Exposure to the sun can damage your hair, so be sure to wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF when spending time outside.

3. Avoid using too much heat

Using too much heat on your hair can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit your use of heat tools as much as possible.

That wraps up our ultimate guide to shoulder length hair for oval shaped faces! We hope you found these tips and tricks helpful. Happy styling!
