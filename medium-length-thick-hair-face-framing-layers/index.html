Medium Length Thick Hair Face Framing Layers A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your thick hair falling flat and lacking shape? Adding face framing layers to your medium length hair can give it the perfect amount of volume and movement. In this guide, we will walk you through everything you need to know about medium length thick hair face framing layers, including how to style them and tips for proper hair care.

Description

Medium length thick hair face framing layers are layers that are cut around the front of the face to add dimension and shape. The layers are typically shorter than the rest of the hair and are angled to create a flattering frame around the face. This style works well for those with thick hair, as the layers help to remove some of the weight and add movement.

How To

The best way to achieve medium length thick hair face framing layers is to visit a professional stylist. They will be able to assess your hair type and face shape and determine the best length and angle for your layers. However, if you prefer to cut your own hair, here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. It is best to cut your hair when it is dry, as wet hair can appear longer than it actually is.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair by pulling the top section up and securing it with a clip.

Step 3: Cut the layers

Starting with the front section, hold a small piece of hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle. Repeat this process, gradually working your way back to the crown of your head. Be sure to cut the layers at different lengths to create a natural-looking frame around your face.

Step 4: Blend the layers

Once you have cut all of the layers, use a comb to blend them together. You can also use thinning shears to remove some of the bulk, if necessary.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling and caring for your medium length thick hair face framing layers:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and body to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it appear greasy.
  • When blow drying your hair, use a round brush to add volume and shape to your layers.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to enhance the dimension of your layers.
  • Trim your layers every 6-8 weeks to maintain their shape and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What face shape does medium length thick hair face framing layers work best on?

Medium length thick hair face framing layers work well on all face shapes, but are particularly flattering on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Can I still wear my hair up with face framing layers?

Absolutely! Face framing layers can be styled in a variety of ways, including updos and ponytails.

Will face framing layers make my hair look thinner?

No, face framing layers will not make your hair look thinner. In fact, they can add volume and movement to thick hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your medium length thick hair face framing layers looking their best. Here are some tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it appear dull.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock your medium length thick hair face framing layers with confidence and ease!
