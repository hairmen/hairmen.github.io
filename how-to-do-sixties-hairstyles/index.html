How To Do Sixties Hairstyles A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 24, 2023 · 3 min read

The sixties was a decade of bold fashion statements, and hairstyles were no exception. From bouffants to beehives, women experimented with different styles that made a statement. In this article, we will guide you through how to do sixties hairstyles, step-by-step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do Sixties Hairstyles
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Sixties Hairstyles
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Sixties Hairstyles

How to Do Sixties Hairstyles

The sixties hairstyles are all about volume, texture, and height. These hairstyles are perfect for adding a touch of vintage glamour to your look. Here are some of the most popular sixties hairstyles that you can try at home:

  • Bouffant
  • Beehive
  • Mod Cut
  • Pixie Cut
  • Shag Cut
  • Bob Cut

Each of these styles requires a different technique, but the general rule of thumb is to add volume to your hair by teasing it, using hair rollers or a curling iron, and backcombing it.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to do a bouffant, one of the most popular sixties hairstyles:

  1. Start by sectioning off the top half of your hair and pinning it up.
  2. Tease the bottom half of your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up. Use a comb or a teasing brush to backcomb your hair.
  3. Once you have teased your hair, smooth out the top layer with a brush or a comb.
  4. Take the top section of your hair and tease it at the roots, just like you did with the bottom section.
  5. Smooth out the top layer with a brush or a comb.
  6. Gently pull your hair back and secure it in a ponytail, leaving some hair out at the front.
  7. Roll the ponytail under and secure it with bobby pins.
  8. Take the front section of your hair and tease it at the roots.
  9. Smooth out the top layer with a brush or a comb.
  10. Take the teased hair and gently sweep it back, securing it with bobby pins.
  11. Finish with a hairspray to hold your bouffant in place.

Tips for Sixties Hairstyles

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect sixties hairstyle:

  • Tease your hair gently to avoid damaging it.
  • Use a hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.
  • Accessorize your hairstyle with a headband or a scarf.
  • Experiment with different hair textures and colors.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about sixties hairstyles:

What hairstyles were popular in the sixties?

The most popular sixties hairstyles were bouffants, beehives, mod cuts, pixie cuts, shag cuts, and bob cuts.

How do I get a beehive hairstyle?

To get a beehive hairstyle, start by teasing your hair at the crown and smoothing it out with a brush. Then, gather your hair into a high ponytail and backcomb it. Roll the ponytail under and secure it with bobby pins.

What hairstyles suit a sixties theme?

Hairstyles that suit a sixties theme include bouffants, beehives, and mod cuts.

Hair Care for Sixties Hairstyles

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair when styling it in a sixties hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools like a curling iron or a straightener.
  • Wash your hair regularly to avoid product buildup.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray as it can dry out your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily achieve a sixties hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion.
