How To Cut Your Own Hair Short Pixie With Clippers

Written by Daniel Jun 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting your own hair short pixie with clippers can be a daunting task, but with the right tools, technique, and a little bit of patience, you can achieve a salon-worthy haircut right at home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting your own hair short pixie with clippers, from preparation to finishing touches.

Preparation

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have a clean and dry hair. Wash and condition your hair thoroughly and then blow-dry it until it is completely dry. This will help you to see the hair strands clearly and make cutting easier.

Tools Needed

It is important to have the right tools to ensure a successful haircut. Here are the tools you will need:

  • Clippers with different guard sizes
  • Comb
  • Hair cutting scissors
  • Hair clips
  • Mirror

How to Cut Your Own Hair Short Pixie with Clippers

Follow these step-by-step instructions to cut your own hair short pixie with clippers:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: two on the sides, one on the back, and one on the top. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 2: Choose the Guard Size

Choose the desired guard size for the length you want to achieve. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to shorter guard sizes until you reach the desired length.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Start with the sides by using clippers with the guard size you have chosen. Start at the bottom and work your way up, making sure to keep the clippers flush against your head.

Step 4: Move to the Back

Move to the back of your head and use the clippers to cut the hair in an upwards motion. Make sure to blend the sides with the back to create a seamless look.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Use the hair cutting scissors to cut the top of your hair. Start by combing the hair forward and cutting it to the desired length.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Use the scissors to blend the hair, making sure that there are no harsh lines or unevenness.

Step 7: Clean Up

After you have finished cutting your hair, use the clippers without a guard to clean up the hairline around your ears and neck.

Tips for a Successful Haircut

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to shorter guard sizes until you reach the desired length.
  • Use hair clips to keep each section of hair in place.
  • Take your time and be patient. Rushing can lead to unevenness or mistakes.
  • Make sure to blend the sides with the back to create a seamless look.
  • Use the scissors to blend the hair, making sure that there are no harsh lines or unevenness.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my own hair short pixie with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair short pixie with clippers. However, it is important to have the right tools and to take your time to ensure a successful haircut.

Do I need to wash my hair before cutting it?

Yes, it is recommended to wash and dry your hair before cutting it. This will help you to see the hair strands clearly and make cutting easier.

Can I use the same guard size for the entire haircut?

No, it is important to use different guard sizes to achieve the desired length and to create a seamless look.

Hair Care After the Haircut

After cutting your hair short pixie with clippers, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair soft and shiny.

Cutting your own hair short pixie with clippers can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a salon-worthy haircut right at home. Remember to take your time, be patient, and follow the steps carefully to ensure a successful haircut.
