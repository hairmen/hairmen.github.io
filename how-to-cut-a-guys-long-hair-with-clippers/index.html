How To Cut A Guy s Long Hair With Clippers

Written by Robby Jul 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune for haircuts? Are you looking for a way to get your hair trimmed without leaving your home? If you answered yes to either of these questions, you've come to the right place. In this post, we'll show you how to cut a guy's long hair with clippers. We'll provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice. So, without further ado, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Cut a Guy's Long Hair with Clippers
  2. Step-by-Step Instructions
  3. Tips for Cutting Long Hair with Clippers
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care after Cutting

How to Cut a Guy's Long Hair with Clippers

Cutting long hair with clippers can be intimidating, but it's not as difficult as it seems. Here's what you'll need:

  • A set of clippers with guards
  • A comb
  • A spray bottle with water
  • A mirror

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it's completely dry before you start cutting.
  2. Decide on the length you want to cut your hair. Choose the right guard size for the length you want. Start with a larger guard and work your way down if you want it shorter.
  3. Start at the back of your head, and work your way up to the top. Use the longest guard first and move to shorter ones until you reach the desired length.
  4. Use the comb to lift the hair away from your scalp. Run the clippers over the comb, cutting the hair above it.
  5. Repeat this process around your head, making sure to cut the hair evenly.
  6. Next, trim the sides and the back. Use a shorter guard to create a fade or a taper.
  7. Blend the top and the sides using scissors or clippers without a guard. Use the comb to lift the hair up and cut the ends off.
  8. Cut the hair on the top of your head. Use scissors or clippers without a guard. Cut the hair to the desired length and blend it with the sides.
  9. Trim your bangs or fringe. Use scissors to cut them to the desired length, making sure they're even.
  10. Check all angles in the mirror to ensure that your hair is even and at the desired length.

Tips for Cutting Long Hair with Clippers

Here are some tips to make the process easier:

  • Use a spray bottle to dampen your hair. Wet hair is easier to cut and style.
  • Don't cut your hair too short. It's easier to cut more hair if you need to than to wait for it to grow back if you cut it too short.
  • Start with a longer guard to get a feel for the clippers before moving to a shorter one.
  • Use a comb to lift your hair up and away from your scalp. This will ensure an even cut.
  • Take your time. Cutting your hair can be time-consuming, but it's worth it to get it right.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about cutting long hair with clippers:

  • Can I cut my hair wet?

    • Yes, you can cut your hair wet. Wet hair is easier to cut and style than dry hair.

  • Do I need to use scissors?

    • No, you don't need to use scissors. You can use clippers with guards to cut your hair.

  • How often should I cut my hair?

    • It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want it to be. On average, most people get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

  • Can I cut my own hair?

    • Yes, you can cut your own hair. It may take some practice, but it's possible to get a great haircut at home.

Hair Care after Cutting

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair smooth and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy.

And that's it! You now know how to cut a guy's long hair with clippers. With a little practice, you'll be able to give yourself a great haircut in no time.
