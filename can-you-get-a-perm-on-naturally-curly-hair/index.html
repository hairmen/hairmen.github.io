Can You Get A Perm On Naturally Curly Hair

Written by Oliver Oct 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have naturally curly hair, you might be wondering if it's possible to get a perm. Perming is a chemical process that changes the texture of your hair, making it curly or wavy. However, if you already have curly hair, you might be concerned that perming will damage your hair or make it too curly.

Description

In this article, we'll answer the question "can you get a perm on naturally curly hair?" We'll also give you some tips and advice on how to prepare your hair for a perm, how to care for your permed curls, and how to maintain healthy hair after perming.

How to Get a Perm on Naturally Curly Hair

If you have naturally curly hair, getting a perm can be a great way to add some extra volume and texture to your hair. However, there are some steps you should take to prepare your hair for the perming process:

Step 1: Choose the Right Perm Solution

Before you get a perm, you need to choose the right perm solution for your hair type. If you have naturally curly hair, you'll want to choose a mild perm solution that won't damage your hair or make it too curly. Talk to your hairstylist about the best perm solution for your hair.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

To prepare your hair for a perm, you'll need to wash it with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue. Then, you'll need to towel-dry your hair and comb it out to remove any tangles or knots. Your hairstylist will then apply the perm solution to your hair, wrapping it in perm rods to create the desired curl pattern.

Step 3: Wait and Rinse

Once the perm solution has been applied, you'll need to wait for it to set. This can take up to 30 minutes, depending on the strength of the perm solution. Your hairstylist will then rinse your hair with warm water and apply a neutralizer to set the curls.

Tips for Perming Naturally Curly Hair

Perming naturally curly hair can be a bit tricky, but there are some tips and tricks that can help you achieve beautiful, healthy curls:

Tip 1: Choose the Right Hairstylist

When it comes to perming naturally curly hair, it's important to choose a hairstylist who has experience working with curly hair. Look for someone who specializes in curly hair and ask to see some before-and-after photos of their work.

Tip 2: Use a Moisturizing Conditioner

Perming can be drying to your hair, so it's important to use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and healthy. Look for a conditioner that contains natural oils like coconut oil or argan oil.

Tip 3: Don't Over-Process Your Hair

Over-processing your hair can lead to damage and breakage, so it's important to follow your hairstylist's instructions carefully. Don't leave the perm solution on your hair for too long, and don't use too strong of a perm solution.

FAQs

Q: Will perming my curly hair make it too curly?

A: It's possible, but if you choose the right perm solution and follow your hairstylist's instructions, you should be able to achieve beautiful, natural-looking curls.

Q: Will perming damage my curly hair?

A: Perming can be drying to your hair, but if you use a moisturizing conditioner and follow proper hair care practices, you should be able to maintain healthy hair after perming.

Hair Care After Perming

After perming your hair, it's important to take good care of your curls to keep them healthy and beautiful:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for a shampoo that's specially formulated for curly hair.

Tip 2: Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep condition your hair once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy. Use a deep conditioning treatment that contains natural oils and proteins.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your curls and lead to breakage. Instead, embrace your natural curls and use a diffuser to enhance them.

Conclusion

So, can you get a perm on naturally curly hair? The answer is yes, but it's important to choose the right perm solution and follow proper hair care practices to maintain healthy, beautiful curls. If you're considering perming your hair, talk to your hairstylist about the best options for your hair type and follow our tips and advice for healthy, happy curls!
