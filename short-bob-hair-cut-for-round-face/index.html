Short Bob Hair Cut For Round Face

Written by Christine Jun 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering what kind of hairstyle would suit you. One of the most popular options for a round face is a short bob haircut. In this article, we will discuss how to choose the right short bob haircut for your round face and share some tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

A short bob haircut is a classic hairstyle that can be tailored to suit any face shape. For a round face, a short bob can help to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more angular jawline. There are many different types of short bob haircuts to choose from, including blunt bobs, layered bobs, and asymmetrical bobs.

How to Choose the Right Short Bob Haircut for Round Face

When choosing a short bob haircut for a round face, it's important to consider the length, layers, and angle of the cut. Here are some tips to help you choose the right short bob haircut:

Length

The length of the bob should fall somewhere between the chin and the shoulders. If the bob is too short, it can make the face appear wider. If the bob is too long, it can hide the jawline and create a rounder appearance.

Layers

Layers can help to add volume and movement to a short bob haircut. For a round face, layers should be added to the top and sides of the hair to create height and length. Avoid adding layers to the bottom of the hair, as this can make the face appear wider.

Angle

The angle of the bob can also help to elongate the face. A bob that is shorter in the back and longer in the front can create the illusion of a longer face. This is known as an asymmetrical bob.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to get a short bob haircut:

Step 1: Consultation

Before getting a haircut, it's important to have a consultation with your hairstylist. Discuss the type of short bob haircut you want and ask for their opinion on what would suit your face shape.

Step 2: Wash and Dry

Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly.

Step 3: Section

Section your hair into three parts: the top, the sides, and the back.

Step 4: Cut

Cut the back section of your hair first, following the angle you want. Then cut the sides and the top, adding layers where necessary.

Step 5: Style

Style your new short bob haircut using a blow dryer, curling iron, or straightening iron.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short bob haircut:

  • Bring a picture of the short bob haircut you want to your hairstylist.
  • Choose a hairstylist who has experience cutting hair for round faces.
  • Use a round brush when blow drying your hair to create volume.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it appear flat.

FAQ

What face shapes suit a short bob haircut?

A short bob haircut can suit most face shapes, but it's particularly flattering for oval, heart-shaped, and square faces.

How often should I get a short bob haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 8-12 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your short bob haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your short bob haircut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With the right short bob haircut and hair care routine, you can enhance the natural beauty of your round face and feel confident in your new hairstyle.
