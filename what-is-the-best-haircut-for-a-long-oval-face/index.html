What Is The Best Haircut For A Long Oval Face

Written by Daniel Sep 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a long oval face, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that will flatter your face shape and bring out your best features. In this blog post, we will discuss the best haircut for a long oval face, along with some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A long oval face is characterized by a forehead, cheekbones, and jawline that are all roughly the same width. This face shape is longer than it is wide, with a narrow chin and high forehead. The best haircut for a long oval face is one that adds volume and width to the sides of the face, while also creating a balanced look.

How To

The best way to achieve the perfect haircut for a long oval face is to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you determine the best style for your face shape and recommend products and techniques to help you achieve your desired look.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a haircut for a long oval face:

  1. Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best style for your face shape.
  2. Show your stylist pictures of hairstyles that you like.
  3. Ask your stylist to add volume and width to the sides of your face.
  4. Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and texture.
  5. Opt for a side part to create the illusion of width.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut for a long oval face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can make your face appear even longer.
  • Consider bangs to create the illusion of a shorter forehead.
  • Avoid center parts, as they can make your face appear even longer.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume and width to the sides of your face.
  • Consider adding highlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for long oval faces:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long oval face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long, center parts, and hairstyles that add height to the top of your head.

What hairstyles are best for a long oval face?

Hairstyles that add volume and width to the sides of your face are best for a long oval face.

Should I consider bangs if I have a long oval face?

Yes, bangs can help create the illusion of a shorter forehead.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining a healthy and stylish haircut. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Regularly trim your hair to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it appear greasy.

By following these tips and getting the right haircut for your long oval face, you can achieve a stylish and flattering look that brings out your best features. Remember to consult with a professional stylist and take proper care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
