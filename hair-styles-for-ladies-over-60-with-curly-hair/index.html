Hair Styles For Ladies Over 60 With Curly Hair

Written by Petter Jul 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are over 60 and have curly hair, you might be searching for the perfect hairstyle that suits your personality and age group. Although there are plenty of hairstyles for curly hair, not all of them are suitable for older women. In this article, we will guide you through some of the best hair styles for ladies over 60 with curly hair.

Description

As women age, their hair becomes thinner, which makes it challenging to style. However, curly hair is an exception to this rule as it tends to keep its volume and shape even with age. Curly hair is also versatile, which means you can create various hairstyles that suit your style and preference.

How to

First and foremost, it's essential to keep your curly hair healthy and nourished. This means using the right hair products that suit your hair type and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. Next, consider getting a haircut that flatters your face shape and enhances your natural curls. Some of the best hairstyles for ladies over 60 with curly hair include:

1. Short Curls

Short curly hair is an excellent option for older women as it's easy to maintain and style. A layered bob or a pixie cut can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look youthful and vibrant.

2. Long Layers

If you prefer longer hair, opt for long layers that add movement and dimension to your curls. You can also add bangs to frame your face and create a youthful look.

3. Updo

If you have a special occasion, consider an updo that showcases your curly hair. A messy bun or a braided updo can add elegance and sophistication to your overall look.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair while it's still damp.

3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair gently.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define your curls.

5. Finish off with a hairspray or a hair oil to keep your curls intact and frizz-free.

Tips

- Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis as it can damage your hair.

- Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

- Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits your face shape and personality.

FAQ

What hair products are best for curly hair?

Look for sulfate-free shampoos, curl-enhancing creams, and leave-in conditioners that hydrate and nourish your hair.

Can I color my curly hair?

Yes, you can color your curly hair, but it's essential to use hair products that protect your hair from damage.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once a week. If you have oily hair, you can wash it every other day.

Hair Care

To keep your curly hair healthy and vibrant, follow these tips:

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.

- Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

- Avoid using hot water on your hair as it can strip away its natural oils.

- Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or a scarf.

In conclusion, curly hair can be a blessing in disguise for ladies over 60 as it can add volume, texture, and movement to their hair. By following these tips and trying out different hairstyles, you can embrace your natural curls and look stylish and youthful at any age.
