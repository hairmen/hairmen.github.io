How To Point Cut Hair A Step By Step Guide

Point cutting is a hair cutting technique that involves using scissors to cut the hair at an angle, creating a textured and layered look. It's a popular technique that gives a soft and natural finish to the hair. In this article, we'll show you how to point cut hair in a relaxed and easy-to-understand way.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Point Cut Hair

Point cutting is a technique that involves cutting the hair at an angle, creating a textured and layered look. It's important to use high-quality scissors that are sharp and have a pointed tip. It's also important to use the right technique to avoid damaging the hair or creating an uneven look.

Tools You'll Need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Water spray bottle
  • Comb
  • Clips or hair ties

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to point cut your hair:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into manageable sections using clips or hair ties.
  3. Take a section of hair and comb it straight.
  4. Spray a small amount of water on the section of hair.
  5. Hold your scissors at a 45-degree angle and gently cut into the hair, pointing the scissors upwards.
  6. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair, cutting at different angles for a textured look.
  7. After you have finished cutting, comb through your hair to remove any tangles or loose hairs.

Tips for Point Cutting Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when point cutting your hair:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid mistakes.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as this can create an uneven look.
  • Make sure to cut the hair at an angle for a textured and layered look.
  • Use a water spray bottle to dampen the hair before cutting, as this will make it easier to cut.

FAQs

Can I point cut my own hair?

Yes, you can point cut your own hair, but it's important to be careful and start with small sections of hair. It's also important to use sharp scissors and the right technique to avoid damaging your hair.

Is point cutting good for curly hair?

Yes, point cutting is a good technique for curly hair, as it creates a textured and layered look that enhances the natural curls.

How often should I point cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows, but typically, you should point cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and texture.

Hair Care after Point Cutting

After point cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain the shape and texture. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and texture.

Point cutting is a simple and effective technique that can give your hair a soft and natural look. Follow these tips and techniques to achieve the best results and maintain healthy hair.
