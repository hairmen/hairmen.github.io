How To Cut Guys Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this year 2023, cutting your own hair is becoming increasingly popular. With the help of clippers, it has become easier than ever before to achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting guys hair with clippers, including tips and tricks to make the process as smooth as possible.

Description

Clippers are an essential tool for men who want to keep their hair neat and tidy without spending a lot of money on professional haircuts. Even if you have never used clippers before, with a little bit of practice, you can learn how to cut your own hair in no time.

How to Cut Guys Hair with Clippers

Step 1: Prepare Your Clippers

The first step in cutting guys hair with clippers is to ensure that your clippers are clean and well-oiled. You should also make sure that the blades are sharp, as dull blades can pull and tug at your hair, making the process uncomfortable.

Step 2: Choose Your Guard Length

Clipper guards come in various sizes, which determine the length of hair that will be left behind. You should choose a guard length that corresponds to the length of hair that you want to leave behind. If you are unsure, it is always better to start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

The sides of your head are usually the easiest to cut, so it is best to start with them. Begin by attaching the guard to your clippers and then starting at the bottom of your head, work your way up towards your temples. Use short, even strokes to ensure that the hair is cut evenly.

Step 4: Move to the Back

Once you have finished the sides, move to the back of your head. Use the same technique that you used on the sides, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Be sure to check your work in the mirror to ensure that the hair is cut evenly.

Step 5: Cut the Top

The top of your head is usually the most difficult part to cut, as it requires more precision and attention to detail. Start by using a longer guard and then work your way down to a shorter one until you achieve the desired length. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that the clippers may have missed.

Tips

- It is always better to start with a longer guard than you think you need. You can always go shorter, but you can't go longer once the hair has been cut.
- Use a mirror to check your work as you go.
- Don't rush. Take your time and work slowly to ensure that the hair is cut evenly.
- If you are unsure, it is always better to seek the help of a professional.

FAQ

Can I use clippers to cut my hair if it is wet?

It is always better to cut your hair when it is dry. Wet hair is more difficult to cut evenly, and it can also cause your clippers to become clogged with hair.

How often should I cut my hair with clippers?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how neat you want to keep it. As a general rule, it is best to cut your hair with clippers every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair with clippers, it is important to take good care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. Regular trims will also help to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Cutting guys hair with clippers is easier than you might think. With a little bit of practice and the right tools, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. So why not give it a try?
