How To Put Guards On Remington Clippers

Written by Robby Jun 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you want to keep your hair trimmed and styled, the Remington clipper is a great tool to have. However, if you're not familiar with it, putting guards on the clipper can be a little tricky. In this article, we'll show you how to put guards on Remington clippers in a few easy steps.

Description

Guards are attachments that go on the Remington clipper, which help you control the length of your hair. They come in different sizes, ranging from 0.5mm to 25mm. If you're using the clipper for the first time, it's important to know how to put guards on it, so you can get the best results.

How to

Here's how to put guards on Remington clippers:

Step 1:

Turn off the clipper and unplug it from the power source.

Step 2:

Choose the guard size you want to use.

Step 3:

Hold the clipper in one hand and the guard in the other.

Step 4:

Align the teeth of the guard with the blades of the clipper.

Step 5:

Slide the guard onto the clipper until it clicks into place.

Step 6:

Turn on the clipper and start trimming your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you put guards on Remington clippers more easily:

  • Make sure the clipper and guard are both clean before using them.
  • Hold the guard firmly to prevent it from slipping.
  • If the guard doesn't click into place, try adjusting it slightly until it fits.
  • Use a smaller guard size for shorter hair and a larger size for longer hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about putting guards on Remington clippers:

Q: Can I use different brands of guards on my Remington clipper?

A: It's best to use Remington guards with your Remington clipper, as they are designed to fit perfectly.

Q: Do I need to oil the clipper blades before attaching a guard?

A: Yes, it's a good idea to oil the blades before using the clipper, whether you're using a guard or not.

Q: How often should I replace my Remington clipper guards?

A: It depends on how often you use them and how well you take care of them. If they become damaged or worn, it's time to replace them.

Hair Care

Using guards on your Remington clipper can help you maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair looking neat and tidy. However, it's important to take care of your hair as well. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to put guards on your Remington clippers with ease and keep your hair looking great.
