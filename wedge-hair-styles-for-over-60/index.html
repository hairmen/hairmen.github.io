Wedge Hair Styles For Over 60 A Guide To Looking Fabulous

Written by William Sep 29, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're over 60 and looking for a new hairstyle, why not consider a wedge cut? This classic hairstyle is versatile and easy to maintain, making it perfect for seniors. In this article, we'll show you how to get the perfect wedge cut, as well as some tips for caring for your new style.

Description: What is a Wedge Haircut?

A wedge haircut is a short, layered cut that's longer in the front and shorter in the back. It's called a "wedge" because the layers create a triangular shape that looks like a wedge of cheese or cake. The hairstyle was popularized in the 1970s by actress Dorothy Hamill, but it's still a trendy and stylish choice today.

How to: Getting the Perfect Wedge Cut

If you're ready to try a wedge cut, here's how to get the perfect look:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

The first step is to decide how short you want your hair to be. Wedge cuts can vary in length, so consider what will look best with your face shape and hair texture.

Step 2: Find a Stylist

Once you've decided on your length, it's time to find a stylist who can give you the perfect wedge cut. Look for someone who has experience with this type of cut, and bring in pictures of the style you want.

Step 3: Get the Cut

During your appointment, your stylist will cut your hair to the desired length and add layers to create the wedge shape. They may also add bangs or other styling elements to complete the look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Wedge Cut

Now that you have your new wedge cut, here are some tips for maintaining it:

Use the Right Products

Invest in high-quality hair products that are designed for your hair type. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add fullness and body to your hair.

Visit Your Stylist Regularly

Wedge cuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim and to maintain the shape of your cut.

Experiment with Styling

Wedge cuts are versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

What Face Shapes Are Best Suited for a Wedge Cut?

Wedge cuts are versatile and can flatter many different face shapes, but they're especially flattering on heart-shaped and oval faces.

Can Wedge Cuts Be Worn by Women with Thinning Hair?

Yes! Wedge cuts can actually make hair look fuller and thicker. Talk to your stylist about adding layers to create volume and body.

Hair Care: Keeping Your Wedge Cut Healthy

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep your wedge cut looking its best. Here are some tips:

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can damage hair and make it look dry and brittle. Try to air dry your hair whenever possible, and use heat protectant products when you do use styling tools.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage hair and make it look dull and lifeless. Wear a hat or use a hair product with UV protection when you're spending time outdoors.

Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dry and frizzy. Try to wash your hair every 2-3 days, and use a dry shampoo in between washes if needed.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a fabulous wedge cut at any age. So go ahead and book that appointment with your stylist – you won't regret it!
