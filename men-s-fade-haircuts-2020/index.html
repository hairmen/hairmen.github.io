Men s Fade Haircuts 2020 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Jul 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a fresh, trendy hairstyle for 2020, look no further than the men's fade haircut. This classic cut has been updated with modern twists, making it a popular choice for men of all ages and hair types. In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about the men's fade haircut, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care.

Description

A fade haircut is a style that features a gradual transition from short to longer hair. The hair is typically buzzed or trimmed very short at the sides and back, while the top is left longer. This creates a tapered effect that blends the hair seamlessly from one length to another. The men's fade haircut has been around for decades, but the 2020 version is all about adding texture and volume to the top and incorporating bold, creative designs into the sides.

How to Get a Men's Fade Haircut

If you're ready to try out the men's fade haircut for yourself, here's what you need to know:

Step 1: Choose Your Fade Type

There are several different types of fades to choose from, including the high fade, low fade, and mid fade. The high fade starts at the temples and gradually gets shorter towards the top of the head, while the low fade starts at the nape of the neck and gradually gets shorter towards the ears. The mid fade is somewhere in between. Choose the type of fade that suits your face shape and personal style.

Step 2: Decide on Your Hair Length

The length of your hair on top will depend on your personal preference and the style you're going for. Some men prefer a short, textured top, while others like a longer, slicked back look. Talk to your stylist about the best hair length for your face shape and hair type.

Step 3: Bring in Inspiration Pictures

If you have a particular style in mind, bring in pictures to show your stylist. This will help them understand exactly what you're looking for and give them a clear idea of the finished result.

Tips for Styling Your Men's Fade Haircut

Once you've got your new fade haircut, it's time to style it. Here are some tips for getting the most out of your new look:

Tip 1: Use a Hair Styling Product

A good hair styling product can help you achieve the perfect look for your men's fade haircut. Use a pomade, wax, or gel to add texture and hold to your hair. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and styling needs.

Tip 2: Try a Slicked Back Look

For a classic, sophisticated look, try slicking your hair back. Use a comb or your fingers to push your hair back from your forehead, and then apply a styling product to keep it in place.

Tip 3: Embrace the Messy Look

If you prefer a more casual, laid-back style, embrace the messy look. Use your fingers to work a styling product through your hair, creating a tousled, textured effect.

FAQ

What Face Shapes Suit a Men's Fade Haircut?

A fade haircut can suit almost any face shape, but certain types of fades work better with certain face shapes. For example, a high fade can help elongate a round face, while a low fade can balance out a long face.

How Often Should I Get a Men's Fade Haircut?

How often you get a fade haircut depends on how quickly your hair grows and how neat you like to keep it. As a general rule, you should aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Can I Get Creative Designs in My Men's Fade Haircut?

Yes! One of the biggest trends in men's fade haircuts is incorporating bold, creative designs into the sides of the hair. Talk to your stylist about the different design options available.

Hair Care for Men's Fade Haircuts

To keep your men's fade haircut looking fresh and stylish, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type to keep it healthy and nourished.

Tip 2: Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and leave it looking dull and dry. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

To maintain the shape and style of your men's fade haircut, it's important to get regular trims. Aim for a trim every 4-6 weeks.

Conclusion

The men's fade haircut is a classic style that's been updated for 2020 with modern twists and creative designs. Whether you prefer a high, low, or mid fade, there's a style to suit every face shape and personal preference. Use these tips and tricks to get the most out of your new men's fade haircut, and don't forget to take good care of your hair for a fresh, stylish look every day.
