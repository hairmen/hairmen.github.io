Do Bangs Suit Oblong Faces

Written by Frank Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oblong face, you may have wondered if bangs would suit you. Bangs can be a great way to add some dimension to your face and highlight your features. However, with so many different face shapes and types of bangs, it can be hard to know what will work best for you. In this article, we'll explore whether bangs suit oblong faces and offer some tips for finding the perfect style.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oblong face is longer than it is wide, with a straighter jawline and more angular features. Bangs can help to balance out this shape by adding width to the forehead and softening the angles of the face. However, not all types of bangs will work for every oblong face. It's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a bang style.

How to

When considering whether bangs will suit your oblong face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Face shape: Bangs can work for oblong faces, but it's important to choose a style that will complement your features. Soft, side-swept bangs can help to break up the length of the face and add some curves.
  • Hair texture: The texture of your hair can also play a role in whether bangs will work for you. If you have curly or wavy hair, you may need to style your bangs more frequently to keep them looking neat.
  • Personal style: Ultimately, the best bang style for you will depend on your personal style and preferences. Think about whether you want a bold, edgy look or something more subtle and classic.

Step by step

If you're considering getting bangs for your oblong face, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Consider your face shape and features to determine what type of bangs will work best for you.
  2. Consult with a hairstylist to get their opinion and recommendations.
  3. Choose a bang style that complements your face shape and personal style.
  4. Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your hair texture.
  5. Maintain your bangs with regular trims and styling to keep them looking fresh.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for finding the perfect bangs for your oblong face:

  • Consider starting with longer, side-swept bangs before going for a shorter, more dramatic style.
  • Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs, as these can make your face appear even longer.
  • Experiment with different partings and angles to find what flatters your face shape the most.
  • Use a round brush and blow dryer to smooth and shape your bangs, especially if you have thick or curly hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about whether bangs suit oblong faces:

What type of bangs are best for oblong faces?

Soft, side-swept bangs are typically the most flattering for oblong faces, as they help to break up the length of the face and add some curves.

Should I avoid bangs if I have an oblong face?

No, bangs can work for oblong faces as long as you choose a style that complements your features and personal style.

How often do I need to get my bangs trimmed?

This will depend on the length and style of your bangs, as well as how quickly your hair grows. Generally, you'll need to get your bangs trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat.

Hair Care

Once you've decided to get bangs for your oblong face, it's important to take good care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling your bangs with heat tools.
  • Consider using dry shampoo to extend the life of your bangs between washes.
  • Invest in a good quality round brush and blow dryer to help shape and style your bangs.

In conclusion, bangs can definitely suit oblong faces as long as you choose the right style and take good care of them. If you're considering getting bangs, consult with a hairstylist and experiment with different styles to find what works best for your face shape and personal style.
