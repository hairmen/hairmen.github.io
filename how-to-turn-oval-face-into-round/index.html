How To Turn Oval Face Into Round A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Nov 05, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you may feel like your face is too long and narrow. However, there are ways to make your face appear rounder and softer. In this article, we will give you tips and tricks on how to turn oval face into round. Let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • How to Turn Oval Face into Round
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Round Face Shape
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Round Face Shape

How to Turn Oval Face into Round

Before we get started, it's important to note that you can't change the shape of your face permanently. However, you can use makeup, hairstyles, and other tricks to create the illusion of a rounder face shape.

Here are some ways to turn oval face into round:

1. Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using darker shades to create shadows and lighter shades to highlight certain areas of your face. To make your face appear rounder, you can use contouring to create shadows along the sides of your face and under your cheekbones. Then, use a highlighter on the center of your forehead, cheekbones, and chin to create the illusion of a rounder face shape.

2. Blush

Blush is another makeup product that can help you create a rounder face shape. To apply blush, smile and apply it to the apples of your cheeks. Blend it out towards your temples to create a natural-looking flush.

3. Hairstyles

Hairstyles can also play a role in creating the illusion of a rounder face shape. Try hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a layered haircut or a side-swept bang. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight, as they can make your face appear longer.

4. Glasses

If you wear glasses, choose frames that are wider than your face. This will create the illusion of a rounder face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to turn oval face into round:

Step 1: Apply foundation

Start by applying foundation to your face to create an even base.

Step 2: Contour

Use a contouring product to create shadows along the sides of your face and under your cheekbones.

Step 3: Blush

Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks and blend it out towards your temples.

Step 4: Highlight

Use a highlighter on the center of your forehead, cheekbones, and chin to create the illusion of a rounder face shape.

Step 5: Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face, such as a layered haircut or a side-swept bang.

Step 6: Glasses

If you wear glasses, choose frames that are wider than your face to create the illusion of a rounder face shape.

Tips for a Round Face Shape

Here are some tips for maintaining a round face shape:

1. Use SPF

Protect your skin from sun damage by using a sunscreen with SPF 30 or higher.

2. Moisturize

Keep your skin hydrated by using a moisturizer daily.

3. Drink water

Drink plenty of water to keep your skin looking healthy and glowing.

4. Avoid smoking

Smoking can cause premature aging and damage to your skin.

5. Eat a healthy diet

Eat a diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and lean protein to maintain a healthy complexion.

FAQs

Q: Can I permanently change my face shape?

A: No, you cannot permanently change your face shape. However, you can use makeup, hairstyles, and other tricks to create the illusion of a different face shape.

Q: What are some hairstyles that flatter a round face shape?

A: Hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a layered haircut or a side-swept bang, can flatter a round face shape.

Hair Care for Round Face Shape

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining a round face shape:

1. Use a volumizing shampoo

Use a volumizing shampoo to add volume to your hair and create the illusion of a rounder face shape.

2. Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and create a rounder shape.

3. Avoid heavy products

Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make your face appear longer.

In conclusion, turning oval face into round is all about creating the illusion of a different face shape. By using makeup, hairstyles, and other tricks, you can make your face appear rounder and softer. Remember to take care of your skin and hair to maintain a healthy and youthful appearance.
