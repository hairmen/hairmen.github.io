How To Style Messy Spiky Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Are you looking for a hairstyle that is both trendy and low maintenance? Look no further than messy spiky hair. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to achieve a stylish, edgy look without spending hours in front of the mirror. In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to style messy spiky hair.

How to Style Messy Spiky Hair

Messy spiky hair is a versatile hairstyle that can be achieved with any hair type. The key to this style is to create texture and volume by using the right hair products. Here are the basic steps to style messy spiky hair:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair. Towel dry your hair, but make sure it is not completely dry. You want your hair to be slightly damp for better styling.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Use a hair gel or pomade to create texture and hold for your hair. Apply the product to your hair while it's still damp, making sure to distribute it evenly. You can use your fingers or a comb to evenly distribute the product.

Step 3: Use a blow dryer

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while shaping it with your fingers or a brush. Use a diffuser attachment to add volume to your hair. Blow dry your hair in an upward direction to create the spiky look.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

To set your spiky hair, use a hairspray to hold the style in place. Spray the hairspray all over your hair, making sure to focus on the areas where you want more hold.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have an idea of the basic steps to style messy spiky hair, let's go through each step in more detail.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will add volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to style. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently towel dry it.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Take a small amount of hair gel or pomade and rub it between your palms. Apply the product to your hair while it's still damp, making sure to distribute it evenly. Use your fingers or a comb to evenly distribute the product.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while shaping it with your fingers or a brush. Use a diffuser attachment to add volume to your hair. Blow dry your hair in an upward direction to create the spiky look. You can also use a round brush to create more volume.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to create spikes. You can also use a comb or brush to create more defined spikes. Make sure to work the product through your hair as you style it, to ensure that it stays in place.

Step 5: Set Your Style with Hairspray

Finally, use a hairspray to set your spiky hair. Spray the hairspray all over your hair, making sure to focus on the areas where you want more hold. This will help your hair stay in place throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Your Messy Spiky Hair

Now that you know how to style messy spiky hair, here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Use a hair gel or pomade to create texture and hold for your hair
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to add volume to your hair
  • Blow dry your hair in an upward direction to create the spiky look
  • Use your fingers or a comb to create spikes
  • Set your style with hairspray to make it last longer

FAQs

Q: Can I style messy spiky hair without using any hair products?

A: While it is possible to style messy spiky hair without using any hair products, it will be difficult to achieve the desired look. Hair products like hair gel or pomade help create texture and hold for your hair, making it easier to style.

Q: Can I achieve messy spiky hair with any hair type?

A: Yes, messy spiky hair can be achieved with any hair type. The key is to create texture and volume by using the right hair products.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have messy spiky hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day if you have messy spiky hair. This will help maintain the texture and volume of your hair, while preventing it from becoming too oily or greasy.

Hair Care for Messy Spiky Hair

To keep your messy spiky hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it more difficult to style

With these tips and tricks, you can easily achieve and maintain messy spiky hair. So, go ahead and rock that edgy, stylish look!
