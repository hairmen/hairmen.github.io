How To Get Curls With Straight Hair For Guys

Written by Aprilia Aug 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair and want to switch things up? Do you envy guys with curly hair and wish you could have curls too? Well, you're in luck because in this article, we'll be discussing how to get curls with straight hair for guys. Follow these steps and you'll be rocking a new curly hairstyle in no time!

Description

Having straight hair can be a bit dull and boring, especially if you've had it for a long time. Adding some curls can give you a fresh, new look and make you stand out from the crowd. Many guys think that they can't get curls if they have straight hair, but that's not the case. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve curls that will last all day long.

How to

The first step in getting curls with straight hair is to choose the right curling tool. You can use a curling iron, a curling wand, or even a straightening iron to create curls. Make sure that the tool you choose is the right size for the curls you want to achieve. If you want tight curls, go for a smaller barrel. If you want loose waves, go for a larger barrel.

Next, prepare your hair by washing it and applying a heat protectant spray. This will prevent your hair from getting damaged by the heat of the curling tool. Blow dry your hair until it's completely dry.

Section your hair into small sections and start curling. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling tool. Hold it for a few seconds and release. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Once you're finished curling, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and make them look more natural. You can also use a bit of hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get curls with straight hair for guys:

  1. Choose the right curling tool
  2. Wash your hair and apply a heat protectant spray
  3. Blow dry your hair until it's completely dry
  4. Section your hair into small sections
  5. Curl each section of hair using the curling tool
  6. Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls
  7. Apply a bit of hairspray to hold the curls in place

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Don't curl too much hair at once. This will make it harder to get the curls you want.
  • If you're using a curling iron or wand, make sure to wear a heat-resistant glove to protect your fingers from getting burned.
  • If your curls are too tight, gently brush them out with a comb to loosen them up.
  • Use a high-quality hairspray to hold the curls in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting curls with straight hair for guys:

Can you get curls with straight hair?

Yes, you can get curls with straight hair using a curling iron, curling wand, or even a straightening iron.

What's the best curling tool to use?

The best curling tool to use depends on the type of curls you want to achieve. If you want tight curls, go for a smaller barrel. If you want loose waves, go for a larger barrel.

How do you make the curls last all day?

You can make the curls last all day by applying a bit of hairspray to hold them in place.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after you've curled it. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often. This can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. This can cause damage and make your hair more difficult to style.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to get curls with straight hair for guys, it's time to try it out for yourself. With a bit of practice and patience, you'll be able to achieve the curly hairstyle you've always wanted.
