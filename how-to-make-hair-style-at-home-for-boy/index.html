How To Make Hair Style At Home For Boy Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of paying expensive hair salon fees just to get a simple haircut for your boy? Why not try making a hair style at home instead? Not only is it more cost-effective, but it also allows you to experiment with different styles and express your creativity. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make a hair style at home for your boy.

How to Make Hair Style at Home for Boy

Making a hair style at home for your boy may seem intimidating at first, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Prepare the Tools

Before you start, make sure you have the necessary tools such as scissors, comb, clippers, and hair gel or wax. Ensure that your tools are clean and sharp to avoid damaging your boy's hair.

Step 2: Choose the Hair Style

Decide on the hair style you want to create for your boy. You can search for inspiration online or ask your boy for his preference. Ensure that the style you choose is suitable for your boy's face shape and hair type.

Step 3: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash your boy's hair with shampoo and conditioner, then dry it with a towel or a hair dryer until it's damp. Drying the hair completely is important to ensure that the hair is cut evenly.

Step 4: Comb the Hair

Comb the hair to remove any tangles or knots. This makes it easier to cut and style the hair.

Step 5: Cut the Hair

Using scissors or clippers, cut the hair according to the style you have chosen. Start with the sides and back, then move to the top. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length.

Step 6: Style the Hair

Apply hair gel or wax to your boy's hair and style it according to the chosen hair style. Use your fingers or a comb to create the desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to make a hair style at home for your boy:

Step 1: Choose the Hair Style

Discuss with your boy the hair style he wants. Show him pictures of different hair styles and let him choose the one he likes. Ensure that the style he chooses suits his face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Prepare the Tools

Ensure that your tools are clean and sharp. This makes it easier to cut the hair and avoids damaging it. You will need scissors, clippers, a comb, and hair gel or wax.

Step 3: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash your boy's hair with shampoo and conditioner, then dry it with a towel or a hair dryer until it's damp. Drying the hair completely is important to ensure that the hair is cut evenly.

Step 4: Comb the Hair

Comb the hair to remove any tangles or knots. This makes it easier to cut and style the hair.

Step 5: Cut the Hair

Start with the sides and back, then move to the top. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as this can lead to uneven cuts.

Step 6: Style the Hair

Apply hair gel or wax to your boy's hair and style it according to the chosen hair style. Use your fingers or a comb to create the desired look. Be creative and experiment with different styles.

Tips for Making Hair Style at Home

Here are some tips to help you make a hair style at home for your boy:

  • Start with a simple hair style before trying more complex ones.
  • Ensure that your tools are clean and sharp to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Be patient and take your time when cutting and styling the hair.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the ones that work best for your boy's hair.
  • Ask for help if you're not confident in making a hair style at home.

FAQs

What if I make a mistake while cutting my boy's hair?

If you make a mistake, try not to panic. You can always try to fix it by cutting the hair shorter or changing the style. If the mistake is severe, you can always take your boy to a professional hair stylist to fix it.

What are some easy hair styles for boys?

Some easy hair styles for boys include the crew cut, buzz cut, and the classic side part. These styles are simple to make and require minimal styling.

Hair Care Tips

After making a hair style at home for your boy, it's important to take care of his hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your boy's hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle the hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using too much heat on the hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Apply hair oil or serum to the hair to keep it moisturized.

By following these tips, you can keep your boy's hair looking healthy and stylish.
