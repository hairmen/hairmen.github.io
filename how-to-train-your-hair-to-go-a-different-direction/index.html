How To Train Your Hair To Go A Different Direction

Written by Petter Jul 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your hair always going in the same direction? Do you want to switch up your hairstyle without cutting it? Training your hair to go in a different direction is possible and doesn't require any drastic measures. In this article, we'll go through the steps, tips, and hair care necessary to train your hair to go in a different direction.

Description

Training your hair to go in a different direction means conditioning your hair to lay in a specific direction. It's a simple process that requires patience and persistence. It's essential to understand that the process can take time, and the results may not be immediate. However, with time and effort, you can train your hair to go in a different direction.

How to Train Your Hair to Go in a Different Direction

Here are the steps to follow to train your hair to go in a different direction:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step is to wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo. This will remove any product buildup or oils that may be weighing your hair down. Rinse thoroughly with warm water, and towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help detangle and moisturize your strands. A leave-in conditioner will also protect your hair from heat damage. Comb through your hair to distribute the product evenly.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into the direction you want it to go. Use clips to secure each section in place.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go. Be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent heat damage. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 5: Finish with a Styling Product

Finish your hairstyle with a styling product that will hold your hair in place. Use a small amount of product to avoid weighing your hair down. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Training Your Hair to Go in a Different Direction

Here are some tips to keep in mind while training your hair to go in a different direction:

  • Be patient. It can take time for your hair to adapt to a new direction.
  • Use a heat protectant when blow drying your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh your hair down.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid friction that can cause breakage.
  • Be consistent with your hair care routine.

FAQ

How long does it take to train hair to go in a different direction?

It can take anywhere from a few weeks to a few months to train your hair to go in a different direction. The time it takes depends on the texture and thickness of your hair.

Can I train my hair to go in multiple directions?

Yes, you can train your hair to go in multiple directions. However, it's essential to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine.

Can I train my hair to go in a different direction without heat?

Yes, you can train your hair to go in a different direction without heat. However, using heat can help speed up the process.

Hair Care

It's essential to take good care of your hair while training it to go in a different direction. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a clarifying shampoo.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.

Training your hair to go in a different direction is possible with patience and persistence. Follow the steps and tips outlined in this article and take good care of your hair. With time and effort, you can achieve the hairstyle you desire without having to cut your hair.
