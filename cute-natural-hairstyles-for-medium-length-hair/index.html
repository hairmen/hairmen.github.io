Cute Natural Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Daniel Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have medium length hair and you're looking for some cute natural hairstyles, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be sharing some of our favorite hairstyles that are perfect for medium length hair. Whether you're looking for something casual or more formal, we've got you covered!

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we get started with the hairstyles, let's talk about some general tips for styling medium length hair. First and foremost, make sure your hair is clean and well-conditioned. This will make it easier to style and will help your hair look healthier overall.

Another tip is to use styling products that are specifically designed for your hair type. For example, if you have curly hair, use a product that enhances your curls. If you have straight hair, use a product that helps to add volume and texture.

Finally, don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and accessories. Medium length hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, so have fun with it!

Step by Step

Now, let's get into the hairstyles! Here are some step-by-step instructions for a few of our favorites:

1. Braided Crown

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from one side and braid it.
  3. Wrap the braid around the crown of your head and pin it in place.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Messy Bun

  1. Pull your hair back into a low ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around itself to form a bun.
  3. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.
  4. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to create a messy, relaxed look.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. Half-Up, Half-Down

  1. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head.
  2. Pull it back and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Fluff up the ponytail to create volume.
  4. Optional: curl the ends of the ponytail for added texture.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are a few more tips for styling your medium length hair:

  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to help detangle your hair and distribute natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to create natural waves or curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling medium length hair:

What is considered medium length hair?

Medium length hair typically falls between the chin and shoulders.

What are some easy hairstyles for medium length hair?

Some easy hairstyles for medium length hair include a messy bun, a half-up, half-down style, and a low ponytail.

How often should I wash my medium length hair?

This depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people can go several days between washes, while others prefer to wash their hair daily.

Hair Care

Finally, taking care of your hair is important for maintaining its health and beauty. Here are a few tips for keeping your medium length hair in tip-top shape:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair over time.

With these tips and hairstyles, you'll be able to rock your medium length hair with confidence and style. Whether you're going for a casual look or something more formal, there's a hairstyle out there for everyone!
