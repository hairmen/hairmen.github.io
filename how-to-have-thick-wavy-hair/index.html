How To Have Thick Wavy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of having limp, lifeless hair? Do you dream of having thick, luscious waves that add volume and movement to your locks? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss how to have thick wavy hair, from the best hair care practices to styling tips and tricks. Whether you have naturally fine hair or just want to add some oomph to your tresses, this guide has got you covered.

Table of Contents:

  • Hair Care
  • How to Create Thick Waves
  • Step-by-Step Tutorial
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

Hair Care:

Before diving into styling techniques, it's important to establish a strong hair care routine that caters to your hair type. If you want thick, wavy hair, you need to keep your locks healthy and nourished. Here are some tips:

1. Use the Right Products:

Choosing the right shampoo and conditioner is crucial for maintaining healthy hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type and contain ingredients that add volume and hydration. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates, as they can damage your hair and strip it of its natural oils.

2. Don't Overwash:

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and prone to breakage. Try to limit washing to 2-3 times a week, and use dry shampoo in between washes to absorb excess oil.

3. Use a Hair Mask:

Once a week, treat your hair to a deep conditioning mask that nourishes and strengthens your locks. Look for masks that contain ingredients like keratin, biotin, and vitamin E, which promote healthy hair growth and prevent breakage.

How to Create Thick Waves:

Now that you have established a strong hair care routine, it's time to start styling. Here are some techniques for creating thick, wavy hair:

1. Use a Curling Wand:

A curling wand is the perfect tool for creating loose, beachy waves. Start by sectioning your hair and wrapping small sections around the wand, holding for 5-10 seconds before releasing. Repeat until your entire head is curled, then run your fingers through your hair to break up the waves and create volume.

2. Try a Diffuser:

If you have naturally wavy hair, a diffuser attachment for your hair dryer can help enhance your waves and add volume. Apply a curl-enhancing cream to damp hair, then use the diffuser to dry your hair while scrunching it upward. This will create defined waves and prevent frizz.

3. Use Velcro Rollers:

Velcro rollers are a great tool for adding volume to your roots and creating loose waves. Section your hair and roll small sections onto the rollers, then blow dry on low heat. Once your hair is dry, remove the rollers and run your fingers through your hair to create loose waves.

Step-by-Step Tutorial:

Here is a step-by-step tutorial for creating thick, wavy hair:

1. Start with Clean Hair:

Begin by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.

2. Section Your Hair:

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips or ties. This will make it easier to curl each section individually.

3. Use a Curling Wand:

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling wand. Hold for 5-10 seconds, then release. Repeat until all sections are curled.

4. Break up the Waves:

Run your fingers through your hair to break up the waves and create volume. You can also use a volumizing spray or dry shampoo to add more texture.

Tips and Tricks:

Here are some additional tips and tricks for achieving thick, wavy hair:

1. Use a Texturizing Spray:

A texturizing spray can help add volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to style and manipulate. Apply a small amount to your roots and scrunch your hair to create waves.

2. Sleep with Braids:

Sleeping with braids in your hair can help create loose waves overnight. Braid your hair before going to bed, then undo the braids in the morning for instant waves.

3. Use a Teasing Comb:

A teasing comb can help add volume to your roots and create a tousled, beachy look. Tease small sections of hair near your crown, then smooth the top layer over to create a seamless look.

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about having thick, wavy hair:

1. Can I have thick, wavy hair if my hair is naturally fine?

Yes! With the right hair care routine and styling techniques, you can add volume and texture to even the finest of hair.

2. How often should I wash my hair?

It's best to limit washing to 2-3 times a week to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

3. How can I prevent my waves from falling flat?

Try using a volumizing spray or texturizing spray to add texture and hold to your waves. You can also try using a hairspray or mousse to keep your waves in place.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve thick, wavy hair that adds volume and movement to your locks. Whether you have naturally fine hair or just want to add some oomph to your tresses, these methods are sure to leave you with the perfect beachy waves. So go ahead and try them out!
