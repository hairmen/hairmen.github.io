How To Do Easy Hairstyles For Long Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read
If you have long hair, you might find it challenging to come up with easy hairstyles that don't take hours to create. Whether you're running late for work or just want a quick and effortless look, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to do easy hairstyles for long hair that are perfect for any occasion. From simple buns to stylish braids, these hairstyles are sure to turn heads.

Description

This article is designed to help you learn how to do easy hairstyles for long hair. We'll provide step-by-step instructions for each hairstyle, along with tips and tricks to make the process as straightforward as possible. We'll cover a range of styles, from simple updos to more complex braids, so you'll have plenty of options to choose from. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced hairstylist, this guide is perfect for anyone looking to switch up their look.

How to

Here's how to do easy hairstyles for long hair:

1. Simple Bun

This classic hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, from a casual day out to a formal event. To create a simple bun, follow these steps: - Gather your hair into a high ponytail. - Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun shape. - Secure the bun with bobby pins or an elastic hair tie. - Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. French Braid

A French braid is a stylish and practical hairstyle that can be worn for any occasion. Here's how to do it: - Start by sectioning off a small piece of hair at the top of your head. - Split the section into three equal parts and begin braiding by crossing the right section over the middle section, then the left section over the middle section. - Each time you cross a section over the middle, add a small section of hair from the sides to the braid. - Continue braiding until you reach the nape of your neck, then finish with a regular braid and secure with a hair tie.

3. Messy Ponytail

A messy ponytail is a quick and easy hairstyle that can be dressed up or down. Here's how to do it: - Gather your hair into a high ponytail. - Pull out a few strands of hair at the front to frame your face. - Tease the ponytail with a comb to create volume. - Twist the ponytail around itself to create a messy bun shape. - Secure the bun with bobby pins or an elastic hair tie. - Pull out a few strands of hair from the bun to create a messy, undone look. - Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating easy hairstyles for long hair:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. If you have curly or wavy hair, you may want to straighten it first to make the styling process easier. You can also apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Step 2: Choose Your Hairstyle

Decide which hairstyle you want to create and gather any tools or accessories you'll need, such as bobby pins, hair ties, or a comb. It's also a good idea to have some hairspray on hand to hold the style in place.

Step 3: Follow the Instructions

Follow the step-by-step instructions for your chosen hairstyle, making sure to take your time and be gentle with your hair. If you're having trouble with a particular step, don't be afraid to watch a tutorial video or ask for help from a friend.

Step 4: Finish Your Style

Once you've completed your hairstyle, take a moment to admire your work. You can add any finishing touches, such as hairspray or hair accessories, to complete the look. Now you're ready to show off your new hairstyle!

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing easy hairstyles for long hair: - Use hair accessories, such as bobby pins or hair ties, to hold your hair in place and add interest to your style. - Experiment with different textures, such as braids, waves, or curls, to add depth and dimension to your hair. - Don't be afraid to add a pop of color to your hair with temporary hair dye or hair chalk. - Keep your hair healthy by using a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy hairstyles for long hair:

1. What is the easiest hairstyle for long hair?

The easiest hairstyle for long hair is a simple bun. All you need to do is gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it around itself to create a bun shape.

2. How do I keep my long hair healthy?

To keep your long hair healthy, use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, avoid using heat styling tools too often, and trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

3. Can I do easy hairstyles for long hair on my own?

Yes, you can do easy hairstyles for long hair on your own with a little practice and patience. Just follow the step-by-step instructions and take your time.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair: - Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type. - Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. - Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools. - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends. - Eat a healthy, balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals. - Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
