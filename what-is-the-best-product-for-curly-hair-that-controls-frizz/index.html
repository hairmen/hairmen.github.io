What Is The Best Product For Curly Hair That Controls Frizz

Written by Eveline Sep 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that frizz is a constant struggle. It can be frustrating to spend time styling your hair, only to have it turn into a frizzy mess as soon as you step outside. Luckily, there are products out there that can help control frizz and keep your curls looking their best. In this article, we'll explore the best product for curly hair that controls frizz and provide some tips on how to use it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Managing Frizz
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

Description

The best product for controlling frizz in curly hair is a hair serum. Serums are lightweight formulas that are designed to smooth and tame frizz without weighing down your curls. They work by coating the hair shaft and providing a protective barrier against humidity and other environmental factors that can cause frizz. Serums also add shine and definition to your curls, making them look healthier and more vibrant.

When shopping for a hair serum, look for one that is specifically formulated for curly hair. These serums will contain ingredients that are designed to enhance your curls and reduce frizz, such as argan oil, coconut oil, and shea butter. Avoid products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and make frizz worse.

How to Use

To use a hair serum, start with clean, damp hair. Apply a small amount of serum to your hands and work it through your hair, focusing on the ends and any areas that are prone to frizz. Be careful not to apply too much, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy. Once you've applied the serum, you can either air dry your hair or use a diffuser on your blow dryer to dry your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to using a hair serum for curly hair:

  1. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess moisture.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair serum to your hands.
  4. Work the serum through your hair, focusing on the ends and any areas that are prone to frizz.
  5. Comb or brush your hair to distribute the serum evenly.
  6. Either air dry your hair or use a diffuser on your blow dryer to dry your curls.

Tips for Managing Frizz

In addition to using a hair serum, there are several other things you can do to manage frizz in curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and make frizz worse.
  • Consider getting a haircut that is specifically designed for curly hair. A good haircut can help reduce frizz and enhance your curls.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and make frizz worse.

FAQ

What causes frizz in curly hair?

Frizz in curly hair is caused by a combination of factors, including humidity, dryness, and damage to the hair cuticle. When the hair cuticle is damaged, it can't retain moisture as effectively, which can lead to frizz. Humidity can also cause the hair cuticle to swell, which can make frizz worse.

Can I use a hair serum on dry hair?

While hair serums are designed to be used on damp hair, you can also use them on dry hair to tame frizz and add shine. However, be careful not to apply too much serum, as this can make your hair look greasy.

How often should I use a hair serum?

You can use a hair serum every time you wash your hair, or as needed to tame frizz and add shine. Just be careful not to apply too much, as this can weigh down your curls.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

In addition to using a hair serum, there are several other things you can do to care for your curly hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or deep conditioning treatment to hydrate and nourish your curls.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess moisture.
  • Avoid touching your hair throughout the day, as this can cause frizz.

By following these tips and using a hair serum, you can keep your curly hair looking its best and control frizz for a more polished and put-together look.
