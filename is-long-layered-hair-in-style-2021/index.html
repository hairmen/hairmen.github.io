Is Long Layered Hair In Style 2021

Written by Frank Sep 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're wondering if long layered hair is still fashionable in 2021, the answer is a resounding YES! Long layered hair has been a go-to style for many years and has remained a popular trend among women of all ages. In this article, we'll explore why long layered hair is still in style, how to achieve the look, and tips for maintaining healthy locks.

Description

Long layered hair is a versatile hairstyle that involves cutting the hair into layers of different lengths. The layers are designed to add volume, movement, and texture to the hair. The style is popular because it looks great on any hair type, face shape, and age group. Long layered hair can be styled in many ways, such as straight, wavy, curly, or braided.

How to Achieve Long Layered Hair

If you're interested in getting long layered hair, it's essential to consult with a professional stylist. They will be able to assess your hair type and face shape and recommend the best layered cut for you. Generally, long layered hair involves cutting the hair into layers that start at the chin or collarbone and gradually get longer towards the ends. The layers are blended to create a seamless flow of hair.

Once you've got your long layered cut, it's important to maintain it by getting regular trims every six to eight weeks. This will prevent split ends and keep your layers looking fresh and healthy.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving long layered hair:

  1. Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best layered cut for you.
  2. Wash and condition your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.
  3. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a smooth finish.
  5. Use a curling iron or straightener to style your hair as desired.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or serum to hold the style in place and add shine.

Tips for Maintaining Long Layered Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy long layered hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush that won't damage your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long layered hair:

Can long layered hair be worn straight or curly?

Yes, long layered hair can be styled straight, wavy, curly, or braided. The layers add texture and movement to the hair, allowing for multiple styling options.

Is long layered hair suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, long layered hair is suitable for all face shapes. A professional stylist will be able to recommend the best layered cut for your face shape.

How often should I get my long layered hair trimmed?

It's recommended to get your long layered hair trimmed every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and keep your layers looking fresh.

Hair Care

Long layered hair requires proper care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush that won't damage your hair.

With proper care, long layered hair can be a stylish and versatile hairstyle that will never go out of fashion. Whether you're looking for a sleek and sophisticated look or a bohemian vibe, long layered hair can be styled to suit any occasion.
