How To Do Medium Length Hairstyles A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Medium length hair is the perfect length for those who want a hairstyle that is not too short or too long. It also offers a lot of versatility when it comes to styling. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to do medium length hairstyles. We will also share some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to ensure that your medium length hairstyle looks great all the time.

How to Do Medium Length Hairstyles

Medium length hairstyles are versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Here are some popular medium length hairstyles that you can try:

  • Shoulder-length curls
  • Messy waves
  • Sleek and straight
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Layered bob

Before you start styling, make sure that your hair is clean and dry. Apply some heat protectant if you plan on using heat styling tools.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to do a shoulder-length curls hairstyle:

  1. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  2. Starting from the back, take a small section of hair and curl it with a curling iron. Curl away from your face to create a natural-looking curl.
  3. Repeat step two for the rest of your hair.
  4. Once you have curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently comb through the curls to create a more natural look.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Medium Length Hairstyles

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect medium length hairstyle:

  • Invest in good quality heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to prevent breakage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Try different hair accessories, such as headbands, hair clips, and scrunchies, to add some variety to your hairstyle.

FAQs

1. How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week. If you exercise regularly or live in a humid climate, you may need to wash your hair more often.

2. How can I prevent split ends?

Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or a hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hairstyles

Here are some hair care tips to keep your medium length hairstyle looking great:

  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and step-by-step guide, you can achieve the perfect medium length hairstyle. Experiment with different styles and have fun!
