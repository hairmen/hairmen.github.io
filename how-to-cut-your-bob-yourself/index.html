How To Cut Your Bob Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Oct 13, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of spending money at the salon to maintain your bob haircut? Why not try cutting it yourself? Don't worry, it's easier than you think. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your bob yourself. Let's get started!

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It's also a good idea to have a mirror and a spray bottle filled with water.

Step 1: Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it's completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section your hair into four parts - two at the front and two at the back. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 3: Start with the back section. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold it between your index and middle fingers and cut it straight across. Repeat this process for each section of hair in the back.

Step 4: Move on to the front section. Start by cutting the hair at the sides. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down to the desired length. Hold it between your index and middle fingers and cut it straight across. Repeat this process on both sides.

Step 5: Finally, cut the hair at the front. Comb it straight down and cut it at the desired length. For a more textured look, you can point cut the ends by holding the scissors vertically and cutting into the hair at an angle.

Tips

- Start with small sections of hair and cut slowly. You can always cut more, but you can't put it back once it's cut.

- Use a comb to make sure your cuts are straight.

- If you're unsure about cutting your hair, start with a small trim and work your way up.

FAQ

How often should I cut my bob?

You should cut your bob every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go to the salon to fix it. Alternatively, you can try styling your hair differently to hide the mistake.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its shape. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a heat protectant spray. Finally, consider using a hair mask once a week to give your hair an extra boost of hydration.

Now that you know how to cut your bob yourself, you can save money and maintain your haircut at home. Remember to take your time and start with small sections of hair. With practice, you'll become a pro at cutting your own hair!
