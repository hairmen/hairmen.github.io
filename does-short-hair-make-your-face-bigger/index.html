Does Short Hair Make Your Face Bigger The Truth Revealed

Written by William Jun 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Short hair has been a popular trend for many years, and it's understandable why. Not only is it low maintenance, but it can also make you look younger and more fashionable. However, some people are hesitant to chop off their locks because they fear that short hair will make their face look bigger. In this blog post, we'll answer the question - does short hair make your face bigger? Keep reading to find out.

Description

Many people believe that short hair makes your face look bigger because it exposes more of your face. However, this isn't always the case. The way short hair affects your face shape depends on several factors such as your hair texture, face shape, and bone structure.

Short haircuts can either enhance or detract from your facial features. The key is to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features. In the next section, we'll discuss how to determine if short hair is right for you.

How to Determine if Short Hair is Right for You

Short haircuts may not be suitable for everyone. Before you take the plunge, it's essential to consider your face shape and hair texture. Here are some tips to help you determine if short hair is right for you:

  • If you have a round face, opt for a short haircut that adds height to your crown to elongate your face.
  • If you have an oval face, you can rock any short haircut.
  • If you have a square face, opt for a layered haircut that softens your jawline.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, choose a short haircut that adds volume to the sides to balance out your forehead.
  • If you have a diamond-shaped face, choose a short haircut that adds width to your temples.
  • If you have thick hair, choose a short haircut that thins out your hair to prevent your face from looking wider.
  • If you have fine hair, choose a short haircut that adds volume to your hair to make your face look fuller.

Step by Step Guide in Choosing the Right Short Haircut

Choosing the right short haircut can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the perfect short haircut for your face shape and hair texture:

  1. Identify your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline.
  2. Research short haircuts that flatter your face shape.
  3. Consider your hair texture and thickness.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best short haircut for you.
  5. Choose a short haircut that highlights your best features.

Tips for Styling Short Hair to Flatter Your Face

Now that you've chosen the perfect short haircut, it's time to style it to flatter your face shape. Here are some tips to help you style your short hair:

  • If you have a round face, add volume to the crown to elongate your face.
  • If you have a square face, style your hair to soften your jawline.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, add volume to the sides to balance out your forehead.
  • If you have a diamond-shaped face, add width to your temples to balance out your face shape.
  • If you have fine hair, use volumizing products to add fullness to your hair.
  • If you have thick hair, use texturizing products to thin out your hair.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions About Short Hair and Face Shape

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair and face shape:

Q: Does short hair make your face look bigger?

A: Short hair doesn't necessarily make your face look bigger. It depends on your face shape and bone structure.

Q: What short haircut is best for a round face?

A: A short haircut that adds height to your crown is best for a round face.

Q: Can I still wear bangs with short hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear bangs with short hair. Opt for side-swept bangs to frame your face.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Regularly trim your hair to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to add shine to your hair.

Now that you know the truth about short hair and face shape, it's time to take the plunge and chop off your locks. Remember to choose a short haircut that flatters your face shape and hair texture and style it to highlight your best features. With the right short haircut and styling, you'll look and feel confident and fashionable.
