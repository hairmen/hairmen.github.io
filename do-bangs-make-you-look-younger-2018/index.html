Do Bangs Make You Look Younger 2018 A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Aug 09, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're looking for a way to refresh your look and appear more youthful, you might be wondering: do bangs make you look younger? Bangs, or fringe as they're sometimes called, have been a popular hair trend for years. But do they really have the power to shave years off your appearance? In this article, we'll answer that question and provide you with everything you need to know about rocking bangs in 2018.

What Are Bangs?

Bangs are a section of hair that falls over the forehead. They can be cut straight across or at an angle, and can be long or short. Bangs can be worn with any hair length or style, from pixie cuts to long layers.

Do Bangs Make You Look Younger?

The short answer is yes, bangs can make you look younger. This is because they can help to soften your features and make your face appear more youthful and fresh. Bangs can also be a great way to hide forehead wrinkles or fine lines.

However, it's important to note that not all bangs will have this effect. Choosing the right style of bangs for your face shape and hair type is crucial. If you choose the wrong style, your bangs could actually make you look older or more severe.

How to Choose the Right Bangs for Your Face Shape

The key to choosing the right bangs for your face shape is to find a style that will balance out your features. Here are some tips:

Round Face

If you have a round face, opt for long, side-swept bangs that are angled towards the cheekbones. This will help to elongate your face and make it appear more oval.

Square Face

If you have a square face, choose soft, wispy bangs that are longer on the sides. This will help to soften your features and make your face appear more feminine.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, try blunt, straight-across bangs that hit just above the eyebrows. This will balance out your forehead and make your face appear more symmetrical.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you're in luck – almost any style of bangs will work for you! Try experimenting with different lengths and angles to find the style that suits you best.

How to Style Bangs

Styling your bangs is easy and can be done in a variety of ways:

Air-Drying

If you have naturally straight hair, you can simply let your bangs air-dry after washing them. Use a round brush to blow-dry them if you want a little more volume.

Straightening

If you have curly or wavy hair, you may need to straighten your bangs. Use a flat iron and be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Curling

If you want to add some texture to your bangs, you can curl them using a curling iron or hot rollers. Be sure to apply a styling product to hold the curl in place.

Tips for Maintaining Bangs

To keep your bangs looking their best, follow these tips:

Trim Regularly

Bangs can grow quickly, so it's important to trim them every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Wash Frequently

Bangs can get oily quickly, so be sure to wash them frequently to avoid breakouts or an unkempt appearance.

Blow-Dry Immediately

If your bangs get wet, be sure to blow-dry them immediately to prevent them from sticking to your forehead or becoming misshapen.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do bangs work for every hair type?

A: Bangs can work for almost any hair type, but you may need to choose a different style depending on your hair's thickness and texture.

Q: Can I still wear bangs if I have a cowlick?

A: Yes, but you may need to choose a style that works with your cowlick instead of against it. Talk to your hairstylist for advice.

Q: Can I cut my own bangs?

A: We recommend leaving bangs to the professionals, as it can be difficult to get them even and symmetrical on your own.

Final Thoughts

So, do bangs make you look younger? The answer is yes – if you choose the right style for your face shape and hair type. With the right cut and styling, bangs can be a great way to refresh your look and appear more youthful. Just be sure to maintain them properly to avoid any hair mishaps.

Hair Care

Whether you have bangs or not, it's important to take care of your hair. Be sure to wash and condition regularly, and use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools. Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. And most importantly, embrace your unique style and have fun with your hair!
