How To Use Thinning Shears On Thick Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

Written by William Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thinning shears are an essential tool for hairstylists and anyone who wants to achieve a specific look with their hair. If you have thick wavy hair, you may find it challenging to achieve the desired style. However, with the right technique and tools, you can achieve a beautiful look that you will love. In this article, we will discuss how to use thinning shears on thick wavy hair.

Description

Thinning shears, also known as texturizing shears, are scissors with teeth on one side and a smooth blade on the other. These shears are designed to remove bulk from the hair, creating a softer and more natural look. Thinning shears are commonly used on thick hair, as they can help to reduce the amount of hair and make it easier to manage.

How to Use Thinning Shears on Thick Wavy Hair

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

The first step in using thinning shears on thick wavy hair is to wash and dry your hair. Make sure that your hair is completely dry before you begin, as wet hair can be more difficult to cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, you will need to section your hair. Use clips to separate your hair into sections, starting with the bottom layer and working your way up. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 3: Use the Thinning Shears

Once you have sectioned your hair, it's time to use the thinning shears. Start by holding the shears at a slight angle, with the teeth pointing towards the ends of your hair. Use the shears to cut small sections of your hair, taking care not to cut too much at once. You can repeat this process on each section of your hair until you have achieved the desired look.

Step 4: Blend Your Hair

After you have used the thinning shears, you may notice that your hair looks uneven. To fix this, you can use scissors to blend your hair. Hold the scissors vertically and make small cuts along the ends of your hair. This will help to create a more even look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using thinning shears on thick wavy hair:

  • Start with small sections of hair
  • Use the shears sparingly
  • Blend your hair with scissors after using the thinning shears
  • Take breaks to avoid fatigue

FAQ

Can thinning shears damage my hair?

Thinning shears can damage your hair if they are not used correctly. It's important to use the shears sparingly and to avoid cutting too much hair at once.

How often should I use thinning shears on my hair?

It's best to use thinning shears on your hair every 6-8 weeks. This will help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent damage.

Can I use thinning shears on any type of hair?

Thinning shears are best for thick hair, as they can help to reduce the amount of hair and make it easier to manage. However, they can be used on any type of hair.

Hair Care

After using thinning shears on your hair, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Conclusion

Thinning shears are a great tool for achieving a specific look with your hair. If you have thick wavy hair, you may find it challenging to achieve the desired style. However, by using thinning shears and following the tips in this article, you can achieve a beautiful look that you will love.
