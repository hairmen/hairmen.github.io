How To Get A Long Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Sep 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you have a round or square face shape and want to achieve a more elongated look? Luckily, there are various methods to get a long face, from changing your hairstyle to trying facial exercises. In this blog post, we'll discuss some tips and tricks to help you achieve a longer face shape.

How to Get a Long Face

Before we dive into the methods to get a long face, it's essential to understand what face shape you have. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and examine the outline of your face in the mirror. If your face is round, your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your jawline is soft. If your face is square, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly the same width.

Now, if you have a round or square face shape and want to get a long face, here are some tips:

Try a High Ponytail

One of the easiest ways to create the illusion of a longer face is by wearing your hair in a high ponytail. This hairstyle pulls your hair up and back, elongating your face shape. To achieve this look, gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie. You can also tease the crown of your head to add volume.

Contour Your Face

Contouring is a makeup technique that can help you achieve a long face shape. By using light and dark shades, you can create the illusion of shadows and depth, making your face appear longer. Apply a darker shade of foundation or bronzer along the sides of your forehead, temples, and jawline. Blend well for a natural look.

Do Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help tone and tighten the muscles in your face, giving you a more defined and elongated face shape. One exercise is to open your mouth wide and say "ahh" while tilting your head back. Hold for five seconds and repeat ten times. Another exercise is to suck your cheeks in and hold for five seconds, then release. Repeat ten times.

Step by Step Guide: How to Get a Long Face

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a long face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you start trying to elongate your face shape, you need to determine what face shape you have. Look in the mirror and examine the outline of your face.

Step 2: Try a High Ponytail

Wear your hair in a high ponytail to elongate your face shape. Gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie.

Step 3: Contour Your Face

Use makeup to contour your face and create the illusion of shadows and depth. Apply a darker shade of foundation or bronzer along the sides of your forehead, temples, and jawline.

Step 4: Do Facial Exercises

Try facial exercises to tone and tighten the muscles in your face. One exercise is to open your mouth wide and say "ahh" while tilting your head back. Hold for five seconds and repeat ten times. Another exercise is to suck your cheeks in and hold for five seconds, then release. Repeat ten times.

Tips to Get a Long Face

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a long face shape:

Get a Haircut

A haircut can do wonders for your face shape. Ask your hairstylist for a cut that adds height to the top of your head, like a layered cut or a pixie cut. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, like blunt bangs.

Wear Statement Earrings

Statement earrings can draw the eye upwards and elongate your face shape. Choose earrings that dangle or are long and slim.

Avoid Round Glasses

If you wear glasses, avoid round frames that can make your face appear rounder. Instead, opt for rectangular or square frames that can elongate your face shape.

FAQ

Can I get a long face shape naturally?

Yes, some people have a naturally elongated face shape due to genetics. However, if you have a round or square face shape, you can use the tips and tricks mentioned in this blog post to create the illusion of a longer face.

How long will it take to get a long face shape?

The time it takes to get a longer face shape depends on the method you choose. Some methods, like wearing your hair in a high ponytail or contouring your face, can give you instant results. Other methods, like facial exercises, may take longer to see results.

Hair Care Tips for a Long Face Shape

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain a long face shape:

Avoid Heavy Hair Products

Heavy hair products can weigh down your hair and make your face appear wider. Opt for lightweight products that add volume and texture.

Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make it look frizzy. Use a heat protectant spray and limit your use of heat styling tools.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Ask your hairstylist for a trim every six to eight weeks.

Conclusion

Getting a long face shape is achievable with the right tips and tricks. Try wearing your hair in a high ponytail, contouring your face, and doing facial exercises to elongate your face shape. Remember to determine your face shape before trying any methods, and consult with a hairstylist or makeup artist if you're unsure. With these tips, you'll be on your way to a longer, more defined face shape in no time.
