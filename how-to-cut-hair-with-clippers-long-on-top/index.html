How To Cut Hair With Clippers Long On Top

Written by Oliver Aug 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that is both easy to maintain and stylish, a long-on-top cut might be just what you need. This versatile haircut can be worn in many different ways, and it's easy to achieve with clippers. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to cut hair with clippers long on top, including how to prepare, execute the cut, and maintain your new style.

Table of Contents:

  • Prepare
  • Execute the Cut
  • Maintain Your Style
  • Tips for Success
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care

Prepare

Before you start cutting, you'll need to gather a few supplies. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers with a #4 or #5 guard
  • A comb
  • A pair of scissors
  • Clipper oil and a brush for cleaning
  • A towel or cape to protect clothing

Once you have everything you need, you'll want to start by washing and drying the hair. This will help you to see the natural texture and shape of the hair, making it easier to cut. Next, use the comb to part the hair where you want the length to start. This can be anywhere from the forehead to the crown of the head, depending on the desired length.

Execute the Cut

With the hair parted, it's time to start cutting. Begin by attaching the #4 or #5 guard to the clippers and running them over the hair. This will give you an even length across the top of the head. Work slowly and carefully, making sure to cover all areas of the head.

Once you've finished with the clippers, it's time to move on to the scissors. Use the comb to separate a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Then, use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Repeat this process across the top of the head, cutting small sections of hair at a time.

When you've finished cutting, use the clippers without a guard to trim the hair around the edges and back of the head. This will create a clean, finished look.

Maintain Your Style

To keep your long-on-top cut looking fresh, you'll need to maintain it regularly. This means trimming the hair every 4-6 weeks to keep the length under control. You can also use a styling product, such as pomade or gel, to add texture and hold to the hair.

Tips for Success

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  • Use the comb to create a straight part for a clean, professional look.
  • Work slowly and carefully to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Clean the clippers regularly to maintain their performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I cut my own hair with clippers?
A: Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers as long as you have the right tools and take your time.

Q: How do I know what length guard to use?
A: Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you find the desired length.

Q: Can I use regular scissors instead of haircutting scissors?
A: While regular scissors can be used, haircutting scissors are designed for a more precise cut and are recommended.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to take care of it properly. This means using a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoiding excessive heat styling, and eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and nutrients.

By following these tips and using the right tools, you can easily achieve a long-on-top cut with clippers. With a little practice and patience, you'll be able to maintain your new style and enjoy a fresh, modern look.
