Shoulder Length Hair For Heart Shaped Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a heart shaped face, you know how important it is to find the right hairstyle that flatters your features. Shoulder length hair is a great option for heart shaped faces, as it balances out a pointy chin and widens the forehead. In this guide, we'll show you how to rock shoulder length hair with ease and confidence.

Description

Shoulder length hair is a versatile style that works well for heart shaped faces. It falls just below the shoulders, framing the face and creating a soft, feminine look. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, shoulder length locks can be styled in a variety of ways to suit your individual taste and personality.

How to

To achieve the perfect shoulder length hair for your heart shaped face, follow these steps:

Step 1: Consult with your hairstylist

Before making any drastic changes to your hair, it's important to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you determine the best length, cut, and style for your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Choose the right length

Shoulder length hair should fall just below your shoulders, grazing your collarbone. This length will balance out your face shape and create a flattering silhouette.

Step 3: Opt for layers

Layers can add volume and movement to your shoulder length hair. Ask your hairstylist to add layers that start at your chin and work their way down.

Step 4: Experiment with bangs

Bangs can be a great addition to shoulder length hair, especially for heart shaped faces. Consider side swept bangs or blunt bangs that hit just above your eyebrows.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your shoulder length hair look its best:

Tip 1: Use the right products

To keep your shoulder length hair looking healthy and shiny, use high-quality hair products that are designed for your hair type.

Tip 2: Try different styling techniques

Shoulder length hair can be styled in a variety of ways, from loose waves to sleek and straight. Experiment with different techniques to find a style that suits you.

Tip 3: Embrace accessories

Hair accessories, such as headbands, clips, and scarves, can add a fun and playful touch to your shoulder length hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length hair for heart shaped faces:

Q: Can shoulder length hair be styled for formal occasions?

A: Yes! Shoulder length hair can be styled in elegant updos or half up/half down styles for formal occasions.

Q: Will shoulder length hair make my face appear wider?

A: No, shoulder length hair will balance out a heart shaped face by widening the forehead and adding volume around the shoulders.

Q: How often should I trim my shoulder length hair?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your shoulder length hair looking healthy and free of split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

Tip 1: Wash your hair every other day

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, causing it to become dry and brittle. Try washing your hair every other day to keep it healthy and shiny.

Tip 2: Use a deep conditioning treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to nourish and hydrate your hair. Leave the treatment on for at least 10 minutes before rinsing it out.

Tip 3: Protect your hair from heat

Before using heat styling tools, such as a curling iron or straightener, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock shoulder length hair with confidence and ease.
