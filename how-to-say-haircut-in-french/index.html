How To Say Haircut In French A Relaxed English Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 04, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you planning a trip to France and want to know how to ask for a haircut? Look no further! In this guide, we’ll teach you how to say haircut in French and give you some helpful tips for communicating with your stylist. So, let’s get started!

How to Say Haircut in French

If you want to ask for a haircut in French, the word you need to know is “coupe de cheveux”. It’s pronounced like “coo-puh duh shuh-vuh”. You can use this phrase to ask for a basic haircut, but if you want something more specific, you can add additional words to describe the style you want.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know how to say haircut in French, here are some steps you can follow to communicate with your stylist:

  1. Start by greeting your stylist with “Bonjour” (pronounced like “bohn-zhoor”), which means hello in French.
  2. Next, use the phrase “Je voudrais une coupe de cheveux” (pronounced like “juh voo-dray uh-nuh coo-puh duh shuh-vuh”), which means “I would like a haircut”.
  3. If you want a specific style, you can describe it by saying “Je voudrais une coupe de cheveux [insert style here]” (e.g. Je voudrais une coupe de cheveux courte for a short haircut).
  4. If you’re not sure how to describe the style you want, you can bring a picture or show your stylist a photo on your phone.
  5. During the haircut, you can use simple phrases like “c’est bien” (pronounced like “say bee-uhn”), which means “that’s good”.
  6. When the haircut is finished, you can say “Merci” (pronounced like “mehr-see”), which means “thank you”.

Tips for Communicating with Your Stylist

Here are some additional tips to help you communicate with your stylist:

  • Practice your pronunciation before your appointment so you feel more confident speaking French.
  • Bring a friend who speaks French with you to the salon if possible.
  • Use hand gestures or point to pictures if you’re having trouble finding the right words.
  • Ask your stylist to repeat themselves if you’re not sure what they’re saying.

FAQ

What if my stylist doesn’t speak English?

If your stylist doesn’t speak English, don’t worry! You can still use the phrase “coupe de cheveux” and show them a picture of the style you want. They may also be able to understand some basic English phrases, so don’t be afraid to try.

What if I don’t like my haircut?

If you’re unhappy with your haircut, try to explain what you don’t like in simple terms. Your stylist may be able to fix it or offer suggestions for a different style.

Hair Care

Now that you know how to say haircut in French, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair every few months to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals.

With these tips and phrases, you’ll be able to confidently communicate with your French stylist and get the haircut you want. Bonne chance!
