How To Hide Baby Bangs A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with baby bangs that just won't behave? Don't worry, you're not alone! Luckily, there are plenty of ways to hide those pesky bangs and get the hairstyle you want. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about hiding baby bangs, from tips and tricks to hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Hide Baby Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Hiding Baby Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Baby Bangs

How to Hide Baby Bangs

There are several ways to hide baby bangs, depending on your hair type and the look you want to achieve. Here are some popular options:

1. Pin Them Back

One of the easiest ways to hide baby bangs is to simply pin them back. You can use bobby pins, hair clips, or even a headband to keep them in place. This works especially well if your hair is long enough to pull back into a ponytail or bun.

2. Sweep Them to the Side

If you don't want to pin your bangs back, you can sweep them to the side instead. This works well if you have longer hair and can tuck the rest of your hair behind your ears. You can use a little bit of hairspray to keep them in place.

3. Cut Them Shorter

If all else fails, you can always cut your baby bangs shorter. This will make them easier to manage and blend in with the rest of your hair. However, if you're not comfortable cutting your own hair, it's best to leave this to a professional stylist.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to hiding baby bangs:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Decide how you want to hide your baby bangs (pin them back, sweep them to the side, or cut them shorter).
  3. If you're pinning them back, use bobby pins or hair clips to secure them in place.
  4. If you're sweeping them to the side, use a little bit of hairspray to keep them in place.
  5. If you're cutting them shorter, use sharp scissors and cut small sections at a time.
  6. Blow dry your hair to set the style.
  7. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Hiding Baby Bangs

Here are some additional tips for hiding baby bangs:

  • Use a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place without making it stiff or crunchy.
  • If you're pinning your bangs back, try to hide the bobby pins or hair clips under the rest of your hair.
  • If you're sweeping your bangs to the side, try to make the part look natural by following the direction of your hairline.
  • If you're cutting your bangs shorter, start with a little bit at a time and work your way up to the length you want.

FAQs

Q. Can I hide baby bangs with a hat?

A. Yes, a hat can be a great way to hide baby bangs. Just make sure to choose a hat that fits well and doesn't flatten your hair too much.

Q. How often should I trim my baby bangs?

A. It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your bangs start to look uneven. Generally, it's a good idea to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks.

Q. Can I use a curling iron to style my baby bangs?

A. Yes, you can use a curling iron to style your baby bangs. Just be careful not to burn yourself or your hair!

Hair Care for Baby Bangs

Hiding baby bangs can be a challenge, but taking care of them is just as important. Here are some tips for keeping your bangs healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your bangs, as this can cause breakage.
  • If you're using bobby pins or hair clips to secure your bangs, make sure to remove them gently to avoid pulling out hair.

Now that you know how to hide baby bangs, you can enjoy your new hairstyle with confidence. Remember, there are plenty of options to choose from, so don't be afraid to experiment until you find the perfect look for you!
