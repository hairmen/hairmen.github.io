Mens Haircut High Skin Fade The Ultimate Guide For 2023

Written by Oliver Oct 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh and trendy haircut for the new year, look no further than the mens haircut high skin fade. This popular style has been on the rise for several years now, and it's not hard to see why. With its clean and sharp look, it's the perfect style for any modern man. In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about the mens haircut high skin fade, from how to get it to how to take care of it.

Description

The mens haircut high skin fade is a hairstyle that features a gradual fade from the top of the head down to the sides and back. The fade is achieved by using clippers to gradually cut the hair shorter and shorter until it blends seamlessly with the skin. The top of the hair can be left longer or styled in a variety of ways, depending on your personal preference. This style is popular because it looks great on all face shapes and hair types, and it's low maintenance.

How to Get the Mens Haircut High Skin Fade

Step 1: Find a Good Barber

The first step to getting the mens haircut high skin fade is to find a good barber. Look for a barber who has experience with this style and can give you the look you want. Don't be afraid to ask for recommendations from friends or family.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

The next step is to decide on the length of the fade. The high skin fade typically starts around the temple and gradually fades to the skin around the ear. You can choose to have a shorter or longer fade depending on your personal preference.

Step 3: Choose the Top Style

Once you've decided on the length of the fade, you can choose the style for the top of your hair. The top can be left longer and styled in a variety of ways, such as a pompadour, quiff, or slick back. Or you can choose a shorter, more textured style like a crop or buzz cut.

Step 4: Maintain the Look

Once you've got your new haircut, it's important to maintain the look. Regular visits to the barber for touch-ups will keep the fade looking sharp. You can also use styling products to keep the top looking its best.

Tips for Maintaining the Mens Haircut High Skin Fade

Tip 1: Use a Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a quality shampoo and conditioner will help keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and avoid over-washing, which can strip the hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Use Styling Products

Using styling products like gel, pomade, or wax can help keep the top of your hair looking great. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

If you use heat styling tools like a blow dryer or straightener, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair. You can also let your hair air dry to minimize heat damage.

FAQ

How often should I get a touch-up?

It's recommended to get a touch-up every 2-3 weeks to keep the fade looking sharp.

Can I still style the top of my hair?

Yes, the top of your hair can be styled in a variety of ways, depending on your personal preference. Just be sure to use styling products that won't weigh your hair down or make it look greasy.

Is the mens haircut high skin fade suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the mens haircut high skin fade is suitable for all face shapes. The key is to choose the right length of the fade and style for the top of your hair to complement your face shape.

Hair Care for the Mens Haircut High Skin Fade

Tip 1: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair will minimize damage and breakage.

Tip 2: Avoid Over-Washing

Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every 2-3 days or as needed.

Tip 3: Trim Regularly

Regular trims will keep the fade looking sharp and prevent split ends.

With this guide, you're now ready to rock the mens haircut high skin fade in 2023. Just remember to find a good barber, choose the right length and style, and maintain the look with regular touch-ups and styling products.
