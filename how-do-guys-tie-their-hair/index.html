How Do Guys Tie Their Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a guy with long hair, you might be wondering how to tie it up in a stylish and practical way. There are many different ways to tie men's hair, depending on the length, texture, and personal preference. In this article, we'll discuss some popular methods for tying men's hair, from the classic ponytail to the sleek man bun. We'll also provide tips for hair care and answer some common questions about men's hair tying.

Classic Ponytail

The classic ponytail is a simple and timeless way to tie men's hair. To achieve this look, follow these steps:

  1. Brush or comb your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather all of your hair at the back of your head, at the level of your neck.
  3. Hold your hair in place with one hand, and use the other hand to tie a hair elastic around the base of the ponytail.
  4. Adjust the ponytail so that it sits at the desired height and looks neat and tidy.
  5. Optional: Use a small amount of hair gel or pomade to smooth down any flyaways or frizz.

The classic ponytail works well for most hair types and lengths, and it's a great option for sports or outdoor activities when you want to keep your hair out of your face.

Top Knot

The top knot is a trendy and stylish way to tie men's hair, especially for those with longer hair on top and shorter hair on the sides. Here's how to do it:

  1. Brush or comb your hair upwards, away from your face.
  2. Gather all of the hair on top of your head into a high ponytail.
  3. Twist the ponytail until it forms a tight rope of hair.
  4. Wrap the twisted hair around the base of the ponytail, creating a bun shape.
  5. Secure the bun in place with another hair elastic or bobby pins.
  6. Optional: Use a small amount of hair wax or clay to add texture and hold to the bun.

The top knot is a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down, depending on the occasion. It's also a good option for hot weather when you want to keep your hair off your neck.

Man Bun

The man bun is a more polished and refined version of the top knot, and it's a popular hairstyle among men with medium to long hair. Here's how to tie a man bun:

  1. Brush or comb your hair back into a high ponytail at the crown of your head.
  2. Twist the ponytail until it forms a tight rope of hair.
  3. Wrap the twisted hair around the base of the ponytail, creating a bun shape.
  4. Secure the bun in place with another hair elastic or bobby pins.
  5. Use a fine-toothed comb to smooth down any flyaways or frizz.
  6. Optional: Use a small amount of hair spray or serum to add shine and hold to the bun.

The man bun is a sophisticated and versatile hairstyle that can be worn to formal events or casual outings. It's also a good option for men with thick or coarse hair.

Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down style is a simple and easy way to tie men's hair, and it works well for those with longer hair that falls around the shoulders. Here's how to do it:

  1. Brush or comb your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take a section of hair from the front of your head, starting at the hairline.
  3. Pull the section of hair back and secure it with a hair elastic or clip.
  4. Leave the rest of your hair loose and flowing.

The half-up half-down style is a casual and relaxed look that's perfect for everyday wear. It's also a good option for men who want to keep their hair out of their face but still show off their length.

Messy Bun

The messy bun is a carefree and effortless way to tie men's hair, and it's a good option for those who prefer a more relaxed and natural look. Here's how to tie a messy bun:

  1. Brush or comb your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather all of your hair into a high ponytail.
  3. Twist the ponytail until it forms a loose rope of hair.
  4. Wrap the twisted hair around the base of the ponytail, creating a messy bun shape.
  5. Secure the bun in place with another hair elastic or bobby pins.
  6. Use your fingers to gently pull out some strands of hair around your face, creating a messy and tousled effect.

The messy bun is a casual and laid-back hairstyle that's perfect for lazy weekends or beach vacations. It's also a good option for men with wavy or curly hair.

Tips for Hair Care

Whether you tie your hair up or leave it loose, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for men's hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or straighteners, which can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote growth.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to nourish your hair from the inside out.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can guys with short hair tie their hair?

Yes, guys with short hair can tie their hair using methods like the classic ponytail or the half-up half-down style. However, the length and thickness of your hair will determine which styles are most suitable for you.

Is it bad for men to tie their hair up?

No, it's not necessarily bad for men to tie their hair up, as long as they do it properly and don't pull their hair too tight. However, tying your hair up too often or too tightly can cause breakage and damage to your hair over time.

How can I prevent my hair from getting tangled when I tie it up?

To prevent tangling, brush or comb your hair before tying it up, and use a hair elastic or clip that won't snag or pull on your hair. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and smoothness to your hair before tying it up.

Now that you know how to tie men's hair and take care of it, you can experiment with different styles and find the one that suits your personality and lifestyle. Whether you prefer a classic ponytail or a trendy man bun, remember to have fun and express yourself through your hair.
