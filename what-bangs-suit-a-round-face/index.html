What Bangs Suit A Round Face A Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Nov 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your same old hairstyle and looking for a change? One of the easiest ways to switch up your look is by getting bangs! However, if you have a round face, it can be tough to figure out what type of bangs will flatter your features. In this guide, we'll go over what bangs suit a round face, how to style them, and some hair care tips to keep them looking their best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by a soft, circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you'll want to choose bangs that will elongate your face and create the illusion of more angles.

When it comes to bangs, there are several different styles to choose from. Some popular options include:

  • Straight bangs
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Wispy bangs
  • Curly bangs
  • Blunt bangs

Each of these styles can work for a round face, but there are some key things to keep in mind when choosing the right one for you.

How to

First, consider the length of your bangs. Bangs that hit just above your eyebrows will help to elongate your face, while shorter bangs can make your face appear rounder.

Next, think about the thickness of your bangs. Bangs that are too thick can overwhelm your face, so opt for a more wispy or feathered look instead.

Finally, consider the shape of your bangs. Side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of more angles, while blunt bangs can make your face appear more circular.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the best bangs for your round face:

  1. Identify your face shape
  2. Choose a bang length that hits just above your eyebrows
  3. Opt for a wispy or feathered look
  4. Consider side-swept bangs to create more angles
  5. Avoid blunt bangs that can make your face appear more circular

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling your bangs:

  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and shape
  • Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz
  • Consider using a styling cream or serum to add shine and definition
  • If you're not sure which bangs will work best for you, ask your hairstylist for their opinion

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what bangs suit a round face:

What type of bangs should I avoid?

Avoid thick, blunt bangs that can make your face appear more circular.

Can I still wear side-swept bangs with a round face?

Absolutely! Side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of more angles and can be very flattering on a round face.

How often should I trim my bangs?

It's important to keep your bangs trimmed in order to maintain their shape and avoid any awkward grow-out phases. Aim to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

To keep your bangs looking their best, make sure to wash and condition them regularly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is gentle enough for your hair type and won't weigh your bangs down.

When blow drying your bangs, use a round brush to create volume and shape. If you're using a flat iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid heat damage.

Finally, consider using a styling cream or serum to add shine and definition to your bangs. Apply a small amount to your fingertips and work it through your bangs, focusing on the ends.

Now that you know what bangs suit a round face, it's time to head to the salon and try out a new look! With the right bangs, you can accentuate your features and create a flattering, face-framing style that will leave you feeling confident and stylish.
