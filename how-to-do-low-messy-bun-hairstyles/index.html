How To Do Low Messy Bun Hairstyles A Relaxed Guide

Written by Christine Sep 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Low messy bun hairstyles are a great way to get a casual yet chic look. They’re perfect for a day at the beach, running errands, or even a night out. Plus, they’re super easy to do! In this post, we’ll show you how to do low messy bun hairstyles step by step, along with some tips and FAQs. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Do Low Messy Bun Hairstyles
  • Step by Step Guide

How to Do Low Messy Bun Hairstyles

Low messy bun hairstyles are all about creating a relaxed, effortless look. Here’s how to do it:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before starting. If you have curly or wavy hair, you can use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves.

2. Brush your hair

Using a brush, gently brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

3. Gather your hair

Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and hold it in place with one hand.

4. Twist your hair

Using your other hand, twist your hair until it starts to coil around itself.

5. Form a bun

Continue twisting until your hair forms a bun. Use hairpins or bobby pins to secure the bun in place.

6. Pull out some strands

For a messy look, gently pull out some strands of hair around your face and at the back of your head.

Tips for Low Messy Bun Hairstyles

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect low messy bun:

  • Use hairpins or bobby pins to secure your bun in place.
  • For a more textured look, use a dry shampoo or texturizing spray before styling.
  • Use a brush to gently tease your hair at the crown of your head for added volume.
  • Don’t worry about making it look perfect – the messier, the better!

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about low messy bun hairstyles:

Q: Can I do a low messy bun with short hair?

A: Yes! You may need to use more hairpins or bobby pins to secure your bun in place, but it’s definitely doable.

Q: How do I make my messy bun stay in place?

A: Use hairpins or bobby pins to secure your bun in place, and consider using a hairspray to keep it in place.

Q: Can I wear a low messy bun to a formal event?

A: Yes! A low messy bun can be dressed up or down depending on your outfit and accessories.

Hair Care for Low Messy Bun Hairstyles

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray when styling.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Now that you know how to do low messy bun hairstyles, you can rock this look anywhere, anytime. Remember to have fun with it and embrace the messiness – that’s what makes it so cool!
