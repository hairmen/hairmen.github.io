How To Air Dry Frizzy Wavy Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who has frizzy wavy hair, you know the struggle of trying to tame your hair. Heat styling tools can only do so much and can often lead to damaged hair. Air drying your hair is a great alternative, but it can be intimidating for those who don't know how to do it properly. In this article, we'll go through the step-by-step process of how to air dry frizzy wavy hair and some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

How to Air Dry Frizzy Wavy Hair

Air drying your hair is a great way to give your hair a break from heat styling tools, but it requires some effort to get it right. Here's how to air dry frizzy wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to air drying your hair is to wash it. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Rinse your hair thoroughly, making sure there's no shampoo or conditioner left behind.

Step 2: Detangle Your Hair

After washing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle it. Start from the ends and work your way up to the roots. Make sure you're gentle while detangling your hair to avoid breakage.

Step 3: Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product that's suitable for your hair type. If your hair is frizzy, use a frizz control serum or cream. If your hair is wavy, use a curl enhancing product. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair upwards towards your scalp to encourage your natural waves. Do this gently to avoid disrupting the curl pattern.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Once you've applied your styling product and scrunched your hair, it's time to let it air dry. Avoid touching your hair while it's drying to prevent frizz. If you have time, let your hair air dry completely. If you're short on time, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to speed up the drying process.

Tips for Air Drying Frizzy Wavy Hair

Air drying frizzy wavy hair can be a challenge, but here are some tips to make the process easier:

Tip 1: Use a Microfiber Towel

Use a microfiber towel to dry your hair instead of a regular towel. Microfiber towels are gentler on your hair and can help prevent frizz.

Tip 2: Don't Touch Your Hair

Avoid touching your hair while it's drying to prevent frizz. If you need to adjust your hair, do it gently.

Tip 3: Avoid Humidity

Avoid going outside in humid weather while your hair is drying. Humidity can cause frizz, so it's best to stay indoors until your hair is completely dry.

Tip 4: Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz. Silk pillowcases are gentler on your hair and can help prevent breakage and frizz.

FAQ About Air Drying Frizzy Wavy Hair

Q: How long does it take to air dry frizzy wavy hair?

A: The time it takes to air dry frizzy wavy hair can vary depending on your hair type and the weather. It can take anywhere from 30 minutes to a few hours.

Q: Can I use a hair dryer to speed up the drying process?

A: Yes, you can use a hair dryer to speed up the drying process, but make sure you use a diffuser attachment to avoid disrupting your curl pattern.

Q: What products should I use to air dry my hair?

A: Use a styling product that's suitable for your hair type. If your hair is frizzy, use a frizz control serum or cream. If your hair is wavy, use a curl enhancing product.

Hair Care for Frizzy Wavy Hair

To keep your frizzy wavy hair healthy, here are some hair care tips:

Tip 1: Use a Sulfate-Free Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 4: Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

Air drying frizzy wavy hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve beautiful, healthy hair. Follow these tips and tricks to make the process easier and keep your hair looking its best.
