How Long Is A Grade 3 Haircut In Mm

Written by Aprilia Nov 20, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you planning to get a grade 3 haircut but wondering how long it will be? You've come to the right place. A grade 3 haircut is a popular choice for men who want a clean, short look without going completely bald. In this article, we will discuss how long is a grade 3 haircut in mm, and give you tips on how to maintain it.

Description

A grade 3 haircut is a clipper cut that leaves hair 3/8 of an inch or 9.5mm long. It is a versatile haircut that can suit almost any face shape and hair texture. It is also easy to maintain and style, making it a popular choice for men who don't have much time to spend on their hair.

How to

To get a grade 3 haircut, you will need a pair of clippers with a guard size of 3. Start by washing and drying your hair. Then, use the clippers to trim your hair evenly all over your head. Make sure to go against the grain of your hair for a closer cut. Once you finish, use a comb to remove any loose hairs and check for any uneven spots.

Step by Step

1. Wash and dry your hair
2. Choose the right clippers with a guard size of 3
3. Start trimming your hair evenly, going against the grain of your hair
4. Use a comb to remove any loose hairs and check for any uneven spots

Tips

1. Invest in a good pair of clippers
2. Use a mirror to check for any uneven spots
3. Don't cut your hair too short, as it may expose your scalp
4. Trim your hair regularly to maintain the length
5. Use a hair clipper oil to keep your clippers running smoothly and prevent rusting

FAQ

How long will a grade 3 haircut last?

A grade 3 haircut can last up to 4 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows. It is recommended to trim your hair every 2-3 weeks to maintain the length.

Can I style my hair with a grade 3 haircut?

Yes, you can style your hair with a grade 3 haircut. You can use hair products like pomade, wax, or gel to create different styles like a slick back, quiff, or messy look.

Is a grade 3 haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, a grade 3 haircut is suitable for almost all face shapes. It can add definition to round faces, elongate oblong faces, and balance out wide faces.

Hair Care

To maintain a grade 3 haircut, you should wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water and harsh chemicals that can dry out your scalp and hair. Use a hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair gently, starting from the ends and working your way up. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to keep your hair soft and shiny.

In conclusion, a grade 3 haircut is a popular choice for men who want a clean, short look without going completely bald. It leaves hair 3/8 of an inch or 9.5mm long and can suit almost any face shape and hair texture. To maintain the length, trim your hair regularly and use hair products to create different styles. Follow our hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
