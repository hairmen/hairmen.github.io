How To Thin Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Oct 05, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have thick hair, you might want to consider thinning it out to make it more manageable. While there are several ways to thin hair, using scissors is one of the most popular and effective methods. In this article, we’ll show you how to thin hair with scissors in a few easy steps.

How to Thin Hair with Scissors

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start thinning your hair, you’ll need to gather the necessary tools. You’ll need a pair of sharp hair-cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It’s best to start with clean, dry hair. Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before you start thinning it out.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into several sections using clips. This will make it easier to thin out your hair and ensure that you don’t miss any areas.

Step 4: Start Thinning Your Hair

Pick up a small section of hair, and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your fingers, and cut it at an angle, using the scissors to thin out the hair. Repeat this process for all the sections of hair, taking care to blend the hair as you go.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After you’ve finished thinning out your hair, check your progress in a mirror. Make sure that your hair is even and that you haven’t missed any sections.

Tips for Thinning Hair with Scissors

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when you’re thinning out your hair with scissors:

  • Start with small sections of hair
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair
  • Blend the hair as you go to ensure that it looks natural
  • Don’t thin out your hair too much, as it can lead to damage and breakage

FAQs: Thinning Hair with Scissors

Q: Can I thin out my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can thin out your hair at home using scissors. However, if you’re not confident in your abilities, it’s best to visit a professional stylist.

Q: How often should I thin out my hair?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how thick it is. As a general rule, you should thin out your hair every 3-6 months.

Q: Will thinning out my hair make it look thinner?

A: Yes, thinning out your hair can make it look thinner, but it will also make it more manageable and easier to style.

Hair Care after Thinning Hair with Scissors

After you’ve thinned out your hair, it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends

Conclusion

Thinning out your hair with scissors is a great way to make it more manageable and easier to style. By following the steps and tips in this article, you can achieve a natural and even look. Remember to take care of your hair properly after thinning it out to keep it healthy and looking great.
