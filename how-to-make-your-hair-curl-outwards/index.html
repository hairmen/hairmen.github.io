How To Make Your Hair Curl Outwards A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're tired of your straight hair and want to add some volume and bounce to your locks, then you might want to try curling your hair outwards. This style is perfect for people with medium to long hair who are looking for a fun and playful look. In this article, we will show you how to make your hair curl outwards in a few easy steps.

How to Make Your Hair Curl Outwards

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over some of the things you will need to get started.

What You Will Need:

  • Curling iron or wand
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairbrush or comb
  • Hairspray

Now that you have everything you need let's get started.

Step 1:

Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray. This will help protect your hair from damage when using hot tools.

Step 2:

Next, blow-dry your hair until it's completely dry.

Step 3:

Once your hair is dry, divide it into sections. The number of sections you make will depend on the thickness of your hair.

Step 4:

Take one section of hair and start curling it with a curling iron or wand. Make sure to curl the hair outwards, away from your face.

Step 5:

Hold the curl in place for 10-15 seconds, then release it.

Step 6:

Repeat steps 4 and 5 for each section of hair.

Step 7:

Once all of your hair is curled, use a hairbrush or comb to gently brush out the curls. This will give your hair a more natural and relaxed look.

Step 8:

Finish off your look with a spritz of hairspray to help hold the curls in place.

Tips for Making Your Hair Curl Outwards

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when curling your hair outwards.

  • Use a curling iron or wand with a larger barrel to create bigger curls.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly, as this can make your curls look unnatural.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.
  • If your hair is very fine or thin, you may want to use a volumizing mousse or spray before blow-drying.
  • Experiment with different sections sizes to achieve the look you want.

FAQs

How long will my curls last?

The length of time your curls will last will depend on your hair type and the products you use. On average, you can expect your curls to last anywhere from 6-12 hours.

Can I use a curling iron or wand on wet hair?

No, it's not recommended to use a curling iron or wand on wet hair. You should always make sure your hair is completely dry before using hot tools.

How can I make my curls last longer?

To make your curls last longer, you can use a strong hold hairspray or a hair mousse before curling your hair. You can also try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun to help maintain the curls overnight.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy hair.

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to help protect your hair from damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a month to help keep your hair hydrated.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you should be able to make your hair curl outwards in no time. Have fun experimenting with different sections sizes and products to achieve your desired look.
