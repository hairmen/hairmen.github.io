Diy Haircut Straight Across A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Nov 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut but don't want to spend money at the salon? Do you want to try cutting your hair straight across but don't know how? Look no further! In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to give yourself a DIY haircut straight across.

How to Cut Your Hair Straight Across in 10 Steps

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before starting, make sure to have all the tools you need. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It's important to start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure an even cut.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to detangle your hair and make sure it's smooth.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into two parts, parting it down the middle. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 5: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want your hair to be. It's important to be precise and measure carefully.

Step 6: Cut One Section

Start with one section, and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers where you want to cut it. Use the scissors to cut straight across. Be sure to cut slowly and carefully.

Step 7: Cut the Other Section

Repeat the same process on the other section. Make sure the length is even on both sides.

Step 8: Check Your Work

Once you're done, check your work in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments.

Step 9: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired. You can straighten it, curl it, or leave it as is.

Step 10: Clean Up

Clean up any loose hair that may have fallen during the process.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair Straight Across

Here are some additional tips to ensure a successful DIY haircut straight across:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take your time and cut slowly to avoid any mistakes.
  • Measure carefully to ensure an even cut.
  • Don't cut too much at once. You can always go back and cut more if needed.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from different angles.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my hair straight across if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut your hair straight across if it's curly. However, keep in mind that the length may appear shorter when the hair is dry and curly.

Can I cut my hair straight across if it's layered?

It's not recommended to cut your hair straight across if it's layered. This can result in an uneven cut and may require professional help to fix.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair Straight Across

After cutting your hair straight across, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and prevent split ends. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Use a nourishing hair mask once a week to hydrate your hair.

With these tips and steps, you can give yourself a DIY haircut straight across and save money at the salon. Remember to be patient and take your time, and don't hesitate to ask for help if needed. Happy cutting!
