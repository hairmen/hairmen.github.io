Short Hairstyles For Long Face Woman Tips And Tricks To Flatter Your Features

Are you a long-faced woman looking for the perfect short hairstyle? Look no further! In this article, we'll explore the best short hairstyles for long face women, as well as tips, tricks, and hair care advice to keep your locks healthy and happy.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for a Long Face

When choosing a short hairstyle for a long face, it's important to look for styles that add width and volume to the sides of your face while minimizing length. Here are some great options:

Bob Haircut

A classic bob haircut is a great choice for long-faced women. The length should hit just below the chin, with layers that add volume and movement to the sides of the face.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is another great option for long-faced women. Keep the layers short and choppy to add volume and dimension to the sides of the face.

Layered Shag

A layered shag haircut can be a great choice for long-faced women who want a more textured look. Keep the layers choppy and the length around the chin to add width to the sides of the face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for a Long Face

Now that you've chosen the perfect short hairstyle, it's time to style it! Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type. Long-faced women with fine hair should look for lightweight products, while those with thick hair should opt for more hydrating formulas.

Step 2: Towel-Dry

Gently towel-dry your hair, being careful not to rub it too vigorously. Long-faced women with curly hair can use a diffuser attachment on their blow dryer to help define their curls.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, working it through from roots to ends. This will help add texture and hold to your short hairstyle.

Step 4: Blow Dry

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, focusing on the sides to add volume and width to your face. Long-faced women with curly hair can skip this step and let their hair air dry.

Step 5: Style

Once your hair is dry, you can style it as desired. Long-faced women with a bob haircut can use a flat iron to straighten their hair, while those with a pixie cut can use a pomade to add texture and definition.

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Short Hair on a Long Face

Short hair on a long face can be a great look, but it requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips and tricks:

Tip 1: Trim Regularly

Short haircuts require more frequent trims to maintain their shape. Long-faced women should plan to visit the salon every 4-6 weeks to keep their short hairstyle looking fresh.

Tip 2: Use Heat Protectant

When using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 3: Experiment with Accessories

Short hair on a long face can be a great canvas for experimenting with accessories like headbands, barrettes, and scarves.

FAQ: Short Hairstyles for Long Face Women

Q: Can long-faced women wear bangs?

A: Yes! Bangs can be a great way to add balance to a long face. Try side-swept bangs or blunt bangs that hit just above the eyebrows.

Q: Are there any short hairstyles that long-faced women should avoid?

A: Long-faced women should generally avoid hairstyles that add length to the face, such as long layers or a center part. Stick to styles that add width and volume to the sides of the face.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair on a Long Face

Finally, here are some hair care tips to keep your short hair healthy and happy:

Tip 1: Use a Deep Conditioner

Short hair on a long face can be prone to dryness, particularly if you color or heat style your hair. Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your locks hydrated and healthy.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Long-faced women should be particularly careful about protecting their hair from the sun, which can cause dryness and damage. Use a leave-in conditioner with SPF or wear a hat when spending time outdoors.

Tip 3: Avoid Over-Washing

Short hair on a long face doesn't need to be washed every day. In fact, over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and lead to dryness and breakage. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

By following these tips and tricks, you can rock a short hairstyle on a long face with confidence and style. Experiment with different cuts and styles until you find the perfect look for you!
