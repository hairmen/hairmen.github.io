How To Cut A Short Spiky Haircut Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance and edgy hairstyle, a short spiky haircut might be the perfect fit for you. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in various ways. In this guide, we will show you how to cut a short spiky haircut in a relaxed English language. We will cover everything you need to know, including tips and tricks, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips. Let's get started!

  • How to Cut a Short Spiky Haircut

How to Cut a Short Spiky Haircut

The first step in cutting a short spiky haircut is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of scissors, clippers, and a comb. It's also essential to have a clear idea of the type of spiky haircut you want to achieve. You can browse through pictures online or consult with a hairstylist for inspiration.

Once you have all the tools and inspiration, you can start cutting your hair. It's crucial to ensure that your hair is clean and dry before starting the cutting process. Wet hair tends to look longer, and you may end up cutting more than necessary, resulting in a shorter haircut than you intended.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect short spiky haircut:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair. Divide your hair into four sections: front, back, and two sides.
  2. Trim the sides of your hair to your desired length using clippers. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard.
  3. Use scissors to trim the top of your hair. Cut small sections of hair at a time, holding the hair between your fingers and cutting at an angle to achieve texture.
  4. Trim the front of your hair to your desired length. You can opt for a fringe or leave it slightly longer for a messy look.
  5. Create spikes by using your fingers or a comb to lift sections of hair and cutting at an angle. You can also use a styling product to hold the spikes in place.
  6. Check for any uneven sections and trim as necessary.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect short spiky haircut:

  • Always start with a longer guard when using clippers. You can always go shorter, but it's challenging to fix a mistake if you go too short.
  • Hold your hair between your fingers and cut at an angle to achieve texture rather than a blunt cut.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your hair, consult with a professional hairstylist.
  • Use a hair product to hold the spikes in place and add texture.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a short spiky haircut:

How often should I cut my short spiky haircut?

It's recommended to cut your hair every four to six weeks to maintain the shape and style of your short spiky haircut.

Can I achieve a short spiky haircut with curly hair?

Yes, you can achieve a short spiky haircut with curly hair. However, it's crucial to consult with a professional hairstylist who specializes in cutting curly hair to ensure that the haircut complements your hair texture.

What styling products should I use for a short spiky haircut?

You can use a hair wax, pomade, or gel to hold the spikes in place and add texture to your short spiky haircut.

Hair Care Tips

To maintain the health and style of your short spiky haircut, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as blow dryers and flat irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

With this step-by-step guide, tips and tricks, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips, you can now confidently cut your short spiky haircut at home. Remember to take your time and be patient, and always consult with a professional hairstylist if you're unsure about cutting your hair. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve the perfect short spiky haircut that complements your style and personality.
