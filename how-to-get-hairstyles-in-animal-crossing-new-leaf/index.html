How To Get Hairstyles In Animal Crossing New Leaf

Written by Bardi Aug 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In Animal Crossing New Leaf, players can create their own character and customize their appearance, including their hairstyle. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this guide, we will go over how to get hairstyles in Animal Crossing New Leaf, step by step.

How to Unlock Hairstyles

To unlock new hairstyles in Animal Crossing New Leaf, players must first visit Shampoodle, the local hair salon. Shampoodle is located in the city, which becomes accessible after the player has upgraded their town to a certain level. Once in the city, the player can enter Shampoodle and speak to the stylist, Harriet.

Harriet will ask the player a series of questions to determine their preferred hairstyle. Once the questions are complete, the player can choose from a variety of hairstyles that match their preferences. However, not all hairstyles are available from the start. Some must be unlocked by completing certain tasks or meeting specific requirements.

Unlocking New Hairstyles

There are several ways to unlock new hairstyles in Animal Crossing New Leaf:

  • Get a Haircut: Getting a haircut at Shampoodle will unlock new hairstyles. The more haircuts you get, the more options become available.
  • Unlockable Hairstyles: Some hairstyles are unlocked by completing tasks or meeting requirements. For example, the "Afro" hairstyle is unlocked by donating 100,000 Bells to the town fund.
  • Amiibo Cards: Some hairstyles can be unlocked by scanning Amiibo cards. Simply visit the Wisp in the campground and scan the card to unlock the corresponding hairstyle.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to get a new hairstyle in Animal Crossing New Leaf:

  1. Upgrade your town to a certain level to unlock the city.
  2. Visit the city and enter Shampoodle.
  3. Speak to Harriet and answer her questions to determine your preferred hairstyle.
  4. Choose a hairstyle from the available options.
  5. If the desired hairstyle is not available, unlock it by completing tasks or meeting requirements.
  6. Get a haircut to unlock even more hairstyles.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you have unlocked a new hairstyle, you can create different looks by using hair accessories. Accessorizing your hair can add a unique touch to your character's appearance. Here are a few tips:

  • Use Hairpins: Hairpins can be purchased at Able Sisters and can be used to hold back hair or add a pop of color.
  • Experiment with Hats: Hats can be worn over hairstyles and can add personality to your character.
  • Try Headbands: Headbands can be purchased at Able Sisters and can be used to add a cute touch to any hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips

Just like in real life, taking care of your character's hair is important. Here are a few tips to keep your character's hair looking healthy:

  • Get Regular Haircuts: Getting regular haircuts will keep your character's hair looking neat and healthy.
  • Use Hair Products: Hair products can be purchased at Shampoodle and can be used to style your character's hair.
  • Don't Forget to Brush: Regular brushing will prevent tangles and keep your character's hair looking smooth.

FAQ

Can I change my hairstyle once I have chosen it?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle as many times as you like. Simply visit Shampoodle and speak to Harriet to choose a new hairstyle.

Do I have to unlock all hairstyles to access them?

No, you can choose any hairstyle that is available to you at the time of your visit to Shampoodle. However, unlocking new hairstyles will give you more options to choose from.

Can I wear accessories with any hairstyle?

Yes, most hair accessories can be worn with any hairstyle. However, some hairstyles may not work well with certain accessories.

With these tips and tricks, you can create endless hairstyle options for your character in Animal Crossing New Leaf. Don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with it!
