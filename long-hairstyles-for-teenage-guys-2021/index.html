Long Hairstyles For Teenage Guys 2021 The Ultimate Guide

If you're a teenage guy with long hair, you might be wondering what hairstyles are trending in 2021. Long hair is a great way to express your individuality and style, but it can be tricky to manage. In this article, we'll take you through some of the best long hairstyles for teenage guys in 2021, how to style them, and how to care for your locks.

How to Style Long Hair for Teenage Guys

Styling long hair can be a challenge, but there are a few key techniques you can use to make it easier. First, invest in some good quality hair products, like a strong hold styling gel or pomade. These will help to keep your hair in place and prevent flyaways.

Next, experiment with different partings and hairstyles to find what works best for you. Some popular long hairstyles for teenage guys in 2021 include:

  • Man Bun
  • Top Knot
  • Undercut
  • Long and Wavy
  • Braids
  • Ponytail

Man Bun

The man bun is a classic long hairstyle for men. To achieve this look, simply gather your hair at the top of your head and twist it into a bun. Secure with a hair tie or bobby pins. You can also experiment with a half-up, half-down style.

Top Knot

The top knot is similar to the man bun, but instead of twisting your hair into a bun, you tie it in a knot at the crown of your head. This style is great for keeping your hair out of your face and is perfect for a casual look.

Undercut

The undercut is a stylish and edgy long hairstyle for teenage guys. It involves shaving the sides and back of your head while leaving the hair on top long. You can experiment with different lengths and textures to make this look your own.

Long and Wavy

If you have naturally wavy hair, embrace it! This style is all about letting your hair flow naturally. Use a texturizing spray or mousse to enhance your waves and give your hair some volume.

Braids

Braids are a fun and versatile long hairstyle for teenage guys. You can experiment with different types of braids, like a French braid or a fishtail braid. Braids are also a great way to keep your hair out of your face during sports or other activities.

Ponytail

The ponytail is a classic and easy long hairstyle for teenage guys. Simply gather your hair at the back of your head and tie it in a ponytail. You can also experiment with different heights and styles, like a messy or slicked-back ponytail.

Step-by-Step Guide to Long Hairstyles for Teenage Guys

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling a man bun:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Gather your hair at the top of your head, making sure it's smooth and even.
  3. Twist your hair into a bun, making sure it's tight and secure.
  4. Use a hair tie or bobby pins to secure the bun in place.
  5. Use a strong hold styling gel or pomade to smooth down any flyaways.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair

Maintaining long hair takes time and effort, but there are a few tips you can follow to make it easier:

  • Brush your hair regularly to prevent tangles and knots.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

Long Hair Care for Teenage Guys

Here are some tips for taking care of long hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often.
  • Brush your hair regularly to prevent tangles and knots.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock any of the latest long hairstyles for teenage guys in 2021. Remember to experiment and have fun with your hair!
