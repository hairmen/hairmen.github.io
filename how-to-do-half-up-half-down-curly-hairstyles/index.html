How To Do Half Up Half Down Curly Hairstyles

Written by Robby Jun 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair is beautiful, but can also be difficult to manage. One way to style curly hair is by doing a half up half down hairstyle. This style is perfect for any occasion, whether it's a casual day out or a formal event. In this blog post, we will show you how to do half up half down curly hairstyles, step-by-step.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect half up half down curly hairstyle:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and condition your hair with products that are suitable for curly hair. It's important to ensure your hair is completely dry before styling to avoid frizz.

Step 2: Section your hair

Take a section of hair from the crown of your head to the top of your ears. Use a clip to hold the section in place while you style the remaining hair.

Step 3: Curl your hair

Curl the remaining hair using a curling iron or a wand. You can choose to curl your hair in different directions to create a more natural look.

Step 4: Tease the crown section

Tease the crown section lightly to create volume. This will give your hairstyle a more polished look.

Step 5: Secure the crown section

Take the teased section and pull it back. Secure it with bobby pins or an elastic band. You can choose to keep the crown section sleek or add volume by lightly teasing it more.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

Spray your hair with hairspray to keep the style in place.

Tips for Half Up Half Down Curly Hairstyles

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect half up half down curly hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage caused by heat styling tools.
  • Choose a curling iron or wand with a barrel size that is suitable for your hair length and type.
  • Use bobby pins or an elastic band that match your hair color to avoid them being visible.
  • Add hair accessories such as hair clips or headbands to enhance the look of your hairstyle.

FAQs

Can I do a half up half down curly hairstyle on short hair?

Yes, you can still achieve a half up half down curly hairstyle on short hair. Adjust the size of the crown section and the length of the curls according to your hair length.

How do I avoid frizz when styling curly hair?

Use products that are suitable for curly hair and avoid touching your hair too much while styling. Use a diffuser when blow drying and avoid using a towel to dry your hair as this can cause frizz.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to maintain its shape and prevent frizz. Here are some tips:

  • Use shampoo and conditioner that are specifically made for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as this can cause frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as this can damage your hair. Try air drying your hair or using a diffuser when blow drying.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.

With these tips and steps, you can easily achieve a half up half down curly hairstyle. Remember to take care of your curly hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
