Does A Lob Make You Look Thinner

Written by Eveline Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're considering getting a new haircut, you might be wondering if a lob, or long bob, can make you look thinner. The answer is yes, a lob can make you look thinner, but it depends on a few factors. In this blog post, we'll explore how a lob can give you a slimmer appearance, how to achieve the look, and some tips for caring for your new haircut.

Description

A lob is a versatile haircut that falls somewhere between a bob and a shoulder-length cut. It's a popular choice because it flatters most face shapes and hair types. The length of a lob can vary, but it typically falls just above or below the collarbone.

How To

The key to making a lob look slimming is to create volume at the crown of your head. This will elongate your face and draw attention away from any areas you want to minimize. Here's how to achieve the look:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray at the roots
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down, using your fingers to lift and separate the hair
  • Once your hair is dry, use a large barrel curling iron to create loose waves
  • Brush out the curls with a wide-tooth comb to create soft, bouncy waves
  • Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep the style in place

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a slimming lob:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and condition your hair as you normally would, then let it air dry or blow-dry it until it's completely dry.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing product

Use a volumizing mousse or spray at the roots of your hair to create lift and volume.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair upside down

Flip your head upside down and use your fingers to lift and separate your hair as you blow-dry it. This will create additional volume at the crown of your head.

Step 4: Curl your hair

Use a large barrel curling iron to create loose waves throughout your hair. Curl your hair away from your face to open up your features.

Step 5: Brush out your curls

Use a wide-tooth comb to brush out your curls and create soft, bouncy waves.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

Spray a light-hold hairspray all over your hair to keep the style in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your lob:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage
  • Don't use too much product, as it can weigh down your hair
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair
  • Visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks to maintain your haircut

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about lobs:

Q: Will a lob suit my face shape?

A: Lobs are versatile and can flatter most face shapes. However, it's best to consult with your stylist to find the most flattering cut for your face.

Q: How long does a lob take to style?

A: Styling time can vary depending on your hair type and the products you use. On average, it takes around 20-30 minutes to style a lob.

Q: Can I wear my lob straight?

A: Yes, you can wear your lob straight or curly. Experiment with different styles to find the most flattering look for you.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for keeping your lob in top condition:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or swim cap
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage

Overall, a lob can be a flattering and slimming haircut if styled correctly. By creating volume at the crown of your head and adding soft waves, you can elongate your face and create a more slender appearance. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny, and visit your stylist regularly to maintain your haircut.
