Mens Haircut Short Back And Sides Clippers How To Achieve The Look

Written by Aprilia Oct 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a classic, clean-cut hairstyle that's easy to maintain, a mens haircut short back and sides clippers is the way to go. This timeless look has been a staple in men's grooming for decades, and it's still as popular as ever. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect mens haircut short back and sides clippers, step by step.

Description

The mens haircut short back and sides clippers is a simple and versatile hairstyle that involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back while leaving a bit more length on top. This style can be worn with a side part, slicked back, or left messy for a more casual look. It's a great option for guys who want a low-maintenance haircut that looks sharp and professional.

How to Achieve the Look

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect mens haircut short back and sides clippers:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

The first step in achieving the perfect mens haircut short back and sides clippers is to choose the right clippers. Look for a high-quality pair of clippers that are easy to use and come with a variety of guards.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you get an even result.

Step 3: Choose the Right Guard

Select the guard size you want to use for the sides and back of your head. For a classic mens haircut short back and sides clippers, a #2 or #3 guard is usually the way to go.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Begin cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head, using the clippers and guard of your choice. Make sure to keep the clippers level and move them in an upward motion to create an even cut.

Step 5: Blend the Top

Once you've finished cutting the sides and back, it's time to blend the top. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs and create a smooth transition between the top and sides of your head.

Step 6: Style as Desired

Finally, style your hair as desired. You can use a pomade or hair wax to create a slicked-back look or leave it messy for a more casual style.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect mens haircut short back and sides clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  • Use a comb to help guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help if you're not confident in your cutting skills.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens haircut short back and sides clippers:

Is a mens haircut short back and sides clippers suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a mens haircut short back and sides clippers can work for any hair type. However, it may not be the best option for guys with very curly hair, as it can be difficult to achieve a smooth, even cut.

How often should I get a mens haircut short back and sides clippers?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how often you want to maintain the look. Most guys can go 4-6 weeks between haircuts.

Can I achieve a mens haircut short back and sides clippers at home?

Yes, with the right tools and some practice, you can achieve a mens haircut short back and sides clippers at home. However, if you're not confident in your cutting skills, it's best to leave it to a professional.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircut short back and sides clippers looking sharp, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Visit a stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect mens haircut short back and sides clippers and keep it looking great for weeks to come.
