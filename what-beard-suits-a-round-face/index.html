What Beard Suits A Round Face

Written by Aprilia Aug 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering what type of beard suits you best. The good news is that there are plenty of beard styles that can flatter a round face shape. In this article, we will discuss the best beard styles for round faces and provide tips on how to maintain your beard.

Description

A round face is characterized by a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. While this face shape is often associated with a youthful appearance, it can also make it challenging to find a beard style that complements your features. The key is to choose a beard style that adds length and definition to your face, rather than making it appear even rounder.

How to Choose the Right Beard Style

When choosing a beard style for a round face, the goal is to create the illusion of a more angular shape. This can be achieved by selecting a beard style that adds length to your face and creates a sharper jawline. Here are some tips to help you choose the right beard style:

1. Opt for a Longer Beard

A longer beard can help elongate your face and create a more defined jawline. Consider growing a full beard or a goatee that extends past your chin. Avoid shorter beard styles, such as the stubble beard, as they can make your face appear even rounder.

2. Choose a Beard Style with Angular Lines

Beard styles with angular lines, such as the boxed beard or the anchor beard, can help create the illusion of a more chiseled jawline. Avoid rounded beard styles, such as the circle beard, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face.

3. Keep Your Beard Trimmed and Tidy

Keeping your beard well-groomed is essential for any face shape, but it is especially important for a round face. A neat and tidy beard can help create a more polished appearance and prevent your beard from overwhelming your face. Consider investing in a high-quality beard trimmer and regularly trimming your beard to maintain its shape.

Step by Step Guide to Maintaining Your Beard

Maintaining your beard is essential to keep it looking its best. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you keep your beard looking great:

Step 1: Wash Your Beard

Use a mild shampoo or beard wash to keep your beard clean and free from dirt and oil buildup. Be sure to rinse thoroughly and pat dry with a clean towel.

Step 2: Comb Your Beard

Use a beard comb or brush to detangle your beard and remove any knots or tangles. This will help keep your beard looking neat and tidy.

Step 3: Trim Your Beard

Use a beard trimmer to trim your beard to your desired length. Be sure to trim evenly on both sides and use a higher guard setting on the sides of your beard to create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

Step 4: Apply Beard Oil

Apply a few drops of beard oil to your beard to keep it moisturized and healthy. Beard oil can also help prevent itchiness and dryness.

Tips for Maintaining a Healthy Beard

Maintaining a healthy beard is essential for any beard style. Here are some tips to keep your beard looking its best:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet can help promote healthy hair growth and prevent dryness and breakage. Be sure to eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals, such as vitamin C and biotin.

2. Stay Hydrated

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your beard and skin hydrated, preventing dryness and itchiness.

3. Avoid Overwashing

While it is essential to keep your beard clean, overwashing can strip your beard of its natural oils and cause dryness and irritation. Aim to wash your beard 2-3 times per week.

FAQ

Q: What beard styles should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: You should avoid beard styles that accentuate the roundness of your face, such as the circle beard or the chinstrap beard. Instead, opt for beard styles with angular lines and added length.

Q: Can I still wear a short beard if I have a round face?

A: While a short beard can be challenging to pull off with a round face, it is possible. Be sure to keep your beard well-groomed and use a higher guard setting on the sides to create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

Q: How long does it take to grow a full beard?

A: The length of time it takes to grow a full beard varies from person to person. It can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months to grow a full beard.

Conclusion

If you have a round face, don't be discouraged. There are plenty of beard styles that can flatter your features and create a more defined jawline. By choosing a beard style with added length and angular lines and keeping your beard well-groomed, you can achieve a polished and stylish look that complements your face shape.
