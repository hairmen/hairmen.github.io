If you're a fan of Animal Crossing: New Horizons (ACNH), you know that there are plenty of ways to customize your character's appearance, from clothing to hairstyles. Step-by-Step Guide

To get a new haircut in ACNH, you'll need to visit Harriet's salon, Shampoodle. Here's how to do it:

  1. Make sure you have unlocked the Able Sisters clothing store on your island.
  2. Speak to Sable (the hedgehog) at the Able Sisters store for 10 days in a row.
  3. After the 10th day, Sable will give you a Sewing Machine DIY recipe.
  4. Craft the Sewing Machine and place it on your island.
  5. Wait for a random NPC to visit your island (e.g. Labelle, Kicks, etc.)
  6. Speak to the NPC and they will tell you about Shampoodle.
  7. Visit Shampoodle and speak to Harriet (the poodle).
  8. Choose the "Makeover" option and select "Change hairstyle".
  9. Harriet will show you a series of hairstyles to choose from.
  10. Select the one you want and confirm your choice.
  11. Pay the fee (3,000 Bells) and your new haircut will be complete!

Congratulations, you've successfully gotten a new haircut in ACNH!

Tips for Getting New Haircuts ACNH

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting new haircuts in ACNH:

  • Shampoodle is only open from 10am to 8pm, so make sure you visit during those hours.
  • Each visit to Shampoodle costs 3,000 Bells, so make sure you have enough money before you go.
  • You can only change your hair once per day, so choose wisely!
  • Some hairstyles are only available to certain genders, so make sure you check before selecting a new style.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting new haircuts in ACNH:

How often can I change my hair?

You can only change your hair once per day, so choose wisely!

How much does it cost to get a new haircut?

Each visit to Shampoodle costs 3,000 Bells.

Can I get any hairstyle I want?

Some hairstyles are only available to certain genders, so make sure you check before selecting a new style.

Hair Care in ACNH

Now that you have a new haircut, it's important to take care of it! Here are a few tips:

  • Regularly visit Shampoodle to maintain your hairstyle.
  • Use a mirror to see the back of your character's head, so you can make sure your hair looks good from all angles.
  • Accessorize your hair with hats, headbands, and other items.
  • Consider using a wig or hairpiece to change up your look without visiting Shampoodle.

With these tips, you'll be able to get new haircuts in ACNH and take care of your character's hair. Happy styling!
