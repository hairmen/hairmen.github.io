How To Make Thick Straight Hair Wavy A Step By Step Guide

Do you have thick, straight hair and want to try something new? Wavy hair is a great option that adds volume and texture to your locks. In this guide, we'll show you how to make thick straight hair wavy in a few simple steps. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Making Wavy Hair Last Longer

How to Make Thick Straight Hair Wavy

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about the tools and products you'll need to create wavy hair:

  • Heat protectant spray
  • Curling iron or wand
  • Clips or bobby pins
  • Hairspray

Now that you have everything you need, let's move on to the step-by-step guide.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair as you normally would. Make sure to use a moisturizing conditioner that will help protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant Spray

Before you start curling your hair, apply a heat protectant spray to protect your locks from heat damage. This will also help your curls last longer.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair into manageable pieces using clips or bobby pins. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around your curling iron or wand. Hold the curler in place for a few seconds and then release. Repeat this process with all of the sections of your hair.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Cool

After curling your hair, let it cool for a few minutes. This will help set the curls and make them last longer.

Step 6: Finger Comb Your Hair

Once your hair has cooled, use your fingers to gently comb through your hair. This will help loosen the curls and create a more natural, wavy look.

Step 7: Apply Hairspray

To help your waves last longer, apply a light mist of hairspray over your hair. Make sure not to use too much hairspray, as this can weigh down your hair.

Tips for Making Wavy Hair Last Longer

Now that you've created wavy hair, here are a few tips to help your waves last longer:

  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help maintain your waves.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause your waves to fall out.
  • Use a dry shampoo to help absorb oil and keep your waves looking fresh.

FAQs

Q: Can I make my hair wavy without heat?

A: Yes, you can make your hair wavy without heat by braiding your hair while it's damp and leaving it to dry overnight. You can also use a sea salt spray to create beachy waves.

Q: How often should I wash my wavy hair?

A: It's best to wash your wavy hair every two to three days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Wavy hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.

By following these tips and using the right tools and products, you can easily make your thick straight hair wavy. So, go ahead and try out this fun and trendy hairstyle!
