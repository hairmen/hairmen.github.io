How Are Curtain Bangs Cut A Step By Step Guide With Tips And Hair Care Advice

Written by Daniel Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curtain bangs, also known as Bardot bangs, are a popular hairstyle that frames the face and adds a touch of glamor to any look. This type of bangs is characterized by its center-parted, wispy, and face-framing strands that create a curtain-like effect around the forehead. Curtain bangs can suit any face shape and hair texture, making them a versatile and low-maintenance option for those who want to switch up their look without committing to a drastic cut or color.

How to Cut Curtain Bangs

If you're wondering how to cut curtain bangs, the good news is that you don't need to be a professional hairstylist to do it. With a few tools and some basic knowledge, you can achieve the perfect curtain bangs at home. However, keep in mind that cutting your hair can be risky, and mistakes can be costly. If you're unsure about your skills or you have a specific hairstyle in mind, it's always best to seek the help of a professional stylist.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut curtain bangs:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Comb it straight and make sure it's detangled.
  2. Part your hair in the center. Use a comb or your fingers to create a straight line from your hairline to the back of your head.
  3. Section off the hair you want to cut. Use clips or hair ties to separate the rest of your hair from the bangs.
  4. Decide on the length of your bangs. Keep in mind that they'll look shorter when they're dry, so it's best to start with a longer length than you want.
  5. Start cutting. Use sharp scissors and cut the hair at an angle. Start from the center and work your way outwards.
  6. Check your progress. Release the hair from the clips and see if you're happy with the length and shape of your bangs.
  7. Trim any uneven strands. Use the scissors to trim any hairs that are longer or shorter than the rest.
  8. Style your bangs. Use a blow dryer, a round brush, or a straightener to style your bangs the way you like them.

Remember, less is more when it comes to cutting your hair. You can always cut more later, but you can't put it back once it's gone. Take your time, be patient, and don't rush the process.

Tips and Hair Care Advice

Here are some tips and hair care advice for curtain bangs:

  • Use a sharp pair of scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and create split ends.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet. Wet hair is longer and heavier, which can lead to uneven cuts.
  • Start with a longer length than you want. You can always trim more later.
  • Don't cut your bangs straight across. Cut them at an angle to create a more natural look.
  • Style your bangs regularly. Use a blow dryer, a round brush, or a straightener to keep your bangs looking fresh and styled.
  • Get regular trims. To maintain your curtain bangs, trim them every 4-6 weeks.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

How long should curtain bangs be?

Curtain bangs can be any length you like, but they usually fall between the eyebrows and the cheekbones. Keep in mind that they'll look shorter when they're dry, so it's best to start with a longer length than you want.

Can curtain bangs suit any face shape?

Yes, curtain bangs can suit any face shape. However, the length and shape of the bangs can vary depending on your face shape and features. It's best to consult with a hairstylist to find the best style for you.

Can I cut my own curtain bangs?

Yes, you can cut your own curtain bangs, but it's important to be careful and take your time. If you're unsure about your skills or you have a specific hairstyle in mind, it's always best to seek the help of a professional stylist.

How do I style my curtain bangs?

You can style your curtain bangs with a blow dryer, a round brush, or a straightener. Use the tool that works best for your hair texture and the style you want to achieve. You can also use hair products like mousse or hairspray to add volume and hold.

How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

To maintain your curtain bangs, trim them every 4-6 weeks. This will help keep them looking fresh and styled.

Now that you know how to cut curtain bangs, you can try this trendy hairstyle at home. Remember to be patient, take your time, and follow these tips and hair care advice to achieve the best results. With a little practice and some confidence, you can rock the curtain bangs like a pro!
