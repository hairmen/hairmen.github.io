Black Men s Curly Hairstyles 2021 A Guide To Looking Your Best

If you're a black man with curly hair, you may be wondering what hairstyles are popular in 2021. From short and cropped to long and flowing, there are so many options to choose from. In this guide, we'll go over some of the hottest black men's curly hairstyles for 2021, along with tips on how to achieve them and care for your hair.

How to Style Your Curly Hair

When it comes to styling your curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you'll want to choose a style that complements your face shape and features. Second, you'll want to consider the length and texture of your hair. Finally, you'll want to choose products that will help you achieve your desired look.

Some popular styles for black men with curly hair in 2021 include the high top fade, the curly afro, the short textured crop, and the messy curly fringe. Each of these styles can be achieved with a combination of styling products and techniques.

High Top Fade

The high top fade is a classic style that has been popular for decades. To achieve this look, you'll want to have your hair cut short on the sides and back, while leaving the top longer. You can then shape the top of your hair into a flat, squared-off shape, or you can create a more rounded look.

Curly Afro

The curly afro is another classic style that is perfect for black men with curly hair. To achieve this look, you'll want to grow your hair out to a medium length and then shape it into a rounded, curly afro shape. You can also add some product to define your curls and give them extra volume.

Short Textured Crop

The short textured crop is a great option for black men with curly hair who prefer a more low-maintenance style. To achieve this look, you'll want to have your hair cut short all over, but with some texture left on top. You can then add some product to define your curls and give your hair some extra texture.

Messy Curly Fringe

The messy curly fringe is a trendy style that is perfect for guys who like a more relaxed, casual look. To achieve this look, you'll want to have your hair cut to a medium length and then add some product to define your curls and create a tousled, messy look on top.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Here are some general steps you can follow to style your curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, moisturized hair. Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to cleanse and condition your hair without stripping it of its natural oils.
  2. Apply a styling product. Choose a product that is designed for curly hair, such as a curl cream, gel, or mousse. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  3. Shape your hair. Use your fingers or a comb to shape your hair into your desired style. You may need to experiment with different techniques to find what works best for your hair type and texture.
  4. Finish with a hairspray or finishing product. This will help to hold your style in place and add some extra shine and definition to your curls.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Hair

Maintaining your curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can keep your hair looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet. This will help to prevent breakage and damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished. You can use a store-bought deep conditioner or make your own using natural ingredients like avocado, honey, and coconut oil.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as relaxers and perms. These can damage your hair and cause it to become weak and brittle.

FAQs

Q: What products should I use on my curly hair?

A: You should look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl creams, gels, and mousses. These products can help to define your curls and keep them looking sleek and shiny.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and texture, but most experts recommend washing your hair every 2-3 days. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

A: You can prevent frizz by using a leave-in conditioner or anti-frizz serum, avoiding heat styling tools, and keeping your hair well-moisturized.

Hair Care for Black Men with Curly Hair

Black men with curly hair have unique hair care needs, and it's important to choose products and techniques that are specifically designed for your hair type. Here are some key tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to cleanse and moisturize your hair without stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as relaxers and perms, which can damage your hair and cause it to become weak and brittle.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet. This will help to prevent breakage and damage.

By following these tips and choosing the right products and techniques, you can keep your black men's curly hairstyle looking its best in 2021 and beyond.
