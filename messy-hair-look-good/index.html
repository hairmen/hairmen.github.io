Messy Hair Look Good How To Achieve The Perfectly Disheveled Look

Written by Petter Jun 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who wakes up in the morning with hair that's all over the place, you're in luck. The messy hair look is in, and it's a great way to embrace your natural texture and add some extra style to your overall look. Not sure how to pull off the perfectly disheveled look? We've got you covered. Here's everything you need to know about rocking messy hair.

What is the Messy Hair Look?

The messy hair look is all about embracing your natural texture and creating a disheveled, effortless style. It's a great way to add some extra personality to your look, especially if you're someone who likes to keep things low-maintenance. This look works well for both men and women, and it's versatile enough to wear to work, school, or a night out on the town.

How to Achieve the Messy Hair Look

There are a few different ways to achieve the messy hair look, depending on your hair type and texture. Here are some tips to get you started:

1. Use a Texturizing Spray

A texturizing spray is a great way to add some extra volume and texture to your hair. Simply spray it onto your roots and tousle your hair with your fingers to create a messy, undone look.

2. Embrace Your Natural Texture

If you have curly or wavy hair, embrace your natural texture by scrunching your hair with a bit of styling cream. This will help define your curls and create a messy, beachy look.

3. Try a Messy Bun

A messy bun is a great way to create a messy, effortless look. Simply gather your hair into a loose bun at the nape of your neck, leaving a few pieces out around your face. Pull out a few strands to create a more disheveled look.

4. Use a Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo is a great way to add some extra texture and volume to your hair. Spritz it onto your roots and tousle your hair with your fingers to create a messy, undone look.

Tips for Pulling off the Messy Hair Look

Now that you know how to achieve the messy hair look, here are some tips to help you pull it off:

1. Don't Overdo It

The key to pulling off the messy hair look is to keep it natural and effortless. Don't try to over-style your hair, as this will take away from the overall look.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right products is key when it comes to achieving the messy hair look. Look for texturizing sprays, dry shampoos, and styling creams that are designed to create a messy, undone look.

3. Keep it Low-Maintenance

The messy hair look is all about keeping things low-maintenance. Don't spend too much time styling your hair, and embrace your natural texture as much as possible.

4. Add Some Accessories

Adding some accessories can help elevate the messy hair look. Try adding a headband or a few hair clips to create a more polished look.

Messy Hair Look FAQ

Q: Is the messy hair look appropriate for work?

A: Yes! The messy hair look can be worn to work, as long as you keep things natural and effortless. Avoid over-styling your hair and keep it looking as natural as possible.

Q: What hair types work best for the messy hair look?

A: The messy hair look works well for all hair types, but it's especially great for those with wavy or curly hair. If you have straight hair, try using a texturizing spray to add some extra volume and texture.

Hair Care Tips for Messy Hair

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your messy hair look:

1. Use a Clarifying Shampoo

Using a clarifying shampoo once a week can help remove any product buildup from your hair, which can weigh it down and make it look less voluminous.

2. Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can make it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every other day, or even less frequently if possible.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help keep your hair hydrated and healthy, which is especially important if you're using a lot of styling products.

4. Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts can help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair look less voluminous and textured.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to achieving the perfectly disheveled messy hair look. Embrace your natural texture and have fun experimenting with different styles and products. Happy styling!
