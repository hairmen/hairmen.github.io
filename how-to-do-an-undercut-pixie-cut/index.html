How To Do An Undercut Pixie Cut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a bold and daring haircut, an undercut pixie cut might just be the perfect choice for you! This edgy and stylish cut features shaved sides and back with longer hair on top. But how do you go about creating this look? In this guide, we'll take you through the steps of how to do an undercut pixie cut, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Do an Undercut Pixie Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for the Perfect Cut
  • FAQs

How to Do an Undercut Pixie Cut

An undercut pixie cut involves shaving the sides and back of the head, leaving longer hair on top. It's important to have a clear idea of the length and style you want before beginning. This cut can be done at home, but if you're not confident with a razor or clippers, it's best to go to a professional stylist.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a simple guide to creating an undercut pixie cut:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before starting the cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections, leaving the top section longer than the sides and back. Use hair clips to secure the top section out of the way.

Step 3: Shave the Sides and Back

Using clippers or a razor, shave the sides and back of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using a comb to guide the clippers or razor. Be careful not to cut too much off at once.

Step 4: Blend the Top Section

Use scissors or a razor to blend the top section with the shaved sides and back. Blend carefully to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting, style your hair as desired. You can use a styling product to add texture and volume to the top section.

Tips for the Perfect Cut

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating an undercut pixie cut:

  • Start with a longer top section and gradually cut it shorter to ensure you don't take off too much at once.
  • Use a comb to guide your clippers or razor and create a more precise cut.
  • Be patient and take your time to avoid any mistakes.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find the perfect look for you.

FAQs

Q: Will an undercut pixie cut suit me?

A: This cut can suit many face shapes and hair types, but it's best to consult with a stylist to determine if it's the right choice for you.

Q: How often do I need to maintain my undercut pixie cut?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows, but you may need to touch up the sides and back every few weeks.

Q: Can I still style my hair after an undercut pixie cut?

A: Yes! You can still style the top section of your hair as desired, using products to add volume, texture, or hold.

Hair Care After the Cut

After getting an undercut pixie cut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause further damage.
  • Apply a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your cut.

With these tips and steps, you'll be able to create a stunning undercut pixie cut that suits your style and personality. Whether you're looking for a dramatic change or just want to switch up your look, this cut is sure to turn heads!
