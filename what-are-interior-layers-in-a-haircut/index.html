What Are Interior Layers In A Haircut

Written by William Aug 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Content:

  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Interior Layers in a Haircut
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Description

Interior layers are the layers of hair that are cut in the interior of the hair, away from the exterior or outermost layer. These layers are usually cut to add volume, texture, and movement to the hair. Interior layers are a popular hair cutting technique that is used by hairstylists to create a variety of hairstyles, from long layers to short bobs.

Interior layers can be cut in different lengths, depending on the desired effect. Shorter interior layers will create more volume and texture, while longer interior layers will create a more subtle effect. The placement of the interior layers will also vary depending on the hairstyle and the client's hair texture and face shape.

How to Get Interior Layers in a Haircut

The best way to get interior layers in a haircut is to go to a professional hairstylist. A hairstylist will be able to assess your hair texture, face shape, and desired hairstyle, and cut the interior layers accordingly.

Before getting a haircut with interior layers, it is important to have a consultation with the hairstylist. During the consultation, you can discuss your desired hairstyle and the hairstylist can recommend the best interior layering technique for your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to get interior layers in a haircut:

  1. Book a consultation with a professional hairstylist.
  2. Discuss your desired hairstyle and the hairstylist's recommendations for interior layering.
  3. Wash your hair before the appointment and arrive with clean, dry hair.
  4. The hairstylist will section your hair and begin cutting the interior layers, using scissors or a razor.
  5. The hairstylist will continue to cut the interior layers in different lengths, depending on the desired effect.
  6. The hairstylist will blend the interior layers with the exterior layer for a seamless look.
  7. The hairstylist will style your hair and show you how to maintain your new look.

Tips for Maintaining Interior Layers in a Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining interior layers in a haircut:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to enhance the volume of the interior layers.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume to the interior layers.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying to create movement and texture in the interior layers.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down the interior layers.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and texture of the interior layers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will interior layers work on all hair types?

A: Yes, interior layers can be cut on all hair types, but the placement and length of the layers will vary depending on the hair texture and face shape.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut with interior layers?

A: It is recommended to get a haircut with interior layers every 6 to 8 weeks to maintain the shape and texture of the layers.

Hair Care for Interior Layers in a Haircut

Here are some hair care tips for interior layers in a haircut:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep the interior layers healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often, as they can damage the interior layers.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair too vigorously, as this can disrupt the texture of the interior layers.

Interior layers in a haircut can add volume, texture, and movement to the hair, creating a variety of hairstyles. To get the best results, it is important to consult with a professional hairstylist and maintain the layers with proper hair care.
