Face Framing Bangs For Thick Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick hair and are looking for a way to change up your look, face framing bangs may be just what you need. These bangs are a great way to add some style and dimension to your hair, while also helping to frame your face and highlight your best features. In this article, we'll provide a step-by-step guide on how to achieve face framing bangs for thick hair, as well as some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve Face Framing Bangs for Thick Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Face Framing Bangs
  • FAQs About Face Framing Bangs for Thick Hair

How to Achieve Face Framing Bangs for Thick Hair

Before you get started, it's important to note that face framing bangs may not be the best choice for everyone. If you have a particularly wide forehead or a round face shape, you may want to opt for a different style of bangs. However, if you have an oval or heart-shaped face, face framing bangs can be a great way to accentuate your features.

Here's how to achieve face framing bangs for thick hair:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step is to decide how long you want your bangs to be. Ideally, they should start just below your eyebrows and end at the cheekbones or jawline. This will help to accentuate your cheekbones and frame your face.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, you'll need to section off the hair that you want to cut into bangs. Use a comb to create a triangle-shaped section of hair at the front of your head, with the point of the triangle at the center of your forehead. This will ensure that your bangs are evenly spaced and symmetrical.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Once you have your hair sectioned off, it's time to cut your bangs. Start by cutting your hair to the desired length, making sure to cut straight across. Then, use a pair of scissors to create texture and movement in your bangs, by cutting into them at an angle or using a razor to thin them out.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

Once you've cut your bangs, it's time to style them. You can blow dry them straight, or use a curling iron or flat iron to add some waves or curls. You can also add some product, like hairspray or mousse, to help them hold their shape.

Tips for Maintaining Face Framing Bangs

Now that you've achieved your face framing bangs, it's important to take care of them to ensure they look their best. Here are some tips for maintaining your bangs:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long.
  • Use a dry shampoo to help absorb excess oil and keep your bangs looking clean and fresh between washes.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your bangs too often, as this can damage them and make them appear frizzy or brittle.

FAQs About Face Framing Bangs for Thick Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about face framing bangs:

What is the best way to style face framing bangs?

The best way to style face framing bangs is to blow dry them straight, or use a curling iron or flat iron to add some waves or curls. You can also add some product, like hairspray or mousse, to help them hold their shape.

How often should I trim my face framing bangs?

You should trim your face framing bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long.

What face shape is best suited for face framing bangs?

Face framing bangs are best suited for oval or heart-shaped faces. If you have a particularly wide forehead or a round face shape, you may want to opt for a different style of bangs.

Hair Care Advice for Thick Hair with Face Framing Bangs

Thick hair can be prone to becoming dry and brittle, so it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for caring for your thick hair with face framing bangs:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help keep your hair hydrated and prevent it from becoming dry and frizzy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage it and make it appear frizzy or brittle.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and moisturize your hair.

Overall, face framing bangs can be a great way to add some style and dimension to your hair. By following these tips and taking care of your hair properly, you can ensure that your face framing bangs look their best and help you feel confident and stylish.
