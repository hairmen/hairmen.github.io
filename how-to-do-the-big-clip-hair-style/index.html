How To Do The Big Clip Hair Style

Written by Bardi Jun 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a versatile and easy-to-do hairstyle that can make a statement? Look no further than the big clip hair style. This trendy hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, from a night out with friends to a formal event. In this blog post, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions for creating the big clip hair style, as well as tips and tricks to make it last all day.

Description

The big clip hair style is a statement look that involves pulling the hair back from the face and securing it with a large hair clip. This style is perfect for those with medium to long hair and works best with straight or slightly wavy hair. The big clip hair style is versatile and can be dressed up or down, making it perfect for any occasion.

How to

Follow these simple steps to achieve the big clip hair style:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair using your preferred shampoo and conditioner. Blow dry your hair straight or use a flat iron to straighten it.

Step 2: Section your hair

Using a comb, part your hair down the middle and section it into two parts. Clip the top section of your hair up and out of the way.

Step 3: Tease your hair

Using a teasing comb, tease the bottom section of your hair at the crown of your head to create volume.

Step 4: Smooth out the top section of your hair

Using a brush, smooth out the top section of your hair and pull it back towards the crown of your head.

Step 5: Secure your hair with a clip

Using a large hair clip, secure your hair at the crown of your head. Make sure the clip is secure and tight, so it doesn't fall out.

Step 6: Style the bottom section of your hair

Using a brush, smooth out the bottom section of your hair and style it as desired. You can leave it straight or add some waves or curls for added texture.

Step 7: Finish with hairspray

Finish off the look with a spritz of hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect big clip hair style:

  • Use a large hair clip to secure your hair
  • Tease your hair at the crown of your head to create volume
  • Smooth out the top section of your hair for a sleek look
  • Add texture to the bottom section of your hair with waves or curls
  • Use hairspray to keep everything in place

FAQ

Can I do the big clip hair style with curly hair?

Yes, you can do the big clip hair style with curly hair. Follow the same steps but be sure to use a straightening iron to straighten your hair before clipping it back.

How long will the big clip hair style last?

The big clip hair style can last all day with the right amount of hairspray and a secure clip.

Can I dress up the big clip hair style?

Yes, you can dress up the big clip hair style by adding some hair accessories, such as a headband or hairpins.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to wash it regularly and use a conditioner to keep it moisturized. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant before using a straightening iron or curling iron.

Now that you know how to do the big clip hair style, you're ready to rock this trendy look. Whether you're heading to work or out for a night on the town, this versatile hairstyle is the perfect way to make a statement.
