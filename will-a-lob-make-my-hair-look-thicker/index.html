Will A Lob Make My Hair Look Thicker The Truth Revealed

Written by Bardi Sep 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of dealing with thin, lifeless hair? Do you want to add some volume and thickness to your locks? If so, you may be wondering if a lob haircut is the answer. In this article, we'll explore the question "will a lob make my hair look thicker?" and provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision.

Description

A lob, or long bob, is a popular haircut that falls between the chin and the collarbone. It's a versatile style that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. Some people believe that a lob can make their hair look thicker, but is this really the case?

How to Get a Lob Haircut

If you're interested in getting a lob, the first step is to find a stylist who specializes in this type of cut. They can help you determine the best length and shape for your face and hair type. During the appointment, your stylist will use a combination of scissors and a razor to create the desired look.

Step by Step Guide

Getting a lob haircut is a simple process that typically involves the following steps:

  1. Consult with a stylist to determine the best length and shape for your hair.
  2. Wash and dry your hair.
  3. Sit back and relax as your stylist cuts your hair into the desired shape.
  4. Style your new lob using a blow dryer and styling products.

Tips for Making Your Lob Look Thicker

While a lob can't magically make your hair thicker, there are some tips and tricks you can use to create the illusion of thicker hair. Here are a few ideas:

  • Use volumizing shampoos and conditioners to add body to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots.
  • Add layers to your lob to create movement and texture.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and hold to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, like braids and updos, to create the illusion of thicker hair.

FAQ

Will a lob make my hair look thicker?

A lob can create the illusion of thicker hair, especially if you add layers and texture to the cut. However, it won't actually make your hair thicker.

Is a lob easy to maintain?

Yes, a lob is a low-maintenance style that doesn't require frequent trims or styling. It's a great option for people who want a stylish, but easy-to-maintain haircut.

Can a lob work for all hair types?

Yes, a lob can be customized to suit all hair types, from fine and thin to thick and curly. Your stylist can help you determine the best length and shape for your specific hair type.

Hair Care Tips for a Healthy Lob

To keep your lob looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

The Bottom Line

A lob can create the illusion of thicker hair, but it won't actually make your hair thicker. However, with the right styling and hair care techniques, you can make your lob look full and voluminous. If you're interested in getting a lob, consult with a hairstylist who can help you determine if this is the right haircut for you.
