How To Use Thinning Scissors For Your Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Nov 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a way to add texture and movement to your bangs, thinning scissors may be your answer. Thinning scissors are a must-have tool for stylists and hair enthusiasts alike. They have a unique way of cutting hair that removes bulk without compromising length. In this article, we'll show you how to use thinning scissors for your bangs in a few easy steps.

How to Use Thinning Scissors for Your Bangs

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Comb your bangs forward and divide them into small sections. This will help you get a better idea of how much hair you want to thin out.

Step 2: Choose the Right Scissors

Thinning scissors come in different types and sizes. Make sure you choose a pair that is appropriate for your hair type and length. If you have thick hair, you may need a pair with more teeth. If you have fine hair, a pair with fewer teeth may be more suitable.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Take a small section of your bangs and hold it between your fingers. Place the thinning scissors about 1 inch away from the roots and start cutting. Don't cut all the way to the ends of your hair, as this can create an uneven look. Instead, stop about 1 inch from the ends.

Step 4: Repeat

Continue cutting small sections of your bangs until you achieve the desired texture and movement. Be sure to cut each section evenly to avoid any gaps or bald spots. Remember, you can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.

Tips for Using Thinning Scissors for Your Bangs

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your thinning scissors:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Hold the scissors at an angle to achieve a more natural look.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to make several small cuts than one big one.
  • Use the thinning scissors sparingly. Overusing them can result in a choppy or uneven look.

FAQ

Can I use thinning scissors on any type of hair?

Thinning scissors are suitable for most hair types, but they work best on medium to thick hair. If you have fine hair, be careful not to over-thin it, as this can make it look thin and wispy.

How often should I use thinning scissors on my bangs?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. As a general rule, you should use thinning scissors every 6-8 weeks to maintain your desired look.

Hair Care for Thinning Scissors Bangs

After using thinning scissors on your bangs, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you must use heat styling tools.

By following these tips and using thinning scissors properly, you can achieve a stylish and textured look for your bangs. Give it a try and see how you like it!
