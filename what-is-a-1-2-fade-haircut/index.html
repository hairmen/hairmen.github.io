What Is A 1 2 Fade Haircut

Written by Christine Sep 25, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking to spice up your haircut game, you might want to consider a 1 2 fade haircut. This popular men’s haircut is known for its versatility and stylish look. In this article, we’ll break down what a 1 2 fade haircut is, how to get it, and some tips and tricks to keep it looking sharp.

Description

A 1 2 fade haircut is a type of taper haircut where the hair gradually fades from a longer length at the top to a shorter length at the bottom. The “1 2” refers to the length of the hair on the sides and back of the head, with a #1 clipper guard being used at the bottom and a #2 guard being used above it. This creates a seamless blend between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides.

How to Get a 1 2 Fade Haircut

Getting a 1 2 fade haircut can be done at home with the right tools and a steady hand, but it’s recommended to go to a professional barber or stylist for the best results. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to get a 1 2 fade haircut:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

Before you start cutting, decide on the length you want for the top and sides of your hair. This will help you determine where to start the fade.

Step 2: Start with the Bottom

Using a #1 clipper guard, start at the bottom of the head and work your way up to the desired length. Make sure to blend the hair as you go to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 3: Move to the Top

Using scissors or a longer clipper guard, cut the hair on top to the desired length. Make sure to blend the top with the sides to create a seamless transition.

Step 4: Blend the Fade

Using a #2 clipper guard, blend the hair between the bottom and top sections to create a smooth fade. Use a comb to help guide the clippers and ensure an even blend.

Step 5: Finish with Detailing

Use a trimmer or scissors to clean up any stray hairs and create a clean, polished look.

Tips for Maintaining a 1 2 Fade Haircut

Now that you have a fresh new 1 2 fade haircut, you’ll want to keep it looking sharp. Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

  • Get regular touch-ups every 4-6 weeks to keep the fade looking fresh
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized
  • Style your hair with a matte or low-shine product for a natural look
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to add shine and prevent frizz

FAQ

What face shapes does a 1 2 fade haircut work best on?

A 1 2 fade haircut works well on most face shapes, but is particularly flattering on round, square, and oval faces.

Can I get a 1 2 fade haircut with curly hair?

Absolutely! A 1 2 fade haircut can work with any hair texture, including curly hair. Ask your stylist to adjust the length of the fade to accommodate your curls.

How long does a 1 2 fade haircut last?

A 1 2 fade haircut typically lasts 4-6 weeks before it starts to grow out and lose its shape.

Hair Care for a 1 2 Fade Haircut

Maintaining a 1 2 fade haircut requires some extra care to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for a 1 2 fade haircut:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons
  • Avoid harsh chemicals like bleach or relaxers
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent oil buildup

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to rock a fresh 1 2 fade haircut with confidence. Whether you prefer a classic, clean look or a more edgy style, a 1 2 fade haircut is a versatile and stylish option for any man.
