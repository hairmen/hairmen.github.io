How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers Uk A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts every few weeks? With the right tools and a little bit of practice, you can learn how to cut men's hair with clippers UK style. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process, providing tips and tricks along the way.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Preparation
  • The Cut
  • Tips for a Great Cut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After the Cut

Tools You'll Need

Before you get started, you'll need to gather a few essential tools:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Cape or towel to protect clothing
  • Clipper oil

Make sure your clippers are sharp and well-oiled before you begin.

Preparation

Start by washing and drying the hair. Comb it out to remove any tangles or knots. Then, put the cape or towel around the person's neck to protect their clothing from hair clippings.

Decide on the length of the cut and attach the appropriate guard to the clippers. It's always better to start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one if needed. You can always take more off, but you can't put it back on!

The Cut

Begin at the bottom of the head and work your way up, using the clippers to cut the hair. Move the clippers in an upward motion, against the direction of hair growth. Use the comb to lift the hair and make sure all sections are cut to the same length.

Once you've finished the sides and back of the head, move on to the top. Use the scissors to blend the top with the sides, creating a natural look. Take your time and be careful not to cut too much off.

Tips for a Great Cut

Here are some tips to ensure you get a great cut:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if needed
  • Take your time and don't rush the process
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and make sure all sections are cut to the same length
  • Blend the top with the sides using scissors to create a natural look
  • Use clipper oil to keep the blades lubricated and sharp

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair with clippers:

Q: Can I use clippers to cut my own hair?

A: Yes, you can, but it can be difficult to get the back and sides of your head without assistance.

Q: What if I make a mistake?

A: Don't panic! Hair grows back. If it's a minor mistake, you can try to blend it in with the rest of the hair. If it's a major mistake, you may need to cut the hair shorter or seek professional help.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and the style you're going for. Generally, every four to six weeks is a good rule of thumb.

Hair Care After the Cut

Once the cut is finished, wash the hair to remove any loose hairs. Apply a styling product if desired, and style as usual. Remember to clean your clippers and scissors after use and keep them well-oiled for the next time.

Cutting men's hair with clippers UK style is a skill that takes practice, but with the right tools and techniques, you can save money and achieve a great look. Follow these steps and tips for a successful cut every time!
