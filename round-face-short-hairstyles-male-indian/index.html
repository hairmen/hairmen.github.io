Round Face Short Hairstyles For Indian Men

Written by Bardi Oct 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are an Indian man with a round face, you may be wondering what type of short hairstyle would suit you best. In this article, we will discuss some popular round face short hairstyles for Indian men, as well as tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Description

A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. It is important to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape, rather than accentuating its roundness. Short hairstyles are a great option for men with round faces, as they help to create an illusion of length and angularity.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, it is important to consider factors such as hair texture, density, and growth patterns. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

1. Consider the length:

Shorter hairstyles tend to look better on men with round faces, as they help to create a more angular appearance. However, you don't want your hair to be too short, as this can make your face appear even rounder. Aim for a length that falls somewhere between the ear and the chin.

2. Add texture:

Texture can help to create the illusion of volume and dimension in your hair, which can help to balance out the roundness of your face. Consider adding layers, curls, or waves to your hair to create texture.

3. Avoid blunt cuts:

Blunt cuts can make your face appear even rounder, so it is best to avoid them. Instead, opt for hairstyles with soft, feathered edges, as these can help to create a more angular appearance.

Step by step

Here are some popular round face short hairstyles for Indian men:

1. The buzz cut:

The buzz cut is a classic hairstyle that is both low-maintenance and stylish. It involves cutting your hair very short all over your head, usually with clippers. This style works well for men with round faces, as it helps to create a more angular appearance. To style, simply run your fingers through your hair for a messy, textured look.

2. The undercut:

The undercut involves cutting the sides and back of your hair very short, while leaving the top longer. This style can help to create a more angular appearance, while still allowing you to have some length on top. To style, use a pomade or wax to slick back the top of your hair, or create a side part for a more classic look.

3. The textured crop:

The textured crop is a popular short hairstyle that involves cutting your hair short on the sides and back, while leaving the top longer and textured. This style is great for men with round faces, as it helps to create a more angular appearance. To style, use a texturizing spray or pomade to create a messy, textured look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style and care for your short hair:

1. Use the right products:

Using the right hair products can help you achieve the look you want, while also protecting your hair from damage. Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture.

2. Get regular trims:

Regular trims can help to keep your hair looking neat and tidy, while also preventing split ends and breakage.

3. Protect your hair from heat:

Heat styling tools can cause damage to your hair, so it is important to use them sparingly. When you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

4. Embrace your natural texture:

If you have curly or wavy hair, consider embracing your natural texture rather than trying to straighten it. This can help to create a more unique and stylish look.

FAQ

Q: Can men with round faces have long hair?

A: Yes, men with round faces can have long hair, but it is important to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape. Long hairstyles with layers or waves can help to create a more angular appearance.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It is best to wash your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type and texture. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and damage.

Q: Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

A: Yes, you can style your hair differently for different occasions. Experiment with different products and techniques to create a variety of looks.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, strong hair. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

1. Use a gentle shampoo:

Choose a shampoo that is gentle and free from harsh chemicals that can damage your hair. Look for shampoos that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture.

2. Condition regularly:

Conditioning your hair regularly can help to keep it soft, smooth, and hydrated. Use a conditioner that is specifically designed for your hair type and texture.

3. Protect your hair from the sun:

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun.

4. Eat a healthy diet:

A healthy diet can help to promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair loss. Include plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet to keep your hair looking its best.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can create a stylish and flattering look that complements your round face.
