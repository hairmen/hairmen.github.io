Best Haircut For Long Hair Oval Face

Written by Luffy Jul 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have an oval face and long hair, then you are in luck! This face shape is considered to be the most versatile as it can carry off a variety of styles. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the best haircut for your long hair oval face. In this blog post, we will discuss the best haircut for long hair oval face, how to achieve it, step by step guide, tips, FAQ and hair care tips for maintaining the perfect long hair oval face style.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Best Haircut for Long Hair Oval Face
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining the Perfect Long Hair Oval Face Style
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Hair Oval Face Style

Description:

An oval face is characterized by a well-balanced forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. The length of the face is longer than the width, and the chin is slightly narrower than the forehead. If you have long hair, then this face shape is perfect for you as you can experiment with different styles and lengths. However, the best haircut for long hair oval face is one that accentuates your features and complements your face shape.

How to Achieve the Best Haircut for Long Hair Oval Face:

The best haircut for long hair oval face is one that adds volume and texture to the hair while complementing the face shape. Here are some tips to achieve the perfect long hair oval face style:

  • Choose a haircut that adds volume to the hair, such as layers or waves.
  • Consider a side-swept fringe that frames the face and accentuates your features.
  • Opt for a haircut that complements your face shape, such as a long bob or a layered cut.
  • Consult with a professional hairstylist who can recommend the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Step by Step Guide:

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the best haircut for long hair oval face:

  1. Consult with a professional hairstylist who can recommend the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Choose a haircut that adds volume to the hair, such as layers or waves.
  3. Consider a side-swept fringe that frames the face and accentuates your features.
  4. Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair in layers that start from your chin and go all the way down to the ends.
  5. If you prefer a shorter style, consider a long bob that falls just above the shoulders.
  6. Opt for a haircut that complements your face shape, such as a layered cut or a blunt cut with face-framing layers.
  7. If you want to add texture to your hair, ask your hairstylist to add subtle highlights or lowlights.
  8. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  9. Style your hair with a curling iron or a straightener to add texture and volume.
  10. Finish your look with a hairspray or a styling product to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Perfect Long Hair Oval Face Style:

To maintain the perfect long hair oval face style, here are some tips to follow:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or a straightener to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using too many styling products as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for long hair oval face:

What is the best haircut for long hair oval face?

The best haircut for long hair oval face is one that adds volume and texture to the hair while complementing the face shape. Consider a long bob, layers, or waves to achieve the perfect long hair oval face style.

Can I wear my hair straight with an oval face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight with an oval face. However, it is recommended to add some texture and volume to your hair with layers or waves to complement your face shape.

Should I get a fringe with an oval face?

A side-swept fringe can complement an oval face and accentuate your features. However, it is recommended to consult with a professional hairstylist before getting a fringe to ensure that it complements your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair Oval Face Style:

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining the perfect long hair oval face style:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid tying your hair too tight as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect long hair oval face style that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty.
