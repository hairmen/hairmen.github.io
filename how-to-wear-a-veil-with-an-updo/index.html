How To Wear A Veil With An Updo

Written by Christine Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you getting married soon and wondering how to wear a veil with an updo? You've come to the right place! In this blog post, we'll guide you through the steps of wearing a veil with an updo and provide you with some tips and tricks along the way. Let's get started!

Description

Wearing a veil with an updo can be a stunning combination that adds elegance and sophistication to your bridal look. However, it can be tricky to get it just right. You'll need to consider the type of veil you have, the style of your updo, and the placement of your veil. But don't worry, we've got you covered.

How to

The first step in wearing a veil with an updo is to choose the right type of veil. There are various types of veils, including birdcage, fingertip, cathedral, and mantilla. Each type of veil requires a different type of updo, so it's important to choose the veil before deciding on the hairstyle.

Once you've chosen your veil, it's time to decide on your updo. There are many different types of updos to choose from, such as a braided bun, a low chignon, or a messy top knot. You'll want to choose a style that complements your dress and veil while also reflecting your personal style.

Next, you'll need to consider the placement of your veil. If you're wearing a birdcage veil, it can be placed at the top of your head, while a cathedral veil typically sits at the back of your head. You'll want to make sure your veil is secure and won't fall out during the ceremony or reception.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when wearing a veil with an updo:

  1. Choose the right type of veil.
  2. Decide on your updo.
  3. Consider the placement of your veil.
  4. Secure your veil in place with bobby pins or a comb.
  5. Make sure your veil is positioned correctly and is not blocking your face.
  6. Spray your hair with hairspray to keep it in place.
  7. Add any accessories, such as flowers or a tiara.
  8. Check your hair and veil in the mirror to make sure everything looks perfect.
  9. Enjoy your special day!

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing a veil with an updo:

  • Practice your hairstyle and veil placement before the big day.
  • Choose a veil that complements your dress and personal style.
  • Make sure your veil is secure and won't fall out during the ceremony or reception.
  • Consider using a veil weight to keep your veil in place.
  • Use hairspray to keep your hair and veil in place.
  • Consider adding accessories, such as flowers or a tiara, to complete your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a veil with an updo:

Q: Can I wear a veil with any type of updo?

A: No, you'll need to choose an updo that complements the type of veil you have. For example, a birdcage veil pairs well with a low chignon, while a cathedral veil looks best with a voluminous updo.

Q: How do I keep my veil in place?

A: You can use bobby pins or a comb to secure your veil in place. You can also use a veil weight to keep your veil from blowing in the wind.

Q: Can I wear a veil if I have short hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a veil with short hair. You'll just need to choose a veil that complements your hair length and style.

Hair Care

Before your wedding day, it's essential to take good care of your hair. This means using a deep conditioner, avoiding heat styling, and getting regular trims. On your wedding day, make sure your hair is clean and dry before styling it. You can also use a heat protectant spray and hairspray to keep your hair looking healthy and in place.

Now that you know how to wear a veil with an updo, you're ready to create a stunning bridal look that will leave your guests in awe. Remember to choose the right type of veil, consider the placement, and practice your hairstyle before the big day. With these tips and tricks, you'll look and feel your best on your wedding day!
