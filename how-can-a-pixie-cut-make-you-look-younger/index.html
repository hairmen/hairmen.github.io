How Can A Pixie Cut Make You Look Younger

Written by Bardi Jul 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to refresh your look and appear more youthful, a pixie cut may be the perfect solution. Pixie cuts are short, choppy hairstyles that can help you achieve a more youthful appearance. In this article, we'll explore how a pixie cut can make you look younger and provide some tips for making the most of your new hairstyle.

Description

A pixie cut is a short, cropped hairstyle that typically features layers and bangs. This style is perfect for those who want to keep their hair short and low-maintenance while still looking stylish and youthful. Pixie cuts can be tailored to suit any face shape and hair texture, from straight and fine to curly and thick.

How To

If you're considering getting a pixie cut, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Consult with a stylist to determine the best pixie cut for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Prepare your hair by washing and blow-drying it.
  3. Section your hair and cut it in layers, starting with the bottom layer.
  4. Use a razor to create texture and movement in your hair.
  5. Trim your bangs to the desired length.
  6. Style your hair with a texturizing product to enhance the choppy, layered look of your pixie cut.

Step by Step

Here are some more detailed instructions for getting a pixie cut:

Step 1: Consult with a Stylist

The first step in getting a pixie cut is to consult with a stylist. They can help you determine the best style for your face shape and hair texture. They can also provide tips for styling and maintaining your new look.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before your stylist begins cutting your hair, you'll need to wash and blow-dry it. This will ensure that your hair is clean and easy to work with. If you have curly hair, you may want to straighten it before your haircut so that your stylist can see the true length of your hair.

Step 3: Section and Cut Your Hair

Your stylist will likely start by sectioning your hair and cutting the bottom layer first. They may use a razor to create texture and movement in your hair. This will help your pixie cut look choppy and layered.

Step 4: Trim Your Bangs

If you want bangs with your pixie cut, your stylist will likely trim them to the desired length. They may suggest side-swept bangs or blunt bangs, depending on your face shape and personal style.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your haircut is complete, your stylist will likely style your hair with a texturizing product. This will help enhance the choppy, layered look of your pixie cut. They may also suggest using a pomade or wax to define your hair and add shine.

Tips

Here are some tips for making the most of your new pixie cut:

  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.
  • Accessorize with bold earrings, headbands, or scarves to add some personality to your look.
  • Maintain your pixie cut with regular trims to keep it looking fresh and youthful.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about pixie cuts:

What face shape is best for a pixie cut?

Pixie cuts can be tailored to suit any face shape, but they tend to look best on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Will a pixie cut work with curly hair?

Yes! Pixie cuts can work with any hair texture, including curly hair. Your stylist can help you determine the best length and style for your curls.

How often do I need to get my pixie cut trimmed?

You should plan on getting your pixie cut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care

To keep your pixie cut looking fresh and youthful, you'll need to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can damage the hair follicles and lead to hair loss.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a chic and youthful pixie cut that makes you feel confident and stylish!
