What Glasses Look Best On Square Faces

Written by Daniel Oct 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect pair of glasses that will flatter your square face shape? Look no further! In this article, we'll discuss what glasses look best on square faces, and provide you with tips on how to choose the perfect pair for you.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Choose Glasses for Square Faces
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing Glasses for Square Faces
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Square Faces

Description

Square faces have strong jawlines and prominent cheekbones, which can make it difficult to find glasses that balance out these features. The key is to choose frames that soften the angles of your face and draw attention to your eyes.

How to Choose Glasses for Square Faces

When selecting glasses for square faces, look for frames that are wider than they are tall. Round or oval frames work well, as they soften the angles of your face and draw attention to your eyes. Avoid frames with sharp angles, as they will only accentuate your strong jawline.

Frame Materials

When it comes to frame materials, choose ones that are lightweight and won't add any unnecessary bulk to your face. Materials like titanium or acetate are good options.

Frame Colors

When choosing a frame color, opt for neutral or warm tones. Avoid frames with bright, bold colors or patterns that will distract from your face shape.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to choose the perfect pair of glasses for your square face:

  1. Measure the width of your face
  2. Choose frames that are wider than your face
  3. Look for frames with round or oval shapes
  4. Choose lightweight frame materials
  5. Opt for neutral or warm frame colors

Tips for Choosing Glasses for Square Faces

  • Try on different frame shapes to find the perfect fit
  • Consider getting glasses with nose pads, as they can help adjust the fit of the frames to your face shape
  • Don't be afraid to ask for a second opinion when trying on glasses

FAQ

What glasses should I avoid if I have a square face?

Avoid glasses with sharp angles or bold patterns, as they will only accentuate your strong jawline.

Can I wear cat-eye glasses if I have a square face?

Yes, cat-eye glasses can work well on square faces, as long as they have rounded edges and are not too sharp or angular.

Hair Care Tips for Square Faces

If you have a square face, try these hair care tips to balance out your features:

  • Choose hairstyles that soften your jawline, such as long layers or soft waves
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as they will only make your face appear wider
  • Consider getting a side part, as it will help balance out your face shape

By following these tips, you're sure to find the perfect pair of glasses that will flatter your square face shape and make you feel confident and stylish!
