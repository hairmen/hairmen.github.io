Does Long Hair Suit Small Faces?

If you have a small face, you might be wondering if long hair is a good choice for you. In this article, we'll explore whether long hair suits small faces, and provide some tips and advice on how to make it work for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long hair can be a great choice for small-faced individuals, as it can help elongate the face and create the illusion of more length. However, it's important to choose the right style and cut to make it work for you.

How to

The key to making long hair work for a small face is to choose a style that adds volume and length to the hair. This can be achieved through layers, curls, and waves.

Step by Step

1. Choose a style that adds volume to the hair, such as layers, curls, or waves.

2. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add volume to the hair.

3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and length.

4. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create curls or waves.

5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

1. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make the face appear even smaller.

2. Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as they can make the face appear even flatter.

3. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to the hair.

4. Consult with a hair stylist to find the best style for your face shape and hair type.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair straight if I have a small face?

A: Yes, but it's best to add some volume and texture to the hair to help balance out the face.

Q: Will long hair make my face look even smaller?

A: Not necessarily. It's all about choosing the right style and cut to flatter your face shape.

Hair Care

To keep your long hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. This includes regular washing, conditioning, and deep conditioning treatments. It's also important to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with heat tools.
