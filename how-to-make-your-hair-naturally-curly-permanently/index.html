How To Make Your Hair Naturally Curly Permanently

Written by Luffy Nov 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of using heat tools and chemicals to get curly hair? Do you want to embrace your natural curls without damaging your hair? If so, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll show you how to make your hair naturally curly permanently using simple, natural methods.

Description

Curly hair is beautiful, but not everyone is born with it. If you have straight hair and want to make it curly permanently, there are ways to do it without using harsh chemicals or heat tools. By using natural methods, you can enhance your hair's natural curl pattern and keep it healthy and strong.

How to

There are several ways to make your hair naturally curly permanently. Here are some of the most effective methods:

Method 1: Twist Out

A twist out is a popular method for creating curls in natural hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, detangled hair
  2. Apply a moisturizing cream or oil to your hair
  3. Divide your hair into small sections
  4. Twist each section tightly
  5. Allow your hair to dry completely
  6. Untwist each section and fluff your hair to create volume

This method works best on natural hair that already has some curl or wave to it. If your hair is completely straight, you may need to use another method to create curls.

Method 2: Perm

A perm is a chemical treatment that creates curls in straight hair. It works by breaking down the bonds in your hair and reforming them in a curled shape. Here's how to get a perm:

  1. Choose a reputable salon and stylist
  2. Discuss your hair goals with your stylist
  3. Select the size of curl you want
  4. Prepare your hair by washing and conditioning it
  5. Apply the perm solution to your hair
  6. Leave the solution on for the recommended time
  7. Rinse your hair thoroughly
  8. Apply a neutralizing solution to set the curls
  9. Style your hair as desired

A perm is a permanent treatment, so make sure you're ready for the commitment before you get one. It's also important to choose a stylist who is experienced with perming hair to avoid damage or uneven results.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to making your hair naturally curly permanently:

  1. Decide which method you want to use (twist out or perm)
  2. Gather the necessary supplies and tools
  3. Prepare your hair by washing and conditioning it
  4. Follow the instructions for your chosen method
  5. Allow your hair to dry completely
  6. Style your hair as desired

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your hair naturally curly permanently:

Can I make my hair curly permanently without chemicals?

Yes, you can use the twist out method to make your hair curly permanently without chemicals.

Will a perm damage my hair?

A perm can damage your hair if it's not done correctly. Choose a reputable stylist and follow their instructions carefully to minimize the risk of damage.

How long will my curls last?

The length of time your curls last depends on the method you use and how well you care for your hair. A twist out can last for several days, while a perm can last for several months.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair is essential to maintaining healthy, beautiful curls. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent breakage
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat tools on your hair

By following these tips and using natural methods to make your hair naturally curly permanently, you can enjoy beautiful, healthy curls without damaging your hair. Have fun experimenting with different styles and products, and enjoy your new, natural curls!
