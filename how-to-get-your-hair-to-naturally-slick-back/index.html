How To Get Your Hair to Naturally Slick Back

Slicked back hair has been a popular hairstyle for men for many years. It is a classic look that can be worn in any setting, from the office to a night out. However, many men find it difficult to achieve the slicked back look without using hair products. Here are some tips on how to get your hair to naturally slick back:

1. Start with clean hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it is clean and free of any product buildup. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any excess oil or product from your hair.

2. Use a comb

Using a comb, brush your hair back from your hairline. Start at the front and work your way back, making sure your hair is evenly distributed.

3. Apply heat

Use a hairdryer on a low heat setting to dry your hair while combing it back. The heat will help to set your hair in place, making it easier to style.

4. Use natural oils

Apply a small amount of natural oil, such as coconut or argan oil, to your hair. This will help to smooth your hair and add shine, without weighing it down like hair products can.

Step By Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get your hair to naturally slick back:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start with clean hair. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any excess oil or product from your hair.

Step 2: Comb your hair back

Using a comb, brush your hair back from your hairline. Start at the front and work your way back, making sure your hair is evenly distributed.

Step 3: Apply heat

Use a hairdryer on a low heat setting to dry your hair while combing it back. The heat will help to set your hair in place, making it easier to style.

Step 4: Apply natural oils

Apply a small amount of natural oil, such as coconut or argan oil, to your hair. This will help to smooth your hair and add shine, without weighing it down like hair products can.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb

Using a wide-tooth comb will help to evenly distribute your hair and prevent breakage.

2. Don't use too much oil

A little bit of oil goes a long way. Too much oil can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

3. Use a boar bristle brush

A boar bristle brush will help to smooth your hair and add shine.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long will my hair stay slicked back?

A: Your hair should stay slicked back for several hours, depending on the length and texture of your hair.

Q: Can I use gel or pomade to slick back my hair?

A: Yes, you can use hair products to slick back your hair. However, using natural oils is a healthier alternative.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

Here are some tips on how to care for your hair when you have it slicked back:

1. Don't slick back wet hair

Slicking back wet hair can cause breakage and damage. Make sure your hair is dry before styling.

2. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, causing it to become dry and brittle. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy.

3. Don't slick back your hair every day

Slicking back your hair every day can cause breakage and damage. Give your hair a break and style it differently every once in a while.

Now that you know how to get your hair to naturally slick back, you can achieve the perfect polished look without using any hair products. Follow these tips and tricks for a healthy and stylish hairstyle.
