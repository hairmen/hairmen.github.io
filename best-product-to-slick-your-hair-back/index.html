Best Product To Slick Your Hair Back In 2023

Are you tired of your hair constantly falling in your face? Do you want a sleek and sophisticated look? In this article, we will discuss the best product to slick your hair back, as well as how to use it and some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Best Product to Slick Your Hair Back

The best product to slick your hair back is a high-quality pomade. Pomade is a hair styling product that provides a firm hold, but is also pliable and easy to work with. It typically has a shiny or glossy finish, which is perfect for achieving the sleek look you want.

One of the most popular pomades on the market is Suavecito Pomade. This product provides a strong hold, but also allows you to restyle your hair throughout the day. It has a water-soluble formula, which means it washes out easily and won't leave any residue in your hair. It also has a pleasant scent, which is a bonus.

Another great pomade option is Imperial Barber Classic Pomade. This product provides a strong hold, but also has a matte finish, which is great if you're looking for a more natural look. It's also water-soluble and washes out easily. It has a subtle, clean scent that won't overpower your cologne or aftershave.

How to Slick Your Hair Back

Slicking your hair back is a simple process, but it's important to use the right technique to achieve the look you want. Here's how to do it:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Towel dry your hair until it's slightly damp, but not dripping wet.
  2. Take a small amount of pomade and rub it between your palms to warm it up.
  3. Apply the pomade to your hair, starting at the crown and working your way to the front. Use your fingers to comb your hair back, making sure it's evenly distributed.
  4. Use a comb to smooth out any bumps or lumps.
  5. If you want a sleeker look, use a fine-tooth comb to create a sharp part on one side of your head.
  6. Finish with a light layer of hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when slicking your hair back:

  • Use a small amount of pomade to start. You can always add more if you need it.
  • Don't apply pomade to the roots of your hair, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Use a comb to smooth out any bumps or lumps.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that works best for your face shape.
  • Use a light hand when applying hairspray, as too much can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

FAQ

Can I slick my hair back if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can slick your hair back if you have curly hair, but it may require more product and a little extra effort to achieve the look you want. Use a pomade with a strong hold and apply it to damp hair. Use a comb to smooth out your curls as you slick your hair back.

How often should I wash my hair if I slick it back every day?

It's best to wash your hair every other day if you slick it back every day. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can make it dry and brittle.

Can I use hair gel instead of pomade?

You can use hair gel instead of pomade, but it may not provide the same level of hold and may leave your hair looking stiff and unnatural. Pomade is a better option for achieving a sleek, sophisticated look.

Hair Care Tips

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many hair styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With the right product and technique, you can achieve a sleek and sophisticated look by slicking your hair back. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the one that works best for you, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
