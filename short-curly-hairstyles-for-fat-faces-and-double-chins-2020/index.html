Short Curly Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins 2020

Written by Eveline Sep 21, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have a round face or a double chin, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered with some great short curly hairstyles for fat faces and double chins in 2020 that will help you look and feel your best.

Description

Short curly hairstyles can be a great option for those with round faces and double chins. The curls can add volume to the hair, which can help balance out the shape of the face. Additionally, short hairstyles can help elongate the neck, which can help draw attention away from the chin.


How to

When choosing a short curly hairstyle for a round face or double chin, it’s important to consider the length and shape of the cut. A layered bob or pixie cut can be a great option as it can help create a more angular shape to the face. Additionally, adding curls or waves to the hair can help create the illusion of a longer face.


Step by Step

Step 1: Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best short curly hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Wash and condition your hair using products specifically designed for curly hair.

Step 3: Apply a curl enhancing product to your hair and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair.

Step 4: Use a curling iron to add additional curls or waves to your hair if desired.


Tips

- Avoid straight hairstyles that can make the face appear more round.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to the hair.

- Use a volumizing product at the roots of the hair to help create lift.


FAQ

Q: Are there any short curly hairstyles that should be avoided for round faces and double chins?

A: Yes, avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long as they can accentuate the roundness of the face. Additionally, avoid hairstyles that are too flat or lack volume.


Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. When it comes to short curly hairstyles, it’s important to use products specifically designed for curly hair to help maintain the shape and definition of the curls. Additionally, regular trims can help prevent split ends and maintain the shape of the cut.


With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect short curly hairstyle for your round face or double chin in 2020. Remember to consult with a hairstylist to determine the best cut and style for your hair type and face shape.
