Short Layered Hairstyles For Thick Wavy Hair How To Get The Perfect Look

Written by Robby Oct 04, 2023 · 4 min read
If you have thick wavy hair, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want something that will show off your natural texture without overwhelming your face or becoming too difficult to manage. One of the best options for thick wavy hair is a short layered hairstyle. In this article, we’ll show you how to get the perfect look with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get the Perfect Look
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Short Layered Hairstyle
  • FAQs About Short Layered Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Hair
  • Hair Care Advice for Thick Wavy Hair

How to Get the Perfect Look

The key to a great short layered hairstyle is to find the right length and layers for your face shape and hair texture. For thick wavy hair, a layered bob or pixie cut can work well. Both of these styles offer enough length to show off your waves while still being short enough to be low-maintenance. When choosing a layered hairstyle, it’s important to consider the shape of your face. If you have a round face, a layered bob with longer layers in the front can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a pixie cut with layers around the crown can soften your features.

Choose the Right Styling Products

To get the most out of your short layered hairstyle for thick wavy hair, you’ll need to use the right styling products. Look for products that will enhance your natural texture and provide hold without weighing your hair down. A lightweight mousse or curl enhancer can work well for this.

Consider Hair Color

Adding highlights or lowlights to your short layered hairstyle can also help enhance your natural texture. Talk to your stylist about adding color to your hair to create dimension and depth.

Step-by-Step Instructions

To create a short layered hairstyle for thick wavy hair, follow these steps:
  1. Wash and condition your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess moisture.
  3. Apply a lightweight mousse or curl enhancer to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This will help enhance your natural waves and create volume.
  5. Use a flat iron or curling wand to smooth or define any sections of your hair that need it.
  6. Use a texturizing spray or wax to add definition and hold to your layers.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Layered Hairstyle

To keep your short layered hairstyle looking its best, follow these tips:
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it more difficult to manage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushing can disrupt your natural waves.

FAQs About Short Layered Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Hair

What face shape is best for a short layered hairstyle?

A short layered hairstyle can work for many face shapes, but it’s important to choose the right length and layers for your specific features. Talk to your stylist about which style will work best for you.

Can I wear a short layered hairstyle with bangs?

Yes, a short layered hairstyle can look great with bangs. Talk to your stylist about which type of bangs will work best for your face shape and hair texture.

What styling products should I use for a short layered hairstyle?

Look for lightweight products that will enhance your natural texture without weighing your hair down. A mousse or curl enhancer can work well for this.

Hair Care Advice for Thick Wavy Hair

To keep your thick wavy hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it more difficult to manage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushing can disrupt your natural waves.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
In conclusion, a short layered hairstyle can be a great choice for thick wavy hair. By following these tips and using the right styling products, you can create a low-maintenance, stylish look that shows off your natural texture. Talk to your stylist about which style will work best for you, and don’t forget to keep up with regular trims and hair care to keep your hair looking its best.
