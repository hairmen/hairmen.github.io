Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Curly Hair

Written by Daniel Oct 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair and are looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, the mens haircut short sides long top curly hair may be the perfect option for you. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, making it a great choice for any occasion. In this post, we will guide you through the steps on how to achieve this haircut, provide you with tips on how to maintain it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

The mens haircut short sides long top curly hair is a popular haircut that features short hair on the sides and back and longer hair on the top. The longer hair on the top can be styled in a variety of ways, such as slicked back, messy, or curly. This haircut is ideal for men who want a modern and stylish look that is easy to maintain and style.

How To

If you want to achieve the mens haircut short sides long top curly hair, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose the right length

The first step in achieving this haircut is to decide on the length of your hair on the sides and back. You can choose a short, medium, or long length, depending on your preference and the shape of your face. A shorter length on the sides and back will create a more defined contrast with the longer hair on top.

Step 2: Cut the sides and back

Once you have decided on the length of your hair on the sides and back, it is time to cut it. You can either go to a barber or do it yourself if you have the necessary tools and skills. Use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back to the desired length. Start with a longer guard and gradually go shorter until you achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Cut the top

The next step is to cut the hair on the top. You can choose to leave it longer or shorter, depending on your preference. If you want to have curly hair on top, it is best to leave it longer so that the curls have room to form. Use scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length and shape.

Step 4: Style the hair

Once you have cut your hair, it is time to style it. You can use a variety of products, such as pomade, wax, or gel, to achieve the desired look. If you have curly hair on top, you can use a curl-enhancing product to help define your curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your mens haircut short sides long top curly hair:

- Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. - Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. - Regularly trim your hair to keep it looking neat and tidy. - Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens haircut short sides long top curly hair:

Q: Is this haircut suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this haircut can be customized to suit all face shapes. For example, if you have a round face, you can choose a shorter length on the sides and back to create more definition.

Q: Can I achieve this haircut at home?

A: Yes, you can achieve this haircut at home if you have the necessary tools and skills. However, it is recommended to go to a barber for the best results.

Q: How often do I need to trim my hair?

A: It is recommended to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircut short sides long top curly hair looking its best, it is important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for curly hair. - Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized. - Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Overall, the mens haircut short sides long top curly hair is a trendy and stylish haircut that is perfect for men with curly hair. Follow these tips and techniques to achieve and maintain this haircut, and you'll be sure to turn heads wherever you go!
