Is Yarn Braids Haram Exploring The Controversy In 2023

Written by Frank Aug 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Yarn braids have been a popular hairstyle for many years, but there has been controversy surrounding whether or not they are haram. In this article, we will explore the different opinions and beliefs surrounding this topic.

Description

Yarn braids are a type of protective hairstyle that involves wrapping strands of yarn around sections of hair. This creates a braided look without actually braiding the hair. Yarn braids come in a variety of colors and styles, and can be a great way to switch up your look without damaging your natural hair.

How to

To create yarn braids, you will need yarn in the color of your choice, a comb, and hair ties. Here is a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Detangle your Hair

Start by washing and detangling your hair. This will make it easier to create the yarn braids.

Step 2: Divide your Hair into Sections

Using a comb, divide your hair into small sections. The size of the sections will depend on how thick you want your yarn braids to be.

Step 3: Tie the Yarn onto your Hair

Take a piece of yarn and tie it onto the root of one of the sections of hair. Make sure to tie it tightly so that it doesn't come loose.

Step 4: Wrap the Yarn around your Hair

Take the yarn and wrap it tightly around the section of hair, making sure to cover the entire length of the hair. Once you reach the end of the hair, tie another piece of yarn to secure it in place.

Step 5: Repeat

Repeat this process for each section of hair until your entire head is covered in yarn braids.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when creating yarn braids:

  • Make sure to tie the yarn tightly so that it doesn't come loose
  • Choose yarn that is soft and won't damage your hair
  • Be careful not to wrap the yarn too tightly, as this can cause tension on your scalp
  • Take breaks if you start to feel any discomfort or pain

FAQ

Are Yarn Braids Haram?

The answer to this question depends on who you ask. Some people believe that any type of artificial hair is haram, while others believe that it is permissible as long as it is not made from animal products. Ultimately, it is up to each individual to decide what they believe.

Can Yarn Braids Damage your Hair?

Yarn braids can be a great way to protect your hair from damage, but they can also cause damage if not done properly. Make sure to tie the yarn tightly, but not too tightly, and be careful not to pull on your hair too much when wrapping the yarn around it.

How Long do Yarn Braids Last?

Yarn braids can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months, depending on how well you take care of them. To make them last longer, be sure to moisturize your hair and scalp regularly, and avoid using any harsh chemicals or styling tools.

Hair Care

When wearing yarn braids, it is important to take care of your hair and scalp. Here are a few tips:

  • Moisturize your hair and scalp regularly
  • Avoid using any harsh chemicals or styling tools
  • Wash your hair and scalp regularly, but be gentle when doing so
  • Take breaks from wearing yarn braids if you start to feel any discomfort or pain

Conclusion

So, is yarn braids haram? The answer is not clear-cut and depends on individual beliefs and opinions. However, if you do decide to wear yarn braids, make sure to take care of your hair and scalp and be mindful of any discomfort or pain you may experience.
