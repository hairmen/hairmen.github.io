What Hair Is Pomade Best For

Written by Petter Oct 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our blog post all about what hair is pomade best for! As you may know, pomade is a popular hair styling product that can be used to achieve a variety of different looks. However, not all pomades are created equal, and some are better suited to certain hair types and styles than others.

Description

Pomade is a type of hair styling product that is typically used to create a slick, shiny look. It is often used by men to achieve classic, retro styles such as the pompadour or the slick back. However, women can also use pomade to add shine and definition to their hair, particularly if they have short, textured styles.

How to Use Pomade

Using pomade is fairly straightforward, but it can take some practice to get the desired look. Here is a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before applying pomade, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If your hair is wet, the pomade will not distribute evenly and can cause clumping or greasiness.

Step 2: Apply a small amount of pomade

Scoop a small amount of pomade (about a dime-sized amount for short hair, more for longer styles) onto your fingertips. Rub your hands together to warm up the product and distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Apply the pomade to your hair

Starting at the roots, work the pomade through your hair using your fingertips. Make sure to distribute the product evenly, focusing on the areas where you want the most shine and hold.

Step 4: Style your hair

Once the pomade is evenly distributed, use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape. If you want a slick, shiny look, comb your hair straight back or to the side. For a more textured look, use your fingers to create definition and separation.

Tips for Using Pomade

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your pomade:

  • Start with a small amount of pomade and add more as needed. You can always add more, but it's difficult to remove excess product once it's been applied.
  • Use a comb or brush to distribute the pomade evenly and create a smooth, polished finish.
  • If you have fine or thin hair, choose a lightweight pomade that won't weigh your hair down or make it look greasy.
  • If you have thick or coarse hair, choose a heavier pomade that will provide more hold and control.
  • Experiment with different types of pomade to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.

FAQ

What is the difference between pomade and hair wax?

Pomade and hair wax are both hair styling products, but they have different consistencies and finishes. Pomade typically has a smooth, shiny finish and a softer, more pliable texture, while hair wax has a matte, textured finish and a firmer hold.

Can women use pomade?

Absolutely! Pomade can be used by anyone who wants to add shine and definition to their hair. Women with short, textured hairstyles can benefit from using pomade to create a polished, finished look.

Hair Care

It's important to note that using too much pomade can cause buildup on your hair and scalp, which can lead to dandruff and other scalp issues. To avoid this, make sure to wash your hair regularly and use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any product buildup.

Overall, pomade can be a great styling product for anyone looking to add shine and hold to their hair. By choosing the right type of pomade for your hair type and style, and using it correctly, you can achieve a variety of different looks and keep your hair looking polished and healthy.
