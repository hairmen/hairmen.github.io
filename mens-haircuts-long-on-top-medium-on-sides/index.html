Mens Haircuts Long On Top Medium On Sides

If you're looking for a classic and stylish haircut, then a mens haircut long on top medium on sides is a great choice. This hairstyle has been popular for decades and is still a favorite among men of all ages. It's a versatile cut that can be styled in many different ways, so you can find the perfect look to suit your style and personality. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about this popular haircut, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Description

The mens haircut long on top medium on sides is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. It's a versatile cut that can be styled in many different ways, making it a popular choice for men of all ages. The haircut is characterized by longer hair on the top of the head, which is usually styled in a pompadour, quiff, or slicked-back look. The sides are cut shorter, usually to a medium length, and are tapered or faded to create a clean and sharp look.

How To

If you're interested in getting a mens haircut long on top medium on sides, then the first step is to find a good barber or hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. When you go in for your appointment, be sure to bring pictures of the style you like, so your stylist can get an idea of the look you want to achieve.

Choosing the Right Haircut

There are many different variations of the mens haircut long on top medium on sides, so it's important to choose the one that best suits your face shape and hair type. Some popular options include:

  • Pompadour: This is a classic style that features longer hair on top that is styled up and back, with shorter sides and a tapered back.
  • Quiff: This is a similar style to the pompadour, but with a more modern twist. The hair on top is styled up and back, but with a more textured and messy look.
  • Slicked-Back: This is a more formal style that features longer hair on top that is slicked back with a pomade or gel, with shorter sides and a tapered back.

Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen the right haircut, the next step is to learn how to style it. Depending on the look you want to achieve, you may need to use different products and techniques to style your hair. Some common styling techniques include:

  • Blow Drying: This is a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. Use a round brush to lift the hair at the roots while blow drying.
  • Pomade: This is a classic styling product that can be used to create a slicked-back look. Apply a small amount of pomade to your hair and comb it back for a classic look.
  • Texturizing Spray: This is a great product for creating a messy, textured look. Spray it onto your hair and use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair for a messy look.

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a mens haircut long on top medium on sides:

  1. Choose the right haircut for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Find a good barber or hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want.
  3. Bring pictures of the style you like to your appointment.
  4. Discuss the look you want to achieve with your stylist.
  5. Sit back and relax while your stylist cuts your hair.
  6. Style your hair at home using the techniques and products recommended by your stylist.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your mens haircut long on top medium on sides:

  • Choose a haircut that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Find a good barber or hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want.
  • Bring pictures of the style you like to your appointment.
  • Discuss the look you want to achieve with your stylist.
  • Use the right products and techniques to style your hair at home.
  • Experiment with different styles and techniques to find the look that suits you best.

FAQ

What face shape is best suited for a mens haircut long on top medium on sides?

A mens haircut long on top medium on sides can work with many different face shapes, but it's particularly well-suited for those with oval or square faces.

What hair type is best suited for a mens haircut long on top medium on sides?

A mens haircut long on top medium on sides can work with many different hair types, but it's particularly well-suited for those with thick, straight hair.

How often do I need to get a mens haircut long on top medium on sides?

It's recommended that you get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your mens haircut long on top medium on sides.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircut long on top medium on sides looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a styling product that works well with your hair type and the look you want to achieve.

By following these tips, you can keep your mens haircut long on top medium on sides looking great and feeling healthy.
