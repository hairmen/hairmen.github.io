How to Do a Perfect High Ponytail with Short Hair: Step by Step Guide

Are you tired of your short hair and want to change your look? A high ponytail is a classic hairstyle that can make you look chic and stylish. However, creating a high ponytail with short hair can be a bit challenging. In this step-by-step guide, we will show you how to do a perfect high ponytail with short hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

Before we begin, let's gather the tools you will need:

  • Elastic hairband
  • Bobby pins
  • Hairspray
  • Comb

Now, let's get started!

Step by Step

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before starting. It's easier to work with clean, dry hair.

Step 2: Brush your hair

Brush your hair to remove any tangles and knots. This will make it easier to style.

Step 3: Section your hair

Section your hair into two parts, with the top section being slightly larger than the bottom section.

Step 4: Tie the top section into a ponytail

Take the top section of your hair and tie it into a ponytail at the crown of your head. Secure it with an elastic hairband.

Step 5: Add volume

To add volume to your ponytail, tease the hair in the ponytail with a comb. This will create a fuller, more voluminous ponytail.

Step 6: Tie the bottom section into a ponytail

Take the bottom section of your hair and tie it into a ponytail underneath the first ponytail. Secure it with an elastic hairband.

Step 7: Combine the two ponytails

Take the top ponytail and pull it upwards, so it sits on top of the bottom ponytail. Use bobby pins to secure it in place.

Step 8: Smooth out your hair

Smooth out any bumps or lumps in your ponytail with your comb. Use hairspray to keep it in place.

Step 9: Finish the look

You can add accessories like a headband or hair clip to jazz up your high ponytail.

Tips

  • Use a hair tie that is the same color as your hair to make it less noticeable.
  • Tease your hair gently to avoid damaging it.
  • Use hairspray to keep your ponytail in place.
  • Don't pull your hair too tight as it can cause breakage.

FAQ

Can I do a high ponytail with short hair?

Yes, you can! Follow the steps in this guide to create a perfect high ponytail with short hair.

How can I add volume to my ponytail?

You can add volume to your ponytail by teasing the hair in the ponytail with a comb.

How can I keep my ponytail in place?

Use hairspray to keep your ponytail in place.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by washing it regularly and using a good conditioner. Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray if you do use them.

Now that you know how to do a perfect high ponytail with short hair, you can rock this stylish look wherever you go. Have fun experimenting with different accessories and styles!
