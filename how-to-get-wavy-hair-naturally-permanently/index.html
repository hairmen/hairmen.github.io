How To Get Wavy Hair Naturally Permanently

Written by William Nov 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who loves the look of wavy hair but doesn't want to rely on heat styling tools or expensive salon treatments, you're in luck! With a few simple tips and tricks, it's possible to get wavy hair naturally and permanently. In this article, we'll take you through everything you need to know, from how to create waves using braids and twists to how to care for your newly wavy locks.

Description

Wavy hair is a popular hairstyle that can be achieved through a variety of methods, including heat styling, chemical treatments, and natural techniques. For those looking to avoid heat and chemicals, natural methods are a great option. These methods involve using various techniques to create waves in your hair without causing damage. Natural waves can be achieved through braids, twists, and other styling techniques, and can last for days or even weeks with the right care.

How to Get Wavy Hair Naturally Permanently: Step by Step

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you begin creating waves, it's important to start with clean hair. This will help your waves last longer and prevent any buildup that could weigh down your hair. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and then allow it to air dry completely.

Step 2: Choose Your Technique

There are a variety of techniques you can use to create waves in your hair, including braids, twists, and buns. Choose the technique that works best for your hair type and the type of waves you want to create. For example, braids will create more defined waves, while twists will create looser waves.

Step 3: Create Your Waves

Once you've chosen your technique, it's time to create your waves. Divide your hair into sections and begin braiding, twisting, or wrapping your hair around your head. Secure each section with a hair tie or clip, and then allow your hair to dry completely. You can speed up the drying process by using a blow dryer on a low heat setting.

Step 4: Unwrap Your Waves

Once your hair is completely dry, it's time to unwrap your waves. Gently remove the hair ties or clips and carefully unravel your braids or twists. Use your fingers to separate and define your waves, and then apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Step 5: Maintain Your Waves

To keep your waves looking their best, it's important to maintain them properly. Avoid using heat styling tools or harsh chemicals, and instead opt for gentle, nourishing hair products. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing your waves too aggressively. You can also refresh your waves in between washes by spritzing them with a mixture of water and sea salt.

Tips for Getting Wavy Hair Naturally Permanently

Getting wavy hair naturally can take a bit of practice, but with these tips, you'll be a pro in no time:

  • Experiment with different techniques to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Use high-quality hair products that are free from harsh chemicals and sulfates.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Can I get wavy hair naturally permanently?

Yes, you can! By using natural techniques such as braids and twists, you can create waves in your hair that will last for days or even weeks.

Can I get wavy hair naturally without heat?

Absolutely! Natural techniques such as braids and twists don't require any heat, making them a great option for those who want to avoid heat styling tools.

How can I make my waves last longer?

To make your waves last longer, avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools. Use gentle, nourishing hair products and avoid brushing your waves too aggressively. You can also refresh your waves in between washes by spritzing them with a mixture of water and sea salt.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Wavy hair requires some special care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are free from harsh chemicals.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too aggressively, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can get wavy hair naturally and permanently. Embrace your natural texture and enjoy your beautifully wavy locks!
