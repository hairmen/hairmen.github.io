How To Make Short Hair Look Good While Growing It Out

Written by Eveline Nov 20, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you currently in the process of growing out your short hair? It can be a challenging time, as your hair goes through awkward phases and may not be as easy to style as it was before. However, there are a few tips and tricks that can help you make your short hair look good while growing it out. In this article, we will guide you through the process step by step and answer some frequently asked questions about growing out short hair.

How to Make Short Hair Look Good While Growing It Out

The key to making short hair look good while growing it out is to keep it well-maintained and styled properly. Here are some tips to help you achieve that:

1. Keep your hair trimmed regularly

Even though you're growing out your hair, it's essential to keep it trimmed to avoid split ends and maintain a healthy look. A trim every six to eight weeks can help keep your hair looking neat and prevent it from looking too shaggy.

2. Experiment with different haircuts

Just because you're growing out your hair doesn't mean you can't experiment with different haircuts. Consult with your hairstylist to find a style that works for you and complements your face shape. You can try adding layers or bangs to frame your face and add dimension to your hair.

3. Use hair accessories

As your hair grows, you may find it challenging to style it the way you want. Hair accessories like headbands, hair clips, and scrunchies can help keep your hair in place and add some flair to your hairstyles. Experiment with different styles and see what works best for you.

4. Embrace the natural texture of your hair

Short hair can have a lot of texture, and as it grows out, that texture can become more pronounced. Embrace the natural texture of your hair and work with it instead of trying to fight it. You can use texturizing products like sea salt spray to enhance your hair's natural texture and create a beachy, effortless look.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you make short hair look good while growing it out:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Knowing your face shape is essential when it comes to choosing the right haircut. Consult with your hairstylist to determine your face shape and find a style that complements it.

Step 2: Invest in quality hair products

Good hair care starts with quality products. Invest in a good shampoo and conditioner that cater to your hair type and use styling products like mousse or gel to create the look you want.

Step 3: Experiment with different hairstyles

As your hair grows, it may go through awkward phases. Experiment with different hairstyles to find one that works for you and complements your face shape. You can try adding layers or bangs to frame your face and add dimension to your hair.

Step 4: Use hair accessories

As mentioned earlier, hair accessories like headbands, hair clips, and scrunchies can help keep your hair in place and add some flair to your hairstyles. Experiment with different styles and see what works best for you.

Step 5: Keep your hair trimmed regularly

Even though you're growing out your hair, it's essential to keep it trimmed to avoid split ends and maintain a healthy look. A trim every six to eight weeks can help keep your hair looking neat and prevent it from looking too shaggy.

Tips for Growing Out Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you grow out your short hair:

1. Be patient

Growing out your hair takes time, and it may not always look the way you want it to. Be patient and trust the process.

2. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and slow down the growth process. Try to avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners as much as possible.

3. Eat a healthy diet

A healthy diet is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure you're eating a diet rich in vitamins and minerals like iron, zinc, and biotin.

4. Get plenty of rest

Getting enough rest is essential for healthy hair growth. Aim for seven to eight hours of sleep each night.

FAQs

Q: How long does it take to grow out short hair?

A: The length of time it takes to grow out short hair depends on your hair's natural growth rate and how long you want it to be. On average, hair grows about half an inch per month.

Q: Can I color my hair while growing it out?

A: Yes, you can color your hair while growing it out. However, it's essential to be gentle with your hair and avoid excessive coloring or bleaching, which can damage your hair.

Q: How can I make my hair grow faster?

A: While there is no magic formula for making hair grow faster, you can promote healthy hair growth by eating a healthy diet, getting enough rest, and avoiding excessive heat styling or chemical treatments that can damage your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Growing Out Short Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you grow out your short hair:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb

When combing out your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid pulling or damaging your hair.

2. Avoid tight hairstyles

Tight hairstyles like braids or ponytails can pull on your hair and cause breakage. Try to avoid these styles as much as possible.

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment

A deep conditioning treatment can help keep your hair healthy and nourished. Apply a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.

4. Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair and slow down the growth process. Wear a hat or use a UV-protective spray to shield your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make short hair look good while growing it out. Remember to be patient and trust the process, and soon enough, you'll have the long, luscious locks you've been dreaming of!
