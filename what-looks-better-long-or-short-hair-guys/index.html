What Looks Better Long Or Short Hair For Guys

Written by Robby Nov 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to choosing the perfect hairstyle, it can be difficult to decide between long or short hair. While both styles have their pros and cons, it ultimately comes down to personal preference and what looks best on each individual. In this article, we'll discuss the advantages and disadvantages of long and short hair for guys, as well as some tips for hair care and maintenance.

Advantages of Long Hair for Guys

Long hair can be a great look for guys who want to stand out from the crowd. It can be styled in a variety of ways, from messy and tousled to sleek and straight. Some of the advantages of long hair for guys include:

  • More styling options
  • A unique and eye-catching look
  • The ability to tie it back for a more professional appearance

However, there are also some disadvantages to consider.

Disadvantages of Long Hair for Guys

While long hair can be a great look, it's not for everyone. Some of the disadvantages of long hair for guys include:

  • Requires more maintenance and care
  • May not be appropriate for certain professional settings
  • Can be hot and uncomfortable in the summer months

Advantages of Short Hair for Guys

Short hair is a classic look that never goes out of style. It's easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways. Some of the advantages of short hair for guys include:

  • Requires less maintenance and care
  • Is appropriate for most professional settings
  • Can help to keep you cool in the summer months

However, there are also some disadvantages to consider.

Disadvantages of Short Hair for Guys

While short hair is a classic look, it may not be the best choice for everyone. Some of the disadvantages of short hair for guys include:

  • Less styling options
  • May not be as eye-catching or unique as long hair
  • Requires more frequent trips to the barber for maintenance

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for You

Ultimately, the best hairstyle for you will depend on your personal style, face shape, and hair type. Here are some tips to help you choose the best hairstyle:

  • Consider your face shape: Different hairstyles can accentuate different features. If you have a round face, for example, a shorter cut may be more flattering.
  • Think about your lifestyle: If you have a job that requires you to be professional and well-groomed, a shorter cut may be more appropriate.
  • Experiment with different styles: Don't be afraid to try out different hairstyles until you find the one that feels right for you.

Hair Care Tips for Long and Short Hair

Whether you have long or short hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized

Frequently Asked Questions

Is long hair or short hair better for thinning hair?

Short hair is generally better for thinning hair, as it can help to create the illusion of more volume. However, if you have thinning hair and want to grow it out, long hair can still be a great look if styled correctly.

Can I still tie my hair back if it's short?

While it may be more difficult to tie shorter hair back, it is still possible. You may need to use bobby pins or other hair accessories to keep it in place.

Can I dye my hair if it's long?

Yes, you can dye your hair no matter the length. However, keep in mind that longer hair may require more dye and may be more difficult to maintain.


Whether you prefer long or short hair, the most important thing is to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect look for you.
