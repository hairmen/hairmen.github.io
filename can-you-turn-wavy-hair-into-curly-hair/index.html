Can You Turn Wavy Hair Into Curly Hair

Written by Robby Nov 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have naturally wavy hair, you may be wondering if it's possible to turn it into curly hair. The answer is yes, it is possible! With the right techniques and products, you can transform your wavy locks into bouncy, defined curls. In this article, we'll show you how to turn wavy hair into curly hair and provide tips for caring for your new curly locks.

Description

Wavy hair is characterized by loose, flowing waves that are not quite straight but not quite curly. On the other hand, curly hair is defined by tight, spiral-shaped curls that are more voluminous and bouncy. While some people are born with naturally curly hair, others may have wavy hair that can be transformed into curls with the right tools and techniques.

How to Turn Wavy Hair into Curly Hair

Step 1: Start with Clean, Moisturized Hair

Before you begin styling your hair, make sure it is clean and moisturized. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz. Once you've washed and conditioned your hair, gently towel dry it to remove excess moisture.

Step 2: Apply a Curl-Enhancing Product

To enhance your natural curls, apply a curl-enhancing product such as mousse, gel, or cream. These products work by defining your curls and reducing frizz. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, scrunching your hair as you go to encourage curl formation.

Step 3: Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Hold the diffuser close to your hair and use a low heat setting to dry your hair. Gently scrunch your hair with your hands as you dry it to encourage curl formation.

Step 4: Finish with a Holding Product

To keep your curls in place all day, finish with a holding product such as hairspray or a curl-defining cream. Apply the product lightly to your hair, focusing on the ends and any areas that need extra hold.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Once you've turned your wavy hair into curly hair, it's important to take care of your new curls. Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.

FAQ About Turning Wavy Hair into Curly Hair

Is it possible to turn straight hair into curly hair?

Yes, it is possible to turn straight hair into curly hair. However, this will require more effort and potentially more damage to your hair. It's best to consult with a professional stylist before attempting to turn straight hair into curly hair.

What is the best curl-enhancing product?

The best curl-enhancing product will depend on your hair type and personal preferences. Some popular options include mousse, gel, and cream. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

Curly hair tends to be drier than other hair types, so it's best to wash it less frequently. Aim to wash your hair no more than twice a week to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Your New Curls

Now that you know how to turn wavy hair into curly hair, it's important to take care of your new curls. Remember to keep your hair moisturized, use the right products, and avoid heat styling as much as possible. With proper care, your curly hair will be the envy of everyone!
