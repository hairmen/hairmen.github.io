Which Hairstyle Is Good For Wavy Hair

Written by Daniel Jul 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse all at once. While it gives you a natural, effortless look, it can also be difficult to maintain and style. Many people with wavy hair struggle to find the perfect hairstyle that suits their hair type. But worry not, as in this post, we will be discussing the best hairstyles for wavy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Wavy hair is a hair type that falls somewhere between straight and curly hair. It has a natural wave pattern that can vary in intensity and shape, making it a unique and versatile hair type. However, not all hairstyles are suitable for wavy hair, and choosing the wrong style can lead to frizz, flyaways, and an overall messy look.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for wavy hair is to determine your face shape. Different face shapes suit different hairstyles, so it's essential to consider this before making a decision. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking at hairstyles that will accentuate your best features and complement your hair type.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, it's best to opt for hairstyles that add height and length to your face. Long, layered hairstyles, and hairstyles with side-swept bangs are perfect for round faces. Avoid blunt cuts and short hairstyles that accentuate the roundness of your face.

Square Face Shape

Square face shapes have a strong jawline that can be softened with the right hairstyle. Soft, layered hairstyles, and side-swept bangs work well for square faces. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face look even more square.

Oval Face Shape

Oval face shapes have a balanced shape that can pull off almost any hairstyle. From long, straight hair to short, curly hairstyles, oval faces can experiment with different styles without worrying about their face shape.

Heart Face Shape

Heart-shaped faces have a wide forehead and a narrow chin. Hairstyles that add volume to the chin area work best for this face shape. Long, layered hairstyles with side-swept bangs, and bob hairstyles are perfect for heart-shaped faces.

Diamond Face Shape

Diamond face shapes have a narrow forehead and chin and wide cheekbones. Hairstyles that add width to the forehead and chin area work best for this face shape. Long, layered hairstyles, and hairstyles with side-swept bangs are perfect for diamond faces.

Step by Step

Once you've determined your face shape and have a few hairstyle options in mind, it's time to start styling your wavy hair. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect wavy hairstyle:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Wavy hair tends to be dry, so it's important to keep it moisturized.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or a styling cream to your damp hair to define your waves and reduce frizz.
  3. Blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting, using a diffuser attachment to enhance your waves.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to touch up any areas that need more definition.
  5. Finish off your hairstyle with a light hairspray to keep your waves in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your wavy hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can lead to frizz and breakage.
  • Invest in a high-quality diffuser attachment for your hairdryer to enhance your waves.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your wavy hair using a flat iron or straightening brush. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: Can I wear my wavy hair in a ponytail?

A: Yes, you can wear your wavy hair in a ponytail. However, be sure to use a soft, elastic band to avoid pulling your hair too tightly and causing breakage.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful wavy hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair in top condition:

  • Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that's specifically designed for wavy hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dyes, as these can damage your hair and strip it of its natural oils.

In conclusion, wavy hair can be styled in numerous ways, depending on your face shape and personal preferences. Whether you prefer long, layered hairstyles, or short, textured haircuts, there's a perfect style out there for everyone with wavy hair. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this article, you can achieve the perfect wavy hairstyle and keep your hair healthy and beautiful for years to come.
