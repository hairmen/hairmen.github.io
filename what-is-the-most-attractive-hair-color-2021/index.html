What Is The Most Attractive Hair Color 2021

Written by Eveline Jun 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hair color this year? As we enter 2023, it's important to take a look back at what was popular in 2021. In this blog post, we'll be discussing what was considered the most attractive hair color of 2021, and how you can achieve this look for yourself.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Achieve the Look
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintaining the Color
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care for Colored Hair

Description

In 2021, the most attractive hair color was a warm balayage. This is a technique where highlights are hand-painted onto the hair to create a natural, sun-kissed look. The balayage technique creates a softer, more natural look compared to traditional highlights, which can sometimes look too harsh or stripy. The warm tones of the balayage also complemented many skin tones, making it a popular choice for many people.

While the balayage technique is still popular in 2023, there are some variations that have emerged. For example, some people are opting for cooler tones, such as ash blonde or platinum, while others are adding more contrast to their balayage with darker lowlights.

How to Achieve the Look

If you're looking to achieve the most attractive hair color of 2021, a warm balayage, you'll need to visit a professional hair stylist. Balayage is a technique that requires a lot of skill and expertise, so it's not recommended to try this at home.

During your appointment, your stylist will hand-paint highlights onto your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends to create a natural, sun-kissed look. They may also add darker lowlights to create more contrast and depth.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're interested in trying the balayage technique for yourself, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose a professional hair stylist who specializes in balayage
  2. Discuss your desired end result with your stylist
  3. Your stylist will mix the appropriate hair color and apply it to your hair using a hand-painting technique
  4. After processing, your stylist will rinse out the color and apply a toner (if needed) to achieve the desired tone
  5. Your hair will be styled and blow-dried to complete the look

Remember, it's important to leave balayage to the professionals. Attempting this technique at home can lead to uneven results and damage to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining the Color

After you've achieved your desired balayage look, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the color. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip the color
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often as this can also cause fading
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray
  • Visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups and trims

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about the most attractive hair color of 2021:

Q: Is balayage suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, balayage can work on all hair types, from straight to curly, and fine to thick.

Q: How long does balayage last?

A: Balayage can last up to six months, depending on how well you take care of your hair and how quickly it grows.

Q: Can I achieve the balayage look at home?

A: It's not recommended to try balayage at home, as it requires a lot of skill and expertise. Leave it to the professionals!

Hair Care for Colored Hair

Color-treated hair requires special care to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Deep condition your hair regularly
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups and trims

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can maintain the most attractive hair color of 2021 and keep your locks looking healthy and shiny.
