How Can I Get My Hair Curly Without A Perm A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you want to add some bounce and volume to your hair without using harsh chemicals? If so, you’re not alone! Many people are looking for ways to get curly hair without a perm. In this article, we’ll explore different methods to get curly hair naturally without damaging your hair. Let’s dive in!

Table of Contents:

  • How to Get Curly Hair Naturally
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Get Curly Hair Naturally

There are many natural ways to get curly hair without a perm. Here are some methods you can try:

Method 1: Twist-Outs

A twist-out is a popular method for getting curly hair naturally. To do a twist-out, follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air-dry until it’s damp.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and apply a leave-in conditioner to each section.
  3. Take a small section of hair and twist it tightly, starting at the root and twisting all the way to the end.
  4. Repeat this process until your entire head is twisted.
  5. Cover your hair with a satin bonnet or scarf and let it dry overnight.
  6. In the morning, unravel the twists and use your fingers to separate them.
  7. Apply a light oil to your hair to add shine and prevent frizz.

Twist-outs can last for several days, depending on your hair type and how well you maintain them.

Method 2: Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are another popular method for getting curly hair naturally. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air-dry until it’s damp.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and apply a leave-in conditioner to each section.
  3. Take a small section of hair and twist it tightly, starting at the root and twisting all the way to the end.
  4. Wrap the twisted hair around itself to create a knot.
  5. Repeat this process until your entire head is in knots.
  6. Cover your hair with a satin bonnet or scarf and let it dry overnight.
  7. In the morning, unravel the knots and use your fingers to separate them.
  8. Apply a light oil to your hair to add shine and prevent frizz.

Bantu knots can also last for several days, depending on your hair type and how well you maintain them.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting curly hair naturally without a perm:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently, and then let it air-dry until it’s about 80% dry.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing cream to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  4. Section your hair and begin twisting it tightly, starting at the root and twisting all the way to the end.
  5. Repeat this process until your entire head is twisted.
  6. Use a hair dryer on low heat to dry your hair completely.
  7. Once your hair is dry, unravel the twists and use your fingers to separate them.
  8. Apply a light oil or hair serum to your hair to add shine and prevent frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often, as it can damage your curls.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as it can dry out your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I get curly hair without a perm?

A: Yes, you can get curly hair naturally without using a perm. Twist-outs, bantu knots, and other methods can help you achieve curly hair without damaging your hair.

Q: How long do twist-outs and bantu knots last?

A: Twist-outs and bantu knots can last for several days, depending on your hair type and how well you maintain them.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Generally, you should wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness and breakage.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing cream to your hair after washing.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often, as it can damage your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as it can dry out your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Getting curly hair naturally without a perm is easier than you might think. With the right techniques and hair care, you can achieve bouncy, voluminous curls that will turn heads. Give these methods a try and see which one works best for you!
