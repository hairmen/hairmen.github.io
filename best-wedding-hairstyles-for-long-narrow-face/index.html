Best Wedding Hairstyles For Long Narrow Face

Written by Eveline Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a bride-to-be with a long narrow face and struggling to find the perfect wedding hairstyle? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the best wedding hairstyles for long narrow faces that will make you look stunning on your big day. Whether you have long or short hair, we have got you covered. Read on to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape.

Description

Long narrow faces are characterized by their elongated shape and prominent chin. The key to finding the perfect wedding hairstyle for this face shape is to create width and volume around the cheekbones and forehead. This will help to balance out the face and make it appear more oval-shaped.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect wedding hairstyle for your long narrow face is to determine your hair type and length. Once you have this information, you can start exploring different hairstyles that will suit your face shape. Here are some of the best wedding hairstyles for long narrow faces:

1. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add width to your face and make it appear less narrow. This hairstyle works well with long hair and can be paired with loose waves or a sleek ponytail.

2. Loose Waves

Loose waves are a classic wedding hairstyle that works well with long narrow faces. This hairstyle adds volume around the cheekbones and creates a soft, romantic look.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is a great option for brides with long hair. This hairstyle adds volume to the top of the head and creates a balanced look for long narrow faces.

4. Updo

An updo is a classic wedding hairstyle that works well with long narrow faces. This hairstyle adds volume to the top of the head and creates a balanced look for long narrow faces.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to create the perfect wedding hairstyle for long narrow faces:

1. Determine your hair type and length.

Knowing your hair type and length will help you choose the right hairstyle for your face shape.

2. Choose a hairstyle that adds width.

Choose a hairstyle that adds volume around the cheekbones and forehead to balance out your face shape.

3. Consider adding accessories.

Accessories such as flowers, tiaras, and hairpins can add a touch of elegance to your wedding hairstyle.

4. Consult with a hairstylist.

Consulting with a hairstylist is a great way to get expert advice on the best wedding hairstyle for your long narrow face.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the perfect wedding hairstyle for your long narrow face:

1. Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair straight back.

Straight back hairstyles will make your face appear even longer and narrower.

2. Consider adding volume to the top of your head.

Adding volume to the top of your head will balance out your face shape and create a more oval look.

3. Experiment with different hairstyles.

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find the perfect one for your face shape.

FAQ

Q. Can I wear my hair in a bun if I have a long narrow face?

A. Yes, you can wear your hair in a bun if you have a long narrow face. However, make sure to add volume to the top of your head to balance out your face shape.

Q. What accessories can I wear with my wedding hairstyle?

A. You can wear flowers, tiaras, hairpins, and other accessories to add a touch of elegance to your wedding hairstyle.

Hair Care

Before your wedding day, make sure to take care of your hair by using a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and try to air dry your hair as much as possible. This will keep your hair healthy and shiny for your big day.

Now that you know the best wedding hairstyles for long narrow faces, you can confidently choose the perfect hairstyle for your big day. Remember to choose a hairstyle that adds volume to your face and balances out your face shape. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and accessories to find the perfect look for you.
