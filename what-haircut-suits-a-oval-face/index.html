What Haircut Suits A Oval Face

Written by Daniel Aug 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have an oval face, congratulations! You have one of the most versatile face shapes that allows you to pull off almost any hairstyle. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect haircut that complements your face shape. In this article, we'll guide you through the best haircuts for an oval face, how to style them, tips, and hair care advice to keep your locks looking their best.

Description

An oval face has balanced features with a gentle curve along the hairline and jawline. It's longer than it is wide, with a forehead that's slightly wider than the chin. This face shape tends to suit a wide range of hairstyles, from short to long, straight to curly, and with or without bangs.

How to Choose the Perfect Haircut for an Oval Face?

When choosing a haircut for an oval face, the main goal is to enhance your natural features and highlight your best assets. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect haircut:

Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type plays a significant role in the hairstyle you choose. For example, if you have thin hair, you might want to avoid long layers as they can make your hair look flat. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you might want to consider a layered haircut to reduce the weight and add movement.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also influence the haircut you choose. If you have an active lifestyle, you might want a low-maintenance haircut that requires minimal styling. On the other hand, if you have more time to devote to your hair, you might want a more intricate hairstyle that showcases your creativity.

Consider Your Face Shape

While an oval face shape can suit almost any hairstyle, it's essential to consider your face shape when choosing a haircut. For example, if you have a long face, you might want to avoid hairstyles that add height on top, as they can make your face look even longer.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing a Haircut for an Oval Face

Follow these steps to choose the perfect haircut for your oval face shape:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a haircut, it's essential to determine your face shape. Stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are equal or close to equal, you have an oval face shape.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

Consider your hair type when choosing a haircut. If you have thin hair, you might want to consider a short bob or a pixie cut to add volume. If you have thick hair, you might want to consider a layered haircut to reduce the weight and add movement.

Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle

Think about your lifestyle when choosing a haircut. If you have an active lifestyle, you might want a low-maintenance haircut that requires minimal styling. On the other hand, if you have more time to devote to your hair, you might want a more intricate hairstyle that showcases your creativity.

Step 4: Consider Your Face Shape

Consider your face shape when choosing a haircut. If you have an oval face shape, you can pull off almost any hairstyle, from short to long, straight to curly. However, if you have a long face, you might want to avoid hairstyles that add height on top, as they can make your face look even longer.

Step 5: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your oval face. Your hairstylist can recommend a haircut that complements your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. They can also advise you on how to style your hair to enhance your natural features.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen the perfect haircut for your oval face, here are some tips for styling your hair:

Tip 1: Add Volume

If you have thin hair, you can add volume by blow-drying your hair upside down or using a volumizing product like mousse or dry shampoo.

Tip 2: Embrace Your Natural Texture

If you have curly or wavy hair, embrace your natural texture by using a curl-enhancing product like a curl cream or gel. If you have straight hair, you can add texture by using a texturizing spray or sea salt spray.

Tip 3: Use Heat Protectant

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like a curling iron or straightener.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for an oval face?

An oval face can suit almost any hairstyle, but some of the best haircuts include a long bob, layered haircut, pixie cut, and side-swept bangs.

Can oval faces pull off bangs?

Yes, oval faces can pull off almost any type of bangs, from blunt to side-swept to wispy.

How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

Hair Care for an Oval Face

To keep your hair looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that's free of sulfates and parabens to prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

Tip 2: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray to prevent damage and fading.

In conclusion, an oval face shape is one of the most versatile face shapes when it comes to choosing a haircut. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can enhance your natural features and look your best. Follow these tips, consult with your hairstylist, and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
