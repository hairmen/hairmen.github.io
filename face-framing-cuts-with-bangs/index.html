Face Framing Cuts With Bangs The Perfect Hairstyle For A Fresh Look

Written by Petter Sep 22, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking for a hairstyle that will give you a fresh and youthful look, then you should definitely consider face framing cuts with bangs. This hairstyle is perfect for anyone who wants to add a touch of glamour to their hair without sacrificing its natural beauty.

Description

Face framing cuts with bangs are a type of hairstyle that is designed to frame the face and highlight its best features. This hairstyle is perfect for those who have round or square-shaped faces as it helps to soften the angles and make the face appear more oval-shaped. The bangs that come with this hairstyle can be customized to suit your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

How to

Getting a face framing cut with bangs is not as complicated as it may seem. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to get this hairstyle:

Step 1: Consult with a Hair Stylist

The first step is to consult with a hair stylist who has experience in creating face framing cuts with bangs. During the consultation, you should discuss your hair type, face shape, and personal style to determine the best type of bangs and cut that will suit you.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before getting the haircut, you should wash and dry your hair. This will make it easier for the stylist to cut your hair and ensure that it’s clean and free from any product buildup.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

The stylist will then section your hair into small sections and start cutting the hair around your face to create the face framing effect. They will also cut your bangs to the desired length and style.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once the haircut is done, the stylist will style your hair to complete the look. You can choose to straighten, curl, or wave your hair depending on your personal preference.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your face framing cut with bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair to prevent heat damage.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them at the desired length.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF.

FAQ

Q: Will face framing cuts with bangs suit any face shape?

A: While face framing cuts with bangs are generally suited for round or square-shaped faces, they can also work for other face shapes. It’s important to consult with a hair stylist to determine the best type of cut and bangs for your face shape.

Q: How often should I trim my bangs?

A: You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them at the desired length and prevent them from getting in your eyes.

Q: Can I style my face framing cut with bangs in different ways?

A: Yes, you can style your face framing cut with bangs in different ways depending on your personal preference. You can choose to straighten, curl, or wave your hair to create different looks.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as this can cause heat damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF.

With these tips, you can maintain your face framing cut with bangs and keep your hair looking beautiful and healthy.
