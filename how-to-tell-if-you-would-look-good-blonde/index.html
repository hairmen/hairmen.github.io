How To Tell If You Would Look Good Blonde Tips And Tricks

Written by William Aug 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering transforming your hair color to blonde? It's a bold move that can really make a statement, but it's important to make sure it's the right choice for you. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you determine if you would look good blonde. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. What to Consider
  2. Face Shape
  3. Skin Tone
  4. Eye Color
  5. Natural Hair Color
  6. Try a Virtual Makeover

  8. Hair Care Tips for Blonde Hair

What to Consider

Before deciding to go blonde, there are several factors to consider. First, you should think about your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, it may take several sessions to achieve the light blonde color you desire. This can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to be aware of the potential consequences.

You should also consider your skin tone, face shape, and eye color. These factors can all play a role in whether or not you will look good with blonde hair. Let's take a closer look at each of these factors.

Face Shape

The shape of your face can affect how certain hairstyles and colors look on you. If you have a round face, blonde hair can help elongate your face and create a more angular look. If you have a square face, however, blonde hair may accentuate your jawline and create a harsher look.

Skin Tone

Your skin tone is another important factor to consider when deciding if you would look good blonde. If you have warm undertones, a honey or golden blonde may complement your skin tone well. If you have cool undertones, a platinum or icy blonde may work better for you.

Eye Color

Your eye color can also affect how blonde hair will look on you. If you have blue or green eyes, a cooler blonde may bring out the color of your eyes. If you have brown eyes, a warmer blonde may complement your eye color.

Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also play a role in whether or not you will look good blonde. If you have naturally light hair, you may be able to achieve blonde hair with minimal damage. If you have dark hair, however, it may take several sessions to achieve the blonde color you desire.

Try a Virtual Makeover

If you're still unsure if you would look good blonde, try a virtual makeover. There are several websites and apps that allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hair colors and styles. This can give you a better idea of how blonde hair will look on you.

FAQ

1. Will going blonde damage my hair?

It is possible that going blonde can damage your hair, especially if you have dark hair. It's important to have a professional colorist perform the transformation to minimize the risk of damage.

2. How do I maintain blonde hair?

Blonde hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. You should use a purple shampoo to prevent brassiness, avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, and get regular trims to prevent split ends.

3. Can anyone pull off blonde hair?

While blonde hair can look great on many people, it's not for everyone. It's important to consider your skin tone, face shape, and natural hair color before deciding to go blonde.

Hair Care Tips for Blonde Hair

Once you've decided to go blonde, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a purple shampoo to prevent brassiness
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid chlorine and salt water as much as possible

By following these tips and tricks, you can determine if you would look good blonde and keep your blonde hair looking healthy and vibrant.
