Description

Having a square face shape means that your face is nearly as long as it is wide, with a strong jawline and angular features. When it comes to choosing a flattering hairstyle, the goal is to soften those angular lines and balance out the proportions of your face. Short hairstyles are a great option for square faces, as they can help to elongate the face and draw attention away from the jawline. In this article, we'll explore some of the best short hairstyles for square faces, along with tips for choosing and maintaining your new look.

How to Choose the Best Short Hairstyle for Square Faces

When choosing a short hairstyle for a square face, it's important to consider the overall shape of your face, as well as your hair type and personal style. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

Consider Your Face Shape

While square faces all share some common characteristics, there are still variations in terms of width, length, and proportions. Take a close look at your face in the mirror and consider whether you have more of a square oval, square round, or square heart-shaped face. This will help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your unique features.

Opt for Softness

Since square faces already have a lot of angularity, it's important to choose a hairstyle that adds softness and movement. This can be achieved through layers, curls, or choppy, textured cuts.

Consider Your Hair Type

The texture and thickness of your hair will also play a role in determining the best short hairstyle for you. If you have fine hair, for example, a blunt bob may look too flat and lifeless. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, a short pixie cut may be difficult to style and maintain.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Square Faces

Once you've chosen a short hairstyle that flatters your square face, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair:

1. Begin with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before styling. If you have thin or fine hair, you may want to use a volumizing mousse or spray to add some lift.

2. Apply Product

Depending on your hair type, you may want to apply a styling cream, gel, or wax to add texture and hold. Rub a small amount of product between your palms and work it through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

3. Use a Blow Dryer

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. Start at the roots and work your way down, using the brush to lift and shape your hair as you go.

4. Style as Desired

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron, curling iron, or other styling tools to create your desired look. If you have a pixie cut, you may simply want to add a bit of texture with your fingers. If you have a bob, you can add curls or waves to create a more romantic look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hairstyle for Square Faces

Here are a few tips for keeping your short hairstyle looking great:

1. Schedule Regular Trims

Short haircuts require more frequent trims to maintain their shape and style. Aim to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh.

2. Use Quality Haircare Products

Invest in high-quality haircare products that are designed for your hair type and style. Look for products that add volume, texture, and shine, and avoid anything that weighs your hair down or makes it look greasy.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

When using styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

What are the best short hairstyles for square faces?

Some of the best short hairstyles for square faces include pixie cuts, blunt bobs, layered bobs, and asymmetrical cuts. These styles add softness and movement to the face, while also balancing out the proportions.

Can I wear bangs with a square face?

Yes, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs. Side-swept bangs or long, wispy bangs can help to soften the angularity of a square face, while blunt or heavy bangs can make the face look even more square.

What hair colors are best for square faces?

When it comes to hair color, there are no hard and fast rules. However, it's generally a good idea to choose a color that flatters your skin tone and eye color, rather than trying to match your hair to your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair and Square Faces

Here are a few additional tips for keeping your short hair looking healthy and beautiful:

1. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Short hair can be prone to dryness and breakage, so it's important to keep it hydrated with a weekly deep conditioning treatment.

3. Avoid Over-Styling

While it's tempting to style your hair every day, over-styling can damage your hair and cause it to become weak and brittle. Try to limit heat styling and give your hair a break from time to time.

With these tips and styling ideas, you're sure to find a short hairstyle that flatters your square face and makes you feel confident and beautiful. Remember, the key is to choose a style that adds softness and movement, while also highlighting your unique features.
