Short Hairstyles That Suit Fat Faces

Written by William Aug 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of having long hair that just emphasizes your round face? Do you want a fresh new look that will flatter your features and make you feel confident? Look no further than short hairstyles that suit fat faces! In this article, we'll go over some of the best short hairstyles for round faces and provide tips and tricks for keeping your hair looking great.

Description

Short hairstyles can be a great choice for those with round faces, as they can help balance out the proportions of a fuller face. However, it's important to choose the right length and style to flatter your features. Some short hairstyles that suit fat faces include pixie cuts, bobs, and asymmetrical cuts. These styles can help elongate the face and draw attention to your best features.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for a Fat Face

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, there are a few key factors to consider:

  • Length: Short hairstyles are generally a good choice for round faces, but the length should be carefully chosen. Too short and it can make your face look even rounder, while too long can weigh down your features. Aim for a length that hits just below the chin or at the jawline.
  • Layers: Layers can be a great way to add texture and movement to your hair, but be careful not to add too many layers or too much volume. This can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for subtle layers that frame your face.
  • Parting: The way you part your hair can also make a difference in how your face looks. A side part can help create the illusion of length, while a center part can emphasize the roundness of your face.

Short Hairstyles for Round Faces

Some of the best short hairstyles for fat faces include:

  • Pixie Cut: This super short cut can be a great option for those who want a low-maintenance style that still looks chic. Opt for a slightly longer cut on top to add some height and balance out your features.
  • Bob: A bob that hits just below the chin can be a great choice for a round face. Add some subtle layers to frame your face and add movement.
  • Asymmetrical Cut: An asymmetrical cut can help elongate the face and draw attention to your best features. Keep the length slightly longer on one side to create a flattering angle.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for a Round Face

Styling short hair can be quick and easy, but it's important to use the right products and techniques to keep your hair looking great. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair. This will help add texture and hold.
  3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots to add volume.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair or add curls if desired.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for a Round Face

Short hair can be a great choice for a round face, but it does require some maintenance to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styles and partings to find what works best for your face shape.

FAQ: Short Hairstyles for Fat Faces

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a short hairstyle for a round face?

A: Yes, but choose a style that flatters your features. Opt for side-swept bangs or longer bangs that can be styled to the side.

Q: Can I wear my short hair curly?

A: Yes, but be careful not to add too much volume or your hair can look wider. Opt for loose, beachy waves or soft curls.

Hair Care for Short Hair and Round Faces

Short hair requires some special care to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a heat protectant before using any hot tools.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray or other styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a short hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident. Whether you opt for a pixie cut, bob, or asymmetrical style, remember to choose a length and style that works for your face shape and personal style.
