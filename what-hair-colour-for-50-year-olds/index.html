What Hair Colour For 50 Year Olds

Written by Daniel Sep 02, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a 50-year-old woman, you might be wondering what hair colour is best for you. As we age, our skin tone and features change, and it's essential to choose a hair colour that complements these changes. Whether you're looking to enhance your natural colour or try something new, this guide will help you find the perfect hair colour for 50-year-olds.

Description

As we get older, our hair tends to lose its natural shine and volume. It can also become thinner, and the colour can fade. Choosing the right hair colour can help to enhance your natural beauty and make you feel more confident. There are several factors to consider when choosing the best hair colour for 50-year-olds, including skin tone, eye colour, and lifestyle.

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour

When choosing a hair colour, it's important to consider your skin tone. As we age, our skin can become more sallow, and our undertones can become more yellow or green. If you have warm undertones, try to choose a hair colour with warm tones such as golden blonde or copper. If you have cool undertones, try to choose a hair colour with cool tones such as ash blonde or cool brown.

You should also consider your eye colour when choosing a hair colour. If you have blue or green eyes, try to choose a hair colour that complements your eye colour such as blonde or light brown. If you have brown eyes, try to choose a hair colour that contrasts with your eye colour such as dark brown or black.

Lastly, you should consider your lifestyle when choosing a hair colour. If you lead an active lifestyle, you might want to choose a low maintenance hair colour such as highlights or balayage. If you have a more relaxed lifestyle, you might want to choose a more dramatic hair colour such as red or purple.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Step 1: Determine your skin tone – Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones.

Step 2: Determine your eye colour – Look at your eye colour in natural light. If you have blue or green eyes, you have cool tones. If you have brown eyes, you have warm tones.

Step 3: Consider your lifestyle – Think about how much time you can commit to maintaining your hair colour. If you have a busy lifestyle, choose a low maintenance hair colour such as highlights or balayage. If you have more time, consider a more dramatic hair colour.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Colour

Once you've found the perfect hair colour, it's essential to maintain it. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair colour:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair colour from fading.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners as they can damage your hair colour.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.

FAQs

Can I dye my hair if I have grey hair?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have grey hair. However, it's essential to choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone and features. Consider choosing a hair colour that is close to your natural hair colour to ensure a more natural look.

Will a dramatic hair colour make me look older?

No, a dramatic hair colour won't necessarily make you look older. However, it's important to choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone and features.

How often should I colour my hair?

It depends on the hair colour and the rate at which your hair grows. Generally, you should colour your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hair colour.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the health of your hair and your hair colour. Here are some tips for proper hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.

Choosing the right hair colour for 50-year-olds can be a daunting task. However, by considering your skin tone, eye colour, and lifestyle, you can find the perfect hair colour that complements your natural beauty. Remember to maintain your hair colour with proper hair care and regular salon visits to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.
