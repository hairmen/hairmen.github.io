Cute Easy Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair For School

Written by Petter Nov 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old ponytail or messy bun to school every day? Do you want to switch up your hairstyle but don't have a lot of time in the morning? Look no further! We've compiled a list of cute and easy hairstyles for medium length hair that are perfect for school.

Table of Contents:

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to:

Before we dive into the hairstyles, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Invest in some good hair accessories like bobby pins, hair ties, and headbands.
  • Make sure to brush or comb your hair before styling to prevent tangles.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you plan on using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners.
  • Experiment with different hair products like texturizing sprays or dry shampoos to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step by Step:

1. Half-Up Top Knot

This hairstyle is perfect for those days when you want to keep your hair out of your face but still look cute. Here's how to do it:

  1. Take the top half of your hair and gather it into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Let the rest of your hair hang loose or add some curls for extra texture.

2. Braided Crown

This hairstyle is perfect for those days when you want to feel like a queen. Here's how to do it:

  1. Part your hair down the middle and take a small section from one side.
  2. Braid the section and secure it with a clear elastic band.
  3. Repeat on the other side.
  4. Take one of the braids and wrap it over the top of your head, securing it with bobby pins.
  5. Repeat with the other braid, tucking the end under the first braid and securing it with bobby pins.
  6. Let the rest of your hair hang loose or add some curls for extra texture.

3. Messy Bun

This hairstyle is perfect for those days when you hit snooze one too many times. Here's how to do it:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out some pieces of hair around your face to create a messy, effortless look.

Tips:

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve these cute and easy hairstyles:

  • Experiment with different types of braids like fishtail braids or Dutch braids.
  • Try adding some accessories like a scarf or a hair clip to elevate your look.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with different textures like beachy waves or sleek and straight hair.

FAQ:

1. How can I make my hair look less greasy?

If you're short on time and don't have time to wash your hair, try using dry shampoo to soak up any excess oil. You can also try wearing your hair in a bun or braids to hide any greasiness.

2. How can I make my hair look fuller?

Try using a texturizing spray on your roots to add volume. You can also try backcombing or teasing your hair to create some extra lift.

Hair Care:

Remember to take care of your hair by following these tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly and use a conditioner to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often and always use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Trim your hair every few months to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair.

With these cute and easy hairstyles for medium length hair, you'll be the envy of the school hallways. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one for you!
