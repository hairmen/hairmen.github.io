How To Get A Pixie Cut At Home Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and want to try out a new look? A pixie cut might just be what you need! It's a bold and edgy haircut that can transform your entire appearance. But, going to a salon can be expensive and time-consuming. So, why not try getting a pixie cut at home? In this article, we will guide you on how to get a pixie cut at home with our step-by-step guide.

How to Get a Pixie Cut at Home

Getting a pixie cut at home might seem daunting, but it's easier than you think. Follow these steps to get the look:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of scissors, hair clippers, hair ties, and a comb. You may also want to have a mirror and a towel nearby.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: two on the side and two on the back. Tie each part with a hair tie to keep them separate.

Step 3: Start with the Back

Start with the back of your head. Take one of the sections and use the hair clippers to trim the hair to your desired length. Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter. Repeat the process for the other section.

Step 4: Move to the Sides

Unclip one of the side sections and use the scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter. Repeat the process for the other side.

Step 5: Trim the Bangs

If you want bangs, trim them to your desired length. Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

After you have cut all the sections, check for evenness. Use the mirror to see the back and sides of your head. If you notice any unevenness, trim the hair until it's even.

Step 7: Style Your Pixie Cut

Once you're happy with the length and evenness of your hair, style your pixie cut. You can use a hair gel or wax to create texture and volume. Play around with different styles until you find one that suits you.

Tips for Getting a Pixie Cut at Home

Getting a pixie cut at home can be a little intimidating, but these tips can help:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter.
  • Use a mirror to check for evenness.
  • Take your time and don't rush.
  • Watch tutorials or ask a friend for help.

FAQs

Here are some commonly asked questions about getting a pixie cut at home:

Is it easy to cut your own hair?

Cutting your own hair can be challenging, but with practice, it can get easier.

Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

It's best to use hair-cutting scissors as they are sharper and designed for cutting hair.

How do I know if a pixie cut will suit me?

You can use virtual makeover tools or consult with a hairstylist to see if a pixie cut will suit you.

Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

Once you've got your pixie cut, here are some hair care tips to keep it looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your pixie cut.

Now that you know how to get a pixie cut at home, it's time to grab your tools and get started! Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun with your new look.
