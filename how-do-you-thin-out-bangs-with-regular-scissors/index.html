How To Thin Out Bangs With Regular Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your thick and heavy bangs? Thinning them out can give you a more manageable and stylish look. But what if you don't have thinning scissors? Don't worry, you can still thin out your bangs with regular scissors. In this article, we will show you how to do it yourself at home.

How to Thin Out Bangs with Regular Scissors

Before we start, make sure to have these tools ready:

  • Regular scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle with water

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.

Step 2: Section Your Bangs

Use a comb to section your bangs. Pin back the rest of your hair with hair clips.

Step 3: Wet Your Bangs

Spray your bangs with water to make them damp. This will help you to see the thickness of your hair and prevent overcutting.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Hold the scissors vertically and cut into your bangs. This will create a soft and natural look. Make sure to cut small sections at a time and blend the cut hair with the uncut hair.

Step 5: Check Your Bangs

Check your bangs in the mirror to see if they are even and have the desired thickness. If needed, repeat the previous steps until you achieve the desired look.

Step 6: Style Your Bangs

Style your bangs as desired. You can use a hair straightener, curling iron, or blow dryer to add volume and texture.

Tips for Thinning Out Your Bangs

Here are some tips to make the process easier:

  • Start by cutting less hair than you think you need. You can always cut more later.
  • Use a comb to hold your bangs in place while cutting them.
  • Don't cut your bangs straight across. Instead, cut them at an angle for a more natural look.
  • Check your progress in the mirror frequently.
  • Be patient and take your time.

FAQ

Can I thin out my bangs with regular scissors?

Yes, you can. Just make sure to cut small sections at a time and blend the cut hair with the uncut hair.

How much should I thin out my bangs?

It depends on your hair type and the desired look. Start by cutting less hair than you think you need and check your progress frequently.

Can I thin out my bangs without cutting them?

Yes, you can use thinning shears or a razor comb to thin out your bangs without cutting them.

Hair Care

After thinning out your bangs, make sure to take care of them properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup and oiliness.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired thickness and length.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and prevent frizz.

Thinning out your bangs can give you a fresh and new look. With these tips and steps, you can do it yourself at home with regular scissors. Have fun experimenting and enjoy your new hairstyle!
