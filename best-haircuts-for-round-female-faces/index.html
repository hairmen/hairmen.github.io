Best Haircuts For Round Female Faces The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Jul 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut that flatters your round face shape? Look no further! In this guide, we’ll share the best haircuts for round female faces, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Haircut for Round Female Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Round Female Faces

Description

Round faces have soft, curved edges and a wide forehead and cheekbones. The goal of a good haircut for round female faces is to create the illusion of length and angularity. You want to avoid adding volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider.

The right haircut can help balance out your features and enhance your natural beauty. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there are plenty of options to choose from.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Round Female Faces

When choosing a haircut for round female faces, consider the following:

  • Length: Longer hair can elongate your face, while shorter hair can widen it. Opt for a length that falls below your chin but above your shoulders.
  • Layers: Layers can add dimension and volume to your hair, but avoid adding too much volume around the sides of your face.
  • Bangs: Side-swept bangs can help balance out your features, while blunt bangs can make your face appear more round.
  • Texture: Adding waves or curls can create the illusion of length and angularity.

Best Haircuts for Round Female Faces

Here are some of the best haircuts for round female faces:

  • Long layers: Layers that start below your chin and blend into your hair can add length to your face.
  • Angled bob: A bob that is longer in the front and shorter in the back can create the illusion of length and angularity.
  • Side-swept bangs: Bangs that are swept to the side can help balance out your features.
  • Shoulder-length lob: A lob that falls just above your shoulders can elongate your face.
  • Long and straight: Straight hair that falls below your shoulders can create a sleek and elongated look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you’ve chosen the perfect haircut, here’s how to style it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, pulling the hair away from your face to create volume at the crown.
  4. Use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or straighten your hair, depending on your desired look.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or serum to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid pulling or tugging.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQs

What hairstyles should round faces avoid?

Round faces should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, such as blunt bob haircuts or curly hair. Instead, opt for hairstyles that elongate the face, such as layered haircuts or side-swept bangs.

How do I know if I have a round face?

A round face has soft, curved edges and a wide forehead and cheekbones. Measure your face from the top of your forehead to the bottom of your chin, and from cheekbone to cheekbone. If these measurements are roughly equal, you have a round face.

Can I still have long hair with a round face?

Absolutely! Long hair can elongate your face, but be sure to add layers to create dimension and avoid adding volume to the sides of your face.

Hair Care Tips for Round Female Faces

Here are some hair care tips for round female faces:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

With these tips and haircuts for round female faces, you’ll be able to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there’s a style for everyone!
