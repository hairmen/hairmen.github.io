How To Put Clip In Extensions In Fine Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Clip in extensions are a great way to add length and volume to your fine hair without committing to a permanent change. However, figuring out how to put clip in extensions in fine hair can be tricky. In this article, we will discuss the steps you need to take to successfully put clip in extensions in fine hair. We will also provide tips on how to care for your hair and extensions.

Description

Clip in extensions are a popular choice for those who want to add length and volume to their hair. They are easy to use and can be removed whenever you like. However, if you have fine hair, it can be challenging to get the extensions to blend in and stay in place. In this article, we will show you how to put clip in extensions in fine hair so you can achieve the look you want.

How to Put Clip in Extensions in Fine Hair

Step 1: Choose the Right Extensions

Before you start, make sure you have the right clip in extensions for your hair. Look for extensions that are specifically designed for fine hair. These extensions will be lighter and thinner than those designed for thicker hair. They will also have smaller clips that won't weigh down your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before you start. This will help the extensions blend in better with your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add extra volume to your fine hair. Don't use any heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it harder to clip in the extensions.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into two parts, top and bottom. Use a hair clip to hold the top section out of the way while you work on the bottom section. This will make it easier to clip in the extensions.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Tease the roots of your hair where you want to clip in the extensions. This will give the clips something to grip onto and help the extensions stay in place. Use a teasing brush or a comb to tease your hair.

Step 5: Clip in the Extensions

Starting with the bottom section, clip in the extensions one at a time. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward. Clip each extension close to the root of your hair and make sure it feels secure. Repeat this process with the top section of your hair.

Step 6: Blend the Extensions

Once all the extensions are in place, use a comb or brush to blend them into your hair. Make sure there are no visible clips or gaps between the extensions and your hair.

Tips

  • Choose extensions that are a similar color to your hair for a more natural look.
  • Don't use too many extensions as they can weigh down your hair and cause damage.
  • Avoid using any heat styling tools on the extensions as they can damage the hair.

FAQ

Can I wear clip in extensions in fine hair every day?

It's not recommended to wear clip in extensions in fine hair every day as they can cause damage to your hair. Only wear them when you need to.

How long can I wear clip in extensions in fine hair?

You can wear clip in extensions in fine hair for up to 8 hours a day. Anything longer than that can cause damage to your hair.

How do I remove clip in extensions from fine hair?

To remove clip in extensions from fine hair, gently unclip each extension and slide it out of your hair. Be careful not to pull on your hair as this can cause damage.

Hair Care

To keep your hair and extensions looking their best, it's important to take care of them. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh down your hair. Avoid using any heat styling tools on the extensions as this can cause damage. When you're not wearing the extensions, store them in a cool, dry place to prevent tangling and damage.
