How to Style Messy Wavy Hair Guys

The first step in styling your messy wavy hair is to understand your hair type. Wavy hair falls between straight and curly hair and can be thick or thin. To style your messy wavy hair, you will need to use the right products and techniques.

One of the best ways to style messy wavy hair is to go for a natural, tousled look. This style is low-maintenance and looks great on guys. You can also try a textured crop, which is a modern take on the classic Caesar cut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to style your messy wavy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a small amount of sea salt spray to add texture.
  3. Scrunch your hair with your fingers to create waves and texture.
  4. Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment on low heat to enhance your natural waves.
  5. Apply a small amount of styling cream or pomade to your hair to add definition and hold.
  6. Mess up your hair with your fingers to create a natural, tousled look.

Tips for Styling Messy Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your messy wavy hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when wet.
  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Don't overuse products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What products should I use for styling my messy wavy hair?

You can use sea salt spray, styling cream, pomade, or hair wax to style your messy wavy hair.

How often should I wash my messy wavy hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

What hairstyles are best for messy wavy hair?

Natural, tousled hairstyles and textured crops are great options for guys with messy wavy hair.

Hair Care for Messy Wavy Hair

To keep your messy wavy hair looking its best, it's important to follow a hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can style your messy wavy hair with ease and confidence. Whether you prefer a natural, tousled look or a textured crop, there's a perfect hairstyle for every guy with messy wavy hair.
