How Many Ml Is A Number 3 Haircut Everything You Need To Know

Written by Robby Nov 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're new to the world of haircuts, you might be wondering what a number 3 haircut means. Is it a short or long haircut? How many ml of hair does it take to achieve a number 3 haircut? In this article, we'll answer all your questions and give you a detailed guide on how to achieve a number 3 haircut.

Description:

A number 3 haircut is a popular men's haircut that is achieved by cutting the hair to 3/8 of an inch or 9.5mm. It is considered a medium-length haircut, and it is perfect for those who want a clean, neat, and presentable look without going too short or too long.

How to Achieve a Number 3 Haircut:

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools for the job. You will need a pair of hair clippers, a number 3 clipper guard, a comb, and a mirror. Here's how to achieve a number 3 haircut:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair thoroughly with shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt or oil from your hair, making it easier to cut.

Step 2: Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to dry your hair, making sure it is completely dry before you start cutting. Wet hair can be harder to cut, and it can also lead to uneven results.

Step 3: Attach the Number 3 Clipper Guard

Attach the number 3 clipper guard to your clippers. This guard will ensure that your hair is cut to the right length.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair with the clippers, starting at the sides and working your way towards the top. Use the comb to guide the clippers and ensure that the hair is cut evenly.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

Use the mirror to check your progress and make sure that the hair is cut evenly all around.

Step 6: Trim the Top

Use scissors to trim the top of your hair, making sure that it is even with the sides.

Step 7: Clean Up

Use a brush or a towel to remove any loose hair from your head and neck.

Tips:

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect number 3 haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down if you want a shorter haircut.
  • Use a smaller clipper guard to fade the sides and back for a more polished look.
  • Ask for help if you're not comfortable cutting your own hair.
  • Practice makes perfect. Don't worry if you don't get it right the first time.

FAQ:

How many ml of hair does it take to achieve a number 3 haircut?

A number 3 haircut is achieved by cutting the hair to 3/8 of an inch or 9.5mm. This is equivalent to approximately 3.3ml of hair.

Can I achieve a number 3 haircut with scissors?

You can achieve a number 3 haircut with scissors, but it might be more difficult to get an even result. Clippers are preferred for achieving a number 3 haircut.

How often should I get a number 3 haircut?

This depends on your hair growth rate and personal preference. Most people get a number 3 haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care:

After you've achieved your number 3 haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and texture. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the hair follicles.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a hairbrush with soft bristles to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get a regular trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your number 3 haircut.

Now that you know how to achieve a number 3 haircut and how to take care of your hair, you're ready to rock this classic men's haircut. Remember to be patient and take your time, and you'll achieve the perfect number 3 haircut in no time.
