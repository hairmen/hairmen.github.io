How To Make Your Wig Look Fuller Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Sep 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing a wig that looks flat and lifeless? Do you want to know how to make your wig look fuller and more voluminous? Look no further! In this article, we will share some tips and tricks that will help you achieve a fuller look with your wig.

Description

Wearing a wig can be a great way to change up your look, but sometimes wigs can look flat and unnatural. If you want to achieve a fuller, more natural-looking wig, there are a few things you can do to enhance its volume and body.

How to Make Your Wig Look Fuller: Step by Step

Step 1: Choose the Right Wig

The first step to achieving a fuller-looking wig is to choose the right wig. Look for wigs that are made with high-quality synthetic or human hair, as these materials will provide more body and volume. You should also consider the length and texture of the wig, as longer and curlier wigs tend to look fuller and more voluminous.

Step 2: Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Another way to add volume to your wig is to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products are designed to add body and fullness to your hair, and they can work just as well on wigs. Be sure to choose a shampoo and conditioner that is safe for synthetic or human hair wigs.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Wig

Blow drying your wig can also help to add volume and body. Use a low to medium heat setting and a round brush to lift and separate the hair as you blow dry. This will help to create more volume and lift at the roots.

Step 4: Use a Wig Cap

Using a wig cap can also help to add volume to your wig. A wig cap will create a barrier between your wig and your scalp, which can help to lift the hair and create more volume. It can also help to keep your wig in place and prevent it from slipping or shifting.

Step 5: Style Your Wig

Finally, styling your wig can also help to create a fuller, more voluminous look. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add curls and waves to your wig, which will help to create more body and volume. You can also tease the hair at the roots to create more lift and volume.

Tips for Making Your Wig Look Fuller

Here are some additional tips that can help you make your wig look fuller:

  • Use a wig stand or mannequin head to store your wig when you're not wearing it. This will help to maintain its shape and prevent it from becoming flat or lifeless.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as these can weigh down the hair and make it look flat.
  • Consider getting your wig professionally styled or trimmed to enhance its body and volume.

FAQ

Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my wig?

No, you should use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for synthetic or human hair wigs. Regular shampoo and conditioner can damage the hair and reduce its body and volume.

How often should I wash my wig?

You should wash your wig every 6-8 wears, or as needed. Be sure to follow the care instructions that came with your wig, as different types of wigs may require different care.

Can I use a hair dryer to dry my wig?

Yes, you can use a hair dryer to dry your wig, but be sure to use a low to medium heat setting and a cool shot to prevent damage to the hair.

Hair Care for Your Wig

To keep your wig looking full and voluminous, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for caring for your wig:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head when you're not wearing it.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to detangle your wig, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Wash your wig in cool water with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your wig, as these can damage the hair.
  • Keep your wig away from direct sunlight and heat sources, as these can cause the hair to fade or become damaged.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your wig look fuller and more voluminous. Whether you're wearing your wig for fashion or for medical reasons, a fuller-looking wig can help you feel more confident and beautiful.
