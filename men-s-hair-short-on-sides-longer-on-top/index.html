Men s Hair Short On Sides Longer On Top The Trendy Hairstyle For 2023

Written by Petter Aug 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish hairstyle, then the "Men's Hair Short on Sides Longer on Top" is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been popular for a few years now and is still going strong in 2023. In this blog post, we will discuss everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to achieve it, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to achieve the Men's Hair Short on Sides Longer on Top
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Maintaining the Hairstyle
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Description:

The Men's Hair Short on Sides Longer on Top is a popular hairstyle that is also known as the "Undercut." This hairstyle is characterized by having short hair on the sides and longer hair on top. It gives a classic and stylish look to any man who wears it. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in many ways, including slicked back, messy, textured, or with a side part.

How to achieve the Men's Hair Short on Sides Longer on Top:

To achieve the Men's Hair Short on Sides Longer on Top, you will need to visit a hairstylist who is experienced in cutting undercut hairstyles. They will need to use clippers to cut the hair on the sides very short, leaving the hair on top longer.

Choose the Right Length:

When choosing the length of the hair on top, it's essential to consider the shape of your face and the style you want to achieve. For a more classic look, keep the hair on top longer, and for a more modern look, opt for a shorter length.

Decide on the Style:

Once you have the right length, you can decide on the style you want to achieve. You can slick the hair back with a pomade or gel or create a messy, textured look with a styling cream. You can also add a side part or a quiff for a more edgy look.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the Men's Hair Short on Sides Longer on Top:

  1. Visit a hairstylist who is experienced in cutting undercut hairstyles.
  2. Choose the right length for the hair on top.
  3. Decide on the style you want to achieve.
  4. Use a clipper to cut the hair on the sides very short.
  5. Leave the hair on top longer according to the length you have chosen.
  6. Style the hair on top using a pomade, gel, or styling cream.

Tips for Maintaining the Hairstyle:

Here are some tips for maintaining the Men's Hair Short on Sides Longer on Top:

  • Visit the hairstylist every four to six weeks to maintain the length of the hair on the sides.
  • Use a hairdryer to style the hair on top to give it volume and texture.
  • Use a high-quality pomade, gel, or styling cream to keep the hair in place.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions:

1. Is the Men's Hair Short on Sides Longer on Top suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the Men's Hair Short on Sides Longer on Top is suitable for all face shapes. However, the length and style of the hair on top should be adjusted according to the shape of the face.

2. Can I style the hair on top in different ways?

Yes, the hair on top can be styled in many ways, including slicked back, messy, textured, or with a side part.

3. Do I need to use a specific product to style the hair on top?

No, you can use any pomade, gel, or styling cream that works well with your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

Hair Care for Men's Hair Short on Sides Longer on Top:

Here are some hair care tips for Men's Hair Short on Sides Longer on Top:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a suitable shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hairdryer to style the hair on top to give it volume and texture.
  • Use a high-quality pomade, gel, or styling cream to keep the hair in place.
  • Visit the hairstylist every four to six weeks to maintain the length of the hair on the sides.

Overall, Men's Hair Short on Sides Longer on Top is a trendy and stylish hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion. It is versatile and can be styled in many ways to suit your personal style. By following the tips and instructions mentioned in this blog post, you can achieve a perfect Men's Hair Short on Sides Longer on Top hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd.
