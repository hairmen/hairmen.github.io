How Did They Dance In The 70S

Written by Oliver Oct 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

The 70s was an era of disco, funk, and soul music. It was a time when people were grooving to the beat of the music, and dancing was a way of life. In this article, we will explore the different dance styles that were popular in the 70s and how you can learn them today.

Description

The 70s was a time when people were all about letting loose and having fun. Dancing was a huge part of that, and there were plenty of dance styles to choose from. From the disco dance craze to the funky moves of soul music, there was a dance for everyone.

How to Dance in the 70s

If you want to learn how to dance in the 70s, there are a few things you need to know. First of all, it's all about the rhythm. You need to be able to feel the beat of the music and move your body accordingly. It's also important to have good posture and a confident attitude.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to some of the most popular dance styles of the 70s:

Disco Dance

1. Start with your feet shoulder-width apart and your arms at your sides.

2. Move your hips side to side, keeping your feet planted on the ground.

3. Shuffle your feet back and forth, alternating between left and right.

4. Add some arm movements, such as pointing, clapping, or raising your arms above your head.

Soul Train Dance

1. Start with your feet shoulder-width apart and your arms at your sides.

2. Move your hips in a circular motion, shifting your weight from one foot to the other.

3. Add some arm movements, such as pointing, clapping, or raising your arms above your head.

4. Incorporate some funky footwork, such as the grapevine or the hustle.

Funk Dance

1. Start with your feet shoulder-width apart and your arms at your sides.

2. Move your hips side to side, keeping your feet planted on the ground.

3. Add some shoulder movements, such as shrugging or rolling your shoulders.

4. Incorporate some arm movements, such as waving or popping your elbows.

Tips for Dancing in the 70s

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your 70s dance experience:

- Wear comfortable clothing and shoes that allow you to move freely.

- Don't be afraid to let loose and have fun.

- Watch videos of 70s dances to get a better understanding of the style.

- Find a dance partner to practice with.

- Attend a dance class or workshop to learn more about the different styles.

FAQs

What were the most popular dance styles of the 70s?

The most popular dance styles of the 70s were disco, soul train, and funk.

What should I wear to a 70s dance party?

You should wear comfortable clothing and shoes that allow you to move freely. Some popular 70s fashion trends include bell-bottoms, platform shoes, and colorful prints.

Can I learn how to dance in the 70s?

Yes, anyone can learn how to dance in the 70s. It just takes some practice and a willingness to let loose and have fun.

Hair Care for Dancing in the 70s

If you're going to be dancing in the 70s style, you'll want to make sure your hair is on point. Some popular 70s hairstyles include the afro, the shag, and the feathered look. To keep your hair looking its best, be sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a styling product such as mousse or gel to keep your locks in place.

Dancing in the 70s was all about having fun and letting loose. Whether you're into disco, soul, or funk, there's a dance style for everyone. So why not put on your dancing shoes and get grooving to the beat of the music?
