Best Short Hairstyles For A Square Face

Written by Frank Sep 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of trying to find the perfect hairstyle for your square face? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best short hairstyles for a square face, how to achieve them and hair care tips to keep your hair looking fabulous. So, whether you have a pixie cut or a bob, we've got you covered!

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips

Description

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline and a broad forehead. The goal of a good hairstyle for a square face is to soften these features and create a more oval shape. Short hairstyles are perfect for square faces because they draw attention away from the jawline and showcase the eyes and cheekbones.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect short hairstyle for a square face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length and width of your face and comparing the two. If they are equal, you have a square face. Once you've established your face shape, it's time to choose a hairstyle that will flatter your features.

Step by Step

Step 1: Choose a short hairstyle that complements your face shape. This can be a pixie cut, a bob or a lob.

Step 2: Consult with your hairstylist to ensure that the hairstyle you choose will work with your hair type and texture.

Step 3: Style your hair using a curling iron or straightener to add texture and volume.

Step 4: Use a hairspray or styling product to hold your hair in place and prevent flyaways.

Tips

When choosing a short hairstyle for a square face, it's important to keep in mind that the goal is to soften your features. This can be achieved by adding layers, texture or volume to your hair.

Try to avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can accentuate the squareness of your face.

Accessorize your hair with headbands, clips or bobby pins for a fun and trendy look.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a square face?

A: Yes! Bangs can be a great way to soften a square face. Opt for side-swept bangs or wispy bangs that blend into your hair.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight with a square face?

A: Of course! Straight hair can look great on a square face, just be sure to add some volume or texture to prevent it from looking too severe.

Q: What is the best hair color for a square face?

A: The best hair color for a square face is one that complements your skin tone and eye color. Consult with your hairstylist to find a color that works for you.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and shiny is important for any hairstyle. Make sure to wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools.

Avoid over-styling or pulling your hair too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage.

With these tips and hairstyles, you'll be sure to find the perfect short hairstyle for your square face. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
