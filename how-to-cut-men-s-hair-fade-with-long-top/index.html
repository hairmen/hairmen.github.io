How To Cut Men s Hair Fade With Long Top

Written by Petter Jun 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a trendy hairstyle that is easy to maintain? Look no further than the men's hair fade with long top. This hairstyle has been popular for a few years now, and it doesn't seem to be going anywhere. In this post, we'll go over how to cut men's hair fade with long top, step by step, and give you some tips and tricks along the way. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Cut Men's Hair Fade with Long Top

The men's hair fade with long top is a hairstyle that involves gradually cutting the hair shorter from the crown of the head down to the sides and back, creating a fade effect. The top of the hair is left longer, giving the style a modern and trendy look. Here's what you need to do to cut this style:

What You'll Need

  • Clippers with different guard sizes (we recommend a #2, #3, and #4)
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Haircutting cape or towel

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the men's hair fade with long top:

Step 1: Section the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair into four parts: the top, left side, right side, and back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 2: Cut the Sides and Back

Using the clippers with a #2 guard, start at the nape of the neck and work your way up, cutting against the hair growth. Blend the sides and back into the top by gradually increasing the guard size to a #3 and then a #4 as you move up. Make sure to use the comb to keep the hair taut while cutting and to blend the different guard sizes.

Step 3: Define the Hairline

Using the clippers without a guard, define the hairline around the ears and the nape of the neck. You can also use scissors to trim any stray hairs.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Using scissors, cut the top of the hair to the desired length. The length will depend on the individual's preference, but it's recommended to leave it at least 2-3 inches long. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim any uneven ends.

Step 5: Blend the Top and Sides

Use the scissors to blend the top of the hair into the sides and back. Take small sections of hair and cut at an angle, using the comb to blend the hair together.

Step 6: Finish with Detailing

Finally, use the clippers and scissors to detail the style, trimming any stray hairs and making sure the fade is even and smooth.

Tips and Tricks

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use a mirror to check the style from different angles and make sure it's even.
  • Don't forget to blend the sideburns into the hairline.
  • Use hair products, such as pomade or wax, to style the hair and keep it in place.

FAQs

How often should I get this haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows, but we recommend getting a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Can I cut this style at home?

Yes, you can cut this style at home if you have the right tools and feel comfortable doing so. However, we recommend going to a professional if you're unsure or if you want a more polished look.

Hair Care for Men's Hair Fade with Long Top

To keep your men's hair fade with long top looking fresh, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers or straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim the ends of your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and smoothness to your hair.

That's it! Now you know how to cut men's hair fade with long top. With a little practice and the right tools, you can achieve this stylish and low-maintenance look at home. Don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
