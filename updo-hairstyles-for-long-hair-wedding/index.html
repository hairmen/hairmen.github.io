Updo Hairstyles For Long Hair Wedding

Written by Luffy Aug 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for hairstyle inspiration for your upcoming long hair wedding? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing updo hairstyles for long hair wedding, along with tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice to ensure your hair looks its best on your big day.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Create an Updo Hairstyle for Long Hair Wedding
  • Step-by-Step Instruction
  • Tips for Perfect Updo Hairstyle
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Updo Hairstyles

Description

An updo hairstyle is a perfect choice for a long hair wedding, as it keeps your hair off your face and neck while creating an elegant and sophisticated look. There are various styles of updos to choose from, including braids, buns, and twists, each with their own unique twist.

How to Create an Updo Hairstyle for Long Hair Wedding

To create an updo hairstyle for your long hair wedding, you will need some basic hair tools, including a comb, bobby pins, hair elastics, and hair spray. You can also add some hair accessories, such as hairpins or flowers, to add a touch of glamour to your hairstyle.

Step-by-Step Instruction

Follow these steps to create a beautiful updo hairstyle for your long hair wedding:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Separate a small section of hair from the crown and tease it to create volume.
  3. Gently smooth the teased section and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Take two equal sections of hair from either side of your head and tie them together into a knot.
  5. Secure the knot with bobby pins and gently loosen it to create a fuller look.
  6. Continue knotting and pinning sections of hair until you reach the nape of your neck.
  7. Twist the remaining hair into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  8. Spray your hair with a hairspray to keep it in place.
  9. Add any hair accessories you desire.
  10. Finally, check your hairstyle in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Perfect Updo Hairstyle

Here are some tips to ensure your updo hairstyle looks perfect on your long hair wedding:

  • Start with clean, dry hair to ensure your hairstyle lasts longer.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you plan to use heated styling tools.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different updo styles to find the perfect one for you.
  • Book a trial run with your hairstylist to ensure your hairstyle looks perfect on your wedding day.
  • Try hair extensions if you want to add extra length or volume to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about updo hairstyles for long hair wedding:

What kind of updo hairstyle is best for long hair wedding?

There are various styles of updos to choose from, including braids, buns, and twists, each with their own unique twist. Choose the one that best suits your face shape, dress, and wedding theme.

How do I keep my updo hairstyle in place all day?

Use bobby pins and hairspray to keep your updo hairstyle in place all day. You can also use a hairnet to keep your hairstyle in place.

Can I add hair accessories to my updo hairstyle?

Yes, you can add hair accessories, such as hairpins, flowers, or tiaras, to add a touch of glamour to your updo hairstyle.

Hair Care for Updo Hairstyles

Here are some hair care tips for updo hairstyles:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Don't wash your hair on the day of your wedding, as freshly washed hair is harder to style.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and make it harder to style.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and step-by-step instructions, you can create a beautiful updo hairstyle for your long hair wedding. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair. Happy styling!
