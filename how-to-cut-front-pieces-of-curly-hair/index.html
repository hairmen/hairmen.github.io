How To Cut Front Pieces Of Curly Hair

Written by Christine Jun 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how important it is to have a great haircut that works with your curls. One area that can be particularly tricky to cut is the front pieces of curly hair. In this post, we will go over how to cut the front pieces of curly hair and provide some tips and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Front Pieces of Curly Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Cut Front Pieces of Curly Hair

The first thing to consider when cutting the front pieces of curly hair is the length you want. If you want a more dramatic look, you can go shorter, but if you want to maintain some length, you can go longer. Once you have an idea of the length you want, you can start the cutting process.

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be harder to work with and can result in an uneven cut. It's also a good idea to detangle your hair before cutting to make sure you're cutting all of the strands evenly.

2. Section Your Hair

Next, you want to section off the front pieces of your hair. Use a comb to create a triangle-shaped section at the front of your head. Make sure the section is the same width as your forehead and goes back to the crown of your head.

3. Start Cutting

Hold the section of hair with your fingers and start cutting. If you want to go shorter, you can use a razor to create a more textured look. If you want to maintain some length, use scissors to create a blunt cut. Make sure you're cutting the hair at an angle to create a more natural look.

4. Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting, check your work in the mirror. Make sure the length is even on both sides and that the hair is framing your face in a flattering way.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to cutting the front pieces of curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Section off the front pieces of your hair
  3. Hold the section of hair with your fingers and start cutting
  4. Check your work in the mirror

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Use a razor to create a more textured look
  • Use scissors to create a blunt cut
  • Cut the hair at an angle to create a more natural look
  • Check your work in the mirror

FAQ

How often should I cut the front pieces of my curly hair?

You should aim to cut your hair every 8-12 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your curls. However, if you notice your front pieces are getting too long or out of shape, you can trim them in between haircuts.

Can I cut my curly hair myself?

While it is possible to cut your curly hair yourself, it's always best to see a professional stylist. They can help you achieve the look you want and provide advice on how to care for your curls.

Hair Care

Once you've cut the front pieces of your curly hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to keep your curls moisturized and defined. And don't forget to protect your curls from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.

Now that you know how to cut the front pieces of curly hair, you can achieve the perfect look for your curls. Remember to take care of your curls properly to keep them healthy and defined.
