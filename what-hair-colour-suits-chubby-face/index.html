What Hair Colour Suits Chubby Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Sep 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a hair colour that will suit your chubby face? You've come to the right place! In this article, we will provide you with tips, tricks, and recommendations on what hair colour would be perfect for your face shape. From blonde to brunette, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Description

Having a chubby face can make it challenging to find the right hair colour that will complement your facial features. A wrong hair colour can make your face look even rounder, while the right hair colour can enhance your facial features and make your face look slimmer.

In this article, we will discuss the best hair colours for chubby faces, how to choose the right hair colour, and hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour

Choosing the right hair colour is crucial when you have a chubby face. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hair colour:

Consider Your Skin Tone

When choosing a hair colour, it's essential to consider your skin tone. If you have a warm skin tone, go for warm hair colours like honey blonde, caramel, or copper. If you have a cool skin tone, go for cool hair colours like ash blonde, platinum, or black.

Choose a Hair Colour that Complements Your Eyes

Your eye colour also plays a significant role in choosing the right hair colour. If you have green eyes, go for warm hair colours like golden blonde or auburn. If you have blue eyes, go for cool hair colours like ash blonde or platinum.

Consider the Shape of Your Face

The shape of your face also plays a crucial role in choosing the right hair colour. If you have a round face, go for hair colours that create an illusion of length, like balayage or ombre. If you have a square face, go for hair colours that soften your features, like soft highlights or lowlights.

Step by Step Guide on Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you choose the perfect hair colour:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Colour

Consider your eye colour when choosing a hair colour. If you have green eyes, go for warm hair colours. If you have blue eyes, go for cool hair colours.

Step 3: Determine Your Face Shape

Determine your face shape and choose hair colours that complement your features.

Step 4: Consult with a Hair Colour Specialist

If you're still unsure about what hair colour suits you, consult with a hair colour specialist. They will be able to recommend the perfect hair colour for your face shape and complexion.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect hair colour:

Avoid Hair Colours that are Too Dark

Dark hair colours can make your face look even rounder. Instead, go for lighter hair colours that will brighten up your face.

Avoid Hair Colours that are Too Light

Light hair colours can wash out your complexion, making your face look dull. Instead, go for hair colours that complement your skin tone.

Choose Hair Colours that are Multi-Dimensional

Multi-dimensional hair colours like balayage and ombre can create an illusion of depth and dimension, making your face look slimmer.

FAQ

Q: What hair colours should I avoid if I have a chubby face?

A: Avoid hair colours that are too dark or too light. Also, avoid hair colours that are too bold, as they can draw attention to your face.

Q: What hair colours should I go for if I have a chubby face?

A: Go for hair colours that are multi-dimensional and complement your skin tone and eye colour.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

Use a Deep Conditioning Hair Mask Once a Week

Use a deep conditioning hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons as they can damage your hair.

Use a Heat Protectant Spray

If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep your hair healthy.

In conclusion, choosing the right hair colour for a chubby face can be a daunting task. However, by considering your skin tone, eye colour, and face shape, you can find the perfect hair colour that will enhance your features and make your face look slimmer. Remember to take care of your hair by using a deep conditioning hair mask, avoiding heat styling tools, and trimming your hair regularly.
