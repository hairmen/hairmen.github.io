How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle for your round, fat face shape is all about creating balance. You want to create the illusion of a more angular, chiseled face by adding volume and height to the top of your head, while keeping the sides and back short and tight. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Look for styles that add height and volume to the top of your head, such as pompadours, quiffs, and messy spikes.
  • Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your head, such as bowl cuts or mushroom cuts.
  • Consider getting a fade or undercut on the sides and back of your head to create contrast and make your face appear slimmer.
  • Try out different styles with a virtual hairstyle simulator to see what looks best on you.

Best Haircuts for Round Face Shape

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, let's take a look at some of the best haircuts for men with round, fat face shapes:

1. The Pompadour

The pompadour is a classic men's haircut that works well for round faces. This style features short, tapered sides and a longer, voluminous top that is swept back and styled with pomade or wax. The height at the top of the head creates the illusion of a longer, more angular face shape.

2. The Quiff

The quiff is similar to the pompadour, but with a more textured, messy look. This style features short, tapered sides and a longer, textured top that is styled with a comb or fingers. The volume at the top of the head creates the illusion of a longer, more angular face shape.

3. The Messy Spike

The messy spike is a low-maintenance style that works well for men with round faces. This style features short, textured hair that is spiked up with a matte product. The height and texture at the top of the head create the illusion of a longer, more angular face shape.

4. The Side Part

The side part is a classic men's haircut that works well for round faces. This style features short, tapered sides and a longer, side-swept top that is parted to one side. The side part creates the illusion of a longer, more angular face shape.

Styling Tips for Men with Round Face Shape

Once you've chosen the right haircut, it's important to style it correctly to flatter your face shape. Here are some styling tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a matte product, such as clay or wax, to add texture and volume to the top of your head.
  • Avoid using products that add shine or volume to the sides of your head.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go to add volume and height.
  • Use a round brush to lift the hair at the top of your head while blow drying.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Round Face Shape

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips for men with round, fat face shapes:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and well-groomed.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a round, fat face shape?

The best hairstyle for a round, fat face shape is one that adds height and volume to the top of the head, while keeping the sides and back short and tight. Styles such as the pompadour, quiff, messy spike, and side part work well for this face shape.

Can men with round, fat face shapes have long hair?

While shorter hairstyles tend to work best for men with round, fat face shapes, it is possible to have long hair. The key is to keep the hair styled in a way that adds height and volume to the top of the head, while keeping the sides and back shorter and more tapered.

What products should I use to style my hair?

For men with round, fat face shapes, it's best to use matte products, such as clay or wax, to add texture and volume to the hair at the top of the head. Avoid using products that add shine or volume to the sides of the head.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle to flatter your round, fat face shape. Remember to choose a style that adds height and volume to the top of your head, while keeping the sides and back short and tight. With the right haircut and styling products, you can look and feel your best every day.
