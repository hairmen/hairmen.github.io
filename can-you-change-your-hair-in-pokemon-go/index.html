Description

If you're a Pokemon Go player, you've probably wondered if you can change your hair in the game. The answer is yes, you can. However, it's not as simple as just visiting a hair salon in the game. You need to complete certain tasks and meet certain requirements before you can change your hair.

How to Change Your Hair in Pokemon Go

To change your hair in Pokemon Go, you need to first reach level 10. Once you reach that level, you will unlock the ability to change your avatar's appearance. This includes changing your hair, clothes, and accessories.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to change your hair in Pokemon Go:

  1. Reach level 10
  2. Tap on your avatar in the bottom left corner of the screen
  3. Select the "Style" option
  4. Select the "Hair" option
  5. Choose the hairstyle you want
  6. Confirm your selection

It's that simple. Once you confirm your selection, your avatar's hair will change to the new style you selected.

Tips for Changing Your Hair in Pokemon Go

Here are some tips to keep in mind when changing your hair in Pokemon Go:

  • Make sure you have reached level 10 before attempting to change your hair
  • Take your time to choose the hairstyle you want
  • Don't worry if you don't like the new hairstyle - you can always change it again later

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing your hair in Pokemon Go:

Can you change your hair color?

No, you cannot change your hair color in Pokemon Go. You can only change your hairstyle.

Can you change your hair more than once?

Yes, you can change your hair as many times as you want. However, you need to reach level 10 each time you want to change it.

Does changing your hair affect your gameplay?

No, changing your hair does not affect your gameplay in any way. It's purely cosmetic.

Hair Care in Pokemon Go

While you can change your hair in Pokemon Go, there's no need to worry about hair care. Your avatar's hair will always look perfect, no matter what hairstyle you choose.

So go ahead and change your hair to match your mood or style. Have fun!
