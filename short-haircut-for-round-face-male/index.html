Short Haircut For Round Face Male The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Jul 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. However, with the right style, you can enhance your features and look your best. In this article, we will discuss how to choose the best short haircut for round face male, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Description

A round face is characterized by the width and length being nearly the same, with soft angles and a rounded jawline. The goal of a short haircut for a round face male is to create the illusion of length and angles. This can be achieved by creating volume on top and keeping the sides short.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Round Face Male

When choosing a short haircut for a round face male, it's important to consider the following:

Face Shape

Consider your face shape when choosing a short haircut. A round face needs length and angles, so avoid styles that add width to the sides of your face. Opt for styles that add volume to the top and keep the sides short.

Hair Type

Consider your hair type when choosing a short haircut. If you have thick hair, you can opt for a layered cut to add texture and volume. If you have fine hair, a shorter, more structured cut will work better.

Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when choosing a short haircut. If you're always on the go, a low-maintenance style will work best. If you have more time to spend on your hair, you can opt for a more intricate style.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a short haircut for round face male, here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect style:

Step 1: Choose Your Style

Choose a style that suits your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. You can consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect cut for you.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.

Step 3: Towel Dry

Towel dry your hair and remove excess water.

Step 4: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product that's suitable for your hair type. For thick hair, use a volumizing product. For fine hair, use a texturizing product.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a blow dryer and a brush. Create volume on top and keep the sides short. Use a comb to create a parting if necessary.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your style with a hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short haircut for round face male:

  • Visit a hairstylist regularly to maintain your style.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a hairspray to keep your style in place.
  • Consider getting a beard or mustache to add angles to your face.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for a round face male?

The best short haircut for a round face male is one that adds length and angles to your face. A cut with volume on top and short sides is a great option.

Can I style my short haircut for round face male differently?

Yes, you can style your short haircut for round face male differently depending on your preference. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best style for you.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain your style.

With the right short haircut for round face male and proper hair care, you can enhance your features and look your best. Use these tips and tricks to achieve the perfect style.
