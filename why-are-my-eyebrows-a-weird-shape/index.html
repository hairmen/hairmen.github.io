Why Are My Eyebrows A Weird Shape

Written by Eveline Nov 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

In today's world, eyebrows are a big deal. Everyone wants perfect, symmetrical eyebrows that frame their face beautifully. However, sometimes, our eyebrows don't cooperate, and we end up with a weird shape. If you're wondering why your eyebrows are a weird shape, keep reading.

Description

Before diving into the reasons why your eyebrows might be a weird shape, it's essential to understand what a "normal" eyebrow should look like. Eyebrows should be symmetrical, with the highest point of the arch above the outer edge of your iris. The tail of the eyebrow should taper off and end at a point that lines up diagonally with the outer corner of your eye.

How To: Determine Your Eyebrow Shape

The first step in figuring out why your eyebrows are a weird shape is to determine your natural eyebrow shape. Here's how:

  1. Take a close-up photo of your face, making sure to capture your eyebrows.
  2. Print out the photo and draw a line from the inner corner of your eye to the outer corner, passing through the highest point of your eyebrow arch.
  3. Compare the line you drew to the shape of your eyebrows. Do they match up?

Step By Step: Fixing Your Eyebrow Shape

If you've determined that your eyebrows are indeed a weird shape, here's how to fix them:

  1. Invest in a good pair of tweezers.
  2. Pluck any stray hairs that fall outside of your natural eyebrow shape.
  3. Use a brow pencil or powder to fill in any sparse areas.
  4. If needed, use a brow gel to tame unruly hairs.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when fixing your eyebrow shape:

  • Don't overpluck! It's easy to get carried away, but remember that eyebrows are sisters, not twins.
  • When filling in your eyebrows, use short, light strokes to mimic natural hairs.
  • Choose a brow pencil or powder that matches your natural hair color.
  • Don't forget to blend! You don't want harsh lines or an unnatural look.

FAQ

Q: Can I fix my weird eyebrow shape at home?

A: Yes! With a good pair of tweezers and some patience, you can fix your eyebrow shape at home.

Q: Should I go to a professional for help fixing my eyebrows?

A: It's up to you! If you're unsure about what shape would best suit your face or if you're nervous about plucking, it might be worth it to go to a professional.

Q: How often should I pluck my eyebrows?

A: It depends on how quickly your eyebrows grow back. Some people can go a few weeks between plucking, while others need to do it every few days.

Hair Care: Keeping Your Eyebrows Healthy

Now that you know how to fix your eyebrow shape, it's important to keep them healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid overplucking, as this can damage the hair follicles.
  • Don't use harsh chemicals or dyes on your eyebrows.
  • Apply a nourishing serum or oil to your eyebrows to promote healthy growth.
  • Avoid rubbing or touching your eyebrows too much.

Conclusion

If you're wondering why your eyebrows are a weird shape, don't worry! With a little bit of plucking and filling in, you can have perfect eyebrows in no time. Just remember to take care of them and avoid overplucking.
