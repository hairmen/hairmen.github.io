What Hair Color Suits Me Best Quiz Find Your Perfect Shade

Written by Petter Jul 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hair color and looking to switch things up? Or maybe you've never dyed your hair before and want to see what options would suit you best? Whatever your reason may be, taking a "what hair color suits me best quiz" can be a helpful tool in determining your perfect shade. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of taking the quiz, as well as provide some tips and hair care advice for maintaining your new color.

How to Take the Quiz

There are a variety of "what hair color suits me best quiz" options available online, but we recommend using one from a reputable source. A few popular options include:

  • L'Oreal Paris Hair Color Quiz
  • Salon Iris Hair Color Quiz
  • Good Housekeeping Hair Color Quiz

Once you've selected a quiz, simply answer the questions honestly and to the best of your ability. These questions may include things like:

  • What is your natural hair color?
  • What is your skin tone?
  • What is your eye color?
  • What is your current hair texture?
  • What is your lifestyle like?

Based on your answers, the quiz will then provide you with a recommended hair color shade. Some quizzes may even provide hair care tips specific to that shade.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a more detailed breakdown of how to take a "what hair color suits me best quiz" step by step:

  1. Choose a reputable quiz from a trusted source.
  2. Read the instructions carefully and make sure you understand the questions.
  3. Answer each question honestly and to the best of your ability.
  4. Submit your answers and wait for your results.
  5. Read the recommended hair color shade and any accompanying hair care tips.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Color

Congratulations on finding your perfect shade! Now it's time to make sure you maintain it. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip the color.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons, straighteners, and blow dryers too often, as this can also cause color to fade.
  • Consider using a UV protectant spray if you'll be spending a lot of time in the sun, as UV rays can also cause color to fade.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about taking a "what hair color suits me best quiz":

How accurate are these quizzes?

While no quiz can be 100% accurate, these quizzes are typically designed by professionals in the beauty industry and can be a helpful starting point in determining your perfect shade.

Do I have to dye my hair the recommended shade?

No, you don't have to dye your hair the recommended shade. However, it can be a helpful starting point in determining what shades will look best on you.

Can these quizzes be used for all hair types and textures?

Yes, most quizzes are designed for all hair types and textures.

Hair Care

Regardless of what hair color you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are a few general hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Now that you know how to take a "what hair color suits me best quiz," it's time to find your perfect shade! Whether you go for a subtle change or a bold new look, make sure to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
