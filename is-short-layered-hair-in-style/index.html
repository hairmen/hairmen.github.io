Is Short Layered Hair In Style

Written by Aprilia Sep 02, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're considering a hair makeover, you may be wondering if short layered hair is still in style. The answer is yes! Short layered hair is a timeless trend that has been popular for decades and continues to be in style in 2023. In this article, we'll explore how to achieve the perfect short layered hairstyle, share some tips on how to maintain it, and answer some frequently asked questions about this style.

Description

Short layered hair is a haircut that involves cutting the hair into multiple layers of different lengths, creating a textured and voluminous look. This style can be achieved on any hair type, from straight to curly, and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. Short layered hair can be cut into a variety of styles, from a blunt bob to a shaggy pixie cut, making it a versatile and trendy option.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve the perfect short layered haircut, it's important to consult with a professional hairstylist who can help you choose the right style for your face shape and hair type. Here are some general steps to follow to achieve a short layered haircut:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier for your hairstylist to cut and style your hair.

Step 2: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist on the type of short layered haircut you want. They can help you choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Your hairstylist will start by cutting your hair into a blunt bob or pixie cut. They will then add layers to create texture and volume.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, your hairstylist will style it to create the perfect short layered look. This may involve using a curling iron, straightener, or other styling tools.

Tips for Maintaining Short Layered Hair

Short layered hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your short layered haircut:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Use hair products that are designed for short hair, such as texturizing sprays and mousses. These products can help add volume and texture to your hair.

Tip 2: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Short layered hair requires regular trims to maintain its shape and style. Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using styling tools. You can also wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the sun and wind.

FAQ

Q: Is short layered hair suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, short layered hair can be customized to suit any face shape. Your hairstylist can help you choose a style that complements your features.

Q: How do I know if short layered hair will suit me?

A: Consult with your hairstylist to determine if short layered hair is the right style for you. They can help you choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type.

Q: Can I style short layered hair in different ways?

A: Yes, short layered hair can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and straight to tousled and textured. Experiment with different styling products and tools to find the look that works best for you.

Hair Care

Short layered hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your short layered haircut:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.

Tip 2: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Apply the conditioner to damp hair and style as usual.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using styling tools. Avoid using high heat settings on your styling tools, and limit your use of heat styling tools as much as possible.

Tip 4: Get Regular Trims

Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain your short layered haircut. This will help keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.

In conclusion, short layered hair is still in style in 2023 and is a trendy and versatile option for anyone looking for a fresh new look. Follow these tips to achieve the perfect short layered hairstyle and maintain it for weeks to come.
