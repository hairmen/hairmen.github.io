How To Cut Men s Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As a hairstylist, cutting men's curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right technique and tools, it can be done with ease. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps on how to cut men's curly hair in a relaxed, easy-to-understand language. Whether you're a professional hairstylist or someone who wants to cut their curly hair at home, this guide is for you!

How to Cut Men's Curly Hair

Before we start, it's important to note that curly hair is different from straight hair. It requires a different approach and tools, so it's essential to have the right equipment before you start cutting. Here are the things you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have the necessary tools let's begin the cutting process. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Wash and Detangle Hair

Before you start cutting, ensure that the hair is clean and free of knots. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent the hair from drying out. Detangle the hair using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.

Step 2: Section Hair

Section the hair into four parts using hair clips. This will make it easier to cut the hair evenly.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Start with the sides and back of the hair. Use clippers to trim the hair to the desired length. Use a comb to guide the clippers to ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Use scissors to cut the top of the hair. Take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle to create layers. This will add volume and texture to the hair.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Blend the hair by using scissors to trim the hair that connects the top and sides. This will create a seamless transition between the two sections of the hair.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Check the hair for evenness by running your fingers through it. Ensure that there are no uneven spots or areas that need to be trimmed.

Step 7: Style the Hair

Style the hair using a styling product that suits the hair type. Curly hair tends to be dry, so use a product that adds moisture and hold to the hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut men's curly hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends and damage to the hair.
  • Take your time and cut the hair in small sections to ensure an even cut.
  • Use a spray bottle filled with water to dampen the hair before cutting. This will make it easier to cut the hair and prevent frizz.
  • Don't cut curly hair when it's dry. This can cause the hair to shrink, resulting in an uneven cut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's curly hair:

1. How often should I cut men's curly hair?

It depends on the individual's hair growth rate, but it's recommended to get a haircut every four to six weeks to maintain the shape and style.

2. Can I cut men's curly hair at home?

Yes, you can cut men's curly hair at home, but it's essential to have the right tools and technique. If you're not confident, it's best to seek the help of a professional hairstylist.

3. What is the best product for styling men's curly hair?

The best product for styling men's curly hair is one that adds moisture and hold to the hair. Look for products that contain natural ingredients like argan oil, shea butter, and jojoba oil.

Hair Care

Caring for curly hair is essential to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some hair care tips for men with curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent the hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing the hair as it can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle the hair gently.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like blow dryers and flat irons as they can cause damage to the hair.
  • Protect the hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protective spray.

Cutting men's curly hair requires a different approach and technique, but with practice, it can be done with ease. Follow the steps and tips in this guide to achieve a great haircut. Don't forget to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance.
