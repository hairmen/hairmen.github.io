What Is The Best Way To Cut Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Robby Oct 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to get your thick wavy hair under control? Have you been looking for the perfect haircut to manage your hair? Look no further than this guide on the best way to cut thick wavy hair! In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about cutting thick wavy hair, including how to do it, step by step instructions, tips, frequently asked questions and hair care advice.

Description

Thick wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it looks beautiful when it's styled perfectly, it can be difficult to manage and style on a daily basis. That's why it's essential to find the right haircut that suits your hair type and texture. The right haircut can make all the difference in how easy it is to style and maintain your hair.

How to Cut Thick Wavy Hair

The best way to cut thick wavy hair is to start with a consultation with your stylist. Discuss what you want to achieve with your haircut, including the length, layers, and texture. Your stylist will also take into consideration your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle to recommend the best haircut for you.

Once you've decided on the style, your stylist will begin by washing and conditioning your hair. They will then section your hair and start cutting. It's important to remember that cutting thick wavy hair requires precision and patience. Your stylist will need to cut the hair in small sections to ensure that the layers are even and the texture is consistent.

Step by Step Guide to Cutting Thick Wavy Hair

1. Start with a consultation with your stylist to discuss what you want to achieve with your haircut.

2. Wash and condition your hair to prepare it for cutting.

3. Section your hair into manageable sections.

4. Begin cutting the hair in small sections, making sure to cut evenly and consistently.

5. Use a razor or scissors to create texture and movement in the hair.

6. Finish the haircut by blow-drying and styling the hair to your desired look.

Tips for Cutting Thick Wavy Hair

1. Use a sharp pair of scissors to ensure a clean cut.

2. Cut the hair when it's dry to see how it will look when styled.

3. Use a razor to create texture and movement in the hair.

4. Cut the hair in small sections to ensure even layers and texture.

5. Don't be afraid to add layers to your hair to reduce bulk and create movement.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for thick wavy hair?

The best haircut for thick wavy hair is one that suits your face shape and hair texture. A layered haircut with texture and movement is ideal for thick wavy hair.

How often should I cut my thick wavy hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

To keep your thick wavy hair looking its best, it's important to care for it properly. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and reduce frizz. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid brushing it when it's wet, as this can cause breakage. Finally, get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Now that you know the best way to cut thick wavy hair, you can confidently go to your stylist and get the perfect haircut for your hair type and texture. Remember to communicate with your stylist and follow the tips and advice in this guide to keep your hair looking its best.
