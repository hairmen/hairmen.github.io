How To Get Rid Of Dandruff And Hair Fall In Hindi

Written by Eveline Aug 06, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of dealing with dandruff and hair fall? Do you want to know how to get rid of them in Hindi? Well, you're in luck. In this article, we will discuss some effective tips and tricks to help you achieve a healthy scalp and hair.

Step by Step Guide

To get rid of dandruff and hair fall, you need to follow a proper hair care routine. Here's a step by step guide to help you achieve a healthy scalp and hair:

Step 1: Use a Mild Shampoo

Use a mild shampoo to wash your hair. Avoid using harsh chemicals or strong shampoos as they can damage your hair and scalp. Choose a shampoo that suits your hair type and is free from harmful chemicals.

Step 2: Massage Your Scalp

Massage your scalp gently while washing your hair. This will help improve blood circulation and stimulate hair growth. Use your fingertips to massage your scalp in circular motions.

Step 3: Rinse Your Hair Properly

Rinse your hair properly to remove all the shampoo and conditioner. Use lukewarm water to wash your hair as hot water can damage your hair and scalp.

Step 4: Apply Hair Oil

Apply hair oil to your scalp and hair. This will help nourish your hair and prevent dandruff and hair fall. Choose an oil that suits your hair type and massage it gently into your scalp.

Step 5: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Avoid using too many hair styling products as they can damage your hair and scalp.

Effective Tips

Here are some effective tips to help you get rid of dandruff and hair fall:

Tip 1: Use a Dandruff Shampoo

Use a dandruff shampoo to treat dandruff. Look for a shampoo that contains ingredients like pyrithione zinc or ketoconazole. Use it twice a week to get rid of dandruff.

Tip 2: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals. Include foods like eggs, green leafy vegetables, and nuts in your diet to promote hair growth.

Tip 3: Use Natural Remedies

Try using natural remedies like coconut oil, aloe vera, and onion juice to treat dandruff and hair fall. These remedies are safe and effective.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What causes dandruff?

A. Dandruff is caused by a yeast-like fungus called Malassezia. It can also be caused by a dry scalp or an allergic reaction to hair products.

Q. How often should I wash my hair?

A. It is recommended to wash your hair 2-3 times a week to keep it clean and healthy.

Q. Can stress cause hair fall?

A. Yes, stress can cause hair fall. It can disrupt the hair growth cycle and lead to hair fall.

Hair Care Routine

Here's a hair care routine to help you achieve a healthy scalp and hair:

Morning Routine:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo
  • Massage your scalp gently
  • Rinse your hair properly
  • Apply hair oil and comb your hair

Night Routine:

  • Brush your hair gently
  • Tie your hair loosely
  • Avoid using hair styling products
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent hair breakage

Follow this routine regularly to get rid of dandruff and hair fall. Remember to eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote hair growth.
