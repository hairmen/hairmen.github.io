Best Products For Aging Hair 2019 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Aug 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Welcome to our guide on the best products for aging hair 2019! As we age, our hair goes through certain changes that require a different approach to hair care. In this article, we will be discussing the best products that can help you maintain healthy and youthful-looking hair. From shampoos to conditioners, we have got you covered. Let's get started!

Description

As we age, our hair tends to lose its elasticity, become dry and brittle, and thin out. This can be caused by a variety of factors, including hormonal changes, genetics, and environmental factors. Therefore, it is important to choose products that are specifically designed for aging hair.

How to Choose the Right Products

Choosing the right products for aging hair can be overwhelming. Here are some tips to help you choose the right products:

1. Look for Products that are Sulfate-Free

Sulfates can strip the hair of its natural oils, which can make it dry and brittle. Look for products that are sulfate-free to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

2. Choose Products with Natural Ingredients

Natural ingredients such as argan oil, coconut oil, and shea butter can help nourish and moisturize your hair. Look for products that contain these ingredients to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

3. Consider Your Hair Type

Not all products are created equal, so it's important to consider your hair type when choosing products. If you have fine hair, for example, you may want to look for products that are lightweight and won't weigh your hair down.

Best Products for Aging Hair 2019

Now that you know what to look for, here are some of the best products for aging hair:

1. Shampoo and Conditioner

The right shampoo and conditioner can make all the difference in keeping your hair healthy and vibrant. Look for products that are specifically designed for aging hair, such as L'Oreal Paris EverPure Moisture Shampoo and Conditioner.

2. Hair Oil

Hair oil can help nourish and moisturize your hair, making it look shiny and healthy. Try Moroccanoil Treatment, which is made with argan oil and can help repair and protect your hair.

3. Hair Mask

A hair mask can help restore moisture and elasticity to your hair. Try the Briogeo Don't Despair, Repair! Deep Conditioning Mask, which is made with natural ingredients like avocado and argan oil.

Step by Step Guide

1. Shampoo and Conditioner

Step 1: Wet your hair thoroughly.
Step 2: Apply a small amount of shampoo to your scalp and massage it in.
Step 3: Rinse your hair thoroughly.
Step 4: Apply a small amount of conditioner to the ends of your hair.
Step 5: Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes.
Step 6: Rinse your hair thoroughly.

2. Hair Oil

Step 1: Apply a small amount of hair oil to your palms.
Step 2: Rub your hands together to distribute the oil evenly.
Step 3: Apply the oil to the ends of your hair.
Step 4: Use a comb or brush to distribute the oil throughout your hair.
Step 5: Style your hair as desired.

3. Hair Mask

Step 1: Shampoo your hair as usual.
Step 2: Apply the hair mask to your hair, focusing on the ends.
Step 3: Leave the mask on for 10-20 minutes.
Step 4: Rinse your hair thoroughly.
Step 5: Condition your hair as usual.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy and youthful-looking hair:

1. Protect Your Hair from Sun Damage

UV rays can damage your hair, so it's important to protect it from the sun. Wear a hat or use a UV-protective spray to keep your hair healthy.

2. Don't Overuse Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools can damage your hair, so it's important to use them sparingly. If you do use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

FAQs

1. Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on aging hair?

While you can use regular shampoo and conditioner on aging hair, it's best to use products that are specifically designed for aging hair. These products are formulated to address the specific needs of aging hair, such as dryness and thinning.

2. Can hair oil make my hair greasy?

If you use too much hair oil, it can make your hair greasy. It's best to start with a small amount and add more as needed.

3. Can I use a hair mask every day?

No, it's not recommended to use a hair mask every day. Hair masks are designed to be used once or twice a week to help restore moisture and elasticity to your hair.

Hair Care: Final Thoughts

As we age, our hair requires a different approach to hair care. By choosing the right products and following these tips, you can maintain healthy and youthful-looking hair.
