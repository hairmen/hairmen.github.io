How To Do Hair Like Fran Fine A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 07, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're a fan of the hit '90s sitcom The Nanny, you're sure to remember Fran Fine's iconic hair. Her voluminous, curled locks were the envy of many viewers. If you want to achieve a similar look, you're in luck! In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to recreate Fran Fine's signature hairstyle.

Description

Fran Fine's hair was characterized by its volume and curls. She often wore it in a half-up, half-down style with loose waves cascading down her back. Her hair was typically cut in layers to add even more volume.

How to:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to achieving Fran Fine's hair is to start with clean, freshly washed hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume. Use a round brush to smooth your hair and create curls at the ends.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Use a large barrel curling iron to create loose curls throughout your hair. Wrap sections of your hair around the barrel, holding for a few seconds before releasing.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Tease the crown of your hair to add even more volume. Use a fine-tooth comb to backcomb small sections of hair at the crown, then smooth the top layer with a comb.

Step 5: Create a Half-Up, Half-Down Style

Gather the top section of your hair into a half-up, half-down style. Secure with a clip or hair tie.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Spray your hair with a strong-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place all day.

Tips

- Use a volumizing mousse or spray before blow-drying your hair to add even more body.

- Don't be afraid to tease your hair! This is key to achieving Fran Fine's voluminous look.

- Use a curling iron with a large barrel to create loose, bouncy curls.

FAQ

What if my hair is too short for this style?

If your hair is too short to achieve the half-up, half-down style, try creating loose curls throughout your hair and adding a clip or headband for a similar look.

How do I prevent my curls from falling flat throughout the day?

Use a strong-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place, and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or curling your hair.

Now that you know how to do hair like Fran Fine, you can rock this iconic look with confidence. With a little practice, you'll be able to recreate her signature style in no time!
