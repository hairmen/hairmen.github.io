  2. Take a section of hair and wrap it around a sock, starting at the ends and rolling it up towards your scalp. Secure the sock in place with a hair tie or bobby pin.

  • If you want to speed up the curling process, blow dry your hair for a few minutes before wrapping it around the socks.
