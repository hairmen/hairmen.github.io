Oval Face Shape Hairstyles For Men With Long Hair

Written by Aprilia Aug 14, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have an oval face shape and long hair, you're in luck! There are many great hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and show off your beautiful locks. In this article, we'll explore some of the best hairstyles for men with oval faces and long hair, as well as provide tips for hair care and maintenance.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by a well-balanced and proportionate facial structure. Men with oval faces have a forehead that is slightly wider than their jawline, and their cheekbones are the widest part of their face. Long hair can complement this face shape by adding volume and framing the face.

How to

The first step in choosing a hairstyle for your oval face and long hair is to consider your personal style and hair texture. Do you prefer a sleek and polished look, or do you like a more relaxed and messy style? Are your locks thick and curly, or thin and straight? Once you have a sense of your hair type and style preferences, you can begin to explore different hairstyles that will work well with your face shape.

Step by step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a hairstyle for your oval face and long hair:

  1. Consider your hair type and texture.
  2. Explore different hairstyles that are popular for men with oval faces and long hair.
  3. Talk to your hairstylist about which styles will work best for your face shape.
  4. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the perfect look for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your oval face and long hair:

  • Avoid styles that are too narrow or angular, as they can make your face appear longer.
  • Opt for styles that add volume to the sides of your face to balance out your features.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and texture.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the most flattering look for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about oval face shape hairstyles for men with long hair:

What are the best hairstyles for men with oval faces and long hair?

Some of the best hairstyles for men with oval faces and long hair include layered cuts, messy waves, and slicked-back styles.

Should I avoid bangs if I have an oval face shape?

Not necessarily. Bangs can work well with oval faces, but it's important to choose a style that complements your features.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any hairstyle, but it's especially important for men with long hair. Here are some tips for keeping your locks looking their best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your locks moisturized and shiny.

By following these tips and choosing a flattering hairstyle, you can show off your oval face shape and long hair with confidence!
