How To Cut Thick Wavy Hair Shorter

Written by Christine Aug 16, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have thick wavy hair and are looking to make a change, cutting it shorter can be a great option. But before you grab the scissors and start chopping away, there are a few things you should know. In this article, we’ll go over how to cut thick wavy hair shorter in a relaxed and easy-to-understand way.

Description

Thick wavy hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right cut, it can be both stylish and low-maintenance. Cutting your hair shorter can help to reduce the weight and volume of your hair, making it easier to style and manage.

How to Cut Thick Wavy Hair Shorter

Before you start cutting your hair, it’s important to have the right tools on hand. You’ll need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It’s also a good idea to have some hair clips on hand to keep your hair in place as you work.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will make it easier to work with and will help to prevent split ends.


2. Use a comb to part your hair down the middle, or on the side if you prefer.


3. Take a section of hair from the top of your head and clip it out of the way.


4. Use the comb to gently tease the section of hair you want to cut. This will help to create a natural-looking layer.


5. Use the scissors to trim the ends of the hair. Start by cutting a small amount, and then work your way up to the desired length.


6. Continue working your way around your head, taking small sections of hair and cutting them to the desired length.


7. Once you’ve finished cutting all of your hair, use the comb to blend the layers together.


8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

- It’s always a good idea to start with a small amount of hair and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can’t undo a bad cut.


- If you’re not comfortable cutting your hair yourself, consider going to a professional stylist.


- Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.

FAQ

Q: How short should I cut my thick wavy hair?


A: The length you choose will depend on your personal preferences and face shape. A short bob or lob can be a great option for thick wavy hair.


Q: How often should I cut my hair?


A: It’s recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair after you’ve cut it short is important to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated, and avoid using heat tools too often. Regular trims will also help to keep your hair looking its best.

By following these tips and techniques, you can successfully cut your thick wavy hair shorter at home. Remember to start small, use sharp scissors, and take good care of your hair after the cut.
