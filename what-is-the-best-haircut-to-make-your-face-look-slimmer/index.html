The Best Haircut To Make Your Face Look Slimmer

Written by Christine Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for ways to make your face look slimmer, a new haircut is a great place to start. Not only can a good haircut help to frame your face and highlight your best features, but it can also create the illusion of a slimmer and more sculpted face shape. In this article, we'll explore what is the best haircut to make your face look slimmer, and provide you with some tips and tricks for achieving the perfect look.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircuts for slimming your face, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you to choose a haircut that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong.

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back from your face. Take note of the following features:

  • The width of your forehead
  • The width of your cheekbones
  • The length of your face
  • The shape of your jawline

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start to consider which haircuts will flatter your features and create a slimmer appearance.

The Best Haircuts for Slimming Your Face

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to explore the best haircuts for slimming your face. Here are some ideas to get you started:

Oval Face

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck - this is considered the most versatile face shape and can pull off almost any haircut. To create a slimmer appearance, consider a long layered cut or a side-swept fringe.

Round Face

For a round face shape, the goal is to elongate the face and create more definition. A long bob with layers or a side part can help to achieve this look. Avoid blunt cuts and super short styles, as these can make the face appear rounder.

Square Face

A square face shape benefits from softening the angles with layers and texture. Try a shoulder-length cut with long layers or a side-swept fringe to draw attention away from the jawline.

Heart Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to balance out the width of the forehead with the width of the chin. A long bob with side-swept bangs or a layered cut with face-framing layers can help to achieve this balance.

Diamond Face

A diamond face shape benefits from haircuts that create volume and width at the forehead and chin. Consider a shaggy bob or a layered cut with choppy ends to achieve this look.

Oblong Face

For an oblong face shape, the goal is to create width and volume to balance out the length of the face. A shoulder-length cut with layers or a side-swept fringe can help to achieve this look. Avoid super long styles that can make the face appear even longer.

Tips for Slimming Your Face with a Haircut

In addition to choosing the right haircut for your face shape, there are a few other tips and tricks that can help to create a slimmer appearance:

  • Avoid blunt cuts and super short styles
  • Add layers and texture to soften the angles of the face
  • Consider a side part to create more asymmetry
  • Use styling products to add volume at the crown of the head
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you

FAQ

What if I have multiple face shapes?

It's possible to have a combination of face shapes, in which case you'll want to choose a haircut that flatters your most prominent features.

Can hair color make my face look slimmer?

While hair color can't change the shape of your face, it can create the illusion of depth and dimension. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create the appearance of more volume and movement.

Hair Care

To maintain your new haircut and keep your face looking slim, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid over-styling with heat tools. Consider using a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add volume and definition to your hair.

In conclusion, choosing the right haircut can make a big difference in the appearance of your face shape. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a slimmer and more sculpted look that highlights your natural beauty.
