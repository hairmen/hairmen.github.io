How To Cut My Own Curly Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 26, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune for a salon haircut every few months? Do you have curly hair that requires special attention? If you answered yes to both of these questions, you're in luck! In this article, we'll show you how to cut your own curly hair at home. Follow our step-by-step guide, and you'll have a fresh new haircut in no time.

How to Cut My Own Curly Hair at Home

Cutting your own curly hair may seem daunting at first, but with the right technique and tools, it's completely doable. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp haircutting scissors
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Hair clips
  • Mirror

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your own curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. It's easier to cut hair when it's dry because you can see the natural curl pattern.
  2. Part your hair down the middle and clip one side away.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the unclipped side and comb it out with a wide-tooth comb. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut off the desired length. Start with a small amount and cut more if needed.
  4. Repeat step 3 with the rest of the hair on the unclipped side.
  5. Unclip the other side of your hair and repeat steps 3-4.
  6. Check both sides of your hair in the mirror to make sure they're even.
  7. Trim any pieces that are uneven or too long.

Congratulations! You've just cut your own curly hair.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Always start with a small amount of hair. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's been cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Don't cut your hair too short. Curly hair tends to bounce up when it dries, so what looks like a small trim when wet can turn into a major cut when dry.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your hair, start with a small section or consider getting a friend to help.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting curly hair:

  • Can I cut my hair when it's wet? It's not recommended to cut curly hair when it's wet because it can be difficult to see the natural curl pattern.
  • How often should I cut my curly hair? It's recommended to trim curly hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Can I use regular scissors to cut my curly hair? No, it's important to use sharp haircutting scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Should I cut my hair in layers? Layering curly hair can help add volume and definition to curls, but it's best to leave this to a professional stylist.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you've cut your own curly hair, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage curly hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair to define curls and reduce frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel, which can cause frizz.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to cut your own curly hair at home like a pro.
