How To Thin Out Hair With Scissors Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Thinning out hair with scissors is a popular technique that a lot of men use to achieve a more natural and textured look. It can also help reduce the volume of thick hair, making it more manageable. If you want to learn how to thin out your hair with scissors, then you’ve come to the right place. This article will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to do it properly.

How to Thin Out Hair with Scissors Male: Step-by-Step Guide

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You’ll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It’s important to start with clean, dry hair. This will give you a better idea of how your hair naturally falls and the areas that need thinning out.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use hair clips to section your hair into manageable parts. Start with the back of your head and work your way up.

Step 3: Hold the Scissors Properly

Hold the scissors with your dominant hand and use your other hand to hold the hair. Use the tips of the scissors to make small, quick snips.

Step 4: Start Thinning Out Your Hair

Start thinning out your hair by snipping at the ends of your hair, working your way up towards the roots. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After thinning out a section, let the hair fall naturally and check your progress. If you need to thin out more, repeat the process until you achieve the desired look.

Step 6: Move to the Next Section

Unclip the next section of hair and repeat the process until you’ve thinned out all the sections.

Step 7: Blend the Layers

Once you’ve thinned out all the sections, use the comb to blend the layers together. This will help create a more natural look.

Step 8: Clean Up

Use the comb to comb out any loose hair and clean up any stray hairs with the scissors.

Tips for Thinning Out Hair with Scissors Male

Here are some tips to keep in mind when thinning out your hair with scissors:

  • Start with small sections and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from different angles.
  • Don’t cut too much hair at once.
  • Be patient and take your time.
  • Use the right scissors – sharp scissors will make the process easier and more precise.

FAQs About Thinning Out Hair with Scissors Male

Is it better to thin out hair when it’s wet or dry?

It’s better to thin out hair when it’s dry. This will give you a better idea of how your hair naturally falls and the areas that need thinning out.

How often should I thin out my hair?

This depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it’s recommended to thin out your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Thinning Out Hair with Scissors Male

Here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind when thinning out your hair with scissors:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and style.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

By following these steps and tips, you’ll be able to thin out your hair with scissors like a pro. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and be patient. Good luck!
