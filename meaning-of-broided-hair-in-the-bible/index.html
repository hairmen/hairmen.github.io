The Meaning Of Broided Hair In The Bible

Written by Eveline Jun 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

In the Bible, broided hair has a special meaning. It is mentioned several times in both the Old and New Testaments. This article will explore the significance of broided hair in the Bible, as well as provide a few tips on how to style your hair in a broided way.

What is Broided Hair?

Broided hair, also known as braided hair, is a hairstyle that involves weaving three or more strands of hair together. This hairstyle has been around for centuries and is still popular today.

The Significance of Broided Hair in the Bible

In the Bible, broided hair is mentioned in 1 Peter 3:3-4: "Your beauty should not come from outward adornment, such as elaborate hairstyles and the wearing of gold jewelry or fine clothes. Rather, it should be that of your inner self, the unfading beauty of a gentle and quiet spirit, which is of great worth in God's sight."

This passage suggests that women should not focus on their outward appearance, such as their hairstyles, but rather on their inner beauty. Broided hair is also mentioned in Isaiah 3:24, where it is used as a symbol of luxury and excess.

How to Broid Your Hair

If you want to try broiding your hair, here are some simple steps to follow:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to broid your hair.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into three sections. Hold one section in each hand and let the third section hang down the back of your head.

Step 3: Braid Your Hair

Take the section in your right hand and cross it over the middle section. Then, take the section in your left hand and cross it over the new middle section. Repeat this process until you reach the end of your hair.

Step 4: Secure Your Braid

Secure your braid with a hair tie or clip. You can also add accessories, such as ribbons or flowers, to your braid for a decorative touch.

Tips for Broiding Your Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when broiding your hair:

  • Start with clean, dry hair to make it easier to braid.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to separate your hair into even sections.
  • Practice makes perfect! Don't be discouraged if your first braid doesn't turn out perfectly.
  • Experiment with different braid styles, such as fishtail or French braids.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does broided hair symbolize in the Bible?

Broided hair is often used as a symbol of luxury and excess in the Bible. However, it is also mentioned in a passage that suggests women should focus on their inner beauty rather than their outward appearance.

Is broided hair still popular today?

Yes, broided hair is still a popular hairstyle today. There are many different braid styles to choose from, and they can be worn in both casual and formal settings.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and strong, here are a few hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Broided hair has a special meaning in the Bible, and it is still a popular hairstyle today. Whether you choose a simple three-strand braid or a more intricate style, broided hair can add a touch of elegance and beauty to your look.
