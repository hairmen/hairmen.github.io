How To Do Simple Hairstyles By Yourself For Short Hair

Written by Robby Sep 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Short hair can be a challenge when it comes to styling, but with a little creativity and patience, you can create simple hairstyles that are both easy and elegant. In this article, we will show you how to do simple hairstyles by yourself for short hair.

Table of Content

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we start, there are a few things you will need:

  • A comb
  • A hairbrush
  • A hair tie
  • Bobby pins
  • Hair spray

Now that you have all the necessary tools, let's get started.

Step by step

1. The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle. Simply comb your hair forward and use a hair spray to keep it in place. You can also add a headband or a hair accessory to add some style to your look.

2. The Side Sweep

The side sweep is a classic hairstyle that is easy to do. Simply part your hair to one side and sweep it to the other side. Use bobby pins to keep it in place and add some hair spray to keep it in place.

3. The Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their hair out of their face. Simply pull the top half of your hair up and tie it with a hair tie. You can also add some curls or waves to add some volume to your look.

4. The Messy Bun

The messy bun is a great hairstyle for those who want a casual look. Simply tie your hair into a bun and use bobby pins to secure it in place. You can also pull some strands out to create a messy look.

Tips

  • Use a hair spray to keep your hairstyle in place
  • Add some hair accessories to add some style to your look
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you

FAQ

Q. Can I do these hairstyles on curly hair?

A. Yes, these hairstyles can be done on curly hair. However, you may need to use some hair products to keep your curls in place.

Q. Can I do these hairstyles on colored hair?

A. Yes, these hairstyles can be done on colored hair. However, you may need to use some hair products to keep your color from fading.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

With these simple hairstyles and hair care tips, you can keep your short hair looking its best.
