How To Do A Man Bun Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your usual hairstyle and want to try something new? A man bun might be the perfect option for you! This trendy hairstyle has been around for a while now and is still going strong. In this article, we will guide you on how to do a man bun haircut. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to do a Man Bun Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect Man Bun
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Man Buns

How to do a Man Bun Haircut

A man bun is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in different ways. The basic idea is to tie your hair in a bun at the top of your head. This hairstyle is suitable for men with long hair or medium-length hair that can be tied up. Here are some things you need to consider before attempting a man bun:

  • Your hair needs to be long enough to be tied up. Ideally, it should be at least shoulder length or longer.
  • You will need a hairband or a hair tie to secure your bun.
  • You can use hair products like wax, pomade, or hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect man bun:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles and knots.
  2. Gather all your hair at the top of your head using your hands. Make sure to leave some hair at the front for a relaxed look.
  3. Twist your hair tightly to form a bun. You can twist it in a clockwise or anti-clockwise direction depending on your preference.
  4. Secure your bun with a hair tie or a hairband. Make sure it is tight enough to hold your hair in place.
  5. Use a comb or your fingers to tidy up any loose hair around the bun.
  6. If you want a more polished look, you can use a hair product like wax or pomade to keep your hair in place.

Tips for a Perfect Man Bun

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect man bun:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to avoid it being too visible.
  • If you have thin hair, you can add volume to your bun by teasing your hair before tying it up.
  • Experiment with different styles of man buns like a low bun, side bun, or a half-up bun.
  • Accessorize your man bun with a headband, a scarf or a beanie for a stylish look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about man buns:

1. Can I do a man bun if I have short hair?

No, you need to have long hair or medium-length hair that can be tied up to do a man bun.

2. How do I prevent my man bun from falling out?

Make sure to tie your bun tightly and use a hair tie that can hold your hair in place. You can also use hair products like wax or pomade to keep your hair in place.

3. Is it bad for my hair to tie it up in a bun?

Tying your hair up in a bun can cause some damage to your hair if done too tightly. Make sure to tie your hair up loosely and avoid doing it too often.

Hair Care for Man Buns

Here are some tips to take care of your hair when you are wearing a man bun:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends and breakage.

Now that you know how to do a man bun haircut, it’s time to try it out! Remember to follow the steps carefully and experiment with different styles to find your perfect look. Happy styling!
