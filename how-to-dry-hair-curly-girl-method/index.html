Hair Care for Curly Hair

The curly girl method involves a specific hair care routine that aims to enhance and embrace natural curls. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Avoid using sulfates and silicones in your hair care products, as these can strip natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • Apply styling products while your hair is still wet to ensure even distribution.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can cause damage to curly hair.
