Easy Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair Step By Step

Written by Daniel Aug 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have medium length hair, you know how versatile it can be. From simple ponytails to elegant updos, there's no shortage of ways to style your hair. In this article, we'll show you some easy hairstyles for medium length hair step by step, so you can switch up your look without spending hours in front of the mirror.

How to Style Medium Length Hair

Before we get into the hairstyles, it's important to know how to style your medium length hair for the best results. Here are some tips:

  • Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is damp, it will be harder to style and may not hold the shape you want.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you plan to use heat styling tools. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Invest in good quality hair products. Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type (dry, oily, curly, etc.) and that will help you achieve the look you want.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment! Half the fun of having medium length hair is trying out new styles and seeing what works best for you.

Easy Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair Step by Step

1. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that's perfect for medium length hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a messy, relaxed look.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Low Ponytail

A low ponytail is a simple and elegant hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie.
  3. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to soften the look.
  4. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is a great way to show off your medium length hair while keeping it out of your face. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a section of hair from each side of your head and pull it back to the crown of your head.
  3. Secure the two sections of hair together with a hair tie.
  4. Finish by curling the loose ends of your hair for added texture.

4. Braided Crown

The braided crown is a romantic and bohemian hairstyle that's perfect for weddings and other special occasions. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from one side of your head and braid it.
  3. Wrap the braid around the top of your head, securing it with bobby pins.
  4. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  5. Finish by pulling out a few strands of hair around your face to create a relaxed, bohemian look.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your medium length hair:

  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush. A brush with natural bristles will help distribute oils throughout your hair, keeping it healthy and shiny.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Don't wash your hair every day. Washing too often can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling medium length hair:

1. How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference, but most people find that washing every 2-3 days is sufficient.

2. How can I add volume to my medium length hair?

Try blow-drying your hair upside down or using a volumizing mousse before blow-drying.

3. How can I prevent frizz in my medium length hair?

Use a smoothing serum or oil before blow-drying, and avoid using hot tools on wet hair.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

Here are some general tips for keeping your medium length hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools (like straighteners and curling irons) too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and overall health.

With these easy hairstyles for medium length hair step by step, you'll be able to switch up your look in no time. Whether you're going for a casual, everyday look or a more formal style, there's a hairstyle out there for you. Just remember to take good care of your hair, and don't be afraid to experiment!
