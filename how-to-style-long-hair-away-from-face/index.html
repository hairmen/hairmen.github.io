How To Style Long Hair Away From Face A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair is beautiful, but it can be challenging to manage, especially when it keeps getting in your face. Fortunately, there are plenty of ways to style long hair away from your face, whether you're going for a sleek and sophisticated look or something more casual and relaxed. In this article, we'll go over some of the best techniques for keeping your long hair under control.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Long Hair Away from Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Long Hair
  • FAQs

How to Style Long Hair Away from Face

Before we get into specific styling techniques, let's talk about some general principles that apply to all long hair. First, make sure your hair is clean and well-conditioned. Long hair is prone to tangles and breakage, so you want to treat it gently. Second, use the right tools. A wide-tooth comb, a paddle brush, and some bobby pins are all you need to get started.

Now, let's look at some specific ways to style long hair away from your face:

Ponytail

The ponytail is a classic hairstyle that's perfect for keeping long hair out of your face. To create a sleek, sophisticated look, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with an elastic band. For a more casual look, you can position the ponytail higher up on your head or leave a few strands loose around your face.

Braids

Braids are another great way to style long hair away from your face. There are many different types of braids to choose from, including French braids, Dutch braids, and fishtail braids. To create a simple braid, divide your hair into three sections and cross the outer sections over the middle section. Continue braiding until you reach the end of your hair, then secure with an elastic band.

Bun

A bun is a chic and elegant way to keep your hair out of your face. To create a bun, gather your hair into a high ponytail, then twist the hair around the base of the ponytail and secure with bobby pins. You can also create a messy bun by pulling a few strands of hair loose around your face.

Headband

A headband is a quick and easy way to keep your hair out of your face. Choose a headband that complements your outfit and slide it over your hair, positioning it just behind your hairline. You can wear a headband with your hair down, or you can pull your hair back into a ponytail or bun.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your long hair away from your face:

  1. Start with clean, conditioned hair.
  2. Choose a styling technique that suits your hair type and the occasion.
  3. Gather your hair into a ponytail, braid, bun, or use a headband.
  4. Secure your hair with an elastic band or bobby pins.
  5. Use a comb or brush to smooth any flyaways.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray or styling product to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Styling Long Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling long hair:

  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use hair accessories like clips, barrettes, and scrunchies to add interest to your hairstyle.
  • If your hair is particularly thick or heavy, you may need to use more than one elastic band or bobby pins to keep it in place.
  • Try using a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQs

How do I keep my hair out of my face when I'm exercising?

If you're going to be active, the best way to keep your hair out of your face is to use a headband or a ponytail. You may also want to consider using a sweat-wicking hair tie to keep your hair in place.

How often should I wash my long hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but most people with long hair can get away with washing it every two to three days. If you have particularly oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Finally, here are some hair care tips to keep your long hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can keep your long hair looking great and keep it away from your face. Whether you're going for a casual look or something more formal, there's a hairstyle that's perfect for you.
