Hairstyles For 60 Year Old Woman With Fine Hair 2018

As we age, our hair goes through a lot of changes. Fine hair can become even thinner and lose its volume. It's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and hair type. In this article, we will discuss some of the best hairstyles for 60-year-old women with fine hair in 2018.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step by Step Guide to Styling Fine Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Fine Hair
  • FAQs

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for fine hair, it's important to create the illusion of volume. Layered haircuts work well for fine hair because they add texture and movement. Avoid blunt cuts that can make your hair look flat and lifeless.

Short hairstyles like pixie cuts and bobs can also work well for fine hair. They create the illusion of fullness and are easy to style. However, if you prefer longer hair, you can opt for a layered haircut that falls just below your shoulders.

Trending Hairstyles for 60-Year-Old Women with Fine Hair in 2018

Some of the popular hairstyles for 60-year-old women with fine hair in 2018 include:

  • Layered bob
  • Pixie cut
  • Shoulder-length layered haircut
  • Side-swept bangs

Step by Step Guide to Styling Fine Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling fine hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products specifically designed for fine hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair while it's still damp.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume. Focus on the roots to create lift.
  4. If you have long hair, you can curl the ends using a curling iron or a straightener to create movement.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Maintaining Fine Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining fine hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add thickness to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often. Heat can damage fine hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for fine hair?

The best haircut for fine hair is a layered haircut that adds texture and movement. Short hairstyles like pixie cuts and bobs also work well for fine hair.

How can I add volume to my fine hair?

You can add volume to your fine hair by using volumizing shampoos and conditioners, blow-drying your hair with a round brush, and using a volumizing mousse.

Hair Care for Fine Hair

Here are some tips for caring for fine hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair. Use lukewarm water instead.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with a towel. Instead, gently pat it dry.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray or styling products that can weigh down your hair.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can make your fine hair look voluminous and healthy. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best!
