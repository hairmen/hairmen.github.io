Hairstyles To Suit Round Face With Double Chin

Written by Bardi Oct 17, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have a round face with a double chin, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, there are many hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and enhance your features. In this article, we'll be discussing some of the best hairstyles for round faces with double chins.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Hairstyles for Round Faces with Double Chins
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  • FAQs about Hairstyles for Round Faces with Double Chins
  • Hair Care Tips for Round Faces with Double Chins

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before you start looking for hairstyles, it's important to determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and examine your face in the mirror. Here are the different face shapes:

  • Round: your face is as wide as it is long, with a soft jawline and full cheeks.
  • Square: your face is as wide as it is long, with a strong jawline and forehead.
  • Oval: your face is longer than it is wide, with a slightly curved jawline and forehead.
  • Heart: your face is wider at the forehead and temples, with a narrow chin.
  • Diamond: your face is widest at the cheekbones, with a narrow forehead and chin.

If you have a round face with a double chin, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and draws attention away from your chin. Let's take a look at some options.

Hairstyles for Round Faces with Double Chins

Here are some hairstyles that can flatter round faces with double chins:

Long Layers

Long layers can help to elongate your face and draw attention away from your chin. Ask your stylist to add layers starting from your cheekbones and cascading down the length of your hair.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of a longer face. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle, starting from the outer corner of your eyebrow and sweeping across your forehead.

Bob with Volume

A bob that is chin-length or shorter can help to elongate your face, but be sure to add volume at the crown to create the illusion of height. This will draw attention away from your chin.

Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob is a great way to add dimension to your hair and flatter your face shape. Ask your stylist to cut your hair shorter on one side and longer on the other, creating an angled look that elongates your face.

Low Ponytail

A low ponytail can help to elongate your face and draw attention away from your chin. Be sure to keep the ponytail loose and add volume at the crown to create height.

Updo

An updo can also help to elongate your face and draw attention away from your chin. Try a messy bun or a low chignon, and be sure to add volume at the crown.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a round face with a double chin:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your cheeks or chin, such as blunt bangs or a bob that hits at the jawline.
  • Choose hairstyles that elongate your face and draw attention away from your chin, such as long layers or an asymmetrical bob.
  • Consider adding volume at the crown to create height and draw attention away from your chin.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that flatters your face shape the most.

FAQs about Hairstyles for Round Faces with Double Chins

What is the best hairstyle for a round face with a double chin?

The best hairstyle for a round face with a double chin is one that elongates your face and draws attention away from your chin. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and an asymmetrical bob are all great options.

Should I avoid blunt bangs?

Yes, you should avoid blunt bangs if you have a round face with a double chin. Blunt bangs add volume to your cheeks and draw attention to your chin, which can make your face look rounder.

What should I do if I'm not sure which hairstyle is right for me?

If you're not sure which hairstyle is right for you, talk to your stylist. They can offer suggestions based on your face shape and hair type, and help you find a style that flatters your features.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces with Double Chins

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind if you have a round face with a double chin:

  • Avoid hairstyles that require a lot of maintenance, such as daily blowouts or straightening.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add height to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy or greasy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know which hairstyles flatter round faces with double chins, it's time to experiment and find the one that works best for you. Remember to take your hair type and personal style into consideration, and don't be afraid to try something new!
