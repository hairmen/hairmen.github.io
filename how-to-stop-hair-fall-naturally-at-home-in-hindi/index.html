How To Stop Hair Fall Naturally At Home In Hindi

Written by Eveline Sep 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Hair fall is a common problem that affects many people around the world. It can be caused by various factors such as stress, poor nutrition, hormonal imbalances, and more. If you're looking for natural ways to stop hair fall, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing how to stop hair fall naturally at home in Hindi.

  • How to Stop Hair Fall Naturally at Home in Hindi
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Prevent Hair Fall
  • FAQs

How to Stop Hair Fall Naturally at Home in Hindi

There are several natural remedies that you can try at home to stop hair fall. Here are a few:

1. Amla

Amla, also known as Indian gooseberry, is a powerhouse of nutrients that can help strengthen hair follicles and prevent hair fall. You can use amla in the form of oil, powder, or juice.

2. Onion Juice

Onion juice is rich in sulfur, which can help improve blood circulation to the hair follicles and promote hair growth. Apply onion juice to your scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

3. Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is an excellent natural conditioner that can help nourish your hair and prevent hair fall. Massage your scalp with warm coconut oil and leave it on overnight before washing it off in the morning.

4. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is known for its healing and moisturizing properties. Apply aloe vera gel to your scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to use these natural remedies to stop hair fall:

Step 1: Choose a natural remedy

Choose one of the natural remedies mentioned above that suits your hair type and needs.

Step 2: Prepare the remedy

Prepare the natural remedy according to the instructions given above.

Step 3: Apply the remedy

Apply the natural remedy to your scalp and hair using your fingertips. Massage your scalp gently to improve blood circulation.

Step 4: Leave it on

Leave the natural remedy on for the recommended time.

Step 5: Wash it off

Wash off the natural remedy with a mild shampoo and lukewarm water.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are a few tips to prevent hair fall:

  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet
  • Avoid using hot styling tools
  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid brushing wet hair
  • Get enough sleep
  • Manage your stress levels

FAQs

Q: How often should I use these natural remedies to stop hair fall?

A: You can use these natural remedies once or twice a week for best results.

Q: Can these natural remedies cause any side effects?

A: These natural remedies are generally safe to use, but if you experience any allergic reactions or side effects, stop using them immediately and consult a doctor.

Hair Care Routine to Stop Hair Fall

Here's a simple hair care routine that you can follow to prevent hair fall:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and lukewarm water
  • Apply a natural conditioner such as coconut oil or aloe vera
  • Avoid using hot styling tools
  • Brush your hair gently
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet
  • Manage your stress levels

In conclusion, there are several natural remedies that you can try at home to stop hair fall. However, it's important to remember that hair fall can be caused by various factors, and it's best to consult a doctor if you experience excessive hair fall.
