Do Side Bangs Suit Square Faces

Description

If you have a square face shape, you may be wondering if side bangs would suit your features. Square faces are characterized by a strong, angular jawline and forehead, with the width of the face being almost equal to its length. Side bangs are a popular hairstyle that can soften the features of a square face, but it's important to consider a few factors before getting this style.

How to Determine if Side Bangs Suit Square Faces

Before getting side bangs, it's important to determine if they will suit your face shape. One way to do this is to consider the length of your forehead. If you have a shorter forehead, side bangs can help to elongate it and balance out the proportions of your face. However, if you have a longer forehead, side bangs may not be the best option, as they can make your face look longer and more angular.

Another factor to consider is the width of your face. If your face is wider than it is long, side bangs can help to create the illusion of length and balance out the proportions of your face. However, if your face is already long and narrow, side bangs may not be the best option, as they can make your face look even longer.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cut Side Bangs for Square Faces

If you have determined that side bangs will suit your square face, here is a step-by-step guide to cutting them:

  1. Start by sectioning off the hair that you want to cut into bangs. Use a comb to create a triangular section of hair that starts at the top of the head and ends at the outer corner of the eyebrow.
  2. Hold the hair between your fingers and use scissors to cut it to the desired length.
  3. Gradually angle the scissors downward as you move toward the outer corner of the eyebrow, creating a longer, tapered effect.
  4. Repeat on the other side of the head, making sure that the two sides are even.
  5. Blend the bangs into the rest of the hair using thinning shears, if desired.

Tips for Maintaining Side Bangs for Square Faces

Once you have cut your side bangs, it's important to maintain them properly to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a round brush and a blow dryer to style your bangs, directing them to one side for a sleek, polished look.
  • Use a small amount of hair gel or pomade to smooth down any flyaways or frizz.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them at the desired length.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh between washes.

FAQs About Side Bangs for Square Faces

Q: Can side bangs make a square face look more feminine?

A: Yes, side bangs can soften the features of a square face and create a more feminine look.

Q: Are there any side bang styles that are particularly flattering for square faces?

A: Side-swept bangs that are longer on one side than the other can help to create a more asymmetrical look and balance out the proportions of a square face.

Q: Can side bangs be styled in different ways?

A: Yes, side bangs can be styled in a variety of ways, including swept to the side, straight across, or curled under.

Hair Care for Side Bangs and Square Faces

When caring for your hair and side bangs, it's important to use products that are suitable for your hair type and texture. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to add shine and nourishment to your hair.

Overall, side bangs can be a flattering and stylish hairstyle for those with square faces, but it's important to consider your individual features and consult with a stylist before making the cut.
