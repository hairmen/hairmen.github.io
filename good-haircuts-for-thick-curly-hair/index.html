Good Haircuts For Thick Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jul 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unruly, thick curly hair? Do you struggle to find the right haircut that suits your hair type? Look no further, because we have compiled a list of the best haircuts for thick curly hair that will make you love your curls even more!

Description

Thick curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have beautiful voluminous hair that many people envy. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. Finding the perfect haircut for your hair type can make all the difference in the world.

How to

Before we dive into the best haircuts for thick curly hair, it's important to understand what works best for your hair type. Generally, curly hair thrives with layers and texture. This helps to define the curls and prevent excessive bulkiness.

When getting a haircut, make sure to communicate with your stylist about your hair type and your desired look. Bring in pictures of haircuts that you like and discuss with your stylist how it can be adapted to your hair.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape

The first step in finding the perfect haircut is to determine your face shape. This will help you find a style that flatters your features.

Step 2: Research haircuts

Look online or in magazines for haircuts that appeal to you. Make sure to consider your hair type and face shape when selecting a style.

Step 3: Consult with a stylist

Bring in pictures of the haircuts you like and discuss with your stylist how they can be adapted to your hair type and face shape.

Step 4: Maintenance

After getting your haircut, make sure to maintain it with regular trims and proper hair care.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when selecting a haircut for thick curly hair:

  • Opt for layers to define the curls and prevent bulkiness
  • Consider getting bangs to balance out the face
  • Look for haircuts that enhance your natural curls
  • Avoid haircuts that require excessive heat styling

FAQ

What haircuts are best for thick curly hair?

The best haircuts for thick curly hair are ones that incorporate layers and texture to define the curls and prevent bulkiness. Some popular haircuts include long layers, shoulder-length bobs, and curly shags.

What should I avoid when getting a haircut for thick curly hair?

When getting a haircut for thick curly hair, it's best to avoid haircuts that require excessive heat styling, such as blunt bangs or super short cuts. These can be difficult to style and maintain with curly hair.

How can I maintain my haircut for thick curly hair?

Regular trims and proper hair care are essential for maintaining a haircut for thick curly hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and avoid excessive heat styling.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is key to maintaining healthy, beautiful curls. Here are some tips for taking care of your thick curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to moisturize
  • Consider a deep conditioning treatment once a week

With these tips and haircuts, you'll be rocking your thick curly hair with confidence and ease!
