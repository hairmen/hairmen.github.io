How To Do Slightly Wavy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Oct 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old straight hair or tight curls? Do you want to switch things up with a relaxed and effortless look? Look no further than slightly wavy hair!

Description

Slightly wavy hair is a versatile and low-maintenance hairstyle that can be achieved with minimal effort. It's perfect for those who want to add a bit of texture and volume to their hair without committing to tight curls or pin-straight locks.

How to

Follow these simple steps to achieve slightly wavy hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is naturally curly or frizzy, use a smoothing serum to tame it before styling.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. The number of sections you create will depend on the thickness of your hair. For thicker hair, create more sections to ensure that every strand is curled.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron, curl each section of hair away from your face. Hold the curl for a few seconds before releasing it. Be sure to leave the ends of your hair uncurled for a more natural look.

Step 4: Finger Comb Your Hair

After curling your hair, use your fingers to gently comb through the curls. This will loosen them up and create a more relaxed look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to help the waves hold their shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for achieving the perfect slightly wavy hair:

  • Use a curling iron with a larger barrel for looser waves.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly. The goal is to create loose waves, not tight ringlets.
  • Try using a sea salt spray to help enhance the waves and add texture.
  • For a more natural look, curl the hair in different directions.

FAQ

How long does slightly wavy hair last?

The length of time that your slightly wavy hair will last depends on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, it should last at least a day or two.

Can I achieve slightly wavy hair without a curling iron?

Yes! You can achieve slightly wavy hair by braiding your hair before bed and then unraveling the braids in the morning. You can also use a hair straightener to create waves by twisting your hair around the straightener and pulling it down.

Hair Care

To keep your slightly wavy hair looking its best, be sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and dryness. Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and hydrated.

By following these simple steps, you can achieve the perfect slightly wavy hair that will turn heads and make you feel confident and beautiful.
