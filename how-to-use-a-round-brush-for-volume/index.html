How To Use A Round Brush For Volume A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to add some volume to your hair, a round brush can be your best friend. But how do you use it properly? In this post, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve voluminous locks with a round brush.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Round Brush
  • How to Prep Your Hair
  • How to Use a Round Brush for Volume
  • Tips for Achieving Maximum Volume
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Choose the Right Round Brush

Before you can start adding volume to your hair with a round brush, you need to make sure you have the right brush for your hair type and desired style.

For fine hair, choose a brush with a smaller barrel to create more lift at the roots. For thicker hair, opt for a larger barrel to achieve a smoother finish.

If you have short hair, a smaller brush will work best, while longer hair can benefit from a larger brush.

How to Prep Your Hair

To achieve maximum volume with a round brush, you need to start with clean, dry hair. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots before blow-drying to give your hair some extra lift.

Once your hair is dry, section it off into manageable sections. This will ensure that you're able to get every strand with the round brush.

How to Use a Round Brush for Volume

Now it's time to start using your round brush for volume. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1:

Take a section of hair and place the round brush at the roots.

Step 2:

Use your blow dryer to blast hot air at the roots while you pull the brush through your hair, rolling the brush upwards towards the ends.

Step 3:

Once you reach the ends of your hair, hold the brush in place for a few seconds to set the curl.

Step 4:

Repeat this process on all sections of your hair, working from the bottom up.

Tips for Achieving Maximum Volume

To get the most out of your round brush, keep these tips in mind:

  • Work in small sections to ensure that every strand gets volume.
  • Use a concentrator nozzle on your blow dryer to direct the heat at the roots.
  • For extra volume, flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair in that position.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your volume in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use a round brush on wet hair?

While it's best to use a round brush on dry hair, you can use it on wet hair as well. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray and be extra gentle with your hair to avoid damage.

How often should I use a round brush for volume?

It's safe to use a round brush for volume every time you wash your hair, but be mindful of how much heat you're applying to your hair. To avoid damage, try to limit your use of heat styling tools.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and happy, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Limit your use of heat styling tools and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and your new knowledge of how to use a round brush for volume, you'll be able to achieve the voluminous hair of your dreams. Happy styling!
