Easy Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair Step By Step

Written by Robby Aug 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair step by step? Look no further, as we have compiled a list of simple yet stylish hairstyles that you can try out on your shoulder length hair. These hairstyles are perfect for any occasion, whether it's a formal event or a casual day out with friends.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's go through some basic tips on how to style shoulder length hair:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools
  • Invest in good quality hair accessories such as hair ties, bobby pins, and hair clips
  • Use hairspray to set your hairstyle in place

Step by Step

1. Messy Bun

This is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for a casual day out. Here's how you can achieve the messy bun look:

  1. Brush your hair into a high ponytail
  2. Twist your hair and wrap it around the base of the ponytail
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins
  4. Pull out some strands of hair from the bun to create a messy look

2. Half-Up Half-Down

This hairstyle is perfect for a formal event or a date night. Here's how you can achieve the half-up half-down look:

  1. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tie it into a ponytail
  2. Tease the ponytail to create volume
  3. Take a small section of hair from each side of your head and twist them towards the back
  4. Secure the twisted sections with bobby pins at the base of the ponytail

3. Braided Crown

This hairstyle is perfect for a boho chic look. Here's how you can achieve the braided crown look:

  1. Take a small section of hair from one side of your head and braid it
  2. Secure the end of the braid with a small elastic band
  3. Take the braid and wrap it around the crown of your head, securing it with bobby pins
  4. Repeat on the other side of your head

4. Sleek Low Ponytail

This hairstyle is perfect for a sophisticated look. Here's how you can achieve the sleek low ponytail:

  1. Brush your hair back and tie it into a low ponytail
  2. Use a flat iron to straighten any flyaways
  3. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band, securing it with a bobby pin
  4. Use hairspray to set the hairstyle in place

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your shoulder length hair:

  • Experiment with different hair accessories such as headbands and scarves
  • Try out different partings to change up your look
  • Use dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or hairstylist

FAQ

Q. Can I still achieve these hairstyles if I have thin hair?

A. Yes, you can still achieve these hairstyles if you have thin hair. You can use hair extensions or volumizing products to add more volume to your hair.

Q. How long will these hairstyles last?

A. These hairstyles can last for several hours, depending on the type of hair accessories and products you use. You can use hairspray or hair gel to set the hairstyle in place.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining your shoulder length hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals

Now that you have some easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair step by step, you can experiment with different looks and find the one that suits you best. Remember to have fun and be creative with your hair!
