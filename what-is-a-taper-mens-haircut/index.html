What Is A Taper Men s Haircut A Complete Guide

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that is versatile, stylish, and low maintenance? A taper men's haircut might just be the perfect solution for you. In this complete guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about the taper haircut, from what it is to how to style and care for it. So, let's get started!

  2. How to Get a Taper Haircut

Definition

A taper haircut is a men's hairstyle that features hair that gradually gets shorter from the top of the head down to the neckline. The hair on the sides and back of the head is typically cut shorter than the hair on the top, creating a tapered effect. This style is known for its versatility and low maintenance, making it a popular choice for men of all ages and hair types.

How to Get a Taper Haircut

If you're interested in getting a taper haircut, the first step is to find a good barber or hairstylist who is experienced in this style. You can bring in a picture of the style you want or ask for recommendations based on your face shape and hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a taper haircut:

  1. Start by determining the length you want on the top of your head. This will depend on your personal preference and hair type.
  2. Next, your barber will use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head, gradually tapering it shorter as they move down towards your neckline.
  3. They will then use scissors to blend the longer hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back, creating a seamless transition.
  4. Finally, they will style your hair to your desired look using products like pomade or wax.

Tips for Styling a Taper Haircut

Here are some tips for styling your taper haircut:

  • Use a small amount of product to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Consider using a blow dryer to add volume and shape to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your taper haircut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about taper haircuts:

What face shape is best for a taper haircut?

A taper haircut works well for most face shapes, but it is particularly flattering for those with a square or oval face shape.

Can I still style my hair if I have a receding hairline?

A taper haircut can actually help to make a receding hairline less noticeable. Your barber can adjust the length and shape of the haircut to complement your hairline and create a more balanced look.

Is a taper haircut difficult to maintain?

No, a taper haircut is actually quite low maintenance. You may need to get trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape, but other than that, it requires minimal styling and upkeep.

Hair Care for Taper Haircut

Here are some tips for taking care of your taper haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • If you have dry or damaged hair, consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep it hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a stylish and low maintenance taper haircut with ease. So, what are you waiting for? Book your next haircut appointment and give this versatile style a try!
