How To Wear High Waisted Jeans Over 50 Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Oct 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you over 50 and wondering how to wear high waisted jeans? Don’t worry, you’re not alone. High waisted jeans are a classic style that can be worn at any age. In fact, they can be very flattering and comfortable for women over 50. Here are some tips and tricks for wearing high waisted jeans with confidence.

Description

High waisted jeans are a style of jeans that sit above the hips and usually have a waistband that reaches the belly button or higher. They can be skinny, straight leg, or wide leg, and come in a variety of washes and colors. High waisted jeans can be dressed up or down, making them a versatile addition to any wardrobe.

How to

When it comes to wearing high waisted jeans over 50, there are a few things to keep in mind. Here are some tips to help you rock this timeless style:

Choose the Right Fit

Just like any other pair of jeans, finding the right fit is key. Look for high waisted jeans that fit snugly around the waist and hips without being too tight. Avoid jeans that are too loose or baggy, as they can make you look frumpy.

Pair with the Right Top

When it comes to tops, you want to balance out the high waist of your jeans. Try pairing your high waisted jeans with a tucked-in blouse or sweater. This will help define your waist and create a flattering silhouette. You can also add a blazer or jacket for a polished look.

Accessorize Wisely

Accessories can make or break an outfit, so choose wisely. A statement necklace or earrings can add a pop of color and draw attention to your face. A belt can also help define your waist and add some interest to your outfit. Just be careful not to overdo it.

Wear the Right Shoes

The right shoes can make all the difference when it comes to wearing high waisted jeans. Try wearing heels to elongate your legs and create a more streamlined look. If you prefer flats, opt for pointed-toe styles, which can also help lengthen your legs.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide to wearing high waisted jeans over 50:

Step 1: Choose the Right Fit

Look for high waisted jeans that fit snugly around the waist and hips without being too tight. Make sure they’re comfortable to sit and move in.

Step 2: Pair with the Right Top

Try pairing your high waisted jeans with a tucked-in blouse or sweater. This will help define your waist and create a flattering silhouette.

Step 3: Accessorize Wisely

Add a statement necklace or earrings to draw attention to your face. A belt can also help define your waist and add some interest to your outfit.

Step 4: Wear the Right Shoes

Opt for heels or pointed-toe flats to elongate your legs and create a more streamlined look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for wearing high waisted jeans over 50:

  • Stick with classic washes like dark blue or black for a timeless look.
  • Avoid distressed or ripped styles, which can look too trendy.
  • Make sure your jeans hit at the right spot on your ankles. They should either hit right at the ankle or be slightly cropped.
  • Experiment with different styles, like wide leg or cropped jeans, to find what works best for your body type.

FAQ

Can I wear high waisted jeans if I have a belly?

Absolutely! High waisted jeans can actually be very flattering if you have a belly. Look for jeans with a wider waistband, which can help smooth and flatten your tummy.

What shoes should I wear with high waisted jeans?

Heels or pointed-toe flats can help elongate your legs and create a more streamlined look. Avoid shoes with ankle straps, as they can cut off your leg line.

Can I wear high waisted jeans with a tucked-in shirt?

Yes, a tucked-in shirt can actually be very flattering with high waisted jeans. Just make sure the shirt isn’t too bulky or doesn’t create too much volume around your waist.

Hair Care

When wearing high waisted jeans, it’s important to keep your hair in check. A sleek ponytail or bun can help elongate your neck and create a more streamlined look. If you prefer to wear your hair down, try adding some volume at the crown to balance out the high waist of your jeans.
