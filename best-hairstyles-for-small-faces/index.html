Best Hairstyles For Small Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a small face, it can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that will complement your features. However, with the right cut and style, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a look that is both stylish and flattering. In this article, we will take a look at some of the best hairstyles for small faces and provide you with tips on how to achieve them.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Small Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Creating the Perfect Hairstyle
  • Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • FAQs About Hairstyles for Small Faces
  • Hair Care for Small Faces

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Small Face

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for a small face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to create the illusion of length to balance out your features. This can be achieved by adding layers or volume to the top of your head.

Another thing to consider is your hair texture. If you have thin or fine hair, you may want to opt for a shorter cut that will create the illusion of thickness. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you can go for longer styles that will help to elongate your face.

Lastly, you want to consider your personal style and preferences. While there are certain hairstyles that work best for small faces, it's important to choose a style that you feel comfortable and confident wearing.

Step-by-Step Guide to Creating the Perfect Hairstyle

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a hairstyle for small faces, let's take a look at some of the best styles to try:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a popular choice for small faces because it is short and layered, which helps to create the illusion of length. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back while leaving the top longer. You can also add some texture with a razor or scissors for a more edgy look.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another great option for small faces. This style is versatile and can be customized to fit your face shape and hair type. For a small face, opt for a chin-length or shorter bob that is cut at an angle to create a longer appearance.

3. Layered Lob

The layered lob is a longer version of the bob that is perfect for small faces. This style features layers that start at the chin and gradually get longer towards the back. This creates a flattering shape that elongates the face and adds movement and texture to the hair.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

For a simple yet effective way to enhance your small face, try adding side-swept bangs to your hairstyle. This style helps to create the illusion of length and draws attention to your eyes, making them appear bigger and brighter.

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle for your small face, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

FAQs About Hairstyles for Small Faces

Q: Can I wear long hair if I have a small face?

A: Yes, you can wear long hair if you have a small face. However, it's important to choose a style that is layered and textured to create the illusion of length and balance out your features.

Q: What is the best way to style side-swept bangs?

A: To style side-swept bangs, use a round brush to blow-dry your hair and create volume. Then, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preferences. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less often, such as every 2-3 days.

Hair Care for Small Faces

Now that you know how to choose the best hairstyle for your small face and how to maintain it properly, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and trying out some of the best hairstyles for small faces, you can create a look that is both stylish and flattering. Remember to choose a style that you feel comfortable and confident wearing, and don't be afraid to experiment with different cuts and styles until you find the perfect one!
