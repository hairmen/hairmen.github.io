How To Cut Wispy Bangs For Curly Hair

Written by Eveline Jun 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Cutting wispy bangs for curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right technique and tools, it can be done easily. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut wispy bangs for curly hair in relaxed English language. Here’s a table of content to help you navigate through the article:

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to Cut Wispy Bangs for Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Wispy Bangs for Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Description

Wispy bangs are a great way to add some dimension to your hair and frame your face beautifully. However, cutting wispy bangs for curly hair can seem daunting. Curly hair tends to shrink when it dries, so it’s important to cut the bangs when the hair is dry to avoid cutting them too short. Additionally, the curls can make it difficult to achieve a straight line, so it’s crucial to use the right tools and technique.

How to Cut Wispy Bangs for Curly Hair

If you're wondering how to cut wispy bangs for curly hair, follow these simple steps:

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Allow it to air dry or use a diffuser to dry your curls.

Step 2: Comb your hair with a wide-toothed comb to detangle it. Then, section off the hair that you want to cut for your bangs. Make sure to create a clean part that’s straight.

Step 3: Use hair cutting scissors for best results. Regular scissors can cause split ends and uneven cuts. Hold the scissors vertically and make small, quick cuts to achieve the wispy look.

Step 4: Start cutting from the middle of the hair section and work your way towards the end. Cut the hair at an angle, making sure to avoid cutting too much at once. This will help you achieve a natural-looking wispy effect.

Step 5: Cut the bangs longer than you want them to be. Remember, curly hair tends to shrink when it dries, so it’s better to err on the side of caution. You can always go back and trim them later if needed.

Step 6: Once you’ve finished cutting the bangs, shake your hair out to see how they look. If you’re happy with the length, you’re done! If you want to make any adjustments, repeat the cutting process.

Tips for Cutting Wispy Bangs for Curly Hair

Here are some tips to make the process of cutting wispy bangs for curly hair easier:

  • Use hair cutting scissors for best results
  • Section off your hair when cutting to avoid uneven cuts
  • Start cutting from the middle and work your way towards the end
  • Cut the hair at an angle for a natural-looking wispy effect
  • Cut the bangs longer than you want them to be to avoid cutting them too short

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I cut my bangs when my hair is wet?

A: It’s best to cut your bangs when your hair is dry. Curly hair tends to shrink when it dries, so you may end up cutting them too short if you cut them when they’re wet.

Q: How often should I cut my bangs?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want your bangs to be. As a general rule, you should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Q: Can I cut my own bangs?

A: Yes, you can cut your own bangs, but it’s important to use the right tools and technique. If you’re not confident in your ability to cut your own bangs, it’s best to go to a professional stylist.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your curly hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized

Cutting wispy bangs for curly hair doesn’t have to be difficult. With the right technique and tools, you can achieve a beautiful, natural-looking result. Follow the steps and tips outlined in this article to get the perfect wispy bangs for your curly hair.
