What To Wear In 40 Degree Weather Camping

Written by Eveline Jul 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you are planning a camping trip in 40 degree weather, it is important to dress appropriately to stay warm and comfortable. In this article, we will discuss what to wear in 40 degree weather camping, including how to dress in layers, what fabrics to choose, and tips for staying warm and dry.

Table of Contents

  • How to Dress in Layers
  • What Fabrics to Choose
  • Tips for Staying Warm and Dry
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Dress in Layers

When camping in 40 degree weather, it is important to dress in layers to stay warm and comfortable throughout the day and night. Layering allows you to adjust your clothing based on the temperature and activity level. Here's how to dress in layers:

Base Layer

Your base layer should be made of a moisture-wicking fabric that will keep you dry and comfortable even if you sweat. Choose a lightweight long-sleeve shirt and pants made of synthetic materials or wool. Avoid cotton, as it retains moisture and can make you feel cold.

Insulating Layer

Your insulating layer should be made of a warm material that will trap heat close to your body. Fleece, down, or synthetic insulation are all good options. Choose a jacket or vest that fits snugly and can be easily removed if you start to feel too warm.

Outer Layer

Your outer layer should be windproof and waterproof to protect you from the elements. A jacket made of Gore-Tex or a similar material is a good choice. Look for a jacket with adjustable cuffs, a hood, and a high collar to keep the wind and rain out.

What Fabrics to Choose

Choosing the right fabrics is important when camping in 40 degree weather. Here are some fabrics to consider:

  • Synthetic fabrics, such as polyester and nylon, are lightweight and quick-drying.
  • Wool is warm, breathable, and naturally moisture-wicking.
  • Fleece is warm and lightweight, making it a good choice for insulating layers.
  • Down is very warm and compressible, but loses its insulating properties when wet.
  • Gore-Tex and similar materials are waterproof and windproof, but still breathable.

Tips for Staying Warm and Dry

Here are some tips for staying warm and dry when camping in 40 degree weather:

  • Bring extra layers, including socks, gloves, and a hat.
  • Stay dry by avoiding cotton and wearing waterproof outer layers.
  • Stay warm by eating high-energy foods and staying active.
  • Use a sleeping bag rated for cold temperatures and wear warm pajamas.
  • Bring a hat and gloves to wear while sleeping.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear jeans when camping in 40 degree weather?

A: While jeans are a popular choice for camping, they are not ideal for cold weather. Jeans are made of cotton, which retains moisture and can make you feel cold. Choose synthetic or wool pants instead.

Q: Do I need a waterproof jacket?

A: Yes, a waterproof jacket is essential when camping in 40 degree weather. Look for a jacket made of Gore-Tex or a similar material that is both waterproof and breathable.

Hair Care

When camping in 40 degree weather, it's important to protect your hair from the elements. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wear a hat to keep your head and hair warm.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Tie your hair back to prevent tangling and damage from wind.
  • Bring a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

By following these tips for what to wear in 40 degree weather camping, you can stay warm, dry, and comfortable on your next camping trip.
