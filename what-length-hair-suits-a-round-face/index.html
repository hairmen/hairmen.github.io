What Length Hair Suits A Round Face

Written by Bardi Nov 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you someone with a round face and wondering what length hair would suit you best? Well, you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss the best hair lengths for round faces, how to style them, and some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When it comes to choosing the right hair length for a round face, there are a few things to consider. First, you want to avoid styles that make your face look even rounder. Instead, you want a style that will elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. Second, you want to choose a length that complements your facial features and highlights your best features.

How To

The best hair lengths for round faces are typically those that fall just below the chin or longer. This length helps to elongate the face and create a more oval shape. Hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head or have layers that start at the jawline can also help to create the illusion of a longer face.

Short Hair

Short hair can work for a round face if it is cut in a way that adds height to the top of the head. A pixie cut with lots of layers or a short bob with choppy layers can both work well. Avoid blunt cuts that fall at or above the jawline, as this can make the face look wider.

Medium Hair

Medium length hair that falls just below the chin is a great option for those with round faces. This length helps to elongate the face and create a more oval shape. Adding layers can also help to add volume and movement to the hair.

Long Hair

Long hair can also work well for those with round faces, as long as it is layered and styled in a way that adds height to the top of the head. Layers that start at the jawline and continue down the length of the hair can help to create movement and add volume.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to choose the right hair length for a round face:

  1. Consider your facial features and what length will highlight your best features.
  2. Avoid styles that make your face look even rounder.
  3. Choose a length that falls just below the chin or longer.
  4. Add layers or volume to the top of the head to create the illusion of a longer face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hair length for a round face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that fall at or above the jawline.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and texture.
  • Choose a style that complements your personal style and lifestyle.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length for your face shape.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid styles that make your face look even rounder, such as blunt cuts that fall at or above the jawline or styles that add volume to the sides of the head.

Can short hair work for a round face?

Yes, short hair can work for a round face if it is cut in a way that adds height to the top of the head, such as a pixie cut with lots of layers or a short bob with choppy layers.

How do I know if I have a round face?

A round face is typically characterized by a wider forehead, rounded cheeks, and a rounded chin. The length and width of the face are approximately equal.

Hair Care

No matter what length hair you choose, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often to prevent damage.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know what length hair suits a round face, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new style. Remember to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
