Best Short Hair For Rectangular Face

Written by Petter Oct 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on the best short hair for rectangular face shapes. If you have a rectangular face shape, you may find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle, but don't worry; we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be discussing the best short haircuts for rectangular faces, how to style them, and some tips and tricks to keep your hair looking its best. So let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Rectangular face shapes are characterized by a long, narrow shape with a strong jawline and forehead. The goal of choosing a short haircut for a rectangular face shape is to balance out the length of the face and soften the strong lines of the jaw and forehead. Short haircuts that are textured or layered can add volume and movement to the hair, creating a more balanced look.

How to

The first step in choosing the best short haircut for a rectangular face shape is to identify your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length of your face from the forehead to the chin and the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have determined that you have a rectangular face shape, you can start looking for short haircuts that will flatter your features.

Step by Step

Here are some of the best short haircuts for rectangular face shapes:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that can work well for rectangular face shapes. This cut is short on the sides and back and longer on top, creating volume and movement. The textured layers can soften the strong lines of the jaw and forehead, creating a more balanced look.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another classic short haircut that can work well for rectangular face shapes. This cut is typically chin-length or shorter and can be styled with textured layers or blunt ends. The bob cut can add volume to the sides of the face, creating a more balanced look.

3. Shag Cut

The shag cut is a layered haircut that can work well for rectangular face shapes. This cut is typically shoulder-length or shorter and features layers that add volume and movement to the hair. The shag cut can soften the strong lines of the jaw and forehead, creating a more balanced look.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling short haircuts for rectangular face shapes:

- Use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add volume to the hair.

- Use a round brush to create volume and movement in the hair.

- Consider adding bangs to the haircut to soften the forehead.

- Use a straightening iron to create sleek, straight hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a short haircut if I have a rectangular face shape?

A: Yes! Short haircuts can work well for rectangular face shapes if they are styled correctly.

Q: What should I avoid when choosing a short haircut for a rectangular face shape?

A: Avoid haircuts that are too long or too short, as they can elongate the face or make it appear wider. Also, avoid haircuts with blunt edges, as they can accentuate the strong lines of the jaw and forehead.

Hair Care

To keep your short haircut looking its best, be sure to trim it regularly to maintain the shape. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated, and use a heat protectant spray when styling with hot tools. Also, be sure to protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf when outdoors.

Now that you know the best short haircuts for rectangular face shapes, it's time to book your next haircut! Remember to choose a style that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
