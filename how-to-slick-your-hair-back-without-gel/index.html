How To Slick Your Hair Back Without Gel A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Want to create a sleek and sophisticated look without using gel? You're in luck. In this article, we'll show you how to slick your hair back without gel, using simple techniques that are easy to master. From preparing your hair to styling it, we've got you covered. Read on to find out more.

Table of Contents

  • Preparing Your Hair
  • Choosing the Right Comb
  • Styling Your Hair
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

Preparing Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to prepare it properly. Here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any dirt, excess oil, and product buildup, leaving your hair clean and fresh. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it gently.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair. This will help to detangle your hair, reduce frizz, and add moisture. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. This will help to smooth your hair and create volume. Use the brush to lift your hair at the roots and direct the air flow downwards. Once your hair is dry, use a cool shot to set the style.


Choosing the Right Comb

The key to slicking your hair back without gel is to choose the right comb. Here's what you need to look for:

Step 1: Choose a Wide-Tooth Comb

Start by choosing a wide-tooth comb. This will help to detangle your hair without causing breakage. Make sure the teeth are spaced evenly and are not too sharp.

Step 2: Look for a Comb with Rounded Teeth

Next, look for a comb with rounded teeth. This will help to prevent your hair from getting caught or snagged, reducing the risk of damage.

Step 3: Avoid Plastic Combs

Avoid using plastic combs, as they can create static and damage your hair. Instead, opt for a comb made from natural materials, such as wood or horn.


Styling Your Hair

Now that your hair is prepared and you have the right comb, it's time to start styling. Here's how to slick your hair back without gel:

Step 1: Part Your Hair

Start by parting your hair where you want it. You can create a middle part or a side part, depending on your preference. Use the end of the comb to create a straight line.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair Back

Next, comb your hair back using the wide-tooth comb. Start at the front and work your way towards the back, smoothing your hair as you go. Make sure to comb your hair all the way to the ends to create a sleek look.

Step 3: Use a Hair Tie

If you have long hair, you can use a hair tie to keep it in place. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie. You can also use bobby pins to keep any loose strands in place.


Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

  • Use a pomade or hair wax to add shine and hold to your hair.
  • Create a deep side part for a more dramatic look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as a slicked back bun or ponytail.
  • Use a hairspray to set your style and reduce flyaways.

FAQs

Can I slick my hair back without gel?

Yes, you can slick your hair back without gel. Use a leave-in conditioner and a wide-tooth comb to create a sleek and sophisticated look.

What kind of comb should I use?

Use a wide-tooth comb with rounded teeth to prevent damage to your hair. Avoid plastic combs, as they can create static.

How do I keep my hair in place?

You can use a hair tie or bobby pins to keep your hair in place. You can also use a pomade or hair wax to add hold.


Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to follow a good hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a conditioner, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. You can also use a hair mask once a week to add moisture and nourishment to your hair.

Now that you know how to slick your hair back without gel, you can create a sleek and stylish look anytime, anywhere. Just remember to prepare your hair properly, choose the right comb, and follow our styling tips and tricks. Happy styling!
