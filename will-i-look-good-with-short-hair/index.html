Will I Look Good With Short Hair

Written by Aprilia Aug 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering chopping off your long locks and going for a shorter hairstyle? If so, you might be wondering, "Will I look good with short hair?" It's a common question that many women ask themselves before making the big chop. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to determine if short hair is right for you. We'll also give you some tips on how to care for your new hairstyle.

Description

Short hair is a popular hairstyle among women. It's a bold and daring look that can be both chic and edgy. However, not everyone can pull off this look. The key to a successful short hairstyle is finding the right cut that complements your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

How to Determine If Short Hair Is Right for You

Before you make the decision to chop off your hair, there are a few things you should consider:

1. Face Shape

One of the most important factors to consider when choosing a short hairstyle is your face shape. Certain cuts work better with certain face shapes. For example, a bob haircut looks great on women with oval, heart, and square-shaped faces. A pixie cut, on the other hand, is best suited for women with oval and heart-shaped faces.

2. Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another factor to consider when choosing a short hairstyle. If you have naturally curly hair, you might want to opt for a layered cut that will help to define your curls. If you have fine, straight hair, a blunt cut with some texture will add volume and dimension.

3. Personal Style

Your personal style is also important when choosing a short hairstyle. If you're someone who likes to keep things simple and low-maintenance, a pixie cut might be the right choice for you. If you're someone who likes to experiment with different styles and looks, a layered bob might be more your style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you determine if short hair is right for you:

Step 1: Consider Your Face Shape

Take a look at your face shape and determine which short hairstyles are best suited for you.

Step 2: Think About Your Hair Texture

Consider your hair texture and choose a cut that will work well with your natural hair.

Step 3: Look at Your Personal Style

Think about your personal style and choose a cut that will complement your fashion sense and personality.

Step 4: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist to get their opinion on what short hairstyle will work best for you.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle:

1. Look for Inspiration

Before you make the chop, look for inspiration online or in magazines. Find pictures of short hairstyles that you like and bring them to your hairstylist.

2. Consider the Maintenance

Short hair requires more maintenance than long hair. Make sure you're willing to commit to regular salon visits and styling your hair every day.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Once you've cut your hair, don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. Play around with different hair products and accessories to find a look that you love.

FAQ

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the cut. Certain short hairstyles can make your face look rounder, while others can help to elongate your face. Consult with your hairstylist to find a cut that will flatter your face shape.

Q: Will short hair make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. A well-chosen short hairstyle can actually make you look younger and more vibrant.

Hair Care

Here are a few tips on how to care for your new short hairstyle:

1. Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for short hair. Look for products that add volume, texture, and shine.

2. Style Your Hair Every Day

Short hair requires daily styling. Use a blow dryer, flat iron, or curling iron to create your desired look.

3. Visit Your Hairstylist Regularly

Visit your hairstylist every four to six weeks to maintain your short hairstyle.

In conclusion, whether or not you will look good with short hair depends on a variety of factors. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style before making the chop. Consult with your hairstylist to find a cut that will flatter your features. And once you've cut your hair, experiment with different styles and products to find a look that you love.
