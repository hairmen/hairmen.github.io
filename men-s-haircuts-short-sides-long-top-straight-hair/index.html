Short sides and long top hairstyles have been popular among men for quite some time now. This style is versatile and can be adapted to suit different hair types, including straight hair. In this guide, we'll discuss everything you need to know about men's haircuts short sides long top straight hair.

  • How To

Description

A men's haircut with short sides and long top typically involves shaving or trimming the hair on the sides of the head while keeping the hair on top long. This style can be adapted to suit different hair types, including straight hair. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in different ways, making it a popular choice among men.

How To

If you're interested in getting a men's haircut with short sides and long top straight hair, here's what you need to do:

  1. Find a reliable hair stylist who's experienced in cutting men's hair.
  2. Show them a picture of the style you want, or describe it to them in detail.
  3. Decide on the length of the hair on top and the sides, and communicate this clearly with your stylist.
  4. Ask your stylist for advice on how to style your hair at home.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style men's haircuts with short sides and long top straight hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as a hair gel or pomade, to your hair.
  4. Use a comb to style your hair, starting from the front and working your way back.
  5. Once you've styled your hair, use a blow dryer to dry it and set the style.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a men's haircut with short sides and long top straight hair:

  • Consider your face shape and hair type when deciding on the length of your hair.
  • Use a high-quality styling product to keep your hair in place.
  • Regularly trim the hair on the sides to maintain the style.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's haircuts with short sides and long top straight hair:

  • Is this style suitable for all hair types?
  • How often should I get the sides of my hair trimmed?
  • What should I do if I'm not happy with the way my hair has been cut?
  • What styling products should I use for this style?

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, too often.
  • Regularly trim the ends of your hair to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Overall, men's haircuts with short sides and long top straight hair are a popular and versatile style that can be adapted to suit different hair types. With the right styling products and techniques, you can keep your hair looking great and feeling healthy.
