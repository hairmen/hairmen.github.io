How To Wear Skinny Jeans In Your 40 S

Written by Bardi Nov 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you in your 40's and wondering how to wear skinny jeans without looking like you're trying too hard? Don't worry, you're not alone! In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to wear skinny jeans in your 40's while still looking stylish and age-appropriate.

Description

Skinny jeans have been a staple in fashion for over a decade now, and they're not going anywhere anytime soon. However, as we age, our bodies change, and what may have looked good on us in our 20's may not be as flattering in our 40's. But that doesn't mean we have to give up skinny jeans altogether. With a few simple styling tips, you can wear skinny jeans in your 40's and look fabulous.

How to

Here are some steps you can follow to wear skinny jeans in your 40's:

Step 1: Choose the Right Fit

When it comes to skinny jeans, fit is everything. Look for a pair that fits snugly but not too tight. Avoid low-rise styles, as they can be unflattering on most body types, and opt for a mid-rise or high-rise fit instead.

Step 2: Pair with Classic Pieces

To keep your look age-appropriate, pair your skinny jeans with classic pieces such as a white button-down shirt, a blazer, or a trench coat. These timeless pieces will elevate your outfit and give it a sophisticated touch.

Step 3: Accessorize Wisely

Accessorizing can take your outfit from basic to stylish. However, it's important to do it wisely. Avoid trendy accessories that scream "I'm trying too hard" and opt for classic pieces such as a statement necklace or a structured handbag.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when wearing skinny jeans in your 40's:

  • Choose a darker wash for a more slimming effect.
  • Avoid distressed or ripped styles, as they can be too casual for most occasions.
  • Pair with heels or ankle boots to elongate your legs.
  • Avoid tucking in your shirt, as it can draw attention to your midsection.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear skinny jeans if I'm not a size 2?

A: Absolutely! Skinny jeans come in all sizes, and there's no "right" body type for them. The key is to find a pair that fits well and makes you feel confident.

Q: Can I wear skinny jeans to work?

A: It depends on your workplace dress code. If your office is more casual, you can definitely wear skinny jeans with a blazer or a sweater. However, if your workplace is more formal, you may want to stick to tailored pants or a skirt.

Q: Can I wear skinny jeans with flats?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure the flats are sleek and not too chunky, as they can make your legs look shorter.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair, wearing your hair down with skinny jeans is a classic and effortless look. If you want to add some volume to your hair, try using a volumizing spray or mousse before blow-drying. You can also add some loose waves with a curling iron for a more polished look.

Now that you know how to wear skinny jeans in your 40's, go ahead and rock them with confidence!
