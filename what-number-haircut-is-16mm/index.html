What Number Haircut Is 16Mm

Written by Robby Oct 15, 2023 · 3 min read

Welcome to our blog post about "what number haircut is 16mm". If you're looking for a specific haircut measurement, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll discuss what number haircut is 16mm, how to achieve this hairstyle, step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and how to take care of your hair after the cut.

Description

16mm is equivalent to 5/8 inches, which is the length of the hair on your head after a haircut. This is a popular haircut length for men who want a short, clean-cut look. It can also be a good option for women who want to keep their hair short without going for a pixie cut.

How to Achieve a 16mm Haircut

If you want to achieve a 16mm haircut, you'll need to use a clipper guard number 4. This guard will leave your hair 5/8 inches long, which is equivalent to 16mm. You can also achieve this haircut with scissors, but it may take longer and require more precision.

Step by Step Instructions

Here are the step by step instructions for achieving a 16mm haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Attach a clipper guard number 4 to your clipper.
  3. Starting at the back of your head, run the clipper up towards the crown, going against the grain of your hair.
  4. Continue to use the clipper around the sides and top of your head until all the hair is the same length.
  5. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or to create a more precise look.
  6. Use a comb to check for any uneven spots and trim as needed.
  7. Finish by styling your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips for achieving a great 16mm haircut:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use a mirror to check for any uneven spots.
  • Have someone else help you if needed.
  • Practice on a mannequin head before attempting to cut your own hair.
  • Invest in a quality clipper and guard set.

FAQs

Q: Is a 16mm haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, a 16mm haircut can work for all hair types, but it may look different depending on the texture and thickness of your hair.

Q: Can I achieve a 16mm haircut with scissors?

A: Yes, you can achieve a 16mm haircut with scissors, but it may take longer and require more precision.

Q: How often should I get a 16mm haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your haircut. Generally, a 16mm haircut can last for 4-6 weeks before needing to be trimmed again.

Hair Care

After getting a 16mm haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and health. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape.
  • Use hair products, such as gel or wax, to style your hair as desired.

Now that you know what number haircut is 16mm, you can achieve this stylish and versatile look with ease. Remember to take care of your hair after the cut to maintain its health and shape. Happy styling!
