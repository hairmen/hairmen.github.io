Medium Top Fade Sides A Classic Men s Haircut

Written by Daniel Sep 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and classic haircut that will never go out of style? Look no further than the medium top fade sides. This haircut has been popular for decades and continues to be a favorite among men of all ages. In this blog post, we will discuss how to achieve the perfect medium top fade sides, tips for maintaining the look, and answer some commonly asked questions about this haircut.

How to Achieve the Perfect Medium Top Fade Sides

The medium top fade sides haircut is a versatile style that can be customized to fit your individual preferences. Here's how to achieve the perfect look:

Step 1: Choose Your Desired Length

The first step is to decide how long you want the hair on the top of your head to be. This can range from a short buzz cut to several inches of length. Once you have decided on the length, your barber will trim the hair to the desired length and shape it to fit your head.

Step 2: Fade the Sides

The next step is to fade the sides of the head. This means gradually blending the hair from short to long, creating a seamless transition from the top to the sides. The fade can be low, medium, or high, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Style the Top

Once the top and sides are cut and faded, it's time to style the top. This can be done with a variety of techniques, including blow-drying, combing, or using hair products like pomade or wax. Experiment with different styles to find the look that works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Now that you have achieved the perfect medium top fade sides, it's important to maintain the look to keep it looking fresh and clean. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Visit Your Barber Regularly

To keep the fade looking sharp, it's important to visit your barber regularly for touch-ups. Depending on how quickly your hair grows, this may be every few weeks or every few months.

Tip 2: Use Quality Hair Products

Invest in quality hair products like pomade or wax to keep the top looking styled and in place. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh the hair down and make it look greasy.

Tip 3: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to avoid buildup and keep the hair looking and feeling clean.

FAQ

Q: Is the medium top fade sides suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, the medium top fade sides can be customized to fit any hair type and texture.

Q: How long does it take to achieve the perfect medium top fade sides?

A: The time it takes to achieve the perfect medium top fade sides depends on a variety of factors, including the length and texture of your hair and the skill level of your barber. It can take anywhere from 30 minutes to a few hours.

Q: Can I style the top of the hair differently?

A: Yes, the top of the hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a part.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking and feeling its best, it's important to follow a proper hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo, using quality hair products, and visiting your barber regularly for touch-ups. With the right care and attention, your medium top fade sides will look sharp and stylish for years to come.
