How to Apply Serum to Curly Hair

Step 2: Squeeze Out Excess Water

Step 3: Apply Serum to Your Hair

Take a small amount of serum and rub it between your palms. Work the serum through your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. Be sure to apply the serum evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

After applying the serum, you can style your hair as desired. You can let it air dry, use a diffuser to dry it with a blow dryer, or use a curling iron to define your curls even further.

Tips for Applying Serum to Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you apply serum to your curly hair:

  • Use a small amount of serum to avoid weighing down your curls.
  • Apply the serum to wet hair to help distribute it evenly.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls after applying the serum.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much after applying the serum to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
