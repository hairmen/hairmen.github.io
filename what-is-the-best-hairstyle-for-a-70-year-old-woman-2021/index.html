What Is The Best Hairstyle For A 70 Year Old Woman 2021

Written by Daniel Nov 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner, drier, and more prone to breakage. This makes it crucial to choose a hairstyle that not only flatters your face shape but also suits your hair texture and lifestyle. So, what is the best hairstyle for a 70-year-old woman in 2021? Let's explore some options.

Description

At the age of 70, many women experience hair loss and thinning, which can make styling a bit challenging. However, with the right hairstyle, you can still look chic and stylish. The ideal hairstyle should be easy to maintain, complement your face shape, and reflect your personality.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle?

Before choosing a hairstyle, you need to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips to help you choose the best hairstyle for you:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Hairstyles that complement your face shape can enhance your natural beauty. The four main face shapes are round, square, oval, and heart-shaped. To determine your face shape, measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair also plays a vital role in determining the best hairstyle for you. If you have thin hair, consider a short bob or a layered cut. If you have thick hair, a shoulder-length cut or a long layered cut will suit you.

Step 3: Think about Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle also plays a crucial role in choosing the best hairstyle. If you have an active lifestyle, consider a short and low-maintenance hairstyle. If you prefer a more sophisticated look, a medium-length or long hairstyle might be more suitable.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Best Hairstyle

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you choose the best hairstyle for a 70-year-old woman in 2021:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your features.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Thin hair requires a shorter haircut or a layered cut. Thick hair can carry a longer hairstyle or a shoulder-length cut.

Step 3: Think about Your Lifestyle

If you have an active lifestyle, a low-maintenance short hairstyle might be more suitable. If you prefer a more elegant look, a medium-length or long hairstyle might be more suitable.

Step 4: Consider Your Personality

Your hairstyle should reflect your personality. If you are adventurous, consider a bold and trendy haircut. If you prefer a more classic look, a timeless haircut will be perfect.

Tips for Choosing the Best Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the best hairstyle:

  • Consult with a professional stylist who can help you choose the best hairstyle for your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.
  • Consider adding layers to your haircut to add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Experiment with different haircuts and styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn't require too much styling or upkeep.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the best hairstyle for a 70-year-old woman:

What is the best haircut for thinning hair?

A short bob or a layered cut is a great option for thinning hair as it adds volume and texture to your hair.

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

A layered cut or a pixie cut is a great option for a round face as it adds volume and texture to your hair.

What is the best hairstyle for an active lifestyle?

A short, low-maintenance hairstyle like a pixie cut or a bob is perfect for an active lifestyle as it requires minimal styling and upkeep.

Hair Care Tips for 70-Year-Old Women

As we age, our hair becomes thinner, drier, and more prone to breakage. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy and beautiful hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like flat irons, curling irons, and hot rollers.
  • Limit your use of chemical treatments like perms and hair dyes.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Get regular trims to remove split ends and maintain the health of your hair.

Conclusion

Choosing the best hairstyle for a 70-year-old woman in 2021 requires considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. With the right hairstyle, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident and stylish. Remember to consult with a professional stylist and experiment with different haircuts and styles to find the one that suits you best.
