How Do You Thin Your Hair With Thinning Shears

Written by Oliver Sep 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have thick or dense hair, you may want to consider thinning it with thinning shears. Thinning shears are a type of scissors that have teeth on one of its blades. This helps to remove some of the bulk from your hair without sacrificing the length.

Description

Thinning shears can be used for various hair types, but they are particularly useful for those who have thick or curly hair. By thinning out some of the hair, it can help to make it more manageable and easier to style. Thinning shears can also add texture and volume to your hair.

How to Thin Your Hair with Thinning Shears

Before you start thinning your hair with thinning shears, it's important to make sure that your hair is clean and dry. Start by combing your hair and determining which areas need to be thinned out. You can then follow these steps:

Step 1: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips or hair ties. This will help you to work on one section at a time, making it easier to thin out your hair evenly.

Step 2: Start Thinning Your Hair

Hold the thinning shears at a slight angle and start to cut into the hair. Make sure that you are cutting only a small amount of hair at a time, as you don't want to remove too much hair all at once. Work your way through each section until you have thinned out your hair to your desired level.

Step 3: Blend the Hair

Once you have thinned out your hair, you may notice that there are some uneven areas. To fix this, use regular scissors to blend the hair and create a more natural look.

Tips for Thinning Your Hair with Thinning Shears

Thinning your hair with thinning shears can be tricky, so here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Always use sharp thinning shears to prevent damaging your hair
  • Start with a small amount of hair and work your way up
  • Never cut your hair when it's wet as it can lead to uneven results
  • Blend your hair with regular scissors to create a natural look
  • Don't over thin your hair as it can lead to split ends and breakage

FAQ

Can thinning shears be used on all hair types?

While thinning shears can be used on all hair types, they are particularly useful for those with thick or curly hair.

Will thinning my hair with thinning shears make it look thinner?

Thinning your hair with thinning shears will remove some of the bulk, but it won't make it look thinner. It can, however, make it more manageable and easier to style.

Hair Care

After thinning your hair with thinning shears, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage. Regular trims can also help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
