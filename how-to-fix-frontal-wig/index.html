How To Fix Frontal Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Wearing a frontal wig can be a great way to change up your look, but it can also be frustrating when it doesn't stay in place. Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll walk you through how to fix frontal wig, step-by-step. We'll also provide some tips and answer some frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Fix Frontal Wig
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Fixing Frontal Wig
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Frontal Wig

How to Fix Frontal Wig

Before we get started, let's talk about some of the things you'll need to fix your frontal wig:

  • Wig clips
  • Adhesive tape or glue
  • Scissors
  • Wig cap
  • Comb or brush

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you put on your frontal wig, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can also braid your hair or put it in a bun to make it easier to put on the wig.

Step 2: Put on a Wig Cap

Put on a wig cap to keep your hair in place and to prevent the wig from sliding around. The wig cap should fit snugly on your head.

Step 3: Apply Adhesive

If you're using adhesive tape, measure out the length you'll need and cut it with scissors. Apply the adhesive tape to the perimeter of the wig cap. If you're using glue, apply a thin layer of glue to the perimeter of the wig cap.

Step 4: Attach the Wig

Line up the wig with your hairline and press it down onto the adhesive tape or glue. Use a comb or brush to smooth out any bumps or lumps.

Step 5: Add Wig Clips

If you want extra security, you can add wig clips to the wig. Use scissors to make small holes in the wig cap where you want to attach the clips. Then, sew the clips onto the wig cap.

Tips for Fixing Frontal Wig

  • Use a wig cap to keep your hair in place and to prevent the wig from sliding around.
  • Apply adhesive tape or glue to the perimeter of the wig cap to keep the wig in place.
  • Use wig clips for extra security.
  • Don't apply too much glue or adhesive tape, as this can damage the wig and your natural hair.
  • Be gentle when combing or brushing the wig, as it can easily become tangled or damaged.

FAQs

How long will the wig stay in place?

The length of time the wig stays in place depends on the type of adhesive used and how active you are. Some adhesives can last up to two weeks, while others may need to be reapplied after a few days.

Can I swim or shower with the wig on?

We do not recommend swimming or showering with the wig on, as this can damage the wig and the adhesive. If you need to wash your wig, take it off and wash it separately.

How do I remove the wig?

To remove the wig, gently lift the edges and peel it off. If there is any residue left from the adhesive, use a wig adhesive remover to remove it.

Hair Care for Frontal Wig

To keep your frontal wig looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash the wig regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Hang the wig up to air dry.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on the wig, as this can damage the fibers.
  • Store the wig on a mannequin head or wig stand to maintain its shape.

Now that you know how to fix frontal wig, you can confidently wear your wig without worrying about it sliding around. Remember to always take good care of your wig to keep it looking its best.
