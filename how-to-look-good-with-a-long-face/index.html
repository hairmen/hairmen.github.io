How To Look Good With A Long Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Nov 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a long face, you may feel like it's difficult to find hairstyles and makeup looks that flatter your features. However, with the right tips and tricks, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance. In this article, we'll walk you through how to look good with a long face.

Hair Care

Before we dive into hairstyles for long faces, it's important to take care of your hair. Long faces can sometimes be associated with thin, fine hair, so it's essential to nourish your locks to keep them healthy and full. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils and make it appear limp.
  • Invest in a good hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Try using a hair mask or treatment once a week to hydrate and strengthen your hair.

Understanding Face Shapes

Before choosing a hairstyle or makeup look, it's important to understand your face shape. Long faces are characterized by a lengthier shape with a high forehead, prominent chin, and narrow width. Other face shapes include round, square, oval, and heart-shaped.

Knowing your face shape can help you choose hairstyles and makeup looks that flatter your features and balance out any asymmetry. If you're unsure about your face shape, try using a measuring tape to measure the length and width of your face.

Hairstyles for Long Faces

When it comes to hairstyles for long faces, the goal is to create width and volume around the sides of your face. Here are some hairstyles to try:

  • Layers: Layers are a great way to add volume and movement to your hair. Ask your hairstylist for face-framing layers that start at your cheekbones.
  • Bangs: Bangs can help shorten the appearance of a long face. Consider getting side-swept bangs or blunt bangs that hit right above your eyebrows.
  • Bobs: Bobs are a classic hairstyle that can work well for long faces. Opt for a chin-length bob that adds width to your face.
  • Curls: Curly hair can add volume and bounce to your hair. Consider using a curling iron or hot rollers to add curls to the sides of your face.

Makeup Tips for Long Faces

When it comes to makeup for long faces, the goal is to balance out your features and create symmetry. Here are some makeup tips to try:

  • Contouring: Use a contouring powder or cream to create shadows around the sides of your face. This will help create the illusion of width and balance out your features.
  • Blush: Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks to create a rounder, softer appearance.
  • Eyebrows: Fill in your eyebrows to create a more defined shape. This will help frame your face and balance out any asymmetry.
  • Lipstick: Choose a lipstick color that complements your skin tone and adds balance to your face. Avoid bold, bright colors that can overpower your features.

Fashion Tips for Long Faces

When it comes to fashion for long faces, the goal is to create balance and proportion. Here are some fashion tips to try:

  • V-necklines: V-necklines can help create the illusion of a shorter face. Consider wearing tops and dresses with V-necklines to balance out your features.
  • Accessories: Use accessories like statement necklaces and earrings to draw attention to your neck and shoulders. This will help balance out your features and create proportion.
  • Belts: Use belts to cinch in your waist and create a more defined shape. This will help balance out your features and create proportion.
  • Prints: Wear prints and patterns that add width to your frame. Avoid vertical stripes, which can elongate your appearance.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, as this can make your face appear longer. Avoid center parts and straight, blunt cuts that can also elongate your face.

What makeup looks should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid heavy contouring that creates harsh lines around the sides of your face. Also, avoid bold, dark lipstick colors that can overpower your features.

Can I still wear a ponytail if I have a long face?

Absolutely! Just make sure to add volume to the sides of your face to create balance. Consider adding a few loose strands around your face to soften the look.



Looking good with a long face is all about creating balance and proportion. By following these tips and tricks, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance.
