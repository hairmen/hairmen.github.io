What Hairstyle Suits Your Face Shape A Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape. Different hairstyles can flatter different face shapes, enhancing your features and giving you a more polished look. In this article, we'll be discussing what hairstyle suits face shape, and how to choose the perfect hairstyle for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Choose The Perfect Hairstyle
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips And Tricks

Description

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape can make all the difference in how you look and feel. The right haircut can accentuate your best features and make you feel confident and beautiful. On the other hand, choosing the wrong hairstyle can make you feel self-conscious and frumpy.

How To Choose The Perfect Hairstyle

The first step in choosing the perfect hairstyle for your face shape is to determine what your face shape is. There are four main face shapes: round, square, oval, and heart. Each face shape has its own unique characteristics and requires a different approach to styling.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you want to create the illusion of length and height. A shoulder-length cut with layers that fall below the chin is a great option. You can also add some volume to the top of your head with a side-parted bob or a messy updo.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you want to soften your strong jawline. A long, layered cut with soft waves is a great option. You can also add some volume to the top of your head with a messy updo or a side-parted bob.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! You can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, a long, layered cut with soft waves is a great option. You can also add some volume to the top of your head with a messy updo or a side-parted bob.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, you want to balance your forehead with your chin. A long, layered cut with soft waves is a great option. You can also add some volume to the top of your head with a messy updo or a side-parted bob.

Step By Step Guide

Now that you know your face shape and what hairstyles work best for you, it's time to choose the perfect hairstyle. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Research hairstyles that work well with your face shape
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best haircut for you
  4. Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair
  5. Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and fits your lifestyle
  6. Invest in quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and looking great

Tips And Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a hairstyle
  • Choose a hairstyle that works well with your natural hair part
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny
  • Don't be afraid to try something new!

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about what hairstyle suits face shape:

Q: Can I pull off a short haircut if I have a round face?

A: Yes, a short haircut can work well with a round face shape. However, you'll want to choose a cut that elongates your face and creates the illusion of height.

Q: What's the best way to style a messy updo?

A: The key to a great messy updo is to keep it loose and relaxed. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair, then loosely pin your hair up in a bun or chignon.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of natural oils
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot styling tools
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated

Choosing the perfect hairstyle for your face shape can be a daunting task, but with a little research and some help from a professional hairstylist, you can find the perfect cut that flatters your features and makes you feel beautiful.
