How To Wear A Maxi Dress That Is Too Short

Written by Eveline Sep 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Maxi dresses are perfect for any occasion! They are comfortable, versatile, and fashionable. However, sometimes we may find ourselves with a maxi dress that is too short. Don't worry, there are several ways to wear a maxi dress that is too short and still look stylish. In this article, we will show you how to wear a maxi dress that is too short in relaxed English language. Let's get started!

How to wear a maxi dress that is too short

Step by step

Step 1: Wear leggings or tights
If your maxi dress is too short, you can wear leggings or tights underneath it. Not only will this add some coverage, but it will also keep you warm in cooler weather. You can choose to wear black leggings or tights for a classic look or opt for colored ones to add some fun to your outfit.

Step 2: Pair with ankle boots
Ankle boots are perfect for wearing with a maxi dress that is too short. They add some height and provide additional coverage. You can choose to wear flat or heeled ankle boots depending on your preference.

Step 3: Add a belt
If you have a maxi dress that is too short, you can try adding a belt to cinch your waist. This will make your dress look more fitted and add some definition to your silhouette. You can choose a thin or thick belt depending on your preference.

Step 4: Layer with a jacket or cardigan
You can layer your maxi dress with a jacket or cardigan to add some warmth and coverage. You can choose a denim jacket for a casual look or a blazer for a more formal look. A cardigan is also a great option for a cozy and comfortable outfit.

Tips

  • Choose a maxi dress that is made of a lightweight fabric such as cotton or chiffon. This will make it easier to layer and add more coverage.
  • Wear a statement necklace or earrings to draw attention away from the length of your dress.
  • Accessorize with a scarf or hat for a fun and fashionable look.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a maxi dress that is too short to a formal event?
A: Yes, you can wear a maxi dress that is too short to a formal event. Just make sure to add some heels and a statement piece of jewelry to dress it up.

Q: Can I wear a maxi dress that is too short to work?
A: It depends on your workplace's dress code. If your workplace is more casual, you can wear a maxi dress that is too short with leggings and ankle boots. However, if your workplace has a formal dress code, it may not be appropriate.

Hair Care

When wearing a maxi dress that is too short, your hair can make a big difference in completing your look. Depending on the occasion and your personal style, you can choose to wear your hair up or down.

Updo: An updo is perfect for a formal event or when you want to show off your statement earrings. You can choose a sleek bun, a messy braid, or a chic ponytail.

Down: Wearing your hair down is perfect for a casual and effortless look. You can leave your hair straight, add some waves, or create a half-up half-down style.

Now that you know how to wear a maxi dress that is too short, you can confidently rock this versatile piece of clothing. Remember to have fun with your outfit and add your personal touch to make it unique and stylish!
