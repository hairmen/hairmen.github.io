Cute Easy Hairstyles For Mid Length Hair

Written by Christine Aug 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you in search of cute and easy hairstyles for your mid length hair? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with some fantastic and chic hairstyles that are easy to do and perfect for any occasion. Whether you have a busy day ahead or need a quick fix for a night out, these hairstyles are perfect for you. So, let's get started!

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips

Description

Mid length hair is versatile and can be styled in many ways. From braids to updos, there is no shortage of options. The best part? These hairstyles are easy to achieve and won't take up too much of your time. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there is a hairstyle that will work for you. So, let's take a look at some of the best cute easy hairstyles for mid length hair!

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's go over some basic tips on how to style your mid length hair. First, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Second, use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools. Third, invest in some good quality hair accessories like bobby pins, hair ties, and clips. Fourth, don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and techniques.

Step by step

The Messy Bun

The messy bun is perfect for those days when you don't have time to wash your hair or want to add some volume to your hair. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.
  2. Twist the ponytail around the hair tie and secure it with bobby pins.
  3. Take out some pieces of hair around your face to create a messy look.
  4. Spray some hairspray to hold the look in place.

The Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out or a night out. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Take two sections of hair from the front of your head.
  2. Tie the two sections together at the back of your head.
  3. Take another section of hair from the back and tie it together with the previous two sections.
  4. Let the rest of your hair down and use a curling iron to create some loose waves.
  5. Spray some hairspray to hold the look in place.

The French Twist

The French twist is a classic and elegant hairstyle that is perfect for any formal event. Here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it upwards.
  2. Secure the twist with bobby pins.
  3. Take any loose ends and tuck them into the twist.
  4. Use hairspray to hold the look in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your mid length hair:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Invest in a good quality brush and comb to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair products like mousse, hairspray, and dry shampoo to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to try out different hairstyles and techniques. Practice makes perfect!

FAQ

Q: Can I do these hairstyles with curly hair?

A: Yes! These hairstyles can be done with any hair type, including curly hair. Just make sure to use a curl defining cream and diffuser to enhance your curls.

Q: How long does it take to do these hairstyles?

A: These hairstyles can take anywhere from 5 to 20 minutes, depending on the complexity of the style and your hair type.

Q: Do I need any special tools or accessories to achieve these hairstyles?

A: No, you don't need any special tools or accessories. However, investing in some good quality bobby pins, hair ties, and clips can make styling your hair easier.

Hair Care

Lastly, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some basic tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, these cute easy hairstyles for mid length hair are perfect for any occasion and can be achieved in just a few minutes. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Have fun experimenting with different hairstyles and techniques!
