How To Cover Eyebrow Hair With Makeup A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your unruly eyebrow hair ruining your makeup look? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will guide you through the process of covering eyebrow hair with makeup, using simple and easy-to-understand steps.

Description

Covering eyebrow hair with makeup has become a popular trend in recent years. This technique can be used to achieve a more defined and polished look, especially for those with sparse or unruly eyebrow hair. By using makeup, you can fill in gaps, add shape, and create a natural-looking brow that complements your face.

How to Cover Eyebrow Hair with Makeup

Follow these steps to cover eyebrow hair with makeup:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you start, make sure you have the necessary supplies. You will need an eyebrow pencil or powder that matches your hair color, a spoolie brush, and a small angled brush.

Step 2: Brush Your Eyebrow Hair

Using a spoolie brush, comb your eyebrow hair upwards and outwards. This will help you see the natural shape of your eyebrow and any gaps that need to be filled in.

Step 3: Fill in Any Gaps

Using short, light strokes, fill in any gaps in your eyebrow hair with an eyebrow pencil or powder. Start at the inner corner of your eyebrow and work your way outwards, following the natural shape of your eyebrow.

Step 4: Define Your Eyebrow Shape

Using the same eyebrow pencil or powder, outline the shape of your eyebrow. Start at the arch and work your way towards the tail of your eyebrow. Make sure the shape is symmetrical on both eyebrows.

Step 5: Blend the Color

Using a small angled brush, blend the color of the eyebrow pencil or powder into your eyebrow hair. This will create a natural-looking brow and prevent any harsh lines.

Step 6: Set Your Eyebrows

Using a clear or tinted brow gel, set your eyebrows in place. This will keep your eyebrow hair in place throughout the day and prevent any smudging or fading of the makeup.

Tips for Covering Eyebrow Hair with Makeup

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect brow:

  • Choose an eyebrow pencil or powder that matches your hair color. If you have blonde hair, choose a lighter shade. If you have dark hair, choose a darker shade.
  • Use short, light strokes when filling in your eyebrow hair. This will create a natural-looking brow.
  • When outlining the shape of your eyebrow, use a light hand. You can always go back and darken the lines if needed.
  • Blend the color of the eyebrow pencil or powder into your eyebrow hair for a natural look.
  • Set your eyebrows with a clear or tinted brow gel to keep your eyebrow hair in place.

FAQ

What is the best way to fill in eyebrow hair?

The best way to fill in eyebrow hair is to use an eyebrow pencil or powder that matches your hair color. Use short, light strokes to fill in any gaps and create a natural-looking brow.

What is the difference between an eyebrow pencil and powder?

An eyebrow pencil is a wax-based product that is used to fill in gaps and define the shape of your eyebrow. An eyebrow powder is a loose or pressed powder that can be used to fill in gaps and create a more natural-looking brow.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your eyebrow hair to ensure it stays healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy eyebrow hair:

  • Avoid over-plucking or waxing your eyebrows. This can damage the hair follicles and cause the hair to stop growing.
  • Use a gentle cleanser and moisturizer on your eyebrows to keep them clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your eyebrows, as this can cause damage to the hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, you can achieve the perfect brow and maintain healthy eyebrow hair.
