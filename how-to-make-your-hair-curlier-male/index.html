How To Make Your Hair Curlier Male Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Nov 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a man who wants to achieve curly hair, you may be wondering how to make your hair curlier male. While straight hair is often admired, curly hair can also be a great look that adds personality and style to your appearance. In this article, we'll explore some tips and tricks to help you achieve the curly hair you desire.

How to Make Your Hair Curlier Male: Description

Curly hair is hair that has a natural curl or wave to it. However, if you have straight hair, you may be wondering how to create curls. There are a few methods you can use to make your hair curlier, including using styling products, heat styling, and getting a perm.

How to Make Your Hair Curlier Male: How to

Using Styling Products

If you want to make your hair curlier without using heat or chemicals, you can try using styling products. Start with clean, damp hair, and apply a curl-enhancing product such as a mousse or curl cream. Work the product through your hair with your fingers, scrunching as you go. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair while scrunching it with your hands.

Heat Styling

If you want more defined curls, you can use heat styling tools such as a curling iron or wand. Apply a heat protectant product to your hair before styling. Divide your hair into sections, and wrap each section around the barrel of the curling iron or wand. Hold for a few seconds, then release. Continue until you've curled all of your hair. Use hairspray to set the curls.

Getting a Perm

If you want long-lasting curls, you can get a perm. A perm is a chemical treatment that changes the structure of your hair to create curls. It's important to go to a professional stylist for a perm, as the chemicals can be damaging if not applied correctly. The stylist will apply a solution to your hair, then wrap it around rods. After a set amount of time, the rods will be removed, and your hair will be rinsed and styled.

How to Make Your Hair Curlier Male: Step by Step

Step 1: Clean Your Hair

Start with clean, damp hair. Shampoo and condition your hair, then towel dry it.

Step 2: Apply Product

If you're using a styling product, apply it to your hair, working it through with your fingers. If you're using heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant product.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

For styling products, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair while scrunching it with your hands. For heat styling, divide your hair into sections and curl each section with a curling iron or wand.

Step 4: Set Your Hair

Use hairspray to set your curls in place.

How to Make Your Hair Curlier Male: Tips

  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without creating frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair for added moisture.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can create frizz.
  • If you're getting a perm, make sure to follow the aftercare instructions provided by your stylist.

How to Make Your Hair Curlier Male: FAQ

Can I make my hair curlier without using heat or chemicals?

Yes, you can use styling products such as a curl-enhancing mousse or cream to make your hair curlier without using heat or chemicals.

How long will my curls last?

The longevity of your curls will depend on the method you use. Styling products and heat styling will create temporary curls that will last until your next shampoo. A perm will create longer-lasting curls that can last for several months.

How to Make Your Hair Curlier Male: Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized, and use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet. Avoid using a brush on your dry hair, as this can create frizz.

Now that you know how to make your hair curlier male, you can try out these tips and tricks to achieve the curly hair you desire. Whether you prefer a natural look or defined curls, there's a method that will work for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
