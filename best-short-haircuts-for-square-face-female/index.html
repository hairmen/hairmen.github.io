Best Short Haircuts For Square Face Female A Guide For 2023

Written by Bardi Nov 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a square-faced female looking for the perfect short haircut? Look no further! In this guide, we’ll explore the best short haircuts for square face females. We’ll cover everything from how to determine your face shape to tips for maintaining your new cut.

Table of Contents:

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Best Short Haircuts for Square Face Females
  • How to Style Your Short Haircut
  • Tips for Maintenance
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Short Haircuts

Determining Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best short haircuts for square face females, it’s important to determine your face shape. Here’s how:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Use a hair clip or tie to keep your hair away from your face.
  3. Use a lipstick or washable marker to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  4. Step back and look at the shape you’ve traced.

If your face shape is square, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline will be roughly the same width.

Best Short Haircuts for Square Face Females

Now that you know your face shape, let’s explore the best short haircuts for square face females:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that works well for square face females. This cut is short on the sides and back and longer on top, creating a soft, feminine look.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another great option for square face females. This cut can be angled or straight, and can be styled with or without bangs.

Short Shag

The short shag is a trendy and versatile haircut that works well for square face females. This cut is layered and textured, creating movement and volume.

How to Style Your Short Haircut

Now that you’ve found the perfect short haircut for your square face, it’s time to learn how to style it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your roots.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and definition.
  • Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintenance

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove product buildup.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools every day to prevent damage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for square face females:

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look wider?

A: No, a properly styled short haircut can actually create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with a short haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with a short haircut. Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It’s best to wash your short hair every other day to prevent over-drying.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require a different hair care routine than longer hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent over-drying.
  • Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat tools.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you’re sure to find the perfect short haircut for your square face. Remember to maintain your cut with regular trims and a healthy hair care routine for best results.
