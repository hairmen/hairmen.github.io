What Hair Color Suits Me Male A Guide For 2023

Written by Aprilia Jun 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As a man, deciding what hair color suits you can be a challenging task. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this guide, we will help you find the perfect hair color that will suit your skin tone, personality, and style. Read on to learn more.

Description

The first step in deciding what hair color suits you is to understand your skin tone. Skin tones are classified into three categories: warm, cool, and neutral. Warm skin tones have yellow, peach, or golden undertones. Cool skin tones have pink, red, or blue undertones. Neutral skin tones have a mix of both warm and cool undertones.

How to

To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If they appear both blue and green, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step by Step

Once you have determined your skin tone, you can now choose the hair color that will suit you. If you have a warm skin tone, go for hair colors that have warm undertones such as golden blonde, copper, or honey. If you have a cool skin tone, go for hair colors that have cool undertones such as ash blonde, platinum, or burgundy. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can go for any hair color.

Tips

When choosing a hair color, consider your lifestyle and personality. If you have a conservative job, go for natural-looking hair colors. If you have a creative job, you can go for bolder hair colors. Also, consider the maintenance involved in the hair color you choose. Some hair colors require frequent touch-ups, while others require minimal maintenance.

FAQ

What hair color suits me if I have a beard?

If you have a beard, choose a hair color that will complement it. If you have a warm-toned beard, go for a warm-toned hair color. If you have a cool-toned beard, go for a cool-toned hair color.

Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but it is recommended to visit a professional hair colorist. They can help you choose the right hair color and ensure that it is applied correctly.

What if I don't like the hair color I choose?

If you don't like the hair color you choose, don't worry. Hair color can be changed or corrected. Visit a professional hair colorist to help you make the necessary changes.

Hair Care

Once you have chosen your hair color, it is essential to take care of your hair. Use shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed for colored hair. Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons as they can damage your hair. Also, protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

In conclusion, choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, but with the tips mentioned above, you can find the perfect hair color that suits you. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its color and health.
