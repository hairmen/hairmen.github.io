Best Haircut For Oval Face 2021 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Sep 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oval face, you are in luck! You have a face shape that can pull off almost any haircut. However, with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to decide which one is the best for you. In this guide, we will help you find the perfect haircut for your oval face shape in 2021.

Description

An oval face is characterized by balanced proportions, with the forehead being slightly wider than the jawline. This face shape is considered the most versatile, as it can pull off a variety of hairstyles, from short to long, curly to straight. The goal of choosing a haircut for an oval face is to enhance and showcase its natural symmetry.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Oval Face

When choosing the best haircut for your oval face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you can dedicate to styling your hair
  • Consider any facial features you want to highlight or downplay
  • Take inspiration from celebrities with oval faces

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some steps to follow when choosing the best haircut for your oval face:

  1. Determine your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline
  2. Find inspiration from celebrity oval faces with similar hair texture and thickness
  3. Consider your lifestyle and how much time you can dedicate to styling your hair
  4. Choose a haircut that enhances your facial features
  5. Consult with a hairstylist who can provide professional advice on the best haircut for your face shape and features

Tips for Choosing the Best Haircut for Oval Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best haircut for your oval face:

  • Choose a haircut that adds volume to the top of your head to balance out the proportions of your face
  • Consider a side-swept or asymmetrical bang to draw attention to your eyes
  • Avoid haircuts that are too long, as they can elongate the face and make it look unbalanced
  • Experiment with layers to add movement and dimension to your hair
  • Consider a bob or lob, which are universally flattering haircuts for oval faces

FAQ

What is the most flattering haircut for an oval face?

The most flattering haircut for an oval face is one that adds volume to the top of the head and balances out the proportions of the face. A bob or lob is a universally flattering haircut for oval faces.

Can oval faces pull off short haircuts?

Yes, oval faces can pull off short haircuts. However, it's important to consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a short haircut.

What should I avoid when choosing a haircut for my oval face?

Avoid haircuts that are too long, as they can elongate the face and make it look unbalanced. Also, avoid haircuts that are too short or too blunt, as they can make the face look too angular.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face

After you've chosen the best haircut for your oval face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips for oval face:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type and texture
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your haircut
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair

Choosing the best haircut for your oval face can be a fun and exciting process. By following these tips and guidelines, you can find a haircut that enhances your natural beauty and showcases your unique features.
