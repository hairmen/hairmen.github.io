How To Style Skirts 2021

Written by Robby Jul 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Skirts are a timeless fashion piece that has been around for many years. They come in different lengths, styles, and materials, making them a versatile wardrobe staple. However, styling skirts can be a bit daunting, especially if you're new to this fashion piece. In this article, we'll be discussing how to style skirts 2021 in a relaxed English language.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Skirts 2021
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to Style Skirts 2021

The first step in styling skirts is to choose the right length and style that suits your body type and occasion. You can choose from maxi, midi, mini, pencil, A-line, and many more. Once you have chosen the right style and length, the next step is to pair it with the right top.

For instance, if you're wearing a midi skirt, you can pair it with a crop top, a blouse, or a sweater. If you're wearing a maxi skirt, you can pair it with a tank top, a t-shirt, or a denim jacket.

Choose the Right Shoes

The right shoes can make or break your entire outfit. When it comes to styling skirts, you can never go wrong with a pair of heels. Heels can instantly make your legs look longer and slimmer, and they add a touch of elegance to your outfit. However, if you're not comfortable wearing heels, you can opt for flats, sneakers, or sandals.

Accessorize

Accessories can take your outfit to the next level. When it comes to styling skirts, you can accessorize with a belt, a scarf, a hat, or a statement necklace. Accessories can add a pop of color or texture to your outfit and make it look more interesting.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when styling skirts:

  1. Choose the right length and style that suits your body type and occasion.
  2. Pair it with the right top.
  3. Choose the right shoes.
  4. Accessorize.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling skirts:

  • Don't be afraid to mix and match different prints and textures.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you.
  • Always choose the right undergarments to avoid visible panty lines.
  • When wearing a midi or maxi skirt, make sure to wear heels or wedges to avoid looking frumpy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling skirts:

Q: Can I wear a mini skirt to the office?

A: It depends on your company's dress code. If your office allows it, you can wear a mini skirt to the office. However, make sure to pair it with a modest top and avoid showing too much skin.

Q: Can I wear sneakers with a skirt?

A: Yes, you can wear sneakers with a skirt. It's a great way to add a casual and comfortable touch to your outfit.

Hair Care

When it comes to styling skirts, your hair can either make or break your outfit. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • If you're wearing a maxi or midi skirt, you can style your hair in an updo to show off your neckline and shoulders.
  • If you're wearing a mini skirt, you can leave your hair down for a more relaxed and casual look.
  • Always use a heat protectant when styling your hair with hot tools to avoid damage.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

In conclusion, styling skirts can be fun and easy once you know the right tips and tricks. Remember to choose the right length and style, pair it with the right top, choose the right shoes, and accessorize. Don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your outfits.
