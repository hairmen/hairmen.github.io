Will I Look Good With Platinum Blonde Hair

Written by Oliver Oct 11, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're considering going platinum blonde, you may be wondering if it's the right look for you. Platinum blonde hair can be a stunning and dramatic change, but it's important to consider whether it will suit your skin tone, features, and personal style. In this guide, we'll take a look at everything you need to know to answer the question, "will I look good with platinum blonde hair?"

Description

Platinum blonde hair is a bold and striking look that can instantly transform your appearance. This hair color is a very light shade of blonde, with little to no warmth or yellow tones. It's a high-maintenance color that requires regular touch-ups and can be damaging to your hair, but the results can be well worth it if you're looking for a head-turning new look.

How to

If you're considering going platinum blonde, the first step is to consult with a professional hair stylist. They can assess your hair type, skin tone, and face shape to determine whether this color will suit you. They can also help you choose the right shade of platinum blonde to complement your features and personal style.

Once you've decided to go for it, your stylist will likely need to bleach your hair to remove any existing color and create a blank canvas for the platinum blonde dye. This process can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to take good care of your locks before and after the treatment.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow if you want to go platinum blonde:

Step 1: Choose a Professional Stylist

Look for a stylist who has experience with platinum blonde hair and can advise you on whether the color will suit you.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before your appointment, make sure your hair is in good condition. Avoid using heat styling tools and consider a deep conditioning treatment to strengthen and nourish your locks.

Step 3: Bleach Your Hair

Your stylist will apply bleach to your hair to remove any existing color and lighten it to the desired shade. This process can take several hours and may need to be repeated over multiple sessions.

Step 4: Tone Your Hair

Once your hair is bleached, your stylist will tone it to create the platinum blonde shade. They may also add highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.

Step 5: Maintain Your Color

Platinum blonde hair requires regular touch-ups to maintain the color and prevent brassiness. Your stylist can advise you on how often you'll need to come in for touch-ups and how to care for your hair between appointments.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind if you're considering platinum blonde hair:

  • Choose a shade of platinum blonde that complements your skin tone and features.
  • Take good care of your hair before and after bleaching to minimize damage.
  • Use a purple shampoo and conditioner to prevent brassiness and maintain your color.
  • Consider adding lowlights or highlights to create depth and dimension.
  • Be prepared for the high maintenance of platinum blonde hair, including regular touch-ups and deep conditioning treatments.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about platinum blonde hair:

Will platinum blonde hair suit me?

It depends on your skin tone, features, and personal style. Consult with a professional stylist to determine whether this color is right for you.

Is platinum blonde hair high maintenance?

Yes, platinum blonde hair requires regular touch-ups and deep conditioning treatments to maintain the color and prevent damage.

Will bleaching my hair damage it?

Bleaching can be damaging to your hair, but a professional stylist can minimize the damage by using high-quality products and techniques.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for caring for your platinum blonde hair:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a purple shampoo and conditioner to prevent brassiness and maintain your color.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, and use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

In conclusion, going platinum blonde can be a stunning and dramatic change, but it's important to consider whether it will suit your skin tone, features, and personal style. Consult with a professional stylist, take good care of your hair, and be prepared for the high maintenance of this color. With the right preparation and care, you can rock platinum blonde hair like a pro!
