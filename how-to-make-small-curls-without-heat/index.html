How To Make Small Curls Without Heat A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a way to style your hair without using heat, you're in luck! Small curls are a great way to add volume and texture to your hair, and they can be achieved without the use of hot tools. In this article, we'll show you how to make small curls without heat, step by step. You'll also find some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you get the best results.

Table of Contents

  2. Step by Step Guide

How to Make Small Curls Without Heat

The key to making small curls without heat is to use the right products and techniques. Here's what you'll need:

  • Hair mousse or curl-enhancing cream
  • Bobby pins or small hair elastics
  • A wide-toothed comb

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to make small curls without heat:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual, then towel dry.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair mousse or curl-enhancing cream to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  3. Use a wide-toothed comb to gently comb through your hair, making sure the product is evenly distributed.
  4. Divide your hair into small sections, about 1 inch wide.
  5. Twist each section of hair tightly, then coil it into a tiny bun at the base of your scalp.
  6. Secure each bun with a bobby pin or small hair elastic.
  7. Leave your hair in the buns for several hours or overnight.
  8. Remove the bobby pins or elastics and gently unravel each bun.
  9. Use your fingers to separate the curls and add volume.
  10. Finish with a light-hold hairspray if desired.

Tips for Making Small Curls Without Heat

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with clean, damp hair. This will help the product distribute evenly and make it easier to twist and coil your hair.
  • Use a hair mousse or curl-enhancing cream that's designed for your hair type. This will help define your curls and keep them in place.
  • Don't twist your hair too tightly, or you may end up with kinks instead of curls.
  • Be patient! It may take a few tries to get the hang of making small curls without heat, but the results are worth it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about making small curls without heat:

Can you make small curls without heat on short hair?

Yes! The technique works on hair of all lengths.

How long do you need to leave your hair in the buns?

You can leave your hair in the buns for several hours or overnight, depending on how tight you want your curls to be.

Can you make small curls without heat on naturally curly hair?

Yes! The technique can enhance your natural curls and add definition.

Hair Care Advice for Small Curls

If you want your small curls to last, here are some tips:

  • Avoid brushing your hair once the curls are set. This can cause frizz and make your curls fall out.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your curls, as this can damage your hair and cause them to lose their shape.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and hydrated.

Now that you know how to make small curls without heat, you can experiment with different styles and find the look that works best for you. Whether you have short hair or long, straight or curly, this technique is a great way to add some texture and volume to your hair without damaging it with heat.
