Short Curly Hairstyles For Oval Faces Over 50

Written by Daniel Aug 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oval face and are over 50, you may be wondering what type of hairstyle would suit your face shape and age. Short curly hairstyles are a great option for women over 50 with oval faces as they can accentuate the natural curves of the face and add volume to thinning hair. In this article, we will discuss some of the best short curly hairstyles for oval faces over 50.

Description

Oval faces are considered to be the most versatile face shape as it can suit almost any hairstyle. Women over 50 with oval faces need to choose hairstyles that complement their facial features and make them look younger. Short curly hairstyles are perfect for oval faces as they can add volume and texture to the hair, making it look fuller and thicker.

How to

Short curly hairstyles for oval faces over 50 can be achieved in different ways. Here are some options:

1. Curly Bob

The curly bob is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It is perfect for women over 50 as it adds volume to thinning hair and defines the natural curls. To achieve this hairstyle, ask your stylist to cut your hair into a shoulder-length bob and add layers to create volume. Style your hair with a curling iron to define the curls and add texture.

2. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a great option for women over 50 with oval faces as it can make them look younger and more stylish. This hairstyle is perfect for women with thinning hair as it can add volume and texture to the hair. To achieve this hairstyle, ask your stylist to cut your hair into a short pixie cut and add layers to create volume. Style your hair with a curling iron to define the curls and add texture.

3. Messy Curls

Messy curls are a great option for women over 50 with oval faces as they can add volume and texture to the hair, making it look fuller and thicker. To achieve this hairstyle, ask your stylist to cut your hair into a short bob and add layers to create volume. Style your hair with a curling iron and use a texturizing spray to create a messy, tousled look.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieve a curly bob:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.

2. Towel dry your hair

Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to your hair to add volume and texture.

3. Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and add texture to your hair.

4. Curl your hair

Curl your hair with a curling iron to define the curls and add texture.

5. Finish with hairspray

Finish with a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short curly hairstyle for oval faces over 50:

- Choose a hairstyle that complements your facial features and makes you look younger.
- Use volumizing products to add volume and texture to your hair.
- Use a curling iron to define the curls and add texture to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Will a short curly hairstyle suit my oval face?

A: Yes, short curly hairstyles are perfect for oval faces as they can accentuate the natural curves of the face and add volume to thinning hair.

Q: How can I add volume to my thinning hair?

A: You can add volume to your thinning hair by using volumizing products, adding layers to your hair, and using a curling iron to define the curls and add texture to your hair.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
- Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
- Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.

With these short curly hairstyles for oval faces over 50, you can look younger and more stylish. Choose a hairstyle that suits your facial features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
