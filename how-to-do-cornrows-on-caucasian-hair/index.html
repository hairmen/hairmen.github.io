How To Do Cornrows On Caucasian Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Cornrows are a traditional African hairstyle that has been around for centuries. While they are typically associated with African hair, cornrows can be done on any hair type, including Caucasian hair. In this guide, we will show you how to do cornrows on caucasian hair and provide some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

Description

Cornrows are a type of braid that is created by weaving small sections of hair close to the scalp. The braids can be worn in a variety of styles and can be left in for several weeks. Cornrows are a popular protective style, as they help to promote hair growth and prevent breakage.

How to Do Cornrows on Caucasian Hair: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

You will need a few supplies to get started. These include:

  • Comb
  • Sectioning clips
  • Hair extensions (optional)
  • Hair gel

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into small sections. Use sectioning clips to keep each section separate. The size of the sections will depend on how thick you want the cornrows to be.

Step 3: Prep Your Hair

Apply a small amount of hair gel to each section of hair. This will help to keep the hair smooth and make it easier to braid. If you want to add length or thickness to your cornrows, you can use hair extensions at this point.

Step 4: Begin Braiding

Start at the front of your head and take a small section of hair. Divide the section into three equal parts and begin braiding. As you braid, continue to add small sections of hair to each strand. Keep the braids tight and close to the scalp.

Step 5: Continue Braiding

Continue braiding each section of hair until you have completed all of the sections. If you are using hair extensions, be sure to include them in the braids.

Step 6: Secure the Braids

Once you have braided all of the sections, use a small elastic band to secure the end of each braid. You can also tuck the end of the braid under the last weave to hide it.

Tips for Doing Cornrows on Caucasian Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when doing cornrows on Caucasian hair:

  • Use hair gel to make the hair easier to braid.
  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Keep the braids tight and close to the scalp.
  • Use hair extensions to add length or thickness to your cornrows.
  • Be patient and take your time.

FAQs

How long do cornrows last?

Cornrows can last for several weeks if they are well-maintained. You will need to touch up the braids at the roots every few weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Can I do cornrows on my own hair?

Yes, you can do cornrows on your own hair. However, it can be challenging to do the back of your head without help from someone else.

Do cornrows promote hair growth?

Cornrows can help to promote hair growth by protecting the hair from breakage and damage.

Hair Care Tips for Cornrows

Here are some tips to keep your cornrows looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid pulling your cornrows too tight, as this can cause damage to your hair and scalp.

Now that you know how to do cornrows on Caucasian hair, you can experiment with different styles and create a unique look that is all your own. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you will have beautiful cornrows in no time!
