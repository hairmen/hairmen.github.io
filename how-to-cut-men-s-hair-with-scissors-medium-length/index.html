How To Cut Men s Hair With Scissors Medium Length A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of paying too much money for a mediocre haircut? Or maybe you just want to learn a new skill that can come in handy in the future? Whatever your reason may be, we're here to help you learn how to cut men's hair with scissors medium length. This step-by-step guide will show you everything you need to know to give a great haircut at home.

Description

Medium length hair is defined as hair that falls between the chin and shoulders. It is a popular hairstyle for men as it is versatile and easy to maintain. Cutting medium length hair with scissors can be a bit tricky, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done at home.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Scissors Medium Length

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting men's hair with scissors medium length is to gather your tools. You will need a good pair of scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. You may also want to invest in a hair clip to help section off the hair.

Step 2: Section Off the Hair

Next, you will want to section off the hair. Start by parting the hair down the middle and clipping one side up. Then, section off the hair on the bottom of the head and clip it up as well. This will make it easier to work with the hair.

Step 3: Cut the Hair on the Sides

Begin by cutting the hair on the sides. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight out from the head. Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Repeat this process on both sides of the head.

Step 4: Cut the Back of the Hair

Next, move to the back of the head. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it to the desired length. Work your way up the back of the head, taking small sections of hair at a time.

Step 5: Cut the Top of the Hair

Finally, move to the top of the head. Take small sections of hair and comb them straight up. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it to the desired length. Work your way across the top of the head, taking small sections of hair at a time.

Tips

- Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections as you become more comfortable.

- Use a comb to keep the hair straight and even while cutting.

- Take breaks if you feel tired or if your hands start to cramp.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair. However, it may be easier to have someone else do it for you, especially if you are new to cutting hair.

What kind of scissors should I use?

You should use a pair of scissors specifically designed for cutting hair. These scissors are sharper and have a better grip, making it easier to cut hair evenly.

How often should I cut my hair?

This depends on your personal preference and hair type. Some people may need to cut their hair every 4-6 weeks, while others may be able to go 8-10 weeks between haircuts.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it properly. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. You may also want to use styling products to keep your hair looking its best.

By following these steps and taking proper care of your hair, you can give yourself or someone else a great haircut at home. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll be a pro in no time!
