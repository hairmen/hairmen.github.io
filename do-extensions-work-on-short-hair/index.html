Do Extensions Work On Short Hair

Written by Christine Nov 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hair is a trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle that many women love. However, sometimes we crave for long and voluminous locks. Extensions are a great way to achieve this look, but do extensions work on short hair? In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about getting extensions on short hair.

Description

There are various types of hair extensions, including clip-ins, tape-ins, sew-ins, and micro-links. Hair extensions are made of natural or synthetic hair, and they can add length, volume, and thickness to your hair. Extensions come in different shades and textures, so you can choose the one that matches your natural hair.

How to

Before getting extensions, you need to decide which type of extensions you want to get. Clip-ins are the easiest and most temporary option, while sew-ins and micro-links are more permanent. Tape-ins are a good option for short hair because they are lightweight and won't damage your natural hair.

To get tape-in extensions on short hair, you need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Consultation

Book a consultation with a hair extension specialist to discuss your hair goals and the type of extensions that are suitable for your hair. The specialist will assess your hair and recommend the best extensions for your hair type and texture.

Step 2: Select the Extensions

Select the extensions that match your hair color, texture, and length. Tape-in extensions come in different lengths, so choose the one that matches your natural hair length.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before the installation. Avoid using any hair products that contain silicone or oil, as they can weaken the bonds.

Step 4: Install the Extensions

The hair extension specialist will section your hair and attach the extensions using a special tape. The tape is applied to the roots of your hair, sandwiching the extensions in between the sections of your hair. The process takes around 1 to 2 hours, depending on the number of extensions you want to install.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once the extensions are installed, the specialist will style your hair to blend the extensions with your natural hair. You can style your hair as you normally would, but avoid using heat tools on the bonds.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your extensions last longer:

  • Brush your hair gently and avoid pulling on the extensions.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol or sulfates, as they can dry out the extensions.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent tangling and frizzing.
  • Visit the specialist every 6 to 8 weeks for maintenance and removal.

FAQ

Q: Can I get extensions on very short hair?

Yes, you can get extensions on very short hair, but you need to choose the right type of extensions. Tape-in extensions are a good option for short hair because they are lightweight and won't damage your natural hair.

Q: How long do tape-in extensions last?

Tape-in extensions can last up to 8 to 12 weeks with proper care and maintenance. You can visit the specialist every 6 to 8 weeks for maintenance and removal.

Hair Care

To care for your extensions, follow these steps:

  • Brush your hair gently using a soft-bristle brush or a wide-tooth comb.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on the extensions, as it can damage your natural hair.
  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools on the bonds, as it can weaken the tape.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair and extensions.

In conclusion, extensions do work on short hair, but you need to choose the right type of extensions and follow the proper care and maintenance routine. Consult with a specialist to determine the best type of extensions for your hair type and texture. With the right extensions and care, you can have long and voluminous locks that look natural and beautiful.
