How Long Until I Can Slick My Hair Back

Written by Robby Nov 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your usual hairstyle and want to try something new, like slicking your hair back? Before you do, it's important to know how long until you can slick your hair back, especially if you have just undergone a hair treatment. In this article, we'll discuss the details on how long you should wait before you can style your hair and what you can do to prepare for it.

Table of Contents

  • How to Slick Your Hair Back
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back
  • FAQs

How to Slick Your Hair Back

Slicking your hair back is a classic and timeless hairstyle that can give you a polished and sophisticated look. Here are the steps on how to slick your hair back:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you slick your hair back, make sure that it's clean and free of any tangles. You can use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup, followed by a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

Step 2: Apply Product

Apply a hair product that can help keep your hair in place, such as a pomade, gel, or wax. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Using a fine-toothed comb, comb your hair back starting from your hairline. Make sure that your hair is slicked back evenly and there are no bumps or lumps.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're new to slicking your hair back, here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a hair product of your choice.
  3. Comb your hair back starting from your hairline.
  4. If necessary, apply more product to areas that need more hold.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back

Here are some tips to keep in mind when slicking your hair back:

  • Use a hair product that suits your hair type and the style you want to achieve.
  • Don't apply too much product as it can make your hair look greasy or stiff.
  • Use a fine-toothed comb to ensure that your hair is slicked back evenly.
  • Experiment with different hair partings to find the one that suits you best.
  • Consider getting a haircut that complements the slicked-back hairstyle.

FAQs

How long until I can slick my hair back after a relaxer?

You should wait at least two weeks after getting a relaxer before you can slick your hair back. This allows your hair to recover and prevents breakage.

Can I slick my hair back if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can slick your hair back even if you have curly hair. However, it may be more challenging as curly hair tends to have more volume and texture. You may need to use more product and a stronger hold hairspray.

Can I slick my hair back if I have short hair?

Yes, you can slick your hair back even if you have short hair. However, you may need to use more product and a stronger hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Hair Care After Slicking Your Hair Back

After slicking your hair back, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup.
  • Use a deep conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair ties or rubber bands that can cause tension and breakage.
  • Take breaks from slicking your hair back to give your hair a rest.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and maintain its health.

Now that you know how long until you can slick your hair back and how to do it, you can experiment with this classic hairstyle and see if it suits you. Just remember to take care of your hair and use the right products to prevent damage and breakage.
