Long Straight Layered Haircuts Without Bangs A Timeless Style

Written by Robby Sep 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long straight layered haircuts without bangs are a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. This haircut can give you a sleek and sophisticated look, while still maintaining a natural and effortless vibe. If you're looking for a new hairstyle to try, read on for tips on how to achieve the perfect long straight layered haircut without bangs.

Description

Long straight layered haircuts without bangs involve cutting the hair in layers, typically starting at the chin or collarbone. The layers help to add movement and volume to the hair, while the lack of bangs keeps the look simple and streamlined. This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks chic and put-together.

How To

To achieve the perfect long straight layered haircut without bangs, start by washing and drying your hair. You can use a straightening iron to create a sleek and straight look, or leave your hair natural for a more relaxed vibe. Once your hair is dry, use a comb to create a middle part.

Next, section your hair into layers, starting at the chin or collarbone. Use a sharp pair of scissors to trim each layer, making sure to cut at an angle to create a natural-looking gradient. Be sure to only cut a small amount of hair at a time, as it's easier to add more layers than to take them away.

Once all of your layers are cut, use a texturizing spray to add volume and movement to your hair. You can also use a serum or oil to add shine and smoothness.

Step By Step

1. Wash and dry your hair.

2. Use a straightening iron to create a sleek look, or leave your hair natural for a more relaxed vibe.

3. Create a middle part using a comb.

4. Section your hair into layers, starting at the chin or collarbone.

5. Use a sharp pair of scissors to trim each layer, making sure to cut at an angle to create a natural-looking gradient.

6. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and movement to your hair.

7. Use a serum or oil to add shine and smoothness.

Tips

- If you're not sure how to cut your hair in layers, it's best to go to a professional stylist.

- Be sure to only cut a small amount of hair at a time, as it's easier to add more layers than to take them away.

- Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and movement to your hair.

- Use a serum or oil to add shine and smoothness.

FAQ

What face shapes are best suited for long straight layered haircuts without bangs?

Long straight layered haircuts without bangs look great on all face shapes, but are particularly flattering on those with oval or long faces.

Can I still style my hair in different ways with this haircut?

Absolutely! You can still curl your hair, create beachy waves, or even put it up in a ponytail or bun.

Is this haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut works for all hair types, whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair.

Hair Care

To keep your long straight layered haircut looking its best, be sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat tools. Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

Long straight layered haircuts without bangs are a timeless and versatile style that can work for anyone. With a little bit of styling and hair care, you can achieve a sleek and sophisticated look that's perfect for any occasion.
