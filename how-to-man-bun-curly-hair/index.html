How To Man Bun Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Curly hair can be a challenge to style, especially when it comes to creating a man bun. But fear not, we've got you covered with this step by step guide on how to man bun curly hair. Whether you have tight coils, loose waves, or somewhere in between, we've got tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect man bun.

How to Man Bun Curly Hair

The first step to creating a man bun with curly hair is to make sure your hair is long enough. Depending on the length and texture of your curls, you may need at least 6-8 inches of hair to create a proper man bun. Once your hair is long enough, you can follow these steps:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush specifically designed for curly hair to gently detangle your hair. This will help make it easier to create a smooth and even man bun.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Using your fingers, gather all of your hair at the crown of your head. Be sure to leave some hair loose around your hairline to create a more natural look.

Step 3: Tie Your Hair

Once you have all of your hair gathered, use a hair tie to secure it in place. You can use a regular hair tie or a scrunchie, depending on your preference. Make sure the hair tie is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it causes discomfort or pulls on your scalp.

Step 4: Tuck in Loose Ends

If you have any loose ends or flyaways, you can tuck them in using bobby pins or hair clips. This will help create a neater and more polished look.

Step by Step Guide

If you're still having trouble creating a man bun with curly hair, you can follow this step by step guide:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash and condition your hair using products specifically designed for curly hair. This will help keep your curls healthy and defined.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or gel, to your hair to help define your curls and keep them in place.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair while keeping your curls intact. This will help create volume and texture.

Step 4: Brush Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush specifically designed for curly hair to gently detangle your hair and create a smooth surface for the man bun.

Step 5: Gather Your Hair

Using your fingers, gather all of your hair at the crown of your head. Be sure to leave some hair loose around your hairline to create a more natural look.

Step 6: Tie Your Hair

Use a hair tie to secure your hair in place, making sure it's tight enough to hold your hair in place but not so tight that it causes discomfort or pulls on your scalp.

Step 7: Tuck in Loose Ends

If you have any loose ends or flyaways, you can tuck them in using bobby pins or hair clips. This will help create a neater and more polished look.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair

Styling curly hair can be a challenge, but these tips can help:

  • Use products specifically designed for curly hair to help define your curls and keep them in place.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or a brush specifically designed for curly hair to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to help create volume and texture.
  • Experiment with different hair tie styles, such as a scrunchie or a cloth hair tie, to see what works best for your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about man buns and curly hair:

Can I create a man bun with short curly hair?

Depending on the length and texture of your curls, you may be able to create a small man bun with short hair. However, if your hair is too short, it may be difficult to create a proper man bun.

What type of hair tie should I use for curly hair?

You can use a regular hair tie or a scrunchie, depending on your preference. However, avoid using elastic bands, as they can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Can I wear a man bun every day?

While wearing a man bun every day is possible, it's important to give your hair a break from time to time to prevent damage and breakage. Try alternating between different hairstyles to give your hair a break.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and defined. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Wash your hair using products specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or a brush specifically designed for curly hair to detangle your hair when it's wet.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create a perfect man bun with your curly hair. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and defined, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
