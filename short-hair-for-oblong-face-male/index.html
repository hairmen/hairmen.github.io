Short Hair For Oblong Face Male The Ultimate Guide

Are you an oblong face male looking for the perfect short hairstyle? You're in luck! In this guide, we'll be sharing everything you need to know about short hair for oblong face males, including how to choose the right cut, styling tips, and hair care advice. So, let's dive in!

How to Choose the Right Cut

When it comes to short haircuts for oblong face males, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First and foremost, you want to choose a cut that will help balance out the length of your face. This means avoiding styles that are too short on the sides or too long on top.

Instead, opt for a cut that is shorter on the sides and slightly longer on top. This will help add some width to your face and create a more balanced look. Some great short haircuts for oblong face males include the buzz cut, crew cut, and textured crop.

Styling Tips

Once you've chosen the right cut for your oblong face, it's time to start thinking about styling. One of the best things about short hair is that it's easy to style and maintain, but there are still a few tips to keep in mind.

First, make sure you're using the right products for your hair type. If you have thin or fine hair, opt for lightweight products like mousses and gels. If you have thick or coarse hair, go for heavier products like waxes and pomades.

Second, consider adding some texture to your hair to create more visual interest. You can do this by using a texturizing spray or by styling your hair with your fingers instead of a comb.

Step-by-Step Guide

Ready to try out a short haircut for your oblong face? Here's a step-by-step guide to get you started:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's slightly damp.
  3. Apply your chosen styling product to your hair, focusing on the roots and working your way up to the tips.
  4. Using a comb or your fingers, style your hair into the desired shape.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your style in place.

Hair Care Tips

Short haircuts require less maintenance than longer styles, but that doesn't mean you can neglect your hair care routine. Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking and feeling its best:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using high heat when blow-drying your hair.
  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your style looking fresh.

FAQ

Q: Can oblong face males pull off longer hairstyles?

A: While shorter haircuts tend to be more flattering for oblong face males, it's not impossible to pull off longer styles. Just make sure to add some layers or texture to create more movement and visual interest.

Q: What products should I use for my short hair?

A: The best products for your short hair will depend on your hair type. For thin or fine hair, opt for lightweight products like mousses and gels. For thick or coarse hair, go for heavier products like waxes and pomades.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: Short haircuts require less maintenance than longer styles, but you should still wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect short haircut for your oblong face and keep it looking great all year round. Happy styling!
