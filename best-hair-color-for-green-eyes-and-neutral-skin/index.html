Best Hair Color For Green Eyes And Neutral Skin

Written by Oliver Jun 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have green eyes and neutral skin, you're in luck! There are a variety of hair colors that will complement your features and bring out the best in your natural beauty. In this article, we'll explore the best hair colors for green eyes and neutral skin, as well as tips for maintaining your hair's health and vibrancy.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Hair Color
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair's Health
  • FAQs

Description

Green eyes are relatively rare, occurring in only about 2% of the world's population. However, they are also considered one of the most beautiful eye colors. Neutral skin, which has a balance of cool and warm undertones, is also a desirable trait as it allows for a wide range of hair color options.

When choosing a hair color for green eyes and neutral skin, it's important to consider the undertones of your skin as well as the intensity of your eye color. Here are some of the best hair colors for green eyes and neutral skin:

1. Warm Brown

Warm brown hair colors, such as caramel, chestnut, and honey, complement green eyes and neutral skin by bringing out the warmth in both. These hair colors also add depth and dimension to your hair, making it look thicker and more voluminous.

2. Auburn

Auburn is a reddish-brown hair color that looks stunning on green-eyed individuals with neutral skin. This hair color adds a touch of warmth to your skin and enhances the green tones in your eyes.

3. Blonde

Blonde hair can also work well with green eyes and neutral skin, but it's important to choose the right shade. For those with cool undertones, ashy blonde shades like platinum or icy blonde can look great. For those with warm undertones, golden blonde or honey blonde can complement your features.

4. Red

Red hair is a bold choice that can look amazing on green-eyed individuals with neutral skin. Shades like copper, mahogany, and cherry red add warmth to your skin and make your green eyes pop.

How to Choose the Best Hair Color

Choosing the right hair color for green eyes and neutral skin can be a daunting task, especially with so many options to choose from. Here are some tips to help you narrow down your choices:

1. Consider Your Skin Undertones

As mentioned earlier, it's important to consider the undertones of your skin when choosing a hair color. If you have cool undertones, opt for hair colors with cool tones like ash brown or platinum blonde. If you have warm undertones, warm hair colors like caramel or golden blonde will complement your skin.

2. Look at Your Eye Color

Consider the intensity of your green eyes when choosing a hair color. If your eyes are a bright, emerald green, opt for a hair color that will enhance the green tones like auburn or cherry red. If your eyes are a softer green, warm brown or blonde shades can complement them.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hair color, it's important to consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance you're willing to do. If you're looking for a low-maintenance hair color, warm brown or blonde shades may be a better choice than red or platinum blonde.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the best hair color for green eyes and neutral skin:

1. Determine Your Skin Undertones

Look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear both blue and green, you have neutral undertones.

2. Consider Your Eye Color

Look at the intensity of your green eyes. If they are a bright, emerald green, opt for hair colors that enhance the green tones like auburn or cherry red. If they are a softer green, warm brown or blonde shades can complement them.

3. Choose Your Hair Color

Based on your skin undertones and eye color, choose a hair color that complements your features. Warm brown, auburn, blonde, and red are all great choices for green eyes and neutral skin.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair's Health

Once you've chosen a hair color, it's important to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair looking its best:

1. Use a Color-Safe Shampoo

Color-safe shampoos are formulated to help preserve the vibrancy of your hair color. Look for shampoos that are sulfate-free and contain UV protectants.

2. Avoid Hot Tools

Hot tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and cause your color to fade. If you must use hot tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can also cause your hair color to fade. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when you're spending time outside.

FAQs

1. Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home. However, it's important to follow the instructions carefully and choose a high-quality hair dye that won't damage your hair.

2. How often should I touch up my roots?

How often you touch up your roots depends on how fast your hair grows and how noticeable your roots are. Most people touch up their roots every 4-6 weeks.

3. Can I go from dark hair to blonde?

Yes, you can go from dark hair to blonde, but it's a process that should be done gradually to avoid damaging your hair. It's best to consult a professional stylist for this type of color change.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for keeping your hair color looking vibrant. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

1. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

2. Use a Deep Conditioner

Deep conditioners help nourish and hydrate your hair, keeping it soft and shiny.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet rich in vitamins and nutrients can help promote healthy hair growth.

Overall, the best hair color for green eyes and neutral skin depends on your skin undertones and eye color. Warm brown, auburn, blonde, and red are all great choices to complement your features. Remember to maintain the health of your hair with regular trims and deep conditioning treatments, and protect your hair color from fading with color-safe shampoos and UV protectants.
