Examples Of 60S Music A Guide To The Best Relaxing Tunes

Written by Christine Aug 30, 2023 · 6 min read

Welcome to our guide on the best examples of 60s music! If you're looking for some relaxing tunes to unwind to, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be exploring some of the top hits from the 60s, including songs from The Beatles, The Beach Boys, and more. So sit back, relax, and enjoy the trip down memory lane!

Table of Contents

  • The Beatles
  • The Beach Boys
  • Simon & Garfunkel
  • The Rolling Stones
  • Jimi Hendrix

The Beatles

No list of 60s music would be complete without mentioning The Beatles. This legendary band from Liverpool took the world by storm in the 60s, and their music continues to inspire and delight listeners to this day. Some of their most popular and relaxing tunes include "Let It Be," "Hey Jude," and "Yesterday."

How to Listen to The Beatles

If you're new to The Beatles, then we recommend starting with their greatest hits album, which features all of their most popular songs. You can also find their music on streaming platforms like Spotify and Apple Music.

Tips for Enjoying The Beatles

One of the best ways to enjoy The Beatles is to listen to their music while relaxing with a cup of tea or coffee. You can also listen to their music while taking a walk or doing some light exercise.

FAQs About The Beatles

  • Q: Who were the members of The Beatles?
  • A: The Beatles were comprised of John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison, and Ringo Starr.
  • Q: What was The Beatles' biggest hit?
  • A: The Beatles' biggest hit was "Hey Jude," which spent nine weeks at number one on the charts.

The Beach Boys

The Beach Boys are another iconic band from the 60s that are known for their relaxing and upbeat tunes. Their music is often associated with the beach and the California lifestyle, making it the perfect soundtrack for a lazy summer day. Some of their most popular songs include "Surfin' USA," "Good Vibrations," and "God Only Knows."

How to Listen to The Beach Boys

You can find The Beach Boys' music on streaming platforms like Spotify and Apple Music, as well as on their greatest hits albums.

Tips for Enjoying The Beach Boys

The best way to enjoy The Beach Boys is to listen to their music while soaking up the sun at the beach or by the pool. You can also listen to their music while driving with the windows down and the wind in your hair.

FAQs About The Beach Boys

  • Q: Who were the members of The Beach Boys?
  • A: The Beach Boys were comprised of Brian Wilson, Carl Wilson, Dennis Wilson, Mike Love, and Al Jardine.
  • Q: What was The Beach Boys' biggest hit?
  • A: The Beach Boys' biggest hit was "Good Vibrations," which reached number one on the charts in 1966.

Simon & Garfunkel

Simon & Garfunkel are a folk-rock duo that rose to fame in the 60s with their melodic and introspective songs. Their music is known for its hauntingly beautiful harmonies and poetic lyrics, making it the perfect choice for a relaxing evening at home. Some of their most popular songs include "The Sound of Silence," "Scarborough Fair," and "Mrs. Robinson."

How to Listen to Simon & Garfunkel

You can find Simon & Garfunkel's music on streaming platforms like Spotify and Apple Music, as well as on their greatest hits albums.

Tips for Enjoying Simon & Garfunkel

The best way to enjoy Simon & Garfunkel is to listen to their music while curled up with a good book or taking a relaxing bath. You can also listen to their music while going for a long walk in nature.

FAQs About Simon & Garfunkel

  • Q: Who were the members of Simon & Garfunkel?
  • A: Simon & Garfunkel were comprised of Paul Simon and Art Garfunkel.
  • Q: What was Simon & Garfunkel's biggest hit?
  • A: Simon & Garfunkel's biggest hit was "The Sound of Silence," which reached number one on the charts in 1965.

The Rolling Stones

The Rolling Stones are a legendary rock band that formed in the 60s and are still going strong today. Their music is known for its gritty and bluesy sound, and their lyrics often explore themes of rebellion and hedonism. Some of their most popular and relaxing songs include "Wild Horses," "Angie," and "Paint It Black."

How to Listen to The Rolling Stones

You can find The Rolling Stones' music on streaming platforms like Spotify and Apple Music, as well as on their greatest hits albums.

Tips for Enjoying The Rolling Stones

The best way to enjoy The Rolling Stones is to listen to their music while driving or doing some high-energy exercise. You can also listen to their music while hanging out with friends and having a few drinks.

FAQs About The Rolling Stones

  • Q: Who were the members of The Rolling Stones?
  • A: The Rolling Stones were comprised of Mick Jagger, Keith Richards, Charlie Watts, Bill Wyman, and Brian Jones.
  • Q: What was The Rolling Stones' biggest hit?
  • A: The Rolling Stones' biggest hit was "(I Can't Get No) Satisfaction," which reached number one on the charts in 1965.

Jimi Hendrix

Jimi Hendrix was a guitar virtuoso and rock icon who rose to fame in the 60s with his electrifying live performances and innovative sound. His music is known for its psychedelic and bluesy elements, and his guitar playing has influenced countless musicians over the years. Some of his most popular and relaxing songs include "Little Wing," "The Wind Cries Mary," and "Castles Made of Sand."

How to Listen to Jimi Hendrix

You can find Jimi Hendrix's music on streaming platforms like Spotify and Apple Music, as well as on his greatest hits albums.

Tips for Enjoying Jimi Hendrix

The best way to enjoy Jimi Hendrix is to listen to his music while chilling out with friends or getting creative with some art or writing. You can also listen to his music while practicing yoga or meditation.

FAQs About Jimi Hendrix

  • Q: What was Jimi Hendrix's biggest hit?
  • A: Jimi Hendrix did not have a single "biggest hit," as his music was more focused on his live performances and albums rather than individual songs.
  • Q: What was Jimi Hendrix's signature sound?
  • A: Jimi Hendrix was known for his innovative use of guitar effects, including distortion, feedback, and wah-wah.
