How To Tell If Front Bangs Will Look Good On You

Written by Oliver Nov 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Front bangs are a popular hairstyle that can be flattering and stylish. However, not every face shape or hair type can pull off this look. In this article, we will explore how to tell if front bangs will look good on you and provide tips on how to care for them.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

How to Tell If Front Bangs Will Look Good on You

Before you decide to get front bangs, it's important to consider whether they will suit your face shape and hair type. Here are some tips on how to tell:

Face Shape

The shape of your face plays a big role in how well front bangs will suit you. Here are some general guidelines:

  • If you have a round face, avoid blunt bangs and opt for side-swept or wispy bangs instead.
  • If you have a square face, avoid heavy, straight-across bangs and go for soft, curved bangs that will soften your features.
  • If you have an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any type of front bangs.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, opt for side-swept bangs that will balance out your forehead.

Hair Type

Your hair type also plays a role in how well front bangs will suit you. Here are some things to consider:

  • If you have curly hair, be prepared to straighten your bangs every day to keep them looking neat.
  • If you have fine hair, go for wispy or layered bangs that won't look too heavy.
  • If you have thick hair, you can pull off heavier, blunt bangs.
  • If you have oily skin, avoid blunt bangs that will stick to your forehead.

Step by Step

If you've decided that front bangs will suit you, here's how to get them:

  1. Start by sectioning off the hair you want to cut into bangs. Use a comb to create a triangle shape at the front of your hairline.
  2. Use hair cutting scissors to trim the hair to the desired length. It's best to start by cutting them longer than you want, as you can always trim more later.
  3. Use a razor to feather the ends of the bangs so they blend in with the rest of your hair.
  4. Style your bangs using a blow dryer, straightener, or curling iron as desired.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when getting front bangs:

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you play sports or exercise frequently, bangs may get in the way and be difficult to manage.
  • Be prepared for upkeep. Bangs require regular trimming to keep them looking neat.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what suits you best.
  • Consider the climate. In humid weather, bangs may stick to your forehead and require more maintenance.

FAQ

How long do front bangs take to grow out?

It generally takes about 3-4 months for front bangs to grow out enough to be blended in with the rest of your hair.

How often should I trim my front bangs?

It's best to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat.

Can I get front bangs if I wear glasses?

Yes, you can still get front bangs if you wear glasses. Consider the length and thickness of your bangs to ensure they won't interfere with your glasses.

Hair Care

Once you have front bangs, it's important to take care of them properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep your bangs clean.
  • Blow dry your bangs after washing to keep them looking neat.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as oils from your fingers can make them greasy.
  • Use a round brush when blow drying to add volume and shape to your bangs.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your front bangs will look great and be easy to manage.
