How To Make Your Hair Naturally Curly African American

Written by Frank Nov 29, 2023 · 3 min read
How to Make Your Hair Naturally Curly African American | Tips & Hair Care

Are you tired of straightening your hair every day? Do you want to embrace your natural curls? Well, you’re in luck because there are many ways to make your hair naturally curly without using heat or chemicals. In this article, we’ll give you tips, tricks, and hair care advice to help you achieve beautiful, bouncy curls.

How to Make Your Hair Naturally Curly African American

There are a few ways to make your hair naturally curly without damaging it. Here are some methods you can try:

1. Twist-Out Method

The twist-out method involves twisting your hair while it’s wet and letting it dry overnight. When you untwist your hair in the morning, you’ll have defined curls. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section tightly.
  3. Cover your hair with a silk scarf or bonnet and let it dry overnight.
  4. Untwist your hair in the morning and use a pick to fluff out your curls.

2. Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are a traditional African hairstyle that involves twisting your hair into small knots. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section tightly.
  3. Wrap each twisted section into a small knot and secure it with a bobby pin.
  4. Let your hair dry overnight.
  5. Untwist the knots in the morning and use a pick to fluff out your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to making your hair naturally curly:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Apply a curl enhancing cream or gel to each section.
  5. Twist each section tightly or create Bantu knots.
  6. Cover your hair with a silk scarf or bonnet and let it dry overnight.
  7. Untwist or unravel the knots in the morning.
  8. Use a pick or your fingers to fluff out your curls.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Once you’ve achieved your desired curls, here are some tips for maintaining them:

  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair every day.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or pick to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your hair naturally curly:

  • Can I make my hair naturally curly if it’s relaxed? Yes, you can still make your hair naturally curly if it’s relaxed. Just be sure to use gentle methods and avoid using heat or chemicals.
  • How long will my curls last? Your curls should last for several days if you maintain them properly.
  • Can I use a diffuser to dry my curls? Yes, you can use a diffuser on low heat to dry your curls.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some general hair care tips for maintaining healthy, curly hair:

  • Wash your hair no more than once a week.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a month.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to make your hair naturally curly, it’s time to embrace your beautiful curls. Remember to be patient and gentle with your hair, and it will reward you with healthy, bouncy curls.
