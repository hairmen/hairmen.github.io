Which Haircut Suits For Short Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 08, 2023 · 5 min read

Short haircuts are a popular trend these days. They are easy to manage, stylish, and can give you a fresh look. However, choosing the right haircut for short hair can be a daunting task. With so many styles to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which one will suit you the best. In this blog post, we will guide you through the different haircuts that suit short hair and help you find the perfect style for you.

Description

Short haircuts can be defined as haircuts that are above the shoulders. They are perfect for people who want to change their look or have low maintenance hair. Short haircuts can suit any face shape, but it’s important to choose the right one for your face shape and hair type. Short haircuts can be versatile and can be styled in different ways to suit different occasions.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Short Hair

Choosing the right haircut for short hair can be a challenging task. Here are some tips that can help you choose the perfect haircut:

Consider your face shape

Your face shape plays a crucial role in determining which haircut will suit you. For example, if you have a round face, you can opt for a pixie cut or a bob with side-swept bangs to create an illusion of length. If you have a square face, you can go for a layered bob or a lob to soften your features.

Consider your hair type

Your hair type is another important factor to consider when choosing a haircut. If you have thin hair, you can opt for a blunt bob or a pixie cut to create an illusion of thickness. If you have thick hair, you can go for a layered bob or a shag to add texture and movement to your hair.

Consider your lifestyle

Your lifestyle is also an important factor to consider when choosing a haircut. If you have a busy lifestyle and don’t have time to style your hair, you can opt for a low-maintenance haircut like a pixie cut or a bob. If you have an active lifestyle, you can go for a short haircut that can be easily styled and maintained.

Step by Step Guide to Different Haircuts for Short Hair

Here is a step by step guide to different haircuts that suit short hair:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a popular short haircut that is perfect for people who want a low maintenance haircut. Here is a step by step guide to getting a pixie cut:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts.
  3. Start cutting your hair from the back to the front.
  4. Use scissors to create layers.
  5. Style your hair using a texturizing spray.

Bob

The bob is a classic short haircut that can be styled in different ways. Here is a step by step guide to getting a bob:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts.
  3. Cut your hair to the desired length.
  4. Use scissors to create layers.
  5. Style your hair using a straightening iron or a curling iron.

Lob

The lob is a longer version of the bob and is perfect for people who want a short haircut that is not too short. Here is a step by step guide to getting a lob:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts.
  3. Cut your hair to the desired length.
  4. Use scissors to create layers.
  5. Style your hair using a straightening iron or a curling iron.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking good. Here are some tips for maintaining short haircuts:

Trim your hair regularly

Short haircuts require regular trimming to maintain their shape. It’s recommended to get your hair trimmed every four to six weeks.

Use the right hair products

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in how your hair looks and feels. It’s important to use products that are specifically designed for short hair.

Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. It’s recommended to avoid heat styling as much as possible or use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair.

FAQ

1. Can short haircuts suit any face shape?

Yes, short haircuts can suit any face shape. It’s important to choose the right haircut for your face shape to enhance your features.

2. Can short haircuts be styled in different ways?

Yes, short haircuts can be styled in different ways. You can use different hair products and styling tools to create different looks.

3. Do short haircuts require regular maintenance?

Yes, short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking good. It’s recommended to get your hair trimmed every four to six weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

1. Wash your hair regularly

It’s important to wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for short hair.

2. Use a hair mask

Using a hair mask once a week can help nourish and moisturize your hair. Choose a hair mask that is suitable for your hair type.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Exposure to the sun can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. It’s recommended to wear a hat or use a hair sunscreen when you are out in the sun.

Conclusion

Short haircuts are a great way to change your look and have low maintenance hair. Choosing the right haircut for short hair can be a challenging task, but with the right guidance, you can find the perfect style for you. Consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle when choosing a haircut. Remember to maintain your short haircut regularly and use the right hair products to keep it looking good.
