What Is The Best Haircut For Someone With A Round Face

Written by William Nov 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you someone with a round face? Do you find it difficult to choose the right haircut that suits your face shape? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss what is the best haircut for someone with a round face, how to get it, and tips to maintain it.

Description

A round face has soft and curved features, with a width and length that are almost the same. The goal of a haircut for a round face is to create an illusion of length and angles, which can help to elongate the face shape. The right haircut can make a huge difference in enhancing your features and highlighting your best assets.

How to

The best haircut for someone with a round face is one that creates an illusion of length and angles. Here are some options that you can consider:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help to add movement and texture to your hair, which can create an illusion of length. The layers should start from the jawline and go downwards, which can help to elongate the face shape. You can style your hair with loose waves or curls to add more volume and dimension.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to add more angles to your face shape, which can make it look less round. The bangs should be long enough to cover your forehead and sweep to the side, which can create a diagonal line that can break up the roundness of your face. You can style your bangs with a round brush and blow dryer to add more volume and movement.

3. Bob with Angled Layers

A bob with angled layers can help to add more shape and structure to your hair, which can create an illusion of length. The bob should be chin-length or longer, and the layers should be angled downwards, which can help to elongate the face shape. You can style your hair with a side part and straighten it with a flat iron to add more sleekness.

Step by Step

If you want to get the best haircut for someone with a round face, here are the steps that you can follow:

Step 1: Consult with a Professional

Before getting a haircut, it is important to consult with a professional stylist who can assess your face shape and suggest the right haircut for you. They can also give you tips on how to style and maintain your haircut.

Step 2: Choose the Right Haircut

Based on your face shape and personal preferences, choose the right haircut that suits you. You can consider the options mentioned above or ask your stylist for recommendations.

Step 3: Maintain Your Haircut

To maintain your haircut, you should follow a regular hair care routine, which includes washing, conditioning, and styling. You should also trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Tips

Here are some tips that can help you to get the best haircut for someone with a round face:

1. Avoid blunt cuts

Blunt cuts can make your face shape look rounder, so it is better to avoid them. Instead, go for layered cuts that can add more movement and texture to your hair.

2. Add volume to the crown

Adding volume to the crown of your hair can create an illusion of length and height, which can balance out the roundness of your face. You can use a volumizing spray or mousse to add more lift to your hair.

3. Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and hair colors. You can try different partings, updos, and braids to find the style that suits you the most.

FAQ

1. Can I get a pixie cut if I have a round face?

Yes, you can get a pixie cut if you have a round face, but it is important to choose the right style that suits your face shape. A pixie cut with longer layers on top and shorter sides can help to elongate your face shape.

2. Should I go for a center or side parting?

A side parting can create a diagonal line that can break up the roundness of your face, while a center parting can make your face shape look rounder. So, it is better to go for a side parting if you have a round face.

3. How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Hair Care

To maintain your haircut, you should follow a regular hair care routine, which includes washing, conditioning, and styling. Here are some tips that can help you to take care of your hair:

1. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type

Using the right shampoo and conditioner can help to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Choose a product that suits your hair type and use it regularly.

2. Avoid heat styling tools

Using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make it look dull. So, it is better to avoid them or use them sparingly.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Exposure to the sun can damage your hair and make it look dry and frizzy. So, it is important to protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF.

So, these are some tips and tricks that can help you to get the best haircut for someone with a round face. Remember, the right haircut can make a huge difference in enhancing your features and highlighting your best assets. So, choose wisely!
