Is Silver Hair Still In Style 2021

Written by Robby Sep 12, 2023 Â· 2 min read

Are you considering dyeing your hair silver or maintaining your current silver locks? This article will discuss if silver hair is still in style in 2021 and provide tips and tricks for maintaining silver hair.

Description

Silver hair has been a popular trend for a few years now, but is it still in style in 2021? The short answer is yes. Silver hair is still a trendy and fashionable hair color choice for many people.

How To

If you want to achieve silver hair, the first step is to bleach your hair to a pale blonde shade. This can be done at home or by a professional stylist. Once your hair is the desired shade of blonde, a silver toner can be applied to achieve the silver look.

Step by Step

1. Bleach hair to a pale blonde shade.

2. Apply a silver toner to achieve the silver look.

3. Maintain silver hair by using purple shampoo and conditioner to prevent brassiness.

Tips

1. Use purple shampoo and conditioner to maintain silver hair.

2. Avoid washing hair too frequently to prevent fading.

3. Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve silver hair without bleaching my hair?

A: No, silver hair requires a pale blonde base.

Q: How often should I wash my silver hair?

A: It is recommended to wash silver hair every 2-3 days to prevent fading.

Q: What should I do if my silver hair turns yellow or brassy?

A: Use purple shampoo and conditioner to neutralize the yellow tones.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of silver hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. Using a hair mask once a week can help keep hair moisturized and prevent breakage. Additionally, using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools can help prevent damage to hair.

In conclusion, silver hair is still a trendy and fashionable hair color choice in 2021. With proper maintenance and care, silver hair can look beautiful and vibrant for months to come.
