Is Purple Hair Unprofessional A Guide To Rocking Your Unconventional Hair Color In The Workplace

Written by Aprilia Oct 28, 2023 · 5 min read

As we move towards a more inclusive and accepting society, unconventional hair colors like purple, blue, and pink have become increasingly popular. However, many people still wonder if these bright hues are appropriate for professional settings. In this article, we'll explore the question: is purple hair unprofessional? We'll provide tips for rocking your unconventional hair color in the workplace and answer some frequently asked questions about hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Wear Purple Hair Professionally
  • Step by Step: How to Dye Your Hair Purple
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Purple Hair
  • FAQs About Purple Hair in the Workplace
  • Hair Care: How to Keep Your Hair Healthy and Vibrant

Description

Purple hair, like any unconventional hair color, can be seen as unprofessional in certain workplaces. However, the answer to the question "is purple hair unprofessional?" ultimately depends on the company culture and dress code. Some workplaces may be more accepting of unconventional hair colors, while others may have strict guidelines on personal appearance.

That being said, there are ways to wear purple hair in a professional manner. By choosing the right shade, styling your hair appropriately, and pairing your hair color with professional attire, you can rock your purple hair in any workplace.

How to Wear Purple Hair Professionally

Here are some tips for wearing purple hair in a professional setting:

Choose the Right Shade

When it comes to unconventional hair colors, there are many shades to choose from. If you're looking for a more professional look, opt for a darker, more muted shade of purple. This can include eggplant, plum, or a deep violet.

Style Your Hair Appropriately

How you style your hair can also affect how professional it looks. In general, it's best to keep your hair neat and tidy. This can include wearing it in a sleek ponytail, a low bun, or straight and down. Avoid wild or messy styles that can distract from your professional appearance.

Pair Your Hair Color with Professional Attire

Your purple hair will look more professional when paired with appropriate attire. Stick to classic, professional clothing like suits, blouses, and slacks. Avoid anything too casual or revealing.

Step by Step: How to Dye Your Hair Purple

Now that you know how to wear purple hair professionally, let's talk about how to dye your hair. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Choose Your Shade

As mentioned earlier, there are many shades of purple to choose from. Consider your skin tone and natural hair color when selecting your shade.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before dyeing your hair, you'll need to prep it. This can include washing and conditioning your hair, as well as brushing out any tangles.

Step 3: Apply the Dye

Follow the instructions on your hair dye kit to apply the dye evenly to your hair. Be sure to wear gloves to avoid staining your hands.

Step 4: Let the Dye Set

Allow the dye to set according to the instructions on the kit. This can range from 20 minutes to an hour, depending on the product.

Step 5: Rinse and Condition

Rinse out the dye with cool water until the water runs clear. Apply the conditioner that came with your kit to lock in the color and keep your hair healthy.

Tips for Maintaining Your Purple Hair

Once you have your purple hair, you'll want to keep it looking vibrant and healthy. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair color:

Use Color-Safe Products

Regular shampoo and conditioner can strip the color from your hair. Use products specifically designed for colored hair to keep your purple looking its best.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and cause the color to fade. If you must use these tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.

Get Regular Touch-Ups

As your hair grows, your roots will begin to show. Schedule regular touch-ups to keep your hair color looking fresh.

FAQs About Purple Hair in the Workplace

Is purple hair unprofessional?

As mentioned earlier, the answer to this question depends on the company culture and dress code. In general, it's best to choose a more muted shade of purple and style your hair neatly to maintain a professional appearance.

Can I dye my hair purple if I work in a conservative workplace?

You'll need to check with your employer's dress code policy to determine if unconventional hair colors are allowed. If they're not, you may need to wait until you work in a more accepting environment.

Can I wear my hair in a wild style if it's a professional color?

Even if you choose a professional shade of purple, wearing your hair in a wild style can still be distracting. Stick to neat and tidy styles for a professional appearance.

Hair Care: How to Keep Your Hair Healthy and Vibrant

Finally, let's talk about hair care. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and vibrant:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Eat a Healthy Diet

Your hair needs nutrients to stay healthy. Be sure to eat a balanced diet full of protein, vitamins, and minerals.

Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Use a Deep-Conditioning Treatment

Deep-conditioning treatments can help keep your hair soft and shiny. Use a treatment once a week for best results.

In conclusion, purple hair can be professional if you choose the right shade, style your hair appropriately, and pair it with professional attire. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can rock your unconventional hair color in any workplace.
