How To Cut Curly Hair For Round Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying to style your curly hair in a way that flatters your round face? The solution might be as simple as getting a haircut that is tailored to your face shape. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to cut curly hair for round face.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Curly Hair for Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Curly Hair for Round Face
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

Description

Curly hair can be challenging to cut, especially if you have a round face. The goal is to create a hairstyle that elongates your face and draws attention away from its roundness.

The best way to achieve this is by opting for a haircut that has layers, volume, and texture. This type of haircut will add dimension to your hair and create the illusion of a longer face.

How to Cut Curly Hair for Round Face

The first step in cutting curly hair for a round face is to find a stylist who has experience working with curly hair. They will be able to give you the best advice on what type of haircut will suit your face shape and hair type.

Once you have found a stylist, it's important to communicate your preferences and concerns. Let them know what you like and dislike about your hair and what you want to achieve with your new haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cut curly hair for round face:

  1. Start by sectioning the hair into four parts: two at the front and two at the back.

  2. Begin cutting the hair at the back, starting from the nape of the neck. Use a pair of sharp scissors to create soft layers that blend into each other.

  3. Move on to the sides, creating layers that start at the cheekbones and blend into the back layers.

  4. For the front sections, cut the hair at an angle to create a face-framing effect. The longest layers should be at the chin, while the shortest layers should be at the cheekbones.

  5. Once you have finished cutting, style the hair using a diffuser to enhance the natural curl pattern.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair for Round Face

Here are some additional tips for cutting curly hair for round face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts as they tend to make the face appear rounder.

  • Opt for layers that start at the cheekbones or higher to create the illusion of a longer face.

  • Use a diffuser when styling to enhance the natural curl pattern and create volume.

  • Avoid cutting the hair too short as it can make the face appear rounder.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best haircut for a round face with curly hair?

The best haircut for a round face with curly hair is one that has layers, volume, and texture. This type of haircut will elongate the face and draw attention away from its roundness.

Should I cut my curly hair wet or dry?

It's best to cut curly hair when it's dry as this will give you a more accurate idea of how the hair will look once it's styled.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It's recommended to cut curly hair every 8-12 weeks to maintain its shape and health.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair is essential to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for curly hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.

  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep the hair hydrated.

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle the hair to avoid breakage.

  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage the hair and cause frizz.

By following these tips and getting a haircut that suits your face shape, you can achieve beautiful, healthy, and well-styled curly hair.
