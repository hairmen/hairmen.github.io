What Body Type Guys Find Attractive: Tips and FAQ (2023 Guide)

Are you curious about what body type guys find attractive? Do you want to know how to look your best and attract the right kind of attention? Look no further than this guide, where we'll break down everything you need to know about what body type guys find attractive. From body types to hairstyles, we've got you covered.

Body Types

When it comes to body types, guys tend to have different preferences. Some guys like curvy girls, while others prefer a leaner physique. However, there are a few body types that tend to be universally attractive:

Mesomorphs

Mesomorphs are people who have a naturally athletic build, with broad shoulders and a narrow waist. This body type is often associated with strength and fitness, and guys tend to find it very attractive. If you're a mesomorph, you're in luck â you're already ahead of the game in terms of attracting guys.

Hourglass Figures

Hourglass figures are characterized by a small waist and curvy hips and thighs. This body type is often considered the epitome of femininity, and guys tend to find it incredibly attractive. If you're an hourglass, you can emphasize your curves by wearing clothing that cinches at the waist and flares at the hips.

Slim Builds

While curvier body types are often celebrated, many guys also find slim builds very attractive. If you have a naturally thin frame, you can enhance your attractiveness by focusing on your posture and keeping your body toned through exercise.

Hairstyles

Your hairstyle can also play a big role in how attractive guys find you. While everyone's tastes are different, there are a few hairstyles that tend to be universally appealing:

Long, Flowing Hair

Long, flowing hair has been considered attractive for centuries, and it's still a popular choice today. If you have long hair, you can enhance its appeal by keeping it healthy and well-maintained.

Soft Waves

Soft waves are a great way to add some texture and movement to your hair. They can be achieved with a curling iron or by braiding your hair overnight. Soft waves are a universally flattering style that can work for any hair type.

Messy Buns

A messy bun is a low-maintenance style that can still look incredibly chic. If you're short on time or just want a casual look, a messy bun is a great option.

Tips for Attractiveness

While body type and hairstyle can play a big role in how attractive guys find you, there are a few other things you can do to enhance your appeal:

Focus on Your Posture

Good posture can make a big difference in how you're perceived by others. Stand up straight, keep your shoulders back, and hold your head high to exude confidence and poise.

Wear Clothes That Fit

Ill-fitting clothing can make you look sloppy and unkempt. Make sure you're wearing clothes that fit properly and flatter your body type.

Take Care of Your Skin

Healthy, glowing skin is always attractive. Establish a skincare routine that works for you, and make sure you're drinking plenty of water to keep your skin hydrated.

Remember, everyone has different preferences when it comes to body type. While some guys might prefer a curvier figure, others might find a slim build more attractive. The most important thing is to feel confident in your own skin.
