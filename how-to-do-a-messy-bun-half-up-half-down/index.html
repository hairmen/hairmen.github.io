How To Do A Messy Bun Half Up Half Down

Written by Luffy Nov 04, 2023 · 3 min read
If you're looking for a quick and easy hairstyle to elevate your look, then a messy bun half up half down may be just what you need. This hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, whether you're going to a formal event or just running errands. In this post, we'll go over some simple steps to help you achieve the perfect messy bun half up half down look.

Description

A messy bun half up half down is a hairstyle where the top half of your hair is pulled back into a bun, while the bottom half remains loose. This creates a stylish and effortless look that can be dressed up or down.

How to

To achieve a messy bun half up half down, follow these simple steps:
  1. Start by brushing your hair to get rid of any tangles.
  2. Take the top half of your hair and pull it back towards the crown of your head.
  3. Twist the hair and wrap it into a bun. Use bobby pins to secure it in place.
  4. Leave the bottom half of your hair loose.
  5. Use your fingers to create a messy look by pulling out a few strands of hair from the bun and around your face.

Step by Step

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Before starting, make sure your hair is brushed to get rid of any tangles.

Step 2: Pull Back the Top Half of Your Hair

Take the top half of your hair and pull it back towards the crown of your head.

Step 3: Twist and Wrap into a Bun

Twist the hair and wrap it into a bun. Use bobby pins to secure it in place.

Step 4: Leave the Bottom Half of Your Hair Loose

Leave the bottom half of your hair loose.

Step 5: Create a Messy Look

Use your fingers to create a messy look by pulling out a few strands of hair from the bun and around your face.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect messy bun half up half down:
  • Use hair spray or texturizing spray to help create the messy look.
  • Don't worry if your bun isn't perfect. A messy bun should look effortless.
  • Try adding some accessories, such as a headband or hair clip, to elevate the look.

FAQ

Is a messy bun half up half down suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a messy bun half up half down is suitable for all hair types, whether you have curly, straight, or wavy hair.

How long does it take to do a messy bun half up half down?

It should only take a few minutes to do a messy bun half up half down, making it a perfect hairstyle for when you're short on time.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to:
  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
In conclusion, a messy bun half up half down is an easy and stylish hairstyle that can be achieved in just a few minutes. Follow these simple steps and tips to create the perfect look for any occasion. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
