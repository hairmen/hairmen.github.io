Best Short Haircuts For Long Thin Faces

Written by Luffy Aug 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your long and thin face? Look no further because we have compiled a list of the best short haircuts for long thin faces. Whether you want to add volume or elongate your face, these haircuts will flatter your features and bring out your best side. Let's dive in!

Description

Long and thin faces can be a bit challenging to work with when it comes to hairstyles. The aim is to create an illusion of a broader face while maintaining a proportionate look. Finding the right short haircut can be a game-changer for your appearance. But before we jump into the best haircuts, let's understand the features of long thin faces.

Features of Long Thin Faces

Long thin faces are characterized by:

  • An elongated forehead
  • A prominent chin
  • A narrow bone structure
  • A pointed jawline

It's essential to choose a haircut that balances out these features and brings symmetry to your face. Now, let's explore the best short haircuts for long thin faces.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut?

When choosing a short haircut for long thin faces, keep the following things in mind:

  • Choose a style that adds volume to the sides of your face
  • Avoid styles that accentuate your forehead and chin
  • Opt for haircuts that create the illusion of width
  • Consider the texture of your hair

By keeping these things in mind, you can select a short haircut that flatters your features and brings out your best side.

Best Short Haircuts for Long Thin Faces

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic and versatile haircut that works wonders for long thin faces. It adds volume and texture to the sides of your face, creating an illusion of width. Plus, it's easy to maintain and style, making it a perfect choice for busy individuals.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another popular short haircut that flatters long thin faces. It creates volume and shape around your face, making it look fuller and more proportionate. You can opt for a chin-length bob or a longer bob that grazes your shoulders.

3. Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. It creates movement and dimension, making your hair look fuller and thicker. You can choose to have long layers or short layers, depending on your preference.

4. Blunt Cut

A blunt cut is a no-fuss haircut that adds structure and shape to your hair. It's a great way to create an illusion of width while maintaining a polished look. You can opt for a chin-length blunt cut or a shorter cut that grazes your ears.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Long Thin Faces

Styling short haircuts for long thin faces is easy and straightforward. Follow these steps to create a polished and voluminous look:

  • Wash and condition your hair according to your hair type
  • Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to add more dimension to your hair
  • Finish off with a light hairspray to hold your style in place

By following these steps, you can create a stylish and voluminous look that flatters your long thin face.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Long Thin Faces

To maintain your short haircut and keep it looking fresh, follow these tips:

  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape
  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage
  • Try different styling techniques to create different looks

By following these tips, you can keep your short haircut looking great and maintain its shape for longer.

FAQs

1. Will a short haircut make my face look longer?

No, a short haircut can actually create an illusion of width and balance out your long thin face. Choose a haircut that adds volume to the sides of your face to create a more proportionate look.

2. Can I style my short haircut differently every day?

Yes, short haircuts are versatile and easy to style. You can try different techniques, such as curling or straightening, to create different looks every day.

3. Will a blunt cut work for my long thin face?

Yes, a blunt cut can work wonders for long thin faces. It adds structure and shape to your hair, creating an illusion of width and balance.

Hair Care for Long Thin Faces

When it comes to hair care for long thin faces, it's essential to use products that add volume and texture to your hair. Look for volumizing shampoos, conditioners, and sprays that can give your hair a boost. Additionally, avoid using too much heat on your hair and trim it regularly to maintain its shape.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect short haircut for your long thin face and rock it with confidence. Remember to choose a style that flatters your features and makes you feel great!
