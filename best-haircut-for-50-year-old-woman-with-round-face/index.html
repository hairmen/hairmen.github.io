Best Haircut for 50 Year Old Woman with Round Face - Tips and Ideas

If you're a 50-year-old woman with a round face, you might be wondering what the best haircut for you is. Having a round face can be challenging when it comes to finding a hairstyle that complements your features. In this article, we'll give you tips and ideas on the best haircuts for 50-year-old women with round faces.

How to Determine Your Face Shape?

Before we dive into the best haircuts for round faces, it's essential to determine your face shape. There are four basic face shapes: round, oval, heart, and square. To determine your face shape, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Step 2: Determine Your Face Length

Measure the length of your face from your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Step 3: Determine Your Face Shape

If your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrows at the forehead and jawline, you have a round face shape. If your forehead is wider than your cheekbones and jawline, you have an oval face shape. If your forehead and jawline are similar in width, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you have a heart-shaped face. And finally, if your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar in width, you have a square face shape.

Best Haircuts for 50 Year Old Woman with Round Face

Now that you know your face shape let's discuss the best haircuts for 50-year-old women with round faces. The goal is to create height and length to elongate the face while slimming the cheeks.

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for women with round faces. The layers add volume and movement to the hair, creating a slimming effect on the face. You can wear your hair straight or wavy with this haircut.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold option for women over 50, but it's a great way to show off your features. The short layers draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones while slimming the face. This cut works best for women with thick hair.

3. Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. The length of the hair should be chin-length or longer, with layers that angle towards the face. This haircut creates a slimming effect on the face while framing the jawline.

4. Shoulder-Length Hair

Shoulder-length hair is a great option for women with round faces. The length of the hair draws attention away from the cheeks, creating a slimming effect on the face. Layers can be added to create movement and volume to the hair.

Hair Care Tips for 50 Year Old Woman with Round Face

Once you've found the perfect haircut, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and health. Here are some hair care tips for 50-year-old women with round faces:

1. Use a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Look for products that contain natural ingredients that nourish and strengthen your hair.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair, making it look dull and lifeless. Instead, let your hair air-dry or use a diffuser on low heat to style your hair.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential to maintain the shape of your haircut. Schedule a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

FAQs

1. What is the best haircut for a 50-year-old woman with a round face?

The best haircut for a 50-year-old woman with a round face is one that creates height and length to elongate the face while slimming the cheeks. Long layers, pixie cuts, bob cuts, and shoulder-length hair are great options.

2. What should I avoid when choosing a haircut for a round face?

Avoid haircuts that are too short, as they can make your face look wider. Also, avoid blunt cuts that end at the cheekbones as they can make your face look rounder.

3. How can I style my hair to make my face look slimmer?

You can style your hair by adding volume and height to the crown of your head. You can also wear your hair in an updo, which pulls the hair away from your face, creating a slimming effect.

Conclusion

Choosing the best haircut for a 50-year-old woman with a round face can be challenging, but hopefully, this article has given you some tips and ideas on how to find the perfect hairstyle. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and health.
