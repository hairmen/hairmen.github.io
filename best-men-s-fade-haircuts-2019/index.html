Description

Are you looking for a fresh new look in 2019? Look no further than the men's fade haircut. This classic style has been popular for years, but it's still going strong. With so many variations, there's a fade haircut for every man. From low fades to high fades, there's something for everyone. Keep reading to learn more about the best men's fade haircuts of 2019.

How to

If you're looking to get a fade haircut, it's best to visit a professional barber. They'll be able to give you a clean, even cut that's tailored to your hair type and facial structure. Before you head to the barber, do some research and find a few styles that you like. This will give your barber a better idea of what you're looking for.

Choose Your Fade Type

There are several types of fades to choose from. Here are a few of the most popular:

  • Low Fade: The hair is cut short around the neckline and gradually gets longer as it goes up the head.
  • High Fade: The hair is cut very short or shaved around the sides and back of the head, leaving more length on top.
  • Mid Fade: The hair is cut to a medium length around the sides and back of the head, with a gradual taper towards the top.
  • Bald Fade: The hair is cut extremely short or shaved down to the skin.

Choose Your Hair Length and Style

Once you've decided on the type of fade you want, it's time to choose your hair length and style. Some popular options include a short, textured crop or a longer, slicked-back style. Your barber can help you choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a fade haircut:

  1. Choose your fade type and hair length.
  2. Show your barber a picture of the style you want.
  3. Sit back and relax while your barber works their magic.
  4. Once the cut is finished, ask your barber for styling tips and product recommendations.

Tips

  • Be open to your barber's suggestions. They're the experts and know what will look best on you.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products to keep your hair looking and feeling its best.
  • Maintain your fade haircut by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

What is a fade haircut?

A fade haircut is a style where the hair gradually gets shorter as it goes down the sides and back of the head. There are several types of fades, including low fades, high fades, mid fades, and bald fades.

What face shapes work best with a fade haircut?

Fade haircuts work well on most face shapes, but they're particularly flattering on men with square or angular faces. If you have a round face, consider a longer style on top to balance out your features.

How do I style a fade haircut?

The styling process will vary depending on your hair length and type, but some popular techniques include using a pomade or wax to create texture, blow-drying your hair for volume, or using a straightening iron for a sleek look.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is key to maintaining a fade haircut. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straightening irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush or comb to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain your fade haircut.
