Best Bangs For Round Faces 2021 Create A Flattering Look

Written by Oliver Sep 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for the best bangs for round faces 2021? Whether you have a round face or not, choosing the right type of bangs can help frame your face and create a flattering look. In this article, we will discuss the different types of bangs that work best for round faces, how to style them, and some hair care tips to keep your bangs looking their best.

Description

Round faces have soft, curved lines, which means that the goal of bangs is to create angles and balance out the shape of the face. The key is to choose bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. This will help elongate the face and make it look slimmer. Additionally, you should avoid blunt bangs that end at the cheekbones, as they can make your face look wider.

How to Choose the Best Bangs for Round Faces

When choosing the best bangs for round faces, there are a few things to keep in mind:

Consider Your Hair Texture

The thickness and texture of your hair can play a role in which type of bangs will work best for you. For example, if you have thin hair, wispy bangs can help create volume and texture. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, blunt bangs can add structure and definition.

Think About Your Lifestyle

When choosing bangs, it's important to consider your lifestyle. If you're busy and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, you may want to opt for low-maintenance bangs that can be swept to the side. However, if you have more time to devote to styling your hair, you may want to choose a more intricate style.

Consult with Your Hairstylist

Your hairstylist can be a great resource when it comes to choosing the best bangs for your face shape. They can provide you with recommendations based on your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style preferences.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Bangs

Once you've chosen the best bangs for your round face, it's important to know how to style them. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any product buildup and prepare your hair for styling.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Bangs

Using a round brush, blow dry your bangs in the direction that you want them to fall. This will help create volume and give them a polished look.

Step 3: Style Your Bangs

Once your bangs are dry, use a styling product to add texture and hold. You can use a small amount of pomade or wax to create a piecey, tousled look, or use a styling cream to add shine and smoothness.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your bangs in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold the style without making your hair feel stiff or crunchy.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

Now that you know how to style your bangs, it's important to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining your bangs:

Trim Your Bangs Regularly

To keep your bangs looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long, it's important to trim them every 4-6 weeks. This will help maintain their shape and prevent them from looking too bulky.

Use Dry Shampoo

If you don't have time to wash your hair every day, use dry shampoo to absorb oil and refresh your bangs. Simply spray a small amount onto your roots and massage it in with your fingers.

Avoid Touching Your Bangs

Touching your bangs throughout the day can cause them to become greasy and lose their shape. To prevent this, try to avoid touching them as much as possible.

FAQs

What Are the Best Bangs for a Round Face?

The best bangs for a round face are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. This helps create angles and balance out the shape of the face. Additionally, you should avoid blunt bangs that end at the cheekbones, as they can make your face look wider.

How Do You Style Bangs for a Round Face?

To style bangs for a round face, start by washing your hair and blow drying your bangs in the direction that you want them to fall. Use a styling product to add texture and hold, and finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep them in place all day.

How Often Should You Trim Your Bangs?

You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long. This will help maintain their shape and prevent them from looking too bulky.

Hair Care for Bangs

When it comes to hair care for bangs, it's important to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent product buildup. Additionally, you should avoid using hot tools on your bangs too often, as this can cause damage and breakage. Finally, be sure to keep your hair well-hydrated by using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

In conclusion, choosing the best bangs for round faces 2021 is all about creating angles and balance. By choosing the right type of bangs and styling them correctly, you can create a flattering look that enhances your natural beauty. Remember to consult with your hairstylist, trim your bangs regularly, and use gentle hair care products to keep your bangs looking their best.
