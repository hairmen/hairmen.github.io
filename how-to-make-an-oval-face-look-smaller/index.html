How To Make An Oval Face Look Smaller

Written by William Sep 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you may find that your face looks a bit rounder or fuller than you would like. Fortunately, there are many ways to make your oval face look smaller and more balanced. In this article, we will go over some tips and tricks to help you achieve a more slender and sculpted look.

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that is wider than the chin, and a face that is longer than it is wide. While this face shape is considered symmetrical and balanced, it can sometimes look a bit rounder or fuller than desired. To achieve a more slender and sculpted look, there are several things you can do, from makeup techniques to hairstyle choices.

How to Make an Oval Face Look Smaller: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Contour Your Face

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using darker shades of makeup to create shadows and definition on your face. To make your oval face look smaller, focus on contouring the areas that you want to appear slimmer, such as your cheeks, temples, and jawline. Use a matte bronzer or contour powder that is one or two shades darker than your natural skin tone, and blend it well for a natural-looking finish.

Step 2: Highlight Your Features

In addition to contouring, you can also use highlighting to draw attention to your features and create the illusion of a more sculpted face. Apply a highlighter to the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, brow bones, and the bridge of your nose. This will add dimension and make your face appear more angular and defined.

Step 3: Choose the Right Hairstyle

Your hairstyle can also play a big role in the overall appearance of your face. To make your oval face look smaller, opt for hairstyles that add volume and width to the sides of your face, such as layered cuts or hairstyles with waves or curls. Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as these can elongate your face and make it look even slimmer.

Step 4: Try Bangs

If you're looking for a more drastic change, consider getting bangs. Bangs can help to shorten the appearance of your face and create a more balanced look. Opt for side-swept bangs or choppy bangs that hit just above your eyebrows for the most flattering effect.

Tips for Making an Oval Face Look Smaller

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a more sculpted and balanced look:

  • Wear statement earrings or necklaces to draw attention away from your face.
  • Experiment with different makeup techniques, such as highlighting your lips or eyes to create a focal point.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or flat, as these can make your face look even longer.
  • Invest in a good skincare routine to keep your skin looking healthy and radiant.
  • Consider getting a professional facial or microdermabrasion treatment to help refine and sculpt your face.

FAQ about Making an Oval Face Look Smaller

Q: Can I still wear my hair straight if I have an oval face?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair straight, but try to add some volume and texture to the sides of your face to create a more balanced look. You can use a texturizing spray or volumizing mousse to add body and movement to your hair.

Q: Will contouring make my face look unnatural?

A: Not if you blend it well and use the right shades. Choose a matte bronzer or contour powder that is one or two shades darker than your natural skin tone, and blend it well for a natural-looking finish.

Q: What type of bangs are best for an oval face?

A: Side-swept bangs or choppy bangs that hit just above your eyebrows are the most flattering for an oval face shape.

Hair Care Tips for an Oval Face Shape

In addition to the tips mentioned above, there are some general hair care tips that can help keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and buildup.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and keep it soft and shiny.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily make your oval face look smaller and more balanced. Whether you opt for a new hairstyle, experiment with makeup techniques, or invest in a professional facial, there are many ways to achieve a sculpted and refined look that flatters your unique face shape.
