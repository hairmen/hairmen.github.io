How To Get The Messy Wet Hair Look

Written by Petter Jul 11, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's easy to achieve and requires minimal effort, the messy wet hair look might be just what you need. This effortless style is perfect for those days when you want to look put together without putting in too much effort. Here's how to achieve it:

How to

Step 1: Start with damp hair

The first step in achieving the messy wet hair look is to start with damp hair. You can achieve this by showering or simply wetting your hair with a spray bottle. Make sure your hair is damp, not soaking wet.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. This can be a mousse, gel, or texturizing spray. The product will help to create the messy look and keep your hair in place. Make sure to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair.

Step 3: Scrunch your hair

Once you've applied the styling product, use your hands to scrunch your hair. This will help to create the messy texture that's characteristic of the wet hair look. Scrunch your hair from the bottom up, making sure to get all of your hair.

Step 4: Let your hair air dry

After you've scrunched your hair, let it air dry. Don't use a blow dryer or towel to dry your hair, as this will disturb the messy texture you've created. Simply let your hair dry naturally.

Step 5: Tousle your hair

Once your hair is dry, use your hands to tousle it. This will help to create even more texture and give your hair a more natural, messy look. Tousle your hair by running your fingers through it and shaking it out.

Tips

Here are some additional tips that can help you achieve the messy wet hair look:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to evenly distribute the styling product
  • Try using a salt spray to add more texture to your hair
  • Don't overdo it with the styling product – a little goes a long way
  • Experiment with different products to see which works best for your hair type

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the messy wet hair look:

Q: Can I achieve this look with short hair?

A: Yes, the messy wet hair look can be achieved with short hair. Simply follow the same steps and adjust the amount of product you use accordingly.

Q: How long will this style last?

A: The messy wet hair look is meant to be a more relaxed, natural style, so it won't last as long as a more structured hairstyle. However, it should last for a few hours or until your hair dries completely.

Q: Can I achieve this look if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, the messy wet hair look can be achieved with curly hair. Simply follow the same steps and adjust the amount of product you use accordingly. You may also want to use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help enhance your curls.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to ensure it looks its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the messy wet hair look in no time. Remember to have fun and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
