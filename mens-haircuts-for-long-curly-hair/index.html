Mens Haircuts For Long Curly Hair A Guide For Men With Natural Curls

Written by Petter Oct 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a man with long curly hair and looking for the perfect haircut? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing mens haircuts for long curly hair, providing you with a guide on how to achieve the perfect style for your natural curls. Whether you have tight coils or loose waves, we have got you covered. Read on to discover the best hairstyles for men with long curly hair.

Description

Long curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it is unique, visually appealing and exudes personality. On the other hand, it can be challenging to manage and style. Men with long curly hair often struggle with finding the right haircut that complements their natural curls while also being easy to maintain. This article aims to provide you with a range of mens haircuts for long curly hair that will make you look and feel confident.

How to

Before we dive into the different mens haircuts for long curly hair, let's talk about the basics of styling curly hair. Firstly, it's important to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair. This will help to keep your curls hydrated and defined. Secondly, avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet. Finally, use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and add moisture to your hair.

Step by Step

Step 1: Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair by gently patting it with a towel. Do not rub or twist your hair as this can cause frizz.

Step 3: Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Start from the ends and work your way up to the roots.

Step 5: Style your hair using one of the mens haircuts for long curly hair discussed below.

Tips

When it comes to styling long curly hair, there are a few tips that can help you achieve the best results:

  • Avoid using too much product as this can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.
  • Invest in a diffuser attachment for your hairdryer as this will help to define your curls while also reducing frizz.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Experiment with different mens haircuts for long curly hair to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

What are the best mens haircuts for long curly hair?

There are several mens haircuts for long curly hair that are popular among men with natural curls:

  • The undercut
  • The messy curly top
  • The curly fringe
  • The curly side part
  • The curly man bun

How do I maintain my mens haircut for long curly hair?

Maintaining your mens haircut for long curly hair is relatively simple. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair to keep your curls hydrated and defined. Avoid using too much product, and invest in a diffuser attachment for your hairdryer to reduce frizz.

Can I straighten my long curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your long curly hair using a hair straightener. However, it's important to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair. It's also worth noting that straightening your hair frequently can cause dryness and breakage, so it's best to limit how often you do it.

Hair Care

Caring for your long curly hair is essential if you want it to look its best. Here are some tips for keeping your curls healthy and hydrated:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to help define your curls and add moisture.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Now that you have a better understanding of mens haircuts for long curly hair, it's time to experiment and find the style that suits you best. Remember to take care of your curls by using the right products and following a good hair care routine. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can rock your natural curls with confidence!
