Best Medium Haircuts For Thick Straight Hair In 2023

Written by Oliver Aug 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you blessed with thick straight hair? Lucky you! However, it can be challenging to style and maintain. Fortunately, there are a lot of medium haircuts that work well with this hair type. In this article, we will discuss the best medium haircuts for thick straight hair in 2023.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Medium haircuts are perfect for thick straight hair because they can help lighten the weight of your hair while still maintaining its natural volume. It is crucial to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture. The following are some of the best medium haircuts for thick straight hair in 2023.

How to

The best way to achieve a medium haircut is by visiting a professional stylist. They can help you choose the right style and cut your hair to the desired length. However, if you prefer to do it yourself, make sure to invest in a good pair of scissors and watch tutorials online to avoid any mistakes.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.

2. Blow-dry your hair until it is completely dry.

3. Divide your hair into sections and start cutting it to the desired length.

4. Use a styling product to set your hair in place.

Tips

- Choose a style that complements your face shape.

- Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help enhance your hair's natural volume.

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair as this can damage it.

- Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.

FAQ

Q: Can I still achieve a medium haircut if my hair is not straight?

A: Yes! You can still achieve a medium haircut even if your hair is not straight. However, you may need to use styling tools or products to help achieve the desired look.

Q: Will a medium haircut work with all face shapes?

A: No, it is essential to choose a style that complements your face shape. Talk to your stylist to find the best style for you.

Hair Care

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

- Avoid using too many styling products as this can weigh your hair down.

- Use a hair mask once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.

- Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Overall, there are many medium haircuts that work well with thick straight hair. Whether you prefer a bob, lob, or shag, make sure to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture.
