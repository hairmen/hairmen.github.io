Description

Long face shapes are characterized by a lengthened forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If you have a long face shape, finding a hairstyle that flatters your features can be challenging. However, short hairstyles can work wonders to balance out a long face shape and create a more proportional appearance.

In this guide, we will discuss the best short hairstyles for long face girls in 2023. Whether you have straight, curly, or fine hair, there is a short hairstyle that will suit your face shape and complement your features.

How to Choose a Short Hairstyle for Long Face Girl

When choosing a short hairstyle for long face girl, it is important to consider the following factors:

  • Face shape: Long face shapes can be paired with hairstyles that add width and volume to the sides of the face to balance out the length. Short hairstyles with layers or bangs can help create the illusion of a wider face shape.
  • Hair texture: Your hair texture should also be considered when choosing a short hairstyle. Fine hair can benefit from a blunt bob or pixie cut, while thick hair can be styled with choppy layers or a shaggy cut.
  • Personal style: Your personal style and preferences should also be taken into account. Whether you prefer a classic bob, edgy pixie cut, or messy shag, there is a short hairstyle that will suit your style.

Short Hairstyles for Long Face Girl

Here are some of the best short hairstyles for long face girl:

  • Pixie Cut: A pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that can work for long face shapes. The short layers and choppy texture can help add volume to the sides of the face, balancing out the length.
  • Bob: A blunt bob or chin-length bob can also flatter a long face shape. The clean lines of a bob can help create the illusion of a wider face shape.
  • Shag: A shaggy cut with choppy layers can add volume and texture to fine hair, while also balancing out a long face shape.
  • Side-Swept Bangs: Side-swept bangs can help soften a long face shape and add width to the forehead.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hairstyles for Long Face Girl

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling short hairstyles for long face girl:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is curly or wavy, use a straightening iron to straighten it.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair. This will help add volume and hold to your hairstyle.
  3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, focusing on the roots to add volume.
  4. If you have bangs, use a straightening iron to smooth them out and create a side-swept effect.
  5. Use a curling iron to add waves or curls to your hair, if desired.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hairstyles for Long Face Girl

Here are some tips for maintaining short hairstyles for long face girl:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.
  • Use a dry shampoo to extend the time between washes and keep your hair looking voluminous.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and cause breakage.
  • Invest in quality hair care products, such as shampoo, conditioner, and styling products, to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for long face girl:

  • Can I wear bangs with a short hairstyle? Yes, side-swept bangs can be a great addition to a short hairstyle for long face girl.
  • What is the best hair texture for a short hairstyle? A variety of hair textures can work for a short hairstyle, including fine, thick, straight, and curly hair.
  • Will a short hairstyle make my face look wider? No, a short hairstyle can actually help balance out a long face shape by adding volume and width to the sides of the face.

Hair Care for Short Hairstyles for Long Face Girl

Here are some tips for caring for your hair when you have a short hairstyle for long face girl:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray before styling with heat tools.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and short hairstyles for long face girl, you can achieve a stylish and flattering look that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty.
