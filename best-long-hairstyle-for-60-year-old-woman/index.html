Best Long Hairstyle For 60 Year Old Woman

Written by Daniel Aug 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Content:

  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ

Description

As we age, our hair tends to lose its thickness and fullness. However, that doesn’t mean that women over 60 cannot rock long hairstyles. In fact, long hair can be a great choice for women in their 60s, as it can help them look youthful and vibrant.

How to

If you’re a 60-year-old woman looking for the best long hairstyle, you should first consider your face shape and hair texture. Some hairstyles may not work well with certain face shapes or hair textures, so it’s important to choose a style that complements your unique features.

Face Shape

If you have a round face, you may want to consider a long hairstyle with layers that frame your face. If you have a square face, a layered hairstyle with soft waves can help soften your features. If you have an oval face, you can pretty much rock any long hairstyle you like.

Hair Texture

If you have fine hair, you may want to consider a long hairstyle with layers to add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, a long hairstyle with blunt ends can help control its weight and keep it from looking too bulky.

Step by step

Once you’ve determined your face shape and hair texture, it’s time to choose a specific long hairstyle. Here are some of the best long hairstyles for 60-year-old women:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can add movement and volume to fine hair, making it look fuller and more youthful. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to add long layers throughout your hair, starting at your chin and working their way down.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help soften square or angular face shapes, while adding a touch of sophistication. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle, starting at the outer corner of your eyebrow and gradually getting longer towards the other side.

3. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves can add texture and movement to any long hairstyle, giving it a carefree and youthful vibe. To achieve this look, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves throughout your hair, then use your fingers to tousle them for a more natural look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your long hairstyle:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dull and lifeless.

FAQ

Q: Can women over 60 wear long hair?

A: Yes, women over 60 can definitely wear long hair. However, it’s important to choose a style that complements your unique features, such as your face shape and hair texture.

Q: What are some of the best long hairstyles for fine hair?

A: Some of the best long hairstyles for fine hair include long layers, side-swept bangs, and beachy waves.

Hair Care

To keep your long hairstyle looking its best, it’s important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it’s wet, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it looking healthy.

By following these tips and choosing a long hairstyle that complements your unique features, you can look and feel your best at any age.
