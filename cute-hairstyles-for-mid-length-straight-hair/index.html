Cute Hairstyles For Mid Length Straight Hair

Written by Oliver Jun 14, 2023 · 4 min read

In this article, we will be discussing cute hairstyles for mid length straight hair. Mid length hair is versatile and can be styled in various ways. Whether you're going to the office, a party or a casual day out, we've got you covered. These hairstyles are easy to do and will make you look chic and stylish.

Description

Mid length straight hair is hair that falls between the chin and the shoulders. It's a popular length because it's not too short or too long. It's versatile and can be styled in various ways. Mid length straight hair is easy to maintain and style, making it perfect for busy women.

How to

To achieve these cute hairstyles, you will need a few hair tools such as a hairbrush, hair ties, bobby pins, and a curling iron. These hairstyles can be done in under 10 minutes, making them perfect for women on the go.

1. Half Up Half Down

The half up half down hairstyle is a classic and easy hairstyle. This hairstyle is perfect for mid length straight hair because it shows off the length of your hair. To achieve this hairstyle, brush your hair back and tie the top half of your hair into a ponytail. Use bobby pins to secure the ponytail in place. Curl the ends of your hair for a more polished look.

2. Low Ponytail

The low ponytail is a simple and chic hairstyle. This hairstyle is perfect for mid length straight hair because it keeps your hair out of your face while still showing off the length of your hair. To achieve this hairstyle, brush your hair back and tie it into a low ponytail. Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail in place. Curl the ends of your hair for a more polished look.

3. Side Part

The side part is a classic and sophisticated hairstyle. This hairstyle is perfect for mid length straight hair because it adds volume and dimension to your hair. To achieve this hairstyle, part your hair to the side and brush it out. Use a curling iron to curl the ends of your hair for a more polished look.

Step by step

1. Half Up Half Down

  1. Brush your hair back.
  2. Tie the top half of your hair into a ponytail.
  3. Use bobby pins to secure the ponytail in place.
  4. Curl the ends of your hair for a more polished look.

2. Low Ponytail

  1. Brush your hair back.
  2. Tie it into a low ponytail.
  3. Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail in place.
  4. Curl the ends of your hair for a more polished look.

3. Side Part

  1. Part your hair to the side.
  2. Brush it out.
  3. Use a curling iron to curl the ends of your hair for a more polished look.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your mid length straight hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using a curling iron.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color for a more natural look.
  • Use a hair tie that won't damage your hair.
  • Don't use too much product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles.

FAQ

1. Can I use a straightener instead of a curling iron?

Yes, you can use a straightener to curl the ends of your hair.

2. Can I do these hairstyles on curly hair?

Yes, you can do these hairstyles on curly hair. You may need to straighten your hair first for a more polished look.

Hair Care

Mid length straight hair is easy to maintain, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair.

In conclusion, these cute hairstyles for mid length straight hair are easy to do and will make you look chic and stylish. Whether you're going to the office, a party, or a casual day out, these hairstyles are perfect for any occasion. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
