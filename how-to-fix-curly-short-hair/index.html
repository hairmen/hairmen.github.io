How To Fix Curly Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

How to Fix Curly Short Hair | Step by Step Guide 2023

Do you have curly short hair that just won't cooperate? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle with managing their curly locks, especially when they're short. But fear not, there are simple solutions that can help you fix your curly short hair in no time. In this article, we'll go over how to fix curly short hair, step by step.

How to Fix Curly Short Hair

Step 1: Start with a Good Haircut

The first step to fixing curly short hair is to start with a good haircut. You want to make sure that your hair is cut in a way that enhances your natural curls and doesn't make them look frizzy or messy. Look for a stylist who has experience working with curly hair and ask for a cut that is tailored to your specific curl pattern.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make a huge difference in managing curly short hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl enhancing creams or gels. These products will help define your curls and keep them looking bouncy and shiny.

Step 3: Blow Dry with a Diffuser

When blow drying your curly short hair, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This will help distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz. Start by drying your hair on low heat and gradually increase the heat as needed.

Step 4: Finger Comb Your Hair

After blow drying, use your fingers to gently comb through your hair. This will help separate your curls and give them a more defined look. Avoid using a brush or comb, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

Step 5: Use a Curling Iron

If you want to add more definition to your curls, use a curling iron. Choose a curling iron with a small barrel size, as this will help create tighter curls. Wrap small sections of hair around the barrel and hold for a few seconds.

Tips for Fixing Curly Short Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when fixing curly short hair:

  • Don't over-wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it frizzy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider getting a silk pillowcase, as this can help prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about fixing curly short hair:

Q: Can I use a brush on my curly short hair?

A: No, it's best to avoid using a brush on curly short hair, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Use your fingers to gently comb through your hair instead.

Q: How often should I wash my curly short hair?

A: It's best to wash curly short hair every 2-3 days, as over-washing can strip it of its natural oils and make it frizzy.

Q: Can I straighten my curly short hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your curly short hair, but it's important to use a heat protectant spray and not do it too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Hair Care for Curly Short Hair

Here are a few tips for taking care of your curly short hair:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos that contain sulfates, as these can dry out your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using towels to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently blot your hair dry.

Conclusion

Fixing curly short hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, it's definitely doable. Remember to start with a good haircut, use the right products, and be gentle when styling your hair. With a little patience and practice, you'll have beautiful, defined curls in no time.
