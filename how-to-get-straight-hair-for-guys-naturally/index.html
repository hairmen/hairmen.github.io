How To Get Straight Hair For Guys Naturally A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Sep 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a guy with curly or wavy hair, you may have considered straightening it. Going to the salon and getting a chemical treatment or using a flat iron may not be the best options for everyone. Fortunately, there are natural ways to get straight hair for guys. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to get straight hair for guys naturally.

  • How to Get Straight Hair for Guys Naturally
  • Step by Step Guide

Description

Straight hair can give you a polished and put-together look. However, chemical treatments and heat styling can cause damage to your hair. The good news is that there are natural ways to straighten hair without causing harm. By using natural ingredients and techniques, you can get straight hair that looks healthy and shiny.

How to Get Straight Hair for Guys Naturally

Here are some natural ways to get straight hair for guys:

1. Milk and Honey

Milk and honey can help straighten hair naturally. Mix one cup of milk with one tablespoon of honey and apply it to your hair. Leave it on for an hour and then wash it off with shampoo.

2. Coconut Milk and Lemon Juice

Coconut milk and lemon juice can also help straighten hair. Mix one cup of coconut milk with one tablespoon of lemon juice and apply it to your hair. Leave it on for an hour and then wash it off with shampoo.

3. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera contains enzymes that can help straighten hair. Apply aloe vera gel to your hair and leave it on for an hour. Then, wash it off with shampoo.

4. Olive Oil

Olive oil can help straighten hair and add shine. Apply olive oil to your hair and leave it on for 30 minutes. Then, wash it off with shampoo.

5. Brushing and Blow Drying

Brushing your hair while blow-drying can help straighten it. Use a paddle brush and point the blow dryer nozzle down while brushing your hair.

6. Hair Rollers

Using hair rollers can also help straighten hair naturally. Roll your hair while it's damp and leave the rollers in overnight. In the morning, remove the rollers and brush your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to get straight hair for guys naturally:

Step 1: Choose a Natural Straightening Method

Decide which natural method you want to use to straighten your hair. You can choose from milk and honey, coconut milk and lemon juice, aloe vera, olive oil, brushing and blow drying, or hair rollers.

Step 2: Prepare the Ingredients

Prepare the ingredients for the natural method you've chosen. For example, if you've chosen milk and honey, mix one cup of milk with one tablespoon of honey.

Step 3: Apply the Mixture to Your Hair

Apply the mixture to your hair and make sure it's evenly distributed. Use a comb to ensure that the mixture reaches all parts of your hair.

Step 4: Leave the Mixture on for the Recommended Time

Leave the mixture on your hair for the recommended time. This can vary depending on the natural method you've chosen.

Step 5: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo to remove the natural mixture.

Step 6: Dry Your Hair

Dry your hair using a blow dryer or let it air dry, depending on your preference.

Tips for Getting Straight Hair for Guys Naturally

Here are some tips for getting straight hair for guys naturally:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before applying any natural mixture.
  • Wrap your hair in a towel after applying the natural mixture to help it penetrate your hair.
  • If you're using hair rollers, make sure your hair is completely dry before removing them.
  • Be patient. Natural methods may take longer to straighten your hair than chemical treatments or heat styling.

FAQs

Can I use these natural methods on colored or chemically treated hair?

Yes, you can use these natural methods on colored or chemically treated hair. However, it's best to do a patch test first to make sure the natural mixture doesn't cause any adverse reactions.

How often should I use these natural methods?

You can use these natural methods once a week or as often as needed. However, it's important to give your hair a break in between treatments to prevent damage.

Will these natural methods make my hair greasy?

It's possible that these natural methods may make your hair greasy if you use too much of the mixture or if you don't wash it off properly. Make sure to follow the instructions carefully and use the recommended amount of mixture.

Hair Care for Straight Hair

Once you've achieved straight hair using natural methods, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for straight hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to get straight hair for guys naturally, you can achieve a polished and put-together look without causing damage to your hair. Remember to be patient and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.
