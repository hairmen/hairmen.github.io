How To Cut A Bob To Curl Under The Ultimate Guide

  • Tips for Cutting a Bob to Curl Under

Description

A bob haircut is a classic look that never goes out of style. But if you want to add some extra flair to your bob, you can cut it to curl under. This style is perfect for those who want a sophisticated and polished look without spending hours on styling their hair every day.

Cutting a bob to curl under may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done at home. In this guide, we will take you through a step-by-step process on how to cut a bob to curl under. We will also provide you with some tips and tricks to make the process easier and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Cut a Bob to Curl Under

The first step in cutting a bob to curl under is to choose the right length for your hair. The length will depend on your face shape and personal style. Generally, a bob that ends just below the chin or at the collarbone is ideal for this style.

Next, you will need to gather the necessary tools, including sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. You may also want to invest in a razor comb to help create a textured look.

Once you have your tools, it’s time to section your hair. Start by parting your hair down the middle and then divide it into four sections – two in the front and two in the back. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut a bob to curl under:

  1. Start with the back section of your hair. Take a small section of hair from the nape of your neck and comb it straight down. Cut it straight across at the desired length.
  2. Continue to work your way up the back of your head, taking small sections of hair and cutting them straight across. Be sure to use the first section as a guide for the rest of your hair.
  3. Once you have finished the back, move on to the front sections. Take a small section of hair from the front and comb it straight down. Use the guide from the back to cut the hair straight across.
  4. Repeat this process on the other side of your head.
  5. Once you have finished cutting all of the sections, use the razor comb to create texture and volume. This will help the hair to curl under more easily.
  6. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create a curled-under effect. You can also use a straightener or curling iron to create more defined curls.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Cutting a Bob to Curl Under

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curled-under bob:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take your time and work in small sections to ensure an even cut.
  • Don’t cut your hair too short. It’s easier to cut more hair off than it is to add it back on.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while cutting.
  • Consider getting your hair cut by a professional if you are unsure.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a bob to curl under:

Q: Will a curled-under bob work for all hair types?
A: Yes, a curled-under bob can work for all hair types, but it may require different styling techniques depending on your hair’s texture. Q: Can I cut a curled-under bob on myself?
A: Yes, but it may be easier to have someone else help you with the process, especially if you are new to cutting hair. Q: How often should I get my curled-under bob trimmed?
A: It’s recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your bob.

Hair Care Tips for Your New Bob

Once you have cut your bob to curl under, you will want to take proper care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair too often.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Cutting a bob to curl under is a great way to update your look and add some extra style to your hair. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a polished and sophisticated look that is easy to maintain. Follow our step-by-step guide, use our tips and tricks, and take proper care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
