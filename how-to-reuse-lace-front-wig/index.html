How To Reuse Lace Front Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of buying new lace front wigs every time you want to change your hairstyle? Reusing lace front wigs is a great way to save money and reduce waste. In this guide, we will show you how to reuse a lace front wig, from removing it to cleaning and storing it properly. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Removing the Wig
  • Step 2: Cleaning the Wig
  • Step 3: Storing the Wig
  • Tips for Reusing a Lace Front Wig
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Lace Front Wigs

Step 1: Removing the Wig

The first step in reusing a lace front wig is to remove it properly. Start by gently lifting the lace from your forehead and pulling it back. Use a wig cap or hair net to protect the wig from tangling or getting caught in your hair. Once the wig is off, carefully remove any adhesive or tape residue from the lace using a cotton ball or swab soaked in rubbing alcohol.

Next, clean your scalp and hairline with a gentle cleanser or oil-based remover. This will help remove any residue from the adhesive or tape and prevent skin irritation. Once your scalp is clean, let it dry completely before reapplying the wig.

Step 2: Cleaning the Wig

Before reusing your lace front wig, it is essential to clean it thoroughly. Start by brushing out any tangles or knots using a wide-tooth comb or wig brush. Then, fill a sink or basin with cool water and add a small amount of wig shampoo or gentle cleanser. Gently agitate the wig in the water, being careful not to rub or twist it too much.

Rinse the wig thoroughly with cool water until all the soap is gone. Avoid using hot water or a hair dryer, as this can damage the fibers and cause the wig to lose its shape. Instead, gently pat the wig dry with a towel and let it air dry on a wig stand or mannequin head.

Step 3: Storing the Wig

Proper storage is essential to keep your lace front wig in good condition and ready for reuse. Start by brushing out any tangles or knots and spraying the wig with a detangling spray or leave-in conditioner. Then, carefully fold the wig in half, with the lace side facing inwards. Place the wig in a clean plastic bag or wig box and store it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight or heat sources.

Tips for Reusing a Lace Front Wig

  • Use a wig cap or hair net to protect the wig and prevent tangling or matting.
  • Avoid using hot tools or styling products on the wig, as this can damage the fibers and cause frizz or breakage.
  • Store the wig in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight or heat sources.
  • Clean the wig regularly to remove dirt, oil, and product buildup.
  • Consider using a wig spray or leave-in conditioner to keep the wig looking fresh between washes.

FAQs

Can I reuse a lace front wig more than once?

Yes, you can reuse a lace front wig multiple times with proper care and maintenance. Clean the wig regularly, store it properly, and avoid using hot tools or styling products that can damage the fibers.

How often should I clean my lace front wig?

You should clean your lace front wig after every 8-10 wears, or as needed if it becomes dirty or oily. Use a gentle cleanser or wig shampoo and avoid using hot water or a hair dryer to prevent damage to the fibers.

Can I sleep in my lace front wig?

We do not recommend sleeping in your lace front wig, as this can cause tangling, matting, and damage to the fibers. Instead, remove the wig before bed and store it properly to keep it in good condition.

Hair Care for Lace Front Wigs

Proper hair care is essential to keep your lace front wig looking and feeling great. Use a gentle cleanser or wig shampoo to clean the wig, and avoid using hot tools or styling products that can damage the fibers. Brush the wig regularly to prevent tangles or knots, and store it properly when not in use.

By following these simple tips and steps, you can reuse your lace front wig and save money while still achieving the perfect hairstyle. Whether you're looking for a sleek and straight look or curly and voluminous locks, a lace front wig can help you achieve any style you want with ease.
