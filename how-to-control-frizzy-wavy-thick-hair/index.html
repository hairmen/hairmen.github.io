How To Control Frizzy Wavy Thick Hair

Written by Frank Nov 03, 2023 · 5 min read

Frizzy hair can be a real struggle, especially for those with wavy and thick hair. It can be difficult to manage and style, leaving you feeling frustrated and unhappy with your hair. In this article, we'll discuss how to control frizzy wavy thick hair and provide you with some helpful tips to keep your hair looking its best.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Control Frizzy Wavy Thick Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Managing Frizzy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Frizzy Hair

Description:

Frizzy hair can be caused by a number of factors, including genetics, humidity, and damage from heat styling tools. Those with wavy and thick hair are particularly prone to frizz, as the hair tends to be more porous and susceptible to moisture. Fortunately, there are several ways to control frizzy hair and achieve a smooth, sleek look.

How to Control Frizzy Wavy Thick Hair:

Here are some tips for controlling frizzy wavy thick hair:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

One of the best ways to combat frizz is by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that contain hydrating ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, or argan oil. These ingredients will help to smooth the hair cuticle and prevent moisture from penetrating the hair shaft.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons can damage the hair and cause frizz. Instead of using heat, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. If you must use heat, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

3. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Leave-in conditioners are great for controlling frizz and keeping hair hydrated throughout the day. Apply a small amount to damp hair and comb through with your fingers or a wide-toothed comb.

4. Invest in a Good Hairbrush

A good hairbrush can make all the difference when it comes to managing frizz. Look for a brush with natural bristles, as they are gentler on the hair and help to distribute oils evenly throughout the hair.

5. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and frizz. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every third day, and use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

Step by Step Guide:

Here is a step by step guide on how to control frizzy wavy thick hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Be sure to rinse thoroughly and avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, as this can cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to damp hair and comb through with your fingers or a wide-toothed comb.

Step 3: Air-Dry or Use a Diffuser Attachment

Air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to minimize damage and prevent frizz.

Step 4: Use a Good Hairbrush

Use a good hairbrush with natural bristles to distribute oils evenly throughout the hair and prevent frizz.

Step 5: Style as Desired

Style your hair as desired, using minimal heat styling tools and a heat protectant spray if necessary.

Tips for Managing Frizzy Hair:

Here are some additional tips for managing frizzy hair:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Wear a silk or satin scarf or bonnet to bed to prevent friction and frizz.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out the hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQs:

Q: Can I use a straightener or curling iron on frizzy hair?

A: Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using heat styling tools too frequently.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: Aim to wash your hair every other day or every third day, and use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

Q: What should I do if my hair is still frizzy after following these tips?

A: Consider seeing a hairstylist for a professional treatment, such as a keratin treatment or a hair smoothing treatment.

Hair Care for Frizzy Hair:

Here are some additional tips for caring for frizzy hair:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb instead of a brush to avoid pulling on the hair and causing frizz.
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to the ends of your hair to keep them hydrated and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles, as they can cause friction and breakage.

Controlling frizzy wavy thick hair can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, it is possible to achieve a smooth, sleek look. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and don't be afraid to seek professional help if necessary.
