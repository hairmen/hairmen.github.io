What Does Tapering Your Hair Mean

Written by Bardi Nov 29, 2023 · 3 min read

If you’re someone who loves to experiment with hairstyles, you may have come across the term “tapering your hair”. Tapering your hair means cutting your hair in a way that gradually decreases the length of your hair from top to bottom. This creates a gradual fade effect that looks stylish and modern.

How to Taper Your Hair?

If you’re looking to taper your hair, you can follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

Choosing the right clippers is important when tapering your hair. You’ll need clippers with adjustable blades so that you can gradually decrease the length of your hair.

Step 2: Decide on Your Fade Style

There are different styles of taper fades such as low, mid, and high fades. You can choose the style that suits your face shape and personal style.

Step 3: Start with the Right Length

Start with a longer length at the top of your head and gradually decrease the length as you move towards the bottom.

Step 4: Blend the Hair at the Sides and Back

In order to achieve a smooth and seamless fade, you’ll need to blend the hair at the sides and back of your head. Use a comb and clippers to blend the hair.

Step 5: Finish with Scissors

Once you’ve achieved your desired length, finish with a pair of scissors to trim the hair around your ears and neckline.

Tips for Tapering Your Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect taper:

  • Start with clean and dry hair
  • Use a mirror to check your progress
  • Take your time and don’t rush
  • Use different clipper guards to achieve different lengths
  • Practice on a mannequin head before trying it on yourself

FAQs About Tapering Your Hair

1. How often should I taper my hair?

It depends on your hair growth rate and personal preference. Some people prefer to taper their hair every few weeks, while others prefer to do it every few months.

2. Can I taper my hair at home?

Yes, you can taper your hair at home if you have the right tools and know-how to do it. However, if you’re not confident in your skills, it’s best to go to a professional barber.

3. What should I do if I make a mistake while tapering my hair?

If you make a mistake while tapering your hair, it’s best to stop and let a professional barber fix it. Trying to fix it yourself could make it worse.

Hair Care Tips After Tapering Your Hair

After tapering your hair, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the taper
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind

Now that you know what tapering your hair means, you can try it out for yourself. Whether you decide to do it at home or go to a professional barber, a tapered hairstyle is a great way to update your look and stay stylish.
