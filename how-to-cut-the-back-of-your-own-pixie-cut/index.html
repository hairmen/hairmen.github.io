How To Cut The Back Of Your Own Pixie Cut

Written by Robby Jul 31, 2023 · 4 min read
How to Cut the Back of Your Own Pixie Cut | Tips and Tricks

If you have a pixie cut, you know how important it is to keep it trimmed and looking fresh. However, going to the hair salon every few weeks can be time-consuming and expensive. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut the back of your own pixie cut in the comfort of your own home.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is usually cut short on the sides and back, and longer on the top. It's a low-maintenance hairstyle that looks great on anyone, regardless of their hair type. Pixie cuts require regular trimmings to maintain their shape, and while it's easy to cut the sides and front, the back can be a bit tricky.

How to Cut the Back of Your Own Pixie Cut

Cutting the back of your own pixie cut may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Comb your hair and remove any tangles. It's also a good idea to trim any split ends before you start cutting the back of your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into two parts, using a comb to create a clean and straight part down the back of your head. Use hair clips to clip the top section of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the Back of Your Hair

Using sharp hairdressing scissors, start cutting the back of your hair. Start at the bottom of your hairline and work your way up. Take small sections of hair at a time and use your fingers as a guide. Cut in a straight line, using the bottom of your hairline as a guide.

Step 4: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting the back of your hair, unclip the top section and check your work. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure that your hair is even.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you cut the back of your own pixie cut:

  • Invest in a good pair of hairdressing scissors. Don't use regular scissors, as they can damage your hair.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time.
  • Have a mirror in front of you and behind you to ensure that you're cutting your hair evenly.
  • If you're not confident cutting your hair, ask a friend or family member to help you.

FAQ

Can I cut the back of my own pixie cut with clippers?

While it's possible to cut the back of your hair with clippers, it's not recommended unless you have experience using them. Clippers can be difficult to maneuver and can leave your hair looking uneven.

How often should I cut the back of my pixie cut?

It's recommended that you cut the back of your pixie cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape.

Can I color my hair after cutting the back of my pixie cut?

Yes, you can color your hair after cutting the back of your pixie cut. However, it's recommended that you wait a few days after cutting your hair to give it time to settle.

Hair Care

Maintaining your pixie cut takes more than just cutting the back of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently cut the back of your own pixie cut and maintain your hairstyle without breaking the bank. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
