Hairstyles For Long Face Lady Tips Tricks And Ideas

Written by Frank Jul 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a long face, you may feel like your hair is your only saving grace. However, finding the right hairstyle can be challenging. Fortunately, there are plenty of hairstyles that flatter long faces. From short to long, curly to straight, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll discuss some of the best hairstyles for long face ladies, along with tips, tricks, and ideas for keeping your hair healthy and looking great.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Long Face

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, let's talk about how to choose the right one for your face shape. The goal is to create balance and proportion, so you want to avoid styles that elongate your face even more. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Look for Hairstyles with Volume

If you have a long face, you want to avoid hairstyles that are flat or sleek. Instead, look for styles that add volume and body. This could be anything from loose waves to a curly updo. The key is to create the illusion of width, which will help balance out the length of your face.

2. Avoid Straight, Blunt Bangs

Straight, blunt bangs can make a long face look even longer. If you want to try bangs, go for something wispy or side-swept. This will help break up the length of your face and add some softness to your features.

3. Consider Layers

Layers are a great way to add dimension and movement to your hair. They can also help balance out a long face. If you have long hair, consider adding some layers around your face. If you have shorter hair, try a layered bob or pixie cut.

Hairstyles for Long Face Ladies

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, let's talk about some specific styles that flatter long faces:

1. Long, Loose Waves

Long, loose waves are a great option for long face ladies. They add volume and body, and the waves help create the illusion of width. You can wear your hair down or pull it back into a loose ponytail or bun.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add some softness to your features. They also help break up the length of your face. You can wear them with your hair down or pull them back into a ponytail or updo.

3. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a great option if you have shorter hair. The layers help add volume and dimension, and the length helps balance out your face. You can wear it straight or add some loose waves for extra body.

4. Curly Updo

If you have curly hair, an updo can be a great option for a special occasion. A curly updo adds volume and dimension, and the height helps balance out the length of your face. You can add some braids or twists for extra detail.

Tips for Healthy Hair

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips for keeping your locks healthy and looking great:

1. Use a Sulfate-Free Shampoo

Sulfates can be harsh on your hair, stripping away natural oils and leaving it dry and damaged. Look for a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling wands can damage your hair over time. Try to avoid them as much as possible, and if you do use them, use a heat protectant spray.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are a few common questions about hairstyles for long face ladies:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid hairstyles that are flat or sleek, as well as straight, blunt bangs. These styles can make a long face look even longer.

Can I wear my hair up if I have a long face?

Absolutely! Just make sure to add some volume and dimension to your updo. Curly updos are a great option for long face ladies.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but most people can get away with washing their hair every 2-3 days. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Conclusion

If you have a long face, don't despair! There are plenty of hairstyles that flatter your features. Look for styles with volume, avoid straight, blunt bangs, and consider adding layers. And don't forget to take care of your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo, regular trims, and heat protectant spray. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look and feel great every day.
