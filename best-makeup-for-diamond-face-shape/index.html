Best Makeup For Diamond Face Shape A Complete Guide

Are you someone with a diamond-shaped face and want to know the best makeup techniques to enhance your features? Look no further as we have got you covered. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on the best makeup for diamond face shape, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A diamond-shaped face is characterized by high cheekbones, a narrow forehead, and a pointed chin. The main objective of makeup for this face shape is to balance out the cheekbones, minimize the width of the forehead, and soften the angles of the chin.

How To

Before we dive into the makeup techniques, it is essential to prep the skin. Start by cleansing and moisturizing the face with products suitable for your skin type. This will ensure your makeup goes on smoothly and lasts longer.

Foundation

Apply foundation all over your face, focusing on the T-zone area. Use a shade that matches your skin tone to avoid any harsh lines. You can also use a color-correcting primer to neutralize any redness or discoloration.

Contouring

Contouring is crucial for a diamond-shaped face as it helps balance out the high cheekbones. Use a contour shade to create a shadow under the cheekbones, temples, and jawline. Blend it out well to avoid any harsh lines.

Blush

Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks and blend it upwards towards your temples. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone, and avoid applying too much as it can make your cheeks look even fuller.

Eyebrows

Eyebrows can make or break a makeup look. For a diamond-shaped face, fill in your eyebrows with soft strokes to create a natural arch. Avoid making them too angular as it can add to the sharpness of your face.

Eyes

For the eyes, opt for neutral shades like browns, taupes, and grays. Apply a light shade all over the lid and a darker shade to the outer corner and crease. Use eyeliner to create a soft wing and curl your lashes to open up your eyes.

Lips

Keep your lips simple with a nude or soft pink shade. Avoid dark or bold colors as they can make your lips appear smaller.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the best makeup for diamond face shape:

  1. Cleanse and moisturize the skin.
  2. Apply foundation all over the face.
  3. Use a contour shade to create shadows under the cheekbones, temples, and jawline.
  4. Apply blush to the apples of the cheeks and blend upwards.
  5. Fill in eyebrows with soft strokes.
  6. Apply neutral eyeshadow shades.
  7. Use eyeliner to create a soft wing.
  8. Curl lashes and apply mascara.
  9. Apply a nude or soft pink lip color.
  10. Set the makeup with a setting spray to make it last longer.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when doing makeup for diamond-shaped faces:

  • Use a highlighter on the high points of your face to add dimension.
  • Avoid using too much product on the forehead as it can make it appear wider.
  • Blend out all harsh lines to create a seamless look.
  • Choose makeup products that suit your skin type and tone.
  • Experiment with different makeup techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about makeup for diamond face shape:

What is the best way to contour a diamond-shaped face?

The best way to contour a diamond-shaped face is to create shadows under the cheekbones, temples, and jawline. Use a contour shade that is one to two shades darker than your skin tone and blend it out well.

Can I wear bold lip colors with a diamond-shaped face?

It is best to avoid bold lip colors as they can make your lips appear smaller. Opt for nude or soft pink shades to keep the focus on your eyes and cheekbones.

What is the best eyeshadow color for a diamond-shaped face?

Neutral shades like browns, taupes, and grays work best for a diamond-shaped face. Avoid bright or bold colors as they can make your eyes appear smaller.

Hair Care

Along with makeup, your hairstyle can also enhance your diamond-shaped face. Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head to balance out the wider cheekbones. Layered haircuts or a side-swept fringe can also soften the angles of your face.

In conclusion, the best makeup for diamond face shape is all about balancing out the features and softening the angles. Use these makeup techniques and tips to enhance your natural beauty and create a flawless look.
