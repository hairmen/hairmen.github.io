How To Contour Oval Face Step By Step

Written by Daniel Oct 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered to be the most versatile and balanced, which means that it's easy to contour and highlight to create a variety of looks. In this article, we'll share step-by-step instructions on how to contour oval face, along with some tips and tricks to help you get the most out of your makeup routine.

Table of Contents

  • How to Contour Oval Face
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Oval Faces

How to Contour Oval Face

Contouring is all about creating shadows and highlights to enhance your natural features. When it comes to oval faces, the goal is to create definition around the jawline and cheekbones, while also highlighting the forehead and chin.

What You'll Need

  • Contouring powder or cream
  • Highlighter powder or cream
  • Blending brush
  • Angled brush
  • Sponge or beauty blender

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prep Your Skin

Before you start contouring, make sure your skin is clean and moisturized. Apply a primer if you have one, and then apply your foundation as you normally would.

Step 2: Contour Your Cheekbones

Using an angled brush, apply contouring powder or cream just below your cheekbones, starting at your ears and moving towards the center of your face. Blend the contouring product upwards and outwards towards your temples. Make sure to blend well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 3: Define Your Jawline

Apply the same contouring product along your jawline, blending downwards towards your neck. This will help define your jawline and create a more sculpted look.

Step 4: Highlight Your Forehead and Chin

Using a highlighter powder or cream, apply the product to the center of your forehead and the center of your chin. Blend well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 5: Blend Everything Together

Using a blending brush or sponge, blend all of the products together to create a seamless look. Make sure to blend well around your hairline and jawline to avoid any noticeable lines.

Tips and Tricks

Tip 1: Choose the Right Shade

When choosing a contouring product, make sure to choose a shade that is one or two shades darker than your natural skin tone. This will help create definition without looking too dramatic. For highlighter, choose a shade that is one or two shades lighter than your natural skin tone.

Tip 2: Use a Light Hand

Contouring is all about subtlety. Use a light hand when applying your products, and build up the intensity slowly until you achieve your desired look.

Tip 3: Don't Forget to Blend

Blending is the key to a flawless contour. Make sure to blend well between each step, and use a clean brush or sponge to blend everything together at the end.

FAQs

Q: Can I contour an oval face with a round brush?

A: While you can use a round brush to apply contouring products, an angled brush will give you more precision and control.

Q: Can I contour an oval face without foundation?

A: While it's possible to contour without foundation, we recommend starting with a clean, moisturized face and applying a light layer of foundation to create a smooth base.

Hair Care for Oval Faces

In addition to contouring, your hairstyle can also help enhance your oval face shape. Consider trying a side-swept bang or a layered bob to frame your face and create balance. Avoid hairstyles that add too much height to the top of your head, as this can make your face appear longer.

Now that you know how to contour oval face, you can experiment with different looks and find the one that works best for you. Remember to take your time, use a light hand, and blend well for a flawless finish.
