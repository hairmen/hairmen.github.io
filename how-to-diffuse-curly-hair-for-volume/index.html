How To Diffuse Curly Hair For Volume

Written by Aprilia Nov 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of flat, lifeless curls? Diffusing your hair is a great way to add volume and definition to your curls. In this article, we will go over the steps on how to diffuse curly hair for volume. Let's get started!

How to Diffuse Curly Hair for Volume: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you begin the diffusing process, make sure your hair is clean. Shampoo and condition your hair, then gently towel dry to remove excess water.

Step 2: Apply Products

Next, apply your favorite curl enhancing products to your hair. This can be a curl cream, gel, or mousse. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Flip Your Hair

Flip your hair upside down and gently scrunch your curls upwards towards your scalp. This will help to encourage your curls to form and create volume.

Step 4: Attach the Diffuser

Attach the diffuser to your hair dryer. A diffuser is a special attachment that helps to evenly distribute heat and prevent frizz. Make sure to use a low heat setting.

Step 5: Begin Diffusing

Starting at the roots, place sections of your hair into the diffuser and gently lift towards your scalp. Hold the diffuser in place for 30-60 seconds, then move on to the next section. Continue this process until all of your hair is dry.

Step 6: Finish with Cold Air

Once your hair is dry, switch your hair dryer to the cool air setting and use the diffuser to gently cool down your hair. This will help to set your curls and reduce frizz.

Tips for Diffusing Curly Hair for Volume

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your diffusing process:

  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to gently dry your hair before diffusing. This will help to prevent frizz.
  • Don't touch your hair while diffusing. This can disrupt the curl formation and create frizz.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help moisturize your hair and enhance your curls.
  • Try diffusing your hair on a low heat setting to help prevent damage.

FAQs

Can I diffuse my hair every day?

While diffusing your hair is a great way to add volume and definition, it's important not to overdo it. Diffusing your hair every day can cause damage and dryness. Try to limit diffusing to 1-2 times per week.

What if I don't have a diffuser attachment?

If you don't have a diffuser attachment, you can try using a t-shirt or microfiber towel to scrunch your hair and encourage curl formation. You can also try plopping your hair in a t-shirt or microfiber towel for 10-15 minutes before diffusing.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, voluminous curls. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to help prevent dryness.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to help keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz.

Now that you know how to diffuse curly hair for volume, you can rock those beautiful, bouncy curls with confidence!
