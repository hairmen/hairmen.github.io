Does A Lob Suit A Long Face

Written by Christine Oct 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you contemplating getting a lob haircut but unsure if it will suit your long face shape? The lob, also known as a long bob, has been a popular hairstyle for years, but it's essential to consider if it will flatter your face shape before taking the plunge. In this article, we'll explore if a lob suits a long face, how to style it, and tips for maintaining it.

Description

If you have a long face, you may feel self-conscious about hairstyles that elongate your face shape. The lob is a versatile haircut that can work with different face shapes, including long faces. A lob typically sits just above the shoulders and can be styled in various ways, such as adding waves or layers. It's a low-maintenance haircut that's perfect for those who want to switch up their hairstyle without committing to a shorter cut.

How to

When getting a lob haircut for a long face, consider adding layers and texture to create volume and movement. This will help balance out the length of your face and create the illusion of width. You can also consider adding bangs or a side-swept fringe to shorten the appearance of your forehead. When styling your lob, try adding waves or curls to create texture and volume. You can achieve this look using a curling iron or hair rollers.

Step by Step

Step 1: Consult with your stylist

Before getting a lob haircut, consult with your stylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape. They can also recommend products and styling techniques to help maintain your lob.

Step 2: Add layers and texture

When getting your lob haircut, ask your stylist to add layers and texture to create volume and movement. This will help balance out the length of your face and create the illusion of width.

Step 3: Style your lob

When styling your lob, try adding waves or curls to create texture and volume. You can achieve this look using a curling iron or hair rollers. You can also use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and hold.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your lob:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your lob.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage and split ends.

FAQ

Q: Will a lob make my face look longer?

A: A lob can elongate your face if it's not styled correctly. Adding layers and texture can help balance out the length of your face and create the illusion of width.

Q: Should I get bangs with a lob?

A: Bangs can be a great addition to a lob, especially if you have a long face. Consider getting a side-swept fringe or curtain bangs to shorten the appearance of your forehead.

Q: How often should I get a trim with a lob?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your lob and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

When it comes to maintaining a lob, it's essential to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type and avoid using too much heat when styling. Regular trims and deep conditioning treatments can help keep your hair healthy and prevent breakage. With the right care and styling, a lob can be a flattering and low-maintenance hairstyle for those with long faces.
