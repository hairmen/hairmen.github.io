How To Cut A High And Tight Fade Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Sep 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a clean, stylish, and easy-to-maintain haircut, you might want to try a high and tight fade. This haircut has been popular for decades, and it's still going strong in 2023. The good news is that you don't have to spend a lot of money or time at a barbershop to get this look. With a few basic tools and some patience, you can cut a high and tight fade yourself.

How to Cut a High and Tight Fade Yourself

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather a few tools:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Mirror
  • Hair cutting cape or towel
  • Clipper guards

Once you have your tools ready, you can start cutting your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut a high and tight fade:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Put on your hair cutting cape or towel to protect your clothes.
  3. Use the clippers with the longest guard to trim the top of your hair evenly.
  4. Switch to a shorter guard and work your way down the sides and back of your head, blending the hair as you go.
  5. Use the scissors to trim any longer hair that the clippers missed.
  6. Use the clippers without any guard to create a bald fade around the sides and back of your head.
  7. Blend the bald fade into the longer hair on top using the clippers and scissors.
  8. Check your work in the mirror and make any final adjustments.
  9. Wash your hair again to remove any loose hairs.

Tips for Cutting a High and Tight Fade

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with a longer guard on the clippers and work your way down to a shorter guard. It's easier to take off more hair than to add it back.
  • Use the mirror to check your work from different angles, including the back of your head.
  • Take your time and be patient. It's better to cut a little bit at a time than to make a big mistake.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help you with the back of your head if you need it.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a high and tight fade:

Can I cut a high and tight fade with scissors only?

No, you'll need clippers to create the bald fade around the sides and back of your head.

What's the difference between a high and tight fade and a military haircut?

A high and tight fade is a type of military haircut that's shorter on the sides and back and longer on top. However, there are many variations of military haircuts, and not all of them are high and tight fades.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your High and Tight Fade

Now that you've cut your hair, you'll want to keep it looking sharp. Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your high and tight fade:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy.
  • Use a styling product like pomade or wax to shape your hair and add texture.
  • Get regular touch-ups every 2-4 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

With these tips, you can enjoy your high and tight fade for weeks to come.
