Mens Hairstyles For Long Thick Curly Hair How To Style And Maintain Them

Written by Christine Nov 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re one of the lucky men who have long, thick, and curly hair, then you know the struggle of finding the right hairstyle that suits your hair type. Long thick curly hair can be difficult to manage and style, but with the right techniques, products, and tools, you can achieve a stylish and sophisticated look that will make heads turn.

Description

In this article, we’ll discuss the different mens hairstyles long thick curly hair that you can try, how to style them, and how to maintain your hair’s health and condition. We’ll also answer some frequently asked questions about long thick curly hair and provide tips on how to manage it.

How to Style Long Thick Curly Hair

Styling long thick curly hair can be a bit tricky, but with the right products, you can achieve a sleek and sophisticated look that will last all day. Here’s how:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Before styling your hair, make sure to wash and condition it first. Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s formulated for curly hair to keep it moisturized and frizz-free. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently pat it dry with a towel. Avoid rubbing or pulling your hair as it can cause breakage and damage.

Step 2: Apply styling products

Apply a styling product that’s designed for curly hair. You can use a mousse, gel, or cream, depending on your hair’s texture and thickness. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends to prevent dryness and split ends.

Step 3: Use a diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and prevent frizz. Make sure to use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 4: Finish with a hair oil

Finish off your hairstyle with a hair oil to add shine and control frizz. Apply a small amount of oil on your palms and run it through your hair, focusing on the ends. Avoid applying too much oil as it can weigh down your hair.

Tips for Managing Long Thick Curly Hair

Here are some tips on how to manage your long thick curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry as it can cause frizz and breakage
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair
  • Wear a hat or use a scarf to protect your hair from the sun and wind

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long thick curly hair:

Q: How often should I wash my long thick curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair’s texture and thickness. If you have a dry scalp or coarse hair, you can wash it once or twice a week. If you have a normal scalp or fine hair, you can wash it every other day.

Q: Can I use a hair straightener on my long thick curly hair?

A: It’s not recommended to use a hair straightener on curly hair as it can cause damage and breakage. If you want to achieve a straight look, you can use a blow dryer with a round brush or a flat iron that’s designed for curly hair.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my long thick curly hair?

A: You can prevent frizz by using a styling product that’s designed for curly hair, using a diffuser when blow drying your hair, avoiding brushing your hair when it’s dry, and wearing a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the elements.

Hair Care for Long Thick Curly Hair

To keep your long thick curly hair healthy and shiny, you need to take care of it on a regular basis. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s formulated for curly hair
  • Deep condition your hair once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and sophisticated look that will make you stand out from the crowd. Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for your long thick curly hair. Remember to take care of your hair on a regular basis to keep it healthy and strong.
