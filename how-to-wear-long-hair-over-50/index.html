How To Wear Long Hair Over 50 Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Aug 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Long hair is often associated with youthfulness and vitality, but that doesn't mean it's only for the young. Women over 50 can look just as stunning with long hair, as long as it's styled and cared for properly. If you're considering growing your hair out, or already have long hair, here are some tips and tricks on how to wear long hair over 50.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Long Hair Over 50
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Long Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Long Hair

How to Wear Long Hair Over 50

First and foremost, it's important to find a style that works for you and your lifestyle. Long hair can be versatile, but it also requires more maintenance than shorter styles. Some popular long hair styles for women over 50 include:

  • Long layers with side-swept bangs
  • Beachy waves
  • Straight and sleek
  • Braids and updos

When choosing a style, keep in mind your face shape and hair texture. Consult with your stylist to find the best style for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style long hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray
  3. Choose your styling tool (curling iron, straightener, etc.)
  4. Section off your hair
  5. Style each section, starting from the bottom and working your way up
  6. Finish with hairspray or other styling products

For braids and updos, follow a tutorial or seek the help of a stylist.

Tips for Styling Long Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling long hair:

  • Invest in quality styling tools
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

Remember, long hair requires more maintenance than shorter styles, but it can be worth it for the right look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing long hair over 50:

Is long hair appropriate for women over 50?

Yes, as long as it's styled and cared for properly.

What long hair styles are popular for women over 50?

Popular styles include long layers with side-swept bangs, beachy waves, straight and sleek, and braids and updos.

How can I care for my long hair?

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, avoid over-washing, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle, and get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for long hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools every day
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Avoid tight hair ties and braids, as they can cause breakage
  • Get regular trims to maintain healthy hair

With the right care and styling, long hair can be a beautiful and youthful look for women over 50. Consult with your stylist to find the best style and care routine for you.
