How To Keep Your Hair Curly Till The Next Day

Written by Eveline Oct 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Having curly hair is a blessing and a curse. While it looks gorgeous, it can be challenging to maintain the curls throughout the day, and it can be even harder to keep them looking fabulous until the next day. In this article, we will discuss how to keep your hair curly till the next day.

Table of Contents

  • How to Keep Your Hair Curly Till the Next Day
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

How to Keep Your Hair Curly Till the Next Day

The following are things you can do to keep your hair curly until the next day:

1. Use the Right Products

Curly hair requires different products from straight hair. Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to boost your curls. Apply a curl-defining cream or mousse to damp hair to hold the curls.

2. Don’t Touch Your Hair

The more you touch your hair, the more likely it is to lose its shape. Avoid running your fingers through your hair, and don’t brush or comb it. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair.

3. Sleep with a Satin Bonnet or Pillowcase

Sleeping on a cotton pillowcase can cause friction, which can lead to frizz and breakage. Instead, sleep with a satin bonnet or pillowcase. Satin is smooth, so your hair will slide against it instead of rubbing, which can help keep your curls intact.

4. Use Hair Pins

You can use hair pins to pin up your curls before bed. This will help to maintain their shape and prevent them from getting tangled or frizzy while you sleep.

5. Use a Diffuser

A diffuser attachment on a hair dryer can help to dry your hair without disturbing the curls. To use, apply your curl-defining product, then use the diffuser to dry your hair. This will help to set your curls in place.

Step by Step

The following are the steps to keep your hair curly until the next day:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse thoroughly.

Step 2: Apply Curl-Defining Product

Apply a curl-defining cream or mousse to damp hair. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Don’t use a towel to dry your hair as this can cause frizz.

Step 4: Pin Up Your Curls

Use hair pins to pin up your curls before bed. This will help to maintain their shape and prevent them from getting tangled or frizzy while you sleep.

Step 5: Sleep on a Satin Pillowcase or Bonnet

Sleep on a satin pillowcase or bonnet to prevent friction and keep your curls intact.

Tips

The following are additional tips to keep your hair curly until the next day:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools and let your hair air dry as much as possible.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Don’t use too much product as this can weigh your curls down.
  • Use a hair spray with a light hold to keep your curls in place.
  • If you have long hair, consider putting it in a loose braid before bed.

FAQ

How often should I wash my curly hair?

Curly hair should be washed every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

What is the best way to dry curly hair?

The best way to dry curly hair is to use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer, or let it air dry as much as possible.

Can I brush my curly hair?

No, you should avoid brushing curly hair as it can cause frizz and disrupt the curls’ shape. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. The following are tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Don’t rub your hair with a towel as this can cause frizz.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can keep your curly hair looking fabulous until the next day. Remember to use the right products, avoid touching your hair, sleep on a satin pillowcase, and use hair pins to keep your curls intact.
