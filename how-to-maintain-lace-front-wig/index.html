How To Maintain Lace Front Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with bad hair days? Lace front wigs are a great solution for anyone looking for a quick and easy way to switch up their hairstyle. But it’s important to know how to properly maintain your wig to keep it looking fresh and natural. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps to make sure your wig stays in top condition.

Table of Contents

  1. Step 1: Pre-Wash
  2. Step 2: Washing
  3. Step 3: Conditioning
  4. Step 4: Drying
  5. Step 5: Styling
  6. Tips for Lace Front Wig Maintenance
  7. Frequently Asked Questions
  8. General Hair Care Tips

Step 1: Pre-Wash

Before washing your lace front wig, it’s important to detangle any knots or tangles. Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to gently comb through your wig, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

Next, fill a sink or basin with lukewarm water and add a small amount of wig shampoo. Swirl the water around to create suds.

Step 2: Washing

Place your lace front wig in the water and gently swirl it around. Avoid rubbing or twisting the hair, as this can cause damage. Let the wig soak for a few minutes, then rinse it thoroughly with lukewarm water.

Repeat the washing process if necessary.

Step 3: Conditioning

After washing your wig, it’s important to condition the hair to keep it soft and manageable. Apply a small amount of wig conditioner to the hair and distribute it evenly with your fingers. Let it sit for a few minutes, then rinse it out thoroughly with lukewarm water.

Step 4: Drying

After washing and conditioning your lace front wig, gently pat it dry with a towel to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing or twisting the hair.

Next, place the wig on a wig stand or mannequin head and let it air dry completely. Avoid using a hair dryer or other heat styling tools, as this can cause damage to the hair.

Step 5: Styling

Once your lace front wig is dry, you can style it as desired. Use a wig brush or wide-tooth comb to gently comb through the hair. Avoid using regular brushes or combs, as these can cause damage to the lace and hair.

You can also use heat styling tools on your wig, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using high heat settings.

Tips for Lace Front Wig Maintenance

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head when not in use
  • Avoid sleeping in your wig
  • Avoid swimming or showering in your wig
  • Use a wig cap to protect your natural hair under the wig
  • Avoid using products with alcohol or oil, as these can damage the hair

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I wash my lace front wig?

It depends on how often you wear your wig and your personal preferences. Generally, it’s recommended to wash your wig every 2-4 weeks.

Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my lace front wig?

No, it’s important to use specially formulated wig shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging the hair.

Can I dye my lace front wig?

It’s not recommended to dye your wig, as this can cause damage to the hair and lace. If you want to change the color of your wig, consider purchasing a pre-colored wig or consulting a professional stylist.

General Hair Care Tips

In addition to maintaining your lace front wig, it’s important to take care of your natural hair as well. Here are some general hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water

By following these tips and steps for lace front wig maintenance, you can keep your wig looking natural and beautiful for longer. Happy styling!
