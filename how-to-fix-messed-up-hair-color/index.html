How To Fix Messed Up Hair Color A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jul 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever had a hair coloring disaster that left you with hair that looked nothing like what you had envisioned? Whether you tried to dye your hair at home or went to a salon, sometimes things can go wrong. But don't worry, we're here to help you fix your messed up hair color!

Description

There are many reasons why your hair color may not have turned out the way you wanted it to. Perhaps the color was too dark, too light, or too brassy. Maybe the dye didn't take evenly, leaving you with patchy color. Whatever the reason, there are things you can do to fix your hair color and get the look you want.

How to Fix Messed Up Hair Color

Here are some steps you can take to fix your messed up hair color:

Step 1: Assess the Damage

The first step to fixing your messed up hair color is to assess the damage. Take a good look at your hair and determine what went wrong. Is the color too dark or too light? Is it patchy? Is it brassy? Once you know what the problem is, you can start to fix it.

Step 2: Decide What You Want

Before you can fix your hair color, you need to decide what you want. Do you want to go back to your natural color? Do you want to try a different shade? Do you want to add highlights or lowlights? Knowing what you want will help you choose the right solution.

Step 3: Wash Your Hair

The first thing you should do is wash your hair. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup from styling products or other chemicals. This will help prepare your hair for the next steps.

Step 4: Add Color

If you want to change the color of your hair, you can add a new color on top of the old one. Choose a color that is a shade or two lighter or darker than what you have now. Apply the color evenly, following the instructions on the box.

Step 5: Use a Toner

If your hair is brassy, you can use a toner to neutralize the color. Choose a toner that is the opposite of the color you want to cancel out. For example, if your hair is too yellow, use a purple toner to neutralize it.

Step 6: Get a Color Correction

If your hair color is really messed up, you may need to go to a salon for a color correction. A professional colorist can help you fix your hair color and get the look you want.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you fix your messed up hair color:

  • Don't panic. Hair color disasters are fixable.
  • Be patient. It may take a few tries to get the color you want.
  • Read the instructions carefully before using any hair color products.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment to help repair your hair after coloring.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools until your hair has fully recovered.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about fixing messed up hair color:

Q: Can I fix my hair color at home?

A: Yes, you can fix your hair color at home using color correctors or by applying a new color on top of the old one. However, if your hair color is really messed up, you may need to go to a salon for a color correction.

Q: How long should I wait to color my hair again?

A: It's best to wait at least four weeks before coloring your hair again. This will give your hair time to recover and minimize damage.

Q: Can I use a toner on my own?

A: Yes, you can use a toner on your own. Just make sure to follow the instructions carefully and choose a toner that is the opposite of the color you want to cancel out.

Hair Care

After fixing your messed up hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner formulated for color-treated hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip the color and cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help repair your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

By following these tips and steps, you can fix your messed up hair color and get the look you want. Don't be afraid to experiment with different colors and styles, but always remember to take good care of your hair!
