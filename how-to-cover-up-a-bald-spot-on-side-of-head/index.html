How To Cover Up A Bald Spot On Side Of Head A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jul 07, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have a bald spot on the side of your head, you may feel self-conscious and want to find ways to cover it up. In this guide, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cover up a bald spot on the side of your head using various methods. We will also provide you with tips on how to maintain your hair and prevent further balding.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Cover Up a Bald Spot on Side of Head
  2. Step-by-Step Instructions
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Products and Treatments

How to Cover Up a Bald Spot on Side of Head

There are many ways to cover up a bald spot on the side of your head. Some of these methods include:

  • Using hair fibers or spray
  • Wearing a wig or hairpiece
  • Getting a hair transplant or scalp micropigmentation

Each method has its own benefits and drawbacks, and the best option for you will depend on your individual needs and preferences.

Using Hair Fibers or Spray

Hair fibers or spray work by attaching to the existing hair on your head, making it appear thicker and fuller. They are available in a range of colors to match your natural hair, and can be easily applied at home.

To use hair fibers or spray:

  1. Clean your hair and scalp
  2. Dry your hair with a towel
  3. Shake the fibers or spray onto the bald spot
  4. Distribute the fibers or spray evenly using a comb or brush
  5. Style your hair as desired

Hair fibers or spray can be a quick and easy way to cover up a bald spot, but they may not be suitable for those with severe or widespread hair loss.

Wearing a Wig or Hairpiece

A wig or hairpiece is a more long-term solution for covering up a bald spot. Wigs and hairpieces come in a variety of styles and colors, and can be custom-made to fit your head perfectly.

To wear a wig or hairpiece:

  1. Choose a style and color that matches your natural hair
  2. Have the wig or hairpiece professionally fitted
  3. Secure the wig or hairpiece to your head using clips or adhesive
  4. Style your hair as desired

Wigs and hairpieces can be expensive and may require regular maintenance, but they can provide a natural-looking and convenient solution for those with hair loss.

Getting a Hair Transplant or Scalp Micropigmentation

A hair transplant involves taking hair from one part of your head and transplanting it to the balding area. This is a surgical procedure that requires a recovery period and can be expensive.

Scalp micropigmentation involves tattooing small dots onto the scalp to create the appearance of hair follicles. This is a non-surgical procedure that can be completed in a few hours, but may require touch-up sessions.

Both hair transplants and scalp micropigmentation can provide a long-lasting solution for hair loss, but they may not be suitable for everyone.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are step-by-step instructions for using hair fibers or spray to cover up a bald spot on the side of your head:

  1. Clean your hair and scalp with shampoo and conditioner
  2. Dry your hair with a towel
  3. Choose a hair fiber or spray that matches your natural hair color
  4. Shake the fibers or spray onto the bald spot, holding the container 4-6 inches away from your head
  5. Use a comb or brush to distribute the fibers or spray evenly
  6. Style your hair as desired

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your hair and preventing further balding:

  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Avoid harsh chemical treatments and heat styling
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate blood flow
  • Consider using hair growth products such as minoxidil or finasteride
  • Wear a hat or sunscreen to protect your scalp from the sun

FAQs

Q: Can hair fibers or spray damage my hair?

A: Hair fibers or spray are designed to be safe and non-damaging to your hair. However, it is important to follow the manufacturer's instructions carefully and avoid using too much product.

Q: Will a wig or hairpiece look natural?

A: Yes, a wig or hairpiece can look very natural if it is properly fitted and styled.

Q: Is scalp micropigmentation painful?

A: Scalp micropigmentation is generally not painful, but some people may experience mild discomfort during the procedure.

Hair Care Products and Treatments

Here are some hair care products and treatments that may be helpful for those with hair loss:

  • Minoxidil: A topical solution that can promote hair growth
  • Finasteride: A prescription medication that can prevent further hair loss
  • Nioxin: A line of hair care products that can thicken and strengthen hair
  • HairMax Laser Comb: A device that uses low-level laser therapy to stimulate hair growth
  • Hair transplant: A surgical procedure that involves transplanting hair from one part of the scalp to another

It is important to speak with a healthcare professional before using any new hair care products or treatments, especially if you have any underlying medical conditions.

In conclusion, there are many ways to cover up a bald spot on the side of your head. Whether you choose to use hair fibers, wear a wig, or undergo a hair transplant, the most important thing is to find a solution that makes you feel confident and comfortable. By following the tips and advice in this guide, you can maintain your hair and prevent further balding.
