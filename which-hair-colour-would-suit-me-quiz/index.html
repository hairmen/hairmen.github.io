Which Hair Colour Would Suit Me Quiz Find Your Perfect Shade

Written by Daniel Jul 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you thinking of changing your hair colour but not sure which one would suit you best? Take our "Which Hair Colour Would Suit Me Quiz" to find your perfect shade. Whether you want to go bold and bright or subtle and natural, this quiz will help you determine the best colour for your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style.

Description

The "Which Hair Colour Would Suit Me Quiz" is a fun and easy way to discover your ideal hair colour. It takes into account your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour to suggest shades that will complement your features and enhance your overall look. The quiz is suitable for all hair types and textures, and can be taken by anyone looking to switch up their hair colour.

How to Take the Quiz

Taking the "Which Hair Colour Would Suit Me Quiz" is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Gather Your Materials

Before you start the quiz, make sure you have a few things handy:

  • A mirror
  • A camera or smartphone
  • A pen and paper

Step 2: Answer the Questions

Answer the questions in the quiz honestly and to the best of your ability. The questions will ask about your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour, as well as your personal style and preferences.

Step 3: Review Your Results

After you complete the quiz, you will receive a list of hair colours that would suit you best. Take note of the shades that stand out to you and consider them for your next hair colour appointment.

Tips for Choosing a Hair Colour

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair colour:

  • Consider your skin tone: Cool-toned skin looks best with cool-toned hair colours like ash blonde or cherry red, while warm-toned skin looks best with warm-toned hair colours like honey blonde or copper.
  • Think about your eye colour: Blue or green eyes look great with cool-toned hair colours, while brown eyes look great with warm-toned hair colours.
  • Take your lifestyle into account: If you have a low-maintenance lifestyle, choose a hair colour that will require minimal upkeep, like a natural-looking balayage or highlights.

FAQ

Can I Take the Quiz if I Have Dyed Hair?

Yes, you can still take the quiz if you have dyed hair. The quiz takes into account your natural hair colour, but it will also suggest shades that would complement your current hair colour.

Can I Take the Quiz if I Have Curly Hair?

Absolutely! The quiz is suitable for all hair types and textures, including curly hair.

What if I Don't Like the Shades Suggested by the Quiz?

The quiz is meant to be a guide to help you choose a hair colour that will suit you best. If you don't like the shades suggested by the quiz, feel free to explore other options and consult with a hair stylist for their professional opinion.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen your new hair colour, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, which can damage your hair and cause your colour to fade.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know which hair colour would suit you best, it's time to book that salon appointment and transform your look. Have fun and enjoy your new hair!
