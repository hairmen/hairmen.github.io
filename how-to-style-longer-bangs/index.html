How To Style Longer Bangs A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to hair styling, longer bangs are a popular choice for many people. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, longer bangs can add a touch of sophistication and style to your look. However, styling longer bangs can be a bit tricky, especially if you are new to this trend. In this post, we will show you how to style longer bangs in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language. We will cover everything from how to style longer bangs to hair care tips and frequently asked questions. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Longer Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Longer Bangs
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Longer Bangs

How to Style Longer Bangs

The key to styling longer bangs is to find the right balance between texture, length, and shape. Here are some tips to help you style your longer bangs like a pro:

  • Start by blow-drying your hair in the direction you want your bangs to fall. Use a round brush to add volume and shape.
  • If you have curly or wavy hair, use a flat iron to straighten your bangs before styling.
  • Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your fingertips and work it through your bangs. This will add texture and hold.
  • If your bangs are too long, trim them gradually with scissors. Start by cutting a small amount and then adjust the length until you get the desired look.
  • Experiment with different styles and parting options until you find the one that suits your face shape and hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you style your longer bangs:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, blow-dry it until it is completely dry.
  2. Section off your bangs and clip the rest of your hair away.
  3. Take a small section of your bangs and comb it straight down. Hold the section between your index and middle fingers.
  4. Using a round brush, roll the hair away from your face and blow-dry it. Repeat this process until your bangs are dry and styled.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your fingertips and work it through your bangs to add texture and hold.
  6. Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or waves in your bangs.
  7. Experiment with different parting options and styles until you find the one that suits your face shape and hair type.

Tips for Styling Longer Bangs

Here are some tips to help you style your longer bangs like a pro:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using a flat iron to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality round brush and flat iron for best results.
  • If you have thin or fine hair, use a volumizing mousse before blow-drying your bangs.
  • Don't overdo it with hair products. A little goes a long way.
  • Keep a pair of hair cutting scissors handy in case you need to trim your bangs between salon visits.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling longer bangs:

How often should I trim my longer bangs?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your bangs start to look too long. As a general rule, you should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking their best.

Can I style my longer bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can. However, you may need to use a flat iron to straighten your bangs before styling them. You can also use a curling wand to add some texture and shape to your bangs.

What face shape suits longer bangs?

Longer bangs can suit most face shapes, but they tend to look best on oval and heart-shaped faces. If you have a round or square face, consider a side-swept fringe instead.

Hair Care Tips for Longer Bangs

Here are some hair care tips to keep your longer bangs looking their best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep your bangs clean and oil-free.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as it can transfer oil from your hands to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your longer bangs like a pro. Whether you prefer a sleek and straight look or a messy and textured style, these tips will help you achieve the perfect look. So, go ahead and experiment with different styles and find the one that suits you best!
