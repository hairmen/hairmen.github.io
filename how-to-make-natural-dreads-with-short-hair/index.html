How To Make Natural Dreads With Short Hair

Written by Frank Oct 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having short, boring hair? Do you want to try something new and unique? Look no further than dreadlocks! Dreads are a popular hairstyle that many people love. They are easy to maintain and can be customized to fit your personality. In this article, we will show you how to make natural dreads with short hair.

Description

Dreadlocks are a hairstyle that involves twisting and matting the hair into rope-like strands. They can be made with any type of hair, but they are often associated with people who have coarse, curly hair. Dreadlocks are a great way to express your individuality and can be styled in many different ways.

How to Make Natural Dreads with Short Hair

Making natural dreads with short hair can be challenging, but it is possible. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo. This will remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup from your hair. It is important to have clean hair before you begin making your dreads.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section your hair into small, even sections. The sections should be about the size of a pencil. Use hair ties or clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Backcomb Your Hair

Take one section of hair and hold it taut. Starting at the roots, use a fine-toothed comb to backcomb your hair. This means combing your hair backwards towards your scalp. Continue backcombing until the section of hair is tightly matted and forms a dreadlock.

Step 4: Twist Your Hair

Once you have backcombed your hair, twist it in the direction you want your dreadlock to go. This will help the dreadlock stay in place and prevent it from unraveling.

Step 5: Repeat

Repeat steps 3 and 4 for each section of hair. It may take some time to make all of your dreads, but be patient and take breaks if you need to.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make natural dreads with short hair:

- Use a crochet hook to tighten your dreads and help them form more quickly. - Be gentle with your hair to prevent breakage. - Use a dreadlock wax or locking gel to help your dreads stay in place. - Sleep with a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making natural dreads with short hair:

Q: Can I make dreads with straight hair?

A: Yes, you can make dreads with any type of hair. However, it may take longer for straight hair to form into dreadlocks.

Q: How long does it take to make dreads?

A: It depends on how many dreads you are making and the length of your hair. It can take anywhere from a few hours to several days.

Q: How do I maintain my dreads?

A: To maintain your dreads, you should wash them regularly with a residue-free shampoo. You can also use a dreadlock spray to keep them smelling fresh.

Hair Care

Making natural dreads with short hair can be damaging to your hair if not done properly. It is important to take care of your hair to prevent breakage and promote healthy growth. Here are some hair care tips:

- Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair. - Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized. - Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips, you can make natural dreads with short hair and keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
