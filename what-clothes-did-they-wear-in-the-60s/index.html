What Clothes Did They Wear In The 60S

Written by Frank Jun 25, 2023 · 5 min read

The 60s was a decade of change, revolution and self-expression. The fashion industry during this time was no exception. The clothing styles of the 60s were influenced by the changing cultural, social and political landscape of the world. In this article, we will take a closer look at the fashion trends of the 60s and the clothes that defined this era.

What was the fashion trends in the 60s?

The 60s fashion trends were characterized by a shift towards liberation and individuality. The fashion industry was heavily influenced by the youth culture and the hippie movement. The clothing styles of the 60s were inspired by the music, art and social movements of the time.

The Mini Skirt

The mini skirt was one of the most iconic fashion trends of the 60s. It was a symbol of liberation and women's rights. The mini skirt was a short skirt that ended several inches above the knee. It was worn with tights or stockings and was often paired with a blouse or a sweater.

The Bell-bottoms

The bell-bottoms were another iconic fashion trend of the 60s. They were wide-legged pants that flared out from the knee downwards. Bell-bottoms were often worn with platform shoes and were a symbol of the hippie movement.

The Tie-dye

The tie-dye was a popular fashion trend during the 60s. It was a technique of dyeing fabric where the fabric was tied in knots or twisted before being dyed. This created a unique and colorful pattern on the fabric. The tie-dye was often worn by the hippies and was a symbol of the counterculture movement.

How to Dress like in the 60s?

If you are looking to dress like in the 60s, here are some tips to get you started:

Go for Bold Prints

The 60s fashion was all about bold prints and colors. Look for dresses, pants, and blouses with psychedelic prints and vibrant colors.

Accessorize with the Right Accessories

The right accessories can make or break your 60s look. Look for big sunglasses, headbands, and hoop earrings to complete your outfit.

Choose the Right Shoes

The right shoes can make a huge difference in your 60s look. Look for platform shoes, knee-high boots, and sandals to complete your look.

Step by Step Guide to Dress like in the 60s

Here is a step-by-step guide to dressing like in the 60s:

Step 1: Find the Right Clothes

The first step to dressing like in the 60s is to find the right clothes. Look for dresses, skirts, pants, and blouses with bold prints and colors.

Step 2: Choose the Right Accessories

Once you have the right clothes, it's time to choose the right accessories. Look for big sunglasses, headbands, and hoop earrings to complete your look.

Step 3: Choose the Right Shoes

The right shoes can make a huge difference in your 60s look. Look for platform shoes, knee-high boots, and sandals to complete your outfit.

Hair Care in the 60s

The hair during the 60s was a reflection of the changing times. Women started to embrace their natural hair texture and let their hair grow long. Men also started to grow their hair long and often styled it in a shaggy or messy look.

If you want to achieve the 60s hair look, here are some tips:

Tip 1: Embrace your Natural Hair Texture

The 60s was all about embracing your natural hair texture. If you have curly hair, let it be curly. If you have straight hair, let it be straight.

Tip 2: Let your Hair Grow Long

Long hair was a big part of the 60s hair look. Let your hair grow long and experiment with different hair styles.

Tip 3: Experiment with Different Hair Styles

The 60s was all about experimentation. Try out different hair styles and find the one that suits you the best.
