Why Is My Hair Curly Now Male

Written by Petter Jul 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a male who has noticed that your hair has become curly recently? This can be a confusing and frustrating experience, especially if you have always had straight hair. In this blog post, we will explore some of the reasons why your hair may have become curly and what you can do to manage it.

Description

Curly hair is caused by the shape of the hair follicle. Straight hair has round follicles, while curly hair has oval-shaped follicles. The shape of the follicle determines the shape of the hair strand. If your hair has become curly, it could be due to a change in the shape of your hair follicles.

How to

If you are wondering why your hair has become curly, there are several factors that could be at play. Here are some of the most common reasons:

Genetics

One of the most common reasons why hair becomes curly is genetics. If you have a family history of curly hair, it is likely that you have inherited the genes that cause curly hair. In this case, there is not much you can do to change the texture of your hair.

Hormones

Another reason why hair can become curly is due to changes in hormone levels. This is particularly true for young men who are going through puberty. During this time, the body produces more hormones, which can affect the shape and texture of your hair.

Heat Damage

If you regularly use heat styling tools on your hair, this could be causing damage to the hair follicles. This damage can result in changes to the texture of your hair, including making it more curly.

Step by Step

If you are unhappy with the texture of your hair, there are steps you can take to manage it. Here are some tips:

Use the Right Products

If you have curly hair, it is important to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Look for shampoos and conditioners that contain moisturizing ingredients, such as shea butter or coconut oil. These ingredients will help to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz.

Avoid Heat Styling

If you want to maintain the natural texture of your hair, it is important to avoid using heat styling tools on a regular basis. If you do need to use a heat styling tool, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage to your hair.

Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This is particularly important if you have curly hair, as split ends can cause your hair to become frizzy and difficult to manage.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for managing curly hair:

Experiment with Different Hairstyles

If you are new to having curly hair, it can be fun to experiment with different hairstyles. Try out different lengths and styles to find what works best for you.

Be Patient

Managing curly hair can take time and patience. It may take a while to find the right products and styling techniques that work for you. Be patient and don’t give up!

Consult a Professional

If you are struggling to manage your curly hair, consider consulting a professional stylist. They can provide you with advice and tips for managing your hair, as well as recommend products that are tailored to your hair type.

FAQ

Is it normal for hair to become curly?

Yes, it is normal for hair to become curly. This can be due to genetics, changes in hormone levels, or damage to the hair follicles.

Can I straighten my curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten curly hair using heat styling tools. However, it is important to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage to your hair.

What products should I use on curly hair?

You should use products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as shampoos and conditioners that contain moisturizing ingredients. Look for products that are free from sulfates and silicones, as these can be drying to curly hair.

Hair Care

Caring for curly hair requires a bit of extra effort, but it can be well worth it. With the right products and styling techniques, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and styles to find what works best for you.
