How To Thin Out Ends Of Hair With Thinning Shears

Written by Daniel Jul 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your hair looking too bulky at the ends? Thinning shears may be the solution you need. Thinning shears are a type of scissors that have teeth on one blade and a straight blade on the other. They are used to remove bulk from hair and create a softer, more natural look. In this article, we will guide you on how to thin out ends of hair with thinning shears.

How To

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of thinning shears, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water.

Step-by-Step Process

Step 1: Start by washing and drying your hair.

Step 2: Comb through your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 3: Divide your hair into sections.

Step 4: Take a small section of hair and hold it vertically.

Step 5: Use the thinning shears to cut the ends of the hair at a diagonal angle.

Step 6: Move the shears down the section of hair, cutting at different angles to create texture and remove bulk.

Step 7: Repeat this process on all sections of hair until you have thinned out the ends to your desired level.

Step 8: Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style.

Tips

Tip 1: Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.

Tip 2: Don't use thinning shears on wet hair as this can lead to uneven cutting.

Tip 3: Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as this can result in uneven and choppy layers.

FAQ

Q: Can thinning shears be used on all hair types?

A: Thinning shears work best on thick, coarse hair. If you have fine hair, it is best to avoid using thinning shears as they can create too much texture and make your hair appear wispy.

Q: How often should you thin out your ends with thinning shears?

A: It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it is recommended to thin out your ends every 6-8 weeks to maintain your desired style.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it is essential to take good care of it. Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. It is also important to trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage. By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great and make the most of your thinning shears.
