Do Short Hairstyles Make You Look Younger The Truth Behind It All

Written by Oliver Aug 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we age, we often look for ways to maintain our youthful appearance. One way to achieve this is by getting a short hairstyle. Many people believe that short hairstyles make you look younger, but is this really true? In this article, we'll explore the truth behind this popular belief and give you some tips on how to choose the perfect short hairstyle for you.

Description

Short hairstyles have been popular for decades, and they continue to be a popular choice for women of all ages. One of the main reasons why short hairstyles are so popular is because they can make you look younger. When you have short hair, your face is more visible, and this can create the illusion of a more youthful appearance. Additionally, short hairstyles tend to be more modern and trendy, which can also make you look younger.

However, not all short hairstyles are created equal. Some short hairstyles can make you look older, while others can make you look younger. The key is to choose a short hairstyle that complements your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

How to Choose the Perfect Short Hairstyle

Choosing the perfect short hairstyle can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect short hairstyle:

  • Consider your face shape: Different face shapes require different hairstyles. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to choose a short hairstyle that adds height to your crown to elongate your face. If you have a square face, you may want to choose a short hairstyle with soft layers to soften your features.
  • Think about your hair texture: Your hair texture can also affect the way your short hairstyle looks. If you have thin hair, you may want to choose a short hairstyle with layers to add volume. If you have curly hair, you may want to choose a short hairstyle that showcases your curls.
  • Consider your personal style: Your short hairstyle should reflect your personal style. If you prefer a more edgy look, you may want to choose a short hairstyle with shaved sides. If you prefer a more classic look, you may want to choose a short hairstyle with blunt bangs.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Short Hair

Styling short hair can be quick and easy. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your short hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to your damp hair. This will help to add texture and hold to your hair.
  4. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, lifting your hair at the roots for added volume.
  5. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add curls or waves to your hair.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hair

Maintaining short hair is relatively easy, but there are some tips you should keep in mind:

  • Get regular trims: Short hair tends to grow out quickly, so it's important to get regular trims to maintain your style.
  • Use a dry shampoo: Short hair can get oily quickly, so using a dry shampoo can help to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Protect your hair from heat: Using heat tools on your short hair can cause damage, so it's important to use a heat protectant spray before using any heat tools.
  • Experiment with different styles: Short hair is versatile, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the perfect look for you.

FAQs

Do short hairstyles make you look younger?

Short hairstyles can make you look younger by creating the illusion of a more youthful appearance. However, not all short hairstyles are created equal, and it's important to choose a short hairstyle that complements your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

What is the best short hairstyle for a round face?

The best short hairstyle for a round face is one that adds height to your crown to elongate your face. A short hairstyle with layers can also help to add volume to your hair, making your face appear slimmer.

Can I still style my short hair if it's thin?

Absolutely! There are many short hairstyles that are perfect for thin hair, such as a pixie cut with layers or a bob with blunt bangs. Adding texture to your hair with a styling product can also help to create the illusion of thicker hair.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care for short hair:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your style.

Now that you know the truth behind whether or not short hairstyles make you look younger, you can confidently choose the perfect short hairstyle for you. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a short hairstyle, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the perfect look for you.
