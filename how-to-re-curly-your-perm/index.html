How To Re Curl Your Perm Tips For Restoring Curls

Written by William Jun 22, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of your once-beautiful perm now looking limp and lifeless? Don't worry, you can easily restore those curls! In this article, we'll show you how to re-curl your perm and provide some tips for keeping your curls bouncy and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  • How to Re-Curl Your Perm
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Keeping Your Curls
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Permed Hair

How to Re-Curl Your Perm

To re-curl your perm, you'll need to use a curling iron or perm rods. Here are the steps:

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can make it harder to curl.
  2. Blot your hair dry with a towel. Do not rub your hair vigorously, as this can damage your curls.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent heat damage.
  4. Divide your hair into sections. The number of sections will depend on the thickness of your hair.
  5. Take a section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron or perm rod. Hold for 10-15 seconds (or longer, depending on the thickness of your hair) and release.
  6. Move on to the next section and repeat until all of your hair is curled.
  7. Once all of your hair is curled, spray it with a flexible hold hairspray to set the curls.

And that's it! You should now have beautifully restored curls.

Tips for Keeping Your Curls:

Here are some tips for keeping your curls looking their best:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can disrupt your curls.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • When you do wash your hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun to prevent tangling and frizz.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about re-curling your perm:

Can I re-curl my perm without using heat?

Yes, you can use perm rods to re-curl your hair without using heat. Simply wrap a section of hair around a perm rod and secure it in place. Leave the rods in your hair for a few hours (or overnight) and then remove them.

How often should I re-curl my perm?

You can re-curl your perm as often as you like, but it's best to wait at least 6-8 weeks between perms to avoid damaging your hair.

Can I use a curling iron on wet hair?

No, you should never use a curling iron on wet hair. This can damage your hair and create an uneven curl pattern.

Hair Care for Permed Hair

Here are some tips for caring for permed hair:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your curls.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.

By following these tips, you can keep your perm looking beautiful and healthy.

Now that you know how to re-curl your perm, you can enjoy bouncy, beautiful curls all year round. Just remember to take good care of your hair and avoid using heat styling tools too often. With a little TLC, your perm will stay looking gorgeous for months to come!
