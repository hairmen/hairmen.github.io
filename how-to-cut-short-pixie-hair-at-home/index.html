How To Cut Short Pixie Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 31, 2023 ¬∑ 5 min read

Cutting your own hair can seem daunting, but with the right tools and technique, cutting a short pixie haircut at home is easier than you might think. Not only will you save time and money, but you'll also have the satisfaction of mastering a new skill. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of cutting short pixie hair at home, from start to finish.

Description

A short pixie haircut is a daring and stylish hairstyle that's perfect for those who want a low-maintenance, edgy look. It's a popular choice for women of all ages and hair types, and it's easy to customize to suit your individual style. Whether you're looking to chop off your long locks or refresh your existing pixie cut, cutting your hair at home is a great way to achieve a fresh new look.

How to Cut Short Pixie Hair at Home

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting your short pixie hair at home is to gather all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a handheld mirror. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to help keep your hair damp while you cut it.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you begin cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will help you get a more accurate cut and prevent your hair from slipping out of your fingers. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual, and then towel dry it gently.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Next, use your comb to section your hair into four parts: the top, sides, and back. Secure each section with a hair clip, so it's out of the way while you work on the other sections.

Step 4: Cut the Back

Start with the back section of your hair. Take a small section of hair at the nape of your neck and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, and then cut it straight across with your scissors. Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way up to the crown of your head.

Step 5: Cut the Sides

Once you've cut the back, move on to the side sections. Take a small section of hair from one side and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle, following the shape of your head. Repeat on the other side.

Step 6: Cut the Top

Finally, it's time to cut the top section of your hair. This is the most important part of the cut, as it will determine the overall shape and style of your pixie haircut. Take small sections of hair and comb them straight up. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it to the desired length.

Step 7: Blend and Check

Once you've cut all the sections of your hair, take down the clips and blend the sections together. Check your hair in the mirror to make sure it's even and the length is what you want.

Tips for Cutting Short Pixie Hair at Home

  • Start with a longer length and work your way up gradually to avoid cutting off too much hair.
  • Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head as you cut.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so don't be afraid to take a break and come back to it later.
  • If you're not confident in your cutting skills, start with just trimming the ends of your hair.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut your hair if it's curly. Just keep in mind that curly hair tends to shrink when it dries, so you may want to cut it a little longer than you think you need to.

What if I mess up?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. You can always go to a professional stylist to have it fixed. Alternatively, you can try to fix it yourself by trimming the hair around the mistake to even it out.

Hair Care for Short Pixie Haircuts

Short pixie haircuts require little maintenance, but you'll still need to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you want to add texture or volume to your hair, use a styling product like a texturizing spray or pomade.

Cutting short pixie hair at home is a fun and rewarding experience that can save you time and money. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a salon-worthy haircut in the comfort of your own home. So go ahead and give it a try!
