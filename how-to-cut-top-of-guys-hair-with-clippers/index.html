How To Cut Top Of Guys Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 11, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking to give yourself or someone else a haircut, using clippers can be a great option. However, if you’re new to using clippers, it can be intimidating to cut the top of a guy’s hair. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the process step-by-step, so you can confidently give a great haircut.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Cut Top of Guys Hair with Clippers
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting Hair with Clippers
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care After Cutting

How to Cut Top of Guys Hair with Clippers

Before we get started, it’s important to have the right tools. You’ll need a set of clippers, a comb, and hair cutting scissors. You may also want to have a cape or towel to protect clothing from hair clippings.

Once you have your tools ready, there are a few things to keep in mind when cutting the top of a guy’s hair with clippers.

Choose the Right Clippers:

When it comes to choosing clippers, there are a few things to consider. First, make sure the clippers are designed for cutting hair. Clippers designed for cutting fur or nails won’t work as well. Second, consider the length of the guard you want to use. Guard length determines how much hair will be cut.

Choose the Right Guard:

The guard you choose will depend on the length of hair you want to leave. If you want to leave the hair longer, choose a larger guard. If you want to leave the hair shorter, choose a smaller guard.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Wet hair is harder to cut with clippers and can clog the blades.

Step 2: Choose Your Guard Size

Choose the guard size based on the length of hair you want to leave.

Step 3: Start at the Back of the Head

Start at the back of the head and work your way towards the front. Use the clippers in an upward motion, against the direction of hair growth, to cut the hair.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Top

Once you’ve cut the back of the head, move to the sides and top. Blend the sides and top by using the clippers in an upward motion, against the direction of hair growth, to cut the hair.

Step 5: Use Scissors to Cut Any Stray Hairs

Use scissors to cut any stray hairs that the clippers missed.

Step 6: Clean Up Any Loose Hair

Use a comb and scissors to clean up any loose hair around the ears and neck.

Tips for Cutting Hair with Clippers:

Tip 1: Start with a Larger Guard

If you’re not sure what guard size to use, start with a larger guard and work your way down. It’s easier to cut more hair off than to try to fix a mistake.

Tip 2: Use a Mirror

Use a mirror to check your work as you go. This can help you see any areas you missed or need to touch up.

Tip 3: Take Your Time

Don’t rush the process. Take your time and make sure you’re cutting the hair evenly.

FAQ:

Q: Can I Cut Wet Hair with Clippers?

A: While it’s possible to cut wet hair with clippers, it’s not recommended. Wet hair is harder to cut and can clog the blades.

Q: Do I Need to Oil My Clippers?

A: Yes, it’s important to oil your clippers regularly to keep them running smoothly.

Hair Care After Cutting:

After cutting hair with clippers, it’s important to clean up any loose hair and wash the hair. This can help prevent irritation and itching.

Cutting the top of a guy’s hair with clippers doesn’t have to be intimidating. With the right tools and techniques, you can give a great haircut.
