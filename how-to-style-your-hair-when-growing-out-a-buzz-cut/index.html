How To Style Your Hair When Growing Out A Buzz Cut

Written by Oliver Oct 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you growing out a buzz cut and struggling to style your hair in the in-between stages? Don't worry, we've got you covered with tips and tricks to help you look your best during the awkward growing out phase.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Growing Out Your Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Style Your Hair

The first step in styling your hair when growing out a buzz cut is to decide on a length. Whether you want a short, medium, or long hairstyle, it's important to work with what you have and embrace the natural texture of your hair.

For short hair, consider a messy, textured look with a matte finish. Use a lightweight styling product like a wax or pomade to add definition and hold without weighing down your hair.

For medium-length hair, try a side-parted style or a quiff. You can also experiment with different textures and finishes, such as a slicked-back look or a tousled, beachy vibe.

For longer hair, you can play around with different styles like a top knot or a man bun. You can also try braids, twists, or other updos to keep your hair out of your face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair when growing out a buzz cut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a wax or pomade. Use a small amount and work it evenly through your hair with your fingers.
  3. Use a comb or brush to create your desired style. For a messy, textured look, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create volume.
  4. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold your style in place.

Tips for Growing Out Your Hair

Here are some tips to help you grow out your hair:

  • Be patient. Growing out your hair takes time, so don't expect immediate results.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as this can damage your hair and slow down the growing process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing out a buzz cut:

How long does it take to grow out a buzz cut?

The length of time it takes to grow out a buzz cut depends on several factors, such as the length you want to achieve and the rate at which your hair grows. On average, it can take anywhere from 3 to 12 months to grow out a buzz cut.

What can I do to make my hair grow faster?

While there's no guaranteed way to make your hair grow faster, you can try the following tips to help speed up the process:

  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Get regular exercise to improve blood flow to your scalp.
  • Get enough sleep to allow your body to recharge and repair.
  • Avoid stress, as this can affect hair growth.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair while growing out a buzz cut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as this can strip away natural oils and cause damage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently style your hair as you grow out your buzz cut. Remember to be patient and embrace the natural texture of your hair, and you'll have a great hairstyle in no time!
