Is Scruffy Facial Hair Attractive The Ultimate Guide To Relaxed And Carefree Style

Written by Robby Oct 21, 2023 · 4 min read
When it comes to facial hair, there's no denying that there are many different styles to choose from. From a clean-shaven look to a full beard, each style has its own unique charm. However, in recent years, there's been a growing trend towards scruffy facial hair. But is scruffy facial hair attractive? In this ultimate guide, we'll take a closer look at this carefree and relaxed style.

Description

Scruffy facial hair is a style that's characterized by a short, unkempt beard. It's a style that's often associated with a carefree and relaxed attitude. While it's not as polished as other facial hair styles, it has its own unique charm.

How to Achieve Scruffy Facial Hair

If you're interested in achieving scruffy facial hair, the first step is to let your beard grow out for a few days. Once you have some growth, you can start shaping your beard using a trimmer. Keep the length short and uneven to achieve that scruffy look.

Step by Step Guide

  1. Let your beard grow out for a few days.
  2. Trim your beard using a trimmer.
  3. Keep the length short and uneven.
  4. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that are sticking out.
  5. Apply beard oil or balm to keep your beard looking healthy and shiny.

Tips for Maintaining Scruffy Facial Hair

While scruffy facial hair is a low-maintenance style, there are still a few things you can do to keep it looking its best. One of the most important things is to keep your beard well-groomed. This means trimming it regularly and using beard oil or balm to keep it healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Q: How long should I let my beard grow before shaping it?

A: You should let your beard grow for at least a few days before shaping it. This will give you enough growth to work with.

Q: Can I achieve a scruffy look with a long beard?

A: While scruffy facial hair is typically characterized by a short beard, you can still achieve a similar look with a longer beard. The key is to keep your beard well-groomed and slightly uneven.

Q: Do I need to use beard oil or balm?

A: While it's not necessary to use beard oil or balm, it can help keep your beard healthy and shiny.

How to Care for Scruffy Facial Hair

Caring for scruffy facial hair is relatively easy. One of the most important things is to keep your beard well-groomed. This means trimming it regularly and using beard oil or balm to keep it healthy and shiny. You should also wash your beard regularly to keep it clean and free of dirt and oil.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my beard?

A: You should wash your beard at least once a week, or more often if it starts to feel dirty or oily.

Q: Can I use regular shampoo on my beard?

A: While you can use regular shampoo on your beard, it's best to use a beard-specific shampoo. Regular shampoo can be too harsh on your beard and strip it of its natural oils.

Q: How do I apply beard oil or balm?

A: To apply beard oil or balm, simply rub a small amount into your hands and then massage it into your beard. Be sure to work the oil or balm into your beard from the roots to the tips.

Conclusion

In conclusion, scruffy facial hair can be a fun and carefree style that's perfect for those who want a relaxed look. While it may not be as polished as other facial hair styles, it has its own unique charm. By following the tips and tricks outlined in this guide, you can achieve the perfect scruffy look and keep your beard looking healthy and shiny.
