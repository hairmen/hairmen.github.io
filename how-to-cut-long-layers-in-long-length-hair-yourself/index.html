How To Cut Long Layers In Long Length Hair Yourself

Written by Petter Jun 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune at the salon every time you need a haircut? Cutting long layers in long length hair yourself is easier than you think! In this article, we'll show you how to get the perfect layered haircut at home.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long layers are a popular haircut that can add volume, depth, and movement to long length hair. This haircut involves cutting the hair at different lengths, creating a cascading effect that frames the face and adds texture. The good news is that you can achieve this look at home with just a few simple tools and techniques.

How To

To cut long layers in long length hair yourself, you will need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that your hair is clean and free of any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, divide your hair into sections. Start by creating a part down the middle of your head, then divide your hair into two equal sections. Clip one section out of the way and work with the other section first.

Step 3: Cut the First Layer

Take a small section of hair from the top of the section you are working with. Hold the hair between your fingers and gently pull it straight up. Using your scissors, cut the hair at an angle, creating a soft layer.

Step 4: Continue Cutting Layers

Continue cutting layers using the same technique. Take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle, working your way down the section. Be sure to cut the hair at different lengths, creating a cascading effect.

Step 5: Repeat on the Other Side

Once you have finished one side, unclip the other section and repeat the process.

Tips

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut and prevent damage to your hair.
  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Be patient and take your time. Cutting hair can be tricky, so be sure to work slowly and carefully.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you are unsure of what to do. A friend or family member can assist you with cutting the back of your hair.

FAQ

Can I cut long layers in curly hair?

Yes, you can cut long layers in curly hair. However, it is important to remember that curly hair can be unpredictable, so it is best to work with smaller sections of hair and be careful not to cut too much at once.

How often should I cut my hair?

It is recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it properly. Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated, and avoid using heat tools too often to prevent damage. You can also use hair masks or treatments to give your hair a boost of nourishment.

By following these steps, you can achieve a beautiful long layered haircut at home. Remember to be patient and take your time, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
