How To Put Your Hair Into A Chignon A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 11, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking for an elegant and sophisticated hairstyle, the chignon is a perfect option. It's a classic updo that's perfect for any occasion, from weddings to work events. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to put your hair into a chignon in a few easy steps.

How to Put Your Hair into a Chignon

Before you start, make sure you have all the tools you need. You'll need a hairbrush, bobby pins, and hairspray. You can also use a hair elastic if you want to secure your chignon even more.

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. You want your hair to be smooth and easy to work with.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Make sure it's smooth and secure with a hair elastic.

Step 3: Twist Your Hair

Take your ponytail and twist it until it starts to coil around itself. Keep twisting until you have a tight coil of hair.

Step 4: Create the Chignon

Take the twisted ponytail and wrap it around the base of the ponytail, creating a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins, making sure to tuck in any loose ends. You can also use hairspray to keep everything in place.

Step 5: Final Touches

Once your chignon is secure, you can add some final touches. You can use bobby pins to create a more intricate style, or you can leave it simple and elegant. You can also add some hair accessories, such as a hairpin or a flower, to add some extra flair.

Tips for Creating the Perfect Chignon

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect chignon:

  • Make sure your hair is smooth and knot-free before you start.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose ends or flyaways.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use hairspray to keep your chignon in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I create a chignon with short hair?

Yes, you can create a chignon with short hair. You may need to use more bobby pins to secure it in place, but it's still possible.

What's the best way to add volume to my chignon?

You can add volume to your chignon by backcombing your hair before you gather it into a ponytail. This will create a fuller, more voluminous look.

Hair Care for Your Chignon

To keep your chignon looking its best, make sure you take care of your hair. This means washing it regularly, using a good conditioner, and protecting it from heat damage. You can also use a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

Now that you know how to put your hair into a chignon, you can create this elegant and sophisticated hairstyle whenever you want. Whether you're attending a wedding or a work event, the chignon is a perfect option.
