Perfect Bangs For Round Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jul 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. One of the best ways to frame your face and accentuate your features is by adding bangs to your hairstyle. But not all bangs are created equal. In this article, we'll discuss the perfect bangs for round faces and how to achieve them.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve Perfect Bangs for Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Perfect Bangs

Description

When it comes to finding the perfect bangs for a round face, the key is to add length and dimension to the face. This means avoiding blunt, straight-across bangs and opting for longer, side-swept bangs or wispy, textured bangs. The right bangs can help create a more oval shape to the face, which is the most flattering for those with round faces.

How to Achieve Perfect Bangs for Round Face

The first step in achieving perfect bangs for a round face is to consider the length and texture of your hair. If you have thick, straight hair, longer side-swept bangs can help balance out the roundness of your face. If you have thinner hair, textured, wispy bangs can add volume and dimension. It's also important to consider your face shape and features, such as the size of your forehead and the placement of your cheekbones.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by sectioning off your hair into the desired size and shape for your bangs.
  2. Use a comb to create a straight line for your bangs. For side-swept bangs, angle the line slightly.
  3. Cut the bangs in small sections, starting from the center and working outwards. This will help create a natural, blended look.
  4. Check the length and shape of your bangs as you go, making adjustments as needed.
  5. Finish by blow-drying and styling your bangs to your desired look.

Tips and Tricks

When it comes to styling your perfect bangs for a round face, there are a few tips and tricks to keep in mind:

  • Use a round brush and blow-dryer to create volume and shape in your bangs.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your bangs and make them look greasy.
  • Consider adding layers to the rest of your hair to help balance out the roundness of your face.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

What type of bangs are best for a round face?

The best type of bangs for a round face are longer, side-swept bangs or textured, wispy bangs. These styles help create length and dimension in the face, which can help balance out the roundness.

Should I avoid blunt bangs if I have a round face?

Yes, blunt, straight-across bangs can make the face appear even rounder. It's best to opt for longer, side-swept bangs or textured, wispy bangs instead.

Hair Care for Perfect Bangs

Maintaining perfect bangs for a round face requires some hair care tips and tricks. Here are a few:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep your bangs looking fresh and clean.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your bangs too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking neat and tidy.

With the right hairstyle and care, perfect bangs for a round face are achievable. Take the time to experiment with different styles and lengths to find what works best for you, and don't forget to maintain your hair with proper care.
