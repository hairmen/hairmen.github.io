Does Curly Hair Look Better Long Or Short

Written by Robby Jul 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair is a beautiful and unique hair type that can be styled in various ways. One of the most common debates among curly-haired individuals is whether long or short hair looks better. In this article, we will dive into this topic and provide insights on the pros and cons of having long or short curly hair.

Description

Curly hair is characterized by its natural curl pattern, which can range from loose waves to tight coils. This hair type is prone to dryness and frizz, which means that proper hair care is essential to maintaining healthy and defined curls. One of the biggest decisions that curly-haired individuals make is whether to keep their hair long or cut it short. The length of curly hair can affect its volume, shape, and overall appearance.

How to

When deciding on whether to have long or short curly hair, there are several factors to consider. Here are some things to keep in mind:

Face Shape

The shape of your face can affect how long or short hair looks on you. For instance, individuals with round faces may want to avoid having extremely short hair, as it can accentuate the roundness of their face. On the other hand, those with oval faces can pull off both short and long hair.

Hair Type

The type of curly hair you have can also impact how it looks when cut short or long. For example, individuals with tighter curls may find that their hair appears shorter when it's dry, so they may want to consider keeping it longer. In contrast, those with looser curls may benefit from having shorter hair, as it can add volume and bounce.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in deciding on hair length. If you have an active lifestyle and prefer low-maintenance hair, you may want to consider a shorter haircut. On the other hand, if you don't mind spending time styling your hair, longer hair can provide more styling options.

Step by Step

If you're still undecided on whether to keep your curly hair long or short, here are some steps to help you make a decision:

Step 1: Research

Look up pictures of individuals with curly hair who have short and long hair. Pay attention to those who have a similar hair type and face shape as you. This can give you an idea of how each length would look on you.

Step 2: Consult with a Hairstylist

A professional hairstylist can provide valuable insights on what haircut would work best for your hair type and face shape. They can also recommend styling products and techniques to maintain your curls.

Step 3: Try a Temporary Solution

If you're not ready to commit to a shorter or longer haircut, try a temporary solution. For instance, you can try a wig or hair extensions to see how you would look with longer hair. Alternatively, you can try a shorter haircut by styling your hair in an updo or using hair pins to create a faux bob.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when deciding on hair length:

  • Consider the maintenance required for each length. Short hair may require more frequent trims, while longer hair may require more time to style.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to see what works best for you.
  • Invest in quality hair care products that are specifically designed for curly hair.

FAQ

Q: Does curly hair look better long or short?

A: It depends on your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. There is no one-size-fits-all answer, as what looks good on one person may not look good on another.

Q: Will cutting my curly hair make it less curly?

A: Cutting your hair does not affect its curl pattern. However, how you cut your hair can impact how it looks. For instance, cutting curly hair too short can make it appear frizzy or undefined.

Hair Care

Regardless of whether you decide to keep your curly hair long or short, proper hair care is crucial to maintaining healthy and defined curls. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Invest in a quality hair mask to nourish and hydrate your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt to dry your hair, as regular towels can cause frizz.

Ultimately, whether curly hair looks better long or short is subjective and depends on personal preference. However, by considering factors such as face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, you can make an informed decision on what haircut would work best for you.
