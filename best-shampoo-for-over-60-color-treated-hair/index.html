Best Shampoo For Over 60 Color Treated Hair

Written by Petter Oct 18, 2023 · 4 min read

As we age, our hair goes through many changes, including thinning, graying, and becoming more brittle. Additionally, if you color your hair, it can become even more delicate and require special care. That’s why finding the best shampoo for over 60 color treated hair is crucial to maintaining healthy and vibrant locks. In this article, we’ll provide a guide to help you find the best shampoo for your hair’s specific needs.

Description

Color treated hair requires special attention because the color can fade quickly if not properly cared for. As we age, our hair naturally loses its elasticity and can become more prone to breakage. The best shampoo for over 60 color treated hair will be gentle enough to not strip the hair of its natural oils, while still providing the necessary hydration and nourishment to keep the hair healthy.

How To Choose the Best Shampoo for Over 60 Color Treated Hair

Choosing the best shampoo for over 60 color treated hair can be a bit overwhelming, but it’s important to consider a few key factors:

1. Look for a sulfate-free formula

Sulfates are harsh detergents that can strip the hair of its natural oils and cause color to fade faster. Look for shampoos that are sulfate-free to help maintain your color longer.

2. Consider a moisturizing formula

As we age, our hair becomes more prone to dryness, so look for a shampoo that provides added moisture to keep your locks hydrated and healthy.

3. Look for natural ingredients

Natural ingredients like aloe vera, coconut oil, and lavender are great for nourishing and protecting color treated hair.

Step by Step Guide to Wash Over 60 Color Treated Hair

Washing your hair may seem like a simple task, but there are a few steps you can take to ensure that you’re properly caring for your color treated hair:

1. Wet your hair thoroughly

Before applying shampoo, make sure your hair is completely wet. This will help the shampoo spread evenly and prevent any tugging or pulling on the hair.

2. Apply shampoo

Apply a quarter-sized amount of shampoo to your hand and work it into a lather. Focus on your scalp and roots, as this is where the most oil and buildup accumulate.

3. Massage your scalp

Using your fingertips, gently massage your scalp in circular motions for about a minute. This will help stimulate circulation and promote healthy hair growth.

4. Rinse thoroughly

Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly, ensuring that there is no residue left behind.

5. Apply conditioner

Apply a quarter-sized amount of conditioner to the ends of your hair, avoiding the roots. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Tips for Caring for Over 60 Color Treated Hair

Aside from using the best shampoo for over 60 color treated hair, there are a few additional tips you can follow to keep your locks looking their best:

1. Limit heat styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can cause damage to color treated hair, so try to limit their use or use a heat protectant spray before styling.

2. Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays can cause color to fade faster, so protect your hair by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

3. Get regular trims

As we age, our hair can become more prone to split ends and breakage. Regular trims can help keep your locks healthy and prevent damage.

FAQ

What is the best shampoo for over 60 color treated hair?

There are many great shampoos for over 60 color treated hair, but some top picks include Pureology Hydrate Shampoo, Redken Color Extend Magnetics Shampoo, and Joico K-Pak Color Therapy Shampoo.

How often should I wash my over 60 color treated hair?

It’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping it of its natural oils.

Can I use regular shampoo on my over 60 color treated hair?

Regular shampoos can be too harsh on color treated hair and cause the color to fade faster. It’s best to use a shampoo specifically formulated for color treated hair.

Hair Care for Over 60 Color Treated Hair

Taking care of over 60 color treated hair requires a bit of extra attention, but it’s worth it to maintain healthy and vibrant locks. By choosing the best shampoo for your hair’s specific needs and following a few simple tips, you can help keep your hair looking its best.
