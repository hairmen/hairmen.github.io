Long Face With Short Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Frank Jun 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a long face and are considering going for the short hair look, this guide is for you. We'll cover everything from how to choose the right cut for your face shape, to styling tips and hair care advice. Let's dive in!

Description

A long face is defined as having a length that is longer than the width of your face. If you have a long face, it's important to choose a hairstyle that balances out the proportions of your face. Short hair can work well with a long face, but it's important to choose the right cut and style to flatter your features.

How to Choose the Right Cut

When choosing a short haircut for a long face, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, you want to create width and volume around the sides of your face to balance out the length. This can be achieved with layers, curls, or waves.

Another important factor to consider is the shape of your face. If you have a long face with a prominent forehead, a side-swept fringe can help to soften your features. If you have a long face with a more angular jawline, a bob or pixie cut can help to balance out the proportions of your face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow when getting a short haircut for a long face:

  1. Consult with your stylist to determine the best cut and style for your face shape.
  2. Choose a length that works well with your hair texture and lifestyle.
  3. Ask for layers or texture around the sides of your face to create volume and width.
  4. Consider adding a side-swept fringe to soften your features.
  5. Ask your stylist for styling tips and product recommendations to maintain your new look.

Tips for Styling Short Hair on a Long Face

Once you've got your new short haircut, it's important to know how to style it to flatter your features. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to create texture and volume around the sides of your face.
  • Avoid a center part, as this can accentuate the length of your face.
  • Consider adding waves or curls to create width and volume.
  • Use a side-swept fringe to soften your features.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Absolutely! Short hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including updos and braids. Just be sure to add volume and texture around the sides of your face to balance out the length.

Q: What products should I use to style my short hair?

A: This will depend on your hair type and the style you're going for. Your stylist can recommend products that will work best for your hair.

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look even longer?

A: Not if you choose the right cut and style. Adding volume and width around the sides of your face can actually help to balance out the length and create a more proportionate look.

Hair Care Tips

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your new short haircut:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider adding a hair mask to your weekly hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a short haircut with confidence, no matter your face shape. Remember to consult with your stylist to find the best cut and style for you, and have fun experimenting with different looks!
