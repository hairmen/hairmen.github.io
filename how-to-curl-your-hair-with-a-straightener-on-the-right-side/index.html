How To Curl Your Hair With A Straightener On The Right Side

Written by Eveline Sep 02, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're someone who loves to experiment with different hairstyles, then curling your hair with a straightener is a must-try. Not only is it a quick and easy way to curl your hair, but it also gives you defined and long-lasting curls. In this post, we will specifically focus on how to curl your hair with a straightener on the right side. Let's dive in!

Description

Curling your hair with a straightener on the right side is a popular technique used by many hairstylists. It is a simple process that involves using a straightener to create curls on the right side of your hair. This technique is perfect for those who want to add a little bit of curl to their hair without going for a full head of curls.

How to

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to curl your hair with a straightener on the right side:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent any damage from the straightener.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into small sections, starting from the right side of your head. Clip the other sections of your hair away so that they don't get in the way.

Step 3: Start curling

Take a small section of your hair and clamp the straightener at the roots. Twist the straightener away from your face and glide it down to the ends of your hair. Hold the straightener in place for a few seconds and release.

Step 4: Repeat

Repeat the same process with the remaining sections of your hair on the right side.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Once you've curled all the sections on the right side, finish off with a light mist of hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while curling your hair with a straightener on the right side:

  • Use a straightener with smooth plates to prevent any snagging or pulling on your hair
  • Don't rush through the process – take your time with each section of hair for the best results
  • If you want looser curls, use bigger sections of hair
  • For tighter curls, use smaller sections of hair
  • Always use a heat protectant spray to prevent any heat damage to your hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling your hair with a straightener on the right side:

Is it better to curl your hair with a straightener or a curling iron?

It depends on the type of curls you want. If you want loose and relaxed curls, then a straightener is the way to go. If you want tight and defined curls, then a curling iron is the better option.

Can you curl your hair with a straightener on wet hair?

No, it is not recommended to use a straightener on wet hair. Always make sure your hair is completely dry before using any heat styling tools.

How long will the curls last?

The curls should last for a few hours to a whole day depending on your hair type and the products you use.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after heat styling. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Drink plenty of water and eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth

And that's it! Now you know how to curl your hair with a straightener on the right side. It's a simple and easy technique that can give you beautiful and defined curls in no time.
