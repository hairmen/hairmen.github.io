How To Cut Hair Short Back And Sides With Clippers

Written by Daniel Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy haircut, cutting your hair short back and sides with clippers is a great option. Not only is it cost-effective, but it also saves you time and effort. In this article, we will guide you through the step-by-step process of cutting your hair short back and sides with clippers.

Description

The short back and sides haircut is a classic look that has been around for decades. It involves trimming the hair on the sides and back of the head short and leaving the hair on top longer. This style is popular among men and women and can be achieved with scissors or clippers. Clippers are a great tool for this style because they allow for a precise and even cut.

How to Cut Hair Short Back and Sides with Clippers

Before you begin cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a good pair of clippers, a comb, and a mirror. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting it. Wet hair can be harder to cut evenly.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clipper Guard

Clipper guards come in different sizes, so choose the right one for the length you want. If you're not sure which guard to use, start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Start at the bottom of one side of your head, and work your way up. Hold the clipper at a 45-degree angle against your head and move it upwards. Use the comb to guide the hair into the clipper blades. Repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Move to the Back

Once you've finished the sides, move to the back of your head. Start at the base of your neck and work your way up. Use the same technique as on the sides, holding the clipper at a 45-degree angle and moving it upwards.

Step 5: Blend the Top and Sides

Use scissors to blend the hair on top with the short sides. Start at the front of the head and work your way back. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length.

Step 6: Clean Up the Edges

Use the clippers to clean up the edges around your ears and the back of your neck. Use a smaller guard or no guard at all for a clean, sharp look.

Step 7: Check Your Work

Take a look in the mirror to make sure your hair is even and the length is what you wanted. Make any necessary adjustments before moving on.

Step 8: Style Your Hair

You can style your hair with gel or pomade to give it a finished look. Use your fingers or a comb to create the style you want.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short back and sides haircut with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one if you're not sure which one to use.
  • Use the comb to guide the hair into the clipper blades for a more even cut.
  • Take your time and make sure you're cutting the hair evenly.
  • Use scissors to blend the hair on top with the short sides for a more natural look.
  • Use a smaller guard or no guard at all to clean up the edges around your ears and the back of your neck.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair short back and sides with scissors?

Yes, you can cut your hair short back and sides with scissors, but clippers are a more efficient and precise tool for this style.

Can I cut my hair short back and sides myself?

Yes, you can cut your hair short back and sides yourself with clippers, but it's always better to have someone else do it for you to ensure an even cut.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.

By following these tips, you can keep your short back and sides haircut looking fresh and stylish for weeks to come.
