How To Cut Ladies Hair With Clippers Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of spending hundreds of dollars at the salon every few months to get your hair trimmed? With the right tools and a little bit of practice, you can learn how to cut your own hair at home. In this guide, we will walk you through how to cut ladies hair with clippers yourself, saving you time and money in the long run.

How to

First and foremost, you will need the right tools. Invest in a good pair of clippers with various guards, a comb, scissors, and a mirror. Once you have everything you need, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair thoroughly. This will help you to see any split ends or uneven layers that need to be trimmed.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using the comb, create sections in your hair. Start by sectioning off the top of your head and securing it with a clip. Then, divide the sides and back of your head into sections, securing them as well.

Step 3: Choose the Right Guard

Choose the guard size that corresponds to the length of hair you want. If you are unsure, start with a longer guard and work your way down.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Begin cutting your hair with the clippers, starting at the nape of your neck and working your way up. Use the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

Step 5: Trim the Sides and Top

Unclip the top section of your hair and use scissors to trim any longer pieces. Then, use the clippers to trim the sides and top of your head, following the same process as before.

Step 6: Blend and Check

Blend any harsh lines by using a shorter guard size and going over the areas that need to be blended. Once you are happy with the length and texture of your hair, check for any missed spots and trim as necessary.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while cutting your own hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down
  • Use the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut
  • Blend harsh lines by using a shorter guard size
  • Check for missed spots and trim as necessary
  • Practice makes perfect - don't be afraid to try again if you're not happy with the results

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting ladies hair with clippers:

Can I cut my hair wet or dry?

You can cut your hair either wet or dry, but it is generally easier to see any uneven spots when your hair is wet.

How often should I cut my hair?

This will depend on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it becomes damaged. On average, most people cut their hair every 6-8 weeks.

Can I use clippers on long hair?

Yes, you can use clippers on long hair. Just make sure to use a longer guard and go slowly to avoid any mistakes.

Hair Care

Now that you know how to cut ladies hair with clippers yourself, it's important to take care of your hair between cuts. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling tools, and trim any split ends as they appear.

With a little bit of practice and patience, you can save time and money by cutting your own hair at home. Follow these steps and tips, and you'll be a pro in no time!
