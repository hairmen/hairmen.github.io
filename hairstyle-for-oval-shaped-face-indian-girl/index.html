Hairstyle For Oval Shaped Face Indian Girl Tips And Tricks

If you're an Indian girl with an oval-shaped face, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off a wide range of hairstyles. However, with so many options out there, it can be challenging to figure out which hairstyle would suit you best. In this article, we'll discuss how to choose the perfect hairstyle for your oval-shaped face, step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the look, hair care tips, and frequently asked questions.

How to Choose the Perfect Hairstyle for an Oval Shaped Face

Before you start experimenting with different hairstyles, it's essential to understand your face shape. An oval-shaped face is longer than it is wide, with a slightly rounded jawline and forehead. The cheekbones are the widest part of the face. This face shape is considered the most versatile as it can pull off most hairstyles.

When choosing a hairstyle, the aim is to create balance and proportion. Avoid styles that add too much height or length to your face. Instead, opt for styles that add volume to the sides or crown of your head.

Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture plays a vital role in choosing the right hairstyle. If you have fine hair, avoid styles that require a lot of layering or volume. Thick hair can handle more layers and textures. Curly hair looks great in long layers, while straight hair looks best in blunt cuts.

Think About Your Lifestyle and Personality

Your lifestyle and personality should also influence your hairstyle choice. If you're a busy person who doesn't have much time to style your hair, opt for low-maintenance styles that are easy to maintain. If you're adventurous and love trying new things, experiment with bold hair colors and edgy cuts.

Best Haircuts for Oval Shaped Face Indian Girls

Now that you know how to choose the perfect hairstyle let's take a look at some of the best haircuts for oval-shaped face Indian girls:

Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for oval-shaped faces as they add volume and movement to the hair. Layers that start at the chin or collarbone work best for this face shape. If you have thick hair, add more layers to reduce the weight.

Blunt Bob

A blunt bob is a classic hairstyle that works well for oval-shaped faces. The length of the bob should be just above the chin or slightly longer. This cut creates a clean and sharp look, while the length adds volume to the sides of the face.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs work well for oval-shaped faces as they add width to the forehead and balance out the face. The bangs should be long enough to sweep to the side of the face, and the rest of the hair should be kept long.

Hairstyles for Oval Shaped Face Indian Girls

Now that you have a better understanding of the best haircuts for oval-shaped faces let's take a look at some hairstyles that work well for Indian girls:

High Ponytail

A high ponytail is a simple and elegant hairstyle that works well for any occasion. To create this look, gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with an elastic band. You can also add a hair accessory to dress up the look.

Half Up Half Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is perfect for oval-shaped faces. To create this look, section off the top half of your hair and secure it with an elastic band. Leave the rest of your hair down. You can also add waves or curls for a more polished look.

Braids

Braids are a great way to add texture and volume to your hair. You can try a simple braid, fishtail braid, or a braided crown. Braids also work well with any hair length.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Hairstyle

Now that you've chosen the perfect hairstyle let's take a look at how to achieve the look:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply Styling Products

Apply any styling products, such as mousse or serum, to your hair. This will help to tame frizz and add volume.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair. Use a round brush to add volume and create a smooth finish.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Use a curling iron or straightener to style your hair as desired. You can also add hair accessories to dress up the look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've achieved the perfect hairstyle, it's essential to maintain it. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair looking great:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

To keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, it's essential to get regular trims. Aim to trim your hair every six to eight weeks.

Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling tools can damage your hair. Always use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every two to three days.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best haircut for an oval-shaped face?

The best haircut for an oval-shaped face is a long-layered cut, a blunt bob, or side-swept bangs.

What is the most versatile face shape?

The oval-shaped face is considered the most versatile as it can pull off most hairstyles.

How can I add volume to my hair?

You can add volume to your hair by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow-drying your hair with a round brush, or using a volumizing mousse or spray.

Hair Care Tips for Indian Girls with Oval Shaped Faces

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

Oil Your Hair

Oil your hair regularly to keep it healthy and nourished. You can use coconut oil, olive oil, or almond oil.

Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to deep condition your hair. You can make your hair mask using natural ingredients such as honey, avocado, or yogurt.

Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eat a diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth. Foods such as spinach, nuts, and eggs are great for your hair.

In conclusion, an oval-shaped face is considered the most versatile and can pull off most hairstyles. When choosing a hairstyle, consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personality. Some of the best haircuts for oval-shaped face Indian girls include long layers, blunt bobs, and side-swept bangs. Experiment with different hairstyles and have fun! Remember to maintain your hair by getting regular trims, protecting it from heat, and avoiding over-washing.
