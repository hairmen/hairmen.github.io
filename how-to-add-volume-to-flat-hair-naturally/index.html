How To Add Volume To Flat Hair Naturally A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having flat and lifeless hair? Do you want to add volume to your locks but don't want to use harsh chemicals or expensive products? Look no further than these natural remedies to achieve bouncy and voluminous hair. In this article, we'll cover everything from how to add volume to flat hair naturally to hair care tips and frequently asked questions. Let's get started.

How to Add Volume to Flat Hair Naturally

If you're looking to add volume to your hair naturally, there are several options to choose from. Here are some of the most effective methods:

1. Use Apple Cider Vinegar

Apple cider vinegar is a natural hair volumizer that can help remove excess oil and product buildup, leaving your hair looking fuller and more voluminous. To use, mix one part apple cider vinegar with two parts water and apply to your hair after shampooing. Rinse thoroughly with cool water.

2. Try a DIY Hair Mask

A DIY hair mask can help add volume to flat hair by nourishing and strengthening your locks. Mix together one ripe avocado, one tablespoon of honey, and one tablespoon of olive oil. Apply the mixture to your hair and leave on for 30 minutes before rinsing with cool water.

3. Use a Volumizing Shampoo

Opt for a volumizing shampoo that's designed to add volume to flat hair. Look for ingredients like biotin and keratin, which can help strengthen and thicken your hair. Massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair, then rinse thoroughly.

4. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow drying your hair upside down can help create volume at the roots. After washing your hair, flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair in that position until it's about 80% dry. Then, flip your head back up and finish blow drying as usual.

Step by Step Guide to Adding Volume to Flat Hair

Adding volume to flat hair isn't as difficult as it may seem. Follow these simple steps to achieve bouncy and voluminous locks:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo. Massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair, then rinse thoroughly with cool water.

Step 2: Apply a Hair Mask

Apply a DIY hair mask to your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends. Leave the mask on for 30 minutes before rinsing with cool water.

Step 3: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair until it's about 80% dry. Then, flip your head back up and finish blow drying as usual.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired using a volumizing mousse or spray for added volume.

Tips for Adding Volume to Flat Hair

Here are some additional tips for achieving voluminous hair naturally:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can flatten your locks.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair, like oils or serums.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to add volume and texture.

FAQ

Is it bad to blow dry your hair upside down?

No, blow drying your hair upside down is not bad for your hair. In fact, it can help create volume at the roots and add body to flat hair.

Can I add volume to my hair without using products?

Yes, there are several natural remedies for adding volume to flat hair, like using apple cider vinegar or a DIY hair mask.

How often should I wash my hair to maintain volume?

It's best to wash your hair every other day to maintain volume and prevent excess oil buildup.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and voluminous locks. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top shape:

  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to help prevent damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

Adding volume to flat hair naturally is easy with these tips and tricks. Whether you opt for a DIY hair mask or a volumizing shampoo, you'll be able to achieve bouncy and voluminous locks in no time. Remember to take good care of your hair to maintain its health and vitality.
