Long Hairstyles For Oval Face Male The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 24, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you are in luck! This face shape is considered to be the most versatile, and you can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, if you want to rock long hair, you need to choose a style that complements your face shape. In this post, we will go over some of the best long hairstyles for oval face male.

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is longer than its width. This shape is considered to be the most balanced and symmetrical, and you can easily experiment with different hairstyles. Long hairstyles for oval face male can be anything from shoulder-length hair to hair that falls to your waist.

How to Choose the Right Long Hairstyle for Oval Face Male

Choosing the right long hairstyle for oval face male can be a daunting task. Here are some tips to help you make the right decision:

Consider your hair type:

The first thing you need to consider is your hair type. If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to opt for a hairstyle that will help you manage your curls. If you have straight hair, you can go for a sleeker look.

Consider your lifestyle:

Your lifestyle should also play a role in the hairstyle you choose. If you are a busy person who doesn't have much time to style your hair, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance style.

Consider your personal style:

Your personal style should also be taken into consideration. If you like to keep things simple, you may want to opt for a classic, no-fuss hairstyle. If you like to make a statement, you may want to go for something bold and edgy.

Step by Step Guide to Achieving Long Hairstyles for Oval Face Male

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving some of the best long hairstyles for oval face male:

Shoulder-Length Hair

To achieve shoulder-length hair, follow these steps:

  1. Grow your hair out to shoulder length.
  2. Part your hair down the middle.
  3. Add some texture to your hair by using a texturizing spray.
  4. Style your hair using a blow dryer and a round brush.
  5. Finish off the look with a hairspray.

Long Layered Hair

To achieve long layered hair, follow these steps:

  1. Grow your hair out to your desired length.
  2. Ask your hairstylist to add layers to your hair.
  3. Add some texture to your hair by using a texturizing spray.
  4. Style your hair using a blow dryer and a round brush.
  5. Finish off the look with a hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hairstyles for Oval Face Male

Maintaining long hairstyles for oval face male can be challenging. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hair:

Get Regular Trims:

Getting regular trims will help you maintain the health and shape of your hair.

Use Quality Hair Products:

Using quality hair products can make a big difference in maintaining your hair. Invest in a good shampoo, conditioner, and styling products.

Avoid Heat Styling:

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair as much as possible. Heat can damage your hair and make it difficult to manage.

FAQ

What are the best long hairstyles for oval face male?

Some of the best long hairstyles for oval face male include shoulder-length hair, long layered hair, and long curly hair.

How do I choose the right long hairstyle for oval face male?

To choose the right long hairstyle for oval face male, consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style.

How do I maintain long hairstyles for oval face male?

To maintain long hairstyles for oval face male, get regular trims, use quality hair products, and avoid heat styling.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential to maintaining healthy, long locks. Here are some hair care tips:

Wash Your Hair Regularly:

Washing your hair regularly will keep it clean and healthy.

Condition Your Hair:

Conditioning your hair will keep it moisturized and prevent breakage.

Avoid Over-brushing:

Over-brushing your hair can cause breakage and damage.

Now that you know some of the best long hairstyles for oval face male, it's time to experiment and find the perfect style for you. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain healthy, long locks.
