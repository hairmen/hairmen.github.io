Best Haircut For Round Shaped Face Flatter Your Features

Written by Christine Jul 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you someone with a round-shaped face? Do you often find it difficult to choose the right hairstyle that can flatter your face shape? Worry not; we have got you covered. In this article, we will discuss what is the best haircut for round-shaped faces, along with some tips and tricks to maintain your hair’s health.

Table of Content

  3. Step by Step Guide to Get the Perfect Haircut

Description

A round-shaped face has soft features, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The goal of choosing the right haircut for a round-shaped face is to elongate the face and create the illusion of more defined cheekbones and jawline. The right haircut can make you look slimmer, sharper, and more confident.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Round Shaped Face

When choosing a haircut for a round-shaped face, keep in mind that you need to create the illusion of length. Here are some tips to consider:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume on the crown and top of the head, such as layered cuts, textured bobs, and pixie cuts.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume on the sides of the face, such as blunt bobs, straight bangs, and center parts.
  • Consider hairstyles that have asymmetrical lines, such as side-swept bangs, angled bobs, and long layers.
  • Choose hairstyles that are longer than chin-length, as they can help to elongate the face.
  • Try hairstyles that add width to the forehead and temples, such as wispy bangs and side parts.

Step by Step Guide to Get the Perfect Haircut

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect haircut for a round-shaped face:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Bring inspiration pictures of hairstyles that you like to your stylist.
  3. Discuss the length, texture, and layers that will flatter your face shape.
  4. Ask your stylist to add volume on the crown and top of the head.
  5. Avoid adding volume on the sides of the face.
  6. Consider adding asymmetrical lines to your haircut.
  7. Choose a length that is longer than chin-length.
  8. Add width to the forehead and temples with wispy bangs and side parts.
  9. Ask your stylist to show you how to style your hair at home.
  10. Maintain your haircut with regular trims every 6-8 weeks.

Tips to Maintain Your Hair’s Health

Here are some tips to maintain your hair’s health:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as it can cause breakage and damage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for round-shaped faces:

Q. Can I have bangs if I have a round-shaped face?

A. Yes, you can have bangs if you have a round-shaped face. Opt for wispy bangs or side-swept bangs that add width to the forehead and temples.

Q. Should I choose a short or long haircut for a round-shaped face?

A. It is recommended to choose a haircut that is longer than chin-length, as it can help to elongate the face.

Q. Can I have a bob if I have a round-shaped face?

A. Yes, you can have a bob if you have a round-shaped face. Opt for an angled bob or a textured bob that adds volume on the crown and top of the head.

Hair Care for Round Shaped Face

Here are some hair care tips for round-shaped faces:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid over-washing.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Avoid tying your hair back tightly, as it can cause breakage and damage.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can cause dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol or sulfates, as they can cause dryness and damage.

Now that you know what is the best haircut for round-shaped faces, it’s time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and flaunt your new look. Remember to maintain your haircut and hair’s health with proper care and nourishment. Happy styling!
