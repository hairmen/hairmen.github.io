How To Set Your Hair Without Gel A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're tired of using gel to set your hair, you'll be happy to know that there are other ways to achieve the same results. In this post, we'll show you how to set your hair without gel.

How to Set Your Hair Without Gel

Here are the steps you need to follow to set your hair without gel:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any product buildup and make it easier to style your hair.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a clean towel to gently dry your hair. Don't rub your hair too hard, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help moisturize and protect your hair from damage.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb your hair. This will help detangle your hair and prepare it for styling.

Step 5: Use a Styling Cream or Mousse

Apply a styling cream or mousse to your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help give your hair volume and hold.

Step 6: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to style your hair and ensure that each section is evenly styled.

Step 7: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry each section of your hair. Use a round brush to create volume and shape.

Step 8: Set Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use hot rollers or a curling iron to set your hair. This will help create long-lasting curls or waves.

Step 9: Remove the Rollers

Once your hair has cooled down, remove the rollers or curling iron. Use a comb or your fingers to gently separate the curls.

Step 10: Finish with Hairspray

Finish by spraying your hair with a light hold hairspray. This will help keep your hair in place without making it look stiff or crunchy.

Tips for Setting Your Hair Without Gel

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when setting your hair without gel:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't over-brush your hair, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to avoid making your hair look stiff or unnatural.

FAQ

Can I set my hair without using any products?

While it's possible to set your hair without using any products, using a styling cream or mousse can help give your hair volume and hold.

Can I set my hair without using heat?

Yes, you can set your hair without using heat by using rollers or flexi rods. This method will create soft, natural-looking curls without damaging your hair.

How long will my set last?

Your set will last for several days if you take care of your hair properly. To make your set last longer, sleep with a silk or satin scarf or pillowcase to avoid frizz and tangling.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to:

  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein and vitamins.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to moisturize your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful, even when setting it without gel.
