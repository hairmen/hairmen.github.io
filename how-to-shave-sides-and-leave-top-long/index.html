How To Shave Sides And Leave Top Long A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and edgy haircut, shaving the sides and leaving the top long is a great choice. This look has become increasingly popular in recent years, and it's easy to see why. It's sleek, modern, and versatile, allowing you to style your hair in a variety of ways. In this guide, we'll show you how to shave the sides and leave the top long, step by step.

How to Shave Sides and Leave Top Long

Before we get started, it's important to note that shaving your hair is a personal choice. If you're not comfortable with the idea, you can always opt for a shorter cut instead. However, if you're ready to take the plunge, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Clippers

The first step is to choose the right clippers. You'll need a pair that can handle both short and long hair, as well as a variety of textures. Look for clippers with adjustable blades and a range of guard sizes.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before you start shaving, it's important to prep your hair. Wash it thoroughly and towel-dry it. Comb your hair so that it's smooth and tangle-free. You can also apply a styling product to help keep your hair in place while you shave.

Step 3: Start Shaving

Begin by shaving the sides of your head. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to a shorter one. Use the clippers to create a clean, even line around your head. You can also use scissors to trim any stray hairs that the clippers miss.

Once you've finished shaving the sides, move on to the back of your head. Use the same technique to create a clean, even line.

Step 4: Shave the Top

Once you've shaved the sides and back of your head, it's time to tackle the top. Use scissors to trim any split ends or uneven parts. Make sure that the top is long enough to style in your desired look.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Now that you've shaved the sides and left the top long, it's time to style your hair. You can slick it back, part it to the side, or leave it messy and tousled. The choice is yours!

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to shaving your sides and leaving your top long:

  1. Choose your clippers and a range of guard sizes.
  2. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  3. Comb your hair to remove any tangles.
  4. Apply a styling product to help keep your hair in place.
  5. Start shaving the sides of your head, using a longer guard size.
  6. Work your way down to a shorter guard size to create a clean, even line.
  7. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs that the clippers miss.
  8. Move on to the back of your head and repeat the same process.
  9. Use scissors to trim any split ends or uneven parts on the top of your head.
  10. Style your hair in your desired look.

Tips for Shaving Sides and Leaving Top Long

Here are some tips to keep in mind when shaving your sides and leaving your top long:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to a shorter one.
  • Use scissors to trim any stray hairs that the clippers miss.
  • Trim the top of your head to the desired length for styling.
  • Use a styling product to help keep your hair in place.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Can I shave my sides and leave my top long if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can shave your sides and leave your top long if you have curly hair. However, you may need to use a smaller guard size to ensure that your hair is cut evenly.

How often should I shave my sides?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows. You may need to shave your sides every few weeks to keep the look fresh.

Can I shave my sides and leave my top long if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can still shave your sides and leave your top long if you have thin hair. However, you may want to avoid using a very short guard size, as this can make your hair look even thinner.

Hair Care for Shaved Sides and Long Top

Now that you've shaved your sides and left your top long, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Apply conditioner to the ends of your hair to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to prevent tangles.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to shave your sides and leave your top long with ease. It's a bold and edgy look that's perfect for anyone looking to switch up their hairstyle. Whether you wear it slicked back or messy and tousled, you'll be sure to turn heads with this chic and modern look.
