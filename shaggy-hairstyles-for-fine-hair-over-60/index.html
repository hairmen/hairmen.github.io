Shaggy Hairstyles For Fine Hair Over 60

Written by Petter Aug 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you over 60 and looking for a new hairstyle to freshen up your look? Shaggy hairstyles for fine hair over 60 can be a great option for adding volume and texture to your hair. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to achieve a shaggy hairstyle that suits your hair type and face shape.

Description

Shaggy hairstyles for fine hair over 60 are a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. This type of hairstyle involves layering the hair and cutting it into different lengths to create a messy, textured look. The layers can be short or long, depending on the desired look. This hairstyle is perfect for those with fine hair as it adds volume and dimension to the hair.

How to Achieve a Shaggy Hairstyle

Here are the steps to achieve a shaggy hairstyle for fine hair over 60:

Step 1: Choose the Right Cut

The first step to achieving a shaggy hairstyle is to choose the right cut. This will depend on your hair type and face shape. If you have fine hair, you may want to consider a shorter cut with lots of layers. If you have a round face, you may want to opt for longer layers to elongate your face.

Step 2: Add Layers

The next step is to add layers to your hair. This will help create the messy, textured look that is characteristic of shaggy hairstyles. Your stylist will use scissors to cut your hair into different lengths to create layers. The layers can be short or long, depending on the look you want to achieve.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut and layered, it's time to style it. You can use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy look. You can also use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or curls to your hair.

Tips for Shaggy Hairstyles for Fine Hair Over 60

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a shaggy hairstyle for fine hair over 60:

  • Choose a cut that works with your hair type and face shape
  • Ask your stylist to add layers to create volume and texture
  • Use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume
  • Tousle your hair with your fingers to create a messy look
  • Use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or curls

FAQ

What is the best haircut for fine hair over 60?

A shaggy hairstyle with lots of layers is a great option for fine hair over 60. This type of haircut adds volume and texture to the hair, making it look fuller and more youthful.

Can shaggy hairstyles work for all face shapes?

Yes, shaggy hairstyles can work for all face shapes. The key is to choose a cut that flatters your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to opt for longer layers to elongate your face.

Hair Care for Shaggy Hairstyles

Here are some tips to keep your shaggy hairstyle looking great:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape of your cut
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a shaggy hairstyle that looks great and is easy to maintain. Remember to choose a cut that works with your hair type and face shape, and use the right products to keep your hair looking healthy and voluminous.
