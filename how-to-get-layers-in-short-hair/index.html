How To Get Layers In Short Hair

Written by Eveline Sep 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have short hair, you may think that getting layers is not possible. But with the right technique and tools, you can add layers to your short hair and create a beautiful, textured look. In this article, we will show you how to get layers in short hair step by step, along with some tips and FAQs.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Layers in Short Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Get Layers in Short Hair

The first step to getting layers in short hair is to decide on the length and style of the layers. Layers can range from short, choppy layers to longer, blended layers. It is important to choose a style that will complement your face shape and hair type.

Next, you will need to gather the necessary tools and products. You will need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips. You may also want to use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any product buildup. Use a towel to gently blot your hair dry, and then use a blow dryer to dry your hair completely.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section your hair into layers. Start by dividing your hair into two sections: the top section and the bottom section. Use hair clips to secure each section in place.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Starting with the bottom section, use your scissors to cut small sections of hair at a time. Hold the hair between your fingers and angle the scissors to create a diagonal cut. This will create a layered effect. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 4: Blend Your Layers

Once you have cut all of your layers, use your scissors to blend the layers together. This will create a seamless, natural-looking style. Use your comb to comb through your hair and make sure that all of the layers are blended properly.

Tips

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Consider getting a professional haircut if you are unsure about cutting your own hair.

FAQ

Can I get layers in short hair?

Yes, you can get layers in short hair. Short hair can be layered to create a textured, voluminous look.

How do I choose the right length for my layers?

The length of your layers will depend on your face shape and hair type. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best length for your layers.

Do I need to use special products to maintain my layers?

You may want to use a texturizing spray or mousse to maintain the volume and texture of your layers. It is also important to regularly trim your hair to keep your layers looking fresh.

Hair Care

To keep your layers looking their best, it is important to take good care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot tools on your hair too often. Regularly trim your hair to maintain the shape of your layers, and use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture.

With these tips and steps, you can easily get layers in short hair and achieve a beautiful, textured look. Remember to take good care of your hair and regularly trim your layers to keep them looking their best.
