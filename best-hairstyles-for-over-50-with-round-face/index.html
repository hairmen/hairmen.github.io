Best Hairstyles For Over 50 With Round Face

Written by Daniel Jul 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair tends to thin out and lose its luster. It's important to find hairstyles that not only flatter our round faces but also give us volume and texture. Here are some of the best hairstyles for women over 50 with a round face.

Description

Women over 50 with a round face often look for hairstyles that can make them look younger and more vibrant. The right hairstyle can help to accentuate the best features of your face while hiding any flaws. Whether you have long, medium, or short hair, there are many styles that can work for you.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. A round face has a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The goal is to create height and length to balance out the roundness of your face.

Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds height to the crown of your head
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head
  • Long layers can help to elongate your face
  • Side-swept bangs can create a slimmer look

Step by Step

Here are some of the best hairstyles for women over 50 with a round face:

1. Shoulder-Length Layers

Shoulder-length layers can add movement and dimension to your hair. This style works well for women with thin or fine hair because it creates the illusion of fullness. Layers can also help to elongate your face.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, cropped hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways. This style works well for women with thick hair because it can be layered to create texture and volume. A pixie cut can also help to elongate your face.

3. Bob with Side Swept Bangs

A bob with side swept bangs can create a slimmer look for women with a round face. The bangs should be long enough to cover your forehead and swept to the side to create a diagonal line. This style works well for women with fine or thin hair because it can create the illusion of fullness.

4. Long Layers with Side Bangs

Long layers with side bangs can help to elongate your face and create a slimmer look. The layers should be long enough to create movement and texture, while the bangs should be swept to the side to create a diagonal line. This style works well for women with thick hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personal style
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths

FAQ

Q: What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

A: The best hairstyle for a round face is one that creates height and length to balance out the roundness of your face. Shoulder-length layers, a pixie cut, a bob with side swept bangs, and long layers with side bangs are all great options.

Q: What should I avoid when choosing a hairstyle for a round face?

A: Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, and avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long. These styles can make your face appear wider and rounder.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can look and feel your best at any age.
