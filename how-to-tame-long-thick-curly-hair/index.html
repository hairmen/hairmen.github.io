How To Tame Long Thick Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Having long, thick, curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it’s beautiful and unique, it can also be difficult to manage and style. If you’re struggling with taming your long, thick, curly locks, don’t worry! In this article, we’ll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to manage and style your curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tame Long Thick Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Managing Long Thick Curly Hair
  • FAQs about Long Thick Curly Hair
  • Hair Care for Long Thick Curly Hair

How to Tame Long Thick Curly Hair

The first step to taming your long, thick, curly hair is to understand your hair type. Curly hair comes in different types, including wavy, curly, and coily. Understanding your hair type will help you choose the right products and styling techniques.

The next step is to find the right products for your hair type. Look for shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Avoid products that contain sulfates and silicones, as they can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause frizz.

When washing your hair, use lukewarm water and gently massage your scalp with your fingertips. Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as it can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out the excess water.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics of managing long, thick, curly hair, let’s take a look at a step-by-step guide on how to tame your curls.

Step 1: Detangle

Start by detangling your hair using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers. Work from the ends of your hair to the roots, and be gentle to avoid breakage.

Step 2: Apply Products

Apply a leave-in conditioner or a curl defining cream to your hair. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Diffuse

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Hold the diffuser close to your hair and use a low heat setting.

Step 4: Scrunch

Once your hair is dry, use your hands to scrunch your curls. This will help to enhance your curls and add volume.

Step 5: Finish

Finish with a light hold hairspray or an anti-frizz serum to keep your curls in place and prevent frizz.

Tips for Managing Long Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing long, thick, curly hair:

  • Don’t brush your hair when it’s dry, as it can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.

FAQs about Long Thick Curly Hair

Can I straighten my curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your curly hair using a flat iron or a blow dryer. However, it’s important to use heat protectant products and avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and your personal preference. Some people with curly hair can go several days without washing their hair, while others prefer to wash their hair every other day.

How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

Use a leave-in conditioner or a curl defining cream to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz. Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.

Hair Care for Long Thick Curly Hair

Finally, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for hair care for long, thick, curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily tame your long, thick, curly hair and enjoy beautiful, healthy curls.
