Medium Length Hair With Layers And Side Swept Bangs A Versatile Look For Any Occasion

Written by Luffy Jul 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that is both chic and versatile? Look no further than medium length hair with layers and side swept bangs. This style is perfect for those who want a fresh and modern look without sacrificing length.

Description

Medium length hair with layers and side swept bangs is a popular hairstyle that features layers throughout the hair to add texture and movement, with a side swept fringe that frames the face. This style is perfect for those who want to add volume and dimension to their hair, and it can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to Achieve the Look

If you are interested in trying out this hairstyle, here are the steps to achieve it:

Step 1: Consult with a Professional Stylist

Before making any changes to your hair, it is important to consult with a professional stylist who can assess your hair type and face shape to determine the best cut for you. They can also recommend the right products and tools to maintain your new look.

Step 2: Cut and Layer the Hair

Once you have consulted with a stylist, they will cut and layer your hair to create the desired look. This may involve cutting the hair to shoulder length or slightly below, and adding layers throughout the hair to create movement and texture.

Step 3: Add Side Swept Bangs

The final step is to add side swept bangs that frame the face. These can be cut to different lengths and thicknesses depending on your preference and face shape.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Once you have achieved the medium length hair with layers and side swept bangs look, there are a few tips you can follow to maintain it:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to the hair.
  • Blow dry the hair using a round brush to create volume and smoothness.
  • Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition to the layers.
  • Trim the hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Is medium length hair with layers and side swept bangs suitable for all face shapes?

This hairstyle is versatile and can be adapted to suit different face shapes. Your stylist can help determine the best cut and bang length for your face shape.

Can this hairstyle be worn straight or curly?

Yes, this hairstyle can be worn straight or curly depending on your preference. The layers will add texture and movement to both styles.

What hair types are suitable for medium length hair with layers and side swept bangs?

This hairstyle is suitable for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. Your stylist can recommend the best products and tools to maintain the look for your hair type.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the health and appearance of your medium length hair with layers and side swept bangs. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily or dry.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

With the right cut, styling, and maintenance, medium length hair with layers and side swept bangs can be a versatile and stylish look for any occasion. Consult with a professional stylist to determine if this is the right hairstyle for you.
