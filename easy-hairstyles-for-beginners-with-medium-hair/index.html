Easy Hairstyles For Beginners With Medium Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Oct 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old ponytail or messy bun? Do you want to try something new with your medium length hair but don't know where to start? Look no further! We've compiled a list of easy hairstyles for beginners with medium hair that are perfect for any occasion. From braids to updos, these hairstyles are simple, stylish, and sure to impress. So grab your hairbrush and let's get started!

Description

Medium length hair can be tricky to style, especially if you're new to the hair game. But don't worry, we've got you covered. Our easy hairstyles for beginners with medium hair are perfect for those who are short on time or don't want to spend hours in front of the mirror. These hairstyles are simple, elegant, and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's important to have a few tools on hand. You'll need a hairbrush, hair ties, bobby pins, and possibly some hairspray or styling gel. Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is oily or greasy, it can be harder to style and may not hold as well.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  3. Decide on the hairstyle you want to try and gather any necessary accessories (such as a headband or hair clip).
  4. Follow the step-by-step instructions below!

Step-by-Step

1. Double Dutch Braids

Double Dutch braids are a fun and stylish way to keep your hair out of your face. Here's how to do it:

  1. Part your hair down the middle and clip one side out of the way.
  2. Starting at the hairline, divide a small section of hair into three parts.
  3. Cross the right section under the middle section, then the left section under the middle section.
  4. Repeat steps 2-3, adding more hair to each section as you go.
  5. When you reach the nape of your neck, finish the braid with a regular three-strand braid and secure with a hair tie.
  6. Repeat on the other side.

2. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a classic hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion. Here's how to do it:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun shape.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to create a messy, relaxed look.
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold in place.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a simple and elegant way to style your medium length hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Divide your hair into two sections: top and bottom.
  2. Gather the top section of hair and secure with a hair tie or clip.
  3. Fluff up the top section to add volume.
  4. Optional: curl the bottom section of hair for added texture.

Tips

  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and accessories.
  • If you have trouble keeping your hair in place, try using bobby pins or hairspray.
  • Practice makes perfect! The more you try these hairstyles, the easier they'll become.

FAQ

What if my hair is too short for these hairstyles?

If your hair is too short for these hairstyles, try searching for "easy hairstyles for short hair" or consult a hairstylist for recommendations.

Can I wear these hairstyles to formal events?

Yes! These hairstyles can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

How can I make my hairstyles last longer?

Use hairspray or styling gel to hold your hair in place, and consider using a hair primer or heat protectant before styling.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by washing and conditioning regularly, using a heat protectant when styling with heat tools, and avoiding tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause damage.

With these easy hairstyles for beginners with medium hair, you'll be able to switch up your look in no time. Whether you're heading to work or out for a night on the town, these hairstyles are sure to turn heads. So grab your hairbrush and get styling!
