Common Hairstyles for Different Face Shapes

Here are some common hairstyles that work well with different face shapes:

  • Oval: Almost any hairstyle works well with an oval face shape, as it is considered the most versatile.
  • Round: Hairstyles with volume on top and length on the sides can help elongate a round face shape.
  • Square: Soft, layered hairstyles that frame the face can help soften the angles of a square face shape.
  • Heart: Side-swept bangs and chin-length hairstyles can help balance out a heart-shaped face.
  • Diamond: Short hairstyles that accentuate the cheekbones can help enhance a diamond-shaped face.
  • Oblong: Shoulder-length hairstyles with layers can help add width to an oblong face shape.
