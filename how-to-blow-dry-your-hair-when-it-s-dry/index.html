How To Blow Dry Your Hair When It s Dry

Written by Frank Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Blow drying your hair is a great way to achieve a sleek and polished look, but what do you do when your hair is already dry? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll show you how to blow dry your hair when it's dry and provide some tips to help you achieve the best results.

Description

If you have dry hair, you may be hesitant to use heat styling tools like a blow dryer. However, with the right technique and products, you can safely and effectively blow dry your hair when it's dry. Blow drying your hair when it's dry can help you achieve a smooth and shiny finish, and it's a great way to refresh your style between washes.

How To

Follow these steps to blow dry your hair when it's dry:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start blow drying, make sure your hair is clean and free of tangles. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to protect it from heat damage.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to blow dry each section and ensure that you don't miss any spots.

Step 3: Start Blow Drying

Begin blow drying your hair on the lowest heat setting. Hold the dryer about 6 inches away from your hair and use a round brush to smooth your hair as you dry it. Work your way through each section of your hair, using the brush to pull your hair taut and direct the airflow down the hair shaft.

Step 4: Increase the Heat

Once your hair is about 80% dry, you can increase the heat setting on your dryer to speed up the drying process. Just be careful not to use too much heat, as this can damage your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Cool Air

When your hair is completely dry, finish with a blast of cool air to help set the style and add shine.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you blow dry your hair when it's dry:

  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage
  • Divide your hair into sections to make it easier to blow dry
  • Start on the lowest heat setting and work your way up as needed
  • Use a round brush to smooth your hair and direct the airflow down the hair shaft
  • Finish with a blast of cool air to set the style and add shine

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about blow drying dry hair:

Can I blow dry my hair when it's dry?

Yes, you can blow dry your hair when it's dry. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.

What's the best way to blow dry dry hair?

The best way to blow dry dry hair is to use a low heat setting and a round brush to smooth your hair and direct the airflow down the hair shaft.

Can blow drying dry hair damage it?

Blow drying dry hair can cause damage if you use too much heat or don't use a heat protectant spray or serum. Be sure to use a low heat setting and protect your hair from heat damage.

Hair Care

Blow drying your hair when it's dry can be a great way to refresh your style and achieve a sleek and polished look. However, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Use a heat protectant spray or serum before blow drying, and be sure to use a low heat setting and a round brush to smooth your hair and direct the airflow down the hair shaft. With the right technique and products, you can safely and effectively blow dry your hair when it's dry.
