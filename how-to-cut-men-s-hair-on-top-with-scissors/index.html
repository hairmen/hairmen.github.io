How To Cut Men s Hair On Top With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Oct 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on haircuts for men? Do you want to learn how to cut men's hair on top with scissors? In this guide, we will give you step by step instructions on how to achieve a professional-looking haircut right in the comfort of your own home.

Why Cut Men's Hair on Top with Scissors?

There are several reasons why cutting men's hair on top with scissors is a great option. First of all, it's much cheaper than visiting a barber or hair salon. Secondly, it's a great way to maintain a great-looking hairstyle without having to constantly visit a professional. And finally, it's a fun and enjoyable experience that you can share with your friends and family.

How to Cut Men's Hair on Top with Scissors

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting men's hair on top with scissors, you will need to gather the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, a spray bottle filled with water, and a mirror.

Step 2: Prep the Hair

Start by washing and drying the hair. Then, use the comb to part the hair where you want to cut it. Spray the hair with water to make it easier to cut.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Hold the hair between your fingers and start cutting. Use the comb as a guide to ensure that the hair is cut evenly. Cut the hair in small sections and work your way from the back to the front.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

Once you have finished cutting the hair, use the comb to check for evenness. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure that the hair is evenly cut.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Once you have cut the hair, you can style it as desired. Use a hair gel or pomade to style the hair and give it a polished look.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair on Top with Scissors

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting men's hair on top with scissors:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up
  • Use a comb to guide your scissors and ensure evenness
  • Don't cut too much hair at once
  • Take breaks if your hands get tired

FAQs

Can I cut men's hair on top with scissors if I have never done it before?

Yes, you can! Cutting men's hair on top with scissors is easy and with practice, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut.

Do I need to use special scissors to cut men's hair on top?

No, you don't need special scissors. However, it's important to use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.

Hair Care

After cutting men's hair on top with scissors, it's important to maintain the hair with proper hair care. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and style. Avoid using too much heat when styling the hair and use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.

Conclusion

Learning how to cut men's hair on top with scissors is a great way to save money and maintain a great-looking hairstyle. Follow our step by step guide and tips to achieve a professional-looking haircut right in the comfort of your own home.
