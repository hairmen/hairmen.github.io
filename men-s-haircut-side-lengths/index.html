Men s Haircut Side Lengths A Complete Guide In 2023

Are you tired of not knowing what side length to ask for when getting a haircut? Look no further! This guide will provide you with all the information you need to know about men's haircut side lengths.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Men's haircut side lengths refer to the length of hair on the sides of the head. The side length can range from a buzz cut (0.5mm) to a longer length (8mm or more). Different side lengths can create different hair styles and can be used to achieve a variety of looks.

How to

When getting a haircut, it's important to know what side length you want. Here's how to communicate with your barber:

1. Do your research

Before going to the barber, research different hair styles and side lengths to determine what you want. Bring pictures to show your barber.

2. Communicate clearly

When speaking with your barber, use specific terms such as "short sides and back" or "tapered sides". Be clear about the length you want.

3. Ask questions

If you're not sure about a certain length or style, ask your barber for their opinion. They are the experts and can provide valuable insight.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve different side lengths:

1. Buzz Cut (0.5mm)

To achieve a buzz cut, use clippers with a 0.5mm guard and buzz the entire head. This style is low maintenance and perfect for summer.

2. Short Sides and Back (2mm)

For a classic short sides and back style, use clippers with a 2mm guard on the sides and back of the head. Leave the top longer for a sleek look.

3. Tapered Sides (4mm)

A tapered sides style is achieved by using clippers with a 4mm guard on the sides and back of the head. The hair gradually gets longer towards the top of the head.

4. Medium Length (6mm)

A medium length style is achieved by using clippers with a 6mm guard on the sides and back of the head. The top can be styled in a variety of ways.

5. Long Sides (8mm or more)

To achieve a long sides style, use clippers with a longer guard (8mm or more) on the sides and back of the head. This style is perfect for those who want a more dramatic look.

Tips

  • Invest in a good pair of clippers for at-home maintenance
  • Visit your barber regularly for maintenance and trims
  • Experiment with different side lengths to find the perfect style for you

FAQ

What side length should I choose?

The side length you choose depends on your personal style and the look you want to achieve. Consider your face shape and hair type when choosing a length.

How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips and using this guide, you'll be able to achieve the perfect men's haircut side length for your personal style. Happy grooming!
