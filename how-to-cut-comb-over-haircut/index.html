How To Cut Comb Over Haircut Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and trendy haircut that can make you stand out from the crowd? If yes, then the comb over haircut is a perfect choice for you. This classic hairstyle has been in trend for decades and is still popular among men of all ages. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to cut comb over haircut at home.

Description

The comb over haircut is a classic hairstyle that involves sweeping the hair to one side of the head. The hair on the top is usually longer than the hair on the sides and back. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in different ways, depending on your hair type and face shape. The comb over haircut is suitable for men with straight or wavy hair, and it can be customized according to your preference.

How to Cut Comb Over Haircut

Before you start cutting your hair, you should have the right tools, such as a pair of scissors, a comb, and a hair clipper. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to cut comb over haircut:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure to wash and dry it thoroughly. Wet hair is easier to cut, and it will give you a better idea of how your hair will look after cutting it. Use a towel to dry your hair, and comb it to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Determine the Length

Determine how long you want your hair to be on the top, sides, and back. Use a comb to separate the hair on the top from the hair on the sides and back. Clip the hair on the sides and back with a hair clipper, using a #2 or #3 guard. Use scissors to cut the hair on the top, leaving it longer than the hair on the sides and back.

Step 3: Create a Part

Create a part on the left or right side of your head, using a comb. The part should be about an inch long and should extend from the front to the back of your head. Use the comb to sweep the hair on the top to one side of your head, over the part.

Step 4: Trim the Hair

Use scissors to trim the hair on the top, making sure it is even and blended with the hair on the sides and back. Use a comb to guide the scissors, and cut the hair in small sections. Take your time and be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

After cutting your hair, you can style it in different ways, depending on your preference. You can use a hair gel, pomade, or wax to add texture and hold to your hair. Use a comb to style your hair, and make sure it looks neat and clean.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut comb over haircut:

  • Use a mirror to see the back of your head
  • Start cutting your hair from the sides and back, and then move to the top
  • Trim your hair in small sections, to avoid cutting too much hair at once
  • Keep your scissors sharp, to make cutting easier and more precise
  • Use a hair clipper with a guard to avoid cutting your hair too short

FAQ

Can I cut comb over haircut at home?

Yes, you can cut comb over haircut at home, if you have the right tools and follow the right steps.

How often should I cut my hair?

You should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks, to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Can I style my comb over haircut in different ways?

Yes, you can style your comb over haircut in different ways, depending on your preference. You can use a hair gel, pomade, or wax to add texture and hold to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, you should take good care of it. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo, and avoid using too much hot water
  • Condition your hair regularly, to keep it moisturized and soft
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons
  • Eat a healthy diet, rich in vitamins and minerals, to promote hair growth and strength
  • Drink plenty of water, to keep your hair hydrated and healthy

By following these tips, you can maintain healthy and strong hair, and enjoy your comb over haircut for a long time.
