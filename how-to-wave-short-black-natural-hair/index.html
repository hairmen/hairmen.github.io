How To Wave Short Black Natural Hair

Written by Robby Sep 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and want to switch things up? Why not try waving your short black natural hair? Wavy hair is a fun and trendy look that can add volume and dimension to your hair. In this tutorial, we'll go through the step-by-step process of how to wave short black natural hair.

Description

Waving short black natural hair involves using a curling iron or wand to create loose, beachy waves. This style works best on hair that is clean and dry, so make sure to wash and dry your hair before getting started. You'll also need a heat protectant spray, a curling iron or wand, and some hairspray to set the waves in place.

How to

Follow these steps to wave your short black natural hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before starting. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron or wand.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Use hair clips to keep the sections in place.

Step 3: Start Curling

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand, holding it for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process for all sections of hair, alternating the direction of the curls for a more natural look.

Step 4: Set the Waves

Once you've curled all sections of hair, use a light hairspray to set the waves in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect waves:

  • Use a smaller curling iron or wand for tighter waves and a larger one for looser waves.
  • Alternate the direction of the curls for a more natural look.
  • Don't curl your hair all the way to the ends to create a more relaxed look.
  • Use a light hairspray to set the waves without weighing them down.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about waving short black natural hair:

Can I wave my hair without a curling iron or wand?

Yes, you can use other methods such as braiding your hair overnight or using a straightener to create waves.

Can I still wave my hair if it's not naturally curly?

Yes, you can still wave your hair even if it's not naturally curly. Using a curling iron or wand can create waves on any hair type.

How long will my waves last?

Your waves should last for a few days with the right care and maintenance.

Hair Care

Wavy hair requires proper care to maintain its shape and volume. Here are some tips for caring for your waved hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Don't brush your hair when it's dry to prevent frizz.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to wave your short black natural hair like a pro. Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you!
