How To Do A Half Up Half Down Messy Bun With Short Hair

Written by William Aug 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short hair, you may think that a half up half down messy bun is out of the question. However, with a few simple steps and some hair accessories, you can achieve this hairstyle with ease. In this blog post, we will cover how to do a half up half down messy bun with short hair, as well as provide some tips and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

The first step in achieving a half up half down messy bun with short hair is to gather all of the necessary tools and supplies. You will need:

  • Bobby pins
  • Hair ties
  • Texturizing spray or dry shampoo
  • Hair accessories (optional)

Once you have all of your supplies, you can begin the process of creating your half up half down messy bun.

Step by Step

Follow these simple steps to achieve a half up half down messy bun with short hair:

  1. Start by spraying your hair with texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add some volume and texture.
  2. Take a small section of hair from each side of your head, just above your ears.
  3. Pull the two sections of hair back and secure them together at the back of your head with a hair tie.
  4. Use your fingers to gently tease the hair in the ponytail to create a messy, voluminous look.
  5. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun and secure it in place with bobby pins.
  6. If desired, add some hair accessories like a headband or hairpins to complete the look.

And there you have it â a half up half down messy bun with short hair!

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when creating your half up half down messy bun:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to help them blend in and be less noticeable.
  • If you have trouble getting your hair to stay in place, try spraying it with hairspray before securing it with bobby pins.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories to find the ones that work best with your hair type and personal style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing a half up half down messy bun with short hair:

Can I do a half up half down messy bun with very short hair?

Yes, you can! Just follow the same steps as outlined above, but use smaller sections of hair and be gentle when teasing and twisting your hair.

Do I need to use texturizing spray or dry shampoo?

No, you don't have to, but using a texturizing product can help add volume and texture to your hair, which can make it easier to work with and create a more messy, tousled look.

How long does this hairstyle take to do?

It should only take a few minutes to do a half up half down messy bun with short hair, especially once you get the hang of it.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, be sure to follow a regular hair care routine that includes washing and conditioning your hair, using a heat protectant when using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners, and avoiding over-styling and harsh chemical treatments.

With these tips and tricks, you should be able to easily achieve a half up half down messy bun with short hair. Whether you're heading to work or out for a night on the town, this hairstyle is versatile and easy to customize to your personal style. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
