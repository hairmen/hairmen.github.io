Monat Made My Hair Fall Out A Hair Care Horror Story

Written by Frank Sep 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're reading this, chances are you've heard of Monat, a hair care brand that has taken the industry by storm. With promises of luscious locks and healthy hair, it's no wonder that so many people have jumped on the Monat bandwagon. Unfortunately, for many of us, the reality of using Monat has been far from the dreamy hair transformation we were promised. In fact, for some of us, Monat made our hair fall out. Here's my story.

The Description

Before I dive into my personal experience with Monat, let's start with a brief overview of the brand. Monat is a direct sales company that specializes in hair care products. Their products are marketed as being naturally-based and free from harmful ingredients like sulfates and parabens. They claim to use only the best and most effective ingredients to promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair loss. Unfortunately, for many of us, these claims couldn't be further from the truth.

The How To

So, how did Monat make my hair fall out? It all started when I decided to give their products a try. I had heard so many great things about Monat from friends and influencers, and I was eager to see if their products would work for me. I purchased their shampoo, conditioner, and hair serum and started using them as directed.

Step 1: Using Monat

At first, everything seemed fine. My hair felt soft and looked shiny. But after a few weeks of using Monat, things started to take a turn for the worse. I noticed that my hair was becoming increasingly dry and brittle. It was also falling out in clumps every time I brushed or washed it.

Step 2: Seeking Help

Concerned, I reached out to Monat's customer service team for help. They assured me that this was just my hair "adjusting" to their products and that things would get better with time. But as the weeks went on, my hair continued to fall out at an alarming rate. I started to panic.

Step 3: Consulting a Professional

Finally, I decided to seek the advice of a professional. I went to see my hairstylist, who was shocked at the state of my hair. She asked me if I had been using any new products, and when I mentioned Monat, she immediately knew what the problem was. She told me that she had seen countless clients come in with similar issues after using Monat's products.

The Tips

If you're currently experiencing hair loss or damage as a result of using Monat, here are a few tips to help you deal with the aftermath:

  • Stop using Monat's products immediately.
  • Consult with a professional stylist or dermatologist to assess the damage and determine the best course of action.
  • Be patient. It may take time for your hair to recover, but with the right care, it will eventually grow back.

The FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about Monat and hair loss:

Q: Is Monat safe to use?

A: While Monat's products are marketed as being natural and free from harmful ingredients, many people have reported experiencing hair loss, damage, and other issues after using them. It's important to do your own research and consult with a professional before using any new hair care products.

Q: Can Monat make your hair fall out?

A: Unfortunately, yes. Many people have reported experiencing hair loss and damage after using Monat's products.

Q: What should I do if I'm experiencing hair loss after using Monat?

A: Stop using Monat's products immediately and consult with a professional stylist or dermatologist to assess the damage and determine the best course of action.

The Hair Care

If you're looking for hair care products that are safe, effective, and won't cause damage or hair loss, here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Read the ingredients list carefully. Avoid products that contain sulfates, parabens, and other harmful chemicals.
  • Do your research. Look for reviews and testimonials from other people who have used the products you're considering.
  • Consult with a professional. Your hairstylist or dermatologist can help you find products that are best suited to your individual needs.

The Conclusion

My experience with Monat was a hair care horror story, but I'm not alone. Many people have reported experiencing hair loss and damage after using their products. It's important to do your own research and consult with a professional before using any new hair care products. And if you're currently dealing with the aftermath of using Monat, know that you're not alone and that with time and the right care, your hair will recover.
