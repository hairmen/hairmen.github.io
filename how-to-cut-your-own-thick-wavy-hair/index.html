How To Cut Your Own Thick Wavy Hair At Home

Written by Petter Nov 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Cutting your own thick wavy hair can seem like a daunting task, but with the right techniques and tools, it can be done at home. Not only can it save you money, but it also gives you the freedom to experiment with different styles and lengths. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own thick wavy hair, as well as tips and FAQs to help you achieve the perfect cut.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Cut Your Own Thick Wavy Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair
  4. FAQs on Cutting Your Own Hair
  5. Hair Care after Cutting Your Own Hair

How to Cut Your Own Thick Wavy Hair

The key to cutting your own thick wavy hair is to have the right tools on hand. You will need sharp scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a handheld mirror. It is also important to have an idea of the length and style you want to achieve before you begin cutting.

When cutting your own hair, it is best to work in small sections and cut less than you think you need. You can always go back and cut more, but once you have cut too much, there is no going back.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your own thick wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Comb your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to secure each section.
  3. Start with the back section. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Repeat this process for each section of hair in the back.
  4. Move on to the side sections. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair at an angle, following the shape of your head. Repeat this process for each section of hair on both sides.
  5. Finally, move on to the top section. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair at an angle, following the shape of your head. Repeat this process for each section of hair on the top of your head.
  6. Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Work in small sections to ensure even cutting.
  • Start with a longer length and cut gradually to avoid cutting too much.
  • Ask someone for help if you are unsure about cutting the back of your head.

FAQs on Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair:

  • Can I cut my own hair if it is very thick and wavy? Yes, you can cut your own hair regardless of the thickness and texture. Just make sure you have the right tools and take it slow.
  • What if I make a mistake? Don't panic! Hair grows back, and you can always go to a professional to fix any mistakes.
  • How often should I cut my own hair? It depends on the length and style you want to maintain. As a general rule, it is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Hair Care after Cutting Your Own Hair

After cutting your own hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain a healthy and vibrant look. Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims can also help prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your cut.

Cutting your own thick wavy hair can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect cut from the comfort of your own home. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and lengths.
