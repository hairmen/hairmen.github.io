How To Control Frizzy Hair Instantly Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Nov 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Frizzy hair can be a real hassle, especially when you're trying to look your best. Fortunately, there are some easy tips and tricks you can use to control frizzy hair instantly. In this article, we'll cover everything from how to prevent frizz in the first place to how to tame it when it does occur.

Table of Contents

  • Preventing Frizz
  • Using the Right Products
  • Using the Right Hair Tools
  • Proper Hair Care
  • Instant Fixes for Frizzy Hair

Preventing Frizz

The best way to control frizzy hair is to prevent it from happening in the first place. Here are some tips to help you do that:

1. Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to frizz. Try to limit your hair washing to every other day or even less frequently if possible.

2. Use Lukewarm Water

Hot water can also strip your hair of its natural oils, so stick to lukewarm water when you're washing your hair.

3. Don't Rub Your Hair with a Towel

After you wash your hair, avoid rubbing it dry with a towel. Instead, gently pat it dry with a microfiber towel or an old cotton t-shirt.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz. Try to limit your use of these tools, or use them on a lower heat setting.

Using the Right Products

The right hair products can also help control frizz. Here are some things to look for:

1. Silicone-Based Products

Products that contain silicone can help smooth down the cuticle of your hair, which can reduce frizz. Look for products that contain dimethicone or cyclomethicone.

2. Anti-Frizz Serums

Anti-frizz serums are specifically designed to help control frizz. Apply a small amount to your hair after you wash it, focusing on the ends.

3. Leave-In Conditioners

Leave-in conditioners can help keep your hair hydrated, which can prevent frizz. Look for products that contain ingredients like coconut oil or argan oil.

Using the Right Hair Tools

The right hair tools can also make a big difference when it comes to controlling frizz. Here are some things to consider:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

After you wash your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle it. This will help prevent breakage and frizz.

2. Invest in a Good Quality Hairbrush

A good quality hairbrush can help smooth down the cuticle of your hair, which can reduce frizz. Look for a brush with boar bristles, which are gentle on your hair.

3. Use a Diffuser Attachment on Your Hair Dryer

A diffuser attachment can help distribute air evenly through your hair, which can reduce frizz. Use it on a low heat setting, and avoid touching your hair with the dryer nozzle.

Proper Hair Care

Proper hair care can also help control frizz. Here are some things to keep in mind:

1. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends, which can lead to frizz. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

2. Use a Hair Mask Once a Week

Using a hair mask once a week can help keep your hair hydrated and healthy, which can prevent frizz. Look for a mask that contains ingredients like shea butter or avocado oil.

3. Avoid Brushing Your Hair When it's Dry

Brushing your hair when it's dry can cause it to break and become frizzy. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle it.

Instant Fixes for Frizzy Hair

If you need to control frizzy hair instantly, here are some things you can try:

1. Use a Dryer Sheet

Rub a dryer sheet over your hair to help reduce static and frizz. This is a quick fix that can be done anywhere.

2. Apply a Small Amount of Hair Oil

Apply a small amount of hair oil to the ends of your hair to help smooth them down. Be careful not to use too much, as this can make your hair look greasy.

3. Use a Boar Bristle Brush

Use a boar bristle brush to smooth down your hair. This will help distribute your hair's natural oils, which can reduce frizz.

FAQ

Q: Can I use hairspray to control frizz?

A: Yes, you can use hairspray to control frizz. Look for a hairspray that is specifically designed to combat frizz, and use it sparingly.

Q: Can I use a straightening iron to control frizz?

A: Yes, you can use a straightening iron to control frizz. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using the iron, and use it on a low heat setting.

Q: How often should I wash my hair to prevent frizz?

A: Try to limit your hair washing to every other day or even less frequently if possible. This will help prevent your hair from becoming too dry, which can lead to frizz.

Conclusion

Controlling frizzy hair can be a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, it's definitely possible. Remember to focus on preventing frizz in the first place, and to use the right products, tools, and hair care techniques to keep your hair looking its best.
