Best Volumizing Mousse For Fine Curly Hair Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Jun 11, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have fine curly hair, you know that achieving volume can be a challenge. But fear not – with the right volumizing mousse, you can add fullness and body to your curls! In this article, we’ll be discussing the best volumizing mousse for fine curly hair, as well as providing tips on how to use it and care for your hair.

  • Hair Care Tips for Fine Curly Hair

The Best Volumizing Mousses for Fine Curly Hair

When it comes to choosing a volumizing mousse for fine curly hair, it’s important to look for products that are lightweight and won’t weigh down your curls. Here are some of the best volumizing mousses on the market:

1. Moroccanoil Volumizing Mousse

This mousse is infused with argan oil, which helps to nourish and protect your hair while adding volume. It’s also free from parabens and sulfates, making it a great choice for those with sensitive scalps.

2. Aveda Phomollient Styling Foam

This styling foam adds fullness and shine to fine curly hair without leaving any sticky residue. It’s made with organic honey and aloe vera, which help to hydrate and protect your hair.

3. TRESemmé Thermal Creations Volumizing Mousse

This mousse is designed to protect your hair from heat damage while adding volume and hold. It’s also affordable and readily available at most drugstores.

How to Use Volumizing Mousse on Fine Curly Hair

Using a volumizing mousse on fine curly hair is easy – simply apply it to damp hair before styling. Here are some tips to ensure you get the best results:

1. Start with a small amount

It’s always best to start with a small amount of mousse and add more as needed. Using too much can weigh down your curls and leave them looking limp.

2. Apply evenly

Make sure to apply the mousse evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and ends. This will help to create lift and volume from the roots.

3. Use a diffuser

If you have curly hair, using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer can help to enhance your curls and add volume. Simply scrunch your hair with the diffuser while drying to create natural-looking curls.

Step-by-Step Guide to Applying Volumizing Mousse

Ready to try using volumizing mousse on your fine curly hair? Here’s a step-by-step guide:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.

2. Towel-dry your hair

Gently towel-dry your hair until it’s damp, being careful not to rub it too vigorously. This can cause frizz and damage your curls.

3. Apply the mousse

Dispense a small amount of mousse into your hand and work it through your hair, focusing on the roots and ends. Make sure to apply it evenly throughout your hair.

4. Style your hair

You can now style your hair as desired, using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer if you have curly hair. Once your hair is dry, you should have added volume and fullness!

Tips for Getting the Most Out of Your Volumizing Mousse

Here are some additional tips for using volumizing mousse on fine curly hair:

1. Use it sparingly

As mentioned earlier, using too much mousse can weigh down your curls. Start with a small amount and add more if needed.

2. Don’t touch your hair too much

Touching your hair too much can cause frizz and disrupt your curls. Try to avoid touching your hair as much as possible once you’ve applied the mousse.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb

When combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid tugging or pulling on your curls. This will help to keep them intact and prevent breakage.

FAQs About Volumizing Mousse for Fine Curly Hair

1. Can volumizing mousse damage my hair?

No, volumizing mousse should not damage your hair if used correctly. However, using too much or using it too frequently can lead to build-up and weigh down your curls.

2. How often should I use volumizing mousse?

You can use volumizing mousse as often as you like, but it’s best to use it sparingly to avoid build-up. Once or twice a week should be sufficient.

3. Will volumizing mousse make my hair feel crunchy?

No, most volumizing mousses are designed to provide hold without leaving your hair feeling crunchy or sticky. Look for mousses that are lightweight and won’t leave any residue.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Curly Hair

Finally, here are some hair care tips for keeping your fine curly hair healthy and beautiful:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage. Look for sulfate-free products that are gentle on your hair.

2. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can cause damage to your hair and lead to breakage. Whenever possible, air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

3. Deep condition regularly

Deep conditioning can help to nourish and hydrate your hair, keeping it healthy and strong. Look for deep conditioning treatments that are specifically designed for curly hair.

With the right volumizing mousse and hair care routine, you can achieve beautiful, voluminous curls that are full of life and body. Try out some of the products and tips we’ve recommended in this article and see what works best for you – your hair will thank you for it!
