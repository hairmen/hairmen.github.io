How To Style Less Volume Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with hair that just won't cooperate? Do you struggle to style your hair due to its lack of volume? If so, you're not alone. Many people struggle to style less volume hair, but don't worry - there are plenty of tips and tricks you can try to give your hair a little more life. In this article, we'll cover how to style less volume hair, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How to Style Less Volume Hair
  • Step by Step

Hair Care

Before we dive into how to style less volume hair, it's important to make sure your hair is healthy. Healthy hair is more likely to hold a style, and it will look better overall. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too frequently. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can make it look flat and lifeless.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that we've covered some hair care basics, let's move on to how to style less volume hair.

How to Style Less Volume Hair

The key to styling less volume hair is to create the illusion of volume. Here are some tips:

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner

Start with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products are designed to add volume to your hair, making it easier to style. Make sure to choose a product that's suited to your hair type.

2. Blow dry your hair upside down

When blow drying your hair, flip your head upside down. This will help to lift your roots and add volume. Use a round brush to further add volume and shape to your hair.

3. Use a volumizing mousse or spray

Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair before blow drying. This will help to add even more volume and hold to your hair.

4. Tease your hair

Teasing, or backcombing, is a great way to add volume to your hair. Take small sections of hair at the crown of your head and backcomb them with a teasing comb. This will create a little bit of texture and lift.

5. Use the right hair accessories

The right hair accessories can make a big difference when it comes to styling less volume hair. Use a hair clip or headband to create volume and lift at the crown of your head.

Step by Step

Now that we've covered some tips for how to style less volume hair, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair, focusing on the roots.
  3. Flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  4. Tease your hair at the crown of your head to add texture and lift.
  5. Use a hair clip or headband to create volume and shape at the crown of your head.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are a few more tips for styling less volume hair:

  • Experiment with different haircuts and styles. Some cuts and styles can be more flattering for less volume hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Consider getting a perm or body wave to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer with a diffuser attachment. This can help to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I style less volume hair without heat?

A: Yes! Try using a volumizing dry shampoo or a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair without heat.

Q: Are there any hairstyles that work well for less volume hair?

A: Yes! Hairstyles like bobs, lobs, and pixie cuts can be very flattering for less volume hair. Talk to your stylist about which cut would work best for your hair type and face shape.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have less volume hair?

A: It depends on your hair type, but generally you should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Conclusion

Styling less volume hair can be a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can create a beautiful style that lasts all day. Remember to take care of your hair, use the right products, and experiment with different styles until you find what works best for you.
