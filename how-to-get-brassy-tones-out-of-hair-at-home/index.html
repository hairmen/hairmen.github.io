How To Get Brassy Tones Out Of Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of seeing yellow or orange tones in your hair? Don't worry, you're not alone. Brassy tones can be a nightmare for anyone who wants to maintain their hair color. Luckily, there are several ways to get rid of brassy tones at home without visiting a salon. In this blog post, we'll show you how to get brassy tones out of hair using simple and effective methods.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Brassy Tones Out of Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Get Brassy Tones Out of Hair

Before we dive into the methods, it's important to understand what causes brassy tones in hair. Brassy tones are usually caused by exposure to UV rays, chlorine, hard water, and hair products with sulfates. If you have blonde or light-colored hair, you're more likely to see brassy tones. Here are some ways to get rid of brassy tones:

Use Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo is a popular option for getting rid of brassy tones. The purple pigments in the shampoo neutralize the yellow and orange tones in your hair. You can use purple shampoo once or twice a week to maintain your hair color. Make sure to leave the shampoo in your hair for at least 5 minutes before rinsing it out.

Apply a Toner

A toner is a hair product that helps to correct hair color. Toners are often used after bleaching hair to remove brassy tones. You can also use a toner to get rid of brassy tones in your natural hair color. Look for a toner with cool undertones if you have blonde hair, and warm undertones if you have brown hair. Apply the toner to your hair and leave it on for the recommended time before rinsing it out.

Brew Some Tea

Believe it or not, tea can help to get rid of brassy tones in hair. Black tea, in particular, contains tannins that can darken hair and neutralize brassy tones. Brew a strong pot of black tea and let it cool down. After washing your hair, pour the tea over your hair and leave it on for 10-15 minutes before rinsing it out.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the different methods to get rid of brassy tones in hair, here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup of hair products.
  2. Choose a method to get rid of brassy tones (purple shampoo, toner, or tea).
  3. Apply the product to your hair and leave it on for the recommended time (check the instructions on the product).
  4. Rinse the product out of your hair with cool water.
  5. Condition your hair to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  6. Repeat the process once a week to maintain your hair color.

Tips to Prevent Brassy Tones in Hair

The best way to get rid of brassy tones is to prevent them from happening in the first place. Here are some tips to keep your hair color looking fresh:

  • Avoid exposure to UV rays by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.
  • Use hair products that are sulfate-free and formulated for color-treated hair.
  • Install a water filter in your shower to avoid hard water.
  • Use a swimming cap or rinse your hair with fresh water after swimming in a chlorinated pool.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use vinegar to get rid of brassy tones in hair?

Yes, you can use apple cider vinegar to get rid of brassy tones. Mix equal parts of apple cider vinegar and water and pour it over your hair after washing it. Leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Can I use purple shampoo on brown hair?

Yes, you can use purple shampoo on brown hair. However, make sure to choose a shampoo with a low concentration of purple pigments to avoid over-toning your hair.

How often should I use purple shampoo?

You can use purple shampoo once or twice a week to maintain your hair color. However, don't use it too often as it can dry out your hair.

Hair Care After Getting Rid of Brassy Tones

After getting rid of brassy tones, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and color. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

With these tips and methods, you can easily get rid of brassy tones in your hair at home. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and color.
