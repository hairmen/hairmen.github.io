How To Fake Blonde Hair For Halloween

Written by Oliver Aug 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you ready for Halloween but struggling to figure out how to fake blonde hair? Look no further! Whether you're dressing up as Marilyn Monroe, a surfer, or just want to change up your hair color for the night, we've got you covered with these easy steps.

How to

First, decide on the shade of blonde you want to achieve. Do you want to go for a platinum look or a more natural, golden blonde? This will determine the products you'll need to use.

Next, gather your materials. You'll need a blonde wig or hair extensions, blonde hair spray or temporary hair dye, a comb, and bobby pins.

If you're using a wig, put it on and adjust the fit. If you're using extensions, clip them in where you want them.

Once you've got the wig or extensions in place, it's time to add some color. Use the blonde hair spray or temporary hair dye to color your natural hair to match the wig or extensions. Be sure to follow the instructions on the packaging and spray or dye in a well-ventilated area.

Once your hair is colored, use a comb to blend your natural hair with the wig or extensions. This will help the look appear more natural.

Finally, use bobby pins to secure the wig or extensions in place. Be sure to hide any clips or pins so they're not visible.

Step by Step

Step 1: Decide on the Shade

Determine the shade of blonde you want to achieve.

Step 2: Gather Materials

Collect a blonde wig or extensions, blonde hair spray or temporary hair dye, a comb, and bobby pins.

Step 3: Put on Wig or Extensions

Put on the wig or clip in the extensions where you want them.

Step 4: Add Color

Use the blonde hair spray or temporary hair dye to color your natural hair to match the wig or extensions.

Step 5: Blend

Use a comb to blend your natural hair with the wig or extensions.

Step 6: Secure

Use bobby pins to secure the wig or extensions in place.

Tips

- Test the hair spray or dye on a small section of hair before applying to your entire head.

- Use a hairspray to help keep the wig or extensions in place.

- Don't forget to accessorize to complete your Halloween look!

FAQ

Can I use regular hair dye to achieve a blonde look?

While it's possible, it's not recommended. Regular hair dye can damage your hair and may not give you the desired shade of blonde.

How long will the temporary hair dye last?

The length of time will depend on the brand of hair dye you use. Be sure to follow the instructions on the packaging.

Hair Care

After Halloween, be sure to remove the wig or extensions carefully to avoid damaging your natural hair. Wash your hair to remove any remaining hair spray or dye. Use a deep conditioning treatment to restore moisture to your hair.

Now you're ready to rock your fake blonde hair for Halloween! Have fun and be safe.
