Does Long Hair Make Your Forehead Look Bigger

Written by Oliver Jul 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a high forehead, you may have heard the myth that long hair makes it look bigger. However, is there any truth to this? In this article, we'll explore the science behind this myth and give you some tips on how to style your hair to flatter your features.

Table of Contents

  • Myth Busting: Does Long Hair Make Your Forehead Look Bigger?
  • Styling Tips for Flattering Your Forehead

How to Measure Your Forehead

Before we dive into the myth, let's first talk about how to measure your forehead. Using a soft measuring tape, measure from the highest point of your eyebrows to your hairline. This measurement will give you an idea of whether you have a high forehead or not.

Myth Busting: Does Long Hair Make Your Forehead Look Bigger?

Now, let's tackle the myth. The truth is, long hair does not necessarily make your forehead look bigger. It all comes down to how you style your hair. If you have bangs or layers that frame your face, they can actually help to balance out the height of your forehead. On the other hand, if you pull all of your hair back into a tight ponytail or bun, it can draw attention to your forehead and make it appear larger.

It's also important to consider your face shape when choosing a hairstyle. If you have a round face, adding height at the crown of your head can help elongate your face and balance out your features. If you have a square face, softening your hairline with bangs or layers can help to soften your features.

Styling Tips for Flattering Your Forehead

Now that we've debunked the myth, let's talk about some styling tips to help flatter your forehead:

  • Add bangs or layers to frame your face
  • Avoid pulling your hair back into a tight ponytail or bun
  • Consider adding height at the crown of your head if you have a round face
  • Softening your hairline with bangs or layers if you have a square face
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you

Hair Care Tips for a Healthy Scalp

While styling is important, it's also essential to take care of your hair and scalp. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions
  • Eat a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth

FAQs

Does cutting your hair make your forehead look smaller?

Cutting your hair can help to balance out the height of your forehead, but it's not a guaranteed fix. It's important to consider your face shape and choose a hairstyle that flatters your features.

What hairstyles are best for high foreheads?

Hairstyles with bangs or layers that frame your face can help to balance out the height of your forehead. Adding height at the crown of your head can also help elongate your face if you have a round face shape.

Can hair loss affect the appearance of your forehead?

Hair loss can make your forehead appear larger if it causes your hairline to recede. If you're experiencing hair loss, it's important to speak with a healthcare professional to determine the underlying cause and explore treatment options.

How can I promote healthy hair growth?

Eating a balanced diet, avoiding heat styling tools, and protecting your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions can all help promote healthy hair growth. Additionally, using gentle hair care products and avoiding harsh chemicals can help keep your scalp healthy.

What should I do if I'm unhappy with the appearance of my forehead?

If you're unhappy with the appearance of your forehead, there are a variety of cosmetic procedures that can help. These include forehead reduction surgery, hairline lowering surgery, and Botox injections. It's important to speak with a qualified healthcare professional to determine the best option for you.

Now that you know the truth about long hair and forehead size, you can confidently choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Remember to take care of your hair and scalp to promote healthy hair growth and always consult a healthcare professional if you're considering a cosmetic procedure.
