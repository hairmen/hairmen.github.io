Do Bangs Make Your Face Look Skinnier

Written by Frank Jul 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Many people wonder if bangs can make their face look skinnier. The answer is yes! Bangs can help give your face a slimmer appearance by creating a focal point on your forehead and drawing attention away from the sides of your face. However, not all bangs are created equal. The style and length of your bangs can greatly affect how they make your face look. In this article, we will discuss how to choose the right type of bangs to make your face look skinnier.

How To

The first step to achieving a slimmer-looking face with bangs is to choose the right style. The best bangs for a slimmer-looking face are:

  • Side-swept bangs: These bangs are longer on one side and shorter on the other, creating an asymmetrical look that draws attention to the forehead and away from the sides of the face.
  • Blunt bangs: These bangs are cut straight across the forehead and can create a more angular look to the face.
  • Textured bangs: These bangs have layers and texture, which can add volume and dimension to the hair while still drawing attention to the forehead.

When choosing the right type of bangs, it's important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, side-swept bangs can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, blunt bangs can help soften your features. If you have a long face, textured bangs can help add width to your face.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your bangs for a slimmer-looking face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off your bangs and clip the rest of your hair back.
  3. Blow dry your bangs straight, using a round brush to add volume at the roots.
  4. If you have side-swept bangs, use a flat iron to curl the longer side away from your face.
  5. If you have blunt bangs, use a flat iron to create a slight curve at the ends.
  6. If you have textured bangs, use a texturizing spray to add definition and volume.
  7. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your bangs in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your bangs:

  • Don't cut your bangs too short. Bangs that are too short can make your face look wider.
  • Consider your hair texture. If you have curly hair, side-swept bangs may be easier to style than blunt bangs.
  • Experiment with different styles. Don't be afraid to try out different types of bangs to see what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bangs and a slimmer-looking face:

Q: Can bangs make my face look rounder?

A: Yes, if you choose the wrong type of bangs. Avoid straight, heavy bangs that fall right at the eyebrows, as these can make your face look rounder.

Q: How often do I need to trim my bangs?

A: It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful bangs. Here are some tips to keep your bangs looking their best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup and oiliness.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your bangs, as these can weigh them down.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools on your bangs.
  • Consider using dry shampoo between washes to keep your bangs looking fresh.

In conclusion, bangs can make your face look skinnier if you choose the right style and take proper care of your hair. Experiment with different types of bangs to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice.
