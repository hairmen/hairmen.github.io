How To Cut Thick Men s Hair With Clippers

Written by Oliver Nov 01, 2023 · 3 min read

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Cutting men's hair with clippers is a popular grooming routine for many. While some men have thin hair, others have thick hair that may require some extra attention. This article will guide you on how to cut thick men's hair with clippers at home.

How To

The first step in cutting thick men's hair with clippers is selecting the right clippers. You'll need clippers with a powerful motor and sharp blades that can cut through thick hair. You can find these clippers at your local beauty supply store or online.

Next, you'll need to choose the right clipper guards. The length of the guard will determine how short or long the hair will be cut. Start with a higher guard number and work your way down until you reach the desired length.

Step by Step

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair. Comb through the hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Attach the appropriate clipper guard onto the clippers.

Step 3: Begin at the sides of the head and work your way up to the top of the head. Use long, even strokes to cut the hair.

Step 4: Once you reach the top of the head, use shorter strokes to blend the hair on top with the sides.

Step 5: Move to the back of the head and repeat the same process.

Step 6: Trim around the ears and neckline using a smaller clipper guard or without a guard at all.

Tips

- Use a mirror to check the back of the head while cutting.

- Take breaks if your hand gets tired.

- Always start with a higher guard number and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.

FAQ

Q: How often should I cut thick men's hair with clippers?

A: It depends on how fast the hair grows. Generally, it's recommended to cut hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape.

Q: Can I cut thick men's hair with clippers without a guard?

A: Yes, but be cautious as it can easily result in uneven cuts or cuts that are too short.

Hair Care

After cutting thick men's hair with clippers, it's important to take care of the hair. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and healthy. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals or alcohol as they can dry out the hair. Regular trims and haircuts can also help maintain the hair's health and shape.
