Best Short Hairstyles For Square Faces Over 50

Written by Frank Jul 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As we age, our face shape can change and we may find ourselves looking for a new hairstyle that flatters our features. For those with a square face shape, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. In this article, we will explore the best short hairstyles for square faces over 50.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline and a broad forehead. The goal of a short hairstyle for this face shape is to soften the features and create balance. A short hairstyle can also give the appearance of a more youthful and energetic look.

How to

Before choosing a short hairstyle, it is important to consider your hair texture and thickness. Fine hair may benefit from a layered cut, while thick hair may require a heavier cut to remove some of the bulk. It is also important to consider your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend styling your hair each day.

Step by Step

1. Start by consulting with your stylist to determine the best short haircut for your face shape and hair texture.

2. Choose a hairstyle that is easy to maintain and style daily.

3. Consider adding layers or texture to add dimension to your hair.

4. Incorporate side-swept bangs to soften your features and draw attention away from your jawline.

5. Use styling products, such as mousse or hairspray, to add volume and hold to your hair.

6. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.

Tips

- Avoid hairstyles that are too short or severe, as they can emphasize your strong jawline.

- Consider adding texture or layers to your hair to soften your features.

- Incorporate side-swept bangs to draw attention away from your jawline.

- Use styling products to add volume and hold to your hair.

- Consult with your stylist to determine the best short haircut for your face shape and hair texture.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have a short hairstyle if I have thick hair?

A: Yes, but you may require a heavier cut to remove some of the bulk.

Q: Will a short hairstyle make me look older?

A: No, a short hairstyle can actually give the appearance of a more youthful and energetic look.

Hair Care

It is important to maintain your short hairstyle with regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep the shape and style looking fresh. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent damage.

Now that you know the best short hairstyles for square faces over 50, it's time to book an appointment with your stylist and try out a new look!
