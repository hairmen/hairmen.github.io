How To Get The Perfect Man Bun A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and convenient way to keep your hair out of your face, the man bun may be the perfect hairstyle for you. However, getting the perfect man bun can be challenging, especially if you're new to styling your hair. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to get the perfect man bun, including tips and tricks to make the process easier.

How to Get the Perfect Man Bun

Step 1: Grow Your Hair

To get the perfect man bun, you'll need to grow your hair out to the right length. Most experts recommend that your hair be at least 6 inches long to achieve a good man bun.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, it's essential to wash it thoroughly. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner, and make sure you rinse your hair well.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

After washing your hair, comb it straight back to remove any tangles or knots. Use a wide-toothed comb or a boar bristle brush, and be gentle to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 4: Tie Your Hair in a High Ponytail

Next, gather all your hair at the top of your head and tie it in a high ponytail. Make sure your hair is smooth and even, and use a hair elastic that won't damage your hair or leave a dent.

Step 5: Twist Your Hair

Once you've tied your hair in a ponytail, twist it tightly to form a rope-like shape. Make sure the twist is even and tight, and hold the end of the twist with your other hand.

Step 6: Wrap Your Twist Around the Base of the Ponytail

Now, wrap the twist around the base of the ponytail, forming a bun shape. Tuck the end of the twist under the bun, and use bobby pins to secure it in place. Make sure the bun is tight and even, and adjust it as needed.

Step 7: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish your man bun with a light mist of hairspray to hold it in place. Use a hairspray that won't make your hair stiff or sticky, and apply it evenly.

Tips for Getting the Perfect Man Bun

Here are some tips and tricks to help you get the perfect man bun:

  • Use a hair elastic that won't damage your hair or leave a dent.
  • Twist your hair tightly to form a rope-like shape for a secure bun.
  • Use bobby pins to secure your bun in place, and adjust it as needed.
  • Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your bun in place.
  • Experiment with different bun shapes and sizes to find the perfect look for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long should my hair be for a man bun?

Most experts recommend that your hair be at least 6 inches long to achieve a good man bun.

What kind of hair elastic should I use for a man bun?

Use a hair elastic that won't damage your hair or leave a dent, such as a fabric-covered elastic or a silicone band.

How do I secure my man bun in place?

Use bobby pins to secure your bun in place, and adjust it as needed.

Hair Care for the Perfect Man Bun

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a boar bristle brush to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to get the perfect man bun in no time. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find the perfect look for you.
