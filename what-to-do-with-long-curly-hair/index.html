What To Do With Long Curly Hair Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Eveline Jul 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide on what to do with long curly hair. If you’re one of the lucky people blessed with long curly hair, you may have faced problems like frizz, dryness, and tangling. In this article, we’ll provide you with tips, tricks, and hair care advice to maintain, style, and flaunt your beautiful curls.

Table of Contents

  1. Hair Care
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ

Hair Care

The first and most important step in maintaining long curly hair is taking good care of it. This means washing, conditioning, and moisturizing your hair regularly. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair. Avoid using hot water as it can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and frizzy. Instead, use lukewarm water to wash your hair.

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture to your curls. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Another important hair care tip is to avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair while it’s wet. This will prevent breakage and frizz. Also, avoid using a towel to dry your hair as it can rough up the cuticle and cause frizz. Instead, use an old t-shirt or a microfiber towel to gently blot your hair.

How To

Now that you know how to take care of your long curly hair, let’s move on to styling it. There are several ways to style long curly hair, depending on your personal preference and the occasion.

1. Braids

Braids are a great way to keep your long curly hair tamed and stylish. You can do a simple braid, a French braid, or a Dutch braid. There are also several variations of braids you can try, such as a fishtail braid, a waterfall braid, or a crown braid. Braids are perfect for a casual day out or a more formal occasion.

2. Buns

Buns are a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. You can do a messy bun, a low bun, a high bun, or a braided bun. Buns are perfect for a formal event or a lazy day at home.

3. Ponytails

Ponytails are another easy and versatile hairstyle for long curly hair. You can do a high ponytail, a low ponytail, a side ponytail, or a braided ponytail. Ponytails are perfect for a workout, a casual day out, or a formal event.

Step By Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to do a simple braid:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Divide your hair into three equal parts.
  3. Take the right section and cross it over the middle section.
  4. Take the left section and cross it over the middle section.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until you reach the end of your hair.
  6. Tie the end of the braid with a hair tie or a ribbon.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style your long curly hair:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your hair before styling to enhance your curls.
  • Add some accessories like hair clips, headbands, or scarves to add some flair to your hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my long curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you’re active and sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more often.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my long curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz, avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture to your hair, and avoid using hot water to wash your hair. You can also use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls.

Q: How can I add volume to my long curly hair?

A: To add volume to your long curly hair, flip your hair over and scrunch it with a diffuser when blow-drying. You can also use a volumizing mousse or spray to add some lift to your curls.

Now that you know what to do with long curly hair, it’s time to experiment and have fun with different hairstyles. Remember to take good care of your hair and embrace your beautiful curls.
