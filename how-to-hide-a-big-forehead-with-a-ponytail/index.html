How To Hide A Big Forehead With A Ponytail

Written by Daniel Nov 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you have a big forehead that you're self-conscious about? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people have big foreheads, and it can be a source of insecurity for some. However, there's a simple solution: a ponytail. In this article, we'll show you how to hide a big forehead with a ponytail.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Instructions

How to Hide a Big Forehead with a Ponytail

A ponytail is a simple but effective way to hide a big forehead. By pulling your hair back, you create the illusion of a smaller forehead. Here's how to do it:

What You'll Need

  • Hair tie
  • Hairbrush
  • Bobby pins (optional)

Instructions

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the crown of your head.
  3. Tie your hair with a hair tie.
  4. If you want a more polished look, take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie. Secure it with a bobby pin.

That's it! You now have a ponytail that will hide your big forehead.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here's a more detailed breakdown of the steps:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. This will make it easier to gather your hair into a ponytail.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Next, gather your hair at the crown of your head. You can do this by tilting your head forward and using your hands to gather your hair. Make sure to include all of your hair in the ponytail.

Step 3: Tie Your Hair

Once you've gathered your hair, tie it with a hair tie. You can use a regular hair tie, or a scrunchie if you want a more casual look.

Step 4: Wrap a Section of Hair Around the Hair Tie (optional)

If you want a more polished look, take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie. Secure it with a bobby pin.

Tips for Hiding a Big Forehead with a Ponytail

Here are some tips to help you perfect your ponytail:

  • If you have fine hair, use a volumizing mousse or dry shampoo to add texture and body to your hair before you tie it into a ponytail. This will help to create the illusion of a fuller ponytail.
  • If you have a lot of flyaways or baby hairs, use a small amount of hairspray to tame them.
  • If your hair is too short to tie into a ponytail, try a half-up, half-down hairstyle instead. This will still pull your hair away from your forehead, but won't require as much length.

FAQs

Can I still wear a ponytail if I have bangs?

Yes! You can still wear a ponytail if you have bangs. Simply leave your bangs out of the ponytail, or sweep them to the side. This will create a more relaxed, effortless look.

What if I don't want to tie my hair into a ponytail?

If you don't want to tie your hair into a ponytail, you can try a side-swept hairstyle instead. Simply part your hair to the side and sweep it across your forehead. This will create the illusion of a smaller forehead.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to help you keep your hair healthy:

  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools.
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and silky.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily hide a big forehead with a ponytail. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
