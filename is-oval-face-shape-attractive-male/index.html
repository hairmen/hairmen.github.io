Is Oval Face Shape Attractive Male

Written by Christine Aug 11, 2023 · 5 min read

When it comes to men's facial features, the shape of their face is often a topic of discussion. One of the most desirable face shapes among men is the oval face shape. In this blog post, we will discuss whether an oval face shape is attractive for men, how to determine if you have an oval face shape, and some tips for hair care for men with an oval face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Determine if You Have an Oval Face Shape
  • Tips for Hair Care for Men with an Oval Face Shape
  • FAQs

Description

The oval face shape is considered one of the most attractive face shapes for men. It is characterized by a balanced and symmetrical appearance, with a slightly curved jawline and high cheekbones. Men with an oval face shape tend to have a wider forehead and a narrower chin, giving their face a more elongated appearance.

Men with oval face shapes are lucky as they can pull off a variety of hairstyles, from short to long and from slick to messy. The oval face shape is also versatile when it comes to facial hair – men with oval faces can easily rock a clean-shaven look or a beard.

How to Determine if You Have an Oval Face Shape

If you are unsure whether you have an oval face shape or not, there are a few key characteristics to look for. First, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are roughly the same, you likely have an oval face shape.

You can also look at the shape of your jawline. Men with oval face shapes tend to have a slightly rounded or curved jawline, rather than a sharp or angular one. Additionally, men with oval faces tend to have a longer face shape, with a high forehead and a narrow chin.

Step-by-Step Guide

To determine if you have an oval face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  2. If all three measurements are roughly the same, you likely have an oval face shape.
  3. Look at the shape of your jawline – men with oval face shapes tend to have a slightly rounded or curved jawline.
  4. Men with oval faces tend to have a longer face shape, with a high forehead and a narrow chin.

Tips for Hair Care for Men with an Oval Face Shape

Men with oval face shapes have the luxury of being able to pull off a variety of hairstyles. However, there are a few tips to keep in mind when it comes to hair care for men with oval faces.

Firstly, consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a hairstyle. Men with thin or fine hair may want to opt for shorter styles that add volume and thickness, while men with thick or curly hair can experiment with longer styles that embrace their natural texture.

Additionally, consider the length of your hair in relation to your face shape. Men with oval faces can pull off both short and long hairstyles, but it's important to ensure that the length of your hair complements the shape of your face. For example, a short buzz cut can accentuate the elongated shape of an oval face, while a longer, layered cut can soften the face shape.

Finally, consider facial hair when choosing a hairstyle. Men with oval faces can easily rock a clean-shaven look or a beard. However, if you choose to grow a beard, be sure to keep it well-groomed and trimmed to avoid overwhelming your face.

FAQs

Q: Can men with oval face shapes pull off any hairstyle?

A: Men with oval face shapes are lucky as they can pull off a variety of hairstyles, from short to long and from slick to messy. However, it's important to consider your hair texture and thickness, as well as the length of your hair in relation to your face shape.

Q: Should men with oval faces grow a beard?

A: Men with oval faces can easily rock a clean-shaven look or a beard. However, if you choose to grow a beard, be sure to keep it well-groomed and trimmed to avoid overwhelming your face.

Q: Are there any hairstyles that men with oval faces should avoid?

A: Men with oval faces can pull off a variety of hairstyles, but it's important to avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of the head, as this can make the face appear too elongated. Additionally, avoid hairstyles that are too short, as this can make the face appear too round.

In conclusion, an oval face shape is considered one of the most attractive face shapes for men. Men with oval faces are lucky as they can pull off a variety of hairstyles and facial hair styles. When choosing a hairstyle, consider your hair texture and thickness, the length of your hair in relation to your face shape, and facial hair. By following these tips, men with oval face shapes can embrace their unique features and enhance their overall appearance.
