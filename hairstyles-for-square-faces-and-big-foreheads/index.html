Introduction

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline and a broad forehead, while a big forehead is defined as having a prominent forehead that is larger than average. If you have both of these features, you might find it challenging to find the right hairstyle that complements your face shape. However, there are plenty of hairstyles that can accentuate your features and make you look stunning.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyles for Square Faces and Big Foreheads

When choosing a hairstyle for square faces and big foreheads, it's essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the best hairstyle:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as this will balance out your face's proportions.
  • Avoid hairstyles that make your forehead appear even larger, such as slicked-back styles or high ponytails.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair, as this can soften your features and create a more flattering shape.
  • Experiment with bangs, as they can draw attention away from your forehead and add some dimension to your face.
  • Play around with different lengths and textures to find the right hairstyle that complements your unique features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Hairstyle

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect hairstyle for square faces and big foreheads:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on the roots to create volume.
  4. If you have bangs, use a flat iron to straighten them and create a sleek look.
  5. Use a curling iron or flat iron to add some waves or curls to the rest of your hair, depending on your preferred style.
  6. Finish off with a light hold hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To keep your hairstyle looking fresh and stylish, follow these tips:

  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking clean and voluminous.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add some variety to your style.

FAQs

What are some hairstyles that work for square faces and big foreheads?

Some hairstyles that work well for square faces and big foreheads include layered cuts, side-swept bangs, soft waves, and pixie cuts.

Can I still wear a high ponytail with a square face and big forehead?

While a high ponytail may not be the most flattering hairstyle for a square face and big forehead, you can still wear it by adding some volume to the top of your head and opting for a side-parted style.

Should I avoid short hairstyles if I have a square face and big forehead?

Not necessarily! Short hairstyles can be incredibly flattering for square faces and big foreheads, as they can draw attention to your facial features and create a more balanced look.

Hair Care Tips for Square Faces and Big Foreheads

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can cause breakage and damage to your strands.
  • Invest in a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and tangles while you sleep.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find a hairstyle that flatters your square face and big forehead. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
