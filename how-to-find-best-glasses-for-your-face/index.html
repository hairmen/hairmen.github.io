How To Find The Best Glasses For Your Face In 2023

Written by William Oct 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to find the best glasses for your face in 2023. Whether you wear glasses for vision correction or as a fashion statement, it's important to choose the right pair that complements your face shape and enhances your look. In this article, we'll show you how to determine your face shape, what glasses styles work best for each shape, and some tips on how to choose the perfect pair. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Determine Your Face Shape
  2. Glasses Styles for Different Face Shapes
  3. Tips on How to Choose the Perfect Glasses
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Glasses Wearers

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the best glasses for your face is to determine your face shape. Here are the different face shapes and their characteristics:

Round

A round face has soft curves and no angles. The width and length of the face are roughly the same.

Square

A square face has a strong jawline and forehead, with a width and length that are roughly the same.

Heart

A heart-shaped face has a wider forehead and narrower jawline and chin.

Oval

An oval face has balanced proportions and gently rounded edges.

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror. Step back and compare your outline to the characteristics of each face shape. Alternatively, you can take a selfie and use a face shape app to determine your face shape.

Glasses Styles for Different Face Shapes

Now that you know your face shape, let's look at the best glasses styles for each shape:

Round Face

To balance the soft curves of a round face, choose glasses with angular frames and sharp lines. Rectangular or square frames are a great choice, as they add definition to the face. Avoid round or oval frames, as they make the face appear rounder.

Square Face

To soften the strong angles of a square face, choose glasses with round or oval frames. Cat-eye frames are also a great choice, as they add curves to the face. Avoid square or rectangular frames, as they will emphasize the angles.

Heart Face

To balance the wider forehead of a heart-shaped face, choose glasses with wider frames that are bottom-heavy. Aviators or butterfly frames are a great choice, as they add width to the lower part of the face. Avoid glasses with heavy top frames, as they will emphasize the forehead.

Oval Face

Lucky you! Almost any glasses style works well on an oval face. Experiment with different shapes and sizes to find the perfect pair that suits your personality.

Tips on How to Choose the Perfect Glasses

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect glasses:

  • Consider your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, choose glasses with brown or gold frames. If you have cool undertones, choose glasses with silver or black frames.
  • Think about your lifestyle. If you're active, choose glasses that are durable and lightweight. If you work in an office, choose glasses with a more professional look.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new. Glasses are a fun accessory that can add personality to your look. Don't be afraid to experiment with different colors and styles.

FAQs

What if I have a combination of face shapes?

Many people have a combination of face shapes. In this case, choose glasses that balance the different features of your face. For example, if you have a square jawline and a round forehead, choose glasses with round frames that are wider at the bottom.

Should I choose glasses based on my hairstyle?

Yes! Your hairstyle can affect how your glasses look on your face. If you have bangs, choose glasses with a shorter frame so they don't cover your eyes. If you have long hair, choose glasses with a bolder frame so they don't get lost in your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Glasses Wearers

Wearing glasses can cause your hair to get tangled or flattened. Here are some hair care tips for glasses wearers:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a round brush to add volume to your hair at the crown.
  • Wear your glasses behind your ears so they don't press against your hair.
  • Use dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

Now that you know how to find the best glasses for your face, go out and find the perfect pair! Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles until you find the perfect fit. Happy shopping!
