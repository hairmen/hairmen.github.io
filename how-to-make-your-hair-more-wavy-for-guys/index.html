How To Make Your Hair More Wavy For Guys

Written by Aprilia Aug 14, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair and want to add some waves to your locks? Look no further! In this article, we’ll go over how to make your hair more wavy for guys. From simple tips to step-by-step instructions, we’ve got you covered. Let’s get started!

Description

To achieve wavy hair, you’ll need to put in some effort, but the results are well worth it. Wavy hair adds texture and volume to your look, making it more interesting and eye-catching. There are several ways to make your hair more wavy, from using hair products to heat styling. Keep reading to learn how!

How to

To make your hair more wavy, you’ll need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with clean hair

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or oils. This will help your hair better absorb the styling products you’ll be using.

Step 2: Apply mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your damp hair, focusing on the roots. This will give your hair more body and hold.

Step 3: Twist and scrunch

Take small sections of your hair and twist them around your fingers, scrunching them up towards the roots. This will create natural-looking waves. Repeat this process all over your head.

Step 4: Let your hair air dry

Avoid using a blow dryer as this can cause frizz and damage your hair. Instead, let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer on a low heat setting.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Once your hair is dry, spritz some hairspray all over to hold the waves in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your hair more wavy:

  • Use a sea salt spray to add texture to your hair
  • Try using a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet as this can cause breakage
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your hair more wavy:

Can I make my hair wavy without using heat?

Yes! You can achieve wavy hair without using heat by trying the tips and steps mentioned above.

What if my hair is naturally curly?

If your hair is naturally curly, you can still enhance your waves by using the tips and steps mentioned above. Just be sure to use products that won’t weigh down your curls.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by using products that are gentle on your strands. Avoid using too much heat or harsh chemicals that can damage your hair. Regular trims can also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to make your hair more wavy for guys, give it a try! With a little effort and the right products, you’ll have wavy hair in no time.
