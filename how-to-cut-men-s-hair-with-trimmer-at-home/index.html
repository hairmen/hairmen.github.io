How To Cut Men s Hair With Trimmer At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're tired of going to the barber every few weeks or want to save some money, cutting your own hair at home can be a great solution. With the right tools and techniques, it's possible to achieve a professional-looking haircut without leaving your house. In this article, we'll show you how to cut men's hair with a trimmer at home, step by step, including tips and FAQs.

  • How to Cut Men's Hair with Trimmer at Home
  • Step by Step Guide

How to Cut Men's Hair with Trimmer at Home

Before starting to cut your hair at home, you'll need to gather some tools and prepare the area. Here are the things you'll need:

  • A trimmer with various comb attachments
  • A mirror
  • A towel or cape to cover your shoulders
  • Clippers to trim the back of your neck (optional)
  • A broom or vacuum cleaner to clean up the hair afterwards

Once you have all the necessary tools, you can start by choosing the right comb attachment for the length you want. It's best to start with the longest attachment and work your way down gradually until you reach the desired length.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut men's hair with a trimmer at home:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Cover your shoulders with a towel or cape to avoid getting hair on your clothes.
  3. Choose the longest comb attachment and attach it to your trimmer.
  4. Start at the top of your head and work your way down, trimming the hair against the direction it grows.
  5. Repeat the previous step with shorter comb attachments until you reach the desired length.
  6. Use a comb to separate the hair on the sides and back of your head from the hair on top.
  7. Choose a shorter comb attachment and trim the sides and back, again going against the direction of hair growth.
  8. Blend the sides and back with the top by using a longer comb attachment and trimming the hair around the edges.
  9. Use clippers to trim the hair on the back of your neck if necessary.
  10. Use a mirror to check for any uneven spots and trim them as needed.

Tips for a Better Haircut

Here are some tips to help you achieve a better haircut:

  • Start with a longer comb attachment and work your way down gradually to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair before trimming it, to avoid cutting it too short.
  • Trim the hair in small sections, rather than trying to do it all at once.
  • Check your progress frequently by using a mirror, to avoid any mistakes.
  • Be patient and take your time, especially if you're new to cutting hair at home.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair with a trimmer at home:

Can I use a trimmer without comb attachments?

Yes, but it's not recommended, as it can be difficult to achieve an even haircut without them.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim the hair shorter or go to a barber to fix it. It's better to take your time and avoid mistakes, but they can happen.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. On average, men should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care After the Haircut

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to style your hair as desired.
  • Trim the ends regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can cut men's hair with a trimmer at home and achieve a professional-looking haircut. Remember to be patient and take your time, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. With practice, you'll become a pro at cutting your own hair and save money in the process.
