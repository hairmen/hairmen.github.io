How To Style Spiky Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Spiky hairstyles have been a popular trend for decades. This edgy and bold style is perfect for those who want to add some personality to their hair. Whether you have short or long hair, spiky hairstyles can work for anyone. In this article, we will show you how to style spiky hair in a few easy steps.

Description

Spiky hairstyles are characterized by hair that is styled upwards in a spiky manner. This style can be achieved by cutting the hair short and using hair products such as gel or wax to hold the hair in place. This hairstyle is popular among men and women and can be worn for casual or formal occasions.

How to Style Spiky Hair

Here are the steps to style spiky hair:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

The first step to styling spiky hair is to wash and dry your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. After washing your hair, towel dry it or blow dry it on a low heat setting.

Step 2: Apply Hair Product

Apply a small amount of hair product to your hair. Gel or wax is the most common hair product used to achieve a spiky hairstyle. Rub the product between your palms and apply it to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair upwards. Create spikes by pulling small sections of hair upwards and twisting them slightly. Continue styling your hair until you achieve the desired look.

Step 4: Finish with Hair Spray

To ensure that your spiky hairstyle stays in place all day, finish with a hair spray. Hold the can of hairspray 8-10 inches away from your head and spray it evenly over your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling spiky hair:

  • Use a small amount of hair product to avoid a greasy or weighed-down look.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • If you have long hair, consider cutting it short to make it easier to style into spikes.

FAQ

Can anyone wear a spiky hairstyle?

Yes, anyone can wear a spiky hairstyle. However, it is important to consider your hair type and face shape when deciding if a spiky hairstyle is right for you.

How long does it take to style spiky hair?

The time it takes to style spiky hair depends on the length and thickness of your hair. On average, it takes about 10-15 minutes to style spiky hair.

How often should I wash my hair when I have a spiky hairstyle?

You should wash your hair as often as you normally would when you have a spiky hairstyle. However, be sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important when styling spiky hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons or flat irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and avoid split ends.

With these tips and steps, you can easily achieve a spiky hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd. So go ahead and try it out!
