Asian Haircut Short Sides Long Top A Stylish And Versatile Look

Written by Oliver Jul 25, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile haircut, the Asian haircut short sides long top is an excellent choice. This hairstyle has become increasingly popular in recent years, and it's easy to see why. With short sides and a long top, this cut offers a unique and modern look that can be customized to suit your individual style.

Description

The Asian haircut short sides long top is a popular hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. This look is often referred to as an undercut or fade haircut, as the hair is shaved or faded on the sides, creating a contrast with the longer hair on the top. The length of the hair on top can vary, and it can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and textured.

How to Achieve the Look

If you're interested in getting an Asian haircut short sides long top, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, it's important to find a skilled stylist who has experience with this type of cut. They will be able to guide you on the best length and style for your face shape and hair type.

Once you've found a stylist, the first step is to shave or fade the hair on the sides. This can be done using clippers or scissors, depending on your preference. The length of the hair on the sides will depend on your desired look, but a popular option is a skin fade, where the hair gradually tapers down to the skin.

Next, the hair on top will be cut and styled. Again, the length and style will depend on your preference, but popular options include a slicked-back look, a messy and textured style, or a pompadour. Your stylist can help you choose the best option for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving an Asian haircut short sides long top:

  1. Find a skilled stylist who has experience with this type of cut.
  2. Discuss your desired look and the best length and style for your face shape and hair type.
  3. Start by shaving or fading the hair on the sides, using clippers or scissors.
  4. Gradually taper the hair down to the skin for a skin fade.
  5. Cut and style the hair on top, using scissors or a razor.
  6. Style the hair on top according to your preference, such as slicking it back or creating texture.
  7. Finish with a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

To maintain your Asian haircut short sides long top, it's important to visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups. This will keep the sides and top looking clean and fresh, and prevent any uneven growth. Additionally, using a styling product can help hold the hair in place and maintain the desired style throughout the day.

FAQ

What face shapes does the Asian haircut short sides long top suit?

The Asian haircut short sides long top can suit a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and diamond. It's important to work with a skilled stylist to determine the best length and style for your specific face shape.

What hair types does the Asian haircut short sides long top work for?

The Asian haircut short sides long top can work for a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly. It's important to work with a skilled stylist who can recommend the best styling products and techniques for your hair type.

Can the Asian haircut short sides long top be customized?

Yes, the Asian haircut short sides long top can be customized to suit your individual style. The length and style of the hair on top can be adjusted, and the sides can be faded or shaved to different lengths.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take care of it properly. This includes washing and conditioning regularly, using a heat protectant before styling with hot tools, and avoiding over-styling or pulling on the hair excessively. Additionally, eating a healthy diet and staying hydrated can help promote healthy hair growth.

Overall, the Asian haircut short sides long top is a stylish and versatile look that can be customized to suit your individual style. With the right stylist and proper hair care, this haircut can be a great choice for anyone looking for a modern and trendy hairstyle.
