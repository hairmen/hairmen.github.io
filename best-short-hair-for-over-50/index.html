Best Short Hair For Over 50

Written by Luffy Nov 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on the best short haircuts for women over 50. As we age, it's important to choose a haircut that flatters our features and suits our lifestyle. In this article, we'll share some of the most popular short haircuts for women over 50 and provide tips on how to care for your hair. Let's get started!

Description

Choosing a short haircut can be a refreshing change, especially if you've been wearing long hair for years. Short haircuts can be low maintenance, stylish, and easy to manage. However, finding the right haircut for your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle is key. In this article, we'll share the best short haircuts for women over 50, including pixie cuts, bobs, and shags.

How to

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a popular short haircut that's been around for decades. It's a versatile haircut that can be worn sleek and polished or edgy and textured. To get the best pixie cut for your face shape, consult with your hairstylist. They'll be able to recommend a style that flatters your features and complements your style.

2. Bob

The bob is another classic short haircut that's perfect for women over 50. It's a low maintenance hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. A bob can be worn straight and sleek or curly and textured. It's a timeless haircut that will never go out of style.

3. Shag

A shag haircut is a layered cut that's perfect for women over 50. It's a low maintenance style that can be worn with or without bangs. A shag can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. It's a versatile haircut that can be worn straight or curly.

Step by Step

1. Pixie Cut

To get a pixie cut, follow these steps:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to choose the right style for your face shape.
  2. Wash and condition your hair.
  3. Towel dry your hair.
  4. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel.
  5. Blow dry your hair with a round brush.
  6. Style your hair with a flat iron or curling iron.
  7. Apply a finishing product, such as hairspray or serum.

2. Bob

To get a bob, follow these steps:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to choose the right style for your face shape.
  2. Wash and condition your hair.
  3. Towel dry your hair.
  4. Blow dry your hair with a round brush.
  5. Style your hair with a flat iron or curling iron.
  6. Apply a finishing product, such as hairspray or serum.

3. Shag

To get a shag haircut, follow these steps:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to choose the right style for your face shape.
  2. Wash and condition your hair.
  3. Towel dry your hair.
  4. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel.
  5. Blow dry your hair with a round brush.
  6. Style your hair with a flat iron or curling iron.
  7. Apply a finishing product, such as hairspray or serum.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut:

  • Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape.
  • Consider your lifestyle when choosing a haircut.
  • Choose a haircut that works with your hair texture.
  • Consult with your hairstylist before making a drastic change.
  • Invest in quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

1. What short haircuts are best for women over 50?

The best short haircuts for women over 50 include pixie cuts, bobs, and shags.

2. How do I choose the right short haircut for my face shape?

Consult with your hairstylist to choose the right short haircut for your face shape.

3. Will a short haircut make me look older?

A short haircut can actually make you look younger and more refreshed.

Hair Care

To keep your short haircut looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use quality hair products that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair to prevent damage.

We hope you found our guide on the best short haircuts for women over 50 helpful. Remember to consult with your hairstylist before making a drastic change and to invest in quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Happy styling!
