What Hair Color Suits My Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially when you are not sure which shade to pick. The right hair color can enhance your natural beauty and make you feel more confident. In this post, we will discuss the factors that determine which hair color suits your face and provide you with some tips to help you make the right choice.

Description

The first step in determining which hair color suits your face is to understand your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. These factors play a crucial role in choosing the right shade. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral, and it can be determined by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they are green, you have a warm undertone, and if they are blue, you have a cool undertone. If you can't tell, you may have a neutral undertone.

Your eye color can also influence your hair color choice. For example, if you have blue eyes, blonde or light brown hair can enhance your eye color. On the other hand, if you have brown eyes, darker shades like black or dark brown can make your eyes pop.

Your natural hair color can also play a role in choosing the right shade. If you have naturally light hair, going too dark can be too drastic of a change. Similarly, if you have naturally dark hair, going too light can also be too much of a change.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

Now that you understand the factors that determine which hair color suits your face, it's time to choose the right shade. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

As mentioned earlier, your skin tone is one of the most important factors in choosing the right hair color. If you have a warm undertone, go for shades like golden blonde, honey brown, or copper. If you have a cool undertone, go for shades like ash blonde, platinum blonde, or cool brown. If you have a neutral undertone, you can experiment with both warm and cool shades.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in choosing the right hair color. If you have blue eyes, shades like blonde, light brown, or even red can enhance your eye color. If you have green eyes, shades like copper, auburn, or warm brown can make your eyes pop. If you have brown eyes, shades like dark brown, black, or even red can complement your eye color.

Step 3: Determine Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also influence your hair color choice. If you have naturally light hair, going too dark can be too drastic of a change. Similarly, if you have naturally dark hair, going too light can also be too much of a change. It's best to stick to shades that are close to your natural hair color, especially if you are new to coloring your hair.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle: If you have a busy lifestyle, choose a low-maintenance hair color that doesn't require frequent touch-ups.
  • Consult a professional: If you are not sure which shade to pick, consult a professional stylist who can help you make the right choice.
  • Do a patch test: Before coloring your hair, do a patch test to make sure you are not allergic to the product.
  • Use hair care products: Once you have colored your hair, use hair care products that are specifically designed for colored hair to maintain the color and prevent fading.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hair color:

Q: Can I go from dark to light hair in one sitting?

A: Going from dark to light hair in one sitting can be damaging to your hair. It's best to do it gradually over several sessions to minimize damage.

Q: Can I color my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can color your hair at home, but it's important to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test before applying the product to your hair.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: It depends on your hair growth rate, but it's usually recommended to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Once you have chosen the right hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the color and prevent damage. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for colored hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently to prevent the color from fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Choosing the right hair color can be a fun and exciting process. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can maintain a beautiful and healthy color for longer.
