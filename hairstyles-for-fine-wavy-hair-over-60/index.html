Hairstyles For Fine Wavy Hair Over 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Sep 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair undergoes a lot of changes. One of the most common changes is that our hair texture tends to become finer and wavier. This can make it challenging to find the right hairstyle that suits our hair type and our age. However, with the right tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your fine wavy hair over 60. In this article, we will share some of the best hairstyles for fine wavy hair over 60.

Description

As we age, our hair loses its natural oils and becomes thinner, making it more prone to damage. Fine wavy hair is no exception. In fact, fine wavy hair over 60 is often more challenging to style because it can easily become flat or frizzy. However, with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can enhance your hair's natural texture and create a youthful and elegant look.

How to Determine Your Hair Type

Before you choose a hairstyle, it's essential to determine your hair type. Fine hair refers to the thickness of your individual hair strands. Wavy hair refers to the shape of your hair strands, which have a slight wave or curl. To determine your hair type, take a close look at your hair strands. If they are relatively thin and have a slight wave, you have fine wavy hair. Once you know your hair type, you can choose a hairstyle that complements your natural texture.

Step by Step Hairstyles for Fine Wavy Hair over 60

Now that you know your hair type, let's take a look at some of the best hairstyles for fine wavy hair over 60:

1. Pixie Cut

The Pixie Cut is a short, chic hairstyle that is perfect for women over 60. This hairstyle is low maintenance and adds volume to fine hair. To style, apply a small amount of volumizing mousse to your hair and blow-dry with a round brush. Finish with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

2. Bob Cut

The Bob Cut is a classic hairstyle that is timeless and elegant. This hairstyle is ideal for fine wavy hair as it adds volume and texture to your hair. To style, apply a texturizing spray to your hair and blow-dry with a medium-sized round brush. Finish with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

3. Layered Cut

The Layered Cut is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. This hairstyle is perfect for fine wavy hair over 60 as it adds volume and texture to your hair. To style, apply a small amount of volumizing mousse to your hair and blow-dry with a round brush. Finish with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Styling Fine Wavy Hair over 60

Here are some tips for styling fine wavy hair over 60:

  • Use a volumizing mousse to add volume to your hair
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and texture
  • Use a texturizing spray to enhance your hair's natural waves
  • Apply a hairspray to keep your hair in place
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair

FAQ

What are the best haircuts for fine wavy hair over 60?

The best haircuts for fine wavy hair over 60 are Pixie Cut, Bob Cut, and Layered Cut. These hairstyles add volume and texture to your hair and are low maintenance.

How do I enhance my hair's natural waves?

To enhance your hair's natural waves, use a texturizing spray and blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment. This will create volume and texture in your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Wavy Hair over 60

To keep your fine wavy hair healthy and vibrant, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for fine hair
  • Avoid using hot tools that can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather with a hat or scarf
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these hair care tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can create a youthful and elegant look that complements your fine wavy hair over 60.
