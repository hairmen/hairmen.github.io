How To Style Shoulder Length Wavy Layered Hair

Written by Petter Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Do you want to try something new and fresh? If you have shoulder length wavy layered hair, you are in luck! This versatile hairstyle is perfect for any occasion and can be styled in many different ways. In this article, we will show you how to style your shoulder length wavy layered hair in a relaxed and easy way.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Shoulder Length Wavy Layered Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Style Shoulder Length Wavy Layered Hair

Before we begin, it's important to note that wavy hair requires more moisture than straight hair. Make sure to use a good quality conditioner and leave-in treatment to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny. Here are some easy ways to style your shoulder length wavy layered hair:

1. Beachy Waves

To achieve this look, start by applying a texturizing spray to your damp hair. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to curl small sections of your hair away from your face. Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and spray with a light-hold hairspray.

2. Half Up Half Down

This is a simple yet stylish way to wear your shoulder length wavy layered hair. Start by parting your hair in the middle. Take a small section of hair from each side of your head and tie them together at the back of your head with an elastic. Leave the rest of your hair down and tousled.

3. Braided Crown

This is a great hairstyle for when you want to add a little something extra to your look. Start by parting your hair in the middle. Take a small section of hair from one side of your head and braid it. Wrap the braid around the top of your head like a crown and secure it with bobby pins. Repeat on the other side and leave the rest of your hair down.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you have some ideas for how to style your shoulder length wavy layered hair, let's go through the steps:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Wavy hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it's important to use a moisturizing conditioner. Rinse out the conditioner thoroughly and gently squeeze out any excess water from your hair.

Step 2: Apply Leave-In Treatment

Apply a leave-in treatment to your damp hair to help detangle and moisturize your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry

Blow dry your hair on low heat using a diffuser attachment. This will help to enhance your natural waves without causing frizz. Once your hair is dry, use a light-hold hairspray to set your style.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style your shoulder length wavy layered hair:

  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and definition to your waves
  • Don't overuse heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands and hair clips
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I straighten my wavy hair?

Yes, you can straighten your wavy hair if you want to change up your look. However, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

What type of brush should I use on my wavy hair?

Use a wide-tooth comb or a paddle brush to gently detangle your wavy hair. Avoid using a brush with bristles, as this can cause frizz.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your shoulder length wavy layered hair looking its best:

  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry out your hair and scalp
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dye
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage

Now that you know how to style your shoulder length wavy layered hair, it's time to experiment and have fun with your new look!
