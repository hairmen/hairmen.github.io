How To Know If You Suit Blonde Hair

Written by Robby Sep 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're thinking about going blonde, it's important to know if the color will suit you. Blonde hair is a bold choice that can completely change your look, so you want to make sure it's the right decision for you. In this article, we'll go through some tips on how to know if you suit blonde hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in knowing if you suit blonde hair is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone is the color of your skin, and it can be warm, cool, or neutral. Warm skin tones have yellow undertones, cool skin tones have pink undertones, and neutral skin tones have a mix of both.

Next, you'll want to consider your natural hair color. If you have naturally light hair or if you've had highlights in the past, blonde hair may be a good option for you. However, if you have naturally dark hair, going blonde may be a more drastic change and may not suit you as well.

Finally, you'll want to consider your eye color. Blonde hair looks particularly good with blue or green eyes, but it can also work with other eye colors.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to determine if blonde hair will suit you:

  1. Determine your skin tone.
  2. Consider your natural hair color.
  3. Think about your eye color.
  4. Look at photos of people with similar skin tone, natural hair color, and eye color who have gone blonde.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist to get their opinion.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when considering blonde hair:

  • Start with highlights or lowlights if you're not ready to go fully blonde.
  • Consider the maintenance required for blonde hair, as it may require more upkeep than other colors.
  • Think about the shade of blonde you want â there are many different shades to choose from, so find one that suits your skin tone and natural hair color.
  • Consider getting a wig or trying on blonde hair extensions to see if you like the look before committing to dyeing your hair.

FAQ

Is blonde hair right for everyone?

No, blonde hair may not suit everyone. It's important to consider your skin tone, natural hair color, and eye color before deciding to go blonde.

What shade of blonde is best for warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones look best with honey or golden blonde shades.

What shade of blonde is best for cool skin tones?

Cool skin tones look best with platinum or ashy blonde shades.

Hair Care

Blonde hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for caring for your blonde hair:

  • Use a purple shampoo to combat brassiness.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as they can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and considering your skin tone, natural hair color, and eye color, you can determine if blonde hair will suit you. Remember to also take into account the maintenance required and the shade of blonde that will work best for you. With the right preparation and care, you can rock blonde hair with confidence.
