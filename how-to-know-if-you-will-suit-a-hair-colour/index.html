How To Know If You Will Suit A Hair Colour

Written by Daniel Jul 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure whether the colour will suit you or not. However, with the right guidance, you can easily determine which hair colour will enhance your natural beauty. In this article, we will be sharing some tips and tricks on how to know if you will suit a hair colour.

Table of Contents

  • How to Know If You Will Suit a Hair Colour
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Know If You Will Suit a Hair Colour

Knowing whether a hair colour will suit you or not depends on various factors, such as skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour. Here are some tips to help you decide:

1. Determine Your Skin Tone

One of the most critical factors in choosing the right hair colour is your skin tone. If your skin has warm undertones, such as yellow or gold, you will look best in warm hair colours like golden blonde or caramel brown. If your skin has cool undertones, such as blue or pink, you will look best in cool hair colours like ash blonde or black.

2. Consider Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also play a significant role in determining which hair colour suits you. If you have blue or green eyes, warm hair colours like copper or auburn can bring out the intensity of your eyes. If you have brown eyes, cool hair colours like ash blonde or black can make your eyes stand out.

3. Look at Your Natural Hair Colour

Your natural hair colour can also give you a clue as to which hair colour will suit you best. If you have warm undertones in your hair, warm colours like honey blonde or chestnut brown will complement your natural hair colour. If you have cool undertones in your hair, cool colours like platinum blonde or jet black will look best.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right hair colour:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Look at the veins on the underside of your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Colour

Look at your eyes in natural light. If you have blue or green eyes, warm hair colours will complement your eyes. If you have brown eyes, cool hair colours will make your eyes stand out.

Step 3: Look at Your Natural Hair Colour

Examine the colour of your hair in natural light. If your hair has warm undertones, warm hair colours will enhance your natural hair colour. If your hair has cool undertones, cool hair colours will look best.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hair colour:

  • Consider your personality when choosing a hair colour. If you're outgoing, you may want a bold hair colour like red. If you're more reserved, you may want a more natural hair colour.
  • Look for inspiration in magazines or on social media. Find pictures of hair colours you like and bring them to your hairstylist.
  • Consider the maintenance required for the hair colour you choose. Some colours require more upkeep than others.
  • Consult with a hairstylist before making a final decision. They can help you choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone and natural hair colour.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hair colours are best for warm skin tones?

Hair colours with warm undertones, such as golden blonde or caramel brown, are best for warm skin tones.

What hair colours are best for cool skin tones?

Hair colours with cool undertones, such as ash blonde or black, are best for cool skin tones.

Can I dye my hair a completely different colour?

Yes, you can dye your hair a completely different colour. However, it's best to consult with a hairstylist before doing so to ensure the colour complements your skin tone and natural hair colour.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen your hair colour, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair colour from fading.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily determine which hair colour will suit you and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.
