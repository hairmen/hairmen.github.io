Men's haircuts can be confusing, with different names, styles, and numbers to choose from. If you're looking for a simple and classic haircut, a grade 4 haircut might be the perfect choice. This guide will explain what a grade 4 haircut is in mm and how to get it, along with tips and FAQs about men's haircuts.

What is a Grade 4 Haircut?

A grade 4 haircut is a style where the hair is cut to 1/2 inch or 12 mm in length. This is a medium length haircut that is not too short or too long. It's also known as a "number 4" haircut, as it is the fourth guard size on most clippers.

How to Get a Grade 4 Haircut

To get a grade 4 haircut, you can either visit a barber or cut your own hair at home with clippers. If you're going to a barber, simply ask for a grade 4 haircut, and they will use clippers with a number 4 guard to cut your hair to the desired length. If you're cutting your own hair, make sure to use clippers with a number 4 guard and go against the grain of your hair to get an even cut.

Step by Step Guide to Cutting Your Own Hair

If you're cutting your own hair, here's a step by step guide to getting a grade 4 haircut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Attach the number 4 guard to your clippers.
  3. Begin at the sides of your head, moving the clippers up and down to cut the hair to 1/2 inch or 12 mm in length.
  4. Move to the back of your head, making sure to keep the clippers level to get an even cut.
  5. Finally, trim the top of your head by combing your hair straight up and using the clippers to trim the ends to the same length as the sides and back.
  6. Check for any uneven spots and touch up as needed.
  7. Clean up any loose hair and you're done!

Tips for Maintaining a Grade 4 Haircut

To keep your grade 4 haircut looking sharp, here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Style your hair with a small amount of pomade or wax for a polished look.
  • Consider adding some texture or layers to your haircut for a more modern look.

FAQs About Men's Haircuts

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's haircuts:

  • What is the difference between a fade and a taper haircut?

    • A fade haircut is where the hair gradually gets shorter towards the neck and sides, while a taper haircut is where the hair is cut shorter towards the neck and sides with a gradual blend into longer hair on top.

  • What is a buzz cut?

    • A buzz cut is a short, simple haircut where the hair is cut to the same length all over the head.

  • How often should I get a haircut?

    • It depends on your hair type and style, but most men should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain their look.

Hair Care for Men

Good hair care is important for men, whether you have long or short hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo to remove dirt and oil.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot water and high heat when styling your hair, as this can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With this guide, you should now understand what a grade 4 haircut is in mm and how to get it. Whether you go to a barber or cut your own hair, a grade 4 haircut is a classic and stylish choice for men of all ages.
