Description

Men with oblong face shapes often struggle to find the perfect haircut that flatters their features. An oblong face shape is characterized by a longer face and a more prominent forehead. The goal of a haircut for an oblong face shape is to create balance by adding width to the face and minimizing the length.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect oblong face shape haircut is to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back off your face.
  2. Examine the shape of your face. Is it round, oval, square, heart-shaped, or oblong?
  3. Measure the length and width of your face with a tape measure.

If your face is longer than it is wide, you likely have an oblong face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Perfect Haircut

Once you've determined that you have an oblong face shape, it's time to find the perfect haircut. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Determine your hair type

The first step in finding the perfect oblong face shape haircut is to determine your hair type. Do you have thick, curly hair or fine, straight hair? Your hair type will influence the best haircut for you.

Step 2: Consider the length

The length of your hair is important when it comes to an oblong face shape. You want to avoid haircuts that are too long, as they can elongate the face even more. Instead, consider a shorter style that adds width to the face.

Step 3: Look for volume

Adding volume to the sides of the face can help to balance an oblong face shape. Look for haircuts that add volume to the sides, such as a layered cut or a textured crop.

Step 4: Avoid center parts

Center parts can elongate the face even more, so it's best to avoid them if you have an oblong face shape. Instead, consider a side part or a messy, textured look.

Tips for Oblong Face Shape Haircuts

Here are some additional tips for finding the perfect oblong face shape haircut:

  • Avoid haircuts that are too short, as they can make the face look even longer.
  • Consider a style with bangs to help shorten the forehead.
  • Experiment with different hair textures, such as waves or curls, to add volume to the sides of the face.
  • Ask your barber or stylist for their recommendations based on your face shape and hair type.

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oblong face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can elongate the face even more. Center parts should also be avoided.

What hairstyles flatter an oblong face shape?

Hairstyles that add width to the face, such as layered cuts or textured crops, are flattering for oblong face shapes. Bangs can also help to shorten the forehead.

Hair Care for Oblong Face Shapes

Once you've found the perfect oblong face shape haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different hair products, such as pomades or texturizing sprays, to find the perfect style for you.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect oblong face shape haircut that flatters your features and makes you feel confident.
