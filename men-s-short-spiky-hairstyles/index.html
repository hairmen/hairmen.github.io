Men s Short Spiky Hairstyles The Ultimate Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Jul 16, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's edgy, trendy, and easy to maintain, then short spiky haircuts are the perfect option for you. Whether you have straight, curly, or wavy hair, there's a short spiky hairstyle that will suit your style and personality. In this article, we'll provide you with all the information you need to know about men's short spiky hairstyles, including how to style them, tips on maintenance, and answers to some frequently asked questions. So, let's get started!

Description

Short spiky hairstyles have become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. They're versatile, low-maintenance, and can be customized to suit any face shape or hair type. Short spiky hairstyles are characterized by their textured, spiked-up look, which can range from subtle and sleek to bold and daring. They can be styled with a variety of products, such as gels, waxes, and pomades, to achieve the desired texture and hold.

How to

To achieve a short spiky hairstyle, you'll need to start with a short haircut. The length can vary, but generally, it should be no longer than 2-3 inches on top. The sides and back can be tapered or faded, depending on your preference. Once you have the right haircut, you can start styling your hair into spikes. Here's how to do it:

Step-by-Step

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's slightly damp.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair gel, wax, or pomade to your fingertips.
  4. Rub the product between your palms to warm it up.
  5. Apply the product evenly to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way up to the tips.
  6. Use your fingers or a comb to create spikes by pulling your hair upwards and outwards.
  7. Continue until you've achieved your desired look.
  8. Finish with a hairspray to keep your spikes in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short spiky hairstyle:

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Don't overdo it with the product – a little goes a long way.
  • Use a blow dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's short spiky hairstyles:

Q: What face shapes suit short spiky hairstyles?

A: Short spiky hairstyles can suit a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and diamond. However, if you have a round or heart-shaped face, it's best to avoid short spiky hairstyles as they can accentuate the roundness of your face.

Q: Can short spiky hairstyles be worn for formal occasions?

A: Short spiky hairstyles are more suitable for casual or semi-formal occasions. For formal occasions, it's best to opt for a more traditional hairstyle, such as a slicked-back or side-parted look.

Hair Care

To keep your short spiky hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape and style.

In conclusion, short spiky hairstyles are a great option for men who want a trendy, low-maintenance look. With the right haircut, styling products, and hair care routine, you can achieve a variety of short spiky hairstyles that suit your individual style and personality. So, go ahead and try out a short spiky hairstyle – you won't regret it!
