How To Fade Hair With Long Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle? Perhaps you want to keep your long top but add some edge to your look. Fading your hair is a great way to do just that. In this article, we’ll walk you through the process of how to fade hair with long top. We’ll cover what a fade is, how to do it, and some tips and tricks for maintaining your new hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • What is a Fade?
  • How to Fade Hair with Long Top
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintenance
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

What is a Fade?

A fade is a type of haircut where the hair gradually gets shorter towards the bottom of the head. The hair is typically shorter on the sides and back, while the top remains longer. There are different types of fades you can choose from, including high fades, low fades, and skin fades. The type of fade you choose will depend on your personal preference and the look you’re going for.

How to Fade Hair with Long Top

If you’re looking to fade your hair but want to keep your long top, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you’ll want to make sure you have the right tools on hand. You’ll need clippers, guards, scissors, and a comb. You may also want to invest in some styling products to help maintain your new look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to fade hair with long top:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Use clippers to trim the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one to create the fade effect.
  3. Blend the fade into the longer top using scissors. Be sure to use a comb to create a natural-looking transition between the two lengths.
  4. Once you’ve achieved the desired length, style your hair using a pomade or other styling product.

Tips for Maintenance

Once you’ve achieved your new look, you’ll want to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your fade:

  • Visit your barber regularly to keep your fade looking fresh.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Invest in a good styling product to keep your hair in place throughout the day.
  • If you have curly hair, consider using a diffuser when blow-drying to enhance your natural curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about fading hair with long top:

Q: How long does a fade last?

A: A fade typically lasts around two weeks, but this can vary depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Q: Can I fade my hair at home?

A: While it’s possible to fade your hair at home, it’s recommended that you visit a barber to ensure you get the best results.

Q: What styling products should I use for my fade?

A: This will depend on your hair type and the look you’re going for. Speak to your barber for recommendations.

Hair Care

Regardless of your hairstyle, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to fade hair with long top, it’s time to give it a try. Remember to take your time and be patient, especially if you’re doing it for the first time. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and modern look that’s sure to turn heads.
