Easy Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair To Do At Home Step By Step

Are you looking for easy hairstyles you can do at home without spending money at a salon? Do you have medium length hair and need some inspiration for new looks? Look no further! In this blog post, we'll show you step by step how to create easy hairstyles for medium length hair at home.

How to Create Easy Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

Creating easy hairstyles for medium length hair at home is simple and requires only a few essential tools such as a hairbrush, bobby pins, hair ties, and hairspray. You can create various styles, from messy buns to sleek ponytails, and it all depends on your personal preference.

Step by Step Guide

Below are step-by-step instructions on how to create five easy hairstyles for medium length hair at home:

1. Messy Bun

Step 1: Brush your hair to remove any knots or tangles.

Step 2: Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.

Step 3: Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to create a bun.

Step 4: Secure the bun with bobby pins and pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.

Step 5: Spray hairspray over the bun to keep it in place.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

Step 1: Brush your hair to remove any knots or tangles.

Step 2: Take the top section of your hair and gather it into a half ponytail.

Step 3: Secure the half ponytail with a hair tie.

Step 4: Optional - curl the remaining hair for a more polished look.

Step 5: Spray hairspray over the hairstyle to keep it in place.

3. Sleek Ponytail

Step 1: Brush your hair to remove any knots or tangles.

Step 2: Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.

Step 3: Take a small section of hair from the bottom of the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to cover it.

Step 4: Secure the wrapped hair with a bobby pin.

Step 5: Spray hairspray over the hairstyle to keep it in place.

4. French Twist

Step 1: Brush your hair to remove any knots or tangles.

Step 2: Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.

Step 3: Twist the ponytail upwards and tuck it into the base of the ponytail.

Step 4: Secure the twisted hair with bobby pins.

Step 5: Spray hairspray over the hairstyle to keep it in place.

5. Fishtail Braid

Step 1: Brush your hair to remove any knots or tangles.

Step 2: Divide your hair into two equal sections.

Step 3: Take a small section of hair from the outside of the left section and cross it over to the inside of the right section.

Step 4: Repeat step 3 but with the right section of hair.

Step 5: Continue braiding until you reach the end of your hair and secure it with a hair tie.

Step 6: Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.

Step 7: Spray hairspray over the hairstyle to keep it in place.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Look

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Use hair products such as mousse or texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to add accessories such as headbands or clips to enhance your hairstyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take to create these hairstyles?
A: It depends on the hairstyle, but most of these styles can be done in under 10 minutes.

Q: Can I create these hairstyles with short hair?
A: Some of these hairstyles can be modified for shorter hair, but others may require longer hair.

Q: Do I need any special tools to create these hairstyles?
A: No, you only need basic tools such as a hairbrush, bobby pins, hair ties, and hairspray.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

Maintaining healthy hair is essential to achieving a great hairstyle. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil buildup.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every few months to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

Creating easy hairstyles for medium length hair at home is simple and fun. With these step-by-step instructions and tips, you can achieve a stylish look without spending money at a salon. Experiment with different hairstyles and find what works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain healthy and strong locks.
