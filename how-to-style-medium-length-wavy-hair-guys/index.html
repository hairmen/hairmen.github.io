How To Style Medium Length Wavy Hair Guys

Written by Luffy Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Medium length wavy hair is a popular hairstyle choice for men. It offers versatility and style that can suit any occasion. However, styling medium length wavy hair can be a challenge for some guys. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to style medium length wavy hair guys in relaxed English language.

Description

Medium length wavy hair is typically hair that falls between the chin and shoulders. This hair type has a natural wave that can be accentuated with the right styling techniques. It provides a relaxed and effortless style that can be worn for any occasion.

How to Style Medium Length Wavy Hair Guys

Step 1: Start with clean and dry hair. Apply a small amount of styling product such as wax or pomade to your hair.

Step 2: Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create texture. If you want more defined waves, use a curling iron to curl small sections of your hair.

Step 3: Comb your hair to create a natural part or sweep it all to one side. This will give your hair a more polished look.

Step 4: Use a hair dryer to set your hair in place. Use a diffuser attachment on the hair dryer to prevent frizz and to enhance the natural wave of your hair.

Tips

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: Can I style my medium length wavy hair without using any products?

A: Yes, you can style your hair without using any products. However, using a small amount of styling product can help you achieve a more polished look and enhance the natural wave of your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my medium length wavy hair?

A: It is recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out. However, if you have an oily scalp, you may need to wash your hair more frequently.

Q: Can I use a hair straightener to straighten my medium length wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can use a hair straightener to straighten your hair. However, using a straightener too often can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your medium length wavy hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to hydrate your hair and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your medium length wavy hair like a pro. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you. Have fun with your hair and embrace your natural waves!
