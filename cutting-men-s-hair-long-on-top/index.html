Cutting Men s Hair Long On Top A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a modern and stylish haircut, you may want to consider cutting your hair long on top. This versatile style can be adapted to suit any face shape and hair type, making it a popular choice among men of all ages. In this guide, we will take you through the process of cutting men's hair long on top, step by step. We'll also share some tips on how to maintain your new haircut and answer some frequently asked questions about long haircuts for men.

How to Cut Men's Hair Long on Top

Cutting men's hair long on top requires some skill and patience, but it can be done at home with the right tools and techniques. Here's how:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools on hand. You'll need a pair of scissors, a comb, and clippers with different guards. You may also want to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen the hair and make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair on top of the head. Use a comb to create a parting from the crown to the forehead, then make another parting from ear to ear. This will create four sections of hair on top of the head.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Use the clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back of the head. Start with the longest guard and work your way down to a shorter guard, depending on how short you want the hair to be. Use the comb to blend the hair on the sides and back with the longer hair on top.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Start cutting the hair on top of the head with the scissors. Use your comb to lift a section of hair and cut it to the desired length. Work your way through each section, cutting the hair to the same length. You may want to leave some extra length at the front to create a fringe or bangs.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the top to the desired length, use your scissors to blend the hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back. Use a comb to lift the hair and trim any strays or uneven pieces.

Step 6: Style the Hair

Once you've finished cutting, style the hair using your preferred product. You can create a slick, polished look or a messy, textured style depending on your preference.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair Long on Top

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting men's hair long on top:

  • Start with clean, dry hair to make it easier to cut.
  • Use sharp scissors and clippers to avoid pulling or damaging the hair.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time to ensure an even result.
  • Blend the hair carefully to avoid any harsh lines or uneven patches.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find the look that suits you best.

FAQs About Cutting Men's Hair Long on Top

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair long on top:

How long should I leave the hair on top?

The length of the hair on top will depend on your personal preference and the style you want to achieve. Typically, the hair on top should be at least two to three inches long to create a long on top look.

Can I cut men's hair long on top at home?

Yes, you can cut men's hair long on top at home with the right tools and techniques. However, if you're unsure or inexperienced, it's always best to visit a professional stylist.

How often should I trim my long hair?

The frequency of trims will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain it. Generally, you should aim to trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Long Hair

Here are some tips to help you care for and maintain your long hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to avoid pulling or breaking the hair.
  • Avoid using heat-styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep the hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Consider getting a trim every few weeks to keep the hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and modern long on top haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. Remember to take your time, work slowly and carefully, and use the right tools to achieve the best results. With a little practice and patience, you can create a polished and sophisticated style that's sure to turn heads.
