How To Hide Eyebrow Hair With Makeup

Written by Robby Nov 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Do you have unruly eyebrow hair that just won't stay in place? Or maybe you just want to change the shape of your eyebrows without the commitment of waxing or threading. Whatever your reason, hiding eyebrow hair with makeup is a simple and effective solution. In this article, we'll show you how to hide eyebrow hair with makeup in just a few easy steps.

How to

Before we get started, gather the following items:

  • Brow pencil or powder
  • Concealer
  • Angled brush
  • Spoolie brush
  • Setting spray (optional)

Step by Step

Follow these steps to hide your eyebrow hair with makeup:

  1. Brush your eyebrow hair upwards using the spoolie brush.
  2. Using the angled brush and brow pencil or powder, fill in your eyebrows in the desired shape.
  3. Apply concealer around the edges of your eyebrows using a small brush. This will help create a sharp and defined look.
  4. Blend the concealer into your skin using a clean brush or your finger.
  5. Finish off with a setting spray to keep your eyebrows in place (optional).

And voila! Your eyebrow hair is now hidden and your eyebrows look flawless.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you hide your eyebrow hair with makeup:

  • Choose a brow pencil or powder that matches your natural eyebrow color.
  • Use light, feathery strokes when filling in your eyebrows to create a natural look.
  • When applying concealer, use a shade that matches your skin tone to avoid a noticeable contrast.
  • Use a spoolie brush to brush your eyebrow hair downwards after filling them in to create a more natural look.
  • Invest in a good quality setting spray to keep your eyebrows in place all day.

FAQ

How long will the makeup last?

The makeup should last all day if you use a good quality setting spray.

Can I use this method to change the shape of my eyebrows?

Yes! This method is great for changing the shape of your eyebrows without the commitment of waxing or threading.

Should I use a brow pencil or powder?

This is a personal preference. Brow pencils are great for a more defined and precise look, while powders create a more natural look.

Hair Care

Don't forget to take care of your eyebrow hair even when using makeup. Here are some tips:

  • Brush your eyebrow hair daily to keep them in place and promote growth.
  • Invest in a good quality eyebrow serum to promote growth and thickness.
  • Avoid over-plucking or waxing your eyebrows, as this can damage the hair follicles.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or hot tools on your eyebrows, as this can cause damage and breakage.

And that's it! You now know how to hide your eyebrow hair with makeup. With a little practice, you'll have flawless eyebrows every day.
