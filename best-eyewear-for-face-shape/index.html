Best Eyewear For Face Shape A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Fit

Written by Robby Oct 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of trying on countless pairs of eyeglasses or sunglasses only to find that none of them seem to look quite right on you? The problem may not be the frames themselves, but rather how they complement your face shape. In this guide, we'll explore the best eyewear for face shape and provide tips on how to choose the perfect fit for you.

Description

Choosing the right eyewear for your face shape can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. The key is to find frames that complement your facial features rather than detract from them. By taking into consideration your face shape and personal style, you can find the perfect pair of glasses or sunglasses to suit your needs.

How to Choose the Best Eyewear for Your Face Shape

Before you start shopping for eyewear, it's important to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes:

  1. Round
  2. Oval
  3. Square
  4. Heart
  5. Diamond
  6. Triangle

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for frames that will complement your features. Here are some general guidelines:

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to look for frames that add angles and definition to your face. Rectangular or square frames work well, as do cat-eye or butterfly styles.

Oval Face Shape

Oval faces are considered the most versatile for eyewear. You can wear almost any style of frame, so have fun with different shapes and colors.

Square Face Shape

Square faces have strong jawlines and angular features. Round or oval frames can help soften these features, while cat-eye or butterfly styles can add some flair.

Heart Face Shape

Heart-shaped faces have wider foreheads and narrower chins. Frames that are wider at the bottom than the top can help balance out your features. Look for cat-eye or butterfly styles, or try frames with detailing or accents on the bottom.

Diamond Face Shape

Diamond faces have angular cheekbones and narrow foreheads and chins. Rimless or oval frames can help soften your features, while cat-eye or butterfly styles can add some drama.

Triangle Face Shape

Triangle faces have wider jawlines and narrow foreheads. Frames that are wider at the top than the bottom can help balance out your features. Look for cat-eye or butterfly styles, or try frames with detailing or accents on the top.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Frames

Now that you know your face shape and what types of frames will work best for you, it's time to start shopping. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Try on different styles of frames to see what you like.
  2. Take note of the shape and size of the frames that look best on you.
  3. Consider the color and material of the frames. Do they complement your skin tone and personal style?
  4. Make sure the frames fit comfortably on your face. They shouldn't slide down your nose or pinch your temples.
  5. Ask for a second opinion. Have a friend or family member take a look and give their feedback.
  6. Take your time and don't feel pressured to make a decision right away.

Tips for Choosing Eyewear Based on Your Face Shape

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing eyewear:

  • Consider your personal style. If you're more conservative, you may prefer classic styles like aviators or wayfarers. If you're more adventurous, you may want to try bold colors or unique shapes.
  • Think about the occasion. Do you need eyewear for everyday wear or a special occasion? Consider the formality of the event and choose frames that fit the occasion.
  • Take care of your frames. Invest in a quality case to protect your eyewear when you're not wearing them. Clean them regularly with a microfiber cloth and avoid using harsh chemicals or paper towels.

FAQs About Choosing Eyewear for Face Shape

Q: Can I wear any style of eyewear if I have an oval face?

A: Yes! Oval faces are considered the most versatile for eyewear, so you can try almost any style of frame.

Q: Can I wear cat-eye frames if I have a square face?

A: Yes! Cat-eye frames can add some flair to a square face, but make sure the frames are not too small or narrow.

Q: Are there any eyewear styles that are universally flattering?

A: Aviator and wayfarer styles are considered universally flattering, as they work well on most face shapes.

Hair Care Tips for Wearing Eyewear

When wearing eyewear, it's important to take care of your hair as well. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid wearing your glasses or sunglasses on the top of your head, as this can cause them to stretch out or lose their shape.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid buildup on your frames.
  • Keep your hair away from the nose pads to avoid tangling or breakage.
  • If you have long hair, consider a low ponytail or bun to keep your hair out of the way and avoid tangling with your frames.

By following these tips and guidelines, you can find the perfect eyewear for your face shape and personal style. Happy shopping!
