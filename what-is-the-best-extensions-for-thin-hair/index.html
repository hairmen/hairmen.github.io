What Are The Best Extensions For Thin Hair In 2023

Written by Petter Jul 27, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have thin hair, you may be wondering what the best extensions are to add volume and length to your locks. In this article, we'll explore some of the top extensions for thin hair, including clip-ins, tape-ins, and more. We'll also provide tips for caring for your extensions and answer some frequently asked questions about this popular hair accessory.

Table of Contents:

  • Clip-In Extensions
  • Tape-In Extensions
  • Sew-In Extensions
  • Halo Extensions
  • Extension Care Tips
  • FAQ

Clip-In Extensions

Clip-in extensions are a popular option for those with thin hair because they are easy to apply and remove. They come in a variety of lengths and colors, so you can find the perfect match for your hair. Clip-ins are also affordable and can be reused multiple times with proper care.

To apply clip-in extensions, first, create a horizontal part in your hair at the crown of your head. Then, clip in the extension wefts starting from the bottom and working your way up. Be sure to blend the extensions with your natural hair by gently brushing them together.

Tape-In Extensions

Tape-in extensions are another great option for thin hair. They are applied by sandwiching a section of your natural hair between two adhesive tape wefts. Tape-ins lay flat against your head, making them a good choice for those with fine hair.

To apply tape-in extensions, a stylist will section your hair and apply the adhesive wefts. They will then use a flat iron to seal the wefts in place. Tape-ins can last up to 8 weeks with proper care.

Sew-In Extensions

Sew-in extensions, also known as weaves, are a more permanent option for those with thin hair. They are applied by braiding your natural hair and sewing the extensions onto the braids. Sew-ins can add significant volume and length to your hair.

To have sew-in extensions applied, you will need to visit a salon. A stylist will braid your hair and sew in the wefts. Sew-ins can last up to 3 months with proper care.

Halo Extensions

Halo extensions are a newer type of extension that attach to a clear wire that sits on top of your head. They are a good option for those with thin hair who don't want to use clips or adhesive. Halo extensions are also easy to apply and remove.

To apply halo extensions, simply place the wire on top of your head and adjust the length to fit your hair. Then, use a comb to gently blend your natural hair with the extensions.

Extension Care Tips

To keep your extensions looking their best, follow these care tips:

  • Brush your extensions daily with a soft-bristled brush
  • Wash your extensions every 1-2 weeks with a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Avoid using heat tools on your extensions
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair

FAQ

Can I use extensions if I have very thin hair?

Yes, you can use extensions if you have very thin hair. However, you should choose extensions that are specifically designed for thin hair, such as clip-ins or tape-ins.

How long do extensions last?

The lifespan of extensions depends on the type of extensions and how well you care for them. Clip-ins and halo extensions can last up to a year with proper care, while tape-ins and sew-ins can last up to 3 months.

Can I color my extensions?

It depends on the type of extensions you have. Some extensions can be colored, while others cannot. Check with the manufacturer or stylist before coloring your extensions.

With the right extensions and proper care, you can have the voluminous, luscious locks you've always wanted. Whether you choose clip-ins, tape-ins, sew-ins, or halo extensions, you're sure to turn heads with your new 'do.
