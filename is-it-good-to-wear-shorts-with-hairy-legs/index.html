Is It Good To Wear Shorts With Hairy Legs The Ultimate Guide In 2023

Written by Eveline Jun 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Summer is here, and with it comes the age-old question: is it good to wear shorts with hairy legs? Some people swear by it, while others cringe at the thought. In this ultimate guide, we’ll explore the benefits and drawbacks of wearing shorts with hairy legs, how to care for your leg hair, and everything else you need to know to make an informed decision.

  • Drawbacks of Wearing Shorts with Hairy Legs

  • Hair Care Products for Hairy Legs

Benefits of Wearing Shorts with Hairy Legs

Let’s start with the good news: there are plenty of benefits to wearing shorts with hairy legs. Here are just a few:

  • It’s more comfortable. Leg hair can actually help to wick sweat away from your skin, keeping you cooler and more comfortable on hot days.
  • It’s natural. Leg hair is a perfectly normal part of the human body, and there’s no reason to be ashamed of it.
  • It’s low-maintenance. Shaving your legs can be a time-consuming and painful process, and letting your hair grow out can be a great way to save time and energy.

Drawbacks of Wearing Shorts with Hairy Legs

Of course, there are also some potential drawbacks to consider:

  • It can be less aesthetically pleasing. Some people simply prefer the look of smooth, hairless legs.
  • It can be itchy. If you’re not used to having hair on your legs, it can be uncomfortable or itchy.
  • It can be stigmatized. Unfortunately, there is still some stigma attached to women who choose not to shave their legs, and you may face criticism or judgment from others.

How to Care for Your Leg Hair

If you do decide to embrace your leg hair, there are a few things you can do to keep it healthy and looking its best:

  • Moisturize regularly. Just like the hair on your head, leg hair can benefit from regular moisture. Apply a gentle lotion or oil to your legs after showering.
  • Exfoliate occasionally. To prevent ingrown hairs and keep your skin smooth, exfoliate your legs once or twice a week.
  • Trim if necessary. If your leg hair is particularly long or unruly, you may want to trim it with scissors or clippers.

Step-by-Step Guide to Wearing Shorts with Hairy Legs

If you’re ready to rock your shorts with pride, here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose shorts that make you feel comfortable and confident. There’s no need to wear anything too short or revealing if it doesn’t make you feel good.
  2. Pair your shorts with a cute top and some comfortable shoes.
  3. If you’re self-conscious about your leg hair, try wearing a pair of sheer tights or leggings underneath your shorts.
  4. Accessorize with sunglasses, jewelry, or a cute bag to complete your look.

Tips for Wearing Shorts with Hairy Legs

Here are a few additional tips to help you feel confident and comfortable in your shorts:

  • Remember that everyone has leg hair, and there’s no need to be ashamed of it.
  • If you’re worried about what other people will think, remind yourself that their opinions don’t matter.
  • Take care of your skin and hair to keep it looking its best.
  • If you’re still feeling self-conscious, try wearing your shorts around the house or in private before venturing out in public.

FAQs About Wearing Shorts with Hairy Legs

Q: Is it unhygienic to have hairy legs?

A: No, leg hair is perfectly hygienic as long as you keep your skin clean and healthy.

Q: Will my leg hair grow back thicker if I shave it?

A: No, this is a common myth. Shaving your legs will not make your hair grow back thicker or darker.

Q: What if I’m worried about being judged or criticized?

A: Remember that everyone has the right to make their own choices about their body, and other people’s opinions don’t matter. If someone does criticize you, try to ignore them and focus on your own feelings of confidence and self-worth.

Hair Care Products for Hairy Legs

Here are a few products that can help you care for your leg hair:

  • Moisturizer: Use a gentle lotion or oil to keep your skin and hair moisturized.
  • Exfoliator: Use a gentle exfoliating scrub or brush to prevent ingrown hairs and keep your skin smooth.
  • Trimming scissors or clippers: If your leg hair is particularly long, you may want to trim it with scissors or clippers.

So, is it good to wear shorts with hairy legs? Ultimately, the decision is up to you. Just remember that there’s nothing wrong with embracing your natural leg hair, and you should wear whatever makes you feel comfortable and confident. Happy summer!
