What Hairstyle Makes Your Face Look Slimmer

Written by William Oct 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your round face and want to make it look slimmer? A simple solution is to change your hairstyle! Certain hairstyles can create the illusion of a slimmer face, while others can make it appear rounder. In this article, we'll discuss what hairstyle makes your face look slimmer and provide tips on how to achieve it.

Table of Contents

  • How To Make Your Face Look Slimmer With A Hairstyle
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips For Choosing The Best Hairstyle
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How To Make Your Face Look Slimmer With A Hairstyle

The key to making your face look slimmer is to create volume at the top and keep it minimal at the sides. This will create an illusion of a longer face and give the appearance of a slimmer face. Here are some hairstyles that can help achieve this:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer face. Ask your hairstylist to add long layers near your cheekbones to help balance your face shape. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin, as they can make your face look rounder.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help draw attention away from your cheekbones and create an illusion of a longer face. Ask your hairstylist to cut your bangs at an angle, starting from the middle of your forehead and ending at the side of your cheek. Avoid straight-across bangs that can make your face look rounder.

3. High Ponytail

A high ponytail can help create volume at the top of your head and elongate your face. Pull your hair up into a high ponytail, leaving some hair at the sides to frame your face. Avoid low ponytails that can make your face look wider.

4. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut can help create volume at the crown of your head and elongate your face. Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair into a chin-length bob, keeping the layers longer at the front to frame your face. Avoid bobs that end at your chin, as they can make your face look rounder.

Step By Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a hairstyle that can make your face look slimmer:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and examining your face in the mirror. There are four basic face shapes: round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. Each face shape requires a different hairstyle to make it look slimmer.

2. Consult With Your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist to determine which hairstyle will suit your face shape. Your hairstylist can also suggest different haircuts and styles that will flatter your features.

3. Add Volume At The Top

Add volume at the top of your head by using a volumizing shampoo and blow-drying your hair upside down. You can also use a root-lifting spray or mousse to add volume at the roots.

4. Keep It Minimal At The Sides

Keep your hair minimal at the sides by avoiding hairstyles that add volume at the sides. This will help create an illusion of a longer face and make it appear slimmer.

Tips For Choosing The Best Hairstyle

Here are some tips for choosing the best hairstyle for your face shape:

1. Round Face

If you have a round face, choose a hairstyle that adds height at the crown of your head and elongates your face. Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the sides, such as blunt cuts and bobs that end at your chin.

2. Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any hairstyle. Choose a hairstyle that flatters your best features and complements your personality.

3. Square Face

If you have a square face, choose a hairstyle that softens your angular features. Avoid blunt cuts and short hairstyles that accentuate your jawline.

4. Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, choose a hairstyle that balances your forehead and chin. Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the crown of your head and accentuate your forehead.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I make my face look slimmer without changing my hairstyle?

Yes, you can make your face look slimmer by contouring your face with makeup. Use a contouring powder or cream to create shadows that can make your face appear slimmer.

2. How often should I change my hairstyle?

It's recommended to change your hairstyle every six to eight weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

3. Will bangs make my face look rounder?

It depends on the type of bangs you choose. Side-swept bangs can help make your face look slimmer, while straight-across bangs can make it appear rounder.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

1. Choose The Right Shampoo And Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and addresses your hair concerns. If you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner that adds body and texture. If you have dry hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that hydrates and nourishes your hair.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.

3. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims every six to eight weeks to get rid of split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

In conclusion, changing your hairstyle can make a big difference in the appearance of your face. By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape, you can create the illusion of a slimmer face and boost your confidence.
