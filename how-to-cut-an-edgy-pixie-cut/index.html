How To Cut An Edgy Pixie Cut

Written by Frank Nov 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a bold and daring haircut, an edgy pixie cut may be just what you need. This short, choppy hairstyle is perfect for those who want to make a statement and show off their individuality. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps of cutting an edgy pixie cut and provide you with tips and tricks to make the process as smooth as possible.

How to Cut an Edgy Pixie Cut

The key to cutting an edgy pixie cut is to keep the length short and choppy. This style is all about texture and movement, so you'll want to use a razor or scissors to create wispy layers throughout the hair. Here are the basic steps to cutting an edgy pixie cut:

Step 1: Section the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair into four sections: top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 2: Cut the Sides and Back

Using scissors or a razor, cut the hair on the sides and back of the head to the desired length. Keep the hair short and choppy, using the razor to create texture and movement.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Next, use scissors or a razor to cut the hair on the top of the head to the desired length. Keep the hair longer on top than on the sides and back, and create wispy layers using the razor.

Step 4: Blend the Layers

Once you've cut all of the hair, use scissors or a razor to blend the layers together. This will create a seamless transition from the shorter hair on the sides and back to the longer hair on top.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed guide to cutting an edgy pixie cut:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Start by washing and drying the hair. This will make it easier to cut and will ensure that you're starting with a clean slate.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Using a comb or your fingers, separate the hair into four sections: top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Starting with the sides and back of the head, use scissors or a razor to cut the hair to the desired length. Keep the hair short and choppy, using the razor to create texture and movement.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Next, move on to the top of the head. Use scissors or a razor to cut the hair to the desired length, keeping it longer than the sides and back. Create wispy layers using the razor to add texture and movement.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Once you've cut all of the hair, use scissors or a razor to blend the layers together. This will create a seamless transition from the shorter hair on the sides and back to the longer hair on top.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect edgy pixie cut:

  • Use a razor to create texture and movement in the hair.
  • Keep the hair short and choppy for a more dramatic look.
  • Create wispy layers on the top of the head to add volume and texture.
  • Blend the layers together for a seamless transition from the shorter hair on the sides and back to the longer hair on top.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to the hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

What face shapes are best suited for an edgy pixie cut?

An edgy pixie cut can work for most face shapes, but it's particularly flattering on those with angular features or a heart-shaped face. If you have a round face, you may want to opt for a longer pixie cut to balance out your features.

How often should I get my edgy pixie cut trimmed?

You should get your edgy pixie cut trimmed every 4 to 6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of the cut.

Can I style my edgy pixie cut in different ways?

Yes, there are many ways to style an edgy pixie cut. You can use styling products to create volume and texture, or you can slick the hair back for a more polished look. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Hair Care for an Edgy Pixie Cut

Here are some tips for caring for your edgy pixie cut:

  • Wash your hair every 2 to 3 days to avoid drying it out.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create an edgy pixie cut that's perfect for your unique style and personality. Have fun experimenting with different styles and remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
