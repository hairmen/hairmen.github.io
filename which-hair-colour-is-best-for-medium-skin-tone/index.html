Which Hair Colour Is Best For Medium Skin Tone

Written by Robby Sep 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a person with medium skin tone, you might wonder which hair colour suits you the best. Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss the best hair colours for medium skin tones. Read on to find out more.

Description

Medium skin tone refers to skin that is neither too light nor too dark. It has a warm golden undertone that complements various hair colours. But, not all hair colours look good on medium skin tones. Choosing the right hair colour is essential as it can make or break your look.

How to Choose the Best Hair Colour for Medium Skin Tone

Choosing the best hair colour for medium skin tone can seem like a daunting task. But, with these tips, you can easily find the right hair colour:

Consider Your Undertone

When choosing a hair colour, it's essential to consider your undertone. If you have warm undertones, opt for warmer colours like honey blonde, caramel, or chestnut brown. If you have cool undertones, go for cooler colours like ash blonde, platinum blonde, or burgundy.

Think About Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also help you choose the right hair colour. If you have brown eyes, warm colours like copper, caramel, or honey blonde can complement your eyes. If you have blue or green eyes, cooler colours like ash blonde, platinum blonde, or burgundy can make your eyes pop.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also influence your hair colour choice. If you're always on the go, choose a low maintenance hair colour like balayage or highlights. If you have more time to spare, go for a full head of colour.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Best Hair Colour for Medium Skin Tone

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the best hair colour for medium skin tone:

Step 1: Identify Your Skin Undertone

Identify your skin undertone by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Colour

Consider your eye colour and choose a hair colour that complements it.

Step 3: Look at Your Natural Hair Colour

Look at your natural hair colour and choose a shade that's not too far from it. Going too light or too dark can be overwhelming.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional

If you're not sure which hair colour suits you the best, consult with a professional stylist. They can help you choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone and eye colour.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Colour

Once you've chosen the best hair colour for medium skin tone, it's essential to maintain it. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair colour:

Use Colour-Safe Products

Use colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your hair colour. Avoid using products that contain sulfates or alcohol as they can strip your hair of its colour.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling your hair too often as it can damage your hair and fade your hair colour. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray that contains SPF.

FAQs

What hair colours should I avoid if I have medium skin tone?

Avoid hair colours that are too light, like platinum blonde or ash blonde. They can wash you out and make you look pale.

Can I dye my hair at home?

You can dye your hair at home, but it's best to consult with a professional stylist first. They can help you choose the right shade and guide you through the process.

How often should I touch up my roots?

It depends on your hair growth and the hair colour you choose. Some hair colours require more touch-ups than others. Consult with your stylist to know how often you should touch up your roots.

Hair Care for Coloured Hair

Coloured hair requires extra care to maintain its vibrancy and shine. Here are some hair care tips for coloured hair:

Use a Colour-Protecting Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a colour-protecting shampoo and conditioner to maintain your hair colour.

Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils and fade your hair colour. Wash your hair every other day or two to maintain its health and vibrancy.

Deep Condition Your Hair Regularly

Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and moisturized. Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

Conclusion

Choosing the best hair colour for medium skin tone can be easy if you follow these tips. Consider your undertone, eye colour, and lifestyle when choosing a hair colour. Consult with a professional stylist if you're not sure which hair colour suits you best. Once you've chosen a hair colour, maintain it with the right hair care products and techniques.
