Can You Get Faux Locs With Long Hair

Written by Daniel Oct 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone with long hair who loves the look of faux locs, you may be wondering if it's possible to achieve this style with your hair length. The good news is that the answer is yes! In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about getting faux locs with long hair, including how to do it, tips for ensuring they look great, and how to take care of your hair while they're in.

Description

Faux locs have become an increasingly popular style in recent years, and for good reason. They're a great way to switch up your look without committing to the permanence of real locs. Faux locs are typically achieved by wrapping hair around extensions or synthetic hair, which creates the look of real locs.

How to Get Faux Locs with Long Hair

The process of getting faux locs with long hair is relatively straightforward. Here are the steps you'll need to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Type of Faux Locs You Want

There are a few different types of faux locs you can choose from, including goddess locs, bohemian locs, and butterfly locs. Each style has a slightly different look, so take some time to research which one you like best.

Step 2: Gather Your Supplies

You'll need the following supplies to get started:

  • Faux locs hair extensions
  • Crochet hook
  • Rubber bands
  • Hair moisturizer
  • Scissors

Step 3: Braid Your Hair

Before you can start wrapping your hair around the faux locs extensions, you'll need to braid your hair into small sections. The smaller the sections, the more natural your faux locs will look.

Step 4: Add the Faux Locs

Using a crochet hook, add the faux locs extensions to each of the braided sections of hair. Start at the root of your hair and work your way down, wrapping the faux locs hair around your own hair as you go. Use rubber bands to secure the faux locs in place.

Step 5: Finish Your Faux Locs

Continue adding the faux locs until you've covered all of your hair. Once you're done, use scissors to trim any excess hair. Apply a hair moisturizer to your scalp to keep it hydrated while the faux locs are in.

Tips for Getting Great Faux Locs with Long Hair

Getting great faux locs with long hair requires a bit of extra care and attention. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose high-quality faux locs extensions to ensure they look and feel natural.
  • Consider getting your faux locs done by a professional stylist if you're not confident in your ability to do it yourself.
  • Avoid using heavy products on your hair while the faux locs are in, as this can weigh them down and make them look less natural.
  • Be gentle with your hair when washing and styling it while the faux locs are in.
  • Consider wrapping your hair at night to prevent tangling and matting.

FAQ

Can you get faux locs with relaxed hair?

Yes, you can get faux locs with relaxed hair. However, it's important to make sure your hair is healthy and strong enough to handle the added weight of the faux locs.

How long do faux locs last?

Faux locs can last anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks, depending on how well you take care of them.

Can you swim with faux locs?

Yes, you can swim with faux locs, but it's important to rinse them thoroughly with fresh water afterwards to remove any chlorine or saltwater.

Hair Care for Faux Locs

While your faux locs are in, it's important to take good care of your hair to prevent breakage and damage. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Keep your scalp hydrated by applying a hair moisturizer regularly.
  • Avoid using heavy products on your hair, as this can weigh down the faux locs and make them look less natural.
  • Be gentle when washing and styling your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as this can damage both your natural hair and the faux locs extensions.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent tangling and matting while you sleep.

With these tips in mind, you can achieve great-looking faux locs with long hair. Whether you're new to the style or a seasoned pro, taking good care of your hair is key to keeping your faux locs looking great for weeks to come.
