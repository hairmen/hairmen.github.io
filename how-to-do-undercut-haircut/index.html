How To Do Undercut Haircut Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Oct 01, 2023 · 4 min read

The undercut haircut has been gaining popularity over the years, and for good reason. It’s a versatile hairstyle that can suit both men and women. This hairstyle features a short cut on the sides and back of the head, while the top is left longer. If you’re looking to try out this trendy haircut, read on for our step-by-step guide on how to do an undercut haircut.

How to

Step 1: Gather Your Tools
Before starting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You’ll need a comb, clippers, scissors, and a mirror. It’s also important to have a clear idea of the kind of undercut you want to achieve.

Step 2: Section Your Hair
Start by sectioning your hair. Use a comb to create a parting along the top of your head, from one ear to the other. Clip the top section of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Trim the Sides and Back
Using clippers, trim the sides and back of your hair to the desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one. Remember to use the mirror to check your progress.

Step 4: Blend the Hair
To create a seamless blend between the top and sides of your hair, use the clippers to create a fade. Start by using a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one, blending the two sections together.

Step 5: Cut the Top
Unclip the top section of your hair and comb it straight up. Use scissors to trim the length to your desired style. You can choose to leave it long or create layers for added texture.

Step 6: Style Your Hair
Once you’re happy with the length and cut, style your hair using your preferred product. You can opt for a slicked-back look or a messy, textured finish.

Tips

Tip 1: Start with a Longer Guard
It’s always better to start with a longer guard and work your way down. This way, you can avoid cutting too much hair and ruining the haircut.

Tip 2: Invest in Quality Tools
Investing in quality hair tools such as clippers and scissors can make a huge difference to the final result. They’re more precise and can make the process much easier.

Tip 3: Consider Your Face Shape
Before deciding on an undercut haircut, consider your face shape. This style works well with oval or square faces but may not suit round or heart-shaped faces.

FAQ

Q1: How often should I get an undercut haircut?
A: It depends on how fast your hair grows. On average, you should get your haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Q2: Can women have an undercut haircut?
A: Yes, women can definitely rock an undercut haircut. It’s a versatile and trendy style that works well for both men and women.

Hair Care

Tip 1: Use a Quality Shampoo and Conditioner
To maintain healthy hair, use a quality shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage
If you use heat styling tools, make sure to protect your hair from heat damage. Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims
To maintain the health and shape of your undercut haircut, make sure to get regular trims. This will prevent split ends and keep your hair looking fresh.

Now that you know how to do an undercut haircut, it’s time to try it out for yourself. Remember to take your time, be patient, and most importantly, have fun with your new hairstyle.
