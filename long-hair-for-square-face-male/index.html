Long Hair For Square Face Male A Guide For 2023

If you have a square face, you might think that long hair is not for you. But with the right style, long hair can actually complement your features and make you look even more handsome. In this guide, we'll show you how to choose the best long hair style for your square face, how to take care of it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Choose the Right Long Hair Style for Square Face Male?

When it comes to choosing the right long hair style for a square face male, there are a few things to keep in mind. You want to choose a style that softens your features and balances out the angles of your face. Here are some styles to consider:

Layered Hair

Layered hair can add some texture and movement to your hair, which can soften your features. You can go for long layers or short layers, depending on your preference. Just make sure that the layers are not too short, as this can make your face look even more square.

Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs can also soften your features and add some asymmetry to your face, which can balance out the angles. You can go for long or short side swept bangs, but make sure that they are not too thick, as this can make your face look even more square.

Wavy or Curly Hair

Wavy or curly hair can add some volume and texture to your hair, which can soften your features. You can go for loose waves or tight curls, depending on your preference. Just make sure that the curls are not too tight, as this can make your face look even more square.

Step-by-Step Guide to Get Long Hair for Square Face Male

Getting long hair for a square face male requires some patience and dedication. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you achieve your desired length:

Step 1: Start with a Short Haircut

If you currently have short hair, you can skip this step. But if you have medium or long hair, it's best to start with a short haircut to get rid of any split ends or damage. This will also help your hair grow healthier and stronger.

Step 2: Take Care of Your Scalp

Healthy hair starts with a healthy scalp. Make sure to shampoo and condition your hair regularly, and use a scalp scrub to exfoliate any buildup. You can also use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

Step 3: Trim Your Hair Regularly

To keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, it's important to trim your hair regularly. Aim for a trim every 6-8 weeks, or whenever you notice any split ends.

Step 4: Be Patient

It can take several months or even years to grow long hair, so be patient and don't get discouraged. You can also try taking hair supplements or using growth-promoting products to speed up the process.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair for Square Face Male

Once you have achieved your desired length, it's important to take care of your long hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When brushing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage and damage. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

Tip 2: Use Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage. You can also air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to minimize heat damage.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun.

Frequently Asked Questions about Long Hair for Square Face Male

Q: Will long hair make my face look even more square?

A: Not necessarily. With the right style, long hair can actually complement your square face and soften your features.

Q: How often should I wash my long hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or work out frequently, you may need to wash your hair more often. But if you have dry or curly hair, you can wash it less frequently to prevent dryness.

Q: Can I still wear a man bun with a square face?

A: Yes, you can still wear a man bun with a square face. Just make sure to keep the bun loose and messy to add some texture and soften your features.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair for Square Face Male

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your long hair looking healthy and beautiful:

Tip 1: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a month, use a deep conditioning treatment to nourish your hair and prevent dryness. You can use a store-bought treatment or make your own with natural ingredients like coconut oil or honey.

Tip 2: Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles like braids or ponytails, as they can cause tension and breakage. Instead, opt for loose hairstyles like a messy bun or half-up half-down style.

Tip 3: Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote hair growth and prevent hair loss. Eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein to nourish your hair from the inside out.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock long hair with your square face and look your best. Remember to be patient, take care of your hair, and have fun with your new style!
