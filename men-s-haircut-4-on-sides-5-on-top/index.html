Men s Haircut 4 On Sides 5 On Top

Written by Christine Nov 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a stylish yet low-maintenance haircut? Look no further than the men's haircut 4 on sides 5 on top! This versatile cut is perfect for guys who want a clean, modern look without spending hours in front of the mirror every morning. In this article, we'll take you through the steps of getting this popular haircut, as well as some tips for maintaining it and keeping your hair healthy and strong.

Description

The men's haircut 4 on sides 5 on top is exactly what it sounds like: the sides are cut to a 4, while the top is left slightly longer at a 5. This creates a clean, tapered look that is both professional and fashionable. Unlike some more high-maintenance hairstyles, this cut is easy to style and maintain, making it a popular choice among busy guys who still want to look great.

How to

If you're interested in getting a men's haircut 4 on sides 5 on top, the first step is to find a skilled barber or stylist who is experienced with this cut. Be sure to bring in a photo or two of the style you're looking for, so that your stylist can get a clear idea of what you want.

Once you're in the chair, your stylist will begin by cutting the sides down to a 4. This will create a clean, tapered look that frames your face and highlights your facial features. Next, your stylist will trim the top to a 5, leaving it slightly longer than the sides. This will give you some length to work with, while still keeping the overall look neat and tidy.

After your cut is complete, your stylist may use some product to help you style your hair. A pomade or wax can help define your hair and give it some texture, while a gel or mousse can help hold it in place. Your stylist can recommend the best products for your hair type and style, so don't be afraid to ask!

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step breakdown of the men's haircut 4 on sides 5 on top:

Step 1: Find a Skilled Barber or Stylist

Start by finding a barber or stylist who is experienced with this cut. Ask for recommendations from friends, or do some research online to find someone in your area.

Step 2: Bring in Photos

Bring in a photo or two of the style you're looking for. This will give your stylist a clear idea of what you want, and help ensure that you're both on the same page.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Your stylist will start by cutting the sides down to a 4. This will create a clean, tapered look that frames your face and highlights your facial features.

Step 4: Trim the Top

Next, your stylist will trim the top to a 5, leaving it slightly longer than the sides. This will give you some length to work with, while still keeping the overall look neat and tidy.

Step 5: Style as Desired

Your stylist may use some product to help you style your hair. A pomade or wax can help define your hair and give it some texture, while a gel or mousse can help hold it in place. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your men's haircut 4 on sides 5 on top:

  • Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your cut looking fresh
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for you
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair
  • Be open to trying new styles and haircuts to keep your look fresh and modern

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's haircut 4 on sides 5 on top:

Is this cut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this cut can work well on most hair types, although it may look slightly different depending on your hair's texture and thickness.

How often should I get this cut?

We recommend visiting your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your cut looking fresh and tidy.

Can I style this cut in different ways?

Yes, there are many ways to style a men's haircut 4 on sides 5 on top. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and strong, be sure to use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are formulated for your hair type (e.g. oily, dry, curly, etc.), and avoid using harsh chemicals or heat-styling tools too often. Additionally, consider adding a hair mask or deep conditioner to your routine once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

With the right cut, styling, and hair care routine, you can rock the men's haircut 4 on sides 5 on top with confidence and style. So why not give it a try?
