Do Curtain Bangs Suit Round Faces

Description

Curtain bangs have been the talk of the town since the past few years. They have been trending all over social media platforms, and it seems like they are here to stay for a while. Curtain bangs are a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in different ways. One of the most common questions that arise when considering curtain bangs is whether they suit round faces or not. The answer is yes! Curtain bangs are an excellent choice for round faces, and they can make your face look more elongated and slimmer.

How to

If you have a round face and are considering getting curtain bangs, the first step is to consult with your hairstylist. They can guide you on the best way to cut and style your bangs based on your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Once you have decided to go for it, follow these steps:

Step 1: Get the Right Length

The length of your curtain bangs should fall between your cheekbones and chin. This length will make your face look more elongated and slimmer.

Step 2: Cut Wispy Bangs

Wispy bangs are the best choice for round faces. They soften the jawline and make your face appear more balanced. Avoid blunt bangs as they can accentuate the roundness of your face.

Step 3: Style Your Bangs

You can style your curtain bangs in different ways. You can wear them parted in the middle or swept to the side. You can also use a round brush and blow dryer to add volume and create a bouncy look.

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your curtain bangs:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling mousse to add volume to your hair.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Lift your hair at the roots to add volume.

Step 3: Style Your Bangs

Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs. You can sweep them to the side or part them in the middle. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting curtain bangs:

  • Choose the right length of bangs that flatter your face shape.
  • Go for wispy bangs as they soften your jawline and make your face appear more balanced.
  • Style your bangs in different ways to create a versatile look.
  • Use a round brush and blow dryer to add volume to your hair.
  • Use a hairspray to hold your style in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs and round faces:

Q. Can I get blunt bangs if I have a round face?

A. Blunt bangs accentuate the roundness of your face. It is best to avoid them if you have a round face.

Q. How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

A. It is recommended to trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and styled.

Q. Can I wear my curtain bangs with a ponytail?

A. Yes, you can wear your curtain bangs with a ponytail. It creates a chic and effortless look.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when getting curtain bangs:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to restore moisture to your hair.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Overall, curtain bangs are an excellent choice for round faces. They can make your face look more elongated and slimmer. Consult with your hairstylist to get the best cut and style for your face shape and hair texture. Follow the tips and hair care tips mentioned above to keep your hair healthy and styled.
