Best Beard Styles For Round Face Bald Head

Written by Daniel Sep 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a round face and a bald head, you might think that growing a beard is out of the question. However, there are plenty of beard styles that can complement your look and make you feel confident. In this article, we'll explore the best beard styles for round face bald head, and provide you with tips on how to maintain your beard.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Beard Style
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Growing a Beard
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Beard
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Bald Heads

Description

When it comes to choosing a beard style for a round face and bald head, you want to find a style that will elongate your face and provide balance to your features. Some of the best beard styles for this face shape include:

  • Stubble
  • Short Beard
  • Extended Goatee
  • Soul Patch
  • Van Dyke

Each of these beard styles can be tailored to suit your personal taste and style. Whether you prefer a clean-shaven look or a full beard, there's a style that will work for you.

How to Choose the Best Beard Style

The key to choosing the best beard style for a round face and bald head is to find a style that will create the illusion of length. This means avoiding styles that are too wide or full, as they can make your face appear rounder.

Aim for a beard style that is narrower at the chin and fuller at the sides. This will help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look. You can also experiment with different lengths and shapes to find a style that suits your face shape and personal style.

Stubble

Stubble is a great option for men with a round face and bald head. This style is low maintenance and easy to maintain, making it perfect for guys who don't want to spend a lot of time grooming their beard. To achieve the perfect stubble, simply let your facial hair grow for a few days and then trim it down to a short length using a beard trimmer.

Short Beard

A short beard is another great option for guys with a round face and bald head. This style is slightly longer than stubble but still maintains a narrow shape. To achieve a short beard, let your facial hair grow for a few weeks and then trim it down using a beard trimmer. You can also use scissors to trim any stray hairs and shape your beard to your desired length.

Extended Goatee

An extended goatee is a popular beard style that works well for guys with a round face and bald head. This style features a small beard on the chin that extends down to the jawline. To achieve an extended goatee, let your facial hair grow for a few weeks and then trim it down using a beard trimmer. You can also use scissors to shape your beard and create a narrow, elongated look.

Soul Patch

A soul patch is a small patch of hair located just below the lower lip. This style is simple and easy to maintain, making it a great option for guys with a round face and bald head. To achieve a soul patch, simply let your facial hair grow for a few days and then shave everything except the patch of hair just below your lower lip.

Van Dyke

A Van Dyke is a beard style that features a mustache and a small beard on the chin. This style is narrow and elongated, making it a great option for guys with a round face and bald head. To achieve a Van Dyke, let your facial hair grow for a few weeks and then trim it down using a beard trimmer. You can also use scissors to shape your beard and create a narrow, elongated look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Growing a Beard

Growing a beard requires patience and dedication. Here are some steps you can follow to grow a beard that complements your round face and bald head:

  1. Stop shaving and let your facial hair grow for at least a few weeks.
  2. Use a beard trimmer or scissors to trim your beard to your desired length.
  3. Use a comb or brush to shape your beard and remove any tangles or knots.
  4. Apply beard oil or balm to keep your beard moisturized and healthy.
  5. Trim your beard regularly to maintain its shape and length.

Tips for Maintaining Your Beard

Maintaining a beard requires more than just trimming and shaping. Here are some tips to keep your beard looking and feeling its best:

  • Wash your beard regularly with a beard shampoo to remove dirt and oil.
  • Apply beard oil or balm to keep your beard moisturized and healthy.
  • Use a beard comb or brush to remove tangles and knots.
  • Trim your beard regularly to maintain its shape and length.
  • Avoid using a razor to shave your beard, as this can cause irritation and ingrown hairs.

FAQs

What beard styles are best for a round face and bald head?

Some of the best beard styles for a round face and bald head include stubble, short beard, extended goatee, soul patch, and Van Dyke.

How long does it take to grow a beard?

It can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months to grow a full beard, depending on your genetics and personal grooming habits.

How do I maintain my beard?

To maintain your beard, you should wash it regularly with a beard shampoo, apply beard oil or balm to keep it moisturized, and trim it regularly to maintain its shape and length.

Hair Care Tips for Bald Heads

If you have a bald head, it's important to take care of your scalp to prevent dryness and irritation. Here are some hair care tips for bald heads:

  • Wash your scalp regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Apply sunscreen to your scalp when spending time outdoors.
  • Moisturize your scalp with a scalp balm or oil.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.

By following these hair care tips and choosing the right beard style, you can rock a bald head and a confident beard.
