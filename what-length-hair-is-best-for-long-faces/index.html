What Length Hair Is Best For Long Faces

Written by Daniel Jun 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a long face, you may be wondering what length hair is best for your face shape. The right hairstyle can help balance out the features of a long face and make it appear more proportional. In this article, we'll discuss the best length hair for long faces, as well as some tips for styling and caring for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Length Hair for Long Faces
  • Styling Tips for Long Faces
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best length hair for long faces, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and highlights your best assets.

There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong (or long). To determine your face shape, take a close-up selfie of your face and trace the outline with a marker. Then, compare the shape to the six options and choose the one that matches best.

Best Length Hair for Long Faces

When it comes to long faces, the best length hair is typically shoulder-length or longer. This helps to elongate the face and create the illusion of more width. Long layers can also help add volume and movement to the hair, which can balance out the features of a long face.

If you prefer shorter hair, a bob that hits at the chin or above can work well for long faces. However, it's important to add some texture and volume to the hair with layers or waves to prevent the face from appearing too long and narrow.

Other Hairstyles to Consider

In addition to length, there are a few other hairstyles that can work well for long faces:

  • Side-swept bangs: These can help break up the length of the face and create a more balanced look.
  • Messy updos: These can add volume and texture to the hair, which can help balance out the features of a long face.
  • Loose waves: These can add volume and movement to the hair, which can create the illusion of more width.

Styling Tips for Long Faces

When styling your hair, there are a few tips to keep in mind to flatter a long face:

  • Avoid center parts: These can elongate the face even further. Instead, opt for a side part.
  • Add volume to the crown: This can help balance out the length of the face.
  • Avoid straight, sleek styles: These can make the face appear even longer. Instead, add some texture and volume to the hair.

FAQs

What if I have a long face and thin hair?

If you have a long face and thin hair, it's important to add volume and texture to the hair. Layers and waves can help create the illusion of thicker, fuller hair.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a long face?

A low ponytail or messy bun can work well for long faces. However, avoid slicked-back styles that can make the face appear even longer.

What if I have a long face and curly hair?

If you have a long face and curly hair, embrace your natural texture. Long layers can help add volume and movement to the hair, which can balance out the features of a long face.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

If you have long hair, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair gently.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Deep condition hair once a week to keep it nourished.
  • Trim hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By choosing the right length hair and styling it properly, you can flatter a long face and create a balanced, proportional look. Whether you opt for long layers, a bob, or loose waves, embrace your unique features and rock your new hairstyle with confidence.
