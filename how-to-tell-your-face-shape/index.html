How To Tell Your Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Knowing your face shape is important when it comes to choosing hairstyles, glasses, and makeup. It can help enhance your natural features and make you feel more confident. In this article, we will show you how to tell your face shape and provide tips on how to style your hair accordingly.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tell Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

How to Tell Your Face Shape

The first step to determining your face shape is to pull your hair back and stand in front of a mirror. Look for the following characteristics:

  • The width of your forehead
  • The length of your face
  • The shape of your jawline
  • The width of your cheekbones

After identifying these features, you can use them to determine your face shape.

Face Shapes

There are six main face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

Each face shape has its own unique characteristics and proportions.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to determine your face shape:

  1. Measure the width of your forehead with a tape measure.
  2. Measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.
  3. Measure the width of your cheekbones.
  4. Measure the width of your jawline.
  5. Use these measurements to determine your face shape.

Alternatively, you can try the following:

  1. Take a selfie with your hair pulled back.
  2. Trace the outline of your face on the photo.
  3. Use a ruler to measure the length and width of your face.
  4. Compare your measurements to the characteristics of each face shape.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you have determined your face shape, you can use the following tips to style your hair:

  • Oval: Almost any hairstyle works with this face shape.
  • Round: Add volume and height to elongate the face.
  • Square: Soften the jawline with textured layers.
  • Heart: Add volume to the top of the head to balance the face.
  • Diamond: Create the illusion of a shorter face with layers.
  • Triangle: Add volume to the crown of the head to balance the face.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about determining face shape:

How do I measure my face shape?

Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, as well as the length of your face. Use these measurements to determine your face shape.

What is the most common face shape?

The most common face shape is oval.

Can face shape change over time?

No, your face shape remains the same throughout your life.

Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

Here are some hair care tips for each face shape:

  • Oval: You can pull off almost any hairstyle, so experiment with different looks.
  • Round: Keep hair off the face to elongate the face shape.
  • Square: Soften the jawline with textured layers and fringes.
  • Heart: Add volume to the top of the head to balance the face.
  • Diamond: Create the illusion of a shorter face with layers.
  • Triangle: Add volume to the crown of the head to balance the face.

Remember, your face shape is just one factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. You should also take into account your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style.

Now that you know how to tell your face shape, you can choose hairstyles and makeup that flatter your natural features. Experiment with different looks and have fun!
