How To Cut Women s Curly Hair Short

Written by Frank Nov 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to switch up your look and chop off those curly locks? Cutting curly hair can be intimidating, but don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll go through the steps of how to cut women's curly hair short. From how to section the hair to tips for maintaining your new cut, read on to learn everything you need to know.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Section the Hair
  2. How to Cut the Hair
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your Short Curly Hair
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair

How to Section the Hair

The first step in cutting curly hair is sectioning it. To do this, start by parting your hair down the middle and then into four sections, two in the front and two in the back. You can use hair clips to hold each section in place. Make sure that the sections are even so that your haircut is balanced.

Once you have the sections, you can start to work on each one individually. Start with the back sections and work your way to the front. This will ensure that the back of your hair is cut evenly and that the front layers are longer.

How to Cut the Hair

Now that your hair is sectioned, it's time to start cutting. If you're not confident in your cutting abilities, it's always best to go to a professional. However, if you're feeling brave, here are the steps to follow:

  1. Start by wetting your hair. Curly hair tends to shrink when it dries, so it's important to cut it when it's wet to get an accurate length.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the back and comb it out. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle. This will create layers and add texture to your hair.
  3. Continue to cut small sections of hair at an angle, working your way around your head. Remember to cut each section evenly.
  4. Once you've finished cutting the back sections, move on to the front. Again, take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle.
  5. Once you've finished cutting the front, check to make sure that both sides are even. If they're not, make small adjustments until they are.
  6. Finally, trim any flyaways or uneven pieces of hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Curly Hair

Cutting your curly hair short can be a big change, but it's important to know how to maintain your new cut. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Invest in a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

FAQ

Can I cut my curly hair short myself?

While it's possible to cut your own curly hair, it's always best to go to a professional. They have the experience and training to cut your hair in a way that will flatter your face shape and hair type.

What is the best way to cut curly hair?

The best way to cut curly hair is to do it when it's wet and to cut it at an angle. This will create layers and add texture to your hair.

How often should I trim my curly hair?

It's recommended that you trim your curly hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair

Keeping your short curly hair healthy and shiny requires some extra care. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.

Now that you know how to cut women's curly hair short, it's time to book that appointment or grab those scissors and get to work. Remember to take your time, follow the steps, and have fun with your new look!
