What Would I Look Like With Short Blonde Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you've been considering chopping off your locks and going blonde, you might be wondering, "what would I look like with short blonde hair?" It's a valid question, and one that can be tough to answer without actually making the change. But fear not! We've put together a complete guide on what to expect when going short and blonde. So let's dive in!

Description

Short blonde hair is a popular trend that is perfect for those looking for a fresh new look. Short haircuts are perfect for those who want a low-maintenance style that still looks chic and stylish. Blonde hair, on the other hand, is a great way to add some brightness and lightness to your look. When combined, short blonde hair can create a fun and playful vibe that's perfect for any occasion.

How to

If you're ready to take the plunge and go short and blonde, there are a few things you should know. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look.

Step 1: Choose the Right Shade of Blonde

Before you go blonde, you'll need to decide on the perfect shade for your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, go for a honey or caramel blonde. If you have cool undertones, try a platinum or ash blonde.

Step 2: Find the Right Short Haircut

When it comes to short haircuts, there are plenty of options to choose from. A pixie cut is a classic choice that's perfect for those who want a bold, edgy look. A bob is another great option that's versatile and easy to style. Talk to your stylist about which cut will work best for your face shape and lifestyle.

Step 3: Take Care of Your New Hair

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but that doesn't mean you can completely neglect it. To keep your new blonde locks looking their best, make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you make the most of your new short blonde hair:

  • Experiment with different styling products to create different looks
  • Accessorize with bold earrings and statement necklaces to draw attention to your face
  • Don't be afraid to add some texture to your hair with a texturizing spray or pomade

FAQ

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the style you choose. A pixie cut can make your face appear rounder, while a bob can elongate your face.

Q: How often will I need to touch up my roots?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how noticeable your roots are. Typically, you'll need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

To keep your short blonde hair looking its best, make sure to follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage

So there you have it! A complete guide on what to expect when going short and blonde. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful. So go ahead and take the plunge!
