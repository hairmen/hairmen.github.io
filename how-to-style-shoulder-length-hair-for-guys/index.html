How To Style Shoulder Length Hair For Guys

Written by Christine Jul 26, 2023 · 5 min read

Shoulder length hair for guys is becoming popular nowadays. Whether you’re growing your hair out or you’ve been blessed with naturally long locks, there are plenty of ways to style your shoulder length hair as a guy. In this article, we’ll provide tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice on how to style shoulder length hair for guys.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Shoulder Length Hair for Guys
  2. Step-by-Step Instructions
  3. Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair for Guys

There are many ways to style shoulder length hair for guys, but here are a few popular options:

  • Slicked back
  • Messy and textured
  • Half-up half-down
  • Ponytail
  • Bun

Before you start styling, it’s important to have clean and well-moisturized hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that works well for your hair type, and consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Slicked Back

The slicked back look is a classic hairstyle that works well for shoulder length hair. Here’s how to achieve it:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair, focusing on the roots and the top of your head.
  3. Use a comb to slick your hair back, starting from the front and working your way back.
  4. Smooth out any bumps or flyaways with your comb.
  5. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep everything in place.

This look works well for formal events or for a polished and put-together look.

Messy and Textured

If you want a more casual and relaxed look, try a messy and textured style:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of texturizing spray or sea salt spray to your hair.
  3. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair and create texture.
  4. Use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to dry your hair while scrunching it.
  5. Use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to define any pieces or add more texture as needed.

This look works well for everyday wear or for a more laid-back vibe.

Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down style is a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still showing off your length:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Take the top section of your hair and pull it back, securing it with a hair tie or clip.
  3. Leave the rest of your hair down.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.
  5. Optional: add a hair accessory like a headband or bandana for a fun and stylish touch.

This look works well for both casual and formal events.

Ponytail

A ponytail is a simple and classic hairstyle that works well for shoulder length hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Optional: use a comb or your fingers to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.
  4. Optional: leave a few face-framing pieces out for a softer look.

This look works well for everyday wear or for working out.

Bun

A bun is a stylish and practical way to keep your hair out of your face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Twist your ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Optional: use a comb or your fingers to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

This look works well for formal events or for a polished and sleek look.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are some general step-by-step instructions for styling shoulder length hair for guys:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Choose a hairstyle that works well for your hair type and the occasion.
  3. Apply any necessary products (e.g. gel, wax, texturizing spray) to your hair.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired.
  5. Smooth out any bumps or flyaways as needed.
  6. Finish with a light mist of hairspray or other finishing product to keep everything in place.

Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some tips to make styling your shoulder length hair easier:

  • Invest in high-quality hair products that work well for your hair type.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you.
  • Consider visiting a professional stylist for a consultation or to get a haircut that’s tailored to your face shape and hair type.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can guys have shoulder length hair?

Absolutely! Many guys are choosing to grow out their hair and embrace shoulder length locks.

What products should I use for styling shoulder length hair?

The products you use will depend on your hair type and the style you’re going for. Some popular options include hair gel, pomade, wax, texturizing spray, and sea salt spray.

How often should I wash my shoulder length hair?

It’s generally recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days, but this can vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

In addition to styling your shoulder length hair, it’s important to take care of it to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools (e.g. blow dryer, flat iron) too frequently, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and trying out different hairstyles and products, you can rock your shoulder length hair with confidence and style.
