How To Take Care Of Straightened Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jun 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Straightened hair looks sleek and gorgeous, but it can be challenging to maintain. Without proper care, straightened hair can become dry, brittle, and prone to breakage. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks for taking care of straightened hair, so you can keep your locks looking healthy and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Straighten Your Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Taking Care of Straightened Hair
  3. Tips for Maintaining Straightened Hair

How to Straighten Your Hair

Straightening your hair can be done in several ways. The most common methods are:

  • Using a straightening iron
  • Using a blow dryer and a round brush
  • Using a chemical relaxer or keratin treatment

Regardless of the method you choose, it’s important to use a heat protectant spray or serum to prevent damage to your hair. You should also avoid straightening your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Step-by-Step Guide to Taking Care of Straightened Hair

Now that you’ve straightened your hair, it’s time to take care of it. Follow these steps to keep your locks looking healthy and beautiful:

  1. Wash your hair: Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for straightened hair. Avoid using hot water, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  2. Dry your hair: Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to blot your hair dry. Avoid rubbing your hair with a regular towel, as this can cause frizz.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner: This will help to detangle your hair and add moisture.
  4. Use a wide-tooth comb: This will help to prevent breakage and damage. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots.
  5. Avoid heat styling: Try to limit your use of hot tools, as this can cause damage and breakage. If you must use a straightening iron, use a low heat setting and apply a heat protectant spray or serum.
  6. Avoid tight hairstyles: Tight hairstyles, such as braids and ponytails, can cause damage and breakage. If you must wear your hair up, use a scrunchie or a hair tie that’s designed to prevent breakage.
  7. Protect your hair at night: Use a satin or silk pillowcase, or wrap your hair in a silk scarf, to prevent breakage and frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Straightened Hair

Here are some additional tips for maintaining straightened hair:

  • Get regular trims: This will help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals: Chemical treatments, such as hair dye and relaxers, can cause damage and breakage. If you must use these products, make sure to use a deep conditioner afterwards.
  • Stay hydrated: Drinking plenty of water can help to keep your hair and skin looking healthy.
  • Eat a healthy diet: Foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals, such as leafy greens, nuts, and fish, can help to promote healthy hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I wash my straightened hair?

A: You should wash your hair every two to three days. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can cause dryness and breakage.

Q: Can I still use hair oil on straightened hair?

A: Yes, you can use hair oil on straightened hair. Just make sure to apply it sparingly, and avoid applying it to your roots, as this can cause greasiness.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my straightened hair?

A: To prevent frizz, make sure to use a heat protectant spray or serum before using hot tools. You can also apply a smoothing serum or cream to your hair after styling.

Essential Hair Care Products for Straightened Hair

Here are some essential hair care products for maintaining straightened hair:

  • Gentle shampoo and conditioner: Look for products that are designed for straightened hair.
  • Leave-in conditioner: This will help to detangle your hair and add moisture.
  • Heat protectant spray or serum: This will help to prevent damage from hot tools.
  • Smoothing serum or cream: This will help to prevent frizz and add shine.
  • Wide-tooth comb: This will help to prevent breakage and damage.
  • Satin or silk pillowcase: This will help to prevent breakage and frizz.

By following these tips and using the right hair care products, you can keep your straightened hair looking healthy and beautiful. Remember to be gentle with your hair, avoid harsh chemicals, and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.
