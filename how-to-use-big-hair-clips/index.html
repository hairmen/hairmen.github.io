How To Use Big Hair Clips A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Big hair clips are a great accessory for those with long, thick hair. They can help keep your hair in place and add a touch of style to your look. However, if you're new to using big hair clips, you may be unsure of how to use them properly. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about using big hair clips.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Use Big Hair Clips
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Using Big Hair Clips
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Use Big Hair Clips

Using big hair clips is easy, but there are a few things to keep in mind to ensure they work properly. Here are some tips:

  • Choose the right size: Big hair clips come in different sizes, so make sure you choose one that's appropriate for your hair length and thickness.
  • Place them correctly: Big hair clips work best when placed at the crown of your head or at the back of your head.
  • Use them on dry hair: Big hair clips work best on dry hair, so avoid using them on wet hair.
  • Secure them tightly: To ensure your hair stays in place, make sure to secure the big hair clip tightly.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics of using big hair clips, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Choose the right size big hair clip for your hair.
  3. Section your hair: Divide your hair into sections, starting at the crown of your head or the back of your head.
  4. Clip your hair: Take a section of hair and twist it slightly. Then, place the big hair clip over the twisted section and secure tightly.
  5. Repeat: Continue to section your hair and clip it until you've achieved the desired look.

Tips for Using Big Hair Clips

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your big hair clips:

  • Experiment with different styles: Big hair clips can be used in a variety of ways, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and placements.
  • Accessorize: Big hair clips come in a variety of styles, so choose one that complements your outfit and adds a touch of flair.
  • Don't overdo it: While big hair clips can be a great accessory, using too many can look overwhelming. Stick to one or two for a more subtle look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about using big hair clips:

Q: Can I use big hair clips on wet hair?

A: No, big hair clips work best on dry hair. Using them on wet hair can cause damage and may not hold your hair in place.

Q: How do I clean my big hair clips?

A: To clean your big hair clips, simply wipe them down with a damp cloth. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive materials that can damage the clip.

Hair Care Tips

Using big hair clips can be a great way to style your hair, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye, too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using big hair clips properly, you can achieve a stylish and healthy look for your hair. Happy styling!
