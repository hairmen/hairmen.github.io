Haircuts For Fine Straight Hair 2020 How To Get The Perfect Cut

Written by Petter Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have fine straight hair, you know how challenging it can be to find the perfect haircut that complements your hair type. However, with the right haircut, you can make your locks look fuller and thicker. In this blog post, we will discuss the most popular haircuts for fine straight hair 2020 and provide you with tips on how to get the perfect cut for you.

Description

Fine straight hair is delicate and requires a haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair. A good haircut can make your hair look thicker, healthier and bouncier. In 2020, there were several haircuts that were popular among women with fine straight hair. These haircuts were versatile, easy to style and added volume to the hair.

How to Get the Perfect Haircut for Fine Straight Hair

To get the perfect haircut for your fine straight hair, you need to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. The right haircut should complement your face shape and enhance your natural features. Here are some tips to help you get the perfect haircut:

Step 1: Research

Before getting a haircut, research different hairstyles that are suitable for fine straight hair. Look for pictures of celebrities or models who have similar hair type and face shape as you. You can also ask your hairstylist for recommendations.

Step 2: Consultation

Book a consultation with your hairstylist to discuss the best haircut for you. Your hairstylist will consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle before recommending a haircut. They will also advise you on how to style and maintain your new haircut.

Step 3: Maintenance

After getting a haircut, it is important to maintain it to keep it looking fresh and healthy. Invest in good quality hair products that are suitable for fine straight hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner that adds volume and texture to your hair. Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.

Tips for Haircuts for Fine Straight Hair

Here are some tips to help you choose the best haircut for your fine straight hair:

Tip 1: Layered Haircuts

Layered haircuts are perfect for fine straight hair as they add volume and texture to the hair. Layers create movement in the hair and make it look thicker and fuller. You can opt for long layers or short layers depending on your hair length.

Tip 2: Bob Haircuts

Bob haircuts are versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. A bob haircut that is chin-length or shorter can add volume to your hair and make it look thicker. You can also add layers to your bob haircut to create more texture and movement.

Tip 3: Pixie Haircuts

Pixie haircuts are short and edgy, and they are perfect for fine straight hair. A pixie haircut can make your hair look thicker and fuller, and it is low maintenance. You can also add bangs to your pixie haircut to frame your face.

FAQs

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on your hair growth and the length of your hair. On average, you should get a haircut every 6 to 8 weeks to maintain your haircut's shape and style.

Q: Can I color my fine straight hair?

A: Yes, you can color your fine straight hair. However, it is important to use hair color products that are suitable for fine hair. Avoid using harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

Q: How can I add volume to my fine straight hair?

A: You can add volume to your fine straight hair by using volumizing hair products, blow-drying your hair upside down, and using a round brush to lift your hair at the roots.

Hair Care for Fine Straight Hair

To keep your fine straight hair healthy and shiny, you need to follow a hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you take care of your fine straight hair:

Tip 1: Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner that adds volume and texture to your hair. These products can make your hair look thicker and fuller.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can damage your fine straight hair. Avoid using them as much as possible, or use them on a low heat setting.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun's UV rays can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. Protect your hair by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF.

By following these tips and getting the right haircut, you can make your fine straight hair look voluminous and bouncy. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect haircut for you and maintain it with proper hair care.
