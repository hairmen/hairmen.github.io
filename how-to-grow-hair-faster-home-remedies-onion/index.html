How to Use Onion for Hair Growth

Onion is a natural source of sulfur, which helps in the production of collagen. Collagen is one of the essential proteins that promote hair growth. When applied to the hair, onion juice can help to strengthen hair follicles, reduce hair fall, and promote hair growth. Here are some ways to use onion for hair growth:

Onion Juice Hair Mask

To make an onion juice hair mask, you will need:

  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of honey

Blend the onion in a blender until it turns into a smooth paste. Add olive oil and honey to the onion paste and mix well. Apply the mixture to your hair and leave it on for 30 minutes. Rinse your hair with a mild shampoo.

Onion Juice and Coconut Oil Hair Mask

To make an onion juice and coconut oil hair mask, you will need:

  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons of coconut oil

Blend the onion in a blender until it turns into a smooth paste. Mix the onion paste with coconut oil and apply the mixture to your hair. Leave it on for 30 minutes and then rinse your hair with a mild shampoo.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to use onion for hair growth:

  1. Peel and chop one onion.
  2. Blend the onion in a blender until it turns into a smooth paste.
  3. Squeeze the juice out of the onion paste using a cheesecloth or a fine strainer.
  4. Apply the onion juice to your scalp and hair.
  5. Massage your scalp with your fingertips for 5 minutes.
  6. Leave the onion juice on your hair for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  7. Rinse your hair with a mild shampoo and cool water.

Tips for Using Onion for Hair Growth

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using onion for hair growth:

  • Always do a patch test before using onion on your hair to avoid any allergic reactions.
  • Add essential oils such as lavender, rosemary, or peppermint to the onion juice to reduce the strong smell.
  • Apply the onion juice to your hair once a week for best results.
  • Use a mild shampoo to rinse your hair after applying onion juice to avoid scalp irritation.
  • Avoid using onion juice on your hair if you have any scalp infections or wounds.

FAQs

Does onion juice really help in hair growth?

Yes, onion juice is rich in sulfur, which helps in the production of collagen. Collagen is one of the essential proteins that promote hair growth.

How long should I leave onion juice on my hair?

You can leave onion juice on your hair for 30 minutes to 1 hour. Rinse your hair with a mild shampoo after that.

Can I add other ingredients to onion juice for hair growth?

Yes, you can add other ingredients such as coconut oil, olive oil, honey, or essential oils to onion juice for hair growth.

Hair Care Tips for Faster Hair Growth

Here are some hair care tips to promote faster hair growth:

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in proteins, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Massage your scalp with essential oils such as lavender, rosemary, or peppermint.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools such as straighteners and curling irons.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Avoid washing your hair with hot water.
