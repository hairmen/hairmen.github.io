What Colour Hair Would Suit Me Buzzfeed Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Aug 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to change up your hair colour, you're probably wondering what colour hair would suit you. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. That's where Buzzfeed comes in! In this guide, we'll show you how to determine what colour hair would suit you, according to Buzzfeed.

Description

Buzzfeed is a popular website that offers quizzes and articles on a variety of topics, including beauty and fashion. They have a quiz that can help you determine what hair colour would suit you best. By answering a few simple questions, the quiz can give you a personalized recommendation for your hair colour.

How to

The first step in determining what hair colour would suit you is to take the Buzzfeed quiz. The quiz will ask you questions about your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour. It will also ask about your style and personality. Once you've completed the quiz, you'll receive a recommendation for your hair colour.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to take the Buzzfeed quiz:

  1. Go to the Buzzfeed website and search for the "What Colour Hair Should You Have?" quiz
  2. Click on the quiz and start answering the questions
  3. Be honest with your answers and choose the option that best describes you
  4. Once you've completed the quiz, you'll receive a recommendation for your hair colour

Tips

If you're not sure about the recommendation you received from the Buzzfeed quiz, here are some tips to help you choose the right hair colour:

  • Consider your skin tone - if you have warm undertones, consider a warm hair colour like golden blonde or copper. If you have cool undertones, consider a cool hair colour like ash blonde or platinum.
  • Think about your lifestyle - if you're low-maintenance, consider a hair colour that doesn't require a lot of upkeep. If you're willing to put in the work, consider a more high-maintenance hair colour.
  • Look at your natural hair colour - if you want a subtle change, consider a hair colour that's close to your natural shade. If you want a drastic change, consider a hair colour that's completely different from your natural shade.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what hair colour would suit you:

Q: Will a certain hair colour make me look younger?

A: It's possible! Generally, lighter hair colours can make you look younger, while darker hair colours can make you look older. However, this isn't a hard and fast rule - it depends on your individual features and skin tone.

Q: Can I dye my hair a colour that's completely different from my natural shade?

A: Yes, you can! Just keep in mind that if you're going for a drastic change, you may need multiple appointments to achieve the desired result. You should also be prepared for some upkeep - if you dye your hair a bright colour, for example, you may need to touch up your roots more frequently.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen a hair colour, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the colour
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can also damage the colour
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized

With these tips, you'll be able to keep your hair looking its best!

So there you have it - everything you need to know about what colour hair would suit you, according to Buzzfeed. Whether you're looking for a subtle change or a drastic transformation, there's a hair colour out there that's perfect for you!
