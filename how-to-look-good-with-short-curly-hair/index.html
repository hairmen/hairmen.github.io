How to Look Good with Short Curly Hair - Tips and Tricks

If you have short curly hair, you may think that your styling options are limited. However, that couldn't be further from the truth! With the right techniques and products, you can rock your curls and look fabulous. In this article, we'll walk you through how to look good with short curly hair, step by step.

Table of Contents

  1. Hair Care
  2. How To Style Short Curly Hair
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Expert Tips
  5. FAQs

Hair Care

Before we get into styling, it's important to establish a good hair care routine. Curly hair tends to be dry and prone to breakage, so you'll want to take extra care to keep your curls healthy and hydrated. Here are some tips:

1. Shampoo less often

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so you don't want to strip it of its natural oils by shampooing too often. Aim to wash your hair no more than twice a week, and use a sulfate-free shampoo that won't dry out your curls.

2. Condition, condition, condition

Conditioner is your friend when you have curly hair. Use a thick, moisturizing conditioner after every shampoo, and consider using a leave-in conditioner as well. This will help to keep your curls soft and hydrated.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle

Curly hair is prone to tangling, so you'll need to be careful when detangling. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently remove tangles, starting at the ends and working your way up.

4. Sleep on a silk pillowcase

Cotton pillowcases can cause friction and breakage, so consider investing in a silk pillowcase. This will help to keep your curls smooth and frizz-free while you sleep.

How To Style Short Curly Hair

Now that you have a good hair care routine in place, it's time to start styling your curls. Here are some tips:

1. Start with wet hair

Curly hair is easiest to style when it's wet, so start by washing and conditioning your hair.

2. Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or gel, to your wet hair. This will help to define your curls and prevent frizz.

3. Use a diffuser

A diffuser is a great tool for styling curly hair. Attach it to your blow dryer and use it to dry your curls, using a low heat and speed setting. This will help to keep your curls intact and reduce frizz.

4. Finish with a hairspray

Once your curls are dry, finish with a light hold hairspray to keep them in place. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look limp.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to styling short curly hair:

1. Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and moisturizing conditioner.

2. Apply a styling product

Apply a curl cream or gel to your wet hair, working it through from root to tip.

3. Use a diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and use it to dry your curls, using a low heat and speed setting.

4. Finish with a hairspray

Once your curls are dry, finish with a light hold hairspray to keep them in place.

Expert Tips

Here are some expert tips for styling short curly hair:

1. Don't touch your curls too much

Touching your curls too much can cause frizz and disrupt their shape, so try to keep your hands off them as much as possible.

2. Embrace the messiness

Short curly hair is naturally messy and voluminous, so embrace it! Don't try to tame your curls too much, as this can make them look flat and lifeless.

3. Experiment with different products

Every curly head is different, so it may take some experimentation to find the right products for your hair. Try different curl creams, gels, and mousses until you find the ones that work best for you.

FAQs

1. How often should I wash my short curly hair?

Aim to wash your hair no more than twice a week, and use a sulfate-free shampoo that won't dry out your curls.

2. How can I prevent frizz?

Use a styling product, such as a curl cream or gel, to define your curls and prevent frizz. Avoid touching your curls too much, as this can cause frizz.

3. Can I straighten my short curly hair?

You can straighten your short curly hair, but be aware that this can cause damage and breakage. If you do choose to straighten your hair, use a heat protectant and a low heat setting.

There you have it - our top tips for how to look good with short curly hair. Remember, the key is to embrace your curls and work with them, rather than trying to fight against them. With the right hair care routine and styling techniques, you'll be rocking your curls in no time!
