What Hairstyle Matches My Face

Written by Oliver Oct 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle that suits your face can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it's easy to get overwhelmed. However, finding the perfect hairstyle that complements your facial features is essential to enhance your overall appearance. In this article, we will guide you on how to determine what hairstyle matches your face.

Description

Facial shape plays a crucial role in determining what hairstyle suits you best. If you have a round face, you should avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides, as they will only make your face look rounder. Similarly, if you have a square face, you should avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top, as they will only accentuate your jawline.

On the other hand, if you have an oval face, you are lucky, as almost any hairstyle will suit you. However, it's essential to keep in mind your hair texture, thickness, and personal style before choosing a hairstyle.

How to Determine What Hairstyle Matches Your Face?

To determine what hairstyle matches your face, you need to know your facial shape. Follow these simple steps to determine your facial shape:

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Take a measuring tape and measure the following dimensions of your face:

  • Forehead: Measure from the hairline to the top of your eyebrows
  • Cheekbones: Measure across your cheekbones, starting and ending at the pointiest part below the outer corner of each eye
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jaw angles upward. Multiply that number by two to get your jawline measurement.
  • Face Length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Step 2: Determine Your Facial Shape

Once you have your measurements, compare them to the following facial shapes:

  • Round: Your face length and cheekbones are roughly equal, and your jawline is soft and less defined.
  • Square: Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly equal in width.
  • Oval: Your face length is greater than the width of your cheekbones, and your forehead is wider than your jawline.
  • Heart: Your forehead is wider than your cheekbones, and your chin tapers to a point.
  • Diamond: Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrow.
  • Pear: Your jawline is wider than your forehead, and your chin is more pointed.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Now that you know your facial shape, here are some tips for choosing the right hairstyle:

  • Round Face: Go for hairstyles that add height to your crown while keeping the sides sleek. Long layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and high ponytails will work best for you.
  • Square Face: Soften your jawline with hairstyles that add volume to the top. Medium length haircuts, side-parted cuts, and soft waves will suit you best.
  • Oval Face: Almost any hairstyle will suit you, but avoid haircuts that cover your forehead. Go for long layers, blunt bobs, and side-swept bangs to enhance your facial features.
  • Heart Face: Soften your forehead with hairstyles that add volume to the bottom. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and chin-length bobs will suit you best.
  • Diamond Face: Create width at your forehead and chin with hairstyles that add volume to the sides. Pixie cuts, long bobs, and side-swept bangs will suit you best.
  • Pear Face: Draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones with hairstyles that add volume to the top. Layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and bouncy curls will suit you best.

FAQ

What if I have a combination of facial shapes?

If you have a combination of facial shapes, choose a hairstyle that complements the dominant feature of your face. For example, if you have a round face with a square jawline, opt for a hairstyle that softens your jawline while keeping the sides sleek.

Can hair color affect the way my face looks?

Yes, hair color can enhance or detract from your facial features. For example, highlights and balayage can add depth and dimension to your facial features, while dark colors can make your face look smaller.

Hair Care

Choosing the right hairstyle is just the first step. To ensure that your hair looks its best, you need to take proper care of it. Follow these tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and make it brittle.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your hair looks its best, no matter what hairstyle you choose.
