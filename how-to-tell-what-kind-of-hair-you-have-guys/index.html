How To Tell What Kind Of Hair You Have Guys

Written by Daniel Oct 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you unsure about what kind of hair you have? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many guys struggle to identify their hair type, which can make it difficult to choose the right products and styling techniques. This guide will help you determine your hair type and provide some tips for taking care of it.

Table of Contents

  • How to Identify Your Hair Type
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

How to Identify Your Hair Type

The first step in determining your hair type is to understand the different categories. There are three main types of hair: straight, wavy, and curly. Within each of these categories, there are subcategories that describe the texture and thickness of your hair.

Straight hair is the easiest to identify. It is characterized by a lack of curl or wave and tends to be shiny and smooth. Wavy hair has a slight curl or wave and is often thicker than straight hair. Curly hair has a more pronounced curl or wave and can be thick or fine.

Once you've determined your hair type, you can further classify it based on texture and thickness. Texture refers to the diameter of each individual hair strand, which can range from fine to coarse. Thickness refers to the overall amount of hair on your head, which can range from thin to thick.

Texture

Fine hair is delicate and prone to breakage, but it is also easy to style and can be manipulated into different shapes. Coarse hair is stronger and more resilient, but it can be difficult to style and may require more effort to manage.

Thickness

Thin hair is often referred to as "fine" hair and is characterized by a lack of volume and body. Thick hair, on the other hand, is full and dense and can be difficult to style or manage.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to identify your hair type:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as you normally would.
  2. Allow your hair to air dry without using any styling products.
  3. Observe the natural texture and shape of your hair.
  4. Determine whether your hair is straight, wavy, or curly.
  5. Assess the texture and thickness of your hair.

It's important to note that your hair type may change depending on factors such as humidity, heat styling, and chemical treatments. If you're unsure about your hair type, consult with a stylist or barber for professional advice.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've identified your hair type, it's important to use products and styling techniques that are appropriate for your hair's unique needs. Here are some tips for taking care of each hair type:

Straight Hair

  • Use a lightweight shampoo and conditioner to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Avoid heavy styling products, which can make your hair look greasy or flat.
  • Consider using a flat iron or straightening brush to create sleek styles.

Wavy Hair

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to enhance your hair's natural wave pattern.
  • Avoid using a brush, which can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Consider using a sea salt spray or curl-enhancing product to define your waves.

Curly Hair

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, which can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Consider using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls.

FAQs

What if I have a combination of hair types?

It's not uncommon to have a combination of hair types, such as wavy hair with some curls. In this case, focus on the dominant hair type and use products and techniques that work for that type.

What if I'm still unsure about my hair type?

If you're still unsure about your hair type, consult with a stylist or barber for professional advice. They can help you determine your hair type and recommend products and techniques that are right for you.

How often should I wash my hair?

The frequency with which you should wash your hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. Generally, it's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.

By following these tips, you can determine your hair type and take proper care of your hair. Remember to embrace your unique texture and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
