Can Hair Color Make You Look Thinner

Written by Luffy Jun 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of carrying around extra weight and looking for a quick fix? Did you know that changing your hair color might help you look thinner? In this article, we'll explore whether or not hair color can make you look thinner and provide some tips on how to achieve the look.

Description

Many people believe that hair color can make you look thinner. The idea behind this theory is that lighter hair colors reflect more light, making your face look brighter and more youthful. Additionally, lighter hair colors can create the illusion of more volume, which can help to balance out a rounder face shape.

How to

If you're interested in trying out a new hair color to help you look thinner, there are a few things you can do to make sure you get the best results:

1. Choose the right shade

When it comes to hair color, not all shades are created equal. To achieve the illusion of a slimmer face, you'll want to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and brings out the best in your features. Some good options include honey blonde, caramel, and light brown.

2. Consider highlights

If you're not ready to commit to a full-color change, highlights can be a great way to add some brightness and dimension to your hair. Consider getting highlights around your face to draw attention to your best features and create the illusion of a slimmer face.

3. Consult a professional

While there are plenty of DIY hair coloring options available, it's always a good idea to consult a professional stylist before making any major changes to your hair. A professional can help you choose the right shade and technique to achieve the results you're looking for.

Step by Step

If you're ready to take the plunge and try a new hair color to help you look thinner, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

1. Choose your shade

Start by choosing the right shade for your skin tone and features. Consider consulting with a professional stylist to help you make the best choice.

2. Prepare your hair

Before coloring your hair, make sure it's in good condition. Use a deep conditioning treatment to help strengthen and moisturize your hair.

3. Apply the color

Follow the instructions on your hair color kit to apply the color evenly throughout your hair. If you're unsure about the process, consider asking a friend or family member to help you.

4. Rinse and style

Once the color has set, rinse your hair thoroughly and style as desired. Consider adding some volume around your face to help create the illusion of a slimmer face shape.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you get the most out of your new hair color:

1. Embrace your new look

Changing your hair color can be a big step, but it can also be a lot of fun. Embrace your new look and have fun experimenting with different styles and products.

2. Maintain your color

To keep your new hair color looking its best, make sure to use color-safe products and avoid over-washing your hair. Consider getting touch-ups every few weeks to keep your color looking fresh.

3. Be patient

It may take a few days or even weeks for you to fully adjust to your new hair color. Be patient and give yourself time to get used to your new look.

FAQ

Q: Will any hair color make me look thinner?

A: Not necessarily. The key is to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and features. Some good options include honey blonde, caramel, and light brown.

Q: Do I need to go to a salon to get my hair colored?

A: While there are plenty of DIY hair coloring options available, it's always a good idea to consult a professional stylist before making any major changes to your hair.

Q: How long will my new hair color last?

A: The longevity of your hair color will depend on a variety of factors, including the shade you choose and how often you wash your hair. In general, most hair colors will last anywhere from four to six weeks.

Hair Care

Whether you're changing your hair color to help you look thinner or just for fun, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking its best:

1. Use the right products

Make sure to use products that are designed for your hair type and color. This can help to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

2. Protect your hair from the sun

Exposure to the sun can cause damage to your hair, so make sure to wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF when spending time outdoors.

3. Avoid over-washing

Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and damage. Try to wash your hair no more than every other day.

In conclusion, changing your hair color can be a great way to help you look thinner. By choosing the right shade and taking good care of your hair, you can achieve the results you're looking for.
