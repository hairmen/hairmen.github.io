How To Do Anime Hairstyles A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a fan of anime, you may have wondered how to achieve the unique hairstyles that are often seen in these shows. Anime hairstyles can range from simple to complex, but with the right tools and techniques, you can recreate your favorite characters' looks at home. In this guide, we'll show you how to do anime hairstyles and provide tips for maintaining them.

How to Do Anime Hairstyles

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to choose a character whose hairstyle you want to recreate. Look for images of the character from different angles to get a better idea of their hair. Once you have a reference image, you can start styling your hair.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using clips or hair ties, section your hair into manageable sections. This will make it easier to style your hair and ensure that all sections are evenly styled.

Step 3: Use a Straightening Iron

For anime hairstyles that require straight hair, use a straightening iron to achieve a smooth and sleek look. Start at the roots of your hair and slowly glide the iron down to the ends. Repeat this process until all sections are straightened.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

For anime hairstyles that require curls or waves, use a curling iron to achieve the desired look. Start at the roots of your hair and wrap small sections around the curling iron barrel. Hold for a few seconds and release the hair. Repeat this process until all sections are curled or waved.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is straightened or curled, use a comb or brush to style it according to the reference image. You may need to use hairspray or hair gel to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Anime Hairstyles

Once you've achieved your desired anime hairstyle, you'll want to maintain it for as long as possible. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking great:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and tangles.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about anime hairstyles:

Q: Can I achieve anime hairstyles with short hair?

A: Yes, many anime characters have short hairstyles that can be recreated with the right techniques.

Q: How long does it take to style anime hair?

A: The time it takes to style anime hair depends on the complexity of the hairstyle and your skill level. Some hairstyles may take only a few minutes, while others may take an hour or more.

Q: Do I need special products to achieve anime hairstyles?

A: While you don't need special products, using a good quality heat protectant spray and hairspray can help to keep your hair healthy and in place.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is key to achieving great anime hairstyles. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water, which can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, which can cause breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes foods that promote hair growth, such as leafy greens, nuts, and fish.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve your favorite anime hairstyles and keep your hair healthy and looking great.
