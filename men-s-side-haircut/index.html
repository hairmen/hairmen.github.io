Men s Side Haircut A Guide To The Perfect Look

Written by Oliver Jun 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your old hairstyle and want to switch things up? Look no further than the men's side haircut! This classic style is versatile and can work for any occasion. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about the men's side haircut, including how to achieve it, tips for maintaining it, and more.

Description

The men's side haircut is a classic style that involves cutting the hair short on the sides and leaving it longer on top. The length on top can vary depending on personal preference and face shape. This style is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, from a slicked-back look to a messy, textured look.

How to Achieve the Men's Side Haircut

If you're interested in getting a men's side haircut, here's how to achieve the look:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

Decide how short you want the sides and how long you want the top. This will depend on your personal style and face shape. It's best to consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length for you.

Step 2: Cut the Sides

Use clippers to cut the hair on the sides. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if needed. Make sure to blend the hair on the sides with the hair on top for a seamless look.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Use scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length. You can choose to leave it longer in the front and shorter in the back for a classic look.

Step 4: Style

Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to style the hair on top. You can slick it back for a more polished look or leave it messy and textured for a more casual look. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining the Men's Side Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your men's side haircut:

- Get regular trims to keep the sides and top looking neat and clean. - Use a styling product to keep the hair on top in place. - Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup of product and oil. - Consider using a deep conditioner once a week to keep the hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQ

What face shape is best for the men's side haircut?

The men's side haircut can work for any face shape, but it's best to consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your specific face shape.

How often should I get a trim?

It's best to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the sides and top looking neat and clean.

What styling product should I use?

It depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Pomade and wax are good options for a sleek, polished look, while a texturizing spray is great for a messy, textured look.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. - Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often as they can damage the hair. - Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray. - Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of vitamins and nutrients that promote healthy hair.

Conclusion

The men's side haircut is a classic style that can work for any occasion. With the right length and styling product, you can achieve a variety of looks, from a sleek, polished look to a messy, textured look. Follow these tips for maintaining your men's side haircut and taking good care of your hair, and you'll be sure to turn heads wherever you go.
