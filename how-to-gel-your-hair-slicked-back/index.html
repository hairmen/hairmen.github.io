How To Gel Your Hair Slicked Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Gel is a popular product for styling hair, and slicking it back is a timeless look that never goes out of style. Whether you're getting ready for a night out or just want to switch up your everyday look, slicked back hair is a great choice. In this guide, we'll show you how to gel your hair slicked back in just a few easy steps.

How to Gel Your Hair Slicked Back

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Wash your hair with a shampoo that's appropriate for your hair type, then dry it thoroughly with a towel. It's important to start with clean, dry hair so the gel can work properly.

Step 2: Apply Gel to Your Hair

Next, apply a small amount of gel to your hair. Start with a dime-sized amount and work your way up if you need more. Rub the gel between your hands to warm it up, then apply it to your hair. Make sure you distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair Back

Using a comb, start combing your hair back. Make sure the gel is evenly distributed throughout your hair. You may need to use more gel or a little bit of water to help with the combing process. Keep combing until your hair is slicked back to your liking.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray (Optional)

If you want your slicked back hair to stay in place all day, finish off with a little bit of hairspray. This will help keep your hair in place and prevent any flyaways. Hold the hairspray about 10 inches away from your head and spray lightly all over your hair.

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when slicking your hair back:

  • Use a gel that's appropriate for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use a lightweight gel. If you have thick hair, use a stronger hold gel.
  • Don't use too much gel. A little goes a long way.
  • Use a comb to ensure the gel is evenly distributed throughout your hair.
  • If you have a cowlick or a stubborn piece of hair that won't stay in place, try blow-drying it in the direction you want it to go before applying gel.

FAQs

Is slicked back hair suitable for all hair types?

Yes, slicked back hair can work for all hair types. Just make sure you use the right products for your hair type.

Do I have to use hairspray?

No, hairspray is optional. If you want your hair to stay in place all day, hairspray can help.

Hair Care Tips

It's important to take care of your hair to ensure it stays healthy and looks great. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair (blow dryers, flat irons, etc.) as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily gel your hair slicked back and achieve a timeless, stylish look. Whether you're going out for the night or just want to switch up your everyday look, slicked back hair is a great choice.
