Low Cut Hair Styles For Round Face

Written by Luffy Nov 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with long hair that never seems to cooperate with your round face? It's time to consider a low cut hair style that will bring out the best in your features. In this article, we will discuss the best low cut hair styles for round face and how to achieve them. Keep reading for tips, step by step instructions, FAQ, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Low cut hair styles are perfect for round faces because they help to elongate the face and create a more defined jawline. With the right cut and styling, a low cut can be both feminine and edgy, depending on your preference. Additionally, low cut hair styles are low maintenance and can save you time and money on styling products and salon visits.

How To

Before you decide on a low cut hair style, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. For round faces, hair styles that are longer on top and shorter on the sides and back work best. This helps to create the illusion of length and definition in the face.

Choosing the Right Cut

The most popular low cut hair styles for round faces are pixie cuts, cropped cuts, and buzz cuts. Pixie cuts are longer on top and shorter on the sides and back, while cropped cuts are shorter all around. Buzz cuts are the shortest of the three and are perfect for those who want a low maintenance style.

Styling Your Low Cut

Styling your low cut is simple and requires minimal effort. To add volume and texture, use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray on damp hair. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, effortless look. Alternatively, you can use a pomade or wax to create a sleek, polished look.

Step by Step

Follow these step by step instructions to achieve the perfect low cut hair style for your round face:

  1. Choose the right cut for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Wash and condition your hair, then towel dry.
  3. Apply a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to damp hair.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  5. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, effortless look.
  6. Alternatively, apply a pomade or wax to create a sleek, polished look.
  7. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing and styling a low cut hair style:

  • Consult with a stylist to determine the best cut for your face shape and hair texture.
  • Invest in quality hair care products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Will a low cut hair style make my face look too round?

No, a low cut hair style can actually help to elongate the face and create a more defined jawline. Consult with a stylist to determine the best cut for your face shape and hair texture.

Do I need to use a lot of product to style my low cut?

No, a low cut hair style requires minimal effort and product. Use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray on damp hair, and finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Hair Care

Keep your low cut hair style looking healthy and shiny with these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your cut.

With the right cut and styling, a low cut hair style can be a chic and low maintenance option for those with round faces. Consider your face shape and hair texture, consult with a stylist, and experiment with different styling techniques to find the perfect low cut for you.
