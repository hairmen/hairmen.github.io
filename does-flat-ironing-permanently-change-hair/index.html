Does Flat Ironing Permanently Change Hair

Written by Christine Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Flat ironing is one of the most popular styling methods for those who want straight, sleek, and shiny hair. However, many people wonder if flat ironing permanently changes their hair. In this blog post, we'll explore the answer to this question and provide tips for maintaining healthy hair while using a flat iron.

Table of Contents

  • How to Flat Iron Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks

Does Flat Ironing Permanently Change Hair?

The answer to this question is both yes and no. Flat ironing will not permanently change the texture or structure of your hair, but it can cause damage that may be difficult to repair. When you use a flat iron, the heat breaks down the hydrogen bonds in your hair, causing it to straighten. However, when your hair is exposed to moisture or humidity, those bonds will reform and your hair will revert back to its original texture.

Repeated use of a flat iron can cause damage to your hair, including split ends, breakage, and dryness. If you use a flat iron too often or at too high of a temperature, the damage can become permanent. Your hair may become brittle and prone to breakage or split ends, which can only be fixed by trimming your hair.

How to Flat Iron Hair

Before you start flat ironing your hair, you should make sure that it is clean, dry, and free of tangles. You should also apply a heat protectant spray or serum to prevent damage to your hair. You can then follow these steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Section your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties.
  2. Start with the bottom section and work your way up.
  3. Take a small section of hair and clamp the flat iron at the roots.
  4. Gently slide the flat iron down the section of hair, making sure to keep the plates closed.
  5. Repeat until all sections of hair have been straightened.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you get the best results from your flat iron:

  • Use a flat iron with ceramic plates, which will distribute heat evenly and prevent hot spots.
  • Don't use a flat iron on wet hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a lower temperature setting if you have fine or damaged hair.
  • Don't flat iron your hair every day. Try to limit it to once or twice a week.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQ

Can flat ironing cause split ends?

Yes, flat ironing can cause split ends if you use it too often or at too high of a temperature. Split ends can only be fixed by trimming your hair.

Can flat ironing make hair permanently straight?

No, flat ironing will not permanently change the texture or structure of your hair.

What is the best temperature to use for flat ironing?

The best temperature to use for flat ironing depends on your hair type. If you have fine or damaged hair, use a lower temperature setting. If you have thick or coarse hair, you can use a higher temperature setting.

Hair Care Tips

It's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage from flat ironing. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, flat ironing can cause damage to your hair, but it will not permanently change the texture or structure of your hair. By following the tips and tricks in this blog post and taking care of your hair, you can enjoy the benefits of flat ironing without damaging your hair.
