How To Handle Thick Short Hair Tips And Tricks

Thick short hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right techniques, products and tools, you can make your hair look its best. In this article, we will discuss how to handle thick short hair, including step-by-step tips, frequently asked questions and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Handle Thick Short Hair

Thick short hair can be difficult to manage, but with these tips, you can make your hair look its best:

  • Use the right products: Look for products that are designed for thick hair, such as thickening shampoos and conditioners. Avoid products that contain sulfates and parabens, as they can dry out your hair and make it harder to manage.
  • Invest in a good brush: A quality brush can make a big difference in how your hair looks and feels. Look for a brush with natural bristles, as they are gentler on your hair and won't cause as much damage as synthetic bristles.
  • Get regular haircuts: Regular haircuts can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Short haircuts are especially important for thick hair, as they can help reduce the weight and make your hair more manageable.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to handle your thick short hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a thickening shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and texture.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to create your desired style.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or styling cream to keep your hair in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for handling thick short hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Don't use too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment to make your hair more manageable.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about handling thick short hair:

Q: Can I use a hair straightener on thick short hair?

A: Yes, a hair straightener can be used on thick short hair, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Q: How often should I wash my thick short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is, but generally, you should wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Q: How do I prevent my thick short hair from frizzing?

A: Use a smoothing serum or oil to tame frizz, and avoid using a brush on dry hair, as it can cause static and frizz.

Hair Care Advice

In addition to the tips and tricks above, here are some general hair care tips for handling thick short hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of protein, vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Avoid using tight hair ties or clips, as they can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your thick short hair looking its best. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to seek advice from a professional stylist if you need additional help.
