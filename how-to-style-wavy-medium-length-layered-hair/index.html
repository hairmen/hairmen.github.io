How To Style Wavy Medium Length Layered Hair In 2023

Written by Robby Jun 18, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking for a relaxed and effortless hairstyle that’s perfect for any occasion, then wavy medium length layered hair is the way to go. This hairstyle is easy to style and maintain, and it looks great on anyone. In this article, we’ll show you how to style your wavy medium length layered hair in 2023.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Wavy Medium Length Layered Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Wavy Medium Length Layered Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Wavy Medium Length Layered Hair

How to Style Wavy Medium Length Layered Hair

Wavy medium length layered hair is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways. Here are some popular ways to style your wavy medium length layered hair:

  • Beachy waves
  • Soft curls
  • Straight and sleek
  • Messy bun or ponytail

The key to styling wavy medium length layered hair is to embrace the natural texture of your hair. Don’t try to fight against the waves, instead, work with them to create a relaxed and effortless style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style your wavy medium length layered hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat. This will help to enhance your natural waves and add volume to your hair.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or soft curls. Start at the mid-length of your hair and wrap small sections of hair around the barrel of the curling iron or wand.
  5. After curling your hair, run your fingers through your hair to gently separate the curls and create a more natural look.
  6. Finish off your look with a texturizing spray to add volume and hold to your hair.

Tips for Styling Wavy Medium Length Layered Hair

Here are some tips for styling wavy medium length layered hair:

  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel. This will help to reduce frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. High heat can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.
  • Invest in a good quality curling iron or wand. This will help to create beautiful and long-lasting curls.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles. Wavy medium length layered hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling wavy medium length layered hair:

Q: Can I style my wavy medium length layered hair without heat?

A: Yes, you can style your wavy medium length layered hair without heat. Try using a texturizing spray or mousse to enhance your natural waves and create a more relaxed look.

Q: How often should I wash my wavy medium length layered hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or you exercise frequently, you may need to wash your hair more often. If you have dry hair, you can wash your hair less frequently to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my wavy medium length layered hair?

A: You can prevent frizz in your wavy medium length layered hair by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and using a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair.

Hair Care for Wavy Medium Length Layered Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your wavy medium length layered hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. High heat can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your wavy medium length layered hair in 2023 with ease. Whether you’re going for a beachy look or a more polished style, wavy medium length layered hair is a versatile hairstyle that looks great on everyone.
