How To Wear Your Hair To Bed With Extensions

Written by Bardi Oct 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Extensions are a great way to add length and volume to your hair without having to wait for your natural hair to grow. However, it's important to take proper care of your extensions to ensure they last as long as possible. One key aspect of extension care is how you wear your hair to bed. In this article, we'll discuss some tips for how to wear your hair to bed with extensions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Your Hair to Bed With Extensions
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing Your Hair to Bed With Extensions
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Extensions

How to Wear Your Hair to Bed With Extensions

When it comes to wearing your hair to bed with extensions, there are a few things to keep in mind. First and foremost, you want to avoid tangling and matting your extensions while you sleep. Here are some steps to follow:

1. Brush Your Hair

Before you go to bed, make sure to brush your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles. Use a soft-bristled brush or a wide-tooth comb to avoid pulling on your extensions.

2. Tie Your Hair Up

Next, tie your hair up in a loose ponytail or braid. Avoid pulling your hair too tightly, as this can cause tension on your extensions and lead to breakage. You can also use a silk or satin scarf to wrap your hair for added protection.

3. Use a Sleep Cap

If you want to ensure maximum protection for your extensions, consider using a sleep cap. A sleep cap is a silk or satin cap that you wear over your hair while you sleep. It helps to keep your hair in place and prevents friction that can cause tangling and matting.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to wearing your hair to bed with extensions:

1. Brush your hair thoroughly.

2. Tie your hair up in a loose ponytail or braid.

3. Use a silk or satin scarf to wrap your hair (optional).

4. Put on a sleep cap.

Tips for Wearing Your Hair to Bed With Extensions

Here are some additional tips to help you wear your hair to bed with extensions:

1. Avoid sleeping with wet hair.

Wet hair is more prone to tangling and matting, so make sure your hair is completely dry before you go to bed. You can use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to speed up the drying process.

2. Use a silk or satin pillowcase.

Silk and satin are softer and smoother than cotton, which can cause friction and tangle your hair. Consider investing in a silk or satin pillowcase to protect your extensions while you sleep.

3. Don't sleep on your back.

Sleeping on your back can cause your hair to rub against the pillow and lead to tangling and matting. Try sleeping on your side or stomach instead.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my hair down to bed with extensions?

A: It's not recommended to wear your hair down to bed with extensions, as it can cause tangling and matting. Always tie your hair up in a loose ponytail or braid, or use a sleep cap.

Q: How often should I wear a sleep cap?

A: You can wear a sleep cap every night if you want to ensure maximum protection for your extensions. However, it's not necessary if you tie your hair up in a loose ponytail or braid.

Hair Care Tips for Extensions

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind when you have extensions:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

Sulfates can strip the natural oils from your hair and cause your extensions to dry out and become brittle. Look for a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that is designed for extensions.

2. Avoid using heat tools.

Heat tools can damage your extensions and cause them to break and split. If you must use heat tools, use a low heat setting and apply a heat protectant spray first.

3. Don't pull or tug on your extensions.

Be gentle when brushing and styling your extensions, and avoid pulling or tugging on them. This can cause tension on the hair and lead to breakage.

By following these tips, you can wear your hair to bed with extensions without worrying about tangling, matting, or damage. Remember to take good care of your extensions to keep them looking great for as long as possible.
