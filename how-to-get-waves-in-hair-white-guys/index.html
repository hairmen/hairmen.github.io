How To Get Waves In Hair White Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 19, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re a white guy with straight hair, you might think that getting waves is impossible. But with the right technique and a little bit of patience, you can achieve the wavy hair of your dreams. In this post, we’ll show you how to get waves in hair white guys can rock with confidence. From the basics to advanced tips, we’ve got you covered.

The Basics: What Are Waves?

Waves are a popular hairstyle for black men, but they can also be achieved by white guys with the right technique. Essentially, waves are a pattern of ripples or waves that appear in your hair when it is brushed and trained in a certain way. This style is achieved by training your hair to grow in a particular direction and brushing it regularly to create the waves.

How to Get Waves in Hair White Guys: Step-by-Step

Getting waves in hair white guys can be broken down into a few simple steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step to getting waves is to choose the right haircut. A shorter cut with a tapered back and sides will make it easier to train your hair and create the wave pattern. You may also want to consider getting a fade or undercut to make the waves more prominent.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before you start training your hair, it’s important to wash it thoroughly. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup or oils that may be weighing your hair down. Rinse well and towel dry your hair.

Step 3: Apply Wave Cream

Next, apply a wave cream to your hair. This will help to hold the waves in place and make them more defined. Use a dime-sized amount and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Brush Your Hair

Now it’s time to start training your hair. Use a wave brush to brush your hair in a circular motion, starting at the crown of your head and working your way outwards. Be sure to brush your hair in the same direction every time to create the wave pattern.

Step 5: Tie Your Hair Down

Once you’ve brushed your hair, it’s time to tie it down. Use a wave cap or durag to hold your hair in place and help create the wave pattern. Wear the cap overnight, or for at least a few hours, to give your hair time to set.

Step 6: Repeat Daily

Repeat these steps daily to train your hair and create the wave pattern. Over time, your hair will start to grow in the desired direction and the waves will become more defined.

Tips for Getting Waves in Hair White Guys

Here are some additional tips to help you get the best waves possible:

  • Brush your hair at least twice a day to maintain the wave pattern.
  • Use a soft-bristle brush to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Apply a wave cream or pomade daily to hold the waves in place.
  • Wear a wave cap or durag at night to help maintain the wave pattern.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it harder to create the waves.

FAQs About Getting Waves in Hair White Guys

Q: How long does it take to get waves?

A: It can take anywhere from a few weeks to a few months to get waves, depending on how quickly your hair grows and how often you brush it.

Q: Can I get waves with long hair?

A: It is possible to get waves with longer hair, but it may take longer to train your hair and create the wave pattern.

Q: Do I need to use a specific type of brush?

A: Yes, it’s important to use a wave brush with firm bristles to create the wave pattern in your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Waves

Once you’ve achieved the perfect wave pattern, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain the look. Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your waves:

  • Wash your hair no more than once a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and disrupt the wave pattern.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your waves.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect waves in hair white guys can rock with confidence. So grab your wave brush, wave cap, and wave cream, and get ready to show off your new look.
