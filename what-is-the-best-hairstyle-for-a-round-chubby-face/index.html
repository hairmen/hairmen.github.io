What Is The Best Hairstyle For A Round Chubby Face

Written by Petter Jun 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that do not seem to suit your round chubby face? Do you want to know what the best hairstyle for a round chubby face is? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the best hairstyles for round chubby faces that will make you look fabulous.

Description

Round chubby faces have a circular shape with full cheeks and a soft jawline. The key to finding the best hairstyle for a round chubby face is to create the illusion of length and slimness. A good hairstyle can make a round chubby face look more oval, which is considered the ideal face shape.

How To

Here are some tips on how to choose the best hairstyle for a round chubby face:

1. Layers

Layers can add volume and texture to your hair, making your face look slimmer. Long layers that start at the chin or collarbone are ideal for round chubby faces. Avoid short layers as they can make your face look wider.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs can make your face look longer and more oval. Avoid straight bangs or blunt cuts as they can make your face look rounder.

3. Bob Hairstyle

A bob hairstyle that falls below your chin can create the illusion of length. Avoid short bobs that end at the chin as they can make your face look wider.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut that is longer on top and shorter on the sides can make your face look longer. Avoid a pixie cut that is too short as it can make your face look wider.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when choosing the best hairstyle for a round chubby face:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and looking in the mirror. If your face is circular with full cheeks and a soft jawline, you have a round chubby face.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also influence your hairstyle choice. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a layered hairstyle that can remove some of the bulk. If you have thin hair, you may want to consider a bob hairstyle that can add volume and texture.

3. Consult with Your Hairstylist

Your hairstylist can offer you the best advice on which hairstyle will suit your face shape and hair texture. They can also suggest different styling techniques that can create the illusion of length and slimness.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to consider when choosing the best hairstyle for a round chubby face:

1. Avoid Middle Parts

Avoid middle parts as they can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for a side part that can create the illusion of length.

2. Use Hair Accessories

Use hair accessories such as headbands, clips, and scarves to add interest and texture to your hair.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best. Take pictures of your favorite hairstyles and show them to your hairstylist for their opinion.

FAQ

1. Can I wear my hair in a ponytail?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail. However, avoid tight ponytails that can make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for loose, low ponytails that can create the illusion of length.

2. Can I wear my hair straight?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight. However, avoid a blunt cut as it can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for layers or side swept bangs that can create the illusion of length.

3. Should I avoid short hairstyles?

No, you do not have to avoid short hairstyles. However, opt for a style that is longer on top and shorter on the sides to create the illusion of length.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Use hair oils and serums to add moisture and protect your hair from damage.

In conclusion, the best hairstyle for a round chubby face is one that creates the illusion of length and slimness. Layers, side swept bangs, bobs, and pixie cuts are all great options to consider. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best. With the right hairstyle, you can look and feel fabulous!
