How To Add Volume To Short Hair With A Flat Iron

Written by Christine Dec 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have short hair, you might think that adding volume is impossible. But with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a fuller, more voluminous look. One of the best tools for adding volume to short hair is a flat iron. In this article, we'll show you how to use a flat iron to add volume to your short hair, step by step.

How to Add Volume to Short Hair with a Flat Iron

Before we get started, it's important to note that not all flat irons are created equal. Look for a flat iron with ceramic or tourmaline plates, which will help to distribute heat evenly and reduce damage to your hair. Here's how to add volume to short hair with a flat iron:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before you begin. If your hair is damp, it won't hold a curl or wave as well.

2. Apply heat protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to minimize damage from the flat iron's heat.

3. Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections, starting at the bottom and working your way up.

4. Clamp the flat iron at the roots

Take a small section of hair and clamp the flat iron at the roots. Hold the flat iron in this position for a few seconds to create volume at the roots.

5. Twist the flat iron

Twist the flat iron away from your face, making sure to keep the hair taut. Hold the flat iron in this position for a few seconds to create a loose wave.

6. Repeat on all sections

Repeat steps 4 and 5 on all sections of your hair, working your way up from the bottom. Make sure to alternate the direction of the twist for a more natural look.

7. Shake out your hair

Once you've curled all of your hair, shake your head to loosen the curls and create more volume.

Tips for Adding Volume to Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you add volume to your short hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create more volume at the roots
  • Tease your hair at the roots to create more lift
  • Use a texturizing spray to create more body and definition
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and techniques to find what works best for your hair type

FAQs

Q: Can I use a flat iron on wet hair?

A: No, you should never use a flat iron on wet hair. Wet hair is more susceptible to damage from heat, and using a flat iron on wet hair can cause breakage and split ends.

Q: How often should I use a flat iron on my hair?

A: It's best to use a flat iron sparingly to minimize damage to your hair. Limit your use to once or twice a week, and always use a heat protectant spray or serum.

Q: Can I use a flat iron to straighten my hair?

A: Yes, a flat iron can be used to straighten hair as well as add volume and curl.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and voluminous:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid drying it out
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends
  • Invest in a good quality brush or comb to prevent breakage and damage to your hair

By following these tips and using a flat iron to add volume, you can achieve a full, voluminous look for your short hair. Experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for your hair type, and always prioritize the health and well-being of your hair.
