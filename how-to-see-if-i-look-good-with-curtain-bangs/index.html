How To See If You Look Good With Curtain Bangs

Written by Oliver Oct 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh new hairstyle, curtain bangs might be the perfect choice. This style has been trending for a few years now, and it's not going away anytime soon. But before you take the plunge and cut your hair, you might be wondering, "How can I see if I look good with curtain bangs?"

Table of Contents

  1. How to See If You Look Good with Curtain Bangs
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Choosing the Right Bangs
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Step-by-Step Guide

The best way to see if you look good with curtain bangs is to try them out for yourself. But before you make any drastic changes to your hair, follow these steps:

1. Research different styles of curtain bangs.

There are many different variations of curtain bangs, so it's important to find a style that will suit your face shape and hair type. Look for pictures of celebrities or models who have similar hair to yours and see if you like how their bangs look.

2. Try on virtual bangs.

There are many websites and apps that allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles, including curtain bangs. This is a great way to see how you might look with bangs without committing to a haircut.

3. Use a hair clip to create faux bangs.

If you don't want to use an app or website, you can create faux bangs with a hair clip. Simply clip your hair back to create the illusion of bangs. This will give you an idea of how you might look with curtain bangs.

4. Consult with a hairstylist.

If you're still unsure if curtain bangs are right for you, consult with a hairstylist. They can give you personalized advice based on your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Tips for Choosing the Right Bangs

Choosing the right type of curtain bangs can be a bit tricky. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Consider your face shape.

Not all curtain bangs will look good on all face shapes. For example, if you have a round face, you might want to avoid thick, heavy bangs that will make your face look even rounder. Instead, opt for wispy, tapered bangs.

2. Think about your hair type.

If you have thin or fine hair, you might want to avoid heavy, blunt bangs. Instead, opt for wispy, textured bangs that will add volume and movement to your hair.

3. Consider your personal style.

Curtain bangs can be styled in many different ways, so think about how you typically wear your hair. If you prefer a more natural, effortless look, opt for wispy, textured bangs. If you prefer a more polished, put-together look, opt for thicker, more structured bangs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do curtain bangs work for all hair types?

Yes, curtain bangs can work for all hair types, but the style of bangs might need to be adjusted based on your hair type. For example, if you have curly hair, you might want to opt for longer, more textured bangs that will blend better with your curls.

How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

You will need to trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from becoming too long.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with curtain bangs?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with curtain bangs. You can either leave your bangs out or sweep them to the side and secure them with bobby pins.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Once you've decided to get curtain bangs, it's important to take care of them properly to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips:

1. Wash your bangs regularly.

Since your bangs will be in contact with your forehead, they can get oily quickly. Be sure to wash your bangs every day or every other day to keep them looking clean and fresh.

2. Blow dry your bangs.

Blow drying your bangs will help them look smoother and prevent them from sticking to your forehead. Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall.

3. Use dry shampoo.

If you don't have time to wash your bangs, use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and refresh your hair.

4. Trim your bangs regularly.

As mentioned earlier, you will need to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking their best.

By following these tips and tricks, you'll be able to see if you look good with curtain bangs and keep them looking fabulous. So go ahead and try out this trendy hairstyle!
