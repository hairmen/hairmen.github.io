How To Fake Bangs Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 01, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of your same old hairstyle? Do you want to switch it up but not commit to a full-blown haircut? Look no further! Faking bangs with curly hair is a fun and easy way to change up your look without the commitment. In this guide, we’ll walk you through how to fake bangs curly hair in just a few simple steps.

How To:

Before we get started, gather the following supplies:

  • A brush
  • A comb
  • Hairpins
  • A hair tie
  • Hairspray

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing out your curly hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to manipulate your hair into faux bangs.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, use your comb to create a triangular section of hair at the front of your head. The base of the triangle should start at your temples, and the point should be at the center of your forehead.

Step 3: Twist Your Hair

Twist this section of hair tightly away from your face, and then fold it backward towards the crown of your head.

Step 4: Secure Your Hair With Pins

Secure the twisted section of hair with hairpins. Make sure to use enough pins to keep the twist in place.

Step 5: Style Your Faux Bangs

Use your comb to gently tease the ends of the twisted section of hair to create a more natural-looking faux bang. You can also use hairspray to hold the twist in place and smooth down any flyaways.

Tips:

  • For a more natural look, try leaving some tendrils of hair loose around your face.
  • If you want to add some extra volume to your faux bangs, try backcombing the twisted section of hair before securing it with pins.
  • If you have shorter hair, you may need to use bobby pins instead of hairpins to secure your faux bangs in place.

FAQ:

Q: How long will my faux bangs last?

A: Your faux bangs should last all day if you use enough hairpins and hairspray to secure them in place.

Q: Can I fake bangs with any type of curly hair?

A: Yes! This technique works with all types of curly hair, from loose waves to tight coils.

Hair Care:

To keep your curly hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. You should also deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and prevent breakage.

Now that you know how to fake bangs curly hair, go ahead and give it a try! It’s a fun and easy way to change up your hairstyle and try something new.
