How Short Is A 3 Guard

Written by Luffy Nov 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're new to haircutting, you may be wondering what a "3 guard" means. It's a common term used by barbers and hairstylists to refer to a specific clipper guard size. In this article, we'll discuss how short a 3 guard is, how to use it, and some tips for maintaining your hair after using it.

Description: What is a 3 guard?

A 3 guard is a clipper guard that measures 3/8 of an inch or 9.5mm. It attaches to the clipper and adjusts the length of the hair that's cut. The higher the number of the guard, the shorter the hair will be. A 3 guard is considered a medium length guard and is commonly used for buzz cuts, fades, and taper haircuts.

How to Use a 3 Guard:

Using a 3 guard is relatively simple. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Choose the right clipper

Make sure you have the right type of clipper that is compatible with the 3 guard. Not all clippers will work with all guards, so be sure to check the manufacturer's instructions or consult with a barber or hairstylist.

Step 2: Prepare your hair

Before using the 3 guard, make sure your hair is washed and dried. This will make it easier to cut and ensure the guard glides smoothly over your hair.

Step 3: Attach the 3 guard to the clipper

Attach the 3 guard to the clipper according to the manufacturer's instructions. Make sure it's securely in place before using it.

Step 4: Start cutting

Start cutting your hair using the clipper with the 3 guard attached. Begin at the bottom of your hairline and work your way up. Use slow, steady movements and make sure the guard is always in contact with your scalp.

Step 5: Trim the sides and back

Use the clipper with the 3 guard to trim the sides and back of your hair. Use the same slow, steady movements and make sure the guard is always in contact with your scalp.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair After Using a 3 Guard:

After using a 3 guard, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the length and texture. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Wash and condition regularly

Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.

Tip 2: Use a leave-in conditioner

Using a leave-in conditioner can help keep your hair hydrated and smooth. Apply it after washing your hair and leave it in for the recommended time.

Tip 3: Trim regularly

To maintain the length of your hair, trim it regularly. You can use a 3 guard or a shorter guard if you want to keep it shorter.

FAQ:

Q: Can I use a 3 guard for all types of hair?

A: A 3 guard is suitable for most hair types, but it may not work for very thick or curly hair. In these cases, a shorter guard may be more appropriate.

Q: How often should I wash my hair after using a 3 guard?

A: It's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days after using a 3 guard. This will help maintain the length and texture of your hair.

Q: Can I use a 3 guard for a beard?

A: Yes, you can use a 3 guard for a beard, but it may not work for all beard types. It's best to consult with a barber or hairstylist for advice on the best guard size for your beard.

Hair Care:

Using a 3 guard can be a great way to maintain the length of your hair and keep it looking neat and tidy. Just remember to take care of your hair after using it to keep it healthy and strong.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can ensure that your hair looks its best and stays in great condition. If you're unsure about using a 3 guard or have any questions about hair care, don't hesitate to speak with a barber or hairstylist for advice.
