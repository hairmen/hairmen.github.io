Best Hairstyles For Square Face Male A Complete Guide In 2023

If you're a man with a square-shaped face, choosing the right hairstyle can be a bit of a challenge. The goal is to soften your strong jawline and create a more balanced look. In this blog post, we'll share some of the best hairstyles for square face male, along with tips on how to style them and take care of your hair.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Square Face Male
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Square Face Male Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Square Face Male Hairstyle
  • FAQs About Square Face Male Hairstyles
  • Hair Care Tips for Square Face Male

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Square Face Male

The key to finding the best hairstyle for square face male is to create a balance between the angular jawline and the rest of your face. Hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head and soften the edges of your face are typically the most flattering.

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too flat on top, as they will only emphasize the squareness of your face. Instead, opt for hairstyles that are longer on top and tapered on the sides, such as:

  • Crew Cut
  • Side Part
  • Quiff
  • Textured Crop
  • Pompadour

Consider your hair texture as well. If you have thick, straight hair, a textured crop or crew cut may be the best option. If you have curly or wavy hair, a longer style with layers may work better.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Square Face Male Hair

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle for your square face, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to your hair. Use a small amount and work it through your hair evenly.
  3. Using a comb or your fingers, style your hair in the desired direction. If you have a side part, comb your hair to one side. If you have a quiff, push your hair up and back.
  4. Use a blow dryer to set the style and add volume to the top of your head.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Remember, less is more when it comes to styling products. You don't want your hair to look greasy or weighed down.

Tips for Maintaining Your Square Face Male Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you maintain your square face male hairstyle:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid over-drying it.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine if you swim regularly.

FAQs About Square Face Male Hairstyles

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a square face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too flat on top, as they will only emphasize the squareness of your face. Avoid center parts and hairstyles with bangs.

Can I have long hair if I have a square face?

Yes, you can have long hair if you have a square face. Just make sure to add layers to soften your features and create a more balanced look.

What hair products should I use for my square face male hairstyle?

Use lightweight styling products, such as pomade or wax, to avoid weighing your hair down. Hairspray can also help hold your style in place.

Hair Care Tips for Square Face Male

Good hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy head of hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top condition:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos and conditioners that contain sulfates.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet that includes plenty of protein and vitamins.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption.
  • Get plenty of rest and exercise regularly.

By following these tips, you'll be able to maintain healthy hair that looks great with any of the best hairstyles for square face male.
