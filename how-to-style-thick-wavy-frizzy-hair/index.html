How To Style Thick Wavy Frizzy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by William Sep 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick wavy frizzy hair, you know how difficult it can be to style. It can be frustrating to spend hours trying to tame your hair, only to end up with a frizzy mess. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this post, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to style your thick wavy frizzy hair.

Table of Contents:

  • Hair Care
  • How to Style Thick Wavy Frizzy Hair

Hair Care

Before we get into styling, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's specifically designed for curly or wavy hair. Avoid products that contain sulfates, which can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair to help hydrate and tame frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz.

By taking good care of your hair, you'll make it easier to style and keep it looking healthy and shiny.


How to Style Thick Wavy Frizzy Hair

Now, let's talk about how to style your thick wavy frizzy hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair and applying a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help hydrate and tame frizz.
  2. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or mousse, to your hair. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your natural waves and curls while reducing frizz.
  5. If you prefer to air dry your hair, scrunch your hair with a microfiber towel or t-shirt to help enhance your natural waves and curls.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define any loose curls or waves. You can also use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

Tips for Styling Thick Wavy Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling your thick wavy frizzy hair:

  • Try not to touch your hair too much while it's drying, as this can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • For a quick and easy hairstyle, try a messy bun or braid.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • If you're in a rush and don't have time to style your hair, pull it back into a ponytail or bun.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling thick wavy frizzy hair:

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. If you have thick wavy frizzy hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Q: What's the best way to dry my hair?

A: Using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer is the best way to dry your hair if you want to enhance your natural waves and curls. If you prefer to air dry your hair, use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to scrunch your hair and enhance your natural texture.

Q: What's the best styling product for thick wavy frizzy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and the look you're trying to achieve. Some popular styling products for thick wavy frizzy hair include curl creams, mousses, and hair oils.

Styling thick wavy frizzy hair can be challenging, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, defined waves and curls. Remember to take good care of your hair and experiment with different styling products to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
