Short Hair For Round Face Asian 2021 A Guide

Written by Christine Nov 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a round-faced Asian looking for a trendy and chic hairstyle to rock in 2021? Look no further as we have rounded up some short hair ideas that will flatter your facial shape and make you look fabulous! In this article, we will discuss how to choose the best short hair for round face Asian, step-by-step guide, hair care tips, and FAQs to help you achieve your desired look.

Description

Round face Asians have a facial shape that is wider at the cheeks and has a rounded chin. The perfect short hair for round face Asian is one that adds height and volume to the crown while slimming down the cheeks and jawline. The hairstyle should elongate the face and create an illusion of an oval or heart-shaped face.

How to Choose the Best Short Hair for Round Face Asian

Here are some tips to help you choose the best short hair for round face Asian:

Consider Your Face Shape

Go for hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown and slim down the cheeks and jawline. The hairstyle should elongate the face and create an illusion of an oval or heart-shaped face.

Look for Haircuts with Layers

Short haircuts with layers help to add texture, dimension, and movement to your hair. This will make your hair look thicker and fuller, which is perfect for round face Asians.

Avoid Blunt Bangs

Avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face look rounder. Instead, go for side-swept or asymmetrical bangs that will slim down your face.

Consider Your Hair Type

Choose a hairstyle that suits your hair type. If you have thick hair, go for a layered bob or a pixie cut. If you have fine hair, go for a short shag or a textured pixie cut.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieve Short Hair for Round Face Asian

Follow these steps to achieve short hair for round face Asian:

Step 1: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist and discuss your desired hairstyle. Your hairstylist will help you choose the best haircut that suits your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Wash and condition your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Use a towel to dry your hair and detangle it gently with a wide-tooth comb.

Step 3: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent heat damage from styling tools.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the crown. Blow dry your hair in sections and use a nozzle attachment to focus the heat on each section.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a curling iron or a straightener. For a textured look, use a sea salt spray or a texturizing spray.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair for Round Face Asian

Here are some hair care tips for short hair for round face Asian:

Use a Sulfate-Free Shampoo

Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent hair damage and breakage. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause frizz and dryness.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can cause heat damage and breakage. Use them sparingly and always apply a heat protectant before styling.

FAQs About Short Hair for Round Face Asian

1. What is the best haircut for round face Asian?

The best haircut for round face Asian is one that adds height and volume to the crown while slimming down the cheeks and jawline. Short haircuts with layers are perfect for round face Asians.

2. Can round face Asians pull off a pixie cut?

Yes, round face Asians can pull off a pixie cut. However, make sure to choose a pixie cut with volume on top and tapered sides to create an illusion of an oval or heart-shaped face.

3. How often should I wash my short hair?

You should wash your short hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness and breakage.

4. How can I add volume to my short hair?

You can add volume to your short hair by blow-drying it with a round brush, using a volumizing mousse or spray, or getting a layered haircut.

In conclusion, short hair for round face Asian in 2021 can be achieved by following the tips and steps mentioned above. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and choose a haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. With proper hair care and maintenance, you can rock a fabulous short hairstyle and turn heads wherever you go!
