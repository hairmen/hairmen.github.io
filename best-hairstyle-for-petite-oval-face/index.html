Best Hairstyle For Petite Oval Face

Written by Daniel Oct 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a petite oval face, you're in luck! This face shape is one of the most versatile and can pull off a variety of hairstyles. However, with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to find the perfect one. In this article, we will explore the best hairstyle for petite oval face, how to achieve it, and tips for maintaining it.

Description

A petite oval face is characterized by a narrow forehead, high cheekbones, and a pointed chin. This face shape is well-proportioned and can pull off both short and long hairstyles. The key to finding the best hairstyle is to create balance and enhance your natural features.

How to

The best hairstyle for a petite oval face is a layered cut with soft waves or curls. This style adds volume and dimension to the hair, creating an illusion of fullness. To achieve this look, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Prep

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.

Step 2: Blow Dry

Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and create a smooth base. Make sure to lift the roots of your hair while blow-drying to add height to the crown.

Step 3: Curl

Using a curling wand, create loose curls or waves. Start at the mid-length of your hair and wrap sections around the wand, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Continue until all sections are curled.

Step 4: Style

Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to gently brush through the curls to create a natural-looking wave. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your best hairstyle for petite oval face:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day to prevent heat damage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, like headbands or barrettes, to add interest to your style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for petite oval face:

What if I have straight hair?

If you have straight hair, you can still achieve this hairstyle by using a curling iron or hot rollers to create waves or curls.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail?

A low ponytail can be a great option for a casual look. Make sure to leave some volume at the crown to balance out your features.

What if I have a fringe?

A side-swept fringe can be a great addition to this hairstyle, adding softness and framing your face. Make sure to keep it long enough to blend in with the rest of your hair.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential to achieving the best hairstyle for petite oval face. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate hair growth and improve circulation.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the best hairstyle for petite oval face and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. Experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and glamorous!
