How To Hide Face Fat With Makeup

Written by Daniel Aug 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have face fat and want to know how to hide it with makeup, you've come to the right place. In this article, we will be discussing how to use makeup to contour and conceal your face fat. We will also be providing tips, step-by-step instructions, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Description

Face fat can be a source of discomfort and insecurity for many people. Fortunately, with the right makeup techniques, you can easily hide your face fat and achieve a slimmer, more defined look. By contouring and concealing strategically, you can create the illusion of a more chiseled face without having to resort to drastic measures.

How to Hide Face Fat with Makeup

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to hide face fat with makeup:

Step 1: Prep Your Skin

Before you start applying makeup, it's important to prep your skin. This means cleansing, toning, and moisturizing your face to create a smooth, even canvas. Use a lightweight moisturizer that won't add any extra weight to your face.

Step 2: Apply Foundation

Apply foundation all over your face, making sure to blend it well. Choose a shade that matches your skin tone perfectly. If you have a double chin, apply the foundation down your neck as well.

Step 3: Contour Your Cheeks

Use a contour powder or cream to create shadows on your cheeks. Apply the contour powder/cream in the hollows of your cheeks, starting from your ears and moving toward the center of your face. Blend it well with a brush or a sponge.

Step 4: Contour Your Jawline

Use the same contour powder/cream to contour your jawline. Apply it along your jawline, blending it well with a brush or sponge. This will create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

Step 5: Highlight Your Cheekbones

Use a highlighter powder or cream to highlight your cheekbones. Apply it on top of your contour powder/cream, making sure to blend it well. This will make your cheekbones stand out and create a more sculpted look.

Step 6: Highlight Your Nose

Use the same highlighter powder/cream to highlight the bridge of your nose. Apply it down the center of your nose, making sure to blend it well. This will make your nose appear slimmer and more defined.

Step 7: Apply Blush

Apply a subtle blush to the apples of your cheeks. This will add a pop of color to your face and create a more youthful look. Be sure to blend it well with your contour and highlight.

Step 8: Finish with Powder

Finish your makeup look with a translucent powder to set everything in place. This will prevent your makeup from creasing or smudging throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you hide face fat with makeup:

  • Use a matte foundation to avoid adding shine to your face.
  • Blend your makeup well to avoid any harsh lines.
  • Use a small brush to contour your nose for a more precise application.
  • Don't forget to blend your makeup down your neck if you have a double chin.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to hide face fat with makeup:

What is the best makeup for hiding face fat?

The best makeup for hiding face fat is contour powder/cream and highlighter powder/cream. These products can create shadows and highlights on your face to create a more defined, sculpted look.

Can makeup really hide face fat?

Yes, makeup can definitely hide face fat. By using contouring and highlighting techniques, you can create the illusion of a slimmer, more defined face.

Hair Care

In addition to makeup, your hairstyle can also play a role in hiding face fat. Opt for a hairstyle that creates volume at the crown of your head to draw attention away from your face. Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or sleek, as these can make your face appear wider.

Now that you know how to hide face fat with makeup, you can confidently go about your day knowing that you look your best. Remember to practice your makeup techniques and experiment with different products to find what works best for you. With a little practice and patience, you'll be a pro at hiding face fat in no time!
