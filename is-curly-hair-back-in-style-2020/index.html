Is Curly Hair Back in Style 2020: A Guide to Rocking Your Natural Curls

Description

Curly hair has been in and out of style over the years, but in 2020, it's definitely back in. More and more people are embracing their natural curls, and there are plenty of celebrities to look to for inspiration, from Zendaya to Tracee Ellis Ross.

But rocking your natural curls can be intimidating if you're used to straightening or smoothing them out. That's why we've put together this guide to help you embrace your curls and make them look their best.

How to Embrace Your Natural Curls

The first step to rocking your natural curls is to embrace them. Stop straightening or smoothing them out, and start working with your curls instead of against them. This might mean changing your hair care routine, but it will be worth it in the end.

Another important step is to find a stylist who knows how to work with curly hair. Not all stylists are created equal, and someone who is used to working with straight hair might not know how to bring out the best in your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Curls

Once you've embraced your curls, it's time to start styling them. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Start with clean, conditioned hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner formulated for curly hair to help define your curls and keep them healthy.
  2. Avoid using a brush or comb on your hair. Instead, use your fingers to gently detangle your curls while they're wet.
  3. Apply a curl-defining product to your hair, like a curl cream or gel. This will help enhance your curls and keep them looking defined throughout the day.
  4. If you're in a rush, you can diffuse your curls with a hair dryer on a low heat setting. Otherwise, let your hair air dry for the best results.
  5. Once your hair is dry, you can use a bit of oil or serum to add shine and tame any frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Curls

Keeping your curls healthy is important if you want them to look their best. Here are a few tips to help you maintain healthy, beautiful curls:

  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your curls moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair whenever possible, as this can damage your curls and cause frizz.
  • Protect your hair from the elements, especially the sun and wind. Wear a hat or scarf to protect your curls when you're outside.
  • Avoid using products that contain sulfates, as these can be drying and damaging to curly hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs About Curly Hair

Here are a few frequently asked questions about curly hair:

  • Q: Can I still straighten my hair if I want to?
  • A: Of course! Embracing your natural curls doesn't mean you can never straighten your hair again. Just be sure to use heat protectant and avoid straightening your hair too often.
  • Q: How often should I wash my hair?
  • A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp gets. Some people with curly hair can go a week or more without washing, while others need to wash every other day.
  • Q: What should I do if my curls are looking flat or lifeless?
  • A: Try using a volumizing product or flipping your part to add some volume to your curls.

Hair Care Products for Curly Hair

Here are a few hair care products that are great for curly hair:

  • Shampoo and conditioner formulated for curly hair
  • Curl-defining cream or gel
  • Deep conditioner
  • Oil or serum for shine and frizz control
  • Hair dryer with diffuser attachment

With these tips and products, you'll be able to rock your natural curls with confidence. Say goodbye to straighteners and embrace your curls in 2020!
