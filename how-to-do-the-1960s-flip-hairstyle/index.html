How To Do The 1960S Flip Hairstyle

Written by Eveline Dec 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

The 1960s was a decade of bold fashion statements, and the flip hairstyle was no exception. This iconic hairstyle is still loved and recreated today, and it's relatively easy to do. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect 1960s flip hairstyle.

Description

The 1960s flip hairstyle is a classic look that involves flipping out the ends of your hair. It's typically done with medium to long hair and was made famous by celebrities like Mary Tyler Moore and Brigitte Bardot. The flip hairstyle is perfect for adding volume and movement to your hair, making it a great choice for special occasions or just for everyday wear.

How To

Now that you know what the 1960s flip hairstyle is, let's get into how to do it:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. The flip hairstyle works best with clean, dry hair. If you have thin or flat hair, you may want to consider using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some extra body.

Step 2: Section your hair

Next, section your hair into two parts, with the top half of your hair clipped up and out of the way.

Step 3: Curl the ends

Using a curling iron, curl the ends of your hair under. Make sure to curl the ends of your hair in the same direction for a uniform look.

Step 4: Tease your hair

Using a teasing comb, gently tease the roots of your hair to add volume. Be careful not to tease too aggressively, as this can damage your hair.

Step 5: Flip your hair

Finally, flip the ends of your hair out using a round brush or your fingers. Use a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect 1960s flip hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Start with a small section of hair and work your way up to avoid damaging your hair.
  • For a more natural look, use a smaller curling iron and curl your hair in different directions.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add extra body to your hair before blow-drying.

FAQ

Can you do the 1960s flip hairstyle on short hair?

The 1960s flip hairstyle is typically done with medium to long hair, but you can still achieve a similar look with short hair. Simply curl the ends of your hair under using a curling iron and flip them out using a round brush or your fingers.

How long does the 1960s flip hairstyle last?

The 1960s flip hairstyle can last for several hours or even all day with the right styling products. Use a light hairspray to hold the style in place and touch it up throughout the day as needed.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to do the 1960s flip hairstyle, give it a try and see how it looks on you. With a little practice, you'll be able to recreate this iconic hairstyle in no time.
