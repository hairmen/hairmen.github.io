What Kind Of Dog Has No Hair

Written by Christine Nov 07, 2023 · 2 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance dog that doesn't shed, you may be wondering what kind of dog has no hair. The answer is simple: the hairless dog breeds.

What Are Hairless Dog Breeds?

Hairless dog breeds are exactly what they sound like: dogs that have little to no hair on their bodies. There are several breeds of hairless dogs, including:

  • Chinese Crested
  • Xoloitzcuintli (Mexican Hairless)
  • American Hairless Terrier
  • Peruvian Inca Orchid

Why Do Hairless Dogs Exist?

Hairless dogs exist due to a genetic mutation that causes them to lack hair. While some people may find this trait unappealing, others love the unique look of hairless dogs.

How to Care for Hairless Dogs

While hairless dogs may not require regular grooming, they do have some unique care needs. Here are some tips for caring for your hairless dog:

Protect Their Skin

Without hair to protect their skin, hairless dogs are more susceptible to sunburn and other skin issues. Be sure to apply sunscreen to your dog's skin when spending time outdoors.

Bathe Them Regularly

Because hairless dogs don't have fur to absorb oils, they can get oily and dirty quickly. Bathe your hairless dog regularly to keep their skin clean and healthy.

Moisturize Their Skin

Keep your hairless dog's skin moisturized with a pet-friendly lotion or oil. This will help prevent dryness and cracking.

Frequently Asked Questions about Hairless Dogs

Are Hairless Dogs Hypoallergenic?

While hairless dogs may be a good choice for people with allergies, they are not technically hypoallergenic. This is because allergies are caused by a protein found in a dog's skin, not their hair.

Do Hairless Dogs Get Cold?

Yes, hairless dogs can get cold easily. Be sure to keep your hairless dog warm with sweaters or blankets, especially during colder months.

Are Hairless Dogs Good with Kids?

Hairless dogs can be great with kids, as long as they are socialized properly. However, it's important to supervise interactions between children and dogs to ensure everyone stays safe.

Final Thoughts

So, what kind of dog has no hair? The hairless dog breeds, of course. While they may require some extra care, hairless dogs can make great pets for those looking for a low-maintenance, unique-looking dog.
