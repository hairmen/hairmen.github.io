How To Style Hair For Beginners A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a beginner looking to style your hair but don't know where to start? Don't worry! We've got you covered. In this article, we'll guide you through the basics of hair styling, step-by-step. From choosing the right tools to creating different hairstyles, we'll cover everything you need to know to get started. So, let's dive in!

Choosing the Right Tools

The first step in styling your hair is choosing the right tools. Here are the basic tools you'll need:

  • Hairbrush or comb
  • Hair dryer
  • Hair straightener or curling iron
  • Hair clips or bobby pins
  • Hairspray or styling gel

When choosing these tools, make sure to consider your hair type and the style you want to achieve. For example, if you have thick, curly hair, you may need a more powerful hair dryer and a wider straightener or curling iron.

Washing and Drying Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Here's how to wash and dry your hair:

  1. Wet your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  2. Apply a small amount of shampoo to your scalp and massage it in with your fingertips.
  3. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  4. Apply conditioner to the ends of your hair and leave it on for a few minutes.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.
  6. Wrap your hair in a towel and gently squeeze out the excess water.
  7. Allow your hair to air dry or use a hair dryer on a low heat setting.

Make sure not to rub your hair with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Brushing and Detangling Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, it's time to brush and detangle it. Here's how:

  1. Start by brushing the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots.
  2. If you have tangles, use a wide-toothed comb or a detangling brush to gently remove them.
  3. Divide your hair into sections using hair clips or bobby pins.

Brushing your hair helps to distribute natural oils throughout your hair, which can make it look shinier and healthier.

Creating Basic Hairstyles

Now that your hair is clean and detangled, it's time to start styling! Here are some basic hairstyles you can try:

  • Straight hair: Use a hair straightener to create sleek, straight hair. Start at the roots and slowly glide the straightener down to the ends of your hair.
  • Curly hair: Use a curling iron to create bouncy, defined curls. Wrap small sections of hair around the curling iron and hold for a few seconds before releasing.
  • Ponytail: Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie.
  • Bun: Twist your hair into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.

Creating Advanced Hairstyles

If you're feeling more adventurous, here are some advanced hairstyles you can try:

  • Braids: Create different types of braids, such as a French braid or a fishtail braid.
  • Updo: Create an updo by twisting and pinning your hair into a stylish bun or chignon.
  • Half-up, half-down: Divide your hair into two sections and secure the top section with a hair tie or clip.
  • Bouncy waves: Use a curling iron to create loose, bouncy waves.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some extra tips to help you style your hair:

  • Don't use too much product. A little goes a long way.
  • Use heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Be patient. Styling your hair takes practice and patience.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's right for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or exercise frequently, you may need to wash your hair more often. If you have dry hair, you may be able to wash it less often.

Q: How can I make my hair look less frizzy?

A: Use a smoothing serum or oil to tame frizz, and avoid using hot water when washing your hair. You can also try using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

Q: Can I style my hair if it's wet?

A: It's best to wait until your hair is dry before styling it. Wet hair is more fragile and prone to damage.

Now that you know the basics of hair styling, it's time to get started! Remember to be patient and have fun experimenting with different hairstyles. With practice, you'll become a pro in no time!
