How To Style Wet Hair After Shower

Written by Petter Oct 29, 2023 · 5 min read

Styling wet hair can be tricky. If you’re someone who doesn’t have much time in the morning and prefers to take a quick shower before heading out, then you might be wondering how to style your hair when it’s still wet. Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll be discussing how to style wet hair after shower and provide you with some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Wet Hair After Shower
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Wet Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Style Wet Hair After Shower

Styling wet hair is not as difficult as it seems. Here are some easy ways to style your hair when it’s still wet:

1. Air Dry

If you have time on your hands, air drying your hair is the best option. Simply towel dry your hair and let it air dry. This will help prevent damage caused by heat styling tools.

2. Blow Dry

If you don’t have time to air dry your hair, then blow drying is the next best option. Use a heat protectant spray and a round brush to add volume and style to your hair while blow drying.

3. Braid

Braiding your hair when it’s still wet is a great way to achieve beachy waves. Simply braid your hair and let it dry naturally or use a blow dryer to speed up the process.

4. Scrunch

If you have curly hair, scrunching your hair when it’s still wet will help define your curls. Use a curl-enhancing product and scrunch your hair with your hands to achieve the desired look.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide on how to style wet hair after a shower:

Step 1: Towel Dry

After you’ve finished showering, gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water.

Step 2: Apply Product

If you’re using a styling product, apply it to your hair while it’s still wet. This will help distribute the product evenly and make it easier to style your hair.

Step 3: Style

Choose your preferred method of styling and start working on your hair. Use a round brush or your hands to style your hair and achieve the desired look.

Step 4: Finish

Once you’re happy with your hair, finish off with a hairspray or any other finishing product to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Wet Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your wet hair:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

If you’re blow drying your hair, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage caused by heat styling tools.

2. Choose the Right Product

Choose a product that suits your hair type and desired style. If you have curly hair, use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls. If you have straight hair, use a volumizing product to add volume and texture to your hair.

3. Don’t Over Style

Over styling your hair can cause damage and breakage. Avoid using too much heat or styling products on your hair.

FAQs

Q1. Can I straighten my hair when it’s wet?

No, you should never straighten your hair when it’s wet. Wet hair is more prone to damage and using a straightener on wet hair can cause breakage and split ends.

Q2. Can I use a comb on wet hair?

Yes, you can use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet. Avoid using a fine-tooth comb as it can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Q3. How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you may need to wash it less often. Generally, washing your hair 2-3 times a week is recommended.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that’s suitable for your hair type and contains moisturizing ingredients to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often as it can cause damage and breakage. If you must use them, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly will help prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy. Aim to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Styling wet hair may seem daunting at first, but with the right products and techniques, it can be easy and effortless. Experiment with different styles and find what works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair and keep it healthy by following a good hair care routine.
