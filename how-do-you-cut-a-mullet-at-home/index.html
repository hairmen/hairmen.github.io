How Do You Cut A Mullet At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of paying for haircuts and want to try something new? The mullet hairstyle has made a comeback in recent years and is now a popular look among both men and women. In this article, we will guide you through how to cut a mullet at home. We will cover everything from the tools you need to the step-by-step process, tips, and hair care. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Tools You Need
  2. How to Cut a Mullet at Home
  3. Tips for Cutting a Mullet
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Your Mullet

Tools You Need

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to have the right tools. Here are the things you need:

  • Scissors
  • Electric clippers with a guard (size 4 or 5)
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Spray bottle with water
  • Mirror

Make sure your tools are clean and sanitized before use.

How to Cut a Mullet at Home

Follow these step-by-step instructions to cut your own mullet:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Comb your hair straight down and part it in the middle.
  2. Use the electric clippers to trim the sides and back of your head. Use the guard size 4 or 5 to keep the hair length even. Start at the nape of your neck and work your way up to the crown of your head. Leave the hair on top long.
  3. Trim the hair on top using scissors. Start at the front and work your way to the back. Hold the hair straight up and cut it at an angle to create layers. Leave the hair in the back longer than the hair in the front.
  4. Use the scissors to cut the hair in the front to create the classic mullet look. Take a section of hair from the front and cut it straight across at a length that suits you. Use this section as a guide to cut the hair on either side.
  5. Blend the layers by cutting into them with scissors. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut into it in a zigzag motion. This will create a textured look.
  6. Trim the hair around your ears and neckline using the electric clippers. Use the guard size 1 or 2 to create a tapered look.
  7. Check the length of your hair in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments to the length or layers.
  8. Style your hair using your preferred method. You can use gel or mousse to create a messy or sleek look.
  9. Finish by cleaning up any stray hairs and sanitizing your tools.

Tips for Cutting a Mullet

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting a mullet:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way shorter. You can always trim more, but you can't add length back.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from different angles.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring and stressful.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help you if you need it.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a mullet:

  • How long should a mullet be?

    • A mullet can be any length, but typically the hair on top is longer than the hair on the sides and back.

  • What is the difference between a mullet and a shag?

    • A mullet has shorter hair on the sides and longer hair on top and in the back. A shag has layers all over and can be shorter or longer in length.

  • Can I cut a mullet with just scissors?

    • Yes, you can use just scissors to cut a mullet, but electric clippers make the process easier and more precise.

  • Can I cut a mullet on curly hair?

    • Yes, a mullet can look great on curly hair. Just be sure to cut the hair when it is dry and styled in its natural state.

Hair Care for Your Mullet

Now that you have your mullet, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Style your hair with products that won't damage it, such as mousse or a lightweight gel.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray when using heat tools.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.

Now you know how to cut a mullet at home. With the right tools and some patience, you can achieve this trendy look on your own. Just remember to take care of your hair and have fun experimenting with different styles!
