Mens Hairstyles For Medium Length Curly Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Petter Jul 17, 2023 · 5 min read

If you’re a man with medium length curly hair, you may be struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your hair type. Curly hair can be difficult to manage, but it also has a unique texture and volume that can be stylish and attractive. In this guide, we’ll explore the best mens hairstyles for medium length curly hair, as well as tips for hair care and maintenance.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Medium Length Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Medium Length Curly Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Medium Length Curly Hair
  • FAQs About Mens Hairstyles for Medium Length Curly Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Curly Hair

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Medium Length Curly Hair

The first step to finding the perfect mens hairstyle for medium length curly hair is to consider your face shape and hair texture. Curly hair can add volume and width to your face, so it’s important to choose a style that balances your features.

Here are some of the best mens hairstyles for medium length curly hair:

1. The Side Part

The side part is a classic style that works well with medium length curly hair. This style involves combing your hair to one side and using a styling product to keep it in place. The side part is a versatile style that can be dressed up or down, making it perfect for any occasion.

2. The Undercut

The undercut is a modern style that features short sides and a longer top. This style works well with medium length curly hair because it allows you to show off your curls while keeping your hair neat and tidy. The undercut can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and textured.

3. The Messy Curls

If you want a more natural and relaxed look, the messy curls style may be perfect for you. This style involves letting your curls flow freely and using a styling product to enhance their texture and volume. Messy curls are perfect for casual occasions and can be achieved with minimal effort.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Medium Length Curly Hair

Styling medium length curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right technique and products, you can achieve a stylish and polished look. Follow these steps to style your medium length curly hair:

1. Shampoo and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Curly hair tends to be dry, so it’s important to use products that will hydrate your hair and enhance your curls.

2. Towel Dry

Gently towel dry your hair, being careful not to rub it too vigorously. Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to minimize frizz.

3. Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a curl cream or gel. Use a small amount and distribute it evenly through your hair, focusing on the ends.

4. Comb or Finger Style

Comb your hair or use your fingers to style your curls. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently separate your curls and create volume.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Medium Length Curly Hair

Maintaining medium length curly hair requires some extra care and attention. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and vibrant:

1. Don’t Overwash

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it’s important not to overwash your hair. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Use Moisturizing Products

Use products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as moisturizing shampoos, conditioners, and styling products. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and argan oil to hydrate your hair and enhance your curls.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, which can damage your hair and cause frizz. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and set your tools to a lower temperature.

FAQs About Mens Hairstyles for Medium Length Curly Hair

1. Can I get a fade with medium length curly hair?

Yes, you can get a fade with medium length curly hair. A fade involves gradually tapering your hair from short to long, which can be achieved with curly hair.

2. How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

To prevent frizz in your curly hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, avoid overwashing your hair, and use a styling product to enhance your curls. You can also try using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without causing frizz.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for medium length curly hair:

1. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

2. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and breakage in your hair. Silk is gentle on your hair and won’t cause friction like cotton or polyester.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to shield your hair from the sun.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can rock your medium length curly hair with confidence and style.
