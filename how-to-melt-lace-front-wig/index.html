How To Melt Lace Front Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Sep 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your lace front wig looking obvious and unnatural? One way to make your wig look more natural is by melting it. Melting the lace of your wig involves using heat to blend the lace into your skin, making it look as if the hair is growing out of your scalp. In this article, we will guide you through the process of melting your lace front wig.

  • How to Melt Lace Front Wig
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Melt Lace Front Wig

Melting your lace front wig is not a difficult process, but it does require some patience and attention to detail. Here are the basic steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Cut the lace around the perimeter of the wig. Be careful not to cut too close to the hairline. Leave about 1/4 inch of lace.

Step 2: Apply wig adhesive to the perimeter of your hairline. Use a thin layer and make sure to spread it evenly.

Step 3: Use a blow dryer on low heat to dry the adhesive. This will help it become tacky and ready for the lace to be applied.

Step 4: Place the wig on your head and adjust it to fit comfortably. Use a comb to create a part where you want it.

Step 5: Use a flat iron on low heat to press the lace onto your skin. Be sure to press the lace down firmly for 10-15 seconds.

Step 6: Use a makeup sponge to apply foundation or concealer that matches your skin tone onto the lace. This will help blend the lace into your skin and make it look more natural.

Step 7: Style your wig as desired.

Tips for Melting Lace Front Wig

Tip 1: Use a wig cap to protect your natural hair and help the wig stay in place.

Tip 2: Make sure the adhesive you use is safe for skin and doesn't cause irritation.

Tip 3: Practice makes perfect. It may take a few tries to get the melting process just right.

FAQs

Q: Can you melt any type of wig?

A: No, not all wigs can be melted. Only lace front wigs that have a lace base can be melted.

Q: Is it safe to use heat on a wig?

A: Yes, it is safe to use heat on a wig as long as you use it on low heat and for a short amount of time.

Hair Care Tips for Lace Front Wig

Tip 1: Always use a wig brush or comb to detangle your wig. Do not use regular hair brushes or combs as they can damage the wig.

Tip 2: Wash your wig regularly with a wig shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep it clean and soft.

Tip 3: Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help it keep its shape.

Melting your lace front wig can take your wig game to the next level. With a little practice and patience, you can achieve a natural-looking hairline that will make your wig virtually undetectable. Follow these tips and enjoy your new, natural-looking wig!
