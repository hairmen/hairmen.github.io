How To Hide A Big Forehead With Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Aug 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a big forehead, it can be challenging to style your hair in a way that flatters your face shape. However, if you have curly hair, you have an advantage over those with straight hair. With the right techniques and styles, you can easily hide your forehead and showcase your beautiful curls. In this article, we'll show you how to hide a big forehead with curly hair.

Description

Having a big forehead can make some people feel self-conscious, but it shouldn't stop you from feeling confident and beautiful. With the right hair styling techniques, you can easily hide your forehead and feel great about yourself. If you have curly hair, you're in luck, as curls can create volume and texture that can help to hide your forehead.

How to Hide a Big Forehead with Curly Hair

Step 1: Part Your Hair to the Side

Parting your hair to the side is an easy way to hide your forehead. This technique works well for curly hair because the curls will naturally fall over your forehead, softening your face shape. To part your hair to the side, use a comb or your fingers to create a deep part on one side of your head.

Step 2: Use a Volumizing Product

Using a volumizing product can help to create volume and texture in your hair, which can help to hide your forehead. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to damp hair, then blow-dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots. This will help to create a fuller, more voluminous look that will help to hide your forehead.

Step 3: Use a Curling Iron or Wand

Using a curling iron or wand can help to create defined curls that can hide your forehead. To create curls, take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron or wand. Hold the curl for a few seconds, then release. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 4: Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories can be a great way to hide your forehead. Try wearing a headband or scarf to push your curls back and create a more voluminous look. You can also try using bobby pins or hair clips to pin your curls back and create a more defined look.

Tips

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to create more volume in your curls.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your curls and make them look flat.
  • When using a curling iron or wand, be careful not to burn your scalp or hair.

FAQ

Can I still wear my hair up if I have a big forehead?

Absolutely! You can still wear your hair up if you have a big forehead. Try a messy bun or a high ponytail to create a more voluminous look that will help to hide your forehead.

What should I do if my curls are frizzy?

If your curls are frizzy, try using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to smooth them out. You can also try using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to create a more defined look.

What should I do if my curls are flat?

If your curls are flat, try using a volumizing product or blow-drying your hair upside down to create more volume at the roots. You can also try using a curling iron or wand to create more defined curls.

Hair Care

Caring for curly hair can be challenging, but with the right techniques and products, you can keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily hide your big forehead and showcase your beautiful curly hair. Remember to experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you. With a little practice, you'll be able to create a look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
