How To Fade A Guy s Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for an edgy and stylish haircut? Fading a guy's haircut can give you the perfect look. It's a technique that involves gradually blending the hair from short to long, creating a faded effect. In this post, we'll show you how to fade a guy's haircut in simple steps.

How to Fade a Guy's Haircut?

Fading a guy's haircut is a simple process that requires a little patience and attention to detail. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step is to decide on the length of your haircut. You can choose to have a long or short haircut. The length of your hair will determine how much you need to fade.

Step 2: Choose Your Clippers

Choose the clippers that you're comfortable with. It's always better to use clippers with adjustable blades because they allow you to create different lengths. A good pair of clippers will make your work easier and give you the desired results.

Step 3: Start with the Back and Sides

Start by trimming the back and sides of your hair, using the shortest guard on your clippers. Work your way up gradually, changing the guard as you go along. Remember to blend the hair in between the guards, to achieve a seamless fade.

Step 4: Trim the Top

After trimming the back and sides, move to the top of your hair. Use scissors to trim the top, leaving it longer than the sides. The length of the top will depend on your preference, but it's always good to leave it long enough to style.

Step 5: Blend the Top and Sides

The next step is to blend the top and sides of your hair. Use the clippers to blend the hair at the top into the sides, using a longer guard than the one used on the sides. This will create a seamless transition between the top and sides.

Step 6: Clean Up the Edges

Use the clippers to clean up the edges around your hairline and neck. This will give you a clean and polished look.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

After fading your hair, it's time to style it. Use your preferred styling product to achieve the desired look. You can use a pomade, gel, or wax to create different styles.

Tips for Fading a Guy's Haircut

Here are some tips to make your fading process easier:

  • Start with longer guards and work your way down gradually.
  • Blend the hair in between the guards to achieve a seamless fade.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head for any uneven spots.
  • Ask someone for help if you're having difficulty with the back of your head.
  • Use scissors to trim the top of your hair to leave it longer than the sides.

FAQs about Fading a Guy's Haircut

1. How long does a faded haircut last?

A faded haircut can last for up to two weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

2. Can you fade your own hair?

Yes, you can fade your own hair, but it's always good to have someone help you with the back of your head.

3. How do I maintain my faded haircut?

Maintain your faded haircut by trimming it regularly, using styling products, and keeping it clean.

Hair Care for a Faded Haircut

To keep your hair healthy and shiny after fading, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, which can damage it.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Fading a guy's haircut is a simple process that can give you a stylish and edgy look. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to achieve the perfect fade. Remember, practice makes perfect, so keep trying until you get the desired look.
