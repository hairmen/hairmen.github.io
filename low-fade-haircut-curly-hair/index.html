Low Fade Haircut Curly Hair A Guide To The Perfect Cut

Written by Eveline Jun 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles for your curly hair? Do you want a haircut that is stylish, low-maintenance, and easy to maintain? Look no further than the low fade haircut for curly hair. In this guide, we will show you how to get the perfect low fade haircut for your curly locks.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair care

Description

The low fade haircut for curly hair is a popular hairstyle that involves cutting the hair shorter on the sides and gradually fading it towards the top. This style is perfect for those with curly hair as it allows the curls to be showcased while keeping the hair neat and tidy.

The low fade haircut is also versatile and can be customized to suit your personal style. You can choose to have the fade start high or low, and you can also opt for a skin fade or a taper fade depending on your preference.

How to

Getting the perfect low fade haircut for curly hair requires a skilled barber who understands the nuances of curly hair. Here are some tips to help you find the right barber:

  • Look for a barber who specializes in curly hair
  • Read reviews and ask for recommendations
  • Bring in pictures of the style you want

Step by step

Once you have found the right barber, here are the steps they will follow to give you the perfect low fade haircut:

  1. Clean and dampen your hair
  2. Cut the sides of your hair to your desired length
  3. Taper the hair towards the top to create the fade
  4. Trim the top of your hair to your desired length
  5. Blend the hair on the sides and top to create a seamless look

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your low fade haircut for curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated
  • Avoid brushing your curls as this can cause frizz
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your curls
  • Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to enhance your curls

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the low fade haircut for curly hair:

Is the low fade haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the low fade haircut is versatile and can be customized to suit any face shape.

How often do I need to get a trim?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your low fade haircut.

Hair care

Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your curls
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel as this can cause frizz
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent breakage

With these tips, you can rock the perfect low fade haircut for curly hair. Remember to find a skilled barber, maintain your hair with the right products, and care for your curls to keep them healthy and hydrated.
