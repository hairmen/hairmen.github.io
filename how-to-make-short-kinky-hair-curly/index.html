How To Make Short Kinky Hair Curly A Step By Step Guide

If you have short kinky hair, you might be wondering how to make it curly. Well, the good news is that there are a variety of methods you can use to achieve curly hair. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to make short kinky hair curly, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Make Short Kinky Hair Curly

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it's important to note that there are a variety of methods you can use to make your hair curly. Some people prefer using heat, while others prefer using no-heat methods. The method you choose will depend on your hair type, the results you're looking for, and your personal preference.

Here are some of the most popular methods for making short kinky hair curly:

  • Heat tools, such as curling irons, hot rollers, and flat irons
  • No-heat methods, such as twist-outs, braid-outs, and rod sets
  • Chemical treatments, such as perms and texturizers

Step-by-Step Guide

Depending on the method you choose, the steps for making short kinky hair curly will vary. Here's a step-by-step guide for some of the most popular methods:

Heat Tools

If you're using heat tools to make your hair curly, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant product to your hair.
  3. Section your hair into small sections.
  4. Take one section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron, hot roller, or flat iron.
  5. Hold the tool in place for a few seconds, then release.
  6. Repeat until all sections of your hair are curled.
  7. Finish with a hairspray or other holding product.

No-Heat Methods

If you're using no-heat methods to make your hair curly, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as a curl defining cream or gel.
  3. Section your hair into small sections.
  4. Take one section of hair and twist it or braid it tightly.
  5. Repeat until all sections of your hair are twisted or braided.
  6. Allow your hair to air dry completely.
  7. Untwist or unbraid your hair, then use your fingers to separate the curls.
  8. Finish with a hairspray or other holding product.

Chemical Treatments

If you're getting a chemical treatment to make your hair curly, such as a perm or texturizer, follow these steps:

  1. Choose a reputable stylist who has experience with your hair type.
  2. Discuss your desired results with your stylist.
  3. Prepare your hair by washing and conditioning it.
  4. Apply the chemical treatment to your hair, following the instructions provided by your stylist.
  5. Allow the treatment to process for the recommended amount of time.
  6. Rinse your hair with water, then apply a neutralizer to stop the chemical process.
  7. Allow your hair to air dry, then style as desired.

Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making short kinky hair curly:

How long will my curls last?

The length of time your curls will last depends on the method you use and how well you maintain your hair. Heat-styled curls may last a day or two, while no-heat curls can last up to a week with proper care.

Can I make my hair curly without using any products?

While it's technically possible to make your hair curly without using any products, it's not recommended. Using a styling product can help define your curls and keep them in place.

Will making my hair curly damage it?

If you use heat tools on a regular basis, it can cause damage to your hair over time. However, if you use no-heat methods or get a chemical treatment from a professional, the risk of damage is lower.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some general tips for caring for short kinky hair:

  • Wash your hair once or twice a week, depending on how oily your scalp is.
  • Avoid using hot water when you wash your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh hair products, such as those that contain alcohol or sulfates.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant product.

With these tips and methods, you can make your short kinky hair curly and keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Experiment with different methods and products to find what works best for you.
