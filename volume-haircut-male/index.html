Achieve A Stylish Look With A Volume Haircut Male

Written by Petter Sep 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle and add some volume to your locks? A volume haircut male may be just what you need. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want to add some texture and dimension to their hair, while still maintaining a clean and polished look. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about achieving a volume haircut male, including the process, tips, and maintenance.

What is a Volume Haircut Male?

A volume haircut male is a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of the head, while keeping the sides and back short. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want to add some texture and dimension to their hair. The volume is achieved by layering the hair on top and using a blow dryer to style it upwards.

How to Achieve a Volume Haircut Male

Achieving a volume haircut male is a process that involves cutting the sides and back short, while leaving the top longer. Here are the steps to achieve this hairstyle:

Step 1: Find a Good Barber

Finding a good barber is the first step to achieving a great volume haircut male. Look for a barber who has experience with this hairstyle and can give you a clean and polished look.

Step 2: Determine the Length

The length of the hair on top will determine the amount of volume you can achieve. Longer hair will give you more volume, while shorter hair will give you less. Discuss with your barber the best length for your hair type and face shape.

Step 3: Layer the Hair

Layering the hair on top is important to achieve the desired volume. This can be done by cutting the hair at different lengths, creating a textured look.

Step 4: Blow Dry and Style

Using a blow dryer, style the hair upwards to achieve the desired volume. Apply a small amount of product to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining a Volume Haircut Male

Maintaining a volume haircut male requires regular maintenance and styling. Here are some tips to keep your hairstyle looking great:

  • Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks for a trim
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Apply a small amount of product to hold the style in place
  • Blow dry your hair upwards to maintain the volume

FAQ

How do I know if a volume haircut male is right for me?

A volume haircut male is perfect for men who want to add some texture and dimension to their hair, while still maintaining a clean and polished look. Consult with your barber to determine if this hairstyle is right for you.

How often should I visit my barber for a trim?

It is recommended to visit your barber every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain the shape and style of your volume haircut male.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any hairstyle. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair

Achieving a volume haircut male is a great way to switch up your hairstyle and add some texture and dimension to your hair. With the right barber and regular maintenance, you can achieve a stylish look that is sure to turn heads.
