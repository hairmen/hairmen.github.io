How To Cut Front Bangs For Curly Hair

Written by Bardi Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your curly hair getting in your face all the time? Do you want to try out a new look? Cutting front bangs can give you a fresh, new look without sacrificing your curls. In this article, we'll go over the steps to cut front bangs for curly hair and provide tips on how to care for them.

Description

Front bangs are a popular hairstyle that can frame your face and give you a more youthful appearance. However, cutting bangs for curly hair can be tricky. Unlike straight hair, curly hair tends to have more volume and bounce, which can affect the way the bangs fall. That's why it's important to follow the right steps to achieve the perfect look.

How to Cut Front Bangs for Curly Hair

Here are the steps to follow when cutting front bangs for curly hair:

Step 1: Determine the Length

Decide how long you want your bangs to be. It's best to start with longer bangs and gradually cut them shorter if you want a more dramatic look. Use hair clips to separate your bangs from the rest of your hair.

Step 2: Cut the Bangs

Using sharp scissors, cut the bangs straight across. Be sure to cut them slightly longer than your desired length to allow for shrinkage. If you're not sure how short to cut them, start with a small trim and work your way up.

Step 3: Check the Length

After cutting your bangs, let them down and check the length in the mirror. If they're too long, trim them a little shorter. If they're too short, you'll have to wait for them to grow out.

Step 4: Style the Bangs

Apply a styling product to your bangs to keep them in place. You can use a gel, mousse, or curl cream. Scrunch your hair gently to enhance your curls and give your bangs a more natural look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting front bangs for curly hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends
  • Cut your bangs when your hair is dry so you can see the length better
  • Start with longer bangs and gradually cut them shorter
  • Trim your bangs slowly â you can always cut more but you can't add hair back

FAQ

How often should I trim my bangs?

It's best to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Can I cut my bangs myself?

While it's possible to cut your own bangs, it's recommended that you have a professional stylist do it for you. They have the experience and knowledge to give you the best results.

What if I don't like my bangs?

If you're not happy with your bangs, don't worry! Bangs grow out quickly, so you can always wait for them to grow out or have them trimmed shorter. In the meantime, you can try styling them differently or using hair accessories to keep them out of your face.

Hair Care

Caring for your bangs is important to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep your curls bouncy
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your bangs
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much â the oils from your fingers can weigh them down
