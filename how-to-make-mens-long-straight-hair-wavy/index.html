How To Make Men s Long Straight Hair Wavy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a man with long straight hair, you may sometimes feel like your hair is lacking in texture and volume. One way to add some interest to your hairstyle is by creating waves. In this article, we'll show you how to make men's long straight hair wavy in a relaxed and easy-to-understand way. We'll cover the basics, step-by-step instructions, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care. So, let's get started!

The Basics

Before we get started, it's important to understand some basics about hair. Hair is made up of a protein called keratin, and the texture of your hair is determined by the shape of the hair follicle. Straight hair has round hair follicles, while curly hair has oval-shaped hair follicles. To create waves in straight hair, you'll need to train your hair to hold a new shape. This can be done through heat styling, chemical treatments, or natural methods.

How to Make Men's Long Straight Hair Wavy

There are several ways you can make men's long straight hair wavy:

  • Using a curling iron
  • Using a hair straightener
  • Using braids or twists
  • Using a salt spray or texturizing spray

In this article, we'll focus on using a hair straightener to create waves. This method is popular because it's easy to do and doesn't require any special tools or products.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here's how to make men's long straight hair wavy using a hair straightener:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, it will be more difficult to create waves.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help to prevent damage from the heat of the hair straightener.
  3. Section your hair into small sections. The smaller the section, the more defined your waves will be.
  4. Take a section of hair and clamp the hair straightener at the root.
  5. Slowly rotate the hair straightener 180 degrees away from your face.
  6. Hold the hair straightener in place for a few seconds.
  7. Release the hair straightener and gently tug on the end of the hair to release the wave.
  8. Repeat steps 4-7 on all sections of your hair.
  9. Once you've created waves in all sections of your hair, gently comb through your hair with your fingers to blend the waves together.
  10. Finish with a texturizing spray or hairspray to help hold the waves in place.

Tips for Making Men's Long Straight Hair Wavy

Here are some tips to help you make men's long straight hair wavy:

  • Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is oily or greasy, it will be more difficult to create waves.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the heat of the hair straightener.
  • Section your hair into small sections for more defined waves.
  • Rotate the hair straightener away from your face for a more natural look.
  • Don't hold the hair straightener in place for too long, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a texturizing spray or hairspray to help hold the waves in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make my hair wavy without heat?

Yes, you can make your hair wavy without heat by using braids or twists. Simply braid or twist your hair while it's damp, leave it to dry, and then undo the braids or twists for natural-looking waves.

How long will the waves last?

The length of time your waves will last depends on your hair type, the products you use, and how well you maintain your hairstyle. On average, waves created with a hair straightener can last up to 2-3 days with proper care.

Can I use a curling iron to make my hair wavy?

Yes, you can use a curling iron to make your hair wavy. However, this method can be more difficult to master and may take longer to achieve the desired result.

Hair Care for Men's Long Straight Hair

Regardless of whether you're making your hair wavy or keeping it straight, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too frequently, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can make men's long straight hair wavy in no time. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
