How To Make A Stylish Ponytail Step By Step

Written by Aprilia Sep 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this year 2023, ponytails are still a popular hairstyle choice for both casual and formal occasions. However, it's easy for a simple ponytail to look boring and unflattering. That's why we've put together this step-by-step guide on how to make a stylish ponytail that will turn heads. From prep to finishing touches, we'll cover all the details.

Table of Contents

  • Prep
  • Sectioning
  • Tie the Ponytail
  • Accessorize
  • Finishing Touches
  • Hair Care
  • FAQ

Prep

Before starting, make sure your hair is clean and combed through. If you have curly or wavy hair, consider straightening it first for a sleeker look. You can also add some texture by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo.

Next, gather all the tools you'll need, including a brush or comb, hair elastics, bobby pins, and any accessories you want to use.

Sectioning

Divide your hair into two sections, with the top half pinned up or clipped out of the way. Take the bottom section and tie it into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.

For a more dramatic look, you can also create a side part and pull all your hair to one side before tying it into a ponytail.

Tie the Ponytail

Now that you have your base ponytail, it's time to add some style. You can create a sleek, polished look by using a brush or comb to smooth out any bumps or flyaways. Alternatively, you can create a messy, textured look by using your fingers to gently pull at the hair around the crown of your head.

Once you've achieved your desired look, use a hair elastic to tie off the ponytail. Make sure it's tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it causes discomfort.

Accessorize

To take your ponytail to the next level, consider adding some accessories. A simple ribbon tied around the base of the ponytail can add a pop of color and femininity. A decorative hair clip or barrette can add some sparkle and glamour.

For a more casual look, you can also use a scrunchie or hair tie in a fun print or color.

Finishing Touches

Once you've added your accessories, take a moment to adjust the ponytail and make any final tweaks. You can pull some strands of hair loose around your face for a softer, more romantic look. Alternatively, you can use bobby pins to tuck in any loose ends or flyaways.

Hair Care

To keep your ponytail looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Be gentle when brushing or combing, and avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too frequently.

You can also apply a hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to keep them moisturized and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Can I wear a ponytail to a formal occasion?

Yes, a stylish ponytail can be a great choice for a formal occasion. Just make sure to choose an elegant accessory and keep your hair sleek and polished.

What if my hair is too short for a ponytail?

If your hair is too short for a traditional ponytail, you can try a half-up, half-down style or a low bun. You can also use hair extensions to add length and volume.

How often should I wash my hair?

This will depend on your hair type and personal preference. Some people can go several days between washes, while others prefer to wash their hair daily. Experiment and find what works best for you.

With these step-by-step instructions, you can achieve a stylish ponytail that will turn heads. Whether you're getting ready for a night out or just running errands, a ponytail is a versatile and timeless hairstyle that will never go out of style.
