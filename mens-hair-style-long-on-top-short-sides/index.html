Mens Hair Style Long On Top Short Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Sep 21, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking for a versatile hairstyle that is both trendy and classic, mens hair style long on top short sides might be the perfect choice for you. This look has been around for years and remains popular among men of all ages. In this article, we’ll show you how to achieve this look, provide some tips on how to maintain it, and answer some frequently asked questions about this hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The mens hair style long on top short sides is a haircut that features longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. This hairstyle can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a side part. It is a versatile haircut that works for all hair types and face shapes.

How to

To achieve the mens hair style long on top short sides, you will need to have at least three inches of hair on top of your head. The hair on the sides and back should be trimmed short, usually with clippers. The length of the hair on the sides and back can vary depending on your preference, but a good rule of thumb is to keep it less than an inch long.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieve the mens hair style long on top short sides:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow dry your hair until it is completely dry.
  3. Using a comb or your fingers, create a part on one side of your head.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair product, such as pomade or wax, to your hands and work it through your hair from the roots to the ends.
  5. Style your hair as desired, either slicked back, messy, or with a side part.
  6. Use a little more product to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens hair style long on top short sides:

  • Get regular trims to keep the hair on the sides and back looking neat.
  • Use a high-quality hair product to keep your hair in place and looking great all day.
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • If you have curly hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls.
  • If you have straight hair, use a round brush while blow drying to add volume and texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens hair style long on top short sides:

What face shape does this hairstyle work best for?

This hairstyle works well for all face shapes, but it is particularly flattering for men with round or square faces.

What hair type does this hairstyle work best for?

This hairstyle works well for all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and thick hair.

What hair products should I use?

You can use a variety of hair products to achieve this hairstyle, including pomade, wax, gel, or mousse.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner every other day.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Now that you know everything there is to know about the mens hair style long on top short sides, it’s time to book your appointment with your hairstylist and try it out for yourself. With the right care and styling, this timeless haircut is sure to turn heads and make you look your best.
