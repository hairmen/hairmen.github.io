Description

Perms, or permanent waves, have been a popular hairstyle for decades. However, with the rise of natural hair care and the popularity of straight hair, many people wonder if perms are out of style in 2021. While it's true that perms may not be as trendy as they once were, they are still a popular hairstyle option for those who want to add volume and texture to their hair. In this blog post, we'll explore whether or not perms are out of style in 2021 and provide tips on how to care for and style permed hair.

How To

If you're considering getting a perm, there are a few things you should know before making your decision. First, perms can be damaging to your hair if not done correctly. It's important to find a qualified stylist who has experience with perming hair. Second, the type of perm you get will depend on your hair type and desired outcome. There are different types of perms, including spiral, body wave, and root perm. Talk to your stylist about which type of perm would be best for you.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting a perm:

  1. Consult with your stylist about the type of perm you want and whether or not your hair is healthy enough to withstand the process.
  2. Prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue.
  3. Section your hair and wrap it around perm rods. The size and shape of the rods will depend on the type of perm you're getting.
  4. Apply the perm solution to your hair and let it sit for the recommended amount of time.
  5. Rinse the perm solution out of your hair and apply a neutralizer to set the curls.
  6. Remove the perm rods and style your hair as desired.

Tips

If you decide to get a perm, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wait at least 48 hours before washing your hair after getting a perm.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your permed hair, as they can cause damage and frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your permed hair, as brushes can disrupt the curl pattern.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly to keep it healthy and hydrated.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about perms:

Q: How long does a perm last?

A: A perm can last anywhere from 2 to 6 months, depending on your hair type and how well you take care of it.

Q: Can you color your hair after getting a perm?

A: It's best to wait at least a week after getting a perm before coloring your hair. This will give your hair time to recover from the perming process.

Q: Can you swim with a perm?

A: It's best to avoid swimming in chlorinated water with a perm, as the chemicals can cause damage to your hair. If you do swim, wear a swim cap and rinse your hair with fresh water afterwards.

Hair Care

If you decide to get a perm, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for permed hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for permed or curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can cause damage and frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing cream to your hair to keep it moisturized and defined.
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, perms may not be as popular as they once were, but they are still a great hairstyle option for those who want to add volume and texture to their hair. If you're considering getting a perm, make sure to talk to a qualified stylist and take good care of your hair afterwards. With the right care and styling, permed hair can look beautiful and stylish in 2021 and beyond.
