How To Make Your Hair Smooth When Straightening

Written by Christine Sep 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of having frizzy, unruly hair after straightening? It's a common problem, but with the right technique and products, you can achieve smooth, sleek locks. In this article, we'll show you how to make your hair smooth when straightening, step by step. Follow these tips to get the smoothest, shiniest hair of your dreams.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The key to making your hair smooth when straightening is to prepare your hair properly. Start by washing your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner. These products will help to tame frizz and leave your hair feeling soft and silky. After washing, towel-dry your hair until it's damp but not dripping wet.

Next, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help to protect your hair from heat damage and prevent breakage. Divide your hair into sections and use a comb to detangle each section. This will make it easier to straighten your hair and ensure that the heat is evenly distributed.

When straightening your hair, use a flat iron with ceramic plates. Ceramic plates are less damaging to your hair than metal plates and will help to distribute heat evenly. Start at the roots of your hair and slowly glide the flat iron down to the ends. Repeat this process until all sections of your hair are straightened.

Step by Step

1. Wash your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp.

3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.

4. Divide your hair into sections and detangle each section with a comb.

5. Use a flat iron with ceramic plates to straighten your hair.

Tips

- Don't straighten your hair when it's soaking wet. This can cause damage and make your hair frizzy.

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before straightening.

- Don't use too much heat. Use the lowest heat setting that works for your hair type.

- Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my hair without a heat protectant?

A: We don't recommend it. Heat protectant sprays help to prevent damage and breakage.

Q: How often should I straighten my hair?

A: We recommend only straightening your hair once or twice a week to prevent damage.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair to keep it smooth and healthy. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to moisturize your hair and prevent breakage. Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Finally, trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking its best.

By following these tips, you can make your hair smooth when straightening and achieve the sleek, shiny locks of your dreams. Remember to take good care of your hair and use the right products to prevent damage and keep your hair looking its best.
