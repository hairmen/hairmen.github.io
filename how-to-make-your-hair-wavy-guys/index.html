How To Make Your Hair Wavy Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, making your hair wavy is a great option. Not only does it add texture and volume to your hair, but it's also a versatile style that can be dressed up or down. In this guide, we'll show you how to make your hair wavy guys with a few easy steps.

How to Make Your Hair Wavy Guys

Making your hair wavy guys involves a few simple steps that you can easily do at home. Here's how:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt or oil from your hair and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Apply Mousse or Sea Salt Spray

Next, apply a small amount of mousse or sea salt spray to your hair. This will help to add texture and hold to your hair, making it easier to create waves.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair until it is about 80% dry. This will help to set the waves in your hair.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four equal parts, using hair ties or clips to keep them in place.

Step 5: Create Waves

Starting with one section of hair, take small sections of hair and wrap them around a curling iron or wand. Hold for a few seconds, then release. Repeat this process for all sections of hair.

Step 6: Shake Out Your Hair

Once you've created waves in all sections of your hair, gently shake out your hair with your fingers. This will help to loosen the waves and create a natural-looking style.

Step 7: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your wavy hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to hold the waves in place and prevent them from falling out throughout the day.

Tips for Making Your Hair Wavy Guys

Here are a few tips to help you make your hair wavy guys:

  • Use a good-quality curling iron or wand to create waves in your hair.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil to the ends of your hair to add shine and prevent frizz.
  • Experiment with different types of mousse or sea salt spray to find the right product for your hair.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to create more defined waves.

FAQs About Making Your Hair Wavy Guys

1. How long does it take to make your hair wavy guys?

Making your hair wavy guys can take anywhere from 20-30 minutes, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

2. Can you make your hair wavy guys without heat?

Yes, you can make your hair wavy guys without heat by braiding your hair when it's damp and leaving it overnight. When you take out the braids in the morning, you'll have natural-looking waves.

3. How often should you make your hair wavy guys?

You can make your hair wavy guys as often as you like, but it's important to give your hair a break from heat styling every once in a while to prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

If you have wavy hair, here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for wavy hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Apply a weekly hair mask to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Making your hair wavy guys is a great way to switch up your hairstyle and add some texture and volume to your hair. With a few easy steps and the right products, you can create natural-looking waves that will last all day.
