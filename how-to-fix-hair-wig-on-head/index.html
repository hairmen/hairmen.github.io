How To Fix Hair Wig On Head Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Oct 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

A hair wig can be a great option for those who want to change their hairstyle or cover hair loss. However, if the wig is not fixed properly, it can cause discomfort and embarrassment. In this article, we will provide you with some tips and tricks on how to fix hair wig on head.

How to Fix Hair Wig on Head

There are several methods to fix hair wig on head, such as using wig clips, adhesive tapes, or glue. The method you choose will depend on your preference and the type of wig you have. Here are some steps you can follow to fix hair wig on head:

Using Wig Clips

Wig clips are small combs that attach to the wig and your natural hair. They are easy to use and provide a secure hold. Here's how to use wig clips to fix hair wig on head:

  1. Part your natural hair where you want to place the wig.
  2. Attach the wig clips to the wig.
  3. Place the wig on your head and secure the clips to your natural hair.
  4. Adjust the wig to make sure it's in the right position.

Using Adhesive Tapes

Adhesive tapes are double-sided tapes that stick to both the wig and your scalp. They provide a natural look and are easy to remove. Here's how to use adhesive tapes to fix hair wig on head:

  1. Clean your scalp with alcohol to remove any oils or dirt.
  2. Cut the adhesive tapes to the desired length.
  3. Stick the adhesive tapes to the wig.
  4. Place the wig on your head and press it firmly.
  5. Remove the protective film from the tapes.
  6. Stick the tapes to your scalp.
  7. Adjust the wig to make sure it's in the right position.

Using Glue

Glue is a strong adhesive that provides a secure hold for the wig. However, it can be difficult to remove and may cause irritation to the scalp. Here's how to use glue to fix hair wig on head:

  1. Clean your scalp with alcohol to remove any oils or dirt.
  2. Apply a thin layer of glue to your scalp using a brush or cotton swab.
  3. Wait for the glue to dry for a few minutes.
  4. Place the wig on your head and press it firmly.
  5. Adjust the wig to make sure it's in the right position.

Tips for Fixing Hair Wig on Head

Here are some tips that can help you fix hair wig on head:

  • Choose the right type of wig for your hair type and face shape.
  • Use a wig cap to protect your natural hair and provide a smooth surface for the wig.
  • Avoid using too much glue or adhesive tapes, as it can damage your natural hair.
  • Use a wig stand to store the wig when not in use.
  • Wash the wig regularly to maintain its shape and quality.

FAQ

How long does a hair wig last?

The lifespan of a hair wig depends on several factors, such as the quality of the wig, how often it's worn, and how it's maintained. A good quality hair wig can last up to a year or more with proper care.

Can I wear a hair wig while swimming?

It's not recommended to wear a hair wig while swimming, as chlorine and saltwater can damage the wig. If you need to wear a wig while swimming, it's best to use a waterproof wig or a swimming cap to protect the wig.

Can I style a hair wig with heat tools?

Most hair wigs can be styled with heat tools, such as curling irons or straighteners. However, it's important to use low heat settings and avoid applying heat directly to the wig's roots or cap, as it can damage the wig.

Hair Care for Hair Wig

Taking care of your hair wig is essential to maintain its quality and lifespan. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair wig:

  • Wash the wig with a mild shampoo and lukewarm water.
  • Avoid rubbing or twisting the wig, as it can cause tangles or damage.
  • Use a conditioner to keep the wig soft and shiny.
  • Dry the wig with a towel and let it air dry on a wig stand.
  • Avoid exposing the wig to direct sunlight or heat sources, as it can damage the fibers.

In conclusion, fixing a hair wig on head can be easy and comfortable if you follow the right steps and use the right method. By choosing the right type of wig and taking care of it properly, you can enjoy a new hairstyle and boost your confidence.
