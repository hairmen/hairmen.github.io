If you have a round face, you may find it difficult to choose the right hair cut. A good hair cut can help to balance your features and create a more flattering look. In this article, we'll show you some of the best hair cuts for round face woman 2019, along with tips on how to choose the right style and take care of your hair.

How to Choose the Right Hair Cut for Round Face

The first step to finding the right hair cut for round face woman 2019 is to determine your face shape. A round face is characterized by a width that is almost equal to its length, with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The goal of a hair cut for a round face is to create the illusion of a more oval shape by adding height and volume to the crown of the head.

Some of the best hair cuts for round face woman 2019 are:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Chin-length bobs
  • Pixie cuts
  • Asymmetrical cuts

When choosing a hair cut, it's important to consider your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid heavy layers that can weigh down your hair. If you have curly hair, you may want to avoid blunt cuts that can create a triangular shape.

Face-Framing Layers

Face-framing layers can help to create the illusion of a longer face by drawing attention away from the cheeks. These layers can be added to long or medium-length hair, and can be styled with a side-swept part or layered bangs.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to elongate the face by creating a diagonal line that draws the eye upward. These bangs can be paired with any length of hair, from long layers to a short pixie cut.

Chin-Length Bobs

Chin-length bobs can help to create the illusion of a longer face by ending at the jawline. These bobs can be styled straight or with soft waves, and can be paired with side-swept or blunt bangs.

Pixie Cuts

Pixie cuts can be a great option for round face woman who want a low-maintenance hair cut. These cuts can be styled with layers or bangs to add height to the crown of the head, and can be paired with bold accessories like statement earrings or glasses.

Asymmetrical Cuts

Asymmetrical cuts can help to create the illusion of a longer face by adding height to one side of the head. These cuts can be paired with side-swept or blunt bangs, and can be styled with soft waves or straight.

Step-by-Step Guide to Get the Perfect Hair Cut

Now that you know which hair cuts work best for round face woman 2019, it's time to head to the salon. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect hair cut:

  1. Choose the right stylist. Look for a stylist who specializes in hair cuts for round face woman.
  2. Bring pictures. Bring pictures of hair cuts that you like to show your stylist.
  3. Communicate your needs. Be clear about what you want and don't want in your hair cut.
  4. Trust your stylist. Your stylist may have suggestions for hair cuts that you haven't considered.
  5. Ask for styling tips. Your stylist can show you how to style your new hair cut at home.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Cut

Once you've gotten the perfect hair cut, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking great:

  • Get regular trims. Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use the right products. Use products that are designed for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid heat styling. Heat styling can damage your hair, so try to limit your use of blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons.
  • Protect your hair from the sun. Wear a hat or use a UV-protective spray to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

FAQs: Hair Cut for Round Face Woman 2019

What is the best hair cut for a round face?

The best hair cut for a round face is one that adds height and volume to the crown of the head, and lengthens the face. Some of the best hair cuts for round face woman 2019 are long layers, side-swept bangs, chin-length bobs, pixie cuts, and asymmetrical cuts.

Can I wear a bob with a round face?

Yes, you can wear a bob with a round face. Chin-length bobs can be a great option for round face woman, as they can help to create the illusion of a longer face.

What hair cut should I avoid with a round face?

You should avoid hair cuts that add width to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear rounder. Avoid blunt cuts, heavy bangs, and styles that are too short or too long.

Hair Care Tips for Round Face Woman

In addition to getting the right hair cut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some hair care tips for round face woman:

  • Shampoo and condition regularly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid over-washing. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, so try to wash your hair every other day.
  • Use a hair mask. Use a deep conditioning hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles. Tight hairstyles like ponytails and braids can pull on your hair and cause damage.
  • Get regular trims. Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With the right hair cut and hair care routine, you can look and feel your best as a round face woman in 2019.
