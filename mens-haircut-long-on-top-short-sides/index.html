Description

The mens haircut long on top short sides is a classic and versatile hairstyle that has been popular for years. This hairstyle features longer hair on the top of the head, which gradually gets shorter towards the sides and back. The length on top can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. This haircut is perfect for men who want a stylish and low-maintenance look that can be easily adapted to their personal style.

How to Get a Mens Haircut Long on Top Short Sides

If you're interested in getting a mens haircut long on top short sides, it's important to find a skilled barber or stylist who has experience with this particular cut. You'll also want to bring in some photos of the style you're interested in, so your stylist can get a better idea of what you're looking for.

Keywords: mens haircut long on top short sides, barber, stylist, photos

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a mens haircut long on top short sides:

  1. Find a skilled barber or stylist who has experience with this particular cut.
  2. Bring in photos of the style you're interested in.
  3. Discuss your hair type and personal style with your stylist to determine the best approach for your cut.
  4. Your stylist will start by trimming the hair on the sides and back of your head to the desired length.
  5. The hair on the top of your head will be cut to the desired length and styled to your preference.
  6. Your stylist will finish the cut by blending the hair on the sides and top together for a seamless look.

Keywords: step by step guide, haircut, trimming, blending

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Haircut Long on Top Short Sides

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens haircut long on top short sides:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Invest in a good quality styling product, such as pomade or wax, to help you achieve your desired look.
  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Keywords: maintaining, shampoo, conditioner, styling product, trims, styling techniques

FAQs

Q: Is the mens haircut long on top short sides suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can be adapted to suit a variety of face shapes.

Q: How often should I wash my hair with this haircut?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference, but most men find that washing their hair every 2-3 days is sufficient.

Q: Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

A: Absolutely! This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit different occasions and personal style preferences.

Keywords: FAQs, face shapes, wash, occasions, style preferences

Hair Care for Mens Haircut Long on Top Short Sides

Caring for your hair is important to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care for mens haircut long on top short sides:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as hair dryers or straighteners, as much as possible to minimize damage to your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Keywords: hair care, gentle shampoo, conditioner, heat styling tools, leave-in conditioner, hair oil, healthy diet, hair growth

In conclusion, the mens haircut long on top short sides is a classic and versatile hairstyle that can be adapted to suit a variety of personal styles and occasions. With the right care and maintenance, this hairstyle can look great for weeks on end. So, find a skilled barber or stylist, bring in some photos, and try out this stylish and low-maintenance look for yourself!
