How To Get Big Poofy Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 15, 2023 · 5 min read

Do you have naturally straight or wavy hair and want to achieve big, poofy, curly hair? Look no further! In this article, we’ll give you a step-by-step guide on how to get big poofy curly hair that’ll leave you feeling confident and beautiful.

How to Get Big Poofy Curly Hair

Getting big poofy curly hair isn’t difficult, but it does require some specific techniques and products. Here are the key steps you need to follow:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue. Follow up with a moisturizing conditioner to ensure your hair is hydrated and ready for styling.

2. Apply a Volumizing Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your damp hair, focusing on the roots. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer and a round brush, dry your hair in sections. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, pulling the brush through your hair as you go. This will help create volume and lift at the roots.

4. Section Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, section it into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure each section gets enough heat.

5. Curl Your Hair

Using a 1-inch curling iron, curl each section of hair away from your face. Hold the curling iron in place for 10-15 seconds before releasing the curl. Repeat this process until you’ve curled all of your hair.

6. Flip Your Hair

Once your hair is completely curled, flip your head upside down and run your fingers through your hair to break up the curls. This will create volume and give your hair a more natural look.

7. Apply Hairspray

Finish by applying a strong-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place. Hold the can about 6 inches away from your head and mist lightly over your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Big Poofy Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your big poofy curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to prevent tangling.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve big poofy curly hair if I have naturally straight hair?

A: Yes! Follow the steps in this article to achieve big poofy curly hair even if you have naturally straight hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I want big poofy curly hair?

A: It’s best to wash your hair every other day to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Big Poofy Curly Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your big poofy curly hair healthy and hydrated:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry to prevent frizz.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent tangles.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to get big poofy curly hair, it’s time to try it out for yourself! With the right techniques and products, you can achieve the big, beautiful curls you’ve always wanted.
