How To Put Long Thick Hair Up A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your long, thick hair getting in the way? Do you need a quick and easy solution to put it up and out of your face? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to put long thick hair up, along with some tips and FAQs to make the process as stress-free as possible.

Table of Contents

  • How to Put Long Thick Hair Up
  • Tips for Putting Up Long Thick Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Long Thick Hair

How to Put Long Thick Hair Up

First things first, gather all the necessary items you will need to put your hair up:

  • Hairbrush or comb
  • Hair ties or scrunchies
  • Bobby pins
  • Hairspray (optional)

Now that you have your tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Using a hairbrush or comb, gently brush out any knots or tangles in your hair. This will make it easier to put up and create a smoother look.

Step 2: Decide on Your Style

There are many different ways to put long thick hair up, so decide on your style preference. Some popular options include:

  • Ponytail
  • Bun
  • Braid

Step 3: Tie Your Hair

Using a hair tie or scrunchie, gather your hair at the base of your neck or the crown of your head (depending on your style preference) and tie it securely. If you have a lot of hair, you may need to use multiple hair ties or scrunchies.

Step 4: Secure with Bobby Pins

Using bobby pins, secure any loose strands or flyaways to create a neat and polished look. You can also use bobby pins to shape your bun or braid.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray (optional)

If you want to ensure your hair stays in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will also add some extra hold and shine to your look.

Tips for Putting Up Long Thick Hair

Putting up long thick hair can be a challenge, so here are some tips to make the process easier:

  • Use a hair tie or scrunchie that is strong enough to hold all of your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to use multiple hair ties or scrunchies if needed.
  • Try different styles to find what works best for your hair type and face shape.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or flyaways.
  • If you have layers, use bobby pins to tuck them in and create a cleaner look.

FAQs

Q: How do I put my hair up if it's too thick for a regular hair tie?

A: If your hair is too thick for a regular hair tie, try using a scrunchie or multiple hair ties. You can also try using a hair clip or claw clip to hold your hair up.

Q: How can I make my bun or ponytail look fuller?

A: To make your bun or ponytail look fuller, try teasing your hair at the crown of your head before tying it up. You can also use a hair donut or sock bun to add some extra volume.

Q: How can I prevent my hair from falling out of my bun or ponytail?

A: To prevent your hair from falling out of your bun or ponytail, use bobby pins to secure the base of the style. You can also try using a hair tie or scrunchie that is tight enough to hold your hair in place.

Hair Care for Long Thick Hair

Long thick hair requires special care to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using our step-by-step guide, you can put your long thick hair up with ease and style. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best!
