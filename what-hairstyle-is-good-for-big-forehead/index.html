What Hairstyle Is Good For Big Forehead Tips How To And Faqs

Written by Frank Aug 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect hairstyle that will flatter your big forehead? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people have a larger forehead than they would like, but there are plenty of hairstyles that can help reduce the appearance of a big forehead. Read on to find out what hairstyle is good for big forehead, how to style it, and tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Style a Hairstyle for Big Forehead

Description

The ideal hairstyle for a big forehead is one that covers the forehead or creates the illusion of a smaller forehead. Bangs are the most popular choice for people with a big forehead, but there are other hairstyles that can work just as well. The key is to find a hairstyle that complements your face shape and highlights your best features.

How to Style a Hairstyle for Big Forehead

Styling a hairstyle for a big forehead requires some planning and experimentation. Here are some tips to help you find the perfect hairstyle:

Consider Your Face Shape

The first step in finding a hairstyle for a big forehead is to consider your face shape. Some hairstyles work better on certain face shapes than others. For example, if you have a round face, a bob with bangs can help balance out your features. If you have an oval face, you can try a side-parted hairstyle with long layers.

Experiment with Bangs

Bangs are a popular choice for people with a big forehead because they can help create the illusion of a smaller forehead. However, not all bangs are created equal. The best bangs for a big forehead are long and side-swept. Avoid blunt bangs or short bangs, as they can make your forehead look even bigger.

Try a Layered Hairstyle

A layered hairstyle can also help reduce the appearance of a big forehead. Layers can add volume to your hair, which can help balance out your forehead. Consider a layered bob or a layered shag haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling a hairstyle for a big forehead:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Decide on the type of bangs you want. Side-swept bangs are the best for a big forehead.
  3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs. This will help them lie flat and stay in place.
  4. If you're not sure about bangs, try a layered hairstyle. Layers can help add volume to your hair and balance out your forehead.
  5. Use a curling iron or straightener to style your hair. Experiment with different hairstyles until you find one that flatters your face shape and complements your big forehead.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle for your big forehead, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair looking great:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools on your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQs

Q: Are bangs the only hairstyle option for a big forehead?

A: No, there are plenty of other hairstyles that can work well for a big forehead. Consider a layered hairstyle or a side-parted hairstyle.

Q: How do I know what face shape I have?

A: You can determine your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. You can then compare these measurements to a face shape chart to determine your face shape.

Hair Care Tips

Keeping your hair healthy is essential for maintaining a great hairstyle. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and strength.

Now that you know what hairstyle is good for big forehead, it's time to experiment and find the perfect hairstyle for you. Remember to consider your face shape, experiment with bangs, and try a layered hairstyle. With the right hairstyle and some hair care tips, you can look and feel your best.
