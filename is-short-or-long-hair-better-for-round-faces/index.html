Is Short Or Long Hair Better For Round Faces

Written by Eveline Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering whether short or long hair is better for you. The answer is not simple, as it depends on several factors such as your hair texture, personal style, and facial features. In this article, we will explore the pros and cons of short and long hair for round faces, as well as tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Description

Round faces are characterized by soft, curved lines and fuller cheeks. The width and length of the face are almost equal, and the chin is usually rounded. While round faces are considered attractive and youthful, they can also appear chubby or baby-faced if not styled properly. This is where hair comes in - the right hairstyle can balance out the roundness of the face and create a more flattering look.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we delve into the best hairstyles for round faces, it's important to determine your actual face shape. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Use a tape measure or ruler to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Write down the numbers for each measurement.

Step 2: Calculate Your Face Shape

Use the following formula to calculate your face shape:

Round Face: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline have similar measurements

Oval Face: forehead is slightly wider than the jawline, and the face tapers towards the chin

Square Face: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline have similar measurements, and the jawline is angular

Heart Face: forehead is wider than the jawline, and the chin is pointed

Diamond Face: cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the forehead and jawline are narrower

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, let's talk about the best hairstyles for round faces. Here are some tips:

Short Hair

Short hair can be a great option for round faces, as it can create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face. However, not all short hairstyles are created equal. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a short hairstyle that adds height to the crown of your head, such as a pixie cut or a spiky style.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face.
  • Consider adding layers or texture to your hair to create movement and volume.

Long Hair

Long hair can also work for round faces, as long as it's styled properly. Here are some tips:

  • Opt for longer layers that frame your face and create the illusion of length.
  • Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that shorten your face.
  • Consider adding texture to your hair, such as waves or curls, to create movement and volume.

FAQ

Q: Can I pull off a bob if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can. However, make sure the bob is longer in the front and shorter in the back, as this will create the illusion of length.

Q: Should I avoid updos if I have a round face?

A: Not necessarily. Updos can elongate your face if they're styled properly. Opt for a high bun or a half-up, half-down style that adds height to the crown of your head.

Q: What if I have curly or straight hair?

A: Your hair texture doesn't necessarily determine the best hairstyle for your face shape. However, you may want to avoid styles that require a lot of heat styling or products that can damage your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Regardless of whether you choose short or long hair, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

In Conclusion

So, is short or long hair better for round faces? The answer is that it depends on your personal style, hair texture, and facial features. However, with the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can create a flattering look that accentuates your best features. Use the tips and advice in this article to experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that works best for you.
