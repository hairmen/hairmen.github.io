How To Make 40S Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 30, 2023 · 3 min read
How to Make 40s Hair: A Step-by-Step Guide | Hair Care Tips

Do you want to rock a vintage hairstyle from the 40s? Perfect for any occasion, 40s hair is a classic look that never goes out of style. In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to make 40s hair, along with some tips and hair care advice to keep your locks healthy and strong.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make 40s Hair
  • Tips for Perfecting Your 40s Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for 40s Hair

How to Make 40s Hair

Step 1: Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help create the volume and body needed for a 40s hairstyle.

Step 2: Blow-dry your hair and use a round brush to create volume at the roots.

Step 3: Part your hair to one side and use a curling iron to create loose waves. Curl your hair in sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up.

Step 4: Once you have curled all of your hair, brush through the curls with a paddle brush to create soft waves.

Step 5: Pin your hair to one side, using bobby pins to secure it in place. Leave some loose strands around your face for a romantic and feminine touch.

Alternative Method:

If you have shorter hair, you can still achieve a 40s look. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Use a volumizing mousse to create volume and texture in your hair.

Step 2: Blow-dry your hair, using a round brush to create volume at the roots.

Step 3: Use a curling iron to create loose waves all over your head.

Step 4: Gently brush through the curls with a paddle brush, creating soft waves.

Step 5: Use bobby pins to pin your hair to one side or back, creating a vintage-inspired look.

Tips for Perfecting Your 40s Hair

Tip 1: Use hot rollers instead of a curling iron for a more authentic 40s look.

Tip 2: Add a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to tame frizz and add shine.

Tip 3: Use a hairspray with a strong hold to keep your curls in place all day.

Tip 4: Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, scarves, or flower clips, to add some extra flair to your 40s look.

FAQs

Can I achieve 40s hair without heat styling?

Yes, you can achieve a 40s-inspired look without heat styling. Try using foam rollers or braiding your hair overnight for natural-looking waves.

What if my hair is too short for 40s hair?

If your hair is too short for traditional 40s hair, you can still achieve a vintage-inspired look. Try a pixie cut or a bob with waves and curls.

Can I wear 40s hair for everyday occasions?

Absolutely! 40s hair is a versatile style that can be dressed up or down for any occasion.

Hair Care for 40s Hair

To keep your 40s hair looking healthy and strong, follow these hair care tips:

Tip 1: Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for your hair type.

Tip 2: Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

Tip 3: Use a heat protectant spray before heat styling your hair.

Tip 4: Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

By following these steps and hair care tips, you can achieve a gorgeous 40s hairstyle that is sure to turn heads. So go ahead and channel your inner vintage vixen!
