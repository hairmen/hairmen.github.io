How To Care For Dry Frizzy Wavy Hair

Written by Aprilia Jul 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Having wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. While the waves can add volume and texture to your hair, it can also make it prone to dryness and frizziness. If you have dry frizzy wavy hair, don't worry! With the right hair care routine, you can tame your locks and achieve beautiful, healthy-looking hair. In this guide, we'll show you how to care for your dry frizzy wavy hair, step by step.

How to Care for Dry Frizzy Wavy Hair

The key to caring for dry frizzy wavy hair is to keep it moisturized and nourished. Here are some tips:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates are harsh detergents that can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and frizzy. Look for a sulfate-free shampoo that is designed for wavy hair. This will help to gently cleanse your hair without stripping it of its natural oils.

2. Condition, condition, condition

Conditioning is a crucial step in caring for dry frizzy wavy hair. Use a moisturizing conditioner after shampooing to help restore moisture and nourish your hair. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to give your hair an extra boost of hydration.

3. Detangle your hair gently

Wavy hair can be prone to tangles, so it's important to detangle your hair gently. Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to gently work through any knots or tangles. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots.

4. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil

A leave-in conditioner or hair oil can help to keep your hair moisturized throughout the day. Apply a small amount to the ends of your hair and work it up towards the roots. This will help to seal in moisture and prevent frizz.

5. Avoid heat styling tools

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can be damaging to your hair, especially if it's already dry and frizzy. If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to caring for dry frizzy wavy hair:

1. Shampoo your hair

Start by shampooing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo. Massage the shampoo into your scalp and work it through the lengths of your hair. Rinse thoroughly.

2. Condition your hair

Apply a moisturizing conditioner to your hair, focusing on the lengths and ends. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes, then rinse thoroughly.

3. Detangle your hair

Gently detangle your hair using a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots.

4. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil

Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair and work it up towards the roots. This will help to seal in moisture and prevent frizz.

5. Air-dry your hair

Avoid using heat styling tools and let your hair air-dry instead. If you need to speed up the drying process, use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to gently scrunch your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Dry Frizzy Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your dry frizzy wavy hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to work through any knots or tangles.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to give your hair an extra boost of hydration.
  • Avoid using harsh hair products that contain alcohol or sulfates.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my dry frizzy wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily or dry your scalp is. Some people with dry hair may only need to wash their hair once or twice a week, while others may need to wash their hair every other day.

Q: Can I use a hair straightener on my dry frizzy wavy hair?

A: While it's not recommended to use heat styling tools on dry frizzy wavy hair, if you do use a hair straightener, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

Q: What are some good hair care products for dry frizzy wavy hair?

A: Look for hair care products that are designed specifically for wavy hair and that contain moisturizing ingredients like coconut oil, shea butter, and argan oil. Some good options include the Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl & Shine Shampoo and Conditioner and the Moroccanoil Treatment.
