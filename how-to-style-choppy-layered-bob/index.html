How To Style A Choppy Layered Bob

Written by Robby Nov 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a chic and edgy hairstyle? Look no further than a choppy layered bob. This versatile haircut can be styled in various ways, making it perfect for any occasion. In this article, we’ll show you how to style a choppy layered bob in relaxed English language. Let’s get started!

How to Style a Choppy Layered Bob

A choppy layered bob is a low-maintenance hairstyle that can be styled in many ways. To get the perfect choppy layered bob, you need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition Your Hair

Before styling your hair, you need to make sure it’s clean and well-conditioned. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some texture and bounce to your hair.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture to your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before moving on to the next step.

Step 3: Apply Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, it’s essential to apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will prevent your hair from getting damaged due to the heat.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron or wand, curl small sections of your hair away from your face. This will create a messy and textured look.

Step 5: Apply Hair Wax

To add more texture and definition to your hair, apply a small amount of hair wax to your fingertips and run it through your hair. This will also help hold the curls in place.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your look by spritzing some hairspray all over your hair. This will help hold the style in place and add some extra volume.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’re still unsure about how to style a choppy layered bob, here’s a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Wash and condition your hair using volumizing products.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush.
  3. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  4. Curl small sections of your hair away from your face using a curling iron or wand.
  5. Run your fingertips through your hair with some hair wax to add texture and definition.
  6. Spray some hairspray all over your hair to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling a Choppy Layered Bob

Here are some tips to help you style your choppy layered bob:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some texture and bounce to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Apply a heat protectant to your hair before using any heat styling tools.
  • Curl your hair away from your face to create a messy and textured look.
  • Use hair wax to add more texture and definition to your hair.
  • Finish off your look by spritzing some hairspray all over your hair.

FAQs

How often should I wash my choppy layered bob?

You should wash your choppy layered bob every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

What kind of products should I use for my choppy layered bob?

You should use volumizing products, such as a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, to add some texture and bounce to your hair. You can also use hair wax to add more texture and definition to your hair.

Hair Care for Choppy Layered Bob

To keep your choppy layered bob looking its best, you need to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some texture and bounce to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use hair wax to add more texture and definition to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the choppy layered look.

Now that you know how to style a choppy layered bob, it’s time to try it out for yourself. With the right products and techniques, you can achieve a chic and edgy look that’s perfect for any occasion.
