How To Layer Short Hair On The Sides

Written by Aprilia Sep 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Layering short hair on the sides can add volume and dimension to your hair. It's a great way to give your hair a new look without cutting off too much length. In this article, we'll go over how to layer short hair on the sides in a relaxed English language. We'll cover everything from how to do it to hair care tips. So, let's get started!

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Layer Short Hair on the Sides

Layering short hair on the sides can be quite easy if you follow the right steps. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner. Then, towel dry your hair until it's damp.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, use a comb to section off the hair on the sides of your head. You should have two sections of hair, one on each side of your head.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start cutting the hair on one side of your head. Use a pair of scissors and start at the bottom of your hair, cutting upward. Make sure to cut small sections of hair at a time to avoid cutting too much off.

Step 4: Repeat on the Other Side

Once you've finished layering one side of your hair, repeat the process on the other side. Make sure to keep the length and layers even on both sides.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're still unsure about how to layer short hair on the sides, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and dry your hair until it's damp.
  2. Section off the hair on the sides of your head using a comb.
  3. Start cutting the hair on one side, making sure to cut small sections at a time.
  4. Repeat the process on the other side, keeping the length and layers even.
  5. Once you've finished layering, style your hair as desired.

Tips for Layering Short Hair on the Sides

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when layering short hair on the sides:

  • Always cut small sections of hair at a time to avoid cutting too much off.
  • Use a pair of sharp scissors to make clean cuts.
  • Start at the bottom of your hair and work your way up.
  • Keep the length and layers even on both sides of your head.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're unsure about how to layer your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about layering short hair on the sides:

Is it easy to layer short hair on the sides?

Yes, it can be quite easy if you follow the right steps and use the right tools.

What tools do I need to layer short hair on the sides?

You'll need a comb, a pair of sharp scissors, and some hair clips to section off your hair.

Can I layer my hair myself?

Yes, you can layer your hair yourself if you're comfortable doing so. However, if you're unsure about how to do it, it's best to ask for help.

Hair Care Tips for Layered Short Hair

Here are a few hair care tips to keep your layered short hair looking great:

  • Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep the layers looking fresh.
  • Consider using styling products to add volume and texture to your hair.

Layering short hair on the sides can be a great way to give your hair a new look. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a layered hairstyle that's easy to maintain and looks great. Remember to take care of your hair by using good hair care products and avoiding too much heat. If you're unsure about how to layer your hair, don't be afraid to ask for help. With a little practice, you'll be able to master the art of layering short hair on the sides.
