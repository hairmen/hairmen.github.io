Good Hairstyles For Rectangular Faces A Comprehensive Guide

If you have a rectangular face shape, you might be wondering what hairstyles would suit you best. In this article, we'll provide you with a comprehensive guide on good hairstyles for rectangular faces. We'll cover everything from how to determine if you have a rectangular face shape to tips on hair care. So, without further ado, let's get started!

How to Determine if You Have a Rectangular Face Shape

The first step to finding good hairstyles for rectangular faces is determining if you actually have a rectangular face shape. Here are the characteristics of a rectangular face shape:

  • Your face is longer than it is wide
  • Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width
  • Your jawline is sharp and defined

If you have these characteristics, then you have a rectangular face shape. Now, let's move on to the fun part - finding good hairstyles for your face shape!

Good Hairstyles for Rectangular Faces

There are several good hairstyles for rectangular faces, including:

  • Long, layered hairstyles
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Soft, curly hairstyles
  • Short, layered hairstyles
  • Bob hairstyles

These hairstyles work well with a rectangular face shape because they help to soften the sharp angles of the jawline and forehead. Now, let's talk about how to style your hair based on your face shape.

How to Style Your Hair Based on Your Face Shape

Once you've chosen a good hairstyle for your rectangular face shape, it's important to style it correctly. Here are some tips:

  • Long, layered hairstyles - use a volumizing mousse and blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and bounce
  • Side-swept bangs - use a flat iron to create a sleek, straight look
  • Soft, curly hairstyles - use a curling wand to create loose curls and finish with a light-hold hairspray
  • Short, layered hairstyles - use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Bob hairstyles - use a flat iron to create a sleek, straight look or use a curling wand to create loose curls

Now that you know how to style your hair, let's go over a step-by-step guide to styling your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair based on your rectangular face shape:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Apply any styling products (e.g. mousse, texturizing spray, etc.)
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume
  4. Use a flat iron or curling wand to style your hair
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray

And that's it! Now, let's move on to some tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent stripping it of its natural oils
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair (e.g. bleach, hair dye, etc.)

Now, let's move on to some frequently asked questions about good hairstyles for rectangular faces.

FAQs

Q: Can I still have short hair if I have a rectangular face shape?

A: Yes! Short, layered hairstyles can look great on rectangular face shapes.

Q: Do I have to blow dry my hair if I have a rectangular face shape?

A: No, you don't have to blow dry your hair. However, blow drying with a round brush can help add volume to long, layered hairstyles.

Q: Can I still have bangs if I have a rectangular face shape?

A: Yes! Side-swept bangs can look great on rectangular face shapes.

And that concludes our comprehensive guide on good hairstyles for rectangular faces. We hope you found this article helpful and feel confident in choosing a hairstyle that suits you best!
