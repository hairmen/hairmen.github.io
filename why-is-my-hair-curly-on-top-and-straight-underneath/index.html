Why Is My Hair Curly On Top And Straight Underneath

Written by Frank Jun 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you've ever wondered why your hair is curly on top and straight underneath, you're not alone. This is a common hair type, and many people struggle to understand why their hair behaves this way. In this article, we'll explore the reasons behind this phenomenon and provide some tips on how to care for and style your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Having curly hair on top and straight underneath is a result of genetics. This hair type is often referred to as "mixed texture hair," and it can be challenging to style and care for. The reason for this is that the hair on top is exposed to more elements than the hair underneath. The top layer of your hair is exposed to the sun, wind, and other environmental factors, which can cause it to become damaged and dry. On the other hand, the hair underneath is protected by the top layer, which means it is less exposed to these elements.

How to

When it comes to styling your hair, it's essential to use products that are designed for mixed texture hair. Look for products that are moisturizing and nourishing, as these will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent breakage. It's also important to avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and make your hair harder to manage.

Step by step

Here are some steps you can follow to care for your curly on top, straight underneath hair:

  1. Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  2. Avoid using too much heat on your hair. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray first.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  4. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  5. Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  6. When styling your hair, use products that are designed for mixed texture hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you care for your curly on top, straight underneath hair:

  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but it's important to use a heat protectant spray first to prevent damage.

Q: Can I wear my hair curly on top and straight underneath?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair curly on top and straight underneath. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

Caring for curly on top, straight underneath hair can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, you can keep your hair healthy and beautiful. Remember to be gentle when detangling and styling your hair, and always use products that are designed for mixed texture hair. With a little bit of patience and care, you can embrace your unique hair type and enjoy beautiful, healthy hair.
