How To Make Short Thick Hair Look Good Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Nov 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short and thick hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right techniques, it can look stunning. In this article, we will cover how to make short thick hair look good, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice. Whether you have curly, wavy, or straight hair, these tips will help you achieve a polished and stylish look.

How to Make Short Thick Hair Look Good

There are several ways to make short thick hair look good. The key is to choose a style that complements your hair type and face shape. Here are some popular styles to try:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that looks great on thick hair. This style features short hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on top. To make the most of your thick hair, ask your stylist to add texture and layers to your pixie cut.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another popular short hairstyle that works well on thick hair. This style features hair that is cut straight around the head, with the ends sitting just above the shoulders. To add volume and movement to your bob cut, ask your stylist to add layers and texture to your hair.

3. Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great option for those with thick hair. This style features hair that is cut in layers, with shorter layers on top and longer layers at the bottom. To make the most of your thick hair, ask your stylist to add texture and volume to your layers.

Step-by-Step Instructions

If you want to style your short thick hair at home, here are some step-by-step instructions to follow:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water to help seal the cuticle and prevent frizz.

2. Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to gently pat your hair dry, being careful not to rub it too hard. Rubbing your hair can cause damage and frizz.

3. Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a mousse or gel, to help add volume and hold. Start with a small amount, and add more as needed.

4. Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume and shape. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to lift and shape your hair as you go.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your style with a light mist of hairspray to help hold your hair in place.

Tips for Styling Short Thick Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short thick hair:

  • Invest in good quality hair products designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, especially when it's wet.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQs

How often should I wash my short thick hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Can I use a hair straightener on my short thick hair?

Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant product first to help prevent damage. Also, be sure to use the straightener on a low or medium heat setting, as high heat can cause damage to your hair.

What is the best haircut for short thick hair?

The best haircut for short thick hair depends on your face shape and personal style. Consult with your stylist to find the best style for you.

Hair Care Tips for Short Thick Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short thick hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as it can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to help prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to help keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your short thick hair look good every day. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice.
