Short Hair For Long Faces 2021 A Guide To Flattering Cuts

Written by Aprilia Oct 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a long face shape, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. But don't worry! With the right style, you can accentuate your features and create a flattering look. In this guide, we'll explore the best short haircuts for long faces in 2021. From bobs to pixie cuts, we'll cover everything you need to know to find the perfect style for you.

Description

A long face shape is characterized by a vertical measurement that is longer than the width of the face. This shape can make your features appear elongated, but with the right haircut, you can balance your proportions and create a more harmonious look. Short haircuts are a great option for long faces, as they can add volume and width to the sides of the face.

How to

Before heading to the salon, consider the following tips:

  • Look for styles that add volume to the sides of the face
  • Avoid styles that are too long, as they can elongate the face further
  • Consider bangs to shorten the forehead and balance the proportions of the face
  • Bring photos of styles you like to show your stylist

Step by Step

Once you've found the perfect style, follow these steps to maintain your new look:

  1. Wash and condition your hair regularly
  2. Use a volumizing product to add body to your hair
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and movement to your hair
  5. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style

Tips

Here are a few extra tips to keep in mind:

  • Consider your lifestyle when choosing a haircut. Short haircuts can be low-maintenance, but they may not be suitable for all activities.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Accessorize with statement earrings or necklaces to draw attention away from your face shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for long faces:

Q: Will a pixie cut suit a long face shape?

A: Yes, a pixie cut can be a great option for long faces, as long as it adds volume to the sides of the face.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight with a short haircut?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight with a short haircut, but consider adding texture or movement to avoid a flat look.

Q: Do I need to use special products for short hair?

A: It's a good idea to use products that are specifically formulated for short hair, as they can add volume and texture without weighing your hair down.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but it's still important to take care of your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage, and avoid using heat styling tools too frequently. Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect short haircut for your long face shape. Remember to consult with your stylist and experiment with different styles to find the most flattering look for you. Happy styling!
