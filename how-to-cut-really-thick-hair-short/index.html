How To Cut Really Thick Hair Short A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Cutting really thick hair short can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done with ease. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut really thick hair short, along with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve the best results.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Really Thick Hair Short
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Really Thick Hair Short

How to Cut Really Thick Hair Short

Cutting really thick hair short requires a different approach than cutting thin or normal hair. Thick hair tends to be heavier and more difficult to style, so cutting it short requires a bit of finesse. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Use sharp, high-quality scissors or clippers
  • Work with smaller sections of hair to ensure evenness
  • Consider the natural texture of the hair when cutting
  • Take your time and don't rush the process

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know what to keep in mind, let's dive into the step-by-step process for cutting really thick hair short:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If the hair is wet, it will be more difficult to cut evenly.
  2. Section the hair into manageable sections using hair clips or elastics.
  3. Start at the bottom of the hair and work your way up. Take small sections of hair and cut straight across, using the previous section as a guide.
  4. Continue to work your way up the hair, making sure to cut each section evenly.
  5. Once you reach the top of the hair, use the scissors or clippers to shape the hair as desired.
  6. Check for any uneven sections and make adjustments as needed.
  7. Style the hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Really Thick Hair Short

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting really thick hair short:

  • Use thinning shears to remove some of the bulk and make the hair more manageable.
  • Consider adding layers to the hair to create movement and texture.
  • Consult with a professional stylist if you're unsure how to achieve the look you want.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting really thick hair short:

  • Can I cut really thick hair short myself? Yes, but it's best to have some experience or to consult with a professional stylist if you're unsure.
  • What tools do I need to cut really thick hair short? Sharp, high-quality scissors or clippers, thinning shears, hair clips or elastics, and a comb.
  • What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting really thick hair short? Don't panic! Take a break and step away from the hair for a few minutes. Then, assess the situation and make adjustments as needed.

Hair Care Tips for Short, Thick Hair

Now that you've cut your really thick hair short, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly with products designed for your hair type.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or detangling brush to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your short, thick hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your really thick hair short and enjoy a fresh, new look. Remember to take your time, work with small sections of hair, and consult with a professional if you're unsure. Happy cutting!
