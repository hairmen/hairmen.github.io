How To Tame Frizzy Hair After Blow Drying Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Jun 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you struggle with frizzy hair after blow drying? Don't worry, you're not alone. Frizzy hair is a common problem that many people face, especially after blow drying. But the good news is that there are ways to tame frizzy hair and achieve a sleek, smooth look. In this article, we'll discuss some tips and tricks on how to tame frizzy hair after blow drying.

How to Tame Frizzy Hair After Blow Drying

Blow drying your hair can cause frizz because the heat can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and prone to flyaways. However, there are ways to control frizz and achieve a smooth, shiny finish. Here are some tips on how to tame frizzy hair after blow drying:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

Before blow drying, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage and reduce frizz. A heat protectant will create a barrier between your hair and the heat of the blow dryer, helping to lock in moisture and prevent frizz. Look for a product that contains ingredients like argan oil or keratin, which can help smooth and hydrate your hair.

2. Use the Right Brush

The type of brush you use can make a big difference in how your hair looks after blow drying. A round brush with natural bristles is a good choice for taming frizz, as it can help distribute the natural oils in your hair and smooth out any flyaways.

3. Use a Lower Heat Setting

High heat can cause damage and frizz, so try using a lower heat setting on your blow dryer. This will take longer to dry your hair, but it will help prevent frizz and keep your hair healthy.

4. Use a Cold Shot

After blow drying your hair, finish with a blast of cold air to help set your style and reduce frizz. The cold air will help seal your hair cuticles, which can help prevent moisture from escaping and causing frizz.

5. Apply a Hair Serum

After blow drying, apply a hair serum to your hair to help smooth out any remaining frizz and add shine. Look for a serum that contains ingredients like argan oil or coconut oil, which can help hydrate and nourish your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to tame frizzy hair after blow drying:

Step 1: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before blow drying, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage and reduce frizz.

Step 2: Use the Right Brush

Use a round brush with natural bristles to help distribute the natural oils in your hair and smooth out any flyaways.

Step 3: Use a Lower Heat Setting

Use a lower heat setting on your blow dryer to help prevent frizz and keep your hair healthy.

Step 4: Use a Cold Shot

Finish with a blast of cold air to help set your style and reduce frizz.

Step 5: Apply a Hair Serum

After blow drying, apply a hair serum to your hair to help smooth out any remaining frizz and add shine.

Tips for Taming Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips for taming frizzy hair:

1. Avoid Over-Washing

Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.

3. Avoid Touching Your Hair Too Much

Touching your hair too much can cause frizz, so try to avoid playing with your hair throughout the day.

FAQ

Q: Can I prevent frizz while blow drying my hair?

A: Yes, you can prevent frizz by using a heat protectant, the right brush, and a lower heat setting on your blow dryer.

Q: How can I keep my hair smooth and shiny after blow drying?

A: You can keep your hair smooth and shiny by using a cold shot and applying a hair serum after blow drying.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential for preventing frizz and achieving a sleek, smooth look. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth.

2. Avoid Heat Damage

Use a heat protectant and avoid using high heat on your hair to prevent damage and frizz.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can tame frizzy hair after blow drying and achieve a sleek, smooth look. Remember to take care of your hair by eating a healthy diet and avoiding heat damage.
