Spiky Hairstyles For Thick Hair A Guide For Men In 2023

Written by Bardi Aug 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

For men with thick hair, spiky hairstyles are the perfect way to add some edge and personality to your look. Whether you prefer a messy, textured style or a slick, polished look, there are plenty of spiky hairstyles to choose from. In this guide, we'll explore some of the best spiky hairstyles for thick hair, along with tips on how to achieve these styles and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Spiky Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Spiky Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Spiky Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Style Spiky Hair

The key to styling spiky hair is to work with your hair's natural texture and thickness. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and fullness. Then, apply a styling product such as a pomade, wax, or gel to your damp hair. Use a comb or your fingers to create spikes by pushing your hair upwards and away from your scalp. For a more tousled look, use your fingers to separate and define individual pieces of hair.

Best Products for Spiky Hair

When it comes to styling products for spiky hair, there are a few options to choose from:

  • Pomade: A lightweight, water-based pomade is a good choice for a natural-looking, textured style.
  • Wax: A hair wax provides a stronger hold and is ideal for creating defined, sculpted spikes.
  • Gel: A hair gel provides the strongest hold and is great for creating slick, polished spikes.

Step-by-Step Guide to Spiky Hair

Follow these steps to create a classic spiky hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a styling product such as a pomade or wax to your hair, working it in evenly from roots to tips.
  3. Use your fingers or a comb to create spikes by pushing your hair upwards and away from your scalp.
  4. Finish with a hairspray or finishing product to hold your style in place.

For a more textured, messy look, simply use your fingers to tousle and separate individual pieces of hair. You can also add some volume by blow-drying your hair with a round brush before styling.

Tips for Maintaining Spiky Hair

To keep your spiky hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and fullness.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip natural oils and make your hair dry and brittle.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy, even texture.
  • Use a styling product with UV protection to prevent damage from the sun's rays.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can spiky hairstyles work for all hair types?

Spiky hairstyles can work for most hair types, but they tend to work best for thick, coarse hair with a lot of texture. If you have fine or thin hair, you may need to use more product or try a different styling technique to create spikes that hold their shape.

How often should I wash my hair if I have spiky hair?

It's generally best to avoid over-washing your hair if you have spiky hair, as this can strip natural oils and make your hair dry and brittle. Depending on your hair type and lifestyle, you may only need to wash your hair every other day or even less frequently.

What's the best way to maintain a spiky hairstyle?

The best way to maintain a spiky hairstyle is to use a high-quality styling product and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day. You can also use a hairspray or finishing product to help hold your style in place.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Thick hair requires a little extra care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for caring for your thick hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for thick hair to help add volume and fullness.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy texture.

By following these tips and experimenting with different spiky hairstyles, you can create a unique and stylish look that showcases your personality and sense of style.
