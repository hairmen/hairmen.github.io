Can Round Faces Have Short Hair Tips And Tricks For Flattering Short Hairstyles

Description

Short hair has always been a popular choice for women. However, if you have a round face, you may be wondering if short hair is a good option for you. The good news is that round faces can definitely pull off short hair! With the right cut and styling, short hair can be incredibly flattering for round faces.

How to

If you're considering a short hairstyle, here are some tips for finding the perfect cut:

1. Consider your face shape

As mentioned earlier, round faces can definitely have short hair. However, it's important to find a cut that flatters your face shape. A good rule of thumb is to look for styles that add height to the top of your head, as this can create the illusion of a longer face. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face appear even rounder.

2. Look for inspiration

Before you head to the salon, do some research to find styles that you like. Look for celebrities or models with round faces who have rocked short hair, and save pictures of styles that you like. This will help your stylist understand the look you're going for.

3. Consult with a stylist

Once you have some ideas in mind, schedule a consultation with your stylist. They can help you determine which styles will work best for your face shape and hair type. They may also have suggestions for modifications to make a style work better for you.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a short hairstyle:

1. Choose your cut

Based on your research and consultation with your stylist, choose the cut you want. This may be a bob, pixie cut, or something in between.

2. Wash your hair

Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow dry it. This will make it easier for your stylist to work with.

3. Head to the salon

Go to the salon and let your stylist work their magic! They will cut and style your hair to create the look you want.

4. Style at home

Once you're home, you may need to do some styling to maintain your new look. This could involve blow drying, straightening, or using styling products.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for rocking short hair with a round face:

  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Consider adding layers to create movement and texture.
  • Try out different styling techniques, such as curling or straightening, to create different looks.
  • Accessorize with headbands, clips, or other hair accessories to add interest to your style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair for round faces:

Can round faces have short hair?

Yes! With the right cut and styling, short hair can be incredibly flattering for round faces.

What are the best short hairstyles for round faces?

Styles that add height to the top of the head, such as bobs or pixie cuts, can be very flattering for round faces. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of the head.

How do I style short hair for a round face?

Experiment with different partings, layers, and styling techniques to find the most flattering look for your face shape.

Hair Care

Short hair can be very low maintenance, but it's still important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

Overall, short hair can be a great option for round faces. With the right cut and styling, you can create a look that's both stylish and flattering. So go ahead and take the plunge – you might be surprised at how much you love your new short 'do!
