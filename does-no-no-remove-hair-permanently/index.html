Description

The No No Hair Removal Device is a handheld device that uses heat to remove hair. It works by gliding the device over the skin, and the heat energy is transferred to the hair shaft, which then disrupts the growth cycle of the hair. Over time, this leads to a reduction in hair growth, and with regular use, the hair becomes finer and less noticeable.

How to

Using the No No Hair Removal Device is simple. First, make sure your skin is clean and dry. Then, turn on the device and select the appropriate treatment level. Glide the device over the skin in the direction of hair growth, making sure to keep the device in constant contact with the skin. After each use, clean the device with the included brush.
