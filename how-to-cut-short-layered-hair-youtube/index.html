How To Cut Short Layered Hair Youtube A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, especially if you're going for a layered look. Luckily, with the help of YouTube tutorials, it's easier than ever to achieve professional-looking results from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to cut short layered hair using YouTube videos as your guide.

Description

Short layered hair is a popular style that can add volume and dimension to your hair. It's also relatively easy to maintain and style, making it a great choice for those who want a low-maintenance look. However, going to a salon to get your hair cut can be expensive, and not everyone has the time or money to do so. By learning how to cut your own hair using YouTube videos as your guide, you can save money and achieve the look you want.

How to Cut Short Layered Hair YouTube: Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, hair clips, and a comb. If you have curly hair, you may also want to consider using a spray bottle to spritz your hair with water to make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It's important to start with clean, dry hair before you start cutting. This will help you get a more accurate cut and prevent any unevenness. Make sure to towel dry your hair thoroughly and comb it out before starting.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using the hair clips, section your hair into four parts: the top, left side, right side, and back. This will make it easier to cut each section separately and ensure that you get a more even cut.

Step 4: Cut the Back Section

Start with the back section of your hair, as this is the easiest section to cut. Comb out any tangles and use the comb to hold the hair taut. Use your scissors to cut the hair in a straight line, using the comb as a guide.

Step 5: Cut the Side Sections

Next, move on to the side sections of your hair. Take a small section of hair and comb it out, holding it taut with the comb. Use your scissors to cut the hair at an angle, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Make sure to cut each side evenly.

Step 6: Cut the Top Section

Finally, move on to the top section of your hair. Take a small section of hair and comb it out, holding it taut with the comb. Use your scissors to cut the hair at an angle, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Make sure to cut the top section evenly with the side sections.

Tips

- Start with a small amount of hair and cut slowly to ensure you get the desired length.

- Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut.

- If you're not sure how short to cut your hair, start with a longer length and work your way up.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair if I have never done it before?

Yes, you can. However, it's important to start with a simple cut and work your way up to more complicated styles.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

No, it's important to use hair cutting scissors as they are sharper and designed specifically for cutting hair.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to ensure it stays healthy and looks great. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant if you use heat styling tools, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

Cutting short layered hair using YouTube videos as your guide can be a great way to save money and achieve professional-looking results. By following these steps and tips, you'll be on your way to a great new look in no time!
