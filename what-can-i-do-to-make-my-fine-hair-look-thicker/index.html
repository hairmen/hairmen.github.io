What Can I Do To Make My Fine Hair Look Thicker

Written by Petter Sep 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have fine hair, you may feel that your locks lack volume and body. The good news is that there are many things you can do to make your hair look thicker and more voluminous. In this article, we'll explore some tips, tricks, and hair care advice to help you achieve fuller-looking hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Fine Hair for More Volume
  • Hair Care Tips for Thicker-Looking Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Style Fine Hair for More Volume

When it comes to styling fine hair, the right haircut and color can make a big difference. A shorter haircut with layers can add movement and volume to fine hair. Adding highlights or lowlights can also create the illusion of thicker hair. But there are also many styling techniques you can use to make your hair look fuller:

1. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow drying your hair upside down can create instant volume. Start by flipping your head upside down and directing the blow dryer at your roots. Use your fingers to lift your hair as you dry it, and finish with a blast of cool air to set the style.

2. Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner can help give your hair more body. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, or collagen, which can help strengthen and thicken your hair.

3. Try a Root-Lifting Spray

A root-lifting spray can add lift and volume to your hair, especially at the crown. Apply the spray to damp hair before blow drying, focusing on the roots. Use your fingers or a round brush to lift the hair as you dry it.

4. Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair can create the illusion of thicker, fuller locks. Use a fine-toothed comb to backcomb your hair at the roots, working from the crown to the front. Gently smooth the top layer of hair over the teased section, and finish with hairspray to hold the style.

Hair Care Tips for Thicker-Looking Hair

In addition to styling techniques, there are also some hair care tips you can follow to help make your fine hair look thicker:

1. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it limp and lifeless. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days, and use a dry shampoo in between washes to absorb excess oil and add volume.

2. Use a Lightweight Conditioner

Using a heavy conditioner can weigh down fine hair, making it look even thinner. Look for a lightweight conditioner that won't leave residue on your hair. Apply the conditioner only to the ends of your hair, and rinse it out thoroughly.

3. Avoid Heat Damage

Using hot styling tools like flat irons, curling irons, and hot rollers can damage your hair and make it look thinner. Try to limit your use of these tools, and use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.

4. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair look thinner. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I use hair extensions to make my hair look thicker?

Yes, hair extensions can be a great way to add volume and length to fine hair. Look for extensions that are made from human hair, as they will look more natural and blend in better with your own hair.

2. Is it okay to brush my hair when it's wet?

No, brushing your hair when it's wet can cause breakage and damage. Use a wide-toothed comb or a detangling brush to gently work out any tangles, starting at the ends of your hair and working your way up.

3. How can I prevent my hair from getting greasy?

To prevent your hair from getting greasy, try to wash it less often and use a dry shampoo in between washes. You can also try using a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup and excess oil from your hair.

4. Can changing my diet help make my hair look thicker?

Yes, a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and nutrients can help strengthen and thicken your hair. Make sure you're eating plenty of protein, vitamins A, C, and E, and omega-3 fatty acids, which can all promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and tricks, you can help make your fine hair look thicker and more voluminous. Remember to be gentle with your hair, avoid harsh chemicals, and take good care of your locks to keep them looking their best.
