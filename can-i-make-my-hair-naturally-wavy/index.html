Can I Make My Hair Naturally Wavy Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Eveline Jul 11, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of straight hair and want to add some waves to your locks? You're not alone! Many people are looking for ways to make their hair naturally wavy without relying on heat styling tools or chemical treatments. In this article, we'll explore some tips, tricks, and hair care advice to help you achieve those waves you've been dreaming of.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Make Your Hair Naturally Wavy

If you're wondering if you can make your hair naturally wavy, the answer is yes! There are several ways to achieve wavy hair without damaging your locks. Here are some methods you can try:

1. Braid Your Hair

One of the easiest ways to create waves is to braid your hair. Simply divide your hair into sections and braid each section before going to bed. In the morning, undo the braids and you'll have natural waves. You can also use smaller braids for tighter waves or larger braids for looser waves.

2. Twist Your Hair

Another method to create waves is to twist your hair. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section before going to bed. In the morning, undo the twists and you'll have natural waves. This method works best for people with straight or slightly wavy hair.

3. Use a Salt Spray

A salt spray is a great product to create beachy waves. Simply spray the product onto damp hair and scrunch your hair with your fingers. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to dry your hair. The salt in the spray creates texture and enhances your natural waves.

4. Use a Curl Enhancing Product

If you have naturally wavy hair but want to enhance your waves, you can use a curl enhancing product. Look for products that contain ingredients like aloe vera, coconut oil, or shea butter. Apply the product to damp hair and scrunch your hair with your fingers. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to dry your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some methods to make your hair naturally wavy, here's a step-by-step guide to create those waves:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Use a towel to remove excess water from your hair.

2. Apply a Product

Apply a product to your hair to create texture and hold. You can use a salt spray, curl enhancing product, or mousse.

3. Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections. The number of sections will depend on the thickness of your hair. Use clips to hold each section in place.

4. Braid or Twist Your Hair

Choose a method to create waves - braid or twist your hair. Use smaller braids or twists for tighter waves or larger braids or twists for looser waves.

5. Let Your Hair Dry

Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to dry your hair. Avoid using a hair dryer as it can damage your hair and create frizz.

6. Undo the Braids or Twists

Once your hair is dry, undo the braids or twists. Use your fingers to gently separate the waves and create volume.

7. Apply a Finishing Spray

Apply a finishing spray to hold the waves in place. You can use a hairspray, texturizing spray, or sea salt spray.

Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to maintain your natural waves:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as it can create frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair and create frizz.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

FAQs

Can I make my hair naturally wavy if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can! You can try braiding or twisting your hair before going to bed or using a salt spray to create texture.

How long will the waves last?

The waves will last until your next wash. You can use a finishing spray to hold the waves in place for longer.

Can I make my hair naturally wavy without using any products?

Yes, you can! You can try braiding or twisting your hair before going to bed to create natural waves.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Wavy hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your wavy locks:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as it can create frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair and create frizz. If you must use a hair dryer, use a diffuser attachment.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

With these tips, tricks, and hair care advice, you can make your hair naturally wavy and keep it looking healthy and gorgeous. Try out different methods to find what works best for you and enjoy your new wavy locks!
