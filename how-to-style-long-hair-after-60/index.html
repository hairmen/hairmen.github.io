How To Style Long Hair After 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Sep 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're over 60 and have long hair, you might be wondering how to style it to look your best. Long hair can be beautiful at any age, but it requires some extra care and attention as we get older. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks for styling long hair after 60, as well as answer some common questions and give you some hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Long Hair After 60
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Long Hair After 60
  • FAQ

How to Style Long Hair After 60

First things first: you need to decide what kind of look you want to achieve. Do you want a sleek and polished style, or something more casual and relaxed? Once you've decided on your desired look, you can start experimenting with different hairstyles.

One classic style for long hair is the French Twist. This elegant updo is perfect for formal occasions, but can also be dressed down for a more everyday look. Another option is the Half-Up Half-Down style, which is perfect for those who want to show off their long locks while still keeping their hair out of their face.

If you're looking for something more casual, you might try a messy bun or a simple braid. These styles are easy to do and require minimal effort, making them perfect for busy days or running errands.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a simple step-by-step guide for achieving the French Twist:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Use a comb to smooth out any tangles or knots.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it upwards, tucking the ends in towards your head.
  3. Secure the twist with bobby pins, making sure to hide them as much as possible.
  4. Use hairspray to keep everything in place.

For a Half-Up Half-Down style, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add some loose curls or waves.
  2. Gather the top half of your hair into a ponytail, leaving the bottom half down.
  3. Tease the crown of your head to add some volume.
  4. Wrap a small section of hair around the base of the ponytail to hide the elastic.
  5. Use hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips for Styling Long Hair After 60

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your long hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look stiff.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products.
  • Consider getting a haircut or trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about styling long hair after 60:

Q: Is it okay to wear my hair in a ponytail or bun every day?

A: While it's fine to wear your hair in a ponytail or bun occasionally, doing so every day can cause damage and breakage. Try to mix up your hairstyles to avoid putting too much stress on your hair.

Q: What products should I use to keep my hair looking healthy?

A: Look for products that are specifically designed for mature hair, as they will contain ingredients that help nourish and strengthen your hair. A good shampoo and conditioner, as well as a leave-in conditioner or hair oil, can work wonders.

Hair Care Advice

Here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can cause damage and dryness.
  • Invest in a high-quality brush or comb to avoid tangles and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your long hair after 60 with confidence and ease. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and always take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
