Face Framing Short Haircuts With Bangs

Written by William Jun 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh new look this year, consider getting a face framing short haircut with bangs. This trendy style is perfect for those who want to change up their hair without losing too much length. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about getting and maintaining this stylish cut.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining the Style
  • FAQs

Description

A face framing short haircut with bangs is a style that features short, layered hair that frames the face. The bangs add an extra touch of style and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal preferences. This style is perfect for those who want to change up their hair without losing too much length.

How to Get the Cut

The first step in getting a face framing short haircut with bangs is to find a reputable stylist who has experience with this style of cut. Once you've found a stylist, it's important to bring in pictures of the style you want to ensure that you and your stylist are on the same page.

During the appointment, your stylist will likely start by cutting your hair to the desired length. They will then add layers to create the face framing effect. Finally, they will cut the bangs to the desired length and style them to flatter your face shape.

Choosing the Right Bangs

One of the most important aspects of a face framing short haircut with bangs is choosing the right type of bangs. Some popular options include:

  • Straight across bangs
  • Side swept bangs
  • Wispy bangs
  • Micro bangs

It's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape and complements your overall look.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're interested in trying this style at home, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle.
  3. Section off the hair in front of your ears and clip it away.
  4. Cut the back of your hair to the desired length.
  5. Add layers to create the face framing effect.
  6. Unclip the front sections of your hair.
  7. Cut the bangs to the desired length and style.
  8. Use a flat iron to style your hair.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

Here are a few tips for keeping your face framing short haircut with bangs looking great:

  • Get regular trims to keep the style looking fresh.
  • Invest in a quality flat iron to keep your hair looking sleek.
  • Use a volumizing mousse to add volume to your roots.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQs

Is this style suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, a face framing short haircut with bangs can be customized to suit any face shape.

How often should I get a trim?

It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking fresh.

Can I still wear my hair up with this style?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with this style. Experiment with different updos to find what works best for you.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts with Bangs

Here are a few tips for caring for your hair after getting a face framing short haircut with bangs:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Invest in a quality leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.

A face framing short haircut with bangs is a stylish and versatile option for those who want to change up their look. Whether you go to a stylist or try the style at home, be sure to choose the right bangs for your face shape and maintain the style with regular trims and proper hair care.
