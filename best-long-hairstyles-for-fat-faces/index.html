Best Long Hairstyles For Fat Faces

Written by Petter Dec 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Having a fat face doesn't mean you can't rock long hair. In fact, there are many long hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and make you feel confident and beautiful. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there's a long hairstyle out there that will suit you. In this article, we'll be discussing the best long hairstyles for fat faces and how to style them.

  • Best Long Hairstyles for Fat Faces

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we get into the best long hairstyles for fat faces, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a hairstyle that will flatter your face and enhance your features. Here are the steps to determine your face shape:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Using a lipstick or eyeliner, trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and look at the shape you've traced.

There are six common face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. If you're not sure which shape you have, there are many online face shape quizzes that can help you determine your shape.

Best Long Hairstyles for Fat Faces

Now that you know your face shape, let's take a look at the best long hairstyles for fat faces:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck. This face shape can pull off almost any long hairstyle. Consider trying long layers, beachy waves, or a sleek high ponytail.

Round Face Shape

For a round face shape, you'll want to create length and angles to slim down your face. Try a long layered cut with side-swept bangs or a deep side part. Avoid blunt cuts and center parts.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you'll want to soften your jawline with layers and waves. Consider a layered cut with long, loose waves or a side-swept fringe.

Heart Face Shape

For a heart face shape, you'll want to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones. Try a long layered cut with face-framing layers or a half-up half-down style with loose waves.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you'll want to create balance between your forehead and chin. Try a long layered cut with side-swept bangs or a deep side part. Avoid center parts and heavy blunt cuts.

Oblong Face Shape

For an oblong face shape, you'll want to create width and volume. Consider a long layered cut with loose waves or a side-swept fringe to balance out your features.

Tips for Styling Long Hair on a Fat Face

Now that you have some ideas for long hairstyles that will flatter your face shape, here are some tips for styling long hair on a fat face:

  • Consider getting layers to add movement and dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt cuts or heavy bangs.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to see what flatters your face shape the most.
  • Try adding volume at the crown of your head to elongate your face.
  • Accessorize with headbands, clips, and scarves to draw attention away from your face.

FAQs

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a fat face?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a fat face. Just make sure to keep it high and sleek to elongate your face.

What's the best way to add volume to my hair?

The best way to add volume to your hair is by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow-drying your hair upside down, and using a round brush when styling.

Can I still wear heavy bangs if I have a round face?

It's best to avoid heavy bangs if you have a round face, as they can make your face appear wider. Consider side-swept or wispy bangs instead.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Long hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for long hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you're sure to find a long hairstyle that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
