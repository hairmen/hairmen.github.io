Mens Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut? Look no further than the mens haircut shaved sides long top! This haircut has been gaining popularity in recent years and is perfect for those who want to maintain a professional look while still having a bit of edginess to their style.

Description

The mens haircut shaved sides long top is a versatile style that allows you to keep some length on the top while keeping the sides shaved or buzzed. This style can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type, making it a great option for men with thick or thin hair.

One of the reasons this style has become so popular is because it is low maintenance. You won't have to worry about styling your hair every morning, as the shaved sides will give you a clean and polished look. However, you can still play around with the length on the top and style it with products if you want to switch things up.

How to Get the Mens Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top

If you're interested in getting the mens haircut shaved sides long top, it's important to find a reputable barber or stylist who has experience with this style. They will be able to give you a personalized consultation and recommend the best length and style for your hair type and face shape.

When you're in the chair, your barber will start by shaving or buzzing the sides of your head. They may leave a bit of length around the ears and back of the head, depending on your preference. Then, they will trim the hair on the top of your head to your desired length.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to getting the mens haircut shaved sides long top:

  1. Find a reputable barber or stylist who has experience with this style.
  2. Consult with your barber to determine the best length and style for your hair type and face shape.
  3. Sit back and relax while your barber shaves or buzzes the sides of your head.
  4. Trim the hair on the top of your head to your desired length.
  5. Style the hair on top using products if desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens haircut shaved sides long top:

  • Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Invest in a good styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to keep the hair on top looking polished.
  • If you have thinning hair, consider using a volumizing product to give the illusion of more volume on top.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens haircut shaved sides long top:

What face shapes does this haircut suit?

This haircut can suit a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and diamond.

Can I still style the hair on top?

Absolutely! The hair on top can be styled using products such as pomade or wax to achieve your desired look.

Is this haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut can be customized to suit all hair types, whether you have thick, thin, straight, or curly hair.

Hair Care for Mens Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top

To keep your mens haircut shaved sides long top looking its best, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, which can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a good styling product to keep the hair on top looking polished.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.

Overall, the mens haircut shaved sides long top is a stylish and versatile option that can suit a variety of face shapes and hair types. By following a good hair care routine and visiting your barber or stylist regularly, you can keep your haircut looking fresh and polished.
