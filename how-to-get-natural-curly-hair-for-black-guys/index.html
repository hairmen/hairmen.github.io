How To Get Natural Curly Hair For Black Guys

Written by Christine Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a black guy who wants to achieve natural curly hair? You might think that it's impossible because of your natural hair type, but it's actually achievable through proper hair care and styling techniques. In this article, we'll show you how to get natural curly hair for black guys.

Description

Natural curly hair is a popular hairstyle for black guys, but not everyone is born with it. If you have straight or relaxed hair, you might want to achieve natural curls for a more unique and trendy look. This hairstyle is not only stylish but also low maintenance, making it perfect for busy guys who don't have much time to style their hair.

How to

Step 1: Avoid Relaxers and Chemical Treatments

The first step in achieving natural curly hair is to avoid relaxers and other chemical treatments that straighten your hair. These products can damage your hair and prevent it from growing naturally. Instead, embrace your natural hair texture and work with it to achieve the curls you want.

Step 2: Moisturize Your Hair

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it's important to keep it moisturized to prevent breakage and damage. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated throughout the day. For best results, use products that are specifically designed for curly hair.

Step 3: Use the Right Tools

To achieve natural curls, you'll need to use the right tools. Start with a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and then use a curling cream or gel to define your curls. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls and add volume.

Step 4: Experiment with Different Hairstyles

Once you've achieved natural curls, you can experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you. You can wear your hair in a twist-out, braid-out, or wash-and-go style, depending on your hair texture and personal preferences. Don't be afraid to try new styles and techniques to find the perfect look for you.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve natural curly hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and prevent it from curling naturally.
  • Protect your hair while you sleep by wearing a silk or satin bonnet or using a silk or satin pillowcase.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote hair growth.

FAQ

Can I achieve natural curly hair if I have a relaxer?

If you currently have a relaxer, it's possible to transition to natural hair over time. You'll need to stop using relaxers and other chemical treatments and let your hair grow out. It may take several months or even a year for your hair to fully transition, but with proper care, you can achieve natural curly hair.

What products should I use for natural curly hair?

For natural curly hair, it's important to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Look for shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that contain moisturizing ingredients, such as shea butter, coconut oil, and argan oil. You may also want to use a deep conditioner or hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

How often should I wash my natural curly hair?

How often you should wash your hair depends on your hair type and personal preferences. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once a week, while those with oily hair may need to wash it more frequently. Experiment with different washing schedules to find the one that works best for you.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for achieving natural curly hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote hair growth.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve natural curly hair for black guys. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
