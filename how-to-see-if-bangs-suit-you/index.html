How to See If Bangs Suit You - Tips, Step by Step and FAQ

Are you considering getting bangs but not sure if they will suit you? Bangs can be a great way to switch up your look and add some edge to your hairstyle, but they are not for everyone. In this article, we will provide you with tips, step-by-step guide and FAQ on how to see if bangs suit you.

Tip 1: Consider Your Face Shape

One of the most important factors to consider when deciding if bangs will suit you is your face shape. Bangs can highlight or minimize certain features of your face, so it's important to choose a style that complements your face shape.

Oval face shape: If you have an oval face shape, you are in luck! Almost any style of bangs will suit you.

Round face shape: If you have a round face shape, go for long, side-swept bangs that will lengthen your face. Avoid blunt bangs that will make your face appear rounder.

Square face shape: If you have a square face shape, go for soft, wispy bangs that will soften your features. Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs that will make your face appear more angular.

Heart face shape: If you have a heart face shape, go for side-swept bangs that will draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones. Avoid blunt bangs that will make your forehead appear wider.

Diamond face shape: If you have a diamond face shape, go for blunt, straight-across bangs that will balance your features. Avoid wispy bangs that will make your face appear longer.

Tip 2: Try a Temporary Bangs

If you are still unsure if bangs will suit you, try a temporary solution first. You can purchase clip-in bangs or try a faux-bangs hairstyle to see how you look with bangs. This will give you a better idea of whether or not you want to commit to the real thing.

Tip 3: Consult with Your Stylist

Your stylist is your best resource when it comes to deciding if bangs will suit you. They can provide you with recommendations based on your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in determining if bangs will suit you is to determine your face shape. Use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, use these measurements to determine your face shape.

Step 2: Look at Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider when deciding if bangs will suit you. If you have curly hair, bangs may be difficult to maintain and may require more styling. If you have fine hair, blunt bangs may not be the best option as they can appear too heavy.

Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

Another important factor to consider when deciding if bangs will suit you is your lifestyle. If you are someone who enjoys working out or swimming, bangs may not be the most practical option for you. If you have a busy schedule, you may not have the time to style your bangs every day.

FAQ

Q: Will bangs suit me if I wear glasses?

A: Yes, bangs can look great with glasses! Choose a style that complements the shape of your glasses.

Q: Can I still wear a ponytail with bangs?

A: Yes, you can still wear a ponytail with bangs. You can either leave your bangs out or sweep them to the side.

Q: How often do I need to trim my bangs?

A: Bangs require more maintenance than the rest of your hair. You should plan on trimming them every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

1. Wash your bangs more often: Bangs can become oily and greasy more quickly than the rest of your hair. Wash them more often to keep them looking fresh.

2. Blow-dry your bangs: Use a round brush and blow-dry your bangs to keep them looking smooth and sleek.

3. Use dry shampoo: If your bangs become oily between washes, use dry shampoo to refresh them.

4. Avoid touching your bangs: Avoid touching or playing with your bangs throughout the day, as this can make them look greasy and unkempt.

5. Use a small amount of styling product: Use a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or hairspray, to keep your bangs in place throughout the day.

By following these tips, you can determine if bangs will suit you and keep them looking great. Remember, bangs can be a fun and edgy way to switch up your look, so don't be afraid to try them out!
