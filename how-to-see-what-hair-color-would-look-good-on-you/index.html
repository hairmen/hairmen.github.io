How To See What Hair Color Would Look Good On You Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Sep 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you thinking of changing your hair color but not sure what would look good on you? You're not alone! Choosing a hair color that complements your skin tone, eye color, and personal style can be tricky. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to see what hair color would look good on you.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Hair Colors for Warm Skin Tones
  • Hair Colors for Cool Skin Tones
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right hair color is to determine your skin tone. There are two main categories of skin tones: warm and cool. Warm skin tones have yellow, golden, or peach undertones, while cool skin tones have pink, red, or blue undertones.

You can determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If your veins appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If your veins appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell whether your veins are blue or green, you may have a neutral skin tone.

Another way to determine your skin tone is to look at the jewelry that looks best on you. If you look better in gold jewelry, you have a warm skin tone. If you look better in silver jewelry, you have a cool skin tone.

Hair Colors for Warm Skin Tones

If you have a warm skin tone, you'll look best in hair colors that have warm undertones, such as golden brown, honey blonde, or auburn. These colors will complement your skin tone and bring out the warmth in your complexion.

Some examples of hair colors for warm skin tones include:

  • Golden brown
  • Honey blonde
  • Auburn
  • Copper
  • Caramel
  • Red

Hair Colors for Cool Skin Tones

If you have a cool skin tone, you'll look best in hair colors that have cool undertones, such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, or cool brown. These colors will complement your skin tone and bring out the coolness in your complexion.

Some examples of hair colors for cool skin tones include:

  • Ash blonde
  • Platinum blonde
  • Cool brown
  • Jet black
  • Blue-black
  • Burgundy

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips for choosing the right hair color:

  • Consider your eye color: Your eye color can also play a role in choosing the right hair color. If you have blue or green eyes, you may want to consider a cooler hair color. If you have brown eyes, you may want to consider a warmer hair color.
  • Consult with a professional: If you're still unsure about which hair color to choose, it's always a good idea to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you determine the best color for your skin tone, eye color, and personal style.
  • Try temporary hair color: If you're hesitant about committing to a permanent hair color, you can always try a temporary hair color first. This will give you a chance to see how the color looks on you before making a more permanent change.
  • Take care of your hair: No matter what hair color you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling tools, and protect your hair from the sun and pool chemicals.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hair color suits me?

The hair color that suits you depends on your skin tone, eye color, and personal style. Warm skin tones look best in warm hair colors, while cool skin tones look best in cool hair colors.

How do I know if a hair color will look good on me?

You can get a good idea of how a hair color will look on you by considering your skin tone, eye color, and personal style. You can also try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to see how different hair colors look on you.

Can I change my hair color if I have dyed hair?

Yes, you can change your hair color if you have dyed hair. However, it may be more difficult to achieve the desired color if you have previously dyed your hair. It's best to consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best approach.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your colored hair:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Avoid heat styling tools
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pool chemicals
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips, you can help your hair color look its best and last longer.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, but by considering your skin tone, eye color, and personal style, you can find a color that looks great on you. Remember to take care of your hair and consult with a professional hairstylist if you're unsure about which color to choose. With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking a new hair color in no time!
