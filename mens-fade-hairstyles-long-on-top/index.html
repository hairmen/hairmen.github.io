Mens Fade Hairstyles Long On Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Jul 29, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and modern haircut that will make you stand out from the crowd, then you might want to consider getting a mens fade hairstyle long on top. This type of haircut is characterized by a short back and sides that gradually blend into longer hair on top. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of ways, from messy and textured to slick and polished. In this article, we'll show you how to get the perfect mens fade hairstyle long on top, as well as provide you with tips on how to maintain and care for your hair.

Description

A mens fade hairstyle long on top is a haircut that features a gradual taper from short hair on the sides and back to longer hair on top. The hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, textured, or messy. This hairstyle is popular among men of all ages and is suitable for all hair types.

How to Get a Mens Fade Hairstyle Long on Top

Getting a mens fade hairstyle long on top can be done in a few simple steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Fade

The first step in getting a mens fade hairstyle long on top is to choose the type of fade you want. There are many different types of fades to choose from, including low fade, mid fade, and high fade. A low fade starts just above the ear, while a high fade starts at the temples. A mid fade is somewhere in between.

Step 2: Decide on Hair Length

Once you've chosen your fade, you need to decide on the length of hair you want on top. This can range from a few inches to several inches, depending on your personal preference and the style you want to achieve.

Step 3: Cut the Hair on the Sides and Back

The next step is to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head, gradually blending it into the longer hair on top. This should be done by a professional barber or stylist, as it can be difficult to achieve the perfect fade on your own.

Step 4: Style the Hair on Top

Once the hair on the sides and back is cut, it's time to style the hair on top. This can be done in a variety of ways, depending on your personal preference and the look you want to achieve. Some popular styles include slicked back, messy and textured, and pompadour.

Tips for Maintaining a Mens Fade Hairstyle Long on Top

Once you've got your mens fade hairstyle long on top, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Regular Trims

To keep your mens fade hairstyle looking sharp, it's important to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks. This will help to keep the fade looking fresh and prevent split ends.

Tip 2: Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in how your mens fade hairstyle looks and feels. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, as well as styling products that are designed for your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Exposure to heat from styling tools like blow dryers and flat irons can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. Use a heat protectant spray before using these tools and avoid using them too often.

FAQ

Q: Is a mens fade hairstyle long on top suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, a mens fade hairstyle long on top can be adapted to suit all hair types, from straight and fine to curly and thick.

Q: How often should I get a trim for my mens fade hairstyle?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your mens fade hairstyle looking sharp.

Q: Can I style my mens fade hairstyle in different ways?

A: Yes, the hair on top of a mens fade hairstyle can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and textured.

Hair Care for Mens Fade Hairstyles Long on Top

Mens fade hairstyles long on top require proper hair care to maintain their shape and texture. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

Tip 1: Use a Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a quality shampoo and conditioner is key to maintaining healthy hair. Look for products that are suitable for your hair type and free from harsh chemicals.

Tip 2: Avoid Excessive Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. Use a heat protectant spray before using styling tools like blow dryers and flat irons and avoid using them too often.

Tip 3: Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts are important to keep your mens fade hairstyle looking sharp and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle.

In conclusion, a mens fade hairstyle long on top is a stylish and versatile haircut that can be adapted to suit any hair type and personal style. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can achieve the perfect mens fade hairstyle and keep it looking great for weeks to come.
