How To Curl The Left Side Of Your Hair With A Curling Iron

Written by Christine Oct 06, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking to add some extra volume and bounce to your hair, curling it with a curling iron is a great way to achieve that look. However, curling the left side of your hair can be a bit tricky. In this blog post, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to curl the left side of your hair with a curling iron.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Curl the Left Side of Your Hair with a Curling Iron

Before we get started, it's important to note that the process of curling the left side of your hair is similar to curling the right side. The only difference is the angle at which you hold the curling iron. To curl the left side of your hair, you'll need to hold the curling iron in your right hand and vice versa.

What You'll Need

To curl the left side of your hair, you'll need the following:

  • Curling iron
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hair clips
  • Hairbrush

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to curl the left side of your hair with a curling iron:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from damage.
  3. Blow dry your hair until it's completely dry.
  4. Section your hair into two parts: the left and right side.
  5. Take a small section of hair from the left side and clip the rest of the hair away.
  6. Hold the curling iron in your right hand and place it at the roots of the section of hair you're holding.
  7. Wrap the section of hair around the curling iron away from your face.
  8. Hold the curling iron in place for 10-15 seconds before releasing the curl.
  9. Repeat steps 5-8 until you've curled all of the hair on the left side of your head.
  10. Let your hair cool for a few minutes before brushing it out with a hairbrush.
  11. Repeat the process on the right side of your head.
  12. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Curling the Left Side of Your Hair

Here are some tips to make curling the left side of your hair easier:

  • Practice makes perfect. If you're new to curling your hair, it may take some time to get the hang of it.
  • Use a curling iron with a rotating barrel to make it easier to curl your hair.
  • Don't hold the curling iron in one place for too long as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a lower heat setting if you have thin or damaged hair.
  • Use a higher heat setting if you have thick or coarse hair.

FAQs

Can I curl the left side of my hair with a flat iron?

Yes, you can curl the left side of your hair with a flat iron. The process is similar to curling your hair with a curling iron, but you'll need to twist your hair around the flat iron instead of wrapping it around the barrel.

How do I prevent my hair from getting tangled in the curling iron?

To prevent your hair from getting tangled in the curling iron, make sure to brush it out before curling it. You can also use hair clips to keep your hair in place while you're curling it.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, make sure to:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you'll be able to curl the left side of your hair with ease. Remember to take your time and practice until you get the hang of it. Happy styling!
