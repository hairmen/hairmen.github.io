How To Cut The Back Of Someone s Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to save money on haircuts or just want to learn how to cut hair, it's important to know how to cut the back of someone's hair. This can be a tricky area to tackle, but with the right tools and techniques, you can give a professional-looking cut in no time. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about how to cut the back of someone's hair.

How to

Before you begin, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section off the hair. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen the hair as you cut.

Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Section the Hair

Start by dividing the hair into sections. Use the comb to create a horizontal parting at the back of the head, just above the occipital bone. Clip the top section out of the way for now.

Step 2: Cut the Bottom Section

Take the bottom section of hair and comb it straight down. Use your fingers to hold the hair at the desired length and cut straight across. Make sure to cut small sections at a time to control the length and avoid mistakes.

Step 3: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the bottom section, you'll need to blend it into the top section. Release the top section of hair and comb it over the cut section. Use your scissors to blend the two sections together by cutting at a slight angle. This will create a seamless transition between the two sections.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

After you've blended the hair, comb it out and check for any uneven areas. Make any necessary adjustments by cutting small sections as needed.

Step 5: Finish the Look

Once you're satisfied with the cut, you can finish the look by styling the hair as desired. You may want to use a blow dryer, straightener, or curling iron to create the desired style.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get a great cut:

  • Start with small sections and work your way up to larger sections as you gain confidence.
  • Use a mirror to check your work as you go.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Keep your scissors sharp to avoid damaging the hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting the back of someone's hair:

Can I cut my own hair?

While it's possible to cut your own hair, it can be difficult to get the back of your head without help. It's best to have someone else cut your hair or practice on a friend or family member.

How often should I cut my hair?

How often you cut your hair depends on the length and style. Generally, shorter haircuts require more frequent trims to maintain the shape, while longer hair can go longer between cuts.

What should I do if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. Small mistakes can often be corrected by blending the hair or cutting off a little more. If you're not sure how to fix the mistake, it's best to seek the help of a professional.

Hair Care

After you've cut the back of someone's hair, it's important to take care of the hair to maintain the style. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too frequently. Regular trims will also help to keep the hair healthy and looking its best.

Now that you know how to cut the back of someone's hair, you can save money on haircuts and impress your friends and family with your new skills. Just remember to take your time and practice, practice, practice!
