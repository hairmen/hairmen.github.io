Best Haircut For Long Thick Hair Finding The Perfect Style

Written by Petter Sep 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long thick hair can be both beautiful and challenging to manage. While it's great to have a lot of hair, it can also be heavy, difficult to style, and prone to tangles. To make the most of your locks, it's essential to choose the right haircut. In this article, we'll explore some of the best haircuts for long thick hair, as well as tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful locks.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Haircut for Long Thick Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Long Thick Hair
  • FAQs

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Long Thick Hair

When it comes to choosing a haircut for long thick hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your face shape. Some styles may look better on round faces, while others flatter square or heart-shaped faces. Next, think about your hair texture. Some haircuts work better on straight hair, while others are ideal for curly or wavy locks.

Another important factor to consider is your lifestyle. If you're always on the go, you may prefer a low-maintenance cut that's easy to style. On the other hand, if you have more time to devote to your hair, you might opt for a more intricate style that requires more attention.

Popular Haircuts for Long Thick Hair

Here are some of the most popular haircuts for long thick hair:

  • Layered Cut: Layers can add movement and dimension to thick hair, making it easier to style. This cut works well for both straight and wavy hair.
  • Long Bob: Also known as a lob, this cut is a shorter version of the classic bob and works well for all face shapes and hair textures.
  • Side-Swept Bangs: Bangs can help frame your face and add interest to your hairstyle. Side-swept bangs are a great option for long thick hair, as they can be blended seamlessly into the rest of your hair.
  • Blunt Cut: A blunt cut can give your hair a sleek, polished look. This cut works best for straight or slightly wavy hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut

Once you've chosen the right haircut, it's time to visit your stylist. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect haircut for long thick hair:

  1. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair.
  2. Consult with your stylist about the cut you want.
  3. Section your hair and clip away the top layers.
  4. Starting at the back of your head, cut small sections of hair at a time.
  5. Work your way around your head, cutting each section to the desired length.
  6. Once you've finished cutting your hair, style it as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Long Thick Hair

Long thick hair requires a bit more care than other hair types. Here are some tips for keeping your locks healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid tying your hair up too tightly, as this can cause breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for long thick hair:

  • Q: Can I get a pixie cut with long thick hair?
  • A: While it's possible to get a pixie cut with long thick hair, it may be more difficult to style and maintain. It's best to consult with your stylist to find a cut that works for your hair type and face shape.
  • Q: How often should I get my hair cut?
  • A: It's generally recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Q: Will layers make my hair look thinner?
  • A: Layers can actually make your hair look thicker, as they add volume and movement to your locks.

Hair Care Products for Long Thick Hair

Here are some recommended hair care products for long thick hair:

  • Shampoo and conditioner: Look for products that are designed for thick hair, such as those that contain keratin or biotin.
  • Deep conditioner: Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Leave-in conditioner: A leave-in conditioner can help detangle your hair and add shine.
  • Hair oil: Apply a hair oil to the ends of your hair to prevent split ends and add moisture.
  • Hair mask: Use a hair mask once a month to give your locks an extra boost of moisture and nutrients.

With the right haircut and proper care, long thick hair can be a stunning and manageable asset. Whether you opt for layers, a long bob, or another style entirely, make sure to choose a cut that works for your unique hair type and face shape. And don't forget to take care of your locks with regular trims, deep conditioning treatments, and high-quality hair care products.
