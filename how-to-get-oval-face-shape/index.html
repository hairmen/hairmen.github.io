How To Get Oval Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Oct 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're unhappy with the shape of your face, you're not alone. Many people wish they had a more oval-shaped face, as it is considered the most aesthetically pleasing. Fortunately, there are steps you can take to achieve this look, whether through makeup, hairstyles, or facial exercises. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about how to get an oval face shape.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get an Oval Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining an Oval Face Shape
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for an Oval Face Shape

How to Get an Oval Face Shape

There are several ways to achieve an oval face shape, including:

  • Makeup
  • Hairstyles
  • Facial exercises

Combining these methods can help you achieve the best results.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting an oval face shape:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can work on achieving an oval face shape, you need to determine your current face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. You can determine your face shape by pulling your hair back and examining your face in the mirror.

2. Use Makeup to Contour Your Face

Using makeup to contour your face can help create the illusion of an oval face shape. Apply bronzer to the hollows of your cheeks, along your jawline, and around your hairline to create shadow and definition. Use a highlighter on the tops of your cheekbones, the bridge of your nose, and above your eyebrows to add dimension.

3. Choose the Right Hairstyle

Your hairstyle can also play a role in the shape of your face. Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, such as a long bob or side-swept bangs. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a blunt bob.

4. Do Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help tone and strengthen the muscles in your face, which can help create a more defined and oval shape. Try exercises such as jaw clenching, cheek puffing, and tongue pressing to help sculpt your face.

Tips for Maintaining an Oval Face Shape

Once you've achieved an oval face shape, there are steps you can take to maintain it:

  • Continue to use makeup to contour your face
  • Maintain a healthy diet and exercise regularly to keep your face toned
  • Choose hairstyles that flatter your face shape
  • Avoid hairstyles and accessories that add width to your face

FAQs

Q: Can I really change the shape of my face?

A: While you can't completely change the bone structure of your face, you can create the illusion of a different shape through makeup, hairstyles, and facial exercises.

Q: How long does it take to see results from facial exercises?

A: It may take several weeks or even months of consistent facial exercises to see results.

Hair Care for an Oval Face Shape

Choosing the right hairstyle for your oval face shape can help highlight your best features. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head, such as a layered cut
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a blunt bob
  • Consider a side part or side-swept bangs to create balance
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and volume to your hair

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve an oval face shape and feel more confident in your appearance. Remember to be patient and consistent with your efforts, as it may take time to see results.
