How To Get Waves With Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Are you tired of your curly hair and want to try something new? Well, you're in luck! Getting waves with curly hair is easier than you think. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to get waves with curly hair, step by step. We'll also provide you with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to ensure that your waves look their best. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Waves with Curly Hair

How to Get Waves with Curly Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about what exactly waves are. Waves are a hairstyle that involves creating light curls that form a "S" pattern. This hairstyle is popular among men and women and is perfect for those who want to add some texture to their hair.

Getting waves with curly hair requires some work, but it's definitely achievable. Here are some things you'll need:

  • Wave brush
  • Durag or wave cap
  • Pomade or hair gel
  • Wide-tooth comb

Step by Step Guide

Now that you have everything you need let's get started with our step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls.
  2. Apply a small amount of pomade or hair gel to your hair. Make sure to distribute it evenly.
  3. Brush your hair in a circular motion using your wave brush. Start at the crown of your head and work your way to the back.
  4. Once you've brushed your hair in a circular motion, switch to a side-to-side brush motion. Brush from side to side, starting at the front of your head and working your way to the back.
  5. Put on your durag or wave cap to keep your hair in place. Leave it on for at least 30 minutes.
  6. Take off your durag or wave cap and use your wave brush to brush your hair again in a circular motion.
  7. Put your durag or wave cap back on and leave it on overnight.
  8. The next day, take off your durag or wave cap and use your wave brush to brush your hair again in a circular motion.
  9. Apply a small amount of pomade or hair gel to your hair to hold the waves in place.
  10. Repeat this process daily to maintain your waves.

Tips for Getting Perfect Waves

Now that you know how to get waves with curly hair let's talk about some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Brush your hair twice a day: Brushing your hair twice a day will help train your hair to form the "S" pattern that waves are known for.
  • Use a good quality wave brush: A good quality wave brush will help you achieve better results. Look for a brush with firm bristles that can penetrate your hair.
  • Be patient: Getting waves with curly hair takes time and patience. Don't get discouraged if you don't see results right away.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase: Sleeping on a satin or silk pillowcase will help prevent frizz and keep your waves looking their best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting waves with curly hair:

Can I get waves with short hair?

Yes, you can get waves with short hair. However, it may take longer to achieve the desired results.

How long does it take to get waves with curly hair?

It can take anywhere from 2-6 weeks to get waves with curly hair. It all depends on your hair type and how often you brush your hair.

Do I need to use pomade or hair gel to get waves?

Yes, using pomade or hair gel will help hold your waves in place and give you a more defined look.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Waves

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your waves:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to get waves with curly hair in no time. Just remember to be patient and consistent with your brushing routine. Good luck!
