How To Style Men s Hair Like The 50S

Written by Bardi Aug 26, 2023 · 3 min read

The 1950s were a time of classic style and grooming for men, and one of the most iconic looks of the era was the slicked-back, pompadour hairstyle. If you're looking to recreate this retro style, we've got you covered with this step-by-step guide on how to style men's hair like the 50s.

Description

The 50s were a time when men's hair was slicked back, with a high pompadour that was often styled with a side part. This classic look is still popular today and can be achieved with the right products and techniques.

How to

To achieve the 50s hairstyle, you'll need a few essential tools and products:

  • Comb
  • Hair gel or pomade
  • Hair dryer
  • Hairspray

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect 50s hairstyle:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

Before you begin styling your hair, make sure it's clean and damp. Shampoo and condition your hair, then towel dry until it's slightly damp.

Step 2: Apply hair gel or pomade

Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up to the tips. Make sure you apply enough product to hold your hair in place.

Step 3: Comb your hair back

Using a comb, comb your hair back away from your forehead. Create a side part if desired.

Step 4: Blow dry your hair

Use a hair dryer on low heat to dry your hair while combing it back. This will help set the style and add volume to your hair.

Step 5: Add hairspray

Once your hair is dry, add a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you achieve the perfect 50s hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality hair gel or pomade for best results.
  • Don't apply too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • If you have longer hair, consider getting a haircut to achieve the classic 50s look.
  • Experiment with different combs and brushes to find the best one for your hair type.

FAQ

What type of hair gel or pomade should I use?

Look for a high-quality, water-based hair gel or pomade that provides a strong hold without weighing your hair down or making it look greasy.

Do I need to blow dry my hair?

Blow drying your hair can help set the style and add volume, but it's not necessary. If you prefer, you can let your hair air dry naturally after applying product.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's generally recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days, as washing too often can strip your hair of its natural oils and lead to dryness and breakage.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair straighteners and curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to recreate the classic 50s hairstyle and achieve a timeless, retro look that's sure to turn heads.
