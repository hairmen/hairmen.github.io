How To Dry Curly Hair Naturally A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 19, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that drying your hair can be a challenge. Blow-drying can cause frizz and damage, while air-drying can take forever. Luckily, there are ways to dry curly hair naturally that can help you achieve beautiful, bouncy curls without the heat damage. In this guide, we’ll take you through the steps to dry your curly hair naturally.

How to Dry Curly Hair Naturally

When you’re drying your curly hair naturally, it’s important to be gentle and patient. You don’t want to rough up your hair and cause frizz. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Squeeze Out Excess Water

After you’ve washed your hair, gently squeeze out the excess water with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt. Avoid rubbing your hair with a regular towel, as this can cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help detangle and moisturize your curls. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to distribute the conditioner evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and set it to a low or medium heat setting. Hold the diffuser up to your hair, and scrunch your curls gently as you dry them.

Step 4: Finish with a Hair Oil

Once your hair is dry, apply a hair oil to help seal in moisture and add shine. Apply a small amount to your palms, rub your hands together, and then scrunch your hair gently.

Tips for Drying Curly Hair Naturally

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when drying your curly hair naturally:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much while it’s drying. This can cause frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to squeeze out the excess water.
  • Don’t rub your hair with a regular towel.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle and moisturize your curls.
  • Use a diffuser on your blow dryer to help dry your hair without causing frizz.
  • Finish with a hair oil to help seal in moisture and add shine.

FAQ

How long does it take for curly hair to air-dry naturally?

This can vary depending on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as the humidity in your environment. On average, it can take anywhere from 2-4 hours for curly hair to air-dry naturally.

Can I use a regular towel to dry my curly hair?

It’s best to avoid using a regular towel to dry your curly hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out the excess water.

Can I blow-dry my curly hair without a diffuser?

Blow-drying curly hair without a diffuser can cause frizz and damage. It’s best to use a diffuser on your blow dryer to help dry your hair without causing frizz.

Hair Care

Taking care of your curly hair can help it look its best. Here are some tips for curly hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help moisturize your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz.

By following these tips and using the natural drying method outlined in this guide, you can achieve beautiful, healthy-looking curls without the heat damage.
