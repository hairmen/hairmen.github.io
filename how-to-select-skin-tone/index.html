How To Select Skin Tone A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Selecting the right skin tone can make all the difference in achieving a flawless makeup look. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect shade. In this guide, we’ll walk you through how to select skin tone, step by step.

Table of Content

  1. How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Choosing the Right Skin Tone
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Your Skin Tone

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

Before selecting a skin tone, it’s essential to determine your skin’s undertone. Your skin’s undertone is the underlying color beneath the surface of your skin. There are three common undertones: cool, warm, and neutral.

The easiest way to determine your skin’s undertone is to look at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear blue, you have a cool undertone. If your veins appear green, you have a warm undertone. If you can’t tell if your veins are blue or green, you likely have a neutral undertone.

Another way to determine your undertone is to look at the color of your skin. If you burn easily in the sun and have pink or rosy undertones, you have a cool undertone. If you tan easily in the sun and have golden or peachy undertones, you have a warm undertone. If your skin doesn’t burn or tan easily and appears to have a mix of cool and warm undertones, you have a neutral undertone.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you’ve determined your skin’s undertone, it’s time to select the right skin tone. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Determine the Coverage You Need

Do you want full coverage or something more sheer? This will determine the type of product you need, such as a foundation, tinted moisturizer, or BB cream.

Step 2: Choose a Formula

Consider your skin type and the finish you want. If you have oily skin, a matte formula may work best. If you have dry skin, a dewy formula may be more flattering.

Step 3: Consider Your Undertone

Now that you know your undertone, use it to guide your selection. If you have a cool undertone, look for shades with blue or pink undertones. If you have a warm undertone, look for shades with yellow or golden undertones. If you have a neutral undertone, you can wear a range of shades.

Step 4: Test the Product

Always test the product before purchasing it. Apply it to your jawline and blend it out. The shade that disappears into your skin is the right one for you.

Tips for Choosing the Right Skin Tone

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when selecting a skin tone:

  • Consider the season. Your skin tone may change throughout the year, so you may need to adjust your shade accordingly.
  • Check the lighting. Natural light is the best for testing makeup shades.
  • Ask for a sample. Many makeup stores offer samples to try before purchasing.
  • Don’t forget about your neck. Your face and neck should match in color.

FAQs

What if I can’t find a perfect match?

If you can’t find a perfect match, consider mixing two shades together to create a custom shade.

Can I use the same skin tone all year round?

No, your skin tone may change throughout the year due to sun exposure and other factors. It’s essential to reevaluate your shade as the seasons change.

What if I have multiple undertones?

If you have multiple undertones, you likely have a neutral undertone. Look for shades that have a mix of warm and cool undertones.

Hair Care for Your Skin Tone

Your hair color can also complement your skin tone. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Cool undertones: ash blonde, platinum blonde, cool brown, black
  • Warm undertones: golden blonde, caramel, warm brown, red
  • Neutral undertones: a range of shades can work

Remember, these are just guidelines, and ultimately, you should choose a hair color that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Selecting the right skin tone takes time and patience, but it’s worth it for a flawless makeup look. Use these tips and guidelines to find the perfect shade for you.
