What Is The Best Small Dog That Doesn t Shed

Written by Frank Oct 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a small dog that doesn't shed, you're in luck. There are many breeds of small dogs that are hypoallergenic and don't shed, making them perfect for people with allergies or those who don't want to deal with the mess of shedding. In this article, we'll explore the best small dog breeds that don't shed and give you tips on how to care for their hair.

Description

Hypoallergenic dogs are breeds that produce fewer allergens, which can cause allergic reactions in some people. These breeds are perfect for people who have allergies or who live in small spaces where shedding can be a problem. Small dogs, in particular, are great for apartments or houses with limited space.

How to Choose the Best Small Dog that Doesn't Shed

When choosing a small dog that doesn't shed, there are a few things to consider. First, think about your lifestyle and how much time you have to devote to your pet. Some breeds require more exercise and attention than others. Second, consider your allergies. Some hypoallergenic breeds may still cause allergic reactions in some people. Finally, think about the breed's temperament and whether it will fit in with your family's lifestyle.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Best Small Dog that Doesn't Shed

Step 1: Research hypoallergenic dog breeds. Look for breeds that are known for producing fewer allergens and that are small in size.

Step 2: Consider your lifestyle. Do you have time to exercise and play with your pet? Some breeds require more attention than others.

Step 3: Think about your allergies. While hypoallergenic breeds produce fewer allergens, they may still cause allergic reactions in some people.

Step 4: Consider the breed's temperament. Some breeds are more high-strung than others and may not be a good fit for families with young children.

Step 5: Meet the breed before committing to a pet. Spend time with the breed to get a sense of its temperament and whether it will fit in with your family's lifestyle.

Tips for Caring for Your Small Dog's Hair

While small dogs that don't shed require less grooming than other breeds, there are still some things you can do to keep their hair healthy and looking its best.

Tip 1: Brush your dog regularly. Regular brushing helps remove loose hair and dirt, and keeps your dog's coat looking shiny and healthy.

Tip 2: Bathe your dog as needed. Use a mild, hypoallergenic shampoo and conditioner to keep your dog's coat clean and moisturized.

Tip 3: Keep your dog's hair trimmed. Regular grooming, including haircuts and nail trims, can help keep your dog healthy and looking its best.

FAQ

What are the best small dog breeds that don't shed?

Some of the best small dog breeds that don't shed include the Bichon Frise, the Chinese Crested, the Havanese, the Maltese, the Poodle, and the Shih Tzu.

Do hypoallergenic dogs still produce allergens?

Yes, hypoallergenic dogs produce fewer allergens, but they still produce some allergens. Some people with allergies may still have allergic reactions to hypoallergenic breeds.

Do small dogs require less exercise than larger breeds?

While small dogs may require less exercise than larger breeds, they still need daily exercise and playtime to stay healthy and happy.

Hair Care for Your Small Dog

Small dogs that don't shed require less grooming than other breeds, but they still need regular care to keep their hair healthy and looking its best.

Regular brushing, bathing, and grooming can help keep your dog's coat clean and shiny, and reduce the risk of skin infections and other health problems. If you're not sure how to care for your dog's hair, talk to your veterinarian or a professional groomer for advice.
