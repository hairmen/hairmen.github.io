How To Cut Long Hair Blunt Cut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Cutting long hair can be intimidating, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a beautiful blunt cut. In this guide, we'll go over the steps to cut long hair bluntly, as well as tips and frequently asked questions to help you achieve your desired look.

Table of Contents

  1. Tools Needed
  2. Preparation
  3. Sectioning
  4. Cutting
  5. Blending
  6. Tips for Blunt Cutting Long Hair
  7. Frequently Asked Questions
  8. Hair Care Tips After Cutting

Tools Needed

Before you begin, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Sharp hairdressing scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Large mirror
  • Towel or cape to protect clothes

Preparation

Start by washing and drying your hair. Comb through any tangles and make sure your hair is completely dry. Wet hair can appear longer than it actually is, so it's important to cut it when it's dry.

Sectioning

Section your hair into four parts: two at the front and two at the back. Use hair clips to secure each section. Take one section at a time and comb through it to ensure it's tangle-free.

Cutting

Take one section of hair and twist it into a tight rope. Hold the rope firmly between your fingers and use your other hand to cut across the bottom. Start by cutting a small amount and work your way up to your desired length. Repeat this process with each section of hair.

Blending

Once you have cut all the sections, it's time to blend them together. Comb through your hair to make sure there are no visible lines or uneven lengths. Take small sections of hair and cut at an angle to blend them together. This will create a seamless, blunt look.

Tips for Blunt Cutting Long Hair

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to your desired length. You can always cut more, but you can't add hair back once it's cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends and frayed hair.
  • Take breaks if your arms get tired. Cutting hair requires a lot of arm strength and endurance.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time and make sure each section is cut evenly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I cut my long hair?

It's recommended to get a trim every six to eight weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, but it's important to have the right tools and technique to avoid damaging your hair. If you're unsure, it's best to see a professional hairdresser.

Can I blunt cut my curly hair?

Yes, but make sure to cut it when it's dry and use a technique that works best for your curls. It's also important to blend the sections together to avoid a choppy look.

Hair Care Tips After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain a healthy and shiny look. Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair, avoid using hot tools too often, and protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf.

Blunt cutting long hair may seem daunting, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a beautiful and seamless look. Remember to take your time, start small, and blend the sections together for the best results.
