Is The Perm Back In Fashion

Written by William Nov 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old straight hair? Do you crave a new look that will give you volume and texture? Look no further than the perm! Yes, you read that right. The perm, which was popular in the 80s and 90s, is making a comeback in 2023. Read on to find out why and how you can jump on this trend.

Description: What is a Perm?

A perm is a chemical treatment that changes the structure of your hair to create curls or waves. The process involves applying a solution to your hair, then wrapping it around rods and applying heat to activate the solution. After a few hours, the rods are removed and your hair is left with a permanent curl pattern. The result is bouncy, voluminous curls that can last for several months.

How to: Getting a Perm

Getting a perm is a process that should be done by a professional hairstylist. Here are the steps involved:

Step 1: Consultation

Before you get a perm, you should have a consultation with your hairstylist. They will assess your hair type and determine if a perm is right for you. They will also discuss the type of curl pattern you want and recommend the appropriate rod size.

Step 2: Preparation

Your hair will need to be washed and dried before the perm. Your hairstylist may also apply a protective cream to your scalp and hairline to prevent irritation.

Step 3: Rolling

Your hair will be sectioned and wrapped around rods. The size and type of rod will depend on the type of curl pattern you want. Once all the sections are rolled, the hairstylist will apply the perm solution to your hair.

Step 4: Processing

Your hair will be covered with a plastic cap and left to process for about 30 minutes. The hairstylist will check your hair regularly to make sure it is processing properly.

Step 5: Neutralizing

The perm solution will be rinsed out of your hair, and a neutralizing solution will be applied to stop the chemical process. Your hair will be rinsed and the rods will be removed.

Step 6: Styling

Your hairstylist will style your hair by blow-drying and adding any additional products you may need.

Tips: Maintaining Your Perm

Now that you have a beautiful new perm, it's important to take care of it to make it last. Here are some tips:

  • Wait at least 48 hours before washing your hair to allow the curl pattern to set.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent the curls from becoming dry and frizzy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your curls.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair to keep your curls looking bouncy and defined.

FAQ: Common Questions About Perms

Q: Will a perm damage my hair?

A: Perm solutions can be damaging to your hair if not applied correctly or if your hair is already damaged. That's why it's important to have a consultation with a professional hairstylist before getting a perm.

Q: Can I color my hair after getting a perm?

A: It's best to wait at least two weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair. This will give your hair time to recover from the chemical treatment.

Q: How long will my perm last?

A: Perms can last anywhere from three to six months, depending on your hair type and how well you take care of your curls.

Hair Care: Keeping Your Hair Healthy

Getting a perm is a chemical process that can be damaging to your hair if not done correctly. That's why it's important to take care of your hair before and after the perm. Here are some tips:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.

So, is the perm back in fashion? Yes! With modern techniques and products, the perm is a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. Talk to your hairstylist to see if a perm is right for you.
