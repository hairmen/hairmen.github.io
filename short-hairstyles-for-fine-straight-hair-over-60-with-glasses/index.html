Short Hairstyles For Fine Straight Hair Over 60 With Glasses

Written by Aprilia Sep 02, 2023 · 4 min read

As we age, our hair changes, and we may need to switch up our hairstyle to suit our changing hair type and face shape. For those with fine straight hair over 60 who wear glasses, finding the perfect hairstyle can be challenging. But fear not! We’ve compiled a list of short hairstyles that will not only flatter your face but also complement your glasses.

Description

Short hairstyles are perfect for those with fine straight hair over 60. They are low maintenance, easy to style, and can make you look younger and fresher. Whether you have a round, square, or heart-shaped face, there is a short hairstyle that will suit your face shape and glasses.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle

Choosing the right short hairstyle can be a daunting task, but there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, consider your face shape. If you have a round face, opt for a hairstyle that adds height and length to your face. If you have a square face, go for a hairstyle that softens your features. If you have a heart-shaped face, choose a hairstyle that balances your face shape.

Another thing to consider is your glasses. If you have large frames, choose a hairstyle that doesn’t compete with your glasses. If you have small frames, opt for a hairstyle that adds some volume to your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve some of the short hairstyles for fine straight hair over 60 with glasses:

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that is perfect for those with fine straight hair over 60. Here’s how to achieve it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Section your hair into four parts using hair clips
  3. Starting at the back, use a pair of scissors to cut your hair short
  4. Work your way up to the front, making sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides
  5. Once you’ve finished cutting, use a comb to style your hair into the desired shape

The Bob

The bob is a versatile short hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and glasses. Here’s how to achieve it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Section your hair into four parts using hair clips
  3. Starting at the back, use a pair of scissors to cut your hair short
  4. Work your way up to the front, making sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides
  5. Once you’ve finished cutting, use a round brush to style your hair into a bob shape
  6. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and add shine

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hairstyle:

  • Use a lightweight, volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my glasses with a short hairstyle?

A: Absolutely! Glasses can add a stylish touch to any short hairstyle.

Q: Will a short hairstyle make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. Short hairstyles can make you look younger and fresher if done correctly.

Q: Can I still style my short hair if I have fine straight hair over 60?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure to use lightweight styling products and avoid heavy ones that can weigh down your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy scalp and hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can dry out your scalp and hair
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy

With these short hairstyles for fine straight hair over 60 with glasses, you can look stylish and confident at any age. Remember to choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and glasses, and don’t forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
