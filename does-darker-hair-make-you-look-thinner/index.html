Does Darker Hair Make You Look Thinner

Written by William Sep 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Darker hair has been a go-to for people who want to change their look for years. But does it really make you look thinner? If you're looking for a way to slim down your face or body without hitting the gym, changing up your hair color might be worth considering.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

For many people, darker hair can create the illusion of a slimmer face and body. This is because darker shades of hair create a shadow effect that can help to define your facial features and create more depth. This can make your face look more sculpted and less round, which can make you appear thinner.

Additionally, darker hair can also help to draw attention away from problem areas. If you're self-conscious about your stomach, for example, having darker hair can help to create a focal point on your face and draw attention away from your midsection.

How to

If you're considering dyeing your hair to a darker shade, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

  • Consider your skin tone: If you have fair skin, going too dark can create a harsh contrast that can make your face look washed out. On the other hand, if you have a darker skin tone, you might want to avoid going too light, as this can create the opposite effect.
  • Think about maintenance: Darker hair can require more upkeep than lighter shades. Be prepared to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks to keep your color looking fresh.
  • Consult a professional: If you're not sure what shade of dark hair will look best on you, consider consulting a professional hair stylist. They can help you choose a color that will flatter your skin tone and enhance your features.

Step by Step

If you're ready to take the plunge and dye your hair darker, here are the basic steps you'll need to follow:

  1. Choose your color: Consider your skin tone and consult a professional if you're not sure what shade will look best on you.
  2. Prepare your hair: Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you begin. If you have any buildup from styling products, use a clarifying shampoo to remove it.
  3. Protect your skin: Apply petroleum jelly or a thick conditioner around your hairline and ears to prevent the dye from staining your skin.
  4. Apply the dye: Follow the instructions on the box to apply the dye evenly throughout your hair. Use a brush or applicator bottle to help you get into hard-to-reach areas.
  5. Wait: Depending on the brand and color, you may need to wait anywhere from 20-45 minutes for the dye to develop.
  6. Rinse: Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water until the water runs clear. Follow up with a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when dyeing your hair darker:

  • Invest in color-safe products: Using a color-safe shampoo and conditioner can help prolong the life of your color and prevent it from fading too quickly.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and cause your color to fade more quickly. Try to air dry your hair as often as possible, and use heat protectant products when you do use hot tools.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: UV rays can cause your hair color to fade more quickly, so be sure to wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray when you're out in the sun.

FAQ

Is darker hair slimming for everyone?

While darker hair can create the illusion of a slimmer face and body for many people, it's not a one-size-fits-all solution. The effectiveness of darker hair in creating a slimming effect will depend on a number of factors, including your skin tone, facial features, and body shape.

What shade of dark hair is most slimming?

The shade of dark hair that will be most slimming will depend on your individual features and skin tone. Generally, cooler shades of dark hair like espresso or chocolate brown tend to be more slimming than warmer shades like caramel or honey.

Hair Care

Once you've dyed your hair darker, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair and cause your color to fade more quickly.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your style looking fresh.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause your color to fade more quickly. Aim to wash your hair no more than every other day.

In conclusion, darker hair can be a great way to create the illusion of a slimmer face and body. By choosing the right shade and taking good care of your hair, you can create a look that enhances your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
