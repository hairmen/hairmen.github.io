How To Set Layered Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have layered hair, you know how challenging it can be to style it at home. Layered hair can look amazing, but it requires a bit of effort to make it look its best. In this article, we'll show you how to set your layered hair at home. We'll go over the tools and techniques you'll need to achieve the perfect style.

Table of Contents

  • How to Prepare Your Hair
  • Choosing the Right Tools
  • Setting Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Style
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Prepare Your Hair

Before you start setting your hair, you need to prepare it properly. The first step is to wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup from your hair, making it easier to style.

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage and frizz. Instead, gently squeeze out the excess water with the towel.

Choosing the Right Tools

To set your layered hair, you'll need a few tools. First, you'll need a good quality hair dryer. Look for a hair dryer that has multiple heat settings and a diffuser attachment. A diffuser will help to distribute the heat evenly through your hair, preventing damage and frizz.

You'll also need a round brush. Look for a brush with natural bristles, as these are gentler on your hair. The size of the brush will depend on the length of your hair. If you have short hair, choose a small brush. If you have long hair, choose a larger brush.

Setting Your Hair

Now that you've prepared your hair and chosen your tools, it's time to start setting your hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by dividing your hair into sections. If you have thick hair, you may need to divide it into more sections.
  2. Take one section of hair and wrap it around the round brush.
  3. Use the hair dryer on a low heat setting to dry the hair while gently pulling the brush downwards.
  4. Repeat this process for each section of hair.
  5. Once you've finished drying all of your hair, use a bit of hairspray to set the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Style

To keep your layered hair looking great, here are a few tips:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • To add volume to your hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my layered hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Q: Can I set my layered hair with hot rollers or curling irons?

A: Yes, you can. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, here are a few hair care tips:

  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to set your layered hair at home with ease. Just remember to be patient and gentle with your hair, and you'll achieve the perfect style in no time.
