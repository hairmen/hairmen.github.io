How To Pick A Haircut That Suits You

Written by Oliver Nov 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of getting haircuts that don't suit you? Picking the right haircut can be challenging, especially if you don't know what to look for. In this article, we'll be sharing tips on how to pick a haircut that suits you.

Table of Contents:

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Pick a Haircut That Suits You

Before making any decisions, it's important to understand your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. These factors play a crucial role in determining the right haircut for you.

Here are some tips to help you pick the perfect haircut:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in picking a haircut is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and triangle. Each face shape has its own unique characteristics and requires a specific type of haircut.

If you're not sure what your face shape is, try taking a selfie and tracing the outline of your face. You can also consult a hairstylist for help.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture also plays a role in picking the right haircut. If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to opt for a layered cut to add movement and reduce bulk. If you have fine, straight hair, a blunt cut may work better to create the illusion of volume.

3. Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle also plays a role in picking a haircut. If you're always on-the-go, you may want a low-maintenance cut that requires little upkeep. If you have a more relaxed lifestyle, you may be able to opt for a more high-maintenance style.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to picking a haircut that suits you:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

As mentioned earlier, determining your face shape is the first step in picking a haircut. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for haircuts that flatter your features.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Next, consider your hair texture. This will help you determine what type of haircut will work best for you.

3. Think About Your Lifestyle

Finally, think about your lifestyle. This will help you decide how much time and effort you're willing to put into styling your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you pick the perfect haircut:

1. Bring Pictures to Your Stylist

If you're not sure what type of haircut you want, bring pictures to your hairstylist for inspiration. This will help them understand your vision and make suggestions based on your face shape and hair texture.

2. Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone can also play a role in picking a haircut. If you have warm undertones, you may want to opt for a warm hair color. If you have cool undertones, a cool hair color may work better.

3. Don't Be Afraid to Experiment

Don't be afraid to try something new! Hair grows back, so if you're not happy with your haircut, you can always try something different next time.

FAQ

What haircut suits a round face?

A round face looks best with haircuts that add height and length to the face. A layered cut, long bob, or side-swept bangs are good options.

What haircut suits an oval face?

An oval face can pull off almost any haircut, but a long layered cut, blunt bob, or pixie cut are popular choices.

What haircut suits a square face?

A square face looks best with haircuts that soften the jawline, such as a layered cut, side-swept bangs, or long bob.

Hair Care

Once you've picked the perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for a shampoo that's sulfate-free and contains natural ingredients.

2. Condition Regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy. Use a conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting low.

Picking the right haircut can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. By following these tips and considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, you can find a haircut that suits you and makes you feel confident.
