Best Shoulder Length Hair For Round Face

Written by Christine Jun 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect hairstyle for your round face? Look no further than shoulder length hair! Not only does it frame your face beautifully, but it also offers a variety of styling options. In this article, we'll go through the best shoulder length haircuts for round faces, how to style them, and tips for maintaining your gorgeous locks.

Table of Contents

  • Best Shoulder Length Haircuts for Round Faces
  • How to Style Shoulder Length Hair for Round Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

Best Shoulder Length Haircuts for Round Faces

When it comes to choosing the best shoulder length haircut for a round face, it's important to find a style that elongates the face and adds dimension. Here are some of the top options:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for those with round faces as they help to create a slimming effect. This cut creates movement in the hair, which helps to break up the roundness of the face.

Blunt Bob

A blunt bob that falls just below the chin is another great option for those with round faces. This cut creates the illusion of a longer face and draws attention to the jawline.

Side-Swept Bangs

Adding side-swept bangs to a shoulder-length cut can help to elongate the face and create a slimming effect. This style draws attention to the eyes and away from the roundness of the face.

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair for Round Faces

Now that you've chosen the perfect shoulder length haircut for your round face, it's important to know how to style it. Here are some tips:

Blow Dry with a Round Brush

Blow drying your hair with a round brush can help to add volume and movement to your shoulder length cut. This technique also helps to elongate the face by lifting the roots.

Add Texture

Adding texture to your shoulder length hair can help to create dimension and break up the roundness of your face. Try curling your hair with a curling iron or adding some sea salt spray for a beachy look.

Accessorize

Adding accessories like headbands, clips, or scarves can draw attention away from the roundness of your face and add some personality to your look.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair

Now that you've got your perfect shoulder length haircut for your round face, here are some tips for keeping it healthy and beautiful:

Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims every 6-8 weeks will help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Use Heat Protectant

Using a heat protectant spray before styling your hair with heat tools can help to prevent damage and breakage.

Deep Condition

Deep conditioning your hair once a week can help to keep it moisturized and healthy.

FAQs

What is the best shoulder length haircut for a round face?

The best shoulder length haircut for a round face is one that creates dimension and elongates the face. Some great options include long layers, a blunt bob, or side-swept bangs.

How do I style shoulder length hair for a round face?

You can style shoulder length hair for a round face by blow drying with a round brush, adding texture, and accessorizing.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is gentle and nourishing for your hair type. Avoid using products with harsh chemicals that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Brushing

Brush your hair gently with a wide-tooth comb to avoid pulling and breaking your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

Drying

Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel to dry it as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, gently squeeze out the excess water and let your hair air dry or use a blow dryer on low heat.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect shoulder length haircut for your round face, style it with ease, and keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Happy styling!
