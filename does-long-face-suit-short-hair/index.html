Does Long Face Suit Short Hair

Written by Aprilia Nov 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hair has been trending for a while now, and many people are opting for the short hair look. However, one common question that arises when it comes to short hair is whether it suits people with long faces. Long faces are characterized by a high forehead, a narrow jawline, and a long chin. In this article, we will explore whether long faces suit short hair and provide tips on how to choose the right short hairstyle for a long face.

Description

Short hair is a great way to switch up your look and make a bold statement. However, it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape. Long faces can be a bit tricky to style, but that doesn't mean short hair is out of the question. With the right cut and styling techniques, short hair can look great on long faces.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for a Long Face

When it comes to choosing a short hairstyle for a long face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First and foremost, you want to choose a cut that will create the illusion of width in the face. This can be achieved by adding volume to the sides of the hair or by creating layers that frame the face.

Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as this can emphasize the length of the face. Instead, opt for styles with curls or waves that add volume and texture. Bangs can also be a great way to break up the length of the face, but be sure to choose a style that complements your face shape.

Short Hairstyles for Long Faces

Here are a few short hairstyles that are perfect for long faces:

  • Pixie cut with side-swept bangs
  • Bob with layers and curls
  • Asymmetrical bob
  • Short shag
  • Textured lob

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to style short hair for a long face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suited for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to avoid damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture.
  4. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add curls or waves to your hair.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling short hair for a long face:

  • Avoid styles that are too flat or straight, as this can emphasize the length of the face.
  • Create volume and texture using products like mousse or texturizing spray.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume to the roots.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Can people with long faces wear short hair?

Yes, people with long faces can wear short hair. The key is to choose a style that complements the face shape and creates the illusion of width.

What short hairstyles are best for long faces?

Short hairstyles with volume and texture are best for long faces. Styles with curls, waves, or layers that frame the face are ideal.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are a few hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly with products that are suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage the hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep the hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to keep the style looking fresh.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and frizz.

Now that you know that long faces can suit short hair, it's time to try out a new style. Remember to choose a cut that complements your face shape and to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
