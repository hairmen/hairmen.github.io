How To Make A Chignon With Medium Length Hair

Written by Luffy Sep 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for an elegant and sophisticated hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion? Look no further than the chignon! This classic updo is a timeless look that can be adapted to suit any hair type or length. In this article, we'll show you how to create a chignon with medium length hair in just a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make a Chignon with Medium Length Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Creating the Perfect Chignon
  • FAQs

How to Make a Chignon with Medium Length Hair

Before we get started, it's important to prepare your hair. Start by washing and conditioning it thoroughly to ensure it's clean and free of any tangles. Then, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent any damage from styling tools.

Next, gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Use a hair tie to secure it in place. Then, twist the ponytail tightly to create a rope-like effect. Coil the twisted ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Use as many bobby pins as necessary to keep the bun in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a chignon with medium length hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair, then apply a heat protectant.
  2. Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  3. Twist the ponytail tightly to create a rope-like effect.
  4. Coil the twisted ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  5. Use as many bobby pins as necessary to keep the bun in place.
  6. Spray your chignon with a light hold hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips for Creating the Perfect Chignon

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect chignon:

  • If you have fine or thin hair, consider using a volumizing mousse or spray before creating your chignon to add extra volume and texture.
  • For a more polished look, use a comb to smooth out any bumps or flyaways before securing your chignon with bobby pins.
  • Add a hair accessory, such as a jeweled hairpin or a delicate flower, to your chignon for a touch of glamour.
  • Experiment with different chignon styles, such as a messy chignon or a side-swept chignon, to find the perfect look for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about creating a chignon with medium length hair:

Q: Can I create a chignon with short hair?

A: Yes, you can create a chignon with short hair. However, you may need to use hair extensions or accessories to add volume and length to your hair.

Q: How long does a chignon last?

A: A chignon can last anywhere from a few hours to a full day, depending on the style and the products used to create it. To help your chignon last longer, use a strong hold hairspray and avoid touching or pulling at your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

Creating a chignon is a great way to style your hair, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often.
  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create a beautiful chignon with medium length hair in no time. Whether you're attending a wedding, prom, or just want to add a touch of elegance to your everyday look, a chignon is a classic and versatile hairstyle that's sure to impress.
