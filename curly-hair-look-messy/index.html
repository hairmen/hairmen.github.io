Curly Hair Look Messy Embrace The Natural Look

Written by Aprilia Aug 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks absolutely stunning when styled correctly, it can also look messy and unkempt at times. However, messy hair is in trend right now and if you have curly hair, it's time to embrace the natural look. In this article, we will discuss how to make your curly hair look messy in a way that looks intentional and fashionable.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Make Your Curly Hair Look Messy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks

Description

Curls are known for their bouncy and voluminous texture that adds an extra edge to your personality. Curly hair is versatile and can be styled in different ways from sleek and polished to wild and messy. The messy look is perfect for those who want to add a bit of edge and flair to their hair without spending hours in front of the mirror. Messy curls can be achieved with minimum effort and time using the right technique and products.

How to Make Your Curly Hair Look Messy

Messy hair is all about creating the illusion of effortlessness. The key to achieving the perfect messy look is to not overthink it. However, there are a few things you can do to make your curls look more defined and voluminous while still looking messy.

1. Start with the right products

Start with a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair. Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so opt for a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that will keep your hair hydrated and frizz-free. Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and add some hold.

2. Don't brush your hair

Brushing your curls will only make them frizzy and undefined. Instead, use your fingers to detangle your hair and gently scrunch your curls to enhance their natural shape.

3. Use a diffuser

A diffuser attachment for your hairdryer can work wonders for curly hair. It helps to distribute heat evenly and gives your curls more volume and definition. Dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat and low speed to avoid frizz and maintain the shape of your curls.

4. Embrace the messy look

Once your hair is dry, don't be afraid to mess it up a little. Use your fingers to separate your curls and create a messy, tousled look. Add some texturizing spray or dry shampoo to give your hair more volume and hold.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect messy curls:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair on low heat and low speed.
  4. Use your fingers to gently separate your curls and create a messy, tousled look.
  5. Add some texturizing spray or dry shampoo to give your hair more volume and hold.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect messy look:

  • Don't wash your hair every day. Curly hair tends to be drier, so washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to avoid frizz and maintain the shape of your curls while you sleep.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.
  • Experiment with different texturizing sprays and dry shampoos to find the one that works best for your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about messy curly hair:

Q: Can I achieve the messy look with short curly hair?

A: Yes, you can. The messy look works well with all lengths of curly hair. Just make sure to use the right products and techniques to enhance your curls.

Q: Will my hair look messy all day?

A: The messy look is meant to look effortless, so don't worry too much about your hair looking perfect all day. However, you can use a bit of texturizing spray or dry shampoo throughout the day to refresh your curls and add some hold.

Q: Can I still straighten my hair if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can. However, straightening your hair too often can damage your curls and make them harder to manage. Embrace your natural curls and try out different curly hairstyles instead.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and frizzy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushing can make your curls frizzy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect messy look for your curly hair. Remember to embrace your natural curls and have fun experimenting with different hairstyles and products.
