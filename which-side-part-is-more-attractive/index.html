Introduction

When it comes to men's hairstyles, the side part has been a classic for decades. However, the question remains: which side part is more attractive? While both the left and right side parts have their own distinct styles, there are certain factors to take into consideration when choosing the perfect side part for your face shape and hair type. In this article, we'll explore how to choose the right side part, a step-by-step guide to create the perfect side part, tips and tricks for maintaining your side part, frequently asked questions, and hair care for the perfect side part.

How to Choose the Right Side Part

The first thing to consider when choosing the right side part is your face shape. Generally, a side part works well with oval or rectangular face shapes. If you have a round face shape, a side part may not be the best option for you as it can make your face appear wider.

Another factor to consider is your hair type. If you have thick hair, a deep side part may work well for you as it can add volume and texture. However, if you have fine hair, a more subtle side part may be more suitable.

Left or Right Side Part?

When it comes to choosing between a left or right side part, there's no hard and fast rule. However, some hairstylists recommend choosing a side part that's opposite to your natural hair growth pattern. For example, if your hair naturally falls to the left, a right side part may be more suitable.

Step-by-Step Guide to Create the Perfect Side Part

Now that you've chosen the right side part for your face shape and hair type, it's time to create the perfect side part. Follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Using the end of a comb, create a straight line from the front of your hairline to the crown of your head
  3. Comb the hair on the left side of the line to the left and the hair on the right side of the line to the right
  4. Apply a small amount of pomade or hair wax to keep your side part in place

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Your Side Part

Once you've created the perfect side part, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips and tricks:

  • Use a comb to touch up your side part throughout the day
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair
  • If you have long hair, consider tying it back in a low ponytail or bun to avoid disrupting your side part

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I touch up my side part?

It depends on your hair type and how active you are throughout the day. Generally, it's a good idea to touch up your side part every few hours.

Can I create a side part with curly hair?

Yes, you can create a side part with curly hair. However, you may need to use more product to keep your curls in place.

Can I create a side part with short hair?

Yes, you can create a side part with short hair. However, you may need to use a smaller comb to create a more subtle part.

Hair Care for the Perfect Side Part

To keep your side part looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any buildup of product or oil
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too frequently as they can damage your hair

Ultimately, the choice between a left or right side part depends on your personal style and preference. By following these tips and tricks, you can create the perfect side part that's both attractive and practical.
