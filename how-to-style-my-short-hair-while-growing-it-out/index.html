How To Style My Short Hair While Growing It Out

Written by Robby Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you've recently cut your hair short and are now in the process of growing it out, you may be wondering how to style it in the meantime. Fortunately, there are plenty of ways to make the most of your short hair while it's growing out. In this article, we'll cover all the tips, tricks, and techniques you need to know to style your short hair while growing it out.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Hair While Growing It Out
  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • FAQs

How to Style Short Hair While Growing It Out

The key to styling short hair while growing it out is to work with the length you have. This means embracing your hair's natural texture and finding styles that flatter your face shape. Here are some ideas to get you started:

1. Add Accessories

One of the easiest ways to style short hair is to add accessories like headbands, clips, and scarves. These can help keep your hair out of your face and add some interest to your look. Try experimenting with different styles to see what works best for you.

2. Experiment with Braids

Braids are a great way to add texture and interest to short hair. You can try a French braid or a Dutch braid for a more intricate look, or a simple braid for a more casual style. You can also experiment with different types of braids like fishtail or waterfall braids.

3. Use Hair Extensions

If you're really struggling with the length of your hair, you can always use hair extensions to add some extra length and volume. Just make sure to choose extensions that match your natural hair color and texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair while growing it out:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will help your style hold better and last longer.

2. Add Some Product

Add some product to your hair to help it hold its shape. You can use a styling cream, mousse, or gel depending on your hair type and the style you're going for.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Try out different styles to find what works best for you. Don't be afraid to experiment with braids, accessories, and other techniques.

4. Secure Your Style

Once you've found a style you like, secure it in place with hair spray, bobby pins, or other accessories.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style your short hair while growing it out:

1. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Short hair tends to have more texture than longer hair, so embrace it! You can use products like sea salt spray or texturizing paste to enhance your hair's natural texture.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When combing or brushing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid damaging your hair or causing breakage.

3. Don't Overdo It with Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can be damaging to your hair, so try to limit your use of them. If you do use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray first.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and personal preferences. Some people with oily hair may need to wash their hair every day, while others with dry hair may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Q: How can I make my hair grow faster?

A: Unfortunately, there's no magic solution for making your hair grow faster. However, you can try eating a healthy diet, taking biotin supplements, and avoiding heat styling tools to help promote healthy hair growth.

Hair Care During the Growing Out Process

While you're growing out your hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

1. Get Regular Trims

Even though you're growing out your hair, it's still important to get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

2. Use a Deep Conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

3. Avoid Harsh Chemicals

Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach or color on your hair while you're growing it out. These can cause damage and breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can style your short hair while growing it out and feel confident and stylish in the process.
