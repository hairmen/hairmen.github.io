How To Cut A Face Frame At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your same old hairstyle and want to try something new? Why not try cutting a face frame at home? This style is perfect for framing your face and adding some dimension to your hair. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting a face frame at home. Read on to learn more.

How to Cut a Face Frame at Home

To cut a face frame at home, you will need a few tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors (hairdressing scissors are preferable)
  • Comb
  • Clips to section your hair
  • A mirror

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and style. Use your regular shampoo and conditioner, and towel dry your hair until it's damp.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use clips to section your hair into four sections: the front, left side, right side, and back. Start with the front section, which will be your face frame.

Step 3: Decide on the Length of Your Face Frame

Decide how long you want your face frame to be. Use the comb to comb out the front section of your hair and hold it up to your face to determine the length.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Take the front section of your hair and hold it straight out in front of you. Use your scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Make sure to cut straight across and not at an angle.

Step 5: Trim the Sides

Now that you've cut the front section of your hair, it's time to trim the sides. Take the left side section and comb it out. Hold it up to your face and cut it to the same length as the front section. Repeat on the right side.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Once you've cut the front and side sections of your hair, it's time to blend the layers. Use your scissors to cut into the hair at a 45-degree angle, creating layers that blend together.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Now that you've cut your hair, it's time to style it. Use a hair straightener or curling iron to create the desired look. You can also use hair products such as mousse or hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Cutting a Face Frame at Home

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect face frame:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back once it's cut.
  • Use hairdressing scissors instead of regular scissors. They are sharper and will give you a cleaner cut.
  • Make sure to cut your hair when it's dry. Wet hair can shrink, making it difficult to get the desired length.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help. Cutting your own hair can be tricky, so ask a friend or family member for assistance if needed.

FAQs About Cutting a Face Frame at Home

Is it easy to cut a face frame at home?

While cutting your own hair can be challenging, cutting a face frame at home is relatively easy. Just make sure to follow the steps carefully and take your time.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always visit a hair salon to have your hair fixed.

Can I cut a face frame on curly hair?

Yes, you can cut a face frame on curly hair. Just make sure to cut the hair when it's dry, so you can see the length better.

Hair Care After Cutting a Face Frame at Home

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it. Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant when using heated styling tools. Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain your face frame.

Conclusion

Cutting a face frame at home is an easy and affordable way to change up your hairstyle. With the right tools and a little patience, you can achieve the perfect face frame. Just remember to follow the steps carefully and take care of your hair afterwards. Happy cutting!
