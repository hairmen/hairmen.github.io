What Haircut To Ask For Curly Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Christine Sep 24, 2023 · 3 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it’s beautiful and unique, it can be difficult to manage and style. One way to make styling easier is to get the right haircut. In this guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know about what haircut to ask for curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Curly hair can be categorized into three types: wavy, curly, and coily. Each type has its own unique characteristics and requires different haircuts. Wavy hair has loose curls and is the easiest to manage. Curly hair has tighter curls and can be more difficult to manage. Coily hair has the tightest curls and requires the most maintenance.

When getting a haircut for curly hair, it’s important to consider the shape of your face, the texture of your hair, and your personal style. A good haircut can enhance your natural curls and make styling easier.

How to

The first step in getting the right haircut for curly hair is to find a stylist who specializes in curly hair. Look for someone who has experience with your specific hair type and can guide you in choosing the right haircut.

When discussing your haircut with your stylist, be specific about what you want. Bring in pictures or examples of haircuts you like and explain what you like about them. Be open to your stylist’s suggestions and advice.

Step by Step

1. Research stylists who specialize in curly hair.

2. Schedule a consultation with your chosen stylist.

3. Bring in pictures or examples of haircuts you like.

4. Discuss your hair type, face shape, and personal style with your stylist.

5. Listen to your stylist’s suggestions and advice.

6. Schedule your haircut appointment.

Tips

  • Consider getting layers to enhance your curls.
  • Avoid getting a blunt cut, as it can make your hair look too heavy.
  • Ask your stylist to cut your hair when it’s dry, as wet hair can shrink when it dries.
  • Consider getting bangs to frame your face.

FAQ

Q: Should I get a short or long haircut for curly hair?

A: It depends on your personal preference and face shape. Short haircuts can be easier to manage and show off your curls, while long haircuts can be more versatile.

Q: How often should I get a haircut for curly hair?

A: It’s recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your curls healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Getting the right haircut is just the first step in managing curly hair. To keep your curls healthy and beautiful, it’s important to follow a good hair care routine. This includes using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and using a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can rock your curls with confidence. Remember to embrace your natural texture and have fun experimenting with different styles!
