What Makes Bleach Make Your Hair Fall Out Understanding The Science Behind It

Have you ever wondered why bleach can cause your hair to fall out? Bleaching your hair may be a popular trend, but it can also be damaging to your hair. In this article, we will discuss what makes bleach make your hair fall out and provide some tips on how to prevent damage to your hair.

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to bleach hair safely
  • Step by step guide to bleaching hair
  • Tips for healthy hair after bleaching
  • Frequently asked questions
  • Hair care tips for bleached hair

Description:

Bleaching hair uses chemicals to strip the hair of its natural color, resulting in a lighter shade. The chemicals used in bleaching hair can be damaging to the hair, causing it to become dry, brittle, and prone to breakage. One of the most common side effects of bleaching hair is hair loss, which occurs when the hair becomes too damaged to remain attached to the scalp.

So, what exactly makes bleach cause hair loss? The answer lies in the way that bleach works.

How to bleach hair safely:

Before we discuss what makes bleach cause hair loss, let's first talk about how to bleach hair safely. Here are some tips to minimize the damage caused by bleach:

  • Consult with a professional hair stylist to determine if your hair is healthy enough to be bleached.
  • Use a high-quality bleach that is specifically designed for hair, and follow the instructions carefully.
  • Avoid bleaching hair that has already been chemically treated or damaged.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment before and after bleaching to help keep hair healthy.
  • Wait at least a few weeks between bleaching sessions to give your hair time to recover.

Step by step guide to bleaching hair:

If you are going to bleach your hair at home, here is a step-by-step guide to follow:

  • Start with dry, unwashed hair.
  • Wear gloves to protect your hands.
  • Mix the bleach according to the instructions on the package.
  • Apply the bleach to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Leave the bleach on your hair for the recommended amount of time.
  • Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.
  • Shampoo and condition your hair with a deep conditioning treatment.

Tips for healthy hair after bleaching:

Now that you know how to bleach hair safely, here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy after bleaching:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, which can further damage your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as hair dyes or relaxers, on your hair for at least a few weeks after bleaching.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to remove split ends and prevent further breakage.

Frequently asked questions:

Here are some frequently asked questions about what makes bleach cause hair loss:

Q: Can bleach cause permanent hair loss?

A: In some cases, bleach can cause permanent hair loss if the damage is severe enough.

Q: How can I tell if my hair is too damaged to be bleached?

A: A professional hair stylist can examine your hair and determine if it is healthy enough to be bleached.

Q: Can I bleach my hair if I have a sensitive scalp?

A: You should avoid bleaching your hair if you have a sensitive scalp, as the chemicals in bleach can cause irritation.

Q: How can I prevent hair loss from bleaching?

A: To prevent hair loss from bleaching, be sure to follow the instructions carefully and use a deep conditioning treatment before and after bleaching.

Hair care tips for bleached hair:

If you have bleached hair, here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can further dry out your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage.

In conclusion, bleaching your hair can be damaging, and one of the most common side effects is hair loss. However, by following the tips in this article, you can minimize the damage caused by bleach and keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
