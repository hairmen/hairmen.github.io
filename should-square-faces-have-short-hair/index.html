Should Square Faces Have Short Hair Debunking The Myth

Written by Robby Sep 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of hearing that your square face shape means you should stick to short haircuts? Well, it's time to debunk the myth once and for all. While short hair can complement a square face shape, it's not the only option. In this article, we'll explore the various hairstyles that work well for square faces and provide tips and tricks for maintaining healthy hair.

Description

Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline, broad forehead, and angular features. The width of the face is almost equal to its length, making it one of the more distinct face shapes. While some may argue that short haircuts are the only way to balance out the strong features of a square face, this simply isn't true. The key is to find a hairstyle that complements your features and brings out your best qualities.

How To

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for square faces, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, layers can help soften the angles of the face and create movement. Second, hairstyles that add volume at the crown can elongate the face and create a more oval appearance. And finally, avoiding blunt cuts and harsh lines can help balance out the features.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape by measuring the width and length of your face.

2. Research hairstyles that complement square faces, such as layered cuts, long bobs, and side-swept bangs.

3. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best cut for your face shape and hair type.

4. Maintain your hairstyle by getting regular trims and using hair care products that nourish and protect your hair.

Tips

1. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and soften the features of a square face.

2. Avoid harsh, blunt cuts that can make the face appear boxy.

3. Experiment with different hairstyles and don't be afraid to try something new.

FAQ

Q: Can square faces pull off short haircuts?

A: Yes, square faces can pull off short haircuts, but it's not the only option. Longer hairstyles with layers and volume can also complement square faces.

Q: What hairstyles should square faces avoid?

A: Square faces should avoid blunt cuts, center parts, and hairstyles that add width to the face.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any face shape, but it's especially important for square faces. To keep your hair looking its best, use products that nourish and protect your hair, and avoid heat styling tools as much as possible. Regular trims can also help prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Overall, square faces should not feel limited to short haircuts. With the right hairstyle and care, square faces can look just as stunning with long hair as they do with short hair. Don't be afraid to experiment and find the style that works best for you.
