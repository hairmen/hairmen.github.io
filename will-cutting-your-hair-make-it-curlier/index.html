Will Cutting Your Hair Make It Curlier

Written by Bardi Nov 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who's looking for ways to make your hair curlier, you might be wondering whether cutting your hair will do the trick. It's a common question that many people have, and in this blog post, we'll answer it for you. So, will cutting your hair make it curlier? Let's find out!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Make Your Hair Curlier
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Curlier Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

Description

Cutting your hair will not make it curlier. The natural curl pattern of your hair is determined by your genetics and cannot be changed by a haircut. However, cutting your hair can give the illusion of curlier hair, especially if you have long, weighed-down hair.

When you cut your hair, you remove some of the weight that's pulling your curls down. This can result in bouncier, more defined curls, giving the appearance of curlier hair.

How to Make Your Hair Curlier

While cutting your hair won't make it curlier, there are things you can do to enhance your natural curl pattern. Here are a few tips:

1. Use a curl-enhancing product

Using a curl-enhancing product can help define your curls and make them look curlier. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, or argan oil, which can help nourish and hydrate your hair.

2. Try the "plopping" method

Plopping is a technique that involves wrapping your hair in a T-shirt or microfiber towel to help enhance your curls. After washing your hair, apply your curl-enhancing product and then wrap your hair in the towel, scrunching it up toward your scalp. Leave the towel on for 20-30 minutes, then remove and let your hair air-dry.

3. Use a diffuser

A diffuser attachment for your hair dryer can help enhance your curls and make them look curlier. Attach the diffuser to your hair dryer and use it on a low heat setting, holding it close to your hair but not touching it.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to making your hair curlier:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Apply your curl-enhancing product

Apply your curl-enhancing product to your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Plop your hair

Wrap your hair in a T-shirt or microfiber towel and leave it on for 20-30 minutes.

Step 4: Use a diffuser

Once your hair is dry, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls.

Tips for Curlier Hair

Here are a few additional tips for making your hair curlier:

1. Don't brush your hair

Brushing your hair can disrupt your natural curl pattern and make your hair look frizzy. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

2. Don't use heat styling tools

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and disrupt your natural curl pattern. Try to avoid using them as much as possible.

3. Sleep on a silk pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and keep your curls intact.

FAQs

Will cutting my hair make it curlier?

No, cutting your hair will not make it curlier.

Can I make my hair curlier without using heat styling tools?

Yes, you can enhance your natural curl pattern by using curl-enhancing products, plopping your hair, and using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can be harsh on curly hair, stripping away natural oils and leaving it dry and brittle. Look for a sulfate-free shampoo that's gentle on your hair.

2. Deep condition regularly

Curly hair can be prone to dryness, so it's important to deep condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy.

3. Avoid heat styling tools

Heat styling tools can damage curly hair and disrupt the natural curl pattern. Try to avoid using them as much as possible.

In conclusion, cutting your hair will not make it curlier. However, there are things you can do to enhance your natural curl pattern and make your hair look curlier. By using curl-enhancing products, plopping your hair, and using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer, you can achieve bouncier, more defined curls.
