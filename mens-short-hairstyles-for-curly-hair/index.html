Mens Short Hairstyles For Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 06, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how hard it can be to find the perfect hairstyle. But fear not, we’ve got you covered! In this blog post, we’ll provide you with all the information you need to choose a short hairstyle that suits your curly hair. From how to pick the right style to hair care tips, we’ve got it all. So, let’s dive in!

Table of Contents

  • How to Pick the Right Style
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Pick the Right Style

When it comes to picking a short hairstyle for curly hair, there are a few things you need to consider. The first is your face shape. Depending on whether you have a round, square, oval or heart-shaped face, certain hairstyles will suit you better than others. It’s important to speak to your stylist about what styles will work best for you.

Another factor to consider is the texture of your hair. Some curly hair is coarse and thick, while others are fine and soft. This will impact the type of style you choose. Coarse hair will require more maintenance, while fine hair will need a lighter touch.

Lastly, you should consider your personal style. Do you prefer a classic look or something more modern? Are you comfortable with a lot of volume, or do you prefer a sleeker style? Once you’ve taken all of these factors into account, you’ll be ready to choose the perfect style for your curly hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Once you’ve chosen the right style, it’s important to know how to maintain it. Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your short curly hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp.
  3. Apply a curl-defining product to your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment, keeping the heat on low to prevent damage.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to fluff it up and add volume.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep your short curly hair looking its best:

  • Don’t wash your hair too often. Curly hair can become dry and brittle if it’s washed too frequently. Try to wash it every two to three days.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Avoid using a brush, which can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often. Curly hair is more fragile than straight hair, so it’s important to limit your use of curling irons and straighteners.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for curly hair:

Q: Can I still have a fade with curly hair?

A: Yes! A fade can look great with curly hair, as long as it’s done properly. Speak to your stylist about the best way to incorporate a fade into your short curly hair.

Q: Will a short hairstyle make my curly hair look frizzy?

A: Not necessarily. With the right products and styling techniques, you can keep your short curly hair looking smooth and defined.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, but it’s important to be careful. Straightening curly hair can cause damage and breakage, so it’s best to limit your use of straightening tools.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips for short curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Invest in a good quality curl-defining product to keep your curls looking their best.
  • Consider getting a silk or satin pillowcase, which can help prevent frizz and breakage.

With the right style and care, short curly hair can look stylish and sophisticated. Follow these tips to keep your hair looking its best!
