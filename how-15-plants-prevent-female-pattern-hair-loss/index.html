What is Female Pattern Hair Loss?

Female pattern hair loss is a type of hair loss that occurs in women. It is also known as androgenetic alopecia, and it is caused by a combination of genetic, hormonal, and environmental factors. As the name suggests, female pattern hair loss is a patterned hair loss that typically starts at the crown of the head and spreads outwards. It can also occur at the hairline and around the temples.

How to Prevent Female Pattern Hair Loss

Preventing female pattern hair loss can be challenging, but there are several things you can do to help reduce your risk. One of the most important things you can do is to maintain a healthy lifestyle. This includes eating a healthy diet, getting regular exercise, and managing stress. You should also avoid smoking and limit your alcohol consumption.

Another important step in preventing female pattern hair loss is to take care of your hair. This means avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools, and using gentle, natural products instead. You should also avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair, and use a wide-toothed comb or brush to detangle your hair gently.
