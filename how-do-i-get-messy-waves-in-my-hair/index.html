How Do I Get Messy Waves In My Hair

Written by Eveline Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a relaxed and effortless hairstyle, messy waves might just be the perfect fit for you. This hairstyle is popular among women of all ages and it's not difficult to see why. It's a style that looks great on both short and long hair, and it's perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll explore how to get messy waves in your hair, step by step. We'll also provide tips, answer some frequently asked questions, and discuss hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Messy Waves in Your Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Perfect Messy Waves
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Messy Waves

How to Get Messy Waves in Your Hair

The first step to getting messy waves in your hair is to prepare it properly. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Then, towel dry your hair until it's damp.

Next, apply a good quality styling product to your hair. This could be a mousse, a gel, or a texturizing spray. The product you choose will depend on your hair type and the look you want to achieve.

Once you've applied the styling product, it's time to blow dry your hair. Use a round brush to create volume and texture. Blow dry your hair until it's about 80% dry.

Now it's time to create the messy waves. There are several ways to do this, depending on your hair type and the tools you have available.

Step by Step Guide

Method 1: With a Curling Wand

If you have a curling wand, this is a great tool for creating messy waves. Here's how to use it:

  1. Divide your hair into sections.
  2. Take a section of hair and wrap it around the curling wand.
  3. Hold the hair in place for a few seconds, then release.
  4. Repeat this process with all the sections of your hair.
  5. Once all the sections are curled, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.
  6. Spray a light hold hairspray to set the style.

Method 2: With a Flat Iron

If you don't have a curling wand, you can still create messy waves with a flat iron. Here's how:

  1. Divide your hair into sections.
  2. Take a section of hair and clamp the flat iron about halfway down the section.
  3. Twist the flat iron away from your face, then pull it down the length of your hair.
  4. Release the flat iron and let the hair fall.
  5. Repeat this process with all the sections of your hair.
  6. Once all the sections are done, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the waves.
  7. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips for Perfect Messy Waves

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect messy waves:

  • Don't overdo it with the styling product. Too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different tools and techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with the size of the sections you curl or wave.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to set the style.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long will my messy waves last?

A: The longevity of your waves will depend on your hair type and the weather conditions. However, you can expect them to last for at least a day or two.

Q: Can I create messy waves without heat?

A: Yes, you can create messy waves without heat. One way to do this is to braid your hair while it's damp and leave it overnight. When you take out the braids in the morning, you'll have beautiful, natural-looking waves.

Q: Will messy waves work on short hair?

A: Yes, messy waves work great on short hair. Just adjust the size of the sections you curl or wave to suit your hair length.

Hair Care for Messy Waves

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve the perfect messy waves in your hair. Whether you're going for a casual day look or a night out, messy waves are a versatile and stylish choice.
