What Kind Of Hair Color Suits Me Tips And Faqs

Written by Oliver Nov 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you are new to experimenting with different hues. But fret not, as we have compiled a guide on what kind of hair color suits you best. Keep reading to find out more!

How to Determine Your Skin Undertone

Before we dive into the different hair colors that suit you, it is essential to determine your skin undertone. Your skin undertone can be either warm, cool, or neutral. To find out, you can look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool undertone. If they appear green, you have a warm undertone. If they are a mix of both, you have a neutral undertone.

What Hair Color Suits Warm Undertones?

If you have warm undertones, it is best to opt for hair colors that have golden or honey tones. Shades of copper, caramel, and auburn also complement warm undertones. In contrast, hair colors that have a blue or ash undertone may clash with your skin tone.

What Hair Color Suits Cool Undertones?

If you have cool undertones, hair colors that have an ash or blue undertone will suit you best. Shades of chocolate brown, icy blonde, and black also complement cool undertones. Avoid hair colors that have a warm undertone, such as golden or honey hues, as they may clash with your skin tone.

What Hair Color Suits Neutral Undertones?

If you have neutral undertones, you are lucky as you can pull off a wide range of hair colors. Shades of auburn, honey blonde, and caramel complement neutral undertones. However, it is best to avoid extreme shades, such as jet black or platinum blonde.

Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to color your hair:

  1. Choose a hair color that complements your skin undertone.
  2. Do a patch test to ensure that you are not allergic to the hair dye.
  3. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup.
  4. Apply petroleum jelly around your hairline to prevent the dye from staining your skin.
  5. Prepare the hair dye according to the instructions on the box.
  6. Divide your hair into sections and apply the dye from the roots to the ends.
  7. Leave the dye on for the recommended time.
  8. Rinse your hair with warm water until the water runs clear.
  9. Apply the conditioner that comes with the hair dye and leave it on for a few minutes.
  10. Rinse your hair with cool water to seal the cuticles.

Tips for Maintaining Colored Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your colored hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent the color from fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as it can strip the color.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair as it can cause the color to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

FAQs

How often should I color my hair?

The frequency of coloring your hair depends on the type of hair dye you use, the shade of the color, and how fast your hair grows. Generally, it is best to color your hair every four to six weeks.

Can I color my hair at home?

Yes, you can color your hair at home. However, it is essential to follow the instructions on the box and do a patch test to ensure that you are not allergic to the hair dye.

Can I color my hair if I have chemically treated hair?

Yes, you can color your hair even if you have chemically treated hair. However, it is best to wait at least two weeks after getting a chemical treatment before coloring your hair.

What is the best hair color for grey hair?

For grey hair, it is best to opt for hair colors that have warm undertones, such as caramel or honey blonde. Shades of auburn and chocolate brown also complement grey hair.

Can I color my hair if I am pregnant?

It is best to avoid coloring your hair during the first trimester of pregnancy. However, after the first trimester, it is safe to color your hair as long as you use hair dyes that are free from harsh chemicals.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair color can enhance your features and boost your confidence. By determining your skin undertone and following our tips and guidelines, you can find the perfect hair color that suits you best. Remember to take care of your colored hair by using the right products and following a hair care routine. Happy coloring!
