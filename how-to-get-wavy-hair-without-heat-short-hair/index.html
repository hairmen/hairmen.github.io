How To Get Wavy Hair Without Heat Short Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Aug 30, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have short hair and want to achieve those perfect beachy waves without using heat, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll show you how to get wavy hair without heat short hair, with easy-to-follow steps, tips, and hair care advice. So, let’s get started!

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Get Wavy Hair Without Heat Short Hair

There are several ways to get wavy hair without using heat, and most of them are easy and affordable. Here are some of the most popular methods:

Braids

Braids are a great way to get wavy hair without using heat. Simply braid your hair when it’s damp, and leave it overnight. In the morning, undo the braids and use your fingers to separate the waves. You can use one large braid or several smaller ones, depending on the size of the waves you want.

Twists

Twists are another easy way to get wavy hair without heat. Twist small sections of your hair and secure them with bobby pins. Leave them overnight, and in the morning, remove the pins and loosen the twists with your fingers.

Buns

Buns are a classic way to get wavy hair without heat. Simply twist your hair into a bun when it’s damp, and secure it with bobby pins or a hair tie. Leave it overnight, and in the morning, remove the pins or hair tie and loosen the bun with your fingers.

Salt Spray

Salt spray is a great way to add texture and waves to short hair. Simply spray it onto damp hair and scrunch it with your fingers. You can also use it on dry hair to refresh your waves throughout the day.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to get wavy hair without heat short hair:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it until it’s damp, but not soaking wet.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or salt spray, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections, depending on how many waves you want.
  4. Braid, twist, or bun each section and secure it with a bobby pin or hair tie.
  5. Leave your hair overnight, or until it’s completely dry.
  6. Undo the braids, twists, or buns and use your fingers to separate the waves.
  7. Finish with a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold the waves in place.

Tips for Perfect Waves

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect wavy hair without heat:

  • Use a styling product to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Don’t brush your hair after you’ve created the waves, as it will break them up.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to separate the waves.
  • Experiment with different braids, twists, and buns to create different types of waves.

FAQs

Can I get wavy hair without using any products?

While it’s possible to get wavy hair without using any products, using a styling product will make it easier to create and hold the waves. Products like mousse, salt spray, and texturizing spray add texture and hold to your hair, making it easier to create waves that last.

How long will the waves last?

The length of time the waves last will depend on your hair type, the method you used to create the waves, and the products you used. Typically, the waves will last for a day or two, but you can refresh them with a texturizing spray or salt spray if they start to fall out.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair no more than 2-3 times a week to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to get wavy hair without heat short hair, it’s time to try it out for yourself. With these simple steps, tips, and hair care advice, you’ll be able to achieve those perfect beachy waves in no time. Happy styling!
