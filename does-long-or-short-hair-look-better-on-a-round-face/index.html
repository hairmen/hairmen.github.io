Does Long Or Short Hair Look Better On A Round Face

Written by William Nov 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to hairstyles, one of the most common questions people ask is, "does long or short hair look better on a round face?" This is a valid concern, as the shape of your face can greatly affect the way your hair looks. In this article, we will explore the best hairstyles for round faces, whether you prefer long or short hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wide forehead. The goal of any hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of a more elongated face shape. This can be achieved by adding volume to the crown of the head, creating height, and adding angles to the face. Whether you prefer long or short hair, there are hairstyles that can flatter your face shape.

How To

The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face is to focus on creating angles. This can be achieved through layers, bangs, and asymmetrical cuts. It's also important to consider the texture of your hair, as some styles may work better with straight hair, while others may be better suited for curly or wavy hair.

Long Hair

Long hair can work well for round faces, as long as it's styled correctly. Adding layers to long hair can create the illusion of length and angles, while side-swept bangs can help to elongate the face. Loose waves or curls can also add volume and movement to the hair, which can help to balance out the roundness of the face.

Short Hair

Short hair can also work well for round faces, as long as the cut is flattering. Asymmetrical cuts, such as a pixie cut with longer bangs on one side, can create angles and elongate the face. Adding volume to the crown of the head can also help to create height and length. Soft waves or curls can also add texture and movement to short hair.

Step By Step

The following are step-by-step instructions for creating a hairstyle that flatters a round face:

Long Hair

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair with a round brush, focusing on adding volume to the crown of the head.
  2. Add layers to your hair, focusing on the area around the face. This will help to create angles and elongate the face.
  3. Create side-swept bangs to help elongate the face.
  4. Finish by adding loose waves or curls to the hair, which will add volume and movement.

Short Hair

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair with a round brush, focusing on adding volume to the crown of the head.
  2. Cut your hair into an asymmetrical style, with longer bangs on one side to create angles and elongate the face.
  3. Add volume to the crown of the head to create height and length.
  4. Finish by adding soft waves or curls to the hair, which will add texture and movement.

Tips

The following are some tips for finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face:

  • Focus on creating angles and elongating the face.
  • Add volume to the crown of the head to create height and length.
  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a style.
  • Side-swept bangs can help to elongate the face.
  • Soft waves or curls can add movement and balance to the hair.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, as this can make the face appear wider. This includes blunt bangs, blunt cuts that fall at the jawline, and hairstyles that are too short or too long.

Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a round face?

A middle part can work for some people with round faces, but it's important to create volume at the crown of the head to elongate the face. Consider adding layers and volume to your hair to create angles and balance out the roundness of the face.

Hair Care

Regardless of the hairstyle you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. This includes regular washing and conditioning, as well as using products that are suited to your hair type. It's also important to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray when styling with hot tools.

In conclusion, whether you prefer long or short hair, there are hairstyles that can flatter a round face. Focus on creating angles, adding volume to the crown of the head, and choosing a style that works with your hair texture. With the right hairstyle, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident and stylish.
