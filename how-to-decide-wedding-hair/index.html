How To Decide Your Wedding Hair In 2023

Written by Petter Jun 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Choosing a hairstyle for your wedding day can be a daunting task. With so many options, it can be overwhelming to decide on the perfect look. But don't worry, we've got you covered! Here are some tips on how to decide your wedding hair in 2023.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

When deciding on your wedding hair, the first step is to consider the overall style of your wedding. Are you having a formal or casual affair? Will it be indoors or outdoors? These factors can influence your hairstyle choice.

Next, think about your personal style and what makes you feel confident and beautiful. Do you prefer an updo or wearing your hair down? Do you want to incorporate any accessories like a veil, tiara, or flowers?

It's also important to consider the length and texture of your hair. If you have short hair, you may want to consider adding extensions for a more dramatic look. If you have curly hair, you may want to embrace your natural texture or straighten it for a sleeker look.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to help you decide your wedding hair:

  1. Consider the overall style of your wedding
  2. Think about your personal style and what makes you feel confident and beautiful
  3. Consider the length and texture of your hair
  4. Look for inspiration in magazines, online, and on social media
  5. Try out different hairstyles with your hairdresser
  6. Take photos of your favorite styles to show your hairdresser on the day of your wedding

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Book your hair appointment well in advance to ensure availability
  • Consider getting a hair trial before your wedding day to ensure you're happy with the final look
  • Bring any hair accessories you plan to wear on the day of your wedding to your hair appointment
  • Communicate clearly with your hairdresser about your preferences and any concerns you may have

FAQ

What if I can't decide on a hairstyle?

If you're having trouble deciding on a hairstyle, consider booking a consultation with your hairdresser. They can help you choose a style that complements your features and works with your hair type.

Should I wear my hair up or down?

Whether you wear your hair up or down depends on your personal preference and the style of your wedding. If you're having a formal wedding or want a more polished look, an updo may be more appropriate. If you're having a more casual wedding or want a more relaxed look, wearing your hair down may be a better choice.

Hair Care

In the weeks leading up to your wedding day, be sure to take good care of your hair. This includes regular trims, deep conditioning treatments, and avoiding excessive heat styling. On the day of your wedding, your hair should be clean and dry, but avoid washing it the day of as it can make it harder to style.

Now that you have some tips on how to decide your wedding hair in 2023, you can start planning the perfect hairstyle for your big day. Remember, the most important thing is that you feel confident and beautiful!
