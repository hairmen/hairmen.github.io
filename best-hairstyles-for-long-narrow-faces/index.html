Description

If you have a long narrow face, you know that finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. The good news is that there are plenty of hairstyles that can help elongate your face and make it look more balanced. In this article, we'll explore the best hairstyles for long narrow faces and provide tips on how to care for your hair.

How To

The first step in finding the best hairstyle for your long narrow face is to determine your face shape. To do this, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin and then measure the width of your face at the widest point (usually around your cheekbones). If the length is more than twice the width, you have a long narrow face shape.

Once you know your face shape, you can start exploring different hairstyles that will flatter your features. Some of the best hairstyles for long narrow faces include:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help add volume and dimension to your hair, which can help balance out a long narrow face. You can wear your hair straight or curled to create different looks.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help shorten the appearance of a long face and add softness to your features. You can wear your bangs straight or curled, depending on your preference.

3. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut can help balance out a long narrow face by adding fullness around the cheeks and chin. You can wear your bob straight or curled, depending on your preference.

4. Wavy Hair

Wavy hair can help soften the angles of a long narrow face and add volume and dimension to your hair. You can wear your waves loose or styled into a beachy look.

Step By Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating some of the best hairstyles for long narrow faces:

1. Long Layers

  1. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  2. Section your hair into manageable sections.
  3. Using a straightener or curling iron, create loose curls or waves throughout your hair.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Section your hair into manageable sections.
  2. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, directing your bangs to the side.
  3. Use a flat iron to smooth your bangs and create a polished look.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style.

3. Bob Haircut

  1. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  2. Section your hair into manageable sections.
  3. Using a straightener or curling iron, create loose curls or waves throughout your hair.
  4. Use a texturizing spray to add volume to your roots.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style.

4. Wavy Hair

  1. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  2. Section your hair into manageable sections.
  3. Using a curling iron, create loose waves throughout your hair.
  4. Use a texturizing spray to add volume to your roots.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for finding the best hairstyle for your long narrow face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face look even longer.
  • Avoid middle parts, as they can emphasize the length of your face.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and texture.
  • Experiment with different styles to see what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for long narrow faces:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long narrow face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face look even longer. Also avoid middle parts, as they can emphasize the length of your face.

What are the best hairstyles for adding volume to my hair?

Long layers and wavy hair are both great options for adding volume to your hair.

Hair Care

If you have a long narrow face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep split ends at bay.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can find the perfect look for your long narrow face and keep your hair looking its best.
