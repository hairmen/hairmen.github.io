How To Use Gel To Slick Back Hair A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of your unruly hair getting in the way of your daily routine? Slicking back your hair with gel is a trendy and practical way to keep your hair in place all day. In this guide, we'll show you how to use gel to slick back your hair and achieve a stylish look that lasts.

How to Slick Back Your Hair with Gel

Gel is a versatile hair styling product that can be used to achieve a variety of looks, including slicked-back hair. Here's what you need to know:

  • Gel is best suited for short to medium-length hair.
  • Choosing the right gel is key. Look for a strong hold gel that won't leave residue or flakes.
  • Start with clean, towel-dried hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to slick back your hair with gel:

  1. Take a small amount of gel (about the size of a dime) and rub it between your palms to warm it up.
  2. Starting at the crown of your head, use your fingers to distribute the gel evenly through your hair.
  3. Use a comb to slick back your hair, starting at the hairline and working your way back.
  4. Smooth out any bumps or flyaways with your fingers or a comb.
  5. Let the gel dry completely before touching your hair.

Tips for Slicking Back Your Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back look:

  • Experiment with different amounts of gel to find the right hold for your hair.
  • Don't use too much gel, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • If you have longer hair, try using a hair tie or clip to keep your hair in place while the gel dries.
  • Use a comb or brush to create a part or add texture to your hair.

FAQs

How long does gel last in your hair?

Gel can last anywhere from a few hours to all day, depending on the strength of the hold and your hair type.

Can gel damage your hair?

Using gel occasionally is unlikely to cause damage to your hair. However, using it regularly can cause buildup and make your hair look dull and lifeless.

Can I use gel on wet hair?

While it's best to use gel on towel-dried hair, you can also use it on wet hair. Just be aware that the hold may not be as strong.

Hair Care Tips

When using gel to slick back your hair, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and keep it looking healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove buildup and keep it clean.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and overall health.

By following these tips and using gel to slick back your hair, you can achieve a trendy and practical look that lasts all day. Experiment with different amounts of gel and techniques to find what works best for you, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
