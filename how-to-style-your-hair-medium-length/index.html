How To Style Your Hair Medium Length

Medium length hair is versatile and easy to manage. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there are many ways to style it. In this article, we will explore how to style your hair medium length and provide tips on hair care.

How to Style Your Hair Medium Length

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to have the right tools on hand. You'll need a hairbrush, a comb, hair elastics, hairpins, and hair styling products like hairspray, mousse, or gel. Here are some popular ways to style medium length hair:

1. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a popular hairstyle that is perfect for medium length hair. To achieve this look, start by washing your hair and towel-drying it. Apply a sea salt spray to your hair and scrunch it with your hands. Use a diffuser on your hair dryer to enhance the waves.

2. Straight and Sleek

If you have naturally straight hair, you can create a sleek and polished look by using a flat iron. Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair in sections. Finish with a shine serum or hairspray for a polished look.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is a simple yet chic way to style medium length hair. Start by sectioning off the top half of your hair and securing it with a hair elastic. You can leave the bottom half of your hair straight or add waves for a more textured look.

4. Braids

Braids are another popular hairstyle for medium length hair. You can create a simple braid or a more elaborate one like a fishtail or Dutch braid. To create a braid, start by dividing your hair into three sections and crossing them over each other. Continue braiding until you reach the end of your hair and secure with a hair elastic.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair medium length:

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and towel-dry your hair.

2. Apply styling products

Apply a styling product like mousse or gel to your hair to add texture and hold. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

3. Use a hairbrush

Use a hairbrush to brush your hair and remove any tangles. This will make it easier to style your hair.

4. Choose a hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that you want to create. You can try beachy waves, a straight and sleek look, a half-up half-down style, or braids.

5. Style your hair

Use a hair dryer, flat iron, or curling iron to style your hair according to your chosen hairstyle. Use hairpins or hair elastics to secure your hair in place.

6. Finish with hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a hairspray to hold it in place. You can also use a shine serum or hair oil to add shine to your hair.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair

Here are some tips for styling medium length hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a flat iron or curling iron to prevent heat damage.
  • Experiment with different hair styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Don't overdo it with hair products as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a diffuser on your hair dryer to enhance curls and waves.
  • Invest in good quality hair tools like a flat iron or curling iron to get the best results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I style my medium length hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can style your medium length hair if it's curly. Use a diffuser on your hair dryer to enhance your curls and use hair products like mousse or curl defining cream to add texture and hold.

How often should I wash my hair?

The frequency of washing your hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people prefer to wash their hair every day, while others can go longer between washes. It's important to use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid product buildup.

What is the best way to prevent split ends?

The best way to prevent split ends is to get regular haircuts to trim off any split ends. You can also use a hair serum or oil to nourish and protect your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

Here are some hair care tips for medium length hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

In conclusion, there are many ways to style your hair medium length. Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or beachy waves, with the right tools and products, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle. Don't forget to take care of your hair by using suitable hair care products and avoiding excessive heat styling.
