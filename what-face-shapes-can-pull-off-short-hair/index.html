What Face Shapes Can Pull Off Short Hair

Written by Bardi Nov 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are thinking about cutting your hair short, it is important to consider which face shape you have. Not all face shapes can pull off short haircuts, but there are certain styles that work best for each. In this article, we will discuss what face shapes can pull off short hair and provide tips on how to choose the right style for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts have become increasingly popular in recent years, but not everyone can pull off the look. Your face shape plays a major role in determining which hairstyles will flatter you the most. There are several basic face shapes, including round, square, oval, heart, and diamond. Each face shape has its own unique characteristics and requires a different approach when it comes to choosing a short haircut.

How To

The first step in determining which short haircut will work best for your face shape is to identify your face shape. This can be done by looking in the mirror and examining the proportions of your face. Once you have determined your face shape, you can start to research different short haircuts that are suitable for your face shape.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you should consider short haircuts that add height and volume to the crown of your head. This will help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Avoid short haircuts that are flat on top or have too much volume on the sides, as this will only make your face look rounder.

Square Face Shape

For a square face shape, short haircuts that soften the angles of your face work best. This can be achieved with a short bob or a layered haircut that falls just above your chin. Avoid blunt cuts or severe angles, as this will only emphasize the squareness of your face.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you are lucky in that almost any short haircut will flatter you. However, if you want to accentuate your cheekbones, consider a short pixie cut with long bangs swept to the side.

Heart Face Shape

For a heart face shape, short haircuts that add width to the bottom of your face work best. This can be achieved with a chin-length bob or a layered haircut that falls just below your chin. Avoid short haircuts that add volume to the top of your head, as this will only emphasize the pointiness of your chin.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, short haircuts that add fullness to the sides of your face work best. This can be achieved with a short bob or a layered haircut that falls just below your ears. Avoid short haircuts that are too flat on top or have too much volume on the crown of your head, as this will only emphasize the narrowness of your forehead.

Step by Step

Now that you know which short haircuts work best for each face shape, here are some steps to follow when getting a short haircut:

  1. Research different short haircuts that are suitable for your face shape.
  2. Choose a haircut that you like and that flatters your face shape.
  3. Show your stylist a picture of the haircut you want.
  4. Discuss any concerns or questions you have with your stylist.
  5. Trust your stylist to give you the best cut for your face shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when getting a short haircut:

  • Consider your hair type and texture when choosing a short haircut.
  • Be prepared to style your hair every day with a short haircut.
  • Invest in quality hair products to keep your short haircut looking its best.
  • Be open to trying new styles and experimenting with your short haircut.

FAQ

Can everyone pull off a short haircut?

No, not everyone can pull off a short haircut. Your face shape and hair type play a major role in determining which short haircuts will flatter you the most.

How do I know which face shape I have?

You can determine your face shape by looking in the mirror and examining the proportions of your face. There are several basic face shapes, including round, square, oval, heart, and diamond.

What should I consider when choosing a short haircut?

You should consider your face shape, hair type, and texture when choosing a short haircut. It is also important to choose a haircut that you like and that flatters your face shape.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for caring for your short haircut:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly with products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools to style your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.

Now that you know what face shapes can pull off short hair, you can confidently choose a short haircut that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident and stylish. Remember to consider your hair type and texture, as well as your personal style, when choosing a short haircut. With these tips and tricks, you can rock a short haircut like a pro.
