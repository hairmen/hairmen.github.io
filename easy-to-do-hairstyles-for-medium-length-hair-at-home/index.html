Easy To Do Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair At Home

Written by Daniel Aug 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same hairstyle every day? Do you want to switch up your look without going to a salon? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we'll be discussing easy to do hairstyles for medium length hair that you can do at home. Whether you're heading to work or attending a special event, these hairstyles will have you looking fabulous in no time.

Table of Contents:

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to:

Before we jump into the hairstyles, let's talk about the basics. To achieve the perfect hairstyle, you'll need a few things:

  • A comb or brush
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Bobby pins
  • Hairspray or other styling products

Once you have these items, you're ready to start experimenting!

Step by Step:

1. Messy Bun

This hairstyle is perfect for those days when you don't have time to wash your hair.

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base
  3. Secure with bobby pins
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold in place

2. Half Up, Half Down

This hairstyle is perfect for any occasion and is super easy to do.

  1. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head
  2. Tie it into a ponytail
  3. Tease the ponytail to add volume
  4. Wrap a small strand of hair around the hair tie to hide it
  5. Leave the rest of your hair down

3. Braided Crown

This hairstyle is perfect for weddings, proms, or any special occasion.

  1. Part your hair in the middle
  2. Take a small section of hair from one side and braid it
  3. Wrap the braid around the back of your head, securing it with bobby pins
  4. Repeat on the other side
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold in place

4. Low Ponytail

This hairstyle is perfect for work or a casual day out.

  1. Gather your hair into a low ponytail
  2. Secure with a hair tie
  3. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to hide it
  4. Spray with hairspray to hold in place

Tips:

Here are a few tips to keep in mind while styling your medium length hair:

  • Invest in good quality styling products to help hold your hairstyle in place.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories like headbands, hair clips, and barrettes.
  • Don't be afraid to add some volume to your hair by teasing or using a volumizing spray.
  • Always use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools.

FAQ:

Q: Can I still achieve these hairstyles if my hair is thin?

A: Yes! You can still achieve these hairstyles even if you have thin hair. Just be sure to use a volumizing spray and tease your hair to add some volume.

Q: How long does it take to do these hairstyles?

A: These hairstyles should take no more than 10-15 minutes to do.

Hair Care:

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair and opt for natural products instead.

With these easy to do hairstyles for medium length hair, you'll never have a bad hair day again. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
