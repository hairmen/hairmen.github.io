Half Updo Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair

Written by Oliver Oct 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that's perfect for your shoulder-length hair? Half updo hairstyles are the answer! Not only are they easy to do, but they're also versatile and can be worn for any occasion. In this article, we'll show you how to create different half updo hairstyles for shoulder length hair. We'll also provide tips on how to maintain them and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

Half updo hairstyles involve pulling the top half of your hair away from your face and securing it with pins or ties. This leaves the bottom half of your hair flowing freely. With shoulder length hair, half updo hairstyles can be styled in many ways, from simple to intricate. They can be curled, braided, twisted, or straightened, depending on your preference.

How to Create Half Updo Hairstyles for Shoulder Length Hair

Creating half updo hairstyles for shoulder length hair is easy and can be done in just a few simple steps:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly. Apply a heat protectant spray if you plan to use heat tools. Brush your hair to remove tangles.

Step 2: Section your hair

Separate the top half of your hair from the bottom half. Use a comb to create a horizontal parting from ear to ear. Secure the top half of your hair with a clip or tie.

Step 3: Style the bottom half

Style the bottom half of your hair as desired. You can leave it straight, curl it, or add some waves.

Step 4: Style the top half

Take a small section of hair from the top half of your hair and tease it lightly. This will give your hair volume and texture. Smooth the teased section with a comb and then pull it back. Pin the section at the back of your head using bobby pins or a hair tie.

Step 5: Finish the look

Add some hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place. You can also add some accessories such as clips, flowers, or pins to enhance the look.

Tips for Maintaining Half Updo Hairstyles for Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your half updo hairstyles for shoulder length hair:

  • Use a light hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place without making it stiff.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much as this can cause frizz and ruin your hairstyle.
  • Apply a hair serum to keep your hair shiny and smooth.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hairstyle if it starts to look greasy.

FAQs

Can I create a half updo hairstyle if I have short hair?

Yes, you can create a half updo hairstyle if you have short hair. You may need to use more pins or ties to secure your hair in place.

Can I wear a half updo hairstyle for a formal event?

Yes, you can wear a half updo hairstyle for a formal event. Just add some accessories such as a tiara or a hairpin to make it look more elegant.

How long does it take to create a half updo hairstyle?

Creating a half updo hairstyle for shoulder length hair can take between 5-15 minutes depending on the style you choose.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Get enough sleep and exercise regularly.

With these tips, you can create beautiful half updo hairstyles for your shoulder length hair. Experiment with different styles and accessories to find the one that suits you best. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
