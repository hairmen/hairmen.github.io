Can You Get Bangs If You Have Curly Hair

Written by Bardi Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your curly hair always getting in your face? Do you want to change up your look but don't know if you can get bangs with curly hair? The answer is yes, you can get bangs with curly hair! In this article, we'll discuss how to get bangs with curly hair, tips for styling them, and how to take care of your curls.

Description

Curly hair can be challenging to style, but adding bangs can give your hair a new look and frame your face. However, it's essential to consider your curl pattern when getting bangs. Not all bang styles work with curly hair, but there are still plenty of options to choose from.

How To Get Bangs With Curly Hair

The first step in getting bangs with curly hair is to make sure you find a stylist who understands curly hair. Cutting curly hair requires different techniques than straight hair, so it's crucial to find someone who knows how to work with your curls.

Once you've found a stylist, you'll want to discuss the type of bangs you want. Side-swept bangs work well with curly hair as they blend in with your curls and can be styled in different ways. Blunt bangs can also work with curly hair, but it's essential to make sure they're cut correctly to avoid the "triangle" effect.

When getting your bangs cut, it's best to have them cut dry so the stylist can see how your curls will fall. It's also essential to communicate with your stylist about how much you want to be cut. Curly hair tends to shrink when cut, so keep that in mind when discussing length.

Step By Step Guide

1. Find a stylist who understands curly hair.

2. Discuss what type of bangs you want.

3. Get your bangs cut dry so the stylist can see how your curls will fall.

4. Communicate with your stylist about how much you want to be cut.

Tips For Styling

Styling curly bangs can be tricky, but here are some tips to help:

- Use a diffuser when blow-drying your bangs to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

- Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and keep them in place.

- Avoid using a brush or comb on your bangs as it can disrupt your curls.

- Experiment with different styles, such as pinning your bangs back or parting them to the side, to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Can you get bangs with naturally curly hair?

Yes, you can get bangs with naturally curly hair. Just make sure to find a stylist who understands curly hair and discuss the type of bangs you want.

What type of bangs work best with curly hair?

Side-swept bangs and blunt bangs can both work with curly hair, but it's essential to make sure they're cut correctly to avoid the "triangle" effect.

How do you take care of curly hair with bangs?

Here are some tips for taking care of curly hair with bangs:

- Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and keep them in place.

- Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day to prevent them from becoming oily or frizzy.

- Use a diffuser when blow-drying your bangs to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

Hair Care Tips

Taking care of curly hair with bangs requires some extra effort, but here are some tips to help:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your curls.

- Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

- Use a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel, which can cause frizz.

- Avoid using heat tools on your hair as much as possible to prevent damage.

Getting bangs with curly hair can be a fun way to change up your look, but it's essential to find a stylist who understands curly hair and knows how to cut it correctly. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can have beautiful, curly bangs that frame your face and enhance your natural curls.
