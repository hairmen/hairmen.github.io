How To Cut A Stacked Bob Haircut Tutorial

Written by Bardi Sep 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's trendy, chic, and easy to maintain, a stacked bob might be just what you need. This popular haircut is characterized by short layers in the back that gradually get longer towards the front. It's a versatile look that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture.

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

Description

A stacked bob is a haircut that's shorter in the back and longer in the front. The layers in the back are stacked on top of each other, creating volume and movement. The length in the front can vary from chin-length to shoulder-length, depending on your preference. This haircut is great for those with fine or thin hair, as it adds body and texture.

How to Cut a Stacked Bob Haircut Tutorial

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut a stacked bob:

  1. Start by sectioning off the hair. Divide the hair from ear to ear and clip the top section away.
  2. Using a comb, create a horizontal part at the nape of the neck. This will be your guide for the first layer.
  3. Cut the first layer, making sure to keep the length shorter in the back and gradually longer towards the front. Use a razor or scissors, depending on your preference.
  4. Continue sectioning off the hair and cutting layers, making sure to blend each layer into the previous one.
  5. Once you've cut all the layers, remove the top clip and section off the hair at the crown of the head.
  6. Cut the top layer, making sure to blend it into the previous layers. The length of the top layer can vary depending on your preference.
  7. Finish the haircut by trimming any stray hairs or uneven sections.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting a stacked bob:

  • Make sure to use sharp scissors or a razor for a clean cut.
  • Keep the hair wet while cutting to ensure an even cut.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time to ensure precision.
  • Use a comb to guide your cuts and create even layers.
  • Consult with your client or hairstylist to determine the best length and style for their face shape and hair texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about stacked bob haircuts:

  • Q: Will a stacked bob haircut work for all hair types?
  • A: A stacked bob can work for most hair types, but it's best for those with fine or thin hair.
  • Q: How often should I get a stacked bob haircut?
  • A: It's recommended to get a stacked bob haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style.
  • Q: Can I style my stacked bob with curls or waves?
  • A: Yes, a stacked bob can be styled with curls or waves for a textured look.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for caring for your stacked bob haircut:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and style of your stacked bob.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

With these tips and step-by-step instructions, you can confidently cut a stacked bob haircut. Whether you're a hairstylist or just looking to try a new look, a stacked bob is a versatile and trendy haircut that's sure to turn heads.
