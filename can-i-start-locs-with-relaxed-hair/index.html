Description

If you have relaxed hair and you're considering starting locs, you might be wondering if it's possible. The answer is yes, you can start locs with relaxed hair. However, it's important to understand that the process will be different than starting locs with natural hair. In this article, we'll go over how to start locs with relaxed hair, step by step guide, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

How to Start Locs with Relaxed Hair

Starting locs with relaxed hair requires a slightly different process than starting locs with natural hair. Here are the steps:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Stop Relaxing Your Hair: The first step is to stop relaxing your hair. This will allow your natural hair to grow out and prepare it for the locing process.
  2. Get a Consultation: It's recommended to get a consultation with a professional loctician to determine the best method for starting your locs. They can also give you advice on how to care for your hair during the process.
  3. Decide on a Method: There are a few different methods for starting locs with relaxed hair, including palm rolling, two-strand twists, and interlocking. Your loctician can help you decide which method is best for your hair type and texture.
  4. Start the Locing Process: Once you've decided on a method, your loctician will begin the locing process. This typically involves creating small sections of hair and twisting or interlocking them to create the locs.
  5. Maintain Your Locs: Once your locs are started, you'll need to maintain them regularly to prevent them from unraveling. This includes regular retwisting or interlocking, and keeping your scalp and hair clean and moisturized.

Tips for Starting Locs with Relaxed Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when starting locs with relaxed hair:

  • Be Patient: The process of starting locs can take several months, so be patient and don't get discouraged if your hair doesn't look the way you want it to right away.
  • Protect Your Hair: During the locing process, it's important to protect your hair from damage. Avoid using heat tools and harsh chemicals, and sleep with a satin or silk scarf or bonnet to reduce friction.
  • Stay Consistent: Regular maintenance is essential for keeping your locs healthy and looking their best. Make sure to stick to a regular maintenance routine and visit your loctician regularly for touch-ups.

FAQs

Can I start locs with relaxed hair?

Yes, you can start locs with relaxed hair, but the process will be different than starting locs with natural hair.

How long does it take to start locs with relaxed hair?

The process of starting locs with relaxed hair can take several months, depending on the method used and the rate of your hair growth.

What is the best method for starting locs with relaxed hair?

The best method for starting locs with relaxed hair depends on your hair type and texture. It's recommended to consult with a professional loctician to determine the best method for your hair.

Hair Care for Locs with Relaxed Hair

Once your locs are started, it's important to take care of them to keep them healthy and looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash Your Hair: It's important to keep your scalp and hair clean to prevent buildup and odors. Use a gentle shampoo and avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Moisturize Your Hair: Locs with relaxed hair can become dry and brittle, so it's important to moisturize regularly. Use a light oil or leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Maintain Your Scalp: Keeping your scalp healthy is essential for healthy locs. Massage your scalp regularly to promote blood flow and use a light oil or scalp tonic to keep it moisturized.

Starting locs with relaxed hair is possible, but it requires special care and attention. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can start your loc journey with confidence and achieve healthy, beautiful locs.
