How To Decide What Hair Color Looks Best On You

Written by Luffy Jul 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hair color and looking for a change? Choosing a new hair color can be a daunting task, but with a little bit of research and some expert advice, you can find the perfect color for you. In this article, we will discuss how to decide what hair color looks best on you.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Choosing the right hair color can make a huge difference in your appearance. It can enhance your natural features, make you look younger, and boost your confidence. But with so many options available, how do you choose the right one? The key is to find a shade that complements your skin tone, eye color, and overall style.

How to

There are several factors to consider when choosing a new hair color:

  • Your skin tone
  • Your eye color
  • Your natural hair color
  • Your lifestyle
  • Your personal style

By taking these factors into account, you can narrow down your choices and find a color that will look great on you.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when choosing a new hair color:

  1. Determine your skin tone: Are you warm, cool, or neutral?
  2. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone: If you have warm skin tones, go for golden, caramel, or honey shades. If you have cool skin tones, try ash blonde, platinum, or auburn. If you have neutral skin tones, you can pull off almost any shade.
  3. Consider your eye color: Your eye color can also play a role in determining the best hair color for you. For example, if you have blue eyes, try a cool-toned blonde or brown. If you have green eyes, try a warm-toned blonde or brown.
  4. Think about your natural hair color: If you have dark hair, it may be best to stick with a shade that is within a few shades of your natural color. If you have light hair, you can go for a more dramatic change.
  5. Take your lifestyle into account: Are you willing to spend a lot of time and money on upkeep? If not, choose a low-maintenance color.
  6. Consider your personal style: Do you want a bold, dramatic change, or something more subtle?
  7. Consult with a professional stylist: A stylist can help you choose the perfect shade and can also provide tips on how to maintain your new color.

Tips

  • When trying on wigs or using virtual hair color apps, make sure to use a photo with natural lighting to get the most accurate representation of the color.
  • If you're nervous about making a drastic change, try a semi-permanent or temporary hair color first.
  • Keep in mind that different hair colors require different levels of maintenance. Make sure you're prepared to commit to the upkeep before making a change.

FAQ

What if I don't know my skin tone?

You can determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you likely have cool skin tones. If they appear green, you likely have warm skin tones. If they appear blue-green, you likely have neutral skin tones.

What if I have multiple colors in my hair?

If you have highlights or lowlights, you can still choose a new overall hair color. Just make sure to take all of the colors in your hair into account when choosing a new shade.

What if I can't afford a professional stylist?

You can still achieve great results at home with a little bit of research and some practice. Just make sure to follow the instructions on the box and choose a color that is close to your natural shade.

Hair Care

After you've chosen your new hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your color from fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause your color to fade faster.
  • Consider using a UV-protectant spray if you spend a lot of time in the sun, as UV rays can cause your color to fade.

By following these tips and taking the time to choose the right hair color for you, you can achieve a stunning new look that will make you feel confident and beautiful.
