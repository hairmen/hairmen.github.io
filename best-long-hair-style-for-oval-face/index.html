Best Long Hair Style For Oval Face

Description

If you have an oval face, you are in luck! You have one of the most versatile face shapes and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, when it comes to long hair, some styles may be more flattering than others. In this article, we will be discussing the best long hair style for oval face shapes, as well as tips on how to maintain and care for your long locks.

How to

The best long hair style for oval face shapes is one that accentuates your facial features while balancing out the length of your face. You want to choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face, rather than the top or bottom.

Consider Layers

Layers are a great way to add volume and movement to your hair. For oval faces, long layers that start at your cheekbones and blend into the ends of your hair are the most flattering. This style will add dimension to your hair and draw attention to your cheekbones, which are one of your best features.

Avoid Heavy Bangs

While bangs can be a great way to change up your hairstyle, they can also be tricky for oval face shapes. Heavy bangs that fall straight across your forehead can make your face appear shorter and wider, which is not the most flattering look. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that blend into the rest of your hair.

Try a Middle Part

A middle part is a great option for oval face shapes because it can help balance out the length of your face. You can wear your hair straight and sleek or add some waves or curls for a more textured look.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to achieve the best long hair style for oval face shapes:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Part your hair down the middle
  3. Add long layers that start at your cheekbones and blend into the ends of your hair
  4. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, adding volume and movement to your layers
  5. If you want to add waves or curls, use a curling wand or iron to create loose, beachy waves
  6. Finish with a lightweight hairspray to hold your style in place

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling long hair for oval face shapes:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head, as this can make your face appear longer
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and split ends

FAQ

What hairstyles should oval faces avoid?

Oval faces should avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of the head, as this can make the face appear longer. They should also avoid heavy bangs that fall straight across the forehead.

What hairstyles are best for oval faces?

The best hairstyles for oval faces are those that add volume to the sides of the face, rather than the top or bottom. Long layers that start at the cheekbones and blend into the ends of the hair are the most flattering for this face shape.

Hair Care

In addition to styling your hair, it is important to take care of it to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips and choosing the best long hair style for oval face shapes, you can achieve a flattering and stylish look that highlights your natural beauty.
