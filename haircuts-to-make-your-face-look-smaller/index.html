Haircuts To Make Your Face Look Smaller Everything You Need To Know In 2023

Written by William Aug 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of feeling self-conscious about the size of your face? We've got you covered. In this blog post, we'll be discussing the best haircuts to make your face look smaller. Whether you have a round face or a square one, we've got a solution for you. Keep reading to learn more!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The right haircut can do wonders for your face shape. If you have a round face, you want to create the illusion of length. If you have a square face, you want to soften your features. The great news is that there are plenty of haircuts that can help you achieve these goals. Even better news? They're all super trendy right now.

How to

Before we dive into specific haircuts, let's talk about some general tips for making your face look smaller:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin or cheekbones. These will only make your face look wider.
  • Side-swept bangs are your friend. They're great for creating the illusion of length.
  • Layers are also great for adding length and movement to your hair.
  • Consider highlights or ombre coloring. This can help create the illusion of depth and dimension.

Step by Step

Now, let's get into some specific haircuts:

Long Layers

Long layers are great for creating the illusion of length. They add movement and dimension to your hair, which can help elongate your face. Plus, they're super trendy right now. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for long layers that start around your collarbone.

Side-Swept Bangs

We mentioned this earlier, but it's worth repeating. Side-swept bangs are great for adding length to your face. They create a diagonal line that draws the eye down, which can help make your face look narrower. Ask your stylist for long, side-swept bangs that start around your cheekbones.

Bob with Face-Framing Layers

If you don't want long hair, a bob with face-framing layers can be a great option. This haircut is perfect for those with round faces, as it creates the illusion of length. Ask your stylist for a chin-length bob with layers that start around your cheekbones.

Tips

  • Bring pictures to your stylist. It can be hard to describe exactly what you want, so having a visual can be helpful.
  • Consider your hair texture. Some haircuts work better with certain textures. Talk to your stylist about what will work best for you.
  • Remember that hair grows back. If you're unsure about a new haircut, keep in mind that it will eventually grow out.

FAQ

What if I have a square face?

If you have a square face, you want to soften your features. Consider a haircut with soft layers or a side-swept fringe. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your jawline.

What if I have a heart-shaped face?

If you have a heart-shaped face, you want to balance out your forehead and chin. Consider a haircut that's chin-length or longer, with layers that start around your cheekbones.

What if I have curly hair?

Curly hair can be tricky, but there are still plenty of haircuts that can work for you. Consider a long bob with layers, or a haircut with face-framing layers. Talk to your stylist about what will work best for your specific curl pattern.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage. Avoid using heat tools too often, and always use a heat protectant when you do. And don't forget to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and split-end-free!

So there you have it: everything you need to know about haircuts to make your face look smaller. With the right haircut and some hair care tips, you'll be feeling confident and fabulous in no time.
