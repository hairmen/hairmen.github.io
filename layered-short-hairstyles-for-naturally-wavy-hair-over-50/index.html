Layered Short Hairstyles For Naturally Wavy Hair Over 50

Written by Luffy Jun 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you a woman over 50 with naturally wavy hair? Do you want to try a new hairstyle that will make you look younger and more stylish? If yes, then you should consider layered short hairstyles. Layered short hairstyles are perfect for women over 50 because they add volume, texture, and movement to the hair. In this article, we will show you how to achieve layered short hairstyles for naturally wavy hair over 50.

How to Achieve Layered Short Hairstyles for Naturally Wavy Hair Over 50

The first step in achieving layered short hairstyles for naturally wavy hair over 50 is to find a hairstylist who specializes in this type of hairstyle. A good hairstylist will take into consideration your hair texture, face shape, and lifestyle before recommending a hairstyle. They will also give you tips on how to maintain your new hairstyle.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Find a hairstylist who specializes in layered short hairstyles for naturally wavy hair over 50.

Step 2: Discuss with your hairstylist the type of layered short hairstyle that will suit your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

Step 3: Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair into layers, starting from the crown of your head down to the nape of your neck.

Step 4: Use a curling iron or a flat iron to style your hair. For a wavy hairstyle, use a curling iron to curl the ends of your hair.

Step 5: Apply a small amount of hair wax or hair gel to add texture and hold to your hair.

Step 6: Finish off with hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips

1. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.

2. Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.

3. Use hair products that are specifically designed for wavy hair to enhance the natural texture of your hair.

4. Get regular trims to maintain the shape and volume of your hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: You should get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and volume of your hairstyle.

Q: Can I achieve layered short hairstyles for naturally wavy hair over 50 at home?

A: It is recommended that you visit a hairstylist who specializes in this type of hairstyle to achieve the best results. However, if you have experience in cutting hair, you can try to achieve this hairstyle at home.

Hair Care

1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming dry and frizzy.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.

3. Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent damage.

4. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Layered short hairstyles for naturally wavy hair over 50 are a great way to add volume, texture, and movement to your hair. By following the tips and steps in this article, you can achieve a stylish and youthful look that will make you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to take good care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty.
