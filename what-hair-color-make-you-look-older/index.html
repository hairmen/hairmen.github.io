What Hair Color Makes You Look Older

Written by Robby Aug 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering what hair color makes you look older? As we age, we all strive to look younger and more vibrant. However, sometimes our hair color can work against us, making us look older than we really are. In this article, we will explore the hair colors that can make you look older and offer some tips on how to choose a more youthful hair color.

Table of Contents

  • Introduction
  • How to Choose a More Youthful Hair Color
  • What Hair Colors Make You Look Older?
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Introduction

As we age, our skin tone changes and our hair color can make a big difference in how we look. Choosing the wrong hair color can make you look older, tired, and dull. So, it's important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and adds brightness to your face.

How to Choose a More Youthful Hair Color

Here are some tips on how to choose a more youthful hair color:

  • Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone. Warmer tones like golden blondes, rich browns, and reds can add warmth and radiance to your skin.
  • Avoid hair colors that are too dark or too light. These shades can wash you out and make you look older.
  • Choose a hair color that adds depth and dimension to your hair. Highlights and lowlights can add dimension and make your hair appear fuller.
  • Consider going a few shades lighter or warmer than your natural hair color. This can add a youthful glow to your complexion.

What Hair Colors Make You Look Older?

Now, let's explore the hair colors that can make you look older:

1. Jet Black

While jet black hair can look stunning on some people, it can also make you look older and harsh. It can also wash out your complexion and make your features appear harsh.

2. Ash Blonde

Ash blonde hair can be a beautiful shade, but it can also make you look older and dull. This hair color can wash out your skin tone and make you look tired.

3. Cool Brown

Cool brown hair colors can be very flattering on some people, but they can also make you look older. These shades can be too harsh, and they can wash out your complexion.

4. Platinum Blonde

Platinum blonde hair can look striking, but it can also make you look older. This hair color can wash out your skin tone and make your features appear harsh.

5. Red-Orange

Red-orange hair can be a bold and beautiful choice, but it can also make you look older. This hair color can clash with your skin tone and make you look washed out.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair a lighter color to look younger?

A: Yes, going a few shades lighter can add a youthful glow to your complexion.

Q: What hair colors are best for older women?

A: Warmer tones like golden blondes, rich browns, and reds can add warmth and radiance to your skin.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking youthful, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to add moisture and shine to your hair.

Now that you know what hair color makes you look older, you can make an informed decision about which hair color is right for you. Remember to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and adds radiance to your complexion. With the right hair color and hair care routine, you can look and feel more youthful and vibrant.
