What Is The Best Shape Of Eyeglasses For My Face

Written by Oliver Oct 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right eyeglasses can be a daunting task, especially if you’re not sure what shape of eyeglasses would best suit your face. The right pair of glasses can not only help you see better, but they can also enhance your facial features and complement your personal style. In this article, we’ll discuss how to determine the shape of your face and which eyeglasses are best for your face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right eyeglasses for your face is to determine your face shape. There are five basic face shapes: round, square, heart-shaped, oval, and diamond. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and examine your face in a mirror. Look for the following characteristics:

Round Face: A round face has full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a rounded hairline. The width and length of the face are approximately the same.

Square Face: A square face has a strong jawline, a square or rectangular forehead, and a straight hairline.

Heart-Shaped Face: A heart-shaped face has a wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin.

Oval Face: An oval face has a balanced proportion with a slightly curved jawline. The forehead is slightly wider than the chin.

Diamond Face: A diamond face has a narrow forehead, high cheekbones, and a pointed chin.

Best Eyeglasses for Round Face

If you have a round face, look for eyeglasses that will help elongate your face and make it appear less round. Rectangle and square-shaped frames with sharp angles can help create a more angular appearance. Avoid round or oval frames, as they will only accentuate the roundness of your face.

Best Eyeglasses for Square Face

If you have a square face, look for eyeglasses that will soften your angular features. Round and oval-shaped frames can help balance out your face shape. Avoid square or rectangular frames, as they will only make your face appear more angular.

Best Eyeglasses for Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, look for eyeglasses that will balance out your forehead and chin. Frames with wider bottoms and narrow tops, such as cat-eye or butterfly frames, can help balance out your face shape. Avoid frames that are wider at the top than at the bottom, as they will only accentuate the width of your forehead.

Best Eyeglasses for Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you’re in luck! Oval faces can pull off almost any eyeglass frame shape. Look for frames that are proportional to your face and complement your personal style.

Tips for Choosing Eyeglasses

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing eyeglasses:

  • Consider your skin tone and personal style when choosing the color and material of your frames.
  • Make sure the frame fits properly and comfortably on your face.
  • Consider the size of the frame in proportion to your face.
  • Think about your lifestyle and choose frames that will suit your daily activities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I wear any eyeglass shape if I have an oval face?

Yes, oval faces can pull off almost any eyeglass frame shape.

Can round faces wear round eyeglasses?

Avoid round or oval frames if you have a round face, as they will only accentuate the roundness of your face. Look for rectangle and square-shaped frames with sharp angles instead.

What are the best eyeglasses for a heart-shaped face?

Look for frames with wider bottoms and narrow tops, such as cat-eye or butterfly frames, to balance out your forehead and chin.

Hair Care Tips for Eyeglass Wearers

If you wear eyeglasses, you may notice that they can cause your hair to become flattened or tangled. Here are some tips to help keep your hair looking great while wearing eyeglasses:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  • Use a round brush to add volume and curl to your hair.
  • Avoid wearing your glasses on top of your head, as this can cause your hair to become flattened.

Choosing the right eyeglasses for your face shape can be a fun and rewarding experience. By following these tips, you can find a pair of glasses that not only help you see better but also enhance your facial features and complement your personal style.
