Indian Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a chubby face or a double chin, finding the right hairstyle can be a bit challenging. However, with the right haircut and styling, you can accentuate your best features and look your best. In this guide, we will take a look at some of the best Indian hairstyles for fat faces and double chins, and provide you with tips and tricks to help you rock your look.

Description

Having a round face or a double chin is quite common among Indian women. However, this doesn't mean you can't look your best. By choosing the right hairstyle, you can accentuate your best features and look stunning. In this guide, we will provide you with some of the best hairstyles for fat faces and double chins, and tips to help you rock your look.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle for a round face or a double chin is to determine your face shape. Here are some common face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

If you have a round face or a double chin, you should choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and makes it appear slimmer. Hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head or have layers can also help to balance out your face shape.

Step by Step Guide

Here are some step-by-step instructions to help you achieve some of the best Indian hairstyles for fat faces and double chins:

  1. Bob Cut - This is a classic hairstyle that works well for women with round faces. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair to chin-length and add layers to the sides to create volume.
  2. Layered Cut - This hairstyle is perfect for women with thick hair. The layers help to create volume and add movement to the hair. You can also add bangs to frame your face.
  3. Pixie Cut - This is a short and sassy hairstyle that works well for women with round faces. It helps to create a slimming effect and adds volume to the top of your head.
  4. Side Swept Bangs - This is a simple yet effective hairstyle for women with round faces. It helps to create an illusion of length and makes your face appear slimmer.
  5. Braid Crown - This is a beautiful hairstyle that works well for women with round faces. To achieve this look, braid the front section of your hair and wrap it around the crown of your head. You can also add flowers or accessories to complete the look.

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips to help you style your hair for a round face or a double chin:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face
  • Avoid blunt cuts
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head
  • Use hair accessories to add interest to your hairstyle
  • Use a round brush when blow drying your hair to add volume and create a sleek look

FAQs

Q. Can I still wear my hair down if I have a round face?

A. Yes, you can still wear your hair down if you have a round face. However, you should choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head and avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.

Q. Should I avoid bangs if I have a round face?

A. No, you can still wear bangs if you have a round face. However, you should choose bangs that are side-swept or asymmetrical to create an illusion of length.

Q. Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a double chin?

A. Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a double chin. However, you should choose a high ponytail to create an illusion of length and avoid low ponytails that draw attention to your chin.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy and beautiful hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly and use a conditioner to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny

With the right hairstyle and styling, you can accentuate your best features and look your best. Use these tips and tricks to help you choose the right hairstyle and rock your look!
