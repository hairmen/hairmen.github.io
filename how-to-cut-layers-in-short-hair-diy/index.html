How To Cut Layers In Short Hair Diy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Sep 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your short hair looking plain and boring? Adding layers to your hair can give it more texture and volume, making it look more interesting and stylish. In this article, we will walk you through the steps on how to cut layers in short hair DIY style.

How to Cut Layers in Short Hair DIY

Before we start, make sure that you have the right tools and products. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a fine-tooth comb, hair clips, and a spray bottle filled with water.

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Spray your hair with water until it is damp but not soaking wet. This will make it easier to cut and will prevent split ends.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Using your fine-tooth comb, divide your hair into sections. Start by parting your hair in the middle and then divide it into four sections: two at the front and two at the back.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Layer

Start by cutting the bottom layer. Take the section of hair at the back and comb it straight down. Hold the ends of the hair between your fingers and cut straight across. This will be the shortest layer of your haircut.

Step 4: Cut the Next Layer

Take the next section of hair above the bottom layer and comb it down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle. This will create a longer layer that will blend with the bottom layer.

Step 5: Repeat the Process

Repeat the process with the remaining sections of hair. Cut each layer at a slightly longer length than the previous one.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Once you have cut all the layers, use your scissors to blend them together. Cut small pieces of hair at an angle to create a seamless transition between the layers.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Finally, style your hair as desired. You can use a hair dryer and a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair.

Tips

  • Start by cutting your hair a little longer than you want it to be. You can always go back and cut more if needed.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while cutting.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time to ensure that each layer is cut evenly.

FAQ

Can I cut layers in my own hair?

Yes, you can cut layers in your own hair. Just make sure that you have the right tools and follow the steps carefully.

How often should I cut my hair?

You should cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to use a good shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray when styling it.

Cutting layers in short hair DIY style can be a fun and rewarding experience. Just follow the steps carefully and take your time. With a little practice, you can achieve a stylish and trendy haircut that will make you feel confident and beautiful.
