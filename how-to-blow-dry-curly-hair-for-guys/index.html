How To Blow Dry Curly Hair For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Oct 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that it can be a challenge to style it just the way you want. Blow drying curly hair for guys can be especially tricky, but with the right techniques and tools, you can achieve a great look that will last all day. In this article, we'll show you how to blow dry curly hair for guys in a way that's easy to understand and follow. We'll also share some tips and tricks to help you get the best results possible.

Description

Blow drying curly hair for guys can be a challenge because curly hair tends to be more prone to frizz and breakage than straight hair. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great look that will last all day. Blow drying your hair can help you achieve a more polished, professional look, and it can also help you add volume and texture to your hair.

How to Blow Dry Curly Hair for Guys: Step by Step

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

Before you start blow drying your hair, make sure that it's clean and damp. You can wash your hair with your favorite shampoo and conditioner, or you can use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your curls. Make sure to towel dry your hair gently and avoid rubbing it vigorously, as this can cause breakage and frizz.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant

Before you start blow drying your hair, it's important to protect it from heat damage. Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair to help prevent damage and breakage. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends where your hair is most prone to damage.

Step 3: Use a diffuser attachment

To blow dry curly hair for guys, it's important to use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This attachment helps to distribute the heat more evenly and reduce frizz. To use the diffuser, attach it to your blow dryer and hold it close to your hair. Use a low heat setting and gently move the diffuser around your head, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths of your hair.

Step 4: Use a low heat setting

When blow drying curly hair for guys, it's important to use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair. High heat can cause breakage and frizz, so make sure to use a low heat setting and move the blow dryer around your head gently. You can also use a cool shot button to help set your style and reduce frizz.

Step 5: Finish with a serum or oil

Once you've finished blow drying your hair, you can apply a serum or oil to your hair to help reduce frizz and add shine. Apply a small amount of product to your hands and run it through your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help to keep your curls defined and prevent frizz throughout the day.

Tips for Blow Drying Curly Hair for Guys

Blow drying curly hair for guys can be challenging, but with these tips and tricks, you can achieve a great look that will last all day:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls before blow drying
  • Don't use a brush, as this can cause breakage and frizz
  • Avoid touching your hair too much while blow drying, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or deep conditioner regularly to help keep your curls healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy

FAQs

Can I blow dry my curly hair every day?

It's not recommended to blow dry your curly hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage. However, if you do need to blow dry your hair, make sure to use a low heat setting and a heat protectant to minimize damage.

Can I use a regular blow dryer on curly hair?

It's best to use a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment on curly hair, as this helps to distribute the heat more evenly and reduce frizz. If you don't have a diffuser attachment, you can try using a low heat setting and holding the blow dryer further away from your hair to minimize damage.

What type of products should I use on my curly hair?

It's best to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as leave-in conditioners, curl creams, and serums. These products can help to define your curls and reduce frizz.

Hair Care for Curly Hair: Final Thoughts

Blow drying curly hair for guys can be a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great look that will last all day. Remember to use a diffuser attachment, a low heat setting, and a heat protectant to minimize damage and reduce frizz. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to blow dry your curly hair like a pro!
