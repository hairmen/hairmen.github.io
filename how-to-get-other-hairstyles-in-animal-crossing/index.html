How To Get Other Hairstyles In Animal Crossing

Written by Daniel Jun 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're an avid player of Animal Crossing, you know how important it is to have a great hairstyle. But sometimes, you get tired of the same old look and want to switch things up. Luckily, there are ways to get other hairstyles in the game. In this article, we'll go over how to do just that.

How to

The first way to get new hairstyles in Animal Crossing is to visit Harriet's salon. Harriet is a poodle who runs a hair salon in the game. You can find her in the city area of your town. When you visit her salon, she will offer you a few different hairstyles to choose from. If you don't like any of the options she gives you, you can ask her to try again. Keep in mind that changing your hairstyle at Harriet's salon costs 3,000 bells.

Another way to get new hairstyles is to scan amiibo cards. If you have any Animal Crossing amiibo cards, you can use them to invite villagers to your town. When you invite a villager using an amiibo card, they may offer to give you a new hairstyle. This is a great way to get a unique look that you won't find at Harriet's salon.

Step by Step

1. Visit Harriet's salon in the city area of your town.

2. Talk to Harriet and ask her to give you a new hairstyle.

3. Choose from the options she gives you, or ask her to try again if you don't like them.

4. Pay 3,000 bells to change your hairstyle.

5. Alternatively, scan an Animal Crossing amiibo card to invite a villager to your town.

6. If the villager offers to give you a new hairstyle, accept their offer.

7. Enjoy your new look!

Tips

- If you're looking for a specific hairstyle, keep visiting Harriet's salon until she offers it to you.

- Don't forget to save up 3,000 bells if you want to change your hairstyle at Harriet's salon.

- If you have a favorite villager, try inviting them to your town using an amiibo card. They may offer to give you a new hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: Can I get all the hairstyles in the game?

A: No, there are some hairstyles that are exclusive to certain characters or events. However, you can get a wide variety of hairstyles by visiting Harriet's salon and using amiibo cards.

Q: How often can I change my hairstyle?

A: You can visit Harriet's salon as often as you like, but it will cost you 3,000 bells each time you want to change your hairstyle.

Q: How do I use amiibo cards?

A: To use amiibo cards, you'll need an NFC reader. You can either buy a separate NFC reader or use the one built into your Nintendo Switch or 3DS. Once you have an NFC reader, simply scan the amiibo card by holding it up to the reader.

Hair Care

Once you have your new hairstyle, it's important to take care of it. Make sure to brush your hair regularly and use a shampoo and conditioner that works well for your hair type. You can also use styling products like gel or hairspray to keep your hair in place.

With these tips, you'll be able to get a variety of new hairstyles in Animal Crossing. Whether you want to try something new or just switch things up, there's a hairstyle out there for everyone.
