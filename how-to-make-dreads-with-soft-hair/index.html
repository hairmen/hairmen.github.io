How To Make Dreads With Soft Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have soft hair and have always wanted to try dreadlocks, you may feel discouraged by the belief that only people with coarse hair can achieve this style. However, with the right technique and products, you can create beautiful, well-formed dreads with soft hair. In this article, we will guide you through the process of making dreads with soft hair, including tips and hair care advice to keep your locks healthy and looking great.

How to Make Dreads with Soft Hair

Contrary to popular belief, soft hair can hold dreadlocks just as well as coarse hair. However, the process may take longer and require more care to prevent damage. Before starting, make sure your hair is healthy and free of any chemical treatments or heat damage. Here are the basic steps to create dreads with soft hair:

1. Choose the Right Method

There are different methods to make dreads, such as backcombing, twisting, and braiding. For soft hair, the most recommended method is the twisting method, as it creates less tension and damage.

2. Get the Right Products

Using the right products can make a big difference in how your dreads turn out and how easy it is to maintain them. You will need a residue-free shampoo, a locking gel or wax, and a crochet hook for maintenance.

3. Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections with a rat-tail comb. The size of the sections will depend on your preference and hair thickness. Some people prefer thicker dreads, while others like thinner ones.

4. Twist Your Hair

Take one section of hair and twist it tightly from the root to the end. You can use a locking gel or wax to help the hair hold its shape. Repeat the process with each section until you have twisted all your hair.

5. Palm Roll Your Dreads

After twisting your hair, you need to palm roll each dread to help it set and remove any lumps or bumps. Rub the dread between your palms in a rolling motion, starting at the root and moving towards the tip. Repeat this step every few days to keep your dreads tight and well-formed.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide to making dreads with soft hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a residue-free shampoo to remove any oils or product buildup. Avoid using conditioner, as it can make your hair too soft and difficult to dread.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a rat-tail comb to divide your hair into small sections. The size of the sections will determine the size of your dreads.

Step 3: Twist Your Hair

Take one section of hair and twist it tightly from the root to the end. Use a locking gel or wax to help the hair hold its shape. Repeat the process with each section until you have twisted all your hair.

Step 4: Palm Roll Your Dreads

After twisting your hair, you need to palm roll each dread to help it set and remove any lumps or bumps. Rub the dread between your palms in a rolling motion, starting at the root and moving towards the tip. Repeat this step every few days to keep your dreads tight and well-formed.

Step 5: Maintain Your Dreads

Use a crochet hook to maintain your dreads every few weeks. This involves pulling loose hairs into the dread and tightening the roots.

Tips for Maintaining Soft Hair Dreads

Maintaining dreadlocks requires some effort, but it is worth it to keep them looking neat and healthy. Here are some tips to help you maintain your soft hair dreads:

  • Wash your hair with a residue-free shampoo every 1-2 weeks
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh your hair down
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap

FAQs

Can I make dreads with straight hair?

Yes, you can make dreads with straight hair. However, it may take longer and require more maintenance to achieve the desired look.

How long does it take to make dreads with soft hair?

The time it takes to make dreads with soft hair depends on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as the method you choose. It can take anywhere from a few hours to a few days to complete the process.

Can I wash my dreads?

Yes, you can and should wash your dreads regularly to keep them clean and healthy. Use a residue-free shampoo and avoid using conditioner.

Hair Care for Soft Hair Dreads

To keep your soft hair dreads looking great, you need to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh your hair down
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap

By following these tips and techniques, you can make dreads with soft hair and enjoy this unique and stylish look.
