How To Know What Face Shape You Have A Guide For Women

Written by Bardi Jun 28, 2023 · 5 min read

As a woman, knowing your face shape can help you choose the right hairstyles, makeup, and accessories that flatter your features. But how do you determine what face shape you have? In this guide, we’ll go over how to know what face shape you have female and give you tips on how to enhance your natural beauty.

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Each Face Shape

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the different face shapes, it’s important to know how to measure your face. Here are the key measurements to take:

  • Forehead: Measure across the widest part of your forehead, from one temple to the other.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across your cheekbones, starting and ending at the pointiest part below the outer corner of each eye.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jaw angles upwards. Then double that number.
  • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Once you’ve taken these measurements, you can compare them to the following face shapes:

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is longer than it is wide, with gently rounded contours. The forehead and jawline are slightly narrower than the cheekbones.

Round Face Shape

A round face shape is roughly equal in length and width, with soft curves and no sharp angles. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape has a strong, angular jawline and a broad forehead. The face is roughly as wide as it is long, with straight sides and a square chin.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape has a wide forehead and cheekbones, with a narrow chin that comes to a point. The face may be widest at the temples.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape is widest at the cheekbones and has a narrow forehead and jawline. The face is angular, with sharp, defined features.

Pear Face Shape

A pear face shape has a narrow forehead and wide jawline. The face may be widest at the jaw, with a rounded chin.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to determine your face shape:

  1. Measure your forehead, cheekbones, jawline, and face length.
  2. Compare your measurements to the different face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and pear.
  3. Identify which face shape is closest to your measurements.
  4. Take note of the key features of your face shape.

Tips for Each Face Shape

Now that you know what face shape you have, here are some tips on how to enhance your natural beauty:

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is considered the ideal shape, so you can pull off almost any hairstyle or accessory. Try to keep your hair off your face to show off your features.

Round Face Shape

For a round face shape, try hairstyles that add height and lengthen your face. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too curly, as they can make your face appear wider.

Square Face Shape

For a square face shape, try hairstyles that soften your features, such as soft curls or layers. Avoid straight, blunt hairstyles that can emphasize your angular jawline.

Heart Face Shape

For a heart face shape, try hairstyles that add volume to the chin area, such as chin-length bobs or side-swept bangs. Avoid hairstyles that are too top-heavy, as they can make your forehead appear wider.

Diamond Face Shape

For a diamond face shape, try hairstyles that add width at the forehead and chin, such as a layered bob or a side-parted hairstyle. Avoid hairstyles that are too narrow or too voluminous, as they can make your face appear longer.

Pear Face Shape

For a pear face shape, try hairstyles that add volume at the crown, such as a high ponytail or an updo. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too straight, as they can make your face appear even longer.

FAQs

What if my face shape doesn’t fit into any of these categories?

It’s possible that you have a combination of face shapes, or a unique shape that doesn’t fit into any of these categories. In that case, focus on enhancing your best features and finding hairstyles and accessories that make you feel confident.

Do I need to take these measurements every time I want to try a new hairstyle?

No, once you’ve determined your face shape, you don’t need to take these measurements again. However, it’s always a good idea to consult with a hairstylist before making a major change.

Hair Care for Your Face Shape

Regardless of your face shape, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh.
  • Try different hairstyles and accessories to find what works best for your face shape.

By following these tips and knowing your face shape, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance.
