How To Cut Guys Hair On Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 13, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a guy who likes to keep his hair looking fresh, you may be considering cutting your own hair at home. But if you're not a professional barber, you may be wondering how to cut guys hair on top without making any mistakes. In this blog post, we'll walk you through the process of cutting guys hair on top, from preparing your tools to finishing touches.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Preparing Your Hair
  • Cutting Your Hair
  • Blending Your Hair
  • Finishing Touches
  • Tips for Cutting Guys Hair on Top
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather the right tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers with guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Mirror
  • Hair cape or towel
  • Spray bottle with water

Preparing Your Hair

Once you have your tools, it's time to prepare your hair for cutting. Here's what you need to do:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Comb your hair to get rid of any tangles.
  3. Use hair clips to section off the hair you're not going to cut.
  4. Put on your hair cape or towel to protect your clothes.
  5. Use a spray bottle to dampen your hair.

Cutting Your Hair

Now it's time to cut your hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with the longest guard on your clippers.
  2. Begin at the back of your head and work your way forward.
  3. Move the clippers in an upward motion, cutting your hair against the grain.
  4. Use your comb to help guide your clippers.
  5. Move to a shorter guard as you work your way up your head.

Blending Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair with the clippers, it's time to blend your hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Use scissors to trim the hair on top of your head.
  2. Comb your hair straight up and use scissors to trim any excess hair.
  3. Use the scissors to blend the hair on the top of your head with the hair on the sides.

Finishing Touches

Once you've finished cutting and blending your hair, it's time for the finishing touches. Here's what to do:

  1. Use your clippers to trim any stray hairs around your ears and the back of your neck.
  2. Use scissors to trim any remaining long hairs.
  3. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Guys Hair on Top

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting guys hair on top:

  • Start with a longer guard on your clippers and work your way down.
  • Use scissors to blend your hair and create a natural look.
  • Take your time and be patient.
  • Use your comb to help guide your clippers.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting guys hair on top:

  • Q: Can I cut my own hair with just scissors?
  • A: It's possible, but it's much easier to use clippers with guards.
  • Q: How often should I cut my hair on top?
  • A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it.
  • Q: How can I prevent mistakes when cutting my own hair?
  • A: Take your time, use the right tools, and ask for help if you need it.

Hair Care After Cutting

Once you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Style your hair as desired.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking fresh.

In conclusion, cutting guys hair on top is a simple process that anyone can do with the right tools and a little patience. Follow these steps and you'll have a fresh new look in no time!
