Best Hairstyles For Long Faces Over 40

As we age, our face shape tends to change, and long faces are a common occurrence. If you have a long face, finding the right hairstyle can be a daunting task. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be discussing the best hairstyles for long faces over 40, including how to achieve them, tips, and hair care.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles, it's essential to determine your face shape. To do this, you'll need to measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, you have a square face shape. If your forehead is the widest part of your face, and your jawline is narrow, you have a heart-shaped face. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have a long face shape.

Best Hairstyles for Long Faces Over 40

Now that you know your face shape let's dive into the best hairstyles for long faces over 40.

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for those with long faces as they add volume and dimension to the hair. This style works well with both straight and wavy hair and can be worn with a middle or side part.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a fantastic option for those with long faces as they help to shorten the forehead and add volume to the sides of the face. This style works well with both short and long hair and can be worn straight or wavy.

3. Bob with Volume

A bob with volume is a great option for those with long faces as it adds volume to the sides of the face, making it appear wider. This style works well with both straight and wavy hair and can be worn with a middle or side part.

4. Shoulder-Length Hair with Layers

Shoulder-length hair with layers is a great option for those with long faces as it adds volume and movement to the hair. This style works well with both straight and wavy hair and can be worn with a middle or side part.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving These Hairstyles

Now that you know the best hairstyles for long faces over 40 let's take a look at how to achieve them.

1. Long Layers

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair, removing any excess water.
  3. Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair, working it through from roots to ends.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on the ends to create volume and movement.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or curls.
  6. Finish with a lightweight hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair, removing any excess water.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on the roots to create volume.
  4. Using a straightener, create a side part in your hair.
  5. Take a section of hair from the front and curl it away from your face using a curling iron.
  6. Finish with a lightweight hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. Bob with Volume

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair, removing any excess water.
  3. Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair, working it through from roots to ends.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on the roots to create volume.
  5. Use a straightener to create volume and movement in your hair.
  6. Finish with a lightweight hairspray to hold the style in place.

4. Shoulder-Length Hair with Layers

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair, removing any excess water.
  3. Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair, working it through from roots to ends.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on the roots to create volume.
  5. Use a straightener to add waves or curls to your hair.
  6. Finish with a lightweight hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you know how to achieve these hairstyles, let's take a look at some tips for maintaining them:

  • Invest in a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools on your hair.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

1. Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a long face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a long face. However, be sure to add volume to the sides of your hair to balance out the length of your face.

2. Should I avoid bangs if I have a long face?

No, you don't have to avoid bangs if you have a long face. Side-swept bangs are a great option for those with long faces as they help to shorten the forehead and add volume to the sides of the face.

Hair Care Tips for Long Faces Over 40

Now that you know the best hairstyles and how to achieve them let's take a look at some hair care tips for long faces over 40:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and dyes that can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated.

With these tips, you can maintain healthy and beautiful hair that complements your long face. Remember to experiment with different styles and find what works best for you.
