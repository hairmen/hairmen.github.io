Which Haircut Suits For Round Face Male A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

When it comes to choosing a haircut, it's essential to consider your face shape. For men with round faces, selecting the right hairstyle is crucial to achieve a balanced and flattering look. In this guide, we'll discuss which haircut suits for round face male, how to choose the right style, and some tips to maintain your hairstyle.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Choose the Right Haircut
  2. Best Haircuts for Round Face Male
  3. Tips to Maintain Your Haircut
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Choose the Right Haircut

Before deciding on a specific hairstyle, it's essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. For round face males, it's best to opt for haircuts that add volume to the crown and elongate the face shape.

Another crucial factor to consider is the hair texture. If you have thin hair, avoid long hairstyles as they can make your face appear rounder. Instead, opt for a shorter style that adds volume and texture to your hair. If you have thick hair, you can experiment with longer hairstyles that add texture and volume to your hair.

Lastly, consider your lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. If you have a busy schedule and prefer low-maintenance haircuts, opt for short styles that require minimal upkeep. If you have more time to style your hair, longer hairstyles can provide more versatility and styling options.

Best Haircuts for Round Face Male

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a hairstyle let's take a look at some of the best haircuts for round face males:

1. Short Buzz Cut

A short buzz cut is an excellent choice for those who prefer low-maintenance haircuts. This style adds volume to the crown and elongates the face shape, making it a perfect choice for round face males.

2. Short Pompadour

The short pompadour is a classic hairstyle that adds volume to the crown and creates a more defined hairline. This style works well with thick hair and requires minimal upkeep.

3. Side Part

The side part is a timeless hairstyle that works well with both short and long hair. This style adds volume to the crown and creates a more defined hairline, making it a great choice for round face males.

4. Textured Crop

The textured crop is a popular hairstyle that adds volume and texture to the hair. This style works well with both thick and thin hair and is perfect for those who prefer a more relaxed and casual look.

5. Messy Quiff

The messy quiff is a versatile hairstyle that works well with both short and long hair. This style adds volume to the crown and creates a more defined hairline, making it a great choice for round face males.

Tips to Maintain Your Haircut

Now that you've chosen the right haircut, it's essential to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

1. Regular trims

Regular trims are essential to keep your haircut looking fresh and neat. It's recommended to visit your hairstylist every four to six weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

2. Use the right products

Using the right products can help you maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair healthy. It's recommended to use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and a styling product that provides hold and texture.

3. Avoid excessive heat

Avoid using excessive heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair and affect your hairstyle. It's recommended to use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

FAQ

1. Can round face males have long hair?

Yes, round face males can have long hair. However, it's essential to choose a style that adds volume and texture to your hair and elongates your face shape.

2. What face shape is best for short haircuts?

Short haircuts work well with most face shapes, but they're especially flattering for those with angular or square face shapes.

3. How can I add volume to my hair?

You can add volume to your hair by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow-drying your hair with a round brush, or using a styling product that adds texture and volume to your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Aside from maintaining your haircut, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to help you achieve healthy and beautiful hair:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help remove dirt, oil, and product buildup, keeping your hair clean and healthy. It's recommended to wash your hair every two to three days, depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

2. Condition your hair

Conditioning your hair can help keep it hydrated and prevent damage. It's recommended to use a conditioner that suits your hair type and apply it to the mid-lengths and ends of your hair.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Exposure to the sun can damage your hair and affect your hairstyle. It's recommended to wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

4. Eat a healthy diet

A healthy diet can help promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair loss. It's recommended to eat a diet rich in vitamins, minerals, and protein to keep your hair healthy and strong.



Choosing the right haircut can make a significant difference in your appearance and confidence. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and suits your personality. Remember to maintain your haircut and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. We hope this guide has helped you choose the best haircut for your round face male.
