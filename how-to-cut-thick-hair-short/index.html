How To Cut Thick Hair Short A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on how to cut thick hair short. We understand that cutting thick hair can be a challenging task, especially if you want to go for a shorter hairstyle. However, with the right technique and tools, you can achieve a fabulous look that you will love. Keep reading to learn more.

Description

Thick hair can be challenging to cut because it requires more effort and time to make it look neat and stylish. However, cutting thick hair short has become a popular trend in recent years. It is a great way to change your look and add volume to your hair. Short hair is also easy to manage and style, making it a popular choice for busy people.

How to Cut Thick Hair Short

The following is a step-by-step guide on how to cut thick hair short:

Step 1: Gather the Necessary Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, ensure you have the necessary tools. These include a pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips. You may also need a hair razor to add texture to your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair and let it dry naturally or use a hair dryer. Make sure your hair is free of tangles, and it is easy to comb through.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts using hair clips. This will make it easier to cut your hair and ensure that you do not miss any sections.

Step 4: Start Cutting Your Hair

Start cutting your hair from the back using a comb and scissors. Cut your hair to the desired length, ensuring that it is even. Work your way to the sides and then the front.

Step 5: Use a Razor

Use a hair razor to add texture to your hair. This will give your hair a more natural look and make it easy to style.

Step 6: Check for Any Uneven Sections

Check your hair to ensure that it is even. If you notice any uneven sections, cut them until they are even.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Style your hair to your desired look. You can use a hair gel, mousse, or spray to hold your hair in place.

Tips

The following are some tips to help you cut thick hair short:

  • Start cutting your hair from the back and work your way to the front.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Do not rush the process. Take your time to ensure that your hair is even and the right length.
  • Use a hair razor to add texture to your hair.
  • Style your hair to your desired look.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my thick hair short with a razor?

A: Yes, you can use a razor to add texture to your hair, but make sure you do not overdo it.

Q: How often should I cut my thick hair short?

A: You can cut your hair short every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and style.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is essential to take good care of it. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Avoid using too many hair products as they may damage your hair. Also, make sure you protect your hair from heat when using styling tools.

Now that you know how to cut thick hair short, it is time to give it a try. Follow the steps we have outlined above, and you will achieve a fabulous look that you will love. Remember to take good care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty.
