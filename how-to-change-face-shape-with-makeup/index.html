How to Change Face Shape with Makeup

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's first understand how makeup can change your face shape. Makeup can help in creating an illusion of a different face shape by contouring and highlighting certain areas of your face. Contouring involves using a matte product that is two shades darker than your skin tone to create shadows, while highlighting involves using a product that is two shades lighter than your skin tone to create the illusion of light.

Now that you understand the basics, let's get started with the step-by-step guide.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in changing your face shape with makeup is to determine your face shape. The most common face shapes are round, square, oval, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look at your face in the mirror. You can also take a photo of yourself and trace the outline of your face on the photo.

Step 2: Contour Your Face

Once you have determined your face shape, you can start contouring. Use a matte contouring product that is two shades darker than your skin tone and apply it to the areas where you want to create shadows. For example, if you have a round face, you can contour your cheeks to make them appear slimmer. Blend the contouring product well with a brush or a sponge.

Step 3: Highlight Your Face

The next step is to highlight your face. Use a highlighting product that is two shades lighter than your skin tone and apply it to the areas where you want to create the illusion of light. For example, if you have a square face, you can highlight your cheekbones to make them appear more prominent. Blend the highlighting product well with a brush or a sponge.

Step 4: Blend Well

After contouring and highlighting, it is important to blend well to create a natural-looking effect. Use a clean brush or sponge to blend the contouring and highlighting products together. Make sure there are no harsh lines.

Step 5: Add Blush

To add color to your face, apply blush to the apples of your cheeks. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone and blend well with a brush or a sponge.

Step 6: Define Your Brows

Defined brows can help in framing your face and creating a more defined look. Use a brow product to fill in any sparse areas and define the shape of your brows.

Step 7: Apply Mascara

Mascara can help in opening up your eyes and making them appear more prominent. Apply mascara to your top and bottom lashes to complete your look.

Tips for Changing Face Shape with Makeup

Here are some additional tips that can help in changing your face shape with makeup:

  • Use a good quality makeup product that blends well.
  • Blend well to create a natural-looking effect.
  • Choose a contouring and highlighting product that complements your skin tone.
  • Experiment with different shades and techniques to find what works best for your face shape.
  • Use a setting spray to make your makeup last longer.

FAQs

Can makeup really change my face shape?

Yes, makeup can help in creating an illusion of a different face shape by contouring and highlighting certain areas of your face.

Do I need to contour and highlight every time I do my makeup?

No, you don't need to contour and highlight every time you do your makeup. It is an optional step that can help in changing your face shape.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, you can use a clean brush or a sponge to blend it out. You can also use a makeup remover to remove the product and start over.

Hair Care Tips for Your New Look

Changing your face shape with makeup can also impact how your hair looks. Here are some hair care tips for your new look:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your new face shape.
  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Don't forget to wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy.

Changing your face shape with makeup is a fun and easy way to experiment with your look. With these tips and tricks, you can easily transform your face shape without undergoing any surgery. Remember to have fun and experiment with different shades and techniques to find what works best for you.
