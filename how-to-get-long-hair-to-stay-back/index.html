How To Get Long Hair To Stay Back Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Oct 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Learn how to keep long hair from falling forward and getting in your face with these easy tips and tricks.

Description

Long hair can be beautiful, but it can also be a hassle when it constantly falls in your face. Whether you're trying to work, exercise, or simply enjoy a windy day, keeping long hair back can be a challenge. Fortunately, there are several ways to get long hair to stay back without resorting to drastic measures like cutting it off. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for keeping long hair under control.

How to Get Long Hair to Stay Back

Step 1: Find the Right Hair Products

The first step to keeping long hair back is to use the right hair products. Look for products that are designed to hold hair in place, such as hairspray, gel, or mousse. Apply the product to your hair before styling it, focusing on the roots and the areas where your hair tends to fall forward.

Step 2: Experiment with Hairstyles

There are several hairstyles that can help keep long hair back. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you. For example, a high ponytail, a bun, or a braid can all be effective ways to keep long hair out of your face.

Step 3: Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories can be a lifesaver when it comes to keeping long hair back. Try using hair clips, headbands, or scrunchies to hold your hair in place. You can also use a scarf or a bandana to tie your hair back.

Step 4: Consider Hair Extensions

If you have long hair but it's not thick enough to stay back on its own, consider getting hair extensions. Extensions can add volume and length to your hair, making it easier to style and keep back.

Tips for Keeping Long Hair Healthy

Tip 1: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style. Try to avoid using heat tools like curling irons and flat irons on a regular basis. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray first.

Tip 2: Don't Wash Your Hair Too Often

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days instead.

Tip 3: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

A deep conditioning treatment can help keep long hair healthy and strong. Use a conditioning mask or oil once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

FAQ

Q: Can I keep long hair back without using hair products?

A: Yes, you can use hair accessories like clips or headbands to keep your hair back without using hair products. However, using products like hairspray or gel can help keep your hair in place for longer periods of time.

Q: How often should I trim my long hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 8-12 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I keep long hair back while exercising?

A: Yes, you can keep long hair back while exercising by using a tight ponytail, braids, or a headband.

Hair Care

Remember, keeping long hair back is not only about style, it's also about hair care. Taking care of your hair will ensure that it stays healthy and strong. Use these tips and tricks to keep your long hair looking great and staying back all day long.
