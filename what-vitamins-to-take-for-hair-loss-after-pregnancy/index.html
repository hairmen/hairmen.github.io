What Vitamins To Take For Hair Loss After Pregnancy

Written by Petter Jun 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are experiencing hair loss after pregnancy, you are not alone. Many women experience postpartum hair loss, also known as telogen effluvium, which is a temporary condition that causes excessive hair shedding. Fortunately, there are vitamins and supplements that can help promote hair growth and reduce hair loss. In this article, we will discuss what vitamins to take for hair loss after pregnancy.

Vitamin D

Vitamin D is an essential vitamin that plays a crucial role in many bodily functions, including hair growth. It helps regulate the growth and differentiation of hair follicles and can prevent hair loss. Vitamin D can be found in foods such as fatty fish, egg yolks, and fortified foods. However, it can be difficult to get enough vitamin D through diet alone, so taking a supplement may be necessary.

Vitamin C

Vitamin C is a powerful antioxidant that helps protect hair from damage caused by free radicals. It also helps the body absorb iron, which is essential for hair growth. Vitamin C can be found in many fruits and vegetables, including oranges, strawberries, and broccoli. Taking a vitamin C supplement can also help promote healthy hair growth.

Vitamin B7

Vitamin B7, also known as biotin, is a water-soluble vitamin that is essential for healthy hair, skin, and nails. It helps strengthen hair and prevent breakage, and can also improve hair thickness and shine. Biotin can be found in many foods, including eggs, nuts, and whole grains. However, taking a biotin supplement can help ensure that you are getting enough of this important vitamin.

Iron

Iron is essential for healthy hair growth, as it helps carry oxygen to the hair follicles. Iron deficiency can lead to hair loss, so it is important to make sure you are getting enough iron in your diet. Iron can be found in foods such as red meat, poultry, and seafood. If you are not getting enough iron through your diet, taking an iron supplement may be necessary.

Omega-3 Fatty Acids

Omega-3 fatty acids are essential fats that help reduce inflammation and promote healthy hair growth. They can be found in fatty fish, such as salmon and tuna, as well as in walnuts and flaxseeds. Taking an omega-3 supplement can also help ensure that you are getting enough of these important fats.

Zinc

Zinc is an essential mineral that is important for many bodily functions, including hair growth. It helps regulate the production of oil in the scalp and can prevent hair loss. Zinc can be found in foods such as oysters, beef, and pumpkin seeds. Taking a zinc supplement can also help promote healthy hair growth.

Biotin

Biotin, also known as vitamin B7, is an essential vitamin that is important for healthy hair growth. It helps strengthen hair and prevent breakage, and can also improve hair thickness and shine. Biotin can be found in many foods, including eggs, nuts, and whole grains. However, taking a biotin supplement can help ensure that you are getting enough of this important vitamin.

Collagen

Collagen is a protein that is essential for healthy hair, skin, and nails. It helps strengthen hair and prevent breakage, and can also improve hair thickness and shine. Collagen can be found in bone broth, as well as in supplements. Taking a collagen supplement can help promote healthy hair growth.

FAQs

What causes hair loss after pregnancy?

Hair loss after pregnancy, or postpartum hair loss, is caused by hormonal changes in the body. During pregnancy, high levels of estrogen can cause the hair to remain in a growth phase, resulting in thicker, fuller hair. However, after giving birth, estrogen levels drop, causing the hair to enter a resting phase and eventually fall out.

When should I start taking vitamins for hair loss after pregnancy?

You can start taking vitamins for hair loss after pregnancy as soon as you notice excessive shedding. However, it is always best to consult with your healthcare provider before starting any new supplements.

How long does postpartum hair loss last?

Postpartum hair loss is a temporary condition that typically lasts for 6-12 months after giving birth. However, it can take up to 18 months for hair to fully recover.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to taking vitamins and supplements, there are some hair care tips that can help reduce hair loss and promote healthy hair growth:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on the hair, such as braids and ponytails
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, or use them on a low heat setting
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet
  • Get enough rest and manage stress

By following these tips and taking the right vitamins and supplements, you can promote healthy hair growth and reduce hair loss after pregnancy.
