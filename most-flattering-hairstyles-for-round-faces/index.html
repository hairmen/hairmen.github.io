Most Flattering Hairstyles For Round Faces

Written by Luffy Jul 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your round face shape? Look no further! We’ve compiled a list of the most flattering hairstyles for round faces, along with tips on how to style them and take care of your hair. Read on to discover your new go-to look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Most Flattering Hairstyles for Round Faces
  • Styling Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for round faces, it’s important to first determine if you have a round face shape. Round faces are typically characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. To determine if you have a round face shape, try the following:

  • Take a selfie or stand in front of a mirror
  • Trace the outline of your face onto the mirror using lipstick or a washable marker
  • Compare the shape to the following face shapes: oval, square, heart, and round

If your face shape is round, then keep reading for the most flattering hairstyles.

Most Flattering Hairstyles for Round Faces

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for those with round faces. The layers help to elongate the face and create a more angular look. Be sure to avoid blunt cuts and instead opt for soft, wispy layers that frame the face.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for those with round faces. The angled cut helps to create a more oval shape and draws attention to the eyes. Be sure to avoid straight across bangs, as they can make the face appear more round.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold and daring option for those with round faces. The short cut helps to create a more angular look and draws attention to the cheekbones. Be sure to avoid a cut that is too short, as it can make the face appear wider.

4. Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic and timeless option for those with round faces. The length should hit just below the jawline and be slightly angled to create a more oval shape. Be sure to avoid a blunt cut, as it can make the face appear wider.

Styling Tips

Now that you’ve found the perfect hairstyle, it’s important to know how to style it to flatter your round face. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid adding volume around the cheeks and instead focus on adding height at the crown
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying to create volume at the roots
  • Avoid center parts and instead opt for a side part to create a more angular look

FAQ

What if I have curly hair?

Curly hair can be challenging to style, but don’t worry – there are still plenty of options for those with round faces. Try a curly bob or long layers with a side part to create a more elongated look.

What if I have a short neck?

If you have a short neck, be sure to avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long. Instead, opt for a mid-length cut that hits just below the jawline.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently and always use a heat protectant spray
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle

Now that you know the most flattering hairstyles for round faces, it’s time to book your next salon appointment and try out a new look. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one for you.
