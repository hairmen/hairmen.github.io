What Is Point Cutting Hair Technique

Written by Aprilia Jul 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

In the world of hairdressing, point cutting is a popular technique that can add texture and movement to hair. Essentially, it involves cutting into the ends of the hair with scissors, creating a pointy or textured effect. This technique can be used on all hair types and lengths, and is often used to add volume to thin or fine hair.

Description

Point cutting is a hair cutting technique where the scissors are held vertically and small notches are taken out of the hair. The cuts are made at an angle, creating a textured look that adds volume and movement. The technique is used to create layers, remove bulk, and add texture to the hair.

How To

If you're interested in trying point cutting on your hair, here's how to do it:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair into small sections. The smaller the section, the easier it is to point cut.
  2. Take a small section of hair and lift it up vertically with your fingers.
  3. Hold the scissors vertically and snip into the ends of the hair at an angle. The angle can be steep or shallow depending on the effect you want to create.
  4. Repeat this process on all sections of hair.
  5. Once you're finished, style your hair as usual to see the textured effect.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to point cutting:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into small sections. Use clips to hold each section in place.

Step 2: Lift a Section of Hair

Take a small section of hair and lift it up vertically with your fingers.

Step 3: Hold the Scissors Vertically

Hold the scissors vertically and snip into the ends of the hair at an angle. The angle can be steep or shallow depending on the effect you want to create.

Step 4: Repeat on All Sections

Repeat this process on all sections of hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you're finished, style your hair as usual to see the textured effect.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when point cutting:

  • Use sharp scissors to get clean cuts.
  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to bigger ones.
  • Take breaks to rest your hands and avoid cramping.
  • Practice on a mannequin head before trying it on your own hair.

FAQ

Is point cutting suitable for all hair types?

Yes, point cutting can be used on all hair types and lengths.

Does point cutting damage hair?

No, point cutting does not damage hair. In fact, it can help remove split ends and add volume to fine or thinning hair.

Can I point cut my own hair?

While it is possible to point cut your own hair, it is recommended to have a professional do it for you to avoid any mistakes.

Hair Care

After point cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain its health and texture. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to the hair.
  • Apply a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish the hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and texture of your point cut.
