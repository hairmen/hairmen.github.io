How To Get Dreadlocks White Girl A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Dreadlocks have been around for centuries, and they are a popular hairstyle choice for people of all cultures and backgrounds. However, many people assume that dreadlocks are only for people with coarse, curly hair. This is simply not true! In fact, anyone with any hair type can get dreadlocks, including white girls.

Table of Contents

  • How to Prepare Your Hair
  • How to Create Dreadlocks
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Dreadlocks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Dreadlocks

How to Prepare Your Hair

Before you start creating your dreadlocks, you need to prepare your hair properly. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Clean Your Hair

Clean hair is essential for creating dreadlocks. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup or excess oil. It's important to avoid using conditioner as it can make your hair too soft and difficult to work with.

Step 2: Dry Your Hair

Dry your hair completely before starting the dreadlocking process. You can either air dry your hair or use a blow dryer on a low heat setting.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small, even sections using a rat-tail comb. The size of each section will depend on the thickness of your hair and the size of the dreadlocks you want to create. Use hair ties or clips to secure each section.

How to Create Dreadlocks

Now that your hair is prepared, it's time to create your dreadlocks. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Apply Product

Apply a locking product to each section of your hair. You can use a wax or gel specifically designed for dreadlocks. Rub the product between your palms and then apply it to each section of hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the tips.

Step 2: Backcomb the Hair

Using a fine-toothed comb, backcomb each section of hair from the tips to the roots. This will create knots in your hair, which will eventually turn into dreadlocks. Be gentle and avoid pulling too hard on your hair.

Step 3: Twist and Roll

Twist and roll each section of hair between your hands to further encourage the knots to form. You may need to repeat this step several times until the hair starts to take on a dreadlock shape.

Step 4: Secure the Dreadlocks

Once you have created a dreadlock, you can secure it using a hair tie or rubber band. Make sure the band is not too tight, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Dreadlocks

Here are some tips to help you maintain your dreadlocks:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo to prevent buildup.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Avoid using a hairbrush or comb as this can cause your locks to unravel.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Consider using a crochet hook to tighten any loose strands of hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting dreadlocks as a white girl:

Can I get dreadlocks if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can! However, you may need to use a product to help your hair hold its shape.

How long does it take to create dreadlocks?

The amount of time it takes to create dreadlocks will depend on the length and thickness of your hair. It can take anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Do I need to shave my head to get dreadlocks?

No, you don't! You can create dreadlocks with any length of hair.

Hair Care for Dreadlocks

Here are some tips for taking care of your dreadlocks:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a month to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the elements by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Consider using a hair oil to keep your scalp moisturized.

Now you know how to get dreadlocks as a white girl! With the right preparation and care, your dreadlocks can be a stylish and unique hairstyle that you'll love.
