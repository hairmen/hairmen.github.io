How To Style Thick Curly Hair For Guys Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Sep 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thick curly hair can be a blessing and a curse for guys. On one hand, it adds volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and more unique. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style, especially if you're not sure what products to use or how to work with your natural curls. In this guide, we'll show you how to style thick curly hair for guys, from the basics of hair care to advanced styling techniques.

Hair Care

Before you can start styling your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for keeping your thick curly hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to moisturize and define your curls.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

By taking good care of your hair, you'll be able to achieve better styling results and keep your curls looking their best.

How to Style Thick Curly Hair for Guys

When it comes to styling thick curly hair, there are a few key products and techniques that can help you achieve the look you want:

  • Use a curl-enhancing product like a cream or gel to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Try diffusing your hair with a blow dryer on a low heat setting to enhance your curls and add volume.
  • Consider getting a layered haircut to help your curls fall more naturally and create a more textured look.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a messy, tousled look or a more defined, slicked-back style.

The key is to find the products and techniques that work best for your hair type and personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling thick curly hair for guys:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, such as a cream or gel.
  3. Use your hands to scrunch your hair and define your curls.
  4. Diffuse your hair with a blow dryer on a low heat setting, using a circular motion to enhance your curls.
  5. For a more defined look, use a comb or brush to slick back your hair.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Remember to experiment with different styles and techniques until you find the one that works best for you.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for styling thick curly hair:

  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • If you have trouble with frizz, try using a satin pillowcase to reduce friction on your hair while you sleep.
  • Don't be afraid to use a little bit of hair oil or pomade to add shine and definition to your curls.

By following these tips, you'll be able to achieve the perfect style for your thick curly hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling thick curly hair for guys:

What products should I use for thick curly hair?

You should use a curl-enhancing product like a cream or gel to define your curls and reduce frizz. You may also want to consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to moisturize your hair and add shine.

Should I blow dry my hair?

You can blow dry your hair on a low heat setting with a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls and add volume. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using high heat, which can damage your hair.

How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

By following these tips and techniques, you'll be able to style your thick curly hair with confidence and achieve the perfect look every time.
