How To Do Pretty Hairstyles For Short Hair

Written by Frank Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hair doesn't have to be boring! With the right techniques and tools, you can create a variety of pretty hairstyles that will make your short locks look amazing. In this article, we'll show you how to do pretty hairstyles for short hair, step by step. From braids and buns to curls and waves, you'll have plenty of options to choose from. So let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step to doing pretty hairstyles for short hair is to make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can use a dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair if needed. Then, gather the tools you'll need, such as bobby pins, hair elastics, and a curling iron or straightener.

Next, decide on the style you want to create. Look for inspiration online or in magazines, and choose a style that complements your face shape and personal style. Once you've chosen a style, follow the steps below to create it.

Step by Step

Braided Headband

This style is perfect for short hair and adds a feminine touch to any outfit.

  1. Part your hair to one side.
  2. Section off a small section of hair at the front of your head.
  3. Braid the section of hair and secure it with a small elastic.
  4. Wrap the braid around the top of your head, like a headband, and secure it with bobby pins.
  5. Fluff out the rest of your hair and you're done!

Messy Bun

A messy bun is a quick and easy style that looks great on short hair.

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to create a messy look.
  5. Spray with hairspray to keep the style in place.

Curling Iron Waves

If you have a curling iron, you can create beautiful waves in your short hair.

  1. Section off your hair into small sections.
  2. Curl each section away from your face, holding the curling iron vertically.
  3. Once all sections are curled, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more natural look.
  4. Spray with hairspray to keep the waves in place.

Tips

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or straightener.
  • Experiment with different accessories, such as headbands and hair clips, to add a pop of color or sparkle to your hairstyle.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles! Short hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways.

FAQ

What products should I use on my short hair?

Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type. If you have oily hair, use a clarifying shampoo to remove buildup. If you have dry hair, use a moisturizing conditioner to add hydration. You can also use a dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair.

How often should I wash my short hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less frequently, such as every 2-3 days. If you exercise or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more often to remove sweat and dirt.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can create pretty hairstyles for short hair that will make you feel confident and stylish. Try out different styles and have fun experimenting with your hair!
