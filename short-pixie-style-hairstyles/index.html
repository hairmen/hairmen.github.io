Short Pixie Style Hairstyles A Bold And Beautiful Choice

Written by Christine Sep 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance, edgy, and chic hairstyle, the short pixie style is the perfect choice. It's a bold statement that allows you to showcase your facial features and personality. In this blog post, we'll guide you through the process of getting the perfect short pixie style, tips for care, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Get the Perfect Short Pixie Style

The first step in getting the perfect short pixie style is to find a stylist who specializes in this type of cut. Your stylist will assess your face shape and help you choose the right length and style that suits your features. There are different variations of the short pixie style, such as asymmetrical, layered, and textured, so it's essential to communicate your preferences to your stylist.

Once you've decided on the style, the stylist will start by cutting the hair at the back of your head to create a base. They'll then work on the sides and top of your hair, creating layers and texture to add volume and dimension. Finally, they'll shape the hair around your ears and neck to create a polished finish.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Short Pixie Haircut

Styling your short pixie haircut is simple and easy. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help maintain the texture and prevent frizz.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a lightweight styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to add volume and hold.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to add volume. Use a diffuser attachment to define your curls or waves.
  4. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Pixie Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining and caring for your short pixie haircut:

  • Trim your hair every four to six weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently as they can damage your hair. Instead, opt for air-drying or a low-heat setting.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQs About Short Pixie Style Hairstyles

1. Who can pull off a short pixie style?

Anyone can pull off a short pixie style, regardless of their face shape or hair type. It's all about finding the right length and style that suits your features.

2. Will a short pixie style suit my age?

Short pixie styles can work for any age group, from teenagers to seniors. It's all about finding the right style that complements your features and personality.

3. Can I still style my short pixie hair in different ways?

Yes, you can still style your short pixie hair in different ways, such as adding accessories, braiding, or creating a messy look. Experiment and have fun with your hair!

Final Thoughts

Short pixie style hairstyles are a bold and beautiful choice that can make you feel confident and stylish. With the right stylist and care, you can rock this look effortlessly. Remember to communicate your preferences to your stylist and maintain your hair regularly to keep it looking its best.
