How To Get New Hair In Animal Crossing

Written by Robby Sep 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your character's same old hairstyle in Animal Crossing? Do you want to switch things up and try out a new look? Well, you're in luck because getting new hair in Animal Crossing is easy and fun! In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about getting new hair in Animal Crossing, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care.

How to Get New Hair in Animal Crossing

Getting new hair in Animal Crossing is a breeze. All you need to do is head over to the Able Sisters' shop, which is located in the center of your island. Once inside, you'll see a mirror where you can change your hairstyle. Simply walk up to the mirror, and select the "Change Hairstyle" option.

From there, you'll be able to choose from a variety of hairstyles, including short, medium, and long hair. You can also choose from different hair colors, such as blonde, brown, black, and more. Once you've selected your desired hairstyle and color, simply confirm your selection, and voila! You now have a brand new hairstyle.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting new hair in Animal Crossing:

Step 1:

Head over to the Able Sisters' shop on your island.

Step 2:

Walk up to the mirror and select "Change Hairstyle."

Step 3:

Choose your desired hairstyle and color.

Step 4:

Confirm your selection.

Step 5:

Enjoy your new hairstyle!

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting new hair in Animal Crossing:

  • Make sure to save up enough Bells to purchase a new hairstyle.
  • Don't be afraid to try out different hairstyles and colors.
  • Consider asking friends for their opinions on your new hairstyle.
  • Remember that you can always change your hairstyle again in the future.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting new hair in Animal Crossing:

Can I change my hair multiple times?

Yes, you can change your hair as many times as you want.

Do I need any special items to change my hair?

No, you don't need any special items to change your hair. Just head over to the Able Sisters' shop and use the mirror to change your hairstyle.

Can I change my hair color?

Yes, you can change your hair color in addition to your hairstyle.

Hair Care

Once you have your new hairstyle, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining your new hair:

  • Brush your hair regularly to prevent tangles.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using hair accessories to change up your look.

Now that you know how to get new hair in Animal Crossing, it's time to head over to the Able Sisters' shop and try out some new hairstyles! With so many options to choose from, you're sure to find a hairstyle that suits you.
