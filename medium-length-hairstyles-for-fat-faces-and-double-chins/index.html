Medium Length Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins

Are you tired of trying to hide your double chin or chubby cheeks? Do you want a hairstyle that flatters your facial features and makes you feel confident? Look no further than medium length hairstyles! In this article, we will discuss the best medium length hairstyles for fat faces and double chins, along with tips for styling and caring for your hair.

How to Choose the Right Medium Length Hairstyle

The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for a fat face and double chin is to choose a style that elongates and slims your face. Medium length hairstyles are ideal because they provide enough length to create a slimming effect without overwhelming your face. Here are some things to consider when choosing a medium length hairstyle:

Face Shape

Before selecting a hairstyle, determine your face shape. If you have a round face, opt for a cut that adds height to the crown and length to the face. If you have a square face, choose a style that softens the jawline. Oval faces can pull off almost any hairstyle, while heart-shaped faces look best with a side-swept bang that balances the forehead and chin.

Hair Texture

The texture of your hair will also play a role in determining the best medium length hairstyle for you. If you have thin hair, choose a style that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, opt for a layered cut that thins out the hair without losing length.

Personal Style

Ultimately, the best hairstyle for you is one that you feel comfortable and confident wearing. Consider your personal style and how you typically wear your hair. Do you prefer a sleek and polished look, or do you like to embrace your natural curls?

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Medium Length Hair

Once you have selected the perfect medium length hairstyle, it's time to learn how to style it. Follow these steps for a flawless look:

1. Prep Your Hair

Before styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.

2. Add Volume

To add volume to your hair, use a round brush to blow-dry your hair in sections. Start at the roots and pull the brush through to the ends, lifting the hair as you go.

3. Curl or Straighten

Depending on the style you want to achieve, use a curling iron or straightener to create loose waves or sleek, straight locks.

4. Finish with Hairspray

To keep your style in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. Choose a formula that provides hold without weighing down your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

In addition to styling your hair, it's important to take care of it to ensure it stays healthy and strong. Follow these tips for maintaining healthy hair:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that is gentle on your hair and scalp. Look for formulas that are free of sulfates, parabens, and other harsh chemicals.

2. Condition Regularly

To keep your hair moisturized and healthy, use a conditioner every time you wash your hair. Focus on the ends of your hair, which tend to be more dry and prone to damage.

3. Protect from Heat

When using hot styling tools, always use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. Additionally, avoid using high heat settings and limit your use of styling tools as much as possible.

4. Trim Regularly

To prevent split ends and breakage, get regular trims every 6-8 weeks. This will keep your hair looking healthy and prevent the need for drastic cuts down the road.

FAQs about Medium Length Hairstyles for Fat Faces and Double Chins

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a fat face and double chin?

A: Yes! Just make sure to choose a style that flatters your face shape. Side-swept bangs are a great option for balancing out a heart-shaped face, while blunt bangs can add height to a round face.

Q: Will layers make my face look wider?

A: Not necessarily. Layers can add texture and movement to your hair, which can actually create a slimming effect. Just make sure to choose the right type of layers for your hair type and face shape.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail or bun?

A: Absolutely! Just make sure to position your ponytail or bun in a way that elongates your face. Try a high ponytail or top knot to create the illusion of length.

Hair Care Products for Medium Length Hair

To keep your medium length hair looking its best, invest in high-quality hair care products. Here are some of our favorites:

1. Moroccanoil Treatment

This lightweight oil provides hydration and shine without weighing down your hair. Apply a few drops to damp hair before styling.

2. Bumble and Bumble Thickening Spray

If you have thin or fine hair, this spray is a game-changer. It provides volume and texture without leaving your hair feeling sticky or stiff.

3. Living Proof Perfect Hair Day Dry Shampoo

This dry shampoo absorbs oil and sweat, leaving your hair looking and smelling fresh. Plus, it adds volume and texture for a just-styled look.

4. Olaplex Hair Perfector No. 3

If you color your hair or use hot styling tools frequently, this treatment is a must-have. It repairs and strengthens damaged hair, leaving it soft and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a medium length hairstyle that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products until you find what works for you.
