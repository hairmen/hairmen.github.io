What Is The Best Haircut For Long Thick Hair

Written by Eveline Aug 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have long and thick hair, you know that it can be a blessing and a curse at the same time. While having voluminous hair is enviable, it can also be challenging to manage and style. One way to make your hair more manageable is by getting the right haircut. In this article, we will discuss what is the best haircut for long thick hair, and provide tips on how to care for your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.

Description

Long and thick hair is beautiful, but it can be difficult to manage without the right haircut. The best haircut for long thick hair should be able to reduce the weight of your hair and make it easier to style. It should also complement your face shape and enhance your features. A good haircut can transform your look and give you the confidence to rock your long and thick hair.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Long Thick Hair

Choosing the best haircut for long thick hair can be overwhelming, especially with so many options available. Here are some factors to consider when choosing the right haircut:

Face Shape

Your face shape plays a significant role in determining the best haircut for you. Some haircuts look better on certain face shapes than others. For example, a long layered haircut works well for a round face, while a blunt cut complements an oval face.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture also matters when choosing a haircut. If you have thick and coarse hair, a layered haircut can help to reduce the weight and make it more manageable. On the other hand, if you have fine hair, a blunt cut can create an illusion of thickness and volume.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also influence your choice of haircut. If you have an active lifestyle, you may want a low-maintenance haircut that requires minimal styling. On the other hand, if you have more time to style your hair, you can go for a more elaborate haircut.

Step by Step: How to Get the Best Haircut for Long Thick Hair

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a haircut, here are some steps to follow to get the best haircut for long thick hair:

Step 1: Research

Before you visit the salon, do some research on the different haircuts available for long thick hair. Look for inspiration from magazines, social media, and celebrity hairstyles. Take note of the haircuts that catch your eye and discuss them with your hairstylist.

Step 2: Consultation

Once you have an idea of what you want, book a consultation with your hairstylist. During the consultation, discuss your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle to determine the best haircut for you. Your hairstylist may also suggest some modifications to make the haircut more suitable for your hair.

Step 3: Maintenance

After getting your haircut, ask your hairstylist for tips on how to maintain it. Long and thick hair requires regular washing, conditioning, and deep conditioning to keep it healthy and shiny. Your hairstylist may also recommend some hair products to use to enhance your haircut.

Tips for Maintaining Long Thick Hair

Long and thick hair requires proper care to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for maintaining your long thick hair:

Regular Trimming

Regular trimming can help to prevent split ends and breakage. It also helps to maintain the shape of your haircut and keep it looking fresh. Aim to trim your hair every six to eight weeks.

Deep Conditioning

Deep conditioning can help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle. Use a deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.

Protective Styles

Protective styles can help to reduce the stress on your hair and prevent breakage. Styles such as braids, twists, and buns can help to protect your hair while also looking stylish.

Avoid Heat Damage

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling wands as much as possible. If you must use them, apply a heat protectant spray to minimize the damage.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for thick hair?

The best haircut for thick hair depends on your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. A layered haircut can help to reduce the weight of your hair and make it more manageable. A blunt cut can create an illusion of thickness and volume for fine hair.

How often should I wash my long thick hair?

Long and thick hair should be washed every two to three days to prevent it from becoming greasy and weighed down. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Can I color my long thick hair?

Yes, you can color your long thick hair. However, coloring can damage your hair if not done properly. It's best to consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best color and technique for your hair.

Hair Care for Long Thick Hair

Long and thick hair requires proper care to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for long thick hair:

Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush. A brush can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid wearing tight hairstyles such as ponytails and braids as they can pull on your hair and cause breakage. Opt for looser hairstyles that don't put too much stress on your hair.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun's UV rays can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to shield your hair from the sun.

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help to promote healthy hair growth. Eat foods rich in vitamins and minerals such as fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.

Conclusion

Long and thick hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right haircut and proper care, you can transform your hair into a beautiful and manageable mane. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing the best haircut for you. Maintain your hair with regular trimming, deep conditioning, and protective styles. Follow these tips, and you'll have healthy and shiny long thick hair that turns heads.
