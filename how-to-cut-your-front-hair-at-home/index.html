How To Cut Your Front Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 18, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking to save some money on haircuts or just want to try something new, cutting your front hair at home can be a great option. However, it can be intimidating to pick up a pair of scissors and start cutting away. That's why we've put together this step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect front hair cut at home.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Cut Your Front Hair at Home
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting Your Front Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care After Cutting Your Front Hair

How to Cut Your Front Hair at Home

Before you start cutting your front hair at home, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

  • Make sure you have the right tools, including a pair of hair cutting scissors and a comb.
  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Take your time and go slowly. You can always take more hair off, but you can't put it back on.
  • Remember that your hair will look shorter when it's wet, so don't cut too much off at once.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you're ready to start cutting your front hair, let's get started:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a section of hair from the front, about the width of your forehead.
  3. Hold the section of hair between your index and middle fingers, and comb it straight down.
  4. Use your hair cutting scissors to cut the hair straight across, just below the desired length.
  5. Repeat steps 2-4 with the other side of your hair.
  6. Check to make sure that both sides are even.
  7. If one side is longer than the other, repeat steps 2-4 on the longer side until they are even.
  8. If you want to add layers to your front hair, take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle, starting at the desired length and working your way down.
  9. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you've achieved the desired amount of layers.
  10. When you're finished, brush your hair out and style it as desired.

Tips for Cutting Your Front Hair

Here are a few additional tips to help you achieve the perfect front hair cut:

  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you go.
  • If you're nervous about cutting too much off, start with a little and work your way up.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're having trouble.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your hair can be tiring!

FAQs

Can I cut my front hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can! Just make sure to cut your hair when it's dry so you can see exactly how much you're cutting off.

How often should I cut my front hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your desired length. Generally, cutting your front hair every 6-8 weeks is a good rule of thumb.

Do I need to use special scissors to cut my front hair?

While you don't need to use special scissors, it's a good idea to invest in a pair of hair cutting scissors. They are sharper and more precise than regular scissors, which will help you achieve a more even cut.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Front Hair

Once you've finished cutting your front hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain your new cut:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a heat protectant if you plan on using hot tools on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain your desired length.

With these tips and tricks, you should be able to achieve the perfect front hair cut at home. Happy cutting!
