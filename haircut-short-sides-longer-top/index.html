Haircut Short Sides Longer Top The Trendy And Low Maintenance Hairstyle

Are you looking for a hairstyle that is trendy and low maintenance? The haircut short sides longer top might just be the perfect hairstyle for you. In this post, we will discuss everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, tips to maintain it, and answers to frequently asked questions.

  • How to Get the Haircut Short Sides Longer Top

Description

The haircut short sides longer top is a versatile hairstyle that combines short hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. This hairstyle has been popular for years and continues to be a trendy choice for men of all ages. The short sides create a clean and fresh look, while the longer top can be styled in different ways, such as slicked back, messy, or side-swept.

This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low maintenance look that is easy to style and maintain. It is also suitable for different face shapes and hair types, making it a versatile option for any man.

How to Get the Haircut Short Sides Longer Top

To get the haircut short sides longer top, you need to visit a professional hairstylist. It is essential to choose a hairstylist who is experienced in creating this type of hairstyle. You can bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to show your hairstylist as a reference. This will help them understand the haircut you are looking for.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get the haircut short sides longer top:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Visit a professional hairstylist.
  3. Show them a picture of the hairstyle you want.
  4. Ask for a short fade or undercut on the sides.
  5. Ask for the top to be left longer.
  6. Style the top according to your preference.
  7. Maintain the hairstyle regularly.

Tips to Maintain the Haircut Short Sides Longer Top

Here are some tips to maintain the haircut short sides longer top:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Trim the sides regularly to maintain the short length.
  • Style the top using a hair product that suits your hair type and style.
  • Use a hairdryer to style the hair and create volume.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the shape and style of the haircut.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the haircut short sides longer top:

1. Is the haircut short sides longer top suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this hairstyle is suitable for different hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

2. How often should I visit my hairstylist to maintain the haircut short sides longer top?

You should visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the haircut.

3. Can I style the top of the haircut short sides longer top in different ways?

Yes, you can style the top of the haircut short sides longer top in different ways, such as slicked back, messy, or side-swept.

Hair Care for the Haircut Short Sides Longer Top

Here are some hair care tips for the haircut short sides longer top:

  • Wash your hair regularly.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much hair product.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to heat, such as hairdryers or straighteners.
  • Trim the sides regularly to maintain the short length.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the shape and style of the haircut.

In conclusion, the haircut short sides longer top is a trendy and low maintenance hairstyle that is suitable for different face shapes and hair types. By following the tips and advice in this post, you can get the perfect haircut short sides longer top and maintain it for a long time.
