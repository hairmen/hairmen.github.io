How To Curl Hair With A Small Flat Iron A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking to add some curls to your hair but don't have a large curling iron, don't worry! With a small flat iron, you can create beautiful and bouncy curls in just a few simple steps. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of how to curl hair with a small flat iron, including some tips and tricks to make the process easier and more effective.

Table of Contents

  1. What You'll Need
  2. How to Prep Your Hair
  3. How to Curl Your Hair
  4. Tips for Better Results
  5. FAQs
  6. Conclusion

What You'll Need

Before you begin, make sure you have the following items:

  • A small flat iron (preferably one with rounded edges)
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray
  • Sectioning clips (optional)

With these tools, you'll be able to create beautiful curls that last for hours.

How to Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling your hair, it's important to make sure it's prepped and ready. Here's what to do:

  1. Wash and dry your hair completely. Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  3. Section your hair into 1-2 inch sections using sectioning clips (if you have them). This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

How to Curl Your Hair

Now it's time to start curling your hair! Follow these steps:

  1. Take a small section of hair (about 1-2 inches) and clamp the flat iron near the roots.
  2. Twist the flat iron away from your face (or towards your face if you want the curls to go in the opposite direction), making sure to keep the hair taut.
  3. Slowly move the flat iron down the length of the hair, continuing to twist it as you go.
  4. Release the hair from the flat iron and let it cool for a few seconds before moving on to the next section.
  5. Repeat this process for all sections of hair, making sure to curl in the same direction for a more uniform look.

When you're finished, you should have beautiful, bouncy curls!

Tips for Better Results

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when curling your hair with a small flat iron:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.
  • Don't use sections of hair that are too large or too small. Aim for sections that are about 1-2 inches in width.
  • Make sure to twist the flat iron as you move it down the length of your hair. This will help create more defined curls.
  • Hold each section of hair in the flat iron for about 5-10 seconds before releasing it for best results.
  • For a more natural look, curl your hair in different directions (away from your face, towards your face, etc.).

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling hair with a small flat iron:

Can you curl short hair with a small flat iron?

Yes, you can! Just make sure to use smaller sections of hair to create tighter curls.

Can you curl long hair with a small flat iron?

Yes, you can! Just make sure to use smaller sections of hair and take your time to ensure that each section is properly curled.

Can you use a small flat iron to straighten hair?

Yes, you can! Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray and take your time to ensure that each section is properly straightened.

Conclusion

With a small flat iron, you can create beautiful curls in just a few simple steps. By following the tips and tricks outlined in this guide, you'll be able to get the best results possible. So go ahead and give it a try – you might just fall in love with your new curly look!
