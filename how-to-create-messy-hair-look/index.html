How To Create Messy Hair Look A Relaxed Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a way to add an effortless and carefree touch to your hairstyle, the messy hair look is definitely worth trying. Whether you're going for a beachy vibe, a rocker chic style or just a casual and relaxed look, creating a messy hairstyle is easier than you think. In this post, we'll guide you through the process of creating a messy hair look, step by step. We'll also give you tips on how to maintain your messy hairstyle and answer some frequently asked questions about this trendy hair trend.

  1. How to Create Messy Hair Look
  2. Step by Step Guide

How to Create Messy Hair Look

To create a messy hair look, you'll need to start by prepping your hair with some products that add texture and volume. Here are some products you can use:

  • Dry Shampoo: spritz some dry shampoo on your roots and massage it in to add volume and texture.
  • Hair Powder: this product adds grip and texture to your hair, making it easier to style.
  • Sea Salt Spray: this product mimics the texture and volume of beachy hair, giving you a relaxed and effortless look.

Once you've prepped your hair, you can start creating your messy hair look. Here are some techniques you can use:

  • Curl your hair with a curling wand or iron, then tousle your curls with your fingers to create a messy and undone look.
  • Create loose braids or twists, then gently pull them apart to create a messy and voluminous look.
  • Tease your hair at the roots to add volume and texture, then gently comb through the teased sections to create a messy and tousled look.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to create a messy hair look:

  1. Prep your hair with some texture and volume-enhancing products, such as dry shampoo or hair powder.
  2. Section your hair and curl it with a curling wand or iron.
  3. Tousle your curls with your fingers to create a messy and undone look.
  4. Tease your hair at the roots to add volume and texture.
  5. Gently comb through the teased sections to create a messy and tousled look.
  6. Create loose braids or twists, then gently pull them apart to add volume and texture.
  7. Finish off with some hairspray to keep your messy hairstyle in place.

Tips for Creating and Maintaining Messy Hair

Here are some tips to help you create and maintain your messy hair look:

  • Use the right products: choose products that add texture and grip to your hair, such as dry shampoo, hair powder, or sea salt spray.
  • Don't overdo it: the key to creating a messy hair look is to keep it natural and effortless. Avoid using too much product or styling your hair too much.
  • Use your fingers: instead of using a brush or comb, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy and undone look.
  • Refresh your hair: if your hair starts to lose its texture and volume throughout the day, refresh it with some dry shampoo or hairspray.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about creating a messy hair look:

  • Can I create a messy hair look on short hair? Yes, you can create a messy hair look on short hair. Use a texturizing product, such as hair powder, to add volume and texture to your hair, then style it with your fingers to create a messy and tousled look.
  • Do I need to use heat styling tools to create a messy hair look? No, you don't need to use heat styling tools to create a messy hair look. You can create a messy and undone look by simply using your fingers to tousle your hair and add texture.
  • How can I maintain my messy hair look? To maintain your messy hair look, avoid using too much product or brushing or combing your hair too much. If your hair starts to lose its texture and volume throughout the day, refresh it with some dry shampoo or hairspray.

Hair Care for Messy Hair

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for hair care for messy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

Now that you know how to create a messy hair look, it's time to give it a try. Whether you're going for a casual and effortless look or a more edgy and rocker chic style, a messy hairstyle is a great way to add some personality and flair to your look. Just remember to use the right products, style your hair with your fingers, and keep it natural and effortless. Happy styling!
