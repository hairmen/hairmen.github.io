How to Give the Top of My Hair Volume: Tips and Tricks

Are you struggling with limp and flat hair? Do you want to add volume to the top of your hair to achieve a fuller and more voluminous look? You’ve come to the right place! In this article, we’ll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to give the top of your hair volume. Let’s get started!

How to Add Volume to the Top of Your Hair

Adding volume to the top of your hair is not as difficult as it may seem. With the right techniques and products, you can achieve a fuller and more voluminous look in no time. Here’s how:

Step 1: Start with a Good Haircut

The first step to achieving voluminous hair is to start with a good haircut. Ask your hairstylist to add layers to your hair, especially around the crown area. Layers will help create movement and volume in your hair, making it look fuller and thicker.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make a huge difference in adding volume to your hair. Look for volumizing shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed to add volume to your hair. You can also use volumizing mousses, sprays, and powders to give your hair an extra boost of volume.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow drying your hair upside down is a great way to add volume to the top of your hair. Start by flipping your hair upside down and blow drying it in that position. Use a round brush to lift the hair at the roots and create volume. Once your hair is dry, flip it back and style as desired.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair is another great way to add volume to the top of your hair. Using a teasing comb, gently backcomb your hair at the roots to create volume. Be careful not to tease too much, as this can damage your hair and create knots and tangles.

Step 5: Use Velcro Rollers

Velcro rollers are a great tool for adding volume to your hair. Start by blow drying your hair until it’s about 80% dry. Then, section your hair and roll each section onto a velcro roller. Leave the rollers in for about 10-15 minutes, then remove and style as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Voluminous Hair

Now that you’ve achieved voluminous hair, it’s important to take care of it to maintain its fullness. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh and full.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it look limp.

Frequently Asked Questions

What causes flat hair?

Flat hair can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, hormonal changes, over-washing your hair, using heavy products, and not using the right styling techniques.

Can I add volume to my hair without using heat?

Yes! You can add volume to your hair by using velcro rollers, teasing your hair, or using a volumizing mousse or powder.

What is the best product for adding volume to hair?

The best product for adding volume to hair is a volumizing mousse or spray. Look for products that are specifically designed to add volume to your hair.

Hair Care for Voluminous Hair

Now that you know how to add volume to your hair, it’s important to take care of it to maintain its fullness. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won’t weigh down your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look flat.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it look limp.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve voluminous hair in no time. Remember to use the right products and techniques, and take care of your hair to maintain its fullness. Good luck!
