Short Hair For Long Faces Over 40 How To Rock The Look

Written by Eveline Jul 22, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you over 40 with a long face and looking for a fresh new hairstyle? Look no further than short hair! Short haircuts are versatile, easy to maintain, and can make you look younger and more stylish. In this article, we’ll give you tips and step-by-step instructions on how to rock short hair for long faces over 40.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts work well with long faces because they can balance out the length of your face and draw attention to your features. A pixie cut or a bob can be a great option, but it’s important to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and fits your personal style.

How to

Before you make the chop, it’s important to consider your face shape and lifestyle. Look for inspiration online or in magazines, and make an appointment with a trusted stylist who can help you choose a haircut that works for you.

Choose the Right Cut

There are many short hairstyles that work well with long faces. Here are some options:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob
  • Layered haircut
  • Asymmetrical cut

Avoid hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head, as this can elongate your face even more.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Short haircuts require less maintenance than longer hair, but they still need some upkeep. Make sure you choose a style that fits your lifestyle and hair type. If you have fine hair, for example, a pixie cut may be a better option than a bob.

Step by Step

Once you’ve chosen your new hairstyle, follow these steps to maintain your short hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently, then use a blow dryer to style it.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to add texture and hold.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add some waves or curls.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

  • Invest in a good quality blow dryer and flat iron to make styling easier.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Try different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut.
  • Experiment with different parts and styles to keep your look fresh.

FAQ

Can short hair make you look younger?

Yes, short hair can make you look younger by lifting your features and giving you a more modern, edgy look.

Can short hair be feminine?

Absolutely! Short hair can be very feminine and chic, especially when styled with soft waves or curls.

Will short hair work for my face shape?

Short hair can work for any face shape, but it’s important to choose a cut that flatters your features and balances your face shape.

Hair Care

Short hair requires less maintenance than longer hair, but it still needs some TLC. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

With the right cut and styling, short hair can be a game-changer for women over 40 with long faces. Follow these tips and step-by-step instructions to rock the look and feel confident and stylish.
