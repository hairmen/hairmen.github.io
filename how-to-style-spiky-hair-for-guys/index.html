How To Style Spiky Hair For Guys The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Jul 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Spiky hair is a classic hairstyle that has been popular for decades. It's a versatile look that can be dressed up or down, making it perfect for any occasion. However, styling spiky hair can be tricky, especially if you're new to the hairstyle. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style spiky hair for guys, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Spiky Hair for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Spiky Hair

How to Style Spiky Hair for Guys

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, here's a quick overview of how to style spiky hair:

  1. Choose the right haircut.
  2. Wash and dry your hair.
  3. Apply a styling product.
  4. Spike your hair using your fingers or a comb.
  5. Finish with hairspray.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now let's go through each step in more detail:

1. Choose the right haircut.

The first step in styling spiky hair is to choose the right haircut. You'll want a cut that's short on the sides and longer on top, with enough length to spike up. Some popular spiky haircuts include the textured crop, the messy spiky hairstyle, and the classic spiky hairstyle.

2. Wash and dry your hair.

Before styling your hair, you'll need to wash and dry it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type, then towel-dry your hair until it's damp. Avoid using a blow dryer, as this can make your hair look flat and lifeless.

3. Apply a styling product.

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. A pomade or wax works well for spiky hair, as it provides hold without making your hair look greasy. Rub the product between your palms, then work it through your hair, focusing on the top and front sections.

4. Spike your hair using your fingers or a comb.

Now it's time to spike your hair. Use your fingers or a comb to create spikes, starting at the front and working your way back. For a messy look, use your fingers to create random spikes. For a more structured look, use a comb to create even spikes.

5. Finish with hairspray.

To keep your spiky hairstyle in place, finish with a spritz of hairspray. Hold the can about 6 inches away from your head and spray evenly over your hair. Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can make your hair look crunchy and unnatural.

Tips for Styling Spiky Hair

Here are some additional tips that will help you style spiky hair like a pro:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match products to achieve your desired look.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to create texture and volume in your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair on a cool setting to add volume and texture.
  • Use a matte hairspray for a natural-looking hold.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about styling spiky hair:

What is the best product for spiky hair?

The best product for spiky hair is a pomade or wax. These products provide a strong hold without making your hair look greasy or crunchy.

Can I style spiky hair without product?

You can style spiky hair without product, but it won't hold as well or look as defined. If you want a natural look, try using a lightweight styling cream instead of a heavier product.

How often should I wash spiky hair?

You should wash spiky hair every other day or every two days to avoid over-drying your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Hair Care Tips for Spiky Hair

Here are some hair care tips that will keep your spiky hairstyle looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style spiky hair like a pro in no time. Remember to choose the right haircut, use the right styling products, and take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
