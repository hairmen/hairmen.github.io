The Bob

The bob is a classic hairstyle that looks great on anyone with medium length hair and a long face. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your individual style. Hereâs how to achieve the perfect bob:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, left, right, and back.
  3. Starting at the back, use a straightener to create a sleek, straight look.
  4. Once you have straightened all of your hair, use a round brush to add volume to the ends.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

The bob is a low-maintenance hairstyle that is perfect for busy women who want to look chic and put together.

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Updo

An updo is a sophisticated hairstyle that is perfect for special occasions. It is a great way to show off your medium length hair and long face. Hereâs how to achieve the perfect updo:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, left, right, and back.
  3. Create a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a long face?

The best hairstyle for a long face is one that adds width and volume to the sides of your face. This can be achieved with a layered haircut, bangs, or an updo.

Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a long face?

A middle part can be flattering on a long face if it is paired with a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face, such as a layered haircut or beachy waves.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and free of split ends.
