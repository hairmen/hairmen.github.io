Medium Long Hairstyles For Thick Hair Male

Written by Daniel Jun 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you are looking for a new hairstyle that will suit your thick hair, then medium long hairstyles might be the perfect option for you. These hairstyles are versatile, easy to maintain, and can work for any occasion. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve medium long hairstyles for thick hair male, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Medium long hairstyles are usually considered to be hairstyles that fall between the chin and the shoulders. These hairstyles work well for men with thick hair as they add volume and texture to the hair. The length of the hair can be adjusted to suit your personal style and preferences.

How to

To achieve medium long hairstyles for thick hair male, you will need to have your hair cut to the desired length. You can visit a hairstylist or barber to have this done, or you can do it yourself if you are confident in your skills. Once you have the right length, you can style your hair in a variety of ways.

Styles

Some of the most popular medium long hairstyles for thick hair male include:

  • The messy look
  • The slicked-back look
  • The side-swept look
  • The quiff
  • The pompadour

Step by step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the messy look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair (such as a wax, pomade, or paste).
  3. Ruffle your hair with your fingers to create a messy, textured look.
  4. Use a comb or brush to shape your hair into the desired style.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling medium long hairstyles for thick hair male:

  • Use the right styling products for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist or barber for advice.
  • Keep your hair healthy by using a good quality shampoo and conditioner.

FAQ

Q: Will medium long hairstyles work for all face shapes?

A: Yes, medium long hairstyles can work for all face shapes. However, you may need to adjust the length and style to suit your specific face shape.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain your hairstyle. As a general rule, you should get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, thick hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking and feeling its best:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools (such as blow dryers and straighteners) too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to support healthy hair growth.

With these tips and ideas, you can easily achieve medium long hairstyles for thick hair male that will make you look and feel great. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
