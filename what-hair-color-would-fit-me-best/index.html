What Hair Color Would Fit Me Best

Written by Oliver Sep 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Choosing the right hair color that would suit you best can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose one that would complement your skin tone, eye color, and personality. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to find the perfect hair color for you.

Description

The first step in finding the right hair color for you is to determine your skin tone. There are three main types of skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral. Warm skin tones have yellow, peach, or golden undertones, while cool skin tones have pink, red, or blue undertones. Neutral skin tones have a mix of warm and cool undertones.

Once you have determined your skin tone, you can choose a hair color that would complement it. If you have warm skin tones, hair colors with warm undertones like honey blonde, caramel, or copper would suit you best. On the other hand, if you have cool skin tones, hair colors with cool undertones like ash blonde, platinum, or espresso would complement your skin.

How To

One way to determine your skin tone is to look at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If your veins appear green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell if your veins are blue or green, you have neutral undertones.

Another way to determine your skin tone is to look at the jewelry you wear. If you look best in silver jewelry, you have cool undertones. If you look best in gold jewelry, you have warm undertones. If you can wear both silver and gold jewelry, you have neutral undertones.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your skin tone

Step 2: Choose a hair color with undertones that complement your skin tone

Step 3: Consider your eye color and personality in choosing a hair color

Step 4: Consult with a professional hair colorist before making a drastic change

Tips

Consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance you are willing to do before choosing a hair color. Some hair colors require more upkeep than others.

Don't be afraid to try something new. Hair color can always be changed if it doesn't suit you.

FAQ

What hair color would suit me if I have green eyes?

If you have green eyes, hair colors with warm undertones like honey blonde, copper, or auburn would suit you best.

Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

If you have sensitive skin, it is best to consult with a professional hair colorist before dyeing your hair. They can recommend hair color brands that are gentle on the skin.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain its color and health. Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for color-treated hair. Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and blow dryers as they can cause damage to your hair. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with UV protection.

Choosing the right hair color can be a fun and exciting experience. With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hair color that would suit you best. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its color and health.
