How To Shave Sides Of Head Woman A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Oct 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you ready to try a bold new look and shave the sides of your head? Shaved hairstyles for women have been trending for a while, and it's no surprise why. They're edgy, low-maintenance, and perfect for summer. But before you grab a razor and start hacking away, there are a few things you need to know. In this guide, we'll show you how to shave the sides of your head like a pro.

How to Shave Sides of Head Woman: What You'll Need

Before we get started, here's a list of things you'll need:

  • A good quality razor
  • Clippers or scissors
  • Combs
  • Shaving cream or gel
  • A mirror (or two)
  • Patience and a steady hand

Step-by-Step Guide to Shaving the Sides of Your Head

Step 1: Decide on Your Hair Length and Style

The first step is to decide on the length and style of your hair. Do you want a buzz cut or a longer shaved style? Use clippers or scissors to trim the hair on the sides to the desired length.

Step 2: Section Off the Hair

Use a comb to section off the hair on top of your head. This will keep it out of the way while you shave the sides.

Step 3: Apply Shaving Cream or Gel

Apply shaving cream or gel to the sides of your head. This will help the razor glide smoothly over your skin and prevent irritation.

Step 4: Start Shaving

Using a good quality razor, start shaving the sides of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using short strokes. Be sure to rinse the razor frequently to prevent clogging.

Step 5: Use a Mirror

Use a mirror (or two) to check your progress. Make sure you're shaving evenly and not leaving any patches of hair.

Step 6: Rinse and Repeat

Rinse your razor frequently and repeat the shaving process until you've removed all the hair from the sides of your head.

Step 7: Rinse and Moisturize

Rinse your head with cool water to remove any stray hairs and shaving cream. Apply a moisturizing lotion or oil to soothe your skin.

Tips for Shaving the Sides of Your Head

Here are a few tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with a longer guard on your clippers or scissors and work your way down to your desired length.
  • Use a steady hand and take your time. Shaving too quickly can result in cuts and nicks.
  • Invest in a good quality razor to prevent irritation and ingrown hairs.
  • Don't forget to rinse your razor frequently to prevent clogging.
  • Consider enlisting the help of a friend or professional stylist for hard-to-reach areas.

FAQ: Shaving the Sides of Your Head

Is it hard to shave the sides of your head?

Shaving the sides of your head can be challenging, especially if you're doing it for the first time. But with patience, practice, and a steady hand, you can achieve a great look.

Will shaving the sides of my head make my hair grow back thicker?

No, shaving your hair doesn't affect its texture or thickness.

How often do I need to shave the sides of my head?

How often you need to shave the sides of your head will depend on how quickly your hair grows. On average, you may need to shave every two to four weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Shaved Hair

Now that you've shaved the sides of your head, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Moisturize your scalp daily to prevent dryness and flakiness.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your shaved hair to prevent damage.
  • Protect your scalp from the sun with a hat or scarf.
  • Consider using a specialized shampoo and conditioner for shaved hair.

By following these tips, you'll be able to maintain your shaved hair and rock your new look with confidence.
