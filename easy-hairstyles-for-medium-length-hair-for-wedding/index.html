Easy Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair For Wedding A Guide To Looking Beautiful Without Breaking A Sweat

Written by Daniel Nov 02, 2023 · 5 min read

Weddings can be overwhelming, especially when it comes to the preparation. One of the things that most brides-to-be worry about is their hair. But don't fret, we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll be sharing easy hairstyles for medium length hair for wedding that you can do yourself without having to spend hours in the salon.

Description

Medium length hair can be a challenge to style, especially for special occasions like weddings. But with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and elegant look that will make you stand out on your big day.

How To

The key to achieving easy hairstyles for medium length hair for wedding is to keep it simple. You don't need to have complex braids or intricate updos to look beautiful. Here are some tips on how to create easy hairstyles for medium length hair for wedding:

1. Curl Your Hair

Curling your hair can add volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to style. You can use a curling iron or a curling wand to achieve loose curls or tight ringlets. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up, half-down hairstyle is a classic look that is perfect for weddings. It's easy to do and can be customized to suit your personal style. You can add braids, twists, or accessories to make it more unique.

3. Low Bun

A low bun is a chic and elegant hairstyle that is perfect for medium length hair. It's easy to do and can be dressed up or down depending on your wedding theme. You can add a flower crown or a sparkly hair accessory to make it more festive.

4. Side Swept

A side-swept hairstyle is a romantic and feminine look that is perfect for weddings. It's easy to do and can be achieved by simply sweeping your hair to one side and securing it with bobby pins. You can add curls or waves to make it more textured.

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to create easy hairstyles for medium length hair for wedding:

1. Prep Your Hair

Wash and condition your hair as usual. Apply a heat protectant spray before you start curling your hair.

2. Curl Your Hair

Use a curling iron or a curling wand to curl your hair. Start from the bottom and work your way up. Hold the curling iron for a few seconds and release. Repeat until you've curled all of your hair.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

Take a section of hair from the top of your head and secure it with a hair tie. Leave the rest of your hair down. You can add braids, twists, or accessories to the top section to make it more unique.

4. Low Bun

Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it into a bun. Secure it with bobby pins. You can leave some strands of hair loose to frame your face.

5. Side Swept

Sweep your hair to one side and secure it with bobby pins. You can add curls or waves to make it more textured.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating easy hairstyles for medium length hair for wedding:

1. Use the Right Products

Use products that are suitable for your hair type. For example, if you have fine hair, use volumizing products to add thickness and volume.

2. Don't Overdo It

Keep it simple and don't overdo it. You want to look elegant and sophisticated, not like you're trying too hard.

3. Practice

Practice your chosen hairstyle before the wedding day to avoid any mishaps. You want to make sure that you're comfortable with the style and that it will last throughout the day.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy hairstyles for medium length hair for wedding:

1. Can I do my own hair for my wedding?

Yes, you can. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and elegant hairstyle without having to spend hours in the salon.

2. What hairstyles are best for medium length hair?

Half-up, half-down, low buns, and side-swept hairstyles are all great options for medium length hair.

3. How can I make my hairstyle last all day?

Use hairspray and bobby pins to secure your hairstyle in place. You can also use a hair tie to tie your hair in a low ponytail before you start styling to add extra hold.

Hair Care

Don't forget to take care of your hair leading up to your wedding day. Make sure to wash and condition regularly and use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

There you have it - easy hairstyles for medium length hair for wedding that you can do yourself. Remember to keep it simple, practice beforehand, and most importantly, have fun!
