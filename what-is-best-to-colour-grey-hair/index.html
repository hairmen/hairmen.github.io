What Is Best To Colour Grey Hair In 2023

Written by Luffy Jul 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of seeing those pesky grey hairs? Do you wish to get rid of them and give your hair a new look? Colouring your grey hair is the perfect solution for you! In this article, we will discuss what is the best way to colour grey hair, step by step process, tips, FAQ and hair care. Let's get started!

  • Description
  • How to Colour Grey Hair
  • Step by Step Process
  • Tips for Colouring Grey Hair

Description

Gone are the days when grey hair was seen as a sign of ageing. Nowadays, many people choose to embrace their grey hair, but others prefer to colour it. Colouring grey hair can give you a new look, boost your confidence, and make you feel younger. However, it is essential to choose the right hair colour to ensure that it looks natural and complements your skin tone.

How to Colour Grey Hair?

The best way to colour grey hair is to use a permanent hair dye. This type of hair dye contains ammonia and peroxide, which can penetrate the hair shaft and change the hair's colour permanently. When choosing a hair dye, it is essential to consider your natural hair colour and skin tone. If you have warm undertones, choose a warm hair colour, and if you have cool undertones, choose a cool hair colour.

Step by Step Process

Here is the step-by-step process for colouring grey hair:

  1. Choose a hair dye that complements your natural hair colour and skin tone.
  2. Read the instructions carefully and perform a patch test to check for any allergic reactions.
  3. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any build-up.
  4. Section your hair and apply the hair dye to your roots first.
  5. Spread the hair dye evenly throughout your hair, making sure to cover all the grey strands.
  6. Leave the hair dye on for the recommended time and rinse it off with lukewarm water.
  7. Apply the conditioner that comes with the hair dye to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes.
  8. Rinse off the conditioner with cold water to seal the hair cuticles and prevent colour fading.

Tips for Colouring Grey Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when colouring grey hair:

  • Choose a hair dye that is specifically formulated for grey hair.
  • Use a hair dye with a low ammonia content to reduce damage to your hair.
  • Don't wash your hair for at least 24 hours before colouring it to ensure that the natural oils in your hair protect your scalp.
  • Apply petroleum jelly around your hairline to prevent the hair dye from staining your skin.
  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your hair's colour and prevent fading.

FAQ

1. Can I colour my grey hair at home?

Yes, you can colour your grey hair at home using a permanent hair dye. However, it is essential to follow the instructions carefully and perform a patch test to check for any allergic reactions.

2. How often should I colour my grey hair?

It depends on the rate at which your hair grows and how quickly the grey roots appear. Usually, you should colour your grey hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the colour.

3. Will colouring my grey hair damage it?

Colouring your hair can cause some damage, but using a low-ammonia hair dye and following a proper hair care routine can minimize the damage and keep your hair healthy and strong.

Hair Care After Colouring Grey Hair

After colouring your grey hair, it is essential to take care of it to maintain the colour and prevent damage. Here are some tips for hair care after colouring your grey hair:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to moisturize and nourish your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Colouring your grey hair can give you a new look and boost your confidence. By following the tips mentioned in this article, you can achieve the best results and keep your hair healthy and strong. So, go ahead and give your hair a new lease of life!
