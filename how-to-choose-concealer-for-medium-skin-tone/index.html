How To Choose Concealer For Medium Skin Tone Tips And Faqs

Written by Petter Oct 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the right concealer for your medium skin tone? Fear not, as we have compiled a guide on how to choose the perfect concealer that will complement and enhance your natural skin tone. Here are some tips, steps, and FAQs to help you in your search for the right concealer for your skin tone.

How to Choose Concealer for Medium Skin Tone

Choosing the right concealer for your skin tone can be challenging, but it is not impossible. Here are some tips that you can follow to help you in your search for the perfect concealer:

1. Determine your skin undertone

Knowing your skin undertone is crucial in choosing the right concealer. If you have a warm undertone, go for a concealer with yellow or golden undertones. For cool undertones, choose a concealer with pink or peach undertones. If you have a neutral undertone, opt for a concealer with a mix of yellow and pink undertones.

2. Test it on your skin

Always test concealer on your skin before purchasing it. Apply a small amount of concealer on your jawline and blend it into your skin. If the concealer disappears into your skin, then it's a perfect match.

3. Consider your skin concerns

If you have dark circles under your eyes, opt for a concealer with peach or orange undertones to neutralize the blue or purple hues. If you have acne scars or blemishes, choose a concealer with a high coverage formula.

4. Choose the right formula

There are different types of concealer formulas available in the market, such as liquid, cream, stick, and powder. Choose a formula that suits your skin type and concerns. For example, if you have dry skin, go for a hydrating liquid concealer. If you have oily skin, choose a matte formula.

Step by Step Guide on How to Apply Concealer

Once you have found the perfect concealer for your skin tone, here's how you can apply it:

1. Prep your skin

Before applying concealer, cleanse and moisturize your skin. This will ensure that the concealer goes on smoothly and lasts longer.

2. Apply foundation

Apply a light layer of foundation all over your face. This will even out your skin tone and provide a base for the concealer to adhere to.

3. Apply concealer

Use your fingers, a brush, or a beauty blender to apply the concealer to your problem areas, such as dark circles, blemishes, or scars. Blend the concealer into your skin using gentle patting motions.

4. Set with powder

To ensure that the concealer stays in place all day, set it with a translucent powder. This will also prevent creasing and smudging.

Tips for Choosing Concealer for Medium Skin Tone

Here are some additional tips that you can follow to help you in your search for the perfect concealer:

1. Don't go too light

A common mistake that people make when choosing concealer is going too light. This can make your under-eye area look ashy or gray. Always choose a concealer that is one or two shades lighter than your skin tone.

2. Invest in a color corrector

If you have severe discoloration or dark circles, invest in a color corrector. Color correctors come in shades of green, peach, and purple, and they can neutralize and cancel out any discoloration on your skin.

3. Don't forget to blend

Blending is key when it comes to applying concealer. Make sure that you blend the concealer into your skin seamlessly for a natural-looking finish.

FAQs

1. Can I use the same concealer for my under-eye area and blemishes?

It's best to use separate concealers for your under-eye area and blemishes. Under-eye concealers should be lightweight and hydrating, while blemish concealers should have a high coverage formula.

2. How often should I replace my concealer?

Concealers should be replaced every six to twelve months. Using expired products can lead to skin irritation and breakouts.

3. Can I use concealer without foundation?

Yes, you can use concealer without foundation. However, applying foundation first can provide a base for the concealer to adhere to and even out your skin tone.


In conclusion, finding the perfect concealer for medium skin tone can be challenging but not impossible. Follow these tips and steps, and don't be afraid to experiment with different formulas and shades until you find the perfect match. Remember to always test the concealer on your skin before purchasing it and blend it seamlessly for a natural-looking finish.
