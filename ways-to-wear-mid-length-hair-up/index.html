Ways To Wear Mid Length Hair Up A Relaxed Guide

Written by Robby Nov 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Mid length hair is versatile and easy to style, making it a popular choice for many women. However, when it comes to wearing your hair up, it can be challenging to find new and exciting ways to switch up your look. Fortunately, there are several easy and stylish ways to wear mid length hair up, and this guide will walk you through them step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Mid Length Hair Up
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing Mid Length Hair Up
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Mid Length Hair

How to Wear Mid Length Hair Up

Before diving into the different ways to wear mid length hair up, it's essential to know what styles work best for your hair type and face shape. If you have thin hair, you may want to avoid styles that require a lot of volume, whereas those with thick hair can easily pull off more intricate updos.

Additionally, consider your face shape when choosing a style. For example, those with round faces may want to avoid high buns or top knots as they can make the face appear wider. Instead, opt for styles that elongate the face, such as low ponytails or braids.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these step-by-step instructions to achieve some of the most popular ways to wear mid length hair up:

1. Messy Bun

Step 1: Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band.

Step 2: Twist the ponytail into a bun shape and secure it with bobby pins.

Step 3: Pull out a few strands of hair from the bun to create a messy, effortless look.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

Step 1: Take the top section of your hair and pull it back to the crown of your head.

Step 2: Secure the section with an elastic band or hair clip.

Step 3: Optional - curl or wave the remaining hair for added texture.

3. Braided Crown

Step 1: Part your hair down the middle and create two braids on either side of your head.

Step 2: Cross the braids over the top of your head and secure them with bobby pins.

Step 3: Tuck the ends of the braids under the opposite braid and secure them with bobby pins.

Tips for Wearing Mid Length Hair Up

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when wearing mid length hair up:

  • Use hair accessories such as barrettes, hairpins, and headbands to add interest to your updo.
  • Experiment with different textures, such as curls or waves, to create a unique look.
  • Prep your hair with a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add grip and hold.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear mid length hair up if I have layers?

A: Yes, you can! In fact, layers can add texture and interest to your updo. Just be sure to secure any loose pieces with bobby pins.

Q: How do I prevent my updo from falling out?

A: Use an elastic band or hair tie that is tight enough to hold your hair in place but not so tight that it causes discomfort. Additionally, use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or pieces.

Hair Care for Mid Length Hair

To keep your mid length hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently wear your mid length hair up in a variety of stylish and effortless ways.
