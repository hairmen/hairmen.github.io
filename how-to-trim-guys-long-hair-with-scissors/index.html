How To Trim Guys Long Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair for guys is a trend that is here to stay. However, maintaining it can be a bit of a challenge. One of the most important things is to trim it regularly. But how do you do that at home without messing it up? In this article, we will discuss how to trim guys long hair with scissors in a simple and easy-to-understand way.

Description

Trimming long hair is not as difficult as you might think. However, it does require some patience and practice. With the right technique and tools, you can give yourself a neat and stylish haircut without having to spend a lot of money at a salon.

How to Trim Guys Long Hair with Scissors

Before you start trimming your hair, you need to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, a mirror, and a towel. Once you have all these items, you can start with the following steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before trimming your hair, it is important to wash it thoroughly. This will help remove any dirt or oil that may be present. This will make it easier to comb and cut your hair.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to remove any tangles or knots in your hair. This will help you get an even cut. Make sure you comb your hair in the direction you want it to fall.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will help you work on one section at a time. Start with the back of your head and move to the sides and front.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Hold your hair between your fingers and start cutting. Make sure you cut small sections at a time. This will help you avoid cutting too much hair at once. Remember to keep the scissors perpendicular to your hair to get a straight cut.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After you have finished cutting all the sections, check your progress in the mirror. If you notice any unevenness or stray hairs, trim them carefully.

Tips

Here are some tips that can help you get the best results:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take breaks in between sections to avoid fatigue.
  • Trim your hair when it is dry to get a more accurate cut.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time and be patient.

FAQ

Can I trim my hair with regular scissors?

It is recommended to use hair cutting scissors as they are designed to cut hair smoothly and accurately. Regular scissors may damage your hair and give you an uneven cut.

How often should I trim my long hair?

It is recommended to trim your long hair every 8-12 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I trim my hair by myself?

Yes, you can trim your hair at home with the right tools and technique. However, if you are not comfortable doing it yourself, it is best to visit a professional stylist.

Hair Care

Trimming your hair is just one part of hair care. To keep your long hair healthy and shiny, you need to take care of it regularly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Trimming your long hair with scissors is a simple task that can be done at home with the right technique and tools. By following the steps and tips mentioned in this article, you can give yourself a neat and stylish haircut without having to spend a lot of money at a salon.
