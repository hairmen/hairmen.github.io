When Does Your Hair Stop Falling Out After Having Covid

Written by Petter Aug 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're one of the many people who have experienced hair loss after having Covid, you're not alone. This is a common side effect of the illness, and it can be distressing to see your hair falling out in clumps. However, the good news is that this hair loss is usually temporary, and your hair should start to grow back once you've recovered from the virus. So, when does your hair stop falling out after having Covid?

How Long Does Hair Loss Last After Covid?

Hair loss after Covid can last for several months, but it usually starts to improve around three months after the illness. This is because hair loss is often a result of the body going through a period of stress, and it can take some time for the body to recover fully. However, some people may experience hair loss for up to six months after having Covid, so it's important to be patient and take steps to care for your hair during this time.

How to Care for Your Hair After Covid

If you're experiencing hair loss after Covid, there are some things you can do to care for your hair and encourage healthy regrowth. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Be Gentle

Your hair may be more fragile than usual after Covid, so it's important to be gentle when brushing and styling it. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid pulling or tugging at it when you're styling it.

2. Avoid Heat

Heat can be damaging to hair, so try to avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons as much as possible. If you do need to use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet is important for overall health, but it can also help promote healthy hair growth. Make sure you're getting plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet to support your hair health.

4. Consider Supplements

There are a variety of supplements that can help promote healthy hair growth, including biotin, vitamin D, and iron. Talk to your doctor about whether any of these supplements might be right for you.

FAQs

Why Does Hair Fall Out After Covid?

Hair loss after Covid is often a result of the body going through a period of stress. When the body is under stress, it can divert nutrients away from the hair follicles, which can cause hair to fall out.

Will My Hair Grow Back After Covid?

Yes, most people will experience regrowth of their hair after Covid. However, it can take several months for the hair to start growing back fully.

Is Hair Loss After Covid Permanent?

In most cases, hair loss after Covid is temporary and will resolve on its own. However, some people may experience more long-term hair loss as a result of the illness.

Conclusion

Hair loss after Covid can be distressing, but it's usually temporary and will resolve on its own over time. By taking steps to care for your hair and support healthy regrowth, you can help minimize the impact of this side effect and promote healthy hair growth. If you're concerned about your hair loss or have questions about how to care for your hair after Covid, talk to your doctor or a dermatologist for advice.
