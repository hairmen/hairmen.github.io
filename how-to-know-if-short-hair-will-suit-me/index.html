How To Know If Short Hair Will Suit You Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jun 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Short hair is a bold and trendy hairstyle that can elevate your look and make you feel confident. However, before making the chop, it's important to determine whether a short hairstyle will suit your face shape and features. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of figuring out whether short hair is right for you.

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in determining whether short hair will suit you is to figure out your face shape. This will help you choose a haircut that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Here are the main face shapes and the haircuts that suit them:

Round Face

If you have a round face, avoid haircuts that add width to your face. Instead, opt for a short haircut that elongates your face and creates the illusion of more height. A layered bob or a pixie cut with volume on top are great options.

Square Face

If you have a square face, choose a short haircut that softens your features and adds roundness to your face. A shaggy pixie or a layered bob with side-swept bangs can achieve this effect.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, choose a short haircut that balances your features and draws attention away from your forehead. A side-parted pixie or a layered bob with volume on the sides can achieve this effect.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! Almost any short haircut will suit your features. Choose a style that fits your personality and showcases your best features.

Considering Your Hair Texture

Another important factor to consider when deciding whether short hair will suit you is your hair texture. Different hair textures require different haircuts to achieve the desired effect. Here are some tips for choosing a short haircut based on your hair texture:

Straight Hair

If you have straight hair, opt for a blunt bob or a sleek pixie cut. Avoid cuts with too much texture or layering, as they can make your hair look thin and flat.

Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, choose a short haircut that enhances your curls and adds volume to your hair. A layered bob or a curly pixie cut are great options.

Wavy Hair

If you have wavy hair, choose a short haircut that adds texture and movement to your hair. A choppy bob or a wavy pixie cut can achieve this effect.

Factoring in Your Lifestyle

When deciding whether short hair will suit you, it's also important to consider your lifestyle. Short hair requires more frequent maintenance and styling than long hair, so it's important to choose a style that fits your daily routine. Here are some things to consider:

  • Do you have enough time to style your hair every day?
  • Will your short haircut be appropriate for your job or school?
  • Are you willing to invest in styling products and tools?

Short Haircut Ideas for Different Face Shapes

Now that you've determined your face shape and hair texture, it's time to choose a short haircut that suits you. Here are some ideas:

Layered Bob

A layered bob is a versatile short haircut that can flatter any face shape. It adds volume and texture to your hair and can be styled in many different ways.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and daring haircut that can enhance your features and make you stand out. Choose a pixie cut with volume on top or a side-swept fringe to flatter your face shape.

Buzz Cut

A buzz cut is a minimalist and low-maintenance haircut that can be edgy and stylish. It's a great option for those with a round or oval face shape.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hair requires more frequent maintenance and styling than long hair. Here are some tips for keeping your short haircut looking great:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.
  • Invest in high-quality styling products and tools.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories.

FAQs

How do I know if short hair will suit me?

Determine your face shape and hair texture, and choose a short haircut that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Consider your lifestyle and daily routine when choosing a short hairstyle.

What short haircut is best for a round face?

A layered bob or a pixie cut with volume on top are great options for those with a round face shape.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires more frequent washing and styling than long hair. Here are some tips for keeping your short haircut healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair gently.

By following these tips and tricks, you can determine whether short hair will suit you and choose a haircut that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles and accessories to make your short hair your own!
