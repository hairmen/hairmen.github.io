Who Has The Longest Hair In Bts A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we all know, BTS is one of the most popular boy bands in the world. With their catchy tunes, amazing performances, and handsome looks, they have captured the hearts of millions of fans worldwide. But one thing that sets BTS apart from other boy bands is their unique hairstyles. From colorful hair to shaved heads, the members of BTS have experimented with a variety of looks over the years. But who has the longest hair in BTS? In this article, we will answer that question and provide you with some tips on how to take care of long hair like a K-pop idol.

Who Has the Longest Hair in BTS?

The member of BTS with the longest hair is currently Jungkook. His hair has grown past his shoulders and is often tied back in a ponytail or left down for performances. However, it is important to note that the hairstyles of BTS members are constantly changing, so this could change in the future.

How to Grow Long Hair Like a K-Pop Idol

If you want to grow your hair like a K-pop idol, there are a few things you can do to promote healthy hair growth:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth. Make sure to eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Excessive heat from styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and prevent it from growing. Try to avoid using these tools as much as possible, and if you do use them, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.

3. Use Gentle Hair Products

Harsh hair products can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage. Look for gentle shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed for your hair type.

4. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can prevent your hair from growing. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair Like Jungkook

If you want to style your hair like Jungkook, here are some steps to follow:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with gentle products. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry.

2. Apply a Heat Protectant Spray

If you plan on using heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a smooth finish.

4. Straighten Your Hair

Use a flat iron to straighten your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray.

5. Tie Your Hair Back

Finally, tie your hair back in a ponytail or leave it down for a natural look.

Tips for Taking Care of Long Hair

If you have long hair like Jungkook, here are a few tips to help you take care of it:

1. Brush Your Hair Regularly

Brushing your hair regularly can help distribute the natural oils in your hair and prevent tangles.

2. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles like braids and buns can put stress on your hair and cause breakage. Try to avoid these styles as much as possible.

3. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent tangles and breakage, as silk is less abrasive than other fabrics.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Exposure to the sun can cause damage and dryness to your hair. Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair if I want to grow it long?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair while trying to grow it long. Just be sure to use gentle hair dyes and avoid excessive bleaching.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type, but most people can get away with washing their hair every two to three days. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage.

Q: Can I use hair extensions to make my hair look longer?

A: Yes, you can use hair extensions to make your hair look longer. Just be sure to use high-quality extensions that match your hair color and texture.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Jungkook currently has the longest hair in BTS. If you want to grow your hair like a K-pop idol, be sure to eat a balanced diet, avoid heat styling tools, use gentle hair products, and get regular trims. When styling your hair, follow our step-by-step guide to achieve a look like Jungkook. And finally, follow our tips for taking care of long hair to keep your locks healthy and beautiful.
