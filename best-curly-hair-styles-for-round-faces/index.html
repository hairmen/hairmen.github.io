Best Curly Hair Styles For Round Faces

Curly hair can be a challenge to manage, especially if you have a round face. Finding the perfect style that flatters your face shape can be difficult, but not impossible. In this article, we will discuss the best curly hair styles for round faces, how to achieve them, and hair care tips to maintain your beautiful curls.

  2. How to

Description

Curly hair can be beautiful, but it can also be a challenge to style. If you have a round face, you want to choose a style that will flatter your face shape. The best curly hair styles for round faces are those that add volume and length to your hair. This can be achieved with layers, bangs, and a variety of styling techniques.

How to

The first step in achieving the best curly hair style for your round face is to determine your face shape. This can be done by looking in the mirror and measuring the distance between your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If the measurements are roughly equal, you have a round face.

Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to choose a style that will flatter your features. The best curly hair styles for round faces include long layers, side-swept bangs, and a center part. These styles add length and volume to your hair, which can help balance out the roundness of your face.

Another important factor to consider is the texture of your hair. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid styles that are too heavy or voluminous, as this can make your face appear rounder. Instead, opt for lighter layers and a more natural, tousled look.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the best curly hair styles for round faces:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and use a curling iron or wand to create loose, natural-looking curls.
  4. Once you have curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create more volume.
  5. If you have bangs, use a round brush to blow dry them to the side, creating a side-swept look.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for achieving the best curly hair style for your round face:

  • Choose a style that adds length and volume to your hair.
  • Avoid styles that are too heavy or voluminous, as this can make your face appear rounder.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Use a curling iron or wand to create loose, natural-looking curls.
  • Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots to add more volume.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best curly hair styles for round faces:

What are the best curly hair styles for round faces?

The best curly hair styles for round faces include long layers, side-swept bangs, and a center part.

How do I determine my face shape?

You can determine your face shape by measuring the distance between your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If the measurements are roughly equal, you have a round face.

What should I avoid when styling my curly hair?

If you have a round face, you should avoid styles that are too heavy or voluminous, as this can make your face appear rounder.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, it's important to use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair. These products will help keep your curls hydrated and defined.

Second, avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Instead, opt for air-drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

Finally, be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, as curly hair is more prone to tangling and breakage. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, and avoid pulling or tugging on your curls.

In conclusion, the best curly hair styles for round faces are those that add length and volume to your hair. With the right style and hair care routine, you can achieve beautiful, healthy curls that flatter your face shape.
