Animal Crossing How Can I Change My Hairstyle

Written by William Aug 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to the world of Animal Crossing, where you can create your own character and live in a virtual village filled with friendly animals. One of the fun things about the game is the ability to change your hairstyle whenever you want. If you're wondering how to do this, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll go over step-by-step instructions on how to change your hairstyle in Animal Crossing, as well as some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Change Your Hairstyle in Animal Crossing

Changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing is a simple process that you can do from the comfort of your own home. To get started, you'll need to have unlocked the mirror item, which can be purchased from Nook's Cranny for 1,200 bells. Once you have the mirror, follow these steps:

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Place the mirror in your house or on your island.
  2. Stand in front of the mirror and press A to interact with it.
  3. Select "Change My Look" from the menu.
  4. Select "Hairstyle" from the list of options.
  5. Browse through the available hairstyles and select the one you want.
  6. If you have a specific hair color in mind, select "Hair Color" and choose from the available options.
  7. Once you've made your selections, confirm your new look.
  8. That's it! Your character will now have a new hairstyle.

It's important to note that changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing does not cost any money, so feel free to experiment with different looks until you find one you love.

Tips for Choosing a Hairstyle

With so many hairstyles to choose from in Animal Crossing, it can be overwhelming to decide which one to go with. Here are some tips to help you choose a hairstyle that you'll be happy with:

  • Think about your personal style. Are you someone who likes to keep it simple, or do you like to make a statement with your hair?
  • Consider your character's personality. If you're playing as a peppy character, you might want to choose a fun and playful hairstyle.
  • Try out different hairstyles and colors to see what looks best on your character.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your friends for their opinion. They might have some great ideas that you haven't considered.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing:

  • Can I change my hairstyle more than once? Yes, you can change your hairstyle as many times as you want.
  • Do I need to have a certain level of experience to change my hairstyle? No, changing your hairstyle is available to all players regardless of their level of experience.
  • Can I change the hair color of my character? Yes, you can change both the style and color of your character's hair.
  • Do I need to buy any special items to change my hairstyle? Yes, you will need to unlock the mirror item, which can be purchased from Nook's Cranny for 1,200 bells.

Hair Care Advice

Now that you've changed your hairstyle, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care in Animal Crossing:

  • Use a hairbrush or comb to keep your hair neat and tidy.
  • Wear a hat to protect your hair from the sun.
  • If your character's hair gets wet, use a towel to dry it off.
  • Consider using hair accessories such as barrettes or headbands to add some extra style to your look.

Changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing is a fun way to switch things up and give your character a fresh new look. With these step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice, you'll be able to create a hairstyle that you love and that will make your character stand out from the crowd.
