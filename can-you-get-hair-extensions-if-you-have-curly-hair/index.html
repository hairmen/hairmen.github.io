Can You Get Hair Extensions If You Have Curly Hair

Written by Bardi Sep 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of waiting for your curly hair to grow long? Do you want to add some length and volume to your locks? If you're considering hair extensions, you may be wondering whether they're suitable for curly hair. The good news is that you can get hair extensions if you have curly hair. In this blog post, we'll answer some frequently asked questions and provide tips on how to care for your curly hair extensions.

How To

Getting hair extensions for curly hair is similar to getting them for straight or wavy hair. However, there are a few things you need to consider to ensure that your extensions look natural and blend well with your curls. Here's how to get hair extensions for curly hair:

Step 1: Choose the Right Type of Hair Extensions

When it comes to hair extensions, there are different types to choose from, including clip-ins, sew-ins, tape-ins, and fusion extensions. For curly hair, we recommend clip-ins or sew-ins as they're less damaging to your natural hair and allow you to style your curls as you like. Also, choose hair extensions that match your curl pattern and texture.

Step 2: Consult with a Professional Hair Stylist

If you're not sure which type of hair extensions to choose or how to install them, consult with a professional hair stylist who specializes in curly hair. They'll be able to recommend the best type of extensions and installation method for your curls.

Step 3: Install the Hair Extensions

Once you've chosen the right type of hair extensions and consulted with a professional hair stylist, it's time to install them. If you're using clip-ins, simply clip them onto your natural hair. If you're using sew-ins, your stylist will braid your natural hair and sew the extensions into the braids.

Tips

Now that you know how to get hair extensions for curly hair, here are some tips to ensure that your extensions look natural and last longer:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heating tools on your extensions as they can damage the hair and alter the curl pattern.
  • Detangle your hair extensions gently using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent tangling and breakage.
  • Protect your hair extensions from chlorine and saltwater by wearing a swimming cap or tying them up.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting hair extensions for curly hair:

Can You Get Hair Extensions If You Have Natural Curly Hair?

Yes, you can get hair extensions if you have natural curly hair. In fact, hair extensions can add volume, length, and definition to your curls.

Can You Straighten Hair Extensions for Curly Hair?

It's not recommended to straighten hair extensions for curly hair as they may lose their curl pattern and become damaged. If you want straight hair, it's better to get straight hair extensions instead.

How Long Do Hair Extensions for Curly Hair Last?

The lifespan of hair extensions for curly hair depends on the type of extensions, how well you care for them, and how often you wear them. Clip-ins can last up to a year, while sew-ins can last up to six months.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair extensions is essential to keep them looking natural and healthy. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair extensions:

  • Wash your hair extensions every 2-3 weeks with sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Detangle your hair extensions gently using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  • Avoid using heating tools on your extensions as they can damage the hair and alter the curl pattern.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent tangling and breakage.
  • Protect your hair extensions from chlorine and saltwater by wearing a swimming cap or tying them up.

With proper care and maintenance, you can enjoy your curly hair extensions for a long time. Follow these tips and consult with a professional hair stylist to get the best results.
