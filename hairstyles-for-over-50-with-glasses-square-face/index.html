Hairstyles For Over 50 With Glasses Square Face

Written by Daniel Jun 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are over 50 and have a square face shape, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a bit of a challenge. Fortunately, there are plenty of hairstyles that can work with your face shape and glasses. In this article, we will be discussing some of the best hairstyles for over 50 with glasses square face, along with some tips and tricks to keep your hair looking its best.

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and angular features. If you wear glasses, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that complements your frames and flatters your face shape. The right hairstyle can help soften your features and create a more balanced look.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle, consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style. Some of the best hairstyles for over 50 with glasses square face include:

Short Bob

A short bob is a classic hairstyle that can work well with a square face shape. Opt for a chin-length cut that's slightly angled to soften your jawline. This hairstyle is low-maintenance and perfect for women who want a simple yet stylish look.

Long Layers

If you have long hair, consider adding layers to create movement and volume. Soft, face-framing layers can help balance out your features and create a more flattering look. This hairstyle works well with glasses and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can work wonders for a square face shape. They help create a more oval-shaped face by softening your forehead and elongating your face. This hairstyle can be paired with a variety of hairstyles, including a bob or long layers.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions to help you achieve some of the best hairstyles for over 50 with glasses square face:

Short Bob

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair.
  3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, curling the ends under.
  4. Once your hair is dry, add some texturizing spray to create volume and texture.

Long Layers

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair.
  3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, focusing on the ends to create volume.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves.
  5. Finish with some texturizing spray for added volume and texture.

Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair straight using a round brush.
  3. Part your hair to the side, creating a deep side part.
  4. Using a flat iron, straighten your hair and create a slight curl at the ends.
  5. Sweep your bangs to the side, using a bit of hairspray to hold them in place.

Tips

When it comes to styling your hair, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use the right products for your hair type, whether you have fine, thick, or curly hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find one that works for you.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for over 50 with glasses square face:

What is the best hairstyle for a square face shape?

The best hairstyle for a square face shape is one that softens your features and creates balance. Some good options include a short bob, long layers, or side-swept bangs.

What should I avoid if I have a square face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can accentuate your square jawline. Avoid blunt bangs or hairstyles that are too angular.

How often should I visit my hairstylist?

It's recommended to visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can look and feel your best at any age. Whether you prefer a short bob, long layers, or side-swept bangs, there's a hairstyle that's perfect for you. So go ahead and experiment until you find the perfect look!
