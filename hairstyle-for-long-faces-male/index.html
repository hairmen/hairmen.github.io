Best Hairstyles For Long Faces Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a long face, finding the perfect hairstyle that suits you can be a challenge. Your face shape can limit your options and make it difficult to choose a style that complements your features. However, with the right hairstyle, you can enhance your facial features and create a balanced look. In this article, we will guide you through the best hairstyles for long faces males, how to style them, tips, FAQ, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to choose the right hairstyle for long faces male
  • Step by step guide to style your hair
  • Tips to maintain your hairstyle
  • FAQ
  • Hair care for long faces male

How to choose the right hairstyle for long faces male

Before you choose a hairstyle, it's important to determine your face shape. Long faces are characterized by a high forehead, a long chin, and a narrow jawline. The ideal hairstyle for long faces is one that adds width to the face and balances out the features. Here are some hairstyles that work well for long faces male:

1. Short hairstyles

Short hairstyles like buzz cuts, crew cuts, and short crops work well for long faces male. These styles add volume and width to the face, creating a balanced look. You can add texture to your hair to create more volume and dimension.

2. Side-swept hairstyles

Side-swept hairstyles like the side part and the comb-over are great for long faces male. These styles add volume to the sides of the face, creating a more balanced look. You can also add texture to your hair to create more volume and dimension.

3. Medium hairstyles

Medium hairstyles like the quiff, the pompadour, and the messy crop work well for long faces male. These styles add volume and width to the face, creating a more balanced look. You can add texture to your hair to create more volume and dimension.

Step by step guide to style your hair

Now that you know the best hairstyles for long faces male, let's take a look at how to style them:

1. Short hairstyles

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair product to your hair and distribute it evenly.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair in the desired direction.
  4. Use a hair dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

2. Side-swept hairstyles

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair product to your hair and distribute it evenly.
  3. Use a comb to create a side part.
  4. Sweep your hair to one side using your fingers or a comb.
  5. Use a hair dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

3. Medium hairstyles

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair product to your hair and distribute it evenly.
  3. Use a comb to create volume and height at the crown of your head.
  4. Use your fingers to create texture and tousle your hair.
  5. Use a hair dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips to maintain your hairstyle

Here are some tips to maintain your hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain your haircut.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Q: Can long faces male wear long hairstyles?

A: Yes, long faces male can wear long hairstyles, but it's important to add volume and width to the face to create a more balanced look.

Q: What hair products should I use?

A: It depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. For short hairstyles, use a matte finish product like clay or wax. For medium hairstyles, use a pomade or cream to add shine and hold.

Hair care for long faces male

Here are some hair care tips for long faces male:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo that suits your hair type.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and health.
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle is important for long faces male. With the right style, you can enhance your features and create a balanced look. Follow our guide and tips to find the perfect hairstyle for you and maintain it with proper hair care.
