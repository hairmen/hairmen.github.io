How To Get Rid Of A Double Cowlick

Written by Robby Sep 17, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a double cowlick, you know how frustrating it can be to style your hair. Cowlicks are natural growth patterns in your hair that can create a swirl or bump, and having two cowlicks can make it even more difficult to style. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks on how to get rid of a double cowlick.

Description

Before we dive into the how-to, let’s talk a bit more about what a double cowlick is. A cowlick is a section of hair that grows in a different direction than the rest of your hair. This can create a swirl, bump, or even a bald spot. A double cowlick means that you have two of these growth patterns in close proximity to each other, making it harder to style your hair.

How to Get Rid of a Double Cowlick

Unfortunately, there isn’t a way to completely get rid of a double cowlick. However, there are some things you can do to make it easier to style your hair:

1. Get a Good Haircut

A good haircut can make all the difference when it comes to styling your hair with a double cowlick. Speak to your hairdresser about the best cut for your hair type and face shape. They may recommend a short, layered cut that can help to minimize the appearance of your cowlicks.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can also help to make it easier to style your hair. Look for products that are designed to add texture and volume to your hair. You may also want to consider using a styling cream or gel to help hold your hair in place.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can also help to tame your cowlicks. Use a round brush to help smooth out your hair as you blow dry it. You may also want to consider using a diffuser attachment to help distribute the heat evenly throughout your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide on how to get rid of a double cowlick:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type. This will help to remove any dirt and oil from your hair and make it easier to work with.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling cream or gel to your hair, focusing on the areas where your cowlicks are located. This will help to add texture and hold to your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in the opposite direction of your cowlicks. This will help to smooth out your hair and make it easier to style.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a comb or brush to style your hair as desired. You may want to use a bit of hairspray to help hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to get rid of a double cowlick:

- Try using a small amount of hair wax to help hold your hair in place

- Experiment with different hairstyles and cuts to find what works best for you

- Consider speaking to a hairdresser or stylist for additional advice and tips

FAQ

Q: Can I completely get rid of my double cowlick?

A: Unfortunately, there isn’t a way to completely get rid of a cowlick. However, there are ways to make it easier to style your hair and minimize the appearance of your cowlicks.

Q: Will cutting my hair make it easier to style?

A: Yes, getting a good haircut can make it easier to style your hair, especially if you have a double cowlick. Speak to your hairdresser about the best cut for your hair type and face shape.

Hair Care

Remember to take good care of your hair by washing it regularly, using a conditioner, and avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools. This can help to keep your hair healthy and minimize any damage that may make it harder to style.

Now that you know how to get rid of a double cowlick, you can feel more confident in styling your hair. Remember to be patient and experiment with different techniques to find what works best for you.
