Men s Haircut Medium Length Short Sides A Guide For Relaxed Styling

Description

The men's haircut medium length short sides is a popular and versatile hairstyle that works for all face shapes and hair types. It's a great option for those who want a stylish and low-maintenance haircut that can be easily styled for any occasion. The haircut features short hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on top that can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked-back to messy and textured.

How to

To achieve the men's haircut medium length short sides, you'll need to go to a professional barber or hairstylist. They will use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back to a short length, usually a #2 or #3 clipper guard. The hair on top will be left longer, around 2-4 inches, and can be cut with scissors or a razor to create texture and movement.

Step by step

1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt and buildup.
2. Towel dry your hair and comb it to remove any tangles.
3. Visit a professional barber or hairstylist and show them a picture of the men's haircut medium length short sides that you like.
4. They will use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back to a short length, usually a #2 or #3 clipper guard.
5. The hair on top will be left longer, around 2-4 inches, and can be cut with scissors or a razor to create texture and movement.
6. Style your hair using a hair product that suits your hair type and desired look, such as a pomade, wax, or gel.

Tips

- Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and hair type.
- Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
- Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
- Schedule regular appointments with your barber or hairstylist to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

FAQ

Q: Will the men's haircut medium length short sides work for all hair types?
A: Yes, the haircut can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How often should I get my haircut to maintain the men's haircut medium length short sides?
A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of the haircut.

Q: Can I style the men's haircut medium length short sides in different ways?
A: Yes, the haircut is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked-back to messy and textured.

Hair Care

- Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and strong.
- Use a hair product that suits your hair type and desired look.
- Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as it can damage the hair.
- Schedule regular appointments with your barber or hairstylist to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

The men's haircut medium length short sides is a stylish and versatile hairstyle that can work for all face shapes and hair types. With the right styling products and regular maintenance, you can easily achieve a relaxed and effortless look that's perfect for any occasion. So, go ahead and try out this trendy haircut for a fresh and modern look!
