What Shape Glasses Suit A Fat Face

Written by Daniel Aug 13, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have a round or chubby face, you might be wondering what type of glasses look best on you. With so many different shapes and styles of glasses available, it can be tough to know where to start. In this article, we'll explore the different types of glasses that are best suited for a fat face, along with some tips and tricks for finding the perfect pair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Shapes of Glasses for a Fat Face
  • Tips for Choosing Glasses for a Fat Face
  • FAQs About Glasses for a Fat Face
  • Hair Care Tips for Glasses Wearers

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can choose the perfect pair of glasses for your face shape, you need to determine what your face shape actually is. The easiest way to do this is to stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back, and examine the shape of your face.

There are several different face shapes, including:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart-shaped
  • Diamond-shaped

If your face is round, you'll have a circular face with full cheeks and a rounded chin. If you have an oval face, your face will be longer than it is wide, with a slightly curved jawline. If you have a square face, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline will all be roughly the same width. Heart-shaped faces have a wider forehead and cheekbones, with a narrower chin, while diamond-shaped faces have a narrow forehead and jawline, with wide cheekbones.

Best Shapes of Glasses for a Fat Face

When it comes to choosing glasses for a fat face, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, you'll want to choose glasses that are proportionate to your face. This means avoiding small or narrow glasses that will make your face look even rounder.

Instead, opt for glasses with a wide frame that will balance out the proportions of your face. Square or rectangular frames are also a good choice, as they will help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Avoid round or circular frames, as these will only make your face look rounder.

Another great option for a fat face is cat-eye glasses. These glasses have a slightly upward sweep at the outer corners, which can help to lift and elongate your face. You can also choose glasses with a bold or unusual design, as these will draw attention away from the roundness of your face.

Examples of Glasses Shapes for a Fat Face

  • Rectangular
  • Square
  • Cat-eye
  • Geometric
  • Bold or unusual designs

Tips for Choosing Glasses for a Fat Face

When shopping for glasses, there are a few additional tips you can keep in mind to ensure you find the perfect pair for your face shape. These include:

  • Choose glasses with a wide bridge, as this will help to balance out your face.
  • Opt for glasses with a low-set temple, as this will help to elongate your face.
  • Avoid glasses with a lot of embellishments or decorations, as these can make your face look even rounder.
  • Consider getting glasses with anti-reflective coating, as this can help to reduce the appearance of glare and reflections on your lenses.
  • Don't be afraid to try on lots of different styles and shapes of glasses before making a decision.

FAQs About Glasses for a Fat Face

What color of glasses frames should I choose for a fat face?

The color of your glasses frames is largely a matter of personal preference. However, if you have a round or chubby face, you might want to avoid glasses with brightly colored frames, as these can draw attention to the roundness of your face. Instead, opt for neutral or muted colors that will complement your skin tone and hair color.

Can I wear oversized glasses if I have a fat face?

Oversized glasses can be a great option for a fat face, as they can help to balance out the proportions of your face and make it appear slimmer. However, it's important to choose glasses that are still proportionate to your face - don't go too big or you could end up looking like you're wearing clown glasses!

Do I need to get my glasses adjusted if I have a fat face?

If you have a round or chubby face, you might find that your glasses slip down your nose or feel uncomfortable after a few hours of wear. This is because the curvature of your face can affect the fit of your glasses. To ensure a comfortable and secure fit, you may need to get your glasses adjusted by a professional optician.

Hair Care Tips for Glasses Wearers

Finally, if you wear glasses, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that cover your face, as these can make your glasses look too prominent.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements the shape of your face. For example, if you have a round face, a layered or textured bob can help to elongate your face and balance out the roundness.
  • Consider getting bangs. Bangs can help to draw attention to your eyes and away from your glasses.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and fullness to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.

By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect pair of glasses for your round or chubby face. Whether you opt for a square frame, cat-eye glasses, or something bold and unusual, you're sure to look and feel fabulous!
