How To Determine Face Shape Male App

Written by Daniel Jul 10, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you struggling to identify your face shape? Do you want to know what hairstyle suits you best? Luckily, there are plenty of apps available that can help you determine your face shape. In this article, we'll show you how to determine your face shape using a male app and provide you with some tips on how to take care of your hair based on your face shape.

Description

Before we dive into the details of how to determine your face shape using a male app, let's first understand what face shape is. Face shape refers to the structure of your face, including the jawline, cheekbones, forehead, and chin. There are several face shapes, including oval, round, square, diamond, heart, and oblong. Knowing your face shape can help you choose the right hairstyle, beard style, and even glasses that complement your features.

How to Determine Face Shape Male App

Now that you know what face shape is let's move on to how to determine your face shape using a male app. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Download a Face Shape App

The first step is to download a face shape app that is specifically designed for males. There are several apps available on both iOS and Android platforms, such as Men's Hairstyles, Hairstyle Makeover, and Face Shape Meter. Choose an app that has good reviews and ratings.

Step 2: Take a Selfie

Once you've downloaded the app, take a selfie of your face using the app's camera. Make sure that your face is well-lit, and you're looking straight into the camera. Avoid tilting your head or making any facial expressions.

Step 3: Analyze the Results

After taking the selfie, the app will analyze your face and determine your face shape. The app may ask you to mark certain points on your face, such as the corners of your forehead, jawline, and chin, to get a more accurate result. Once the app has analyzed your face, it will display your face shape.

Tips

Knowing your face shape can help you choose the right hairstyle, beard style, and even glasses that complement your features. Here are some tips based on your face shape:

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is considered the most versatile face shape as it suits most hairstyles. You can try a short buzz cut, a quiff, or a slicked-back hairstyle.

Round Face Shape

For a round face shape, choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the top of your head, such as a pompadour or a side-swept fringe. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as a bowl cut.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape has a strong jawline and angular features. Choose a hairstyle that softens your features, such as a textured crop or a messy fringe. Avoid hairstyles that accentuate your strong jawline, such as a buzz cut.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape has a narrow forehead and chin with wide cheekbones. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head and narrows the width of your cheekbones, such as a messy fringe or a side-swept hairstyle.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape has a wide forehead and cheekbones with a narrow chin. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to your chin, such as a chin-length bob or a messy fringe. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your forehead, such as a slicked-back hairstyle.

Oblong Face Shape

An oblong face shape has a long and narrow face with a prominent chin. Choose a hairstyle that adds width to your face, such as a textured crop or a quiff. Avoid hairstyles that add length to your face, such as a slicked-back hairstyle.

FAQ

What is the best male face shape app?

There are several male face shape apps available on both iOS and Android platforms, such as Men's Hairstyles, Hairstyle Makeover, and Face Shape Meter. Choose an app that has good reviews and ratings.

Do face shape apps really work?

Yes, face shape apps use facial recognition technology to analyze your face and determine your face shape. However, the results may not always be accurate, so it's best to consult a hairstylist or a barber for personalized advice.

Hair Care Based on Face Shape

Knowing your face shape can help you choose the right hairstyle, but it's also essential to take care of your hair based on your face shape. Here are some tips:

Use the Right Products

Choose hair products that suit your hair type and texture. If you have thick hair, use a styling product that provides hold and volume. If you have fine hair, use a lightweight product that adds texture and definition.

Trim Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your haircut. A regular trim every 4-6 weeks can help prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Style with Fingers

Instead of using a comb, style your hair with your fingers. This will help create a more natural and relaxed look. Use a hair dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.

Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools. Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip your hair of natural oils and cause dryness.

Now that you know how to determine your face shape using a male app and take care of your hair based on your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that complements your features and enhances your overall appearance.
