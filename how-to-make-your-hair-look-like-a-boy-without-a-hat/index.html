How To Make Your Hair Look Like A Boy Without A Hat A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing a hat to make your hair look like a boy's? Do you want to rock a short, masculine hairstyle without any headwear? Well, you're in luck! In this blog post, we'll show you how to achieve a boyish look with your locks, even if you have long hair. Follow our simple steps, and you'll have a fresh, androgynous hairstyle in no time!

Description

Many people want to experiment with their hair and switch things up. However, some folks struggle with making their hair look like a boy's without a hat. This blog post aims to provide a solution for those who want to achieve a more masculine hairstyle without any headwear. We'll cover everything from haircuts to styling techniques, so you can rock a boyish look with confidence.

How to Make Your Hair Look Like a Boy Without a Hat

Step 1: Get a Short Haircut

The first step to achieving a boyish hairstyle is to get a short haircut. You can opt for a classic men's cut, like a buzz cut or a crew cut, or go for a more modern style, like an undercut or a textured crop. Make sure you communicate your desired look with your hairstylist and ask for their opinion on what would suit your face shape and hair type best.


Step 2: Use Hair Products

Once you have your short haircut, you'll need to style it to give it a more masculine edge. Use hair products like wax, pomade, or gel to create texture and hold. Apply the product to your hair when it's damp, and use your fingers or a comb to style it in the direction you want. If you have longer hair, you can use a hair tie to pull it back into a low ponytail or a half-up, half-down style.


Step 3: Experiment with Parting

Parting your hair on the side can instantly make it look more boyish. Use a comb to create a deep side part, or go for a messy, tousled look by parting your hair with your fingers. You can also experiment with a center part or no part at all, depending on your hair type and face shape. Play around with different parting techniques until you find one that suits your style.


Step 4: Try a Hat Trick

If you're not ready to commit to a short haircut or don't have the time to style your hair every day, you can use a hat to achieve a boyish look. Choose a beanie, a baseball cap, or a fedora to complement your outfit and give your hair a more masculine edge. Just make sure you don't wear the hat too tight, so it doesn't flatten your hair.


Tips for Making Your Hair Look Like a Boy Without a Hat

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a boyish hairstyle:

  • Invest in good-quality hair products that suit your hair type
  • Use a hair dryer to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques
  • Accessorize with masculine jewelry or clothing to complete the look
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your short hairstyle

FAQ

Can I make my long hair look like a boy's?

Yes, you can! You'll need to get a short haircut first and then use hair products to create texture and hold. You can also experiment with parting and accessories to give your hair a more masculine edge.

What hair products should I use for a boyish hairstyle?

You can use wax, pomade, or gel to create texture and hold. Make sure you choose a product that suits your hair type and doesn't leave any residue.

How often should I get a haircut?

If you have a short hairstyle, you'll need to get a haircut every four to six weeks to maintain the length and shape. If you have longer hair, you can wait longer between haircuts, depending on how fast your hair grows.


Hair Care for a Boyish Hairstyle

Maintaining a boyish hairstyle requires regular haircuts and proper hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid tying your hair too tight or pulling it back too often
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out

With the right haircut, styling techniques, and hair care, you can make your hair look like a boy's without a hat. Whether you prefer a classic or modern look, there are plenty of options to choose from. So, go ahead and experiment with your hair, and embrace your inner boyish charm!
