Simple Quick Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Luffy Aug 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who always finds themselves pressed for time in the morning, you'll understand the struggle of trying to style your hair in a hurry. However, that doesn't mean you have to compromise on your hairstyle. With medium length hair, there are plenty of simple and quick hairstyles that you can do in just a matter of minutes. Here are some of our favourite hairstyles:

Description

Medium length hair is a versatile length that can be styled in many ways. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, there are plenty of hairstyles that you can choose from. The best part is that most of these hairstyles are simple and quick to do, so you'll never have to spend too much time on your hair.

How to

Here are some quick and easy hairstyles for medium length hair:

1. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for medium length hair. To create this look, simply gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun. Pull out some strands of hair to create a messy, effortless look.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is another simple hairstyle that can be done in minutes. Simply gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie. You can leave the rest of your hair down or create loose waves for added texture.

3. Low Ponytail

A low ponytail is a chic and stylish hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion. Simply gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie. You can add some volume to your ponytail by teasing the crown of your hair.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions for creating these quick and easy hairstyles:

Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist your hair into a bun.
  3. Pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie.
  2. Leave the rest of your hair down or create loose waves for added texture.
  3. Add some volume to your hair by teasing the crown.

Low Ponytail

  1. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck.
  2. Secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Tease the crown of your hair to add volume.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle:

  • Use hair products that work well with your hair type.
  • Invest in good quality hair tools.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use hair accessories to add some flair to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length hairstyles:

1. What are some easy hairstyles for medium length hair?

Some easy hairstyles for medium length hair include a messy bun, half-up half-down, and a low ponytail.

2. How can I add volume to my medium length hair?

You can add volume to your medium length hair by teasing the crown of your hair or using volumizing products.

3. Can I use hair accessories with medium length hair?

Yes, you can use hair accessories like headbands, clips, and bobby pins to add some flair to your medium length hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy medium length hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these simple tips, you can keep your medium length hair looking beautiful and healthy.
