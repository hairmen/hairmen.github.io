An oblong face shape is one of the most common face shapes among men. It is characterized by a long and narrow face with a prominent forehead and a strong jawline. Many people wonder whether an oblong face shape is attractive for males or not. The answer is yes! An oblong face shape can be very attractive for males if they style their hair and beard in the right way.

How to Identify Oblong Face Shape

The easiest way to identify an oblong face shape is to measure the length and width of your face. An oblong face shape is longer than it is wide, with a prominent forehead and a strong jawline. Other characteristics of an oblong face shape include:

  • A high forehead
  • A long and narrow chin
  • Straight cheekbones
  • A straight or slightly curved jawline

Step by Step Guide to Determine Oblong Face Shape

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you determine if you have an oblong face shape:

  1. Measure the length and width of your face using a tape measure or ruler.
  2. Divide the length of your face by the width.
  3. If the result is greater than 1.5, you have an oblong face shape.
  4. Look for other characteristics of an oblong face shape such as a high forehead, a long and narrow chin, straight cheekbones, and a straight or slightly curved jawline.

Tips for Oblong Face Shape Male

If you have an oblong face shape, here are some tips to help you enhance your features:

  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short on the sides and too long on the top.
  • Grow a beard to add width to your jawline.
  • Avoid wearing glasses with rectangular frames that can make your face look longer.
  • Wear hats that add width to your forehead.
  • Avoid wearing hats that sit too low on your forehead.

FAQs

Q: Can an oblong face shape be attractive for males?

A: Yes, an oblong face shape can be very attractive for males if they style their hair and beard in the right way.

Q: What are some hairstyles that suit an oblong face shape?

A: Hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face such as a messy quiff or a textured crop are great for oblong face shape males.

Hair Care Tips for Oblong Face Shape Male

If you have an oblong face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to enhance your features. Here are some hair care tips for oblong face shape males:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to the sides of your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a hairbrush to add volume to your hair while blow-drying.
  • Avoid blow-drying your hair straight, as this can make your face look longer.

In conclusion, an oblong face shape can be very attractive for males if they style their hair and beard in the right way. Follow the tips and hair care advice mentioned in this article to enhance your features and look your best!
