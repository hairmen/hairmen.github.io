How To Get The Haircut You Want In Animal Crossing

Written by Robby Aug 26, 2023 · 3 min read

Welcome to the world of Animal Crossing, where you can create your own island paradise and customize your character to your heart’s content. One of the many ways you can personalize your character is by changing their hairstyle. In this article, we’ll show you how to get the haircut you want in Animal Crossing!

Table of Contents

  • How to Change Your Hairstyle
  • Step by Step
  • Tips for Getting the Hairstyle You Want
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care in Animal Crossing

How to Change Your Hairstyle

To change your hairstyle in Animal Crossing, you need to visit the Nook’s Cranny store. Once you’re there, find the mirror in the back of the store and interact with it. This will bring up a menu of all the different hairstyles you can choose from.

From there, you can select the hairstyle you want and see what it looks like on your character. If you’re happy with the new hairstyle, confirm your choice and it will automatically be applied to your character.

Step by Step

Here’s a step by step guide on how to change your hairstyle:

  1. Visit Nook’s Cranny
  2. Find the mirror in the back of the store
  3. Interact with the mirror
  4. Select the hairstyle you want
  5. Confirm your choice

Tips for Getting the Hairstyle You Want

Here are some tips that can help you get the hairstyle you want:

  • Try on different hairstyles before making a final decision
  • Consider your character’s face shape and skin tone when choosing a hairstyle
  • Don’t be afraid to try something new – you can always change your hairstyle again later
  • Check back at Nook’s Cranny regularly to see if any new hairstyles have become available

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing:

Can I change my hairstyle after I’ve already selected one?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle as many times as you want. Just visit the mirror in Nook’s Cranny and select a new hairstyle.

Do I have to pay to change my hairstyle?

Yes, you’ll need to pay 1,800 Bells to change your hairstyle.

Can I change the color of my hair?

No, you can’t change the color of your hair in Animal Crossing.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing

While there’s no specific hair care routine in Animal Crossing, you can keep your character looking fresh by changing their hairstyle regularly. This can also be a fun way to experiment with different looks and express your personal style.

So there you have it – everything you need to know about getting the haircut you want in Animal Crossing. Happy styling!
