How To Cut In Bangs By Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of having to book an appointment at the salon just to get your bangs trimmed? Well, did you know that you can actually cut in bangs by yourself at home? Not only is it convenient, but it can also save you money in the long run. In this guide, we will show you how to cut in bangs by yourself, step by step.

How to Cut in Bangs by Yourself

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Brush your hair straight and make sure there are no tangles. It's also helpful to have a mirror and a pair of sharp scissors.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Part your hair down the middle and pull the rest of your hair back into a ponytail. This will help you see your bangs more clearly and keep the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Determine the Length

Decide on the length you want your bangs to be. It's best to start longer and gradually cut more if needed. Use a comb to separate your bangs from the rest of your hair.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Hold the hair between your fingers and cut across in a straight line. It's important to cut slowly and carefully, making sure to not cut too much at once. Keep checking the length and adjust as you go.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Take a step back and check your work in the mirror. If you need to make any adjustments, go back and trim as needed.

Tips for Cutting in Bangs by Yourself

- Start longer and go shorter gradually
- Use sharp scissors
- Cut slowly and carefully
- Check your work frequently
- Don't cut too much at once
- Practice makes perfect

FAQ: Cutting in Bangs by Yourself

Q: Do I need special scissors to cut my bangs?
A: While it's not necessary, it's best to use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.

Q: Is it better to cut bangs when hair is wet or dry?
A: It's best to cut bangs when hair is dry, as wet hair can be deceiving and may result in cutting too much.

Q: What if I mess up?
A: Don't worry, hair grows back! If you make a mistake, try to fix it as best as you can or consider booking a professional appointment to fix it.

Hair Care After Cutting in Bangs

After cutting in bangs, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style. Use a heat protectant before using any hot tools, and avoid using too much product that can weigh down your bangs. Regular trims can also help keep your bangs looking fresh and healthy.

Now that you know how to cut in bangs by yourself, you can save time and money by doing it at home. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll have beautiful bangs in no time!
