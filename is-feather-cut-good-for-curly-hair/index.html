Is Feather Cut Good For Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know the struggle of finding the right hairstyle that not only looks good but also suits your hair texture. One hairstyle that has been gaining popularity among curly-haired individuals is the feather cut. But, is feather cut good for curly hair? In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about feather cut for curly hair, including how to get it, its benefits, and hair care tips.

Description

The feather cut is a type of layered haircut that involves cutting the hair at an angle, creating a feather-like effect. This hairstyle works great for curly hair as it adds volume, texture, and movement to the hair. The feather cut is also versatile and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. It is a low-maintenance hairstyle that can be worn for both casual and formal occasions.

How to Get Feather Cut for Curly Hair

Before getting a feather cut for curly hair, it is important to find a hairstylist who specializes in cutting curly hair. Curly hair requires special skills and techniques to avoid frizz and damage. Once you find a hairstylist, you can follow these steps to get a feather cut for curly hair.

Step 1: Consultation

During the consultation, your hairstylist will assess your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle to determine the best feather cut for you. You can also discuss your preferences and expectations for the hairstyle.

Step 2: Shampoo and Condition

Before cutting your hair, your hairstylist will shampoo and condition it to remove any dirt and oil buildup. This will also make it easier to cut and style.

Step 3: Sectioning

Your hairstylist will then section your hair and clip it to make it easier to cut. They will start by cutting the bottom layer of your hair, gradually moving upwards.

Step 4: Feathering

Using a feathering technique, your hairstylist will cut the hair at an angle, creating a layered effect. This will add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 5: Finishing

Your hairstylist will then style your hair using a diffuser or a curling iron to enhance the curls and create a natural-looking finish. They may also apply a styling product to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Feather Cut on Curly Hair

Now that you have a feather cut for your curly hair, it is important to maintain it to keep it looking fresh and healthy. Here are some tips to help you maintain your feather cut.

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or a hair oil to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your feather cut.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry as it can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it is wet.

FAQs

Q: Is feather cut suitable for all face shapes?

A: Feather cut is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit different face shapes. Your hairstylist will assess your face shape and recommend the best feather cut for you.

Q: Can feather cut add volume to my curly hair?

A: Yes, feather cut is great for adding volume and texture to curly hair. It creates a layered effect that enhances the natural curls and adds movement to the hair.

Q: Can I style my feather cut in different ways?

A: Yes, feather cut is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in different ways. You can wear it straight, curly, or wavy, depending on your preference.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair with Feather Cut

Curly hair with a feather cut requires special care to maintain its shape and texture. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair with feather cut.

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach and hair dyes as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair daily as it can dry out your hair and cause frizz. Instead, wash it every 2-3 days.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using tight hair ties and clips as they can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can rock your curly hair with a feather cut and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
