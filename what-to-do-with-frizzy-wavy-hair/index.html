Description

Frizzy wavy hair can be a real challenge to manage. It can be thick, unruly, and prone to tangles. But fear not, with the right tips and tricks, you can tame your frizzy waves and achieve gorgeous, manageable locks. In this 2023 guide, we'll share our best advice for what to do with frizzy wavy hair.

How To Tame Frizzy Wavy Hair

If you're struggling to manage your frizzy wavy hair, there are a few things you can do to help tame your locks. Here are our top tips:

1. Use a Hydrating Shampoo and Conditioner

Frizzy hair is often dry, so it's important to use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to help replenish moisture. Look for products that are designed for curly or wavy hair, as they will help define your waves and reduce frizz.

2. Don't Over-Wash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and frizz. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days instead.

3. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

When combing out your hair, use a wide-toothed comb to prevent tangles and breakage. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make frizz worse. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

Step By Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you manage your frizzy wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a hydrating shampoo and conditioner. Rinse well and gently towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help detangle and moisturize your locks. Use a wide-toothed comb to distribute the product evenly.

Step 3: Use a Curl-Defining Product

Apply a curl-defining product to your hair to help enhance your waves and reduce frizz. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair and define your curls.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Avoid using a blow dryer if possible, as the heat can make frizz worse. Instead, let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on a low setting.

Tips To Manage Frizzy Wavy Hair

Here are a few additional tips to help you manage your frizzy wavy hair:

1. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Silk pillowcases can help reduce frizz and prevent tangles while you sleep.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to help nourish and moisturize your hair.

3. Avoid Touching Your Hair Too Much

Touching your hair too much can lead to frizz and tangles. Try to keep your hands off your hair as much as possible.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a hair oil to help tame frizzy wavy hair?

A: Yes, hair oils can be a great way to help hydrate and smooth frizzy hair. Look for oils like argan or coconut oil, which are lightweight and won't weigh down your hair.

Q: What's the best haircut for frizzy wavy hair?

A: A layered haircut can be a great option for frizzy wavy hair, as it can help reduce bulk and add texture to your locks.

Hair Care Tips To Prevent Frizz

Here are a few additional hair care tips to help prevent frizz:

1. Use a Microfiber Towel

Microfiber towels are gentle on your hair and can help reduce frizz while you dry your locks.

2. Don't Rub Your Hair Dry

Instead of rubbing your hair dry with a towel, gently blot your hair to remove excess moisture.

3. Use a Hair Mask

Once a week, use a hair mask to help nourish and hydrate your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can tame your frizzy wavy hair and achieve gorgeous, manageable locks. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and techniques until you find what works best for your hair.
