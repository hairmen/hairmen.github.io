How To Wear Your Hair When Growing It Out Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Nov 11, 2023 Â· 2 min read

Growing out your hair can be a challenging process, especially when it comes to figuring out how to style it during the awkward in-between stages. However, with the right techniques and products, you can make the transition from short to long hair as seamless as possible. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for how to wear your hair when growing it out.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

When growing out your hair, it's important to find styles that work with your hair's natural texture and length. Here are some options to consider:

1. Braids

Braids are a great way to keep your hair out of your face and showcase your hair's length. Try a classic three-strand braid, a fishtail braid, or a French braid. You can also experiment with different braid sizes and placements to find a style that works for you.

2. Ponytails

A simple ponytail is a classic and easy way to style your hair when growing it out. You can wear it high or low, sleek or messy, and add accessories like scrunchies or hair clips to dress it up.

3. Half-Up, Half-Down

If you're looking for a style that's a little more polished, try a half-up, half-down look. This style works well for medium-length hair and allows you to show off your length while keeping your hair out of your face.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide for creating a simple, low ponytail:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with an elastic band.
  3. If desired, wrap a small section of hair around the elastic band to hide it.
  4. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for wearing your hair when growing it out:

  • Invest in hair accessories like headbands, clips, and scarves to add variety to your hairstyles.
  • Experiment with different partings to create new looks.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and promote growth.

FAQ

How often should I wash my hair when growing it out?

It's important to keep your hair clean when growing it out, but over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed based on your hair type and activity level.

What products should I use when growing out my hair?

Look for products that are designed to promote hair growth and strengthen your hair, such as biotin supplements, hair masks, and leave-in conditioners. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and slow down your hair's growth process.

Hair Care

When growing out your hair, it's important to take good care of it to promote healthy growth. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental damage by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth, such as biotin, iron, and vitamin E.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to wear your hair with confidence as you grow it out. Remember to be patient, stay consistent with your hair care routine, and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
