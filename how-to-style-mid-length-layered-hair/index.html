How To Style Mid Length Layered Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Oct 05, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have mid-length hair with layers, you have a versatile canvas to work with. From bouncy waves to sleek straight styles, there are many ways to style your hair. In this article, we’ll be sharing tips and tricks on how to style mid-length layered hair for any occasion.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Mid-Length Layered Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Mid-Length Layered Hair
  • FAQs

How to Style Mid-Length Layered Hair

Mid-length layered hair can be styled in many ways, depending on the occasion and your personal style. Here are some popular ways to style mid-length layered hair:

Curls and Waves

Curls and waves are a great way to add volume and texture to mid-length layered hair. You can use a curling iron or a hair straightener to create loose waves or tight curls. You can also use a heatless curling method such as braiding your hair overnight for a natural, beachy look.

Sleek and Straight

If you prefer a more polished look, you can straighten your mid-length layered hair using a hair straightener. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before straightening to prevent damage. You can also add a center or side part for a chic look.

Ponytails and Updos

Ponytails and updos are a great way to keep your mid-length layered hair out of your face while still looking stylish. You can create a sleek high ponytail or a messy low ponytail for a casual look. For a more formal occasion, you can create an updo such as a chignon or a braided bun.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to create loose waves on mid-length layered hair:

  1. Apply a heat protectant spray on your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections.
  3. Use a curling iron to curl each section, starting from the bottom and working your way up.
  4. Hold each curl for a few seconds before releasing.
  5. Once all sections are curled, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep the waves in place.

Tips for Styling Mid-Length Layered Hair

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to prevent damage.
  • Invest in quality hair styling products such as hairspray, mousse, and hair serum.
  • Try different hair accessories such as headbands, clips, and scrunchies to add variety to your hairstyle.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different haircuts and styles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Can I style my mid-length layered hair without using heat?

Yes, you can style your mid-length layered hair without using heat. You can try heatless curling methods such as braiding your hair or using foam rollers overnight.

How often should I wash my mid-length layered hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash your hair once or twice a week. If you exercise frequently or live in a humid climate, you may need to wash your hair more often.

Hair Care for Mid-Length Layered Hair

To keep your mid-length layered hair healthy and shiny, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your mid-length layered hair in many different ways. Whether you’re going for a casual or formal look, you can achieve a stylish and trendy hairstyle with mid-length layered hair.
