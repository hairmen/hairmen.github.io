How To Make Your Thick Curly Hair Look Good

Written by Robby Nov 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know that it can be a challenge to style and manage. But don't worry, with the right techniques and products, you can make your hair look great. In this blog post, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to make your thick curly hair look good.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in making your thick curly hair look good is to understand your hair type. Curly hair tends to be drier and more fragile than straight hair, so it requires special care. Here are some tips on how to make your hair look good:

1. Use the right products

Choose products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Look for shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that are moisturizing and nourishing. Avoid products that contain sulfates, alcohol, or other harsh ingredients that can dry out your hair.

2. Detangle your hair gently

Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to gently comb through your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots. Avoid using a regular brush, as it can cause breakage and frizz.

3. Don't over-wash your hair

Curly hair doesn't need to be washed as often as straight hair. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and frizzy. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or even less if your hair is very dry.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to make your thick curly hair look good:

1. Start with clean, damp hair

Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Blot your hair with a towel to remove excess water, but don't rub it vigorously. You want your hair to be damp, but not dripping wet.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help moisturize and define your curls. Use your fingers to work the conditioner through your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.

3. Use a styling product

Choose a styling product that works for your hair type and the look you want to achieve. For example, if you want defined curls, use a curl cream or gel. If you want volume, use a mousse or foam. Apply the product to your hair, using your fingers to distribute it evenly.

4. Diffuse your hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to define your curls and reduce frizz. Use a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair with your hands as you dry it.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help make your thick curly hair look good:

1. Protect your hair when you sleep

Use a silk or satin pillowcase to help reduce friction and prevent breakage. You can also sleep with your hair in a pineapple or a loose bun on top of your head.

2. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and frizzy. If you must use heat tools, use a heat protectant spray and choose a low heat setting.

3. Get regular trims

Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making thick curly hair look good:

1. Can I brush my curly hair?

It's best to avoid brushing curly hair, as it can cause breakage and frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush instead.

2. How can I reduce frizz in my curly hair?

Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid touching your hair too much. You can also use a leave-in conditioner and a styling product to help reduce frizz.

3. How can I make my curls more defined?

Use a curl cream or gel to help define your curls. You can also try diffusing your hair with a hair dryer to enhance your curls.

Hair Care

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your thick curly hair:

1. Deep condition your hair regularly

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

2. Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to help prevent damage from the sun.

3. Use a clarifying shampoo occasionally

Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to help remove buildup from styling products and hard water.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your thick curly hair look great. Embrace your natural curls and experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
