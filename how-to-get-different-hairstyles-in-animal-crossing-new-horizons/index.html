How To Get Different Hairstyles In Animal Crossing New Horizons

Written by Oliver Oct 29, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle in Animal Crossing New Horizons? Do you want to change things up but don't know how? Look no further, because we've got you covered! In this article, we will show you how to get different hairstyles in Animal Crossing New Horizons.

How to

Getting different hairstyles in Animal Crossing New Horizons is easy. All you need to do is visit Harriet at the Shampoodle salon, which is located on the second floor of the Able Sisters store.

Harriet will offer you a variety of hairstyles to choose from, and you can select the one that you like the most. However, keep in mind that some hairstyles might not be available right away, and you might need to unlock them by meeting certain criteria.

For example, you can unlock the "Top 8 Stylish Hairstyles" by buying at least one accessory from the Able Sisters store and unlocking the Able Sisters shop upgrade.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get different hairstyles in Animal Crossing New Horizons:

  1. Go to the Able Sisters store
  2. Go to the second floor of the store
  3. Enter the Shampoodle salon
  4. Talk to Harriet
  5. Select the hairstyle you want
  6. Pay the fee
  7. Enjoy your new hairstyle!

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your new hairstyle:

  • Try different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best
  • Unlock new hairstyles by meeting certain criteria
  • Accessorize your hairstyle with hats or hairpins
  • Experiment with different hair colors
  • Change your hairstyle to match the season or event

FAQ

Q: How much does it cost to get a new hairstyle?

A: The fee for a new hairstyle is 3,000 Bells.

Q: Can I change my hairstyle anytime I want?

A: Yes, you can change your hairstyle anytime you want by visiting the Shampoodle salon.

Q: How do I unlock new hairstyles?

A: You can unlock new hairstyles by meeting certain criteria, such as buying an accessory from the Able Sisters store or unlocking the Able Sisters shop upgrade.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair in Animal Crossing New Horizons to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your new hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair regularly by visiting Harriet at the Shampoodle salon
  • Use hairpins or hats to accessorize your hairstyle
  • Avoid getting your hair wet or dirty
  • Change your hairstyle periodically to keep things fresh

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to get different hairstyles in Animal Crossing New Horizons and keep your hair looking great. Happy styling!
