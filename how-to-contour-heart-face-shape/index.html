How to Contour Heart Face Shape

The key to contouring heart face shape is to create balance and definition. This can be achieved by highlighting the areas you want to bring forward and shading the areas you want to recede. Here are the basic steps to follow:

Step 1: Prep Your Skin

Start by prepping your skin with a moisturizer and primer. This will help your makeup go on smoothly and stay in place.

Step 2: Choose Your Contour Products

For heart face shape, youâll want to choose a contour product that is a shade or two darker than your skin tone. You can use powder or cream products, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Map Out Your Face

To contour properly, youâll need to map out your face. Use a light foundation or concealer to draw lines on your face where you want to contour and highlight.

Step 4: Contour Your Cheeks

Use a contour brush to apply your contour product to the hollows of your cheeks. Blend upwards towards your ears to create a natural-looking shadow.

Step 5: Contour Your Forehead

Apply your contour product to the sides of your forehead, blending towards your temples. This will help to balance out the width of your forehead.

Step 6: Contour Your Jawline

Apply your contour product along your jawline, blending towards your chin. This will help to define your jawline and balance out your pointed chin.

Step 7: Highlight Your Face

Use a highlighter to bring forward the areas of your face that you want to emphasize. Apply it to the tops of your cheekbones, the bridge of your nose, and the center of your forehead.

Step 8: Blend, Blend, Blend

The key to natural-looking contouring is to blend everything out thoroughly. Use a clean brush or sponge to blend out any harsh lines or edges.

FAQs

Q: Can you contour a heart face shape without making the chin look too pointed?

A: Yes! Applying contour along the jawline can actually help to balance out a pointed chin. Just be sure to blend everything out thoroughly for a natural look.

Q: What type of contour product should I use for heart face shape?

A: You can use either powder or cream contour products, depending on your preference. Just be sure to choose a shade that is a few shades darker than your skin tone.

Q: How do I choose the right highlighter for my skin tone?

A: Look for a highlighter that complements your skin tone. If you have fair skin, choose a highlighter with a pink or champagne undertone. If you have medium or dark skin, opt for a gold or bronze highlighter.

Hair Care for Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you can also enhance your features with the right hairstyle. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as loose waves or curls.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or flat, as they can accentuate the wideness of your forehead.
  • Consider a side-swept fringe or bangs to balance out your forehead.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can elongate your face even more.
