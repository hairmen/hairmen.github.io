How To Cut A Pixie Cut Yourself With Clippers

Written by Luffy Sep 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon every few weeks to maintain your pixie cut? If you're feeling brave, you can try cutting your hair yourself with clippers. Not only will you save money, but you'll also have the freedom to cut your hair whenever you want. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut a pixie cut yourself with clippers.

How To

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first thing you need to do is gather all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of hair cutting clippers, scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror. Make sure your clippers are fully charged or plugged in before you start.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair into four parts - two on top and two on the sides. Use the hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin cutting the back of your hair first. Use the clippers with a number 3 guard to start. Start at the nape of your neck and work your way up to the crown. Make sure to use the comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly.

Once you're done with the back, move on to the sides. Use the same guard as before and cut your hair in an upward motion. Make sure to blend the back and sides together.

Finally, move on to the top of your hair. Use scissors to cut the hair on top to your desired length. Keep in mind that the shorter you cut it, the more maintenance it will require.

Step 4: Clean Up

After you've finished cutting your hair, use the clippers without a guard to clean up the back and sides. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs. Use the mirror to make sure everything looks even.

Tips

- Start with a longer guard and work your way to a shorter one to avoid cutting too much hair.

- Use a spray bottle to dampen your hair before cutting. This will make it easier to cut and reduce the risk of pulling your hair.

- Take your time and don't rush. It's better to cut a little at a time than to cut too much and regret it later.

FAQ

How often should I cut my pixie cut?

You should cut your pixie cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Can I cut my pixie cut with regular scissors?

While you can technically cut your hair with regular scissors, it's not recommended. Hair cutting scissors are specifically designed to cut hair and will give you a better result.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, make sure to take good care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray if you do. Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and avoid split ends.
