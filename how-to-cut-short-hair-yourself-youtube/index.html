How To Cut Short Hair Yourself Youtube A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to cut your short hair yourself, YouTube is a great resource. With the help of some helpful tutorials, you can learn how to give yourself a haircut that looks like it was done by a professional. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of cutting short hair yourself using YouTube videos.

Description

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're not used to doing it. But with the right tools and techniques, it can be done. One of the best ways to learn how to cut short hair yourself is by watching YouTube videos. There are plenty of tutorials available that cover everything from the basics to more advanced techniques.

How to Cut Short Hair Yourself YouTube: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather your tools

Before you get started, you'll need to gather some tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have some clips or hair ties to help you section your hair.

Step 2: Watch some tutorials

Now that you have your tools, it's time to start watching some tutorials. Search for "how to cut short hair yourself YouTube" and you'll find plenty of options. Watch a few different videos to get an idea of the techniques and styles you can try.

Step 3: Section your hair

Once you've watched some tutorials and know what style you want to try, it's time to section your hair. Use clips or hair ties to separate your hair into sections. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 4: Start cutting

Now it's time to start cutting. Use your comb to hold the section of hair you want to cut and then use your scissors to trim it. Remember to cut slowly and carefully. You can always cut more later, but you can't put hair back once it's been cut.

Step 5: Check your work

After you've cut a section of hair, check your work in the mirror. Make sure it's even and matches the rest of your hair. If it doesn't, you can always go back and fix it.

Step 6: Continue cutting

Continue cutting each section of hair until you've achieved the style you want. Remember to take your time and be patient. Cutting your own hair can be a slow process, but the end result will be worth it.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting short hair yourself:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to bigger ones.
  • Cut your hair when it's dry, not wet.
  • Don't try to cut too much hair at once.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from different angles.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting short hair yourself:

Q: Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

A: No, you should use hair cutting scissors. Regular scissors can damage your hair and create split ends.

Q: Is it easy to cut short hair yourself?

A: Cutting short hair yourself can be challenging, especially if you're not used to doing it. But with practice and patience, you can learn how to do it effectively.

Q: How often should I cut my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and the style you have. Generally, short hair should be cut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care

Cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience. But it's important to take care of your hair after you've cut it. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim any split ends as soon as you notice them to prevent them from spreading.

Cutting short hair yourself using YouTube tutorials can be a great way to save money and get the style you want. With the right tools, techniques, and patience, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.
