How Much Does It Cost To Get A Pixie Cut In 2023

Written by Eveline Nov 22, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you considering getting a pixie cut? This short and edgy hairstyle has been popular for years and is still in high demand. It's a great way to switch up your look and try something new. But before you jump in and book an appointment, you might be wondering: how much does it cost to get a pixie cut?

Table of Contents:

  • How to Get a Pixie Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Pixie Cut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut
  • FAQ: Answering Your Pixie Cut Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Pixie Cut

How to Get a Pixie Cut

Getting a pixie cut involves cutting your hair short, typically above the ears and around the nape of the neck. It's important to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting short hair and who can help you find the right style for your face shape and hair texture.

You'll also want to consider how often you'll need to get your hair trimmed to maintain your pixie cut. Short hair tends to grow faster, so you may need to visit the salon every 4-6 weeks to keep your cut looking fresh.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Pixie Cut

Here's what you can expect when you go to the salon for a pixie cut:

  1. Consultation: Your stylist will discuss your hair goals and help you decide on the right pixie cut for you.
  2. Wash and Prep: Your hair will be washed and prepped for cutting.
  3. Cutting: Your stylist will cut your hair to the desired length and shape.
  4. Styling: Your stylist will style your new pixie cut and show you how to maintain it at home.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Maintaining a pixie cut requires some effort, but with the right tips, you can keep your hair looking great:

  • Use the right products: Choose products that are specifically designed for short hair, like texturizing spray and pomade.
  • Get regular trims: Schedule appointments every 4-6 weeks to keep your pixie cut looking fresh.
  • Experiment with styling: Pixie cuts are versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, so have fun with it!

FAQ: Answering Your Pixie Cut Questions

Here are some common questions people have about getting a pixie cut:

How much does it cost to get a pixie cut?

The cost of a pixie cut can vary depending on where you live and the salon you go to. On average, you can expect to pay anywhere from $50 to $150 for a pixie cut.

Will a pixie cut look good on me?

Pixie cuts can be flattering on a variety of face shapes and hair textures. Your stylist can help you find the right pixie cut for your features.

How long does a pixie cut last?

A pixie cut can last anywhere from 4-8 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows and how you maintain it.

Hair Care Tips for Your Pixie Cut

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you maintain your pixie cut:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and frizz.

Now that you know how much it costs to get a pixie cut, you can make an informed decision about whether this trendy hairstyle is right for you.
