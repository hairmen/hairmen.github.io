How To Style Growing Hair Male Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Aug 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy with growing hair, you might be wondering how to style it. Growing hair can be challenging to work with, especially if you're used to shorter styles. However, with the right tips and tricks, you can achieve a stylish look that suits your growing hair. In this article, we'll show you how to style growing hair male, step by step.

How to Style Growing Hair Male

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type

The first step to styling your growing hair is to determine your hair type. This will help you choose the right products and techniques. For example, if you have curly hair, you'll need to use different products than if you have straight hair.

Step 2: Choose Your Style

Once you know your hair type, you can choose a style that works for you. There are many different styles to choose from, including the man bun, the undercut, and the slick back. Look for inspiration online or in magazines to find a style that you like.

Step 3: Keep Your Hair Healthy

Healthy hair is essential for styling. Make sure you're taking care of your hair by washing it regularly, using conditioner, and avoiding heat styling tools. You can also use a hair mask or oil treatment to nourish your hair.

Step 4: Use the Right Products

Using the right products is essential for styling your growing hair. Look for products that are designed for your hair type, such as pomades, gels, or waxes. Use a small amount of product and work it through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down.

Step 5: Experiment with Different Techniques

Experiment with different techniques to find what works best for your hair. For example, you can try blow-drying your hair to add volume or using a straightener to tame unruly strands. Don't be afraid to try new things!

Tips for Styling Growing Hair Male

Tip 1: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every other day, or even less if possible. You can also use dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

Tip 2: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

A wide-tooth comb is gentler on your hair than a brush, which can cause breakage and damage. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing, and then style as desired.

Tip 3: Embrace Your Natural Texture

Don't try to fight your hair's natural texture. Instead, embrace it! If you have curly hair, for example, try a style that highlights your curls. This will not only be easier to maintain, but it will also look more natural and effortless.

FAQ

What is the Best Product for Styling Growing Hair Male?

The best product for styling growing hair male depends on your hair type and the style you're going for. Pomades, gels, and waxes are all good options. Look for products that are lightweight and won't weigh down your hair.

How Often Should I Cut My Hair When It's Growing?

You should get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends. However, if you're growing your hair out, you might want to wait longer between cuts to allow it to grow.

Hair Care for Growing Hair Male

Tip 1: Use a Heat Protectant

If you're using heat styling tools, such as a blow dryer or straightener, make sure to use a heat protectant spray. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

Tip 2: Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles, such as ponytails or braids, as they can pull on your hair and cause breakage. If you must wear your hair up, opt for a looser style, such as a messy bun or a low ponytail.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims will keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This is especially important if you're growing your hair out, as split ends can cause your hair to break and become damaged.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your growing hair male with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
