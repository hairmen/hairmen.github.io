How Will I Know If A Hair Colour Will Suit Me

Written by Bardi Sep 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing a new hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you’re not sure if it will suit you. However, there are ways to determine if a hair colour will work for you or not. In this article, we’ll share some tips on how to know if a hair colour will suit you.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before you choose a hair colour, it’s important to consider your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour. These factors can help you determine which hair colour will work best for you. To get started, here are some steps you can take:

1. Determine Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair colour. You can determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If you can’t tell, you may have neutral undertones.

2. Consider Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also help determine which hair colour will suit you best. For example, if you have blue eyes, cool tones like ash blonde or cool brown may work well for you. If you have brown eyes, warm tones like caramel or golden brown may be more flattering.

3. Look at Your Natural Hair Colour

Your natural hair colour can also help you determine which hair colour will work for you. If you have warm undertones, a warm hair colour like golden blonde or copper may work well. If you have cool undertones, a cool hair colour like ash blonde or platinum may be more flattering.

Step by Step

Now that you’ve considered your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour, here are some steps you can take to determine if a hair colour will suit you:

1. Try a Virtual Hair Colour Try-On

Many websites and apps offer virtual hair colour try-ons, which allow you to see what you would look like with different hair colours. This can be a great way to experiment without committing to a colour.

2. Consult with a Professional

A professional hair stylist can help you determine which hair colour will work best for you. They can take into account your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour, and recommend a shade that will be flattering.

3. Test the Colour on a Small Section of Hair

If you’re still unsure, you can test the hair colour on a small section of hair before committing to the full colour. This will give you a better idea of how the colour will look on you.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair colour:

  • Consider the maintenance required for the hair colour you choose.
  • Choose a hair colour that complements your personal style.
  • Consider the season – lighter colours may be more flattering in the summer, while darker colours may be more flattering in the winter.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment – hair colour can always be changed if you don’t like it.

FAQ

What hair colours work best for cool skin tones?

Cool skin tones look best with cool hair colours like ash blonde, cool brown, and platinum.

What hair colours work best for warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones look best with warm hair colours like golden blonde, copper, and caramel.

How often do I need to touch up my hair colour?

This depends on the hair colour and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, you’ll need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Once you’ve chosen a hair colour, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep the colour looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically for colour-treated hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the colour.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, as they can also cause colour to fade.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your hair colour looks great and lasts as long as possible.

In conclusion, choosing a new hair colour can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your look. By considering your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour, you can determine which hair colour will work best for you. Don’t be afraid to experiment – hair colour can always be changed if you don’t like it. And remember to take care of your hair to keep the colour looking its best.
