How To Look Androgynous Makeup A Guide To Gender Neutral Beauty

Written by Petter Aug 14, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking to experiment with gender-neutral or androgynous makeup, you're in the right place. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know to achieve an androgynous look with makeup. From the basics of gender-neutral beauty to step-by-step instructions on how to create an androgynous makeup look, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

Description

Androgynous makeup is all about creating a look that is neither overtly feminine nor masculine. It's a gender-neutral look that celebrates individuality and self-expression. Androgynous makeup can be subtle or bold, but the key is to create a balance between traditionally masculine and feminine features. The result is a look that is unique, confident, and empowering.

How to Achieve an Androgynous Look with Makeup

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving an androgynous makeup look:

Step 1: Start with a Clean Canvas

Before you start applying makeup, make sure your face is clean and moisturized. Use a gentle cleanser to remove any dirt, oil, or makeup from your face.

Step 2: Apply Foundation

Choose a foundation that matches your skin tone and apply it evenly all over your face. Use a makeup sponge or brush to blend it in for a natural look. If you have any blemishes or dark circles, use a concealer to cover them up.

Step 3: Contour Your Face

To create a more androgynous look, you can use contouring to highlight or downplay certain features. Use a contouring powder or cream to create shadows and define your cheekbones, jawline, and temples. Blend it in well for a natural look.

Step 4: Shape Your Brows

Your eyebrows can make a big difference in creating an androgynous look. Use an eyebrow pencil or powder to shape and fill in your brows. For a more masculine look, create a straighter, thicker brow. For a more feminine look, create a more arched, thinner brow.

Step 5: Define Your Eyes

Use a neutral eyeshadow to define your eyes without making them look too feminine. Avoid glitter or shimmery eyeshadows, and stick to matte or satin finishes. Use a black or brown eyeliner to define your lash line and create a subtle wing if desired. Finish with mascara to define your lashes.

Step 6: Add a Touch of Color

To add some color to your androgynous look, choose a neutral blush and apply it to the apples of your cheeks. Use a lip balm or lip gloss in a natural shade to add some shine to your lips.

Tips for Achieving an Androgynous Look with Makeup

Here are some additional tips for achieving an androgynous look with makeup:

  • Experiment with different shades and finishes of makeup to find what works best for you.
  • Keep your makeup simple and minimal for a more natural look.
  • Use makeup to enhance your features, rather than hide them.
  • Have fun and embrace your individuality!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is androgynous makeup?

Androgynous makeup is a gender-neutral look that celebrates individuality and self-expression. It's a balance between traditionally masculine and feminine features, creating a unique, confident, and empowering look.

How can I create an androgynous makeup look?

To create an androgynous makeup look, start with a clean canvas and apply foundation evenly. Use contouring to define your features, shape your brows, define your eyes with neutral eyeshadow and eyeliner, and add a touch of color with blush and lip balm.

What are some tips for achieving an androgynous look with makeup?

Some tips for achieving an androgynous look with makeup include experimenting with different shades and finishes, keeping your makeup minimal and natural, using makeup to enhance your features, and embracing your individuality.

Hair Care for Androgynous Look

Hair can also play a big role in creating an androgynous look. Consider a short haircut with a side part or an undercut for a more masculine look. For a more feminine look, consider a longer haircut with bangs or layers. As with makeup, it's all about finding a balance that works for you and makes you feel confident and empowered.

And there you have it! With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to achieving a gender-neutral androgynous look with makeup. Remember to have fun and experiment with different shades and techniques to find what works best for you. Embrace your individuality and let your true self shine through!
