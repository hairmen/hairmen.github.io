How To Fade Into Top Of Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Oct 18, 2023 ¬∑ 5 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and trendy hairstyle? Fading into the top of hair is a popular and modern hairstyle that can give you a cool and edgy look. This hairstyle is all about blending the sides and back of your hair into a shorter length on the top. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to fade into top of hair. We will also answer frequently asked questions and provide tips on hair care. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Fade into Top of Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care

How to Fade into Top of Hair

Fading into top of hair is a complex hairstyle that requires a lot of precision and attention to detail. Before you start, you need to make sure that you have the right equipment and materials, such as clippers, scissors, and a comb. Here are the basic steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

The first step is to wash and dry your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture. Make sure to dry your hair completely before you start.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clipper Size

The next step is to choose the right clipper size. This will depend on the length of your hair and the desired style. Start with a larger clipper size and work your way down gradually.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Start cutting the sides and back of your hair with the clipper. Use a comb to lift the hair and make sure to cut it evenly. Move the clipper up gradually as you go along, so that you can fade into the top of the hair.

Step 4: Blend the Top and Sides

Once you have cut the sides and back, it's time to blend the top and sides. Use scissors to trim the hair on the top of your head. Make sure to blend it evenly with the sides and back of the head.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting and blending your hair, it's time to style it. You can use a hair gel or wax to create the desired look. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a detailed step-by-step guide on how to fade into top of hair:

Step 1: Gather Your Equipment

Before you start, make sure you have all the necessary equipment, including clippers, scissors, a comb, and a hair dryer. You may also need a mirror and a spray bottle filled with water.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it. Then, use a hair dryer to dry your hair completely.

Step 3: Choose the Right Clipper Size

Choose the right clipper size depending on the length of your hair and the desired style. Start with a larger clipper size and work your way down gradually.

Step 4: Cut the Sides and Back

Start cutting the sides and back of your hair with the clipper. Use a comb to lift the hair and make sure to cut it evenly. Move the clipper up gradually as you go along, so that you can fade into the top of the hair.

Step 5: Blend the Top and Sides

Use scissors to trim the hair on the top of your head. Make sure to blend it evenly with the sides and back of the head. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it gradually to blend it with the sides.

Step 6: Trim the Top

Once you have blended the top and sides, it's time to trim the top. Use scissors to cut the hair on the top of your head to the desired length. Make sure to trim it evenly and create the desired style.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting your hair, it's time to style it. You can use a hair gel or wax to create the desired look. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your hair after fading into the top:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on the length of your hair and how fast it grows. As a general rule, you should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair healthy.

Q: Can I fade into the top of my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can fade into the top of your hair at home, but it requires a lot of precision and attention to detail. Make sure you have the right equipment and follow the steps carefully.

Q: What type of hair works best for fading into the top?

A: Fading into the top works best for thick, straight, and wavy hair. If you have thin or curly hair, it may not work as well.

Hair Care

Fading into top of hair is a stylish and trendy hairstyle that requires a lot of attention to detail. By following the steps outlined in this article and using the tips for maintaining your hair, you can achieve a cool and edgy look that suits your personality and lifestyle. Remember to take good care of your hair by using the right products and getting regular trims. Happy styling!
