Wedding Hairdos For Shoulder Length Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a bride-to-be with shoulder length hair and unsure of what style to choose for your wedding day? Look no further! We have put together a complete guide of wedding hairdos for shoulder length hair that will have you feeling confident and beautiful on your special day. From elegant updos to boho braids, we have something for every style and preference. Let's dive in!

Description

Having shoulder length hair can be a challenge when it comes to finding the perfect wedding hairstyle. But fear not, there are plenty of options available that will complement your hair length and enhance your bridal look. Whether you prefer a classic updo, romantic curls, or something more modern, we have rounded up some of the best wedding hairdos for shoulder length hair that are sure to leave you feeling gorgeous and radiant.

How to Choose the Perfect Wedding Hairstyle

Before diving into the different wedding hairdos for shoulder length hair, it's important to consider a few things when choosing the perfect hairstyle for your big day. Consider your dress style, your face shape, and your personal style when selecting a hairstyle. You also want to make sure your hairstyle complements your overall bridal look and doesn't distract from it.

Step-by-Step Guide to Wedding Hairdos for Shoulder Length Hair

Now that you have some idea of what to consider when choosing a hairstyle, let's take a closer look at some of the best wedding hairdos for shoulder length hair.

Elegant Updo

An elegant updo is a classic choice for a wedding and works especially well for shoulder length hair. This style is perfect for brides who want to keep their hair out of their face while still looking chic and sophisticated. To achieve this look, start by curling your hair and then pulling it back into a low bun. Leave a few strands out in the front to frame your face and add a touch of elegance.

Boho Braids

If you're looking for a more relaxed and bohemian vibe, boho braids are a great option. This style is perfect for outdoor or rustic weddings and can be achieved by braiding your hair into loose, messy braids and then pulling them back into a low bun or leaving them down for a more carefree look.

Half-Up, Half-Down

The half-up, half-down style is a popular choice for brides with shoulder length hair as it allows you to show off your length while still keeping your hair out of your face. This style can be achieved by pulling back the top half of your hair and securing it with a clip or hair tie, while leaving the bottom half down and curled for a romantic look.

Sleek and Straight

If you have naturally straight hair, consider wearing it sleek and straight for your wedding day. This style is modern and chic and works especially well for brides who want a more minimalist look. Simply straighten your hair and add a shine serum for a polished finish.

Tips for Maintaining Your Wedding Hairdo

Once you have chosen your wedding hairdo, it's important to take care of your hair leading up to the big day to ensure your style looks its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your wedding hairdo for shoulder length hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your ends looking healthy
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools
  • Avoid washing your hair too often to prevent dryness

FAQs

What wedding hairdo is best for shoulder length hair?

The best wedding hairdo for shoulder length hair depends on your personal style and the overall look you're going for. Some popular options include elegant updos, boho braids, half-up, half-down styles, and sleek and straight looks.

How do I choose the perfect wedding hairdo?

When choosing a wedding hairdo, consider your dress style, face shape, and personal style. You also want to make sure your hairstyle complements your overall bridal look and doesn't distract from it.

How do I maintain my wedding hairdo for shoulder length hair?

To maintain your wedding hairdo for shoulder length hair, get regular trims, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, avoid using hot tools too often, use a heat protectant spray, and avoid washing your hair too often.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

Even after your wedding day, it's important to take care of your shoulder length hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week

With these tips and ideas for wedding hairdos for shoulder length hair, you're sure to find the perfect style for your big day. Remember to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful, and most importantly, have fun with it!
