How To Cut Men s Hair Long On Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, cutting your hair long on top is a popular choice. It's a versatile style that can be worn in many ways, and it's easy to maintain. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to cut men's hair long on top. From the tools you'll need to the techniques you'll use, we've got you covered.

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting, you'll need to gather some tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape or towel
  • Clipper guards
  • Water spray bottle

Preparation

Once you have your tools, it's time to prepare. Start by washing and drying your hair. You want it to be clean and free of any product buildup. Next, put on your haircutting cape or towel to protect your clothes from hair clippings.

Cutting Men's Hair Long on Top

Now it's time to start cutting. Start by sectioning off the top of your hair with a comb. You want to create a horizontal part from ear to ear, leaving the hair on the sides and back untouched. Use a clip to hold the top section in place.

Using your clippers and a #3 guard, start at the back of the head and work your way up. Run the clippers up the back of the head, following the natural curve of the skull. Make sure to keep the clippers level and use smooth, even strokes. Repeat this process on both sides of the head.

Next, switch to a #2 guard and repeat the process, but only go up about an inch from the previous cut. This will create a fade effect. Repeat this process with a #1 guard, going up another inch from the previous cut.

Blending the Hair

Now it's time to blend the hair. Remove the clip from the top section and use your scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. You can use a comb to help guide your scissors and create a more even cut. Make sure to blend the hair in with the previous cuts to create a seamless transition.

Texturizing the Hair

Once you've cut the hair to the desired length, it's time to texturize it. Use your scissors to point cut the hair, which means cutting into the hair at an angle to create texture. This will help the hair lay better and give it a more natural look.

Trimming the Hairline

Now it's time to trim the hairline. Use your clippers to trim around the ears and the back of the neck. You can also use scissors to trim any stray hairs that are sticking out.

Styling Men's Hair Long on Top

Now that you've cut your hair, it's time to style it. There are many ways to style men's hair long on top, including slicking it back, parting it to the side, or leaving it messy and textured. Experiment with different styles to find what works for you.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair Long on Top

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down. You can always go shorter, but you can't go back.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to take your time and make small cuts than to take off too much and have to start over.
  • Use the right tools. Cheap clippers or scissors can make cutting your hair more difficult and lead to uneven cuts.

FAQs

How often should I cut my hair if it's long on top?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Generally, you should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking its best.

Can I cut my hair long on top myself?

It's possible, but it can be difficult to get an even cut. If you're not confident in your cutting skills, it's best to leave it to a professional.

What products should I use to style my hair?

It depends on the style you're going for. For a slicked-back look, use a pomade or gel. For a messy, textured look, use a styling cream or wax.

Hair Care Tips for Men's Hair Long on Top

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep the ends from splitting.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut men's hair long on top. Whether you're looking for a new style or just want to switch things up, this versatile look is a great choice. Just remember to take your time and use the right tools, and you'll have a great-looking haircut in no time.
