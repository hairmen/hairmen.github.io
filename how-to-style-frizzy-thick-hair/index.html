How To Style Frizzy Thick Hair Tips Tricks

Written by Aprilia Sep 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have frizzy and thick hair, you know how frustrating it can be to style it. However, with the right techniques and products, you can tame your mane and achieve the look you desire. In this article, we'll take a look at how to style frizzy thick hair with ease.

How to

First, it's important to understand that frizzy hair is often caused by a lack of moisture. That's why you need to use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for dry or damaged hair. Look for products that contain nourishing ingredients such as coconut oil, argan oil, or shea butter.

Once you've washed and conditioned your hair, gently towel dry it by blotting it with a soft towel. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously as this can cause further frizz.

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help hydrate and tame any flyaways. You can also use a hair oil to add extra moisture and shine.

If you're planning to blow-dry your hair, make sure to use a heat protectant spray first. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

When blow-drying your hair, use a round brush to help smooth out any frizz. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, pulling the brush through your hair as you go.

For a more natural look, you can let your hair air dry. However, if you're in a rush, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help speed up the process.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style frizzy thick hair:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair with products designed for dry or damaged hair.

Step 2: Gently towel dry your hair.

Step 3: Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add extra moisture.

Step 4: If blow-drying, use a heat protectant spray and a round brush to smooth out any frizz.

Step 5: If air-drying, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to speed up the process.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling frizzy thick hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a detangling brush to prevent breakage and minimize frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair and make it even frizzier.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to help reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles such as braids, buns, and ponytails to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Are there any specific haircuts that work well for frizzy thick hair?

A: Yes, a layered haircut can help reduce bulk and frizz in thick hair. You can also try a long bob or a shoulder-length cut with layers.

Q: Can I use a hair mask to help tame my frizzy hair?

A: Absolutely! A weekly hair mask can help hydrate and nourish your hair, making it easier to style. Look for masks that contain moisturizing ingredients such as avocado, honey, or olive oil.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential for achieving a smooth and shiny look. Here are some hair care tips for frizzy thick hair:

  • Wash your hair no more than twice a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach or hair dye, as they can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep it looking healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily style your frizzy thick hair and achieve the look you want. Remember to take care of your hair by using nourishing products and minimizing heat damage. Happy styling!
