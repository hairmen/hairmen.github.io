How To Know If You Would Look Good With Bleached Hair

Written by Bardi Aug 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering bleaching your hair but not sure if it will suit you? Bleaching your hair can be a major change and it's important to consider if it will look good on you before taking the plunge. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to know if you would look good with bleached hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Bleached hair is a popular trend that has been around for many years. It involves removing the natural pigment from your hair using a bleach or lightening agent. This can be a drastic change, especially if you have dark hair, and it's natural to wonder if it will look good on you. There are a few factors to consider when determining if you would look good with bleached hair.

How to

One way to determine if bleached hair would suit you is to consider your skin tone. If you have a warm skin tone, which means you have yellow or golden undertones, bleached hair can complement your complexion. On the other hand, if you have a cool skin tone, which means you have pink or blue undertones, bleached hair may wash you out.

Another factor to consider is your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, bleaching it can be a dramatic change. It's important to consider if you're ready for that level of change and if it will suit your skin tone. If you have lighter hair, such as blonde or light brown, bleaching can still be a big change but may not be as drastic.

It's also important to consider your personal style and whether bleached hair would fit in with your overall look. If you have an edgy or alternative style, bleached hair can be a great fit. However, if you have a more conservative or classic style, bleached hair may not be the best choice.

Step by Step

Step 1: Consider your skin tone

Step 2: Consider your natural hair color

Step 3: Consider your personal style

Tips

Tip 1: Try a temporary hair color first to see if you like the look before committing to bleaching your hair.

Tip 2: Consider consulting with a hairstylist before bleaching your hair to get their professional opinion.

Tip 3: Take good care of your bleached hair by using a deep conditioning treatment once a week and avoiding heat styling tools.

FAQ

Q: Will bleaching my hair damage it?

A: Bleaching your hair can cause damage, but using a good quality hair care routine can help minimize the damage.

Q: Can I bleach my hair at home?

A: While it is possible to bleach your hair at home, it's recommended to consult with a professional hairstylist to ensure the best results and minimize damage to your hair.

Hair Care

After bleaching your hair, it's important to take good care of it to minimize damage and keep it looking healthy. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible and use a heat protectant spray when you do use them. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help maintain your hair's color and avoid fading.

In conclusion, there are a few factors to consider when determining if you would look good with bleached hair. Consider your skin tone, natural hair color, and personal style before making the decision to bleach your hair. Always take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
