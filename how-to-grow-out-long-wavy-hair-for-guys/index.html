How To Grow Out Long Wavy Hair For Guys

Written by Daniel Sep 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy with wavy hair, you may be wondering how to grow out your hair without it turning into a frizzy mess. It can be a challenge, but with the right hair care routine and a little patience, you can achieve the long, wavy locks you've always dreamed of. In this article, we'll go through the steps you need to take to grow out your long wavy hair, as well as some tips and FAQs to help you along the way.

How to Grow Out Long Wavy Hair for Guys

The first step to growing out your long wavy hair is to be patient. It takes time for hair to grow, so don't expect to see results overnight. Here are some tips to help you along the way:

Step 1: Start with a Fresh Cut

Before you start growing out your hair, it's a good idea to get a fresh cut. You don't need to go super short, but trimming the ends will help get rid of any split ends and prevent your hair from looking too unruly as it grows out.

Step 2: Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in how your hair looks as it grows out. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed for wavy hair, as they will help define your waves and control frizz. You may also want to use a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to help tame your hair.

Step 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz. If you need to use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting on low. It's also a good idea to let your hair air dry as much as possible.

Step 4: Be Gentle When Brushing

Wavy hair can be prone to tangling, so it's important to be gentle when brushing it. Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush specifically designed for wavy hair, and start at the ends and work your way up to avoid pulling and damaging your hair.

Tips for Growing Out Long Wavy Hair for Guys

Here are some additional tips to help you grow out your long wavy hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as a messy bun or a half-up, half-down style.
  • Be patient! Growing out your hair takes time, but it will be worth it in the end.

FAQs About Growing Out Long Wavy Hair for Guys

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing out long wavy hair:

Q: How long will it take to grow out my hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows, but it can take anywhere from six months to a year or more to grow out long hair.

Q: Do I need to use a special shampoo and conditioner for wavy hair?

A: Yes, using a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for wavy hair can help define your waves and control frizz.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's a good idea to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Long Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your long wavy hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can dry out your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be on your way to growing out long wavy hair that looks healthy and stylish. Remember to be patient, take good care of your hair, and experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
