How To Get Volume In Short Curly Hair

Written by Eveline Aug 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short curly hair, you might be wondering how to get more volume. Fortunately, there are several techniques and products that can help you achieve the volume you desire. In this article, we will discuss how to get volume in short curly hair, step by step, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

Short curly hair can be beautiful, but it can also be challenging to style. Without the right techniques and products, curly hair can easily look flat or frizzy. Adding volume can make your hair appear fuller and more vibrant, giving you the confidence to rock your curls.

How to Get Volume in Short Curly Hair

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to getting volume in short curly hair is to start with clean hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils. This will help your curls maintain their shape and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Apply Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair while it's still damp. Mousse can help add volume and hold to your curls. Be sure to distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair, from the roots to the ends.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. A diffuser helps distribute heat evenly and prevent frizz. While drying your hair, scrunch your curls with your hands to encourage volume.

Step 4: Tease Your Roots

Tease your roots with a teasing comb or brush to add even more volume. Be gentle while teasing your hair, as curly hair can be prone to breakage. Focus on teasing the crown of your head to create lift.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your style with a light-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place. Be sure to hold the hairspray can at least 10 inches away from your hair to prevent a sticky or stiff finish.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you get more volume in your short curly hair:

  • Try using a root-lifting spray for extra lift.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or creams, as they can weigh down your curls.
  • Sleep with your hair in a pineapple or loose bun to help maintain volume overnight.
  • Consider getting a layered haircut to add more dimension to your curls.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a diffuser on high heat?

A: It's best to use a diffuser on low to medium heat to prevent damage to your hair. High heat can cause your hair to become brittle and prone to breakage.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people with curly hair can go several days without washing, while others may need to wash more frequently. Experiment with different washing schedules to find what works best for your hair.

Q: Can I use a curling iron to add volume?

A: Yes, you can use a curling iron to add volume to your short curly hair. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using the curling iron to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to maintain its shape and prevent frizz. Here are a few tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when dry, as it can cause frizz.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the volume you desire in your short curly hair. Experiment with different products and styles to find what works best for your hair type and personal preferences. Embrace your curls and rock your voluminous style with confidence!
