How To Wear Your Hair Up With Halo Extensions

Written by Eveline Oct 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering how to wear your hair up with halo extensions? Look no further! In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect updo with your halo extensions. From braids to buns, you'll be able to rock any hairstyle with ease.

Description

Halo extensions are a game-changer when it comes to adding length and volume to your hair. They are easy to use and don't require any clips or glue. However, wearing your hair up with halo extensions can be a bit tricky. You want to make sure that the extensions are secure and not visible, but you also want to create a natural-looking hairstyle.

How to

Here are the steps to follow when wearing your hair up with halo extensions:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. Make sure that your hair is completely dry before you begin.

Step 2: Put on Your Halo Extensions

Place your halo extensions on your head, making sure that they sit comfortably and securely. Ensure that the wire is hidden under your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into two parts: the top and the bottom. Use a hair tie to secure the top section of your hair.

Step 4: Braid the Bottom Section

Braid the bottom section of your hair and secure it with a hair tie. This will provide a solid base for your updo.

Step 5: Create Your Updo

Now it's time to create your updo! There are many different styles you can try, including a messy bun, a sleek ponytail, or a braided updo. Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place and make sure that the halo extensions are hidden.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your updo with a spritz of hairspray to keep everything in place. And voila! You're ready to rock your new hairstyle.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing your hair up with halo extensions:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to ensure they are hidden.
  • Don't pull your hair too tight as this can cause discomfort and damage to your hair and extensions.
  • Experiment with different updo styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your extensions looking their best.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my halo extensions in a high ponytail?

A: Yes! Halo extensions can be worn in a high ponytail. Make sure that the wire is hidden and use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Q: How do I know if my halo extensions are the right color?

A: It's important to choose the right color for your halo extensions. You can match your extensions to your natural hair color or get a shade lighter or darker for a more highlighted effect. You can also consult with a hair extension specialist for advice.

Q: How long can I wear my halo extensions?

A: Halo extensions can be worn for up to 8 hours at a time. It's important to take breaks and remove them to give your hair and scalp a rest.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential when wearing halo extensions. Here are some tips to keep your extensions looking their best:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging the extensions.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on the extensions as this can cause damage.
  • Brush your hair gently to avoid pulling on the extensions.
  • Store your halo extensions in a cool, dry place when not in use.

Now that you know how to wear your hair up with halo extensions, it's time to try out some new hairstyles! With the right technique and a little practice, you can achieve the perfect updo every time.
