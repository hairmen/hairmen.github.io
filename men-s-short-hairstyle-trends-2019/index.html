Men s Short Hairstyle Trends 2019 A Guide To The Latest Styles

Written by Eveline Oct 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a fresh new look this year, you might want to consider one of the many men's short hairstyle trends that were popular in 2019. From buzz cuts to textured crops, there are plenty of options to choose from that will help you stand out from the crowd.

Description

Men's short hairstyle trends 2019 were all about clean, neat cuts that were easy to maintain and style. Many of the most popular styles featured short sides and back with longer hair on top, allowing for plenty of versatility when it comes to styling.

How to

If you're interested in trying out one of the 2019 men's short hairstyle trends, the first step is to find a skilled barber or stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. From there, you'll need to decide on a specific style that works with your hair type and face shape.

Once you've got your new cut, you'll need to learn how to style it properly. Many of the 2019 trends involve using pomade or wax to create a textured, messy look, while others require a more traditional approach with a comb and gel.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving one of the most popular men's short hairstyle trends of 2019:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of pomade or wax to your fingertips.
  3. Rub your fingertips together to warm up the product.
  4. Work the product into your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up.
  5. Mess up your hair with your fingers to create a textured, messy look.
  6. Use a comb to create a side part, if desired.
  7. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when trying out one of the men's short hairstyle trends from 2019:

  • Choose a style that works with your hair type and face shape.
  • Invest in high-quality styling products to achieve the best results.
  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly to keep your cut looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about men's short hairstyle trends 2019:

Q: What are some of the most popular men's short hairstyle trends from 2019?

A: Some of the most popular styles from 2019 include the textured crop, the buzz cut, and the slicked-back undercut.

Q: Are these styles easy to maintain?

A: Yes, most of the men's short hairstyle trends from 2019 are designed to be low-maintenance and easy to style.

Q: Will these styles work for all hair types?

A: Some styles may work better for certain hair types than others, so it's important to consult with a barber or stylist to find the right look for you.

Hair Care

To keep your men's short hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This means washing and conditioning regularly, using high-quality styling products, and protecting your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or styling with hot tools.

With a little bit of effort and the right styling products, you can achieve the perfect men's short hairstyle trend from 2019 and look your best all year long.
