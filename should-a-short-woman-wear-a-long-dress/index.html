Should A Short Woman Wear A Long Dress

Written by Petter Aug 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

As a short woman, it's common to feel like long dresses are not for you. However, long dresses can actually be very flattering on shorter frames when worn correctly. In this article, we'll explore whether short women should wear long dresses, how to style them, and some tips for making the most of this trend.

How to

The key to pulling off a long dress as a short woman is all in the proportions. Opt for dresses that are fitted at the waist to create the illusion of longer legs. A-line or fit-and-flare dresses can also be very flattering, as they skim over the hips and create a longer line.

Choose the Right Length

When it comes to length, shorter women should avoid dresses that hit at the ankle, as they can make you appear even shorter. Instead, opt for dresses that hit just above the ankle, or mid-calf. These lengths will elongate your legs and create a more balanced silhouette.

Accessorize Strategically

When wearing a long dress, it's important to accessorize strategically. Avoid chunky, heavy jewelry or belts, as they can overwhelm your frame. Instead, opt for delicate jewelry and a thin belt to define your waist.

Choose the Right Shoes

Lastly, your shoes can make or break this look. Avoid flats or shoes with ankle straps, as they can visually cut off your legs and make you appear shorter. Instead, opt for nude heels or sandals with a low heel to create the illusion of longer legs.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide for short women on how to wear a long dress:

  1. Choose a long dress that's fitted at the waist or an A-line silhouette.
  2. Opt for a dress that hits just above the ankle or mid-calf.
  3. Accessorize with delicate jewelry and a thin belt.
  4. Choose nude heels or sandals with a low heel.
  5. Enjoy your elongated silhouette!

Tips

Here are some additional tips for short women looking to wear long dresses:

  • Choose a dress with a high waistline to create the illusion of longer legs.
  • Look for dresses with vertical stripes or patterns to elongate your frame.
  • Avoid oversized or baggy dresses, as they can overwhelm your frame.
  • Choose lightweight fabrics that drape well and don't add bulk.
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find what works best for your body type.

FAQ

Q: Can short women wear maxi dresses?

A: Yes, short women can wear maxi dresses as long as they choose the right length and silhouette. Opt for a dress that hits just above the ankle or mid-calf, and choose a fitted or A-line silhouette.

Q: What shoes should short women wear with a long dress?

A: Short women should choose nude heels or sandals with a low heel to create the illusion of longer legs. Avoid flats or shoes with ankle straps, as they can visually cut off your legs and make you appear shorter.

Q: Can short women wear long dresses to formal events?

A: Yes, short women can definitely wear long dresses to formal events. Opt for a dress with a fitted waistline and a floor-length hemline.

Hair Care

When wearing a long dress, it's important to consider your hair as well. Opt for an updo or half-up, half-down hairstyle to elongate your neck and make you appear taller. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as they can make you appear shorter.

In conclusion, short women can definitely wear long dresses as long as they choose the right length, silhouette, and accessories. Follow these tips and you'll be sure to rock this trend with confidence!
