How To See If Bangs Look Good On You Tips And Tricks In 2023

Written by Daniel Nov 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are thinking of getting bangs, you may be wondering if they will suit your face shape and style. Bangs are a great way to change up your look without committing to a drastic haircut. However, not everyone can pull off bangs. In this article, we will discuss how to see if bangs look good on you.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Considering Your Hair Type
  • Choosing the Right Bangs Style
  • Framing Your Face with Bangs
  • Trying Out Bangs without Cutting Your Hair

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in determining if bangs will suit you is to determine your face shape. Your face shape will help you decide which type of bangs will complement your features.

There are six common face shapes: round, square, heart, oval, diamond, and rectangle. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and stand in front of a mirror. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror. Stand back and look at the shape you have drawn.

If your face is round, heart-shaped, or oval, you can usually pull off bangs. If your face is square or rectangular, it may be more difficult to find a bangs style that flatters your features.

Considering Your Hair Type

Another factor to consider when deciding if bangs will suit you is your hair type. If you have fine hair, wispy bangs may be the best option for you. If you have thick hair, blunt bangs may be a better choice.

If you have curly hair, you may want to avoid getting bangs. Curly hair tends to be more difficult to manage, and bangs can be tricky to style.

Choosing the Right Bangs Style

Once you have determined your face shape and hair type, it's time to choose the right bangs style. There are many different types of bangs to choose from, including:

  • Blunt bangs
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Wispy bangs
  • Micro bangs
  • Curtain bangs

The type of bangs that will look best on you will depend on your face shape and hair type. If you have a round face, side-swept bangs can help elongate your face. If you have a heart-shaped face, wispy bangs can soften your features.

Framing Your Face with Bangs

Another factor to consider when deciding if bangs will suit you is how they will frame your face. Bangs can be used to highlight your best features and draw attention away from areas you may not love.

For example, if you have a prominent forehead, blunt bangs can help balance out your features. If you have a long face, wispy bangs can help shorten the appearance of your face.

Trying Out Bangs without Cutting Your Hair

If you are still unsure if bangs will suit you, there are ways to try them out without committing to a haircut. You can use hair extensions or clip-in bangs to experiment with different styles.

You can also use virtual try-on tools to see how different types of bangs will look on you. Many hair styling apps and websites offer this feature.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

Once you have decided to get bangs, it's important to take care of them properly. Here are some hair care tips for bangs:

  • Wash and condition your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh and clean.
  • Blow-dry your bangs immediately after washing to prevent them from becoming frizzy.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to style your bangs as desired.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking neat and tidy.

FAQs

Can I pull off bangs if I have curly hair?

It can be more difficult to pull off bangs if you have curly hair. However, if you have naturally straight hair, you can use a flat iron to straighten your bangs.

What type of bangs should I get if I have a round face?

If you have a round face, side-swept bangs can help elongate your face. Wispy bangs can also soften your features.

What type of bangs should I get if I have a heart-shaped face?

If you have a heart-shaped face, wispy bangs can help soften your features. Blunt bangs can also help balance out a prominent forehead.

In conclusion, there are many factors to consider when deciding if bangs will suit you. By determining your face shape, hair type, and choosing the right style, you can achieve a flattering and stylish look with bangs. Remember to take care of your bangs properly with regular washing and styling to keep them looking their best.
