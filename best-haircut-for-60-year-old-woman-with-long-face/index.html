Best Haircut For 60 Year Old Woman With Long Face A Guide

Written by Petter Nov 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As we age, our hair changes in texture, thickness, and color. For women over 60 years old with long faces, finding the right haircut can be challenging. A good haircut can enhance your features, boost your confidence, and make you look more youthful.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A long face is characterized by a high forehead, long cheeks, and a pointed chin. The best haircut for a 60-year-old woman with a long face is one that creates balance and softens the facial features. A good haircut should also be easy to maintain and style.

How to

Before getting a haircut, you should consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. A long face requires a haircut that adds width and volume to the sides of the face. Layers and curls can help create this effect. If you have thin hair, a short or medium-length haircut can make your hair look fuller.

Step by step

1. Start by consulting with a hairstylist who has experience working with mature clients.

2. Show your hairstylist pictures of hairstyles you like and discuss which one would work best for your face shape and hair texture.

3. Consider adding layers or curls to your hair to add volume to the sides of your face.

4. If you have thin hair, consider a short or medium-length haircut to make your hair look fuller.

5. Ask your hairstylist for styling tips and product recommendations to maintain your new haircut at home.

Tips

1. Avoid straight, blunt haircuts that can make your face look longer.

2. Consider adding bangs to your haircut to soften your forehead.

3. Use volumizing products to add body to your hair.

4. Regularly trim your hair to maintain the shape and avoid split ends.

FAQ

Q. What is the best haircut for a 60-year-old woman?

A. The best haircut for a 60-year-old woman depends on her face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. A good haircut should create balance and enhance her features.

Q. How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A. It is recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and avoid split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, you should:

1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

2. Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with hot tools.

3. Avoid using too many hair products that can weigh down your hair.

4. Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

5. Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth and strength.

By following these tips and getting the right haircut, you can look and feel your best at any age.
