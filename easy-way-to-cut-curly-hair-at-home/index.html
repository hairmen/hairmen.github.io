Easy Way To Cut Curly Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting curly hair at home can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a breeze. Not to mention, it can save you time and money in the long run. In this guide, we will walk you through the easy way to cut curly hair at home. From the tools you need to the step-by-step process, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents:

  1. Tools You Need
  2. How to Cut Curly Hair at Home
  3. Tips for Cutting Curly Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care After Cutting

Tools You Need:

Before you start cutting your curly hair, make sure you have the right tools. Here are some of the essential tools you need for cutting curly hair at home:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Sectioning clips
  • Spray bottle
  • Blow dryer with diffuser attachment (optional)

Having these tools on hand will make the process much easier.

How to Cut Curly Hair at Home:

Now that you have the right tools, it's time to start cutting your curly hair. Follow these step-by-step instructions:

Step 1: Wash and Detangle Your Hair

Start by washing and detangling your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you get an even cut. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while it's wet.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Once your hair is detangled, use sectioning clips to divide it into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. This will make it easier to cut each section evenly.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Starting with one section, take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle. This will help create layers and add volume. Make sure to cut each section evenly and use the previous section as a guide.

Step 4: Repeat on All Sections

Repeat this process on all four sections of your hair. Take your time and make sure to cut each section evenly.

Step 5: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished cutting, check your hair for evenness. If you notice any uneven sections, go back and trim them until your hair is even.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Finally, style your hair as desired. You can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair:

Here are some additional tips to help you cut your curly hair at home:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't add hair back once it's cut.
  • Use the point-cutting technique to create layers and add volume.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's dry. Curly hair shrinks when it dries, so you may end up cutting more than you intended.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so don't be afraid to take a break and come back to it later.

FAQs:

1. Can I cut my curly hair at home?

Yes, you can cut your curly hair at home. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the step-by-step instructions.

2. How often should I cut my curly hair?

It's recommended to cut curly hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

3. Can I cut my hair when it's wet?

It's best to cut curly hair when it's damp, not wet. Wet hair can shrink when it dries, which can result in uneven cuts.

Hair Care After Cutting:

After cutting your curly hair at home, it's important to take care of it to maintain healthy, beautiful curls. Here are some tips:

  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it when it's dry.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Cutting curly hair at home doesn't have to be scary. With the right tools and techniques, you can give yourself a beautiful, even cut. Just remember to take your time, start with small cuts, and follow the step-by-step instructions. Happy cutting!
