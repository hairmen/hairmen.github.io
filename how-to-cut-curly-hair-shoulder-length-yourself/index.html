How To Cut Curly Hair Shoulder Length Yourself

Written by Oliver Sep 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money and time at the hair salon just to get a shoulder-length cut for your curly hair? Why not try cutting your own hair at home? With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect shoulder-length cut for your curls. This article will guide you through the process step by step and provide tips and hair care advice to help you achieve salon-worthy results.

Tools You Will Need

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Clips to hold sections of hair
  • A mirror

Step 1: Wash and Detangle Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Make sure to use products that are suitable for your curly hair type. Once you have washed your hair, detangle it using a wide-tooth comb. Make sure to remove all knots and tangles, as this will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips. Start by dividing your hair down the middle, from your forehead to the nape of your neck. Then, divide each side in half, creating four sections in total. Clip each section out of the way, leaving one section at the back to start cutting.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Starting from the back section, take a small section of hair and twist it. Use your scissors to cut the ends of the hair, making sure to cut straight across. Make sure to only cut a small amount at a time, as curly hair tends to shrink when it dries. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way around your head.

Step 4: Check for Balance

Once you have finished cutting your hair, check for balance by looking in the mirror. Make sure that each side is even and adjust any uneven sections as needed. You can also use your scissors to trim any stray hairs that you may have missed.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair

  • Only cut a small amount of hair at a time, as curly hair shrinks when it dries.
  • Twist each section of hair before cutting to create a more even cut.
  • Avoid cutting your hair when it is wet, as this can cause the hair to appear shorter when it dries.
  • Invest in a good pair of scissors to ensure a clean cut.

FAQ

Can I cut my curly hair when it is wet?

It is not recommended to cut curly hair when it is wet, as this can cause the hair to appear shorter when it dries. It is best to cut your hair when it is dry to achieve the most accurate results.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It is recommended to cut your curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair requires a little extra effort to keep it healthy and hydrated. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

By following these tips and using the right techniques, you can achieve a perfect shoulder-length cut for your curly hair at home. Remember to take your time and only cut a small amount of hair at a time to achieve the best results.
