Hairstyles For Very Thin Hair On Top Tips And Tricks For A Fuller Look

Written by Aprilia Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read
If you have very thin hair on top, you know how frustrating it can be to style your hair. No matter what you do, your hair always seems to look limp and lifeless. But don't worry, there are plenty of hairstyles that can help give your hair more volume and a fuller look. In this article, we'll go over some of the best hairstyles for very thin hair on top, as well as some tips and tricks for hair care and styling.

  • Hairstyles for Very Thin Hair on Top

How to Style Very Thin Hair on Top

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, let's talk about some general tips for styling very thin hair on top. First and foremost, it's important to use the right products. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are designed for thin hair, as these will help add volume and thickness. Avoid heavy styling products like waxes and gels, as these can weigh down your hair and make it look even thinner. When blow-drying your hair, use a round brush to add volume at the roots. Start at the crown of your head and work your way down, using the brush to lift your hair up and away from your scalp. Finish with a blast of cool air to set the style. Finally, consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair look even thinner.

Hairstyles for Very Thin Hair on Top

Now, let's take a look at some specific hairstyles that work well for very thin hair on top. 1. Pixie Cut A pixie cut can be a great option for very thin hair on top. The short, layered style adds volume and texture to your hair, while also giving you a trendy and stylish look. 2. Bob Another great option is a bob. This classic style works well for thin hair, as it creates a full, voluminous look. Consider adding layers or bangs to add even more texture and movement to your hair. 3. Layered Cut A layered cut is a great way to add volume to thin hair. Ask your stylist to cut your hair in layers, with shorter layers on top and longer layers on the bottom. This will create a full, bouncy look that's perfect for thin hair. 4. Updo For a special occasion, consider an updo. This style works well for thin hair, as it allows you to create volume and height at the crown of your head. Consider adding a few curls or waves to add even more texture and dimension to your hair.

Tips and Tricks for Hair Care and Styling

In addition to the specific hairstyles we've mentioned, there are a few general tips and tricks that can help you care for and style your thin hair. 1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are designed to add volume and thickness to hair. 2. Avoid heavy styling products. Heavy gels and waxes can weigh down your hair and make it look even thinner. 3. Blow-dry your hair upside down. This will help add volume at the roots and create a fuller look. 4. Consider hair extensions. If you're looking for a more dramatic change, consider hair extensions. These can add length and volume to your hair, giving you more styling options.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair long if it's very thin on top? A: Absolutely! While shorter styles may be more flattering for thin hair, you can still wear your hair long if you prefer. Just be sure to add layers and texture to create a fuller look. Q: Are there any hairstyles I should avoid if my hair is very thin on top? A: Yes. Avoid styles that are very sleek and flat, as these will only emphasize thinness. Instead, opt for styles that add volume and movement to your hair. Q: How can I make my hair look thicker? A: In addition to the tips and tricks we've mentioned, consider using a thickening spray or powder. These products can help add volume and texture to your hair, giving it a fuller look.

Conclusion

Having very thin hair on top can be frustrating, but with the right hairstyles and products, you can create a full, voluminous look. Try out some of the styles we've mentioned, and don't be afraid to experiment with different products and techniques. With a little bit of effort, you can have the full, luscious hair you've always wanted.
