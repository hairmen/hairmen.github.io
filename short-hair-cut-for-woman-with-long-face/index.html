Short Hair Cut For Women With Long Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 28, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a long face, it can be challenging to find the right hairstyle that flatters your features. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll be discussing the best short hair cut for women with long faces, along with tips, step-by-step guides, and hair care advice. Let’s dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts are perfect for women with long faces as they help to balance out the length of the face. A short haircut can also add volume and texture to your hair, making it look thicker and fuller. There are various types of short haircuts that you can choose from, including pixie cuts, bobs, and choppy layers. The key is to find a cut that suits your face shape and hair type.

How to

Before you decide to chop off your locks, it’s important to do some research and find a short haircut that suits you. Look for inspiration from magazines, social media, or ask your hairstylist for recommendations. Once you’ve found a style that you like, it’s time to book an appointment with your hairstylist.

Consultation

During your consultation, discuss your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle with your hairstylist. This will help them to suggest a short haircut that suits your needs. Your hairstylist will also be able to advise you on how to style and maintain your new haircut.

Preparation

Before you head to the salon, make sure to wash your hair and avoid using any hair products. This will make it easier for your hairstylist to cut your hair.

Step-by-Step

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Your hairstylist will begin by sectioning your hair and pinning it up. This will allow them to work on one section at a time.

Step 2: Cut Your Hair

Your hairstylist will use a pair of scissors or clippers to cut your hair. They will work on each section, cutting the hair to the desired length and shape.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once your hair has been cut, your hairstylist will style it using a blow dryer, flat iron, or curling iron. They may also use hair products to add texture or hold.

Tips

Choose the Right Cut

When choosing a short haircut, make sure to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. If you have a long face, opt for a style that adds volume and width to your hair, such as a bob or pixie cut.

Maintain Your Hair

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking fresh. Make sure to visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

FAQ

What short haircut is best for a long face?

Bob haircuts, pixie cuts, and choppy layers are all great options for women with long faces.

Will a short haircut make my face look longer?

No, a short haircut can actually help to balance out the length of your face and add volume to your hair.

Hair Care

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Regularly washing your hair will help to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair, so try to avoid using a blow dryer, flat iron, or curling iron too often. If you do use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.

Short haircuts are a great option for women with long faces. They can help to balance out the length of your face and add volume to your hair. To find the perfect short haircut for you, make sure to consult with your hairstylist and consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. With the right cut and proper hair care, you can rock a short haircut with confidence!
