Long On Top Short On Sides Boy Hair Cut

Written by Christine Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

The long on top short on sides boy hair cut has been gaining popularity in recent years, and it's easy to see why. This haircut is stylish, versatile, and perfect for boys who want to achieve a trendy and modern look. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care.

Description

The long on top short on sides boy hair cut is a style where the hair on the top of the head is longer than the hair on the sides and back. This creates a contrast between the two lengths, which makes the style more interesting and eye-catching. The hair on the top can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, messy, or spiked. The sides and back are usually buzzed or cropped short to create a clean, sharp look.

How to

The long on top short on sides boy hair cut is best done by a professional hairstylist, but you can also do it at home if you have the right tools and skills. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner to make it clean and easy to work with. Then, dry your hair with a towel or blow dryer, depending on your preference.

Step 2: Cut the sides and back

Use clippers or scissors to cut the hair on the sides and back. Start with a longer guard size and gradually go shorter until you achieve the desired length. Make sure to blend the hair at the top of the sides and back to avoid a harsh contrast.

Step 3: Cut the top

Use scissors to cut the hair on the top of the head. Start with longer sections and gradually go shorter until you achieve the desired length. You can also add layers or texture to create a more interesting look.

Step 4: Style the hair

Apply a styling product to the hair on top, such as pomade, wax, or gel. Style it according to your preference, whether it's slicked back, messy, or spiked. You can also use a hair dryer to add volume and shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a long on top short on sides boy hair cut:

  • Bring a picture of the style you want to show to your hairstylist.
  • Keep your hair type and face shape in mind when choosing a style.
  • Use quality hair products to maintain the style and protect your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep the style looking fresh and clean.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the long on top short on sides boy hair cut:

Q: Is this style suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this style can work for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, the length and texture of the hair on top may need to be adjusted accordingly.

Q: How often should I get a trim?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the style loses its shape. Generally, you should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Q: Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

A: Yes, the long on top short on sides boy hair cut is versatile enough to be styled differently for different occasions. For example, you can slick it back for a formal event or spike it up for a casual outing.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to take care of your hair after getting a long on top short on sides boy hair cut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

Overall, the long on top short on sides boy hair cut is a trendy and stylish choice for boys who want to stand out. With the right tools, skills, and care, you can rock this haircut and make it your own.
