What To Put On Extremely Damaged Hair Tips Products And Faqs

Written by Daniel Oct 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have extremely damaged hair, you might be feeling hopeless and unsure of what to do next. The good news is that there are ways to repair and revitalize your hair, even if it seems beyond repair. In this article, we'll discuss what to put on extremely damaged hair, including tips, products, and frequently asked questions.

How to Determine if Your Hair is Extremely Damaged

Before we dive into what to put on extremely damaged hair, let's first discuss how to determine if your hair is in fact extremely damaged. Some signs of extremely damaged hair include:

  • Split ends
  • Brittleness
  • Dryness
  • Lack of shine
  • Breakage

If your hair is experiencing one or more of these symptoms, it's likely that it is extremely damaged and in need of repair.

What to Put on Extremely Damaged Hair: Products

When it comes to what to put on extremely damaged hair, there are a variety of products available that can help repair and revitalize your hair. Some of the best products to use on extremely damaged hair include:

Deep-Conditioning Treatments

Deep-conditioning treatments are an excellent way to nourish and repair your hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like keratin, biotin, and argan oil, which can help strengthen and hydrate your hair.

Protein Treatments

Protein treatments are another great option for extremely damaged hair. They help to rebuild and strengthen your hair's structure, which can help prevent further damage and breakage.

Leave-In Conditioners

Leave-in conditioners are a great way to provide your hair with ongoing hydration and nourishment. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair after washing and conditioning, and leave it in to help protect and strengthen your hair throughout the day.

What to Put on Extremely Damaged Hair: Tips

In addition to using the right products, there are several tips you can follow to help repair and revitalize your hair:

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can be extremely damaging to your hair. Try to avoid using heat styling tools like blow dryers, curling irons, and straighteners as much as possible. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray first.

Avoid Chemical Treatments

Chemical treatments like coloring and perming can also be very damaging to your hair. Try to avoid these treatments as much as possible, or at least limit them to once every few months.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can contribute to extremely damaged hair. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and strong.

FAQs

Can extremely damaged hair be repaired?

Yes, extremely damaged hair can be repaired with the right products and treatments. Look for products that contain nourishing and strengthening ingredients like keratin, biotin, and argan oil.

How often should I use deep-conditioning treatments?

You should use deep-conditioning treatments once or twice a week, depending on the severity of your hair damage. If your hair is extremely damaged, you may want to use a deep-conditioning treatment every time you wash your hair.

What is the best way to prevent further damage to my hair?

The best way to prevent further damage to your hair is to avoid heat styling and chemical treatments, and to use nourishing and strengthening products on a regular basis. Additionally, make sure to get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

Hair Care for Extremely Damaged Hair

If you have extremely damaged hair, it's important to be gentle with it and take extra care to repair and revitalize it. Use the tips and products mentioned in this article to help restore your hair's health and strength, and remember to be patient - it may take some time to see results, but with consistent care, your hair will begin to look and feel healthier.
