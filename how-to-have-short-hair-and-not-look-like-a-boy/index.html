How To Have Short Hair And Not Look Like A Boy

Written by Petter Oct 03, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're a woman who has always had long hair and is considering going short, you may be hesitant because you're afraid of looking like a boy. However, there are plenty of ways to rock short hair and still look feminine and stylish. In this article, we'll show you how to have short hair and not look like a boy.

Table of Contents

  • How to Have Short Hair and Not Look Like a Boy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

How to Have Short Hair and Not Look Like a Boy

The key to having short hair and still looking feminine is to choose the right cut and style for your face shape and personal style. A good hairstylist can help you choose a cut that will flatter your features and bring out your best qualities.

Another important factor is to embrace your feminine features such as makeup, earrings, and clothing. These will balance out the short hair and give you a more feminine look. Remember, short hair doesn't have to mean masculine.

Here are some popular short haircuts for women:

  • Pixie Cut
  • Bob Cut
  • Short Shag
  • Cropped Cut
  • Asymmetrical Cut

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're ready to take the plunge and chop off your locks, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve a feminine short hairstyle:

  1. Choose a reputable hairstylist who has experience cutting short hair.
  2. Bring in pictures of short hairstyles that you like and discuss them with your stylist.
  3. Choose a cut that flatters your face shape and personal style.
  4. Ask your stylist for tips on how to style your new short hair.
  5. Invest in some quality hair styling products, such as a good hairspray, mousse, and pomade.
  6. Experiment with different styling techniques, such as blow drying and using a flat iron, to find what works best for you.
  7. Accessorize with feminine touches such as earrings, necklaces, and makeup.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

Now that you have a great short haircut, it's important to learn how to style it to keep it looking fresh and feminine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add body to your hair and prevent it from looking flat.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  • Use a flat iron to create sleek, straight styles.
  • Add texture to your hair by using a texturizing spray or pomade.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories such as headbands, clips, and barrettes.

FAQs

Can I still wear feminine clothing with short hair?

Absolutely! Short hair doesn't mean you have to dress like a boy. In fact, short hair can be the perfect accessory to a feminine, flirty dress or a chic, tailored suit.

What if I don't like my short hair?

Remember, hair grows back! If you're not happy with your short hair, talk to your stylist about options for growing it out or changing the style.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires different care than long hair. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair healthy and looking great:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

With the right haircut, styling techniques, and accessories, you can have short hair and still look feminine and stylish. Embrace your new look and enjoy the freedom and versatility that comes with having short hair!
