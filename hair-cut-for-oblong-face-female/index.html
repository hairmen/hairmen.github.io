Best Haircuts For Oblong Face Females A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Jul 02, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your oblong face shape? Look no further. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about the best haircuts for oblong face females.

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a high forehead and a pointed chin. If you have this face shape, you'll want a haircut that can add width to your face and balance out your features.

How to

The first step in finding the right haircut for your oblong face is to determine your face shape. If you're not sure, you can use a tape measure to measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin, and then divide that number by three. If the result is equal to or greater than the width of your face at the temples, you have an oblong face.

Once you've determined your face shape, you'll want to look for haircuts that can add volume and width to your face. Some of the best haircuts for oblong face females include:

1. Long layers

Long layers can add volume and movement to your hair, which can help balance out your features. You can also add side-swept bangs to create the illusion of a wider forehead.

2. Bob

A bob can be a great option for oblong face females, especially if it's cut to chin length or above. This style can add width to your face and create a more balanced look.

3. Pixie

If you're feeling bold, a pixie cut can be a great option for oblong face females. This style can add volume to the top of your head and create the illusion of a wider face.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape by measuring the length of your face and dividing it by three.

2. Look for haircuts that can add volume and width to your face, such as long layers, a bob, or a pixie cut.

3. Consult with your stylist to determine which haircut will work best for your face shape and hair type.

4. Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims and using the right hair care products for your hair type.

Tips

1. Avoid haircuts that are too long, as they can make your face look even longer.

2. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.

3. Invest in a good quality hairbrush to help distribute your hair's natural oils and add shine.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for an oblong face?

The best haircut for an oblong face is one that can add volume and width to your face, such as long layers, a bob, or a pixie cut.

What should I avoid when choosing a haircut for my oblong face?

Avoid haircuts that are too long, as they can make your face look even longer. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as they can make your face look even narrower.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect haircut for your oblong face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

1. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.

2. Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often, as they can damage your hair.

3. Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can enhance your oblong face shape and feel confident in your look.
