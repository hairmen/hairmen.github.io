Toddler Boy Haircut Short Sides Long Top

Written by William Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for your little boy? Look no further than the short sides long top haircut! This popular haircut has been a favorite among parents for years and is perfect for toddler boys who want to look cool and trendy. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look, as well as tips for maintaining it and caring for your child's hair.

Description

The short sides long top haircut is a versatile and stylish cut that features short hair on the sides and longer hair on top. This haircut is perfect for toddler boys who want to look trendy and cool, without sacrificing comfort or functionality. The short sides make it easy for parents to maintain and style, while the long top allows for a variety of different looks and styles.

How To

Here's how to achieve the short sides long top haircut:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

The first step in achieving the short sides long top haircut is to choose the right clippers. You'll need a set of clippers with different guard sizes, as well as a pair of scissors for trimming.

Step 2: Cut the Sides

Start by cutting the sides of your child's hair using a clipper guard. Begin with the longest guard size and work your way down until you reach the desired length. Make sure to keep the clippers level and consistent, and go slowly to avoid any mistakes or uneven cuts.

Step 3: Trim the Top

Next, trim the top of your child's hair using scissors. Start at the front and work your way back, cutting the hair to the desired length. You can leave it longer in the front for a trendy, swept-over look, or trim it evenly for a more classic style.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Finally, use the clippers to blend the sides and top of your child's hair. Use a shorter guard size to create a smooth transition between the short sides and longer top. Be careful not to cut too much or create any harsh lines.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your child's short sides long top haircut:

  • Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and strong.
  • Style the hair with a pomade or gel for a trendy, polished look.
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find what works best for your child.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about the short sides long top haircut:

Is this haircut appropriate for all hair types?

Yes, the short sides long top haircut can be adapted for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

What if my child doesn't like the haircut?

If your child doesn't like the haircut, try experimenting with different styles and lengths until you find one that they are comfortable with.

Can I do this haircut at home?

Yes, you can do this haircut at home with the right tools and some practice. However, if you're not comfortable cutting hair, it's best to leave it to a professional stylist.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for caring for your child's hair after the short sides long top haircut:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle the hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage the hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Protect the hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With the short sides long top haircut, your toddler boy can look stylish and trendy while still being comfortable and functional. Follow these tips and tricks to achieve the perfect haircut, and don't forget to take care of your child's hair to keep it healthy and strong.
