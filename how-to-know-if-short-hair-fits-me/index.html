How To Know If Short Hair Fits Me A Guide For 2023

Written by Robby Aug 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to know if short hair fits me. Short hair can be a great way to change up your look, but it can be tough to know if it's the right choice for you. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to take to determine if short hair is a good fit for your face shape, hair type, and personal style. We'll also provide tips on how to take care of short hair and answer some frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Considering Your Hair Type
  • Evaluating Your Personal Style
  • Choosing the Right Short Haircut
  • Tips for Taking Care of Short Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in figuring out if short hair is right for you is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each shape has different characteristics that can impact what hairstyles look best on you.

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and examine your face in the mirror. Look at the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Measure the length of your face from the hairline to the chin. Once you have these measurements, you can use them to determine your face shape using an online guide or consulting with a hairstylist.

If you have an oval or heart-shaped face, short haircuts are generally more flattering. If you have a round or square face, longer haircuts may be more flattering. However, there are no hard and fast rules, and ultimately it comes down to personal preference.

Considering Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider when deciding if short hair is right for you is your hair type. Short haircuts can work well for thin, fine hair, as they can make hair appear fuller and more voluminous. However, if you have thick, curly hair, short haircuts may require more maintenance and styling to look their best.

It's also important to consider your hair texture. If you have naturally straight hair, a blunt bob or pixie cut can work well. If you have wavy or curly hair, a layered bob or shaggy cut can help to enhance your natural texture.

Evaluating Your Personal Style

When deciding if short hair is right for you, it's important to consider your personal style. Short hair can be edgy, sophisticated, or playful, depending on the cut and styling. If you prefer a more classic or conservative style, a shorter haircut may not be the best fit for you.

It's also important to consider your lifestyle. If you're someone who likes to spend a lot of time styling your hair, a shorter haircut may be more manageable. However, if you prefer low-maintenance hairstyles, a longer cut may be a better fit.

Choosing the Right Short Haircut

Once you've determined that short hair is a good fit for you, it's time to choose the right haircut. There are many different short haircuts to choose from, including pixie cuts, bobs, and shags. Work with your hairstylist to find a cut that complements your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

It's also important to consider factors like the amount of maintenance the cut will require, how often you'll need to get it trimmed, and how much time you're willing to spend styling it.

Tips for Taking Care of Short Hair

Short hair can be easier to care for than longer hair, but it still requires some maintenance. Here are some tips for keeping your short haircut looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Invest in styling products like mousse or texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Consider getting a silk pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and frizz.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is short hair easier to care for than longer hair?

Short hair can be easier to care for than longer hair, as it requires less time and styling. However, it still requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best.

Will short hair flatter my face shape?

Short hair can flatter a variety of face shapes, but it's important to choose a cut that complements your unique features. Work with your hairstylist to find a cut that works for you.

What styling products should I use for short hair?

The best styling products for short hair depend on your hair type and the look you're going for. Mousse, texturizing spray, and pomade can all be great options for adding volume and texture to your hair.

How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

It's generally recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your short haircut looking fresh.

That concludes our guide on how to know if short hair fits me. We hope you found it helpful in determining whether a shorter haircut is right for you. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a haircut that makes you feel confident and comfortable in your own skin!
