Can You Permanently Get Rid Of Curly Hair

Written by Eveline Aug 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with curly hair on a daily basis? Are you wondering if there is a way to permanently get rid of it? The answer is yes, it is possible to permanently straighten your hair, but it’s important to understand the process and the potential risks involved.

How to Permanently Get Rid of Curly Hair

The most common method for permanently straightening hair is through a chemical process called a relaxer. This process involves applying a chemical solution to the hair that breaks down the bonds that give hair its natural curl pattern. Once the bonds are broken, the hair can be straightened using heat, and the new straight pattern is then locked in by neutralizing the chemicals.

It’s important to note that the relaxer process is not a one-time solution. As your hair grows, you will need to continue to apply the relaxer to the new growth to maintain the straightened look. This can be done every 6-8 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Step by Step Guide

If you are interested in permanently straightening your curly hair, it’s important to follow the proper steps to ensure the best results and minimize the risk of damage.

  1. Consult with a professional stylist to determine if your hair is healthy enough for a relaxer.
  2. Choose a high-quality relaxer that is appropriate for your hair type.
  3. Apply the relaxer to your hair, following the instructions carefully.
  4. Rinse the relaxer out thoroughly and neutralize the chemicals.
  5. Blow-dry and flat iron your hair to lock in the new straight pattern.

It’s important to follow up with regular hair care routines and touch-ups to maintain your straightened hair and keep it healthy.

Tips for Permanently Straightening Curly Hair

Here are some tips for achieving the best results when permanently straightening curly hair:

  • Choose a professional stylist who is experienced in the relaxer process.
  • Use high-quality products that are specifically designed for chemically treated hair.
  • Do not wash your hair for at least 48 hours after the relaxer process to allow the chemicals to fully penetrate.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair for at least a week after the process to prevent damage.

FAQs

Can I perm my hair after getting a relaxer?

It is generally not recommended to perm your hair after getting a relaxer. The two processes use different chemicals and can cause damage to your hair if done too close together.

Is it safe to permanently straighten my hair?

While the relaxer process can be safe when done by a professional, there is always a risk of damage to your hair and scalp. It’s important to follow the proper steps and take care of your hair before and after the process to minimize the risk of damage.

How long does the relaxer process take?

The relaxer process can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Hair Care After Permanently Straightening Curly Hair

After permanently straightening your curly hair, it’s important to follow a regular hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment regularly to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can enjoy your permanently straightened hair for years to come.

Conclusion

Permanently straightening curly hair is possible through a chemical process called a relaxer, but it’s important to follow the proper steps and take care of your hair before and after the process to minimize the risk of damage. By following a regular hair care routine, you can enjoy your straightened hair for years to come.
