What Haircut Will Suit Me Best

Written by Eveline Sep 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for a change? It can be overwhelming to choose the right haircut for your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this article, we will guide you through the process of finding the perfect haircut that will suit you best.

Description

Before diving into the specifics of what haircut will suit you best, let's start with a general description of different face shapes and hair textures. Knowing your face shape and hair texture will make it easier to select a haircut that will flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty.

There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Hair texture can be fine, medium, or thick, and may be straight, wavy, curly, or kinky.

How to

The first step in finding the right haircut for you is to determine your face shape. Stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or eyeliner. Then compare the shape to the six main face shapes.

Once you have determined your face shape, consider your hair texture. Fine hair may benefit from layers or a bob, while thick hair may require thinning or texturizing. Curly hair may look best with a layered cut, while straight hair may benefit from blunt ends.

Next, consider your personal style and the amount of maintenance you are willing to do. A pixie cut may be low maintenance, but may not suit your face shape or hair texture. A long layered cut may require more upkeep, but may be more flattering.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to finding the perfect haircut for you:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Consider your hair texture
  3. Think about your personal style and maintenance
  4. Research different haircuts that suit your face shape and hair texture
  5. Consult with a hairstylist for their professional opinion and recommendations
  6. Try on different hairstyles with virtual makeover tools or by trying on wigs
  7. Choose a haircut that you feel comfortable and confident in

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when selecting a new haircut:

  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to show your hairstylist
  • Consider the length and layers of the haircut
  • Think about how the haircut will look with your natural hair color
  • Consider the shape of your forehead and ears when choosing a haircut
  • Be open to trying new styles, but also be true to your personal style

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about selecting the right haircut:

What haircut is best for an oval face?

An oval face shape can pull off almost any haircut, but a long layered cut or a bob with side-swept bangs can be particularly flattering.

What haircut is best for thick and curly hair?

A layered cut can help thin out thick hair and define curls. Consider a shoulder-length cut with long layers or a curly shag.

What haircut is best for a heart-shaped face?

A heart-shaped face can benefit from a chin-length bob or a layered cut with side-swept bangs to balance out the forehead.

Hair Care

Once you have found the perfect haircut for you, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style and keep it healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited for your hair type and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims every 6-8 weeks can also help keep your haircut looking fresh.

In conclusion, finding the right haircut that will suit you best requires considering your face shape, hair texture, personal style, and maintenance level. By following these steps and tips, you can confidently select a haircut that will enhance your natural beauty and make you feel great.
