How to Wear Your Hair Up with Hair Extensions

Wearing your hair up with hair extensions can seem tricky, but with a little practice, it can be a breeze. Here are a few ways to wear your hair up with extensions:

Braids

Braids are a great way to wear your hair up with extensions. You can create a variety of braids, from a simple French braid to a more complex fishtail braid. To make sure your extensions stay hidden, start your braid as close to your scalp as possible, and braid all the way down to the ends. You can also use a little hairspray or styling gel to tame any flyaways.

Buns

Buns are a classic updo that look great with hair extensions. You can create a sleek, polished bun by pulling your hair back into a ponytail, twisting it into a bun, and securing it with bobby pins. To make sure your extensions stay hidden, tuck them into the bun and secure them with bobby pins. You can also create a messy bun by pulling your hair back into a loose ponytail, twisting it into a bun, and pulling out a few pieces to create a more relaxed look.

Ponytails

Ponytails are another easy way to wear your hair up with extensions. To create a sleek ponytail, brush your hair back and secure it with an elastic band. To hide your extensions, wrap a small section of hair around the elastic band and secure it with a bobby pin. You can also create a high ponytail by pulling your hair up towards the top of your head, or a low ponytail by pulling it back towards the nape of your neck.

Step-by-Step Guide

Hereâs a step-by-step guide to wearing your hair up with hair extensions:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start styling, make sure your hair and extensions are clean and dry. Brush your hair to remove any tangles, and apply a little dry shampoo or texturizing spray to add some volume and texture.

Step 2: Choose Your Style

Decide on the style you want to create, whether itâs a braid, bun, or ponytail. Choose a style that works best with your hair type and face shape.

Step 3: Add Your Extensions

Clip in your extensions, making sure they are evenly distributed throughout your hair. Use a mirror to check that the clips are hidden and that your extensions look natural.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Start styling your hair, using the tips and tricks we mentioned earlier. Take your time and be gentle, especially when working with the extensions, to avoid damaging them.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your style with a light mist of hairspray to hold everything in place. Use a brush or your fingers to smooth down any flyaways.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few tips and tricks to help you wear your hair up with hair extensions:

  • Choose extensions that match your hair color and texture for a natural look.
  • Use a rat-tail comb to create a clean part and to help you clip in your extensions.
  • Tease your hair at the roots to add some volume and hold your extensions in place.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to help hide your extensions.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Can I wear my hair up with clip-in extensions?

Yes, clip-in extensions are perfect for wearing your hair up. Just make sure the clips are hidden and that your extensions look natural.

Can I wear my hair up with tape-in extensions?

Yes, tape-in extensions can also be worn up. Just make sure the tape is securely attached and that your extensions are evenly distributed throughout your hair.

Can I wear my hair up with sew-in extensions?

Yes, sew-in extensions can be worn up, but you may need to be more careful when styling to avoid damaging the braids or wefts.

Hair Care for Extensions

To keep your hair extensions looking their best, itâs important to take good care of them. Here are a few tips:

  • Brush your extensions gently with a wide-toothed comb or a soft bristle brush.
  • Wash your extensions with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and let them air dry.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your extensions, or use a heat protectant spray if you do.
  • Store your extensions in a cool, dry place when youâre not wearing them.

By following these tips and tricks, you can wear your hair up with hair extensions and achieve a flawless, natural-looking style. Have fun experimenting with different updos and finding the perfect look for you!
