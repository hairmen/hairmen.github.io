Medium Length Haircuts For Thick Hair Over 50 A Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we age, our hair texture and thickness may change. Thick hair can be a blessing, but it can also be a challenge to manage as we get older. If you have thick hair and are over 50, you may be wondering what haircut will suit you best. In this article, we'll explore some medium length haircuts that are perfect for women with thick hair over 50.

Description

Medium length haircuts are a popular choice for women over 50 as they are versatile and easy to manage. They can also help to frame the face and add volume to the hair. If you have thick hair, a medium length haircut can help to prevent your hair from looking too heavy or weighed down. In this article, we'll explore some of the best medium length haircuts for women with thick hair over 50.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

Choosing the right haircut can make all the difference to how you look and feel. When choosing a medium length haircut for thick hair over 50, consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. If you have a round face, a shoulder-length bob with layers can help to elongate your face. If you have a square face, a long bob with side-swept bangs can soften your features. If you have an oval face, you can experiment with a variety of medium length haircuts.

Consider Your Hair Texture

When choosing a haircut, it's important to consider your hair texture. If you have thick hair, you'll want a haircut that can help to thin out your hair and prevent it from looking too bulky. Layers can help to add movement to your hair and make it easier to style. If you have curly or wavy hair, a medium length haircut can help to enhance your natural texture.

Think About Your Personal Style

Your personal style should also be taken into account when choosing a medium length haircut for thick hair over 50. If you prefer a low-maintenance style, a layered bob or a long bob with side-swept bangs can be a great option. If you like to experiment with different looks, a shaggy haircut with choppy layers can add a touch of edginess to your style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Medium Length Haircuts for Thick Hair Over 50

Here's a step-by-step guide to some of the best medium length haircuts for thick hair over 50:

Shoulder-Length Bob with Layers

A shoulder-length bob with layers is a classic haircut that can suit many face shapes:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  2. Cut the back section of your hair to your desired length, leaving the sides longer.
  3. Add layers to the sides and top sections of your hair to create movement and volume.
  4. Finish with a side-swept bangs to frame your face.

Long Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

A long bob with side-swept bangs is a great option if you want to keep your hair length:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  2. Cut the back section of your hair to your desired length, leaving the sides longer.
  3. Add layers to the sides and top sections of your hair to create movement and volume.
  4. Create side-swept bangs by cutting the front section of your hair diagonally.

Shaggy Haircut with Choppy Layers

A shaggy haircut with choppy layers is a great option if you want to add some edginess to your style:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  2. Cut the back section of your hair to your desired length, leaving the sides longer.
  3. Add choppy layers to the sides and top sections of your hair to create a shaggy look.
  4. Finish with a side-swept bangs to frame your face.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Haircuts for Thick Hair Over 50

Here are some tips for maintaining your medium length haircut:

  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Try different hair accessories to add some variety to your hairstyle.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for thick hair over 50?

There are many great haircuts for women with thick hair over 50, including shoulder-length bobs with layers, long bobs with side-swept bangs, and shaggy haircuts with choppy layers.

What is the best way to style medium length haircuts for thick hair over 50?

The best way to style medium length haircuts for thick hair over 50 is to use a styling product that can help to add volume and texture to your hair. You can also experiment with different hair accessories to add some variety to your style.

Hair Care for Thick Hair Over 50

When it comes to hair care, thick hair requires a bit more maintenance than other hair types. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Try not to wash your hair every day as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends.

Medium length haircuts for thick hair over 50 can be a great way to add some volume and movement to your hair. By choosing the right haircut and taking care of your hair, you can look and feel your best at any age.
