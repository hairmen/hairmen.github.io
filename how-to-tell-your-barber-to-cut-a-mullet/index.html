How To Tell Your Barber To Cut A Mullet

Written by Frank Aug 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you ready to embrace the iconic hairstyle of the 80s and 90s? The mullet is making a comeback, and if you're ready to rock this look, you'll need to know how to tell your barber to cut a mullet. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process step by step, so you can get the perfect mullet.

Description

The mullet is a hairstyle that's short on top and long in the back. It's been around since the 70s, but it really took off in the 80s and 90s. The mullet is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways, from messy and casual to sleek and polished. Whether you're going for a rockstar vibe or just want to try something new, the mullet is a fun and trendy hairstyle that's perfect for anyone who wants to stand out.

How to

Talking to your barber about a mullet can be intimidating, especially if you've never had one before. Here's how to tell your barber to cut a mullet:

Step 1: Find a reputable barber

The first step is to find a barber who specializes in mullets. Look for reviews online, ask your friends for recommendations, and check out the barbershop's website or social media pages to see if they have any photos of mullets they've done in the past.

Step 2: Bring a photo

It's always helpful to bring a photo of the mullet you want, so your barber can see exactly what you're looking for. You can find photos online, in magazines, or even on social media. Make sure the photo is clear and shows the front, sides, and back of the hairstyle.

Step 3: Communicate clearly

When you sit down in the barber's chair, be clear about what you want. Tell your barber that you want a mullet and show them the photo you brought. If there are any specific details you want, such as a certain length or texture, be sure to mention them.

Step 4: Trust your barber

Your barber is a professional, so trust their expertise. They may have suggestions for how to tweak the hairstyle to suit your face shape or hair texture. Listen to their advice and be open to trying something new.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting a mullet:

  • Choose a mullet that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  • Consider getting a fade or undercut on the sides for a more modern twist.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the look that works best for you.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your mullet.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a mullet:

Is the mullet a good hairstyle?

The mullet is a fun and trendy hairstyle that can be a great choice if you're looking for something different. However, it may not be suitable for everyone, so it's important to choose a mullet that suits your face shape and hair texture.

How do I style a mullet?

There are many ways to style a mullet, depending on the look you're going for. You can use styling products like wax or pomade to add texture and hold. You can also experiment with different lengths and layers to create a more unique look.

How often should I get a mullet trim?

It's important to get regular trims to maintain the shape of your mullet. Depending on how fast your hair grows, you may need a trim every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Caring for your mullet is key to keeping it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your mullet:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your mullet.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to confidently tell your barber to cut a mullet and rock this trendy hairstyle with ease.
