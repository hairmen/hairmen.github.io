Do Short Haircuts Look Good On Round Faces

Written by Luffy Oct 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short haircuts are a popular trend, but for those with round faces, the question remains: do they look good? If you have a round face, you may be hesitant to get a short haircut, fearing it will only make your face look wider. However, with the right cut and styling, short haircuts can actually flatter round faces. Read on to find out how.

Description

A round face is characterized by a soft, circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded jawline. Short haircuts can accentuate the roundness of the face, but with the right cut, they can also create angles and balance out the features. The key is to choose a haircut that elongates the face and draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for a round face, consider the following:

1. Length

A short haircut doesn't have to be super short. In fact, a slightly longer haircut, such as a lob or bob, can actually elongate the face. Aim for a length that falls just below the chin or at the collarbone.

2. Layers

Layers can add texture and volume to a short haircut, but be sure to ask for longer layers that frame the face. This will create the illusion of a longer face and draw attention to the cheekbones.

3. Bangs

Bangs can be a great addition to a short haircut, but be sure to choose a style that doesn't cut across the widest part of the face. Side-swept bangs can create angles and draw attention to the eyes.

4. Texture

Adding texture to a short haircut can create movement and dimension. Consider adding subtle waves or curls to soften the face and create a more feminine look.

Step by Step

1. Consult with your hairstylist about the best short haircut for your face shape.

2. Choose a length that falls just below the chin or at the collarbone.

3. Ask for longer layers that frame the face.

4. Consider side-swept bangs.

5. Add texture with waves or curls.

Tips

1. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the widest part of the face.

2. Consider a deep side part to create angles.

3. Use styling products, such as volumizing mousse, to add volume at the crown of the head.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look wider?

A: It depends on the cut. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the widest part of the face and instead choose a cut with longer layers that frame the face. This will create the illusion of a longer face.

Q: Should I avoid bangs?

A: Bangs can be a great addition to a short haircut, but be sure to choose a style that doesn't cut across the widest part of the face. Side-swept bangs are a good option.

Q: How can I add volume to a short haircut?

A: Use styling products, such as volumizing mousse or texturizing spray, to add volume at the crown of the head.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep the cut looking fresh. Schedule regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep the shape of the cut. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and keep the hair healthy. Style with a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.

In conclusion, short haircuts can look good on round faces with the right cut and styling. Consult with your hairstylist and consider length, layers, bangs, and texture to create a flattering look. With regular maintenance and proper hair care, a short haircut can be a stylish and low-maintenance option for those with round faces.
