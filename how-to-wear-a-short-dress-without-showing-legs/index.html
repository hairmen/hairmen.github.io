How To Wear A Short Dress Without Showing Legs

Do you love short dresses but feel self-conscious about showing your legs? Don't worry, there are plenty of ways to wear a short dress and still feel comfortable and confident. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for how to wear a short dress without showing legs.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • FAQ

How to Wear a Short Dress Without Showing Legs

The key to wearing a short dress without showing your legs is to create the illusion of longer legs. Here are a few ways to do that:

1. Pair your dress with tall boots

Tall boots that come up to your knees or thighs can help elongate your legs and cover up any skin you don't want to show. Choose a pair in a neutral color like black or brown to go with any dress.

2. Wear opaque tights

If you want to wear a short dress but don't want to bare your legs, opaque tights are a great option. Choose a pair in a color that matches your dress or go with classic black.

3. Layer with leggings or skinny jeans

Another way to cover up your legs is to layer your dress with leggings or skinny jeans. This is a great option for colder weather or when you want a more casual look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for how to wear a short dress without showing your legs:

Step 1: Choose your dress

Pick out a short dress that you love and feel comfortable in. Make sure it's not too short and that it covers the parts of your legs you don't want to show.

Step 2: Choose your shoes

Decide on the shoes you want to wear with your dress. Tall boots, flats, or even sneakers can all work depending on the look you're going for.

Step 3: Choose your legwear

Decide if you want to wear opaque tights, leggings, or skinny jeans with your dress. Choose a color that complements your dress and shoes.

Step 4: Accessorize

Add some accessories like a belt or scarf to complete your look. Just make sure they don't detract from the overall outfit.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few more tips and tricks for how to wear a short dress without showing your legs:

1. Choose a dress with a longer hemline in the back

A dress with a longer hemline in the back can help create the illusion of longer legs.

2. Opt for a flowy dress

A flowy dress can help distract from any areas you don't want to show while also creating movement and interest in your outfit.

3. Wear a statement necklace

A statement necklace can draw attention to your upper body and away from your legs.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a short dress if I'm self-conscious about my legs?

A: Yes! There are plenty of ways to wear a short dress without showing your legs. Try pairing your dress with tall boots, opaque tights, or leggings to cover up any areas you don't want to show.

Q: What color shoes should I wear with a short dress?

A: You can't go wrong with neutral colors like black, brown, or nude. However, if you want to add some interest to your outfit, try a bold color or pattern.

Hair Care Tips

Don't forget about your hair when putting together your outfit! Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

1. Keep it simple

A simple hairstyle like a ponytail or loose waves can complement a short dress without detracting from it.

2. Consider your neckline

If your dress has a high neckline, consider wearing your hair up to show it off. If it has a low neckline, wearing your hair down can create a nice balance.

3. Add some accessories

A cute headband or hair clip can add some interest to your hairstyle and tie your outfit together.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently wear a short dress without showing your legs. Remember, the most important thing is to feel comfortable and confident in your outfit!
