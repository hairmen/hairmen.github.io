What Type Of Shades Suit Round Face A Guide For 2023

Are you tired of trying on sunglasses only to find they don't quite fit your round face? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll explore what type of shades suit round face and provide you with tips on how to find the perfect pair. So grab a cup of coffee and read on!

What Type of Shades Suit Round Face?

When it comes to finding the perfect pair of sunglasses for round face, there are a few key features to look for:

  • Angular frames: To contrast with the curves of a round face, look for frames with sharp angles, such as square or rectangular shapes.
  • Wide frames: Sunglasses that are wider than your face can help balance out your features and make your face appear slimmer.
  • High temples: Look for sunglasses with high temples or arms that sit higher on your temples. This can help elongate your face and create a more oval shape.
  • Dark lenses: Dark lenses can help create the illusion of depth and structure.

Now that you know what to look for, let's dive into how to choose the right pair of sunglasses for round face.

How to Choose Sunglasses for Round Face

When choosing sunglasses for round face, you want to find a pair that complements your facial features and accentuates your best qualities. Here are a few tips to help you choose the right pair:

  • Consider your face shape: If you have a round face, look for frames that are wider than your face and have angular or geometric shapes.
  • Think about your skin tone: Choose a frame color that complements your skin tone. For cool-toned skin, try frames in shades of blue, black, or silver. For warm-toned skin, opt for frames in brown, gold, or tortoiseshell.
  • Consider your personal style: Choose a pair of sunglasses that reflect your personal style and make you feel confident.

Step-by-step Guide to Finding the Right Shades

Still not sure where to start? Here's a step-by-step guide to help you find the perfect pair of sunglasses for round face:

  1. Measure your face: Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your face at the widest point, just below your eyes.
  2. Choose a frame shape: Look for frames that are wider than your face and have angular or geometric shapes.
  3. Try on different styles: Don't be afraid to try on several different styles to find the perfect match.
  4. Consider the fit: Make sure the sunglasses fit comfortably and don't slide down your nose.
  5. Check the UV protection: Make sure the sunglasses provide adequate UV protection to keep your eyes safe from the sun's harmful rays.

Tips for Round Face Sunglasses

Once you've found the perfect pair of sunglasses for round face, here are a few tips to help you rock them with confidence:

  • Wear your sunglasses on the bridge of your nose: This can help elongate your face and create a more oval shape.
  • Choose the right hairstyle: Consider a hairstyle that complements your sunglasses and balances out your facial features.
  • Consider your outfit: Choose an outfit that complements your sunglasses and creates a cohesive look.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I have a round face and small features?

If you have a round face and small features, look for sunglasses with smaller frames that fit your face proportionately. Avoid oversized frames that can overwhelm your features.

Can I wear aviators if I have a round face?

Absolutely! Aviators can be a great choice for round face. Look for frames that are wider than your face and have a slight angle to create a more oval shape.

What if I want to wear round sunglasses?

While round sunglasses can be tricky for round face, you can still wear them with the right styling. Look for frames that are slightly wider than your face and have a bit of an angle. Pair them with a sleek hairstyle to create a more elongated look.

Hair Care Tips for Wearing Sunglasses

Wearing sunglasses can cause your hair to get tangled or flattened. Here are a few hair care tips to keep your locks looking their best:

  • Choose a hairstyle that works with your sunglasses: If you're wearing sunglasses with high temples, consider a hairstyle that pulls your hair up and away from your face.
  • Use a hair tie: If you're wearing your hair down, use a hair tie to keep your locks from getting tangled in your sunglasses.
  • Carry a brush: Bring a small brush or comb with you to touch up your hair throughout the day.

Now that you know what type of shades suit round face, it's time to start shopping! Remember to look for angular frames, wide frames, high temples, and dark lenses, and consider your face shape, skin tone, and personal style. With these tips in mind, you're sure to find the perfect pair of sunglasses for round face.
