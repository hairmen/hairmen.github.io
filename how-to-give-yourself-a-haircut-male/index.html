How To Give Yourself A Haircut Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to save some money or just want to try something new, giving yourself a haircut at home is a great option. However, it can be a bit intimidating, especially if you've never done it before. Don't worry though, with the right tools and techniques, you can give yourself a great haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll show you how to give yourself a haircut male.

How to Give Yourself a Haircut Male - The Tools You'll Need

Before we get started, let's talk about the tools you'll need to give yourself a haircut male:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Mirror
  • Haircut cape or towel

Make sure you have all of these tools before you start.

How to Give Yourself a Haircut Male - Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before you start cutting, you should wash your hair to remove any dirt or oil. This will make it easier to cut and give you a better result.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want to cut your hair. You can use clippers with different guard sizes to achieve different lengths.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Start with the sides of your head. Use the clippers with the desired guard size and start at the bottom of your hairline. Slowly move the clippers upward, following the natural shape of your head.

Step 4: Move to the Back

Move to the back of your head and use the same technique as you did for the sides. Make sure to use a mirror to check that you're cutting evenly.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Use scissors to cut the top of your hair. Start at the front and work your way to the back. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length.

Step 6: Blend the Sides and Top

Use the scissors to blend the sides and top of your hair. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut at an angle to blend the two sections together.

Step 7: Clean Up the Neckline

Use the clippers to clean up the hairline at the back of your neck. Start at the bottom and move upward, following the natural curve of your neck.

Step 8: Check Your Work

Use the mirror to check your work and make any necessary adjustments.

Step 9: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Giving Yourself a Haircut Male

  • Start with a higher guard size and work your way down to achieve your desired length.
  • Use the mirror to check your work as you go.
  • Take your time and be patient.

FAQ - How to Give Yourself a Haircut Male

Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

No, regular scissors are not designed for cutting hair and can cause damage and split ends. Use scissors designed specifically for cutting hair.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go to a professional to fix it or wait for it to grow out and try again.

Hair Care After Giving Yourself a Haircut Male

After giving yourself a haircut male, make sure to wash your hair again to remove any loose hairs. Apply a conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

That's it! With this guide, you should now know how to give yourself a haircut male. Remember to take your time, be patient, and follow the steps carefully. Good luck!
