Quick Hairstyles For Short Hair Simple And Chic Styles

Written by Robby Nov 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

In a hurry but still want to look stylish? Don't worry, you don't have to sacrifice your style just because you have short hair. There are plenty of quick and easy hairstyles for short hair that you can do in minutes. Whether you're running late for work or just want to switch up your look, these simple styles will have you looking chic in no time. Here are our top picks for quick hairstyles for short hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Invest in some good quality hair accessories, like bobby pins, hair ties, and headbands.

Step by Step

1. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic go-to hairstyle for all hair lengths. To create this look:

  1. Start by gathering your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist your hair around the base of the ponytail to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to create a messy, effortless look.

2. Half-Up Top Knot

The half-up top knot is another popular hairstyle that's perfect for short hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by sectioning off the top half of your hair.
  2. Twist the top section into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  3. Leave the bottom half of your hair down.

3. Headband Tuck

This hairstyle is perfect for those days when you don't have time to wash your hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by putting on a headband.
  2. Tuck your hair into the headband, starting at the back and working your way around.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you rock your short hair:

  • Use dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Try different hair accessories, like scarves and hair clips, to add some flair to your look.
  • Experiment with different partings to switch up your style.

FAQ

1. Can I still curl my short hair?

Absolutely! You can use a curling iron or wand to add some texture and volume to your short hair.

2. What hair accessories should I have for short hair?

Some essential hair accessories for short hair include bobby pins, hair ties, headbands, and hair clips.

Hair Care

Keeping your short hair healthy is important for maintaining your style. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

With these quick and easy hairstyles for short hair, you'll never have to sacrifice style for convenience. Try out these looks and find the perfect one for your lifestyle and personality.
