How To Make Your Natural Hair Kinky Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to add some curl to your locks? Well, you're in luck because we have the ultimate guide on how to make your natural hair kinky curly. Whether you have short, medium, or long hair, these tips and tricks will help you achieve the perfect kinky curls. So, let's get started!

Description

Kinky curly hair is a beautiful and unique hair type that requires special care and attention. It's important to understand that not all natural hair is the same, and what works for one person may not work for another. However, with the right techniques, products, and tools, anyone can achieve beautiful kinky curls.

How to

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it's important to note that achieving kinky curls may take some time and patience. It's also essential to have healthy hair to achieve the best results. Make sure to have a regular hair care routine and avoid damaging your hair with heat styling tools and harsh chemicals.

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help hydrate your hair and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle. After washing, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and remove any knots or tangles.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancer

Apply a curl enhancer or styling cream to your hair while it's still wet. This will help define your curls and add moisture to your hair. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work on each section and ensure that each curl is defined.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help prevent frizz and keep your curls intact. Start at the roots and work your way to the ends, scrunching your hair as you go.

Step 5: Finish with Oil

Once your hair is fully dry, apply a small amount of oil to your hair to add shine and moisture. This will also help prevent frizz and keep your curls looking defined.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect kinky curls:

  • Use a satin pillowcase or bonnet to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often
  • Drink plenty of water and eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and health
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions

FAQ

How often should I wash my hair?

It's recommended to wash your hair once a week or every other week, depending on your hair type and lifestyle. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Can I use gel instead of curl enhancer?

Yes, you can use gel instead of curl enhancer, but make sure to choose a gel that's specifically designed for curly hair. Gel can help define your curls and add hold, but it can also make your hair feel crunchy or stiff.

Hair Care

It's important to have a regular hair care routine to maintain healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips to help you take care of your natural hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to add moisture and prevent breakage
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote hair growth
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a hair serum with SPF

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to make your natural hair kinky curly in no time. Remember to be patient and take care of your hair, and you'll have beautiful, healthy curls that you can be proud of.
