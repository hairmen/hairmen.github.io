How To Get Messy Wavy Hair Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 06, 2023 · 3 min read
If you're a guy who wants to achieve the perfect messy wavy hair, you've come to the right place. Messy wavy hair is a popular hairstyle among men, and it's not hard to see why. It's effortless, stylish, and low-maintenance. In this blog post, we'll show you how to get messy wavy hair guys with a step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Messy Wavy Hair Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Messy Wavy Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Advice

How to Get Messy Wavy Hair Guys

Getting messy wavy hair guys is all about creating texture and volume. Here are some ways to achieve this: 1. Use a texturizing spray or mousse: Texturizing products can help add volume and hold to your hair, making it easier to style. 2. Blow-dry your hair: After washing your hair, use a blow dryer to add volume and texture. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair as you blow-dry it for a messy look. 3. Use a curling iron: If you have straight hair, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Don't curl all of your hair, just a few sections to create a natural-looking wave.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with clean, damp hair. Apply a texturizing spray or mousse to your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. 2. Blow-dry your hair, using your fingers to scrunch your hair as you go. This will create texture and volume. 3. Use a curling iron to create loose waves. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing. 4. Once you've curled a few sections, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy look.

Tips for Messy Wavy Hair

- Don't overuse texturizing products. Too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. - Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, rather than a brush. Brushes can pull and damage your hair, leading to breakage. - Don't wash your hair too often. Washing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle.

FAQ

Q: Can I get messy wavy hair guys with short hair? A: Yes, you can! Use a texturizing product and blow-dry your hair in different directions to create volume and texture. Q: How do I maintain messy wavy hair? A: Use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and refresh your hair between washes. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these tips: - Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. If you have dry hair, look for moisturizing products. If you have oily hair, look for products that can help control oil. - Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair. This will help prevent damage and breakage. - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Conclusion

Getting messy wavy hair guys is easier than you might think. By using the right products and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and low-maintenance look that's perfect for any occasion. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and following a good hair care routine. With a little bit of effort, you can have the perfect messy wavy hair that everyone will envy.
