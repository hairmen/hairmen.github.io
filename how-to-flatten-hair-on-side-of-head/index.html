How To Flatten Hair On Side Of Head A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 27, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have hair that tends to stick out on the side of your head, you may be wondering how to get it to lay flat. This can be a frustrating problem, but there are solutions available. In this article, we’ll go over how to flatten hair on the side of your head, step by step. We’ll also provide tips and tricks for keeping your hair looking sleek and smooth.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Flatten Hair on Side of Head
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Flattening Hair on Side of Head
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Keeping Hair Sleek and Smooth

How to Flatten Hair on Side of Head

Before we get started, it’s important to note that the technique you use to flatten your hair will depend on your hair type and the style you’re going for. However, there are a few general tips that can help you get the look you want.

Use a Flat Iron

One of the easiest ways to flatten hair on the side of your head is to use a flat iron. Start by sectioning off the hair you want to flatten and run the flat iron over it, starting at the roots and moving down to the ends. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Blow Dry with a Round Brush

If you don’t have a flat iron, you can also use a round brush and a blow dryer to flatten your hair. Section off the hair you want to flatten and use the round brush to pull the hair straight while blow drying it. This will help smooth out any bumps or kinks in the hair.

Straighten with a Comb

If you don’t have a flat iron or a round brush, you can also use a wide-toothed comb to flatten your hair. Start by combing the hair straight down, and then run the comb over the hair from roots to ends. This will help smooth out any bumps or kinks in the hair.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know the general techniques for flattening hair on the side of your head, let’s go over a step-by-step guide.

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your hair soft and manageable.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant Spray

Before styling your hair, apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

Step 3: Section Off the Hair You Want to Flatten

Section off the hair on the side of your head that you want to flatten. Use hair clips to keep the rest of the hair out of the way.

Step 4: Use a Flat Iron, Round Brush, or Comb to Flatten Your Hair

Choose the technique that works best for your hair type and style. Use a flat iron, round brush, or comb to flatten your hair. Work in small sections, starting at the roots and moving down to the ends.

Step 5: Repeat on the Other Side

Repeat the process on the other side of your head.

Step 6: Finish with Hair Spray

Finish by spraying your hair with a light hold hair spray to keep it in place.

Tips for Flattening Hair on Side of Head

Here are some additional tips for flattening hair on the side of your head:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  • Work in small sections to ensure that you get every strand of hair.
  • Use a light hold hair spray to keep your hair in place.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a straightening brush to flatten my hair?

A: Yes, a straightening brush can also be used to flatten hair on the side of your head.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: This will depend on your hair type and personal preference, but most people find that washing their hair every 2-3 days is sufficient.

Q: Will using a flat iron damage my hair?

A: Using a flat iron can cause damage to your hair if you don’t use a heat protectant spray or if you use it too frequently. Be sure to take breaks from heat styling to give your hair a chance to recover.

Hair Care Tips for Keeping Hair Sleek and Smooth

Here are some hair care tips for keeping your hair sleek and smooth:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray when heat styling.
  • Take breaks from heat styling to give your hair a chance to recover.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush with natural bristles to avoid pulling or breaking your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as these can cause damage and breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve sleek, smooth hair on the side of your head. Whether you’re going for a sleek and sophisticated look or a more casual, tousled vibe, these tips will help you get the look you want.
