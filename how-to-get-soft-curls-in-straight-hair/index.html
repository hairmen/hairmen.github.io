How To Get Soft Curls In Straight Hair

Written by Oliver Sep 12, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair but want to switch things up with some soft curls? Look no further! This step-by-step guide will show you how to achieve soft curls in straight hair, without damaging your precious locks.

Description

Soft curls are the perfect way to add some texture and volume to your straight hair. They’re easy to achieve and can be done in the comfort of your own home. All you need are a few tools and some patience.

How To

To get soft curls in straight hair, you will need:

  • Heat protectant spray
  • Curling iron
  • Clips
  • Hairspray

Now that you have everything you need, let’s get started!

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is completely dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from any damage.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections, clipping the rest of your hair away. This will make it easier to curl each section and ensure that every strand is curled.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold the curling iron in place for 10-15 seconds and then release the curl. Repeat this process for each section of hair until your entire head is curled.

Step 4: Let Your Curls Cool

Once you have curled your entire head, let your curls cool for a few minutes. This will help set the curls and make them last longer.

Step 5: Shake Out Your Curls

After your curls have cooled, shake them out with your fingers. This will loosen them up and give them a more natural look.

Step 6: Add Hairspray

To ensure your curls last all day, apply a light mist of hairspray to your hair. This will hold your curls in place without making them look stiff or crunchy.

Tips

  • For tighter curls, use a smaller curling iron.
  • For looser curls, use a larger curling iron.
  • Don’t curl your hair too tightly or hold the curling iron in place for too long, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use clips to keep your curls in place while they cool.

FAQs

Can I curl my hair without a curling iron?

Yes, you can use other tools such as a straightener or hair rollers to achieve curls.

How long will my curls last?

Your curls should last all day, especially if you use hairspray to hold them in place.

Will curling my hair damage it?

It’s important to use a heat protectant spray and not hold the curling iron in place for too long to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and prevent damage, make sure to use a heat protectant spray before heat styling, and deep condition your hair regularly.

Now that you know how to get soft curls in straight hair, you can switch up your look and feel confident with your new style.
