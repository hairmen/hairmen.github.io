Best Curling Iron For Short Hair 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Sep 08, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have short hair, you know how hard it can be to find the perfect curling iron that will give you the curls you want without damaging your hair. Luckily, in 2020, there were some amazing curling irons that were released that are perfect for short hair. In this blog post, we will be discussing the best curling iron for short hair 2020 and everything you need to know about them.

Description

The best curling iron for short hair 2020 is a tool that has been designed specifically for those with short hair. These curling irons are generally smaller in size, have a smaller barrel, and have a higher heat setting. This makes it easier to curl short hair without damaging it. They are also designed to be lightweight, making it easier to maneuver and curl your hair.

How to Choose the Best Curling Iron for Short Hair 2020

When choosing the best curling iron for short hair 2020, there are a few important factors to consider. These include:

Barrel Size

The barrel size of the curling iron is an important factor to consider when choosing a curling iron for short hair. A smaller barrel size is generally better for shorter hair as it can create tighter curls or waves.

Heat Setting

The heat setting is also an important factor to consider. Short hair can be more delicate than longer hair, so it is important to choose a curling iron with a lower heat setting to prevent damage.

Material

The material of the curling iron is also important. Ceramic or tourmaline curling irons are great for short hair as they distribute heat evenly and prevent hot spots that can cause damage to your hair.

Step by Step Guide for Using the Best Curling Iron for Short Hair 2020

Using the best curling iron for short hair 2020 is easy. Here is a step by step guide:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before using the curling iron, it is important to prepare your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair as usual. Then, apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Heat Up Your Curling Iron

Heat up your curling iron to the desired heat setting. Remember, if you have short hair, it is important to choose a lower heat setting to prevent damage.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Start curling your hair, starting from the bottom sections and working your way up. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds before releasing the curl.

Step 5: Finish Your Look

After curling your hair, use a hairspray to keep your curls in place. You can also add some texture and volume to your hair by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo.

Tips for Using the Best Curling Iron for Short Hair 2020

Here are some tips for using the best curling iron for short hair 2020:

  • Choose a lower heat setting to prevent damage to your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage
  • Section your hair into small sections to ensure that you don't miss any sections
  • Start curling your hair from the bottom sections and work your way up
  • Use a hairspray to keep your curls in place
  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair

FAQ

Q: Can I use a curling iron on short hair?

A: Yes, you can use a curling iron on short hair. However, it is important to choose a curling iron that has a smaller barrel size and a lower heat setting to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: How do I choose the right curling iron for my short hair?

A: When choosing a curling iron for short hair, consider the barrel size, heat setting, and material. Look for a curling iron that has a smaller barrel size, a lower heat setting, and is made from ceramic or tourmaline.

Q: How do I curl short hair with a curling iron?

A: To curl short hair with a curling iron, section your hair into small sections, heat up your curling iron, and wrap small sections of hair around the curling iron. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds before releasing the curl.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent dryness
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to prevent breakage

Overall, the best curling iron for short hair 2020 is one that has a smaller barrel size, a lower heat setting, and is made from ceramic or tourmaline. By following the tips and steps outlined in this blog post, you can easily curl your short hair without damaging it. Remember to also take care of your hair by following the hair care tips outlined above. Happy curling!
