Shoulder Length Hair Long Layers Side Bangs The Perfect Hairstyle In 2023

Written by Luffy Nov 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and low-maintenance hairstyle, shoulder length hair with long layers and side bangs is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle is easy to style and manage, and it looks great on everyone regardless of their face shape or hair texture.

Description

Shoulder length hair long layers side bangs is a hairstyle that features shoulder-length hair that is cut into long layers with side-swept bangs. The layers add movement and dimension to the hair, while the side bangs frame the face and add a touch of elegance to the look. This hairstyle is perfect for anyone who wants to add volume and texture to their hair while keeping it low-maintenance.

How to

If you want to achieve the shoulder length hair long layers side bangs hairstyle, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to nourish your hair and prepare it for styling.

Step 2: Blow-dry your hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair, making sure to focus on the roots to add volume. Use a round brush to add curls or waves to the ends of your hair.

Step 3: Cut your hair into long layers

Use sharp scissors to cut your hair into long layers, making sure to keep the length at shoulder level. The layers should start at the chin and gradually get longer towards the back.

Step 4: Cut side-swept bangs

Cut side-swept bangs to frame your face. The bangs should be cut at an angle, starting from the middle of your forehead and ending at your cheekbones. Make sure that the bangs are longer on the side of your face.

Step 5: Style your hair

Use a curling iron or a straightener to style your hair. You can also use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair. Finish off the look with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your shoulder length hair long layers side bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep the layers looking fresh.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the shoulder length hair long layers side bangs hairstyle:

1. Is this hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this hairstyle is suitable for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

2. How often do I need to trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep the layers looking fresh.

3. Can I style my hair in different ways?

Yes, you can style your hair in different ways, such as straight, wavy, or curly. You can also experiment with different updos and half-up hairstyles.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or a swim cap.

Overall, shoulder length hair long layers side bangs is a timeless hairstyle that is perfect for anyone who wants a low-maintenance yet stylish look. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, this hairstyle is easy to achieve and versatile enough to wear in different ways.
