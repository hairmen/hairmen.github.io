3 On Top 2 On Sides Fade A Detailed Guide In 2023

Written by Eveline Sep 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that is easy to maintain? Look no further than the 3 on top 2 on sides fade. This haircut has been popular for several years now and shows no signs of going out of style. In this blog post, we will give you a detailed guide on how to get this haircut, tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a 3 on Top 2 on Sides Fade

Description

The 3 on top 2 on sides fade is a popular haircut where the hair on the top of the head is left longer, while the hair on the sides and back is trimmed shorter. This creates a gradual fade that blends the longer hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides. It is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many ways, making it a popular choice for men of all ages.

How to Get a 3 on Top 2 on Sides Fade

Getting a 3 on top 2 on sides fade is relatively simple and can be done at most barbershops or salons. It is a popular haircut, so most barbers and stylists will be familiar with it. However, it is essential to communicate clearly with your barber or stylist to ensure that you get the exact haircut you want.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get a 3 on top 2 on sides fade:

  1. Choose the length you want for the hair on top of your head. Most people go for a length of three inches or less.
  2. Decide how short you want the hair on the sides and back of your head. The hair on the sides and back should be shorter than the hair on top to create a fade.
  3. Communicate clearly with your barber or stylist about the length you want for the hair on top and the sides. It is essential to be specific and show pictures if possible.
  4. The barber or stylist will start by cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head to the desired length.
  5. They will then gradually blend the hair on the top of your head with the shorter hair on the sides and back. This creates the fade effect.
  6. The barber or stylist will then trim any excess hair and style the hair on top of your head according to your preferences.

Tips for Maintaining Your Fade

Now that you have your 3 on top 2 on sides fade, it is essential to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Get a trim every 2-3 weeks to keep the fade looking sharp.
  • Invest in good hair products to keep your hair looking healthy and styled.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free from dirt and oil.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like straighteners or curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting a touch-up in between haircuts to keep the fade looking fresh.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the 3 on top 2 on sides fade:

Is the 3 on top 2 on sides fade suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the 3 on top 2 on sides fade is suitable for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How long does the 3 on top 2 on sides fade last?

The 3 on top 2 on sides fade can last anywhere from 2-4 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain it.

Can I style my hair differently with a 3 on top 2 on sides fade?

Yes, one of the great things about the 3 on top 2 on sides fade is that it is versatile and can be styled in many ways. You can slick it back, comb it over, or even create a messy, textured look.

Hair Care for a 3 on Top 2 on Sides Fade

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a 3 on top 2 on sides fade. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

Getting a 3 on top 2 on sides fade is a great way to update your look and stay on-trend. With proper maintenance and care, your fade will look sharp and stylish for weeks to come.
