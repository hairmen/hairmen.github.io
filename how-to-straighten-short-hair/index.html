How To Straighten Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 14, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of your short hair always being curly or wavy? Do you wish your hair was straighter and smoother? If so, you’re in luck! In this post, we’ll give you a step-by-step guide on how to straighten short hair. We’ll also provide you with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve the best results.

Table of Contents

  • How to Straighten Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Straightening Short Hair
  • FAQ

How to Straighten Short Hair

Straightening short hair can be challenging, especially if you don’t know how to do it properly. But don’t worry! We’ve got you covered. Here’s what you need to do:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step is to wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo. This will remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup from your hair, making it easier to straighten.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before you start straightening your hair, it’s important to apply a heat protectant spray or serum. This will protect your hair from heat damage and keep it healthy.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Once you’ve applied the heat protectant, you’ll need to blow dry your hair. Use a round brush to help straighten your hair as you blow dry it.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

After your hair is dry, you’ll need to section it off. Use hair clips to separate your hair into small sections. This will make it easier to straighten your hair evenly.

Step 5: Straighten Your Hair

Now it’s time to straighten your hair. Use a flat iron with ceramic plates to straighten each section of hair. Start at the roots and slowly move the flat iron down to the ends of your hair.

Step 6: Finish with Hair Spray

Once you’ve straightened your hair, finish it off with a light mist of hair spray. This will help hold your hair in place and keep it straight throughout the day.

Tips for Straightening Short Hair

Straightening short hair can be tricky, but with these tips, you’ll be able to achieve the best results:

  • Use a flat iron with ceramic plates
  • Section your hair off before straightening
  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum
  • Blow dry your hair before straightening
  • Finish with a light mist of hair spray

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my short hair without a flat iron?

A: Yes, you can straighten your short hair without a flat iron. You can use a straightening brush or blow dryer with a comb attachment to straighten your hair.

Q: How often should I straighten my short hair?

A: It’s best to avoid straightening your hair every day, as this can cause damage. Try to limit straightening to once or twice a week.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your short hair healthy and strong, follow these hair care tips:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you’ll be able to straighten your short hair like a pro. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you. Good luck!
