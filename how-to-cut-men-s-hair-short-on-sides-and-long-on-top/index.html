How To Cut Men s Hair Short On Sides And Long On Top

Written by William Aug 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting men's hair short on the sides and long on top is a popular hairstyle that has been around for decades. This style is known by many names, such as the undercut, the disconnected undercut, or the fade. Whatever you call it, the basic idea is the same: the hair on the sides is cut short while leaving the hair on the top long. This look is versatile and easy to maintain, making it a popular choice for men of all ages.

Description

The short on sides and long on top haircut is a trendy and modern hairstyle that has become very popular in recent years. This hairstyle features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on the top. This style is great for men who want a low-maintenance haircut that is easy to style and looks good with minimal effort.

How to Cut Men's Hair Short on Sides and Long on Top

To achieve this look, you will need some basic hair cutting tools, such as clippers, scissors, combs, and a mirror. It is also helpful to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen the hair before cutting. Follow these steps to cut men's hair short on sides and long on top:

Step 1: Section the Hair

The first step is to section the hair. Use a comb to create a part from the crown of the head to the back of the ear on both sides. Clip the top section of the hair out of the way, leaving the sides and back exposed.

Step 2: Cut the Sides

Using clippers with a number 3 or 4 guard, cut the hair on the sides and back of the head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the clipper over comb technique. Blend the hair from the top to the sides, creating a smooth and even transition.

Step 3: Blend the Hair

Using scissors or clippers without a guard, blend the hair from the top section to the sides. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle, creating a natural-looking transition.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Unclip the top section of the hair and comb it straight up. Use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. You can also use clippers with a guard to create a textured look.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Apply a small amount of hair product, such as pomade or wax, to the hair on top. Use your fingers to style the hair into the desired shape. You can slick it back, comb it to the side, or leave it messy and textured.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short on sides and long on top haircut:

  • Start with longer hair on top to give yourself more room to work with.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from all angles.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Use a spray bottle to dampen the hair before cutting to make it easier to work with.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: How long should the hair be on top?

A: The length of the hair on top can vary depending on personal preference. Generally, it should be long enough to style, but not so long that it becomes unmanageable.

Q: Can I cut my own hair short on sides and long on top?

A: It is possible to cut your own hair, but it can be difficult to achieve the desired look without professional training. It is recommended to seek the help of a professional hairstylist.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the short on sides and long on top hairstyle. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, on a daily basis.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect short on sides and long on top haircut. Whether you're looking for a classic look or a modern twist, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and stylish.
