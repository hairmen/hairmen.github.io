How to Change Your Face Shape on Bitmoji

Changing your Bitmoji's face shape is easy! Here are the steps:

Step 1: Open the Bitmoji App

First, open the Bitmoji app on your mobile device. If you don't have the app yet, you can download it from the App Store or Google Play Store.

Step 2: Go to the Avatar Customization Screen

Once you're in the app, go to the avatar customization screen. This is where you can change your Bitmoji's appearance, including their face shape.

Step 3: Select the Face Shape Option

Next, select the face shape option. This should be located near the top of the screen.

Step 4: Choose Your New Face Shape

Finally, choose your new face shape from the options provided. You can select from a variety of shapes, including oval, round, and square.

Once you've selected your new face shape, your Bitmoji will be updated! It's that simple.

You can change your Bitmoji's face shape as many times as you want!

No, you can only change your Bitmoji's face shape on the mobile app.
