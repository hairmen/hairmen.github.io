How To Make Dreads With Short Hair At Home Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short hair and want to add some texture to your locks, dreadlocks can be a great option. However, many people believe that making dreads with short hair is impossible. But, don't worry, it's not true! In this article, we will guide you through a step-by-step process for making dreads with short hair at home.

Description

Dreadlocks are a hairstyle that has been around for centuries. They are formed by matting or braiding hair, which creates a unique and textured look. Many people think that dreadlocks can only be made with long hair, but that's not true. You can make dreads with short hair too.

How to Make Dreads with Short Hair at Home

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make dreads with short hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Before starting the process, wash your hair thoroughly. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup or oil from your hair. Dry your hair with a towel or a hairdryer.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into small sections. The size of each section will depend on the thickness of your hair. Use hair elastics to secure each section and prevent them from getting tangled.

Step 3: Backcomb your hair

Take one section of hair and hold it tightly. Use a fine-toothed comb to backcomb the hair in the opposite direction of growth. Continue backcombing until the hair starts to clump and form a dreadlock.

Step 4: Twist the hair

After backcombing, twist the hair in the same direction as growth. Keep twisting until the hair is tightly coiled and forms a dreadlock. Secure the end with a hair elastic.

Step 5: Repeat the process

Repeat the backcombing and twisting process with each section of hair. Make sure to keep the size of each section consistent.

Step 6: Let the dreads set

Once you have created all the dreadlocks, let them set for a few days. Do not wash your hair during this time. The dreads will tighten and become more defined over time.

Tips for Making Dreads with Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you make dreads with short hair:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup or oil from your hair.
  • Backcomb your hair gently to avoid damaging it.
  • Twist the hair tightly to create a tight dreadlock.
  • Use a hair elastic to secure the end of each dreadlock.

FAQ

Can I make dreads with short hair?

Yes, you can make dreads with short hair. Follow the step-by-step guide mentioned above to create dreadlocks with short hair.

How long does it take to make dreads with short hair?

The time it takes to make dreads with short hair depends on the length and thickness of your hair. It can take anywhere from a few hours to a few days to make dreads with short hair.

How do I maintain my dreadlocks?

To maintain your dreadlocks, wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo. Use a crochet hook to tighten loose hair and remove any debris that may accumulate in the dreads.

Hair Care for Dreadlocks

Here are some hair care tips to keep your dreadlocks healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products on your hair.
  • Use a crochet hook to tighten loose hair and remove debris.
  • Avoid using a hairdryer as it can cause the dreads to unravel.

With the above guide, it is possible to make dreads with short hair at home. It may take a little patience and practice, but the end result will be worth it. Follow the tips mentioned above to keep your dreadlocks healthy and well-maintained.
