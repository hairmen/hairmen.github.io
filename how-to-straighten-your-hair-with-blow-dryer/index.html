How To Straighten Your Hair With Blow Dryer A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read
If you're looking for a simple and effective way to straighten your hair, a blow dryer can be your best friend. Not only is it quick and easy, but it also helps to minimize damage to your hair compared to straightening irons. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps of how to straighten your hair with a blow dryer, as well as some tips and hair care advice.

Description

Straightening your hair with a blow dryer involves using heat and a brush to smooth out your hair strands, resulting in straight, sleek locks. This method is ideal for those with wavy or slightly curly hair who want a straighter look without using chemical treatments or flat irons.

How to Straighten Your Hair with Blow Dryer

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to straighten your hair with a blow dryer:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will help to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup that can interfere with the straightening process. Be sure to use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair too vigorously, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant product to your hair. This will help to reduce the risk of heat damage and keep your hair healthy. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends and any areas that are particularly prone to damage.

Step 4: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to work on one section at a time and ensure that you don't miss any areas.

Step 5: Begin Blow Drying

Starting with the bottom section, use your blow dryer and a brush to straighten your hair. Hold the brush under a section of hair and blow dry from the roots to the ends, pulling the hair taut with the brush. Repeat this process until the section is straight and smooth.

Step 6: Move to the Next Section

Once you've straightened one section, unclip the next section and repeat the process until all sections are straightened.

Step 7: Finish with a Cold Shot

Once you've straightened your hair, finish with a cold shot of air from your blow dryer. This will help to lock in the style and reduce frizz.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when straightening your hair with a blow dryer: - Use a high-quality blow dryer with multiple heat and speed settings. - Choose a brush that's appropriate for your hair type and length. - Don't use too much tension when pulling your hair with the brush, as this can cause breakage. - Use a comb or your fingers to gently detangle any knots before blow-drying. - Don't blow-dry your hair when it's soaking wet, as this can cause damage and frizz. - Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz after blow-drying.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about straightening your hair with a blow dryer:

Q: Can I straighten my hair with a blow dryer every day?

A: It's not recommended to straighten your hair with a blow dryer every day, as this can cause damage and dryness. Try to limit your use to a few times per week and use a heat protectant product to minimize damage.

Q: What if my hair is too curly to straighten with a blow dryer?

A: If your hair is very curly, it may not be possible to straighten it completely with a blow dryer. In this case, you may want to use a flat iron or chemical treatment to achieve a straighter look.

Q: How long does it take to straighten your hair with a blow dryer?

A: The length of time it takes to straighten your hair with a blow dryer depends on your hair length and thickness. Generally, it can take anywhere from 15 to 45 minutes.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant product when blow-drying or styling, and avoiding harsh chemicals or treatments that can damage your hair. By following these tips and steps, you can achieve straight, sleek hair with a blow dryer. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great!
