What Hair Color Suits Me Most Tips For Choosing The Right Shade

Written by Daniel Nov 23, 2023 · 5 min read

Choosing a hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what shade suits you best. With so many options available, it's easy to feel overwhelmed. However, with a few tips and tricks, you can choose the perfect hair color that will enhance your natural beauty. In this article, we'll discuss how to determine what hair color suits you most and provide some helpful tips and advice.

How to Determine the Right Hair Color for You

Before you decide on a hair color, it's important to consider a few factors. These include your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. By taking these into account, you can determine what shade will complement your features and enhance your overall appearance. Here are some tips for choosing the right hair color:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors when it comes to choosing a hair color. If you have warm undertones, you'll want to stick with warm shades like golden blonde, copper, or auburn. If you have cool undertones, you'll want to choose cool shades like ash blonde, platinum, or cool brown.

Look at Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining what hair color suits you best. If you have blue or green eyes, you'll want to choose warm shades that will make your eyes pop, such as golden blonde or copper. If you have brown eyes, you can choose warmer or cooler shades, such as auburn or cool brown.

Consider Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also be a factor in choosing a new shade. If you have dark hair, it can be more challenging to go lighter without damaging your hair. You may want to consider a shade that is closer to your natural color, such as a rich chocolate brown. If you have light hair, you have more options when it comes to going darker or adding highlights.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Color

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a hair color, here is a step-by-step guide to help you make the right decision:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you may have neutral undertones, which means you can wear both warm and cool shades.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Look at your eye color and consider whether it is warm or cool. If you have blue or green eyes, you have cool eyes. If you have brown or hazel eyes, you have warm eyes.

Step 3: Determine Your Natural Hair Color

Look at your natural hair color and consider whether it is warm or cool. If you have warm undertones in your hair, you'll want to stick with warm shades. If you have cool undertones, you'll want to choose cool shades.

Step 4: Choose Your Desired Shade

Once you have determined your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color, you can choose the shade that best suits you. Take into account your lifestyle and how much maintenance you're willing to do. If you want a low-maintenance look, consider a shade that is closer to your natural hair color.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Once you've chosen your hair color, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking fresh and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair color:

Use Color-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Color-safe shampoo and conditioner are designed to protect your hair color and prevent it from fading. Look for products that are specifically formulated for your hair type and color.

Avoid Hot Tools

Hot tools can damage your hair and cause your color to fade faster. If you must use hot tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This will help your color look more vibrant and last longer.

Avoid Chlorine

Chlorine can cause your color to fade and turn green. If you're going to be swimming, wear a swim cap or use a clarifying shampoo after you swim.

FAQs

How often should I touch up my roots?

This will vary depending on your hair growth and the color you've chosen. Generally, you'll want to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks to keep your color looking fresh.

Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home. However, it's important to follow the instructions carefully and choose a shade that is close to your natural hair color to avoid damaging your hair.

What should I do if I don't like my new hair color?

If you don't like your new hair color, you can try to wash it out with a clarifying shampoo or visit a salon to have it corrected.

Will coloring my hair damage it?

Coloring your hair can cause damage if it's not done properly. It's important to choose a high-quality dye and follow the instructions carefully to minimize damage.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair color can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color, you can choose a shade that enhances your natural beauty. Remember to take care of your hair color to keep it looking fresh and vibrant. With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect hair color that suits you most.
