What is a Partial Updo?

A partial updo is a hairstyle that combines elements of an updo and leaving some hair down. It's a versatile hairstyle that works for both formal and casual occasions. The partial updo is a great choice for those with long hair, as it can add some dimension and interest to an otherwise plain hairstyle.
