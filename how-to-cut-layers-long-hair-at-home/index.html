How To Cut Layers Long Hair At Home Step By Step Guide And Tips

Written by Luffy Jul 14, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking to switch up your long hair or give it some volume, cutting layers can do wonders. While it's always a good idea to go to a professional, cutting layers at home can be a fun and cost-effective way to change up your look. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut layers long hair at home with ease.

How to Cut Layers Long Hair at Home

Before you start, you'll need a few tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair elastic

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair completely. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free from any tangles, making it easier to cut.

Step 2: Part Your Hair

Next, part your hair in the middle or to one side, depending on your preference. Use a comb to create a straight part down the middle or to one side of your head.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections using the comb. Start at the crown of your head and create a horizontal part, separating the top section of your hair from the bottom. Use a hair elastic to tie the top section of your hair up and out of the way.

Step 4: Cut the Bottom Layer

Take the bottom layer of your hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle finger, and cut off the desired length using sharp scissors. Be sure to cut straight across and not at an angle.

Step 5: Cut the Middle Layer

Release the top section of your hair and comb it down. Take a small section of hair from the middle layer, comb it straight down, and cut it to the same length as the bottom layer.

Step 6: Cut the Top Layer

Finally, release the remaining top section of your hair and comb it down. Take a small section of hair from the top layer, comb it straight down, and cut it to the same length as the bottom and middle layers.

Step 7: Blend the Layers

Blend the layers by taking small sections of hair from each layer and combing them together. Use your scissors to trim any uneven sections and ensure the layers blend seamlessly.

Tips for Cutting Layers Long Hair at Home

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to avoid cutting too much off
  • Step back and evaluate your hair often to ensure even layers
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as it can appear shorter when dry

FAQ About Cutting Layers Long Hair at Home

Can I cut layers in curly hair?

Yes, you can cut layers in curly hair. However, it's important to cut curly hair when it's dry to avoid cutting too much off.

How often should I cut my layers?

It's recommended to cut your layers every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and volume.

Hair Care After Cutting Layers Long Hair at Home

After cutting your layers, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure it stays healthy and vibrant. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week and avoid using heat tools too often. Additionally, use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair to avoid pulling and breakage.

Cutting layers long hair at home can be a fun and cost-effective way to switch up your look. Follow these step-by-step instructions and tips to achieve salon-worthy layers at home. Remember to take care of your hair post-cutting to ensure it stays healthy and vibrant.
