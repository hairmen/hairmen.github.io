How To Fix Short Frizzy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Aug 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short frizzy hair, you know the struggle of trying to tame it. It can be frustrating to deal with hair that seems to have a mind of its own. But don't worry, there are ways to fix short frizzy hair and make it look smooth and sleek. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to fix short frizzy hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Fix Short Frizzy Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Managing Short Frizzy Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions

How to Fix Short Frizzy Hair

There are several ways to fix short frizzy hair. One way is to use a smoothing serum or cream. These products are designed to tame flyaways and reduce frizz. Another way is to use a hair straightener. Straightening your hair can help to smooth out frizz and make it look sleek.

Another option is to get a haircut. A shorter haircut can help to reduce the appearance of frizz. Ask your hairstylist for a cut that works well with your hair type and face shape. They may recommend a layered cut or a blunt cut, depending on your needs.

Lastly, consider getting a keratin treatment. This treatment can help to reduce frizz and make your hair more manageable. It works by adding a protective layer to your hair, which helps to smooth it out and reduce frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to fix short frizzy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently, being careful not to rub it too hard.
  3. Apply a smoothing serum or cream to your hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths.
  4. Blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting, using a round brush to smooth it out.
  5. Use a hair straightener to further smooth out any frizz.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Managing Short Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing short frizzy hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too often, as this can cause breakage and make your hair more prone to frizz.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about fixing short frizzy hair:

Q: Can I use a hair mask to fix short frizzy hair?

A: Yes, a hair mask can help to hydrate and smooth out frizzy hair. Look for a mask that is designed for your hair type and apply it once a week.

Q: How often should I get a haircut to manage short frizzy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and frizz-free.

Q: Can I use a leave-in conditioner to fix short frizzy hair?

A: Yes, a leave-in conditioner can help to hydrate and smooth out frizzy hair. Look for a product that is designed for your hair type and apply it to damp hair.

Hair Care for Short Frizzy Hair

To keep your short frizzy hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and frizz-free.

By following these tips and tricks, you can fix short frizzy hair and make it look smooth and sleek. Remember to take good care of your hair and use products that are designed for your hair type. If you're not sure which products to use, ask your hairstylist for recommendations.
