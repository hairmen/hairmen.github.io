Mens Cuts Short Sides Long Top A Guide To The Latest Trend In Men s Hairstyles

Mens cuts short sides long top is a trendy hairstyle that has been around for some time now. It is a versatile cut that can be styled in various ways to suit different occasions, making it a popular choice among men of all ages. If you're looking to try this style, then this guide will provide you with all the information you need.

Description

Mens cuts short sides long top is a hairstyle where the hair on the sides of the head is cut short, while the hair on the top is left long. This style is also known as the undercut, and it has been around since the early 1900s. It was initially popular among men in the military, but it has since become a mainstream hairstyle for men of all ages and professions.

How to

The mens cuts short sides long top style is a low-maintenance cut that is easy to style. Here's how to achieve this look:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in achieving this look is to get the right haircut. The hair on the sides of your head should be cut short, while the hair on the top should be left long. You can either go to a barber or a stylist to get this cut, or you can do it yourself if you have the right tools and skills.

Step 2: Style Your Hair

Once you have the right haircut, you can start styling your hair. There are many ways to style this cut, depending on your preferences and the occasion. You can slick the hair back, comb it to the side, or create a messy look by tousling the hair with your fingers. Experiment with different styles to see what works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting and styling mens cuts short sides long top:

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Use the right products to style your hair, such as pomade or wax.
  • Trim the hair on the sides regularly to maintain the look.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens cuts short sides long top:

Is this style suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this style can be adapted to suit different hair types. However, the length and texture of the hair on the top may need to be adjusted depending on your hair type.

Can I style this look myself?

Yes, you can style this look yourself if you have the right tools and skills. However, it's always best to get the initial cut done by a professional to ensure that the style is done correctly.

Is this style suitable for all face shapes?

This style can be adapted to suit different face shapes, but it's best to consult with a stylist or barber to determine the best cut for your face shape.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep your mens cuts short sides long top looking its best. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim the ends regularly to prevent split ends.

With proper care and styling, mens cuts short sides long top can be a stylish and practical hairstyle for any occasion. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance cut for your daily routine or a more polished look for a special occasion, this style is a versatile option that can be adapted to suit your needs.
