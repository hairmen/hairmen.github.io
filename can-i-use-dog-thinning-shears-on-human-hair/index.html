Can I Use Dog Thinning Shears On Human Hair

Written by Christine Jul 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a way to thin out your hair without losing length, you may have heard about using dog thinning shears on human hair. While it may seem like a great idea, it's important to consider the potential risks and drawbacks before you use these shears. In this article, we'll explore whether or not you can use dog thinning shears on human hair, and what you need to know before you try it.

Description

Dog thinning shears are designed to thin out the fur of dogs without cutting it too short. They have a special blade that removes some of the hair without damaging the rest. Many people have wondered whether or not they can use these shears on human hair, and the answer is that it's possible, but not always recommended.

How to Use Dog Thinning Shears on Human Hair

If you're determined to use dog thinning shears on your own hair, there are a few steps you can take to get the best results:

Step 1: Use Clean Shears

Make sure your shears are clean and free of any dirt or debris. You don't want to risk transferring any bacteria or germs to your hair.

Step 2: Start at the Ends

Begin by working on the ends of your hair, rather than the roots. This will help you avoid cutting too much hair and ending up with a shorter style than you intended.

Step 3: Take Small Sections

Work in small sections, taking care not to cut too much hair at once. This will help you achieve a more natural, gradual look.

Step 4: Use a Comb

Use a comb to separate the hair as you work, and to make sure you're only cutting the hair you want to thin out.

Tips for Using Dog Thinning Shears on Human Hair

If you're going to use dog thinning shears on your own hair, there are a few tips you should keep in mind:

  • Start with a small amount of hair, and work your way up to more if needed.
  • Be patient, and take your time. It's better to take small steps than to cut too much hair at once.
  • Use a mirror to make sure you're getting the results you want.
  • Consider asking a friend or family member for help, especially if you're new to using thinning shears.

FAQ

Is it safe to use dog thinning shears on human hair?

While it's possible to use dog thinning shears on human hair, it's not always recommended. These shears are designed for a different type of hair, and may not work as well on human hair.

Can I use regular scissors instead?

If you're looking to thin out your hair, it's best to use shears that are designed specifically for human hair. Regular scissors may not give you the same results.

How can I care for my hair after using dog thinning shears?

After using thinning shears on your hair, it's important to care for it properly. This may include using a deep conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

Thinning out your hair can be a great way to achieve a more natural, effortless look. However, it's important to use the right tools and techniques to avoid damaging your hair. If you're considering using dog thinning shears on your own hair, be sure to follow the tips and advice we've provided above, and take care of your hair properly after you're done.
