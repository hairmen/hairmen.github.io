How To Grow Out Medium Length Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Growing out medium length hair can be a challenging process, but it's definitely worth it in the end. Whether you're looking to add length to your hair for a special occasion or simply want to switch up your look, there are a few key steps you can take to make the process as smooth as possible. In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about how to grow out medium length hair.

How to Grow Out Medium Length Hair

If you're looking to grow out your medium length hair, there are a few key steps you should follow:

Step 1: Be Patient

The first step to growing out your medium length hair is to be patient. Hair growth takes time, and there's no magic formula that will make your hair grow faster overnight. However, if you're consistent with your hair care routine and give your hair the time it needs to grow, you'll start to see results.

Step 2: Get Regular Trims

While it might seem counterintuitive, getting regular trims is actually one of the best things you can do to grow out your hair. Trimming your hair every 6-8 weeks helps to prevent split ends and breakage, which can slow down the growth process. Plus, regular trims help to keep your hair looking healthy and full.

Step 3: Use the Right Products

Using the right hair care products is essential for growing out medium length hair. Look for products that are specifically designed to promote hair growth, such as biotin-infused shampoos and conditioners. You should also use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.

Step 4: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like blow dryers, curling irons, and straighteners can cause damage to your hair, which can slow down the growth process. Try to avoid using these tools as much as possible, and opt for air-drying your hair instead. If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to growing out your medium length hair:

Step 1: Start with a Healthy Foundation

Before you start growing out your hair, make sure it's in good condition. This means getting regular trims, using the right hair care products, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible.

Step 2: Set Realistic Goals

Take a look at your current hair length and decide how much longer you want it to be. Set realistic goals based on your hair's growth rate and the amount of time you're willing to dedicate to the process.

Step 3: Get Regular Trims

As we mentioned earlier, getting regular trims is essential for growing out your hair. Make sure to schedule a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Step 4: Use the Right Products

Using the right hair care products is key for growing out medium length hair. Look for products that are designed to promote hair growth, and use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

Step 5: Be Gentle with Your Hair

Be gentle with your hair when you're washing and styling it. Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, and use a wide-tooth comb to detangle it instead of a brush.

Step 6: Experiment with Styles

As your hair grows, experiment with different styles to keep it looking fresh and new. Try braids, updos, and other hairstyles that work well for medium length hair.

Step 7: Be Patient

Remember, growing out your hair takes time. Be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and you'll start to see results over time.

Tips for Growing Out Medium Length Hair

Here are a few additional tips to help you grow out your medium length hair:

  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote blood flow and stimulate hair growth.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using tight hair elastics or hair accessories that can cause breakage.
  • Consider taking a hair growth supplement, such as biotin or collagen.

FAQs

How long does it take to grow out medium length hair?

The length of time it takes to grow out your medium length hair will depend on a variety of factors, including your hair's growth rate, the length you're starting from, and how well you take care of your hair. On average, hair grows about half an inch per month, so it can take several months or even a year to grow out medium length hair.

Can I use hair extensions to grow out my hair?

Hair extensions can be a great way to add length to your hair while you're growing it out. However, it's important to choose high-quality extensions that blend well with your natural hair and don't cause damage or breakage.

Hair Care Tips for Growing Out Medium Length Hair

Here are a few additional hair care tips to help you grow out your medium length hair:

  • Wash your hair no more than 2-3 times per week to prevent stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hair care products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage.

By following these tips and steps, you can successfully grow out your medium length hair and achieve the long, luscious locks you've been dreaming of.
