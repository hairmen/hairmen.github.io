How To Cut Your Hair Shorter At The Front A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle and want to cut your hair shorter at the front, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look, as well as some tips and frequently asked questions to help you along the way.

How to Cut Your Hair Shorter at the Front

Before we dive into the steps, it's important to note that if you're not confident in cutting your own hair, it's best to leave it to a professional stylist. However, if you're feeling brave and want to give it a go, here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

To cut your hair at home, you'll need a few basic tools:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair ties
  • Hair clips

Make sure your scissors are sharp and clean to ensure a clean cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Once you have your tools, it's time to section your hair. Start by combing your hair straight down in the middle. Then, take a section of hair from the top of your head and tie it up. This will be the hair you cut later.

Next, divide the remaining hair into two sections, one on each side of your head. Clip each section to keep it in place.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Now it's time to start cutting. Unclip one of the side sections and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut a small amount at a time. Remember, it's always better to cut less than more, as you can always trim more later.

Once you've finished one side, repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Cut the Top Section

Now it's time to cut the top section of hair that you tied up earlier. Comb it straight down and cut a small amount at a time, just like you did with the side sections. Be sure to blend the hair in with the sides for a seamless look.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting, check your work in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure your hair is even and the way you want it.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair Shorter at the Front

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you cut your hair shorter at the front:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use sharp scissors
  • Cut small amounts at a time
  • Blend the top section with the sides for a seamless look
  • Don't cut too much at once

FAQ

Can I cut my hair shorter at the front if I have curly hair?

Yes! However, keep in mind that curly hair tends to shrink up when cut. It's best to cut your hair when it's dry, so you can see how much it will shrink.

How can I style my hair after cutting it shorter at the front?

There are many ways to style shorter hair, from messy buns to sleek ponytails. Experiment with different styles to find what works for you.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid heat styling tools
  • Get regular trims to maintain your style
  • Use a hair mask once a week
  • Protect your hair from the sun

With these tips and steps, you'll be able to cut your hair shorter at the front with confidence. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun with it!
