How To Curl Long Thick Coarse Hair A Relaxing Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you have long, thick, coarse hair that seems impossible to curl? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with this hair type struggle to achieve the perfect curl. But with the right techniques and tools, you can achieve beautiful curls that last all day. In this guide, we'll show you how to curl long thick coarse hair step by step, as well as provide some tips and hair care advice along the way.

How to Curl Long Thick Coarse Hair

Curling long thick coarse hair can be challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve beautiful curls that last all day. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling, it's important to prep your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant to prevent damage from the curling iron.

Step 2: Choose Your Curling Iron

Choosing the right curling iron is essential for achieving the perfect curl. For long thick coarse hair, we recommend using a curling iron with a barrel size of 1.5 inches or larger. This will help create loose, beachy waves that look natural and effortless.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Sectioning your hair is important for ensuring that you curl every strand evenly. Divide your hair into sections, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Use clips to hold each section in place.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Starting at the bottom section, take a small strand of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold for 10-15 seconds, then release. Move up to the next section and repeat until all sections are curled.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To help your curls last all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. Hold the can about 10 inches away from your hair and spray evenly.

Tips for Curling Long Thick Coarse Hair

Curling long thick coarse hair can be tricky, but with these tips, you can achieve beautiful curls that last:

  • Use a heat protectant to prevent damage from the curling iron
  • Choose a curling iron with a large barrel size for loose, natural-looking waves
  • Section your hair to ensure that every strand is curled evenly
  • Curl your hair away from your face for a more flattering look
  • Finish with hairspray to help your curls last all day

FAQs

Q: Can I curl my hair without heat?

A: Yes, there are several heat-free methods for curling your hair, such as braiding or using foam rollers. However, these methods may not work as well for long thick coarse hair.

Q: How do I prevent my curls from falling flat?

A: To help your curls last all day, use a strong hold hairspray and avoid touching or brushing your hair too much.

Q: Can I use a curling wand instead of a curling iron?

A: Yes, a curling wand can be used to achieve similar results. However, a curling iron with a clamp may be easier to use for beginners.

Hair Care for Long Thick Coarse Hair

In addition to curling your hair, it's important to take care of your long thick coarse hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use products that are designed for your hair type
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated
  • Avoid using heat tools too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water for overall hair health

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful curls that last all day. Remember to take good care of your long thick coarse hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
