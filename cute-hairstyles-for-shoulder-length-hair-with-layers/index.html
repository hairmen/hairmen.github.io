Cute Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair With Layers

Written by Robby Nov 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Shoulder length hair with layers is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there are plenty of cute hairstyles that you can try. Here are some of our favorite cute hairstyles for shoulder length hair with layers.

Description

Shoulder length hair with layers is a popular hairstyle because it frames the face and adds volume to the hair. Layers can be cut in different lengths to create different looks. Shorter layers can add volume and texture, while longer layers can create a more subtle, blended look. Shoulder length hair with layers is also easy to maintain and style, making it a great choice for busy women.

How to

To create cute hairstyles for shoulder length hair with layers, start by washing and conditioning your hair. Apply a styling product such as mousse or styling cream to add volume and hold. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and smooth out any frizz.

1. Half Up Half Down

The half up half down hairstyle is a classic look that works well with shoulder length hair with layers. To create this look, gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a clip or elastic band. Leave the bottom half of your hair down and add some curls or waves for a romantic touch.

2. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a cute and casual hairstyle that is perfect for shoulder length hair with layers. To create this look, gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it around into a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and pull out a few strands to create a messy, undone look.

3. Braids

Braids are a fun and creative way to style shoulder length hair with layers. Try a simple French braid, a fishtail braid, or a braided crown for a bohemian look. Braids also work well with wavy or curly hair.

Step by step

Here are step-by-step instructions for creating the half up half down hairstyle:

  1. Divide your hair into two sections: the top half and the bottom half.
  2. Gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a clip or elastic band.
  3. Leave the bottom half of your hair down and add some curls or waves with a curling iron or hair straightener.
  4. Release the top half of your hair and tease it at the crown to add volume.
  5. Take a small section of hair from each side of your head and twist it towards the back of your head.
  6. Secure the twisted sections of hair with bobby pins at the back of your head.
  7. Adjust the hairstyle as needed and add hairspray to hold it in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling shoulder length hair with layers:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories such as headbands, hair clips, and scrunchies to add some flair to your hairstyle.
  • Try adding some highlights or lowlights to your hair to create dimension and depth.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes and add some texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cute hairstyles for shoulder length hair with layers:

What is the best way to add volume to shoulder length hair with layers?

The best way to add volume to shoulder length hair with layers is to use a volumizing product such as mousse or styling spray. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to lift the roots and add volume.

Can you wear a ponytail with shoulder length hair with layers?

Yes, you can wear a ponytail with shoulder length hair with layers. Try a high ponytail for a sleek and sophisticated look, or a low ponytail for a more casual look. You can also add some volume to your ponytail by teasing the crown of your hair before pulling it back.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length hair with layers looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and smooth.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and healthy.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these cute hairstyles for shoulder length hair with layers, you can switch up your look and feel confident and stylish. Try out different styles and find the one that works best for you!
