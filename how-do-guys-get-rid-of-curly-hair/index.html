How Do Guys Get Rid Of Curly Hair

Written by William Nov 25, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a guy with curly hair, you may have wondered how to get rid of those curls. While curly hair can be a great look, some guys prefer a more straight, sleek style. In this article, we'll explore the different methods for getting rid of curly hair, including how to straighten your hair and how to permanently remove curls.

How to Straighten Your Hair

One of the most common ways to get rid of curly hair is to straighten it. There are a few different methods for straightening hair, including using a straightening iron, blow drying, and chemical treatments. Here's how to do each method:

Using a Straightening Iron

To straighten your hair using a straightening iron, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage.
  3. Section your hair into small sections.
  4. Clamp the straightening iron on a section of hair near the roots.
  5. Slowly pull the iron down the length of the hair, making sure to keep it taut.
  6. Repeat on all sections of hair until your hair is straight.

Keep in mind that using a straightening iron can damage your hair, so it's important to use a heat protectant and not straighten your hair too often.

Blow Drying

To straighten your hair using a blow dryer, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage.
  3. Section your hair into small sections.
  4. Use a round brush to pull each section of hair taut while blow drying it.
  5. Repeat on all sections of hair until your hair is straight.

Blow drying is less damaging than using a straightening iron, but it can still cause damage if done too often.

Chemical Treatments

If you want a more permanent solution to curly hair, you can try chemical treatments like a relaxer or a keratin treatment. These treatments work by breaking down the bonds in your hair that cause it to curl.

Keep in mind that chemical treatments can be damaging to your hair and should only be done by a professional stylist.

How to Permanently Remove Curls

If you're looking for a more permanent solution to curly hair, you can try a process called thermal reconditioning. This process uses heat to permanently break down the bonds in your hair that cause it to curl.

Thermal reconditioning should only be done by a professional stylist, and it can be expensive. Additionally, the process can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to take good care of it after the treatment.

Tips for Caring for Straightened Hair

If you've straightened your hair, it's important to take good care of it to prevent damage. Here are some tips for caring for straightened hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair too often.
  • Use a heat protectant if you do use heat.
  • Don't brush your hair when it's wet.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I straighten my hair?

It's best to avoid straightening your hair too often, as it can be damaging. Aim to straighten your hair no more than once a week.

How long does a chemical treatment last?

The length of time a chemical treatment lasts depends on the treatment and your hair type. Some treatments last a few months, while others can last up to a year.

Will thermal reconditioning work on all hair types?

Thermal reconditioning works best on hair that is thick and coarse. If you have fine hair, you may not see the same results.

Getting rid of curly hair is possible with the right methods. Whether you choose to straighten your hair temporarily or permanently remove the curls, it's important to take good care of your hair to prevent damage.
