How To Figure Out The Best Haircut For Your Face

Written by Frank Nov 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle, one of the most important things to consider is your face shape. The right haircut can help accentuate your best features and create a flattering look. In this article, we'll go over how to figure out the best haircut for your face shape and provide some tips for maintaining your new 'do.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing a Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQs about Choosing the Best Haircut for Your Face
  • Hair Care Tips for Healthy Locks

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can choose the best haircut for your face, you'll need to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Rectangle

To figure out your face shape, start by pulling your hair back and taking a good look at your face in the mirror. Consider the following:

  • The length and width of your face
  • The shape of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline
  • The overall balance of your features

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement your features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing a Haircut

When choosing a haircut, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a style that flatters your face shape
  • Consider your hair type and texture
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on styling
  • Consult with a stylist for personalized advice

Here are some general guidelines for each face shape:

Round

If you have a round face shape, choose a haircut that adds height and lengthens your face. A layered cut with long, face-framing layers can help elongate your features. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs, as they can make your face appear wider.

Oval

Oval faces are versatile and can pull off a variety of haircuts. Consider a long, layered cut with face-framing layers or a bob with side-swept bangs. Avoid styles that add too much height or volume to the top of your head.

Square

If you have a square face shape, choose a haircut that softens your features. A layered cut with soft, rounded edges can help balance out a strong jawline. Avoid blunt cuts and straight-across bangs, as they can make your face appear boxy.

Heart

Heart-shaped faces look great with haircuts that add volume around the chin area. A long, layered cut with side-swept bangs or a bob with chin-length layers can help balance out a wider forehead. Avoid heavy, blunt bangs and short, choppy cuts.

Diamond

If you have a diamond face shape, choose a haircut that adds width to your forehead and chin. A layered cut with volume at the crown and chin-length layers can help balance out your features. Avoid styles that add too much volume at the sides of your head.

Rectangle

Rectangular faces look great with haircuts that add volume to the sides of the face. A long, layered cut with side-swept bangs or a shoulder-length bob can help balance out a longer face shape. Avoid styles that add too much height at the top of your head.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've chosen the perfect haircut, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your style looking fresh
  • Use the right products for your hair type
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you

FAQs about Choosing the Best Haircut for Your Face

What if I have multiple face shapes?

It's common to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, choose a haircut that flatters your most prominent features.

Can I pull off a short haircut?

Short haircuts can be flattering on a variety of face shapes. Consult with a stylist to find a short style that works for you.

What if I don't like my new haircut?

If you're not happy with your new haircut, talk to your stylist about making adjustments. They may be able to make tweaks to better suit your preferences.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Locks

Finally, to keep your hair looking and feeling its best, it's important to practice good hair care habits. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition regularly to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals

By following these tips, you can keep your new haircut looking great and your hair feeling healthy and strong.
