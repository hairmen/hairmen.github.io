What Is The Best Haircut For An Oblong Face In 2023

Written by Frank Sep 05, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of endlessly scrolling through Pinterest, wondering what haircut would flatter your oblong face shape? Look no further because we have got you covered. In this article, we’ll be discussing the best haircut for an oblong face, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a longer length and a narrower width. This shape can often make the face appear longer, so it’s important to choose a haircut that will create the illusion of width to balance out the face.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for an Oblong Face

When choosing a haircut for an oblong face, it’s best to opt for styles that add width to the face. This can be achieved by adding volume to the sides of the hair, either through layers or curls. It’s also important to avoid styles that are too long, as they can elongate the face even further.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Best Haircut for an Oblong Face

1. Identify your face shape

2. Look for hairstyle inspiration that flatters your face shape

3. Consult with a hairstylist for their professional opinion

4. Consider your hair texture and lifestyle when choosing a haircut

Tips for Styling an Oblong Face

Once you have chosen the perfect haircut for your oblong face, it’s important to style it in a way that accentuates your features. Here are some tips:

- Use volumizing products to add volume to the sides of your hair

- Avoid center parts, as they can elongate the face

- Consider adding bangs to create the illusion of width

- Experiment with different hair accessories to add interest to your hairstyle

FAQ

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have an oblong face?

A: Yes, but it’s important to add layers and volume to the sides of the hair to balance out the face shape.

Q: Are there any hairstyles I should avoid if I have an oblong face?

A: Yes, it’s best to avoid styles that are too long and straight, as they can elongate the face even further.

Hair Care for Oblong Faces

In addition to choosing the right haircut and styling products, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging your hair

- Use a heat protectant before using hot styling tools

- Trim your hair regularly to keep split ends at bay

- Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair

Conclusion

Choosing the best haircut for an oblong face can be a daunting task, but with the right advice and guidance, it’s easy to find a style that flatters your features. Remember to keep in mind your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a haircut, and don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and accessories to create a look that’s uniquely you.
