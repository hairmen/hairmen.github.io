How To Not Have An Oval Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Nov 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of having an oval-shaped face? Do you feel like it limits your hairstyle options? Well, fear not! In this article, we will discuss various tips and tricks to help you avoid having an oval face. From styling your hair to making simple lifestyle changes, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Not Have an Oval Face
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips to Follow
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Not Have an Oval Face

An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that is wider than the chin, with a rounded jawline. If you want to avoid having an oval face, there are several things you can do. First, you can try to reduce the amount of fat in your face by changing your diet and exercise routine. Second, you can try to contour your face with makeup or hair styling. Finally, you can try to embrace your oval face and find hairstyles that work well for you.

Diet and Exercise

If you want to reduce the amount of fat in your face, you need to focus on losing weight overall. This means eating a healthy, balanced diet and engaging in regular exercise. Try to eat plenty of fruits and vegetables, lean protein, and healthy fats. Avoid processed foods, sugary drinks, and excessive amounts of alcohol. Exercise regularly to burn calories and build muscle. This will help to slim down your face and give you a more defined jawline.

Contouring

If you want to contour your face, there are several makeup techniques you can try. Use a foundation that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone to create shadows on your face. Apply it to the sides of your forehead, the hollows of your cheeks, and along your jawline. Use a highlighter on the center of your forehead, the bridge of your nose, and the apples of your cheeks to create contrast. Blend well for a natural look.

Hairstyles

If you want to embrace your oval face, there are plenty of hairstyles that work well for this face shape. Try a layered haircut with volume at the crown to create the illusion of a more defined jawline. Bangs can also be a great option, as they can help to balance out a wider forehead. Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as these can make your face look even longer.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’re not sure where to start, follow these steps to avoid having an oval face:

  1. Eat a healthy, balanced diet and exercise regularly to reduce the amount of fat in your face.
  2. Use makeup techniques like contouring to create shadows and highlights on your face.
  3. Choose hairstyles that work well for your face shape, such as layered haircuts and bangs.

Tips to Follow

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight, as these can make your face look even longer.
  • Try to create volume at the crown of your head to balance out a wider forehead.
  • Embrace your natural beauty and don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques.

FAQs

Q: Can I change the shape of my face without surgery?

A: Yes, you can change the appearance of your face through diet, exercise, makeup, and hairstyling.

Q: What hairstyles work well for oval faces?

A: Layered haircuts with volume at the crown and bangs can help to balance out a wider forehead.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it hydrated.

By following these tips and tricks, you can avoid having an oval face and embrace your natural beauty. Remember to eat a healthy diet, exercise regularly, and experiment with different makeup and hairstyling techniques to find what works best for you. With a little effort and creativity, you can look and feel your best.
