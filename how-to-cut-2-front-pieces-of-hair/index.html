How To Cut 2 Front Pieces Of Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same hairstyle but don't want to go to the salon? Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with this guide, you can easily learn how to cut 2 front pieces of hair. This article will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice to achieve a new look.

Description

The two front pieces of hair are the strands of hair framing your face. These pieces can be cut to add layers, face-framing angles, or a new length. Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut.

How to Cut 2 Front Pieces of Hair

Step 1: Gather Materials

Before beginning, gather the necessary tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips
  • Mirror

Step 2: Section Hair

Using the comb, section off the two front pieces of hair from the rest of your hair. Clip the remaining hair aside to avoid cutting it by accident.

Step 3: Determine Length

Decide on the desired length of the two front pieces of hair. It is important to cut slowly and incrementally to avoid cutting off too much hair.

Step 4: Cut Hair

Start by cutting a small amount of hair at a time. Hold the hair between two fingers and cut with the scissors at an angle. Make sure to cut both pieces of hair evenly. Take frequent breaks to assess the length and avoid cutting too much hair.

Step 5: Blend Hair

Blend the two front pieces of hair with the remaining hair by combing them together. Cut at an angle to create a layered look.

Tips

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging hair
  • Cut small amounts of hair at a time
  • Take frequent breaks to assess length
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your hair

FAQ

Can I cut my hair without damaging it?

Yes, using sharp scissors and cutting small amounts of hair at a time can prevent damage.

How often should I cut my hair?

It is recommended to cut hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its health and shape.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it properly. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using heat styling tools frequently and use a heat protectant when necessary. Trim hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Cutting your own hair can be a fun and cost-effective way to achieve a new look. By following these steps, you can easily learn how to cut 2 front pieces of hair. Remember to take your time, use sharp scissors, and take care of your hair after cutting it.
