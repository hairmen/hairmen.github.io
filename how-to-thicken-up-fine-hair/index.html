How To Thicken Up Fine Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jun 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have fine hair, you may be looking for ways to add volume and thickness to your locks. There are several methods you can use to thicken up fine hair, including styling techniques, hair care products, and natural remedies. In this article, we'll go over some of the best tips and tricks for thickening up fine hair.

How to Thicken Up Fine Hair

Here are some tips and tricks to help you add volume and thickness to your fine hair.

1. Use the Right Products

To thicken up fine hair, you'll want to start by using the right hair care products. Look for volumizing shampoos and conditioners that are designed to add body and fullness to your hair. You may also want to try using a thickening spray or mousse to give your hair extra volume.

2. Avoid Heavy Products

While you want to use products that will add volume to your hair, you should avoid using products that are too heavy or greasy. These can weigh down your hair and make it look even thinner. Stick to lightweight products that won't leave a residue or build-up in your hair.

3. Try a Haircut

Getting a new haircut can also be a great way to thicken up fine hair. Ask your stylist for a cut that will add volume and movement to your hair. Layers, choppy cuts, and blunt cuts can all help make your hair look thicker and fuller.

4. Use Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners can also be used to add volume to your hair. Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume at the roots. You can also use a curling iron to add body and movement to your hair.

5. Try Natural Remedies

There are several natural remedies that can help thicken up fine hair. For example, you can try using a hair mask made from egg whites, which are rich in protein and can help strengthen and thicken your hair. You can also try using aloe vera gel, which can help soothe and moisturize your scalp, promoting healthy hair growth.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to thicken up fine hair:

Step 1: Use the Right Products

Start by using the right hair care products, including volumizing shampoos and conditioners.

Step 2: Avoid Heavy Products

Avoid using heavy or greasy products that can weigh down your hair.

Step 3: Get a Haircut

Get a new haircut that will add volume and movement to your hair.

Step 4: Use Heat Styling

Use heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners to add volume to your hair.

Step 5: Try Natural Remedies

Try natural remedies like egg white hair masks or aloe vera gel to promote healthy hair growth.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you thicken up fine hair:

  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and lead to dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can also dry out your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb instead of a brush to avoid pulling and damaging your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about thickening up fine hair:

What causes fine hair?

Fine hair is usually genetic, but it can also be caused by hormonal changes, stress, or certain medications.

Can fine hair be thickened?

Yes, there are several methods you can use to thicken up fine hair, including using the right hair care products, getting a haircut, and trying natural remedies.

How often should I wash my hair if I have fine hair?

It's best to wash your hair every other day or every few days if you have fine hair, as washing too often can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care

Taking good care of your hair is key to keeping it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for fine hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't dry out your hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.

By following these tips and tricks, you can thicken up your fine hair and enjoy fuller, more voluminous locks.
