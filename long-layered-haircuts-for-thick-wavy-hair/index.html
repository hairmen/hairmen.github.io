Long Layered Haircuts For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Frank Jun 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Long layered haircuts for thick wavy hair are the perfect way to add volume and texture to your locks. Whether you have naturally curly hair or just love the look of waves, these haircuts are perfect for creating a fun and flirty style that is sure to turn heads. In this blog post, we'll cover everything you need to know about long layered haircuts for thick wavy hair, including how to get the look, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care. So, let's get started!

Description

Long layered haircuts for thick wavy hair are all about adding movement and texture to your locks. The layers help to reduce the weight of your hair, making it easier to manage, while also giving it more bounce and body. The key to this haircut is to create long layers that start at your chin and go all the way down to your tips, creating a soft and natural look. This haircut is perfect for those who want to add some volume to their hair without going too short.

How to

If you're ready to get the long layered haircut of your dreams, here's how to do it:

Step 1: Find the Right Stylist

The first step to getting a long layered haircut for thick wavy hair is to find the right stylist. Look for someone who has experience working with wavy hair, as this will ensure that they understand the unique challenges of your hair type. You can also ask for recommendations from friends or read online reviews to find a stylist who has a good reputation.

Step 2: Choose the Right Length

The next step is to choose the right length for your long layered haircut. This will depend on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Generally, long layers that start at your chin and go down to your tips are the most flattering for those with thick wavy hair.

Step 3: Decide on the Type of Layers

There are several types of layers that you can choose from for your long layered haircut, including face-framing layers, choppy layers, and feathered layers. Talk to your stylist about which type of layers would work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step 4: Get the Haircut

Once you've decided on the length and type of layers, it's time to get the haircut. Your stylist will start by washing and drying your hair, then cutting the layers in sections. They may also use thinning shears to reduce the bulk of your hair and create a more textured look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your long layered haircut for thick wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when wet
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can create frizz
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to enhance your waves
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Invest in a good quality hair mask to keep your hair hydrated and healthy

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long layered haircuts for thick wavy hair:

What is the best way to style a long layered haircut for thick wavy hair?

The best way to style a long layered haircut for thick wavy hair is to let your natural waves shine. Use a curl-enhancing product and scrunch your hair with your hands, then let it air dry or use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying.

How often should I get a trim?

You should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain your long layered haircut and prevent split ends.

Can I still wear my hair straight with a long layered haircut for thick wavy hair?

Yes, you can still wear your hair straight with a long layered haircut for thick wavy hair. However, you may need to use a flat iron to smooth out any waves or curls, and you should use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your long layered haircut for thick wavy hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry it out
  • Avoid using too much heat styling, as this can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner

By following these tips and maintaining a regular hair care routine, you can keep your long layered haircut for thick wavy hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Conclusion

If you have thick wavy hair and are looking for a fun and flirty hairstyle, a long layered haircut may be just what you need. With the right length, type of layers, and styling techniques, you can achieve a look that is both easy to manage and stunning. Just be sure to take care of your hair and maintain a regular hair care routine to keep it looking its best.
