How To Curl Your Short Hair With A Straightener

Written by Eveline Jul 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of having the same straight hair every day and want to add some curls and waves to your hair? Well, you are in the right place! In this article, we will guide you on how to curl your short hair with a straightener. We will provide you with a step-by-step guide, some tips and tricks, and also answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Curl Your Hair with a Straightener

Before we start curling, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a hair straightener, some hair clips, a heat protectant spray, and a hairspray.

Step 1: Apply Heat Protectant Spray

Before using any heat styling tool, it is highly recommended to apply heat protectant spray to your hair. This will protect your hair from heat damage and keep your hair healthy.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier for you to curl each section individually.

Step 3: Start Curling

Take a small section of your hair and place it between the straightener. Twist the straightener away from your face and slowly slide it down to the end of your hair. Repeat this step until all your hair is curled.

Step 4: Add Hairspray

Once you have curled all your hair, spray some hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Curling Your Short Hair with a Straightener

Here are some tips and tricks to help you curl your short hair with a straightener:

  • Use a hair straightener with rounded edges.
  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before curling.
  • Don't hold the straightener on your hair for too long as this can damage your hair.
  • For tighter curls, use smaller sections of your hair.
  • For looser curls, use larger sections of your hair.

FAQ

Can I curl my short hair with a straightener?

Yes, you can curl your short hair with a straightener. It is a quick and easy way to add some curls and waves to your hair.

What kind of straightener should I use to curl my hair?

It is best to use a hair straightener with rounded edges as this will help you curl your hair easily.

Can I curl my hair with a straightener without using heat protectant spray?

No, it is not recommended to use any heat styling tool without applying heat protectant spray first. This will protect your hair from heat damage.

Hair Care

After curling your hair, make sure to take care of your hair. Use hair oil or serum to keep your hair moisturized and prevent split ends. Avoid washing your hair every day as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Congratulations! You have successfully curled your short hair with a straightener. Now, you can add some curls and waves to your hair and switch up your hairstyle.
