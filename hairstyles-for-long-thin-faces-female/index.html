Hairstyles For Long Thin Faces Female Tips And Ideas

Written by Christine Jul 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a long and thin face, you may find it challenging to choose the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape. However, with the right haircut and styling, you can create an illusion of width and balance your features. In this blog post, we will share some of the best hairstyles for long thin faces female that you can try out to enhance your beauty.

Description

Long thin faces have a narrow jawline, forehead, and cheekbones. The length of the face is greater than its width, which can make it look elongated. To create balance, the aim is to add volume and width to the sides of the face while avoiding styles that emphasize the length of the face.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Long Thin Faces Female?

Before you choose a hairstyle, it's essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, and personality. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best cut and style that suits your needs. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for long thin faces female:

1. Add Volume and Width:

Choose hairstyles that add volume and width to the sides of the face. Layers, curls, waves, and bouncy hairstyles can create an illusion of fullness and balance your features.

2. Avoid Straight and Flat Styles:

Straight and flat hairstyles can make your face look longer and thinner. Avoid straight bangs, poker-straight hair, and sleek styles that emphasize the length of your face.

3. Consider Face-Framing Layers:

Face-framing layers can soften your features and add dimension to your hair. Ask your hairstylist to add layers that start around your cheekbones and jawline.

4. Choose the Right Length:

The length of your hair can affect the overall balance of your face. Opt for medium or long hairstyles that fall below your chin to add width and volume.

Hairstyles for Long Thin Faces Female: Ideas and Inspiration

1. Layered Bob:

A layered bob is an excellent choice for long thin faces female. The choppy layers add volume and texture, while the length falls just below the chin, creating width. You can style it with loose waves or messy curls for a relaxed look.

2. Side-Swept Bangs:

Side-swept bangs can add width to your forehead and balance your features. Choose long, wispy bangs that start from the side of your part to create a diagonal line across your face.

3. Beachy Waves:

Beachy waves can add volume and texture to your hair, creating an illusion of fullness. Use a curling iron or sea salt spray to create loose waves that frame your face.

4. Half-Up Half-Down:

Half-up half-down hairstyles can add volume to the crown of your head while keeping the length of your hair. You can style it with loose waves or braids for a bohemian look.

5. Layered Long Hair:

Long hair with layers can add volume and dimension, creating an illusion of fullness. Ask your hairstylist to add layers that frame your face and start around your cheekbones and jawline.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut with a long thin face?

A: Pixie cuts can be challenging to pull off with a long thin face, as they can emphasize the length of the face. However, if you choose a layered pixie cut with volume on the sides, it can create an illusion of width.

Q: Can I wear my hair straight with a long thin face?

A: Straight hair can make your face look longer and thinner. However, if you have long straight hair, you can add layers or waves to create an illusion of fullness.

Hair Care Tips for Long Thin Faces Female

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind to maintain healthy, voluminous hair:

1. Use Volume-Boosting Products:

Use volumizing shampoos, conditioners, and styling products to add fullness and texture to your hair.

2. Avoid Overstyling:

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look flat. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to create natural waves and curls.

3. Get Regular Trims:

Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

4. Protect Your Hair:

Protect your hair from sun damage, chlorine, and saltwater by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and ideas, you can find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your long thin face. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
