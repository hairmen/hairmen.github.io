Mens Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair

There are a variety of mens hairstyles that work well for thick curly hair, depending on your personal style and the length of your hair. Here are some popular options:

The Afro

The Afro is a classic mens hairstyle for thick curly hair. It is a full, rounded hairstyle that is achieved by growing the hair out to a few inches in length all around the head. The Afro is a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling, making it a popular choice for guys who want a fuss-free look.

The High Top Fade

The High Top Fade is another iconic mens hairstyle that works well for thick curly hair. It is a short haircut that is longer on top and shorter on the sides. The hair on top is styled into a high, flat top shape, while the sides are faded down to the skin. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, making it a popular choice for guys who like to switch up their look.

The Curly Undercut

The Curly Undercut is a modern mens hairstyle that is popular with guys who have thick curly hair. It is a short haircut that is longer on top and shorter on the sides. The hair on top is left curly and voluminous, while the sides are shaved close to the head. This hairstyle is low-maintenance and easy to style, making it a popular choice for guys who want a stylish yet fuss-free look.

How to Style Mens Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair

Styling mens hairstyles for thick curly hair can be tricky, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a great look. Here are some tips:

  • Start with clean, damp hair. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help tame frizz and add moisture.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair.
  • Apply a curl-defining product, such as a curl cream or gel, to your hair. Work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. Place the diffuser on the end of the blow dryer and gently cup your hair in the diffuser. Use a low heat setting and move the diffuser around your hair to dry it evenly.
  • Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to fluff and shape your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide for Styling Mens Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide for styling mens hairstyles for thick curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it is damp.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair.
  4. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair.
  5. Apply a curl-defining product to your hair.
  6. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment.
  7. Fluff and shape your curls with your fingers.
  8. Finish with a hairspray or pomade to hold your style in place.

Tips for Haircare for Mens Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some tips for haircare for mens hairstyles for thick curly hair:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help tame frizz and add moisture to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
