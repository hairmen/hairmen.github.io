How To Style Thin Hair On Top Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Aug 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have thin hair on top, you may find it difficult to style your hair in a way that looks voluminous and full. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a flattering and stylish look. In this article, we will discuss how to style thin hair on top and provide you with tips and tricks to make your hair look its best.

Description

Thin hair on top can be caused by a variety of factors including genetics, age, and hormonal changes. It can be frustrating to style thin hair, as it can easily fall flat and lack volume. However, with the right styling techniques and products, you can make your hair look fuller and more voluminous.

How to Style Thin Hair on Top

There are several ways to style thin hair on top, including:

1. Blow Dry with a Round Brush

Blow drying your hair with a round brush can add volume and body to your hair. Start by applying a volumizing mousse to your roots and then blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting your hair at the roots as you go.

2. Use a Texturizing Spray

A texturizing spray can add texture and volume to your hair. Spray the product onto your roots and then use your fingers to massage the product into your hair, creating volume and texture.

3. Try a Top Knot

A top knot is a great way to style thin hair on top. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and then twist the hair around the base of the ponytail, securing it with bobby pins. This will create a voluminous bun on top of your head.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling thin hair on top:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add volume and body to your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Volumizing Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your roots, working the product into your hair with your fingers.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair with a round brush, lifting your hair at the roots as you go. This will help to add volume and body to your hair.

Step 4: Use a Texturizing Spray

Spray a texturizing spray onto your roots and then use your fingers to massage the product into your hair, creating volume and texture.

Step 5: Try a Top Knot

Gather your hair into a high ponytail and then twist the hair around the base of the ponytail, securing it with bobby pins. This will create a voluminous bun on top of your head.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling thin hair on top:

1. Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner can help to add volume and body to your hair.

2. Avoid Heavy Products

Avoid using heavy products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

3. Use a Diffuser

Using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer can help to add volume and texture to your hair.

4. Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts can help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and full.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a straightener on thin hair?

A: Yes, you can use a straightener on thin hair. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: Can I use hair extensions to add volume to thin hair?

A: Yes, hair extensions can be a great way to add volume and body to thin hair. However, be sure to choose high-quality extensions that blend well with your natural hair.

Q: Can I use hairspray to add volume to thin hair?

A: Yes, you can use hairspray to add volume to thin hair. However, be sure to choose a lightweight hairspray that won't weigh down your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and full hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Using a gentle shampoo can help to prevent damage to your hair and keep it looking healthy and full.

2. Condition Your Hair

Conditioning your hair can help to add moisture and prevent breakage.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as they can cause damage and breakage.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals can help to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, styling thin hair on top can be challenging, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a flattering and stylish look. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, avoid heavy products, and try blow drying your hair with a round brush or using a texturizing spray. With these tips and tricks, you can make your hair look fuller and more voluminous.
