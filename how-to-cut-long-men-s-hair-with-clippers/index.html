How To Cut Long Men s Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 17, 2023 · 4 min read
If you're looking to cut your own long hair at home, using clippers can be a great option. Not only is it more affordable than going to a salon, but it also gives you more control over the final result. In this guide, we'll go over how to cut long men's hair with clippers, including tips and tricks for getting the best results.

Description

Long hair can be beautiful, but it can also be a hassle to maintain. If you're tired of dealing with tangles and knots, or if you just want to try out a new look, cutting your long hair with clippers can be a great solution. With a few basic tools and some practice, you can achieve a professional-looking cut right from the comfort of your own home.

How to Cut Long Men's Hair with Clippers

Before you get started, you'll need to gather a few essential tools. Here's what you'll need: - Clippers with various guards - Scissors - Comb - Towel or cape - Mirror Once you have your tools ready, you can begin the process of cutting your hair. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started: 1. Start by washing your hair and towel drying it. This will make it easier to work with and help you achieve a more even cut. 2. Comb your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots. If you have particularly thick or curly hair, you may want to use a detangling spray or conditioner to help smooth it out. 3. Decide on the length you want to cut your hair to. You can use different guards on your clippers to achieve different lengths. Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut. 4. Begin by using your clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using smooth, even strokes. Be sure to use the same guard on both sides to ensure an even cut. 5. Once you've finished the sides and back, move on to the top of your hair. Use your scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length. You can use your clippers to blend the sides and top together for a more natural look. 6. Finally, use your clippers to trim the hair around your ears and neckline. Be careful not to cut too much, as this can create an uneven look.

Tips for Cutting Long Men's Hair with Clippers

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with a few tips and tricks, you can achieve great results. Here are some things to keep in mind: - Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut. It's always better to err on the side of caution and take off less than you think you need to. - Take breaks as needed. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so be sure to take breaks and stretch your arms and hands as needed. - Use a mirror to check your progress. It can be hard to see the back of your head, so be sure to use a mirror to check your work and make any necessary adjustments.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my own hair with clippers if I have curly hair? A: Yes, you can. However, you may need to use a different guard or technique to achieve the desired length. Q: Do I need to use scissors when cutting my own hair with clippers? A: Yes, you will likely need to use scissors to trim the hair on top of your head to the desired length. Q: Can I cut my own hair with clippers if I have never done it before? A: Yes, you can. However, it may take some practice to get the hang of it.

Hair Care

After you've finished cutting your hair, you'll want to take care of it properly to ensure it stays healthy and looks great. Here are some tips: - Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized. - Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair. - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth. That's it – you now know how to cut long men's hair with clippers! With a little practice and patience, you can achieve great results right from the comfort of your own home.
