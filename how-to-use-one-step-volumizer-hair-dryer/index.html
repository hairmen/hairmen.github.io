How To Use One Step Volumizer Hair Dryer A Beginner s Guide

Written by Frank Jul 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a hair tool that can give you salon-worthy blowouts at home, the one-step volumizer hair dryer might just be what you need. This versatile tool combines a hair dryer and a brush in one, making it easy to achieve voluminous, shiny hair without having to use multiple tools. In this article, we'll go over how to use one-step volumizer hair dryer, as well as some tips and hair care advice to help you get the best results.

How to Use One-Step Volumizer Hair Dryer

The one-step volumizer hair dryer is a simple tool to use, even if you're not a hair styling expert. Here are the basic steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with towel-dried hair: Before using the one-step volumizer hair dryer, make sure your hair is towel-dried. You don't want your hair to be soaking wet, but it should still be damp enough to work with.

2. Apply heat protectant: To protect your hair from heat damage, apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair before using the one-step volumizer hair dryer.

3. Section your hair: Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you dry all of your hair evenly.

4. Start at the roots: Take one section of hair and place the one-step volumizer hair dryer at the roots. Use the brush to lift your hair and create volume as you dry it. Slowly move the brush and dryer down the length of your hair, continuing to lift and create volume as you go.

5. Repeat on all sections: Continue working on one section of hair at a time, moving the one-step volumizer hair dryer down the length of your hair until all sections are dry.

6. Finish with cool air: Once your hair is dry, use the cool air setting on the one-step volumizer hair dryer to set your style and add shine.

Tips for Using One-Step Volumizer Hair Dryer

1. Use the right heat setting: Most one-step volumizer hair dryers have multiple heat settings. If you have fine or damaged hair, use the low heat setting. If you have thick or coarse hair, use the high heat setting.

2. Don't overload the brush: To get the best results, don't overload the brush with too much hair at once. This will make it harder to create volume and can result in tangles.

3. Use a detangling spray: If you have thick or curly hair, consider using a detangling spray before using the one-step volumizer hair dryer. This will make it easier to brush through your hair and create volume.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a one-step volumizer hair dryer on wet hair?
A: It's best to use a one-step volumizer hair dryer on towel-dried hair. If your hair is too wet, it will take longer to dry and may result in damage.

Q: Can I use a one-step volumizer hair dryer on all hair types?
A: Yes, a one-step volumizer hair dryer can be used on all hair types. However, you may need to adjust the heat setting depending on your hair type.

Hair Care Tips

1. Use a deep conditioning treatment: To keep your hair healthy and shiny, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

2. Limit heat styling: While one-step volumizer hair dryers are less damaging than traditional hair dryers, it's still important to limit heat styling as much as possible. Try to air dry your hair whenever you can.

3. Protect your hair from the sun: Just like your skin, your hair can be damaged by the sun. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from UV rays.

With these tips and a little practice, you'll be able to use your one-step volumizer hair dryer to create beautiful, voluminous hair. Happy styling!
