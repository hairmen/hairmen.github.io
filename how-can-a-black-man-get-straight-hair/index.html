How Can A Black Man Get Straight Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Nov 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a black man who has always wanted straight hair, you're not alone. Many black men struggle with naturally curly or kinky hair and desire a sleek, straight look. Fortunately, there are a variety of options available to help you achieve the straight hair you desire. In this guide, we'll explore how black men can get straight hair and provide tips for maintaining and caring for your new style.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Get Straight Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Straight Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Straightened Hair

How to Get Straight Hair

There are several methods for black men to get straight hair:

Chemical Relaxer

A chemical relaxer is a popular method for black men to get straight hair. This process involves applying a chemical solution to the hair that breaks down the protein bonds and allows the hair to be manipulated into a straight style. It is important to note that a chemical relaxer can cause damage to the hair and scalp if not applied correctly or if used too frequently.

Flat Iron

A flat iron is another popular method for black men to get straight hair. This tool uses heat to straighten the hair and can be used on both natural and relaxed hair. It is important to use a heat protectant spray and to not use the flat iron too frequently, as it can cause damage to the hair.

Blowout

A blowout involves using a blow dryer and a round brush to straighten the hair. This method is less damaging than chemical relaxers and flat irons but may not provide as long-lasting results.

Step-by-Step Guide

Before attempting to straighten your hair, it is important to prepare your hair and scalp. Follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo.
  2. Apply a conditioner and leave it in for a few minutes before rinsing it out.
  3. Towel dry your hair and detangle it with a wide-tooth comb.
  4. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.

Depending on the method you choose, follow these steps:

Chemical Relaxer

  1. Divide your hair into four sections.
  2. Apply the chemical relaxer to each section, starting from the back of your head and working your way to the front. Be sure to follow the instructions on the product carefully.
  3. Leave the relaxer on for the recommended time.
  4. Rinse the relaxer out with warm water.
  5. Apply a neutralizing shampoo to stop the relaxing process.
  6. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  7. Apply a conditioner and leave it in for a few minutes before rinsing it out.
  8. Towel dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner.
  9. Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to straighten it.

Flat Iron

  1. Divide your hair into small sections.
  2. Starting from the back of your head, clamp the flat iron onto a small section of hair and glide it down to the ends. Repeat this process for each section of hair.
  3. Apply a serum or oil to your hair to add shine.

Blowout

  1. Divide your hair into small sections.
  2. Using a round brush, blow dry each section of hair, pulling the brush through the hair to straighten it.
  3. Apply a serum or oil to your hair to add shine.

Tips for Maintaining Straight Hair

Once you have achieved straight hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain the style:

  • Protect your hair from humidity, which can cause it to revert back to its natural state. Apply a humidity-resistant serum or spray to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can cause damage to the hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a paddle brush to detangle your hair, and be gentle when brushing or combing it.
  • Protect your hair while sleeping by wrapping it in a silk or satin scarf or using a silk or satin pillowcase.

FAQs

Is it safe for black men to use chemical relaxers?

When used correctly, chemical relaxers can be safe for black men to use. However, it is important to follow the instructions carefully and not to use the product too frequently, as this can cause damage to the hair and scalp.

How often should I use a flat iron to straighten my hair?

It is best to limit the use of a flat iron to once or twice a week, as frequent use can cause damage to the hair.

Can I get my hair wet after getting a chemical relaxer?

Avoid getting your hair wet for at least 48 hours after getting a chemical relaxer. This will give the relaxer time to fully set and prevent damage to the hair.

Hair Care for Straightened Hair

To keep your straightened hair healthy, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using products that contain sulfates.
  • Apply a deep conditioner to your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hair ties that can cause breakage or damage to the hair. Use a silk or satin scrunchie instead.

With these tips and methods, black men can achieve the straight hair they desire. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain the style and keep it healthy.
