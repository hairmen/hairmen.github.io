Short Hair For Oblong Faces A Guide For 2023

Written by Christine Jul 31, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, you may have struggled to find the perfect haircut that flatters your features. Luckily, short hair can be a great option for oblong faces, helping to balance out the length and showcase your best features. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about short hair for oblong faces, from how to choose the right cut to hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Oblong Faces

When choosing a short haircut for an oblong face, the goal is to create balance and proportion. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Consider a Bob or Lob

A bob or lob (long bob) can be a great option for oblong faces, as they add volume and width to the sides of the face. Opt for a choppy or layered cut to add even more texture and movement.

Avoid Length Above the Chin

Shorter haircuts that fall above the chin can accentuate the length of an oblong face, so it's best to avoid these styles. Instead, opt for a cut that falls at or below the chin to balance out the length.

Add Bangs

Bangs can help to break up the length of an oblong face and create the illusion of a shorter forehead. Consider side-swept bangs or a blunt fringe to add some interest to your haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Oblong Faces

Styling short hair for an oblong face is all about creating volume and texture. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect look:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as mousse or a volumizing spray, to your damp hair. This will help to add texture and hold to your style.

Step 3: Blow Dry

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use the brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume.

Step 4: Add Texture

Once your hair is dry, use a texturizing spray or wax to add texture and definition to your style. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, undone look.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Oblong Faces

Short hair for oblong faces can be a low-maintenance and stylish option, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

Get Regular Trims

To keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy, it's important to get regular trims every 6-8 weeks. This will help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking voluminous and full.

Use Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools, such as a flat iron or curling wand, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, and use dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

FAQs About Short Hair for Oblong Faces

What if I have thin or fine hair?

If you have thin or fine hair, a layered bob or lob can be a great option for adding volume and texture to your hair. Consider using volumizing products and avoiding heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.

Can I still wear my hair up?

Absolutely! Short hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including up-dos and half-up styles. Consider using hair accessories, such as clips or headbands, to add interest to your hairstyles.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair for Oblong Faces

Avoid Over-Processing

If you color your hair, avoid over-processing it to prevent damage and breakage. Consider using a semi-permanent or demi-permanent color to add dimension and shine to your hair.

Use a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to prevent breakage and frizz, leaving your hair looking smooth and shiny.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can be damaging to your hair, so be sure to protect it by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant hair product when spending time outdoors.

By following these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and flattering short haircut for your oblong face shape. Whether you opt for a bob, lob, or pixie cut, remember to have fun with your style and experiment with different looks!
