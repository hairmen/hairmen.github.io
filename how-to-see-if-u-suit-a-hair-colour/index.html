How to See if You Suit a Hair Colour - Tips and FAQ

Changing your hair colour is a big decision. It can completely transform your look and make you feel like a new person. However, before you take the plunge and dye your hair, it's important to make sure that the colour you choose suits you. In this article, we'll go over some tips and frequently asked questions to help you determine if a hair colour will look good on you.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine if a Hair Colour Suits You
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Determine if a Hair Colour Suits You

Determining if a hair colour suits you involves considering a few factors such as your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Your skin tone: This is the most important factor to consider. As a general rule, if you have warm undertones in your skin, you'll look best with warm hair colours such as golden blonde, caramel, or auburn. If you have cool undertones, you'll look best with cool hair colours such as platinum blonde, ash brown, or black.
  • Your eye colour: Your eye colour can also play a role in determining which hair colour suits you. For example, if you have blue eyes, you may want to choose a hair colour that will make your eyes pop such as a warm blonde or caramel. If you have brown eyes, you may want to go for a bold, dark colour such as black or deep red.
  • Your natural hair colour: If you're looking to make a subtle change, you can choose a hair colour that is close to your natural colour. If you want a dramatic change, you can choose a colour that is completely different.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you determine if a hair colour suits you:

  1. Start by determining your skin tone. You can do this by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones.
  2. Next, consider your eye colour. If you have blue or green eyes, you'll look best with warm hair colours. If you have brown or hazel eyes, you'll look best with cool hair colours.
  3. Think about your natural hair colour. If you're looking for a subtle change, choose a hair colour that is close to your natural colour. If you want a dramatic change, choose a colour that is completely different.
  4. Take a picture of yourself and use a photo editing app to try on different hair colours. This can give you a good idea of what you'll look like with a certain colour.
  5. Consult with a professional stylist. They can give you advice on which colours will suit you best based on your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair colour:

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you lead an active lifestyle, you may want a colour that is easy to maintain.
  • Think about your wardrobe. Choose a colour that will complement your clothes.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new. Sometimes stepping outside of your comfort zone can lead to a great new look.
  • Consider the maintenance. If you choose a colour that requires a lot of upkeep, make sure you're willing to put in the time and effort.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hair colours suit fair skin?

People with fair skin typically look best with cool hair colours such as platinum blonde, ash brown, or black.

What hair colours suit medium skin?

People with medium skin can usually pull off warm or cool hair colours depending on their undertones. Some good options include golden blonde, caramel, auburn, and cool brown shades.

What hair colours suit dark skin?

People with dark skin can look great with warm hair colours such as honey blonde, copper, or a deep red.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found the perfect hair colour, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for colour-treated hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as this can strip the colour.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to too much sun as this can fade the colour.

By following these tips and considering your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour, you can determine if a hair colour suits you and achieve a look that you love.
