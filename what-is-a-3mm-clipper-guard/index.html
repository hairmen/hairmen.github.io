What Is A 3Mm Clipper Guard

Written by Oliver Sep 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you are someone who is interested in hair styling or grooming, you have probably heard the term "clipper guard" before. A clipper guard is an attachment that fits onto the blades of a hair clipper, which helps control the length of the hair being cut. The 3mm clipper guard, in particular, is a popular choice for those who want a shorter, yet not too short, haircut. In this article, we will explore what a 3mm clipper guard is, how to use it, some tips and tricks, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Use a 3mm Clipper Guard

Using a 3mm clipper guard is relatively easy. First, make sure that your hair is clean and dry. Then, attach the 3mm clipper guard to the blades of your hair clipper. Begin by running the clipper over the top of your head in the direction that your hair grows. You can then move on to the sides and back of your head, always making sure to go in the direction that your hair grows. Remember to take your time and use gentle, steady movements.

When you are finished, you may want to use a pair of scissors to trim any areas that the clipper may have missed. You can also use the clipper without the guard to create more defined lines around the edges of your hairline and sideburns.

Tips for Using a 3mm Clipper Guard

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your 3mm clipper guard:

  • Always start with a clean and dry head of hair.
  • Make sure that the clipper blades are sharp and well-oiled.
  • Use a comb to help guide the clipper over your head.
  • If you are having trouble reaching certain areas, try using a handheld mirror to help you see the back of your head.
  • Remember to trim any stray hairs with a pair of scissors.

FAQ

What Length is a 3mm Clipper Guard?

A 3mm clipper guard will leave your hair about 3 millimeters long. This is equivalent to about 1/8 of an inch.

Can I Use a 3mm Clipper Guard on Any Hair Type?

Yes, you can use a 3mm clipper guard on any hair type. However, if you have very thick or curly hair, you may need to use a smaller guard or go over the same area multiple times to achieve the desired length.

Should I Use a 3mm Clipper Guard for a Buzzcut?

Yes, a 3mm clipper guard is a great choice for a buzzcut. It will leave your hair very short, but not quite as short as if you were to use a 1mm or 2mm guard.

Hair Care Tips

After using a 3mm clipper guard, it is important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as from blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your desired length.

Using a 3mm clipper guard is a great way to achieve a shorter, yet still stylish haircut. With a little practice and some helpful tips, you can use this clipper guard to create a look that you will love.
