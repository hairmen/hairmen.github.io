Why Does Only One Side Of My Hair Stay Curled

Written by Bardi Dec 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have ever tried curling your hair, you may have noticed that only one side of your hair stays curled, while the other side falls flat. This can be frustrating, especially when you are trying to achieve a particular look. In this post, we will discuss why only one side of your hair stays curled and provide tips on how to fix this issue.

Why Does Only One Side of My Hair Stay Curled?

There are a few reasons why only one side of your hair stays curled:

Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can affect how well it holds a curl. If you have fine or straight hair, it may be more difficult to get your curls to hold. On the other hand, if you have thick or coarse hair, your curls may hold better.

Product Buildup

Product buildup can also affect how well your curls hold. If you use too much product, it can weigh down your hair and make it difficult for your curls to stay in place.

Curling Technique

The way you curl your hair can also affect how well your curls hold. If you are using the wrong size curling iron or not holding the curling iron in place long enough, your curls may not hold as well.

Hair Health

The health of your hair can also affect how well your curls hold. If your hair is damaged or dry, it may not hold a curl as well as healthy hair.

How to Fix One-Sided Curls

If only one side of your hair stays curled, there are a few things you can do to fix the issue:

Use the Right Products

Using the right products can help your curls last longer. Look for products that are designed to hold curls, such as mousse or hairspray. Be sure to apply the products evenly throughout your hair.

Change Your Curling Technique

Changing the way you curl your hair can also help your curls last longer. Try using a different size curling iron or holding the curling iron in place for a longer period.

Try a Different Curling Method

If you are having trouble getting your curls to hold, try a different curling method. You can try using hot rollers, flexi rods, or a curling wand.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you get your curls to last:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  3. Section your hair and apply a curling product.
  4. Curl your hair, holding the curling iron in place for 10-15 seconds.
  5. Release the curl and use a clip to hold it in place while it cools.
  6. Repeat until all sections of your hair are curled.
  7. Once your hair has cooled, remove the clips and gently run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.
  8. Finish with a hairspray to help your curls last longer.

Tips to Help Your Curls Last Longer

Here are some tips to help your curls last longer:

  • Use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't use too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Let your curls cool completely before brushing or running your fingers through your hair.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help your curls last longer.

FAQ

Why does only one side of my hair stay curled?

The texture of your hair, product buildup, curling technique, and hair health can all affect how well your curls hold.

How do I fix one-sided curls?

You can fix one-sided curls by using the right products, changing your curling technique, or trying a different curling method.

Hair Care Tips to Keep Your Curls Looking Great

Here are some hair care tips to keep your curls looking great:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

By following these tips, you can help your curls last longer and keep your hair looking great.
