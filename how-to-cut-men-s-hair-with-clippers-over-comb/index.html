How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers Over Comb

Written by William Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you want to learn how to cut men's hair with clippers over comb? This technique is great for achieving a clean and even haircut. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut men's hair with clippers over comb. We'll also provide you with some tips and tricks to make the process easier. Let's get started!

Description

The clippers over comb technique involves using clippers to cut hair while holding a comb against the head. This technique is great for achieving an even length and blending the hair smoothly. It can be used for various hairstyles, including buzz cuts, fades, and undercuts.

How to

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, a comb, and scissors. You may also want to have a hair clip to hold hair out of the way.

Here are the steps to cut men's hair with clippers over comb:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Hair

Start by washing and drying the hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure a clean cut. Make sure the hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Choose a Clipper Guard

Choose a clipper guard that is appropriate for the desired length of the haircut. Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.

Step 3: Comb Hair

Comb the hair in the direction it naturally falls. This will make it easier to cut and ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Hold the comb against the head and use the clippers to cut the hair against the comb. Start at the bottom of the head and work your way up. Be sure to keep the clippers parallel to the comb to ensure an even cut.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the hair with the clippers over comb technique, use scissors to blend the hair. Cut any longer pieces until the hair is even and smooth.

Step 6: Repeat on Sides and Back

Repeat the same technique on the sides and back of the head. Use a mirror to ensure an even cut.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut men's hair with clippers over comb:

  • Start with a longer clipper guard and work your way down to avoid cutting the hair too short.
  • Use a mirror to ensure an even cut.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting hair takes practice.
  • Use scissors to blend the hair for a smoother finish.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair with clippers over comb:

What clipper guards should I use?

Use a longer clipper guard for a longer haircut and a shorter clipper guard for a shorter haircut. Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.

How do I blend the hair?

Use scissors to blend the hair. Cut any longer pieces until the hair is even and smooth.

How do I ensure an even cut?

Use a mirror to ensure an even cut. Check from all angles to ensure the hair is even and smooth.

Hair Care

After cutting the hair, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash and condition the hair regularly.
  • Use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to avoid damage.
  • Use a comb or brush to style the hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down the hair.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair with clippers over comb, you can achieve a clean and even haircut at home. Just remember to take your time and be patient. With practice, you'll become an expert in no time!
