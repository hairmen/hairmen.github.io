Long On Top Short On Sides Straight Hair A Modern Hairstyle Trend

Long on top short on sides straight hair is a trendy hairstyle that has been popular for years. This hairstyle is versatile and can be worn by both men and women. It is an easy-to-manage hairstyle that requires minimal styling and maintenance. If you are interested in trying this hairstyle, here is everything you need to know.

Description

Long on top short on sides straight hair is a hairstyle that features long hair on the top of the head and short hair on the sides. The hair on the top of the head can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, combed over, or messy. The sides are usually shaved or trimmed short. This hairstyle is popular among men, but women have also been known to rock this look.

How to Get Long on Top Short on Sides Straight Hair

If you are interested in getting long on top short on sides straight hair, the first step is to find a good stylist who can help you achieve the look. You will also need to decide on the length and style of the hair on the top of your head. Once you have decided on the style, your stylist will cut the hair on the sides to the desired length.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting long on top short on sides straight hair:

  1. Find a good stylist who has experience with this hairstyle.
  2. Decide on the length and style of the hair on the top of your head.
  3. Ask your stylist to cut the hair on the sides to the desired length.
  4. Style the hair on the top of your head as desired.
  5. Maintain the hairstyle by trimming the sides regularly and using hair products as needed.

Tips for Maintaining Long on Top Short on Sides Straight Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining long on top short on sides straight hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Use hair products such as gel, pomade, or wax to style the hair on the top of your head.
  • Trim the sides regularly to keep the hairstyle looking fresh.
  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim and touch-up.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about long on top short on sides straight hair:

1. Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this hairstyle is versatile and can be adapted to suit various face shapes.

2. How long does it take to style this hairstyle?

This hairstyle is easy to style and can be done in a matter of minutes.

3. Do I need to use any special products to maintain this hairstyle?

You may need to use hair products such as gel or wax to style the hair on the top of your head.

Hair Care for Long on Top Short on Sides Straight Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of long on top short on sides straight hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

Long on top short on sides straight hair is a versatile and easy-to-manage hairstyle that can be worn by both men and women. If you are interested in trying this hairstyle, be sure to find a good stylist who can help you achieve the look. With proper maintenance and care, this hairstyle can look great for years to come.
