Mens High Fade Haircut With Beard The Perfect Combination

Written by Eveline Nov 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and modern haircut that will turn heads, look no further than the mens high fade haircut with beard. This trendy style combines a high fade on the sides and back with a longer beard for a look that is both sleek and rugged. In this article, we'll show you how to get the perfect mens high fade haircut with beard, along with tips and advice for taking care of your new style.

Description

The mens high fade haircut with beard is a popular style that has been gaining popularity in recent years. The haircut features a high fade on the sides and back, which gradually blends into longer hair on top. The beard is also an essential part of the look, as it adds an extra layer of masculinity and ruggedness. This style is perfect for men who want a modern and stylish look that is both professional and casual.

How to

To get the perfect mens high fade haircut with beard, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose your fade

The first step is to decide how high you want your fade to be. A high fade will start near the top of your head, while a low fade will start closer to your ears. Choose the fade that best suits your style and face shape.

Step 2: Trim your beard

Next, trim your beard to the desired length. Make sure to use a sharp pair of scissors or clippers and take your time to get an even and well-groomed look.

Step 3: Cut your hair

Using a pair of clippers, cut your hair to the desired length on top. Make sure to leave enough length to style your hair later.

Step 4: Fade your hair

Using your clippers, fade your hair on the sides and back. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard for a smooth transition.

Step 5: Style your hair

Finally, style your hair on top using your favorite product. Use a comb or your fingers to create the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens high fade haircut with beard:

- Regularly trim your beard to keep it looking neat and tidy. - Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny. - Visit a professional barber regularly to maintain your fade and keep your hair looking its best. - Experiment with different styles and products to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens high fade haircut with beard:

What face shape is best for the mens high fade haircut with beard?

The mens high fade haircut with beard is suitable for most face shapes, but it looks particularly good on men with oval or square faces.

How often should I trim my beard?

It's best to trim your beard every two to three weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Can I style my hair differently with the mens high fade?

Absolutely! The mens high fade haircut with beard is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, from a slicked-back look to a messy, textured style.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair and beard looking their best requires a little bit of effort. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

- Wash your hair and beard regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. - Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair. - Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools to help protect your hair from damage.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect mens high fade haircut with beard and keep it looking great for weeks to come. Whether you're going for a casual or professional look, this style is sure to turn heads and make a statement.
