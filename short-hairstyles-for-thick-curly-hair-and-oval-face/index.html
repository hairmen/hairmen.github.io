Short Hairstyles For Thick Curly Hair And Oval Face

If you have thick curly hair and an oval face, you might find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that suits your features. In this article, we will show you some of the best short hairstyles for thick curly hair and oval face. These hairstyles are easy to maintain, trendy, and perfect for any occasion.

Description

Thick curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks gorgeous and adds volume to your hair, it can be challenging to style and manage. If you have an oval face, you have a versatile face shape that can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, you need to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and highlights your natural beauty.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Your Oval Face and Thick Curly Hair?

Choosing the right hairstyle is crucial to enhance your facial features and bring out your natural beauty. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape plays a vital role in choosing the right hairstyle. If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, you need to choose a style that complements your features and highlights your natural beauty.

Think about Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you have thick curly hair, you need a style that can manage your curls and add volume to your hair.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you have a busy schedule, you need a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to manage and maintain.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair and Oval Face

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling short hairstyles for thick curly hair and oval face:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Wash and condition your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Rinse thoroughly with water and towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Hair Serum or Oil

Apply a hair serum or oil to your hair to tame your curls and add shine to your hair.

Step 3: Blow-dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls and add volume to your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a curling iron or a flat iron to add texture and shape to your hair. You can also use hair accessories such as hairpins or headbands to add a touch of elegance to your hairstyle.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair and Oval Face

Here are some tips for maintaining short hairstyles for thick curly hair and oval face:

Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Apply Hair Serum or Oil Regularly

Apply a hair serum or oil regularly to tame your curls and add shine to your hair.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to keep it in shape and prevent split ends.

Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair regularly as it can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle.

FAQs

What are the best short hairstyles for thick curly hair and oval face?

The best short hairstyles for thick curly hair and oval face are pixie cuts, bob cuts, curly shags, and layered cuts.

How can I style my short curly hair?

You can style your short curly hair using a curling iron or a flat iron to add texture and shape to your hair. You can also use hair accessories such as hairpins or headbands to add a touch of elegance to your hairstyle.

How can I maintain my short curly hair?

You can maintain your short curly hair by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, applying hair serum or oil regularly, and trimming your hair regularly.

Hair Care for Short Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair and Oval Face

Here are some hair care tips for short hairstyles for thick curly hair and oval face:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Rinse thoroughly with water and towel dry your hair.

Avoid Chemical Treatments

Avoid chemical treatments such as hair coloring or straightening as it can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle.

Protect Your Hair from Heat

Protect your hair from heat by using a heat protectant spray or serum before using heat styling tools.

In conclusion, short hairstyles for thick curly hair and oval face are easy to maintain, trendy, and perfect for any occasion. By following the tips and steps mentioned in this article, you can choose the right hairstyle that suits your features and enhances your natural beauty.
