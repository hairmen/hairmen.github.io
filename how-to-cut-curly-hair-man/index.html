How To Cut Curly Hair Man A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Oct 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to find a barber who knows how to cut it properly. Curly hair requires a different approach than straight hair, and if it's not cut correctly, it can look frizzy and uneven. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to cut curly hair for men. We'll also give you some tips on how to care for your curly hair to keep it looking its best.

Description

Curly hair has a unique texture that requires a different approach than straight hair. It's important to cut curly hair when it's dry, so you can see how the curls will fall. You also need to take into account the natural shape of the curls and the way they grow. Cutting curly hair is a skill that not all barbers possess, so it's important to find someone who knows what they're doing.

How to Cut Curly Hair Man: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to wash and condition it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair to help define your curls and prevent frizz. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it gently, leaving it slightly damp.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to comb your hair and detangle any knots. Make sure you comb your hair in the direction that it naturally falls to avoid pulling on the curls.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips or elastic bands. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly and prevent you from accidentally cutting too much off one side.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Starting at the back of your head, take a small section of hair and twist it into a tight coil. Use hair cutting scissors to trim the ends of the coil, cutting at an angle to create a soft, natural look. Continue cutting small sections of hair, twisting and cutting each one until you have cut all the sections in the back of your head.

Step 5: Move to the Sides

Once you have finished cutting the back of your head, move on to the sides. Take small sections of hair and cut them in the same way you did in the back.

Step 6: Cut the Top

Finally, move on to the top of your head. Take small sections of hair and cut them in the same way you did in the back and sides. Be careful not to cut too much off the top, as this can make your hair look too short and uneven.

Step 7: Check for Evenness

Once you have finished cutting your hair, use a mirror to check for evenness. If you notice any sections that are longer than others, trim them until they are even. Be careful not to cut too much off, as this can create an uneven look.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair Man

Tip 1: Use hair cutting scissors instead of regular scissors. Hair cutting scissors are designed to cut hair cleanly and evenly, while regular scissors can damage your hair.

Tip 2: Cut your hair when it's dry. Wet hair can look longer than it actually is, which can lead to uneven cuts.

Tip 3: Cut your hair in small sections. This will help you to achieve an even cut and prevent you from accidentally cutting too much off one side.

FAQ

What is the best way to care for curly hair?

The best way to care for curly hair is to use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair. You should also avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair and create frizz. Instead, let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to dry it gently.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It's recommended that you cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Curly Hair Man

Curly hair requires a different approach to hair care than straight hair. Here are some tips to help you keep your curly hair looking its best:

Tip 1: Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair to help define your curls and prevent frizz.

Tip 2: Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair and create frizz. Instead, let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to dry it gently.

Tip 3: Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid pulling on the curls.

Tip 4: Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it looking healthy.

Conclusion

Curly hair requires a different approach to hair care than straight hair. By following our step-by-step guide on how to cut curly hair for men and using our tips for hair care, you can keep your curly hair looking its best. Remember to cut your hair when it's dry and in small sections to achieve an even cut. With the right approach to hair care, you can embrace your curly hair and make it a defining feature of your style.
