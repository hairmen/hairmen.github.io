How To Shave Neck With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 31, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're someone who likes to keep their facial hair in check, then you know how important it is to have a clean, close shave. And one of the most difficult areas to shave is the neck. It can be tricky to get the right angle and avoid any cuts or irritation. That's where clippers come in. Using clippers to shave your neck is a quick and easy way to get a smooth, clean shave without the risk of cuts. In this article, we'll show you how to shave your neck with clippers in a step-by-step guide.

How to Shave Neck with Clippers

Step 1: Prepare Your Skin

Before you start shaving, make sure your skin is clean and dry. If you have any stubble, use a trimmer to trim it down to a manageable length. This will make it easier to shave with clippers later on.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clippers

When it comes to choosing clippers, there are a few things to consider. First, make sure they have a range of guard sizes so you can choose the right length for your hair. Second, make sure they have a sharp blade to avoid any pulling or tugging. And finally, make sure they're easy to handle and maneuver around your neck.

Step 3: Attach the Guard

Once you've chosen your clippers, attach the guard that's the right length for your hair. If you're not sure which guard to use, start with a longer one and work your way down until you find the right length.

Step 4: Start Shaving

Now it's time to start shaving. Hold the clippers at a 45-degree angle and start at the bottom of your neck. Move the clippers up in a smooth motion, going against the direction of your hair growth. Be sure to use your free hand to stretch the skin taut as you go to avoid any nicks or cuts.

Step 5: Repeat the Process

Continue shaving in small sections, working your way up your neck until you've shaved the entire area. If you notice any areas that you've missed, go back over them with the clippers.

Step 6: Clean Up

Once you've finished shaving, use a clean towel to wipe away any loose hair. You can also use a post-shave balm or lotion to soothe your skin and prevent any irritation.

Tips for Shaving Your Neck with Clippers

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when shaving your neck with clippers:

  • Always use a guard to avoid accidentally cutting yourself.
  • Stretch the skin taut with your free hand to avoid any nicks or cuts.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you find the right length for your hair.
  • Use a post-shave balm or lotion to soothe your skin and prevent any irritation.

FAQ

Q: Can I use clippers to shave my entire face?

A: While you can use clippers to trim your beard or mustache, they're not recommended for shaving your entire face. Use a razor for a closer shave.

Q: How often should I shave my neck?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows. Some people need to shave every day, while others can go a few days without shaving. Find a routine that works for you.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by keeping it clean, well-groomed, and well-nourished. Use a good shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a beard oil or balm to keep your facial hair soft and healthy.
