How To Know What Size Face Shape You Have

Description

Knowing your face shape can help you choose the best haircuts, hairstyles, and makeup looks that flatter your features. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each face shape has its own unique characteristics, which can help you identify your own. In this article, we'll show you how to determine your face shape with a step-by-step guide and offer tips for taking care of your hair based on your face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in determining your face shape is to pull your hair back away from your face so that your entire face is visible. Then, look in a mirror and examine the following characteristics:

Round Face

A round face is characterized by a soft, circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The width and length of the face are usually the same, and the forehead and jawline are both rounded.

Oval Face

An oval face is characterized by a slightly longer shape with a rounded hairline and jawline. The forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the face gently tapers to a point at the chin.

Square Face

A square face is characterized by a strong, angular shape with a square jawline and a broad forehead. The width and length of the face are usually the same, and the chin may be slightly squared or flat.

Heart Face

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wide forehead and cheekbones that taper to a narrow chin. The chin may be pointed or rounded, and the overall shape of the face is similar to an inverted triangle.

Diamond Face

A diamond-shaped face is characterized by wide cheekbones and a narrow forehead and chin. The face is longer than it is wide, and the chin may be pointed or slightly rounded.

Oblong Face

An oblong face is characterized by a long, narrow shape with a high forehead and a long chin. The face may appear rectangular or elongated, and the cheekbones are not as prominent as they are in other face shapes.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to determine your face shape:

  1. Pull your hair back away from your face so that your entire face is visible.
  2. Measure the length and width of your face with a tape measure.
  3. Measure the length of your forehead from your hairline to your eyebrows.
  4. Measure the width of your cheekbones at their widest point.
  5. Measure the width of your jawline at its widest point.
  6. Compare your measurements to the characteristics of each face shape to determine which one best describes your face.

Tips for Determining Your Face Shape

Here are a few tips to help you determine your face shape:

  • Use a mirror with good lighting to get an accurate view of your face.
  • Be honest about your features and don't try to force your face into a different shape.
  • Ask a friend or family member for their opinion on your face shape.
  • Look at photos of yourself from different angles to get a better idea of your face shape.

FAQs

What if my face doesn't fit any of the six basic face shapes?

It's possible that your face may have a combination of two or more face shapes. In this case, focus on the characteristics that are most prominent and use those to determine your face shape.

Can my face shape change over time?

While your face shape may change slightly as you age, the overall shape of your face should remain relatively consistent throughout your life.

Why is it important to know my face shape?

Knowing your face shape can help you choose the most flattering hairstyles, haircuts, and makeup looks that highlight your best features and minimize any areas you may be self-conscious about.

Hair Care for Your Face Shape

Once you've determined your face shape, you can use that information to choose the best hairstyles and haircuts that flatter your features. Here are a few tips for taking care of your hair based on your face shape:

  • Round faces look best with hairstyles that add height at the crown and lengthen the face, such as long layers and side-swept bangs.
  • Oval faces can pull off just about any hairstyle, but look especially good with shoulder-length hair and blunt bangs.
  • Square faces look best with hairstyles that soften the jawline, such as long layers and side-swept bangs.
  • Heart-shaped faces look best with hairstyles that add volume at the chin and lengthen the forehead, such as chin-length bobs and side-swept bangs.
  • Diamond-shaped faces look best with hairstyles that add volume at the crown and minimize width at the cheekbones, such as layered cuts and side-swept bangs.
  • Oblong faces look best with hairstyles that add volume at the sides and shorten the face, such as chin-length bobs and side-swept bangs.
