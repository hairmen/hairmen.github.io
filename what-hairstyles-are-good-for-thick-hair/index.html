What Hairstyles Are Good For Thick Hair

Written by Oliver Oct 21, 2023 · 3 min read

If you’re blessed with thick hair, you know that it can be both a blessing and a curse. While you have plenty of volume to work with, it can be difficult to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and doesn’t make you look like a poodle. Fortunately, there are plenty of hairstyles that are perfect for thick hair. In this post, we’ll explore some of the best options for those with thick locks.

Description

Thick hair can be a challenge to manage, particularly if it’s long. It can be heavy, difficult to style, and prone to frizz. However, with the right cut and style, you can make the most of your thick locks and achieve a hairstyle that’s both flattering and manageable.

How to Style Thick Hair

The key to styling thick hair is to work with its natural texture and volume. Avoid using heavy products, as they can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy. Instead, opt for lightweight styling products that add shine and control without adding extra weight.

Step by Step

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

Step 2: Apply a lightweight styling product, such as a mousse or texturizing spray, to add volume and control.

Step 3: Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, lifting at the roots to add volume.

Step 4: Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to add texture and shape.

Step 5: Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Styling Thick Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling thick hair:

  • Choose a cut that works with your natural texture and volume.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Use a lightweight styling product to add volume and control.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add lift at the roots.
  • Use a curling iron or straightener to add texture and shape.
  • Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

FAQs

Q: What are the best haircuts for thick hair?

A: The best haircuts for thick hair are those that work with its natural texture and volume. Layered cuts, shaggy bobs, and long layers with face-framing pieces are all great options for those with thick locks.

Q: How do I prevent frizz in thick hair?

A: To prevent frizz in thick hair, avoid using heavy products and opt for lightweight styling products that add control without weighing your hair down. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disrupting its natural texture.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Proper hair care is key to keeping thick hair healthy and manageable. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day, as they can damage your hair over time.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Conclusion

Thick hair can be a challenge to manage, but with the right cut, style, and hair care routine, you can achieve a hairstyle that’s both flattering and manageable. Keep these tips and tricks in mind, and you’ll be well on your way to the perfect hairstyle for your thick locks.
