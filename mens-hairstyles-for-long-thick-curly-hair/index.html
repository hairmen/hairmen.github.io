Mens Hairstyles For Long Thick Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 05, 2023 · 5 min read

If you are a man with long thick curly hair, you know how hard it can be to find the perfect hairstyle. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this guide, we will share with you the best mens hairstyles for long thick curly hair, how to style them, and tips for hair care. Let's get started!

Description

Long thick curly hair is a blessing, but it can also be a curse if you don't know how to style it properly. There are many mens hairstyles that work well with this hair type, including the messy curly undercut, the curly man bun, and the curly top knot. These hairstyles are easy to style and can be worn in many different settings, from casual to formal.

How to Style Mens Hairstyles for Long Thick Curly Hair

Styling mens hairstyles for long thick curly hair can be challenging, but with the right technique, it can be done easily. The key is to use the right products and tools to enhance your curls and keep them looking great all day long. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for curly hair. This will keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing cream or mousse to damp hair. This will help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help prevent frizz and enhance your curls.
  • If you have long hair, consider tying it up in a man bun or top knot. This will keep your hair out of your face and give you a stylish look.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to style mens hairstyles for long thick curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for curly hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it is slightly damp.
  3. Apply a curl-enhancing cream or mousse to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer and dry your hair on a low heat setting.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to define your curls and add volume.
  6. If you have long hair, tie it up in a man bun or top knot.
  7. Apply a small amount of hair gel or styling cream to tame any flyaways.

Tips for Maintaining Long Thick Curly Hair

Maintaining long thick curly hair can be challenging, but with the right hair care routine, it can be done easily. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too dry.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools such as straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens hairstyles for long thick curly hair:

What is the best mens hairstyle for long thick curly hair?

The best mens hairstyle for long thick curly hair depends on your personal style and preferences. Some popular options include the messy curly undercut, the curly man bun, and the curly top knot.

How can I keep my long thick curly hair looking great?

You can keep your long thick curly hair looking great by using a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for curly hair, applying a curl-enhancing cream or mousse, using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer, tying your hair up in a man bun or top knot, and applying a small amount of hair gel or styling cream to tame flyaways.

How often should I wash my long thick curly hair?

You should wash your long thick curly hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too dry.

Hair Care for Long Thick Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for long thick curly hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for curly hair.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing cream or mousse to damp hair.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting.
  • If you have long hair, tie it up in a man bun or top knot.
  • Apply a small amount of hair gel or styling cream to tame flyaways.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.

Now that you know the best mens hairstyles for long thick curly hair, how to style them, and how to care for your hair, you can rock your curly locks with confidence. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to show off your natural curls!
