Men s Haircut Long Hair On Top A Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Written by Petter Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle and try something new, a men's haircut with long hair on top may be just what you need. This trendy style has become increasingly popular in recent years and can be customized to fit any face shape or hair type. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about getting a men's haircut with long hair on top, from how to achieve the look to tips for maintaining it.

Description

A men's haircut with long hair on top is a style that features shorter hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on the top of the head. This style can be worn in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and textured, making it a versatile option for any occasion.

How to

Getting a men's haircut with long hair on top is a process that requires a bit of patience and skill. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect look:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

The first step in getting a men's haircut with long hair on top is deciding how long you want your hair to be. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. A good rule of thumb is to keep the sides and back shorter than the top to create contrast and balance.

Step 2: Find a Barber or Stylist

Once you've decided on your desired length, it's time to find a barber or stylist who can bring your vision to life. Look for someone who has experience with men's haircuts and can offer suggestions based on your face shape and hair type.

Step 3: Communicate Your Style

When you sit down in the barber's chair, be sure to communicate exactly what you're looking for. Bring in photos or examples of the style you want, and be open to suggestions from your barber or stylist. Communication is key to achieving the perfect look.

Step 4: Cut and Style

Once you and your barber or stylist have agreed on the style, it's time to cut and style your hair. The sides and back will be trimmed with clippers, while the top will be cut with scissors. Your stylist may also use a razor or texturizing scissors to add texture and movement. After the cut, your stylist will style your hair using products like pomade or hair gel to achieve the desired look.

Tips

Here are a few tips for getting the most out of your men's haircut with long hair on top:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
  • Ask your stylist for tips on how to style your hair at home
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain your style and keep it looking fresh
  • Experiment with different products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and style

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's haircuts with long hair on top:

What face shape is best for this style?

This style can work for any face shape, but it's important to adjust the length and shape of the cut to complement your features.

How often should I get a trim?

It's a good idea to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

What products should I use?

It depends on your hair type and the style you're trying to achieve. Ask your stylist for recommendations, or try experimenting with different products to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

Maintaining a men's haircut with long hair on top requires a bit of effort, but it's worth it for the stylish and versatile look it provides. Here are a few tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a men's haircut with long hair on top like a pro. Whether you're going for a sleek and polished look or a messy and textured style, this trendy haircut is sure to turn heads.
