What s The Best Hairstyle For Oblong Face

Written by William Jul 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your oblong face shape? Look no further as we have got you covered! In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyle for oblong face and provide you with some tips and tricks to help you achieve your desired look.

Description

Oblong face shape is characterized by a longer face with narrow and angular features. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all of similar width, and the chin is slightly rounded. The oblong face shape is also known as a rectangular face shape. It is essential to choose a hairstyle that balances the length of your face and creates an illusion of width to enhance your features.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you are unsure about your face shape, here is a quick and easy way to determine it. First, pull your hair back and stand in front of a mirror. Use a lipstick or an eyeliner to trace the outline of your face on the mirror. Now, step back and analyze the shape you have drawn. If your face is longer than it is wide and has angular features, then you have an oblong face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some of the best hairstyles for oblong face shape:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for oblong face shape as they create an illusion of width and volume. The layers start from the cheekbones and frame the face, giving it a softer and more feminine look. You can wear your hair straight or add some curls to create more texture and volume.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for oblong face shape as they break up the length of the face and draw attention to the eyes. The bangs should start from the side and fall diagonally across the forehead. You can style them with a round brush for a sleek and polished look or leave them tousled for a more casual vibe.

3. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a classic and timeless hairstyle that suits almost every face shape. For oblong face shape, a chin-length bob with layers and side-swept bangs is perfect. It adds volume and width to the face and accentuates the jawline. You can style it with a middle part or a deep side part, depending on your preference.

4. Pixie Cut

If you are looking for a short and edgy hairstyle, a pixie cut is a great option for oblong face shape. The short layers create volume and texture, and the side-swept bangs add width to the face. You can style it with a side part or a messy and tousled look for a more relaxed vibe.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for oblong face shape:

1. Avoid Center Parts

Avoid center parts as they elongate the face and make it look even longer. Instead, opt for a side part that adds volume and width to the face.

2. Add Volume

Add volume to the crown of your head to balance the length of your face. You can use a volumizing spray or a root lifting powder to achieve this.

3. Avoid Straight Hair

Avoid straight and flat hairstyles as they make the face look longer. Instead, opt for hairstyles with layers and texture that add volume and movement to the hair.

FAQs

1. Can oblong face shape wear short hairstyles?

Yes, oblong face shape can wear short hairstyles such as pixie cuts and bobs. However, make sure to add layers and texture to the hair to create volume and width.

2. Can oblong face shape wear bangs?

Yes, oblong face shape can wear bangs, but they should be side-swept and not straight across the forehead. Side-swept bangs add width to the face and balance the length of the face.

3. Can oblong face shape wear updos?

Yes, oblong face shape can wear updos, but they should be soft and not too tight. Soft updos add volume and width to the face and balance the length of the face.

Hair Care

It is essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and vitality. Here are some tips for hair care:

1. Use a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. This will help to keep your hair clean, nourished, and healthy.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, make sure to use a heat protectant spray before styling.

3. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best hairstyle for oblong face shape, go ahead and try out some of these styles to enhance your features and create a stunning look. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and vitality.
