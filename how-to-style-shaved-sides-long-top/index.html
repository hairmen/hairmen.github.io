How To Style Shaved Sides Long Top A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 27, 2023 · 5 min read

Shaved sides long top is a trendy hairstyle that has been popular for a few years now. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in different ways to suit different occasions. In this article, we will take you through how to style shaved sides long top in a relaxed English language. We will cover everything from how to style it, step by step, tips, FAQ, and hair care.

How to Style Shaved Sides Long Top

Styling shaved sides long top is relatively easy, and it can be done in a few simple steps:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will help to remove any dirt and buildup from your hair and make it easier to style.
  2. Blow-dry your hair and apply some styling gel or wax to the top part of your hair. This will help to hold the hair in place and add some shine to it.
  3. Use a comb to create a part on the top part of your hair. This will help to create a neat and tidy look.
  4. Using a pair of clippers, shave the sides of your head. You can either shave it all the way down to the skin or leave a little bit of hair depending on your preference.
  5. Apply some more styling gel or wax to the top part of your hair and style it according to your preference.

With these simple steps, you can achieve a stylish and trendy shaved sides long top hairstyle.

Step by Step Guide

If you are new to styling shaved sides long top, here is a step-by-step guide to help you:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in styling shaved sides long top is to decide on the length of your hair. You can either go for a completely shaved look or leave a little bit of hair on the sides. Whatever length you choose, make sure it suits your face shape and overall style.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before styling your hair, it is essential to wash and condition it to remove any dirt and buildup. Use a mild shampoo and a conditioner that suits your hair type.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a hairdryer and a brush. This will help to create volume and add some texture to your hair.

Step 4: Apply Styling Gel or Wax

Apply some styling gel or wax to the top part of your hair. This will help to hold the hair in place and add some shine to it.

Step 5: Create a Part

Using a comb, create a part on the top part of your hair. This will help to create a neat and tidy look.

Step 6: Shave the Sides of Your Head

Using a pair of clippers, shave the sides of your head. You can either shave it all the way down to the skin or leave a little bit of hair depending on your preference.

Step 7: Style the Top Part of Your Hair

Apply some more styling gel or wax to the top part of your hair and style it according to your preference. You can either go for a slicked-back look, a messy look, or a pompadour style.

With these simple steps, you can achieve a stylish and trendy shaved sides long top hairstyle.

Tips for Styling Shaved Sides Long Top

Here are some tips to help you style your shaved sides long top:

  • Use a high-quality styling gel or wax that suits your hair type to hold your hair in place.
  • Use a comb to create a neat and tidy look on the top part of your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you the best.
  • Visit a professional barber or stylist to get the perfect shaved sides long top hairstyle.

FAQs About Shaved Sides Long Top

1. Is shaved sides long top suitable for all face shapes?

Shaved sides long top can be customized to suit different face shapes. However, it may not be suitable for people with a round face as it can make the face look wider.

2. How often should I shave the sides of my head?

It depends on how fast your hair grows. If you want to maintain the shaved sides look, you may have to shave it every two to three weeks.

3. Can I style my shaved sides long top with a hairdryer?

Yes, you can use a hairdryer to style your shaved sides long top. It will help to create volume and add some texture to your hair.

Hair Care for Shaved Sides Long Top

Here are some tips to help you take care of your shaved sides long top:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Apply some hair oil or serum to the top part of your hair to keep it nourished and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or hair products that can damage your hair.
  • Visit a professional barber or stylist regularly to maintain your shaved sides long top hairstyle.

With these tips, you can keep your shaved sides long top hairstyle looking stylish and healthy.

In conclusion, shaved sides long top is a trendy and versatile hairstyle that can be styled in different ways to suit different occasions. By following the steps and tips in this article, you can achieve a stylish and trendy shaved sides long top hairstyle.
