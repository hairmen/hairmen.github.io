What Is A Crew Haircut A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jun 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

A crew haircut is a classic and timeless hairstyle that has been around for decades. It is a short haircut that is typically worn by men but can also be worn by women. The crew haircut is popular because of its clean and simple look. In this article, we will discuss what a crew haircut is, how to get it, and how to take care of it.

What is a Crew Haircut?

A crew haircut is a short haircut that is typically tapered on the sides and back and slightly longer on the top. The hair on the top of the head is cut in a way that creates a slightly rounded shape. The sides and back of the head are tapered shorter and can be cut with clippers or scissors. The length of the crew haircut can vary, but it is usually between 1/8 inch to 1 inch.

How to Get a Crew Haircut

The crew haircut is a simple and easy haircut to get. You can either go to a barber or a hairstylist to get it done. Here are the steps to getting a crew haircut:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step is to decide on the length of your crew haircut. You can either go for a shorter or longer crew cut. A shorter crew cut is usually between 1/8 inch to 1/2 inch, while a longer crew cut is between 1/2 inch to 1 inch.

Step 2: Get the Haircut

Once you have decided on the length, you can go to your barber or hairstylist to get the haircut. You can either show them a picture of the crew cut you want or describe it to them. The barber or hairstylist will then cut your hair to the desired length and shape.

Step 3: Style the Haircut

After the haircut, you can style your crew cut by using a styling product. You can use a pomade, wax, or gel to style your hair into the desired shape.

Tips for Maintaining a Crew Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining a crew haircut:

  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and length of your crew cut.
  • Use a styling product to keep your hair in place.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as it can damage your hair.

    • FAQ About Crew Haircuts

    1. Can women wear crew haircuts?

    Yes, women can wear crew haircuts. It is a versatile haircut that can be worn by both men and women.

    2. How often should I get a crew haircut?

    You should get a crew haircut every 4 to 6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of the haircut.

    3. Can I style my crew haircut in different ways?

    Yes, you can style your crew haircut in different ways by using different styling products and techniques.

    Hair Care for Crew Haircuts

    Here are some hair care tips for crew haircuts:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can dry out your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as it can weigh down your hair.

    • With this guide, you now know what a crew haircut is, how to get it, and how to take care of it. Whether you are a man or a woman, the crew haircut is a classic and timeless hairstyle that can be worn by anyone. So, go ahead and try out this simple and stylish haircut!
