How To Keep A Lace Front Wig On Without Glue Or Tape

Written by Aprilia Oct 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of using glue or tape to keep your lace front wig in place? Do you want to know how to keep your wig secure without damaging your hairline or scalp? Look no further, because we have some tips and tricks to help you keep your wig on without any adhesive!

Table of Contents

  • How to Keep a Lace Front Wig on Without Glue or Tape
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Keep Your Wig Secure
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Your Wig

How to Keep a Lace Front Wig on Without Glue or Tape

Firstly, it's important to choose the right size wig cap. If your wig cap is too big or too small, it will not fit properly and will not stay in place. Measure your head circumference and choose a cap that fits snugly without being too tight.

Next, use wig clips to secure your wig onto your natural hair. Wig clips can be sewn onto the wig cap or attached with adhesive. Place the clips along the hairline and at the nape of your neck for maximum security.

You can also use a wig grip band to hold your wig in place. Wig grips are made of a non-slip material that sits between your wig cap and your scalp. Simply place the wig grip over your hairline and adjust your wig on top. The grip will keep your wig from slipping or sliding.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to help you keep your lace front wig on without glue or tape:

  1. Choose the right size wig cap
  2. Sew or attach wig clips onto the wig cap
  3. Clip the wig onto your natural hairline and at the nape of your neck
  4. Adjust the wig for a comfortable fit
  5. Place a wig grip band over your hairline
  6. Adjust the wig on top of the wig grip band
  7. Style your wig as desired

Tips to Keep Your Wig Secure

Here are some additional tips to help keep your wig secure:

  • Use bobby pins to secure the wig at the temples and behind the ears
  • Apply a scalp protector to prevent irritation or damage to your skin
  • Use a wig stand or mannequin to store your wig when not in use
  • Avoid sleeping in your wig to prevent tangling or damage
  • Invest in a high-quality wig to ensure longevity and durability

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about keeping a lace front wig on without glue or tape:

Can I swim or exercise with my wig on?

It's not recommended to swim or exercise with your wig on, as sweat and water can damage the wig and cause it to slip or slide.

How often should I wash my wig?

It depends on how often you wear your wig and how much product you use. Generally, it's recommended to wash your wig every 6-8 wears.

Can I use heat styling tools on my wig?

Yes, you can use heat styling tools on most synthetic and human hair wigs. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using high heat settings to prevent damage.

Hair Care for Your Wig

To keep your wig looking its best, it's important to take proper care of it. Here are some tips for caring for your wig:

  • Brush your wig gently with a wig brush or wide-tooth comb
  • Wash your wig with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can damage the wig
  • Allow your wig to air dry on a wig stand or mannequin
  • Avoid using high heat settings on styling tools

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your lace front wig on without glue or tape and maintain a secure and comfortable fit. Remember to choose the right size wig cap, use wig clips or a wig grip band, and take proper care of your wig to ensure longevity and durability.
