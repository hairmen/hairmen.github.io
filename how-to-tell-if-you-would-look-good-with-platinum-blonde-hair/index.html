How To Tell If You Would Look Good With Platinum Blonde Hair

Written by Eveline Jul 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you thinking about switching up your hair color and trying out platinum blonde? Before you make the leap, it’s important to consider whether this bold color will suit your skin tone and features. In this article, we’ll explore how to tell if you would look good with platinum blonde hair, as well as provide tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tell If You Would Look Good with Platinum Blonde Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Platinum Blonde Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Platinum Blonde Hair

How to Tell If You Would Look Good with Platinum Blonde Hair

Platinum blonde hair can look stunning on the right person, but it’s not a color that suits everyone. Here are some factors to consider when deciding whether this shade is right for you:

Skin Tone

Platinum blonde hair tends to look best on people with cool skin tones. If you have pink or blue undertones in your skin, you’re more likely to pull off this color. On the other hand, if you have warm, yellow undertones, platinum blonde might wash you out.

Eye Color

Another factor to consider is your eye color. Platinum blonde can really make blue, green, or gray eyes pop, but it might not be as flattering on brown or hazel eyes.

Natural Hair Color

If your natural hair color is very dark, it might be more difficult to achieve platinum blonde without damaging your hair. People with lighter hair colors will have an easier time making the transition.

If you’re still unsure whether platinum blonde is right for you, one trick is to try on a wig in this color. This will give you a good idea of how you’ll look with the new hue without making a permanent commitment.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’ve decided to take the plunge and go platinum blonde, here’s a step-by-step guide to achieving the look:

  1. Consult with a professional stylist to determine whether platinum blonde is a good fit for you.
  2. If you have dark hair, you may need to bleach it several times to achieve the right level of blonde. This can be damaging to your hair, so make sure you’re prepared for the commitment.
  3. Once you’ve achieved the desired level of blonde, your stylist will likely tone your hair to get rid of any brassy tones and make the color more even.
  4. Finally, your stylist will cut and style your hair to complete the transformation!

Tips for Maintaining Platinum Blonde Hair

Once you’ve achieved your new color, it’s important to take good care of your hair to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a purple shampoo to keep your color from turning yellow.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can dry out your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

FAQs

Is platinum blonde high maintenance?

Yes, platinum blonde hair requires a lot of maintenance to keep it looking its best. You’ll need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks and use special hair care products to keep your hair healthy.

Can I dye my hair platinum blonde at home?

We recommend seeing a professional stylist to achieve the best results with platinum blonde hair. Bleaching your hair can be tricky and can cause serious damage if done incorrectly.

Will platinum blonde hair damage my hair?

Bleaching your hair to achieve platinum blonde can be damaging if not done correctly. It’s important to take good care of your hair and avoid over-processing it to keep it healthy.

Hair Care for Platinum Blonde Hair

Here are some additional tips for keeping your platinum blonde hair looking its best:

  • Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid chlorine, as it can turn your hair green.
  • Consider using a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to the sun for extended periods of time, as this can cause it to become dry and brittle.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be rocking your new platinum blonde hair in no time!
