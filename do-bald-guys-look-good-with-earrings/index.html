Do Bald Guys Look Good With Earrings

Written by Christine Aug 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a bald guy who's been thinking about getting earrings but not sure if it will look good on you? Well, you're not alone. Many bald guys wonder if they can pull off the earring look. In this blog post, we'll answer the question, "Do bald guys look good with earrings?" and provide some tips on how to rock this look.

Description

Earrings have been a popular fashion accessory for centuries, and they're not just for women. Men have been wearing earrings for years, and it's a great way to add some personality to your style. However, when it comes to bald guys, some people are still unsure if earrings will look good on them.

How To

If you're a bald guy who's thinking about getting earrings, there are a few things to consider. Here are some tips on how to rock the earring look:

1. Start with a small stud

If you're new to wearing earrings, start with a small stud. This will help you get used to the feeling of wearing an earring and give you a chance to see how it looks on you. Once you feel more comfortable, you can experiment with larger earrings.

2. Choose the right earring

When it comes to choosing an earring, there are many styles to choose from. Consider the shape of your face and the size of your ears when choosing an earring. If you have a round face, go for a more angular earring. If you have large ears, choose an earring that's not too big or too small.

3. Match with your outfit

When wearing earrings, it's important to match them with your outfit. If you're wearing a casual outfit, go for a more laid-back earring. If you're dressing up for a formal occasion, choose a more elegant earring.

Step by Step

If you're ready to take the plunge and get earrings, here's a step-by-step guide on what to do:

1. Choose the right piercing location

The first step is to choose the right piercing location. The most common place for men to get their ears pierced is the earlobe. However, you can also get a piercing on the top of your ear or the cartilage.

2. Find a reputable piercer

It's important to find a reputable piercer who uses sterile equipment. Look for a piercer who has experience piercing men's ears and can offer advice on what type of earring to get.

3. Follow aftercare instructions

After you get your ears pierced, it's important to follow the aftercare instructions to avoid infection. This includes cleaning your ears regularly and avoiding touching your earrings with dirty hands.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to rock the earring look:

1. Be confident

The key to pulling off any fashion accessory is confidence. If you feel good wearing earrings, then you'll look good too.

2. Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different earring styles. Try out different shapes and sizes to find the perfect earring for you.

3. Keep your hair groomed

When you're bald, it's important to keep your hair groomed. This will help draw attention to your earrings and complete the look.

FAQ

Q: Can bald guys wear hoop earrings?

A: Yes, bald guys can wear hoop earrings. However, it's important to choose the right size hoop for your face and ears. If you have a round face, go for a smaller hoop. If you have a more angular face, a larger hoop will work well.

Q: Are there any types of earrings bald guys should avoid?

A: There are no types of earrings that bald guys should avoid. It's all about finding the right earring for your face and personal style.

Q: Can bald guys wear more than one earring?

A: Yes, bald guys can wear more than one earring. However, it's important to keep the earrings coordinated and not too flashy.

Hair Care

When you're bald, it's important to take care of your scalp. Moisturize your scalp regularly to avoid dryness and flaking. Also, wear a hat or sunscreen when you're outside to protect your scalp from the sun's UV rays.

Conclusion

So, do bald guys look good with earrings? The answer is yes! With the right earring and a little confidence, bald guys can rock the earring look. If you're thinking about getting earrings, start with a small stud and choose the right earring for your face and personal style. Remember to be confident and have fun with it!
