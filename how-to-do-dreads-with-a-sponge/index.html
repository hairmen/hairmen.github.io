How To Do Dreads With A Sponge A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Sep 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that will make you stand out, dreadlocks might be the perfect choice for you. And if you want to achieve this look without using chemicals or a lot of time, you can use a sponge to create your dreads. In this guide, we'll show you how to do dreads with a sponge, and give you some tips and tricks to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Description

Dreads are a hairstyle that has been popular for centuries, and they are still going strong today. They are created by twisting and matting hair together, and they can be worn by people of all ages and backgrounds. There are many different ways to create dreads, but using a sponge is one of the easiest and most popular methods.

How to Do Dreads with a Sponge

Here are the steps to create dreads with a sponge:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and free of any styling products. This will make it easier to work with and help your dreads form correctly.

Step 2: Choose Your Sponge

You'll need a sponge with small holes to create your dreads. You can find these sponges at most beauty supply stores, or online. Make sure the sponge is clean and dry before you start.

Step 3: Dampen Your Hair

Use a spray bottle to dampen your hair. You want it to be slightly wet, but not dripping. This will help the sponge grip your hair and create the dreads.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section your hair into small sections. The size of the sections will depend on the size of the sponge you're using, but they should be small enough to fit through the holes in the sponge.

Step 5: Start Twisting

Take a section of hair and place the sponge at the base. Use your fingers to twist the hair around the sponge, making sure it's tight and secure. Continue twisting until you reach the end of the section.

Step 6: Repeat

Repeat this process on all of the sections of your hair. Make sure the dreads are even and uniform in size.

Step 7: Let Your Hair Dry

Once you've created all of your dreads, let your hair dry completely. This will help set the dreads and prevent them from unraveling.

Step 8: Style Your Dreads

Once your hair is dry, you can style your dreads however you like. You can use a hair tie or clip to keep them in place, or leave them loose for a more natural look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Dreads

Here are some tips to keep your dreads looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but be gentle with your dreads.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can build up and make your dreads heavy and greasy.
  • Use a scarf or hat to protect your dreads from the sun and the elements.
  • Get regular trims to keep your dreads looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

How long does it take to create dreads with a sponge?

It can take anywhere from a few hours to a full day to create dreads with a sponge, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Can I create dreads with a sponge on any hair type?

Yes, you can create dreads with a sponge on any hair type, but it may be more difficult on very fine or straight hair.

Do I need to use any special products to maintain my dreads?

No, you don't need to use any special products to maintain your dreads. Regular shampoo and conditioner will work just fine.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair when you have dreads. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can damage your hair and make your dreads unravel.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can create and maintain beautiful dreads with a sponge. Whether you're looking for a bold new look or just want to switch things up, dreads are a great choice. So grab your sponge and get started!
