Mens Short Spiky Haircuts 2021 A Guide To The Best Styles

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's easy to maintain and looks great, then a short spiky haircut is the way to go. Whether you have thick or thin hair, there's a style that will suit you. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best mens short spiky haircuts for 2021, and give you tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling and Maintenance
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Short spiky haircuts are a popular choice for men because they are low maintenance and versatile. They can be styled in a variety of ways, from textured and messy to sleek and polished. The key to a great spiky haircut is to have the right cut and product. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and modern look that works for any occasion.

How to Get the Look

The first step to getting a short spiky haircut is to find a style that suits your face shape and hair type. You can consult with a hairstylist or look for inspiration online. Once you have a style in mind, it's time to get the cut. A spiky haircut typically involves short sides and back with longer hair on top that is styled into spikes using a hair product.

Tools You'll Need

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair product (e.g. pomade, wax, gel)

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a short spiky haircut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Use clippers to cut the sides and back short
  3. Use scissors to trim the hair on top to the desired length
  4. Apply a small amount of hair product to your fingertips and work it into your hair
  5. Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair into spikes
  6. Adjust the spikes as needed and add more product if necessary
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place

Tips for Styling and Maintenance

Here are some tips for styling and maintaining your short spiky haircut:

  • Use a high-quality hair product that works with your hair type
  • Experiment with different product textures (e.g. matte, glossy) to achieve different looks
  • Try different styling techniques, such as twisting or spiking your hair in different directions
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and style of your haircut
  • Wash your hair regularly and use a moisturizing conditioner to keep it healthy

FAQs

Q: Can anyone get a short spiky haircut?

A: Short spiky haircuts can work for most hair types and face shapes, but it's best to consult with a hairstylist to find a style that suits you.

Q: What's the best product to use for spiking hair?

A: The best product to use for spiking hair depends on your hair type and the look you want to achieve. Pomade or wax works well for thick hair, while gel is better for thinner hair.

Q: How often do I need to get a haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your short spiky haircut.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short spiky haircut looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy
  • Avoid using hot tools (e.g. blow dryer, straightener) too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and modern short spiky haircut that works for any occasion. Whether you prefer a textured and messy look or a sleek and polished style, there's a spiky haircut that will suit your personality and lifestyle. So why not give it a try and see how it looks on you?
