How To Keep Your Hair Curly Without Products Tips And Tricks

Written by William Oct 18, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of using products to keep your hair curly? Do you want to achieve bouncy curls without the use of chemicals? Well, you’re in luck because in this article, we will be sharing tips and tricks on how to keep your hair curly without products.

Table of Contents

  • How to Keep Your Hair Curly Without Products
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Curls
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Keep Your Hair Curly Without Products

Here are some ways to keep your hair curly without the use of products:

1. Use a diffuser when blow-drying

A diffuser is an attachment for your blow dryer that helps to disperse the air, reducing the amount of heat that is applied to your hair. This is important because high heat can cause your curls to straighten out. Use the diffuser on low heat and low speed, and scrunch your hair as you dry it.

2. Sleep with your hair in a pineapple

A pineapple is a loose, high ponytail that sits at the top of your head. This helps to keep your curls intact while you sleep, preventing them from getting flattened or tangled. Use a scrunchie or a silk scarf to tie your hair up, and make sure it’s not too tight.

3. Finger coil your hair

Finger coiling is a technique where you twist small sections of your hair around your finger to create defined curls. Start with wet hair, apply a leave-in conditioner, and then take small sections of hair and twist them around your finger. Hold the twist for a few seconds, and then release it. Repeat this process throughout your hair.

4. Use a curling iron or wand

If you want more defined curls, you can use a curling iron or wand. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron or wand, holding for a few seconds before releasing.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to keep your hair curly without products:

Step 1: Start with clean, wet hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and then towel dry your hair. Make sure your hair is still damp.

Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help to keep your curls moisturized and defined.

Step 3: Diffuse your hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer and dry your hair on low heat and low speed. Scrunch your hair as you dry it to help define your curls.

Step 4: Finger coil your hair

If you want more defined curls, use the finger coiling technique. Take small sections of hair and twist them around your finger, holding the twist for a few seconds before releasing.

Step 5: Sleep with your hair in a pineapple

Before going to bed, tie your hair up in a loose, high ponytail at the top of your head. Use a scrunchie or silk scarf to tie your hair up, and make sure it’s not too tight.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Here are some tips for maintaining your curls:

1. Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it’s dry

Brushing or combing your hair when it’s dry can cause it to become frizzy and lose its shape. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.

2. Use a silk pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to prevent frizz and breakage. Silk is gentle on your hair, reducing friction and preventing tangles.

3. Avoid using hot tools too often

Using hot tools too often can cause damage to your hair, making it more prone to breakage and frizz. Try to limit your use of hot tools to once or twice a week.

FAQs

Can I use products with these techniques?

Yes, you can still use products with these techniques if you want to. Just make sure to use products that are curly hair-friendly, such as leave-in conditioners or curl-enhancing creams.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how often you exercise or sweat. Generally, it’s recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry or oily.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

1. Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner

Sulfates can be harsh on curly hair, stripping it of its natural oils and causing it to become dry and brittle. Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

2. Deep condition regularly

Deep conditioning can help to keep your curls moisturized and defined. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to give your hair an extra boost of hydration.

3. Trim your hair regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help to prevent split ends and breakage. Aim to get a trim every 8-12 weeks.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair curly without the use of products. Remember to be gentle with your hair, avoid using too much heat, and keep it moisturized to maintain healthy, bouncy curls.
