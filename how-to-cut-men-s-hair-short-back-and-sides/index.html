How To Cut Men s Hair Short Back And Sides A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts every couple of weeks? Do you want to learn how to cut men's hair short back and sides at home? Look no further! In this article, we will provide an easy-to-follow guide on how to achieve a classic short back and sides haircut.

Description

The short back and sides haircut is a timeless style that has been popular for generations. It involves cutting the hair short on the back and sides while leaving the top longer. This style is versatile and can be customized to suit a variety of face shapes and hair types.

How to Cut Men's Hair Short Back and Sides

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the proper tools. You will need a pair of clippers with different guards, scissors, a comb, and a cape or towel to keep the hair off your client's clothes.

Step 1: Preparation

Start by washing and drying the hair. Comb through it to remove any tangles or knots. Then, decide on the length you want to cut the hair. Use a clipper guard to determine the length and adjust accordingly.

Step 2: Cutting the Sides

Begin cutting the sides by using the clippers and the appropriate guard. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using an upward motion. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once. Use the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

Step 3: Cutting the Back

Next, move to the back of the head. Use the same technique as the sides, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Make sure to cut in a straight line and use the comb to guide the clippers.

Step 4: Blending

To blend the sides and back, use a shorter guard on the clippers and go over the area where the two meet. Use a light touch and blend the hair gradually.

Step 5: Cutting the Top

Finally, cut the hair on the top of the head. Use scissors instead of clippers and cut the hair to the desired length. Use the comb to section the hair and make sure to cut it evenly.

Tips

- Start with a longer guard and work your way down to achieve the desired length.

- Use a light touch when blending to avoid any harsh lines.

- Take breaks to step back and assess the haircut.

- Use a mirror to check the back of the head.

FAQ

Q: How often should I get my hair cut in a short back and sides style?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain the style. Generally, a short back and sides style should be trimmed every 2-4 weeks.

Q: Can I achieve a short back and sides haircut with curly hair?

A: Yes, a short back and sides style can work well with curly hair. Just be sure to cut the hair when it is dry to ensure an even cut.

Hair Care

After the haircut, it is important to maintain the style with regular washing and styling. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and hydrated. Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to add texture and hold to the hair.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair short back and sides, you can save money and achieve a classic look at home. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully for the best results. Happy cutting!
