How To Choose Wedding Hair And Makeup

Written by Bardi Jun 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to choose wedding hair and makeup. Your wedding day is one of the most important days of your life, and it’s essential that you look and feel your best. Choosing the perfect hair and makeup can be a daunting task, but with our step-by-step guide, you’ll be able to make informed decisions and look stunning on your big day.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Research and Inspiration
  • Step 2: Consider Your Wedding Theme and Dress
  • Step 3: Find the Right Hair and Makeup Artist
  • Step 4: Schedule a Trial Run
  • Step 5: Final Touches and Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

Step 1: Research and Inspiration

The first step in choosing your wedding hair and makeup is to do some research and find inspiration. Start by browsing wedding magazines, websites, and social media platforms like Pinterest and Instagram. Look for hairstyles and makeup looks that you like and save them for later reference.

It’s also a good idea to consider your personal style and how you want to look on your wedding day. Do you prefer a natural, understated look, or do you want to go all out with bold makeup and an elaborate updo?

Once you have a general idea of what you want, it’s time to move on to the next step.

Step 2: Consider Your Wedding Theme and Dress

When choosing your wedding hair and makeup, it’s essential to consider your wedding theme and dress. Your hair and makeup should complement your overall look and the style of your wedding.

If you’re having a beach wedding, for example, you may want to opt for loose, beachy waves and minimal makeup. If you’re having a more formal wedding, a classic updo and bold makeup may be more appropriate.

Also, consider the neckline and style of your wedding dress. A strapless dress may call for an intricate updo, while a dress with a high neckline may look best with a sleek and simple hairstyle.

Step 3: Find the Right Hair and Makeup Artist

Choosing the right hair and makeup artist is crucial to achieving the look you want on your wedding day. Start by asking for recommendations from friends and family, or search online for reviews and portfolios.

When you find a potential artist, make sure to schedule a consultation to discuss your vision and ensure that they’re the right fit. Ask to see examples of their work and make sure they have experience working with your hair type and skin tone.

Step 4: Schedule a Trial Run

Once you’ve found the right hair and makeup artist, it’s time to schedule a trial run. This is your chance to test out different hairstyles and makeup looks and ensure that you’re happy with the final result.

Be sure to bring photos of hairstyles and makeup looks you like, as well as a photo of your wedding dress. This will help your artist create a cohesive look that complements your dress and style.

Step 5: Final Touches and Hair Care Tips

As your wedding day approaches, it’s important to take care of your hair and skin to ensure that it looks its best. Get regular trims and treatments to ensure that your hair is healthy and shiny, and make sure to drink plenty of water and get enough sleep to keep your skin glowing.

On the day of your wedding, be sure to arrive at your appointment with a clean face and hair that’s been washed the day before. This will ensure that your hair and makeup will last all day and night.

FAQs

How far in advance should I book my hair and makeup artist?

It’s recommended that you book your hair and makeup artist at least six months in advance to ensure that you get the artist you want.

How much should I expect to pay for wedding hair and makeup?

The cost of wedding hair and makeup can vary depending on the artist’s experience and location. On average, you can expect to pay between $150-$600 for hair and makeup.

What should I bring to my trial run?

Be sure to bring photos of hairstyles and makeup looks you like, as well as a photo of your wedding dress. You may also want to bring any hair accessories or jewelry you plan on wearing.

Choosing the perfect wedding hair and makeup can be a daunting task, but with our step-by-step guide, you’ll be able to make informed decisions and look stunning on your big day. Remember to do your research, consider your wedding theme and dress, find the right artist, schedule a trial run, and take care of your hair and skin leading up to your wedding day.
