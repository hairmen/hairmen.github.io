Best Hairstyle For Thick Coarse Wavy Hair

Written by Frank Jul 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick, coarse, and wavy hair, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want to choose a style that will showcase your natural texture while also being easy to manage. In this article, we will explore the best hairstyles for thick coarse wavy hair, as well as some tips for how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Thick coarse wavy hair can be challenging to style, but with the right haircut and styling products, you can create a beautiful look that showcases your natural texture. The best hairstyles for thick coarse wavy hair are those that enhance your hair's thickness and volume while also taming any frizz or flyaways.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your thick coarse wavy hair is to consider your face shape. Not all hairstyles will flatter every face shape, so it's essential to choose a style that complements your features. You should also consider your hair's length and texture when choosing a hairstyle. Shorter styles tend to work well with thick hair, while longer styles can be more challenging to manage.

Step by Step

To achieve the best hairstyle for your thick coarse wavy hair, follow these steps:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specially formulated for thick, coarse hair.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or mousse, to damp hair.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair while enhancing your natural waves.
  5. Use a curling iron or wand to define any loose waves or curls.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or styling serum to tame any frizz or flyaways.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling your thick coarse wavy hair:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as a brush can cause breakage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling thick coarse wavy hair:

What is the best haircut for thick coarse wavy hair?

The best haircut for thick coarse wavy hair is one that enhances your natural texture while also being easy to manage. A layered bob or long layers can work well with thick hair, while a pixie cut can work well with shorter hair.

How can I tame frizz in my thick coarse wavy hair?

To tame frizz in your thick coarse wavy hair, use a smoothing serum or cream. You can also try using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair while enhancing your natural waves.

Hair Care

To keep your thick coarse wavy hair healthy and moisturized, use a shampoo and conditioner that are specially formulated for your hair type. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated. Avoid over-washing your hair, and use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

In conclusion, the best hairstyle for thick coarse wavy hair is one that enhances your natural texture while also being easy to manage. By following these tips and using the right products, you can create a beautiful look that showcases your unique style.
