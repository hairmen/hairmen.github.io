How Can I Tell If A Hairstyle Will Suit Me

Written by William Sep 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're thinking about changing your hairstyle, you might be wondering how to know if a certain cut or style will look good on you. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect look. Fortunately, there are a few things you can do to determine if a hairstyle will suit you.

  • Consider Your Hair Texture
  • Think About Your Lifestyle
  • Factor in Your Hair Color
  • Consider the Length

Determine Your Face Shape

The first thing to consider when choosing a new hairstyle is your face shape. Different styles will complement different face shapes, so it's important to know what shape your face is. The four main face shapes are:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart-shaped

Once you know your face shape, you can look for styles that will complement it. For example, if you have a round face, you might want to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face look even rounder. Instead, look for styles that add height and length to your face.

Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider when choosing a new hairstyle. If you have thick, curly hair, for example, you might want to avoid styles that require a lot of maintenance or straightening. On the other hand, if you have fine, straight hair, you might want to avoid styles that require a lot of volume or curling.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is also an important consideration when choosing a new hairstyle. If you have a busy schedule and don't have a lot of time to spend on your hair, you might want to choose a style that is low-maintenance and easy to style. On the other hand, if you have more time to spend on your hair and enjoy experimenting with different looks, you might want to choose a more complex style.

Factor in Your Hair Color

Your hair color can also play a role in determining which hairstyles will suit you. If you have a bold or unusual hair color, for example, you might want to choose a style that will complement it rather than clash with it. Similarly, if you have highlights or lowlights, you might want to choose a style that will show them off.

Consider the Length

The length of your hair is another important factor to consider. If you have short hair, you might want to avoid styles that require a lot of length or volume. On the other hand, if you have long hair, you might want to avoid styles that require a lot of maintenance or that will weigh your hair down.

Try It Out

One of the best ways to determine if a hairstyle will suit you is to try it out. You can use virtual hairstyle apps or websites to upload a photo of yourself and see how different styles will look on you. Alternatively, you can try on wigs or extensions to get a better idea of how a certain style will look on you.

Choose a Style That Frames Your Face

Another important consideration is how a hairstyle will frame your face. You want to choose a style that will highlight your best features and draw attention to your face. For example, if you have high cheekbones, you might want to choose a style that pulls your hair away from your face and highlights your cheekbones.

Know Your Hair Type

It's also important to know your hair type when choosing a new style. Different styles will work better for different hair types, so it's important to choose a style that will work with your hair type. For example, if you have curly hair, you might want to choose a style that will accentuate your curls.

Consider Your Hair Health

The health of your hair is another important factor to consider when choosing a new hairstyle. If your hair is damaged or dry, for example, you might want to avoid styles that require a lot of heat styling or products that can further damage your hair. Instead, choose a style that will work with your hair's natural texture and health.

Ask for Your Stylist's Advice

Finally, it's always a good idea to ask your stylist for their advice when choosing a new hairstyle. They can help you determine which styles will work best for your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, and can also give you tips on how to style and maintain your new look.

Conclusion:

Choosing a new hairstyle can be a fun and exciting experience, but it can also be overwhelming. By considering your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, hair color, hair length, and other factors, you can determine which styles will suit you best. And don't forget to try out different styles and ask your stylist for their advice!
