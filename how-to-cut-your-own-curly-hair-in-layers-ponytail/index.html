How To Cut Your Own Curly Hair In Layers Ponytail

Written by William Nov 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're tired of going to the salon every few months to get your curly hair cut in layers, why not try cutting your own hair at home? It's easier than you think, and with a little bit of practice, you can achieve the perfect layered look. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of cutting your own curly hair in layers using a ponytail technique.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Your Own Curly Hair in Layers - Ponytail
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure that you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair ties.

Start by washing and drying your hair. Once your hair is completely dry, brush it out and gather it into a ponytail at the back of your head. The ponytail should be at the height where you want the layers to start.

Step 1:

Use the comb to comb out any tangles in the ponytail. Then, take the hair tie and tie the ponytail tightly. Make sure that the hair tie is in the exact spot where you want the layers to start.

Step 2:

Use the scissors to cut the hair below the hair tie. Start by cutting a small amount of hair at a time. It's better to cut less than to cut too much.

Step 3:

After you've cut the hair, release the ponytail and comb through it with your fingers. You should see the layers start to form.

Step 4:

If you want more layers, you can repeat the process by tying another hair tie a little bit lower than the first one and cutting the hair below it.

Tips

- Cut your hair when it's dry. Wet hair can shrink when it dries, which can result in uneven layers.

- Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's been cut.

- Use sharp scissors to cut your hair. Dull scissors can damage your hair and result in split ends.

FAQ

Q: Can I use this technique on any type of curly hair?

A: Yes, this technique can be used on any type of curly hair.

Q: How often should I cut my hair using this technique?

A: It's recommended to cut your hair every 8-12 weeks to maintain the layers.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, make sure to take care of it by using a deep conditioning treatment once a week and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible.

Now that you know how to cut your own curly hair in layers using a ponytail technique, you can save time and money by doing it yourself at home. With a little bit of practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect layered look every time.
