FAQs

Can I create curls without heat on naturally straight hair?

Yes, you can create curls without heat on any hair type, but the results may vary. If your hair is naturally straight, you may not achieve as much curl as someone with naturally curly hair, but you can still create some wave or volume using braids, twists, or scrunching.

How long will my curls last?

This will depend on your hair type and the techniques you use to create your curls. Generally, braids and twists will create longer-lasting curls than scrunching. You can also use hair products such as hairspray or mousse to help your curls last longer.

Are there any hair products that can help enhance my curls?

Yes, there are many hair products specifically designed to enhance curls, such as mousse, gel, and curl creams. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, or argan oil, which can help moisturize and define your curls.
