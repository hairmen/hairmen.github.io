Can You Make Your Forehead Bigger Tips Faqs And Hair Care

Written by Aprilia Aug 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a smaller forehead than you would like, you may be wondering if there is anything you can do to make it bigger. While there are no guaranteed ways to make your forehead bigger, there are some things you can do to give the illusion of a larger forehead. In this article, we will explore tips, FAQs, and hair care for making your forehead appear larger.

Description

Having a smaller forehead can make your face look unbalanced or disproportionate. While some people are born with smaller foreheads, others may have a smaller forehead due to hair loss or hair thinning. In either case, a smaller forehead can make you feel less confident about your appearance. However, there are ways to make your forehead appear larger without resorting to surgery or other invasive procedures.

How to Make Your Forehead Appear Larger

If you want to make your forehead appear larger, there are some things you can try:

1. Change Your Hairstyle

The easiest way to make your forehead appear larger is to change your hairstyle. A hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head will make your forehead look bigger. You can try a messy bun or a high ponytail to achieve this look.

2. Use Makeup

You can use makeup to contour your face and create the illusion of a larger forehead. Apply a lighter shade of foundation to the center of your forehead and blend it outwards. This will create a highlight that makes your forehead appear larger.

3. Wear Headbands or Scarves

Wearing headbands or scarves can draw attention to your forehead and make it appear larger. You can also try wearing hats with a brim that sits above your forehead to create the illusion of a larger forehead.

Step by Step Guide

Here are some step-by-step instructions for making your forehead appear larger:

Step 1: Choose a Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head. A messy bun or a high ponytail are good options.

Step 2: Apply Foundation

Apply a lighter shade of foundation to the center of your forehead and blend it outwards.

Step 3: Wear Headbands or Scarves

Wear headbands or scarves that draw attention to your forehead.

Tips for Making Your Forehead Appear Larger

Here are some additional tips for making your forehead appear larger:

1. Grow Out Your Bangs

If you have bangs, growing them out can make your forehead appear larger.

2. Use a Volumizing Shampoo

Using a volumizing shampoo can help add volume to your hair and make your forehead appear larger.

3. Try Hair Extensions

Hair extensions can add volume to your hair and make your forehead appear larger.

FAQs

Q: Can I make my forehead bigger without surgery?

A: Yes, you can make your forehead appear larger without surgery. Changing your hairstyle, using makeup, and wearing headbands or scarves are all ways to create the illusion of a larger forehead.

Q: Can hair loss make my forehead look smaller?

A: Yes, hair loss or hair thinning can make your forehead look smaller. Using hair extensions or volumizing shampoos can help add volume to your hair and make your forehead appear larger.

Q: Will wearing hats make my forehead appear larger?

A: Wearing hats with a brim that sits above your forehead can create the illusion of a larger forehead.

Hair Care for a Larger Forehead

If you want to make your forehead appear larger, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Using a gentle shampoo can help prevent hair loss and hair thinning.

2. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hairline and make your forehead look smaller.

3. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help increase blood flow to your hair follicles and promote hair growth.

In conclusion, while there are no guaranteed ways to make your forehead bigger, there are things you can do to give the illusion of a larger forehead. Changing your hairstyle, using makeup, and wearing headbands or scarves are all ways to create the illusion of a larger forehead. Taking care of your hair is also important for maintaining a healthy hairline and promoting hair growth.
