How To Thicken Hair On Crown Of Head

Written by Daniel Sep 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling with thinning hair on the crown of your head? This can be a frustrating and confidence-damaging experience for both men and women. However, there are several ways to thicken your hair and restore your confidence. In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about how to thicken hair on crown of head.

Description

The crown of the head is the top of the head, where hair typically grows in a spiral pattern. Thinning hair on this area can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, aging, hormonal changes, and even stress. However, there are several ways to thicken hair on the crown of the head and restore your confidence.

How to Thicken Hair on Crown of Head

1. Use Hair Thickening Products

There are several hair thickening products available in the market that can help you thicken your hair on the crown of your head. These products contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and collagen that can help to strengthen and thicken your hair. Look for shampoos, conditioners, and hair masks that are specifically designed for thinning hair.

2. Massage Your Scalp

A scalp massage can improve blood flow and circulation to the hair follicles, which can help to stimulate hair growth. Use your fingertips to gently massage the crown of your head for a few minutes each day. This can also help to relieve stress and tension in the scalp, which can contribute to hair loss.

3. Take Supplements

Supplements like biotin, iron, and vitamin D can help to promote hair growth and thicken hair on the crown of the head. Talk to your doctor about which supplements may be right for you, and make sure to follow the recommended dosage.

4. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles like ponytails, braids, and cornrows can pull on the hair and cause damage and breakage. Opt for looser hairstyles that don’t put too much tension on the hair, particularly on the crown of the head.

Step by Step

Follow these steps to thicken hair on the crown of your head:

Step 1: Use Hair Thickening Products

Start by using hair thickening products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and collagen. Use a thickening shampoo, conditioner, and hair mask as part of your regular hair care routine.

Step 2: Massage Your Scalp

Use your fingertips to massage the crown of your head for a few minutes each day. This can help to improve blood flow and circulation to the hair follicles, which can stimulate hair growth and thicken your hair.

Step 3: Take Supplements

Talk to your doctor about which supplements may be right for you to promote hair growth and thicken hair on the crown of the head. Follow the recommended dosage for best results.

Step 4: Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on the hair and cause damage and breakage. Opt for looser hairstyles that don’t put too much tension on the hair, particularly on the crown of the head.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for thickening hair on the crown of the head:

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair health.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and excessive heat styling that can damage the hair.
  • Consider hair transplant or other hair restoration treatments if your thinning hair is severe.

FAQ

What causes thinning hair on the crown of the head?

Thinning hair on the crown of the head can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, aging, hormonal changes, and stress.

Can hair thickening products really thicken hair?

Yes, hair thickening products can help to strengthen and thicken hair on the crown of the head. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and collagen.

How often should I massage my scalp?

You can massage the crown of your head for a few minutes each day to improve blood flow and circulation to the hair follicles.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential to promoting hair growth and thickening hair on the crown of the head. Use gentle hair care products that are designed for thinning hair, and avoid harsh chemicals and excessive heat styling. Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair health, and consider hair restoration treatments if your thinning hair is severe.

Conclusion

Thinning hair on the crown of the head can be a frustrating experience, but there are several ways to thicken your hair and restore your confidence. Use hair thickening products, massage your scalp, take supplements, and avoid tight hairstyles to promote hair growth and thicken hair on the crown of the head. Remember to take care of your hair with gentle hair care products and a healthy diet, and consider hair restoration treatments if necessary.
