How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers Short Back And Sides Youtube

Written by Petter Nov 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to cut men's hair with clippers short back and sides YouTube. If you're looking for a quick and easy way to get a fresh haircut in the comfort of your own home, using clippers is a great option. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know to give yourself or someone else a great looking haircut using clippers.

Description

The short back and sides haircut is a classic style that has been around for decades. This cut is characterized by short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on the top. It's a versatile style that can be modified to suit your individual preferences.

How to

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

The first step is to gather all the supplies you will need. You will need a pair of clippers, a comb, a mirror, and a towel. Make sure your clippers have a variety of guards to choose from so you can adjust the length of the hair.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard

Before you begin cutting, you need to choose the right guard for the length you want. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you find the perfect length.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start by cutting the sides and back of the head. Use the clippers to cut the hair in an upward motion, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Use the comb to guide the clippers and make sure the hair is cut evenly.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the sides and back, it's time to blend the hair. Use a shorter guard and gently blend the hair between the sides and top of the head. Make sure the transition is smooth and even.

Step 5: Cut the Top

After you've cut the sides and back, it's time to move on to the top of the head. Use the longer guard to cut the hair on the top, following the natural direction of the hair. Use the comb to guide the clippers and make sure the hair is cut evenly.

Step 6: Blend the Top

After you've cut the top, it's time to blend the hair. Use a shorter guard and gently blend the hair between the top and sides of the head. Make sure the transition is smooth and even.

Step 7: Clean Up

Once you've finished cutting, it's time to clean up. Use the towel to wipe away any loose hair and use the clippers to clean up the edges around the ears and neck.

Tips

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you find the perfect length.
  • Use the comb to guide the clippers and make sure the hair is cut evenly.
  • Blend the hair between the sides and top of the head and between the top and sides of the head for a smooth transition.
  • Clean up any loose hair and use the clippers to clean up the edges around the ears and neck.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair using clippers. Just make sure you have the right supplies and take your time.

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your individual hair growth and style. Generally, getting a haircut every 4-6 weeks is recommended to keep your hair looking its best.

What if I make a mistake?

Don't worry if you make a mistake, hair grows back. If you're not happy with the results, you can always go to a professional stylist to fix it.

Hair Care

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools. Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking its best.

We hope this guide on how to cut men's hair with clippers short back and sides YouTube has been helpful. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully to achieve the best results.
