Best Haircuts For Long Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 06, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have long, thick hair, you know that styling it can be a challenge. While it may seem like your hair has a life of its own, the right haircut can make all the difference. In this guide, we’ll explore the best haircuts for long thick hair, how to maintain them, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Long Thick Hair

When choosing a haircut for long thick hair, it’s important to consider your face shape and hair texture. A layered haircut is a great option for thick hair, as it helps to lighten the weight and add movement. If you have a round face, a long bob or shoulder-length cut can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a soft, textured cut with layers can help soften your features.

One of the most popular haircuts for long thick hair is the long layers. This style works well for all face shapes and hair types, as it helps to add dimension and movement while maintaining length. The layers can be cut at different lengths, depending on your preference, and can be styled curly or straight.

Step-by-Step Guide for Maintaining Long Thick Hair

Once you’ve found the perfect haircut, it’s important to maintain it with regular trims and proper hair care. Here’s a step-by-step guide to maintaining long thick hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently, avoiding rough towel-drying, which can cause breakage.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or detangler to help smooth out any tangles and protect your hair from heat damage.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. Avoid using high heat, as this can damage your hair.
  5. Style your hair using a curling iron or straightener, depending on your preference.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray or styling product to help hold your style in place.

Related Keywords: hair care, regular trims, sulfate-free shampoo, leave-in conditioner, detangler, blow dry, round brush, heat damage, curling iron, straightener, hairspray, styling product

Tips for Styling Long Thick Hair

Styling long thick hair can be a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a gorgeous look. Here are some tips for styling long thick hair:

  • Invest in a high-quality blow dryer and round brush for added volume and control.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to avoid damage.
  • Try using a hair oil or serum to smooth out any frizz and add shine.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids, buns, and half-up styles.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension and break up the thickness of your hair.

Related Keywords: blow dryer, round brush, heat protectant spray, hair oil, serum, frizz, shine, braids, buns, half-up styles, highlights, lowlights

FAQ about Haircuts for Long Thick Hair

Q: Will cutting my hair shorter help with the thickness?

A: While cutting your hair shorter can help reduce the weight and thickness, it’s important to choose a length that works well with your face shape and hair texture. A layered cut is a great option for maintaining length while adding movement.

Q: How often should I get a trim?

A: It’s recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the health and shape of your hair.

Q: Can I still wear my hair curly with a layered cut?

A: Yes! A layered cut can actually help enhance your natural curls by reducing the weight and adding volume.

Related Keywords: haircuts, long thick hair, weight, thickness, layered cut, trim, health, shape, curly

Hair Care Tips for Long Thick Hair

Maintaining long thick hair requires a bit of extra care and attention. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals and dyes, which can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Related Keywords: hair care, hot tools, damage, deep conditioning treatment, nourish, strengthen, harsh chemicals, dyes, sun, chlorine, hat, leave-in conditioner, balanced diet, healthy hair growth

With these tips and tricks, you can find the best haircut for your long thick hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you!
