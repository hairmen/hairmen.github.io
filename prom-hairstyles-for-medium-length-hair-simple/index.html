How to Choose the Right Prom Hairstyle for Medium Length Hair

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, it's important to consider your personal style and the look you want to achieve. Are you going for a classic, elegant look or something more modern and edgy? Do you want your hair up or down? These are all things to consider when choosing your prom hairstyle.

Another factor to consider is your dress. If you have a high neckline or intricate back detailing, an updo may be the best option to showcase your dress. If your dress is more simple, a half-up, half-down style may be the perfect complement.

Step-by-Step Guide to Simple Prom Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair

Now that you have an idea of what you want, letâs explore some simple prom hairstyles for medium length hair. These styles can be done at home with minimal products and tools.

The Low Bun:

  1. Start by parting your hair in the middle and brushing it out.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it into a low bun.
  3. Secure with bobby pins and spritz with hairspray to hold in place.

The Half-Up, Half-Down:

  1. Start by curling your hair with a curling iron or wand.
  2. Take the top half of your hair and pull it back, securing with a clear elastic band.
  3. Optional: tease the crown of your head for added volume.

The Messy Bun:

  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure with bobby pins and pull out a few strands for a messy, effortless look.

Related Keywords: low bun, half-up, half-down, messy bun, curling iron, wand, clear elastic band, volume, ponytail, effortless

Tips for Maintaining Your Prom Hairstyle

Once you have your perfect prom hairstyle, you want it to last all night long. Here are some tips for maintaining your style:

  • Use a strong hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.
  • Bring extra bobby pins and hair ties in case of emergency.
  • Try not to touch or play with your hair too much throughout the night.
