What Hair Colour Would Suit Me Best Quiz

Written by Bardi Sep 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hair colour and looking to switch things up? Have you ever wondered what hair colour would suit you best? If so, you’re in luck! We’ve created a quiz to help you determine the perfect hair colour for you. In this article, we’ll guide you through the quiz, step by step, and provide tips on how to care for your new hair colour.

Description

The “What Hair Colour Would Suit Me Best Quiz” is a fun and easy way to determine the perfect hair colour for your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style. The quiz will ask you a series of questions and provide a hair colour recommendation based on your answers. It’s important to keep in mind that the quiz is just a guide, and you should always consult with a professional stylist before making any drastic hair colour changes.

How To

Take the quiz below to determine what hair colour would suit you best:

  1. What is your skin tone?
    • Fair
    • Medium
    • Olive
    • Dark
  2. What is your eye colour?
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Brown
    • Hazel
  3. What is your current hair colour?
    • Blonde
    • Brunette
    • Red
    • Black
  4. What is your personal style?
    • Classic
    • Bohemian
    • Edgy
    • Preppy

Once you’ve answered all of the questions, the quiz will provide a hair colour recommendation based on your answers. If you’re unsure about the recommended hair colour, consult with a professional stylist to determine if it’s the right choice for you.

Step By Step

Follow these steps to take the “What Hair Colour Would Suit Me Best Quiz”:

  1. Read each question carefully and select the answer that best describes you.
  2. Be honest with your answers to ensure accurate results.
  3. Once you’ve answered all of the questions, review your recommendations.
  4. If you’re unsure about the recommended hair colour, consult with a professional stylist before making any drastic changes.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a new hair colour:

  • Consider your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style when choosing a hair colour.
  • Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best hair colour for you.
  • Use hair care products specifically designed for your new hair colour to keep it looking vibrant and healthy.
  • Be patient with the transition process if you’re making a drastic change. It may take several appointments to achieve your desired look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the “What Hair Colour Would Suit Me Best Quiz”:

Q: Is the quiz accurate?

A: The quiz is just a guide and should not be taken as professional advice. Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best hair colour for you.

Q: Can I take the quiz more than once?

A: Yes, you can take the quiz as many times as you’d like to compare results.

Q: Do I need to make an appointment with a professional stylist before taking the quiz?

A: No, you can take the quiz before consulting with a professional stylist. However, we recommend consulting with a stylist before making any drastic hair colour changes.

Hair Care

Once you’ve chosen your new hair colour, it’s important to take care of it to keep it looking vibrant and healthy. Here are some tips for maintaining your new hair colour:

  • Use hair care products specifically designed for your new hair colour.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent colour fading.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on high heat settings.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups and maintenance.

Now that you’ve taken the “What Hair Colour Would Suit Me Best Quiz” and have some tips for maintaining your new hair colour, it’s time to make an appointment with your stylist and get ready to rock your new look!
