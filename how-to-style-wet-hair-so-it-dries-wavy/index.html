How To Style Wet Hair So It Dries Wavy

Written by Daniel Jun 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Wavy hair is a popular hairstyle that many people want to achieve. The good news is that you can style your hair so it dries wavy. Whether you have naturally wavy hair or not, you can achieve this look by following these simple steps. In this article, we will show you how to style wet hair so it dries wavy. We will also provide some tips and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Wet Hair So It Dries Wavy
  • Step by Step

  • FAQ

How to Style Wet Hair So It Dries Wavy

If you want to style your wet hair so it dries wavy, you should follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step is to wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for wavy hair. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your wet hair. You can use a mousse, gel, or cream. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair using your hands. This will help to enhance your natural waves. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your waves.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Let your hair air dry. Avoid using a hair dryer as this can cause frizz. If you need to use a hair dryer, use a diffuser attachment and set it to low heat.

Step by Step

Here is a step by step guide on how to style wet hair so it dries wavy:

  1. Wash your hair
  2. Apply styling product to your wet hair
  3. Scrunch your hair using your hands or a diffuser attachment
  4. Let your hair air dry

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your wet hair so it dries wavy:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before washing it
  • Avoid using a hair dryer on high heat as this can cause frizz
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while sleeping
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry as this can cause frizz
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling wet hair so it dries wavy:

Can I style my hair so it dries wavy if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can. Follow the steps above and use a curling iron to create waves if necessary.

What kind of styling product should I use?

You can use a mousse, gel, or cream. Choose a product that is designed for wavy hair.

How long does it take for my hair to air dry?

This depends on the length and thickness of your hair. It can take anywhere from 30 minutes to a few hours.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and moisturized, make sure to:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for wavy hair
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Use a hair mask once a week

By following these tips and steps, you can style your wet hair so it dries wavy. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and avoiding heat damage.
