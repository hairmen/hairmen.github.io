How To Make Your Face Look Less Chubby Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Jun 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you feel self-conscious about your chubby cheeks? There are ways to make your face appear slimmer and more defined without resorting to drastic measures. In this post, we’ll share tips and tricks on how to make your face look less chubby. From facial exercises to makeup techniques, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  1. Facial Exercises
  2. Contouring Makeup
  3. Diet and Exercise
  4. Hydration
  5. Hair Care

Facial Exercises

Facial exercises are a great way to tone and strengthen the muscles in your face. Here are a few exercises you can try:

Cheek Puff

Take a deep breath and puff out your cheeks. Hold for a few seconds and then release the air. Repeat 10 times.

Jaw Release

Open your mouth wide and move your jaw up and down as if you’re chewing gum. Do this for about 30 seconds.

Smile and Pout

Smile as wide as you can and then pout your lips. Hold each position for 5 seconds and repeat 10 times.

Facial exercises can help to tone and tighten the muscles in your face, which can help to make your face look less chubby over time.

Contouring Makeup

Contouring makeup can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Here are a few tips:

Choose the Right Products

Use a contouring powder or cream that is one or two shades darker than your natural skin tone. Use a highlighter that is one or two shades lighter.

Apply in a “3” Shape

Apply the contouring powder or cream in a “3” shape along your hairline, under your cheekbones, and along your jawline. Apply the highlighter to the center of your forehead, under your eyes, and on your chin.

Blend, Blend, Blend

Blend the contouring and highlighter products well to create a natural-looking effect.

Diet and Exercise

A healthy diet and regular exercise can help to reduce overall body fat, which can help to make your face look less chubby. Here are a few tips:

Eat a Balanced Diet

Eat a diet that is rich in fruits, vegetables, lean protein, and healthy fats. Avoid processed foods, sugary drinks, and excessive amounts of alcohol.

Exercise Regularly

Get at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise each day, such as brisk walking, cycling, or swimming. Incorporate strength training exercises to build muscle and boost your metabolism.

Hydration

Drinking plenty of water can help to flush out excess sodium and reduce bloating, which can help to make your face look less chubby. Aim to drink at least 8 glasses of water each day.

Hair Care

Believe it or not, your hairstyle can also have an impact on the appearance of your face. Here are a few tips:

Try a Side Part

A side part can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Avoid center parts, which can make your face look wider.

Avoid Volume at the Sides

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as big curls or waves. Instead, opt for a sleek, straight style or a low bun or ponytail.

FAQ

Can chewing gum help to slim down my face?

Chewing gum can help to strengthen the muscles in your jaw and cheek area, but it won’t necessarily make your face look less chubby. Facial exercises and a healthy diet and exercise routine are more effective.

How long will it take to see results?

Results will vary depending on the individual, but you may start to see changes in your face within a few weeks to a few months of starting a facial exercise or diet and exercise routine.

Are there any risks to facial exercises?

Facial exercises are generally safe, but if you experience any pain or discomfort, stop the exercises and consult a healthcare professional.



By following these tips and tricks, you can make your face look less chubby and feel more confident in your appearance. Remember, it’s important to be patient and consistent in your efforts to see results. Good luck!
