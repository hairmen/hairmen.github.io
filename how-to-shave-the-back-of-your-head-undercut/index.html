How To Shave The Back Of Your Head Undercut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Oct 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to create an edgy undercut hairstyle, shaving the back of your head is a must. While it may seem daunting at first, it's actually quite simple with the right tools and technique. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps on how to shave the back of your head undercut with ease.

Description

An undercut is a popular hairstyle that involves shaving the hair on the sides and/or back of the head while leaving the top longer. It's a versatile look that can be customized to suit your personal style. Shaving the back of your head for an undercut can be a bit tricky, but with a few tips and tricks, you can achieve a clean and polished look.

How to Shave the Back of Your Head Undercut

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need clippers, a mirror, a comb, and a towel. If you're new to shaving your head, it's recommended to start with a higher guard and work your way down to a shorter one.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair. Use a comb to create a part from ear to ear, and clip the top section out of the way. This will give you a clear view of the back of your head.

Step 3: Trim the Hair

Using the clippers, trim the hair on the back of your head. Start with a higher guard and work your way down to a shorter one, depending on how short you want the hair to be. Use the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Clean Up the Edges

Once you've trimmed the hair, it's time to clean up the edges. Use the clippers without a guard to create a clean line around the undercut. Take your time and make small, precise movements.

Step 5: Check Your Work

After you've finished shaving the back of your head, use a mirror to check your work. Make sure the hair is even and the edges are clean.

Tips for Shaving the Back of Your Head Undercut

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect undercut:

  • Start with a higher guard and work your way down to a shorter one
  • Use a mirror to check your work as you go
  • Take your time and make small, precise movements
  • Don't rush the process - it's better to take your time and get it right the first time
  • Consider asking a friend or family member for help if you're having trouble reaching the back of your head

FAQs

Can I shave the back of my head by myself?

Yes, you can shave the back of your head by yourself. However, it may be helpful to ask a friend or family member for help, especially if you're new to shaving your head.

What should I do if I make a mistake while shaving?

If you make a mistake while shaving, don't panic. Depending on the severity of the mistake, you may be able to fix it by trimming the hair around the area. If it's a more significant mistake, you may need to wait for the hair to grow back and start over.

Hair Care Tips for Undercut

To keep your undercut looking sharp, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to help you maintain your style:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type
  • Use a styling product to keep your hair in place
  • Visit a stylist regularly to maintain the shape of your undercut
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment to keep your hair healthy and hydrated

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to shave the back of your head undercut with ease. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll have a stylish and edgy look in no time.
