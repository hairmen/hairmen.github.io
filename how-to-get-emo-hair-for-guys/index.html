How To Get Emo Hair For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking to achieve that signature emo hairstyle? Whether you're a fan of My Chemical Romance or just want to switch up your look, achieving emo hair for guys is easier than you might think. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to get that iconic emo hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Step 1: Get the Right Cut
  2. Step 2: Choose Your Color
  3. Step 3: Style Your Hair
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Emo Hair
  5. FAQs About Emo Hair for Guys
  6. Hair Care Tips for Emo Hair

Step 1: Get the Right Cut

The first step to achieving emo hair is getting the right cut. Emo hairstyles typically feature long, choppy layers with side-swept bangs. You'll want to find a hairstylist who is familiar with emo haircuts to ensure that you get the right look.

When you're getting your haircut, make sure to communicate with your stylist about the length and layers you want. You can also bring in pictures of your favorite emo hairstyles to help guide your stylist.

Step 2: Choose Your Color

Emo hair is often dyed in bold and vibrant colors. Some popular colors include black, red, blue, and purple. You can also choose to dye your hair in a more subtle color, like a dark brown or blonde.

If you're unsure about which color to choose, consult with your stylist. They can help you choose a color that will complement your skin tone and enhance your features.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Styling emo hair is all about creating volume and texture. Start by blow-drying your hair with a round brush to create volume. Once your hair is dry, use a hair straightener or curling iron to create loose waves or curls.

Next, use a texturizing spray or pomade to add texture to your hair. Apply the product to the ends of your hair and use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Emo Hair

  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain your layers and prevent split ends.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and vibrant.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQs About Emo Hair for Guys

Q: How long does emo hair take to style?

A: It can take anywhere from 20 minutes to an hour depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Q: Can I achieve emo hair without dyeing my hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve the choppy, layered look without dyeing your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my emo hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too dry and damaged.

Hair Care Tips for Emo Hair

To keep your emo hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

By following these tips and steps, you can achieve the perfect emo hairstyle. Whether you're going for a bold color or a more subtle look, emo hair is all about expressing yourself and having fun with your style.
