How To Cut Men s Hair Back Of Neck With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts for the back of your neck? Why not learn how to do it yourself with a pair of scissors? In this step-by-step guide, we will show you how to cut men's hair back of neck with scissors in relaxed English language.

Description

The back of the neck is a tricky area to cut, and it can be difficult to get the right angle and shape. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking cut at home. This guide will provide you with all the information you need to know to cut your own hair at the back of your neck with scissors.

How To Cut Men's Hair Back of Neck with Scissors - Step-by-Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, you will need to gather your tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Make sure your scissors are sharp, as dull scissors can damage your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before you start cutting. This will make it easier to see the shape of your hair and ensure that you get an even cut.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Comb your hair straight back from your forehead. Make sure that it is evenly distributed and that there are no knots or tangles.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, section your hair at the back of your neck. Start by creating a horizontal parting just above your hairline, and then clip the hair above the parting out of the way.

Step 5: Start Cutting

Using your scissors, start cutting the hair below the parting. Take small sections of hair and cut them using a straight line. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as this can result in an uneven cut.

Step 6: Check Your Work

After you have finished cutting, check your work in the mirror. Make sure that the hair is even and that there are no uneven or jagged lines.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as this can result in an uneven cut.
  • Make sure your scissors are sharp, as dull scissors can damage your hair.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from different angles.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair back of neck with scissors:

  • Do I need to use clippers to cut the back of my neck?
  • No, you can use scissors to cut the back of your neck.
  • Can I cut my own hair at the back of my neck?
  • Yes, you can cut your own hair at the back of your neck, but it can be tricky.
  • How often should I cut the back of my neck?
  • This depends on how fast your hair grows. Generally, you should cut the back of your neck every 3-4 weeks.

Hair Care

After you have finished cutting, make sure to wash and condition your hair. This will help to keep it healthy and prevent split ends. You can also use a styling product to keep your hair in place.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair back of neck with scissors, you can save money and time by doing it yourself. With a little practice and patience, you can achieve a professional-looking cut at home.
