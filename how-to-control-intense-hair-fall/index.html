How To Control Intense Hair Fall Tips Tricks And Faqs

Written by Daniel Jun 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of seeing clumps of hair on your pillow or in the shower drain? Intense hair fall can be a frustrating and worrying experience, but it is not uncommon. There are many reasons why hair fall occurs, including stress, hormonal changes, genetics, and poor nutrition. However, the good news is that there are many ways to control intense hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. In this article, we will explore some effective tips, tricks, and frequently asked questions about how to control intense hair fall.

How to Control Intense Hair Fall

The first step in controlling intense hair fall is to identify the underlying cause. This may involve consulting with a dermatologist or trichologist to determine whether there are any underlying health issues, such as thyroid problems or nutritional deficiencies, that may be contributing to hair loss. Once the underlying cause has been identified, there are several steps that can be taken to control hair fall.

1. Maintain a Healthy Diet

One of the most important steps in controlling hair fall is to maintain a healthy diet. This means eating a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins, minerals, and protein. Foods that are particularly beneficial for hair health include leafy greens, nuts, eggs, and fish. It is also important to drink plenty of water to keep the body hydrated.

2. Manage Stress

Stress can be a major contributor to hair fall, so it is important to manage stress levels. This may involve practicing stress-reducing techniques such as meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises. It may also be helpful to engage in regular exercise, as this can help to reduce stress levels and promote overall health.

3. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, can damage hair and contribute to hair fall. It is best to avoid using these tools as much as possible, or to use them on a low heat setting. If using heat styling tools, it is important to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

4. Use Gentle Hair Products

Harsh hair products, such as those containing sulfates or alcohol, can strip hair of its natural oils and contribute to hair fall. It is important to use gentle, sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners that are designed for your hair type. It may also be helpful to use a hair mask or oil treatment to nourish and strengthen hair.

Step by Step Guide to Control Hair Fall

Here is a step-by-step guide to controlling hair fall:

Step 1: Identify the underlying cause

Determine whether there are any underlying health issues that may be contributing to hair fall.

Step 2: Maintain a healthy diet

Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins, minerals, and protein to support healthy hair growth.

Step 3: Manage stress

Practice stress-reducing techniques such as meditation or yoga to reduce stress levels.

Step 4: Avoid heat styling tools

Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, or use them on a low heat setting.

Step 5: Use gentle hair products

Use gentle, sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners that are designed for your hair type.

Effective Tips to Control Hair Fall

Here are some additional tips that can help to control hair fall:

1. Massage your scalp

Massaging your scalp can help to improve blood circulation and promote hair growth. Use your fingertips to massage your scalp in circular motions for a few minutes each day.

2. Use a silk pillowcase

Silk pillowcases can help to reduce hair breakage and prevent hair fall. This is because they are gentle on hair and do not cause friction like cotton pillowcases.

3. Take hair supplements

Supplements such as biotin, vitamin D, and iron can help to promote healthy hair growth. Consult with a healthcare professional before taking any supplements.

4. Trim your hair regularly

Regular trims can help to prevent split ends and breakage, which can contribute to hair fall.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much hair fall is normal?

It is normal to lose some hair every day, but on average, most people lose between 50 and 100 hairs per day.

2. Can hair fall be reversed?

In some cases, hair fall can be reversed with proper treatment. However, it depends on the underlying cause of the hair fall.

3. Can hair fall be genetic?

Yes, hair fall can be genetic. If hair fall runs in your family, you may be more likely to experience it.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. However, avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

2. Use a wide-toothed comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, as this is less damaging than using a brush.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

The sun can damage hair and contribute to hair fall. Wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

By following these tips and tricks, you can control intense hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. Remember to be patient, as it may take some time to see results. If you have concerns about hair fall, consult with a healthcare professional who can help you determine the underlying cause and recommend appropriate treatment.
