Face Frame Haircut With Bangs Diy A Simple Guide

Written by Petter Aug 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle to try out, the face frame haircut with bangs is a great option. This style is perfect for those who want to frame their face and add some dimension to their hair. The best part? You can do it yourself at home! In this article, we'll walk you through the steps of achieving the face frame haircut with bangs DIY style. Let's get started!

How to Achieve the Face Frame Haircut with Bangs DIY Style

Before we dive into the steps, let's talk about what you'll need. Here are the items you'll need to achieve this look:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Sectioning clips
  • Hair ties

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and allowing it to dry naturally or blow-dry it. Once your hair is dry, use a comb to part it in the middle. Then, use sectioning clips to separate the hair on the sides from the hair in the back.

Step 2: Cut the Bangs

Take the hair in the middle of your forehead and use a hair tie to hold it in place. Then, use scissors to cut the bangs to your desired length. Be sure to cut them at an angle for a more natural look.

Step 3: Cut the Face Frame Layers

Next, take a small section of hair from the front of one side and hold it between your fingers. Use scissors to cut the hair at an angle, starting from the chin level and working your way down to the length you desire. Repeat this step on the other side.

Step 4: Blend the Layers

Take a comb and blend the layers by gently combing the hair from the top to the bottom. This will create a more natural look and blend the layers together.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Finally, style your hair as you like. You can curl it, straighten it, or leave it as is. Use some hair spray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Achieving the Face Frame Haircut with Bangs DIY Style

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect face frame haircut with bangs DIY style:

  • Work slowly and carefully. It's better to take your time than rush and make a mistake.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Start with small sections of hair and gradually work your way up.
  • Ask a friend to help you if you're unsure or nervous about cutting your hair.

FAQs About the Face Frame Haircut with Bangs DIY Style

Here are some frequently asked questions about the face frame haircut with bangs DIY style:

Can I do this style on curly hair?

Yes, you can! Just be sure to cut the hair when it's dry and use a curling iron or diffuser to enhance the curls.

How often should I trim my bangs?

It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Can I use regular scissors?

No, it's best to use hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Face Frame Haircut with Bangs DIY Style

To keep your new style looking fresh, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from getting too oily.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your style.

With these tips and steps, you'll be able to achieve the face frame haircut with bangs DIY style in no time. Happy styling!
