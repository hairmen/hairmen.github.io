How To Cut Your Own Curly Curtain Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're thinking about getting curly curtain bangs but don't want to go to a salon, you're in luck. With a few tools and some patience, you can easily cut your own curly curtain bangs at home. Here's how:

Table of Contents

  1. Tools You'll Need
  2. Prepping Your Hair
  3. Cutting Your Bangs
  4. Styling Your Bangs
  5. Tips and Tricks

Tools You'll Need

Before you get started, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • A spray bottle filled with water
  • A mirror

Prepping Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it's completely dry before you start cutting. Use a comb to part your hair in the middle, where you want your curtain bangs to start. If you're not sure where to part your hair, try a few different places until you find the one you like.

Cutting Your Bangs

Once your hair is prepped and parted, it's time to start cutting your bangs. Follow these steps:

  1. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head, where you want your bangs to start.
  2. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, and pull it straight down.
  3. Using your scissors, cut the hair straight across. Start with a small amount of hair, and cut more if you need to.
  4. Repeat this process with the other side of your hair.
  5. Take a step back and look in the mirror to make sure your bangs are even. If they're not, make adjustments as needed.

Styling Your Bangs

Once your bangs are cut, it's time to style them. Here's how:

  1. Spray your bangs with water to dampen them.
  2. Use your fingers to scrunch your bangs upward, creating a slight curl.
  3. If you want more defined curls, use a curling iron or wand to curl them.
  4. Finish with a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few additional tips to help you achieve the perfect curly curtain bangs:

  • Start with a small amount of hair and cut more as needed. It's better to cut too little than too much.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting your own hair can be tricky, but it's worth it if you get the results you want.
  • If you're not sure about cutting your own bangs, ask a friend or family member to help you.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about cutting your own curly curtain bangs:

Can I cut my curly curtain bangs while they're wet?

It's best to cut your bangs when they're dry, as wet hair can be misleading and result in uneven cutting.

How often should I trim my curly curtain bangs?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how long you want your bangs to be. As a general rule, plan on trimming them every 4-6 weeks.

What if I mess up my bangs?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim your bangs a little more or style them differently to hide any mistakes.

Caring for Your Curly Curtain Bangs

To keep your curly curtain bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your bangs, as it can lead to damage and frizz.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.
  • Consider using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help define your curls.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut your own curly curtain bangs with confidence. Good luck!
