How To Dry Thick Curly Hair Fast Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Aug 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know how frustrating it can be to dry it. It takes forever, and it can often end up frizzy and unmanageable. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll show you how to dry your thick curly hair fast and with minimal damage.

How to Dry Thick Curly Hair Fast

Drying thick curly hair can be a time-consuming process, but there are a few things you can do to speed it up.

1. Use a Microfiber Towel

Microfiber towels are great for drying thick curly hair. They absorb moisture quickly and help reduce frizz. Instead of rubbing your hair with a regular towel, gently squeeze out the excess water with a microfiber towel.

2. Use a Diffuser

A diffuser is a great tool to use when drying thick curly hair. It helps distribute heat evenly and reduces the risk of heat damage. Attach the diffuser to your hair dryer and use it on a low heat setting.

3. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Leave-in conditioner is a great way to add moisture to your hair while it dries. Apply a small amount to your hair before drying, focusing on the ends. This will help prevent frizz and leave your hair feeling soft and manageable.

4. Don't Touch Your Hair

Once you've applied your leave-in conditioner and started drying your hair, try to avoid touching it as much as possible. Touching your hair while it's drying can cause frizz and disrupt your curl pattern.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know some tips and tricks, let's go through the step-by-step process of drying thick curly hair fast.

Step 1: Squeeze Out Excess Water

Use a microfiber towel to gently squeeze out the excess water from your hair.

Step 2: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Attach Diffuser

Attach the diffuser to your hair dryer and use it on a low heat setting.

Step 4: Dry Your Hair

Starting at the roots, use the diffuser to dry your hair in sections. Avoid touching your hair as much as possible.

Tips for Drying Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you dry your thick curly hair fast and with minimal damage.

Tip 1: Use a Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help reduce the risk of heat damage.

Tip 2: Use a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help reduce frizz and prevent breakage.

Tip 3: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair no more than twice a week.

FAQ

Q: How can I prevent frizz when drying my hair?

A: Use a microfiber towel to gently squeeze out the excess water, and apply a leave-in conditioner before drying. Use a diffuser on a low heat setting, and avoid touching your hair as much as possible.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: Try to wash your hair no more than twice a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential to maintaining its health and preventing damage. Here are some tips for caring for thick curly hair.

Tip 1: Use a Deep Conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to add moisture to your hair and prevent breakage.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can cause damage to your hair, so try to avoid it as much as possible.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair, so wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when spending time in the sun.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to dry your thick curly hair fast and with minimal damage. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
