Best Short Haircuts For Wavy Hair 2021 A Guide

Are you looking for the best short haircuts for wavy hair in 2021? If you have naturally wavy hair, then you know that it can be challenging to find the perfect haircut that complements your hair texture. But don't worry, we've got you covered!

Table of Content

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips

Description

Short haircuts are a great option for wavy hair because they are low maintenance and can make your waves more defined. The key to finding the best short haircut for your wavy hair is to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this guide, we will share the best short haircuts for wavy hair in 2021, along with tips on how to style and care for your new haircut.

How to

Before getting a short haircut, it's important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, opt for a short haircut with layers to add some volume and definition to your hair. If you have a square face, a side-swept fringe or asymmetrical bob can soften your features. If you have an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any short haircut!

Step by step

Once you have decided on the best short haircut for your wavy hair, it's time to style it. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair to keep your waves defined and frizz-free. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, scrunching your waves as you go. Finish with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray for added texture and volume.

Tips

  • Invest in a good quality diffuser attachment for your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves.
  • Avoid using a brush on your wavy hair as it can cause frizz and disrupt your natural wave pattern.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Q: Can I still get a short haircut if my wavy hair is thick?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure to ask your hairstylist to add layers to your haircut to avoid a bulky look.

Q: How often should I wash my wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week. If you exercise regularly, you may need to wash your hair more frequently.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy wavy hair requires a good hair care routine. Make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair to keep your waves defined and frizz-free. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently, and avoid using a brush as it can cause frizz and disrupt your natural wave pattern. Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends. And don't forget to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.

Now that you have a better understanding of the best short haircuts for wavy hair in 2021, you can confidently choose the perfect haircut for your hair type and personal style. Remember to follow our tips on how to style and care for your new haircut to keep your wavy hair looking healthy and beautiful!
