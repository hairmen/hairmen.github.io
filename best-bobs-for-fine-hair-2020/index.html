Best Bobs For Fine Hair 2020 A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut to try this year? Whether you're bored with your current look or just want to switch things up, a bob haircut might be the perfect choice. But if you have fine hair, finding the right bob can be tricky. That's why we've put together this guide to help you find the best bobs for fine hair in 2020.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When it comes to bobs for fine hair, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, you want a style that will add volume and texture to your hair without weighing it down. Second, you want a length that will flatter your face shape and complement your features. And third, you want a cut that is easy to maintain and style.

There are many different types of bobs to choose from, including blunt bobs, layered bobs, asymmetrical bobs, and more. The key is to find a style that works for your hair type and face shape.

How to

Before you head to the salon, it's important to do your research and find inspiration for your new haircut. Look online for pictures of bobs that you like, and consider bringing them with you to your appointment.

When you get to the salon, be sure to communicate clearly with your stylist about what you want. Let them know that you have fine hair and that you're looking for a bob that will add volume and texture.

Once you've agreed on a style, your stylist will begin cutting your hair. They may use a variety of techniques, such as razor cutting or point cutting, to create texture and movement in your hair.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.

3. Section your hair and begin cutting your bob. Depending on the style you've chosen, your stylist may use a razor or scissors to create texture.

4. Once your hair is cut, your stylist may add layers or additional texturizing to create more movement.

5. Style your hair using a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and hold.

Tips

- Choose a bob that flatters your face shape and complements your features.

- Opt for a style that adds volume and texture to your hair.

- Consider adding layers or texturizing to create movement in your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a bob if I have fine hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a bob if you have fine hair. Just be sure to choose a style that adds volume and texture to your hair.

Q: What length should I choose for my bob?

A: The length of your bob will depend on your face shape and personal style. Generally, bobs that hit just below the chin or at the collarbone are flattering for most face shapes.

Hair Care

To keep your bob looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and hold, and avoid heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect bob for your fine hair in 2020. So why not take the plunge and try out a new haircut this year?
