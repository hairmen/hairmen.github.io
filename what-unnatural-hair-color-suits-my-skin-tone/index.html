What Unnatural Hair Color Suits My Skin Tone

Written by Daniel Oct 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Looking for a change in your hair color? Unnatural hair colors are becoming more and more popular, and it’s no surprise. They’re fun, expressive, and a great way to show off your personality. But with so many colors to choose from, it can be tough to determine which one will suit your skin tone best. In this blog post, we’ll help you figure out what unnatural hair color will look best on you.

Description

Unnatural hair colors are any color that is not a natural hair color, such as blue, purple, pink, green, etc. These colors are often used to make a statement or show off your individuality. When choosing an unnatural hair color, it’s important to consider your skin tone to ensure that the color complements your complexion.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

Before choosing an unnatural hair color, you need to determine your skin tone. There are two main skin tones: warm and cool. Here’s how to figure out which one you have:

Step 1: Check Your Veins

Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone.

Step 2: Look at Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also give you a clue about your skin tone. If you have black, brown, or blonde hair with blue, grey, or green eyes, you most likely have a cool skin tone. If you have black, brown, or blonde hair with brown, hazel, or amber eyes, you most likely have a warm skin tone.

Step 3: Consider Your Sunburn and Tan

If you tend to burn easily and have a hard time getting a tan, you most likely have a cool skin tone. If you tan easily and rarely burn, you most likely have a warm skin tone.

Step by Step Guide: What Unnatural Hair Color Suits My Skin Tone?

Now that you’ve determined your skin tone, let’s take a look at which unnatural hair colors will look best on you:

Cool Skin Tones

If you have a cool skin tone, you’ll want to choose hair colors with cool undertones. Some great options include:

  • Blue
  • Purple
  • Silver
  • Green
  • Platinum blonde

These colors will complement your skin tone and make your eyes pop.

Warm Skin Tones

If you have a warm skin tone, you’ll want to choose hair colors with warm undertones. Some great options include:

  • Red
  • Orange
  • Pink
  • Yellow
  • Burgundy

These colors will complement your skin tone and give you a warm, vibrant look.

Tips for Maintaining Unnatural Hair Color

Unnatural hair colors require a bit of extra maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid hot water when washing your hair
  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Use a color-safe hair mask once a week
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater

By following these tips, you can help extend the life of your unnatural hair color.

FAQ

Can I dye my hair an unnatural color if I have a neutral skin tone?

Yes! If you have a neutral skin tone, you can rock any unnatural hair color you’d like.

Do I need to bleach my hair to dye it an unnatural color?

It depends on the color you want to achieve. If you’re going for a lighter color, like platinum blonde, you’ll need to bleach your hair first. If you’re going for a darker color, like burgundy, you may not need to bleach your hair.

How often do I need to touch up my unnatural hair color?

It depends on the color and how fast your hair grows. On average, you’ll need to touch up your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care for Unnatural Hair Color

Unnatural hair colors require a bit of extra care to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater
  • Use a color-depositing hair mask once a week

By taking care of your hair, you can keep your unnatural hair color looking vibrant and fresh.

Conclusion

Determining which unnatural hair color suits your skin tone can be a bit tricky, but it’s worth the effort. By choosing the right color, you can enhance your natural beauty and show off your personality. Whether you have a warm or cool skin tone, there’s an unnatural hair color out there that will look great on you. Just remember to take proper care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
