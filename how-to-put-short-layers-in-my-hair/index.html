How To Put Short Layers In My Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your same old hairstyle and want to switch it up? Adding short layers to your hair is a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. In this article, we will go through a step-by-step guide on how to put short layers in your hair. Read on to learn more!

How To:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to putting short layers in your hair is to wash your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type to ensure that your hair is clean and ready for the next steps.

Step 2: Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously as this can cause damage and frizz. Once your hair is damp, use a hair dryer to dry your hair completely.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips. Start by sectioning the top layer of your hair, then work your way down. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly and avoid any mistakes.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Now it's time to cut your hair. Start cutting your hair from the top layer down. Use scissors that are sharp and specifically designed for cutting hair. Cut your hair at an angle to create a layered effect. Remember to cut a small amount of hair at a time to avoid any mistakes.

Step 5: Blend Your Hair

To create a natural look, blend your hair by cutting the hair that is sticking out of the layers. This will create a seamless transition between layers and make your hair look more natural.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you are done cutting your hair, it's time to style your hair. Use a curling iron or a straightener to create the look that you desire. Apply some hair serum to add shine and hold to your hair.

Tips

  • Use sharp scissors that are designed for cutting hair.
  • Cut a small amount of hair at a time to avoid any mistakes.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet as this can cause your hair to look uneven when it dries.
  • Take your time and be patient.

FAQ

Q: Will Short Layers Work on Any Hair Type?

A: Yes, short layers can work on any hair type. However, it's important to consult with a hairstylist to determine the best type of layers that work for your hair type.

Q: How Often Should I Cut My Hair?

A: It's recommended that you cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after putting short layers in your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and avoid using too many styling products. Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.

Now that you know how to put short layers in your hair, it's time to switch up your hairstyle and try something new. Remember to take your time and be patient. If you are unsure about cutting your hair, it's always best to consult with a hairstylist.
