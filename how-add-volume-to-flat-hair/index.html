How To Add Volume To Flat Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with limp, lifeless hair? Do you want to add volume and bounce to your locks? If so, you're in luck! In this blog post, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks for adding volume to flat hair. Whether you have straight hair, curly hair, or anything in between, these tips will help you achieve the voluminous hair of your dreams.

How to Add Volume to Flat Hair

There are several ways to add volume to flat hair, including:

  1. Blow-drying your hair upside down
  2. Using volumizing products
  3. Teasing your hair
  4. Using hot rollers or a curling iron
  5. Getting a haircut with layers

Blow-drying Your Hair Upside Down

One of the easiest ways to add volume to flat hair is to blow-dry your hair upside down. This technique helps to lift the roots of your hair and create volume. To do this, simply flip your hair over and blow-dry it from the roots to the ends. You can also use a round brush to help create volume while blow-drying.

Using Volumizing Products

There are several volumizing products on the market that can help add volume to flat hair. Look for products like volumizing mousse, root lift spray, or texturizing powder. Apply these products to your roots and work your way down to the ends of your hair. Be careful not to use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Teasing Your Hair

Teasing your hair, also known as backcombing or ratting, is a great way to add volume to flat hair. To tease your hair, take small sections of hair and backcomb them with a comb. This will create volume and texture. Be careful not to tease your hair too much, as this can damage your hair and make it difficult to style.

Using Hot Rollers or a Curling Iron

Hot rollers and curling irons are great tools for adding volume and curl to flat hair. To use hot rollers, simply roll sections of your hair onto the rollers and let them cool. To use a curling iron, wrap sections of your hair around the barrel and hold for a few seconds. Both of these techniques will add volume and texture to your hair.

Getting a Haircut with Layers

If you have long, straight hair, getting a haircut with layers can help add volume and movement to your hair. Layers help to create texture and make your hair look fuller. Talk to your hairstylist about the best layers for your hair type and face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to adding volume to flat hair:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair
  2. Apply a volumizing product to your roots
  3. Blow-dry your hair upside down, using a round brush to create volume
  4. Tease your hair at the crown for extra volume
  5. Use hot rollers or a curling iron to add curls and texture
  6. Finish with a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold your style in place

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for adding volume to flat hair:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup and keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a bun or braid to create volume and texture
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, as this will cause less breakage and damage

FAQs

Q: Can I add volume to my hair without using heat?

A: Yes! You can add volume to your hair by blow-drying it upside down or using volumizing products like mousse or root lift spray.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Some people can go several days without washing their hair, while others need to wash their hair every day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Hair Care for Volume

To maintain volume in your hair, it's important to take care of your hair and scalp. Here are some hair care tips for volume:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth

By following these tips and tricks, you can add volume and bounce to your flat hair. Experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for you. With a little bit of effort, you can have the voluminous hair of your dreams!
