What Color Goes Best With Olive Skin Tone Tips And Ideas

Written by Bardi Jul 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you an olive-skinned beauty wondering what color goes best with your skin tone? Look no further! In this article, we will explore some of the best colors that complement olive skin tones, including clothing, makeup, and hair colors. Read on for some tips, ideas, and frequently asked questions about what color goes best with olive skin tone.

How to Choose Colors for Olive Skin Tone

When choosing colors for olive skin tone, it's important to consider your skin's undertones. Olive skin has yellow and green undertones, which means that warm colors tend to look best. Cooler colors, like blues and purples, can make olive skin look sallow or washed out. That being said, there are still a wide range of colors that can complement olive skin beautifully.

Clothing Colors for Olive Skin Tone

If you have olive skin, consider incorporating warm, earthy tones into your wardrobe. Think rich browns, deep greens, and warm oranges. Jewel tones like emerald, ruby, and sapphire also look great on olive skin. When it comes to neutrals, stick to warm shades like beige, camel, and ivory. Avoid cool-toned colors like gray and silver.

Makeup Colors for Olive Skin Tone

For makeup, experiment with warm-toned eyeshadows, like gold, bronze, and copper. These shades will make your eyes pop and complement your skin's undertones. For blush, opt for warm peach or coral shades, rather than cool pink tones. When it comes to lipstick, try warm reds, oranges, and browns, rather than cool pinks or purples.

Hair Colors for Olive Skin Tone

If you're looking to switch up your hair color, warm shades like honey blonde, caramel, and chestnut brown will complement your skin tone beautifully. Avoid cool-toned shades like platinum blonde or ash brown, which can make your skin look dull. If you're feeling bold, try a rich, warm red or auburn shade.

Step-by-Step Guide: Choosing Colors for Olive Skin Tone

Still not sure what color goes best with olive skin tone? Follow these steps to find your perfect palette:

  1. Determine your skin's undertones. Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist – if they appear greenish, you have warm undertones.
  2. Choose warm, earthy tones for clothing. Think browns, greens, oranges, and jewel tones.
  3. Stick to warm neutrals like beige, camel, and ivory.
  4. Experiment with warm-toned eyeshadows, blushes, and lipsticks.
  5. For hair color, opt for warm shades like honey blonde and chestnut brown.
  6. Avoid cool-toned colors like gray, silver, and cool pinks and purples.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair on Olive Skin Tone

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any skin tone, but it's especially important for olive skin. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQ: What Color Goes Best with Olive Skin Tone

What colors should I avoid if I have olive skin?

Avoid cool-toned colors like gray, silver, and cool pinks and purples. These shades can make your skin look sallow or washed out.

What makeup colors look best on olive skin?

Warm-toned eyeshadows like gold, bronze, and copper look great on olive skin. For blush, opt for warm peach or coral shades. For lipstick, try warm reds, oranges, and browns.

Can I wear black with olive skin?

Yes, black can look great on olive skin, especially when paired with warm-toned accessories or makeup. Just make sure to balance it out with warm, earthy tones.

In conclusion, there are plenty of colors that complement olive skin, from warm earthy tones to jewel tones and warm reds. By choosing colors that complement your skin's undertones and taking care of your hair, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in any outfit or makeup look.
