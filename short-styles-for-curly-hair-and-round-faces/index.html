Short Styles For Curly Hair And Round Faces

Written by Petter Sep 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair and a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. Luckily, there are plenty of short styles that can complement your natural curls and flatter your face shape. In this article, we will provide you with a guide on short styles for curly hair and round faces, including how to achieve the looks, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Short hairstyles for curly hair and round faces are designed to draw attention away from the roundness of the face and enhance the natural texture of the hair. These hairstyles typically feature layers and volume on top, with shorter lengths on the sides and back. The goal is to create a more elongated, oval-shaped face.

How To

Before you choose a short hairstyle for your curly hair and round face, it is essential to consider your hair type and texture. Some styles may work better on fine hair, while others may be better suited for thick or coarse hair. Consult with your stylist to determine which style will work best for your hair.

Considerations:

  • Face shape: Round faces typically have wide cheekbones and a round chin. Short hairstyles that add volume to the crown and elongate the face are ideal.
  • Hair texture: Curly hair can range from loose waves to tight coils. Short hairstyles that enhance the natural texture of the hair are best.
  • Hair length: Short hairstyles can vary in length, from a pixie cut to a bob. Choose a length that flatters your face shape and complements your hair texture.

Step By Step

Here are some popular short styles for curly hair and round faces:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well for curly hair and round faces. To achieve this look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or curl defining cream, to enhance your natural curls.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair, scrunching as you go to encourage curl formation.
  4. Use a curling iron or wand to define any areas that need more curl.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is another popular short style for curly hair and round faces. To achieve this look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or curl defining cream, to enhance your natural curls.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume at the crown.
  4. Use a curling iron or wand to define any areas that need more curl.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

  • Invest in a good quality curl defining product to enhance your natural curls.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair to avoid frizz.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Visit your stylist regularly to maintain your short hairstyle.

FAQ

Which short hairstyles are best for curly hair and round faces?

Pixie cuts and bob cuts are popular short hairstyles for curly hair and round faces.

How do I style my short curly hair?

Apply a curl defining product, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer, and use a curling iron or wand to define any areas that need more curl.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires extra care to maintain its natural texture and prevent frizz. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry to prevent frizz.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

With these short styles for curly hair and round faces, you can embrace your natural texture and flatter your face shape. Consult with your stylist to determine which style will work best for you, and remember to maintain a proper hair care routine to keep your curls looking their best.
