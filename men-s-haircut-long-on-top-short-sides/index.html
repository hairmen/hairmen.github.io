Men s Haircut Long On Top Short Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Sep 24, 2023 · 4 min read
If you're a man looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, then the men's haircut long on top short sides might be the perfect choice for you. This haircut has become increasingly popular in recent years, thanks to its versatility and low maintenance. In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, style it, and care for it.

Description

The men's haircut long on top short sides, also known as the "undercut," is a contemporary haircut that features longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. The hair on the top can be styled in various ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured, while the sides and back are typically buzzed or faded.

How to Get the Men's Haircut Long on Top Short Sides

Getting the men's haircut long on top short sides is pretty straightforward, but it's best to go to a professional barber or stylist to ensure the best results. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Choose the Length

Decide on the length of the hair on the top of your head. You can go for a longer or shorter look, depending on your personal preference.

Step 2: Trim the Sides and Back

Trim the hair on the sides and back of your head to the desired length. You can go for a buzz cut or a fade, depending on the look you're going for.

Step 3: Style the Top

Style the hair on the top of your head using a hair product that suits your hair type and the look you're going for. You can use wax, pomade, or gel to achieve different styles.

Tips for Styling the Men's Haircut Long on Top Short Sides

Here are some tips to help you style your men's haircut long on top short sides: - Use a comb or your fingers to style the hair on the top of your head. - Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and the look you're going for. - Don't be afraid to add texture to your hair by using a texturizing spray or powder. - If you have curly hair, embrace the curls and use a product that enhances them.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's haircut long on top short sides:

Q: Is the men's haircut long on top short sides suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, the men's haircut long on top short sides can be adapted to suit different face shapes, so it's a versatile haircut for any man.

Q: How often should I get a trim for the men's haircut long on top short sides?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 3-4 weeks to keep the haircut looking fresh and neat.

Q: Can I style the men's haircut long on top short sides for formal occasions?

A: Yes, the men's haircut long on top short sides can be styled for formal occasions by slicking back the hair on the top and using a stronger hold product.

Hair Care for the Men's Haircut Long on Top Short Sides

To keep your men's haircut long on top short sides looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips: - Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. - Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. - Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray. - Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. In conclusion, the men's haircut long on top short sides is a versatile and stylish haircut that's perfect for any man. With the right styling products and hair care routine, you can keep your haircut looking fresh and trendy. So, go ahead and try out this popular haircut for a new and exciting look.
