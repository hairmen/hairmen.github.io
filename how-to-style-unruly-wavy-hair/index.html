How to Style Unruly Wavy Hair - Tips, Steps & FAQ

Do you have unruly wavy hair that always seems to have a mind of its own? Don't worry, you're not alone! Many people with wavy hair struggle to style it and keep it looking good throughout the day. In this post, we'll share some tips, steps, and frequently asked questions about how to style unruly wavy hair.

Hair Care Tips for Unruly Wavy Hair

The first step to styling unruly wavy hair is to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help tame frizz and add shine.
  • Consider getting regular trims to get rid of split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

How to Style Unruly Wavy Hair

Now that you've got a good hair care routine down, it's time to learn how to style your unruly wavy hair. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Start with wet hair

Wavy hair is easiest to style when it's wet, so start by washing your hair and towel drying it until it's damp. You don't want it to be dripping wet, but you also don't want it to be too dry.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product to your hair to help define your waves and keep them in place. You can use a mousse, gel, or cream, depending on your hair type and the level of hold you want.

Step 3: Scrunch your hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to help define your waves even more. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help enhance your waves.

Step 4: Let your hair air dry

Let your hair air dry if possible, as this will help prevent frizz and keep your waves looking defined. If you're in a hurry, you can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting.

Step 5: Finish with a hairspray or oil

Finish off your style with a light hairspray or hair oil to help keep your waves in place and add shine.


Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to styling unruly wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Gently towel dry your hair until it's damp.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a mousse, gel, or cream. Work the product through your hair evenly.

Step 3: Scrunch your hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to help define your waves. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help enhance your waves.

Step 4: Air dry your hair

Let your hair air dry if possible. If you're in a hurry, you can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting.

Step 5: Finish with a hairspray or oil

Finish off your style with a light hairspray or hair oil to help keep your waves in place and add shine.


Tips for Styling Unruly Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling unruly wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this will help prevent breakage.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Consider getting a layered haircut to help enhance your waves.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.

FAQs About Unruly Wavy Hair

Q: Can I straighten my unruly wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your wavy hair if you want to. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my unruly wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Some people with wavy hair can go several days without washing, while others may need to wash their hair every other day.

Q: Can I use a hair mask on my unruly wavy hair?

A: Yes, using a hair mask once a week can help nourish and hydrate your hair, making it easier to style.

Styling unruly wavy hair can be a challenge, but with the right tips and products, you can achieve a beautiful, defined look. Remember to take care of your hair properly and experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
