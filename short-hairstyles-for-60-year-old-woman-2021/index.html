Short Hairstyles For 60 Year Old Woman 2021 A Guide To Looking Fabulous

Written by Christine Oct 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a 60-year-old woman looking to change up your hairstyle, you're in the right place. Short haircuts are not only trendy, but they're also practical and easy to maintain. Here's a guide to help you choose the perfect short haircut for you.

Description

Short hairstyles for 60-year-old women are all about embracing your natural beauty and highlighting your best features. These hairstyles are designed to make you look and feel confident, stylish, and young at heart. Whether you have fine or thick hair, there's a short haircut that will work for you.

How to Choose the Perfect Short Haircut

Choosing the right short haircut can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. Here are a few things to consider when choosing your new hairstyle:

Face Shape

Your face shape is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a new haircut. If you have a round face, you might want to go for a shorter haircut that adds height and volume to your hair. If you have a square face, a layered haircut will soften your features.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider. If you have fine hair, a short bob or pixie cut will add volume and texture to your hair. If you have thick hair, a layered haircut will prevent your hair from looking too bulky.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is also a factor to consider. If you have an active lifestyle and don't have much time to spend on your hair, a short haircut will save you time and energy.

Step by Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut

Getting a short haircut can be nerve-wracking, but it doesn't have to be. Here's a step by step guide to help you prepare for your haircut:

Step 1: Research Hairstyles

Before your appointment, research short hairstyles that you like. Save pictures to your phone or print them out to show your hairdresser.

Step 2: Consultation

During your consultation, talk to your hairdresser about the hairstyles you like and ask for their opinion. They'll be able to recommend a hairstyle that will suit your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

Step 3: Wash and Dry Hair

Before your haircut, wash and dry your hair. This will make it easier for your hairdresser to cut your hair.

Step 4: Haircut

During your haircut, sit back and relax. Your hairdresser will cut your hair to the length and style that you've agreed on.

Step 5: Styling

After your haircut, your hairdresser will style your hair. They'll show you how to style your hair at home and recommend products to use.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Short haircuts are easy to maintain, but there are a few things you can do to keep your haircut looking fresh:

Tip 1: Regular Trims

Regular trims will keep your haircut looking neat and tidy. Aim to have your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks.

Tip 2: Use the Right Products

Using the right products will help you style your hair and keep it looking healthy. Ask your hairdresser for product recommendations.

Tip 3: Embrace Your Natural Texture

Embrace your natural hair texture and work with it instead of against it. This will make styling your hair easier and more manageable.

FAQs

What Short Haircuts are Best for 60-Year-Old Women?

The best short haircuts for 60-year-old women are pixie cuts, bobs, and layered haircuts. These haircuts are easy to maintain and will make you look and feel younger.

Will a Short Haircut Suit My Face Shape?

Yes, a short haircut will suit any face shape. Talk to your hairdresser about the best haircut for your face shape.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are easy to maintain, but it's important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling your hair with heat tools.

Tip 3: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will prevent breakage and damage.

Short hairstyles for 60-year-old women are a great way to change up your look and embrace your natural beauty. With the right haircut and hair care routine, you'll look and feel fabulous.
