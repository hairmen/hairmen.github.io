Why Do I Look So Much Better With My Hair Up

Written by Frank Sep 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever noticed that you look more put-together and polished when you wear your hair up? You're not alone. Many people find that they look better with their hair up, but they aren't sure why. In this article, we'll explore the reasons why you might look better with your hair up and offer some tips for styling your hair in a way that flatters your face.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Style Your Hair Up
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Styling Your Hair Up
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips

Description

There are several reasons why you might look better with your hair up:

  • It accentuates your facial features: When your hair is up, your face is more visible. This draws attention to your facial features, which can make you look more attractive.
  • It creates a sense of symmetry: When your hair is down, it can be difficult to create a sense of symmetry in your overall look. By putting your hair up, you create a more balanced appearance.
  • It makes you look taller: When your hair is up, your neck appears longer, which can make you look taller and more slender.

How to Style Your Hair Up

There are many different ways to style your hair up, depending on the occasion and your personal preferences. Some popular options include:

  • Ponytail: A classic style that's perfect for any occasion.
  • Bun: A versatile style that can be dressed up or down.
  • Braids: A fun and playful style that's perfect for summer.
  • Half-up, half-down: A stylish option that works well for both casual and formal events.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to create a simple ponytail:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Adjust the position of the hair tie so that it's tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it's uncomfortable.
  4. Use your fingers to gently pull on the hair at the crown of your head to create some volume.
  5. For a sleeker look, use a comb to smooth down any flyaways.

Tips for Styling Your Hair Up

Here are some tips for styling your hair up:

  • Choose the right style: Different styles work better for different face shapes and hair types. Experiment to find the style that flatters your features the most.
  • Use the right tools: Depending on the style you choose, you may need a brush, comb, hair tie, bobby pins, or other tools. Make sure you have everything you need before you start styling.
  • Don't overdo it: While a sleek bun or ponytail can be elegant and sophisticated, an overly complicated updo can look messy and unflattering. Keep it simple and don't try to do too much.

FAQs

Q: Will wearing my hair up damage it?

A: It's possible that wearing your hair up too frequently could cause damage, especially if you use tight hair ties or bobby pins. To minimize the risk of damage, use gentle hair ties and take breaks from wearing your hair up.

Q: Can I wear my hair up if it's short?

A: Absolutely! Even if your hair is short, you can still create a cute and stylish updo. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly: How often you wash your hair depends on your hair type and personal preferences. Generally, it's best to wash your hair every other day or every three days.
  • Use a gentle shampoo: Look for a shampoo that's free of sulfates and other harsh chemicals.
  • Condition regularly: Use a conditioner after shampooing to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid heat styling: Excessive heat styling can damage your hair over time. Try to limit your use of hot tools like flat irons and curling irons.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: If you're going to be spending time outdoors, wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

By following these tips and styling your hair up in a way that flatters your face, you can look your best every day. So go ahead and experiment with different updos until you find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
