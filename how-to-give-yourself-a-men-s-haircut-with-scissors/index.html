How to Give Yourself a Men's Haircut with Scissors | Step by Step Guide

If you're looking to save money or just can't make it to a barber, giving yourself a men's haircut with scissors is a great option. It may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to give yourself a men's haircut with scissors.

How to

What You'll Need:

Before getting started, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Mirror
  • Hair ties or clips (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide:

Follow these steps to give yourself a men's haircut with scissors:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut and style.
  2. Decide on the length you want and use clippers to trim the sides and back, if necessary. If you prefer a longer cut, skip this step.
  3. Divide your hair into sections, using hair ties or clips to keep them separated.
  4. Starting with the back section, comb the hair straight down and trim the ends with scissors, cutting at an angle for a more natural look.
  5. Move to the sides and repeat the process, combing the hair straight down and trimming the ends at an angle.
  6. For the top section, comb the hair straight up and trim the ends to your desired length. You can also use scissors to add texture or layers.
  7. Blend the top section with the sides and back by combing the hair diagonally and trimming any uneven ends.
  8. Check your work in the mirror, making any necessary adjustments.
  9. Style your hair as desired using products like gel or pomade.
  10. Clean up any stray hairs with clippers or scissors.

Tips

Follow these tips for a successful DIY men's haircut with scissors:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to longer lengths.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from all angles.
  • Don't cut too much at once, as you can always go back and trim more if needed.
  • Use hair clips or ties to keep sections of hair separated.
  • Be patient and take your time.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about giving yourself a men's haircut with scissors:

Can I use regular scissors?

While regular scissors can work in a pinch, it's best to use hair-cutting scissors, which are sharper and designed specifically for cutting hair.

How often should I give myself a haircut?

This depends on your hair type and desired style, but most men get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Can I cut my own hair if it's curly?

Cutting curly hair can be more challenging, but it's still possible to give yourself a men's haircut with scissors. Just make sure to cut each curl individually and be extra careful not to cut too much at once.

Hair Care

After giving yourself a men's haircut with scissors, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain your new look. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like blow dryers, as they can damage your hair.
  • Visit a professional stylist every few months to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained.

With these tips and techniques, you can give yourself a men's haircut with scissors and achieve a stylish look without leaving your home. Just remember to be patient, take your time, and have fun experimenting with different styles.
