How To Curl Hair Away From Face On Right Side

Written by Petter Jul 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're tired of your usual hairstyle and want to mix things up, curling your hair is a great way to do it. However, it can be tricky to curl your hair away from your face on the right side. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you out.

Description

This tutorial is for those who want to curl their hair away from their face on the right side. We'll be using a curling iron to create loose curls that add volume and dimension to your hair.

How to

To curl your hair away from your face on the right side, follow these steps:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing and blow-drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the curling iron.

Step 2: Divide your hair

Divide your hair into sections, starting from the back of your head. Use hair clips to keep the sections separate.

Step 3: Curl your hair

Take a section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron away from your face. Hold for a few seconds and release. Repeat with the rest of your hair.

Step 4: Finish up

Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently separate the curls for a more natural look. Finish with a hairspray to keep the curls in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you curl your hair away from your face on the right side:

  • Use a curling iron with a barrel size that suits your hair length and desired curl size.
  • Start curling from the back of your head and work your way forward.
  • Don't hold the curling iron on your hair for too long to avoid damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before curling to protect your hair from heat damage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling hair away from the face on the right side:

Q: How do I prevent my curls from falling flat?

A: Use a hairspray or a texturizing spray to help hold your curls in place.

Q: Can I use a curling wand instead of a curling iron?

A: Yes, you can use a curling wand to achieve the same look.

Q: How often should I curl my hair?

A: It's best to limit heat styling to once or twice a week to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy, make sure to use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Avoid over-washing your hair and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these steps and tips, you can easily curl your hair away from your face on the right side for a fresh and stylish look.
