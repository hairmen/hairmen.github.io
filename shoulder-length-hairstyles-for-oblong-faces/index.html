Shoulder Length Hairstyles For Oblong Faces

Written by Aprilia Nov 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect shoulder-length hairstyle for your oblong face? Look no further! This article will provide you with all the information you need to find the perfect haircut for your face shape. From how to determine your face shape to tips on maintaining your new hairstyle, we’ve got you covered.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best shoulder-length hairstyles for oblong faces, it’s important to determine if you actually have an oblong face shape. An oblong face is longer than it is wide and typically has a straight forehead and jawline. To determine your face shape, measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If your forehead and jawline are about the same width and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, then you likely have an oblong face shape.

Shoulder Length Hairstyles for Oblong Faces

Now that you know you have an oblong face shape, it’s time to find the perfect shoulder-length hairstyle. The key to finding the perfect haircut is to add width to your face. This can be achieved through layers, curls, and waves. Here are some of the best shoulder-length hairstyles for oblong faces:

1. Layered Lob

A layered lob is a perfect shoulder-length hairstyle for oblong faces. The layers add volume to your hair, which adds width to your face. This style is also versatile and can be straight or curled.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for oblong faces. The bangs add width to your forehead, which balances out the length of your face. This style can be paired with a variety of shoulder-length haircuts.

3. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a great way to add width to your face. This style can be achieved with a curling iron or by braiding your hair overnight. The waves add volume and texture to your hair, which adds width to your face.

4. Shaggy Layers

Shaggy layers are a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. This style is perfect for oblong faces because the layers add width to your face. This style can be worn straight or curled.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hairstyle

Now that you’ve found the perfect shoulder-length hairstyle for your oblong face, it’s important to maintain it. Here are some tips to keep your new haircut looking great:

1. Use the Right Products

Using the right products is essential for maintaining your new hairstyle. Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture. This will help keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims are important for maintaining the shape of your haircut. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protecting your hair from heat damage is important for maintaining healthy hair. Use heat protectant products when styling your hair with heat tools and avoid using heat tools every day.

FAQ

What other hairstyles are good for oblong faces?

In addition to shoulder-length hairstyles, other hairstyles that are good for oblong faces include long bobs, side-parted hair, and asymmetrical haircuts.

Can I still wear my hair straight?

Yes, you can still wear your hair straight even if you have an oblong face. Just be sure to add volume and texture to your hair to add width to your face.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail. Just be sure to add volume and texture to your hair to add width to your face. You can also try wearing your ponytail lower on your head to create the illusion of width.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for any hairstyle. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Properly

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water and rinse your hair with cool water to help seal the hair cuticle.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and moisturize your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

In conclusion, finding the perfect shoulder-length hairstyle for your oblong face doesn’t have to be difficult. Use these tips and tricks to find the perfect haircut and maintain healthy hair.
