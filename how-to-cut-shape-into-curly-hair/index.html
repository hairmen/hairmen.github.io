How To Cut Shape Into Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 04, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to find a stylist who understands how to cut it properly. Curly hair requires a different approach than straight hair, and it's important to find a stylist who knows how to work with your hair type. In this post, we'll show you how to cut shape into curly hair, so you can get the perfect cut every time.

How to Cut Shape into Curly Hair

Cutting curly hair requires a different approach than cutting straight hair. The key is to cut the hair while it's dry, so you can see the natural curl pattern and ensure that the cut will work with the hair's natural texture. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Curly hair tends to shrink when it dries, so it's important to cut it at the length you want it to be when it's dry.

Step 2: Section the hair

Section the hair into small sections, starting at the back of the head and working your way forward. Use hair clips to keep the sections separate.

Step 3: Cut the hair

Starting with the back section, take a small section of hair and twist it tightly. Use your scissors to cut the ends of the hair, following the natural curl pattern. Repeat this process on each section of hair, working your way forward.

Step 4: Check the shape

Once you've cut all the sections, check the shape to make sure it's even. If you need to make any adjustments, take small sections of hair and trim as needed.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting shape into curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Section the hair into small sections
  3. Take a small section of hair and twist it tightly
  4. Use your scissors to cut the ends of the hair, following the natural curl pattern
  5. Repeat this process on each section of hair, working your way forward
  6. Check the shape to make sure it's even
  7. Take small sections of hair and trim as needed to adjust the shape
  8. Style the hair as desired

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting curly hair:

  • Always cut curly hair when it's dry, so you can see the natural curl pattern
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Start with small sections and work your way forward
  • Twist each section tightly before cutting to ensure an even cut
  • Check the shape as you go to ensure an even cut
  • Be patient and take your time

FAQs

Can you cut curly hair with regular scissors?

You can, but it's not recommended. Curly hair requires sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut and to prevent split ends.

How often should you cut curly hair?

It depends on the individual's hair growth rate and style preference. As a general rule, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep the hair healthy and maintain the shape.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

After cutting your curly hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid brushing curly hair, as it can cause frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle the hair
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define the curls and prevent frizz
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage the hair

With these tips and techniques, you can cut shape into curly hair like a pro. Remember to be patient and take your time, and you'll end up with a gorgeous cut that works with your natural texture.
