Best Hair Part For Round Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Jul 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles but nothing seems to flatter your round face? Well, worry no more! In this article, we’re going to discuss the best hair part for round face shape. Whether you have short or long hair, we’ve got you covered. Read on to know more.

Description

A round face is characterized by a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for a round face is to create an illusion of length and height. One of the ways to achieve this is by choosing the right hair part. A good hair part can help to elongate the face, making it appear slimmer and more oval-shaped.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hair part for round face shape, it’s important to know how to determine your face shape. Here’s how:

  1. Take a measuring tape and measure the widest part of your forehead.
  2. Measure the widest part of your cheekbones.
  3. Measure the width of your jawline.
  4. Measure the length of your face from the hairline to the chin.

Once you have these measurements, compare them to determine your face shape. If the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost the same and your face is round, then you have a round face shape.

Best Hair Part for Round Face Shape

The best hair part for round face shape is the side part. This is because a side part creates an asymmetrical look that helps to elongate the face. It also creates the illusion of height, making the face appear slimmer. Additionally, a side part helps to draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones, which are often the best features of a round face.

Step by Step Guide on How to Create a Side Part

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to create a side part:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Using a comb, create a straight part from the crown of your head to the nape of your neck.
  3. Take a section of hair from the larger side of the part and comb it over to the opposite side of your head.
  4. Secure the section of hair with bobby pins or hair clips.
  5. Repeat on the other side.
  6. Finish with hairspray to keep the part in place.

Voila! You now have a side part that flatters your round face!

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hair part for round face shape:

  • Avoid a middle part as it can make the face appear wider.
  • If you have bangs, make sure they’re swept to the side to create an asymmetrical look.
  • If you have short hair, consider adding volume on top to create the illusion of height.
  • If you have long hair, consider adding layers to create movement and dimension.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear a middle part if I have a round face?

A: While a middle part isn’t the best hair part for round face shape, you can still wear it if you love it. Just make sure to add some volume on top to create the illusion of height.

Q: Can I wear a side part if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear a side part if you have curly hair. Just make sure to use a wide-tooth comb to avoid disrupting your curls.

Hair Care

Aside from choosing the best hair part for round face shape, it’s also important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

By following these hair care tips, you’ll have healthy, shiny, and beautiful hair that complements your round face!

Conclusion

Choosing the best hair part for round face shape can make a huge difference in how you look and feel. By opting for a side part and following the tips and tricks we’ve shared, you’ll be able to create a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and enhances your best features. Remember to take care of your hair, too, so it stays healthy and beautiful. Happy hairstyling!
