Why Does The Hair On The Side Of My Head Stick Out

Written by Robby Sep 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you've ever wondered why the hair on the side of your head sticks out, you're not alone. This is a common problem that many people face, and it can be frustrating to deal with. In this article, we'll explore the reasons behind this issue and provide some tips and advice on how to tame those unruly strands.

Description

The hair on the side of your head can stick out for a number of reasons. It may be due to the texture of your hair, the way it's cut, or even the products you use. Whatever the cause, it's important to understand why this is happening so that you can take steps to fix the problem.

How to Fix It

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine the cause of the problem. Is it due to the texture of your hair, the way it's cut, or something else?

Step 2: If the problem is due to your hair's texture, you may need to use different products to help smooth and tame the hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type, such as anti-frizz serums or leave-in conditioners.

Step 3: If the problem is due to the way your hair is cut, talk to your stylist about making some changes. They may be able to adjust your haircut to help the hair lay flatter against your head.

Step 4: If the problem persists, consider trying different hairstyles that can help disguise the issue. For example, you could try parting your hair in a different direction or wearing it in a bun or ponytail.

Tips

- Use a wide-toothed comb or detangling brush to help spread your hair's natural oils and prevent frizz.

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straightening irons.

- Use a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent knots and tangles while you sleep.

FAQ

Q: Is it normal for the hair on the side of my head to stick out?

A: Yes, it's quite common for hair to stick out on the sides of the head. However, if it's causing you distress, there are steps you can take to fix the problem.

Q: Can using too much product cause my hair to stick out?

A: Yes, if you use too much product, it can weigh down your hair and cause it to stick out. Use only a small amount of product and focus on applying it to the ends of your hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair to help prevent issues like hair sticking out on the side of your head. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

- Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.

- Use a conditioner to help moisturize your hair and prevent breakage.

- Avoid using harsh chemicals or treatments on your hair, such as bleach or peroxide.

- Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and nutrients to help promote healthy hair growth.

Conclusion

While it can be frustrating to deal with hair that sticks out on the side of your head, there are steps you can take to fix the problem. By understanding the cause of the issue and taking good care of your hair, you can achieve smooth, sleek locks that look great from every angle.
