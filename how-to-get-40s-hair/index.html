How To Get 40S Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Nov 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to channel your inner vintage Hollywood starlet, then you're in the right place! In this article, we'll be exploring how to get 40s hair, from the iconic victory rolls to glamorous waves. Whether you're attending a costume party or just want to add some old-school charm to your everyday look, the 40s hairstyle is a timeless classic that never goes out of style.

How to Get 40s Hair: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

The first step in achieving 40s hair is to prep your hair. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your locks some oomph. Once you've towel-dried your hair, apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots to add extra lift.

Step 2: Create Victory Rolls

Victory rolls are one of the most iconic 40s hairstyles, and they're surprisingly easy to create! To start, take a section of hair from the crown of your head and roll it upwards towards your scalp. Pin it in place with bobby pins, and repeat on the other side. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to keep everything in place.

Step 3: Try Pin Curls

If you're not a fan of victory rolls, then pin curls are another great option for achieving 40s hair. To create pin curls, take a small section of hair and twist it tightly. Roll it upwards towards your scalp and secure it with a bobby pin. Repeat this process all over your head, and leave the curls in place for at least an hour. Once you remove the pins, you'll be left with beautiful, bouncy curls.

Step 4: Use Hot Rollers or Curling Iron

If you're short on time, then hot rollers or a curling iron are an easy way to achieve 40s hair. Simply wrap sections of hair around the rollers or curling iron, and hold for a few seconds before releasing. Once you've curled all of your hair, brush through it with a boar bristle brush to create soft, glamorous waves.

Tips for Achieving 40s Hair

Now that you know the basics of how to get 40s hair, here are a few tips to help you perfect your vintage look:

  • Invest in a good quality hairspray to keep your style in place all day long.
  • Use a boar bristle brush to create smooth, shiny waves.
  • Accessorize your hairstyle with a vintage headband or scarf for extra flair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques until you find the perfect look for you.

FAQ about 40s Hair

Q: Can 40s hair be achieved on short hair?

A: Absolutely! While some 40s hairstyles may be easier to achieve on longer hair, there are plenty of options for shorter hair as well. Try experimenting with pin curls or a sleek bob for a chic, vintage look.

Q: Do I need to use heat styling tools to achieve 40s hair?

A: No, you don't! While heat styling tools can be helpful for creating certain styles, like soft waves or curls, there are plenty of other ways to achieve 40s hair without them. Try pin curls or victory rolls for a heat-free option.

Hair Care for 40s Hair

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are a few tips for maintaining your 40s hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Invest in a high-quality conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect 40s hairstyle in no time. Whether you're channeling your favorite Hollywood starlet or just adding some vintage charm to your everyday look, 40s hair is a timeless classic that never goes out of style.
