What Is A Full Taper Haircut

Written by Bardi Oct 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a classic and timeless haircut, then a full taper haircut is the perfect choice. This haircut is a popular choice for men who want a clean and sharp look. It involves a gradual decrease in hair length from the top of the head to the nape of the neck. The term "taper" refers to the gradual reduction in hair length, resulting in a more defined and polished look.

Description

A full taper haircut typically involves cutting the hair shorter at the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top. This creates a contrast between the shorter and longer hair and gives the haircut its signature look. The hair is usually tapered with clippers or scissors, depending on the desired look. The length of the taper can vary, depending on the person's preferences and the stylist's expertise.

How to

If you're looking to get a full taper haircut, the first step is to find a skilled stylist who has experience with this type of cut. Once you've found a stylist, you can discuss your preferences and the type of look you're going for. The stylist will then use clippers or scissors to gradually reduce the length of your hair, creating a tapered effect. They may also use a comb and scissors to blend the hair for a more natural look.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Determine the length of hair you want to keep on top.

3. Use clippers or scissors to taper the hair at the sides and back of the head.

4. Use a comb and scissors to blend the hair for a more natural look.

5. Style the hair as desired.

Tips

- Bring a picture of the desired haircut to show your stylist.

- Maintain the haircut regularly to keep it looking sharp.

- Experiment with different styling products to find the perfect look.

FAQ

Q: How often should I get a full taper haircut?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how often you want to maintain the look. Generally, every 2-4 weeks is recommended.

Q: Can women get a full taper haircut?

A: Yes, women can also get a full taper haircut. It's a versatile and stylish haircut that can work for any gender.

Hair Care

To keep your full taper haircut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Regular washing and conditioning can help keep your hair healthy and prevent damage. Using styling products, like pomade or gel, can also help keep your hair in place and maintain the desired look. Additionally, trimming the hair regularly can prevent split ends and maintain the shape of the haircut.

Overall, a full taper haircut is a classic and timeless choice for anyone looking for a clean and polished look. With the right stylist and maintenance, this haircut can look sharp and stylish for any occasion.
