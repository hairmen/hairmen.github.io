How To Make Hair Curly Without Products A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Description

Have you ever wanted to add some curls to your hair, but didn't have any curling products on hand? Don't worry, you can still achieve beautiful curls without any products. In this article, we will show you how to make hair curly no products with a step-by-step guide.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Hair Curly Without Products
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Curly Hair
  • FAQs

How to Make Hair Curly Without Products

Making hair curly without any products is possible by using some simple techniques such as braiding or twisting your hair. These methods work best on damp hair, so make sure your hair is slightly wet before you begin.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with damp hair. If your hair is dry, dampen it with a spray bottle.
  2. Section your hair into several parts. The number of sections depends on the thickness of your hair. The thicker your hair, the more sections you'll need to create.
  3. Braid or twist each section of hair. The tighter the braid or twist, the tighter the curl will be.
  4. Once all of your hair is braided or twisted, let it dry completely. You can either air dry or use a blow dryer on low heat.
  5. Once your hair is dry, undo the braids or twists. Use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create a natural, voluminous look.
  6. You can also use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb through your curls, but be careful not to comb too much or your curls will lose their shape.

Tips for Curly Hair

  • Avoid using hot styling tools such as flat irons or curling irons as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-defining cream to your hair before braiding or twisting to help enhance your curls and keep them moisturized.
  • Sleep with a silk or satin pillowcase to help reduce frizz and keep your curls intact.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as it can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your curls.

FAQs

Can I make my hair curly without heat or products?

Yes, you can make your hair curly without heat or products by using techniques such as braiding or twisting your hair.

How long do I need to leave my hair braided or twisted to get curly hair?

You should leave your hair braided or twisted for at least 4-6 hours or overnight for best results.

What should I do if my hair is naturally straight?

If your hair is naturally straight and you want to add some curls, try using a salt spray or mousse to help enhance your curls.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and moisturized. Here are some tips for taking care of curly hair:
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help keep your curls moisturized and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner or hair mask once a week to help nourish your curls and keep them healthy.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol as they can dry out your hair and cause frizz.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your curls looking healthy.
Now that you know how to make hair curly no products, try out these techniques and enjoy your new curly hairdo! Remember to take good care of your curls to keep them looking healthy and beautiful.
