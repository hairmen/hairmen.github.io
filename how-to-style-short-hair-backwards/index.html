How To Style Short Hair Backwards A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Short hair can be stylish and versatile, but it can be challenging to find new ways to wear it. One trend that has been gaining popularity is styling short hair backwards. In this article, we will show you how to achieve this look with ease. Whether you're headed to work, a night out, or just want to switch up your everyday style, this guide will help you create a chic and effortless look.

Description

Styling short hair backwards involves brushing your hair towards the back of your head, creating a sleek and polished look. This style is perfect for those who want to showcase their face and neck, and it works well with a variety of hair lengths and textures. Whether you have curly, wavy, or straight hair, you can achieve this look with a few simple steps.

How to Style Short Hair Backwards

Follow these steps to create the perfect backward style:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

For best results, start with clean, dry hair. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you may want to straighten it first using a flat iron.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a pomade or hair gel. This will help to create a sleek and polished look. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Brush your hair backwards

Using a brush or comb, brush your hair backwards towards the back of your head. Use a hairdryer on low heat to help set the style in place.

Step 4: Add volume

If you want to add volume to your hair, use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots. This will create a fuller, more voluminous look.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finish your look with a light mist of hairspray to help hold your hair in place. You can also use a shine spray to add extra shine and polish to your style.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect backward style:

  • Use a quality styling product to help hold your hair in place
  • Brush your hair backwards in small sections to ensure a smooth and polished look
  • Use a hairdryer on low heat to help set your style in place
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape

FAQ

Can I style short hair backwards if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! You may want to straighten your hair first using a flat iron, but you can still achieve a sleek and polished look with curly hair.

What if I have really short hair?

No problem! You can still style your hair backwards even if it's very short. Follow the same steps, but use a smaller brush or comb to create a polished look.

How long will this style last?

This style should last all day if you use a quality styling product and finish with hairspray.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, be sure to use a quality shampoo and conditioner. If you have dry or damaged hair, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and be sure to protect it with a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.

Styling short hair backwards is a chic and stylish way to switch up your look. With these simple steps and tips, you can achieve a polished and effortless look in no time. Give it a try and see how this trend can work for you!
