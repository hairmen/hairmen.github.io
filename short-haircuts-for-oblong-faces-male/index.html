How to Choose the Right Haircut for Oblong Faces Male

When choosing a short haircut for oblong faces male, it's essential to consider the following factors:

  • Length: Short haircuts work best for oblong faces as they create width and volume. Avoid hairstyles that are too long as they can elongate your face further.
  • Texture: Choose a hairstyle that adds texture to your hair as it helps to create volume and fullness.
  • Sideburns: Short sideburns help to create a square shape, which is ideal for oblong faces. Avoid long, thin sideburns as they can elongate your face further.
