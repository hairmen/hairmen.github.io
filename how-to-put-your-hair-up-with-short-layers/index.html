How To Put Your Hair Up With Short Layers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 30, 2023 · 4 min read
If you have short layers in your hair, you may find it challenging to put your hair up in a style that looks good and stays in place. But don't worry, with a few tips and tricks, you can achieve a chic and effortless updo that's perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll show you how to put your hair up with short layers in a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Put Your Hair Up with Short Layers
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Layers
  • FAQs

How to Put Your Hair Up with Short Layers

Putting your hair up with short layers can be a bit tricky, but there are a few hairstyles that work well. Here are some of the best styles for short layers:
  • Messy bun
  • Half-up, half-down style
  • Ponytail
  • Braids
  • French twist

Messy Bun

The messy bun is a great style for short layers because it's effortless and looks chic. To create this style, follow these steps:
  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a high ponytail, leaving some short layers out around your face.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base, securing it with bobby pins.
  3. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face and at the nape of your neck to create a messy, undone look.
  4. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Half-Up, Half-Down Style

The half-up, half-down style is another great option for short layers. This style is perfect for those who want to show off their layers while still keeping their hair out of their face. Here's how to create this look:
  1. Start by taking a section of hair from the top of your head and pulling it back.
  2. Secure the section of hair with a clip or bobby pins.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the bottom of your hair and wrap it around the clip or bobby pins to hide them.
  4. Leave some short layers out around your face to frame it.
  5. You can curl the ends of the hair that's left down for a more polished look.
  6. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to putting your hair up with short layers:
  1. Start by deciding on the hairstyle you want to create.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  3. Pull your hair back into the desired style, leaving some short layers out around your face.
  4. Secure the style with bobby pins, clips, or an elastic band.
  5. Pull out a few pieces of hair to create a messy, undone look.
  6. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Short Layers

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling short layers:
  • Use bobby pins or clips to keep short layers in place.
  • Don't be afraid to leave some short layers out around your face to frame it.
  • Use hairspray to hold the style in place.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear my hair up if I have short layers?

A: Yes, there are many hairstyles that work well with short layers, including messy buns, half-up, half-down styles, and braids.

Q: Will my short layers fall out of an updo?

A: If you use bobby pins or clips to secure your short layers in place, they should stay put.

Hair Care for Short Layers

Short layers can be a fun and versatile hairstyle, but they require a bit of maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips for caring for short layers:
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your layers.
  • Experiment with different styling products, such as mousse, hairspray, and texturizing sprays.
Now that you know how to put your hair up with short layers, you can experiment with different styles and find what works best for you. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, there's a hairstyle that will suit your needs. With a little bit of practice and some helpful tips, you'll be able to create a chic and effortless updo in no time.
