How To Cut Men s Hair Short Back And Sides With Trimmer

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts? Do you want to learn how to cut men's hair short back and sides with trimmer? If yes, then you are in the right place. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut men's hair short back and sides with trimmer. We will also provide you with tips, FAQ, and hair care tips. So, let's get started.

Step by Step Guide

The following are the steps you need to follow to cut men's hair short back and sides with trimmer:

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Before you start cutting the hair, you need to prepare it. Wash the hair with shampoo and conditioner. Dry the hair with a towel. Comb the hair to remove tangles and knots. Apply hair oil or any other hair product that you prefer. This will make it easier to cut the hair.

Step 2: Choose the Right Trimmer

Choose a trimmer that has a blade guard. The blade guard will prevent the hair from being cut too short. Also, choose a trimmer that has different length settings. This will allow you to cut the hair to the desired length.

Step 3: Set the Length of the Trimmer

Set the trimmer to the desired length. Start with a longer length first. You can always go shorter if needed. Remember, it's easier to cut more hair than to add more hair.

Step 4: Start with the Back

Start cutting the hair at the back of the head. Hold the trimmer with the blade guard against the head. Move the trimmer in an upward motion. Start at the nape of the neck and work your way up to the crown of the head. Cut the hair evenly.

Step 5: Move to the Sides

Move to the sides of the head. Hold the trimmer with the blade guard against the head. Start at the bottom of the sideburns and work your way up to the temples. Cut the hair evenly.

Step 6: Blend the Sides and Back

Blend the sides and back of the head. Use the trimmer without the blade guard. Start at the bottom of the hairline and work your way up to the crown of the head. Use an upward motion. Blend the sides and back of the head evenly.

Step 7: Cut the Top of the Hair

Use scissors to cut the top of the hair. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it evenly. Cut the hair to the desired length.

Step 8: Clean Up the Hairline

Clean up the hairline using a razor or trimmer. Start at the back of the neck and work your way up to the sides and temples. Clean up the hairline evenly.

Step 9: Wash and Style the Hair

Wash the hair to remove any loose hair. Style the hair using hair gel, wax or any other hair product that you prefer.

Step 10: Clean the Trimmer

Clean the trimmer after use. Remove any hair from the blades. Use a brush to remove any hair that is stuck in the blades. Oil the blades to prevent rusting.

Tips

  • Use a comb to guide the trimmer.
  • Start with a longer length first.
  • Use scissors to blend the sides and top of the hair.
  • Clean the trimmer after use.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my own hair?

A: Yes, you can cut your own hair.

Q: What do I need to cut my own hair?

A: You need a trimmer, scissors, comb, and hair product.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on your hair growth. Generally, once a month is enough.

Hair Care Tips

  • Wash your hair regularly.
  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain healthy hair.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair short back and sides with trimmer, you can save money by cutting your own hair. Remember to follow the steps and use the right tools. Good luck!
