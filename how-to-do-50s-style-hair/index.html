How To Do 50S Style Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you feeling inspired by the classic styles of the 1950s? One of the most iconic looks of that era is the 50s style hair, which features big curls, volume, and a glamorous appearance. In this article, we will guide you through the steps on how to achieve this look and provide some tips and tricks to make it last all day.

How to:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and applying a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and hold. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and smoothness.

Step 2: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron or hot rollers, curl your hair in sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up. For an authentic 50s style, use larger rollers or a larger barrel curling iron for bigger curls.

Step 3: Brush Out Your Curls

Once your hair has cooled, gently brush out your curls to create soft waves. Use a paddle brush to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Using a teasing comb, gently tease the crown of your hair to create volume and height. This will give your style that classic 50s bouffant look.

Step 5: Pin and Spray

Using bobby pins, pin any loose sections of hair in place. Finish off with a strong hold hairspray to keep your style in place all day.

Tips:

- For a more polished look, use a hair scarf or headband to cover any exposed bobby pins or hair ties.

- Don't be afraid to play around with different curl sizes to create a unique look.

- To add extra volume, backcomb the underside of your hair before curling.

FAQ:

Q: How long will this style last?

A: With the right products and techniques, this style can last all day and into the night.

Q: Can I achieve this look with shorter hair?

A: Absolutely! Simply adjust the size of your curls and tease accordingly.

Hair Care:

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. Also, be sure to deep condition your hair regularly to prevent damage from styling.

With these steps and tips, you'll be able to achieve a beautiful and classic 50s style hair. Whether you're dressing up for a special occasion or just want to add a touch of vintage glamour to your everyday look, this style is sure to turn heads.
