What Is Mid Length Hair

Written by Aprilia Sep 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Welcome to our guide on mid length hair! If you're wondering what mid length hair is, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll discuss what mid length hair is, how to style it, and some tips on how to take care of it.

Description of Mid Length Hair

Mid length hair is exactly what it sounds like - hair that falls in between short and long lengths. It typically falls between the chin and the shoulders, with the exact length varying from person to person. This length is incredibly versatile and can be styled in many different ways, making it a popular choice for many people.

How to Style Mid Length Hair

There are countless ways to style mid length hair, but here are a few popular options:

  1. Beachy Waves: Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves. Finish with a texturizing spray for a beachy look.
  2. Sleek and Straight: Use a straightener to create a sleek, straight look. Finish with a shine serum for added polish.
  3. Half-Up Half-Down: Pull the top half of your hair up into a bun or ponytail, leaving the bottom half down.

These are just a few ideas - the possibilities are truly endless when it comes to mid length hair!

Step by Step Guide on How to Style Mid Length Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to create beachy waves:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into manageable parts.
  3. Using a curling iron or wand, wrap each section of hair around the barrel, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  4. Repeat until all of your hair is curled.
  5. Finish with a texturizing spray.

And here's how to create a sleek, straight look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into manageable parts.
  3. Using a straightener, run the iron over each section of hair from root to tip.
  4. Finish with a shine serum.

Tips for Taking Care of Mid Length Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your mid length hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about mid length hair:

Q: Is mid length hair suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes! Mid length hair can work for any hair type, whether you have thin, thick, curly, or straight hair.

Q: Can you wear mid length hair in an updo?

A: Absolutely! Mid length hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, including updos.

Q: How often should I wash mid length hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Generally, you should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care for Mid Length Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy mid length hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep hair soft and nourished.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and ideas, you're ready to rock your mid length hair!
