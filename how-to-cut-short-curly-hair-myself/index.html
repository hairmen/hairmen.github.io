How To Cut Short Curly Hair Myself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially if you have short curly hair. However, with the right tools and techniques, it is possible to achieve a salon-quality cut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut short curly hair yourself.

Table of Contents

  • Tools Needed
  • How to Cut Short Curly Hair Myself
  • Tips for Cutting Short Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting

Tools Needed

Before you start cutting your hair, you will need to gather the necessary tools. Here is a list of items that you will need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

It is important to invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. They will provide a clean cut and prevent split ends.

How to Cut Short Curly Hair Myself

Now that you have gathered your tools, it's time to start cutting your hair. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will make it easier to cut and will remove any dirt or product buildup.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use the comb to part your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Pin each section up with a hair clip.

Step 3: Cut the Back of Your Hair

Start with the back section of your hair. Take down one section at a time and use the comb to detangle the hair. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and cut the ends with the hair cutting scissors. Be sure to cut in a straight line and avoid cutting too much at once.

Step 4: Cut the Sides of Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting the back section, move on to the side sections. Take down one section at a time and repeat the same process as before. Be sure to cut in a straight line and avoid cutting too much at once.

Step 5: Cut the Front of Your Hair

The front section can be a bit trickier to cut. Start by parting your hair in the middle and cutting the ends in a straight line. Then, move on to the sides and cut them at an angle to frame your face.

Tips for Cutting Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your short curly hair:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. It's easier to cut more if needed than to fix a mistake.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while cutting.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as this can lead to uneven cuts.
  • Be patient and take your time. Rushing can lead to mistakes.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair myself?

Yes, you can cut your hair yourself. However, it's important to have the right tools and follow the proper techniques to avoid mistakes.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

Curly hair should be trimmed every 2-3 months to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry to prevent frizz.

Cutting your own short curly hair can be a great way to save money and achieve a look that you love. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-quality cut from the comfort of your own home. Just remember to take your time, start with small cuts, and follow our tips for best results.
