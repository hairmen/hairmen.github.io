Mens Haircut Fade Sides Medium Top A Guide For Relaxed And Trendy Look

Written by Eveline Jun 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish yet low-maintenance haircut, the mens haircut fade sides medium top might be perfect for you. This versatile cut combines a short fade on the sides with a medium-length top, creating a trendy and relaxed look that's easy to maintain. In this article, we'll provide a comprehensive guide to mens haircut fade sides medium top, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Description

The mens haircut fade sides medium top is a popular hairstyle that features a short fade on the sides and back, gradually blending into longer hair on the top. The top hair can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured, depending on your preference. This cut is suitable for all face shapes and hair types, making it a versatile choice for men of all ages.

How to Get Mens Haircut Fade Sides Medium Top

To get the mens haircut fade sides medium top, follow these steps:

  1. Decide on the length of the top hair you want.
  2. Show your hairstylist a picture of the desired style to ensure that you both share the same vision.
  3. Ask your hairstylist to use clippers to fade the sides and back of your head gradually.
  4. Choose the length of the fade based on your preference and face shape.
  5. Ask your hairstylist to blend the top hair with the fade using scissors or clippers.
  6. Style your hair as desired using a hair product.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting the mens haircut fade sides medium top:

Step 1: Choose the Length of the Top Hair

Start by deciding on the length of the top hair you want. You can choose a medium-length, textured cut, or a slicked-back style.

Step 2: Show Your Hairstylist a Picture

To ensure that you and your hairstylist share the same vision, show them a picture of the desired style.

Step 3: Clipper the Sides and Back

Ask your hairstylist to use clippers to fade the sides and back of your head gradually. Choose the length of the fade based on your preference and face shape.

Step 4: Blend the Top Hair with the Fade

Ask your hairstylist to blend the top hair with the fade using scissors or clippers. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired using a hair product, such as a wax or pomade. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Styling Mens Haircut Fade Sides Medium Top

Here are some tips for styling your mens haircut fade sides medium top:

  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type and preferred style.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to create texture and volume in the top hair.
  • Don't be afraid to try different styles, such as a messy or slicked-back look.
  • Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

FAQs

What face shapes suit mens haircut fade sides medium top?

The mens haircut fade sides medium top is suitable for all face shapes, but the length and style of the top hair may vary depending on your facial features.

Is mens haircut fade sides medium top easy to maintain?

Yes, this haircut is low maintenance and easy to style. However, you will need regular trims to keep the fade looking sharp.

What hair products should I use for mens haircut fade sides medium top?

You can use a variety of hair products, such as wax, pomade, or clay, depending on your hair type and preferred style.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Haircut Fade Sides Medium Top

To keep your mens haircut fade sides medium top looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as a hair dryer or straightener, too often as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and advice, you can rock the mens haircut fade sides medium top with confidence and style. It's a classic yet trendy look that will never go out of fashion.
