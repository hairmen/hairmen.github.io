How To Know If Short Hair Suits You

Written by Petter Oct 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hair has been in fashion for decades now, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. However, not everyone can pull off the short hair look. If you're thinking about getting a short haircut, but you're not sure if it will suit you, then keep reading to find out.

  • Considering Your Hair Texture

Determining Your Face Shape

Before you decide to get a short haircut, it's essential to determine your face shape. Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which hairstyle will suit you the most. The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

If you have an oval face shape, then congratulations! You can pull off almost any hairstyle, including short hair. If you have a round face shape, then short haircuts with layers would suit you the most. If you have a square face shape, then a short bob haircut would look great on you. For heart-shaped faces, short haircuts with side-swept bangs are ideal. If you have a diamond-shaped face, then short haircuts with volume on top would suit you the most.

Considering Your Hair Texture

Another factor to consider when deciding if short hair suits you is your hair texture. If you have thin hair, then a short haircut can make your hair look fuller and thicker. If you have thick hair, then a short haircut can make your hair more manageable. If you have curly hair, then a short haircut can accentuate your natural curls.

Thinking About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another essential factor to consider when deciding if short hair suits you. Short haircuts require more maintenance than long hair, so if you don't have the time or patience to style your hair every day, then short hair may not be the best option for you. On the other hand, if you're always on the go and need a low-maintenance hairstyle, then a short haircut may be perfect for you.

Being Confident

Confidence is key when it comes to pulling off any hairstyle. If you're confident in yourself and your appearance, then short hair will suit you regardless of your face shape or hair texture. However, if you're not confident in yourself and your appearance, then short hair may not be the best option for you.

Consulting with a Hairstylist

If you're still not sure if short hair suits you, then it's best to consult with a hairstylist. A hairstylist can take into account your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle and recommend a short haircut that will suit you the most. They can also give you tips on how to maintain your short hair and style it in different ways.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short hair may require more maintenance than long hair, but it's worth it if it suits you. Here are some tips for maintaining short hair:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Invest in good quality styling products to keep your hair in place.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Wash your hair less frequently to prevent it from drying out.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

Q: Will short hair make me look older?

A: Short hair can make you look more mature, but it won't necessarily make you look older. It all depends on the style you choose and how you wear it.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a short haircut. You may have to use more bobby pins and hair ties, but it's still possible.

Q: Will short hair suit me if I have a double chin?

A: Short hair can actually make a double chin less noticeable. Short haircuts that frame the face can draw attention away from the chin area.

Q: Can I still color my hair if it's short?

A: Yes, you can still color your hair if it's short. In fact, short hair can make hair color look more vibrant and noticeable.

In conclusion, there's no one-size-fits-all answer to whether short hair suits you or not. It all depends on your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, and confidence. If you're still unsure, then consult with a hairstylist who can recommend a short haircut that will suit you the most. Remember, confidence is key when it comes to pulling off any hairstyle, so wear your short hair with pride!
