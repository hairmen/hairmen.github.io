How To Get Wavy Curls With Thick Hair

Written by Eveline Sep 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your thick hair always falling flat? Do you want to add some volume and texture to your locks? Look no further than wavy curls! Achieving those perfect waves may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to get wavy curls with thick hair.

  • How to Get Wavy Curls with Thick Hair
  • Step by Step Guide

How to Get Wavy Curls with Thick Hair

Before we dive into the step by step guide, let's talk about the tools you'll need to achieve those perfect wavy curls. First, you'll need a curling iron with a barrel size between 1 and 1 1/2 inches. You'll also need a heat protectant spray, a hairbrush, and some hairspray for hold.

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the curling iron. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and ensure it's smooth and ready for styling.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections using hair clips. Starting at the back of your head, clip up the top layers of your hair and begin working on the bottom layers. This will make it easier to curl each section and ensure that all of your hair gets curled.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take one section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, holding it for 10-15 seconds before releasing. Repeat this process for each section, alternating the direction of the curls for a more natural look.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once all of your hair is curled, use a hairspray to hold your curls in place. Be sure to spray from a distance to avoid making your curls crunchy or stiff.

Tips for Achieving Perfect Wavy Curls

Now that you know the basic steps for curling your hair, here are some tips to help you achieve those perfect wavy curls:

  • Use a lower heat setting on your curling iron to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Try using a curling wand instead of a curling iron for a more natural look.
  • Use a sea salt spray for added texture and volume.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with different curling techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQs about Getting Wavy Curls with Thick Hair

Q: How often should I curl my hair?

A: Curling your hair every day can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit heat styling to a few times a week and use heat protectant sprays to minimize damage.

Q: What should I do if my curls fall flat?

A: If your curls lose their shape throughout the day, try using a texturizing spray to add volume and hold.

Q: Can I curl my hair without a curling iron?

A: Yes! You can also use braids, buns, or rollers to achieve wavy curls without heat styling.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Wavy Curls with Thick Hair

Once you've achieved those perfect wavy curls, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your curls from drying out.
  • Avoid brushing your curls too much, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help maintain your curls overnight.

With these tips and techniques, you're well on your way to achieving those perfect wavy curls with your thick hair. Just remember to take care of your hair and avoid over-styling to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
