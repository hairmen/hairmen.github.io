How To Keep Your Hair Out Of Your Face While Playing Sports

Written by Petter Jun 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Sports can be a thrilling and enjoyable activity, but it can be frustrating when your hair keeps getting in your face. Not only can this be distracting, but it can also hinder your performance. Fortunately, there are several ways to keep your hair out of your face while playing sports. Here are some tips and tricks to help you.

Step by Step

Ponytail

One of the easiest ways to keep your hair out of your face is to tie it up in a ponytail. Here's how:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the back of your head.
  3. Tie it up with an elastic band.

Braids

Braids are another great option to keep your hair secured while playing sports. Here's how to do it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the back of your head.
  3. Braid your hair, starting from the nape of your neck.
  4. Secure the end of the braid with an elastic band.

Headband

If you prefer to keep your hair down, a headband can help keep it out of your face. Here's how to wear one:

  1. Choose a headband that fits comfortably on your head.
  2. Place the headband on your head, just above your hairline.
  3. Tuck any loose hair behind the headband.

Hair Clips

Hair clips can also help keep your hair out of your face. Here's how to use them:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head.
  3. Clip it back with a hair clip.
  4. Repeat on the other side if necessary.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help keep your hair out of your face while playing sports:

  • Use a hair tie that won't slip or come loose during physical activity.
  • Apply a little hairspray or gel to keep your hair in place.
  • Wear a sweatband to absorb sweat and keep your hair in place.
  • Consider wearing a hat or visor to shield your face and keep your hair out of the way.

FAQ

Can I use hair products to keep my hair out of my face while playing sports?

Yes, you can use hairspray, gel, or other products to keep your hair in place. Just make sure to choose products that won't weigh your hair down or make it greasy.

What type of hair tie should I use?

Look for hair ties that are designed for sports and physical activity. They should be made with a non-slip material and have a strong hold to keep your hair in place.

What if I have short hair?

If you have short hair, you can use bobby pins or hair clips to keep it out of your face. You can also try a headband or sweatband to keep your hair back.

Hair Care

While it's important to keep your hair out of your face while playing sports, it's also important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove sweat and dirt.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair out of your face while playing sports and focus on enjoying the game. Remember to take care of your hair with regular washing and conditioning to keep it healthy and strong.
