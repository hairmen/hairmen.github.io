Short Spiky Haircut Boy A Guide To The Perfect Cut

Written by Frank Nov 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a cool and trendy hairstyle for your little boy? Look no further than the short spiky haircut. This style is perfect for boys who want to look stylish and edgy. In this guide, we'll show you how to get the perfect short spiky haircut for your boy.

Description

The short spiky haircut is a popular style among boys. It is characterized by short hair on the sides and back and longer hair on the top that is styled into spikes. This style is versatile and can be worn by boys of all ages. It is perfect for those who want to stand out and make a statement with their hair.

How to

Step 1: Choose the Right Cut

The first step in getting the perfect short spiky haircut for your boy is choosing the right cut. A skilled hairstylist will be able to recommend the best cut for your boy's face shape and hair texture. The most important thing is to make sure the cut is short on the sides and back and longer on the top.

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before getting started, make sure your boy's hair is clean and dry. Shampoo and condition the hair, and then towel dry it. Blow dry the hair on low heat until it is completely dry.

Step 3: Style the Hair

Once the hair is dry, it's time to style it into spikes. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to the hair, focusing on the top. Use your fingers or a comb to create spikes by pushing the hair upwards.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect short spiky haircut for your boy:

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your boy's face shape and hair texture.
  • Use a high-quality hair gel or pomade to create the spikes.
  • Don't use too much product, or the hair will look greasy and heavy.
  • Trim the hair regularly to keep the style looking fresh.

FAQ

How often should I get my boy's hair trimmed?

It's recommended to get your boy's hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking fresh.

What type of hair gel or pomade should I use?

Look for a high-quality, water-based hair gel or pomade that is easy to wash out. Avoid products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out the hair.

Hair Care

To keep your boy's hair looking healthy and shiny, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition the hair regularly
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat-styling tools
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle the hair
  • Protect the hair from the sun by wearing a hat

With these tips and tricks, your boy's short spiky haircut will look amazing and stylish.
