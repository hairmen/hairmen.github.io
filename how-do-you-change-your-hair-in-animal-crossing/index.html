How to Change Your Hair in Animal Crossing

Changing your hair in Animal Crossing is a simple process that can be done at any time. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Visit Harriet's Shampoodle

The first step in changing your hair is to visit Harriet's Shampoodle, which is located in the Able Sisters store. Harriet will be able to change your hair and offer you a variety of different hairstyles and colors to choose from.

Step 2: Sit in the Styling Chair

Once you arrive at Harriet's Shampoodle, you will need to sit in the styling chair. This will allow Harriet to access your hair and make any necessary changes.

Step 3: Choose Your New Style

After you've sat in the styling chair, Harriet will ask you what type of hairstyle you're interested in. You can choose from a variety of different styles, including short, long, curly, straight, and more.

Step 4: Choose Your New Color

In addition to choosing a new hairstyle, you can also change the color of your hair. Harriet will offer you a variety of different colors to choose from, including natural colors like brown and blonde, as well as more unique colors like pink and blue.

Step 5: Confirm Your New Look

Once you've made your selections, Harriet will show you a preview of your new hairstyle and color. If you're happy with the new look, you can confirm it and Harriet will make the changes.

Tips for Changing Your Hair in Animal Crossing

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when changing your hair in Animal Crossing:
