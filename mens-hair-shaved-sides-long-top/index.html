Mens Hair Shaved Sides Long Top A Trending Look For 2023

Written by Christine Jul 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a new, stylish look for your hair in 2023, then the mens hair shaved sides long top hairstyle is one that you should consider. This popular trend has been around for a few years now and it's definitely not going anywhere anytime soon. In this blog post, we'll take a look at how to achieve this look, some tips and tricks that you can use to make it your own, and answer some frequently asked questions about this hairstyle.

Description

The mens hair shaved sides long top hairstyle is exactly what it sounds like. The sides of your head are shaved or cut very short, while the top of your head has longer hair that can be styled in a variety of ways. This style is often seen as a more modern and edgy look, and it can be great for guys who want to show off their personality through their hair.

How to Achieve the Mens Hair Shaved Sides Long Top Look

If you're interested in trying out the mens hair shaved sides long top hairstyle, then the first step is to find a good barber or stylist who can help you achieve the look. Make sure to bring in a picture or example of what you're looking for so that they can give you the cut that you want.

Once you have the cut, you'll need to style your hair on top. There are many different ways to style your hair in this look, but some popular options include slicking it back, adding volume, or creating a messy, textured look. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're looking to achieve the mens hair shaved sides long top look at home, then here is a step-by-step guide that you can follow:

  1. Start by shaving or cutting the sides of your hair very short or completely off. Use clippers or scissors to achieve the desired length.
  2. Leave the hair on top of your head longer, at least a few inches, so that you can style it in various ways.
  3. Wash and condition your hair, and then towel dry it so that it's slightly damp.
  4. Apply your chosen styling product to your hair on top, using your fingers or a comb to create your desired style.
  5. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair, using your fingers or a brush to style it as you go.
  6. Finish off with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks that you can use to make the mens hair shaved sides long top hairstyle your own:

  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use a high-quality styling product that works well with your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to add some color or highlights to your hair for a more unique look.
  • Maintain your cut by regularly getting trims and touch-ups.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens hair shaved sides long top hairstyle:

Is this hairstyle difficult to maintain?

Not at all! The shaved sides will require some maintenance to keep them looking neat and tidy, but the longer hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways that are easy to maintain on a daily basis.

Can this hairstyle work for any hair type?

Yes, this hairstyle can work for any hair type, whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair.

Is this a good hairstyle for summer?

Definitely! The shaved sides will help keep you cool on hot summer days, while the longer hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways to suit your personal style.

Hair Care

When it comes to maintaining your mens hair shaved sides long top look, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes washing and conditioning regularly, using high-quality styling products, and getting regular trims and touch-ups to keep your cut looking fresh.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the mens hair shaved sides long top look and make it your own. Whether you're looking for a more modern and edgy look or just want to try something new, this hairstyle is definitely worth considering in 2023.
