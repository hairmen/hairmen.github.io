What Is It Called When You Can See Yourself In A Dream

Written by Eveline Jul 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Have you ever had a dream where you see yourself in it? It's a strange feeling, isn't it? But what is it called when you can see yourself in a dream? In this article, we'll explore the concept of seeing yourself in a dream and what it means.

What is it Called When You Can See Yourself in a Dream?

Seeing yourself in a dream is called a "Lucid Dream". It is a type of dream where you become aware that you are dreaming. In a lucid dream, you are able to control the dream and make decisions about what happens. This type of dream can be both exciting and scary, depending on the dreamer's experience.

How to Have a Lucid Dream

If you want to experience a lucid dream, there are certain steps you can take to increase your chances:

1. Keep a Dream Journal

Keeping a dream journal is an excellent way to increase your chances of having a lucid dream. Write down your dreams as soon as you wake up, and try to identify patterns or recurring themes. This will help you become more aware of your dreams and increase your chances of realizing that you are dreaming.

2. Reality Checks

Perform reality checks throughout the day. Look at your surroundings and ask yourself if you are dreaming. If you do this regularly, you will begin to do it in your dreams, and you will be more likely to realize that you are dreaming.

3. Visualization

Visualize yourself having a lucid dream. Imagine yourself becoming aware that you are dreaming and taking control of the dream. The more you visualize this scenario, the more likely it is to happen.

Step by Step Guide to Lucid Dreaming

Here is a step by step guide to help you achieve lucid dreaming:

1. Relax

Relax your body and mind before going to bed. Practice deep breathing or meditation to calm your mind.

2. Set Your Intention

Set your intention to have a lucid dream. Tell yourself that you will become aware that you are dreaming.

3. Reality Checks

Perform reality checks throughout the day. Look at your surroundings and ask yourself if you are dreaming.

4. Visualization

Visualize yourself having a lucid dream. Imagine yourself becoming aware that you are dreaming and taking control of the dream.

5. Wake Back to Bed

Wake up after a few hours of sleep and stay awake for about 30 minutes. This will increase your chances of having a lucid dream.

6. Go to Bed

Go back to bed and continue visualizing yourself having a lucid dream.

7. Keep Practicing

Lucid dreaming takes practice, so keep practicing the steps above until you are able to have a lucid dream.

Tips for Lucid Dreaming

Here are a few tips to help you achieve lucid dreaming:

1. Keep a Dream Journal

Write down your dreams as soon as you wake up. This will help you become more aware of your dreams and increase your chances of realizing that you are dreaming.

2. Reality Checks

Perform reality checks throughout the day. Look at your surroundings and ask yourself if you are dreaming.

3. Visualization

Visualize yourself having a lucid dream. Imagine yourself becoming aware that you are dreaming and taking control of the dream.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is lucid dreaming dangerous?

A: No, lucid dreaming is not dangerous. However, it is important to remember that you are still asleep and not to engage in any dangerous activities.

Q: Can anyone have a lucid dream?

A: Yes, anyone can have a lucid dream with practice.

Q: How long does a lucid dream last?

A: A lucid dream can last anywhere from a few minutes to over an hour.

In Conclusion

Lucid dreaming is an exciting and fascinating experience that anyone can have with practice. By keeping a dream journal, performing reality checks, and visualizing yourself having a lucid dream, you can increase your chances of experiencing this type of dream. Remember to stay safe and enjoy the experience!
