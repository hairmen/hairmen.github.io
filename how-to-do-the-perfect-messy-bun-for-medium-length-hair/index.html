How to Do the Perfect Messy Bun for Medium Length Hair

If you have medium length hair and are looking for a quick, easy, and stylish way to pull it back, look no further than the messy bun. This versatile hairstyle can be dressed up or down, and is perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll show you how to do the perfect messy bun for medium length hair.

How to:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

The first step to achieving the perfect messy bun is to start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, it will be harder to style and may not hold its shape as well.

Step 2: Brush your hair

Using a brush or wide-tooth comb, gently brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to style and give your bun a smoother appearance.

Step 3: Gather your hair into a high ponytail

Next, gather your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head. Use a hair tie to secure it in place.

Step 4: Twist and wrap your hair into a bun

Twist your ponytail and wrap it around the base of the hair tie, securing it with bobby pins as you go. Make sure to leave some hair out to create a messy, undone look.

Step 5: Pull out some pieces of hair to create a messy look

Once your bun is secured, pull out some pieces of hair to create a messy, undone look. Use your fingers to gently tease and pull at the hair until you achieve the desired look.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

Finally, finish your messy bun with a spritz of hairspray to help hold it in place.

Tips:

- For an even messier look, try using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo before styling your hair.

- If you have shorter layers around your face, try pulling them out to frame your face and create a more relaxed look.

FAQ:

Q: Can you do a messy bun with medium length hair?

A: Yes, absolutely! The messy bun is a great hairstyle for medium length hair.

Q: How long does it take to do a messy bun?

A: The messy bun is a quick and easy hairstyle that can be done in just a few minutes.

Q: Can I wear a messy bun to a formal event?

A: Yes, you can! The messy bun can be dressed up or down, making it a versatile hairstyle for any occasion.

Hair Care:

- To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to use a quality shampoo and conditioner.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair over time.

- Finally, make sure to get regular trims to keep your hair looking its best.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect messy bun for medium length hair in no time. Whether you're running errands or heading out for a night on the town, this versatile hairstyle is sure to become one of your go-to looks.
