Best Hairstyles For Oblong Shaped Faces

If you have an oblong shaped face, then you know how challenging it can be to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your features. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this blog post, we'll go over the best hairstyles for oblong shaped faces, as well as some tips on how to maintain your hair and keep it looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Oblong Shaped Faces

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for oblong shaped faces, it's essential to understand how to determine if you have an oblong shaped face. Here are some features to look out for:

  • Your face is longer than it is wide
  • You have a high forehead
  • Your chin is pointy

If you have these features, then you most likely have an oblong shaped face.

Best Hairstyles for Oblong Shaped Faces

Now that you know you have an oblong shaped face let's look at some hairstyles that will complement your features.

The Bob

The bob is a classic hairstyle that complements oblong shaped faces. It adds volume to the sides of your face and creates an illusion of width. You can choose to have a short bob or a longer one; it's up to you!

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a trendy and stylish hairstyle that works well with oblong shaped faces. It adds volume to the top of your head and makes your face look more proportionate. Plus, it's effortless to maintain!

The Long Layers

If you prefer to keep your hair long, then the long layers hairstyle is perfect for you. It adds volume to the lower half of your face and creates an illusion of width. You can choose to have side-swept bangs or a middle part; it's up to you!

The Side-Swept Bangs

The side-swept bangs hairstyle works well with oblong shaped faces. It adds volume to the sides of your face and creates an illusion of width. Plus, it's effortless to maintain!

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Now that you know the best hairstyles for oblong shaped faces let's look at some tips on how to maintain your hair.

Use a Heat Protectant

Using a heat protectant is essential, especially if you use hot tools to style your hair. It helps to prevent damage and breakage, keeping your hair healthy and shiny.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly helps to prevent split ends and breakage. It also helps to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every other day or every two days to keep it healthy and shiny.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear bangs if I have an oblong shaped face?

A: Yes, you can still wear bangs if you have an oblong shaped face. Side-swept bangs work well with this face shape and add volume to the sides of your face.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have an oblong shaped face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have an oblong shaped face. Just make sure to add volume to the sides of your face, so it doesn't look too long and narrow.

Hair Care Tips

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week helps to keep your hair healthy and shiny. It adds moisture to your hair, preventing it from becoming dry and brittle.

Avoid Using Too Much Product

Using too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Use only the necessary amount of product to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Protect Your Hair From the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray. The sun can damage your hair, making it dry and brittle.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for oblong shaped faces and some tips on how to maintain your hair, it's time to try out some new styles! Remember to take care of your hair and keep it healthy and shiny.
