How To Flatter A Square Face

Written by Daniel Aug 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we will discuss how to flatter a square face. If you have a square face shape, you may find it challenging to find a hairstyle that complements your features. However, with the right cut, color, and styling techniques, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance. Read on for our step-by-step guide on how to flatter a square face.

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, broad forehead, and angular features. To flatter a square face, you want to soften your features and create balance. The goal is to draw attention away from your angular jawline and add dimension to your face.

How To

Here are some steps you can take to flatter a square face:

1. Choose the Right Haircut

The right haircut can make all the difference in flattering a square face. You want to look for styles that soften your features and add volume and movement to your hair. Some great options include:

  • Long layers
  • Soft curls or waves
  • Side-swept bangs
  • A-line bob

Avoid blunt cuts or straight hairstyles that can emphasize the squareness of your face.

2. Add Dimension with Highlights

Adding highlights to your hair can create dimension and soften your features. Consider getting highlights that are a few shades lighter than your natural hair color. You can also try balayage or ombrÃ© techniques to add depth to your hair.

3. Experiment with Accessories

You can draw attention away from your jawline by wearing accessories that draw the eye upward. Try wearing statement earrings or a bold necklace to add interest to your upper half.

4. Contour Your Face

Contouring can help create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. Use a contour kit to define your cheekbones, jawline, and forehead. Blend the product well for a natural look.

5. Emphasize Your Eyes

By emphasizing your eyes, you can draw attention away from your jawline. Experiment with bold eyeshadow or eyeliner to make your eyes stand out.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to flatter a square face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can flatter your face shape, you need to determine what it is. Look in the mirror and examine the shape of your face. If you have a strong jawline, broad forehead, and angular features, you likely have a square face shape.

Step 2: Choose the Right Haircut

As mentioned earlier, the right haircut is crucial in flattering a square face. Look for styles that add volume and movement to your hair. Long layers, soft curls, and side-swept bangs are all great options.

Step 3: Add Dimension with Highlights

Highlights can create dimension and soften your features. Consider getting highlights that are a few shades lighter than your natural hair color. Balayage or ombrÃ© techniques can also add depth to your hair.

Step 4: Experiment with Accessories

Accessories can draw the eye upward and away from your jawline. Try wearing statement earrings or a bold necklace to add interest to your upper half.

Step 5: Contour Your Face

Contouring can create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. Use a contour kit to define your cheekbones, jawline, and forehead. Blend the product well for a natural look.

Step 6: Emphasize Your Eyes

By emphasizing your eyes, you can draw attention away from your jawline. Experiment with bold eyeshadow or eyeliner to make your eyes stand out.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for flattering a square face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long
  • Avoid center parts
  • Avoid hairstyles with too much volume on top
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume
  • Try using a volumizing hair product

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a square face?

Avoid blunt cuts, straight hairstyles, and center parts. These styles can emphasize the squareness of your face.

How can I soften my features?

You can soften your features by adding volume and movement to your hair, wearing accessories that draw the eye upward, and emphasizing your eyes.

Hair Care

In addition to the tips mentioned above, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week

By following these tips, you can flatter your square face and feel confident in your appearance.
