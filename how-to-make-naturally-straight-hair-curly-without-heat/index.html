How To Make Naturally Straight Hair Curly Without Heat

Written by William Nov 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of using heat to curl your hair? Heat can be damaging to your hair and can cause breakage and split ends. Luckily, there are natural ways to make your straight hair curly without using heat. In this article, we will discuss how to make naturally straight hair curly without heat.

  • Step by Step
  • Tips

How to Make Naturally Straight Hair Curly Without Heat

There are several ways to make your straight hair curly without using heat. Some of these methods include using rollers, braids, twists, or scrunching. These methods can create natural-looking curls without causing damage to your hair.

1. Rollers

Rollers are a great way to create curls without using heat. Start by washing your hair and allowing it to air dry until it is about 80% dry. Apply a styling product to your hair to help hold the curls, then section your hair and wrap each section around a roller. Leave the rollers in for a few hours or overnight, then remove them and gently separate the curls with your fingers.

2. Braids

Braids are another great way to make your hair curly without heat. Start by washing your hair and allowing it to air dry until it is about 80% dry. Braid your hair into small sections and leave the braids in for a few hours or overnight. Once you remove the braids, gently separate the curls with your fingers.

3. Twists

Twists are similar to braids but are created by twisting two sections of hair together. Start by washing your hair and allowing it to air dry until it is about 80% dry. Twist small sections of your hair and leave them in for a few hours or overnight. Once you remove the twists, gently separate the curls with your fingers.

4. Scrunching

Scrunching is a great way to create curls if you have naturally wavy hair. Start by washing your hair and allowing it to air dry until it is about 80% dry. Apply a styling product to your hair, then scrunch your hair upwards towards your scalp. Allow your hair to air dry completely, then gently separate the curls with your fingers.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make naturally straight hair curly without heat:

  1. Wash your hair and allow it to air dry until it is about 80% dry.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair to help hold the curls.
  3. Choose a method for curling your hair (rollers, braids, twists, or scrunching).
  4. Section your hair and use the chosen method to create curls.
  5. Leave the curls in for a few hours or overnight.
  6. Remove the curls and gently separate them with your fingers.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when curling your hair without heat:

  • Use a styling product to help hold the curls.
  • Allow your hair to air dry until it is about 80% dry before using any of these methods.
  • Experiment with different methods to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to help maintain the curls overnight.

FAQ

Can I use these methods on short hair?

Yes, these methods can be used on short hair. Just make sure to use smaller sections when creating curls.

How long will the curls last?

The length of time the curls last will depend on your hair type and the method used. Generally, the curls can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Will these methods damage my hair?

No, these methods are gentle on hair and do not cause damage like heat styling can.

Hair Care

When curling your hair without heat, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain healthy, strong locks. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause tension on your hair.

By following these tips and using the methods outlined in this article, you can achieve beautiful, natural-looking curls without using heat. Say goodbye to damaged hair and hello to healthy curls!
