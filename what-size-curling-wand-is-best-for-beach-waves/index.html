What Size Curling Wand Is Best For Beach Waves

Written by Oliver Aug 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Beach waves are a popular hairstyle that gives a relaxed, effortless look to your hair. It’s perfect for a day out in the sun or a casual evening with friends. But, to achieve these perfect beach waves, you need the right tool - a curling wand. With so many sizes available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right size for your hair type and desired look. In this article, we’ll discuss what size curling wand is best for beach waves and provide some tips and tricks to get the perfect beachy waves.

What is a Curling Wand?

A curling wand is a hair styling tool that creates curls or waves in your hair. Unlike a curling iron, a curling wand doesn’t have a clamp that holds your hair in place while curling. Instead, you wrap your hair around the wand and hold it in place for a few seconds before releasing. Curling wands come in different sizes, shapes, and materials, making it easier to create different types of curls and waves.

How to Choose the Right Size Curling Wand for Beach Waves?

The size of the curling wand you choose depends on your hair type, length, and desired look. For beach waves, you need a curling wand with a larger barrel size to create loose, flowing waves. The ideal size for beach waves is between 1 to 1.5 inches. However, if you have short hair, you can go for a smaller barrel size, around 0.75 inches, to create tighter curls that give the illusion of beachy waves.

Factors to Consider When Choosing the Right Size Curling Wand for Beach Waves

1. Hair Type - Thick, coarse hair needs a larger barrel size to create loose waves, while fine hair requires a smaller barrel size to avoid damaging the hair.

2. Hair Length - Longer hair needs a larger barrel size, and shorter hair needs a smaller barrel size.

3. Desired Look - If you want tighter curls, go for a smaller barrel size, and if you want loose, flowing waves, go for a larger barrel size.

Step by Step Guide to Achieve Perfect Beach Waves

1. Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair from the heat of the curling wand.

2. Divide your hair into sections and clip them up. Start from the bottom section and work your way up.

3. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling wand, leaving the ends out. Hold it for 5-10 seconds before releasing.

4. Repeat step 3 for all sections of your hair until you achieve your desired look.

5. Once you’ve curled all sections of your hair, gently run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create a natural look.

6. Finish off with a texturizing spray or a light hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Creating Perfect Beach Waves

1. Always use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair from the heat of the curling wand.

2. Don’t wrap your hair too tightly around the curling wand to avoid creating tight, unnatural curls.

3. Use a texturizing spray or a light hairspray to hold the curls in place and give your hair a natural, beachy look.

4. Experiment with different barrel sizes and techniques to create different types of curls and waves.

FAQs About Beach Waves and Curling Wands

Q. Can I create beach waves with a curling iron?

A. Yes, you can create beach waves with a curling iron, but it’s more challenging to achieve a natural, effortless look than with a curling wand.

Q. Can I create beach waves with a straightener?

A. Yes, you can create beach waves with a straightener, but it requires a different technique and may take longer to achieve the desired look.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Beach Waves

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.

2. Avoid over-washing your hair and use dry shampoo in between washes to maintain the natural texture of your hair.

3. Use hair serum or oil to keep your hair moisturized and avoid frizz.

4. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and let it air dry whenever possible.

Now that you know what size curling wand is best for beach waves, it’s time to experiment and create your perfect look. Remember to take care of your hair and maintain the natural texture of your hair to achieve a natural, effortless look.
