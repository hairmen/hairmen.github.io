Description

Having a fat face or a double chin can sometimes make us feel insecure and unattractive. But, with the right medium hairstyle, you can enhance your features and boost your confidence.

In this article, we will provide you with the best medium hairstyles for fat faces and double chins. We will also give you tips on how to choose the right hairstyle that suits your face shape, how to style it properly, and how to maintain it.

How to Choose the Right Medium Hairstyle for Fat Faces and Double Chins

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape is crucial to bring out the best in you. Here are some tips on how to choose the right medium hairstyle for fat faces and double chins:

Consider Your Face Shape

Knowing your face shape is important in choosing the right hairstyle. If you have a round face, go for a medium hairstyle with layers and side-swept bangs. If you have a square face, opt for a medium hairstyle with soft curls or waves. If you have an oval face, you can rock any medium hairstyle.

Highlight Your Best Features

If you have beautiful eyes, go for a medium hairstyle with side-swept bangs to draw attention to your eyes. If you have a beautiful jawline, opt for a medium hairstyle with layers that accentuate your jawline.

Take Your Lifestyle into Consideration

If you have a busy lifestyle, go for a low-maintenance medium hairstyle that you can easily style in the morning. If you love experimenting with hairstyles, go for a medium hairstyle with layers that you can style in different ways.

Step by Step Guide in Styling Medium Hairstyles for Fat Faces and Double Chins

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your medium hairstyle for fat faces and double chins:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Dry your hair using a towel, but don't rub it harshly to avoid frizz.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product that suits your hair type. If you have thin hair, apply a volumizing mousse to add volume. If you have thick hair, apply a smoothing serum to tame frizz.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create soft curls or waves. If you have a round face, blow dry your hair away from your face to create a slimming effect.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair according to your preference. You can create soft curls or waves using a curling iron or flat iron. You can also straighten your hair using a flat iron.

Tips in Maintaining Your Medium Hairstyle for Fat Faces and Double Chins

Maintaining your medium hairstyle is important to keep it looking fresh and beautiful. Here are some tips:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 6 to 8 weeks to avoid split ends and maintain its shape.

Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or flat iron to avoid heat damage.

Use the Right Hair Products

Use hair products that suit your hair type and the style you want to achieve. If you have curly hair, use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls. If you have straight hair, use a smoothing serum to tame frizz.

FAQ

What is the best medium hairstyle for fat faces and double chins?

The best medium hairstyle for fat faces and double chins is a layered haircut with side-swept bangs. This style draws attention away from the chin and creates an illusion of a slimmer face.

Can I still wear a bob with a fat face and double chin?

Yes, you can still wear a bob with a fat face and double chin. Opt for a chin-length bob with layers and side-swept bangs to create a slimming effect.

What hair color suits a fat face and double chin?

Dark hair colors such as black, brown, and dark red are great for fat faces and double chins. These colors create an illusion of a slimmer face. Avoid light hair colors such as blonde and platinum as they can make the face appear wider.

Hair Care for Medium Hairstyles for Fat Faces and Double Chins

Proper hair care is important to keep your medium hairstyle looking beautiful and healthy. Here are some tips:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2 to 3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Avoid Over-Styling Your Hair

Avoid over-styling your hair as it can lead to hair breakage and damage.

Now that you know the best medium hairstyles for fat faces and double chins, it's time to choose the right one for you. Remember to consider your face shape, highlight your best features, and take your lifestyle into consideration. Follow the step-by-step guide in styling your medium hairstyle and maintain it properly to keep it looking beautiful and healthy.
