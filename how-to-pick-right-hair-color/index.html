How To Pick The Right Hair Color

Written by Robby Jun 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to pick the perfect hair color for you. From understanding your skin tone to picking the right shade, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  1. Understanding Your Skin Tone
  2. Consider Your Natural Hair Color
  3. Determine Your Coloring Goals
  4. Choose the Right Shade
  5. Test the Color
  6. Hair Care Tips

Understanding Your Skin Tone

The first step to picking the right hair color is understanding your skin tone. There are three main types of skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral.

Warm skin tones have a yellow, peachy, or golden undertone. Cool skin tones have a pink, red, or bluish undertone. Neutral skin tones have a mix of warm and cool undertones.

To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If you can't tell, you likely have neutral undertones.

Consider Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also play a role in choosing the right hair color. If you have darker hair, you may need to lighten it before adding a new color. If you have lighter hair, you may be able to achieve the desired color without pre-lightening.

Additionally, consider the undertones in your natural hair color. If you have warm undertones in your hair, you may want to stick with warm shades. If you have cool undertones, cool shades may be a better fit.

Determine Your Coloring Goals

Before choosing a hair color, determine your coloring goals. Do you want a subtle change or a bold new look? Do you want to cover gray hair or add highlights? Knowing your goals can help you narrow down your options.

Choose the Right Shade

Once you have a general idea of what you want, it's time to choose the right shade. Look for shades that complement your skin tone and natural hair color. If you have warm undertones, consider shades like copper, honey, or golden blonde. If you have cool undertones, consider shades like ash blonde or platinum.

It's also important to consider your lifestyle and maintenance preferences. If you don't want to spend a lot of time and money maintaining your hair color, opt for a shade that is close to your natural color.

Test the Color

Before committing to a new hair color, it's important to test it out. Many hair dye brands offer test swatches or small sample sizes. Apply the color to a small section of hair to see how it looks on you.

It's also important to do a patch test to check for allergies or adverse reactions. Apply a small amount of the hair dye to your skin and wait 24 hours to see if there is any reaction.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found the perfect hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the color and keep it looking healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners to avoid stripping the color.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent fading.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often.

FAQs

How often should I color my hair?

It depends on the individual and the type of hair color. Some people may need to color their hair every four to six weeks, while others can go longer. Talk to your stylist about the best schedule for your hair.

Can I color my hair at home?

Yes, you can color your hair at home, but it's important to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test to check for allergies or adverse reactions. If you're unsure, it's best to visit a professional stylist.

How do I fix a hair color mistake?

If you're unhappy with your hair color, it's best to visit a professional stylist to fix the mistake. Trying to fix it at home can lead to further damage and may not achieve the desired result.

Picking the right hair color can be a fun and exciting process. By understanding your skin tone, natural hair color, and coloring goals, you can find the perfect shade to complement your style and personality. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
