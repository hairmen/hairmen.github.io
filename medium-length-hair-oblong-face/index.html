Medium Length Hair For Oblong Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, finding the perfect hairstyle can be quite challenging. But don't worry - we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about medium length hair for oblong faces, including the best styles, tips, and hair care routines.

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a prominent chin and forehead. People with this face shape often have high cheekbones and a straight jawline. The goal of a hairstyle for an oblong face is to create the illusion of width and balance out the face's length.

How to

When it comes to medium length hair for oblong faces, there are several styles that work well. Here are some of our favorites:

1. Layered Lob

A layered lob (long bob) is a great option for oblong faces. The layers add volume and movement to the hair, which helps to balance out the length of the face. This style works well with or without bangs.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are another great option for oblong faces. They help to shorten the forehead and create the illusion of width across the face. Pair them with a shoulder-length cut for a chic and effortless look.

3. Wavy Bob

A wavy bob is a great way to add texture and dimension to medium length hair. It also helps to widen the face and balance out an oblong shape. This style looks great with or without bangs.

Step by Step

Here's how to achieve a layered lob:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the hair into four quadrants.
  3. Starting at the back, take a one-inch section of hair and cut it to the desired length. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way towards the front.
  4. Once you've cut all of the hair to the desired length, go back through and add layers to create volume and movement.
  5. Style as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling medium length hair for an oblong face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can elongate the face even more.
  • Consider adding bangs to shorten the forehead and balance out the face.
  • Use volumizing products to add texture and create the illusion of width.
  • Avoid center parts, as they can make the face appear longer.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for an oblong face?

A layered lob or a wavy bob are both great options for oblong faces. They help to add volume and movement, which balances out the length of the face.

Should I get bangs if I have an oblong face?

Side swept bangs are a great option for oblong faces. They help to shorten the forehead and create the illusion of width across the face.

What products should I use to style my hair?

Use volumizing products to add texture and create the illusion of width. A texturizing spray or mousse works well for medium length hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant before styling with hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect medium length hairstyle for your oblong face shape. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best!
