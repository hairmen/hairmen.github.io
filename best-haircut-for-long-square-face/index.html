Best Haircut for Long Square Face in 2023 - Tips, How To, and FAQ

If you have a long square face, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. You want to choose a style that flatters your face shape and accentuates your features. In this article, we'll discuss the best haircuts for long square faces, including tips, how-to guides, and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A long square face is characterized by a strong jawline and a square-shaped forehead that is almost as wide as the cheekbones. The length of the face is longer than the width, and the chin is usually pointy. The key to finding the perfect haircut for this face shape is to soften the angles and create the illusion of a more oval face.

How To

The first step in finding the best haircut for a long square face is to determine the length of your hair. Long hair that falls below the shoulders is the best option for this face shape. If you prefer shorter hair, a bob or a lob that falls just below the chin can also work well.

Step by Step

When getting a haircut for a long square face, here are a few things to keep in mind:

  1. Choose a style that adds volume to the top of your head to balance out the square jawline.
  2. Consider getting layers to soften the angles and create movement.
  3. A side-swept fringe can also help to create the illusion of an oval face.
  4. Avoid straight, blunt cuts that accentuate the squareness of the face.
  5. Opt for curls or waves to add softness and femininity to the face.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a long square face:

  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut for your specific face shape.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend styling your hair each day.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
  • Accessorize with earrings, necklaces, and scarves to draw attention away from the squareness of your face.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for long square faces:

Q: Can I wear bangs with a long square face?
A: Yes, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs. A side-swept fringe or curtain bangs can work well to soften the angles of the face.
Q: Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a long square face?
A: Not necessarily. A bob or a lob that falls just below the chin can work well with this face shape.
Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long square face?
A: Avoid straight, blunt cuts that accentuate the squareness of the face. Instead, opt for styles with volume and movement.

Hair Care

Regardless of the haircut you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
