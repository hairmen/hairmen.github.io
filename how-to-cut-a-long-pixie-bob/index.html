How To Cut A Long Pixie Bob A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a new haircut? A long pixie bob might be just the style you're looking for! This trendy cut features short, textured layers that frame the face and a longer length in the back. In this article, we'll walk you through how to cut a long pixie bob at home.

Description

A long pixie bob is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your unique features and style. This cut typically features short layers around the crown of the head, with longer layers in the back. The style can be worn straight or with natural texture, and can be dressed up or down for any occasion.

How to

Before you begin, gather the following tools:

  • Sharp haircutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips to section hair
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

Step 2: Section your hair into four quadrants, using clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Begin with the back section. Starting at the nape of your neck, use your comb to hold the hair at a 45-degree angle away from your head. Use your scissors to cut the hair in a straight line, following the angle of your comb.

Step 4: Move on to the side sections. Take a small section of hair from the front of your ear and hold it at a 45-degree angle away from your head. Cut the hair in a straight line, following the angle of your comb. Repeat on the other side.

Step 5: Finally, move on to the top section. Take a small section of hair and hold it straight up from your head. Cut the hair in a straight line, following the length you desire. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you've achieved your desired length and texture.

Step 6: Use your comb to blend any harsh lines and create a seamless look.

Tips

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Be careful not to cut too much hair in the crown area, as this can create a "mushroom" effect.
  • Use your comb to hold hair at a consistent angle for an even cut.
  • Don't forget to trim your bangs, if you have them!

FAQ

How often should I get my long pixie bob cut?

It's recommended to get a trim every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and texture of your haircut.

Can I style my long pixie bob differently for different occasions?

Absolutely! This versatile cut can be styled straight, with natural texture, or with loose waves for a variety of looks.

Hair Care

To keep your long pixie bob looking its best, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and maintain color. Apply a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to damp hair before blow-drying for added volume and texture. Use a small amount of hair oil or serum to smooth down any flyaways and add shine.

Cutting a long pixie bob may seem intimidating, but with the right tools and technique, it can be done at home. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips to achieve a trendy, low-maintenance haircut that's sure to turn heads.
