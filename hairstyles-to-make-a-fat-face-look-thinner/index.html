Hairstyles To Make A Fat Face Look Thinner Tips Tricks And Faqs

Written by Frank Oct 11, 2023 · 5 min read

Welcome to our blog post on hairstyles to make a fat face look thinner! If you've been struggling with a chubby or round face shape, you may have felt self-conscious about your appearance or struggled to find the right hairstyle to flatter your features. Don't worry, though – there are plenty of tricks and tips you can use to create the illusion of a slimmer, more elongated face shape. In this article, we'll walk you through how to choose the right haircut, styling techniques, and hair care tips to help you achieve your ideal look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for a Fat Face
  • Step-by-Step Hairstyling Techniques for a Slimmer Face
  • Pro Tips for Styling Your Hair to Look Thinner

  • Hair Care Tips for Keeping Your Hair Looking Its Best

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for a Fat Face

The first step in creating a slimmer face shape with your hair is to choose the right cut. There are a few key things to keep in mind when selecting a hairstyle:

  • Length: Longer hair can help elongate your face, while shorter hair can make it appear wider.
  • Layers: Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, but be careful not to add too much bulk around your face.
  • Bangs: Bangs can help balance out a wide forehead, but be careful not to choose a style that cuts across your face and makes it appear wider.

Some good hairstyles to consider for a fat face include:

  • Long layers
  • Face-framing layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Asymmetrical bobs
  • High ponytails or updos

On the other hand, some hairstyles to avoid if you're looking to create a slimmer face shape include:

  • Blunt or heavy bangs
  • Chin-length bobs
  • Center parts
  • Voluminous curls or waves

By choosing the right cut and style for your face shape, you can create a more flattering and slimming look.

Step-by-Step Hairstyling Techniques for a Slimmer Face

Once you've chosen the right haircut, it's time to start styling your hair to create the illusion of a thinner face. Here are some techniques to try:

  • Create volume at the crown of your head: This can help elongate your face and draw attention away from the sides. Try backcombing or using a volumizing mousse or spray.
  • Use a side part: A deep side part can create the illusion of a slimmer face by drawing attention away from the center.
  • Sweep your hair to the side: This can help balance out wider cheeks and create a more symmetrical look.
  • Avoid adding too much volume around your face: This can make your face appear wider. Instead, focus on creating volume at the crown or back of your head.
  • Try a sleek, straight style: This can help elongate your face and create a more streamlined look.

There are plenty of styling techniques you can experiment with to find what works best for you.

Pro Tips for Styling Your Hair to Look Thinner

Here are a few more tips to keep in mind when styling your hair for a slimmer face:

  • Avoid styles that are too tight or pulled back: This can create the illusion of a wider face.
  • Consider highlights or lowlights: These can add dimension and depth to your hair, creating a more flattering look.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying: This can help smooth out your hair and create a more elongated look.
  • Experiment with different styles: Don't be afraid to try new things and see what works best for you.

Remember, the goal is to create a flattering and slimming look – so don't be afraid to play around with different styles and techniques.

FAQs: Your Questions About Hairstyles for a Fat Face, Answered

Q: Can I still wear my hair short if I have a fat face?

A: Yes, you can! Just be sure to choose a shorter cut that isn't too blunt or angular. Soft layers and a side-swept bang can help create a more flattering look.

Q: Will a middle part make my face look wider?

A: Yes, a middle part can create the illusion of a wider face. Try a side part instead to draw attention away from the center.

Q: How can I create volume without adding too much bulk around my face?

A: Focus on creating volume at the crown of your head, rather than around your face. Use a volumizing mousse or spray, and be sure to backcomb gently to avoid creating too much frizz.

Hair Care Tips for Keeping Your Hair Looking Its Best

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure it looks its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.

By taking care of your hair, you can ensure that it looks its best – and that your new, slimming hairstyle is the star of the show.

Conclusion

There you have it – our top tips and tricks for creating a slimmer, more flattering face shape with your hair. Remember, the key is to choose the right cut and style, experiment with different techniques, and take care of your hair to ensure it always looks its best. Whether you opt for long layers, a side-swept bang, or a sleek and straight style, there's a hairstyle out there that will flatter your features and make you feel confident and beautiful.
