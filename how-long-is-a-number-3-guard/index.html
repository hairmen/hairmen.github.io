How Long Is A Number 3 Guard

Written by Eveline Aug 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our blog post on how long is a number 3 guard. This is a common question that we get asked quite often by people who are looking to trim their hair or beard. In this post, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about the number 3 guard and how it can be used to achieve the perfect haircut or beard trim. Let's get started!

What is a Number 3 Guard?

A number 3 guard is a clipper attachment that is used to trim hair or beard to a length of 3/8 of an inch. This means that if you attach a number 3 guard to your clipper and run it over your hair or beard, it will leave behind hair that is 3/8 of an inch long. The number 3 guard is one of the most commonly used clipper attachments and is ideal for creating a short, neat, and even look.

How to Use a Number 3 Guard?

Using a number 3 guard is relatively easy, and you don't need to be a professional to get it right. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to use a number 3 guard:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clipper

The first step is to choose the right clipper that is compatible with the number 3 guard. Most clippers come with a set of attachments, including the number 3 guard. Make sure that your clipper is fully charged before you start trimming your hair or beard.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair or Beard

Before you start trimming, it's a good idea to wash your hair or beard to remove any dirt or oil. This will make it easier to trim and give you a more accurate result.

Step 3: Attach the Number 3 Guard to Your Clipper

Attach the number 3 guard to your clipper and make sure that it is securely in place. Turn on the clipper and start trimming your hair or beard. Use a mirror to check that you are trimming evenly and not missing any spots.

Step 4: Trim Your Hair or Beard

Start at the bottom and work your way up. Use short, even strokes to trim your hair or beard. Make sure that you are trimming in the direction of hair growth to avoid any irritation. Keep checking your progress in the mirror to ensure that you are trimming evenly.

Tips for Using a Number 3 Guard

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your number 3 guard:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter trim.
  • Use a comb to separate your hair and make sure that you are trimming evenly.
  • Make sure that your clipper is fully charged to avoid any interruptions during the trimming process.
  • If you are trimming your beard, use scissors to trim any longer hairs that the clipper might have missed.

FAQs About Number 3 Guard

Here are some frequently asked questions about the number 3 guard:

Q: How long is a number 3 guard?

A: A number 3 guard trims hair or beard to a length of 3/8 of an inch.

Q: Can I use a number 3 guard to trim my hair or beard?

A: Yes, a number 3 guard is suitable for trimming both hair and beard.

Q: How often should I trim my hair or beard with a number 3 guard?

A: This depends on how fast your hair or beard grows. As a general rule, you should trim your hair or beard every 2-4 weeks to maintain the length.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to help you keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any dirt or oil.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to keep your hair strong and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.

Conclusion

That's everything you need to know about how long is a number 3 guard. It's a versatile clipper attachment that is perfect for achieving a short, neat, and even look. Remember to choose the right clipper, wash your hair or beard, and work your way up with shorter guards. Don't forget to use a mirror to check that you are trimming evenly and not missing any spots. Happy trimming!
