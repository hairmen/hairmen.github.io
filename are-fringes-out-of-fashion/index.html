Are Fringes Out Of Fashion

Written by Eveline Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Francesca, a fashionista, woke up with a dilemma. She wondered if fringes are still in fashion. She spent the next few hours browsing the internet, trying to find the answer. After reading several articles, she found out that fringes are still in fashion.

Description

Fringes, also known as bangs, are a timeless hairstyle that has been popular for decades. They are versatile and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. Although fringes have been around for a long time, there has been a lot of speculation about whether they are still in fashion.

How to Wear Fringes

If you're thinking of getting a fringe, there are a few things to consider. First, you should decide what type of fringe would suit your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, a side-swept fringe would be ideal. If you have a square face, a soft, wispy fringe would be perfect.

Another thing to consider is the length of your fringe. A short fringe would be suitable for someone with a small forehead, while a longer fringe would work for someone with a bigger forehead.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Fringes

If you have a fringe, you'll need to maintain it to keep it looking good. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your fringe:

  1. Start by washing your hair and blow-drying it until it's almost dry.
  2. Take a round brush and use it to blow-dry your fringe.
  3. Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or curls in your fringe.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your fringe to keep it in place.
  5. Use a hairspray to set your fringe.

Tips for Wearing Fringes

If you're wearing fringes, there are a few tips to keep in mind. First, make sure your fringe is well-maintained. Trim it regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Second, consider your face shape when choosing a fringe. A fringe that suits one person may not suit another.

Finally, experiment with different styles. There are many ways to wear fringes, from side-swept to blunt. Try out different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are fringes still in fashion?

Yes, fringes are still in fashion. They are a timeless hairstyle that has been popular for decades.

What type of fringe should I get?

The type of fringe you should get depends on your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, a side-swept fringe would be ideal. If you have a square face, a soft, wispy fringe would be perfect.

How do I style my fringe?

To style your fringe, start by blow-drying it with a round brush. Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or curls, and apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to keep it in place. Finish off with a hairspray to set your fringe.

Hair Care for Fringes

When it comes to hair care for fringes, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure you are using the right products for your hair type. If you have oily hair, use a clarifying shampoo to keep your fringe clean and oil-free. If you have dry hair, use a moisturizing shampoo to keep your fringe soft and shiny.

Second, avoid touching your fringe too much. The oils from your hands can make your fringe look greasy.

Finally, make sure you are trimming your fringe regularly. A well-maintained fringe will look neat and tidy.

Conclusion

So, are fringes out of fashion? The answer is no. Fringes are still in fashion, and they are a timeless hairstyle that looks good on everyone. With the right styling and hair care, you can rock a fringe that suits your face shape and hair type.
