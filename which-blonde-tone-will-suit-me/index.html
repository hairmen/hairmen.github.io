Which Blonde Tone Will Suit Me

Written by Robby Jun 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our hair care blog! If you're thinking about going blonde, you may be wondering which blonde tone will suit you best. Blonde hair can come in a variety of shades, from icy platinum to warm honey. In this article, we'll help you determine which blonde tone will complement your skin tone and natural hair color.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Step by Step Guide to Choosing a Blonde Tone
  • Tips for Maintaining Blonde Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Blonde Hair

Description

Choosing the right blonde tone can make all the difference in how your hair looks and complements your overall appearance. Skin tone is the most important factor to consider when selecting a blonde tone.

There are three main skin tone categories: cool, warm, and neutral. Cool skin tones have blue or pink undertones and look best in cooler, ashier blonde tones. Warm skin tones have golden or peachy undertones and suit warmer, honey-colored blonde tones. Neutral skin tones can pull off both cool and warm blonde tones.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

If you're not sure what your skin tone is, there are a few ways to figure it out. One way is to look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they look green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you may have a neutral undertone.

You can also consider the jewelry you wear. If you look better in silver jewelry, you likely have cool undertones. If gold jewelry complements you more, you probably have warm undertones.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing a Blonde Tone

Now that you know your skin tone, here's a step by step guide to choosing a blonde tone:

  1. Determine your skin tone (cool, warm, or neutral).
  2. Consider your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, you may want to start with a warmer blonde tone to avoid a drastic change.
  3. Think about your personal style. Do you want a bold, bright blonde or a more natural, subtle tone?
  4. Consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you choose a shade that complements your skin tone and natural hair color.

Tips for Maintaining Blonde Hair

Blonde hair requires extra care to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a purple shampoo to counteract any brassy tones.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage and fade the color.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, as UV rays can cause damage and fade the color.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a blonde tone:

Q: Can anyone pull off blonde hair?

A: Yes, anyone can go blonde, but it's important to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and natural hair color.

Q: Do I need to bleach my hair to go blonde?

A: If you have dark hair, you may need to bleach it to achieve a lighter blonde tone.

Q: How often do I need to touch up my roots?

A: This depends on how fast your hair grows and how noticeable your roots are. Most people need to touch up their roots every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care for Blonde Hair

To keep your blonde hair looking healthy and vibrant, it's important to use the right hair care products. Look for shampoos and conditioners specifically designed for blonde hair, as they can help maintain the color and prevent brassiness. It's also a good idea to use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

When styling your hair, avoid using high heat settings on your styling tools, as this can cause damage and fade the color. If you need to use heat, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature on the lower side.

With the right care and maintenance, blonde hair can be a beautiful and flattering choice for anyone. We hope this article has helped you determine which blonde tone will suit you best!
