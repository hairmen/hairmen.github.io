How To Make Naturally Curly Hair Straight Without Heat

Written by William Jul 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have naturally curly hair, you may have spent hours trying to straighten it. But, using a flat iron or other heating tools can damage your hair in the long run. Luckily, there are a few ways to achieve straight hair without heat. In this article, we will discuss how to make naturally curly hair straight without heat and share some tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Description

Natural curls can be beautiful, but sometimes you may want to switch things up and wear your hair straight. Unfortunately, using heat styling tools like a flat iron can damage your hair and cause split ends, breakage, and dryness. Instead, you can use some simple techniques to straighten your curls without heat. These methods are not only gentle on your hair, but they also help to maintain your hair's natural texture.

How To: Make Naturally Curly Hair Straight Without Heat

There are a few different methods you can use to straighten your hair without heat. Here are some of the most effective ways:

Method 1: Wet Wrap

The wet wrap method involves wrapping your hair tightly around your head while it's wet and allowing it to dry in that position. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and comb each section straight.
  4. Starting at the nape of your neck, wrap each section of hair tightly around your head, securing it with bobby pins.
  5. Allow your hair to dry completely, then remove the bobby pins and gently comb your hair straight.

Method 2: Roller Set

A roller set is a classic method for straightening hair without heat. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and comb each section straight.
  4. Roll each section of hair onto a roller, starting at the ends and rolling up towards the roots.
  5. Secure the rollers in place and allow your hair to dry completely.
  6. Remove the rollers and gently comb your hair straight.

Method 3: Braid Out

A braid out is another effective way to straighten your hair without heat. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and braid each section tightly.
  4. Allow your hair to dry completely, then remove the braids and gently comb your hair straight.

Step By Step Guide

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water, as it can dry out your hair and make it more difficult to straighten.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause frizz.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner or Styling Cream

Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to your hair to help smooth it out and make it easier to straighten. Work the product through your hair from root to tip.

Step 4: Divide Your Hair Into Sections

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that each section is straightened evenly.

Step 5: Straighten Your Hair Using One of the Methods Above

Choose one of the methods above and use it to straighten your hair. Be patient and take your time, as rushing can cause damage to your hair.

Step 6: Finish with a Light Hold Hairspray

Finish by applying a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place and prevent frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, straight hair:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and maintain healthy hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQs

Can I straighten my hair without heat if it's very curly?

Yes, you can straighten your hair without heat even if it's very curly. You may need to use a combination of the methods above, or try a few different techniques to find what works best for your hair.

How long will my straight hair last?

Your straight hair will last until your next wash. However, if you live in a humid environment or sweat a lot, your hair may start to revert back to its natural texture more quickly.

Can I use a blow dryer to speed up the drying process?

Yes, you can use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to speed up the drying process. However, be careful not to over-dry your hair, as this can cause damage.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain its natural texture and health. Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools, and be sure to nourish your hair with regular conditioning treatments. With some patience and the right techniques, you can achieve straight hair without heat and keep your locks looking beautiful.
