How Do You Know What Hairstyle Suits Your Face

Written by Oliver Jul 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle that fits your face shape can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what to look for. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this blog post, we'll show you how to determine your face shape and provide tips on how to choose the perfect hairstyle that suits you.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, you need to determine your face shape. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Take a Picture of Your Face

Take a selfie or ask someone to take a picture of your face. Make sure your hair is pulled back, and your face is fully visible.

Step 2: Trace Your Face Shape

Print the picture and trace the outline of your face shape using a pen or pencil.

Step 3: Identify Your Face Shape

After tracing your face shape, you can now identify which category it falls under:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Rectangle

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, here are some tips for choosing the perfect hairstyle:

  • Round Face: Opt for hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of your head. Layers and side-swept bangs also work well.
  • Oval Face: Lucky for you, oval faces can pull off almost any hairstyle. Experiment with different lengths and styles to see what works best for you.
  • Square Face: Soften your angles with hairstyles that have layers and waves. Side parts and long bangs also work well.
  • Heart Face: Go for hairstyles that add width to your forehead and temples. Side-swept bangs and chin-length bobs work well.
  • Diamond Face: Opt for hairstyles that add fullness to the sides of your face. Side-swept bangs and shoulder-length hair work well.
  • Rectangle Face: Add width to your face with hairstyles that have layers and waves. Bangs also work well.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can I still wear a hairstyle that doesn't suit my face shape?

Of course! These tips are meant to be guidelines, not strict rules. Ultimately, you should choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

How often should I change my hairstyle?

There's no set rule for how often you should change your hairstyle. It's entirely up to you and your personal preference. Some people like to switch up their look every few months, while others prefer to stick with the same hairstyle for years.

Hair Care Tips

Remember that proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to determine your face shape and choose the perfect hairstyle, it's time to experiment and have fun with your hair. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
