How To Know If You Have An Oblong Face A Comprehensive Guide

If you are wondering about your face shape and how to determine it, you have come to the right place. In this blog post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to know if you have an oblong face. Additionally, we will offer some tips on how to style your hair to flatter your face shape.

Table of Contents

2. How to Determine If You Have an Oblong Face

1. What is an Oblong Face?

An oblong face is a face shape that is longer than it is wide. People with oblong faces tend to have a high forehead, elongated chin, and straight cheeks.

2. How to Determine If You Have an Oblong Face

If you are unsure about your face shape, follow these steps to determine if you have an oblong face:

Step 1: Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.

Step 2: Take a measuring tape and measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Step 3: Compare the measurements. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have an oblong face.

Another way to determine your face shape is to trace your face on a piece of paper or take a selfie and draw lines to connect the widest points of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If the lines form a rectangle, you have an oblong face.

3. Tips for Styling an Oblong Face

Here are some tips for styling your hair to flatter your oblong face:

Tip 1: Add volume to the sides of your face with curls or waves. This will help to balance out your face shape.

Tip 2: Avoid straight and long hairstyles that can make your face look even longer. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add width to your face, such as bobs or shoulder-length haircuts.

Tip 3: Avoid center parts that can elongate your face. Instead, try a side part to add dimension to your hair and face.

Tip 4: Use hair accessories such as headbands or hair clips to add width to your face.

4. FAQ

Q1. Can makeup help to flatter an oblong face?

A1. Yes, makeup can be used to contour and highlight your face to create the illusion of a more balanced face shape.

Q2. Can an oblong face shape change over time?

A2. No, your face shape is determined by your bone structure and will not change over time.

5. Hair Care for Oblong Face

In addition to styling your hair, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for hair care for an oblong face:

Tip 1: Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair from drying out.

Tip 2: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Tip 3: Avoid heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons that can damage your hair.

Tip 4: Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote hair growth.

Now that you know how to determine if you have an oblong face and how to style your hair to flatter your face shape, you can confidently choose hairstyles that complement your features.
