What Size Is A 3 Haircut

Written by William Jul 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a haircut that's not too short but also not too long, a 3 haircut might be the perfect choice for you. But what exactly does "3" mean in terms of haircut length? In this blog post, we'll explain everything you need to know about what size a 3 haircut is.

What is a 3 Haircut?

First things first, a 3 haircut is a specific clipper guard size that is used to achieve a certain length of hair on top of the head. Clippers are a type of hair cutting tool that use guards of varying sizes to cut hair to specific lengths. The number on the guard corresponds to the length of hair that will be left after cutting.

How Long is a 3 Haircut?

A 3 haircut leaves 3/8 of an inch of hair on top of the head. This is a medium length haircut that is longer than a 2 haircut (1/4 inch) but shorter than a 4 haircut (1/2 inch).

How to Get a 3 Haircut

If you're interested in getting a 3 haircut, you'll need to visit a barber or hairstylist who is familiar with clipper cuts. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a 3 haircut:

Step 1: Choose Your Style

Before your haircut, you'll need to decide on the style you want. A 3 haircut can be styled in a variety of ways, from a classic crew cut to a textured crop. Your barber or stylist can help you choose a style that will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before your haircut, it's a good idea to wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt or buildup and make it easier for your barber or stylist to work with your hair.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

To begin the haircut, your barber or stylist will likely start with the sides of your head. They will use a clipper with a shorter guard size (such as a 1 or 2) to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head.

Step 4: Move to the Top

Once the sides are trimmed, your barber or stylist will move to the top of your head. They will use a clipper with a 3 guard size to trim the hair on top to the desired length.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

To create a seamless transition between the sides and top of your hair, your barber or stylist will blend the hair using scissors or clippers. They will carefully cut and shape the hair to create a natural-looking finish.

Step 6: Add Texture

If you want a textured look, your barber or stylist may use scissors or a razor to add texture to the hair. This can help create a more relaxed, casual look.

Tips for Maintaining a 3 Haircut

Now that you have a 3 haircut, you'll want to maintain it to keep it looking great. Here are a few tips for caring for your new haircut:

Tip 1: Schedule Regular Trims

To maintain the length of your 3 haircut, you'll need to schedule regular trims every 4-6 weeks. This will help prevent your hair from getting too long and losing its shape.

Tip 2: Use the Right Products

To keep your hair looking healthy and styled, it's important to use the right products. A lightweight pomade or styling cream can help add texture and hold to your hair without weighing it down.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to protect it from damage. This means avoiding excessive heat styling, using a heat protectant spray when you do style your hair, and wearing a hat or sunscreen when you're outside for extended periods of time.

FAQ

Q: Is a 3 haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: A 3 haircut can work for a variety of hair types, but it may not be the best choice for very thick or curly hair. In these cases, a longer haircut may be more flattering.

Q: Can I style my 3 haircut in different ways?

A: Yes! A 3 haircut can be styled in a variety of ways, from a slicked back look to a messy, textured style. Talk to your barber or stylist about the different styling options that would work best for your hair type and face shape.

Q: How long will my 3 haircut last?

A: A 3 haircut will typically last 4-6 weeks before it starts to look overgrown. At this point, you'll need to schedule a trim to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.

Final Thoughts

A 3 haircut is a versatile and stylish choice for anyone looking for a medium length haircut. By understanding what size is a 3 haircut and how to maintain it, you can enjoy a great haircut that looks great and is easy to style.
