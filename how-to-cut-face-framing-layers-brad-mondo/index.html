How To Cut Face Framing Layers Brad Mondo

Written by Eveline Sep 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Face framing layers are a great way to add dimension and movement to your hairstyle. They can also help to draw attention to certain facial features and flatter your face shape. If you're looking for a fresh new look, cutting face framing layers can be a great choice. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut face framing layers Brad Mondo style. Let's get started!

Description

Brad Mondo is a well-known hairstylist and YouTuber who has made a name for himself by sharing his expertise and creativity with his followers. His cutting techniques are known for being modern and edgy, and his approach to hair is always fun and fresh. When it comes to cutting face framing layers, Brad Mondo has some great tips and tricks that can help you achieve the perfect look.

How to Cut Face Framing Layers Brad Mondo Style

Cutting face framing layers Brad Mondo style involves using a specific cutting technique that helps to create soft, natural-looking layers that frame the face. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help you see the natural fall of your hair and ensure that you get an accurate cut.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into three sections: one at the crown of your head, one at the middle of your head, and one at the nape of your neck. Use clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut the top section

Starting with the top section, take a 1-inch section of hair and comb it straight up. Use sharp scissors to cut into the hair at a slight angle, starting at the length you want your layers to start. Cut into the hair in a zigzag pattern, taking small snips to create a soft, layered effect. Repeat this process with each section of hair in the top section.

Step 4: Cut the middle section

Once you have finished cutting the top section, move on to the middle section. Repeat the same process, taking 1-inch sections of hair and cutting into them at a slight angle in a zigzag pattern.

Step 5: Cut the bottom section

Finally, move on to the bottom section and repeat the same process. Make sure to take your time and cut each section carefully so that you achieve the desired effect.

Tips

To achieve the best results when cutting face framing layers Brad Mondo style, keep these tips in mind:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean, precise cut
  • Take your time and work slowly to avoid making mistakes
  • Use a zigzag cutting pattern to create a soft, natural-looking effect
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as this can lead to uneven layers
  • Start with a shorter length and work your way up if you're not sure how short you want your layers to be

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting face framing layers Brad Mondo style:

Q: Can I cut my own face framing layers?

A: Yes, you can cut your own face framing layers. However, it's important to be careful and take your time to avoid making mistakes.

Q: How short should I cut my face framing layers?

A: The length of your face framing layers will depend on your personal preference and face shape. It's best to start with a shorter length and work your way up if you're not sure how short you want your layers to be.

Q: How often should I cut my face framing layers?

A: This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain your layers. Generally, it's a good idea to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your layers looking fresh.

Hair Care

After cutting your face framing layers Brad Mondo style, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling your hair with hot tools
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking its best
  • Use hair masks or deep conditioning treatments once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can enjoy your face framing layers for months to come!
