How To Make Your Hair Straight For Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 24, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're a guy with curly or wavy hair, you may have wondered how to make your hair straight. Fortunately, there are several ways to achieve this look, from using a flat iron to chemical treatments. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about making your hair straight, including how to do it, step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

  2. Step by Step Instructions

How to Make Your Hair Straight for Guys

There are several ways to make your hair straight, including:

  • Using a flat iron
  • Using a blow dryer and round brush
  • Chemical treatments, such as Japanese straightening or keratin treatments

The method you choose will depend on your hair type, the level of straightness you want to achieve, and your personal preferences.

Using a Flat Iron

This is one of the most popular methods for straightening hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup or oils.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's slightly damp.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  4. Divide your hair into sections using clips.
  5. Starting at the back of your head, take a small section of hair and clamp it between the flat iron plates.
  6. Slowly glide the flat iron down the length of the hair, repeating until the section is straight.
  7. Move on to the next section of hair and repeat until your entire head is straight.
  8. Apply a styling product, such as a serum or pomade, to keep your hair straight and smooth.

Using a Blow Dryer and Round Brush

This method requires a bit more skill and practice, but can be a great option if you don't want to use a flat iron. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it until it's slightly damp.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, pulling it straight as you go.
  4. Repeat until your entire head is straight.
  5. Apply a styling product to keep your hair straight and smooth.

Chemical Treatments

If you want longer-lasting straightness, you may want to consider a chemical treatment. Here are two popular options:

  • Japanese straightening: This method uses a combination of chemicals and heat to permanently straighten your hair. It can take several hours and is typically done in a salon.
  • Keratin treatment: This method uses a protein called keratin to smooth and straighten your hair. It can last for several months and is also typically done in a salon.

Step by Step Instructions

Here's a step-by-step guide to straightening your hair using a flat iron:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup or oils.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's slightly damp.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  4. Divide your hair into sections using clips.
  5. Starting at the back of your head, take a small section of hair and clamp it between the flat iron plates.
  6. Slowly glide the flat iron down the length of the hair, repeating until the section is straight.
  7. Move on to the next section of hair and repeat until your entire head is straight.
  8. Apply a styling product, such as a serum or pomade, to keep your hair straight and smooth.

Tips for Straightening Your Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when straightening your hair:

  • Use a high-quality flat iron or blow dryer to get the best results.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Divide your hair into sections to make it easier to straighten.
  • Use a comb to detangle your hair before straightening it.
  • Don't straighten your hair when it's wet, as this can damage it.
  • Use a serum or pomade to keep your hair straight and smooth.

FAQs

Can I straighten my hair if it's damaged?

If your hair is severely damaged, it's best to avoid heat styling tools altogether. If your hair is mildly damaged, you may be able to straighten it, but you should use a heat protectant spray and be gentle when styling it.

How often should I straighten my hair?

It's best to avoid straightening your hair every day, as this can damage it. Try to limit straightening to once or twice a week.

How can I keep my hair straight in humid weather?

Humidity can cause your hair to frizz and lose its straightness. To combat this, use a serum or pomade that's specifically designed to combat humidity.

Hair Care Tips for Straightened Hair

Now that you've straightened your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Aim for every other day instead.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can make your hair straight and keep it looking its best.
