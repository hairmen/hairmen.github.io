How To Grow Hair On Bald Head Home Remedies

Written by Daniel Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling with hair loss and baldness? Do you want to grow hair on bald head home remedies? You have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss some effective home remedies that can help you grow hair on your bald head naturally. Before we dive into the remedies, let us understand why hair loss occurs in the first place.

Description

Hair loss is a common problem faced by many people worldwide. It can occur due to various reasons like genetics, stress, hormonal changes, medication, and other medical conditions. Baldness or hair loss can affect your self-esteem and confidence levels. While there are many treatments available to treat hair loss, they can be expensive and come with side effects. Home remedies are a natural and safe way to grow hair on a bald head without any harmful effects.

How to Grow Hair on Bald Head Home Remedies?

Here are some effective home remedies that can help you grow hair on a bald head:

1. Onion Juice

Onion juice is an effective home remedy for hair growth. It contains sulfur that boosts collagen production, which helps in hair growth. Apply onion juice on your bald head and leave it for 30 minutes. Rinse it off with water. You can do this twice a week.

2. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is known for its hair growth properties. It contains enzymes that stimulate hair growth. Apply aloe vera gel on your bald head and leave it for an hour. Rinse it off with water. You can do this twice a week.

3. Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is rich in fatty acids that nourish the scalp and hair. Massage coconut oil on your bald head for 10-15 minutes. Leave it for an hour and then rinse it off with water. You can do this twice a week.

4. Egg Yolk

Egg yolk is rich in protein that promotes hair growth. Apply egg yolk on your bald head and leave it for 30 minutes. Rinse it off with water. You can do this once a week.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to grow hair on your bald head:

Step 1: Choose a Home Remedy

Choose a home remedy that suits your hair type and preference.

Step 2: Prepare the Remedy

Prepare the remedy as per the instructions given above.

Step 3: Apply the Remedy

Apply the remedy on your bald head and massage it gently.

Step 4: Leave it for Some Time

Leave the remedy on your scalp for the recommended time.

Step 5: Rinse it off

Rinse off the remedy with water.

Tips

Here are some tips that can help you grow hair on your bald head:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your scalp.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet.
  • Get enough sleep.
  • Reduce stress levels.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull your hair.

FAQs

1. Can home remedies really help in growing hair on a bald head?

Yes, home remedies can help in growing hair on a bald head. They are natural and safe.

2. How long does it take to see results?

It varies from person to person. You may see results in a few weeks or months.

3. Can I use more than one home remedy at a time?

Yes, you can use more than one home remedy at a time. However, make sure to choose remedies that complement each other.

Hair Care

Hair care is important to maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips that can help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Condition your hair after shampooing.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair.
  • Do not comb or brush wet hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Growing hair on a bald head can be challenging, but with the right home remedies and hair care, you can achieve healthy and luscious hair. Try these home remedies and see the difference for yourself.
