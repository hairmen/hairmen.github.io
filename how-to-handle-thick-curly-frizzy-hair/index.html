The foundation of any good hair care routine is a quality shampoo and conditioner. A leave-in conditioner can help to keep your curls defined and hydrated throughout the day. Apply a small amount to your hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths. Be careful not to use too much, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look limp.

A diffuser attachment for your hair dryer can help to dry your hair without causing frizz. Use the diffuser on a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair as you dry it. This will help to define your curls and prevent frizz.
