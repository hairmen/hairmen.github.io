How To Cut Women s Hair Short In The Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to change up your look and go for a shorter hairstyle? Cutting your hair short in the back can be a great option for a fresh and stylish new look. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of how to cut women's hair short in the back, as well as provide some tips and tricks for maintaining your new 'do.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Cut Women's Hair Short in the Back
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Women's Hair Short in the Back
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

How to Cut Women's Hair Short in the Back

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over the tools you'll need to cut women's hair short in the back:

  • Scissors
  • Clipper
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Cape or towel to protect clothing

Now that you have your tools ready, let's get started.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by sectioning the hair. Use a comb to create a horizontal parting at the back of the head, just above the nape of the neck. Use hair ties or clips to secure the hair above the parting out of the way.

2. Using your clipper, start at the nape of the neck and work your way up. Use a shorter guard to create a gradual fade up to the desired length.

3. Once you have created the desired length with your clipper, switch to your scissors. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut straight across to create a blunt line.

4. Continue to work your way up the head, cutting small sections of hair at a time. Be sure to check your work in the mirror to ensure that the hair is even and the desired length is achieved.

5. Once you have finished cutting the back section, release the hair that was tied up and blend it into the back section using your scissors.

Tips for Cutting Women's Hair Short in the Back

- Start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter to avoid cutting too much off at once.

- Use a mirror to check your work as you go to ensure that the hair is even and the desired length is achieved.

- Consider getting a friend or family member to help you with the back section, as it can be difficult to see what you're doing.

FAQs

Q: How short should I go in the back?

A: The length you choose will depend on your personal preference and face shape. It's always a good idea to start with a longer length and gradually go shorter if desired.

Q: Can I cut my own hair short in the back?

A: It's possible to cut your own hair, but it can be difficult to achieve an even and balanced cut. Consider getting help from a friend or family member, or visiting a professional stylist.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Now that you've cut your hair short in the back, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain its health and style.

- Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

- Use a styling product, such as mousse or pomade, to create your desired look.

- Visit a professional stylist for regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Cutting women's hair short in the back can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your look. With the right tools, technique, and care, you'll be able to achieve a stylish and chic new 'do.
