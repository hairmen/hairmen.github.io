How To Style Medium Long Hair Male Tips And Tricks

Medium long hair is a versatile length for men, providing plenty of options for styling. From slick back to messy, there are many ways to style medium long hair. In this blog post, we will show you how to style medium long hair male and provide some tips and tricks for maintaining healthy hair.

How to Style Medium Long Hair Male

Before styling your medium long hair, it is important to have a good haircut. A skilled stylist can help you achieve the right length and shape for your hair type and facial features. Once you have the right cut, you can experiment with different styling techniques.

Here are some popular ways to style medium long hair male:

Slick Back

The slick back is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for medium long hair. To achieve this look, apply a pomade or styling cream to damp hair and comb it back. Use a hairdryer to set the style in place, and finish with a matte or shiny finish spray.

Side Part

The side part is a timeless hairstyle that works well with medium long hair. To achieve this look, apply a styling product to damp hair and comb it to one side. Use a hairdryer to set the style in place, and finish with a matte or shiny finish spray.

Messy

The messy hairstyle is a popular choice for medium long hair. To achieve this look, apply a texturizing product to dry hair and use your fingers to style it. Use a hairdryer to set the style in place, and finish with a matte finish spray.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to style medium long hair male:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a product that is suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product to damp hair.
  3. Comb or brush your hair to the desired style.
  4. Use a hairdryer to set the style in place.
  5. Finish with a matte or shiny finish spray.

Tips for Styling Medium Long Hair Male

Here are some tips for styling medium long hair male:

  • Choose a styling product that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use a hairdryer to set the style in place.
  • Finish with a matte or shiny finish spray.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I wash my medium long hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it every other day or every three days.

What is the best styling product for medium long hair?

It depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Pomades, gels, and styling creams are all good options for medium long hair.

How do I choose the right haircut for my face shape?

Consult with a skilled stylist who can help you choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care for Medium Long Hair Male

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy medium long hair male:

  • Wash and condition your hair with a product that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your medium long hair male with confidence and maintain healthy hair. Experiment with different styles and products to find the one that works best for you.
