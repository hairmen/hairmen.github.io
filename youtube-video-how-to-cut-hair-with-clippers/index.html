Learn How To Cut Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Are you looking to save money on haircuts or maybe even start a career as a barber? Cutting hair with clippers is a great skill to have. But where do you start?

  • Step-by-Step Guide

Description

Before we dive into the steps, let's talk about what cutting hair with clippers means. Clippers are an electric tool that barbers use to cut hair to a specific length. They have different guard sizes that control the length of the hair cut. Clippers are a great tool for men's haircuts, but they can also be used for women's hair, especially short haircuts.

How to Cut Hair with Clippers

Before you start cutting hair with clippers, you'll need a few things:

  • Clippers
  • Clipper guards
  • Comb
  • Scissors
  • Cape or towel
  • Chair with adjustable height
  • Mirror

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have everything you need, let's get started:

  1. Prepare the area: Make sure the area is clean and well-lit. Set up the chair and mirror in a comfortable position.
  2. Wash and dry the hair: Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can cause the clippers to jam or pull on the hair.
  3. Select the guard size: Choose the appropriate guard size for the desired length of the haircut. Start with a longer guard and work your way down if needed.
  4. Start cutting: Begin at the nape of the neck and work your way up to the top of the head. Use the clippers in an upward motion against the hair growth. Use the comb to lift the hair and make sure the clippers cut evenly.
  5. Blend the hair: Use a shorter guard size to blend the bottom of the hair. Use a sweeping motion to blend the hair and create a natural look.
  6. Cut the sides: Use a shorter guard size to cut the sides of the head. Use the comb to lift the hair and make sure the clippers cut evenly.
  7. Cut the top: Use scissors to cut the top of the hair to the desired length. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it straight across.
  8. Cut the bangs: Use scissors to cut the bangs to the desired length. Cut them straight across or at an angle for a more modern look.
  9. Clean up: Use a brush or vacuum to clean up any loose hair. Use a damp towel to wipe away any excess hair on the skin.
  10. Show the client: Use the mirror to show the client their new haircut. Make any minor adjustments if needed.

Tips for Cutting Hair with Clippers

Here are some tips to make your haircuts with clippers easier and more professional:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and make sure the clippers cut evenly.
  • Blend the hair with a shorter guard size for a natural look.
  • Use scissors to cut the top and bangs to the desired length.
  • Show the client their new haircut in the mirror and make any minor adjustments if needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about cutting hair with clippers:

  • Can you cut women's hair with clippers? Yes, clippers can be used for women's haircuts, especially short haircuts.
  • What is the best brand of clippers? There are many great brands of clippers, including Wahl, Andis, and Oster.
  • How often should you replace clipper blades? Clipper blades should be replaced every 6-12 months depending on usage.

Hair Care After a Haircut

After a haircut, it's important to take care of the hair to maintain the style and health of the hair:

  • Wash the hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to the hair.
  • Visit a professional stylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim to maintain the style.

Now that you know how to cut hair with clippers, you can start saving money on haircuts or even start a career as a barber. Remember to follow these steps and tips for a professional-looking haircut every time.
