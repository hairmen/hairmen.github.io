How To Style 1950S Short Hair

Written by Frank Jun 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a vintage-inspired haircut, the 1950s short hair is a great option. This hairstyle is classic, stylish, and easy to maintain. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect 1950s short hair look.

Description

The 1950s short hair is a bob-like haircut that was popularized by Hollywood actresses such as Audrey Hepburn and Marilyn Monroe. This hairstyle is characterized by its short length, usually above the shoulders, and its sleek and polished appearance. The hair is usually styled with a side part and curled under at the ends.

How to

To achieve the perfect 1950s short hair look, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.

Step 2: Towel Dry

After washing your hair, towel dry it gently. Do not rub your hair vigorously, as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage. This is especially important if you plan to use hot styling tools, such as a curling iron or flat iron.

Step 4: Blow Dry

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use the brush to create volume and shape.

Step 5: Curl Under

Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to curl the ends under. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release.

Step 6: Style with a Side Part

Create a side part in your hair. You can do this by using a comb or your fingers. Brush your hair to one side and tuck it behind your ear.

Step 7: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to hold your style in place and prevent frizz.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect 1950s short hair look:

  • Invest in a good quality round brush for blow drying.
  • If you have naturally curly hair, you can skip the curling iron and embrace your natural curls.
  • Use a small amount of hair serum to add shine and smoothness to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands and scarves, to add some variety to your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to style 1950s short hair:

Can I achieve this look with short hair?

Yes, the 1950s short hair look is designed for short hair. It works best for hair that is above the shoulders.

Do I need to use a curling iron?

No, you can achieve this look without a curling iron. You can use a round brush to create volume and shape, and embrace your natural texture.

How often do I need to wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less often.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to help you take care of your 1950s short hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect 1950s short hair look in no time. Whether you're going for a classic, vintage-inspired look or just want a stylish and easy-to-maintain haircut, the 1950s short hair is a great option. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
