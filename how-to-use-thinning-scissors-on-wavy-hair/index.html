How To Use Thinning Scissors On Wavy Hair

Written by Oliver Oct 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your wavy hair looking bulky and thick? Thinning scissors may be the solution you need. Thinning scissors are designed to remove bulk from your hair without altering the overall length. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to use thinning scissors on wavy hair, with step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQ and hair care advice.

Description

Thinning scissors are a type of hair cutting scissors with teeth on one or both blades. They are used to remove bulk from your hair without reducing its length. Thinning scissors are especially useful for people with wavy hair, as they can help to reduce the volume and make the waves more defined.

How to Use Thinning Scissors on Wavy Hair

Before you start using thinning scissors on your wavy hair, you need to make sure you have the right tools. You will need:

  • Thinning scissors
  • Regular hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips

Once you have gathered these tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that the hair is clean and free of any product buildup.

Step 2: Section your hair

Use your comb to divide your hair into sections. Clip each section up, leaving one section at a time to work on.

Step 3: Cut with regular scissors

Use your regular hair cutting scissors to trim the ends of the section you are working on. This will give you an idea of how much hair you need to thin out.

Step 4: Use thinning scissors

Now it's time to use the thinning scissors. Hold the scissors with the teeth facing downwards and start cutting into the hair, starting from the middle of the section and working your way down to the ends. Make sure you don't cut too much hair at once, as this can lead to uneven results.

Step 5: Blend the hair

Once you have thinned out the section, use your regular scissors to blend the hair. This will ensure that the hair looks natural and there are no harsh lines.

Step 6: Repeat on other sections

Repeat these steps on the other sections of your hair until you have achieved the desired level of thinness.

Tips

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use your comb to hold the hair taut while cutting.
  • Don't cut too close to the roots, as this can cause the hair to look sparse.
  • Regularly clean and sharpen your thinning scissors to ensure they work properly.

FAQ

Can thinning scissors damage my hair?

Thinning scissors, when used correctly, should not damage your hair. However, if you use them too aggressively or cut too much hair at once, you may cause damage.

How often should I use thinning scissors on my wavy hair?

You can use thinning scissors on your wavy hair as often as you like, but it's best to give your hair a break between sessions to prevent damage.

Can I use thinning scissors on curly hair?

Yes, you can use thinning scissors on curly hair, but you need to be careful not to thin out too much, as this can cause the curls to lose their shape.

Hair Care

After using thinning scissors on your wavy hair, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair to prevent damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Using thinning scissors on wavy hair can help to reduce the bulk and make the waves more defined. By following the steps and tips outlined in this blog post, you can achieve a natural and beautiful look. Remember to take care of your hair after using thinning scissors to maintain its health and appearance.
