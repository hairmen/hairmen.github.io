How To Cut A Shaggy Hairstyle At Home Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance, stylish haircut, a shaggy hairstyle might be the perfect option for you. The good news is that you don't need to go to a salon to achieve this look. With the right tools and techniques, you can easily cut a shaggy hairstyle at home. In this blog post, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut a shaggy hairstyle at home.

How to Cut a Shaggy Hairstyle at Home

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather the necessary tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Mirror

Make sure that your scissors are sharp and suitable for cutting hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to wash and dry it. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that your hair is clean.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by dividing it into three parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 4: Cut the Bottom Layer

Start by cutting the bottom layer of your hair. Take a small section of hair from the back and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the middle and moving towards the ends. Repeat this process until you've cut all of the hair in the bottom layer.

Step 5: Cut the Middle Layer

After you've cut the bottom layer, move on to the middle layer. Take a section of hair from one side and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the middle and moving towards the ends. Repeat this process until you've cut all of the hair in the middle layer.

Step 6: Cut the Top Layer

Finally, move on to the top layer. Take a section of hair from the top and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the middle and moving towards the ends. Repeat this process until you've cut all of the hair in the top layer.

Step 7: Blend the Layers

After you've cut all of the layers, it's time to blend them together. Use your scissors to create texture and blend the layers together.

Step 8: Check Your Work

Use a mirror to check your work and make sure that the cut is even.

Step 9: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, you can style it as desired.

Tips for Cutting a Shaggy Hairstyle at Home

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up.
  • Use a comb to separate the hair and make it easier to cut.
  • Avoid cutting too much hair at once. It's better to cut too little than too much.
  • Take breaks and step back to evaluate your work.

FAQ

Is it easy to cut a shaggy hairstyle at home?

Yes, with the right tools and techniques, cutting a shaggy hairstyle at home can be easy.

Can I cut a shaggy hairstyle if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can cut a shaggy hairstyle even if you have curly hair. Just make sure that you cut your hair when it's dry and use a comb to separate the curls.

Hair Care

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily cut a shaggy hairstyle at home. Remember to take your time and be patient. Good luck!
