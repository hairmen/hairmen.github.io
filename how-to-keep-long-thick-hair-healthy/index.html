How To Keep Long Thick Hair Healthy Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Aug 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Long, thick hair is considered to be one of the most attractive features of a person. However, maintaining such hair can be a daunting task. From hair fall to split ends, long and thick hair requires a lot of attention and care. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to keep long thick hair healthy.

Table of Contents

  • How to Keep Long Thick Hair Healthy
  • Tips for Maintaining Long Thick Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Products for Long Thick Hair

How to Keep Long Thick Hair Healthy

Here are some basic steps to follow in order to keep long thick hair healthy:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair Properly

Washing your hair is essential for maintaining its health. Use a gentle shampoo that suits your hair type and scalp. Avoid using hot water as it can damage your hair. Instead, use lukewarm water to wash your hair.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair Regularly

Conditioning your hair is important for maintaining its health and shine. Use a conditioner that suits your hair type and apply it evenly from the roots to tips. Leave it for a few minutes and then rinse it off with lukewarm water.

Step 3: Apply Hair Oil

Applying hair oil is important for nourishing your hair and preventing hair fall. Massage your scalp with warm hair oil and leave it overnight. Wash your hair the next morning with a gentle shampoo.

Step 4: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly is important for getting rid of split ends and maintaining the shape of your hair. Visit a salon every 6-8 weeks and get a trim.

Tips for Maintaining Long Thick Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining long thick hair:

Tip 1: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will prevent hair breakage and split ends.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools like curling irons, straighteners, and blow dryers. These tools can damage your hair and cause breakage.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf. The UV rays can damage your hair and cause dryness and breakage.

Tip 4: Eat a Balanced Diet

Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals. This will nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: You should wash your hair every 2-3 days. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness.

Q: How can I prevent hair fall?

A: You can prevent hair fall by following a healthy diet, avoiding heat styling tools, using gentle hair products, and avoiding tight hairstyles.

Hair Care Products for Long Thick Hair

Here are some hair care products that are suitable for long thick hair:

  • Shampoo and conditioner for long and thick hair
  • Hair oil for nourishing your hair
  • Wide-tooth comb for detangling your hair
  • Hair mask for deep conditioning

By following these tips and using the right hair care products, you can maintain the health and beauty of your long thick hair. Remember to be patient and consistent in your hair care routine and your efforts will pay off in the long run.
