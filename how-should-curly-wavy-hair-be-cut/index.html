How Should Curly Wavy Hair Be Cut A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly or wavy hair, getting a haircut can be a daunting task. You want to make sure that your hair looks great and that you're getting the best cut possible. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about how to cut curly/wavy hair. We'll cover the basics of what to look for in a stylist, how to communicate your needs, and what styles work best for your hair type.

Choosing the Right Stylist

The first step in getting a great haircut for your curly or wavy hair is choosing the right stylist. Look for someone who specializes in cutting curly/wavy hair and has experience with your hair type. You can ask for recommendations from friends or search online for reviews of local stylists.

When you go in for a consultation, pay attention to how the stylist interacts with you. Do they listen to your needs and concerns? Do they seem knowledgeable about curly/wavy hair? These are all good signs that you've found a stylist who will give you a great cut.

Communicating Your Needs

When you sit down with your stylist, it's important to communicate your needs clearly. Bring in pictures of cuts you like, and be specific about what you want. Let the stylist know if you have any problem areas or if you want to emphasize certain features of your hair.

Don't be afraid to speak up if you're not happy with the cut as it's happening. A good stylist will be happy to work with you to achieve the look you want.

Best Styles for Curly/Wavy Hair

There are many great styles that work well for curly/wavy hair. Some popular options include:

  • Layered cuts
  • Bob cuts
  • Long, loose waves
  • Natural, textured styles

The best style for you will depend on your hair type, face shape, and personal preferences. Your stylist can help you choose a style that will flatter your features and work well with your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Curly/Wavy Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting curly/wavy hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently. Don't rub it vigorously, as this can cause frizz.
  3. Comb your hair gently, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  4. Section your hair into manageable pieces.
  5. Use a pair of sharp scissors to trim the ends of each section. Take off as little as possible to avoid creating split ends.
  6. For more dramatic cuts, create layers or shape the hair around your face.
  7. Finish by styling your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Curly/Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting curly/wavy hair:

  • Always use sharp scissors to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Cut the hair when it's dry, as wet hair can be misleading.
  • Take off as little as possible to avoid creating split ends.
  • Consider a dry cut, as this can help the stylist see the natural texture of your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different cuts and styles until you find what works best for you.

FAQs

How often should I get my curly/wavy hair cut?

It's a good idea to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

What should I do if I don't like my haircut?

If you're not happy with your cut, speak up and let your stylist know. They may be able to make adjustments to achieve the look you want.

Can I cut my own curly/wavy hair?

While it's possible to cut your own hair, it's generally not recommended. It can be difficult to achieve a professional-looking cut, and you may end up damaging your hair.

Hair Care for Curly/Wavy Hair

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your curly or wavy hair healthy and looking great. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut for your curly or wavy hair. Remember to choose the right stylist, communicate your needs clearly, and take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
