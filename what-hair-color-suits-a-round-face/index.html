What Hair Color Suits A Round Face A Guide To Flattering Shades

Written by Bardi Sep 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to choosing a hair color, it's important to consider your face shape. For those with a round face, certain shades can help to elongate and slim the face, while others may accentuate its circular shape. In this article, we'll explore what hair color suits a round face, as well as tips for maintaining healthy, vibrant locks.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into hair color recommendations, it's important to identify your face shape. To do so, pull your hair back and measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are roughly equal, you likely have a round face shape.

What Hair Colors Suit a Round Face?

When it comes to choosing a hair color for a round face, the goal is to create contrast and dimension. This can be achieved through highlights, lowlights, or a combination of the two. Shades that are too dark or too light may emphasize the circular shape of the face, so it's best to stick with mid-tone hues.

Some hair colors that can flatter a round face include:

  • Warm caramel
  • Honey blonde
  • Light brown with highlights
  • Auburn
  • Chestnut

These shades create depth and texture without overwhelming the face, helping to create a more balanced and elongated appearance.

How to Choose the Right Shade

When selecting a hair color, it's important to consider your skin tone as well as your face shape. Warm skin tones tend to look best with warm, golden hues, while cool skin tones are complemented by ashy or cool-toned shades.

Additionally, it's important to consider your natural hair color and whether you want to go lighter or darker. If you're unsure which shade will suit you best, consider consulting with a professional stylist who can provide personalized recommendations based on your features and preferences.

Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair

If you've decided to take the plunge and dye your hair, follow these steps for best results:

  1. Choose a high-quality hair dye in your desired shade.
  2. Perform a patch test on a small section of hair to ensure you're not allergic to the product.
  3. Follow the instructions on the packaging for mixing and applying the dye.
  4. Leave the dye on for the recommended amount of time (usually 20-30 minutes).
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water and apply a color-safe conditioner.

Be sure to follow any additional instructions provided by the manufacturer, as these can vary depending on the product and your hair type.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

To keep your hair looking vibrant and healthy, follow these tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading and maintain shine.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to excessive heat from styling tools, as this can damage the color and cause dryness.
  • Consider using a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your locks.
  • Visit a professional stylist every 4-6 weeks for touch-ups and trims.

FAQ: What Hair Color Suits a Round Face?

Q: Can I dye my hair if it's damaged?

A: It's generally not recommended to dye hair that is severely damaged, as this can cause further breakage and dryness. If your hair is in poor condition, consider using a hair mask or treatment to repair and strengthen it before coloring.

Q: Is it better to dye my hair at home or at a salon?

A: This ultimately comes down to personal preference and expertise. While at-home hair dye kits can be convenient and cost-effective, a professional stylist can provide personalized recommendations and ensure a more precise, even application.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: This can vary depending on your hair growth rate and the color of your hair. Generally, it's recommended to touch up roots every 4-6 weeks to maintain a consistent color.

Conclusion

When it comes to what hair color suits a round face, the key is to create contrast and dimension with mid-tone shades that flatter your skin tone. By following these tips and maintaining healthy hair care habits, you can achieve a beautiful, flattering hair color that enhances your natural features.
