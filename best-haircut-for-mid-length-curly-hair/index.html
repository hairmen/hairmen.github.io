Best Haircut For Mid Length Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. It's beautiful and unique, but it can also be challenging to manage. If you have mid length curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to find the right haircut that works for you. In this article, we will guide you through the best haircut for mid length curly hair and provide tips for maintaining your curls.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When it comes to curly hair, the perfect haircut can make all the difference. The best haircut for mid length curly hair is one that enhances your natural curls, adds volume and movement, and is easy to maintain. There are several options to choose from, including layers, bangs, and bob cuts. The key is to find a style that works for your hair type and face shape.

How to

Before you decide on a haircut, it's important to consider the shape of your face. If you have a round face, you may want to opt for a layered cut that frames your face. If you have a square jawline, a bob cut that hits just above your shoulders can soften your features. If you have an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any style.

Consult with a stylist

If you're unsure what style will work best for you, consult with a stylist who specializes in curly hair. They can recommend a style that will enhance your natural curls and flatter your face shape.

Step by step

Once you've decided on a haircut, it's important to maintain it properly to keep your curls looking their best. Follow these steps for the best results:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair to help define your curls and reduce frizz.

Step 3: Air dry your hair

Avoid using heat tools on your hair as they can damage your curls. Instead, air dry your hair or use a diffuser to dry it.

Step 4: Refresh your curls

Refresh your curls between washes with a curl refresher spray or a mix of water and conditioner in a spray bottle.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your mid length curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair to avoid breakage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair as it can cause frizz.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or a swim cap.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for curly hair?

The best haircut for curly hair is one that enhances your natural curls and flatters your face shape. Some popular options include layers, bangs, and bob cuts.

How often should you wash curly hair?

You should wash your curly hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out.

Should you brush curly hair?

No, you should avoid brushing your curly hair as it can cause frizz and breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.

Hair Care

Maintaining your mid length curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right haircut and proper care, your curls can look amazing. Remember to keep your curls hydrated and avoid using heat tools on your hair. Consult with a stylist who specializes in curly hair for the best results.

Now that you know the best haircut for mid length curly hair and how to maintain it, you can rock your natural curls with confidence!
