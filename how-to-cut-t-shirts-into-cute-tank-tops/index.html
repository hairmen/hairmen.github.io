How To Cut T Shirts Into Cute Tank Tops

Written by Luffy Jun 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your plain old t-shirts and want to switch up your wardrobe? Why not try cutting them into cute tank tops! Not only is it a fun DIY project, but it’s also a great way to recycle old clothes. In this article, we’ll show you how to cut t-shirts into cute tank tops step by step.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Cut T Shirts into Cute Tank Tops
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to Cut T Shirts into Cute Tank Tops

To start, you’ll need a few things:

  • A t-shirt
  • Fabric scissors
  • A ruler or measuring tape
  • A marker or chalk

Once you have these, you’re ready to start cutting!

Step 1: Choose your T-shirt

Pick out a t-shirt that you want to turn into a tank top. It’s best to choose a shirt that is a size or two larger than what you usually wear, as you’ll be cutting off the sleeves and neckline, which will make the shirt smaller.

Step 2: Mark the Neckline

Using your ruler or measuring tape, measure and mark the center of the neckline. Then, draw a line from the center mark to the shoulder seam on each side of the shirt.

Step 3: Cut the Neckline

Using your fabric scissors, carefully cut along the lines you just drew to remove the neckline of the shirt.

Step 4: Mark the Armholes

Next, measure and mark where you want the armholes to be. It’s best to start a few inches below the shoulder seam and make a straight line down the side of the shirt. You can make the armholes as big or as small as you want.

Step 5: Cut the Armholes

Using your fabric scissors, carefully cut along the lines you just drew to remove the sleeves of the shirt and create the armholes.

Step 6: Hem the Edges

If you want to give your tank top a more finished look, you can hem the edges of the armholes and neckline. To do this, fold the edges over once and sew them in place with a sewing machine or by hand.

Step by Step

Here’s a summary of the steps:

  1. Choose your t-shirt.
  2. Mark the center of the neckline.
  3. Draw a line from the center mark to the shoulder seam on each side of the shirt.
  4. Cut along the lines to remove the neckline.
  5. Mark where you want the armholes to be.
  6. Cut along the lines to remove the sleeves and create the armholes.
  7. Hem the edges for a more finished look.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Make sure to use fabric scissors, as regular scissors may not cut through the fabric cleanly.
  • If you’re not sure where to mark the armholes, try the shirt on and use a safety pin to mark where you want them to be.
  • If you want a more fitted tank top, you can cut the sides of the shirt and tie them in a knot at the bottom.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions:

Can I cut any t-shirt into a tank top?

Yes, you can cut any t-shirt into a tank top. However, it’s best to choose a shirt that is a size or two larger than what you usually wear, as you’ll be cutting off the sleeves and neckline.

How do I make sure the armholes are even?

Measure and mark where you want the armholes to be and make sure they are the same distance from the shoulder seam on each side of the shirt.

Do I have to hem the edges?

No, you don’t have to hem the edges, but it will give your tank top a more finished look.

Hair Care

Lastly, don’t forget to tie your hair up before you start cutting to avoid it getting in the way. You can also wear a headband to keep your hair out of your face.

Now you know how to cut t-shirts into cute tank tops! It’s a fun and easy way to switch up your wardrobe without spending any money. Give it a try and see what cute tank tops you can create!
