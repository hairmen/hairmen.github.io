Best Hairstyle For Broad Forehead Male A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

When it comes to choosing the perfect hairstyle, it can be quite challenging for men with broad foreheads. While some men may prefer to keep their hair short, others may want to experiment with different hairstyles. In this article, we will guide you through the best hairstyle for broad forehead male, how to style it, and how to take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

A broad forehead can be a blessing or a curse, depending on how you style your hair. The key to finding the best hairstyle for a broad forehead is to choose a style that will balance out your facial features. A good hairstyle can make your forehead appear smaller, and your face more proportionate.

Some of the best hairstyles for men with broad foreheads include the buzz cut, the crew cut, the side part, the textured fringe, and the quiff. These hairstyles not only look great but are also easy to maintain and style.

How to

Before you choose a hairstyle, you need to consider the shape of your face. If you have a broad forehead, you should opt for a hairstyle that will draw attention away from your forehead. A hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head is a good option.

Once you have chosen a hairstyle, you need to learn how to style it. You can go to a professional stylist or learn how to style your hair yourself. You will need the right tools, such as a hairbrush, a comb, and a hairdryer. You may also need a product to hold your hair in place, such as hair gel or wax.

Step by Step

Here are some basic steps to style your hair:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a hairdryer and a round brush to add volume to the top of your head.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or a hair wax to hold your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long.
  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use the right styling products to hold your hair in place.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a broad forehead?

The best hairstyle for a broad forehead is one that adds volume to the top of your head, such as the textured fringe or the quiff.

How do I style my hair if I have a broad forehead?

You can style your hair by blow-drying it using a round brush to add volume to the top of your head, and then using a comb or your fingers to style it.

Hair Care

It is important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can make your broad forehead look smaller and your face more proportionate. Experiment with different styles and find the one that works best for you.
