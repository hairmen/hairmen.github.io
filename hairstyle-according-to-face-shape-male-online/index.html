Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is determining your face shape. To do this, you can either use a measuring tape or take a photo of yourself and trace the outline of your face. There are seven main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, triangular, and oblong.

Oval Face Shape: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a rounded jawline and forehead.

Round Face Shape: Your face is as wide as it is long, with a rounded jawline and cheekbones.

Square Face Shape: Your face is as wide as it is long, with a strong jawline and forehead.

Heart Face Shape: Your face is wider at the forehead and temples, with a pointed chin.

Diamond Face Shape: Your face is widest at the cheekbones, with a narrow forehead and jawline.

Triangular Face Shape: Your face is wider at the jawline, with a narrow forehead.

Oblong Face Shape: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a strong jawline and forehead.

Hairstyles for Oval Face Shape

Men with oval face shapes have the most versatile face shape and can pull off most hairstyles. However, it's best to avoid hairstyles that add height on top of the head, such as a pompadour or quiff, as this can elongate the face. Instead, opt for a short back and sides with longer length on top, such as a slicked-back undercut or a messy fringe.

Hairstyles for Round Face Shape

Men with round face shapes should aim to create the illusion of length to balance out the width of their face. This can be achieved by adding height on top of the head, such as a high fade with a textured crop or a side-swept fringe. Avoid hairstyles that add volume on the sides, such as a side part or a buzz cut.

Hairstyles for Square Face Shape

Men with square face shapes should aim to soften their strong jawline by adding volume on top of the head and keeping the sides short. A textured crop or a messy fringe are perfect options for this face shape. Avoid styles that are too short on top or too long on the sides, such as a buzz cut or a slicked-back undercut.

Hairstyles for Heart Face Shape

Men with heart face shapes should aim to balance out their wider forehead by adding volume on the sides and keeping the top relatively short. A side-swept fringe or a messy quiff are great options for this face shape. Avoid styles that add too much height on top, such as a pompadour or a slicked-back undercut.

Hairstyles for Diamond Face Shape

Men with diamond face shapes should aim to balance out their wide cheekbones and narrow forehead and jawline. This can be achieved by adding volume on top of the head and keeping the sides short, such as a messy fringe or a textured crop. Avoid styles that are too short on top or too long on the sides, such as a buzz cut or a slicked-back undercut.

Hairstyles for Triangular Face Shape

Men with triangular face shapes should aim to balance out their wider jawline by adding volume on top of the head and keeping the sides short. A textured crop or a messy fringe are great options for this face shape. Avoid styles that are too short on top or too long on the sides, such as a buzz cut or a slicked-back undercut.

Hairstyles for Oblong Face Shape

Men with oblong face shapes should aim to create the illusion of width to balance out the length of their face. This can be achieved by adding volume on the sides and keeping the top relatively short, such as a messy fringe or a textured crop. Avoid styles that are too short on top or too long on the sides, such as a buzz cut or a slicked-back undercut.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

  • Consider your hair type and texture when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Take into account your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair.
  • Consult with a hairstylist for recommendations.
  • Use hair products to enhance your hairstyle and create the desired look.

FAQs

What if I have multiple face shapes?

It's common to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, choose a hairstyle that flatters the most prominent feature of your face shape.

Can I still have a unique hairstyle even if it's recommended for my face shape?

Absolutely! The recommendations for each face shape are simply guidelines to help you choose a flattering hairstyle. You can always make adjustments and add your own personal touch to make it unique.

Hair Care for Different Hairstyles

Regardless of your hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and texture, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. Regular trims and appointments with a hairstylist can also help keep your hairstyle looking fresh.

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape can enhance your features and create a more balanced look. With the availability of resources online, it's easier than ever to find the perfect hairstyle for you. Keep in mind your face shape, hair type and texture, and personal style when choosing a hairstyle, and don't be afraid to experiment and make it your own.
