How To Know What Hair Color Is Best For You

Written by Daniel Sep 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right hair color is not an easy task, especially when you have to consider your skin tone, eye color, and personal style. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide which one will suit you best. In this article, we will guide you on how to know what hair color is best for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Description

Choosing the right hair color can make a huge difference in your appearance. It can brighten up your complexion, enhance your features, and boost your confidence. However, not every hair color will suit everyone, as it depends on your skin tone, eye color, and lifestyle. Therefore, before you decide on a hair color, you need to consider these factors to make the right choice.

How to

The first step to knowing what hair color is best for you is to determine your skin tone. There are three main types of skin tones: cool, warm, and neutral. Cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones, warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones, and neutral skin tones have a mix of both. To find out your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone, and if they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell, you probably have a neutral skin tone.

Next, you need to consider your eye color. Certain hair colors can make your eyes pop, while others can clash with them. For example, if you have blue eyes, blonde, brown, and red hair colors can complement them well. If you have green eyes, red, copper, and auburn hair colors can bring out their color. If you have brown eyes, almost any hair color can suit you, but warm shades like caramel and honey can enhance your eyes.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow to determine the best hair color for you:

  1. Determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist.
  2. Consider your eye color and which hair colors can complement them.
  3. Think about your personal style and whether you want a bold or subtle change.
  4. Consult with a professional hairstylist to get their opinion on what hair color will suit you best.

Tips

  • Consider the maintenance level of the hair color you want. Some colors require more upkeep than others, so make sure you are willing to commit to it.
  • If you are unsure about what hair color to choose, try a temporary or semi-permanent hair dye first to see how it looks on you.
  • Consider your lifestyle and whether the hair color you want will work with your job or hobbies.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair a completely different color from my natural hair?

A: Yes, you can, but keep in mind that it may require more upkeep and maintenance than a color closer to your natural hair color.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair color?

A: It depends on the type of hair color you have and how fast your hair grows. Generally, permanent hair color should be touched up every 4-6 weeks, while semi-permanent color can last up to 8 weeks.

Hair Care

Once you have chosen your new hair color, it's important to take care of it to maintain its vibrancy and health. Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading and dryness. Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair. Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your hair's shape. Finally, protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Choosing the right hair color can seem daunting, but by considering your skin tone, eye color, and personal style, and consulting with a professional hairstylist, you can find the perfect shade for you. Follow our tips and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
