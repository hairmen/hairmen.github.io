How To Style Volume In Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your flat, lifeless hair? Do you want to add some volume and bounce to your locks? If so, you're in luck! In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to style volume in hair. From blow-drying techniques to product recommendations, we've got you covered.

How to

The first step in styling voluminous hair is to start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from damage. Then, use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, lifting at the roots to create volume.

For added volume, you can also use a volumizing mousse or spray. Apply the product to your roots and work it through your hair with your fingers.

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you can enhance your curls by using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help to define your curls and add volume at the same time.

Another way to add volume to your hair is to use hot rollers or a curling iron. Roll your hair up in sections and leave it for a few minutes before removing the rollers. This will give your hair body and bounce.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Dry

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Then, towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray or serum.

Step 2: Blow Dry

Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, lifting at the roots to create volume. For added volume, use a volumizing mousse or spray.

Step 3: Use Hot Rollers

Roll your hair up in sections using hot rollers or a curling iron. Leave the rollers in for a few minutes before removing them.

Step 4: Tease

Tease your hair at the crown to create even more volume. Use a fine-tooth comb to gently backcomb your hair at the roots.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your voluminous hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray to hold it in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style volume in hair:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup that may be weighing down your hair.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun to help add volume and texture.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair, such as oils and serums.
  • Try using a boar bristle brush to distribute your hair's natural oils and add shine.

FAQ

Can I add volume to my hair without heat?

Yes, you can! Try using a volumizing mousse or spray on damp hair and then let it air dry. You can also try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun to add volume and texture.

How often should I wash my hair to maintain volume?

It depends on your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, you may need to wash it more often to maintain volume. If you have thicker hair, you may be able to go longer between washes. As a general rule, aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care

To maintain volume in your hair, it's important to take good care of your locks. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type. Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage. And be sure to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style volume in hair like a pro. Whether you have fine, thin hair or thick, curly locks, these techniques will help you achieve the voluminous hairstyle of your dreams.
