How To Ask For A Low Taper Fade A Guide For Relaxed English Speakers In 2023

Written by Bardi Jun 21, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking to get a fresh haircut and wondering how to ask for a low taper fade? This popular hairstyle involves gradually tapering the hair towards the neckline, resulting in a clean and stylish look. In this guide, we’ll provide you with simple tips on how to communicate with your barber or stylist in relaxed English language to ensure you get the haircut you want.

How to Ask for a Low Taper Fade

When you’re at the barbershop or salon, it’s important to be clear about the type of haircut you want. Here are some steps to follow when asking for a low taper fade:

Step 1: Use Clear and Simple Language

Use simple language to describe the haircut you want. Avoid using technical terms or jargon that may confuse your barber or stylist. You can say something like, “I’d like a low taper fade, please.”

Step 2: Show a Picture

If you have a specific image of the hairstyle you want, show it to your barber or stylist. This can help them understand exactly what you’re looking for.

Step 3: Discuss the Details

Make sure to discuss the details of the haircut with your barber or stylist. This includes the length of the hair on top, the tapering of the sides and back, and the specific style you want. Be open to their suggestions and ask for their professional opinion.

Tips for Communicating with Your Barber or Stylist

Here are some additional tips to ensure clear communication with your barber or stylist:

  • Speak clearly and confidently
  • Don’t be afraid to ask questions
  • Be specific about the length and style you want
  • Bring in a picture or use descriptive language
  • Be open to suggestions

FAQ

What is a low taper fade?

A low taper fade is a hairstyle where the hair gradually tapers towards the neckline. The hair on top is usually longer than the hair on the sides and back.

How long does a low taper fade last?

A low taper fade can last anywhere from two to four weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

How do I maintain a low taper fade?

To maintain a low taper fade, you should visit your barber or stylist every two to four weeks for a trim. You can also use styling products like pomade or wax to keep your hair in place.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair after getting a low taper fade:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or straighteners
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes

Conclusion

Getting a low taper fade is a great way to update your look and stay on-trend. By following these tips and communicating clearly with your barber or stylist, you can ensure you get the perfect haircut for you.
