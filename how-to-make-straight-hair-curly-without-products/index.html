How To Make Straight Hair Curly Without Products A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you ever want to switch up your hairstyle but don't want to use any products? You're in luck! There are a few simple ways to make straight hair curly without using any products. In this post, we'll go over how to achieve curly hair without any heat or styling products.

Table of Contents:

  • Section 1: Braids
  • Section 2: Twist Outs
  • Section 3: Bantu Knots
  • Section 4: Curlformers
  • Section 5: Frequently Asked Questions
  • Section 6: Hair Care Tips

Section 1: Braids

Braids are an easy way to achieve curly hair without any heat or product. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections.
  3. Braid each section tightly, starting at the root and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Once you've braided all of your hair, let it air dry overnight or for a few hours.
  5. Take out the braids and gently separate the curls with your fingers.

This method can also work with smaller braids or twists for tighter curls.

Section 2: Twist Outs

Twist outs are another great way to achieve curly hair without any heat or products. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections.
  3. Twist each section tightly, starting at the root and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Once you've twisted all of your hair, let it air dry overnight or for a few hours.
  5. Take out the twists and gently separate the curls with your fingers.

This method can also work with smaller twists for tighter curls.

Section 3: Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are a fun and easy way to achieve curly hair without any heat or products. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections.
  3. Twist each section tightly, starting at the root and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Wrap the twisted section around itself to create a knot.
  5. Repeat all over your head.
  6. Once you've created all of the knots, let them air dry overnight or for a few hours.
  7. Take out the knots and gently separate the curls with your fingers.

This method can also work with smaller knots for tighter curls.

Section 4: Curlformers

Curlformers are a tool that can help you achieve curly hair without any heat or products. Here's how to use them:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Take a small section of hair and insert it into the curlformer.
  3. Roll the curlformer up to your scalp.
  4. Repeat all over your head.
  5. Once you've inserted all of the curlformers, let them air dry overnight or for a few hours.
  6. Take out the curlformers and gently separate the curls with your fingers.

This method can also work with smaller or larger curlformers for different sized curls.

Section 5: Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use these methods on all hair types?

Yes, these methods can be used on all hair types.

How long will the curls last?

The duration of the curls will vary depending on your hair type and the method you use. Generally, the curls will last for a few days or until your next wash day.

Can I use these methods on dry hair?

It's best to use these methods on damp hair to achieve the best results.

Section 6: Hair Care Tips

To keep your curls looking their best, here are a few hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.

With these tips and methods, you can achieve curly hair without using any products. Try them out and see which method works best for you!
