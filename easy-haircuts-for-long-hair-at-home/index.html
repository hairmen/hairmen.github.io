Easy Haircuts For Long Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and looking for a new haircut? Going to the salon can be expensive and time-consuming, but don’t worry, you can easily give yourself a stylish haircut at home. In this article, we will discuss some easy haircuts for long hair at home, so you can have a fresh new look without breaking the bank.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right haircut, you can easily maintain it. The haircuts discussed in this article are simple, easy to do, and require minimal tools. Whether you want to cut your hair into layers, add some bangs, or trim the ends, we have got you covered.

How to

The first step to cutting your hair at home is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to section your hair. Next, decide on the type of haircut you want and look for some inspiration online. Once you have a clear idea of what you want, follow the step-by-step guide below.

Step by Step

1. Trim the Ends

Start by washing and drying your hair. Once your hair is dry, brush it out and divide it into sections. Take one section at a time and trim the ends using sharp scissors. Be careful not to cut too much hair, as it can ruin the overall look of your haircut.

2. Add Layers

If you want to add some volume and texture to your hair, consider adding layers. Again, divide your hair into sections and cut each section at a time. Start with the bottom section and work your way up. Make sure to blend the layers in with the rest of your hair to avoid a choppy look.

3. Cut Bangs

If you want to add some bangs to your haircut, start by sectioning off the hair you want to cut. Use a comb to create a straight line and cut the hair using sharp scissors. You can experiment with different bang styles, such as side-swept or blunt bangs.

Tips

  • Always use sharp scissors to avoid split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Make sure to cut your hair when it’s dry, as wet hair can shrink and result in a shorter haircut than intended.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help you with hard-to-reach areas.

FAQ

1. How often should I cut my long hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and the style you want to maintain. On average, it’s recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

2. Can I cut my hair with kitchen scissors?

No, it’s not recommended to use kitchen scissors to cut your hair, as they may not be sharp enough and can cause damage to your hair.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it’s essential to take care of it to maintain its health and shine. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it. Instead, air-dry your hair whenever possible or use a low heat setting on your hair dryer.

In conclusion, cutting your hair at home can be a fun and cost-effective way to change up your look. Follow the step-by-step guide and tips provided in this article to achieve a flawless haircut. Remember to take care of your hair after cutting it to keep it healthy and shiny.
