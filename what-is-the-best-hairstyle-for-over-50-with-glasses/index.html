What Is The Best Hairstyle For Over 50 With Glasses

Written by Oliver Jul 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we age, our hair and skin change, as well as our vision. This can make it difficult to choose the best hairstyle to suit our needs. If you wear glasses, the right hairstyle can help to accentuate your features and make you look and feel your best. In this article, we will explore the best hairstyles for those over 50 with glasses and provide tips and advice on how to care for your hair.

Description

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially when you wear glasses. You want a hairstyle that will flatter your face shape, work with your glasses, and be easy to maintain. The good news is that there are plenty of options available, from short and sassy to long and flowing.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Over 50 with Glasses

The key to choosing the best hairstyle for over 50 with glasses is to consider your face shape, glasses style, and hair texture. Here are some tips to help you choose:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the best hairstyle for over 50 with glasses is to determine your face shape. There are five basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that will flatter your features.

Step 2: Consider Your Glasses Style

The next step is to consider your glasses style. Glasses can be heavy or light, thick or thin, and come in a variety of shapes and colors. You want to choose a hairstyle that will work with your glasses, not against them.

Step 3: Think About Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in choosing the best hairstyle for over 50 with glasses. If you have thin hair, you may want to choose a shorter style that adds volume. If you have thick hair, a longer style may work better.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Best Hairstyle for Over 50 with Glasses

Now that you know the basics of choosing the best hairstyle for over 50 with glasses, here is a step by step guide:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Write down your measurements and compare them to the following:

  • Oval: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly equal in width.
  • Round: Forehead and jawline are roughly equal in width, with full cheeks.
  • Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly equal in width, with a strong jawline.
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the jawline, with a pointed chin.
  • Diamond: Forehead and jawline are narrower than the cheekbones, with a pointed chin.

Step 2: Choose a Hairstyle

Once you know your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that will flatter your features. Here are some options:

  • Oval: Almost any hairstyle will work for an oval face shape.
  • Round: A layered bob or shaggy cut can help to elongate the face.
  • Square: Soft layers or a side-swept bang can help to soften the jawline.
  • Heart: A chin-length bob or layered cut can help to balance the face.
  • Diamond: A layered cut with volume at the crown can help to balance the face.

Step 3: Consider Your Glasses

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your glasses. Here are some tips:

  • Heavy glasses: A short, choppy cut can help to balance out heavy glasses.
  • Light glasses: A longer, flowing style can complement light glasses.
  • Thick frames: A shorter style can help to balance out thick frames.
  • Thin frames: A longer style can complement thin frames.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've chosen the best hairstyle for over 50 with glasses, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or scarf.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for over 50 with glasses?

The best hairstyle for over 50 with glasses depends on your face shape, glasses style, and hair texture. Short and sassy or long and flowing can both work, as long as they flatter your features and work with your glasses.

How do I choose the best hairstyle for over 50 with glasses?

You can choose the best hairstyle for over 50 with glasses by considering your face shape, glasses style, and hair texture. Use the step by step guide above to help you choose.

How do I maintain my hairstyle?

You can maintain your hairstyle by getting regular trims, using a sulfate-free shampoo, avoiding hot tools on a daily basis, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and protecting your hair from the sun and heat.

Hair Care

Choosing the best hairstyle for over 50 with glasses is just the first step. To keep your hair looking and feeling its best, you need to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking and feeling its best, no matter what your age or glasses style.
