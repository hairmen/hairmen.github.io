How To Make Naturally Straight Hair Curly After Shower

Written by Daniel Oct 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your naturally straight hair and want to add some curls? Fortunately, you can achieve those bouncy curls without resorting to harsh chemical treatments. In this article, we’ll show you how to make naturally straight hair curly after shower in a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Naturally Straight Hair Curly After Shower
  • Step by Step Process
  • Tips for better curls
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Make Naturally Straight Hair Curly After Shower

The easiest method for making naturally straight hair curly after shower is to use flexi rods or curlformers. These are soft, foam rollers that create uniform curls without damaging your hair. Simply follow these steps:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray or serum to avoid any heat damage. Comb your hair to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections using hair clips. Smaller sections will create tighter curls, while larger sections will create looser curls.

Step 3: Apply Styling Mousse

Apply styling mousse to each section of your hair. This will help hold the curls and prevent frizz.

Step 4: Roll Your Hair

Take each section of your hair and wrap it around the flexi rod or curlformer. Make sure to wrap the hair tightly around the roller and secure it in place. Repeat this process for every section of your hair.

Step 5: Dry Your Hair

Once you’ve rolled all your hair, let it air dry or use a hair dryer on low heat. Avoid using high heat as it can damage your hair.

Step 6: Remove the Rollers

Once your hair is completely dry, remove the rollers by gently unwrapping them in the opposite direction.

Step 7: Style Your Curls

Use your fingers to separate and style your curls. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Better Curls

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best curls:

  • Use smaller sections for tighter curls and larger sections for looser curls.
  • Apply styling mousse or gel to each section before rolling to help hold the curls.
  • Avoid using high heat to dry your hair to prevent damage.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.
  • Don’t brush your curls as it will make them frizzy. Use your fingers to separate and style your curls instead.

FAQs

Q. How long will my curls last?

A. The longevity of your curls depends on your hair type and the products you use. Typically, curls will last 1-3 days.

Q. Can I use this method on wet hair?

A. It’s best to use this method on damp, towel-dried hair. Wet hair can take longer to dry and may not hold the curls as well.

Q. Can I sleep with the rollers in my hair?

A. Yes, you can sleep with the rollers in your hair. However, it’s best to use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and frizz-free. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curls as it can cause breakage and frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers instead.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help dry your curls without causing frizz.

Now that you know how to make naturally straight hair curly after shower, you can add some bounce and volume to your locks without damaging them. Follow these steps and tips for beautiful, healthy curls.
