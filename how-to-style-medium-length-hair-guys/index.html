How to Style Medium Length Hair Guys

Styling medium length hair for guys can be a bit tricky. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a variety of styles that suit your personality and lifestyle. Here are some general steps to follow:

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type

Before styling your hair, it's important to know your hair type. Is your hair straight, wavy, curly, or kinky? Knowing your hair type will help you choose the right products and techniques for styling.

Step 2: Choose Your Style

There are many styles you can choose from, such as slicked back, messy, textured, or layered. Choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Wash and condition your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray if you plan on using heat styling tools. Comb your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 4: Apply Products

Apply a styling product that suits your hair type and style. For example, if you have wavy or curly hair, use a curl-enhancing cream. If you have straight hair, use a pomade or styling cream. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair according to your chosen style. Use a blow dryer or a diffuser attachment to dry your hair if needed. Finish off with a hairspray or a matte paste to hold your style in place.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to style medium length hair guys:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Rinse thoroughly and towel-dry your hair.

Step 2: Choose Your Style

Choose a style that you like and think suits your face shape and hair type. Look for inspiration online or ask your barber for recommendations.

Step 3: Apply Products

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. Use a dime-sized amount for shorter hair and a quarter-sized amount for longer hair. Rub the product between your palms and apply it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer or a diffuser attachment to dry your hair. Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair as you dry it. For a messy look, use your fingers to scrunch your hair as you dry it.

Step 5: Finish Off with Hairspray

Finish off with a hairspray or a matte paste to hold your style in place. Spray the hairspray evenly throughout your hair or apply the paste to your fingertips and run them through your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your medium length hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray if you plan on using heat styling tools.
  • Use a comb or a brush to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and style.

FAQ

What Products Should I Use for Styling Medium Length Hair?

It depends on your hair type and style preference. Use a pomade or a styling cream for straight hair, a curl-enhancing cream for wavy or curly hair, and a texturizing paste for a messy or textured look.

How Often Should I Wash My Hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or sweat a lot, wash your hair every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, wash it every 2-3 days.

Should I Use Heat Styling Tools?

It's okay to use heat styling tools occasionally, but avoid using them every day as they can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your medium length hair:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel as it can cause breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and style.

Styling medium length hair guys can be a fun and creative process. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a variety of styles that suit your personality and lifestyle. Use the tips and steps provided in this blog post to help you style your medium length hair like a pro!
