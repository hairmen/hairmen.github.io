How To Order A Low Fade Haircut Tips And Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle? The low fade haircut is a popular choice for men of all ages. It's a versatile cut that looks great in different lengths and styles. In this article, we will guide you on how to order a low fade haircut, from what to tell your barber to how to take care of your hair after the cut.

Table of Content

  1. How to Order a Low Fade Haircut
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for a Perfect Low Fade Haircut
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care After the Cut

How to Order a Low Fade Haircut

Before going to the barber, it's essential to know what you want. Here are the things you need to tell your barber when ordering a low fade haircut:

  • The length you want for the top part of your hair.
  • The type of fade you want: low, mid, or high.
  • The length you want for the fade part.
  • The style you want for the back and sides of your hair.

It's important to be specific about the details of your haircut to ensure that you get the desired result. If you're not sure what you want, you can ask your barber for suggestions based on your face shape and hair type.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a low fade haircut:

  1. Wash your hair before going to the barber. Clean hair is easier to cut and style.
  2. Tell your barber what you want, as mentioned in the previous section.
  3. The barber will start by cutting the top part of your hair to the desired length.
  4. Next, the barber will start the fade by using clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair to the desired length.
  5. The barber will use different guard sizes to blend the fade into the top part of your hair.
  6. The fade should be gradual and not too abrupt.
  7. The barber will style the back and sides of your hair according to your preference (e.g., slicked back, combed over, etc.).
  8. Inspect your hair in the mirror and ask the barber to make any necessary adjustments.
  9. If you're happy with the result, pay the barber and leave the shop.
  10. If you're not satisfied with the cut, ask the barber to fix it before leaving the shop.

Tips for a Perfect Low Fade Haircut

Here are some tips to ensure that you get a perfect low fade haircut:

  • Bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to show your barber.
  • Communicate clearly with your barber about what you want.
  • Choose a reputable barber with experience in cutting low fade haircuts.
  • Get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your hair.
  • Use hair products to style your hair and keep it in place (e.g., pomade, gel, wax, etc.).

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about low fade haircuts:

1. How long does a low fade last?

A low fade can last up to 4-6 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

2. Can I get a low fade with curly hair?

Yes, you can get a low fade with curly hair. However, it's important to find a barber who is experienced in cutting curly hair to ensure that the fade is done correctly.

3. How do I style my hair after a low fade?

You can style your hair in different ways after a low fade, depending on the length and style of the top part of your hair. Some popular styles include slicked back, side-parted, and messy textured hair.

Hair Care After the Cut

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair after getting a low fade haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair when styling (e.g., blow dryer, flat iron, etc.).
  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and style.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your hair.

In conclusion, ordering a low fade haircut is easy as long as you know what you want and communicate clearly with your barber. By following the tips and steps outlined in this article, you can get a perfect low fade haircut that suits your face shape and style.
