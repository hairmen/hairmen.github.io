Best Medium Length Haircuts Over 50 A Guide For Women

Written by Christine Jul 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're over 50 and looking for a new hairstyle, medium length haircuts are a great option. Not only are they versatile and easy to style, but they can also help you look younger and more vibrant. In this article, we'll discuss the best medium length haircuts for women over 50, as well as tips for styling and caring for your new 'do.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Medium Length Haircut for You
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your New Haircut
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Healthy, Beautiful Hair

How to Choose the Best Medium Length Haircut for You

When it comes to selecting a medium length haircut, there are several factors to consider. First and foremost, you'll want to choose a style that complements your face shape and skin tone. Some popular medium length haircuts for women over 50 include:

1. The Shag

The shag is a classic medium length haircut that's been popular for decades. It features layers throughout the hair, which creates movement and texture. This style works well for women with fine hair, as it adds volume and body.

2. The Bob

The bob is another timeless medium length haircut that can be tailored to suit a variety of face shapes and hair types. A blunt bob can give you a sophisticated, polished look, while a choppy bob adds texture and edge.

3. The Lob

The lob (short for "long bob") is a versatile haircut that falls just above the shoulders. This style works well for women with thick hair, as it can help to remove some of the bulk. It's also a great option if you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut that can be styled in a variety of ways.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you've chosen the perfect medium length haircut, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving three popular looks:

1. Beachy Waves

  1. Start with damp hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and wrap each section around a curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  3. Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls and create a more natural look.
  4. Finish with a light-hold hairspray.

2. Sleek and Straight

  1. Start with clean, dry hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  2. Section your hair and use a straightening iron to smooth out any kinks or waves.
  3. Finish with a shine serum or spray to add gloss and reduce frizz.

3. Messy Updo

  1. Start with second-day hair and apply a texturizing spray.
  2. Create a loose, messy bun at the crown of your head, securing with bobby pins.
  3. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to soften the look.
  4. Finish with a light-hold hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Haircut

Once you've got your new medium length haircut, you'll want to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your style:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools (like curling irons and straighteners) too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors by wearing a hat or scarf.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Will a medium length haircut make me look younger?

Yes! A medium length haircut can help to give you a more youthful, vibrant appearance. By adding volume and texture, it can also help to create a more youthful silhouette.

2. How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It's a good idea to get your hair trimmed every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

3. Can I style my medium length haircut in different ways?

Absolutely! One of the best things about a medium length haircut is its versatility. You can wear it straight, curly, wavy, or in an updo.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy, Beautiful Hair

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to prevent stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when you wash your hair, as it can be damaging.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as it can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle.
  • Protect your hair from the elements by wearing a hat or scarf when you're outside.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your new medium length haircut with confidence and style!
