How To Know Which Hair Suits You Tips And Tricks

Written by William Nov 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your current hairdo and wondering which hairstyle would suit you best? Choosing the right hair can be a daunting task, but with a few tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape, skin tone, and personality. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to help you determine which hair suits you best, and provide tips on how to care for your new hairstyle.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

One of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and stand in front of a mirror. Using a lipstick or washable marker, trace the outline of your face on the mirror.

Once you have your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that accentuates your features.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! This face shape is the most versatile and can handle almost any hairstyle. Consider a long bob or shoulder-length cut to show off your cheekbones.

Round Face

For a round face, choose a hairstyle that lengthens your face. A long layered cut or a side-swept bang can create the illusion of a longer face. Avoid blunt cuts and center parts, which can make your face look rounder.

Square Face

If you have a square face, choose a hairstyle that softens your jawline. A shoulder-length cut with layers or a side-swept bang can help create a more rounded look. Avoid straight, blunt cuts that can accentuate your jawline.

Heart Face

For a heart-shaped face, choose a hairstyle that balances your forehead and chin. A long bob or shoulder-length cut with layers can create a more balanced look. Avoid short, choppy cuts that can make your forehead look wider.

Diamond Face

A diamond-shaped face has a narrow forehead and chin, with wide cheekbones. Choose a hairstyle that softens your cheekbones and creates balance. A shoulder-length cut with layers or a side-swept bang can help create a more balanced look. Avoid short, choppy cuts that can make your face look too angular.

Oblong Face

For an oblong face, choose a hairstyle that adds width to your face. A shoulder-length cut with layers or a side-swept bang can help create a more balanced look. Avoid long, straight cuts that can make your face look longer.

Consider Your Skin Tone

Another factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your skin tone. Your hair color should complement your skin tone, not clash with it. Cool skin tones look best with cool hair colors such as ash blonde, platinum, and cool brown. Warm skin tones look best with warm hair colors such as golden blonde, caramel, and warm brown.

Personality

Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and lifestyle. If you're a busy mom, a low-maintenance hairstyle might be best for you. If you're a fashion-forward trendsetter, a bold, edgy cut might be more your style. Consider your personality and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle.

Tips for Healthy Hair

Once you've chosen your new hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips for healthy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are a few frequently asked questions about choosing the right hairstyle:

How often should I change my hairstyle?

There's no set rule for how often you should change your hairstyle. It's up to you and your personal style preferences. Some people like to switch up their hairstyle every few months, while others prefer to stick with the same style for years.

Can I pull off a short haircut?

Short haircuts can look great on anyone, regardless of face shape or hair type. However, it's important to choose a short haircut that flatters your face shape and complements your features.

How can I make my hair look thicker?

There are several ways to make your hair look thicker, including using volumizing products, blow-drying your hair upside down, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your face shape, skin tone, personality, and lifestyle, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
