Styling Men s Hair Long On Top Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jul 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle, why not try out a long top look? This style has been popular for years and is still trendy in 2023. It's versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit your individual taste. In this article, we'll go over how to style men's hair long on top and provide some tips and tricks to make sure your hair looks its best.

Description

Long top hairstyles for men involve leaving the hair on top of the head longer than the sides and back. This creates a layered look that can be styled in a variety of ways. Whether you want a sleek, polished look or something more relaxed and messy, a long top hairstyle can work for you.

How to Style Men's Hair Long on Top

Here are the steps to follow to style men's hair long on top:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair or blow dry it on a low heat setting until it's completely dry.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply your preferred styling product to your hair. This can be a gel, pomade, or wax, depending on your hair type and the look you're going for. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair. If you want a sleek, polished look, comb your hair straight back. For a more relaxed, messy look, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create some texture.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold your hair in place and prevent flyaways.

Tips for Styling Men's Hair Long on Top

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling men's hair long on top:

1. Choose the Right Styling Product

Choosing the right styling product is key to achieving the look you want. If you have thick, coarse hair, a wax or pomade will work best. If you have fine, thin hair, a gel or mousse may be a better option.

2. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. Try a slicked back look for a formal occasion or a messy, textured style for a more casual look.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

To keep your hair looking its best, make sure to get regular haircuts. This will help prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

FAQs

What is the best hairstyle for men with long hair?

A long top hairstyle is a great option for men with long hair. It's versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit your individual taste.

How do I keep my long hair looking healthy?

To keep your long hair looking healthy, make sure to get regular haircuts, use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Men's Hair Long on Top

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep in mind when styling men's hair long on top:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools on your hair, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

2. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every few days.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style men's hair long on top with confidence. Whether you're going for a sleek, polished look or something more relaxed and messy, a long top hairstyle can work for you.
