How To Style Soft Curls A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Nov 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Soft curls are a popular hairstyle that can be worn by anyone, no matter their hair type or length. This hairstyle is versatile and can be dressed up or down for any occasion. In this guide, we will show you how to style soft curls in a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Soft Curls
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Soft Curls
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Soft Curls

How to Style Soft Curls

Before we dive into the steps for styling soft curls, let's talk about what you'll need. You'll need a curling iron or wand, heat protectant spray, hair clips, and hairspray.

Now, let's get started:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from the heat of the curling iron or wand.
  2. Divide your hair into sections. Use hair clips to separate your hair into sections that are about 2 inches thick.
  3. Take one section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand. Hold the curling iron or wand vertically and wrap the hair around it, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Hold the hair in place for about 10 seconds, then release the curl.
  4. Repeat step 3 for each section of hair. Alternate the direction of the curls to create a natural, tousled look.
  5. Once all of your hair is curled, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create volume.
  6. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Styling Soft Curls

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect soft curls:

  • Use a curling iron or wand with a 1-inch barrel for best results.
  • Don't curl your hair all the way to the ends. Leave the ends out for a more natural look.
  • If you have fine hair, use a volumizing mousse before styling to add volume to your curls.
  • If you have thick hair, use a hair straightener to smooth your hair before curling.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with the direction of your curls. Alternate the direction of the curls for a more natural, tousled look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about styling soft curls:

How long will my soft curls last?

Your soft curls should last all day if you use a good hairspray to hold them in place.

Can I style soft curls on short hair?

Yes, you can style soft curls on short hair. Use a smaller curling iron or wand to create tighter curls.

Can I style soft curls without heat?

Yes, you can use rollers or braids to create soft curls without heat. Simply wrap your hair around the rollers or braid your hair and leave it overnight.

Hair Care for Soft Curls

To keep your soft curls looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

With these tips and steps, you'll be able to style soft curls like a pro. Have fun experimenting with different looks and don't be afraid to try something new!
