Men s Thick Wavy Medium Hairstyles How To Get The Look

Written by Bardi Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a man with thick, wavy hair, you may find it challenging to find the right hairstyle to suit your hair type. However, with a little know-how, you can create a stylish look that shows off your natural waves. In this blog post, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect men's thick wavy medium hairstyle, including tips for styling and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Look
  • Step by Step
  • Tips for Styling and Hair Care
  • FAQs

Description

A medium-length haircut is perfect for men with thick, wavy hair. It allows you to show off your natural texture while still being manageable enough to style. Whether you prefer a messy, tousled look or a more polished style, there's a medium-length haircut that will suit your style.

How to Get the Look

To achieve the perfect men's thick wavy medium hairstyle, you'll need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Cut

The first step to getting the perfect men's thick wavy medium hairstyle is to choose the right cut. The most popular medium-length haircuts for men include the textured crop, the messy fringe, and the side-swept undercut. Talk to your hairstylist about which cut will best suit your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Shampoo and Condition Regularly

Thick, wavy hair can be prone to dryness, so it's essential to shampoo and condition your hair regularly. Choose a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type, and avoid using hot water, which can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Step 3: Use a Heat Protectant

If you're going to use heat styling tools to create your men's thick wavy medium hairstyle, it's essential to use a heat protectant spray. This will help to prevent damage to your hair and keep it looking healthy.

Step 4: Apply Styling Product

To create a men's thick wavy medium hairstyle, you'll need to apply styling product to your hair while it's damp. A matte clay or pomade is perfect for creating texture and hold without making your hair look greasy or weighed down.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you've applied your styling product, you can style your hair using your fingers or a comb. For a messy, tousled look, use your fingers to create texture and movement. For a more polished look, use a comb to create a side part or slick back your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect men's thick wavy medium hairstyle:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type
  2. Dry your hair with a towel, leaving it slightly damp
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair
  4. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, focusing on the ends
  5. Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair as desired
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place

Tips for Styling and Hair Care

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect men's thick wavy medium hairstyle:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair after washing
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Don't overuse styling products, as this can make your hair look greasy
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy

FAQs

What's the best haircut for thick, wavy hair?

The best haircut for thick, wavy hair is a medium-length cut that allows you to show off your natural texture. Popular medium-length haircuts for men include the textured crop, the messy fringe, and the side-swept undercut.

How can I keep my thick, wavy hair healthy?

To keep your thick, wavy hair healthy, it's essential to shampoo and condition regularly, avoid using hot water, and use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized. You should also consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy.

What's the best styling product for men's thick wavy medium hairstyles?

The best styling product for men's thick wavy medium hairstyles is a matte clay or pomade, which will provide texture and hold without making your hair look greasy or weighed down.

In conclusion, achieving the perfect men's thick wavy medium hairstyle takes a little effort, but with the right haircut, hair care, and styling products, you can create a stylish look that shows off your natural waves. Follow these tips, and you'll have the perfect hairstyle in no time!
