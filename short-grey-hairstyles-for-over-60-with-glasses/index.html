Short Grey Hairstyles For Over 60 With Glasses

Written by Eveline Aug 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are over 60 and wear glasses, finding the right hairstyle can be challenging. Short grey hairstyles are a popular option for older women who want a low-maintenance and elegant look. In this article, we will provide you with some tips on how to choose the best short grey hairstyle for your face shape and glasses.

Description

Short grey hairstyles are perfect for women over 60 who want to have a chic and modern look without spending too much time styling their hair. These hairstyles are low-maintenance and require minimal effort to look great. They are also versatile and can be customized to suit different face shapes and glasses.

How to Choose the Right Short Grey Hairstyle

Choosing the right short grey hairstyle depends on several factors, including your face shape, hair texture, and glasses. Here are some tips to help you choose the best hairstyle:

Consider Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right short grey hairstyle is to consider your face shape. A hairstyle that looks great on one person may not look as good on another. The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, and heart-shaped. Once you determine your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that will complement your features.

Choose a Style that Suits Your Glasses

Your glasses can also influence your hairstyle choice. For example, if you wear glasses with thick frames, you may want to choose a hairstyle that is more voluminous to balance the overall look. On the other hand, if you wear glasses with thin frames, you may want to choose a sleeker hairstyle that will not overpower your glasses.

Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also affect your hairstyle choice. If you have thick hair, you may want to choose a layered hairstyle that will add volume and movement. If you have fine hair, you may want to choose a shorter hairstyle that will make your hair look fuller.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to choose the right short grey hairstyle:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Use a measuring tape to determine the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have these measurements, you can determine your face shape.

Step 2: Choose a Hairstyle that Suits Your Face Shape

Once you determine your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that will complement your features. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to choose a layered hairstyle that will add dimension and make your face appear slimmer.

Step 3: Consider Your Glasses

Your glasses can also influence your hairstyle choice. Consider the shape and thickness of your glasses frames when choosing a hairstyle.

Step 4: Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also affect your hairstyle choice. Choose a hairstyle that will work with your hair type and add volume or texture where needed.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right short grey hairstyle:

  • Choose a hairstyle that is easy to maintain and style.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best hairstyle for your face shape and glasses.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have a stylish short grey hairstyle if I wear glasses?

A: Yes, you can still have a stylish short grey hairstyle if you wear glasses. Consider your glasses frames when choosing a hairstyle and opt for a style that will complement your frames.

Q: What is the best short grey hairstyle for a round face?

A: A layered hairstyle with volume at the crown is a great option for a round face. This will add dimension and make your face appear slimmer.

Q: How often should I trim my short grey hairstyle?

A: It is recommended to trim your short grey hairstyle every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and stylish short grey hairstyle. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking great:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

With these tips, you can choose the best short grey hairstyle for your face shape and glasses and keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.
