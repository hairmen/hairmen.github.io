What To Take For Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve

Written by Bardi Nov 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you experiencing hair loss after undergoing gastric sleeve surgery? You're not alone – many people who have had this procedure experience some degree of hair loss. But don't worry, there are steps you can take to minimize the effects of hair loss and promote healthy hair growth. In this guide, we'll discuss what to take for hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery, as well as other tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is a common side effect. This is because the body undergoes a significant amount of stress during the surgery and the period of weight loss that follows. This stress can lead to a condition called telogen effluvium, in which hair follicles enter a resting phase and then fall out. This usually occurs within three to six months after surgery.

Fortunately, in most cases, hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is temporary. In fact, hair growth usually returns to normal within six to 12 months after surgery. However, in some cases, hair loss can be more severe and longer lasting.

How to

If you're experiencing hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery, there are a few steps you can take to help promote healthy hair growth:

1. Take Biotin Supplements

Biotin is a B-vitamin that is essential for healthy hair growth. Taking biotin supplements can help promote hair growth and prevent hair loss. The recommended daily dose of biotin is 2.5 mg.

2. Increase Your Protein Intake

Protein is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure you're consuming enough protein in your diet. Good sources of protein include lean meats, fish, eggs, and legumes.

3. Take Iron Supplements

Iron is important for healthy hair growth. If you're not getting enough iron in your diet, consider taking an iron supplement.

4. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is free of harsh chemicals. Avoid using hot water and vigorous scrubbing, as this can damage your hair follicles.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for what to take for hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery:

1. Consult with Your Doctor

Before taking any supplements, consult with your doctor. They can help you determine which supplements are safe and appropriate for you.

2. Take Biotin Supplements

Take a biotin supplement every day. The recommended daily dose of biotin is 2.5 mg.

3. Increase Your Protein Intake

Increase your protein intake by consuming lean meats, fish, eggs, and legumes.

4. Take Iron Supplements

If you're not getting enough iron in your diet, consider taking an iron supplement. Consult with your doctor to determine the appropriate dosage.

5. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is free of harsh chemicals. Avoid using hot water and vigorous scrubbing, as this can damage your hair follicles.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for promoting healthy hair growth after gastric sleeve surgery:

  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of fruits and vegetables.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, which can damage your hair.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles, such as braids and ponytails, which can pull on your hair and damage your hair follicles.

FAQ

Q. Is hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery permanent?

A. In most cases, hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is temporary and hair growth returns to normal within six to 12 months. However, in some cases, hair loss can be more severe and longer lasting.

Q. Can taking supplements prevent hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery?

A. Taking supplements, such as biotin and iron, can help promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair loss. However, it's important to consult with your doctor before taking any supplements.

Q. How can I promote healthy hair growth after gastric sleeve surgery?

A. To promote healthy hair growth after gastric sleeve surgery, make sure you're consuming enough protein in your diet, taking supplements as recommended by your doctor, using a gentle shampoo, and avoiding heat styling tools and tight hairstyles.

Hair Care

After undergoing gastric sleeve surgery, it's important to take care of your hair to promote healthy hair growth. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to avoid pulling on your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as curling irons and straighteners.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles, such as braids and ponytails.
  • Use a gentle shampoo that is free of harsh chemicals.
  • Massage your scalp to promote blood flow and stimulate hair growth.

With these tips and the right supplements, you can minimize the effects of hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery and promote healthy hair growth.
