How To Find My Face Shape Online

Written by Luffy Aug 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of not knowing what hairstyles or glasses look best on you? The first step in figuring that out is determining your face shape. Fortunately, you don't need to go to a professional for this. There are several online tools that can help you find your face shape in just a few clicks. Here's how to do it:

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Take a Photo
  • Step 2: Choose an Online Face Shape Tool
  • Step 3: Upload Your Photo
  • Step 4: Analyze Your Results
  • Tips for Determining Your Face Shape
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape
  • FAQs

Step 1: Take a Photo

The first step is to take a photo of your face. Make sure the lighting is good and your face is fully visible. You can take a selfie or have someone else take a photo for you. Just make sure you're not wearing any accessories that could obstruct your face.

Step 2: Choose an Online Face Shape Tool

There are several online face shape tools to choose from. Some popular options include:

  • Saks Fifth Avenue's Face Shape Calculator
  • Elle's Eyebrow Shape for Your Face Shape
  • L'Oreal's Face Shape Finder

Step 3: Upload Your Photo

Once you've chosen an online face shape tool, it's time to upload your photo. Follow the instructions on the website to upload your photo. Some tools may require you to position your face within a certain frame or circle.

Step 4: Analyze Your Results

After uploading your photo, the online tool will analyze your face and determine your face shape. The most common face shapes are:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

The online tool may also provide recommendations for hairstyles, glasses, and makeup based on your face shape.

Tips for Determining Your Face Shape

If you're having trouble determining your face shape, try these tips:

  • Look in the mirror and trace the outline of your face with a washable marker or lipstick.
  • Ask someone else to help you determine your face shape.
  • Take multiple photos of your face from different angles to get a better idea of your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

Once you've determined your face shape, you can use this information to choose a flattering hairstyle. Here are some tips:

  • Round faces look best with long, layered styles or styles with volume on top.
  • Oval faces can pull off almost any hairstyle, but look best with a side part and layered cut.
  • Square faces look best with soft, layered styles or styles with volume on top.
  • Heart-shaped faces look best with styles that add volume to the chin area, such as chin-length bobs or side-swept bangs.
  • Diamond-shaped faces look best with styles that add volume to the forehead and chin area, such as layered bobs or side-swept bangs.
  • Triangle faces look best with styles that add volume to the top of the head and balance out the jawline, such as pixie cuts or side-swept bangs.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding your face shape online:

  • What if I have a combination face shape? It's possible to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, choose a hairstyle that flatters the most prominent features of your face.
  • Can I determine my face shape without an online tool? Yes, you can determine your face shape by looking in the mirror and tracing the outline of your face with a washable marker or lipstick.
  • How often should I determine my face shape? Your face shape is unlikely to change, so you only need to determine it once.

Now that you know how to find your face shape online, you can choose hairstyles and glasses that flatter your features. Happy styling!
