What Hairstyle Suits A Small Face

Written by Daniel Aug 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you someone with a small face and wondering what hairstyle would suit you best? You’ve come to the right place. In this article, we will guide you on how to pick the perfect hairstyle that will enhance your facial features and make you look your best.

Description

Having a small face is considered to be a blessing in the fashion world. However, it can be challenging to find a hairstyle that complements your facial features. The right hairstyle can make your face appear longer and slimmer, while the wrong one can make it look even smaller. Therefore, it is essential to choose a hairstyle that enhances the shape of your face.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle?

The key to choosing the right hairstyle is to find one that balances out your facial features. Here are some tips that will help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. Small faces typically fall into one of the following categories:

  • Round face
  • Square face
  • Oval face
  • Heart-shaped face
  • Diamond face

Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that complements it.

2. Determine Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another essential factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a layered cut that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut may be more flattering.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another crucial factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you have a busy schedule, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. Alternatively, if you have more time to spare, you can choose a more elaborate hairstyle.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know the factors to consider when choosing a hairstyle, here is a step-by-step guide on how to pick the perfect hairstyle:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have these measurements, compare them to determine your face shape.

Step 2: Research Hairstyles

Research hairstyles that complement your face shape. You can find inspiration online or in magazines.

Step 3: Consider Your Hair Texture

Choose a hairstyle that works well with your hair texture. If you have fine hair, choose a layered cut that adds volume. If you have thick hair, choose a blunt cut that thins out your hair.

Step 4: Consider Your Lifestyle

Choose a hairstyle that works well with your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule, choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling.

Step 5: Consult with a Hairstylist

Consult with a hairstylist to determine whether the hairstyle you have in mind will work well with your face shape and hair texture. They can also recommend other hairstyles that may suit you better.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or slicked back, as they can make your face appear smaller.
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to your hair, as this can make your face appear longer.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension.
  • If you have a round face, choose a hairstyle with volume at the crown to elongate your face.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a small face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or slicked back, as they can make your face appear smaller.

What hairstyles are best for a small face?

Choose hairstyles that add volume to your hair, as this can make your face appear longer. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension.

What face shape do I have?

You can determine your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have these measurements, compare them to determine your face shape.

Hair Care

It is essential to take care of your hair to ensure that it looks its best. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free from dirt and oil.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips in mind, you are ready to choose the perfect hairstyle for your small face. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. Consult with a hairstylist if you need additional guidance. With the right hairstyle, you can enhance your facial features and look your best.
