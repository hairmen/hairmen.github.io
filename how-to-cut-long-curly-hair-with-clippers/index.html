  3. Section your hair using clips or hair ties. Divide your hair into four sections: front, back, and two sides.
  4. Start with the back section. Use the longest guard on your clippers to trim the hair evenly. Move the clippers from the nape of your neck to the crown of your head. Use a mirror to check the length and adjust as needed.
  5. Move to the sides and use the same guard length as the back. Trim the hair from the bottom to the top, blending the hair with the back section.
  6. Trim the top section using a longer guard than the sides and back. Use scissors to blend the hair with the sides and back. Start at the front and work your way to the back, cutting small sections at a time.

  8. Remove the guard from your clippers and use them to trim around the edges and neck. Use scissors to clean up any stray hairs.
  9. Style your hair as desired.
  10. Clean your tools and store them properly.
