How To Keep My Relaxed Hair Healthy A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have relaxed hair, you know how important it is to keep it healthy. Relaxed hair is susceptible to breakage, heat damage, and dryness, which can lead to split ends and hair loss. But with the right care, you can keep your relaxed hair healthy and beautiful. In this article, we'll provide you with a complete guide on how to keep your relaxed hair healthy.

How to Keep My Relaxed Hair Healthy

The key to keeping your relaxed hair healthy is to provide it with the right care. This includes using the right products, avoiding harsh treatments, and keeping your hair moisturized. Here are some tips to help you keep your relaxed hair healthy:

1. Use a Mild Shampoo

When washing your hair, use a mild shampoo that is formulated for relaxed hair. Avoid shampoos that contain sulfates, which can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness. Look for shampoos that contain moisturizing ingredients, such as shea butter, coconut oil, or argan oil.

2. Deep Condition Your Hair Regularly

Deep conditioning is essential for keeping your relaxed hair moisturized and healthy. You should deep condition your hair at least once a week. Look for deep conditioners that contain keratin, which can help strengthen your hair and reduce breakage.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can cause damage to your relaxed hair, so it's best to avoid it as much as possible. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools to once a week or less.

4. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help prevent split ends and breakage. Aim to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and strong.

Step-by-Step Guide to Healthy Relaxed Hair

Follow these steps to keep your relaxed hair healthy:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a mild shampoo that is formulated for relaxed hair. Rinse thoroughly.

Step 2: Deep Condition Your Hair

Apply a deep conditioner to your hair and let it sit for 20-30 minutes. Rinse thoroughly.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help keep it moisturized.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and limit your use of heat styling tools to once a week or less. Style your hair as desired.

Step 5: Trim Your Hair

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Relaxed Hair

Here are some additional tips for maintaining healthy relaxed hair:

1. Protect Your Hair at Night

Wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf at night to protect it from friction and breakage.

2. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid hairstyles that pull on your hair, such as tight braids or ponytails. This can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals can help keep your hair healthy and strong.

Frequently Asked Questions About Relaxed Hair Care

1. How often should I wash my relaxed hair?

You should wash your relaxed hair once a week to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Can I color my relaxed hair?

Yes, you can color your relaxed hair. However, it's important to use a color that is formulated for relaxed hair and to avoid over-processing your hair.

3. Can I use a flat iron on my relaxed hair?

Yes, you can use a flat iron on your relaxed hair. However, it's important to use a heat protectant spray and to limit your use of heat styling tools to once a week or less.

Additional Hair Care Tips for Relaxed Hair

Here are some additional tips for keeping your relaxed hair healthy:

1. Drink Plenty of Water

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your hair hydrated from the inside out.

2. Avoid Chemical Treatments

Avoid chemical treatments, such as perms or relaxers, as these can cause damage to your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

4. Be Gentle with Your Hair

Be gentle with your hair and avoid pulling or tugging on it. This can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

By following these tips, you can keep your relaxed hair healthy and beautiful. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and your hair will thank you in the long run.
