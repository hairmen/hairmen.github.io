How To Fix Pixie Cut Wig Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are a fan of short hair, then the pixie cut wig is the perfect choice for you. It is a low-maintenance wig style that looks trendy and fashionable. However, after wearing it for a while, it can become a bit messy and tangled. That's why we created this guide to show you how to fix pixie cut wig and keep it looking fresh and fabulous.

How to Fix Pixie Cut Wig

If you own a pixie cut wig, then you may have experienced some challenges in keeping it looking great. Here are some tips to help you fix your wig:

1. Detangle the Wig

The first step in fixing your pixie cut wig is to detangle it. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently comb through the wig, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots. Be careful not to pull the hair too hard, as this can damage the wig.

2. Wash the Wig

If your wig is looking dull or greasy, it may be time to wash it. Fill a sink with cool water and add a small amount of wig shampoo. Gently swirl the wig in the water, being careful not to rub or twist the hair. Rinse the wig thoroughly with cool water and gently squeeze out the excess water.

3. Style the Wig

Once your wig is clean and dry, it's time to style it. Use a wig comb or brush to style the hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the ends. You can also use a styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to add volume or hold to your wig.

Step by Step Guide to Fix Pixie Cut Wig

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you fix your pixie cut wig:

Step 1: Detangle the Wig

Start by detangling the wig using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers. Be gentle and work your way up from the ends to the roots.

Step 2: Wash the Wig

Fill a sink with cool water and add a small amount of wig shampoo. Gently swirl the wig in the water, being careful not to rub or twist the hair. Rinse the wig thoroughly with cool water and gently squeeze out the excess water.

Step 3: Dry the Wig

Lay the wig flat on a towel and gently pat it dry. Avoid rubbing or twisting the hair, as this can cause damage. Once the wig is mostly dry, you can use a wig stand to finish drying it.

Step 4: Style the Wig

Use a wig comb or brush to style the hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the ends. You can also use a styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to add volume or hold to your wig.

Tips for Fixing Pixie Cut Wig

Here are some additional tips to help you fix your pixie cut wig:

1. Store Your Wig Properly

When you're not wearing your wig, store it on a wig stand or in a wig box to keep it in good condition.

2. Avoid Heat

Avoid using heat on your wig, such as blow dryers, curling irons, or straighteners. These can damage the hair and cause frizz.

3. Use Wig Products

Use products that are specifically designed for wigs, such as wig shampoo, conditioner, and styling products. These will help keep your wig looking great for longer.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my pixie cut wig?

A: It is not recommended to dye your wig, as the hair may become damaged or discolored. If you want a different hair color, consider purchasing a new wig in the desired shade.

Q: How often should I wash my wig?

A: It depends on how often you wear your wig and how much styling product you use. As a general rule, you should wash your wig every 6-8 wears, or as needed.

Hair Care for Pixie Cut Wig

Here are some additional hair care tips for your pixie cut wig:

1. Store Your Wig Properly

When you're not wearing your wig, store it on a wig stand or in a wig box to keep it in good condition.

2. Use a Wig Brush or Comb

Use a wig brush or comb to style your wig, as regular brushes and combs can damage the hair.

3. Avoid Heat

Avoid using heat on your wig, such as blow dryers, curling irons, or straighteners. These can damage the hair and cause frizz.

In conclusion, fixing your pixie cut wig is not as difficult as it seems. With the right tools and techniques, you can keep your wig looking fresh and fabulous for longer. Remember to detangle, wash, and style your wig regularly, and follow our tips for hair care and maintenance. Now, you're ready to rock your pixie cut wig with confidence!
