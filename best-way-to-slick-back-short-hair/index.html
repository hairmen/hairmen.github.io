Best Way to Slick Back Short Hair

If you have short hair and want to achieve a slicked-back look, you may be wondering what the best way to do it is. In this article, we will guide you through the process of slicking back short hair so that you can achieve a polished and sophisticated look.

Description

The slicked-back hairstyle has been popular for decades and is still a favorite among men and women today. This hairstyle involves combing the hair back so that it is flat against the head and held in place with a styling product. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn to formal events or as an everyday look.

How To Slick Back Short Hair

The best way to slick back short hair is to follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt or excess oil from your scalp and hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Pat your hair dry with a towel until it is slightly damp. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as gel or pomade. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to focus on the roots and ends.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair Back

Using a fine-toothed comb, comb your hair back starting from the front and working your way to the back. Make sure to comb your hair in the direction of the natural growth pattern.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is slicked back, you can style it to your liking. You can create a part or leave it as is. You can also use a hairdryer to add volume and shape to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best slicked-back hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality styling product that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find the perfect look for you.
  • Don't overdo it with the product. A little goes a long way, and too much product can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Be gentle when combing your hair back to avoid damaging or breaking it.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicking back short hair:

Q: Can I slick back my hair if it is too short?

A: Yes, you can still achieve a slicked-back look even if your hair is very short. Just use a small amount of product and comb your hair back as best you can.

Q: How long will the slicked-back hairstyle last?

A: The length of time that the hairstyle will last depends on the product you use and your hair type. Generally, the hairstyle should last throughout the day.

Q: Is the slicked-back hairstyle appropriate for all occasions?

A: The slicked-back hairstyle is a versatile look that can be worn to a variety of occasions, from formal events to casual outings.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and attractive appearance. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in good condition:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

By following these tips and using the best products and techniques, you can achieve a slicked-back hairstyle that looks polished and sophisticated.
