Best Short Haircut For Chubby Round Face

Written by William Sep 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your round face and looking for a haircut that can make it look slimmer? Look no further because in this article, we will be discussing the best short haircut for chubby round face. Whether you have straight or curly hair, we have got you covered. Keep reading to find out more!

Description

Having a round face can be challenging when it comes to finding the right haircut. It's essential to choose a style that can make your face look slimmer and elongated. Short haircuts are the best option for chubby round faces because they can emphasize your features and make your face look more defined. However, not all short haircuts are suitable for round faces. You need to choose a style that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features.

How to

The first step in finding the best short haircut for chubby round face is to determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and examine the shape of your face. If your face is widest at the cheeks and ears and has a rounded chin, you have a round face. Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to choose a haircut that flatters your features.

Step by Step

Step 1: Consult with your hairstylist and show them pictures of short haircuts that you like.

Step 2: Choose a style that has layers and texture as they can add volume and dimension to your hair.

Step 3: Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs as they can make your face look rounder.

Step 4: Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.

Step 5: Emphasize your best features such as your eyes or cheekbones by adding layers around them.

Tips

When choosing a short haircut for chubby round face, keep the following tips in mind:

  • Choose a style that has volume and texture
  • Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights
  • Emphasize your best features by adding layers around them
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best

FAQ

Q: Can I get a pixie cut if I have a chubby round face?

A: Yes, you can get a pixie cut if you have a chubby round face. However, make sure that the cut has volume and texture and is not too short.

Q: Are bob haircuts suitable for round faces?

A: Yes, bob haircuts can be suitable for round faces as long as they are layered and textured.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking fresh and stylish. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid over-drying it.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.
  • Use a light styling product to add volume and texture to your hair.

Now that you know the best short haircut for chubby round face, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and get a new look that flatters your features. Remember to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
