How To Cut A Man s Wavy Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you someone who wants to cut a man's wavy hair with scissors? If so, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of how to cut a man's wavy hair with scissors in a relaxed, easy-to-understand language. We'll cover everything from the tools you'll need, to the techniques you should use, to the best ways to care for wavy hair after the cut. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Tools
  2. Preparation
  3. Sectioning
  4. Cutting
  5. Finishing
  6. Tips
  7. FAQ
  8. Hair Care

Tools

The first thing you'll need is a good pair of scissors. Make sure they're sharp and designed for cutting hair. You'll also need a comb, hair clips, and a spray bottle filled with water.

Preparation

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be difficult to cut, and it's harder to see the length and shape of the hair when it's wet.

Next, decide on the length of the hair you want to cut. It's best to start with a longer length and gradually go shorter if needed.

Sectioning

Start by sectioning the hair. Use the comb to create a part in the middle of the head, from the forehead to the nape of the neck. Then, create another part from ear to ear. You should now have four sections of hair.

Clip the top two sections of hair out of the way, leaving just the bottom two sections.

Cutting

Start cutting the hair in the bottom section. Hold a small section of hair between your fingers and use the scissors to cut off the desired length.

Move on to the next section of hair and repeat the process. When you're finished with the bottom sections, unclip the top sections and repeat the process.

Remember to cut the hair at an angle to create layers and texture.

Finishing

After you've finished cutting, use the comb to check for any uneven sections. Use the scissors to trim any areas that need it.

Finally, style the hair as desired.

Tips

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting off too much at once.
  • Use a light touch when cutting to avoid creating choppy layers.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting hair can be tiring, and you don't want to rush and make mistakes.

FAQ

How often should I cut wavy hair?

It depends on the length and style of the hair. As a general rule, wavy hair should be cut every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I cut wavy hair when it's wet?

Yes, you can, but it's better to cut wavy hair when it's dry. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut, and it's harder to see the length and shape of the hair when it's wet.

Hair Care

After you've cut the hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for wavy hair to keep it moisturized and healthy.

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage the hair. If you do use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Finally, consider getting regular trims to keep the hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

That's it! We hope this guide has been helpful in teaching you how to cut a man's wavy hair with scissors. Remember to take your time, be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
