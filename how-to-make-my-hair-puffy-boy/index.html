How To Make My Hair Puffy Boy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 12, 2023 · 4 min read
If you're a boy with medium to long hair, you may be wondering how to make your hair look puffy and voluminous. The good news is, it's easier than you think! In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve the puffy boy hair look.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Make My Hair Puffy Boy
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Puffy Boy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Puffy Boy Hair

How to Make My Hair Puffy Boy

To achieve the puffy boy hair look, you'll need to follow a few simple steps. These include: - Blow drying your hair upside down - Using a volumizing mousse or spray - Teasing your hair at the roots - Using a round brush to create volume - Applying hairspray to hold the style in place

Step by Step Guide

1. Start with freshly washed and towel-dried hair. 2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots and mid-lengths. This will help to add volume and hold to your hair. 3. Flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair using a medium heat setting. Use your fingers to lift and separate your hair as you blow dry. This will help to create volume and texture. 4. Once your hair is dry, flip your head back up and use a round brush to create volume at the roots. 5. Take small sections of hair and tease them at the roots using a teasing comb. This will create even more volume and texture. 6. Use your round brush to smooth out any tangles or knots. 7. Apply a strong hold hairspray to your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. 8. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, puffy look.

Tips for Maintaining Puffy Boy Hair

- Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it harder to style. - Use a dry shampoo in between washes to refresh your hair and add volume. - Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help maintain the puffy look overnight. - Avoid using heavy conditioners or oils, as these can weigh your hair down and make it harder to style.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve the puffy boy hair look if I have short hair? A: While the puffy boy hair look is typically associated with medium to long hair, you can still add volume and texture to short hair by using a volumizing mousse or spray and teasing your hair at the roots. Q: Will blow drying my hair upside down damage it? A: Blow drying your hair upside down can be drying, so it's important to use a heat protectant spray and not to use too much heat.

Hair Care for Puffy Boy Hair

To keep your puffy boy hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes: - Using a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh your hair down. - Avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair. - Getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. - Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to hydrate and nourish your hair. With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the puffy boy hair look that you've been dreaming of. Remember to take good care of your hair and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
