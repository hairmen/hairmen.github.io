Shoulder Length Hair With Long Side Swept Bangs A Guide To Perfecting The Look

Written by Aprilia Oct 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on achieving the perfect look with shoulder length hair and long side swept bangs. This hairstyle is a timeless classic that has been around for decades, and it's still as popular as ever. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to style it, tips, and hair care. So, let's get started!

Description

Shoulder length hair with long side swept bangs is a versatile and chic hairstyle that suits almost everyone. The hair is cut to just above the shoulders, with a slight taper towards the ends. The bangs are long and swept to one side, framing the face beautifully. This hairstyle can be worn straight or curled, and it looks great with both casual and formal outfits.

How to Achieve the Look

If you're interested in getting this hairstyle, here's how you can achieve it:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in achieving this hairstyle is to choose the right length for your hair. Shoulder length hair is typically defined as hair that falls between the collarbone and the shoulders. This length is perfect for those who want a shorter hairstyle that's still long enough to style in different ways.

Step 2: Get the Right Cut

To achieve the perfect look, it's important to get the right cut. You'll want to ask your hair stylist for a shoulder-length cut with long, side-swept bangs. The bangs should be cut to just below the eyebrows and should be angled to the side.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you've got the right cut, it's time to style your hair. You can wear your hair straight or curled, depending on your preference. If you want to wear it straight, simply blow dry your hair and use a flat iron to straighten it. If you want to wear it curled, use a curling iron to create loose waves.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you perfect this hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to protect your hair from damage
  • Use a volumizing spray at the roots to add volume and lift to your hair
  • Use a hairspray to keep your hair in place throughout the day

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length hair with long side swept bangs:

Q: Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this hairstyle is suitable for all face shapes. However, you may need to adjust the length and angle of the bangs to suit your face shape.

Q: How often do I need to get my hair cut?

A: You should get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the length and shape of the hairstyle.

Q: Can I wear this hairstyle to a formal event?

A: Yes, this hairstyle is versatile and can be worn to both casual and formal events. Simply style your hair accordingly.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair with this hairstyle:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of the hairstyle

Conclusion

Shoulder length hair with long side swept bangs is a classic and timeless hairstyle that's perfect for almost any occasion. Whether you're going to a formal event or just hanging out with friends, this hairstyle is versatile and chic. With the right cut, styling, and hair care, you can achieve the perfect look with this hairstyle. So, go ahead and give it a try!
