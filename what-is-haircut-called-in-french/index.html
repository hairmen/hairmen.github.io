What Is Haircut Called In French

Written by Luffy Nov 23, 2023 · 3 min read

Welcome to our blog article in 2023 about “What is Haircut Called in French”. In this article, we will discuss the French term for haircut, how to ask for a haircut in French, and some tips and FAQs related to hair care. Let’s dive in!

Table of Contents:

  • Types of Haircuts in French
  • Tips for a Great Haircut

French Term for Haircut

The French term for haircut is “coupe de cheveux”. It literally means “cut of hair”.

Asking for a Haircut in French

If you are in France and want to get a haircut, you can use the following phrases to ask for it:

  • Je voudrais une coupe de cheveux, s’il vous plaît. (I would like a haircut, please.)
  • Je veux me faire couper les cheveux. (I want to get my hair cut.)

It is also important to mention the length and style you want. You can use the following phrases to describe your desired haircut:

  • Je veux les cheveux courts. (I want short hair.)
  • Je veux une frange. (I want bangs.)
  • Je veux une coupe dégradée. (I want a layered cut.)

Types of Haircuts in French

There are various types of haircuts in French. Some of the popular ones are:

  • Coupe au carré (Bob haircut)
  • Coupe à la garçonne (Pixie haircut)
  • Coupe dégradée (Layered cut)
  • Coupe boule (Bowl cut)
  • Coupe mi-longue (Medium length haircut)

Tips for a Great Haircut

Here are some tips that you can follow to get a great haircut:

  • Bring a picture of the hairstyle you want.
  • Communicate clearly with your hairdresser.
  • Choose a haircut that suits your face shape.
  • Consider the texture and thickness of your hair.

FAQs related to Haircuts

Here are some frequently asked questions related to haircuts:

Q. How often should I get a haircut?

A. It depends on your hair type and the length of your hair. Generally, it is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

Q. Should I wash my hair before a haircut?

A. It is not necessary to wash your hair before a haircut. In fact, it is better to leave your hair unwashed for a day or two before the haircut.

Q. How can I make my haircut last longer?

A. You can make your haircut last longer by using the right hair care products, avoiding heat styling, and getting regular trims.

Hair Care after a Haircut

After getting a haircut, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.

We hope this article has helped you understand what a haircut is called in French and how to ask for it. If you have any questions or suggestions, feel free to comment below. Happy hairstyling!
