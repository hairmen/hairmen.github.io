Should I Get Bangs With Wavy Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Oct 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking about getting bangs but not sure if they'll work with your wavy hair? You're not alone! Bangs can be a great way to change up your look, but they can also be tricky to style and maintain. In this guide, we'll answer all your questions about whether or not you should get bangs with wavy hair and give you some tips for making them work.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Bangs can be a great way to change up your look and add some interest to your hairstyle. However, they can also be a bit high maintenance, especially if you have wavy hair. Wavy hair tends to have a mind of its own, and bangs can easily become unruly if not properly styled.

That being said, if you're willing to put in the effort, bangs can be a great addition to your wavy hairstyle. They can help frame your face and add some dimension to your look. Just make sure you're prepared for the upkeep!

How To

Before you jump into getting bangs, there are a few things you should consider. First, think about the shape of your face. Bangs can be flattering on almost any face shape, but it's important to choose the right style for you.

Next, think about your hair texture. Wavy hair can work well with bangs, but it's important to choose a style that complements your natural texture. Some bang styles may require more maintenance than others.

Step by Step

If you've decided to take the plunge and get bangs, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Choose a style that works with your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Find a reputable stylist who has experience cutting bangs for wavy hair.
  3. Communicate your desired length and style to your stylist.
  4. Be prepared to style your bangs daily using a blow dryer and/or flat iron.
  5. Invest in some good styling products to keep your bangs looking their best.

Tips

Here are some tips for making bangs work with wavy hair:

  • Choose a style that allows your natural texture to shine through. A blunt cut may require more styling than a softer, layered cut.
  • Use a blow dryer and/or flat iron to style your bangs, but be careful not to overheat your hair.
  • Invest in some good styling products, such as a heat protectant spray and a smoothing serum.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape of your bangs.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting bangs with wavy hair:

Will bangs work with my face shape?

Almost any face shape can work with bangs, but it's important to choose the right style. Consult with your stylist to find a style that flatters your face shape.

How often will I need to trim my bangs?

This will depend on the style of your bangs and how fast your hair grows. Generally, you'll need to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape.

What styling products should I use?

Invest in a good heat protectant spray and a smoothing serum to keep your bangs looking their best. You may also want to use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add some volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your wavy hair and bangs looking their best, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your natural oils.
  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Overall, getting bangs with wavy hair can be a great way to change up your look and add some interest to your hairstyle. Just be prepared for the upkeep and follow a good hair care routine to keep your hair looking its best.
