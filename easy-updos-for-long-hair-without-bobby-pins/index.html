Easy Updos For Long Hair Without Bobby Pins

Written by Luffy Sep 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of using bobby pins for your updos? Do you want to try a new hairstyle without using any pins? You've come to the right place! In this article, we will show you some easy updos for long hair without bobby pins.

Description

Bobby pins are commonly used to hold updos in place, but they can be uncomfortable and cause headaches. There are many ways to create updos without using bobby pins, and they can look just as stylish and elegant. These hairstyles are perfect for formal events, weddings, or even everyday wear.

How To

Here are some easy updos for long hair without bobby pins:

1. Messy Bun

First, gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band. Twist the hair around the base of the ponytail to create a bun. Use another elastic band to secure the bun in place. Pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look. You can also use a hair tie or scrunchie to secure the bun.

2. French Twist

Start by gathering your hair at the nape of your neck. Twist the hair upwards, and use your fingers to tuck the ends of the hair into the twist. Use a hair clip or comb to hold the twist in place. You can also use hairspray to keep the twist secure.

3. Rope Braid Updo

Part your hair down the middle and create two ponytails. Twist each ponytail into a rope braid, and wrap them around each other to create a bun. Use an elastic band to secure the bun in place. Pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions for creating a messy bun:

Step 1: Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band.

Step 2: Twist the hair around the base of the ponytail to create a bun.

Step 3: Use another elastic band to secure the bun in place.

Step 4: Pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look.

Step 5: Use a hair tie or scrunchie to secure the bun.

Tips

Here are some tips for creating updos without bobby pins:

- Use hair ties or scrunchies instead of bobby pins.

- Use hairspray to keep your updo in place.

- Pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look.

- Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I create updos without bobby pins?

A: Yes, there are many ways to create updos without using bobby pins.

Q: Are updos without bobby pins comfortable?

A: Yes, updos without bobby pins can be comfortable and cause less headaches.

Hair Care

When creating updos without bobby pins, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

- Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

- Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools on your hair.

- Don't use too much hairspray or other styling products, as they can dry out your hair.

Now that you know how to create updos without bobby pins, you can try out these hairstyles for yourself. Whether you're going to a formal event or just want to change up your everyday hairstyle, these updos are easy and stylish.
