How To Know What Hair Color Would Look Best On You

Written by Eveline Jun 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. By following a few simple tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hair color that will enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and fabulous. In this article, we'll go over how to know what hair color would look best on you, step by step, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before you choose a hair color, you need to consider your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. These factors will help you determine which hair colors will look best on you. Here's how to know what hair color would look best on you:

1. Determine your skin tone

The first step in choosing the right hair color is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone is the natural color of your skin, and it can be warm, cool, or neutral. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell whether they're blue or green, you have a neutral skin tone.

2. Consider your eye color

Your eye color can also help you determine which hair color will look best on you. If you have blue or green eyes, warm hair colors like blonde, copper, and red will look great on you. If you have brown or hazel eyes, cool hair colors like ash blonde, ash brown, and black will look best.

3. Look at your natural hair color

Your natural hair color can also help you determine which hair color will look best on you. If you have warm undertones in your hair, warm hair colors like golden blonde, honey blonde, and auburn will look great on you. If you have cool undertones in your hair, cool hair colors like ash blonde, ash brown, and platinum blonde will look best.

Step by Step

Now that you know how to determine your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color, here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the right hair color:

1. Determine your skin tone

Use the vein test to determine whether you have a cool, warm, or neutral skin tone.

2. Consider your eye color

If you have blue or green eyes, choose warm hair colors like blonde, copper, and red. If you have brown or hazel eyes, choose cool hair colors like ash blonde, ash brown, and black.

3. Look at your natural hair color

If you have warm undertones in your hair, choose warm hair colors like golden blonde, honey blonde, and auburn. If you have cool undertones in your hair, choose cool hair colors like ash blonde, ash brown, and platinum blonde.

4. Consider your lifestyle

When choosing a hair color, consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule and don't have time for regular touch-ups, choose a low-maintenance hair color. If you're willing to put in the time and effort to maintain your hair color, choose a high-maintenance hair color.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hair color:

  • Don't be afraid to try something new. If you're feeling adventurous, try a bold new hair color that you've never tried before.
  • Consider your wardrobe. Choose a hair color that complements the colors you wear most often.
  • Consult a professional. If you're unsure about which hair color to choose, consult a professional hairstylist who can help you find the perfect color.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hair color:

What hair color looks best on olive skin?

Warm hair colors like golden blonde, honey blonde, and auburn look great on olive skin.

What hair color looks best on fair skin?

Cool hair colors like ash blonde, ash brown, and platinum blonde look best on fair skin.

What hair color looks best on dark skin?

Warm hair colors like copper, red, and dark brown look great on dark skin.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen the perfect hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair color from fading.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause your color to fade.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hair color that will enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and fabulous.
