How To Shave A Man s Hair With Clippers

Written by Bardi Sep 12, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of going to the barber and spending a lot of money for a simple haircut? Or do you want to try your hand at giving your partner or friend a haircut? Well, you can do so with clippers. Clippers are an excellent tool for cutting hair, and with a little practice, you can give a professional-looking haircut. In this article, we'll show you how to shave a man's hair with clippers.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first thing you need to do is purchase a good quality clipper. You can find them at most beauty supply stores or online. Once you have the clipper, you need to choose the right blade guard. The blade guard determines how much hair will be cut. The lower the number, the shorter the hair will be.

Next, you need to prepare the hair for cutting. Make sure the hair is clean and dry. Comb it out to remove any tangles or knots. If the hair is long, you can use scissors to trim it down to a manageable length.

Before you start cutting, make sure the clipper is oiled and the blades are sharp. Dull blades can pull and tug at the hair, which can be painful for the person getting the haircut.

Step by Step

Now that you have everything ready, it's time to start cutting. Follow these steps:

  1. Attach the blade guard to the clipper.
  2. Start at the back of the head and work your way up to the crown.
  3. Use long, even strokes to cut the hair. Start at the base of the head and move the clipper upward.
  4. Use the same stroke on the sides of the head, starting at the bottom and moving upward.
  5. Use a smaller blade guard to trim around the ears and neckline.
  6. Blend the hair using a blending comb. This will help create a smoother transition between the longer and shorter hair.
  7. Use scissors to trim any long hairs that the clipper missed.
  8. Finish the haircut by trimming the sideburns and cleaning up any stray hairs.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using clippers:

  • Start with a longer blade guard and work your way down to a shorter one. This will help you avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle. This will help create a more natural-looking haircut.
  • Always oil the clipper before use to ensure it runs smoothly.
  • Don't forget to clean the clipper after use. Use a soft brush to remove any hair and a cloth to wipe it down.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about using clippers:

  • Can I use clippers on wet hair?
  • How often should I oil my clipper?
  • What should I do if the clipper gets too hot?
  • Can I use the same clipper on different people?

Hair Care

After giving a haircut, it's important to take care of the hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash the hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on the hair for a few days after the haircut.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to add shine and moisture to the hair.
  • Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape.

Now you know how to shave a man's hair with clippers. With a little practice, you can save money and give a professional-looking haircut. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully. Happy cutting!
