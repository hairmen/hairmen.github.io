Should I Get Bangs if I Have Curly Hair? | Tips, How-to, and FAQ

If you are a curly-haired person, then you know how challenging it can be to maintain your hair. You may have been contemplating getting bangs to switch up your look, but you may be hesitant to do so because of your curls. The answer is yes, you can get bangs if you have curly hair! But, there are some things you should consider before going through with it. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about getting bangs for your curly hair.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Getting bangs can be a significant change, especially if you've never had them before. It can frame your face differently and give you a fresh new look. However, if you have curly hair, there are a few things to consider before getting bangs. Your curly hair may react differently than straight hair, and you'll need to know how to care for your bangs properly.

How to

The first thing you should do when considering getting bangs is to research different styles that will suit your curly hair. You can search online for inspiration or speak to your hairstylist to see what options are available to you. Once you've decided on a style, make sure to bring a photo to your hairstylist to ensure that they understand your vision.

Step by Step

Getting bangs is a commitment, and you'll need to prepare yourself for the upkeep that comes with it. Here are the steps to take when getting bangs for curly hair:

  1. Choose a style that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Speak to your hairstylist and bring a photo of your desired look.
  3. Ensure that your hairstylist understands how to cut curly hair.
  4. Ask your hairstylist to cut your bangs longer than you want them to be, as curly hair tends to shrink when dry.
  5. Make sure to communicate with your hairstylist about how you want your bangs to be styled.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you care for your curly hair with bangs:

  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer with a diffuser attachment to help enhance your curls.
  • Use a light hold styling product to keep your bangs in place without weighing them down.
  • Consider booking regular trims with your hairstylist to maintain the shape of your bangs.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently, as they can damage your curls.

FAQ

Will bangs work for all curly hair types?

Yes, bangs can work for all curly hair types. However, you will need to choose a style that suits your hair texture and face shape.

How often should I trim my bangs?

You should trim your bangs every four to six weeks to maintain their shape.

Can I straighten my bangs?

Yes, you can straighten your bangs, but it's important to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care

Here are some additional tips for caring for curly hair with bangs:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your curls.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Now that you know everything you need to know about getting bangs with curly hair, you can make an informed decision about whether or not to go through with it. Remember, getting bangs is a commitment, but it can also be a fun way to switch up your look and frame your face differently.
