Long Hair With Short Choppy Layers On Top A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that can add some edge to your long locks? Consider the long hair with short choppy layers on top. This trendy haircut can give you a fresh and modern look without sacrificing the length of your hair. In this blog post, we will guide you through everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, tips to maintain it, and answers to frequently asked questions. Let's dive in!

Description of Long Hair with Short Choppy Layers on Top

The long hair with short choppy layers on top is a haircut that features long hair with short, textured layers on the crown of the head. The layers are cut in a choppy way, meaning that they are uneven and jagged to create a messy and edgy look. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add volume and dimension to their hair while keeping the length.

How to Get Long Hair with Short Choppy Layers on Top

If you are interested in getting long hair with short choppy layers on top, here is how you can achieve the look:

Step 1: Find a Good Hair Salon

The first step is to find a reputable hair salon that has experience in cutting this type of hairstyle. Look for salons that have positive reviews and photos of their previous work.

Step 2: Consult with Your Stylist

Before you get the haircut, consult with your stylist about the length and the placement of the layers. They can recommend the best options based on your hair type, face shape, and personal preferences.

Step 3: Cut the Layers

Once you have agreed on the style, your stylist will start cutting the layers on the crown of your head. They will use scissors to create a choppy and textured look, which can add volume and movement to your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

To style your hair, you can use a texturizing spray or a sea salt spray to enhance the layers and create a messy and tousled look. You can also use a curling iron or a straightener to add some waves or curls to your hair.

Tips to Maintain Long Hair with Short Choppy Layers on Top

Here are some tips to keep your long hair with short choppy layers on top looking fresh and stylish:

Tip 1: Regular Trims

To maintain the shape and texture of the layers, it is essential to get regular trims every six to eight weeks. This can also prevent split ends and breakage.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair

Use heat protectant products before using hot tools on your hair to prevent damage. You can also wear a hat or a scarf to protect your hair from the sun and wind.

Tip 3: Use Quality Hair Products

Invest in quality hair products that can nourish and strengthen your hair, such as a hydrating shampoo and conditioner, a hair mask, and a leave-in conditioner.

FAQ about Long Hair with Short Choppy Layers on Top

Q: Is this hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

A: This hairstyle can work for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, your stylist may need to adjust the length and the placement of the layers based on your hair texture and thickness.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with this haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with this haircut. However, the layers on top may create a messy and undone look, which can add some edge to your ponytail.

Q: How long does it take to style this hairstyle?

A: The styling time can vary depending on the length and the thickness of your hair. On average, it can take around 10 to 20 minutes to style this haircut.

Hair Care for Long Hair with Short Choppy Layers on Top

To keep your long hair with short choppy layers on top healthy and shiny, here are some hair care tips:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every two to three days with a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and moisturized.

Tip 2: Avoid Overusing Hot Tools

Minimize the use of hot tools such as blow dryers, curling irons, and straighteners to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair at Night

Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or use a silk pillowcase to prevent tangles and breakage while you sleep.

In conclusion, the long hair with short choppy layers on top is a versatile and trendy haircut that can add some edge to your long locks. It is easy to maintain and style, and it can work for most hair types. If you are interested in trying this hairstyle, consult with a professional stylist to get the best results. Follow our tips and hair care advice to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Happy styling!
