How To Choose A Hairstyle For Your Face

Written by Robby Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing a hairstyle that complements your face shape can be a daunting task. You might have seen a hairstyle that looks fabulous on someone else, but it doesn't look as great on you. The reason is that different face shapes require different hairstyles. In this article, we will guide you on how to choose a hairstyle for your face shape.

Identify Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a hairstyle for your face shape is to identify your face shape. There are six different face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and examine your face in the mirror. Look for the widest part of your face, the shape of your jawline, and the length of your face.

If the width of your forehead is the same as the width of your cheekbones and your jawline, you have an oval face shape. If your face is as long as it is wide and your jawline is rounded, you have a round face shape. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all the same width and your jawline is square, you have a square face shape. If your forehead is wider than your cheekbones and your jawline is pointed, you have a heart-shaped face. If your cheekbones are wider than your forehead and your jawline is narrow, you have a diamond-shaped face. If your face is longer than it is wide and your jawline is squared, you have an oblong face shape.

Hairstyles for Different Face Shapes

Now that you have identified your face shape, it's time to choose a hairstyle that complements it. Here are some hairstyles for different face shapes:

  • Oval face shape - You can rock any hairstyle, but a shoulder-length cut with layers is perfect for an oval face shape.
  • Round face shape - Long, layered hair or a short, pixie cut can look great on a round face shape.
  • Square face shape - Soft waves and layers can soften a square jawline.
  • Heart-shaped face - A side-swept bang or a lob with layers can balance out a heart-shaped face.
  • Diamond-shaped face - A chin-length bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can accentuate your cheekbones.
  • Oblong face shape - A chin-length bob or a shoulder-length cut with layers can add volume to an oblong face shape.

Tips for Choosing a Hairstyle

Here are some tips that can help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness before choosing a hairstyle.
  • Think about the maintenance required for the hairstyle you choose.
  • Consult a hairstylist before making a drastic change to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a hairstyle:

Can I pull off a short haircut if I have a round face shape?

Yes, you can pull off a short haircut if you have a round face shape. Opt for a short cut with layers or a pixie cut to elongate your face.

What hairstyle is best for a square face shape?

Soft waves and layers can soften a square jawline. A shoulder-length cut with layers can also be flattering for a square face shape.

Can I wear bangs if I have a heart-shaped face?

Yes, you can wear bangs if you have a heart-shaped face. A side-swept bang can balance out a heart-shaped face.

Hair Care Tips

Choosing the right hairstyle is just one part of having great hair. Here are some hair care tips that can help you maintain healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo that works for your hair type.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use hair masks and oils to nourish your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

Choosing a hairstyle that complements your face shape can be a fun process. Use the tips and guidelines in this article to find the perfect hairstyle for you!
