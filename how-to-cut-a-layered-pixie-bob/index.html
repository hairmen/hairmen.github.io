How To Cut A Layered Pixie Bob A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut that's both stylish and practical, a layered pixie bob might be just what you need. This versatile haircut can be tailored to suit any face shape, hair type, and personal style. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of cutting a layered pixie bob, from selecting the right tools and products to finishing touches and hair care tips. Let's get started!

Description

A layered pixie bob is a short haircut that typically features layers of varying lengths around the crown and sides, with longer pieces in the front. This creates a textured, voluminous look that can be styled in many different ways. A pixie bob is a great choice for anyone who wants to make a bold statement with their hair or who simply wants a low-maintenance cut that's easy to style.

How to Cut a Layered Pixie Bob

Step 1: Gather Your Tools and Products

Before you begin cutting your hair, you'll need to gather the right tools and products. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, hair clips or elastics, and a spray bottle filled with water. You may also want to have some styling products on hand, such as hair gel or mousse.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: the crown, the sides, and the back. Use hair clips or elastics to keep each section separate.

Step 3: Begin Cutting

Starting with the back section, take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Use your scissors to cut straight across, following the natural curve of your head. Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way up to the crown.

Next, move on to the side sections. Comb each section straight down and cut diagonally, following the shape of your head. Be sure to cut each side evenly.

Finally, move to the crown section. Comb your hair straight up and cut vertically, using the previous layers as a guide. This will create the layered, textured look of a pixie bob.

Step 4: Refine Your Cut

Once you've finished cutting, take a step back and assess your work. If you notice any uneven sections or stray hairs, use your scissors to trim them. You can also use thinning shears to create a more textured look.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Now that your hair is cut, it's time to style it! Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as hair gel or mousse, to create texture and hold. You can also blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind as you cut your hair:

  • Start with clean, dry hair that's free of tangles.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean, even cut.
  • Take your time and work in small sections to avoid mistakes.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and angles to find the perfect look for you.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about cutting a layered pixie bob:

How often should I get my pixie bob cut?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain your cut. As a general rule, you should get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Can I cut a pixie bob myself?

Yes, you can! However, if you're new to cutting hair, it's a good idea to start with a simpler cut and work your way up to a pixie bob. You may also want to consider taking a hair cutting class or watching tutorials online to learn the basics.

Hair Care

Now that you've cut your hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your cut and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut a layered pixie bob that looks great and feels comfortable. Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or a more edgy, textured style, a pixie bob is a versatile and fashionable choice that's sure to turn heads!
