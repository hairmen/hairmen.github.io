How To Get New Hairstyles In Animal Crossing

Written by Petter Oct 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Welcome to the world of Animal Crossing where you can customize your character from head to toe. One of the most exciting things about the game is the ability to change up your hairstyle. In this article, we'll show you how to get new hairstyles in Animal Crossing. From the basics to the more advanced techniques, we've got you covered. So, let's dive in!

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Get New Hairstyles

There are a few ways to get new hairstyles in Animal Crossing. Here are the most popular:

  • Visit Harriet in Shampoodle's Salon
  • Use a Mirror or Vanity
  • Buy a Wig from the Able Sisters

Each method has its own benefits, and we'll go over each one in more detail below.

Step-by-Step Guide

Visit Harriet in Shampoodle's Salon

Shampoodle's is a hair salon run by Harriet the poodle. To unlock Shampoodle's, you'll need to spend at least 10,000 bells at the Able Sisters and have Mabel and Sable visit your town at least once. Once you've met those requirements, Harriet will come to your town and set up shop.

Here's how to get a new hairstyle at Shampoodle's:

  1. Go to Shampoodle's Salon
  2. Sit in the chair and tell Harriet that you want a new hairstyle
  3. Choose a hairstyle from the list of options
  4. Pay 3,000 bells for the new hairstyle

Use a Mirror or Vanity

If you don't want to spend any bells on a new hairstyle, you can use a mirror or vanity to change up your look. To do this:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror or vanity
  2. Press A to interact with the mirror or vanity
  3. Select "Change Look"
  4. Choose a new hairstyle from the list of options

Keep in mind that not all mirrors and vanities will have the "Change Look" option. You'll need to find one that does.

Buy a Wig from the Able Sisters

If you're not ready to commit to a new hairstyle, you can buy a wig from the Able Sisters. The wigs are sold in the back of the store and range in price from 800-1,200 bells.

Here's how to buy a wig from the Able Sisters:

  1. Go to the Able Sisters store
  2. Go to the back of the store
  3. Select the wig you want to purchase
  4. Pay for the wig at the checkout

Tips for Getting the Best Hairstyles

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting a new hairstyle in Animal Crossing:

  • Try on different hairstyles before committing to one
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different colors
  • Consider getting a haircut that matches your character's personality
  • Take care of your hair by using shampoo and conditioner regularly

FAQs

Can I change my hairstyle every day?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle as often as you like.

Do I have to pay real money for new hairstyles?

No, all hairstyles can be purchased with in-game currency.

Hair Care Tips

Just like in real life, it's important to take care of your hair in Animal Crossing. Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use shampoo and conditioner regularly
  • Don't overuse styling products
  • Avoid using heat on your hair too often
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to get the perfect hairstyle for your Animal Crossing character. Have fun experimenting with different looks!
