How To Straighten Men s Thick Wavy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a man with thick and wavy hair, you might find it challenging to style it the way you want. Luckily, there are ways to straighten your hair and achieve a sleek, polished look. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of straightening your hair with a flat iron, as well as provide tips and FAQs to help you get the best results.

How to Straighten Men's Thick Wavy Hair

Straightening men's thick wavy hair can be done in several ways, but using a flat iron is the most popular method. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start straightening your hair, make sure it's dry and clean. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat of the flat iron. Comb your hair to remove any tangles and make it easier to straighten.

Step 2: Heat Up Your Flat Iron

Turn on your flat iron and set the temperature to the appropriate level for your hair type. Thick and wavy hair usually requires a higher temperature than thin hair. Wait for the flat iron to heat up to the desired temperature.

Step 3: Straighten Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections and clip them up. Start straightening the bottom section of your hair first and work your way up. Use the flat iron to clamp down on a small section of hair, then glide it down to the ends. Repeat this process until you've straightened all sections of your hair.

Step 4: Apply Finishing Products

Apply a small amount of hair serum or oil to your hair to add shine and prevent frizz. Use your hands to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed guide on how to straighten men's thick wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the heat of the flat iron.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-toothed comb to remove any tangles in your hair. Divide your hair into four sections: top, sides, and back. Clip each section up so you can work on one section at a time.

Step 3: Heat Up Your Flat Iron

Turn on your flat iron and set the temperature to the appropriate level for your hair type. Thick and wavy hair usually requires a higher temperature than thin hair. Wait for the flat iron to heat up to the desired temperature.

Step 4: Straighten Your Hair

Start with the bottom section of your hair and work your way up. Take a small section of hair and clamp the flat iron down on it near the roots. Glide the flat iron down to the ends of your hair, keeping the tension even as you go. Repeat this process until you've straightened all sections of your hair.

Step 5: Apply Finishing Products

Apply a small amount of hair serum or oil to your hair to add shine and prevent frizz. Use your hands to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Tips for Straightening Men's Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when straightening your thick wavy hair:

  • Choose a flat iron with ceramic plates, as they distribute heat evenly and reduce the risk of damage to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair from the heat of the flat iron.
  • Don't straighten wet or damp hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Divide your hair into small sections to make it easier to straighten.
  • Use a comb or brush to smooth out each section of hair before straightening.
  • Don't clamp the flat iron down too hard on your hair, as this can cause damage and create a crease.

FAQs

Q: How often should I straighten my hair?

A: It's best to limit the amount of heat you apply to your hair, so try not to straighten your hair more than once a week.

Q: Can I straighten my hair if it's colored or chemically treated?

A: Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and set the flat iron to a lower temperature to prevent damage.

Q: How long will the straightened effect last?

A: The effect of straightening your hair with a flat iron can last for one to two days, depending on how well you maintain it.

Hair Care for Straightened Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your straightened hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Apply a hair serum or oil to add shine and prevent frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a sleek, polished look for your thick wavy hair. Remember to take good care of your hair to maintain its health and strength.
