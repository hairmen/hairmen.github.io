Shoulder Length Hair For Round Face Asian

Written by Eveline Jul 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you an Asian with a round face and wondering what hairstyle to rock in 2023? Look no further than shoulder length hair! This hairstyle complements a round face and makes it look slimmer and more elongated. In this blog post, we’ll give you tips, steps, and answer frequently asked questions about shoulder length hair for round face Asian.

Description

Shoulder length hair is hair that falls between the collarbone and the shoulders. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in various ways to suit different occasions. Shoulder length hair is particularly great for round faces because it softens the face shape and gives it a more oval appearance. It also accentuates the cheekbones and jawline, making them look more defined.

How to

Getting shoulder length hair for round face Asian is pretty straightforward. You can either grow your hair to the desired length or get extensions if you want an instant result. Here are some general steps to follow:

Step 1: Consult with a hairstylist

Before getting shoulder length hair, it’s advisable to consult with a hairstylist. They will advise you on whether the hairstyle will suit you and how to maintain it. They can also recommend the best products to use for your hair type and texture.

Step 2: Choose the right cut

The right cut is crucial in achieving shoulder length hair that flatters your round face. For example, you can opt for layers to add volume and texture to your hair. You can also go for a side-swept bang to balance out your face shape. A blunt cut may not be ideal for a round face as it can make it look even rounder.

Step 3: Style your hair

Once you have the right cut, it’s time to style your hair. You can wear it straight, wavy, curly, or in a bun or ponytail. Experiment with different styling tools and products to find what works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when rocking shoulder length hair for round face Asian:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy or blunt bangs as they can make your face look rounder.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories such as headbands, clips, and scarves to add interest to your hairstyle.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or pull your hair back tightly as they can accentuate your face shape.
  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length hair for round face Asian:

Is shoulder length hair suitable for all face shapes?

Shoulder length hair is suitable for most face shapes, but it is particularly flattering for round faces.

What products should I use to maintain my shoulder length hair?

You should use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type and texture. You can also use a leave-in conditioner, hair oil, or hair serum to keep your hair hydrated and smooth.

Can I still wear my hair up with shoulder length hair?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with shoulder length hair. You can try a messy bun, a sleek ponytail, or a half-up half-down hairstyle.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential in maintaining healthy shoulder length hair for round face Asian. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid tying your hair back tightly when it’s wet as it can cause breakage.

Now that you know all about shoulder length hair for round face Asian, it’s time to book that hair appointment and rock that hairstyle with confidence!
