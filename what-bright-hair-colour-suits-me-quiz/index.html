What Bright Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz Find Your Perfect Shade

Written by Eveline Jun 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you ready to switch up your hair colour? Before you take the plunge, it's important to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and personality. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide which bright hair colour is right for you. That's where the "What Bright Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz" comes in. In this article, we'll explain how to take the quiz, offer tips for choosing your perfect shade, and provide hair care advice to keep your new colour looking its best.

How to Take the Quiz

The "What Bright Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz" is a fun and easy way to discover your ideal shade. To get started, visit a hair colour brand's website or search for a quiz online. You'll typically be asked a series of questions about your skin tone, eye colour, natural hair colour, and style preferences. Based on your answers, the quiz will recommend one or more hair colours that are likely to suit you best. Some quizzes may also provide tips on how to style and care for your new colour.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to taking the "What Bright Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz":

  1. Choose a reputable hair colour brand or search for a quiz online.
  2. Read the instructions carefully and answer each question truthfully.
  3. Review your results and take note of any recommended shades.
  4. Research each shade to ensure it's the right fit for you.
  5. Book a consultation with a professional hairstylist to discuss your options.
  6. Follow your stylist's advice and care instructions for your new colour.

Tips for Choosing Your Perfect Shade

Here are some tips for choosing your perfect bright hair colour:

  • Consider your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, opt for shades of red, orange, or yellow. If you have cool undertones, choose shades of blue, purple, or green.
  • Think about your natural hair colour. If you have dark hair, you may need to bleach your hair before applying a bright colour. If you have light hair, you may be able to achieve your desired shade without bleaching.
  • Take inspiration from your favourite celebrities or social media influencers.
  • Consider your lifestyle and career. Some colours may be more appropriate for certain settings than others.
  • Consult with a professional hairstylist to ensure your desired shade is achievable and will suit your skin tone and hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the "What Bright Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz":

  • Is the quiz accurate?
  • While the quiz can provide helpful recommendations, it's important to remember that everyone's hair and skin are unique. Consult with a professional to ensure your desired shade will suit you.
  • Can I take the quiz more than once?
  • Yes, you can take the quiz as many times as you like to explore different options.
  • Can I achieve a bright hair colour at home?
  • It's possible to achieve a bright hair colour at home, but we recommend consulting with a professional to avoid damaging your hair or achieving an unintended result.

Hair Care Tips

After you've achieved your new bright hair colour, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent stripping the colour.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners too frequently, as they can cause damage and fade your colour.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

In conclusion, the "What Bright Hair Colour Suits Me Quiz" is a fun and helpful tool to find your perfect shade. Remember to consider your skin tone, natural hair colour, and lifestyle when choosing a bright shade, and consult with a professional hairstylist to ensure your desired result is achievable and healthy for your hair. Follow our hair care tips to keep your new colour looking vibrant and beautiful.
