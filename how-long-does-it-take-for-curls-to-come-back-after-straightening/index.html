How Long Does It Take For Curls To Come Back After Straightening

Written by Bardi Jun 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have naturally curly hair, you may have thought about straightening it at some point. While straight hair can be a nice change, many people with curly hair wonder how long it will take for their curls to come back after straightening. In this blog post, we will discuss everything you need to know about how long it takes for curls to come back after straightening.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to Straighten Your Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Curls After Straightening
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Healthy Curls

Description

When you straighten curly hair, you are essentially altering the natural curl pattern of your hair. This is done by using heat, chemicals, or a combination of both. The length of time it takes for your curls to come back after straightening depends on several factors, including:

  • The method used to straighten your hair
  • The condition of your hair before and after straightening
  • Your hair's natural texture and curl pattern
  • The products you use to care for your hair

How to Straighten Your Hair

Before we dive into how long it takes for curls to come back after straightening, let's first discuss how to straighten your hair. There are several methods you can use to straighten your hair, including:

  • Using a flat iron
  • Using a hair straightening brush
  • Using a chemical hair relaxer
  • Using a keratin treatment

Step by Step Guide

If you decide to straighten your hair, it is important to do it correctly to minimize damage and ensure your curls come back as quickly as possible. Here is a step-by-step guide to straightening your hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair until it is completely dry.
  4. Section your hair into manageable sections.
  5. Take a small section of hair and run a flat iron or hair straightening brush through it.
  6. Repeat until all sections of your hair are straight.
  7. Apply a serum or oil to your hair to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls After Straightening

Now that you know how to straighten your hair, let's discuss some tips for maintaining your curls after straightening:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after straightening.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair for at least a week after straightening.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment to help restore moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using products with sulfates and alcohol, as these can dry out your hair.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about how long it takes for curls to come back after straightening:

How long does it take for curls to come back after straightening?

The length of time it takes for curls to come back after straightening depends on several factors, but it typically takes anywhere from a few days to a few weeks for curls to fully return.

Can you speed up the process of curls coming back?

You can help speed up the process of curls coming back by using a deep conditioning treatment, avoiding heat styling tools, and using products specifically designed for curly hair.

Will your curls be the same after straightening?

While your curls will eventually come back after straightening, they may not be exactly the same as before. The heat and chemicals used to straighten your hair can alter the natural curl pattern of your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Curls

Finally, here are some hair care tips for maintaining healthy, bouncy curls:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and add moisture.
  • Avoid using products with silicones, as they can weigh down your hair and cause buildup.

Now that you know how long it takes for curls to come back after straightening, you can make an informed decision about whether or not to straighten your hair. Just remember to take care of your curls and use products specifically designed for curly hair to keep them healthy and bouncy!
