How To Get Curly Hair Without Heat A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curls are always in style, but using heat to achieve them can cause damage to your hair. Fortunately, there are ways to get curly hair without heat. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve beautiful, natural curls without using any heat styling tools.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Curly Hair Without Heat
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Curls
  • FAQs

How to Get Curly Hair Without Heat

There are several ways to get curly hair without heat:

  • Twist your hair into sections and leave them overnight
  • Braid your hair and leave it overnight
  • Use flexi rods or perm rods
  • Try a curling product or mousse

Each of these methods can give you beautiful, natural-looking curls. The key is to find the method that works best for your hair type and the look you want to achieve.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting curly hair without heat:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. If your hair is too dry, it won't hold the curls as well.
  2. Decide which method you want to use. If you're using flexi or perm rods, make sure you have enough for your entire head.
  3. Section your hair. You can do this by parting it down the middle and then into smaller sections. Use clips to hold the sections in place.
  4. If you're twisting your hair, take a small section and twist it tightly. If you're braiding your hair, braid each section tightly.
  5. If you're using flexi or perm rods, wrap a small section of hair around the rod, starting at the bottom and rolling it up to the scalp. Secure the rod in place.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 until all of your hair is twisted, braided, or wrapped around the rods.
  7. Leave your hair overnight to let the curls set.
  8. In the morning, carefully remove the twists, braids, or rods. Use your fingers to separate the curls and create the look you want.
  9. Finish with hairspray or a curl-defining product to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Here are some tips for maintaining your curls:

  • Avoid brushing your curls. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle them.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your curls, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a curl-defining product or mousse to help hold your curls in place.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting curly hair without heat:

Can I use this method on any hair type?

Yes! These methods can work on any hair type, whether your hair is straight, wavy, or curly.

How long will the curls last?

This depends on your hair type and the method you use. In general, the curls will last anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Can I use these methods every day?

It's generally not recommended to use these methods every day, as this can cause damage to your hair. It's best to give your hair a break between styling sessions.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a little extra care to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for curly hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to help keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt.
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.

By following these tips and using the methods we've outlined in this article, you can achieve beautiful, natural-looking curls without using any heat styling tools. Give it a try and see how you like the results!
