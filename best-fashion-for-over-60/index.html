Best Fashion For Over 60 A Guide To Ageless Style

Written by Petter Oct 13, 2023 · 3 min read

If you’re over 60, it’s never too late to experiment with fashion and embrace your personal style. With age comes wisdom, confidence, and a better sense of self, so why not let your fashion choices reflect that? In this article, we’ll guide you through the best fashion choices for over 60, from how to dress for your body type to hair care tips that complement your look.

Table of Contents

  • Dressing for Your Body Type
  • Choosing Colors
  • Accessorizing
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

Dressing for Your Body Type

When it comes to fashion, knowing your body type is key. This will help you choose clothes that flatter your figure and make you feel your best. For over 60, comfort is key, so opt for looser, flowing fabrics that don’t cling to your body. Tunics, maxi dresses, and kaftans are great options that offer both comfort and style.

If you’re apple-shaped, opt for empire-waist styles that cinch at the narrowest part of your torso. If you’re pear-shaped, choose A-line or flared skirts that balance out your lower body. Hourglass figures can rock fitted clothing, while rectangle-shaped bodies can create curves with belted dresses and tops.

Choosing Colors

As you age, your skin tone may change, so it’s important to choose colors that complement your complexion. Jewel tones like emerald, ruby, and sapphire are universally flattering, while earth tones like olive and rust complement warmer skin tones. Avoid harsh blacks and opt for softer charcoal or navy instead.

Don’t be afraid to experiment with patterns and prints either! Florals, stripes, and polka dots can add personality to your outfit without being overwhelming.

Accessorizing

Accessories are an easy way to add interest to any outfit. Statement jewelry like chunky necklaces or oversized earrings can elevate a simple look, while scarves and hats can add texture and depth.

When it comes to handbags, opt for crossbody or shoulder bags that are easy to carry and don’t put too much strain on your back.

Hair Care Tips

As you age, your hair may become thinner and more brittle. To combat this, use gentle shampoos and conditioners that hydrate and nourish your strands. Blow-drying and heat styling can also cause damage, so air-dry your hair as much as possible and use heat protectant sprays when styling.

Shorter haircuts like bobs and pixie cuts are low-maintenance and easy to style, while longer hair can be kept healthy with regular trims and deep conditioning treatments.

FAQs

What should I avoid wearing over 60?

Avoid clothes that are too tight or too baggy, as they can make you look shapeless or frumpy. Also, avoid overly trendy pieces that may look outdated in a few months.

Can I wear jeans over 60?

Yes, you can definitely wear jeans over 60! Just make sure to choose a comfortable, flattering pair that fits well and isn’t too tight. Dark washes and straight-leg styles are universally flattering and can be dressed up or down.

How can I make my outfit look more put-together?

Adding accessories like jewelry, scarves, and hats can instantly elevate a simple outfit. You can also experiment with layering, like wearing a blazer over a basic tee or adding a cardigan to a dress.

In conclusion, fashion is all about expressing your personal style and feeling confident in your own skin. With the right clothing choices and hair care tips, you can look and feel ageless at any age.
