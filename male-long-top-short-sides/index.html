Male Long Top Short Sides The Trendy Hairstyle Of 2023

Written by Luffy Jul 06, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking for a hairstyle that is both trendy and versatile, look no further than the male long top short sides. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years and has become a staple in men’s fashion. In this blog post, we will discuss everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to achieve it, style tips, and hair care.

Description

The male long top short sides hairstyle is characterized by a longer length of hair on the top of the head and shorter sides. The contrast between the long top and short sides creates a stylish and modern look that can be customized to suit any face shape and hair type. This hairstyle can be worn with a variety of hair textures, from straight to curly, and can be styled in many different ways.

How to

To achieve the male long top short sides hairstyle, follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume on the top of your head.
  3. Use a comb to create a side part and comb the longer hair on top to one side.
  4. Use clippers or scissors to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head to the desired length.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair product, such as pomade or wax, to the longer hair on top to create texture and hold.
  6. Style the hair on top using your fingers or a brush to achieve the desired look.

Step by Step

Follow these simple steps to achieve the male long top short sides hairstyle:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Wash your hair with a shampoo that is appropriate for your hair type. Follow up with a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Step 2: Blow-dry

Use a round brush and a blow dryer to create volume on the top of your head. Blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to lay.

Step 3: Create a Side Part

Use a comb to create a side part and comb the longer hair on top to one side. Make sure the part is clean and straight.

Step 4: Trim the Sides and Back

Use clippers or scissors to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head to the desired length. You can choose to have a fade or a more gradual transition.

Step 5: Apply Hair Product

Apply a small amount of hair product, such as pomade or wax, to the longer hair on top to create texture and hold. Make sure to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair.

Step 6: Style the Hair on Top

Style the hair on top using your fingers or a brush to achieve the desired look. You can choose to have a messy, textured look or a more polished style.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your male long top short sides hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep the sides and back of your hair looking neat and clean.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Try different styling techniques to create different looks.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

Is the male long top short sides hairstyle easy to maintain?

Yes, this hairstyle is easy to maintain with regular trims and the right hair products.

Can the male long top short sides hairstyle be worn with any face shape?

Yes, this hairstyle can be customized to suit any face shape.

What hair products are best for the male long top short sides hairstyle?

Products such as pomade, wax, and clay are great for creating texture and hold in the longer hair on top.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water and harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.

With the male long top short sides hairstyle, you can achieve a trendy and stylish look that is both versatile and easy to maintain. Follow the steps and tips outlined in this blog post to achieve the perfect look for you.
