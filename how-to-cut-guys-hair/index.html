How To Cut Guys Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 15, 2023 · 4 min read
How to Cut Guys Hair: A Step-by-Step Guide | Hair Care Tips

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts for your guy? Or maybe you're a guy who wants to learn how to cut your own hair? Whatever the case may be, cutting guys hair can save you a lot of time and money. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut guys hair and some tips on hair care.

How to Cut Guys Hair: Step-by-Step

Before we begin, here's a list of things you'll need:

  • Clipper
  • Scissors
  • Cape or towel to drape over the shoulders
  • Comb
  • Water spray bottle

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and style. Use a water spray bottle to dampen the hair if needed.

Step 2: Choose the Length

Decide on the length you want to cut the hair. Start with a longer length and gradually cut shorter if needed. Remember, you can always cut more, but you can't add hair back once it's cut.

Step 3: Use the Clippers

Use the clippers to cut the hair to the desired length. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Use a guard to ensure the hair is cut evenly.

Step 4: Use the Scissors

Once you've used the clippers, use the scissors to blend the hair and remove any excess. Blend the sides and back with the top to create a seamless transition.

Step 5: Clean Up

After you've finished cutting the hair, use a comb to remove any loose hairs. Clean up the neck and around the ears with the clippers or scissors.

Step 6: Style the Hair

Using a styling product, style the hair as desired. Use a comb or your fingers to create the desired look.

Tips for Cutting Guys Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting guys hair:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut shorter if needed
  • Use a guard to ensure the hair is cut evenly
  • Blend the hair to create a seamless transition
  • Clean up around the neck and ears
  • Use styling products to create the desired look

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair. However, it may be more difficult to cut the back of your head without assistance.

How often should I cut my hair?

This depends on the length and style of your hair. Generally, guys should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired length and style.

What if I make a mistake while cutting?

If you make a mistake while cutting, don't panic. It's just hair and it will grow back. If the mistake is minor, you may be able to fix it yourself. If it's a major mistake, seek the assistance of a professional stylist.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the desired length and style

Cutting guys hair is a simple process that can save you time and money. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut in the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time and follow these steps to ensure the best results. And, don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best!
