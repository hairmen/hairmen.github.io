Supplements To Take For Pcos Hair Loss A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 02, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're one of the millions of women who suffer from PCOS, you know that it can be incredibly frustrating. One of the most common symptoms of this condition is hair loss, which can be distressing and impact your confidence. The good news is that there are supplements you can take to help combat this issue. In this article, we'll go over the best supplements for PCOS hair loss and how to use them.

What is PCOS?

PCOS stands for polycystic ovary syndrome. It's a hormonal disorder that affects women of reproductive age. PCOS can cause a variety of symptoms, including irregular periods, weight gain, and acne. One of the most common symptoms of PCOS is hair loss, which can occur on the scalp, face, or body.

Supplements to Take for PCOS Hair Loss

Here are some of the best supplements to take if you're experiencing hair loss due to PCOS:

1. Biotin

Biotin is a B vitamin that is essential for healthy hair, skin, and nails. It helps to strengthen hair and prevent breakage. Biotin is also known to improve the overall health of the scalp, which can help to promote hair growth.

2. Iron

Iron is an important mineral that is essential for healthy hair growth. Women with PCOS are often deficient in iron, which can contribute to hair loss. Taking an iron supplement can help to improve the health of your hair.

3. Zinc

Zinc is a mineral that is essential for healthy hair growth. It helps to keep the oil glands around the hair follicles working properly, which can prevent hair loss. Zinc is also known to improve the thickness and strength of hair.

4. Vitamin D

Vitamin D is essential for overall health, and it's also important for healthy hair growth. Women with PCOS are often deficient in vitamin D, which can contribute to hair loss. Taking a vitamin D supplement can help to improve the health of your hair.

How to Take Supplements for PCOS Hair Loss

When taking supplements for PCOS hair loss, it's important to follow the recommended dosage on the label. You should also talk to your doctor before starting any new supplements, especially if you're taking other medications or have any underlying health conditions.

In addition to taking supplements, there are other things you can do to improve the health of your hair. Eating a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals can help to promote healthy hair growth. You should also avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as hair dyes and relaxers.

Tips for Preventing Hair Loss

Here are some tips for preventing hair loss due to PCOS:

  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Take supplements to improve the health of your hair
  • Manage your stress levels
  • Exercise regularly

FAQs

What is the best supplement for PCOS hair loss?

The best supplement for PCOS hair loss depends on your individual needs. Biotin, iron, zinc, and vitamin D are all good options.

How long does it take for supplements to work for PCOS hair loss?

It can take several weeks or even months to see results from taking supplements for PCOS hair loss. It's important to be patient and consistent with your supplement regimen.

Can supplements cure PCOS hair loss?

There is no cure for PCOS hair loss, but supplements can help to improve the health of your hair and promote healthy growth.

Hair Care for PCOS Hair Loss

If you're experiencing hair loss due to PCOS, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent further damage. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair

By following these tips and taking supplements, you can improve the health of your hair and prevent further hair loss due to PCOS.
