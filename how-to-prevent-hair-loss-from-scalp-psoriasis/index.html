How To Prevent Hair Loss From Scalp Psoriasis

Written by Robby Aug 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Scalp psoriasis is a common skin condition that affects many people around the world. It is a chronic autoimmune disorder that causes skin cells to grow too quickly, resulting in red, itchy, and scaly patches on the scalp. Unfortunately, scalp psoriasis can also lead to hair loss if not properly managed. In this article, we will discuss how to prevent hair loss from scalp psoriasis.

How to Prevent Hair Loss from Scalp Psoriasis

There are several ways to prevent hair loss from scalp psoriasis:

1. Consult a Dermatologist

If you are experiencing hair loss due to scalp psoriasis, it is important to consult a dermatologist. They can prescribe medications that can help manage your symptoms and prevent further hair loss.

2. Use Gentle Hair Products

Using harsh hair products can irritate the scalp and worsen psoriasis symptoms. It is important to use gentle, fragrance-free hair products that are specifically formulated for sensitive scalps.

3. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools such as flat irons, curling irons, and hair dryers can damage the hair and scalp. It is best to avoid using these tools, or at least limit their use as much as possible.

4. Practice Good Scalp Hygiene

Keeping the scalp clean and healthy is crucial in preventing hair loss from scalp psoriasis. Use a gentle shampoo to wash your hair regularly, and avoid scratching or picking at psoriasis patches.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to prevent hair loss from scalp psoriasis:

Step 1: Consult a Dermatologist

If you are experiencing hair loss due to scalp psoriasis, make an appointment with a dermatologist to discuss treatment options.

Step 2: Use Gentle Hair Products

Switch to gentle, fragrance-free hair products that are specifically formulated for sensitive scalps.

Step 3: Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools, or limit their use as much as possible.

Step 4: Practice Good Scalp Hygiene

Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo, and avoid scratching or picking at psoriasis patches.

Tips to Prevent Hair Loss from Scalp Psoriasis

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair loss from scalp psoriasis:

1. Use a Humidifier

A humidifier can help keep the scalp moisturized, which can reduce the severity of psoriasis symptoms.

2. Try Scalp Massage

Scalp massage can help improve blood flow to the scalp, which can promote hair growth and reduce psoriasis symptoms.

3. Wear a Hat in the Sun

Exposure to sunlight can worsen psoriasis symptoms. Wearing a hat can provide shade and protect the scalp.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can scalp psoriasis cause permanent hair loss?

Scalp psoriasis can cause temporary hair loss, but it is usually reversible with proper treatment.

What medications can help prevent hair loss from scalp psoriasis?

Topical corticosteroids, calcipotriol, and coal tar are commonly prescribed medications for scalp psoriasis that can help prevent hair loss.

Can stress cause scalp psoriasis?

Stress can trigger psoriasis flare-ups, including on the scalp.

Hair Care for Scalp Psoriasis

Here are some additional tips for hair care when dealing with scalp psoriasis:

1. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles such as braids, ponytails, and cornrows can pull on the scalp and worsen psoriasis symptoms. Opt for looser hairstyles instead.

2. Be Gentle with Wet Hair

Wet hair is more fragile and prone to breakage. Be gentle when brushing or combing wet hair to avoid further damage.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair, as it is less likely to pull on the scalp and cause damage.

By following these tips and practicing good hair care, you can prevent hair loss from scalp psoriasis and maintain healthy, beautiful hair.
