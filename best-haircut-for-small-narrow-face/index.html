Best Haircut For Small Narrow Face

Written by Eveline Oct 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a small narrow face, finding the right haircut can make a huge difference in enhancing your features. In this article, we'll discuss the best haircuts that can flatter your face shape and make you look your best. Let's dive in and explore the options!

Description

A small narrow face is characterized by a slim jawline, high cheekbones, and a narrow forehead. Choosing the right haircut can help balance out these features and create a more proportionate look. The right cut can also help enhance your facial features and create a more defined look.

How to

When choosing a haircut for a small narrow face, it's important to consider the length, texture, and style of your hair. Here are some things to keep in mind when choosing your next cut:

  • Choose a cut that adds volume and width to your face
  • Avoid cuts that are too short or too long
  • Consider adding layers to create movement and texture
  • Avoid heavy bangs that can overpower your face
  • Opt for soft, face-framing layers to add dimension to your hair

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions to help you achieve the perfect haircut for your small narrow face:

  1. Consult with your stylist and discuss your face shape and hair texture
  2. Choose a cut that adds volume and width to your face
  3. Consider adding layers to create movement and texture
  4. Avoid cuts that are too short or too long
  5. Opt for soft, face-framing layers to add dimension to your hair
  6. Ask your stylist to create a side part to add width to your face
  7. Avoid heavy bangs that can overpower your face
  8. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth
  9. Use styling products to help create volume and texture
  10. Visit your stylist regularly for maintenance and touch-ups

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect haircut for your small narrow face:

  • Consider trying a bob or lob to add volume and width to your face
  • Avoid center parts that can make your face look even narrower
  • Opt for soft waves or curls to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Consider adding a side-swept fringe to create a more balanced look
  • Avoid blunt cuts that can create a harsh, angular look

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail?

A: Absolutely! Just make sure to add some volume and texture to your hair to create a more balanced look.

Q: Are there any haircuts I should avoid?

A: Yes, you should avoid cuts that are too short or too long, blunt cuts, and heavy bangs. These can all make your face look narrower and more angular.

Q: What styling products should I use?

A: Use volumizing mousse, texturizing spray, and a lightweight hairspray to help create volume and texture in your hair.

Hair Care

To maintain your haircut and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup from styling products
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut for your small narrow face, you can create a look that enhances your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful!
