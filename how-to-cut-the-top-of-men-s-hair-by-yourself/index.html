How To Cut The Top Of Men s Hair By Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to cut the top of men's hair by yourself! In the age of social distancing and remote work, many of us have had to take on new responsibilities, including cutting our own hair. While it may seem daunting at first, with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a great haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll take you through the process step-by-step, as well as share some tips and tricks for a successful cut. Let's get started!

How to Cut the Top of Men's Hair: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in any haircut is to gather your tools. For cutting the top of men's hair, you'll need:

  • Clippers with guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clip or hair tie

Make sure your clippers are fully charged and your scissors are sharp.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair. Use a clip or hair tie to pull back the sides and bottom of the hair, leaving just the top section loose.

Step 3: Choose Your Guard

Next, choose the guard size you want to use. For a shorter cut, start with a higher guard and work your way down. For a longer cut, start with a lower guard and work your way up.

Step 4: Start Cutting

With the guard in place, start cutting the hair. Work from the front of the head to the back, following the natural direction of the hair. Use long, smooth strokes and go over each section of hair several times to ensure an even cut.

Step 5: Blend the Sides

Once you've finished cutting the top, it's time to blend the sides. Remove the guard from your clippers and use the bare blades to blend the top into the sides. Use short, upward strokes and work your way around the head until the hair looks even.

Step 6: Trim the Bangs

If your hair has bangs, now is the time to trim them. Use your scissors to cut the bangs to the desired length. Start with a longer length and work your way shorter as needed.

Step 7: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished cutting, check for evenness. Use your comb to lift the hair and compare the length on both sides. Make any necessary adjustments with your clippers or scissors.

Step 8: Clean Up

Finally, clean up any stray hairs around the neck and ears. Use your clippers or scissors to trim any hairs that are sticking out.

Tips for a Successful Cut

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Work slowly and carefully, taking your time to avoid mistakes.
  • Check for evenness frequently to avoid uneven cuts.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, with the right tools and technique, you can cut your own hair.

What tools do I need to cut my own hair?

You'll need clippers with guards, scissors, a comb, and a clip or hair tie.

What guard size should I use?

This depends on the length you want to achieve. For a shorter cut, start with a higher guard and work your way down. For a longer cut, start with a lower guard and work your way up.

Hair Care

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut from the comfort of your own home. Happy cutting!
