Does Gastric Sleeve Cause Hair Loss What You Need To Know

Written by William Aug 31, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're considering getting a gastric sleeve surgery, one question that may be on your mind is whether or not the procedure can cause hair loss. While hair loss is a potential side effect of gastric sleeve surgery, it's important to understand the factors that contribute to this issue and what you can do to prevent it. In this article, we'll take a closer look at the link between gastric sleeve surgery and hair loss, provide tips for minimizing this side effect, and answer some frequently asked questions about the topic.

What Is Gastric Sleeve Surgery?

Gastric sleeve surgery, also known as sleeve gastrectomy, is a type of weight loss surgery that involves removing a portion of the stomach to create a smaller, sleeve-shaped stomach. This reduces the amount of food that can be consumed at one time, which can lead to significant weight loss.

How Does Gastric Sleeve Surgery Cause Hair Loss?

While the exact mechanism behind hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is not fully understood, there are a few factors that may contribute to this issue. First, the surgery itself can be a traumatic event for the body, which can trigger hair loss. Additionally, the rapid weight loss that often occurs after gastric sleeve surgery can cause the body to go into a state of shock, which can also lead to hair loss. Finally, changes in hormones and nutrient deficiencies can also contribute to hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery.

How Can You Minimize Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve Surgery?

While some degree of hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is normal, there are steps you can take to minimize this side effect. First, make sure you're getting enough protein in your diet, as this nutrient is essential for healthy hair growth. You may also want to consider taking a hair growth supplement that contains biotin, which has been shown to improve hair health. Finally, be gentle with your hair and avoid harsh treatments or styling techniques that can damage the hair shaft.

FAQ

How common is hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery?

Hair loss is a common side effect of gastric sleeve surgery, with an estimated 30-40% of patients experiencing some degree of hair loss in the months following the procedure.

When does hair loss typically occur after gastric sleeve surgery?

Hair loss typically occurs 2-4 months after gastric sleeve surgery, and can last for up to a year.

Will my hair grow back after gastric sleeve surgery?

Yes, in most cases hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is temporary and hair will grow back once the body has adjusted to the changes caused by the surgery.

Can I prevent hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery?

While you can't completely prevent hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery, there are steps you can take to minimize this side effect, such as ensuring you're getting enough protein in your diet and taking hair growth supplements.

Hair Care Tips for Post-Gastric Sleeve Patients

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair after gastric sleeve surgery:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on the hair
  • Avoid chemical treatments like perms or relaxers
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in protein and other essential nutrients
  • Consider taking a hair growth supplement

By following these tips and taking steps to promote healthy hair growth, you can minimize the risk of hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery and maintain a healthy head of hair.
