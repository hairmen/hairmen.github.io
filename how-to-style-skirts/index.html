How To Style Skirts A Beginner s Guide

Written by Petter Nov 09, 2023 · 4 min read

In today's fashion world, skirts are a versatile and stylish piece that every woman should have in their wardrobe. From mini to midi to maxi, there are countless styles to choose from, making it easy to find one that fits your personal style. However, figuring out how to style skirts can be a bit tricky. In this guide, we'll give you some tips and tricks to help you rock any skirt with confidence.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Skirts
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Skirts
  • FAQs
  • Hair and Makeup Tips

How to Wear Skirts

Before we dive into the styling tips, let's talk about the basics of wearing skirts. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Choose the right length for your body shape
  • Wear the appropriate undergarments to avoid visible panty lines
  • Pair your skirt with the right shoes
  • Accessorize according to the occasion

Now that you know the basics, let's move on to the fun part - styling!

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style skirts:

Step 1: Choose your skirt

The first step in styling skirts is to choose the right one for your body shape and the occasion. If you're going for a casual look, a denim or floral skirt will do the trick. For a more formal event, opt for a pencil or A-line skirt.

Step 2: Pair it with a top

Next, choose a top that complements your skirt. If you're wearing a flowy maxi skirt, pair it with a fitted crop top. For a more conservative look, pair your skirt with a blouse or button-down shirt.

Step 3: Add some accessories

Accessorizing is key to taking your skirt outfit to the next level. Add a belt to cinch in your waist, a statement necklace to draw attention to your neckline, or a scarf to add some color to your look.

Step 4: Choose the right shoes

The right shoes can make or break your skirt outfit. For a casual look, opt for sneakers or sandals. For a dressier occasion, wear heels or dress shoes.

Tips for Styling Skirts

Here are some additional tips for styling skirts:

  • Experiment with different patterns and textures
  • Try tucking in your top to show off your waistline
  • Layer with a jacket or cardigan for added warmth
  • Accessorize with a hat or sunglasses for a trendy look
  • Always wear the right undergarments to avoid visible panty lines

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a mini skirt to work?

A: It depends on your workplace dress code. If your office is more casual, you can wear a mini skirt with tights and a blouse. However, if your workplace is more formal, it's best to stick to knee-length or midi skirts.

Q: Can I wear a maxi skirt if I'm short?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure to choose a high-waisted maxi skirt and pair it with heels to elongate your legs.

Q: What shoes should I wear with a midi skirt?

A: Midi skirts look great with ankle boots or strappy sandals. Avoid wearing flats as they can make you look shorter.

Hair and Makeup Tips

When it comes to styling skirts, your hair and makeup play an important role in completing your look. Here are some tips:

  • For a casual look, go for natural makeup and loose waves
  • For a dressier occasion, opt for a bold lip and an elegant updo
  • Accessorize with a hairband or headband to add some flair to your hairstyle

Now that you know how to style skirts, it's time to experiment with different looks and find what works best for you. Remember to have fun with fashion and always dress for yourself!
