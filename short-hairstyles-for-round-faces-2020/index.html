Short Hairstyles For Round Faces 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Aug 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you someone with a round face and looking for a short hairstyle that can flatter your face shape? Look no further, as we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing the best short hairstyles for round faces in 2020. Read on to find out more!

Description

Round faces often have soft and feminine features, with the width and length of the face being almost equal. The aim of any hairstyle for a round face is to create an illusion of length, adding angles and height to the face. The right hairstyle can help to accentuate your best features and make you look more confident and attractive.

How To

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle for a round face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length and width of your face and comparing the two. If they are almost equal, then you have a round face shape. Once you have identified your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that suit you.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape - measure the length and width of your face and compare them.
2. Look for hairstyles that flatter your face shape.
3. Consider your hair type and texture.
4. Consult with a hairstylist to get their opinion and advice.
5. Choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Tips

1. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, such as blunt bobs and heavy bangs.
2. Look for hairstyles that add height and angles to the face, such as layered pixie cuts and asymmetrical bobs.
3. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.
4. Play around with different styles and textures, such as curls or waves.
5. Invest in good quality hair products to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

FAQ

What are the best short hairstyles for round faces?

The best short hairstyles for round faces include layered pixie cuts, asymmetrical bobs, and angled cuts. These styles help to create height and angles in the face, making it appear longer.

Can I pull off a short hairstyle with a round face?

Yes, you can! The key is to choose a style that flatters your face shape and adds dimension and angles. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best style for you.

How do I style a short hairstyle for a round face?

Styling a short hairstyle for a round face can be easy and low-maintenance. Simply use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture, and use a hair straightener or curling iron to create waves or curls.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. This is especially true for short hairstyles, as they can be prone to damage and breakage. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
2. Avoid using hot tools too often.
3. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
4. Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
5. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
6. Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a scarf.
7. Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Now that you know all about the best short hairstyles for round faces in 2020, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new look! Remember, the key to finding the perfect hairstyle is to choose a style that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
