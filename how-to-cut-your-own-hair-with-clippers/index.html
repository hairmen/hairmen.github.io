How to Cut Your Own Hair with Clippers

Cutting your own hair with clippers can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be easy and even enjoyable. Before you start, make sure you have a good pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. You may also want to have a spray bottle of water to wet your hair as you cut.

Step 1: Choose the Right Clipper Guard

The first step in cutting your own hair with clippers is to choose the right guard. Clippers come with a variety of guard sizes, ranging from 1/16 inch to 1 inch. The smaller the guard, the shorter the hair will be. For beginners, we recommend starting with a larger guard and working your way down to a shorter length as you become more comfortable.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut evenly, so itâs best to dry your hair completely before starting.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Begin by placing the clipper guard on your clippers and starting at the sides of your head. Use a downward motion to cut the hair, moving from the bottom of your hairline to the top. Make sure to keep the guard flat against your head to ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Move to the Back

Next, move to the back of your head and repeat the process, starting at the bottom and moving upward. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Once you have finished the sides and back, itâs time to move to the top of your head. Use the scissors to trim any longer hairs and use the clippers to blend the hair from the sides and back into the top. Start at the front and work your way back, using the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to trim it to the desired length.

Step 6: Trim the Bangs

If you have bangs, use the scissors to trim them to the desired length. Start by cutting a small amount and then work your way up to the desired length.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to cutting your own hair with clippers:

  1. Choose the right clipper guard.
  2. Wash and dry your hair.
  3. Start with the sides.
  4. Move to the back.
  5. Cut the top.
  6. Trim the bangs.

Tips for a Successful Haircut

Here are some tips to ensure your DIY haircut looks salon-worthy:

  • Start with a larger guard and work your way down to a shorter length as you become more comfortable.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length.
  • Make sure to keep the guard flat against your head to ensure an even cut.
  • Use the scissors to trim any longer hairs and blend the hair from the sides and back into the top.
  • Start by cutting a small amount of bangs and then work your way up to the desired length.

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair with clippers:

  • Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

    • We recommend using hair cutting scissors, as they are designed specifically for cutting hair and will provide a cleaner cut.

  • How often should I cut my hair?

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.
