How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers All Over A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a way to save money and time on haircuts, learning how to cut men's hair with clippers all over is a great option. Not only is it easy to do, but it can also help you achieve a variety of styles. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of cutting men's hair with clippers all over, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Clippers All Over

Cutting men's hair with clippers all over involves using a set of clippers to trim the hair to a uniform length. This style is perfect for men who want a simple, low-maintenance haircut that looks neat and tidy. Here's what you'll need to get started:

  • A set of clippers with guard attachments
  • A comb
  • A pair of scissors
  • A towel or cape to keep hair off the neck and shoulders

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Tools and Work Area

Before you begin, make sure your clippers are clean and oiled. Set up your work area with a chair, towel or cape, and a mirror so you can see what you're doing.

Step 2: Start by Trimming the Sides and Back

Attach a guard to your clippers and start trimming the sides and back of the head. Use a comb to lift the hair and run the clippers over the hair in an upward motion, following the direction of the hair growth. Repeat this process with different guard sizes until you achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Move to the Top of the Head

Change to a longer guard attachment and start trimming the hair on the top of the head. Use the same technique as before, lifting the hair with a comb and running the clippers over the hair in an upward motion.

Step 4: Blend the Top and Sides

Using a smaller guard attachment, blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top. Start by trimming the hair around the temples and work your way towards the back of the head, using the clippers to fade the hair into the longer hair on top. Repeat this process with different guard sizes until you achieve a smooth, even blend.

Step 5: Trim the Hairline and Neck

Use a pair of scissors to trim any hair that wasn't caught by the clippers, especially around the ears and neckline. Use the clippers without a guard attachment to trim the hairline around the ears and neck, being careful not to cut too much.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Clippers All Over

  • Start with a longer guard attachment and work your way down to a shorter length until you achieve the desired look.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair before trimming to ensure an even cut.
  • Make sure your clippers are clean and oiled before use to ensure a smooth cut.
  • Take breaks and step back to assess your work to avoid over-cutting.

FAQs

Can I use clippers to cut my own hair?

Yes, you can use clippers to cut your own hair. However, it may be difficult to achieve a precise cut without the help of a second person.

What is the best guard size to use for a short haircut?

The best guard size to use for a short haircut depends on the desired length. Generally, a #2 or #3 guard attachment is a good starting point.

Hair Care Advice

After cutting your hair with clippers, it's important to keep it clean and moisturized. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type and avoid using too much heat when styling. Regular trims every 4-6 weeks can also help keep your hair looking its best.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair with clippers all over, you can save time and money on haircuts. With a little practice and patience, you'll be able to achieve a variety of styles that look neat and tidy.
