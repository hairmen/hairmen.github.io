Best Curly Hair Styles For Over 50

Written by Oliver Aug 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you over 50 and have curly hair? It can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that not only fits your age but also complements your curls. In this article, we'll be discussing the best curly hair styles for over 50. Read on to find out more.

Description

Curly hair is unique and requires a special kind of care and attention. As we age, our hair changes, and our curls may become more fragile, thinner, and drier. It's essential to choose a hairstyle that not only flatters your face shape but also complements your hair type and texture. In this article, we'll be discussing the best curly hair styles for over 50.

How to Choose the Right Curly Hair Style?

Choosing the right curly hair style can be overwhelming, especially if you're not sure what to look for. Here are a few tips to help you choose the right curly hair style:

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape plays an important role in determining the right curly hair style. If you have a round face, you may want to consider a layered cut that adds volume and height. If you have a square face, you may want to consider a longer cut that softens your features.

Think About Your Hair Texture

Curly hair comes in different textures, and it's essential to choose a hairstyle that complements your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to consider a shorter cut that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a longer cut that allows your curls to fall naturally.

Step by Step: Best Curly Hair Styles for Over 50

Here are some of the best curly hair styles for over 50:

1. Short Curly Bob

A short curly bob is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's a low-maintenance hairstyle that's perfect for women over 50. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair into a chin-length bob and add layers for volume and texture.

2. Shoulder-Length Curly Hair

Shoulder-length curly hair is a versatile hairstyle that's perfect for women over 50. It's long enough to style in different ways, and it's short enough to be low-maintenance. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair to shoulder length and add layers to add volume and texture.

3. Curly Pixie Cut

A curly pixie cut is a bold and daring hairstyle that's perfect for women over 50 who want to make a statement. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your hair into a short pixie cut and add layers to add volume and texture.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Hair Style

Maintaining your curly hair style is essential to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your curly hair style:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, and it's essential to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

2. Avoid Using Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools can damage your curls and make them frizzy. It's best to avoid using heat styling tools and let your curls air dry naturally.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Curly hair is prone to tangles, and it's essential to use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls gently.

FAQs

Q. Can I dye my curly hair?

A. Yes, you can dye your curly hair, but it's essential to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and hair texture.

Q. How often should I trim my curly hair?

A. It's recommended to trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep your curls looking healthy and vibrant.

Q. Can I wear my curly hair in a ponytail?

A. Yes, you can wear your curly hair in a ponytail, but it's essential to use a scrunchie or a hair tie that won't damage your curls.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care and attention to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are a few hair care tips for curly hair:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, and it's essential to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

2. Avoid Using Harsh Shampoos

Harsh shampoos can strip your hair of its natural oils and make your curls dry and frizzy. It's best to use a gentle shampoo that's specifically designed for curly hair.

3. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help keep your curls hydrated and reduce frizz. It's best to use a leave-in conditioner that's specifically designed for curly hair.

In conclusion, choosing the right curly hair style is essential to keep your curls looking healthy and vibrant. We hope that this article has helped you find the best curly hair styles for over 50. Remember to take good care of your curls to keep them looking their best.
