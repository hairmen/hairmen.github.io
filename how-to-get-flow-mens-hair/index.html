How To Get Flow Men s Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that is both effortless and stylish? Look no further than flow men's hair! This hairstyle is characterized by longer locks on top that flow freely, creating a relaxed and carefree look. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this coveted hairstyle.

Table of Contents:

  • How To Get Flow Men's Hair
  • Step-By-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Flow Men's Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Flow Men's Hair

How to Get Flow Men's Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it's important to understand the basic requirements for flow men's hair:

  • Length on top: You'll need at least 6 inches of hair on top to achieve flow men's hair.
  • Shorter sides: The sides and back should be cut shorter than the top to create contrast and emphasis on the longer locks.
  • Texture: Flow men's hair looks best when it has some natural texture, whether that's waves, curls, or a little bit of frizz.

Now that you know the basics, let's get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed to add volume and texture. Avoid using heavy products that will weigh your hair down.

Step 2: Towel-dry your hair until it's damp, but not dripping wet.

Step 3: Apply a small amount of lightweight hair gel or mousse to your hair, focusing on the roots to create volume.

Step 4: Use a blow dryer on low heat to dry your hair while using your fingers to tousle the hair on top. This will create natural-looking texture and volume.

Step 5: Use a round brush to create more volume at the roots, if desired.

Step 6: Once your hair is dry, use a small amount of pomade or wax to define the longer locks on top and create separation between the top and sides.

Step 7: Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips for Maintaining Flow Men's Hair

Now that you've achieved flow men's hair, it's important to know how to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims: To keep your hair looking its best, you'll need to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks.
  • Invest in quality hair products: Using high-quality products designed for your hair type will help you achieve and maintain the look you want.
  • Don't over-wash your hair: Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every other day.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools: Using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons can damage your hair and undo all the hard work you've put into achieving flow men's hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve flow men's hair if I have straight hair?

A: Absolutely! While natural texture can help enhance the look, you can still achieve flow men's hair with straight hair by using the right products and techniques.

Q: Will flow men's hair work for me if I have a receding hairline?

A: It depends on the severity of your hairline. If you have a significant amount of hair loss, flow men's hair may not be the best option for you. However, if you have a minor receding hairline, you can still achieve the look by keeping the longer locks on top and cutting the sides and back shorter.

Hair Care for Flow Men's Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the health and appearance of flow men's hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when you wash your hair, as it can cause damage and dryness.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

With the right products and techniques, achieving flow men's hair is easier than you might think. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips for maintaining your new look, and you'll be turning heads in no time!
