How To Reduce Facial Hair Growth With Pcos

Written by Luffy Oct 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal disorder that affects women of reproductive age. One of the most common symptoms of PCOS is excessive hair growth on the face, chest, and back. This condition can be embarrassing and affect a woman's self-esteem. In this article, we will discuss how to reduce facial hair growth with PCOS.

Table of Contents

  • How to Reduce Facial Hair Growth with PCOS
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

How to Reduce Facial Hair Growth with PCOS

The first step in reducing facial hair growth with PCOS is to consult with a healthcare provider. They can help determine the best course of treatment for your individual needs. Some treatment options include:

1. Medications

There are several medications that can be prescribed to help reduce facial hair growth. These include:

  • Birth control pills
  • Anti-androgen medications
  • Topical creams
  • Steroids

2. Laser Hair Removal

Laser hair removal is a popular option for reducing facial hair growth with PCOS. This treatment involves using a laser to target the hair follicle, which will eventually lead to permanent hair reduction. Multiple treatments are usually necessary for optimal results.

3. Electrolysis

Electrolysis is another option for reducing facial hair growth with PCOS. This treatment involves using an electric current to destroy the hair follicle. Multiple treatments are also necessary for optimal results.

Step by Step

If you decide to pursue medication as a treatment option, here are the steps you can take:

  1. Consult with a healthcare provider to determine the best medication for you
  2. Take the medication as prescribed
  3. Monitor your progress and side effects
  4. Discuss any concerns with your healthcare provider

If you decide to pursue laser hair removal or electrolysis, here are the steps you can take:

  1. Consult with a licensed professional to determine the best treatment plan for you
  2. Follow any pre- and post-treatment instructions given to you
  3. Attend all scheduled appointments
  4. Discuss any concerns with your provider

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help reduce facial hair growth with PCOS:

  • Eat a healthy diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Get enough sleep
  • Reduce stress
  • Consider using hair removal creams or waxing in between treatments

FAQs

Q: Can facial hair growth with PCOS be completely eliminated?

A: While it may not be possible to completely eliminate facial hair growth with PCOS, treatments such as laser hair removal and electrolysis can lead to permanent hair reduction.

Q: Is it safe to take medication for facial hair growth with PCOS?

A: Like any medication, there are potential side effects. It's important to consult with a healthcare provider to determine the best treatment option for you and to monitor any side effects.

Q: How long does laser hair removal or electrolysis take?

A: The length of treatment depends on the size of the area being treated and the individual's hair growth. Multiple treatments are usually necessary for optimal results.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your skin and hair during and after treatment for facial hair growth with PCOS. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle cleanser and moisturizer
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and exfoliants
  • Protect your skin from the sun with sunscreen
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible

Reducing facial hair growth with PCOS can be a challenge, but with the right treatment plan and self-care routine, it is possible to manage this condition and feel confident in your own skin.
