How To Find The Hair Color That Suits You

Written by Aprilia Jul 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hair color? Do you want to spice things up with a new hair color that suits you? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to find the hair color that suits you.

Description

Our hair color plays a significant role in defining our overall appearance. A hair color that suits your skin tone and features can enhance your beauty and make you feel confident. However, choosing the right hair color can be overwhelming, especially if you are unsure where to start.

How to

Follow these steps to find the hair color that suits you:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding the perfect hair color is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral. Warm skin tones have yellow, golden, or peach undertones, while cool skin tones have pink, red, or blue undertones. Neutral skin tones have a mix of warm and cool undertones.

To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't determine whether your veins are blue or green, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in choosing the right hair color for you. Certain hair colors can enhance the color of your eyes and make them pop. For example, if you have blue eyes, a warm hair color like golden blonde or copper can make your eyes stand out.

Step 3: Determine Your Natural Hair Color

It's essential to consider your natural hair color when choosing a new hair color. If you have dark hair, it may not be realistic to go blonde in one sitting. It's best to choose a hair color that is within two shades of your natural hair color for a more natural look.

Step 4: Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in choosing the right hair color for you. If you lead an active lifestyle or spend a lot of time outdoors, a low-maintenance hair color may be best. On the other hand, if you have a lot of free time and enjoy experimenting with different hairstyles, a more high-maintenance hair color may be suitable.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to consider when choosing the right hair color:

  • Bring pictures of the hair color you want to your hairstylist.
  • Consider the health of your hair before choosing a new hair color.
  • Choose a hair color that complements your wardrobe.
  • Try on wigs or use virtual hair color apps to see what different hair colors would look like on you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding the hair color that suits you:

What hair color looks best on warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones look best with hair colors that have warm undertones, such as golden blonde, caramel, or copper.

What hair color looks best on cool skin tones?

Cool skin tones look best with hair colors that have cool undertones, such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, or burgundy.

Can I go from dark hair to blonde in one sitting?

It's not recommended to go from dark hair to blonde in one sitting as it can damage your hair. It's best to gradually lighten your hair over several sessions to prevent damage.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair after coloring it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, colored hair:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fade.
  • Avoid hot tools and excessive heat styling to prevent damage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.

Now that you know how to find the hair color that suits you, it's time to book your appointment with your hairstylist and try something new! Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hair color that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
