How To Straighten Your Dolls Hair Without A Straightener

Written by Christine Aug 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're a doll collector or just someone who loves playing with dolls, you know how important it is to keep their hair looking neat and tidy. However, straightening doll hair can be quite a challenge, especially if you don't have a straightener. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll show you how to straighten your dolls hair without a straightener.

How to Straighten Your Dolls Hair Without a Straightener

Straightening your doll's hair without a straightener is easy and can be done using some simple household items. Here's what you'll need:

  • A comb
  • Boiling water
  • Cold water
  • A towel

Step by Step

Follow these simple steps to straighten your doll's hair:

  1. Fill a pot with water and bring it to a boil.
  2. Hold your doll's hair firmly and dip it into the boiling water for about 30 seconds.
  3. Remove the doll's hair from the boiling water and let it cool for a few seconds.
  4. Using a comb, gently comb through the doll's hair to straighten it.
  5. Dip the doll's hair into a bowl of cold water to set the straightened hair.
  6. Remove the doll's hair from the cold water and gently pat it dry with a towel.

And that's it! Your doll's hair should now be straight and silky.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when straightening your doll's hair:

  • Make sure to hold the doll's hair firmly when dipping it into the boiling water to avoid burning yourself.
  • Be gentle when combing through the doll's hair to avoid damaging it.
  • Use cold water to set the straightened hair and prevent it from frizzing up again.
  • Repeat the process if your doll's hair is still not straight enough.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about straightening doll hair:

  • Can I use a hair straightener on my doll's hair?

    • It's not recommended to use a hair straightener on your doll's hair as it can damage the hair fibers and cause them to melt. Stick to the boiling water method instead.

  • What if my doll's hair is curly?

    • The boiling water method can also be used to straighten curly hair. Just make sure to hold the hair firmly and comb through it gently to avoid damaging it.

  • How often should I straighten my doll's hair?

    • It's best to avoid straightening your doll's hair too often as it can cause the hair fibers to weaken and break. Once every few months should be enough to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keeping your doll's hair looking beautiful and healthy. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a soft-bristled brush or comb to avoid damaging the hair fibers.
  • Wash the hair gently in lukewarm water with a mild shampoo.
  • Avoid exposing the hair to direct sunlight or heat sources as it can cause the hair fibers to weaken and break.
  • Store your doll in a cool and dry place to prevent the hair from frizzing up or getting damaged.

And there you have it! Now you know how to straighten your doll's hair without a straightener. Follow these tips and your doll's hair will always look neat and beautiful!
