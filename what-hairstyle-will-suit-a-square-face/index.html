What Hairstyle Will Suit A Square Face

Written by Oliver Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a square face shape, you may be wondering what hairstyle will suit you best. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to find the perfect cut for your face shape. However, with a little guidance, you can have a hairstyle that accentuates your features and complements your face shape.

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and square chin. The length and width of the face are roughly the same, which can make it challenging to find a hairstyle that balances out the features. The goal is to soften the angles of the face and create the illusion of length.

How To

When choosing a hairstyle for a square face, it's essential to consider the length, layers, and texture of the hair. Here are some styles that will suit a square face:

Long Layers

Long layers are an excellent option for a square face shape. Layers add movement and soften the angles of the face. Long layers that start below the chin are ideal as they create the illusion of length. Wavy or curly hair works well with this style as it adds more texture and volume.

Side Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for a square face shape. They soften the forehead and add volume to the hair. The key is to have the bangs start at the outer corner of the eye and gradually get longer towards the ears. This style works well with long or medium length hair.

Bob

A bob that falls just below the chin is an excellent option for a square face shape. The length of the bob creates the illusion of length, and the soft layers around the face soften the jawline. This style works well with straight or wavy hair.

Updo

An updo is a great way to accentuate the features of a square face shape. A high bun or ponytail elongates the face, while soft tendrils around the face soften the angles. This style works well for formal events or a day-to-day look.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieve a long layered look:

  1. Wash and condition hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry hair and apply a volumizing mousse.
  3. Blow dry hair with a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to create volume.
  4. Curl the hair with a curling iron, starting from the mid-lengths of the hair.
  5. Run your fingers through the curls to create a soft wave.
  6. Apply a texturizing spray to add volume and texture.
  7. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

  • Avoid blunt cuts as they can accentuate the squareness of the face.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the one that best suits your face shape.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to the hair.
  • Invest in a good quality curling iron or straightener to achieve different hairstyles.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect cut for your face shape.

FAQ

What hairstyles should square faces avoid?

Square faces should avoid blunt cuts, hairstyles with too much volume at the sides, and center partings. These styles can accentuate the squareness of the face.

Can square faces have short hair?

Yes, square faces can have short hair, but it's essential to choose a style that adds volume and movement to the hair. A bob or a pixie cut with soft layers around the face can work well.

What hair color suits a square face?

Any hair color can suit a square face as long as it complements your skin tone. Consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect hair color for you.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, it's essential to follow a hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis as they can damage the hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate the hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Now that you know what hairstyle will suit a square face, it's time to experiment with different styles and find the one that best suits you. Remember, the key is to soften the angles of the face and create the illusion of length. With the right cut and styling, you can have a hairstyle that accentuates your features and complements your face shape.
