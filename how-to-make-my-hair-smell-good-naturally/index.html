How To Make My Hair Smell Good Naturally Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are tired of using chemical-laden hair products that leave your hair smelling artificial, you may be wondering how to make your hair smell good naturally. The good news is that there are plenty of natural remedies that can keep your hair smelling fresh and clean without exposing it to harsh chemicals. In this blog post, we will share some effective tips and tricks for making your hair smell good naturally.

Why Does Hair Smell Bad?

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, it's important to understand why hair can smell bad in the first place. There are several reasons why your hair may have an unpleasant odor, including:

  • Buildup of sweat and oil
  • Product buildup
  • Bacterial growth
  • Environmental factors (e.g. smoke, pollution)

How to Make My Hair Smell Good Naturally: Tips and Tricks

1. Use Essential Oils

Essential oils are a natural and effective way to make your hair smell good. Add a few drops of your favorite essential oil to your shampoo or conditioner, or mix it with a carrier oil (such as coconut oil) and apply it directly to your hair. Some popular essential oils for hair include lavender, peppermint, and rosemary.

2. Try a Vinegar Rinse

Vinegar is a natural deodorizer that can help eliminate odors from your hair. Mix equal parts water and apple cider vinegar, and use it as a rinse after shampooing. Rinse it out thoroughly, and follow up with conditioner if desired.

3. Use Baking Soda

Baking soda is another natural deodorizer that can help eliminate odors from your hair. Mix a tablespoon of baking soda with water, and apply it to your scalp. Leave it on for a few minutes, then rinse it out thoroughly with water.

4. Wash Your Hair Regularly

One of the simplest ways to keep your hair smelling fresh is to wash it regularly. This will help remove any buildup of sweat, oil, or product that can cause odors. However, be careful not to over-wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

5. Use a Clarifying Shampoo

A clarifying shampoo is designed to remove buildup from your hair, and can be especially effective at removing product buildup and hard water deposits. Use a clarifying shampoo once a week or as needed to keep your hair smelling fresh and clean.

6. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like hair dryers, straighteners, and curling irons can cause your hair to become dry and damaged, which can lead to unpleasant odors. Try to avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, and let your hair air dry instead.

7. Use a Hair Perfume

If you want to add a pleasant scent to your hair, consider using a hair perfume. Hair perfumes are specially designed to be used on hair, and can help keep your hair smelling fresh and clean throughout the day.

Hair Care FAQ

Q: Can I use perfume on my hair?

A: While you can use perfume on your hair, it's not recommended. Many perfumes contain alcohol, which can be drying to your hair and scalp. Additionally, the strong scent of perfume can be overpowering, and may not be suitable for everyday use.

Q: Can I use essential oils on my hair every day?

A: It's generally safe to use essential oils on your hair every day, but you should use them in moderation. Essential oils are highly concentrated, and using too much can irritate your scalp or cause other problems.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: The frequency with which you should wash your hair depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week. If you exercise frequently or live in a humid environment, you may need to wash your hair more often.

Conclusion

Making your hair smell good naturally doesn't have to be difficult or expensive. By incorporating some of these simple tips and tricks into your hair care routine, you can keep your hair smelling fresh and clean without exposing it to harsh chemicals. Experiment with different natural remedies to find the ones that work best for you, and enjoy the benefits of healthy, great-smelling hair!
