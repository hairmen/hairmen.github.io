How To Cut My Side Bangs At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of spending money on frequent trips to the salon just to maintain your side bangs? With the right tools and techniques, you can easily cut your side bangs at home. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to cut your side bangs at home in a relaxed English language, including tips and frequently asked questions.

How to Cut My Side Bangs at Home

Cutting your side bangs at home can save you time and money. Here are the tools you need to get started:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Water spray bottle

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your side bangs at home:

  1. Start with dry hair. Comb your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles.
  2. Decide on the length of your side bangs. Use your fingers to measure the length you want and mark it with a comb.
  3. Divide your hair into two sections. Use hair clips to secure the rest of your hair to avoid cutting it accidentally.
  4. Take one section of your hair and comb it straight down in front of your face.
  5. Hold your hair between your index and middle fingers and use your scissors to cut it straight across. Do not cut too much hair at once.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 for the other section of your hair.
  7. Check both sides of your bangs to make sure they are even. If not, trim the longer side carefully until they are even.
  8. Comb your bangs to remove any stray hairs and style them as desired.

Tips for Cutting Side Bangs at Home

Here are some tips to help you achieve a professional-looking cut:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair and uneven cuts.
  • Cut your hair in small sections to ensure precision and avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more if you want shorter bangs. You can always cut more hair, but you cannot undo a bad cut.
  • Trim your bangs when your hair is dry to avoid any surprises after your hair dries.
  • Watch tutorial videos online for more guidance and inspiration.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting side bangs at home:

  • Can I cut my side bangs while my hair is wet? It is not recommended to cut your hair while it is wet, as it can result in an uneven cut when your hair dries.
  • How often should I trim my side bangs? It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your bangs. It is recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks.
  • What if I make a mistake while cutting my side bangs? Do not panic. You can always trim more hair or style your bangs differently. If you are not confident in your abilities, seek the help of a professional stylist.

Hair Care After Cutting Side Bangs

After cutting your side bangs, it is essential to take care of your hair to maintain the look and prevent damage:

  • Wash your hair regularly and use a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply hair oil or serum to keep your hair nourished and shiny.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your side bangs at home and save time and money. Remember to be patient and careful, and do not hesitate to seek the help of a professional stylist if you need it.
