How To Look Girly With Short Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Oct 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old look with your short hair? Do you want to look more feminine and girly? You’ve come to the right place! In this blog post, we will discuss how to look girly with short hair, including tips, tricks, and hair care. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Look Girly with Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • FAQ

How to Look Girly with Short Hair

Short hair can be both chic and feminine. To look girly with short hair, it’s all about adding the right accessories and styling it in a feminine way. Here are some simple steps to follow:

1. Add Accessories

Accessories can make a huge difference in how girly your short hair looks. Try adding a headband, clips, bows, or a flower pin to your hair to add some femininity. You can also wear statement earrings or a necklace to draw attention away from your short hair.

2. Use Hair Products

Using the right hair products can help you achieve a more feminine look with short hair. Try using a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add some body and movement to your hair. You can also use a shine spray to give your hair some shine and make it look healthier.

3. Style Your Hair

The way you style your short hair can make a big difference in how girly it looks. Try curling your hair with a curling iron or straightening it with a flat iron to create different looks. You can also try adding some waves or a side part to your hair to make it look more feminine.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you look girly with short hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.

Step 2: Apply Hair Products

Apply a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to your hair to add some body and movement.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair, using your fingers to lift your hair at the roots to create volume.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a curling iron or flat iron to style your hair, creating waves or curls. You can also add a side part to your hair to make it look more feminine.

Step 5: Add Accessories

Add headbands, clips, bows, or flower pins to your hair to add some femininity.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you look girly with short hair:

1. Experiment with Different Styles

Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles to see what works best for you. Try different hair accessories, styling techniques, and products until you find the perfect look.

2. Keep Your Hair Healthy

Healthy hair is key to looking girly with short hair. Make sure to use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and avoid using too much heat on your hair.

3. Embrace Your Short Hair

Short hair can be just as feminine and girly as long hair. Embrace your short hair and have fun with it!

FAQ

Q: Can I still look girly with short hair if I have a pixie cut?

A: Yes! You can still look girly with a pixie cut by adding accessories and styling it in a feminine way. Try adding a headband or a flower pin to your hair.

Q: What type of hair products should I use to make my short hair look girly?

A: Volumizing mousse, texturizing spray, and shine spray can all help make your short hair look more feminine.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to looking girly with short hair. Here are some hair care tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with products that are suitable for your hair type.

2. Use Heat Protectant

When using heat on your hair, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help keep your short hair looking healthy and girly.

With these tips and tricks, you can look girly and feminine with short hair. Remember to embrace your short hair and have fun with it!
