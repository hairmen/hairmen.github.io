Description

Having a round face can make it difficult to find the right hairstyle that complements your face shape. However, with the right shoulder length hairstyle, you can create an illusion of a slimmer face. Shoulder length hairstyles for round face to look slim are versatile and can be worn in different styles to suit your personality and lifestyle.

Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there are various ways to style your shoulder length hair to make your face appear slimmer. The key is to find a style that elongates your face and adds more definition to your features.

How to Achieve Shoulder Length Hairstyles for Round Face to Look Slim

To achieve shoulder length hairstyles for round face to look slim, you need to find a style that creates an illusion of a longer face. This can be achieved by adding layers, highlights, or lowlights to your hair.

You can also opt for a side-swept or asymmetrical cut to create more definition around your face. Additionally, try to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear rounder.

Best Shoulder Length Hairstyles for Round Face to Look Slim

Some of the best shoulder length hairstyles for round face to look slim include:

  • Long Bob
  • Layered Lob
  • Asymmetrical Bob
  • Side-Swept Bangs
  • Blunt Bob
  • Wavy Lob
  • Half-Up Half-Down
  • Messy Bun

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve shoulder length hairstyles for round face to look slim:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best shoulder length hairstyle for your face shape.
  2. Wash and condition your hair using products suitable for your hair type.
  3. Blow-dry your hair straight or create loose waves using a curling iron.
  4. If you have straight hair, add texture by using a texturizing spray or salt spray.
  5. If you have wavy or curly hair, use a hair serum to define your curls.
  6. Style your hair according to your desired shoulder length hairstyle. You can try a side-swept bob or an asymmetrical lob.
  7. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips to Maintain Shoulder Length Hairstyles for Round Face to Look Slim

To maintain your shoulder length hairstyle and keep it looking slim, follow these tips:

  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and maintain your desired length.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently to prevent hair damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using products that weigh down your hair and make it appear flat.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and scalp.

FAQs

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your length and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a round face?

A: Yes, you can still wear bangs with a round face. However, opt for side-swept bangs instead of blunt bangs to create more definition around your face.

Q: Can I still wear my hair curly if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair curly if you have a round face. However, make sure to add more definition and texture to your curls to create a slimmer appearance.

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Hairstyles for Round Face to Look Slim

Follow these hair care tips for shoulder length hairstyles for round face to look slim:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to prevent hair breakage.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair as it can cause hair breakage and damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid tying your hair too tightly as it can cause hair breakage and damage.
