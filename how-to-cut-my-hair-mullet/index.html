How To Cut My Hair Mullet A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle and want to try something bold and edgy, why not try a mullet? Although it may have a bad reputation in the past, the mullet has made a comeback in recent years and is now a popular hairstyle for both men and women. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut your own hair into a mullet, step-by-step, so you can rock this trendy look.

Description

A mullet is a hairstyle that's shorter at the front and sides but longer at the back. It's often associated with the 80s and 90s and was popularized by musicians such as Billy Ray Cyrus and David Bowie. However, today's mullets are more modern and can be customized to suit your personal style.

How to Cut My Hair Mullet

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using the comb, section your hair into two parts: the top and the bottom. Clip the top section of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Section

Take the bottom section of your hair and comb it straight down. Using your scissors, cut your hair to your desired length. Remember, the back should be longer than the front.

Step 4: Blend the Layers

Using your scissors, blend the layers together so they're not too choppy. Make sure the transition from the front to the back is smooth.

Step 5: Cut the Top Section

Release the top section of your hair and comb it forward. Cut the front section to your desired length. You can choose to keep it short or leave it longer for a more dramatic look.

Step 6: Blend the Layers Again

Using your scissors, blend the layers together so they're not too choppy. Make sure the transition from the front to the back is smooth.

Step 7: Style Your Mullet

Once you've finished cutting your hair, it's time to style your mullet. You can choose to straighten it, curl it, or add some texture with a texturizing spray. Play around with different styles until you find one that suits you.

Tips

- Start with longer hair and gradually cut it shorter. You can always go shorter, but you can't go longer.

- Use a mirror or have someone help you to make sure your layers are even.

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair mullet if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still cut your hair into a mullet if you have curly hair. Just make sure to cut it when it's dry so you can see the length and shape better.

How often should I trim my mullet?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. Generally, you should aim to trim it every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape.

Hair Care

To keep your mullet looking its best, make sure to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type and avoid using too much heat when styling. You can also use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With this step-by-step guide, you'll be able to cut your own hair into a mullet in no time. Remember to have fun with it and make it your own. Who knows, you might just start a new trend!
