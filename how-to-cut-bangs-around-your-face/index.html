How To Cut Bangs Around Your Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your plain old hairstyle and want to switch things up a bit? Cutting bangs around your face can be a great way to add some edge to your look. However, not everyone can afford to go to the salon every time they want a trim. In this blog post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut bangs around your face at home. We will also include some tips and hair care advice to ensure you get the best results possible.

How to Cut Bangs around Your Face

Before we get started with the actual cutting process, let's take a look at the tools you will need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • A mirror

Now that you have all the necessary tools, let's move on to the actual cutting process.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it is completely dry before you begin cutting.

Step 2: Take a section of hair from the top of your head and comb it forward. This will be the section of hair you will be cutting.

Step 3: Use your comb to section off the hair you want to cut. If you want shorter bangs, take a smaller section of hair. If you want longer bangs, take a larger section of hair.

Step 4: Use your scissors to cut the hair in a straight line. If you want choppy bangs, cut the hair at an angle. Remember to start with a little bit of hair and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back.

Step 5: Comb the bangs to make sure they are even. If you notice any uneven spots, use your scissors to trim them.

Step 6: Style your new bangs however you like. You can pin them back or leave them down.

Tips for Cutting Bangs

Tip 1: Start with a little bit of hair and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back.

Tip 2: Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.

Tip 3: If you're unsure about the length, start with longer bangs and work your way up.

Tip 4: Cut your bangs when your hair is dry. Wet hair can appear longer than it actually is.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my own bangs?

A: Yes, you can cut your own bangs. Just make sure you have the necessary tools and follow the steps outlined in this blog post.

Q: Can I cut my bangs if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can cut your bangs if you have curly hair. Just make sure you cut them when your hair is dry, as wet curly hair can appear longer than it actually is.

Hair Care Advice

Cutting your own bangs can be a fun and exciting way to change up your look. However, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure it stays healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your hair stays healthy and strong, even after cutting your own bangs.

Cutting bangs around your face can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your look. With the right tools and a little bit of practice, you can achieve salon-worthy results from the comfort of your own home. Just remember to take your time and follow the steps outlined in this blog post. Happy cutting!
