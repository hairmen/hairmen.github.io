What Ingredient Makes Hair Curly

Written by Bardi Oct 17, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're someone who has always had straight hair and wants to add some curl to your locks, you might be wondering what ingredient makes hair curly. The answer is simple: keratin.

What is Keratin?

Keratin is a protein that is naturally found in hair, nails, and skin. It is responsible for providing strength and structure to these parts of the body. In hair, keratin interacts with other proteins and chemicals to give hair its texture, strength, and elasticity.

How Does Keratin Make Hair Curly?

The amount and type of keratin in your hair can affect its texture. Those with straight hair have a different type of keratin than those with curly hair.

Curly hair has more disulfide bonds between individual strands of keratin. These disulfide bonds create a kink in the hair, giving it its curly texture. Straight hair has fewer disulfide bonds, resulting in a smoother texture.

How Can You Use Keratin to Make Your Hair Curly?

While keratin is necessary for curly hair, it is not the only factor that determines your hair's texture. Genetics, environment, and styling habits also play a role.

There are several ways to use keratin to enhance your hair's curl pattern:

1. Keratin Treatments

A keratin treatment is a chemical process that uses heat to break down the disulfide bonds in hair, making it easier to manipulate. These treatments can be used to make straight hair curly, but they can also be used to enhance the curl pattern in naturally curly hair.

2. Keratin-Infused Products

Many hair care products, such as shampoos, conditioners, and styling products, contain keratin. Using these products can help to strengthen and enhance the curl pattern in your hair.

3. Heat Styling

Heat styling tools, such as curling irons and hot rollers, can be used to manipulate the texture of your hair. While these tools can be damaging to hair if used improperly, they can be a quick and easy way to add curl to straight hair.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

If you have naturally curly hair or have used keratin treatments to enhance your curl pattern, there are several tips you can follow to maintain your curls:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep hair hydrated.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can be damaging to hair, especially curly hair. Try to avoid using them as much as possible, and if you do use them, be sure to use a heat protectant product.

3. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

Curly hair is prone to tangling, so it's important to use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle hair gently.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can Keratin Make Straight Hair Curly?

Yes, keratin treatments can be used to make straight hair curly. The treatment breaks down the disulfide bonds in hair, making it easier to manipulate into a curly texture.

Is Keratin Safe for Hair?

Yes, keratin is safe for hair. It is a natural protein that is already present in hair, nails, and skin. However, keratin treatments can be damaging if not done properly.

How Long Does a Keratin Treatment Last?

A keratin treatment can last anywhere from 2 to 6 months, depending on the individual's hair type and how well they maintain the treatment.

Conclusion

Keratin is the key ingredient that makes hair curly. While genetics play a role in determining hair texture, using keratin treatments, keratin-infused products, and heat styling tools can help to enhance the curl pattern in hair. By following proper hair care techniques, those with naturally curly hair or those who have used keratin treatments can maintain healthy, beautiful curls.
