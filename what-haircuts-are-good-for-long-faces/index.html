What Haircuts Are Good For Long Faces A Complete Guide

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect haircut for your long face? Look no further! In this article, we'll guide you through the best haircuts for long faces, with step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice. Keep reading to find your perfect haircut!

Description

Long faces are characterized by a high forehead, long chin, and narrow cheekbones. The best haircuts for long faces are those that add width to the face, creating balance and proportion. These haircuts typically involve layers, bangs, and volume at the sides of the face. By following our guide, you'll be able to find the perfect haircut for your long face shape.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Long Faces

The first step in choosing the best haircut for your long face is to determine your face shape. To do this, measure the length and width of your face, and compare the two. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have a long face shape.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for haircuts that add width to your face. Avoid haircuts that are too long and straight, as these can make your face appear even longer. Instead, opt for haircuts that add volume and texture to the sides of your face, such as layers or bangs.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Your Haircut

Now that you know which haircut to choose, it's time to get your hair cut! Follow these steps to ensure a successful haircut:

  1. Bring a picture of the haircut you want. This will ensure that your stylist understands your vision.
  2. Communicate with your stylist. Let them know that you have a long face shape and that you want a haircut that adds width to your face.
  3. Choose the right length. Opt for a haircut that ends at or above your shoulders, as this will add volume to the sides of your face.
  4. Add layers. Layers add texture and volume to your hair, creating the illusion of width.
  5. Consider bangs. Bangs that sweep across your forehead can add width to your face.
  6. Style your hair. Use a round brush to add volume to the sides of your hair, and avoid parting your hair down the middle, as this can make your face appear longer.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Now that you have your perfect haircut, it's important to maintain it. Follow these tips to keep your haircut looking fresh:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims. This will prevent split ends and keep your haircut looking neat.
  • Use the right products. Use volumizing products to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair. Blow drying your hair with a round brush can add volume and texture to the sides of your hair.
  • Avoid heavy products. Heavy products can weigh down your hair and make it appear flat.

FAQs About Haircuts for Long Faces

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a long face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a long face. However, it's important to add volume to the sides of your hair to balance out your face shape.

Q: Can I wear a center part if I have a long face?

A: It's best to avoid a center part if you have a long face, as this can make your face appear longer. Instead, opt for a side part or no part at all.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a long face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a long face. However, it's best to avoid a high ponytail, as this can elongate your face. Instead, opt for a low ponytail or a side ponytail.

Hair Care Advice for Long Faces

Now that you have your perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair. Follow these tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid heat styling. Heat styling can damage your hair and make it appear flat.
  • Eat a healthy diet. A healthy diet can promote hair growth and keep your hair healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun. Wear a hat or use a UV-protecting product to prevent damage from the sun.

By following our guide, you can find the perfect haircut for your long face and keep your hair healthy and beautiful. Remember to communicate with your stylist, choose the right haircut, and maintain your hair with the right products and care.
