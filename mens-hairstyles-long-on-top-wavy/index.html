How to Achieve Mens Hairstyles Long on Top Wavy

The mens hairstyles long on top wavy look can be achieved in a number of ways, depending on your hair type and personal preferences. Some of the most popular methods include:

  • Using a curling iron or wand to create loose waves
  • Applying a styling product, such as mousse or salt spray, and scrunching hair to create natural waves
  • Using a diffuser attachment on a hair dryer to enhance natural waves or curls
  • Getting a perm or other chemical treatment to add texture and volume

Regardless of how you choose to achieve the look, the key to mens hairstyles long on top wavy is to keep the hair on top longer than the sides and back. This creates a contrast that draws attention to the wavy texture and adds dimension to the overall style.
