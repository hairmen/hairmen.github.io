How To Style Layered Hair With Straightener A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Layered hair is a popular hairstyle that adds volume and texture to your hair. However, it can be challenging to style, especially if you don’t know how to use a straightener. In this guide, we’ll show you how to style layered hair with a straightener like a pro.

Description

Layered hair has been a popular hairstyle for decades. It’s a versatile style that can be customized to suit any face shape, hair type, and texture. Layered hair adds volume, dimension, and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and more beautiful.

But styling layered hair can be a challenge, especially if you’re not familiar with using a straightener. Using a straightener can help you create a sleek and polished look that will last all day. In this guide, we’ll show you how to style layered hair with a straightener to achieve a beautiful and effortless look.

How to Style Layered Hair with Straightener

Follow these steps to style your layered hair with a straightener:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Before you start styling your hair, you need to prepare it. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and blow-dry it to remove excess moisture. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips. This will make it easier to style your hair and ensure that you don’t miss any sections. Start at the bottom and work your way up.

Step 3: Straighten your hair

Take a small section of hair and clamp the straightener down at the root. Gently glide the straightener down the length of your hair, pulling it away from your head. Repeat this process until you’ve straightened all the sections of your hair.

Step 4: Create waves

If you want to create waves, take a small section of your hair and twist it away from your face. Clamp the straightener down on the twisted section and hold it for a few seconds. Release the section and repeat this process on all the sections of your hair.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finish your look by spraying your hair with a light-hold hairspray. This will help your hair stay in place all day.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your layered hair with a straightener:

  • Use a high-quality straightener with adjustable heat settings
  • Start with a low heat setting and gradually increase it if needed
  • Don’t straighten your hair when it’s wet
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage
  • Section your hair to ensure that you don’t miss any sections
  • Twist your hair to create waves
  • Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your hair in place all day

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling layered hair with a straightener:

Can I straighten my hair every day?

Straightening your hair every day can damage your hair. It’s best to avoid using heat styling tools every day, and instead, use them sparingly.

How do I prevent heat damage?

To prevent heat damage, use a heat protectant spray before using a straightener. Also, don’t use a high heat setting, and don’t hold the straightener in one place for too long.

What should I do if my hair gets tangled in the straightener?

If your hair gets tangled in the straightener, turn the straightener off and gently untangle your hair.

Hair Care

Styling your hair with a straightener can cause damage to your hair. To keep your hair healthy, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoid using heat styling tools every day. Also, avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, and brush your hair gently to prevent breakage.

Styling layered hair with a straightener can be challenging, but with these tips and steps, you can achieve a beautiful and effortless look that will last all day. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
