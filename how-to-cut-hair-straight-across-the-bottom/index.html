How To Cut Hair Straight Across The Bottom A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a new haircut, cutting your hair straight across the bottom can give you a fresh and polished look. This style is perfect for those who want to keep their hair at a consistent length without layers or angles. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut your hair straight across the bottom at home.

  • How to Cut Hair Straight Across the Bottom
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Cutting Hair at Home
  • FAQs

How to Cut Hair Straight Across the Bottom

Before starting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair ties.

First, wash and dry your hair. Brush it out to remove any tangles.

Then, divide your hair into two sections by parting it down the middle. Use hair ties to secure each section at the nape of your neck.

Next, take one section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair at the desired length you want to cut. Make sure the hair is even on both sides.

Using your hair cutting scissors, cut straight across the hair at the desired length. Be sure to use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.

Repeat this process on the other side of your head.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Wash and dry your hair.

2. Part your hair down the middle and secure each section with hair ties.

3. Take one section of hair and comb it straight down.

4. Hold the hair at the desired length you want to cut.

5. Cut straight across the hair at the desired length using sharp hair cutting scissors.

6. Repeat on the other side of your head.

Tips for Cutting Hair at Home

- Make sure your scissors are sharp to avoid split ends.

- Cut small sections of hair at a time to ensure an even cut.

- Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to take your time and cut slowly.

- If you're unsure about cutting your hair at home, consider going to a professional stylist.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my hair straight across the bottom if I have layers?

A: It's best to avoid cutting your hair straight across the bottom if you have layers. This style works best for those with hair that is all one length.

Q: How often should I cut my hair straight across the bottom?

A: It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the length and prevent split ends.

Hair Care after Cutting

After cutting your hair straight across the bottom, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair. Avoid using heat tools and use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.

Cutting your hair straight across the bottom is a simple and easy way to refresh your look. By following these steps and taking care of your hair after cutting, you can achieve a polished and professional style at home.
