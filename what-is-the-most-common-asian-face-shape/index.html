What Is The Most Common Asian Face Shape

What Is the Most Common Asian Face Shape? | Blog

If you are an Asian, you may be wondering what your face shape is. There are various face shapes, and it can be difficult to determine which one you have. In this blog post, we will discuss the most common Asian face shape.

Description

The most common Asian face shape is the oval. An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin and a face length that is longer than the width of the cheekbones.


How to Determine Your Face Shape

Determining your face shape can be done by looking at your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the following:

  • Forehead: Measure the width of your forehead from one hairline to the other.
  • Cheekbones: Measure the width of your cheekbones from the pointiest part of your cheekbones on one side of your face to the other.
  • Jawline: Measure the width of your jawline from one end to the other.

Once you have your measurements, you can compare them to the following face shapes:

  • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the face length is longer than the width of the cheekbones.
  • Round: Cheekbones and face length have a similar measurement, and the forehead and jawline are slightly curved.
  • Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline have similar measurements, and the jawline is angled.
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the cheekbones, and the chin is pointed.
  • Diamond: Cheekbones are wider than the forehead and jawline, and the chin is pointed.


Step-by-Step Guide in Determining Your Face Shape

Here is a step-by-step guide in determining your face shape:

  1. Measure your forehead from one hairline to the other.
  2. Measure your cheekbones from the pointiest part of your cheekbones on one side of your face to the other.
  3. Measure your jawline from one end to the other.
  4. Compare your measurements to the face shapes mentioned above.
  5. Determine which face shape has the most similar measurements to yours.


Tips in Choosing Hairstyles for Your Face Shape

Choosing a hairstyle that complements your face shape can enhance your overall look. Here are some tips in choosing hairstyles for different face shapes:

  • Oval: Most hairstyles look good on an oval face shape, so you can experiment with different styles.
  • Round: Choose a hairstyle that adds height to your face, like a high ponytail or an updo.
  • Square: Soften your jawline with a hairstyle that has layers or waves.
  • Heart: A side-swept fringe or a bob with layers can balance out your forehead and chin.
  • Diamond: A bob or a pixie cut can emphasize your cheekbones.


FAQs

  • Q: Can I have a mix of face shapes?
  • A: Yes, you can have a mix of face shapes. It is common to have a combination of two face shapes.
  • Q: Can I change my face shape?
  • A: No, you cannot change your face shape. Your face shape is determined by your bone structure.
  • Q: Can facial exercises change my face shape?
  • A: No, facial exercises cannot change your face shape. They can only tone your facial muscles.


Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

Aside from choosing the right hairstyle, taking care of your hair is also important. Here are some hair care tips for different face shapes:

  • Oval: Avoid hairstyles that cover your forehead, as it can make your face look shorter.
  • Round: Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add height to your hair.
  • Square: Use a hair serum to add shine and soften your hair.
  • Heart: Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Diamond: Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
