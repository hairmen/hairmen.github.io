How To Cut Your Own Hair Short Style A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon every few weeks to maintain your short hairstyle? Why not try cutting it yourself at home? Not only will you save money, but you'll also have more control over the final result. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut your own hair short style in a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Your Own Hair Short Style
  • Tips for a Successful Haircut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care After Your Haircut

Description

Before we dive into the steps, let's talk about what you'll need. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, a spray bottle filled with water, and a mirror. It's also helpful to have a second mirror or a friend to assist you.

When cutting your own hair, it's important to start with clean, dry hair. This will help you see the natural shape of your hair and prevent any surprises once it's dry. If you have curly hair, you may want to straighten it first to make it easier to cut.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Short Style

Step 1: Start with the Sides

Begin by sectioning off the sides of your hair. Use your comb to create a straight line from the back of your ear to the nape of your neck. Pin the top section of your hair up and out of the way.

Using your scissors, cut the bottom section of your hair straight across. Take your time and make sure both sides are even. Once you're satisfied, release the top section of your hair and repeat on the other side.

Step 2: Move to the Back

Next, it's time to tackle the back of your head. Use your comb to create a straight line down the center of your head, from the crown to the nape of your neck. Pin one side up and out of the way.

Using your scissors, cut the bottom section of hair in a straight line, following the shape of your head. Take small sections at a time and work your way up. Once you've finished one side, repeat on the other.

Step 3: Trim the Top

Finally, it's time to trim the top of your hair. Use your comb to create a straight line from the front of your hairline to the crown of your head. Take small sections at a time and cut in a straight line, following the natural shape of your head.

Once you've finished trimming the top, use your scissors to blend the sides and top together. Take small sections and cut at a slight angle to create a seamless transition.

Tips for a Successful Haircut

  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Start with small sections and work your way up to larger ones.
  • Use your fingers to feel for any uneven spots and trim as needed.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if needed.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair wet or dry?

We recommend cutting your hair when it's dry. This will help you see the natural shape of your hair and prevent any surprises once it's dry.

How often should I cut my short hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. On average, we recommend cutting your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your short style.

Hair Care After Your Haircut

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

We hope this guide has helped you learn how to cut your own hair short style. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if needed. Happy cutting!
