What Color Would Look Good With Red Hair

Written by William Jun 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Red hair is a unique and bold hair color that can be difficult to pair with other colors. If you have red hair and are wondering what colors would look good with it, you're in luck. In this article, we'll discuss the best colors to wear with red hair, and give you some tips on how to make the most of your fiery locks.

Description

Red hair is a beautiful and striking hair color that can be difficult to pair with other colors. If you're not sure what colors would look good with your red hair, you're not alone. Many people struggle to find the right colors to wear with their red hair, but with a little bit of guidance, you can find the perfect color combination that will make your red hair stand out.

How to Choose the Right Colors

When it comes to choosing colors to wear with red hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you'll want to consider the shade of your red hair. Is it a bright, fiery red, or a deeper, more subdued red? The shade of your red hair can make a big difference in which colors will look good with it.

Next, you'll want to consider your skin tone. If you have fair skin, you'll want to look for colors that will complement your skin tone and not wash you out. If you have a darker skin tone, you'll want to look for bold, bright colors that will make your red hair stand out.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right colors to wear with your red hair:

  1. Determine the shade of your red hair.
  2. Consider your skin tone.
  3. Look for colors that complement your skin tone.
  4. Choose bold, bright colors to make your red hair stand out.
  5. Experiment with different color combinations to find what works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make the most of your red hair:

  • Avoid wearing colors that clash with your red hair, such as pink or orange.
  • Try wearing colors that are opposite your red hair on the color wheel, such as green or blue.
  • Accessorize with jewelry or scarves in complementary colors.
  • Experiment with different shades of the same color to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what colors would look good with red hair:

1. Can I wear black with red hair?

Yes, black can look great with red hair. Just be sure to accessorize with complementary colors to avoid looking too dark and gloomy.

2. What colors should I avoid wearing with red hair?

You should avoid wearing colors that clash with your red hair, such as pink or orange. You should also be careful with wearing too much red, as it can be overwhelming.

3. Can I wear neutral colors with red hair?

Yes, neutral colors like white, gray, and beige can look great with red hair. Just be sure to add some color with accessories or makeup to avoid looking too washed out.

Hair Care for Red Hair

If you have red hair, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for red hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair and cause it to lose its vibrancy.
  • Use a color-protecting hair mask once a week to keep your red hair looking bright and shiny.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and choosing the right colors to wear with your red hair, you can make the most of your fiery locks and turn heads wherever you go!
