Why Is My Hair Straight After Perm Explained In Simple Terms

Written by Petter Nov 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you feel disappointed after spending hours in a salon for a perm, only to find out that your hair is straight? If yes, then you are not alone. Many people experience the same issue after getting a perm. In this blog post, we will discuss the reasons why is my hair straight after perm and how to deal with it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A perm, short for permanent wave, is a chemical treatment that is used to give curly or wavy texture to the hair. It works by breaking down the protein bonds in the hair and then restructuring them in a new shape using a perming solution. However, sometimes the results are not as expected, and the hair remains straight or slightly wavy instead of being curly or wavy. This can be due to various reasons, and we will discuss them in the next section.

How to

If you are wondering why is my hair straight after perm, then here are some possible reasons:

Reason 1: Improper Perming Technique

The most common reason why your hair may remain straight after a perm is because of the improper perming technique used by the stylist. If the solution is not applied correctly, or if the hair is not wrapped tightly around the rods, then the curls may not form properly.

Reason 2: Hair Type

The hair type plays a significant role in determining the results of a perm. If you have naturally straight or fine hair, then it may not hold the curls well, and the results may be disappointing.

Reason 3: Chemical Damage

If your hair has been previously chemically treated, such as with relaxers or color treatments, then it may be more susceptible to damage during the perming process. This can result in weak or limp curls or even straight hair.

Reason 4: Aftercare

The aftercare of your hair after getting a perm is crucial for maintaining the curls. If you do not follow the recommended aftercare instructions, such as avoiding heat styling or using harsh shampoos, then the curls may fall out or become straight.

Step by Step

Here are some steps you can take to deal with straight hair after a perm:

Step 1: Wait

It is essential to wait for at least 48 hours after getting a perm before washing or styling your hair. This will allow the curls to set properly and prevent them from falling out.

Step 2: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is specifically designed for permed hair. Harsh shampoos can strip the hair of its natural oils and cause the curls to loosen or fall out.

Step 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons or curling wands on your permed hair. Heat can weaken the curls and cause them to fall out.

Step 4: Get a Touch-up

If your hair is still straight after a perm, then you can consider getting a touch-up from a professional stylist. They can re-perm your hair and ensure that the curls form correctly this time.

Tips

Here are some tips to ensure that you get the best results from a perm:

  • Choose a reputable salon and stylist who has experience in perming.
  • Discuss your hair type and expectations with the stylist before getting a perm.
  • Follow the recommended aftercare instructions to maintain the curls.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your permed hair.

FAQ

Q. Can I perm my hair if it is already chemically treated?

A. It is not recommended to perm your hair if it has been previously chemically treated, such as with relaxers or color treatments. This can result in hair damage and breakage.

Q. How long does a perm last?

A. The duration of a perm depends on various factors such as hair type, aftercare, and perming technique. However, on average, a perm can last for 3-6 months.

Hair Care

Permed hair requires special care to maintain the curls and prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for permed hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your permed hair.
  • Do not brush or comb your hair when it is wet.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or hair products on your permed hair.
  • Do not wash your hair too often, as it can cause the curls to loosen or fall out.

In conclusion, getting a perm can be a great way to add some texture and volume to your hair. However, if your hair remains straight after a perm, then it can be disappointing. By understanding the reasons why is my hair straight after perm and taking the necessary steps to deal with it, you can achieve the beautiful curls you desire.
