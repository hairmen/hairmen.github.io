Can Oval Faces Have Bangs

Written by Petter Nov 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oval-shaped face, you might be wondering if bangs would work for you. After all, bangs can be a great way to switch up your hairstyle and add some interest to your look. But do bangs work for oval faces? The short answer is yes! In this article, we'll explore why oval faces can have bangs and give you some tips for making the most of this hairstyle.

Why Can Oval Faces Have Bangs?

One of the great things about an oval-shaped face is that it's versatile. Oval faces are considered the ideal face shape because they're balanced and proportional. This means that you can pull off a wide variety of hairstyles, including bangs. Bangs can help frame your face and draw attention to your eyes, which is great if you have beautiful eyes you want to show off.

How to Choose the Right Bangs for Your Face

Now that you know that oval faces can have bangs, you might be wondering how to choose the right style. The key is to find a style that complements your face shape and enhances your features. Here are some tips:

  • Consider the thickness of your hair. If you have thin hair, you might want to choose wispy or side-swept bangs that won't overpower your face. If you have thick hair, you can go for thicker, blunt bangs.
  • Think about the length of your forehead. If you have a short forehead, you might want to avoid short bangs that will make your forehead appear even shorter. Instead, opt for longer bangs that can be swept to the side. If you have a long forehead, you can go for shorter bangs that will balance out your face.
  • Consider your personal style. Bangs come in all shapes and sizes, from blunt to wispy to side-swept. Choose a style that fits your personality and your lifestyle.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Bangs

If you're ready to take the plunge and cut your own bangs, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut. Use a comb to create a triangle-shaped section of hair in the front of your head.
  3. Decide on the length you want your bangs to be. Keep in mind that you can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back.
  4. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut straight across. Take your time and cut slowly to avoid making any mistakes.
  5. Check your work in the mirror. Make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

Cutting bangs is one thing, but maintaining them is another. Here are some tips for keeping your bangs looking great:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Blow dry your bangs straight with a round brush to give them volume and bounce.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day to prevent them from getting greasy.
  • Use dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking clean and fresh between washes.

FAQs

Still have questions about whether oval faces can have bangs? Here are some frequently asked questions:

Q: Will bangs make my face look shorter?
A: No, bangs can actually make your face appear longer if you choose the right style. Side-swept bangs can help elongate your face. Q: Can I wear my bangs to the side?
A: Absolutely! Side-swept bangs are a great option for oval faces. Q: What if I have curly hair?
A: Bangs can look great on curly hair, but you'll need to choose a style that works with your curls. Opt for longer, side-swept bangs that blend in with your curls.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

Finally, here are some hair care tips to keep your bangs looking their best:

  • Wash your bangs daily to keep them looking clean and fresh.
  • Blow dry your bangs straight with a round brush to give them volume and bounce.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your bangs, as they can weigh them down.
  • Use a small amount of hair oil to tame any flyaways or frizz.

In conclusion, oval faces can definitely have bangs! The key is to choose a style that complements your face shape and enhances your features. With the right cut and some TLC, your bangs will be the envy of all your friends.
