What To Put In Curly Hair To Make It Look Wet A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your dry, frizzy curls? Do you want to achieve that wet, sleek look but don't know how? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you on what to put in curly hair to make it look wet.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Wet Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

Description

The wet look is a popular hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion. It adds shine and definition to your curls, making them look healthy and luscious. To achieve this look, you need to use the right products that will give your hair the wet texture you desire.

How to Achieve the Wet Look

To achieve the wet look, you need to use hair products that will add texture and shine to your hair. These products include gels, mousses, and oils.

Gels

Gels are the most popular product for achieving the wet look. They are perfect for all hair types and provide a strong hold that lasts all day. Gels come in different strengths, so choose one that suits your hair type. Apply the gel to damp hair and style as usual.

Mousses

Mousses are a lightweight alternative to gels. They provide a softer hold and are perfect for fine hair. Apply the mousse to damp hair and scrunch your curls to create texture.

Oils

Oils are perfect for adding shine and moisture to your curls. They also help to define your curls and reduce frizz. Apply the oil to damp hair and distribute evenly.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve the wet look:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply your chosen product (gel, mousse, or oil) to damp hair.
  3. Style your hair as usual, using your fingers or a diffuser to create texture.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect wet look:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your curls before applying any product.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Use a diffuser to dry your curls to create more texture.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about achieving the wet look:

Can I achieve the wet look on dry hair?

No, you need to apply the product to damp hair to achieve the wet look.

How long does the wet look last?

The wet look can last all day if you use the right products and styling techniques.

Can I achieve the wet look on all hair types?

Yes, the wet look can be achieved on all hair types, but you may need to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

To maintain healthy, luscious curls, it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as this can damage your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to nourish your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can create frizz.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental pollutants by wearing a hat or scarf.

Now that you know what to put in curly hair to make it look wet, you can achieve the perfect wet look with ease. Remember to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you. With the right products and care, you can have healthy, luscious curls all day long!
