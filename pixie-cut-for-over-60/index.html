Pixie Cut For Over 60 How To Get The Perfect Style

Written by Luffy Jun 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're over 60 and looking for a new hairstyle, a pixie cut may be just what you need. This trendy and low-maintenance haircut is perfect for women who want to look chic and stylish without spending hours on their hair. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about getting a pixie cut for over 60, including how to do it, step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that typically involves cutting the hair very close to the scalp on the sides and back, while leaving longer pieces on top. This style is named after the mythical creature, the pixie, which is known for its short hair and mischievous nature. Pixie cuts are popular among women of all ages, but they are especially popular among women over 60 who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that is still stylish and modern.

How to Get a Pixie Cut for Over 60: Step by Step

Step 1: Choose Your Pixie Cut Style

There are many different styles of pixie cuts to choose from, so it's important to find one that works for you. Some popular options include a classic pixie cut, a layered pixie cut, or a textured pixie cut. Look online for inspiration and talk to your hairstylist to find the right style for your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Find a Qualified Hairstylist

Once you've chosen your pixie cut style, it's important to find a qualified hairstylist who has experience cutting short hair. Look for reviews online and ask for recommendations from friends and family. Make sure to bring a picture of the style you want to your appointment to ensure that your hairstylist understands your vision.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Before your appointment, make sure your hair is clean and free of any styling products. Your hairstylist will likely want to wash your hair before cutting it, so it's best to come in with clean, dry hair.

Step 4: Get Your Pixie Cut

During your appointment, your hairstylist will cut your hair according to the style you've chosen. This may involve cutting the hair very short on the sides and back, while leaving longer pieces on top. Your hairstylist may also add layers or texture to your hair to create more volume and movement.

Step 5: Style Your Pixie Cut

Once your haircut is complete, your hairstylist will show you how to style your new pixie cut. This may involve using a styling product to add volume or texture, or simply brushing your hair into place. Make sure to ask your hairstylist for any tips or tricks to keep your hair looking great.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Once you have a pixie cut, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips to help you maintain your pixie cut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create more depth and dimension.
  • Have fun with your new hairstyle and don't be afraid to try new things!

FAQs

1. Will a pixie cut make me look older?

No, a pixie cut can actually make you look younger and more modern. It's all about finding the right style for your face shape and hair type.

2. Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with a pixie cut?

Not really, but you can still use hair accessories like headbands, clips, and barrettes to style your hair in different ways.

3. Will a pixie cut work for all hair types?

Yes, a pixie cut can work for all hair types, but you may need to adjust the style to suit your hair texture and thickness.

Hair Care for Pixie Cut for Over 60

It's important to take good care of your hair after getting a pixie cut. Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and happy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Overall, a pixie cut can be a great choice for women over 60 who want a low-maintenance, stylish haircut. With the right style and a little bit of care, you can rock this trendy and chic hairstyle like a pro.
