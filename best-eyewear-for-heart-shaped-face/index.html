Best Eyewear For Heart Shaped Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Oct 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect pair of eyewear that flatters your heart shaped face? Well, you're not alone! Heart shaped faces have a unique structure that can be a bit tricky to work with when it comes to eyewear. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this ultimate guide, we will be discussing the best eyewear for heart shaped faces and everything you need to know to find the perfect pair.

What is a Heart Shaped Face?

Before we dive into the best eyewear options for heart shaped faces, it's important to understand what a heart shaped face actually is. A heart shaped face is characterized by a broader forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. Essentially, your face shape resembles a heart upside down.

What to Look for in Eyewear for Heart Shaped Faces

When shopping for eyewear, there are a few key things to keep in mind to ensure that you choose a pair that complements your heart shaped face.

Frames with Curved Edges

Heart shaped faces tend to have sharp angles, so it's best to choose frames that have curved edges to soften those angles. Round, oval, and cat-eye frames are excellent options for heart shaped faces.

Bottom-Heavy Frames

Since heart shaped faces have a narrow chin, it's important to choose frames that add a bit of width to the lower half of your face. Bottom-heavy frames like aviators and butterfly frames are great options.

Light Colors and Materials

Lighter colors and materials like pastels, tortoise shell, and clear frames can help balance out the wider forehead of a heart shaped face.

Best Eyewear for Heart Shaped Faces

Now that you know what to look for in eyewear for heart shaped faces, let's dive into some of the best options out there.

Cat-Eye Frames

Cat-eye frames are a classic option for heart shaped faces. They have a slight upward sweep at the outer edges that help balance out the wider forehead. Plus, they're super stylish!

Round Frames

Round frames are another excellent option for heart shaped faces. They help soften sharp angles and draw attention to the eyes.

Aviator Frames

Aviator frames are a great option for heart shaped faces because they add width to the lower half of the face without overwhelming the narrower chin. They also have a classic, timeless look.

Butterfly Frames

Butterfly frames are similar to aviator frames in that they add width to the lower half of the face. However, they have a more feminine, delicate look that is perfect for heart shaped faces.

Tips for Choosing the Right Eyewear

Now that you know some of the best eyewear options for heart shaped faces, here are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing the right pair for you.

Consider Your Skin Tone

When choosing the color of your frames, it's important to consider your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, go for frames in warm colors like gold, brown, and tortoise shell. If you have cool undertones, opt for frames in cooler colors like black, silver, and blue.

Think About Your Personal Style

While it's important to choose frames that flatter your face shape, it's also important to choose frames that fit your personal style. Do you prefer classic, timeless styles or more trendy, statement-making frames? Keep your personal style in mind when choosing eyewear.

Consider Your Hair

Lastly, consider your hair when choosing eyewear. If you have long hair, choose frames that won't get tangled or caught in your hair. If you have shorter hair, opt for frames that won't overpower your face.

FAQs

What are the best eyewear options for heart shaped faces?

Some of the best eyewear options for heart shaped faces include cat-eye frames, round frames, aviator frames, and butterfly frames.

How do I know if I have a heart shaped face?

A heart shaped face is characterized by a broader forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. Essentially, your face shape resembles a heart upside down.

What should I look for in eyewear for heart shaped faces?

When shopping for eyewear for heart shaped faces, look for frames with curved edges, bottom-heavy frames, and light colors and materials.

Hair Care for Heart Shaped Faces

When it comes to hair care for heart shaped faces, it's important to choose styles that balance out the wider forehead and narrower chin. Here are a few tips to keep in mind.

Try Bangs

Bangs are a great way to balance out a wider forehead. Opt for side-swept bangs that graze your eyebrows for a flattering look.

Embrace Layers

Layers are an excellent way to add volume and movement to your hair. Try layers that start at your chin to help balance out the narrower chin of a heart shaped face.

Avoid Heavy Hair at the Top

Avoid hairstyles that have a lot of volume at the top, as this can make your forehead appear even wider. Instead, opt for styles that add volume at the bottom.

Now that you know everything there is to know about choosing the best eyewear for heart shaped faces, it's time to go out and find the perfect pair for you! Keep these tips in mind and don't be afraid to try on a variety of styles until you find the perfect fit.
