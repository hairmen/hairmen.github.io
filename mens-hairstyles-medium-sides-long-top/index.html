Mens Hairstyles Medium Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

If you're looking for a fresh new hairstyle, you may want to consider the mens hairstyles medium sides long top. This style has been trending for a few years now and for good reason- it offers versatility and looks great on almost any face shape. In this guide, we'll discuss everything you need to know about this popular hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Look
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining the Style

Description

The mens hairstyles medium sides long top usually involves keeping the sides of the hair shorter while leaving the top longer. This style offers versatility because you can wear it slicked back, messy, or even with some texture. It's a great option for those who want to keep some length but still want to keep their hair manageable.

How to Get the Look

The first step in getting this hairstyle is to find a good barber or stylist who understands the look you're going for. You'll want to make sure that they can give you an even, clean cut on the sides while leaving enough length on top.

When you go for your appointment, be sure to bring in a photo of the style you want. This will help your stylist understand your vision and give you the best results possible.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the mens hairstyles medium sides long top.

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section off the top of your hair and clip it up and out of the way.
  3. Using clippers or scissors, cut the sides and back of your hair to your desired length.
  4. Blend the sides and top together, making sure that there are no harsh lines.
  5. Unclip the top of your hair and blend it into the sides.
  6. Style your hair using your preferred product.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens hairstyles medium sides long top:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Use a hairdryer to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

What face shape does this style work best on?

The mens hairstyles medium sides long top works well on almost any face shape, but it's particularly flattering on those with oval or square-shaped faces.

How often should I get a trim?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

What products should I use?

You can use a variety of products to achieve this style, including pomade, wax, or gel. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair straighteners or curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to ensure that your hair gets the nutrients it needs to stay healthy.
  • Get enough sleep and exercise regularly to promote healthy hair growth.

With the mens hairstyles medium sides long top, you'll have a versatile and stylish look that's easy to maintain. Whether you're going for a sleek, sophisticated look or a more relaxed, messy style, this haircut is sure to turn heads. Remember to take good care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and strong.
