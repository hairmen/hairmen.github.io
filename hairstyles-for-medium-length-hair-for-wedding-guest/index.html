Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair For Wedding Guest

Written by Petter Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Attending a wedding as a guest can be a lot of fun, but it can also be stressful when it comes to choosing the perfect hairstyle. Whether you're the maid of honor or just a guest, you want to look your best for the occasion. Medium length hair is the perfect length for weddings because it offers many styling options. From sleek updos to loose waves, there are many hairstyles for medium length hair that will make you look and feel beautiful. In this article, we'll share some of the best hairstyles for medium length hair for wedding guests.

Description

Medium length hair is versatile and easy to style, making it the perfect length for a wedding guest. Whether you prefer an updo or loose waves, there are many hairstyles that will make you look and feel beautiful on the big day. The key is to find a style that complements your dress and accentuates your features.

How to

There are many different ways to style medium length hair for a wedding. Some popular options include:

1. Sleek Updo

A sleek updo is a classic and elegant style that is perfect for a wedding. To achieve this look, start by brushing your hair back and securing it into a low ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Use hairspray to keep everything in place and add a touch of shine serum for a glossy finish.

2. Loose Waves

Loose waves are a romantic and feminine hairstyle that is perfect for a wedding. To achieve this look, start by curling your hair with a curling iron or wand. Then, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a natural, tousled look. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold everything in place.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a versatile and easy-to-achieve look that is perfect for a wedding. To achieve this look, start by pulling the top half of your hair back and securing it with a clip or bobby pins. Leave the bottom half of your hair loose and add some loose waves for a romantic touch.

Step by step

If you're not sure how to achieve one of these hairstyles, here are some step-by-step instructions to help you out:

Sleek Updo

  1. Brush your hair back and secure it into a low ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  3. Spray with hairspray to keep everything in place.
  4. Add a touch of shine serum for a glossy finish.

Loose Waves

  1. Curl your hair with a curling iron or wand.
  2. Use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a natural, tousled look.
  3. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold everything in place.

Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Pull the top half of your hair back and secure it with a clip or bobby pins.
  2. Leave the bottom half of your hair loose.
  3. Add some loose waves for a romantic touch.

Tips

When choosing a hairstyle for a wedding, it's important to consider your dress and the overall theme of the wedding. You also want to make sure that your hairstyle complements your face shape and accentuates your features. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle for a wedding:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements the neckline of your dress.
  • Consider the weather and choose a hairstyle that will hold up in the heat or humidity.
  • Don't be afraid to accessorize your hair with a sparkly clip or headband.
  • Choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for medium length hair for wedding guests:

What is the best hairstyle for a wedding guest?

The best hairstyle for a wedding guest depends on your personal style and the overall theme of the wedding. Some popular options include sleek updos, loose waves, and half-up half-down styles.

How do I choose a hairstyle for a wedding guest?

When choosing a hairstyle for a wedding guest, consider your dress, the weather, and your face shape. Choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Can I wear my hair down to a wedding?

Yes, you can wear your hair down to a wedding. Loose waves or soft curls are a great option for a romantic and feminine look.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking and feeling healthy, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.

With these tips and hairstyles for medium length hair for wedding guests, you'll be sure to look and feel your best on the big day!
