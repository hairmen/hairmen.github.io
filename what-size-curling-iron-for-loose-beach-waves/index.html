What Size Curling Iron For Loose Beach Waves

Written by Frank Nov 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to achieve those coveted loose beach waves, you may be wondering what size curling iron is best for the job. In this article, we'll break down everything you need to know about choosing the right size curling iron for loose beach waves.

Description

Loose beach waves are a popular hairstyle that can give you a relaxed and effortless look. This style is achieved by using a curling iron to create loose, tousled waves that resemble the waves you get from spending a day at the beach. To achieve this style, you need to use the right size curling iron.

How to Choose the Right Size Curling Iron

Choosing the right size curling iron for loose beach waves depends on your hair length and the type of waves you want to achieve. Here are some general guidelines:

Short Hair

If you have short hair, you'll want to use a curling iron with a smaller barrel size, around 1 inch or less. This will help you create tighter, more defined waves that look great on shorter hair.

Medium Length Hair

If you have medium length hair, you can use a curling iron with a slightly larger barrel size, around 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 inches. This will help you create loose, beachy waves that look great on medium length hair.

Long Hair

If you have long hair, you can use a curling iron with a larger barrel size, around 1 1/2 to 2 inches. This will help you create loose, flowing waves that look great on long hair.

Step by Step Guide to Creating Loose Beach Waves

Now that you know what size curling iron to use, here's a step by step guide to creating loose beach waves:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant and a texturizing spray to your hair to help hold the waves.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into 1-2 inch sections, depending on the thickness of your hair. Use clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take one section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, starting at the root and working your way down to the ends. Hold the curling iron in place for 5-10 seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process with each section of hair.

Step 4: Finish Your Look

Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently loosen the curls and create a more natural, beachy look. Finish with a texturizing spray or hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Tips for Creating Loose Beach Waves

Here are some additional tips to help you create the perfect loose beach waves:

  • Use a curling iron with a ceramic or tourmaline barrel to help reduce heat damage to your hair.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly - this will create more defined curls, rather than loose waves.
  • Experiment with different barrel sizes to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Use a sea salt spray or texturizing spray to add volume and hold to your waves.
  • Don't forget to use a heat protectant before curling your hair to help prevent damage.

FAQs

Q: Can I create loose beach waves without a curling iron?

A: Yes! You can create loose beach waves using a flat iron or by braiding your hair overnight.

Q: How long will my loose beach waves last?

A: This depends on your hair type and the products you use, but generally, loose beach waves will last for 1-2 days.

Q: Can I create loose beach waves on short hair?

A: Yes! Just use a curling iron with a smaller barrel size to create tighter, more defined waves.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Loose Beach Waves

To keep your loose beach waves looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day - this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it harder to hold a curl.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair - this can cause damage and make it harder to hold a curl.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair - this can weigh it down and make it harder to hold a curl.

Now that you know what size curling iron to use for loose beach waves, you're ready to create this popular and stylish look. With a little practice and the right tools, you'll be rocking those beachy waves in no time!
