How To Get Rid Of Brassy Orange Hair With Purple Shampoo

Written by Petter Jul 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you've ever dyed your hair blonde, you know that it can be difficult to maintain the perfect shade. Over time, blonde hair can turn brassy and orange, leaving you with a less-than-desirable look. Fortunately, there's a simple solution: purple shampoo.

Description

Purple shampoo is a type of shampoo that is designed to neutralize brassy tones in blonde hair. It contains a purple pigment that cancels out the orange and yellow tones in your hair, leaving you with a cooler, more neutral blonde.

How to

Using purple shampoo is easy. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Start by wetting your hair thoroughly. Make sure to use warm water, as hot water can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Step 2: Apply Purple Shampoo

Apply a generous amount of purple shampoo to your hair. Make sure to distribute it evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the areas that are the most brassy.

Step 3: Massage Your Scalp

Massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair for at least two minutes. This will help the purple pigment penetrate your hair and neutralize the brassy tones.

Step 4: Rinse Your Hair

Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water. Make sure to rinse out all of the shampoo, as any leftover product can leave a purple tint in your hair.

Step 5: Condition Your Hair

Finish by conditioning your hair as you normally would. Purple shampoo can be drying, so it's important to use a hydrating conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to using purple shampoo:

  1. Wet your hair with warm water
  2. Apply a generous amount of purple shampoo
  3. Massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair for at least two minutes
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water
  5. Condition your hair as you normally would

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your purple shampoo:

  • Use purple shampoo once a week to maintain your blonde hair
  • Leave the shampoo in your hair for a few extra minutes for a more intense color correction
  • Follow up with a hydrating conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized
  • Avoid using purple shampoo on non-blonde hair, as it can leave a purple tint

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about using purple shampoo:

Q: Can I use purple shampoo on natural blonde hair?

A: Yes, purple shampoo can be used on natural blonde hair to prevent brassy tones from appearing.

Q: How often should I use purple shampoo?

A: It's best to use purple shampoo once a week to maintain your blonde hair. Using it more frequently can lead to over-toning and dryness.

Q: Can I leave purple shampoo in my hair overnight?

A: No, it's not recommended to leave purple shampoo in your hair overnight. This can lead to over-toning and dryness.

Hair Care

Using purple shampoo is a great way to keep your blonde hair looking its best. However, it's important to take care of your hair in other ways as well. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid heat styling your hair too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as it can cause breakage
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized

With these tips and the help of purple shampoo, you can say goodbye to brassy orange hair and hello to a beautiful, neutral blonde.
