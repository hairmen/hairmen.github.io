What Hairstyle Suits You Best A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Look

Written by Daniel Aug 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of always wearing your hair in the same old style? Maybe you're looking for a change, but you're not sure what will suit you best. That's where we come in! In this article, we'll guide you through the process of finding the perfect hairstyle for your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. So let's get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. This will help you understand which hairstyles will flatter your features and which ones to avoid. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and examine the proportions of your face. Measure the length and width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline to determine which category you fall into.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This is considered the most versatile face shape and can pull off almost any hairstyle. You can choose from long, short, curly, or straight hair, and it will all look great on you.

Round Face Shape

For those with a round face shape, it's best to avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face. Instead, go for styles that add volume on top or lengthen your face. Long, layered hair or a side-swept bang can be flattering.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, try to soften your angles with hairstyles that have soft layers or waves. Avoid blunt cuts or straight hairstyles that can accentuate your jawline.

Heart Face Shape

For heart-shaped faces, it's all about balance. Look for hairstyles that add volume to the bottom half of your face, such as chin-length bobs or long, layered styles. Avoid blunt bangs or hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head.

Diamond Face Shape

Diamond-shaped faces can pull off a variety of hairstyles, but it's best to avoid styles that add too much volume to the top of your head. Instead, go for styles that frame your face, such as layered bobs or side-swept bangs.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, it's best to avoid long, straight hair that can make your face appear even longer. Instead, go for hairstyles that add volume or width to the sides of your face, such as a curly bob or a layered cut with side-swept bangs.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to find your perfect hairstyle. Follow these steps to narrow down your options:

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Texture

Do you have straight, wavy, curly, or kinky hair? This will affect which hairstyles will work best for you. For example, if you have curly hair, you may want to avoid short, blunt cuts that can make your hair appear poofy. Instead, opt for layered cuts that will enhance your curls.

Step 2: Consider Your Lifestyle

Are you a busy mom who doesn't have time for a high-maintenance hairstyle? Or do you have a job that requires a more polished look? Consider your lifestyle when choosing your hairstyle. If you don't have much time to style your hair, go for a low-maintenance cut that can be styled quickly.

Step 3: Find Inspiration

Browse magazines, Pinterest, or Instagram for inspiration. Look for hairstyles on people with a similar face shape and hair texture as you. Save pictures of hairstyles you like to show your stylist.

Step 4: Consult with Your Stylist

Your stylist is an expert in hair and can help guide you in the right direction. Show them the pictures you've saved and ask for their opinion on which styles will work best for you. They may also have suggestions that you haven't considered.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found your perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed for your hair type and style. For example, if you have curly hair, use products that enhance your curls and reduce frizz. If you have straight hair, use products that add shine and reduce flyaways.

Tip 2: Get Regular Trims

Even if you're growing out your hair, it's important to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. Also, wear a hat or use an umbrella to protect your hair from the sun.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I'm not sure what face shape I have?

You can use a ruler or measuring tape to measure the length and width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, use a face shape chart to determine which category you fall into.

Can I pull off a drastic hair color change?

It depends on your skin tone and personal style. Consult with your stylist to determine which hair color will suit you best.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once a week. Consult with your stylist to determine the best washing schedule for your hair.

Conclusion

Finding your perfect hairstyle can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, you can narrow down your options and find a hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident. Remember to consult with your stylist and take good care of your hair to keep it looking great.
