How To Style A Messy Pixie Cut Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Nov 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a pixie cut, you know that it can be both liberating and frustrating at the same time. On one hand, you have a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal effort to look good. On the other hand, it can be challenging to style a messy pixie cut that doesn't look too messy. In this blog post, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to style a messy pixie cut that looks effortlessly chic.

Description

A messy pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is tousled and textured to create a messy, bedhead look. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to style and maintain. The messy pixie cut can be styled in different ways, from sleek and sophisticated to tousled and edgy.

How to Style a Messy Pixie Cut

Here are some tips and tricks on how to style a messy pixie cut:

1. Start with a clean and dry hair

Before styling your messy pixie cut, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of styling product to add texture and volume.

2. Use a texturizing spray

Texturizing spray is a must-have product for styling a messy pixie cut. It adds volume, texture, and hold to your hair, making it easier to style. Spray a small amount of texturizing spray on your hair and use your fingers to tousle and style your hair.

3. Experiment with different styling techniques

There are many ways to style a messy pixie cut, from slicked back to tousled and edgy. Experiment with different techniques to find the one that works best for you. You can use a comb, your fingers, or a styling brush to create different looks.

4. Add accessories

Accessories can add a touch of glamor to your messy pixie cut. You can use a headband, hair clips, or a scarf to add a pop of color and style to your hair. Choose accessories that complement your outfit and personality.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style a messy pixie cut:

Step 1: Start with clean and dry hair

Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of texturizing spray to add volume and hold.

Step 2: Use a comb or brush to style your hair

Use a comb or brush to create the desired shape and style for your pixie cut. You can slick your hair back, create a side part, or tousle your hair for a messy look.

Step 3: Use your fingers to add texture

Use your fingers to add texture and volume to your hair. Tousle your hair to create a messy, bedhead look. You can also use your fingers to create spikes or define your bangs.

Step 4: Add accessories

Add accessories to your hair to add a touch of style and glamour. You can use a headband, hair clips, or a scarf to complement your outfit and personality.

Tips for Maintaining a Messy Pixie Cut

Here are some tips for maintaining a messy pixie cut:

1. Trim your hair regularly

Regular trims will keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

2. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner

Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using harsh chemicals and products that can damage your hair.

3. Protect your hair from heat

Avoid using heat styling tools that can damage your hair. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair.

4. Experiment with different styles

Experiment with different styles and techniques to find the one that works best for you. Don't be afraid to try something new and bold.

FAQ

Q: Is a messy pixie cut suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, a messy pixie cut can be customized to suit different face shapes. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best style for your face shape.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Q: Can I use a hair straightener to style my messy pixie cut?

A: Yes, you can use a hair straightener to create a sleek and sophisticated look for your messy pixie cut. However, be careful not to overheat your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining a healthy and vibrant messy pixie cut:

1. Eat a healthy diet

Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals will promote healthy hair growth and prevent hair loss.

2. Protect your hair from the sun

Avoid exposing your hair to the sun for prolonged periods, as this can cause damage and dryness.

3. Use a deep conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. This will keep your hair healthy and shiny.

With these tips and tricks, you can style a messy pixie cut that looks effortlessly chic and stylish. Experiment with different techniques and styling products to find the ones that work best for you. Don't be afraid to try something new and bold, as a messy pixie cut is all about expressing your individuality and personality.
