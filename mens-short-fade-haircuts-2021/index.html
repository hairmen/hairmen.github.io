Types of Fades

Before we dive into the different types of fades, it's important to understand what a fade is. A fade is a type of haircut where the hair gradually gets shorter from the top of the head down to the sides and back.

There are three main types of fades:

Low Fade

A low fade starts near the ear and gradually tapers down to the nape of the neck. This type of fade is great for men who want a subtle and natural-looking haircut.

Mid Fade

A mid fade starts higher up on the head and gradually tapers down to the nape of the neck. This type of fade is perfect for men who want a more noticeable and structured haircut.

High Fade

A high fade starts at the temples and gradually tapers down to the nape of the neck. This type of fade is great for men who want a dramatic and edgy haircut.

How to Get a Short Fade Haircut

The first step in getting a short fade haircut is finding a reputable hairstylist or barber who specializes in fades. Once you've found a stylist, it's important to communicate exactly what type of fade you want.

During the haircut, your stylist will use clippers to gradually taper the sides and back of your head. They'll also use scissors to trim the hair on top of your head to your desired length.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a short fade haircut:

  1. Find a reputable hairstylist or barber who specializes in fades.
  2. Communicate exactly what type of fade you want.
  3. Sit back and relax while your stylist gradually tapers the sides and back of your head with clippers.
  4. Trim the hair on top of your head to your desired length with scissors.
  5. Style your hair using your favorite hair products.

Tips for Maintaining a Short Fade Haircut

Maintaining a short fade haircut requires a bit of effort, but it's definitely worth it. Here are some tips for keeping your haircut looking fresh:

  • Visit your stylist or barber every 2-3 weeks for a touch-up.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Invest in a good quality hair trimmer to keep the sides and back of your head looking sharp.
  • Experiment with different hair products, such as pomades or gels, to find the perfect styling product for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: How long does a short fade haircut last?

A: A short fade haircut typically lasts 2-3 weeks before it starts to lose its shape and definition.

Q: Can I style my hair differently with a short fade haircut?

A: Yes, you can style your hair in a variety of ways with a short fade haircut. Experiment with different hair products and styling techniques to find the perfect look for you.

Q: Will a short fade haircut work for all hair types?

A: Yes, a short fade haircut can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Hair Care Tips

Keeping your hair healthy and moisturized is key to maintaining a great-looking short fade haircut. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye, as they can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and nutrients to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to rocking a stylish and trendy mens short fade haircut in 2021.
