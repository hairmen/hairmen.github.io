Is Short Hair For Long Faces The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Jul 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you someone with a long face and wondering whether short hair would suit you? Look no further! In this guide, we will answer all your questions about whether short hair is suitable for long faces.

Description

A long face is characterized by a high forehead, long chin, and narrow cheeks. People with long faces often struggle to find the right hairstyle that suits their face shape. However, short hair has become increasingly popular in recent years, and many wonder if it would suit their long face shape.

How to

If you are considering getting short hair with a long face, it's essential to consider the following:

Face Shape

Different short hairstyles will suit different face shapes, so it's essential to determine your face shape before choosing a hairstyle. If you have a long face shape, consider a short hairstyle that adds width to your face, such as a bob or a pixie cut.

Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can also affect which short hairstyle would suit you. If you have thick hair, a short bob would look great. If you have thin hair, a pixie cut would add volume and texture to your hair.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also affect which short hairstyle would suit you. If you have a busy lifestyle, a short hairstyle that requires minimal maintenance, such as a pixie cut, would be ideal.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to help you decide if short hair would suit your long face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

There are different face shapes, including oval, round, square, and heart-shaped. Determining your face shape will help you choose a short hairstyle that suits you.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can affect which short hairstyle would look great on you. Thick hair would look great with a short bob, while thin hair would look great with a pixie cut.

Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can affect which short hairstyle would suit you. If you have a busy lifestyle, a short hairstyle that requires minimal maintenance, such as a pixie cut, would be ideal.

Step 4: Consult a Hairstylist

If you are still unsure which short hairstyle would suit you, consult a hairstylist. They will help you choose a short hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you choose the right short hairstyle for your long face:

  • Choose a short hairstyle that adds width to your face.
  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a short hairstyle.
  • Consider your lifestyle when choosing a short hairstyle.
  • Consult a hairstylist if you are unsure which short hairstyle would suit you.

FAQ

Is short hair suitable for long faces?

Yes, short hair can be suitable for long faces. However, it's essential to choose a short hairstyle that adds width to your face.

What short hairstyle would suit a long face?

A short bob or a pixie cut would suit a long face. These hairstyles add width to your face and are low maintenance.

Can you have bangs with short hair for a long face?

Yes, you can have bangs with short hair for a long face. However, it's essential to choose the right type of bangs that suit your face shape.

Hair Care

Regardless of whether you have short or long hair, it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use hair products that suit your hair type.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your hairstyle.

With these tips and information, you can now determine whether short hair is suitable for your long face. Remember to choose a short hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Consult a hairstylist if you need further assistance in choosing the right short hairstyle for your long face.
