How Much Do Curly Hair Extensions Cost

Written by Bardi Sep 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair extensions are a great way to add volume and length to your natural curls. But before you invest in a set of curly hair extensions, it's important to know how much they cost. In this article, we'll break down the cost of curly hair extensions, as well as provide tips on how to care for them.

Description

Curly hair extensions come in a variety of styles, lengths, and colors. They can be made from synthetic or human hair, and can be attached to your natural hair using a variety of methods, including clip-ins, tape-ins, sew-ins, and fusion.

How To

The cost of curly hair extensions will depend on several factors, including the type of hair used, the length of the extensions, the method of attachment, and where you purchase them from. Here is a breakdown of the average cost of curly hair extensions:

Synthetic Hair Extensions

Synthetic hair extensions are the most affordable option, with prices ranging from $10 to $100. However, synthetic hair is not as durable as human hair, and may not look as natural.

Human Hair Extensions

Human hair extensions are more expensive than synthetic hair, with prices ranging from $100 to $500 or more. However, human hair is more durable and looks more natural than synthetic hair.

Length of Extensions

The longer the extensions, the more expensive they will be. Expect to pay more for extensions that are 18 inches or longer.

Method of Attachment

The method of attachment will also affect the cost of your curly hair extensions. Clip-in extensions are the most affordable, while fusion extensions are the most expensive.

Where to Purchase

The price of curly hair extensions will also depend on where you purchase them from. Online retailers and beauty supply stores may offer lower prices than specialty salons.

Step by Step

When purchasing curly hair extensions, it's important to consider your budget and your hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you choose the best curly hair extensions for your needs:

  • Choose the right type of hair for your needs: synthetic or human.
  • Consider the length of the extensions you want.
  • Choose the method of attachment that works best for you.
  • Shop around for the best prices.
  • Take good care of your extensions to ensure they last as long as possible.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you care for your curly hair extensions:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent drying out your extensions.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your extensions, as this can damage the hair.
  • Brush your extensions gently to prevent tangling.
  • Store your extensions in a cool, dry place when not in use.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curly hair extensions and their cost:

How long do curly hair extensions last?

With proper care, curly hair extensions can last anywhere from 3 months to a year or more.

Can I dye my curly hair extensions?

Human hair extensions can be dyed, but synthetic hair cannot.

Can I swim with curly hair extensions?

It's best to avoid swimming with your curly hair extensions, as chlorine and salt water can damage the hair.

Hair Care

Taking care of your curly hair extensions is essential to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your extensions every 2-3 weeks, depending on how often you wear them.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or styling products on your extensions.
  • Detangle your extensions gently using a wide-toothed comb.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet extensions, as this can cause tangling.

Now that you know how much curly hair extensions cost and how to care for them, you can make an informed decision about whether or not to invest in a set of extensions. Remember to choose the right type of hair for your needs, consider the length and method of attachment, and take good care of your extensions to ensure they last as long as possible.
