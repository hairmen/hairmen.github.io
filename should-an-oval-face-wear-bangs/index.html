Should An Oval Face Wear Bangs A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but not sure if they will suit your face shape? If you have an oval face, you might be wondering if bangs are a good idea for you. In this article, we will answer the question, "should an oval face wear bangs?" and provide you with tips, step-by-step guidance, and hair care advice to help you make an informed decision.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine If Bangs Are Right for Your Oval Face

  • Hair Care Advice for Oval Faces with Bangs

Description

An oval face is considered to be the ideal face shape, as it is well-balanced and symmetrical. If you have an oval face, you have a slightly wider forehead than your chin, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face. This face shape is versatile and can pull off a variety of hairstyles, including bangs.

How to Determine If Bangs Are Right for Your Oval Face

Bangs can be a great way to change up your hairstyle and add some interest to your look. However, not all types of bangs are suitable for an oval face. Here are some things to consider when deciding whether or not to get bangs:

  • Forehead size: If you have a larger forehead, bangs can help balance out your face and make it appear smaller. However, if you have a small forehead, bangs can make your face look shorter and wider.
  • Hair texture: If you have fine hair, wispy bangs can be a good choice, as they can add volume and texture to your hair. If you have thick hair, blunt bangs can help tame your hair and give it a sleek look.
  • Face shape: While oval faces can pull off many types of bangs, some styles are more flattering than others. For example, side-swept bangs can help soften a square jawline, while blunt bangs can accentuate a round face shape.

Ultimately, the decision to get bangs comes down to personal preference. If you're unsure whether or not bangs are right for you, talk to your hairstylist, who can offer advice based on your face shape and hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Bangs for an Oval Face

If you've decided to get bangs, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the look:

  1. Decide on the type of bangs you want. Consider the factors mentioned above, such as your forehead size, hair texture, and face shape.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist. Your stylist can help you determine the best type of bangs for your face shape and hair type.
  3. Prepare your hair for the cut. Wash and dry your hair as you would normally. Make sure your hair is completely dry before getting it cut.
  4. Sit still and let your stylist work their magic. Your hairstylist will cut your bangs according to the style you've chosen. They may use a razor or scissors, depending on the type of bangs you want.
  5. Style your bangs. Once your bangs are cut, style them according to your preference. You can blow-dry them straight or use a curling iron to add some waves.

Remember, it's important to go to a reputable hairstylist to ensure your bangs are cut correctly and look great.

Tips for Maintaining Bangs on an Oval Face

Once you have bangs, it's important to take care of them to ensure they stay looking great. Here are some tips to help you maintain your bangs:

  • Get regular trims. Bangs grow quickly and can easily become unruly if not trimmed regularly. Plan to get your bangs trimmed every 4-6 weeks.
  • Blow-dry your bangs. Use a round brush and a blow-dryer to style your bangs. This will help them stay in place and prevent them from looking greasy.
  • Avoid touching your bangs. Your hands contain oils that can make your bangs look greasy. Try to avoid touching your bangs throughout the day.
  • Use dry shampoo. If your bangs start to look greasy between washes, use dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and refresh your hair.

FAQs About Bangs and Oval Faces

Here are some frequently asked questions about bangs and oval faces:

What types of bangs are best for oval faces?

Oval faces can pull off many types of bangs, including blunt bangs, side-swept bangs, and wispy bangs. However, it's important to consider your forehead size, hair texture, and face shape when choosing the best type of bangs for you.

Can bangs make an oval face look shorter?

If you have a small forehead, bangs can make your face look shorter and wider. However, this can be balanced out by choosing longer, side-swept bangs that help elongate the face.

How do I know if bangs will suit me?

If you're unsure whether or not bangs will suit you, talk to your hairstylist. Your stylist can offer advice based on your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care Advice for Oval Faces with Bangs

Here are some hair care tips to help you keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly. How often you wash your hair depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day, while if you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.
  • Use a conditioner. Conditioner helps moisturize your hair and keep it looking healthy. Be sure to apply conditioner to the ends of your hair, where it's most needed.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often. Heat tools like curling irons and straighteners can damage your hair over time. Try to limit your use of these tools to once or twice a week.
  • Protect your hair from the sun. The sun's UV rays can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful, whether you have bangs or not.

Final Thoughts

So, should an oval face wear bangs? The answer is yes, an oval face can pull off many types of bangs. However, it's important to consider your forehead size, hair texture, and face shape when choosing the best type of bangs for you. Talk to your hairstylist, follow our step-by-step guide, and use our hair care tips to keep your hair looking great!
