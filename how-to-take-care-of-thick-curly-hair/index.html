How To Take Care Of Thick Curly Hair

Written by William Oct 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Thick curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can be beautiful and voluminous, it can also be difficult to manage and prone to frizz. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks on how to take care of thick curly hair.

How to Take Care of Thick Curly Hair

First and foremost, it’s important to understand that every person’s hair is unique and requires a personalized approach to care. However, there are some general guidelines that can be followed to ensure your thick curly hair stays healthy and beautiful.

1. Shampoo Less Frequently

Thick curly hair does not require daily shampooing. Over-washing can strip hair of its natural oils, leading to dryness and breakage. Instead, consider shampooing every 2-3 days or using a co-wash product to cleanse hair without stripping it of its natural oils.

2. Condition Regularly

Curly hair is prone to dryness, so conditioning is essential. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. Leave the treatment on for at least 10 minutes before rinsing out. Additionally, use a leave-in conditioner after washing to help detangle and moisturize your curls.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Avoid using a brush on your curly hair, as it can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair while it’s damp. Start at the ends and work your way up, taking care to avoid pulling or tugging on knots.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat can damage curly hair, causing it to become brittle and prone to breakage. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature on the lowest setting. Alternatively, embrace your natural curls and try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to taking care of your thick curly hair:

  1. Shampoo every 2-3 days or use a co-wash product
  2. Deep condition once a week
  3. Use a leave-in conditioner after washing
  4. Gently detangle using a wide-tooth comb while damp
  5. Avoid heat styling or use a heat protectant spray

Tips for Managing Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing your thick curly hair:

  • Avoid towel-drying your hair, as it can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently blot excess water.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase or wrap your hair in a silk scarf to prevent breakage and frizz.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to add shine and moisture to your curls.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
