Mens Haircuts Short Sides Medium Top Straight Hair A Guide For 2023

Looking for a trendy and low-maintenance haircut for your straight hair? Mens haircuts short sides medium top straight hair might be the perfect choice for you. This style features short buzzed or faded sides and a longer top that can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about this popular haircut, from how to get it to how to maintain it.

How to Get Mens Haircuts Short Sides Medium Top Straight Hair

The first step to getting mens haircuts short sides medium top straight hair is to find a reputable barber or hairstylist who is experienced in this style. Show them some pictures of the haircut you want, and discuss any specific details or preferences you have, such as how short you want the sides or how long you want the top.

Once you're in the chair, your barber or hairstylist will likely start by using clippers to buzz or fade the sides, leaving the top longer. They may use scissors or a razor to blend the sides and top, depending on the look you're going for. It's important to communicate with your stylist throughout the process to ensure you're happy with the length and shape of your haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Mens Haircuts Short Sides Medium Top Straight Hair

If you're feeling brave and want to try cutting your own mens haircuts short sides medium top straight hair, here's a basic guide:

  1. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair.
  2. Section off the top of your hair with clips or hair ties.
  3. Use clippers to buzz or fade the sides, starting with a longer guard and working your way down to a shorter one.
  4. Blend the sides and top using scissors or a razor, taking care to create a seamless transition.
  5. Release the top section of hair and trim it to your desired length and style.
  6. Finish by styling your hair with product, such as pomade or wax.

Tips for Styling Mens Haircuts Short Sides Medium Top Straight Hair

One of the benefits of mens haircuts short sides medium top straight hair is that it's versatile and can be styled in many ways. Here are some tips to help you achieve your desired look:

  • For a slicked-back style, use a comb to smooth your hair back and apply a generous amount of pomade or gel.
  • For a messy or textured style, use your fingers to tousle your hair and apply a small amount of wax or clay.
  • For a more voluminous style, blow-dry your hair upside down and use a round brush to lift the roots.
  • Experiment with different parting styles, such as a side part or no part at all.
  • Keep your hair trimmed regularly to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions About Mens Haircuts Short Sides Medium Top Straight Hair

Is this haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Mens haircuts short sides medium top straight hair can work well for most face shapes, but it's especially flattering for those with square, oval, or heart-shaped faces.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the shape of your haircut starts to lose its definition. Generally, getting a trim every four to six weeks can help maintain the look.

Can I still style my hair if it's thin or fine?

Absolutely! Mens haircuts short sides medium top straight hair can actually make thin or fine hair appear thicker and fuller. Use lightweight products and avoid heavy styling that can weigh your hair down.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Haircuts Short Sides Medium Top Straight Hair

To keep your mens haircuts short sides medium top straight hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can build up and make your hair look greasy or weighed down.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock mens haircuts short sides medium top straight hair with confidence and style. Whether you go to a professional or try cutting your own hair, remember to communicate your preferences and maintain your haircut regularly for the best results.
