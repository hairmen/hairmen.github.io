What Hair Part Is Best For My Face Shape

Choosing the right hair part can make a big difference in how your hairstyle looks and complements your facial features. If you have ever wondered what hair part is best for your face shape, this article is for you. In this post, we will guide you through the process of finding the perfect hair part for your face shape, and provide you with tips and tricks to style your hair in a way that flatters your features.

What is Face Shape?

Face shape is the outline of your face as determined by the bone structure and the underlying tissue. There are four main face shapes: round, square, oval, and heart-shaped. Each face shape has its own unique features, and it is important to choose a hair part that enhances these features.

How to Determine Face Shape

There are several ways to determine your face shape, but the easiest way is to look in the mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or a washable marker. Alternatively, you can measure your face using a tape measure and compare the measurements. Below are the steps to determine your face shape:

  1. Measure your forehead from the widest point to the hairline.
  2. Measure your cheekbones from the widest point.
  3. Measure your jawline from the widest point.
  4. Measure the length of your face from the hairline to the chin.

Once you have these measurements, compare them to the following guidelines:

  • Round face: The width and length of your face are almost equal, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face.
  • Square face: Your face is almost as wide as it is long, and your jawline is the most prominent feature.
  • Oval face: Your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead is the widest part of your face.
  • Heart-shaped face: Your forehead is wider than your jawline, and your chin is pointed.

Hair Part for Round Face

If you have a round face shape, the best hair part is a side part. This will create the illusion of length and slim down your face. Avoid center parts, as they can make your face appear rounder.

Hair Part for Square Face

If you have a square face shape, the best hair part is a side part as well. This will soften your features and create a more feminine look. Avoid center parts, as they can emphasize your strong jawline.

Hair Part for Oval Face

If you have an oval face shape, you are lucky, as almost any hair part will suit you. However, a center part will emphasize your symmetry and create a classic look.

Hair Part for Heart Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, the best hair part is a side part as well. This will balance your features and create a more harmonious look. Avoid center parts, as they can make your forehead appear larger.

Tips on Styling Hair

Once you have determined the best hair part for your face shape, it is important to style your hair in a way that flatters your features. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair in the direction of your hair part.
  • Use a round brush to add volume and bounce to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as waves, curls, and updos.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my hair part?

Yes, you can change your hair part at any time. However, it may take some time for your hair to adjust to the new part, so be patient.

What if I have a short hairstyle?

Short hairstyles can also be styled with different hair parts. The same guidelines apply, but you may need to use styling products to create volume and texture.

What if I have bangs?

If you have bangs, you can still follow the guidelines for your face shape. However, you may need to adjust the length and thickness of your bangs to complement your features.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Now that you know what hair part is best for your face shape, it's time to experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
