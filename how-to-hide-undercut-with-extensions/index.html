How To Hide Undercut With Extensions A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Undercut hairstyles have been in trend for quite some time now, and they are not going anywhere soon. However, sometimes you might want to switch up your look and hide the undercut temporarily. That's where extensions come in handy. In this blog post, we will show you how to hide undercut with extensions step-by-step.

Description

An undercut is a popular hairstyle where the sides and back of the head are shaved or cut very short, leaving longer hair on top. While undercut hairstyles can look edgy and stylish, they're not suitable for everyone, especially if you have a formal event to attend. That's where extensions come in handy. You can hide your undercut and achieve a more formal look with the help of extensions.

How to Hide Undercut with Extensions

Here's how you can hide your undercut with extensions:

Step-by-step Guide

Step 1: Start by sectioning off the top layer of your hair, leaving the undercut exposed.

Step 2: Take a small section of hair from the back of your head, just above the nape of your neck, and clip in a weft of hair extensions. Repeat this process until you have covered the entire undercut area.

Step 3: Release the top layer of hair and style as desired. Use a curling iron or straightener to blend the extensions with your natural hair.

Step 4: Finish with a spritz of hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Hiding Undercut with Extensions

Here are some helpful tips for hiding your undercut with extensions:

  • Choose high-quality hair extensions that match your natural hair color and texture.
  • Use clip-in extensions for easy installation and removal.
  • Blend the extensions with your natural hair by curling or straightening.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the best one that hides your undercut.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hiding undercut with extensions:

Q: Can I use tape-in or sew-in extensions to hide my undercut?

A: Yes, you can use tape-in or sew-in extensions, but clip-in extensions are the easiest to install and remove.

Q: How long do extensions last?

A: The lifespan of your extensions depends on the quality and how well you take care of them. High-quality extensions can last up to a year with proper care.

Q: Can I wash my hair with extensions?

A: Yes, you can wash your hair with extensions, but be gentle and use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging the extensions.

Hair Care Tips for Extensions

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your extensions:

  • Brush your extensions regularly to prevent tangling.
  • Use heat protectant spray before using hot tools to style your hair.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair, as it can cause matting and tangling.
  • Wash your extensions every 2-3 weeks with sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

Hiding your undercut with extensions is a great way to switch up your look without committing to a new hairstyle. With the help of this guide, you can achieve a formal and stylish look effortlessly.
