Best Volumizer Hair Dryer The Ultimate Guide For 2023

Written by Frank Jul 20, 2023 · 5 min read

If you’re tired of flat and lifeless hair, a volumizer hair dryer might be the solution you’re looking for. A volumizer hair dryer is a great tool for adding volume and lift to your hair without having to use multiple products. In this guide, we’ll be discussing the best volumizer hair dryers on the market, how to use them, and some tips and tricks for achieving the perfect voluminous hairstyle.

What is a Volumizer Hair Dryer?

A volumizer hair dryer combines the functions of a hair dryer and a round brush. It’s designed to add volume and shape to your hair while you dry it. Volumizer hair dryers come in various sizes, shapes, and designs, but they all work in the same way: by blowing hot air through your hair while simultaneously brushing it.

How to Use a Volumizer Hair Dryer

Using a volumizer hair dryer might seem intimidating at first, but it’s actually quite easy once you get the hang of it. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections.
  4. Start with the bottom section and work your way up.
  5. Place the volumizer hair dryer at the roots of your hair and brush it through.
  6. Rotate the brush as you move it down your hair to create volume and shape.
  7. Repeat on all sections of your hair.
  8. Finish with a blast of cool air to set the style.

Step-by-Step Guide on Using a Volumizer Hair Dryer

For a more detailed guide on how to use a volumizer hair dryer, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and towel-dry your hair

Start by washing your hair with your favorite shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair until it’s damp, but not dripping wet.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair

Before using any hot tools on your hair, it’s important to protect it from heat damage. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Divide your hair into sections

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work on one section at a time.

Step 4: Start with the bottom section and work your way up

Start with the bottom section of your hair and work your way up. This will help ensure that you don’t miss any sections of your hair.

Step 5: Place the volumizer hair dryer at the roots of your hair and brush it through

Place the volumizer hair dryer at the roots of your hair and brush it through. Hold it in place for a few seconds to create volume.

Step 6: Rotate the brush as you move it down your hair to create volume and shape

As you move the brush down your hair, rotate it to create volume and shape. This will help prevent your hair from looking flat.

Step 7: Repeat on all sections of your hair

Repeat these steps on all sections of your hair until you’ve achieved your desired look.

Step 8: Finish with a blast of cool air to set the style

Finish by using the cool air setting on your volumizer hair dryer to set the style. This will help your hair hold its shape and prevent frizz.

Tips for Achieving Voluminous Hair

Here are some tips and tricks for achieving the perfect voluminous hairstyle:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  • Use a texturizing spray or powder to create more volume.
  • Tease your hair at the roots for even more volume.
  • Use a lightweight hair spray to hold your style in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the difference between a volumizer hair dryer and a regular hair dryer?

A volumizer hair dryer combines the functions of a hair dryer and a round brush, while a regular hair dryer just blows hot air onto your hair. Volumizer hair dryers are designed to add volume and shape to your hair while you dry it.

Can I use a volumizer hair dryer on wet hair?

No, it’s important to towel-dry your hair before using a volumizer hair dryer. Using it on wet hair can damage your hair and create frizz.

How often should I use a volumizer hair dryer?

It’s best to use a volumizer hair dryer no more than once a week to prevent heat damage to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Voluminous Hair

Here are some hair care tips to help maintain your voluminous hairstyle:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause heat damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With the right volumizer hair dryer and a few tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect voluminous hairstyle every time. Remember to always protect your hair from heat damage and take good care of it to maintain your beautiful locks.
