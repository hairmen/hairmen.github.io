What Is The Best Hairspray For Holding Curls

Written by Daniel Aug 09, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to maintaining curly hair, you want a hairspray that will keep your curls in place without leaving them feeling sticky or crunchy. But with so many hairspray options on the market, it can be challenging to determine which one is the best for your hair type and style. In this article, we’ll break down the top hairsprays for holding curls and provide tips for achieving long-lasting, bouncy curls.

What to Look for in a Hairspray for Curls

When shopping for a hairspray, it’s essential to consider your hair type and the style you’re trying to achieve. Here are a few things to look for in a hairspray for curls:

Hold Strength

The hold strength of a hairspray refers to how long it can keep your curls in place. For curly hair, you want a strong hold to keep your curls bouncy all day long. However, if you have fine hair, a strong hold hairspray may weigh your curls down.

Humidity Resistance

Humidity can wreak havoc on curly hair, causing frizz and flattening curls. Look for a hairspray that can resist humidity to keep your curls looking their best even on humid days.

Non-Sticky Formula

A good hairspray for curls should leave your hair feeling soft and touchable, not sticky or crunchy. Avoid hairsprays that contain high levels of alcohol or leave a residue on your hair.

How to Use Hairspray for Curls

Using hairspray for curls can be a bit of a delicate dance. Here are a few tips for achieving the perfect balance:

Apply Hairspray When Styling

When styling your hair, apply hairspray in small sections as you go. This will help ensure that each curl is properly coated with hairspray and will stay in place.

Don’t Overdo It

Overloading your curls with hairspray can weigh them down and leave your hair feeling sticky or crunchy. Start with a light mist of hairspray and build up as needed.

Keep Hairspray Away from Roots

Applying hairspray directly to your roots can cause build-up and make your hair look greasy. Instead, focus on applying hairspray to the ends of your curls to keep them in place.

Here are our top picks for the best hairsprays for holding curls:

This hairspray provides a strong hold while still being lightweight and non-sticky. It’s also humidity-resistant, making it perfect for curly hair.

Oribe Superfine Strong Hairspray

This hairspray provides a strong hold while still leaving your hair feeling soft and touchable. It’s also humidity-resistant and contains UV protection to keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Curls

In addition to using the right hairspray, there are a few other things you can do to keep your curls looking their best:

Use a Curl-Enhancing Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair can help enhance your curls and keep them looking bouncy and defined.

Avoid Brushing Your Curls

Brushing your curls can cause them to lose their shape and become frizzy. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair.

Avoid Touching Your Hair

Touching your curls throughout the day can cause them to lose their shape and become frizzy. Try to avoid touching your hair as much as possible.

FAQs

Can I Use Hairspray Every Day?

Yes, you can use hairspray every day. However, it’s important to choose a hairspray that won’t leave your hair feeling sticky or crunchy, as this can cause damage to your hair over time.

Can Hairspray Damage My Hair?

Using hairspray every day can cause damage to your hair over time, especially if you’re using a hairspray that contains high levels of alcohol. Look for a hairspray that is lightweight and contains nourishing ingredients to help protect your hair.

How Do I Remove Hairspray Build-Up?

To remove hairspray build-up, use a clarifying shampoo once a week. This will help remove any product build-up and leave your hair feeling clean and fresh.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairspray for holding curls can be a game-changer for maintaining bouncy, defined curls. Look for a hairspray that provides a strong hold, is humidity-resistant, and leaves your hair feeling soft and touchable. And don’t forget to follow our tips for achieving long-lasting, beautiful curls.
