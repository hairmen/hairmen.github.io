Mens Haircut Short On Sides And Back Long On Top

Welcome to our guide on mens haircut short on sides and back long on top. This popular hairstyle has been trending for a while now and it's not going anywhere. It's a versatile and stylish haircut that suits every face shape and hair type. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about this haircut, from how to get it, how to style it and how to take care of it. Let's get started!

How to Get Mens Haircut Short on Sides and Back Long on Top

The first step to getting this haircut is finding a good barber or hairstylist who has experience in cutting this style. Bring a picture of the haircut you want or ask them for their recommendations. Communication is key to achieving the perfect haircut.

The haircut is characterized by having short sides and back, and longer hair on top. The length of the top hair can vary, depending on your preference. Some men prefer to have it longer, while others prefer it shorter. The key is to have a noticeable contrast between the length of the hair on top and the sides and back.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to get mens haircut short on sides and back long on top:

  1. Wash your hair before going to the barber or hairstylist.
  2. Communicate clearly with them about the haircut you want.
  3. The barber or hairstylist will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair with clippers, using a short guard.
  4. They will then blend the hair on top with the sides and back, using scissors or clippers.
  5. The top hair will be cut to the desired length, either with scissors or clippers.
  6. The hair on top will be styled to your preference.
  7. Ask the barber or hairstylist for any styling tips or recommendations.

Tips for Styling Mens Haircut Short on Sides and Back Long on Top

Here are some tips for styling mens haircut short on sides and back long on top:

  • Use a hair product that suits your hair type and desired hairstyle. For a natural look, use a matte product. For a shiny look, use a pomade or wax.
  • Blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to go, using a round brush.
  • Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair, depending on the desired look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what suits you best.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens haircut short on sides and back long on top:

Is this haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this haircut is suitable for all face shapes.

What hair type is best for this haircut?

This haircut suits all hair types, but it's best for thick and wavy hair.

What is the maintenance like for this haircut?

The maintenance for this haircut is relatively low. You will need to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.

Hair Care for Mens Haircut Short on Sides and Back Long on Top

Here are some hair care tips for mens haircut short on sides and back long on top:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

That's it! We hope this guide has been helpful in understanding everything you need to know about mens haircut short on sides and back long on top. Remember to communicate clearly with your barber or hairstylist, experiment with different styles, and take good care of your hair.
