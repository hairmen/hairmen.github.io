How To Cut A Stacked Hair Style Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, a stacked hair style might be just what you need. A stacked haircut is characterized by layers that are stacked on top of each other, creating a voluminous and bouncy look. In this post, we'll guide you through the process of cutting a stacked hair style, step-by-step. We'll also provide some tips on how to maintain your new haircut and answer some FAQs.

How to Cut a Stacked Hair Style

Before we get started, it's important to note that cutting hair can be challenging and requires some skill. If you're not confident in your ability to cut hair, we recommend that you seek the help of a professional hairstylist. However, if you're up for the challenge, here's how you can cut a stacked hair style:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

The first step in cutting a stacked hair style is to wash and dry your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that your hair is clean and free of any product buildup. Once your hair is dry, comb it through to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, use your comb to section your hair into four parts: the top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to secure each section in place.

Step 3: Cut the Back Section

Starting with the back section, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair at a slight angle, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Repeat this process for each section of hair in the back.

Step 4: Cut the Side Sections

Once you've finished cutting the back section, move on to the side sections. Start by taking a small section of hair from the bottom of one side section and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair at a slight angle, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Repeat this process for each section of hair in the side sections.

Step 5: Cut the Top Section

Finally, move on to the top section. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers, then cut the hair at a slight angle, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Repeat this process for each section of hair in the top section.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Once you've finished cutting all the sections, it's time to blend the layers together. Use your scissors to carefully blend the layers together by cutting into them at a slight angle.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Now that you've cut your hair into a stacked style, it's time to style it. Use a blow dryer and a round brush to add volume and bounce to your hair. You can also use a curling wand or flat iron to create curls or straighten your hair.

Tips for Cutting a Stacked Hair Style

  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors
  • Start with small sections of hair
  • Cut at a slight angle to create layers
  • Blend the layers together for a seamless look
  • Take your time and be patient

FAQs

How long does a stacked haircut last?

A stacked haircut can last anywhere from 6-8 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Can I cut a stacked haircut at home?

You can cut a stacked haircut at home, but we recommend that you seek the help of a professional hairstylist if you're not confident in your ability to cut hair.

What products should I use to maintain my stacked haircut?

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to maintain the volume and bounce of your stacked haircut. You can also use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add texture and hold to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Stacked Haircuts

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated

Now that you know how to cut a stacked hair style, it's time to give it a try. Remember to be patient and take your time, and don't be afraid to seek the help of a professional if you need it. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and trendy stacked haircut that will turn heads wherever you go!
