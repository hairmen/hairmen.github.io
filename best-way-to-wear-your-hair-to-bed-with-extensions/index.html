Best Way To Wear Your Hair To Bed With Extensions

Written by Petter Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of waking up to tangled and frizzy hair extensions? Wearing your hair to bed with extensions can be a challenge, but with the right technique, you can maintain your extensions and keep them looking great. In this article, we will share the best way to wear your hair to bed with extensions, along with tips and tricks to ensure your extensions stay healthy and beautiful.

Table of Content

  • How to Wear Your Hair to Bed with Extensions
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Extensions

How to Wear Your Hair to Bed with Extensions

The best way to wear your hair to bed with extensions is to tie it up in a loose, low ponytail or braid. This will prevent your extensions from tangling and getting matted while you sleep. It's important to use a soft, satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage.

Another option is to wrap your hair in a silk scarf or bonnet. This will protect your extensions and keep them from rubbing against your pillowcase. Make sure to choose a silk scarf or bonnet that is large enough to accommodate all of your hair and extensions.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to wear your hair to bed with extensions:

  1. Brush your hair and extensions thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Gently gather your hair and extensions into a loose, low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  3. Tie the ponytail with a soft, fabric hair tie or scrunchie.
  4. Wrap your hair in a silk scarf or bonnet, making sure to cover all of your hair and extensions.
  5. Go to sleep and enjoy waking up to beautiful, tangle-free extensions!

Tips for Maintaining Your Extensions

Here are some tips for maintaining your extensions while you sleep:

  • Use a soft, satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair, as this can cause tangling and matting.
  • Brush your hair and extensions regularly to remove any tangles or knots.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your extensions before bed to keep them moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your extensions before bed, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I wear my hair down to bed with extensions?

A: It's not recommended to wear your hair down to bed with extensions, as this can cause tangling and matting.

Q: How often should I wash my extensions?

A: It's recommended to wash your extensions every 2-3 weeks, depending on how often you wear them and how much product you use.

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my extensions?

A: It's best to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner on your extensions, as sulfates can strip the hair of its natural oils and cause damage.

Hair Care for Extensions

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful extensions. Here are some tips for caring for your extensions:

  • Brush your extensions regularly to remove any tangles or knots.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your extensions, or use a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Wash your extensions every 2-3 weeks with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair, as this can cause tangling and matting.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your extensions to keep them moisturized and healthy.

By following these tips and wearing your hair to bed in a loose, low ponytail or braid, you can maintain your extensions and keep them looking great. Remember to use a soft, satin pillowcase and avoid using heat styling tools before bed. With proper care, your extensions can last for months and give you the long, luscious hair you've always wanted.
