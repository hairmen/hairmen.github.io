How To Thin Out Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that it can be quite thick and difficult to manage at times. One solution to this problem is to thin out your hair, which can help to make it more manageable and reduce the amount of volume you have to deal with. In this article, we will show you how to thin out curly hair in a relaxed and easy-to-understand way.

Table of Contents

  • How to Thin Out Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Thinning Out Curly Hair
  • FAQs

How to Thin Out Curly Hair

There are many ways to thin out curly hair, including using thinning shears, razor cutting, and texturizing. The best method for you will depend on your hair type, length, and personal preferences. Some people prefer a more natural look, while others prefer a more dramatic change. Whatever your preference, there is a method that will work for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to thin out curly hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it is damp, but not soaking wet.
  3. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties.
  4. Take one section of hair and comb it out to remove any tangles.
  5. Using thinning shears, start at the bottom of the hair and work your way up, cutting small sections at a time.
  6. Repeat this process for each section of hair.
  7. Once you have finished thinning out your hair, wash it again with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  8. Style your hair as usual.

Tips for Thinning Out Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when thinning out curly hair:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Be careful not to over-thin your hair, as this can lead to breakage and damage.
  • Use sharp scissors or thinning shears to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Consider going to a professional stylist to have your hair thinned out, especially if you are not comfortable doing it yourself.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about thinning out curly hair:

Q: Will thinning out my hair make it look thinner?

A: Yes, thinning out your hair will reduce the amount of volume you have, which can make it look thinner.

Q: Can I thin out my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can thin out your hair at home using thinning shears or razor cutting techniques. However, it is important to be cautious and follow proper techniques to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: How often should I thin out my hair?

A: This will depend on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Some people may need to thin out their hair every few months, while others may only need to do it once or twice a year.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind if you have curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to avoid frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily thin out your curly hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Remember to be cautious and follow proper techniques to avoid damaging your hair, and consider going to a professional stylist if you are not comfortable doing it yourself.
