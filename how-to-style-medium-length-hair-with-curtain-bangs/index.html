How To Style Medium Length Hair With Curtain Bangs

Written by Christine Aug 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have medium length hair and are looking for a fresh, new hairstyle, consider adding curtain bangs to your look. This style has been popular for a while now and it's easy to create and maintain. In this article, we'll show you how to style medium length hair with curtain bangs, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How To Style Medium Length Hair with Curtain Bangs
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs
  • FAQs About Curtain Bangs

How to Style Medium Length Hair with Curtain Bangs

First, make sure your hair is clean and dry before styling. Then, follow these steps:

  1. Part your hair down the middle to create two equal sections.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of each section and pull it forward.
  3. Trim the hair to the desired length for your bangs.
  4. Using a curling iron or straightener, style your bangs to frame your face.
  5. Finish with hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

That's it! You now have a chic, new hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step by step guide to help you style your medium length hair with curtain bangs:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and smoothness.
  4. Part your hair down the middle, creating two equal sections.
  5. Using a comb, divide the front sections of your hair into two equal parts. These parts will become your curtain bangs.
  6. Trim your bangs to the desired length. Start by cutting a little at a time to avoid cutting too much off.
  7. Using a curling iron or straightener, style your bangs to frame your face. You can create loose waves for a more relaxed look or straighten them for a sleeker look.
  8. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.
  9. Finish with hairspray to hold your style in place.

With these simple steps, you can have gorgeous curtain bangs that perfectly complement your medium length hair.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs

Here are a few tips to help you maintain your curtain bangs:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your hair looking clean and fresh between washes.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them look greasy and lose their shape.
  • Use a small amount of hairspray to keep your bangs in place without making them look stiff.

By following these tips, you can keep your curtain bangs looking great for weeks to come.

FAQs About Curtain Bangs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

Q: What face shapes are best suited for curtain bangs?

A: Curtain bangs look great on most face shapes, but they are particularly flattering on oval and heart-shaped faces.

Q: How long should curtain bangs be?

A: Curtain bangs can be any length, but they typically fall just below the eyebrows or between the eyebrows and the eyelashes.

Q: Can I style my curtain bangs with a side part?

A: Yes, you can style your curtain bangs with a side part for a more dramatic look.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair with Curtain Bangs

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you keep your medium length hair with curtain bangs looking its best:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Limit your use of hair products, as too much product can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your medium length hair with curtain bangs looking healthy and beautiful.

So there you have it - a complete guide on how to style medium length hair with curtain bangs. With these tips and tricks, you can create a chic, new hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion. Give it a try and see how it transforms your look!
