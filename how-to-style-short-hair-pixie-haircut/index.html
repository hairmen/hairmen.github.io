How To Style Short Hair Pixie Haircut

Written by Christine Nov 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Short hair pixie haircut is a trendy and bold hairstyle that is perfect for those who want a low maintenance and chic look. However, styling short hair can be challenging, especially if you're used to longer locks. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style short hair pixie haircut, along with tips and hair care advice to keep your hair looking its best.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair. Use a volumizing mousse to add texture and hold to your hair.

Step 2: Use a 1-inch curling iron to create loose waves throughout your hair. Make sure to curl away from your face for a more natural look.

Step 3: Use your fingers to gently tousle your hair and create volume. Use a texturizing spray to add more texture and hold to your hair.

Step 4: Use a small amount of pomade or wax to define your ends and add shine to your hair. Use your fingers to style your hair into your desired look.

Step 5: Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

Tips for Styling Short Hair Pixie Haircut

Tip 1: Play around with different hair accessories, like headbands or barrettes, to add some flair to your style.

Tip 2: If you have fine hair, use a root lifter spray to add more volume to your hair.

Tip 3: Don't be afraid to experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your hair type and style.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a straightener to style my short hair pixie haircut?

A: Yes, you can use a straightener to create sleek and smooth looks with your short hair pixie haircut.

Q: How often should I trim my short hair pixie haircut?

A: It's recommended to trim your short hair pixie haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care for Short Hair Pixie Haircut

Tip 1: Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Tip 2: Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.

Tip 3: Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

With these tips and steps, you can confidently style your short hair pixie haircut and keep it looking its best. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
