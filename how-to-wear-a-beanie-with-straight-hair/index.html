How To Wear A Beanie With Straight Hair

Written by Bardi Jun 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Beanies are a popular accessory that can be worn with just about any outfit. They can add an extra layer of warmth during the colder months and can also be used to add some style to your outfit. If you have straight hair, you may be wondering how to wear a beanie without sacrificing your hairstyle. In this article, we will discuss how to wear a beanie with straight hair and provide you with some tips and tricks to help you look your best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Beanie with Straight Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

How to Wear a Beanie with Straight Hair

Wearing a beanie with straight hair can be a bit of a challenge, but it is definitely possible. Here are some tips to help you look your best:

  • Choose the right beanie. Look for a beanie that is not too tight and fits your head comfortably. A loose beanie will not only look better but also won't flatten your hair.
  • Style your hair before putting on the beanie. Straighten your hair if needed and create a part if you prefer. This will help the beanie sit correctly on your head.
  • Place the beanie on your head. Position it so that it covers your forehead and sits just above your ears. Make sure it is not too far down on your forehead, as this will make your face look shorter.
  • Adjust the beanie. Pull the beanie down slightly at the back to create a slouchy effect. You can also adjust the front of the beanie to create a more relaxed look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to wear a beanie with straight hair:

  1. Choose a beanie that fits your head well and is not too tight.
  2. Straighten your hair if needed and create a part if desired.
  3. Place the beanie on your head, making sure it covers your forehead and sits just above your ears.
  4. Adjust the beanie by pulling it down slightly at the back and adjusting the front to create the desired look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you wear a beanie with straight hair:

  • Choose a beanie that complements your outfit. A neutral-colored beanie will go with just about anything, while a brightly colored beanie can add a pop of color to your outfit.
  • Experiment with different styles. You can wear your beanie slouchy or pulled down for a more fitted look.
  • Accessorize with other items. Pair your beanie with a scarf or gloves to complete your cold-weather look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a beanie with straight hair:

Can I wear a beanie with bangs?

Yes, you can wear a beanie with bangs. Simply position the beanie so that it covers your forehead and sits just above your bangs.

Will wearing a beanie flatten my hair?

If you choose a beanie that fits well and is not too tight, it should not flatten your hair. You can also style your hair before putting on the beanie to help it sit correctly.

Hair Care

When wearing a beanie with straight hair, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid wearing a beanie for extended periods of time, as this can lead to breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant when straightening your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you can wear a beanie with straight hair and look great. Remember to choose the right beanie, style your hair before putting it on, and adjust it for the perfect look. Stay warm and stylish all winter long!
