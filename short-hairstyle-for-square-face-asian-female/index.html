Short Hairstyles For Square Face Asian Female

Written by Eveline Jun 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we will be discussing the best short hairstyles for square face Asian females. If you have a square face shape, it means your face is almost as wide as it is long, and you have a strong jawline. The key to finding the perfect short hairstyle for your face shape is to choose a style that softens your jawline and elongates your face. Below, we have compiled a list of short hairstyles that are perfect for square face Asian females.

Description

Square face shape is one of the most common face shapes among Asians. It is characterized by a wide forehead, strong jawline, and square chin. If you have a square face shape, you need to choose a hairstyle that will soften your jawline and make your face look longer. Short hairstyles are perfect for square face Asian females because they elongate the face and make the jawline appear less prominent.

How to

The first step to finding the perfect short hairstyle for your square face shape is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They will be able to recommend a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Once you have decided on a hairstyle, you can follow these steps to maintain it:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo that is suitable for your hair type. Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

Step 2: Blow-dry your hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 3: Style your hair

Style your hair using a styling product that is suitable for your hair type. Use a hair spray to keep your hair in place.

Short Hairstyles for Square Face Asian Female

Here are some short hairstyles that are perfect for square face Asian females:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is perfect for square face Asian females. It is a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. The pixie cut elongates the face and softens the jawline.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is a classic short hairstyle that is perfect for square face Asian females. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways. The bob cut elongates the face and softens the jawline.

3. Layered Bob Cut

The layered bob cut is a short hairstyle that is perfect for square face Asian females. It is a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. The layered bob cut elongates the face and softens the jawline.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for square face Asian females:

1. Consider your hair texture

When choosing a short hairstyle, consider your hair texture. If you have fine hair, choose a hairstyle that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, choose a hairstyle that reduces volume.

2. Choose a hairstyle that suits your lifestyle

Choose a short hairstyle that suits your lifestyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling.

3. Experiment with different hairstyles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different short hairstyles. Try different hairstyles until you find the one that suits you the best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for square face Asian females:

1. Can I dye my hair if I have a short hairstyle?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have a short hairstyle. However, be sure to choose a hair color that suits your skin tone and hair texture.

2. How often should I trim my hair if I have a short hairstyle?

You should trim your hair every 4-6 weeks if you have a short hairstyle. Regular trims will keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

1. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. If you have dry hair, choose a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. If you have oily hair, choose a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Avoid using hot styling tools.

Avoid using hot styling tools such as curling irons and straighteners. These tools can damage your hair and make it brittle.

3. Protect your hair from the sun.

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray. The sun can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle.

By following these tips and choosing the right short hairstyle, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance.
