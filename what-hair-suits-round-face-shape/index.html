What Hair Suits Round Face Shape A Beginner s Guide

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your round face shape? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about what hair suits round face shape, including tips, step-by-step guides, and hair care advice. Let's get started!

Description

There's no denying that finding the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially when you have a round face. A round face shape is characterized by a wider forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. If you have a round face shape, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that elongates and slims your face. In this article, we'll help you find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your round face shape.

How To Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your round face shape is to determine your face shape. To do this, follow these simple steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and examine the shape you've drawn.

If the width and length of your face are roughly the same, you have a round face shape. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have an oval face shape. If your forehead is wider than your jawline, you have a heart-shaped face. If your jawline is wider than your forehead, you have a square face. And if your face is angular with prominent cheekbones, you have a diamond face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to find the perfect hairstyle. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that creates height at the crown of your head. This will elongate your face and make it look slimmer.
  2. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will only make your face look rounder.
  3. Opt for styles with layers and texture, as this will create movement and add dimension to your hair.
  4. Consider bangs, but avoid straight, heavy bangs that cut across your forehead. Instead, opt for soft, side-swept bangs that create angles and frame your face.
  5. If you want to wear your hair up, try a high ponytail or bun. This will create the illusion of a longer face.

Remember, the key is to create height and dimension in your hair to elongate your face shape. With these tips in mind, you'll be sure to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face shape.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hairstyle for your round face shape:

  • Avoid center parts, as they can make your face look wider.
  • Try a side part, which will create angles and elongate your face.
  • Consider a lob or long bob, which grazes your collarbone and creates the illusion of a longer neck.
  • Avoid blunt cuts, which can make your face look wider.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and movement in your hair.

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt cuts, chin-length bobs, and blunt bangs. These styles will only make your face look rounder.

Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a round face shape?

A middle part can make your face look wider, so it's best to avoid if you have a round face shape. Instead, opt for a side part, which will create angles and elongate your face.

What length of hair is best for round face shapes?

Longer hair is typically more flattering for round face shapes, as it creates the illusion of a longer face. However, you can also rock a shorter style as long as it has some height and dimension to elongate your face.

Hair Care for Round Face Shapes

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for round face shapes:

  • Use heat protectant when styling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dull.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage and split ends.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these hair care tips and choosing the right hairstyle for your round face shape, you'll be sure to look and feel your best. Good luck!
