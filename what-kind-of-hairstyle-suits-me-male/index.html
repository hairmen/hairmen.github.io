What Kind Of Hairstyle Suits Me Male

Written by Daniel Sep 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially for men. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide which one would suit you the best. In this article, we will help you figure out which kind of hairstyle suits you male, based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Description

Before you decide on a hairstyle, it is essential to understand your face shape. There are six common face shapes: oval, round, square, triangle, diamond, and heart. Each shape requires a different style to enhance your features and balance your proportions.

If you have an oval face, you are lucky because almost any hairstyle would suit you. You can experiment with short, medium, or long hair, depending on your preference. However, if you have a round face, you should avoid hairstyles that add volume on the sides, as it would make your face look wider. Instead, opt for a style that adds height and length to your face, like a quiff or a pompadour.

For men with a square face, a hairstyle that softens the angles would be ideal. A textured crop or a messy fringe would complement your features. If you have a triangle or diamond face shape, you should focus on adding width to the forehead to balance the jawline. A side-swept fringe or a layered haircut would create the illusion of a broader forehead.

How to

Once you have identified your face shape, the next step is to consider your hair texture. If you have thick hair, you can try a layered cut or a buzz cut. On the other hand, if you have thin hair, a short and textured style would add volume and depth to your hair. If you have curly hair, you can embrace your natural texture with a longer cut, or you can keep it short and neat with a fade or a taper.

Your personal style also plays a significant role in choosing the right hairstyle. If you have a classic and timeless style, a side part or a slicked-back style would suit you. However, if you have a more edgy and modern look, a textured crop or a messy quiff would be perfect.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow to determine which kind of hairstyle suits you male:

  1. Identify your face shape.
  2. Consider your hair texture.
  3. Think about your personal style.
  4. Research different hairstyles that suit your features and preferences.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion.
  6. Try out different hairstyles and see which one makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Choose a style that complements your personality and lifestyle.
  • Consider the maintenance required for the hairstyle.
  • Use hair products to enhance the style and keep it in place.
  • Be open to trying new styles and experimenting with different looks.

FAQ

What kind of hairstyle suits a balding man?

A shaved head or a buzz cut would be the most suitable hairstyle for a balding man. It would create a clean and polished look and draw attention away from the balding areas.

What kind of hairstyle suits a man with a receding hairline?

A short and textured style or a messy fringe would be ideal for a man with a receding hairline. It would add volume and depth to the hair and create the illusion of a fuller head of hair.

Hair Care

Maintaining your hairstyle requires proper hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and in good condition:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair to keep it moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the follicles.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and style.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle requires careful consideration of your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. By following the steps and tips mentioned in this article, you can find the hairstyle that suits you the best and enhances your features.
