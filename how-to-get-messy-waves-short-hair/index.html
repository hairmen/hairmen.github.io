How To Get Messy Waves For Short Hair

Written by Petter Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your short hair looking flat and lifeless? Do you want to rock a messy, tousled look? Look no further! In this article, we will provide step-by-step instructions on how to achieve messy waves for short hair. Follow these tips and tricks to transform your short hair into a stylish, effortless look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Messy Waves for Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Messy Waves

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prep Your Hair
Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from hot tools. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and hold to your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair
Divide your hair into sections, starting at the nape of your neck. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair
Using a curling iron, curl each section of hair away from your face. Hold the curling iron vertically and wrap the hair around the barrel. Leave the ends out for a more undone look.

Step 4: Break Up the Curls
Once you have curled your entire head, use your fingers to break up the curls. Gently pull on each curl to loosen it and create a more natural, messy look.

Step 5: Add Texture
Apply a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add more texture and hold to your hair. Scrunch your hair with your hands to enhance the messy waves.

Tips and Tricks

Tip 1: Use the Right Tools
Invest in a good quality curling iron with a smaller barrel size. This will help you achieve tighter curls and more defined waves.

Tip 2: Don't Overdo It
Less is more when it comes to creating messy waves. Don't try to curl every strand of hair. Leave some pieces straight to add dimension to your look.

Tip 3: Experiment with Products
Try different products to find the right ones for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use a lightweight texturizing spray. If you have thick hair, opt for a heavier product.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I achieve messy waves without heat tools?
A: Yes! You can achieve messy waves by braiding your hair while it's damp and leaving it overnight. When you wake up, undo the braids and use your fingers to break up the waves.

Q: How often should I wash my hair to maintain messy waves?
A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp gets. Generally, washing your hair every 2-3 days will help maintain your waves without stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Messy Waves

Maintaining healthy hair is key to achieving and maintaining messy waves. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

Tip 1: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner
Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for your hair type. Look for products that are moisturizing and nourishing to keep your hair healthy.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Damage
Use heat protectant spray when using hot tools. Limit your use of heat tools to prevent damage and breakage.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun
Apply a leave-in conditioner or protective hair oil to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays. Wear a hat or scarf to prevent further damage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve messy waves for short hair that look effortlessly chic. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you. With a little practice, you'll be able to rock this look with confidence!
