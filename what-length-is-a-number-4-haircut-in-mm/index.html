What Length Is A Number 4 Haircut In Mm

Written by Eveline Jul 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you wondering what length a number 4 haircut is in millimeters? If so, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll explore everything you need to know about this popular haircut length, including how to achieve it, tips for maintaining it, and more.

Description: What is a Number 4 Haircut?

A number 4 haircut is one of the most popular clipper guard lengths. It is typically achieved by using a clipper guard that is 1/2 inch or 12.7 millimeters in length. This haircut length is perfect for those who want a short, yet still somewhat noticeable, haircut. It is also a great choice for those who want to keep their hair looking neat and tidy without having to worry about frequent maintenance.

How to Achieve a Number 4 Haircut

If you want to achieve a number 4 haircut, the first step is to make sure you have the right tools on hand. You’ll need a pair of clippers and a number 4 clipper guard. Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you achieve an even result.

Step 2: Attach the Number 4 Clipper Guard

Attach the number 4 clipper guard to your clippers. Make sure it’s securely attached before you start cutting.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin cutting your hair, starting at the sides and working your way up to the top. Use the clippers to create a uniform length. Make sure you’re cutting against the grain of your hair to ensure an even result.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you’ve finished cutting your hair, use a pair of scissors to blend the hair on top with the hair on the sides. This will give you a more natural look and prevent any harsh lines.

Tips for Maintaining a Number 4 Haircut

Now that you’ve achieved your desired haircut length, it’s important to maintain it. Here are a few tips to help you keep your number 4 haircut looking great:

  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking tidy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

FAQ: What Other Haircut Lengths are Available?

There are many different haircut lengths available, ranging from a number 0 to a number 8. Here are a few examples:

  • A number 0 haircut is the shortest length and is achieved by using a clipper guard that is 1/16 inch or 1.5 millimeters in length.
  • A number 1 haircut is slightly longer than a number 0 and is achieved by using a clipper guard that is 1/8 inch or 3 millimeters in length.
  • A number 2 haircut is longer than a number 1 and is achieved by using a clipper guard that is 1/4 inch or 6 millimeters in length.
  • A number 3 haircut is longer than a number 2 and is achieved by using a clipper guard that is 3/8 inch or 9.5 millimeters in length.
  • A number 5 haircut is longer than a number 4 and is achieved by using a clipper guard that is 5/8 inch or 16 millimeters in length.
  • A number 6 haircut is longer than a number 5 and is achieved by using a clipper guard that is 3/4 inch or 19 millimeters in length.
  • A number 7 haircut is longer than a number 6 and is achieved by using a clipper guard that is 7/8 inch or 22 millimeters in length.
  • A number 8 haircut is the longest length and is achieved by using a clipper guard that is 1 inch or 25 millimeters in length.

Hair Care for a Number 4 Haircut

Whether you have a number 4 haircut or another length, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know what length a number 4 haircut is in millimeters, you can achieve this popular haircut length with ease. Just remember to maintain it regularly and take care of your hair to keep it looking great.
