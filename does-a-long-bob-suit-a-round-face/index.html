Does A Long Bob Suit A Round Face

Are you considering getting a long bob but wondering if it will suit your round face shape? Look no further, as we have all the answers to your questions in this blog post.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

A long bob, also known as a lob, is a versatile haircut that suits many face shapes. However, if you have a round face shape, you may be wondering if this trendy haircut is right for you. The good news is that a long bob can actually complement a round face shape and enhance your features. The trick is to choose the right length and style that flatters your face shape.

How To

To determine if a long bob will suit your round face shape, follow these simple steps:

  1. Measure your face shape: Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If these measurements are similar, you have a round face shape.
  2. Find the right length: A long bob that ends just below the chin or collarbone is ideal for round face shapes. This length elongates the face and adds dimension.
  3. Choose the right style: A long bob with layers or angled cuts can also help to elongate a round face shape. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin as they can make the face appear wider.

Step by Step

If you have a round face shape and want to get a long bob, follow these step-by-step instructions:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist: Discuss your desired length and style with your hairstylist to ensure that it flatters your face shape.
  2. Wash and dry your hair: Make sure that your hair is clean and dry before getting a haircut.
  3. Show your hairstylist a picture: Bring a picture of the long bob style that you want to your hairstylist as a reference.
  4. Get the haircut: Sit back and relax as your hairstylist cuts your hair into the desired long bob style.
  5. Style your hair: Use a curling iron or straightener to style your long bob according to your preferences.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a long bob for a round face shape:

  • Avoid center parts as they can make the face appear wider. Instead, opt for a side part.
  • Add texture to your hair with waves or curls to create a more dimensional look.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add depth to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Will a long bob make my face look rounder?

A: No, a long bob can actually elongate a round face shape if it is styled correctly.

Q: Can I still get a long bob if I have thick hair?

A: Yes, a long bob can work for all hair types. However, your hairstylist may need to thin out your hair to reduce volume.

Q: How often do I need to get a haircut to maintain a long bob?

A: It is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your long bob.

Hair Care

To keep your long bob looking fresh and healthy, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid over-drying.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your long bob.

In conclusion, a long bob can definitely suit a round face shape if it is styled correctly. Follow our tips and tricks to get the perfect long bob for your face shape and hair type. Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles to find the look that works best for you.
