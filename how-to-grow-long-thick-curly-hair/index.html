How To Grow Long Thick Curly Hair

Written by Luffy Jun 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of having short, thin hair that never seems to grow? Do you dream of having long, thick, and curly locks? If so, you're in luck! In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to grow long thick curly hair. From hair care to lifestyle changes, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How to
  • Step by Step

Hair Care

Before we dive into the tips and tricks for growing long thick curly hair, it's important to talk about hair care. Taking care of your hair is crucial for healthy growth. Here are a few tips:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Look for shampoos that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients.


2. Don't over-wash your hair

Washing your hair too often can also strip it of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.


3. Use a deep conditioner

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it's important to use a deep conditioner once a week. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and argan oil.


4. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair over time. Try to avoid using them as much as possible. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray.


How to

Now that we've covered hair care, let's talk about how to grow long thick curly hair. Here are a few tips:

1. Eat a healthy diet

Your hair needs nutrients to grow, so it's important to eat a healthy diet. Focus on foods that are high in protein, like chicken, fish, and eggs. You should also eat plenty of fruits and vegetables.


2. Stay hydrated

Drinking plenty of water is important for healthy hair growth. Aim to drink at least 8 glasses of water per day.


3. Get enough sleep

Getting enough sleep is crucial for healthy hair growth. Aim to get 7-8 hours of sleep per night.


4. Reduce stress

Stress can cause hair loss, so it's important to reduce stress in your life. Try meditation, yoga, or other relaxation techniques.


Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to grow long thick curly hair:

1. Trim your hair regularly

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends, which can lead to hair breakage. Aim to get a trim every 8-12 weeks.


2. Massage your scalp

Massaging your scalp can help stimulate hair growth. Use your fingertips to massage your scalp for a few minutes every day.


3. Use a hair oil

Applying a hair oil like coconut oil or argan oil can help moisturize your hair and prevent breakage. Apply the oil to your hair once a week.


4. Use a silk pillowcase

Using a silk pillowcase can help prevent hair breakage and frizz. Silk is gentle on your hair and won't cause friction like cotton pillowcases.


Tips

Here are a few additional tips for growing long thick curly hair:

1. Don't brush your hair when it's wet

Wet hair is more fragile than dry hair, so it's important to be gentle when brushing it. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.


2. Wear protective hairstyles

Protective hairstyles like braids, twists, and buns can help protect your hair from breakage. Just be sure not to wear them too tight, as this can cause hair loss.


3. Don't use too much product

Using too much hair product can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy. Use a small amount of product and focus on applying it to the ends of your hair.


FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about growing long thick curly hair:

1. How long does it take to grow long hair?

Everyone's hair grows at a different rate, but on average, hair grows about half an inch per month. It can take several months or even years to grow long hair.


2. Can I grow long hair if I have thin hair?

Yes, you can still grow long hair if you have thin hair. Just be sure to take care of your hair and avoid using products that can weigh it down.


3. Can I grow long hair if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can definitely grow long hair if you have curly hair. In fact, curly hair tends to grow slower than straight hair, so it's important to be patient and take good care of your hair.


Now that you know how to grow long thick curly hair, it's time to put these tips into action. Remember to take care of your hair, eat a healthy diet, get enough sleep, and reduce stress. With a little patience and dedication, you'll have long, thick, and curly hair in no time!
