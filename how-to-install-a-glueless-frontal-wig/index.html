How To Install A Glueless Frontal Wig Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to change up your hairstyle, a glueless frontal wig might be just the thing for you. Unlike traditional wigs, which require glue or tape to stay in place, glueless wigs use a series of combs and clips to hold them securely in place. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps of installing a glueless frontal wig, and offer some tips and tricks to get the best results.

Description

A glueless frontal wig is a type of wig that is designed to be worn without any adhesive. Instead, it features a lace frontal that is designed to mimic the look of a natural hairline, and combs and clips to hold it securely in place. This type of wig is a great option for anyone who wants to change up their hairstyle without the commitment of a traditional wig.

How to Install a Glueless Frontal Wig: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you begin, it's important to make sure your hair is prepared for the wig. Start by braiding your hair in cornrows, and use a wig cap to keep your hair flat and secure. If you have any hair left out at the front, use a flat iron to smooth it down.

Step 2: Adjust the Wig

When you receive your wig, it may not fit perfectly right out of the box. Use the adjustable straps and hooks to adjust the size of the wig to fit your head. You can also trim the lace at the front to fit your natural hairline.

Step 3: Apply the Wig

Start by placing the wig on your head, with the combs at the front and sides. Secure the combs in place, and then use the clips at the back of the wig to hold it securely in place. Make sure the wig is centered and sits comfortably on your head.

Step 4: Style Your Wig

Once your wig is securely in place, you can style it however you like. Use a curling iron or flat iron to straighten or curl the hair, and use styling products to add texture and volume. Be sure to use heat protectant spray when using hot tools on your wig, to prevent damage to the hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you get the best results when installing a glueless frontal wig:

  • Use a wig cap to keep your hair flat and secure, and to prevent the wig from slipping.
  • If you have trouble getting the wig to sit flat against your head, try using a little bit of hairspray on the underside of the wig cap to create some grip.
  • Be gentle when adjusting the combs and clips, to avoid damaging your natural hair.

FAQ

What is a glueless frontal wig?

A glueless frontal wig is a type of wig that is designed to be worn without any adhesive. Instead, it features a lace frontal that is designed to mimic the look of a natural hairline, and combs and clips to hold it securely in place.

How do I install a glueless frontal wig?

To install a glueless frontal wig, start by preparing your hair by braiding it in cornrows and using a wig cap to keep it flat. Adjust the size of the wig using the adjustable straps and hooks, and then place the wig on your head, securing the combs and clips in place. Style the wig however you like using hot tools and styling products.

Can I wear a glueless frontal wig if I have natural hair?

Yes, you can wear a glueless frontal wig if you have natural hair. Simply braid your hair in cornrows and use a wig cap to keep it flat, and then follow the same steps for installing the wig as you would if you had no hair.

Hair Care

To keep your glueless frontal wig looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your wig regularly using a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Be gentle when combing or brushing your wig, to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your wig too frequently, as this can cause damage.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head when not in use, to help it maintain its shape.
