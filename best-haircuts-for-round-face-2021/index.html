Best Haircuts For Round Face 2021 A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect haircut for your round face? Look no further! In this guide, we will be discussing the best haircuts for round face 2021. From short to long, we have got you covered. Let's get started!

Description

Round faces are characterized by their soft and curved features. The width and length of a round face are almost equal, and the cheekbones are usually the widest part of the face. The aim of a good haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. This can be achieved by adding height and volume to the crown, keeping the sides of the hair short, and adding layers to the bottom of the hair.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for a Round Face

When choosing a haircut for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider your face shape and hair texture
  • Choose a haircut that adds height and volume to the crown
  • Avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of the face
  • Add layers to the bottom of the hair to create movement

Step by Step: Best Haircuts for Round Face 2021

1. Pixie Cut

The Pixie Cut is a short and chic haircut that is perfect for round faces. This haircut adds height to the crown and keeps the sides of the hair short, which slims down the face.

2. Bob Cut

The Bob Cut is a classic haircut that never goes out of style. For round faces, a long bob that hits just below the chin is the best option. This haircut adds length to the face and creates the illusion of an oval shape.

3. Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great option for round faces because it adds movement and texture to the hair. The layers should be added to the bottom of the hair to avoid adding volume to the sides of the face.

4. Side-swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great addition to any haircut for a round face. They add length to the face and draw attention away from the widest part of the face, which is the cheekbones.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you have chosen the perfect haircut for your round face, it is important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Use a heat protectant before styling your hair with heat tools
  • Invest in good quality hair products that are suited for your hair type
  • Avoid over-washing your hair to prevent stripping it of its natural oils

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is one that adds height and volume to the crown, keeps the sides of the hair short, and adds layers to the bottom of the hair to create movement.

Can I pull off a short haircut with a round face?

Yes, you can pull off a short haircut with a round face. The key is to choose a haircut that adds height to the crown and keeps the sides of the hair short.

What should I avoid when choosing a haircut for a round face?

Avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of the face, such as blunt cuts or heavy bangs. These haircuts can make the face appear wider and rounder.

Hair Care for Round Face Haircuts

It is important to take care of your hair, especially after getting a new haircut. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can cause damage
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair

Now that you know the best haircuts for round face 2021, it's time to book your appointment with your hairstylist. Remember to choose a haircut that adds height to the crown, keeps the sides of the hair short, and adds layers to the bottom of the hair to create movement. With the right haircut and hair care, you can rock any hairstyle with confidence!
