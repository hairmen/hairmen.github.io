What s Xiao s Haircut Called A Relaxed Guide To The Popular Hairstyle

Written by Christine Jul 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are a fan of the popular Chinese anime "The Untamed," you have probably noticed the unique and stylish haircut of the character Xiao. This trendy hairstyle has become increasingly popular, especially among young men. In this article, we will be discussing what Xiao's haircut is called, how to achieve the look, and tips on how to maintain it.

What is Xiao's Haircut Called?

Xiao's haircut is commonly referred to as a "curtain haircut." This style features long, layered bangs that are parted in the middle, framing the face on both sides. The back of the hair is typically shorter, with a slight taper towards the nape of the neck.

How to Achieve the Look

If you are interested in getting Xiao's haircut, it is essential to find a skilled hairstylist who can execute the look. Here are the steps to achieve the style:

Step 1: Consultation

Before getting the haircut, it is essential to have a consultation with your hairstylist. Discuss the style you want to achieve, and bring a picture of Xiao's haircut for reference. Your hairstylist can advise you on whether the style is suitable for your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Cut the Hair

The hairstylist will start by cutting the hair to the desired length. The back of the hair will be cut shorter, while the bangs will be left long. The hairstylist will then create layers in the bangs to add texture and movement. The bangs will be parted in the middle and cut at an angle to create the curtain effect.

Step 3: Style the Hair

Once the haircut is complete, the hairstylist will style the hair. Apply a styling product, such as a wax or pomade, to the bangs and comb them into place. The back of the hair can be left natural or styled with a light hold product.

Tips for Maintaining the Curtain Haircut

Here are some tips on how to maintain Xiao's haircut:

  • Regular trims are essential to maintain the shape of the haircut.
  • Use a light hold styling product to keep the bangs in place.
  • Wash the hair regularly to prevent oil buildup.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as hair straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage the hair.

FAQ

Can anyone get Xiao's haircut?

Xiao's haircut is suitable for most face shapes and hair types. However, it is essential to consult with a hairstylist to determine if the style is right for you.

How long does it take to style Xiao's haircut?

Styling time will vary depending on the length and thickness of your hair. On average, it takes about 10-15 minutes to style the bangs.

What styling products should I use for Xiao's haircut?

Use a light hold styling product, such as a wax or pomade, to keep the bangs in place. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down the hair.

Hair Care

It is essential to take care of your hair to maintain the health and integrity of the strands. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dyes, as they can damage the hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, Xiao's haircut, also known as the curtain haircut, is a trendy and stylish hairstyle that has become increasingly popular among young men. By following the steps outlined in this article and taking care of your hair, you can achieve and maintain Xiao's signature look.
