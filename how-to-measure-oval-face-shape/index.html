How To Measure Oval Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Jun 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you wondering if you have an oval face shape? Knowing your face shape is important when it comes to choosing the right hairstyles, makeup, and accessories that flatter your features. This guide will teach you how to measure your face shape, specifically an oval face shape, and provide tips on how to care for your hair based on your face shape.

Table of Content

  • How to Measure Oval Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Caring for Oval Face Shape
  • FAQ

How to Measure Oval Face Shape

Before we dive into measuring your face shape, it's important to understand what an oval face shape looks like. Oval faces are longer than they are wide, with wide cheekbones and a gently curved jawline. The forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the face tapers towards the chin.

Now, let's get to measuring! You will need a tape measure or ruler and a mirror.

Step 1: Start at the Forehead

Place the tape measure or ruler at the widest part of your forehead, just above your eyebrows.

Step 2: Measure Across Your Cheekbones

Next, measure across your cheekbones, which are usually the widest part of your face. Make sure the tape measure or ruler is straight and parallel to the floor.

Step 3: Measure Your Jawline

Measure from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jawbone angles upward. Multiply that number by two to get the total jawline measurement.

Step 4: Measure Your Face Length

Finally, measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Step-by-Step Guide

To summarize, here are the steps to measure your oval face shape:

  1. Start at the forehead
  2. Measure across your cheekbones
  3. Measure your jawline
  4. Measure your face length

Once you have these measurements, you can determine if you have an oval face shape. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, you may have a round face shape. If your face is longer than it is wide, with a pointed chin, you may have a heart-shaped face. If your forehead is wider than your jawline, you may have a diamond face shape.

Tips for Caring for Oval Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, you can use this information to choose hairstyles, makeup, and accessories that flatter your features.

Hairstyles

Oval faces are versatile and can pull off a variety of hairstyles. However, it's important to avoid styles that add too much volume to the top of the head, as this can make your face appear longer. Instead, go for styles that add volume at the sides or back, such as a layered bob or long waves.

Makeup

For makeup, focus on enhancing your natural features. Use a highlighter to accentuate your cheekbones, and choose a lip color that complements your skin tone. Avoid heavy contouring, as this can make your face appear more angular.

Accessories

When it comes to accessories, oval faces can wear just about anything. However, it's important to choose accessories that balance your features. For example, if you have a long face, choose earrings that are wider than they are long to add width to your face.

FAQ

What if my measurements don't match an oval face shape?

That's okay! Not everyone fits neatly into one face shape category. You may have a combination of face shapes, or your face shape may be more unique. Use this information as a starting point, but ultimately, choose hairstyles, makeup, and accessories that make you feel confident and beautiful.

Can face shape change over time?

Yes, your face shape can change over time due to factors such as aging and weight gain or loss. However, your basic bone structure will remain the same, so use this guide as a general reference.

Do I need to measure my face shape every time I want to try a new hairstyle?

No, once you know your face shape, you can use this information to guide your hairstyle choices. However, it's always a good idea to consult with a stylist to determine the best cut and style for your face shape and hair type.

How often should I trim my hair?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. However, the frequency of trims may vary depending on your hair type, length, and style.

What are some good hair care tips for oval face shape?

Regardless of your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair by shampooing and conditioning regularly, using heat protectant when styling, and avoiding tight hairstyles that can pull on your hairline. For oval faces specifically, focus on styles that add volume at the sides or back to balance your features.
