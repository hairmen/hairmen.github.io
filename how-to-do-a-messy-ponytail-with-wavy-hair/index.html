How To Do A Messy Ponytail With Wavy Hair

Written by Eveline Jul 21, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old sleek ponytail and want to switch things up with a messy ponytail? Wavy hair is perfect for a messy ponytail, and we have the perfect guide for you to achieve this look.

Description

A messy ponytail is a casual and effortless hairstyle that can be achieved with wavy hair. It’s a perfect look for a day out with friends, a casual office day, or even a night out. The beauty of a messy ponytail is that it doesn’t have to be neat and perfect, making it perfect for wavy hair, which tends to have a mind of its own.

How to

Follow these simple steps to achieve a messy ponytail with wavy hair:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will give your hair the necessary volume and texture for a messy ponytail. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves.

Step 2: Gather your hair

Gather your hair at the crown of your head and tie it into a loose ponytail with an elastic band. Leave some hair at the front and the sides to create a messy look.

Step 3: Tease your hair

Use a teasing comb to tease the hair at the crown of your head. This will give your ponytail the necessary volume and texture for a messy look.

Step 4: Create the messy look

Use your fingers to loosen the hair at the front and the sides of your head. This will create the messy look that you’re going for. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands that are falling out of place.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finish off the look with a light spray of hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating a messy ponytail with wavy hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair the necessary volume and texture.
  • Tease your hair at the crown of your head to give your ponytail more volume and texture.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands that are falling out of place.
  • Finish off the look with a light spray of hairspray to hold everything in place.

FAQ

Can I create a messy ponytail with straight hair?

Yes, you can create a messy ponytail with straight hair. However, you may need to use more product to give your hair the necessary texture and volume.

What type of elastic band should I use?

Use a soft, fabric elastic band to avoid damaging your hair. Avoid using rubber bands as they can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Can I create this look with short hair?

Yes, you can create this look with short hair. However, you may need to use more bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Hair Care

Make sure to take care of your hair to achieve a messy ponytail with wavy hair. Use a heat protectant when using hot tools, and avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair. Don’t forget to trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know how to do a messy ponytail with wavy hair, it’s time to rock this effortless hairstyle. Remember to have fun with it and embrace the messiness!
