Which Side Part Is More Attractive For Guys

Description

When it comes to hairstyles for men, the side part is a classic and timeless look. But which side part is more attractive? Is it the left or the right side? The truth is, it all depends on the individual and their face shape. However, there are some general guidelines that can help you decide which side part is more attractive for you.

How to

The first step in determining which side part is more attractive for you is to identify your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each face shape has different characteristics that can affect how a side part looks.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you have balanced proportions and a slightly rounded hairline. This face shape can pull off any side part, but a deep side part on either side can add some dimension to your hairstyle.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you have a circular shape with fuller cheeks and a rounded hairline. A side part on the left side can help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you have a strong jawline and a square hairline. A side part on the right side can help soften your features and add some balance to your face.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, you have a wider forehead and a pointed chin. A side part on the left side can help balance out your features and make your face appear more symmetrical.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you have a narrow forehead and jawline with wider cheekbones. A side part on either side can work well for this face shape, but a deep side part can add some definition to your cheekbones.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, you have a longer face with a straight hairline. A side part on the left side can help shorten your face and make it appear more balanced.

Step by Step

Once you have determined which side part is more attractive for your face shape, here are the steps to achieve the look:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it until it is damp.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to your hair. Be sure to distribute it evenly.
  3. Use a comb to create your side part. If you are unsure which side to part on, use the guidelines above.
  4. Style your hair as desired. You can slick it back for a classic look or add some texture for a more modern look.

Tips

  • Use a high-quality styling product to ensure your side part stays in place throughout the day.
  • If you have a receding hairline, avoid deep side parts as they can accentuate the hair loss.
  • If you have a widow's peak, consider a side part on the opposite side to balance out your hairline.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I create a side part with curly hair?

A: Yes, you can create a side part with curly hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to create the part and apply a styling product specifically designed for curly hair to keep it in place.

Q: Can I create a side part with short hair?

A: Yes, you can create a side part with short hair. Use a comb to create the part and apply a styling product to keep it in place.

Hair Care

To keep your side part looking its best, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Overall, which side part is more attractive for guys depends on their individual face shape. By following the guidelines above and experimenting with different styles, you can find the perfect side part for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
