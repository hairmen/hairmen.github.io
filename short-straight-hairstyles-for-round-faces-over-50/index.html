Short Straight Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 50

Written by Frank Sep 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you are over 50 and have a round face, you may be looking for the perfect short hairstyle to complement your features. Short straight hairstyles can be a great option for round faces, as they can help to elongate the face and create a more flattering silhouette. In this article, we will discuss some of the best short straight hairstyles for round faces over 50, as well as provide tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

Short straight hairstyles are a great option for round faces over 50, as they can help to create a more elongated and slimming effect. These hairstyles are typically easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways, depending on your personal preferences. Some popular short straight hairstyles for round faces over 50 include a pixie cut, a bob, or a sleek and straight hairstyle.

How to

If you are interested in trying out a short straight hairstyle for your round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you should consider the length of your hair and how it will complement your face shape. You may want to consult with a stylist to determine the best length and style for your face. Once you have chosen a style, you can then work on styling your hair to create the desired look.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Use a straightening iron to straighten your hair, if necessary.

3. Use a styling product, such as a hair gel or mousse, to create the desired style.

4. Use a hairbrush or comb to smooth out any bumps or kinks in your hair.

5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

When choosing a short straight hairstyle for your round face, there are a few tips to keep in mind. First, you should consider the length of your hair and how it will complement your face shape. You may want to consult with a stylist to determine the best length and style for your face. Additionally, you should consider the texture of your hair and how it will affect the style you choose. Finally, you should consider the maintenance required for the style and whether it will fit into your daily routine.

FAQ

Q: Will a short straight hairstyle work for all types of hair?

A: While a short straight hairstyle can work for many types of hair, it may not be the best option for very curly or thick hair.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut if I have a short straight hairstyle?

A: You should typically get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of the style.

Hair Care

To keep your short straight hairstyle looking its best, it is important to take proper care of your hair. This includes washing your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using a heat protectant when styling with heat tools, and avoiding over-styling or over-processing your hair. You may also want to consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair nourished and hydrated.

Overall, short straight hairstyles can be a great option for round faces over 50. With the right length and styling, you can create a flattering and stylish look that complements your features and enhances your overall appearance.
