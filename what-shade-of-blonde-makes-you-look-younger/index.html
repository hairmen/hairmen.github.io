What Shade Of Blonde Makes You Look Younger

Written by Frank Jun 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're thinking of going blonde, you might be wondering what shade of blonde makes you look younger. Blonde hair has always been associated with youthfulness, but not all shades of blonde are created equal when it comes to looking younger. In this article, we'll explore how to choose the perfect shade of blonde to make you look and feel younger.

Description

Blonde hair is a popular choice for many people, but it's important to choose the right shade of blonde to flatter your skin tone and make you look younger. The right shade of blonde can instantly brighten up your face and give you a more youthful appearance.

How to Choose the Right Shade of Blonde

Choosing the right shade of blonde can be tricky, but the key is to find a shade that complements your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, you'll want to choose a warmer shade of blonde, while cool undertones look best with cooler shades of blonde.

If you're not sure what your skin undertones are, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones, while green veins indicate warm undertones. Another way to determine your undertones is to look at the jewelry you wear. If you look best in silver jewelry, you likely have cool undertones, while those who look best in gold jewelry have warm undertones.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the right shade of blonde:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Undertones

Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist or the jewelry you wear to determine if you have warm or cool undertones.

Step 2: Choose a Shade of Blonde

For warm undertones, choose a warm shade of blonde like honey or golden blonde. For cool undertones, choose a cooler shade of blonde like platinum or ash blonde.

Step 3: Consider Your Natural Hair Color

If you have dark hair, you may want to opt for a lighter shade of blonde like champagne or baby blonde. If you have lighter hair, a darker shade of blonde like caramel or honey blonde may be more flattering.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional

If you're still unsure what shade of blonde is right for you, consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you choose a shade that will complement your skin tone and give you a more youthful appearance.

Tips for Maintaining Your Blonde Hair

Once you've chosen the perfect shade of blonde, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining your blonde hair:

  • Use a purple shampoo to keep your blonde from turning brassy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized

FAQs

What shade of blonde makes you look younger?

A warm shade of blonde like honey or golden blonde can make you look younger if you have warm undertones. If you have cool undertones, a cooler shade of blonde like platinum or ash blonde may be more flattering.

Can anyone go blonde?

Most people can go blonde, but it's important to choose the right shade of blonde to complement your skin tone. If you have naturally dark hair, it may take several sessions to achieve the desired shade of blonde.

Is blonde hair high maintenance?

Blonde hair can be high maintenance, especially if you have naturally dark hair. It's important to use a purple shampoo to prevent your blonde from turning brassy, and to avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.

Hair Care for Blonde Hair

Blonde hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for caring for your blonde hair:

  • Use a purple shampoo to prevent your blonde from turning brassy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized

In conclusion, choosing the right shade of blonde can make all the difference when it comes to looking younger. By following these tips and taking care of your blonde hair, you can achieve a youthful and vibrant look that will make you feel confident and beautiful.
