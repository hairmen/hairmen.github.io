Mid Length Hairstyles For Long Face How To Choose The Perfect Style

Written by Oliver Sep 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a long face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that will balance out your features and make your face appear shorter. Mid length hairstyles are a great option for those with long faces. In this article, we'll discuss how to choose the perfect mid length hairstyle for your long face, as well as provide tips on how to care for your hair.

How to Choose the Perfect Mid Length Hairstyle for Long Face

When choosing a mid length hairstyle for your long face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to avoid styles that are too long or too short. A length that hits just below the collarbone is ideal. This length will help to balance out your features and make your face appear shorter.

Another thing to consider is the cut of the hairstyle. Layers are a great option for those with long faces, as they can help to add volume and movement to the hair. A blunt cut, on the other hand, can make the face appear even longer.

The shape of the hairstyle is also important. A style with soft, rounded edges can help to soften a long face, while a style with sharp angles can make the face appear even longer.

Best Mid Length Hairstyles for Long Face

Here are some of the best mid length hairstyles for those with long faces:

  • Shoulder-length bob
  • Layered lob
  • Textured waves
  • Blunt cut with side-swept bangs
  • Long layers with face-framing highlights

Step by Step Guide to Styling Mid Length Hair for Long Face

Once you've chosen the perfect mid length hairstyle for your long face, it's time to style it. Here's a step by step guide to styling mid length hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray
  3. Use a curling iron or straightener to add texture to the hair
  4. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots
  5. Flip your head over and blow dry the roots using a round brush
  6. Flip your head back over and use the round brush to smooth out the ends
  7. Finish with a light hairspray

Tips for Maintaining Mid Length Hairstyles for Long Face

Here are some tips for maintaining your mid length hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep the ends neat and tidy
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep the hair healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day
  • Use a silk pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage

FAQs About Mid Length Hairstyles for Long Face

What is the ideal length for mid length hairstyles on long faces?

The ideal length for mid length hairstyles on long faces is just below the collarbone.

What kind of cut is best for mid length hairstyles on long faces?

Layers are a great option for mid length hairstyles on long faces, as they can help to add volume and movement to the hair.

What kind of shape is best for mid length hairstyles on long faces?

Soft, rounded edges are best for mid length hairstyles on long faces, as they can help to soften the face.

Hair Care Tips for Mid Length Hair on Long Face

Here are some hair care tips for mid length hair on long face:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness and damage
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, rather than a brush
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry it out

By following these tips, you can keep your mid length hairstyle looking its best and flatter your long face.
