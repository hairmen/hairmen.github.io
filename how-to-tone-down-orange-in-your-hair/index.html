How To Tone Down Orange In Your Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jun 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Orange hair can be a nightmare for anyone, especially those who have recently dyed their hair. It can be frustrating to see your hair turn orange instead of the color you wanted. Luckily, there are ways to tone down orange in your hair without having to go to the salon. In this article, we will discuss how to tone down orange in your hair, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tone Down Orange in Your Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Prevent Orange Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Tone Down Orange in Your Hair

The first thing you need to do is determine the cause of your orange hair. If you recently dyed your hair, it might be because you didn't leave the dye in long enough or used the wrong shade. If you have naturally light hair, it might be because of the minerals in the water you use to wash your hair.

Once you've determined the cause, you can start to tone down the orange in your hair using one of the following methods:

1. Use a Toner

A toner is a product that is used to neutralize unwanted brassy or orange tones in your hair. You can purchase toners at your local beauty supply store or online. Make sure to choose a toner that is specifically formulated for your hair color.

2. Try a Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo is another product that can help tone down orange in your hair. The purple color cancels out the orange tones, leaving your hair looking more natural. You can use purple shampoo once or twice a week, depending on how often you wash your hair.

3. Use a Color-Correcting Conditioner

A color-correcting conditioner is a product that is designed to correct unwanted tones in your hair. You can find color-correcting conditioners in various colors, including blue, purple, and green. Choose a conditioner that is formulated for your hair color and apply it after shampooing.

4. Apply Apple Cider Vinegar

Apple cider vinegar is a natural remedy that can help tone down orange in your hair. Mix one part apple cider vinegar with two parts water and apply it to your hair after shampooing. Leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know the methods to tone down orange in your hair, let's go over the step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Determine the Cause

As mentioned earlier, the first step is to determine the cause of your orange hair. This will help you choose the best method to tone it down.

Step 2: Choose a Method

Once you've determined the cause, choose a method to tone down the orange in your hair. You can choose from toner, purple shampoo, color-correcting conditioner, or apple cider vinegar.

Step 3: Follow the Instructions

Follow the instructions on the product you choose. Whether it's a toner, purple shampoo, color-correcting conditioner, or apple cider vinegar, make sure to follow the instructions carefully.

Step 4: Rinse Thoroughly

After applying the product, rinse your hair thoroughly with water. Make sure to remove all the product to avoid any residue.

Step 5: Repeat if Necessary

If the orange tones are still present, repeat the process. You can repeat the process once a week until you achieve the desired result.

Tips to Prevent Orange Hair

Prevention is always better than cure. Here are some tips to prevent orange hair:

  • Choose a hair dye that is specifically formulated for your hair color.
  • Leave the dye in for the recommended time.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup in your hair.
  • Use a shower filter to remove minerals from the water you use to wash your hair.

FAQs

1. Can I use a toner on natural hair?

Yes, you can use a toner on natural hair. Choose a toner that is formulated for your hair color.

2. How often should I use purple shampoo?

You can use purple shampoo once or twice a week, depending on how often you wash your hair.

3. Can I apply apple cider vinegar to my hair every day?

No, apple cider vinegar should only be applied once a week to avoid drying out your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, toning down orange in your hair is possible with the right products and methods. Follow the step-by-step guide and use the tips to prevent orange hair from happening again. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
