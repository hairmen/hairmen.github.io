How to do Messy Updo Hairstyles

Messy updos are perfect for any occasion and can be customized to suit your personal style. Here are some simple steps to follow:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can either air-dry or blow-dry your hair, depending on your preference. If you have thin or fine hair, you may want to add some texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture.

Step 2: Gather your hair at the crown

Next, gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie or elastic band. This will be the base of your messy updo.

Step 3: Twist and pin your hair

Now, take small sections of hair and twist them around the base, securing them with bobby pins. Repeat this process until all of your hair is twisted and pinned in place.

Step 4: Pull out some pieces for a messy look

Finally, pull out some pieces of hair around your face and at the back of your head to create a messy, undone look. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create more texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're still unsure about how to create a messy updo, follow these step-by-step instructions:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start with clean, dry hair. If you have fine or thin hair, add some texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture.

Step 2: Create a ponytail

Gather your hair at the crown of your head and create a ponytail. You can make it as high or low as you like.

Step 3: Twist and tuck

Take small sections of hair from the ponytail and twist them, tucking them into the elastic band. Continue twisting and tucking until all of your hair is secured in the ponytail.

Step 4: Pull out some pieces

Using your fingers, pull out some pieces of hair around your face and at the back of your head to create a messy, undone look. Tousle your hair with your fingers to create more texture.

Tips for Creating the Perfect Messy Updo

Here are some additional tips to help you create the perfect messy updo:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to hide them.

FAQs

Can messy updos work on short hair?

Yes! Messy updos can work on short hair, too. Simply follow the same steps, but use smaller sections of hair.

How do I prevent my messy updo from falling apart?

Use bobby pins and hairspray to hold everything in place. You can also add some texture spray or dry shampoo to give your hair more grip.

Can I create a messy updo with curly hair?

Absolutely! In fact, curly hair can make for a beautiful messy updo. Simply follow the same steps, but be sure to leave some curls loose around your face for a softer look.

Hair Care Tips for Messy Updos

While messy updos are a low-maintenance style, it's still important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.
