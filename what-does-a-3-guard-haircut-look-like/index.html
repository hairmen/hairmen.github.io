What Does A 3 Guard Haircut Look Like A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Sep 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you planning to get a haircut, and you're not sure what to ask for? Have you heard of a 3 guard haircut, but you're not sure what it looks like? In this article, we'll explain everything you need to know about the 3 guard haircut, from the length to the style options and how to care for it.

Description of a 3 Guard Haircut

A 3 guard haircut is a popular choice for men who want to keep their hair short but not too close to the scalp. The number 3 refers to the length of the guard that attaches to the clippers. The 3 guard measures 3/8 of an inch or 9.5mm. This haircut is perfect for those who want to maintain a clean and neat appearance without too much maintenance.

How to Get a 3 Guard Haircut

Getting a 3 guard haircut is relatively easy. You can go to any barber or stylist and ask for a number 3 guard on the clippers. However, it's essential to communicate clearly with your barber or stylist about the style you want. Bring a picture or describe the style you're looking for.

Step by Step Guide to Get a 3 Guard Haircut

Step 1: Wash your hair and dry it. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free from any product buildup.

Step 2: Sit comfortably on the barber's chair and explain the type of haircut you want.

Step 3: The barber will use a number 3 guard on the clippers and start cutting your hair. The clippers will be run all over your head, starting from the sides to the back and then to the top of your head.

Step 4: Once the barber has run the clippers over your head, they will use scissors to trim and shape your hair. This will help to create a more polished look.

Step 5: The barber will use a comb to check for any unevenness and make any necessary adjustments.

Step 6: After the haircut is complete, the barber will use a brush to remove any loose hair and then offer you a mirror to check the final look.

Tips for Maintaining a 3 Guard Haircut

Now that you have your 3 guard haircut, you'll want to keep it looking neat and stylish. Here are some tips to help you maintain your new haircut:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of the cut.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Style your hair with a pomade or clay to create a polished look.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can make your hair look greasy and limp.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to style your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage.

FAQs

1. Is a 3 guard haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, a 3 guard haircut is suitable for all face shapes. However, if you have a round face, you may want to go for a shorter haircut to create a more defined jawline.

2. Can I style my hair differently with a 3 guard haircut?

Yes, you can style your hair differently with a 3 guard haircut. You can create a messy look or slick it back with a pomade or clay. Experiment with different styles to find what suits you best.

3. How long will a 3 guard haircut last?

A 3 guard haircut can last up to six weeks, but it's recommended to get a trim every four weeks to maintain the length and shape of the cut.

Hair Care for a 3 Guard Haircut

A 3 guard haircut is relatively low maintenance, but it's still essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized. Avoid using hot tools like a hairdryer or straightener too often as they can cause damage to your hair. Use a heat protectant spray if you need to use hot tools.

Now that you know what a 3 guard haircut looks like, you can confidently ask your barber or stylist for the style you want. Remember to communicate clearly about the style you're looking for and follow these tips to keep your hair looking neat and stylish.
