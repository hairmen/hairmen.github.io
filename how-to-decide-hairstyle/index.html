How To Decide Hairstyle A Guide For Every Hair Type

Written by Luffy Nov 12, 2023 · 5 min read

Choosing a hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially with so many options available. But, fear not because we have put together a guide to help you decide which hairstyle is perfect for you. Whether you have curly, straight, or wavy hair, this guide will provide you with the necessary steps to help you make the decision.

Table of Contents

  • Determine Your Hair Type
  • Consider Your Face Shape
  • Take Your Lifestyle into Account
  • Choose a Hair Length
  • Decide on a Hair Color
  • Hairstyle Ideas for Different Hair Types
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

Determine Your Hair Type

The first step in deciding on a hairstyle is to determine your hair type. There are three main hair types: curly, straight, and wavy. Knowing your hair type will help you choose a hairstyle that will work best for you.

If you have curly hair, you may want to consider a hairstyle that will emphasize your curls. On the other hand, if you have straight hair, you may want to consider a hairstyle that will add volume and texture to your hair.

If you have wavy hair, you may want to consider a hairstyle that will enhance your natural waves. Once you have determined your hair type, you can move on to the next step.

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape is another important factor to consider when deciding on a hairstyle. There are several face shapes, including round, square, oval, heart, and diamond. Each face shape has a hairstyle that will complement it best.

For example, if you have a round face, you may want to consider a hairstyle that will add height and length to your face. On the other hand, if you have a square face, you may want to consider a hairstyle that will soften your jawline.

Take Your Lifestyle into Account

Another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your lifestyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, you may want to consider a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. On the other hand, if you have more time to spend on your hair, you may want to consider a more elaborate hairstyle.

Choose a Hair Length

The length of your hair is also an important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. There are several hair lengths to choose from, including short, medium, and long. Each hair length has its own set of advantages and disadvantages.

If you have short hair, you may want to consider a hairstyle that will add volume and texture to your hair. On the other hand, if you have long hair, you may want to consider a hairstyle that will show off your length.

Decide on a Hair Color

Your hair color is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. There are several hair colors to choose from, including blonde, brunette, red, and black. Each hair color has its own set of advantages and disadvantages.

For example, if you have blonde hair, you may want to consider a hairstyle that will show off your color. On the other hand, if you have black hair, you may want to consider a hairstyle that will add dimension to your hair.

Hairstyle Ideas for Different Hair Types

Now that you have determined your hair type, face shape, lifestyle, hair length, and hair color, it's time to choose a hairstyle. Here are some hairstyle ideas for different hair types:

Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, you may want to consider a layered haircut that will emphasize your curls. You can also try a side-swept bang or a pixie cut.

Straight Hair

If you have straight hair, you may want to consider a layered haircut that will add volume and texture to your hair. You can also try a bob or a blunt cut.

Wavy Hair

If you have wavy hair, you may want to consider a haircut that will enhance your natural waves. You can also try a long layered cut or a beachy wave.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you have chosen a hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a conditioner
  • Avoid heat styling tools
  • Get regular trims
  • Use a hair mask

FAQs

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

The best hairstyle for a round face is one that adds height and length to your face. Consider a hairstyle with layers or a side-swept bang.

What is the best hairstyle for a square face?

The best hairstyle for a square face is one that softens your jawline. Consider a hairstyle with curls or a layered cut.

What is the best hairstyle for a busy lifestyle?

The best hairstyle for a busy lifestyle is one that requires minimal styling. Consider a low-maintenance haircut.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Aim for 2-3 times a week.

Choosing a hairstyle can be overwhelming, but with our guide, you can make an informed decision that will leave you feeling confident and beautiful.
