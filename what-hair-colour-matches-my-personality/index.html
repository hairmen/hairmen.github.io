What Hair Colour Matches My Personality A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Shade

Written by Daniel Sep 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to change up your hair colour, it can be overwhelming to decide which shade is right for you. With so many options to choose from, it's important to consider which hair colour matches your personality. In this guide, we'll explore different hair colours and what they say about your personality, as well as tips for choosing the right shade and maintaining your new colour.

What Your Hair Colour Says About Your Personality

Believe it or not, your hair colour can actually say a lot about your personality. Here are some common hair colours and what they may indicate:

Blonde

Blonde hair is often associated with a fun, carefree personality. If you're outgoing, bubbly, and love to have a good time, blonde may be the perfect shade for you. However, blonde can also indicate a lack of seriousness, so if you're looking for a more professional look, you may want to consider a different shade.

Brunette

Brunette hair is often associated with intelligence and sophistication. If you're a no-nonsense type of person who values education and hard work, brunette may be the perfect shade for you. However, if you're looking to break out of your shell and try something new, you may want to consider a more adventurous shade.

Red

Red hair is often associated with passion and creativity. If you're a fiery, passionate person who loves to express yourself through art, music, or other creative outlets, red may be the perfect shade for you. However, if you prefer a more low-key lifestyle, you may want to consider a different shade.

Black

Black hair is often associated with mystery and intensity. If you're a deep, introspective person who values privacy and solitude, black may be the perfect shade for you. However, if you're looking to project a more approachable image, you may want to consider a lighter shade.

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour

Now that you have a better understanding of what different hair colours may indicate about your personality, it's time to choose the right shade for you. Here are some tips to help you make the right decision:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone can play a big role in which hair colours will look best on you. If you have a warm skin tone, you may want to consider shades like golden blonde, caramel, or chestnut. If you have a cool skin tone, shades like ash blonde, platinum, or cool brown may be more flattering on you.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in which hair colour you choose. If you have a busy, active lifestyle, you may want to choose a low-maintenance shade that doesn't require a lot of upkeep. If you have a more laid-back lifestyle, you may be able to experiment with more high-maintenance shades.

Consider Your Natural Hair Colour

If you're not sure which hair colour to choose, it can be helpful to consider your natural hair colour. If you have naturally light hair, you may want to consider going darker. If you have naturally dark hair, you may want to consider going lighter. This can help you achieve a more natural-looking result.

Step-by-Step Guide to Changing Your Hair Colour

Once you've chosen the perfect hair colour, it's time to make the change. Here's a step-by-step guide to changing your hair colour:

Step 1: Choose Your Colour

Use the tips above to choose the perfect hair colour for you.

Step 2: Gather Your Supplies

You'll need hair dye, gloves, a mixing bowl, a brush, and a towel.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Follow the instructions on the hair dye box to prepare your hair. This may involve washing, drying, and sectioning your hair.

Step 4: Apply the Dye

Using the brush, apply the dye to your hair in sections. Make sure to follow the instructions on the box for how long to leave the dye on.

Step 5: Rinse and Condition

Once the dye has set, rinse your hair with cool water until the water runs clear. Apply the conditioner that comes with the dye to help keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Colour

Now that you've changed your hair colour, it's important to take steps to maintain it. Here are some tips to help you keep your new colour looking great:

Use Colour-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Regular shampoo and conditioner can strip your hair of its colour. Use colour-safe products to help your new shade last longer.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like blow dryers, straighteners, and curling irons can damage your hair and cause your colour to fade faster. Avoid using these tools as much as possible.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun's UV rays can also cause your hair colour to fade faster. Wear a hat or use a UV-protective spray when you're outside for extended periods of time.

FAQ

Can I dye my hair if I have a sensitive scalp?

If you have a sensitive scalp, it's important to choose a hair dye that's specifically formulated for sensitive skin. You may also want to do a patch test before applying the dye to your entire head.

How often do I need to touch up my roots?

This will depend on your hair growth rate and the shade of your hair. As a general rule, you'll need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Can I dye my hair at home or should I go to a salon?

If you're comfortable following instructions and have experience dyeing your hair at home, you can definitely dye your hair at home. However, if you're not confident in your abilities, it's best to go to a salon to avoid any mishaps.

Hair Care

Changing your hair colour can be a fun and exciting way to express yourself. By considering your personality, skin tone, and lifestyle, you can choose the perfect shade for you. Follow our step-by-step guide to changing your hair colour and our tips for maintaining your new shade to keep your hair looking great.
