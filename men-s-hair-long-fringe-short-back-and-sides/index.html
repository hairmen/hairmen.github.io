Men s Hair Long Fringe Short Back And Sides

Written by Eveline Oct 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on achieving the popular men's hairstyle, long fringe short back and sides. This look has been trending for years and shows no signs of slowing down. In this post, we'll cover everything you need to know about getting this hairstyle, including how to achieve it, step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice. So, let's dive in!

Description

The long fringe short back and sides hairstyle involves leaving the hair on the top of the head longer while keeping the back and sides shorter. The long fringe falls over the forehead, giving the hairstyle a relaxed and casual look. This style is incredibly versatile, making it a popular choice among men of all ages and hair types.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve the long fringe short back and sides look, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

Before you can achieve the long fringe short back and sides style, you need to choose the right haircut. For this hairstyle, ask your stylist for a scissor cut on the top and a clipper cut on the back and sides. Make sure to communicate the length of the fringe you want, as this will determine the overall look.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure to wash and condition it. This will help remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup on your scalp and hair. Use a hydrating conditioner to keep your hair healthy and prevent breakage.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or wax, to your hair. Work the product through your hair, focusing on the top and fringe. This will help create texture and hold the style in place.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while using your fingers to shape the fringe. Use a round brush to create volume and movement on the top of your head. Once your hair is dry, use a bit more product to define the fringe and hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

Here are some tips for maintaining the long fringe short back and sides hairstyle:

  • Regularly trim the back and sides to maintain the length and shape.
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent oil buildup on your scalp.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

How long does my fringe need to be for this style?

The length of your fringe depends on your personal preference and face shape. However, a good rule of thumb is to keep it no longer than the tip of your nose.

Can I achieve this style with curly hair?

Absolutely! This style works well with all hair types, including curly hair. Just make sure to communicate with your stylist about the best way to achieve the look with your specific hair type.

Is this style appropriate for the workplace?

Yes, the long fringe short back and sides hairstyle is appropriate for most workplaces. However, if you work in a very conservative environment, it's always best to check with your employer's dress code policy.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet rich in protein and vitamins to promote healthy hair growth.

With the right haircut, styling products, and hair care routine, anyone can achieve the long fringe short back and sides hairstyle. Follow the tips and advice in this guide to keep your hair looking great and feeling healthy. Good luck!
