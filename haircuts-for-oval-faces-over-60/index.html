Haircuts For Oval Faces Over 60 Tips And Ideas

Written by Oliver Sep 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're an older woman with an oval face shape, you might be wondering what kind of haircut would look best on you. Fortunately, there are plenty of options that can flatter your face shape and make you look and feel confident and stylish. In this article, we'll explore some of the best haircuts for oval faces over 60, along with tips and advice for caring for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Oval Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • FAQs About Haircuts for Oval Faces Over 60

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Oval Face

When it comes to haircuts for oval faces, the goal is to create balance and symmetry. You want to avoid anything that will elongate your face or make it look too narrow. Some of the best haircuts for oval faces over 60 include:

Layered Bob

A layered bob is a great choice for older women with oval faces. The layers add texture and volume, while the bob length keeps things looking polished and youthful. This haircut is also low-maintenance and easy to style.

Long Layers

If you prefer longer hair, consider adding some soft, face-framing layers. This will draw attention to your features and create an overall balanced look. Just be sure to avoid any harsh, blunt cuts that can make your face look too angular.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a classic choice for oval faces, as they draw attention to your eyes and create a soft, feminine look. This is a great option if you want to add some interest to your hair without committing to a major haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Haircut

Once you've chosen the right haircut for your oval face, the next step is to get it cut. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you prepare:

Step 1: Find a Hairstylist You Trust

Look for a hairstylist who has experience working with older women and who understands your hair type and face shape. Read reviews online and ask for recommendations from friends and family.

Step 2: Bring Inspiration Photos

Bring in photos of haircuts you like to help your hairstylist understand your vision. Be open to their suggestions, but don't be afraid to speak up if you're not happy with something.

Step 3: Communicate Clearly

Make sure you communicate clearly with your hairstylist about what you want. Use specific terms like "long layers" or "side-swept bangs" so that you're both on the same page.

Step 4: Trust the Process

Remember that haircuts can be a process, especially if you're making a major change. Be patient and trust your hairstylist to guide you through the process.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

No matter what kind of haircut you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

Use a Gentle Shampoo

As you age, your hair becomes more delicate and fragile. Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Try air-drying your hair whenever possible, or use a low-heat setting on your styling tools.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and polished. Aim for a trim every 6-8 weeks.

FAQs About Haircuts for Oval Faces Over 60

Here are some common questions and answers about haircuts for oval faces:

What kind of haircut is best for an oval face?

Layered bobs, long layers, and side-swept bangs are all great choices for oval faces.

What should I avoid if I have an oval face?

Avoid anything that will elongate your face or make it look too narrow, such as blunt cuts or heavy bangs.

Hair Care Tips for Older Women

As you age, your hair may become thinner and more delicate. Here are some tips for caring for your hair as you get older:

Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet can help keep your hair strong and healthy. Be sure to eat plenty of protein, fruits, and vegetables.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Wear a hat or use a UV-protective spray when you're outside.

Be Gentle When Brushing and Styling

Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to avoid pulling or tugging on your hair. Be gentle when blow-drying or styling your hair as well.

Conclusion

Getting a haircut can be a great way to refresh your look and boost your confidence. By choosing a haircut that flatters your oval face shape and taking care of your hair, you can look and feel your best at any age.
