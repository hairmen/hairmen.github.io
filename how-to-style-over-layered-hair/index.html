How To Style Over Layered Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jul 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to style over layered hair. Layered hair is a popular choice for many people, as it adds volume and texture to the hair. However, figuring out how to style it can be a challenge. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions and tips on how to style your layered hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Over Layered Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Layered Hair
  • FAQs

How to Style Over Layered Hair

Styling layered hair can be tricky, as you want to show off the layers without making them look messy or uneven. Here are some tips to help you style your layered hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots.
  • Use a round brush to curl the ends of your hair and create a bouncy look.
  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Finish off with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your layered hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture.
  3. Blow dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots.
  4. Use a round brush to curl the ends of your hair and create a bouncy look.
  5. Apply a small amount of dry shampoo to the roots of your hair to add texture and volume.
  6. Brush your hair to distribute the dry shampoo.
  7. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Layered Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling layered hair:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for your hair.
  • Don't use too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and healthy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling layered hair:

How do I know if layered hair will suit me?

Layered hair looks great on most face shapes and hair types. However, it's best to consult with your hairstylist to determine if it will work for you.

Can I style my layered hair without using heat tools?

Absolutely! You can air dry your hair and use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture.

How often should I get my layered hair trimmed?

It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your layers.

Hair Care for Layered Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your layered hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the ends and make your layers look frizzy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your layered hair with confidence. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you, and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
