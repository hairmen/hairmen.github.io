How To Get Permanent Wavy Hair Naturally At Home

Written by Frank Jul 27, 2023 · 3 min read

If you are tired of using heating tools to achieve those perfect beachy waves, then you have come to the right place. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to get permanent wavy hair naturally at home. Say goodbye to the damage caused by heat styling tools and welcome natural, healthy waves!

Description

Permanent wavy hair can be achieved naturally without damaging your hair. Natural waves are not only healthier for your hair, but they also look more effortless and beautiful. This process can take some time, but the results are worth it.

How to Get Permanent Wavy Hair Naturally at Home

Follow these steps to get natural, permanent waves at home:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using any heavy products that might weigh your hair down. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently towel dry.

Step 2: Apply a Wave-Enhancing Product

Apply a wave-enhancing product to your damp hair. This could be a mousse or a curl enhancing cream. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends.

Step 3: Braid Your Hair

Divide your hair into two sections and braid each section tightly. Secure the ends with a hair tie. You could also twist your hair into buns or knots for a different wave pattern.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Dry

Let your hair air dry completely. Avoid using a hair dryer as this could cause frizz and damage. If you are in a hurry, you could use a diffuser attachment on low heat to help dry your hair.

Step 5: Release the Braids

Once your hair is completely dry, gently undo the braids. Use your fingers to separate the waves and create a natural, tousled look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use a wave-enhancing product that works well with your hair type.
  • Braid your hair tightly for a more defined wave pattern.
  • For a looser wave pattern, braid your hair loosely or twist it into buns.
  • Don't brush your hair after undoing the braids as this could create frizz.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQ

How long will the waves last?

The waves can last for a few days or even up to a week depending on your hair type and the products you use. You can refresh the waves by spritzing some water on your hair and re-braiding it.

Can I use this method on short hair?

Yes, you can use this method on short hair. Instead of dividing your hair into two sections, you could create smaller braids or twists.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using heating tools too often.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that could cause hair breakage.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to get permanent wavy hair naturally at home, give it a try and see the beautiful results for yourself!
