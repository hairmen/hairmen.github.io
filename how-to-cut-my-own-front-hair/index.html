How To Cut My Own Front Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to save a bit of money and time by cutting your own hair, you're not alone. Many people have started to take matters into their own hands, especially when it comes to simple haircuts like trimming their front hair. But before you grab your scissors and start snipping away, there are a few things you need to know. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own front hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Cut Your Own Front Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair
  4. FAQs About Cutting Your Own Hair

How to Cut Your Own Front Hair

Cutting your own front hair is relatively easy, but you need to make sure you have the right tools and technique. Here's a quick rundown of what you'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips or ties
  • Good lighting

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have everything you need, here's how to cut your own front hair:

  1. Start by parting your hair where you normally would.
  2. Clip or tie the rest of your hair back so it's out of the way.
  3. Comb your front hair straight down so it's even.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the middle of your forehead, where you want to cut it.
  5. Hold the hair tightly between your fingers and cut it straight across.
  6. Repeat steps 4 and 5 for any other sections you want to cut.
  7. Check that your hair is even on both sides.
  8. Blend any uneven sections by taking a small amount of hair from each side and cutting it to match the length of the other side.
  9. Check your hair in different lighting to make sure it looks even.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting your own front hair:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Make sure your scissors are sharp so you get a clean cut.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you go.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as it will look shorter when it dries.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking fresh.

FAQs About Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are a few frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair:

Is it easy to cut your own hair?

Cutting your own hair can be easy if you have the right tools and technique. Start with a simple haircut like trimming your front hair, and work your way up to more complex styles.

How do you know how much hair to cut?

Start with small sections of hair and cut less than you think you need to. You can always go back and cut more if needed.

What if I mess up?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go to a professional stylist to fix it, or wait for it to grow out.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair

After cutting your own front hair, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking fresh.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your own front hair at home. Just remember to start small and take your time, and you'll have beautiful, healthy hair in no time.
