How To Wear A Wig With Long Thick Hair

Written by Daniel Aug 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have long thick hair, wearing a wig can be a bit of a challenge. However, with the right techniques and tools, you can easily wear a wig and achieve a natural-looking hairstyle. In this blog post, we will guide you through the steps on how to wear a wig with long thick hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Wear a Wig with Long Thick Hair
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Wearing a Wig with Long Thick Hair
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care for Wearing a Wig with Long Thick Hair

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before wearing a wig, it is important to prepare your hair properly. Start by brushing your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles. You can also apply a leave-in conditioner to make your hair smooth and easy to manage.

Step 2: Secure Your Hair

If you have long thick hair, it is best to secure it in a ponytail or a bun. This will prevent your hair from getting tangled and make it easier to wear the wig. You can use bobby pins or a hair elastic to secure your hair tightly.

Step 3: Put on a Wig Cap

A wig cap is a must-have accessory when wearing a wig. It helps to keep your hair in place and creates a smooth surface for the wig. Put the wig cap on your head and adjust it so that it fits snugly.

Step 4: Put on the Wig

Now it's time to put on the wig. Hold the wig at the sides and put it on your head from front to back. Adjust the wig so that it sits comfortably on your head. You can use the adjustable straps at the back of the wig to make it tighter or looser as needed.

Step 5: Style the Wig

Once you have put on the wig, you can style it according to your preference. Use a wig comb or your fingers to style the wig. You can also use hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Wearing a Wig with Long Thick Hair

Here are some tips to make wearing a wig with long thick hair easier:

  • Use a wig cap to keep your hair in place and prevent it from getting tangled.
  • Secure your hair tightly in a bun or ponytail to create a smooth surface for the wig.
  • Adjust the wig straps to make it tighter or looser as needed.
  • Use a wig comb or your fingers to style the wig gently.
  • Apply hairspray to hold the style in place.

FAQ

Can I wear a wig if I have long thick hair?

Yes, you can wear a wig if you have long thick hair. You just need to follow the proper steps and use the right tools to make it easier to wear.

What type of wig is best for long thick hair?

A lace front wig is best for long thick hair as it creates a natural-looking hairline and allows you to part the hair in different ways.

Can I wear a wig if I have a sensitive scalp?

Yes, you can wear a wig if you have a sensitive scalp. Look for wigs made from soft and breathable materials that won't irritate your scalp.

Hair Care for Wearing a Wig with Long Thick Hair

Wearing a wig with long thick hair requires proper hair care to keep your hair healthy and prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your hair as they can cause damage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to make your hair smooth and easy to manage.
  • Brush your hair gently to prevent tangles.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can wear a wig with long thick hair and achieve a natural-looking hairstyle. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a variety of hairstyles and change up your look whenever you want.
