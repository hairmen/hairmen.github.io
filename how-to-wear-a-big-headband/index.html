How To Wear A Big Headband Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jun 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Headbands are a great way to add some extra style to your hairdo. A big headband, in particular, can make a bold statement and give your outfit a touch of glamour. But wearing a big headband can be tricky, especially if you're not used to it. In this article, we'll show you how to wear a big headband and give you some tips and tricks to make it look amazing.

Description

A big headband is a hair accessory that covers a significant portion of your forehead and goes all the way around your head. It can be made of various materials, such as fabric, leather, or plastic. Big headbands come in different styles, colors, and patterns, so you can choose one that fits your style and personality.

How to Wear a Big Headband

Wearing a big headband is easy if you follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Headband

The first step is to choose the right headband that fits your head size and hair type. If you have thick hair, you might need a wider headband to keep your hair in place. If you have a small head, you might need a smaller headband to avoid slipping.

Step 2: Style Your Hair

Style your hair in a way that complements the headband. You can wear your hair up or down, depending on your preference. If you're wearing the headband with your hair down, make sure to brush your hair away from your face to showcase the headband.

Step 3: Place the Headband

Place the headband on your head, starting from the back and sliding it forward. Make sure the headband covers your forehead and sits comfortably on your head. You can adjust the headband by pulling it down or up, depending on the look you want to achieve.

Step 4: Secure the Headband

If your headband is slipping, you can secure it with bobby pins or hair clips. Place the clips behind your ears or at the nape of your neck to keep the headband in place.

Tips for Wearing a Big Headband

Here are some tips to help you rock a big headband:

Tip 1: Keep It Simple

When wearing a big headband, keep the rest of your outfit simple. You don't want to overwhelm your look with too many accessories or patterns.

Tip 2: Match Your Headband to Your Outfit

Choose a headband that matches your outfit's color scheme or pattern. This will create a cohesive look and tie everything together.

Tip 3: Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different headband styles. You can try a wide headband, a skinny headband, or a headband with embellishments, such as beads or flowers.

Tip 4: Wear It with Confidence

The most important tip is to wear your headband with confidence. If you feel good, you'll look good. So, rock that big headband with pride!

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a big headband with short hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a big headband with short hair. Choose a headband that fits your head size and hair type, and style your hair in a way that complements the headband.

Q: How do I choose a headband that fits my head?

A: Measure the circumference of your head, and choose a headband that matches that measurement. If you're unsure, try on different headbands to find the right fit.

Hair Care

Wearing a big headband can put pressure on your hair, especially if you wear it for an extended period. To avoid damaging your hair, make sure to take breaks from wearing headbands, and don't wear them too tightly. Also, don't forget to wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and strong.
