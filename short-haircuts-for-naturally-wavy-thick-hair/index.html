Short Haircuts For Naturally Wavy Thick Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy Aug 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of struggling to tame your naturally wavy thick hair? Do you want a chic and easy-to-maintain haircut that accentuates your gorgeous curls? Look no further! In this guide, we will explore the best short haircuts for naturally wavy thick hair that will have you feeling confident and stylish in 2023.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts are perfect for those who want to embrace their natural waves without the hassle of maintaining long locks. With the right cut and styling techniques, short hair can look just as stunning and glamorous as long hair. For those with naturally wavy thick hair, a short haircut can help to define curls and reduce frizz.

How to

The first step in getting a short haircut for naturally wavy thick hair is to find a stylist who specializes in curly hair. They will be able to recommend the best cut for your unique hair type and face shape. When discussing your desired look with your stylist, be sure to mention any concerns you have about volume and frizz.

Choose the Right Cut

The most flattering short haircuts for naturally wavy thick hair are those that allow curls to bounce and move freely without looking weighed down. Some popular options include:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob
  • Layered cut
  • Shag

Your stylist may also recommend adding bangs to complement your face shape and hair texture.

Style with Care

Once you have your new cut, it's important to style it with care to avoid damaging your curls. Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling wands, as these can cause frizz and dryness. Instead, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to gently dry your curls. You can also apply a curl-defining cream or gel to help enhance your natural texture.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your short haircut for naturally wavy thick hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a small amount of hair oil or serum to tame any frizz.

Tips

  • Invest in a high-quality diffuser attachment for your blow dryer.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and frizz.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make my hair look thinner?

A: Not necessarily. The right cut and styling techniques can help to add volume and bounce to your curls.

Q: Can I still straighten my hair with a short haircut?

A: Yes, but it's important to use heat protectant and avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your short haircut looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes:

  • Washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoiding excessive heat styling.
  • Trimming your hair every 6-8 weeks to avoid split ends.

With the right cut and styling techniques, short haircuts for naturally wavy thick hair can be both stylish and low-maintenance. Embrace your curls and rock your new look in 2023!
