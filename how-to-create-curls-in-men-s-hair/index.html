How To Create Curls In Men s Hair

Written by Robby Aug 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your straight and boring hair? Do you want to add some bounce and texture to your hair? Well, you're in luck because we're here to teach you how to create curls in men's hair. In this article, we'll go over the steps, tips, and hair care you need to know to achieve the perfect curls.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Create Curls in Men's Hair
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Perfect Curls
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Create Curls in Men's Hair

Before we start, you need to have the right tools. You will need a curling iron, heat protectant spray, and hair mousse.

Step 1: Wash your hair and towel-dry it. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to avoid heat damage.

Step 2: Apply hair mousse to your hair starting from the roots to the tips. This will help hold the curls in place.

Step 3: Heat up your curling iron. The ideal temperature should be between 300-350 degrees Fahrenheit.

Step 4: Divide your hair into sections. The number of sections depends on how thick your hair is. If you have thin hair, you can divide it into two sections. If you have thick hair, you can divide it into four sections.

Step by Step Guide

Step 5: Take a section of your hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold it for 5-10 seconds and release. Repeat this process with the rest of your hair.

Step 6: Once you've curled all your hair, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls. This will give it a more natural look.

Step 7: Apply a small amount of hair serum to your hair to add shine and hold the curls in place.

Tips for Perfect Curls

1. Don't use too much heat on your hair. It can cause damage and breakage.

2. Use a heat protectant spray to avoid heat damage.

3. Apply hair mousse to your hair to hold the curls in place.

4. Don't forget to run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.

5. Apply hair serum to add shine and hold the curls in place.

FAQs

Q: Can I create curls in short hair?

A: Yes, you can. You just need to use a smaller curling iron and wrap small sections of your hair around it.

Q: How long will the curls last?

A: It depends on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, curls can last up to 24 hours.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

2. Avoid using heat on your hair every day.

3. Apply hair oil to your hair to keep it moisturized.

4. Don't brush your hair when it's dry. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle it.

5. Get regular trims to avoid split ends.

In conclusion, creating curls in men's hair is not as difficult as it seems. Follow these simple steps, use the right products, and you'll have bouncy and beautiful curls in no time.
