How To Style Long Hair On Top Guys A Step By Step Guide

Long hair on top guys is a trendy hairstyle that has become increasingly popular over the years. If you're one of the many men who have decided to grow out their hair, you may be wondering how to style it properly. In this guide, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to style long hair on top guys, along with some helpful tips and tricks.

  • How to Style Long Hair on Top Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Long Hair on Top Guys

Styling long hair on top guys may seem daunting at first, but it's actually quite simple. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or wax, to your fingertips.
  3. Rub the product between your palms to warm it up.
  4. Starting at the roots, work the product through your hair, ensuring that it's evenly distributed.
  5. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired.
  6. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basic steps, let's dive into a more detailed step-by-step guide.

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Long hair can get greasy and dirty quickly, so it's important to keep it clean.
  2. Towel dry your hair and comb out any tangles or knots.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling product to your fingertips. You don't want to use too much, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  4. Rub the product between your palms to warm it up.
  5. Starting at the roots, work the product through your hair, ensuring that it's evenly distributed. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create texture.
  6. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired. Long hair on top guys can be styled in a variety of ways, such as slicked back, messy and textured, or with a side part.
  7. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place. Be sure to hold the can at least 6 inches away from your head to avoid weighing your hair down.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style long hair on top guys:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find the right one for your hair type and desired look.
  • If you have curly hair, avoid using a brush as this can cause frizz. Instead, use your fingers to style your hair.
  • If you have thin hair, use a volumizing product to add thickness and texture.
  • If you have thick hair, use a smoothing product to tame frizz and flyaways.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling long hair on top guys:

  • How long should my hair be to style it on top?
    Your hair should be at least 4-6 inches long on top to style it properly.
  • What type of styling product should I use?
    This depends on your hair type and desired look. Pomade, wax, and clay are popular choices for styling long hair on top guys.
  • How often should I wash my hair?
    This depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or work out frequently, you may need to wash your hair more often. Otherwise, washing your hair 2-3 times a week is sufficient.

Hair Care for Long Hair on Top Guys

Long hair on top guys requires a bit more maintenance than shorter hairstyles. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style long hair on top guys with ease. Remember to experiment with different products and styles to find the look that works best for you.
