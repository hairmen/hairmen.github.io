How Much Is Prp Hair Restoration

Written by Eveline Sep 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

PRP hair restoration is a non-surgical treatment that uses growth factors in your blood to stimulate hair growth. It is a popular option for those who are experiencing hair loss and want to restore their hair without undergoing surgery. However, one of the most common questions people have about PRP hair restoration is how much it costs.

Description

PRP hair restoration is a procedure that involves drawing a small amount of blood from your body and then using a centrifuge to separate the platelets and growth factors from the rest of the blood. The resulting plasma is then injected into the scalp where hair growth is desired. The growth factors in the plasma help to stimulate the growth of new hair follicles, resulting in thicker, fuller hair.

How To

The PRP hair restoration process typically involves the following steps:

  1. A small amount of blood is drawn from your body.
  2. The blood is placed in a centrifuge to separate the platelets and growth factors from the rest of the blood.
  3. The resulting plasma is injected into the scalp where hair growth is desired.
  4. The entire process takes about 30 minutes.

Step by Step

The step-by-step process of PRP hair restoration is as follows:

  1. The hair is washed and dried.
  2. A numbing cream is applied to the scalp to minimize discomfort during the procedure.
  3. A small amount of blood is drawn from the patient's arm.
  4. The blood is placed in a centrifuge and spun to separate the platelets and growth factors from the rest of the blood.
  5. The resulting plasma is injected into the scalp using a thin needle.
  6. The entire process takes about 30 minutes.

Tips

If you are considering PRP hair restoration, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Make sure you choose a reputable provider who has experience with PRP hair restoration.
  • Be prepared for multiple treatments. Most people need several treatments spaced out over several months to achieve the desired results.
  • Follow your provider's aftercare instructions carefully to ensure the best possible results.

FAQ

Here are some of the most frequently asked questions about PRP hair restoration:

How much does PRP hair restoration cost?

The cost of PRP hair restoration varies depending on several factors, including the provider you choose, the number of treatments you need, and the severity of your hair loss. On average, you can expect to pay between $500 and $2,000 per treatment.

Is PRP hair restoration painful?

Most people experience only mild discomfort during the PRP hair restoration procedure. A numbing cream is applied to the scalp beforehand to minimize discomfort.

How long does it take to see results from PRP hair restoration?

Most people begin to see results from PRP hair restoration after the second or third treatment. However, it can take up to six months to see the full results.

Hair Care

After your PRP hair restoration treatment, it is important to take good care of your hair to ensure the best possible results. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as hair dyes and relaxers.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that support healthy hair growth.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are free of sulfates and other harsh chemicals.

Overall, PRP hair restoration can be an effective way to restore your hair without undergoing surgery. While the cost can vary, most people find that the results are worth the investment. If you are interested in PRP hair restoration, be sure to do your research and choose a reputable provider who has experience with the procedure.
