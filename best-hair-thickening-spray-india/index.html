The Best Hair Thickening Spray In India

Written by Robby Jun 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are looking for a quick and easy way to add volume and thickness to your hair, then a hair thickening spray is the perfect solution. With so many options available in the market, it can be tough to choose the best hair thickening spray in India that suits your hair type and budget. In this article, we will guide you through the process of choosing the best hair thickening spray in India and provide you with some tips on how to use it effectively.

How to Choose the Best Hair Thickening Spray in India?

When choosing a hair thickening spray, you need to consider the following factors:

Type of Hair

The type of hair you have plays a crucial role in choosing the right hair thickening spray. If you have fine hair, then you need a spray that adds volume without weighing your hair down. For thick hair, you need a spray that adds texture and definition.

Ingredients

The ingredients used in the hair thickening spray can make a big difference in the results. Look for sprays that contain natural ingredients like keratin, biotin, and collagen, which help to strengthen and thicken the hair.

Hold Strength

The hold strength of the spray determines how long the hair will stay in place. If you have fine hair, then you need a light hold spray, whereas if you have thick hair, you need a strong hold spray.

Steps to Use Hair Thickening Spray

Here are the steps to use hair thickening spray:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any product buildup and prepare your hair for the spray.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Do not rub your hair vigorously as this can cause damage.

Step 3: Shake the Spray Can

Before using the spray, shake the can well to ensure that the ingredients are well mixed.

Step 4: Apply the Spray

Hold the can about 6 inches away from your hair and spray evenly. Focus on the roots and mid-lengths of your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Use a hair dryer and a round brush to style your hair as desired. The spray will help to add volume and thickness to your hair.

Tips for Using Hair Thickening Spray

Here are some tips to help you get the best results from your hair thickening spray:

Tip 1: Use on Damp Hair

For best results, use the spray on damp hair as it will be easier to distribute the product evenly.

Tip 2: Don't Overuse

Using too much of the spray can make your hair look greasy and weighed down. Start with a small amount and build up if needed.

Tip 3: Use a Round Brush

Using a round brush while blow-drying your hair can help to add more volume and texture.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q. Can hair thickening spray damage my hair?

A. Hair thickening sprays are generally safe to use and do not cause any damage to the hair. However, it is important to choose a spray that contains natural ingredients and does not contain harsh chemicals.

Q. How often can I use hair thickening spray?

A. You can use hair thickening spray as often as you like. However, it is important to wash your hair regularly to remove any product buildup.

Q. Can hair thickening spray be used on all hair types?

A. Yes, hair thickening sprays can be used on all hair types. However, you need to choose a spray that is suitable for your hair type.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips that can help to keep your hair healthy:

Tip 1: Use a Heat Protectant

Using a heat protectant before blow-drying or using hot tools can help to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 2: Avoid Over-washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause damage. Wash your hair 2-3 times a week.

Tip 3: Use a Silk Pillowcase

Silk pillowcases can help to reduce friction and prevent hair breakage while you sleep.

In conclusion, choosing the best hair thickening spray in India is all about understanding your hair type, ingredients, and hold strength. Follow the steps and tips mentioned in this article to achieve thicker and fuller hair.
