How To Refresh Your Lace Front Wig Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jul 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a wig-wearer, you know how important it is to keep your wig looking fresh and new. A lace front wig is a popular choice for those who want a natural-looking hairline. However, over time, your wig may start to look a little worn and tired. In this blog post, we'll go over some tips and tricks on how to refresh your lace front wig so it looks like new again.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Refresh Your Lace Front Wig
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Your Lace Front Wig

How to Refresh Your Lace Front Wig

Before we get started, it's important to note that you should only refresh your wig if it's made of human hair. Synthetic wigs cannot be refreshed in the same way and may be permanently damaged if you try.

Here's what you'll need:

  • Wide-tooth comb or wig brush
  • Mild shampoo
  • Conditioner
  • Leave-in conditioner or detangler spray
  • Wig stand or mannequin head

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to refresh your lace front wig:

Step 1: Detangle

Using a wide-tooth comb or wig brush, gently comb through any tangles or knots in your wig. Be careful not to pull too hard, as this can damage the hair.

Step 2: Shampoo

Fill a sink or basin with lukewarm water and add a small amount of mild shampoo. Swish the wig around in the water to distribute the shampoo, then rinse thoroughly with cool water. Be careful not to rub or scrub the wig, as this can cause tangling.

Step 3: Condition

Apply a generous amount of conditioner to the wig, focusing on the ends of the hair. Let the conditioner sit for a few minutes, then rinse thoroughly with cool water.

Step 4: Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner or detangler spray to the wig, focusing on the ends of the hair. This will help to prevent tangling and keep the hair looking smooth and shiny.

Step 5: Dry

Place the wig on a wig stand or mannequin head and allow it to air dry completely. Avoid using a hair dryer, as this can damage the hair.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for refreshing your lace front wig:

  • Don't use hot water or a hair dryer to dry your wig, as this can damage the hair.
  • Be gentle when combing or brushing your wig, as excessive force can cause hair to fall out.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head when not in use to help maintain its shape.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products or heat tools on your wig, as these can damage the hair.

FAQs

Can I refresh a synthetic wig?

No, synthetic wigs cannot be refreshed in the same way as human hair wigs. Attempting to do so may cause permanent damage to the wig.

How often should I refresh my wig?

It depends on how often you wear your wig and how much maintenance it requires. Generally, you should refresh your wig every 4-6 weeks.

Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my wig?

It's best to use a mild shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for wigs to avoid damaging the hair.

Hair Care for Your Lace Front Wig

Maintaining your lace front wig is important to keep it looking fresh and new. Here are some tips for caring for your wig:

  • Wash your wig every 4-6 weeks or as needed, depending on how often you wear it.
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can damage the hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to detangle your wig, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head when not in use to help maintain its shape.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products or heat tools on your wig, as these can damage the hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can refresh your lace front wig and keep it looking like new. Remember to be gentle when handling your wig and use only mild products specifically designed for wigs. With proper care, your wig can last for months or even years.
