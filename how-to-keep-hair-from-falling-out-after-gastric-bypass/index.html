How To Keep Hair From Falling Out After Gastric Bypass

Written by Christine Jun 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you worried about hair loss after undergoing gastric bypass surgery? You're not alone. Hair loss is a common side effect of this weight loss surgery. But don't worry, there are ways to prevent it from happening. In this article, we'll share some tips on how to keep your hair from falling out after gastric bypass.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Gastric bypass surgery is a weight loss surgery that involves reducing the size of the stomach and rerouting the small intestine. This surgery helps people lose weight by limiting the amount of food they can eat and reducing the absorption of nutrients in the body. However, this surgery can also have some side effects, including hair loss.

How to

There are several things you can do to prevent hair loss after gastric bypass surgery:

Step by Step

  1. Make sure you're getting enough protein. Hair is made up of protein, so it's important to consume enough protein in your diet to keep your hair healthy. After gastric bypass surgery, your body may have trouble absorbing protein, so it's important to talk to your doctor or dietitian about how much protein you need.
  2. Take a multivitamin. After gastric bypass surgery, your body may have trouble absorbing vitamins and minerals. Taking a daily multivitamin can help ensure you're getting the nutrients you need to keep your hair healthy.
  3. Avoid crash diets. Losing weight too quickly can stress your body and cause hair loss. It's important to lose weight at a gradual pace to prevent this from happening.
  4. Be gentle with your hair. Avoid using harsh chemicals or styling products that can damage your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush to prevent pulling on your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep your hair healthy after gastric bypass surgery:

  • Stay hydrated. Drinking plenty of water can help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Get enough sleep. Lack of sleep can cause stress on your body, which can lead to hair loss.
  • Manage your stress. Stress can also cause hair loss, so it's important to find healthy ways to manage it, such as through exercise or meditation.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair loss after gastric bypass surgery:

Q: Is hair loss after gastric bypass surgery permanent?

A: No, hair loss after gastric bypass surgery is usually temporary and will grow back once your body adjusts to the changes.

Q: How long does it take for hair to grow back after gastric bypass surgery?

A: It can take several months for hair to grow back after gastric bypass surgery. However, it may take up to a year for your hair to return to its pre-surgery thickness.

Hair Care

Here are some additional tips for caring for your hair after gastric bypass surgery:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Massage your scalp to promote hair growth.

By following these tips, you can help prevent hair loss after gastric bypass surgery and keep your hair healthy and strong.
