How To Make Nappy Hair Curly For Guys

Written by Petter Jul 14, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of your nappy hair and want to spice it up with some curls? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we will be discussing how to make nappy hair curly for guys. From how to, step by step, tips, FAQ and hair care, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

  • Step by Step

How to Make Nappy Hair Curly for Guys

There are several methods to make nappy hair curly for guys. Here are a few:

1. Twist-Out Method

This method involves twisting small sections of hair and leaving them for a period of time before unraveling them. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream.
  3. Section your hair into small parts.
  4. Twist each section of hair tightly.
  5. Leave the twists in for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  6. Unravel the twists and finger-comb your hair.
  7. Apply a hair oil or serum to add shine.

Repeat this process every few days to maintain the curls.

2. Curl Sponge Method

The curl sponge method involves using a special sponge to create defined curls. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product.
  3. Take the sponge and rub it in a circular motion on your hair.
  4. Continue rubbing until you achieve the desired curl pattern.
  5. Apply a hair oil or serum to add shine.

Repeat this process every few days to maintain the curls.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make nappy hair curly for guys:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Select a curl-enhancing product that works for your hair type.
  3. Choose a method (twist-out or curl sponge) to create the curls.
  4. Follow the steps for your chosen method.
  5. Apply a hair oil or serum to add shine.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when making nappy hair curly for guys:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Choose a product that works for your hair type.
  • Don't use too much product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a satin or silk scarf or bonnet.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly to prevent dryness and breakage.

FAQ

Q: How long will the curls last?

A: The curls can last for a few days to a week depending on the method used and how well you maintain them.

Q: Can I use a hairdryer to create curls?

A: Yes, you can use a hairdryer with a diffuser attachment to create curls. Apply a curl-enhancing product and use the diffuser to dry your hair while scrunching it with your hands.

Q: Can I use a curling iron to create curls?

A: Yes, you can use a curling iron to create curls, but it's not recommended for nappy hair as it can cause heat damage.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain healthy curls. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools.
  • Moisturize your hair with a leave-in conditioner or oil.
  • Protect your hair at night with a satin or silk scarf or bonnet.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and methods, you can now make your nappy hair curly and rock a new look. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type. Remember to take care of your hair and enjoy your new curls!
