How To Hide Eyebrows With Makeup Without Glue

Written by Eveline Sep 09, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for a way to hide your eyebrows without using glue? Whether you want to create a new brow shape or disguise them for a special occasion, there are several makeup techniques you can use to achieve this look. In this article, we’ll show you how to hide eyebrows with makeup without glue, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Hide Eyebrows with Makeup Without Glue
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Success
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Brow Hiding

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with Clean Eyebrows

Before you begin, make sure your eyebrows are clean and free of any oils or residue. Use a gentle cleanser and pat dry with a towel.

Step 2: Apply a Concealer or Color Corrector

Using a small brush or your finger, apply a concealer or color corrector that matches your skin tone over your eyebrows. This will help to neutralize the color and create a blank canvas.

Step 3: Set with Powder

Next, use a translucent powder to set the concealer or color corrector. This will help to prevent smudging and keep your eyebrows concealed throughout the day.

Step 4: Outline Your New Brow Shape

Using a brow pencil or eyeliner, outline your new brow shape directly above your existing eyebrows. This will help to guide you as you apply your new brow color.

Step 5: Fill in Your Brows

Using a brow pomade, powder, or pencil, fill in your brows using short, light strokes. Make sure to stay within the lines of your new brow shape.

Step 6: Set Your Brows

Finally, use a clear brow gel or hairspray to set your new brow shape in place. This will help to keep them looking neat and polished throughout the day.

Tips for Success

  • Use a light hand when filling in your brows to avoid a heavy, unnatural look.
  • Choose a brow color that matches your hair color for a natural look.
  • Practice your new brow shape before committing to it for a special occasion.
  • Invest in a high-quality brow pomade or pencil for the best results.

FAQs

Can I use this technique for a long-term brow change?

While this technique can be used to create a new brow shape, it is not recommended for long-term brow changes. It is best to consult with a professional brow artist for permanent or semi-permanent brow options.

How long will my concealed brows last?

Your concealed brows should last throughout the day with proper setting and touch-ups as needed.

Can I use this technique on oily skin?

Yes, but be sure to use a primer or oil-absorbing product before applying your concealer or color corrector.

Hair Care for Brow Hiding

When hiding your eyebrows with makeup, it is important to take care of your hair to prevent damage or breakage. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot tools or harsh products on your hair. Additionally, be sure to remove your makeup thoroughly each night to prevent clogged pores and breakouts.

With these tips and techniques, you can successfully hide your eyebrows with makeup without glue. Whether you’re experimenting with a new brow shape or looking to disguise them for a special event, this technique is a great way to achieve a polished, flawless look.
