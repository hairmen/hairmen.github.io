How To Wear Wig On Bald Head A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you someone who is experiencing baldness and looking for an alternative to cover it up? Or maybe you want to try out a new look without committing to a haircut? Whatever your reason may be, wearing a wig on a bald head can be a great solution. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to wear a wig on a bald head. We will provide you with tips and tricks, answer your frequently asked questions, and give you insights on how to take care of your wig. Let’s get started!

How to

The first step in wearing a wig on a bald head is to make sure your head is clean and dry. This will help the wig to adhere better and last longer. You can use a mild shampoo and conditioner to wash your scalp and then pat it dry with a towel. Avoid using any hair products or oils as they can interfere with the wig’s adhesive.

Next, you need to choose the right wig for your head size and shape. Measure your head circumference and choose a wig that matches your size. You can also consult with a wig specialist to find the right fit for you. There are various types of wigs such as lace front, full lace, and synthetic wigs. Choose the one that suits your needs and preferences.

Once you have your wig, you need to prepare it for wearing. If it’s a synthetic wig, you can shake it out and put it on. If it’s a human hair wig, you can style it according to your liking. You can use a wig stand to hold the wig while you style it. Use a wig brush or comb to detangle the hair and create your desired look.

Now, it’s time to wear the wig. Start by placing the wig on your head from the front to the back. Adjust the wig until it fits snugly on your head. You can use clips to secure the wig in place. Make sure the wig is aligned with your natural hairline. You can use a mirror to ensure that the wig looks natural and realistic.

Lastly, you can style your wig according to your preferences. You can use heat styling tools such as curling irons or straighteners to create different hairstyles. Be gentle when styling your wig as you don’t want to damage the hair or the wig cap.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing a wig on a bald head:

  • Choose a wig with a breathable cap to prevent sweating and itching.
  • Use a wig cap to secure your natural hair and prevent it from showing through the wig.
  • Trim the wig to fit your face shape and style.
  • Wear a headband or scarf to accessorize and add extra security to the wig.

FAQ

Can I wear a wig if I have a sensitive scalp?

Yes, you can wear a wig even if you have a sensitive scalp. Choose a wig with a breathable cap and avoid using harsh chemicals or hair products that can irritate your scalp.

How often should I wash my wig?

You should wash your wig after 8-10 wears or when it starts to look dull or dirty. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner and gently wash the wig by soaking it in water and then rinsing it thoroughly. Avoid rubbing or twisting the wig as it can damage the hair.

Can I swim or shower with my wig on?

No, it’s not recommended to swim or shower with your wig on. Water can damage the wig’s adhesive and cause it to fall off. It’s best to remove the wig before swimming or showering.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the quality and longevity of your wig. Here are some tips to keep your wig in good condition:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin to prevent tangling and maintain its shape.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to direct sunlight or heat as it can damage the hair fibers.
  • Use a wig brush or comb to detangle the hair and style it gently.
  • Store your wig in a cool and dry place away from moisture and dust.

Now that you know how to wear a wig on a bald head, you can try out different hairstyles and looks without worrying about hair loss or commitment. Remember to choose the right wig, prepare it for wearing, and take care of it properly to enjoy its benefits for a long time. Happy wig wearing!
