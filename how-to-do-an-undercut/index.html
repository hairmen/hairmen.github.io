How To Do An Undercut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a fresh and trendy hairstyle? Look no further than the undercut. This popular hairstyle involves shaving the sides and back of the head while leaving the top longer. It's versatile, stylish, and perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look. In this guide, we'll show you how to do an undercut step by step.

Description

The undercut is a popular hairstyle that involves shaving the sides and back of the head while leaving the top longer. It's versatile, stylish, and perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look. The undercut can be worn by both men and women and can be customized to suit any face shape or personal style.

How to Do an Undercut

Step-by-Step Guide

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Here's how to do an undercut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Section off the hair on top of your head and clip it out of the way.
  3. Use the clippers to shave the sides and back of your head, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  4. Use scissors to trim the hair on top to your desired length.
  5. Blend the top and sides together using scissors or clippers.
  6. Style your hair as desired.

Congratulations, you now have an undercut!

Tips for a Perfect Undercut

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect undercut:

  • Choose a length that flatters your face shape.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a slicked-back undercut or a messy, textured look.
  • Maintain your undercut by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks.
  • Use a high-quality styling product to keep your hair in place.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice or recommendations.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about undercuts:

Q: Can anyone get an undercut?

A: Yes, anyone can get an undercut. However, it may not be the best hairstyle for those with very thick or curly hair.

Q: How long does an undercut take to grow out?

A: It can take anywhere from 3-6 months for an undercut to grow out completely. However, you can always get regular trims to help it grow out more evenly.

Q: Is an undercut easy to maintain?

A: Yes, an undercut is a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. However, you will need to get regular trims to maintain the shape and length.

Hair Care for Undercut

Here are some tips for taking care of your undercut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.
  • Get regular trims to keep your undercut looking sharp.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you're ready to rock a stylish and trendy undercut. Happy styling!
