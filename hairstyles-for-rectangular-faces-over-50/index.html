Hairstyles For Rectangular Faces Over 50 Tips Step By Step And Care

Table of Content: Description, How to, Step-by-Step, Tips, FAQ, Hair Care

Description

Rectangular faces have a longer shape with a prominent jawline, forehead, and cheekbones. Women over 50 with rectangular faces might find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that complements their features and age. The right hairstyle can accentuate the positive aspects of the face, add volume, and soften the lines. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on hairstyles for rectangular faces over 50, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care.

How to

Before choosing a hairstyle, you need to determine your face shape accurately. You can do this by pulling your hair back and analyzing the shape of your face. If you have a rectangular face, consider hairstyles that create volume on the sides of your face and add softness to your features. Look for hairstyles that have layers, waves, or curls. Avoid hairstyles that are too straight or blunt, as they can accentuate the squareness of your jawline.

Step-by-Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to style your hair for a rectangular face:

1. Start with clean hair

Wash and condition your hair according to your hair type. Use a hair mask to nourish and hydrate your hair. Rinse with cold water to close the cuticles and add shine.

2. Apply a heat protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant spray or cream to protect your hair from damage. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly.

3. Blow-dry your hair

Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, adding volume and movement to the ends. Lift the roots of your hair to add height to the crown area. Use a cool shot button to set the style.

4. Curl or wave your hair

Use a curling iron or a flat iron to create loose curls or waves. Curl the ends of your hair away from your face to add softness to your jawline. Use a hair spray to hold the curls in place.

5. Finish with a shine spray

Apply a shine spray or serum to add gloss and smoothness to your hair. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips on choosing the right hairstyle for rectangular faces over 50:

  • Consider a hairstyle that creates volume on the sides of your face, such as layers, waves, or curls.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too straight or blunt, as they can accentuate the squareness of your jawline.
  • Try a side-swept bang to soften your forehead and add asymmetry to your face.
  • Experiment with different hair lengths, from short bobs to long layers.
  • Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and eye color.

FAQ

What hairstyles are best for rectangular faces over 50?

Hairstyles that create volume on the sides of your face, such as layers, waves, or curls, are best for rectangular faces over 50. Avoid hairstyles that are too straight or blunt, as they can accentuate the squareness of your jawline.

How do I determine my face shape?

You can determine your face shape by pulling your hair back and analyzing the shape of your face. Rectangular faces have a longer shape with a prominent jawline, forehead, and cheekbones.

What hair color should I choose for a rectangular face?

You should choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and eye color. Consult a professional hairstylist for advice on the best hair color for you.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools, such as blow dryers, curling irons, or flat irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With the right hairstyle and hair care, you can enhance your features and look fabulous at any age. Experiment with different styles and colors to find the one that suits you best. Consult a professional hairstylist for personalized advice on hairstyles for rectangular faces over 50.
