How To Middle Part Hair Guys Asian A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you an Asian guy looking to rock a middle part hairstyle? Middle part hair for guys has been a popular trend in recent years, and it's not hard to see why. It's a versatile hairstyle that can work for both casual and formal occasions. If you're interested in trying this hairstyle out, but don't know where to start, this guide is for you. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to middle part hair guys Asian:

How to:

Step 1: Preparing Your Hair

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If you have fine hair, you might want to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair more body. If you have thick hair, you might want to use a smoothing shampoo and conditioner to tame your hair.


Step 2: Finding Your Part

The key to a good middle part hairstyle is finding the right part. The easiest way to do this is to use a comb. Start at the center of your hairline and comb your hair straight back. The point where your hair naturally falls is where you should part your hair.


Step 3: Creating Your Part

Once you've found your part, use the end of a comb to create a straight line down the center of your head. Make sure the line is even and straight.


Step 4: Styling Your Hair

Now it's time to style your hair. Use a hair dryer to blow dry your hair in the direction of your part. This will help set your part and give your hair volume. If you have curly or wavy hair, you might want to use a straightening iron to straighten your hair before blow drying.


Step 5: Finishing Touches

Once your hair is dry, use a little bit of hair wax or pomade to add some texture and hold to your hair. Take a small amount of product and rub it between your palms. Then, work it through your hair, focusing on the ends.


Tips:

- If you have a round face, you might want to avoid a middle part hairstyle. It can make your face look wider.

- If you have a long face, a middle part hairstyle can help balance out your features.

- Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.


FAQ:

Q: Can I middle part my hair if I have short hair?

A: Yes, you can still middle part your hair if you have short hair. Just make sure to keep your part small, so it doesn't look too severe.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type. If you have oily hair, you might want to wash your hair every day. If you have dry hair, you might only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Q: Can I middle part my hair if I have a receding hairline?

A: Yes, you can still middle part your hair if you have a receding hairline. Just make sure to keep your part small, so it doesn't draw attention to your hairline.


Hair Care:

To keep your middle part hairstyle looking its best, make sure to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

- Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.

- Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.

- Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.


With these tips and steps, you can confidently rock a middle part hairstyle as an Asian guy. Remember to experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the look that works best for you. Good luck!
