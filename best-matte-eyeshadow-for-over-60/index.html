Best Matte Eyeshadow For Over 60 A Guide To Flawless Eye Makeup

Written by William Aug 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As we age, our skin changes, and so do our makeup needs. One area that can be particularly tricky is our eyes. With wrinkles and fine lines, it can be challenging to find the right eyeshadow that won't accentuate these features. That's where matte eyeshadows come in. They are perfect for mature skin as they create a smooth, crease-free finish that looks natural and elegant. In this article, we'll be exploring the best matte eyeshadows for over 60 and how to apply them to achieve a flawless look.

Description

Matte eyeshadows are perfect for older skin as they don't contain shimmer or glitter, which can highlight wrinkles and fine lines. They create a soft, natural-looking finish that's perfect for everyday wear. They come in a variety of shades, from neutral to bold, making them versatile and suitable for any occasion. However, finding the right matte eyeshadow can be challenging, as they vary in quality and pigmentation. That's why we've put together this guide to help you find the best matte eyeshadow for over 60.

How to Choose the Best Matte Eyeshadow for Over 60

Choosing the right matte eyeshadow can be overwhelming, especially with so many options available. Here are some tips to help you choose the best matte eyeshadow for your skin:

Consider Your Skin Tone

When choosing a matte eyeshadow, it's essential to consider your skin tone. If you have fair skin, go for shades like light browns, taupes, and pinks. For medium skin tones, opt for shades like warm browns, coppers, and oranges. If you have darker skin, go for deep browns, plums, and burgundies.

Choose the Right Texture

Matte eyeshadows come in different textures, from powders to creams. Powders are easier to apply and blend, making them perfect for beginners. Creams, on the other hand, are more pigmented and long-lasting, making them perfect for a bold look.

Check the Pigmentation

Good pigmentation is essential for a matte eyeshadow to show up on your skin. Look for eyeshadows with intense color payoff, as they will create a more vibrant look. Make sure to swatch the eyeshadow before purchasing to ensure it's pigmented enough.

Step by Step Guide to Applying Matte Eyeshadow

Now that you've found the perfect matte eyeshadow, it's time to apply it. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve a flawless look:

Step 1: Prime Your Eyes

Before applying eyeshadow, prime your eyes to create a smooth base. This will help your eyeshadow last longer and prevent creasing. Use a small amount of eyeshadow primer and apply it all over your eyelids.

Step 2: Apply the Base Shade

Apply the base shade all over your eyelids using a fluffy brush. This will create a neutral base for your eyeshadow look. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone.

Step 3: Define Your Crease

Using a smaller brush, apply a deeper shade to your crease to create definition. Blend the shade well to avoid harsh lines. This will add depth to your eyes and make them look bigger.

Step 4: Add Dimension

To create dimension, apply a lighter shade to the inner corners of your eyes and under your brow bone. This will make your eyes appear brighter and more awake.

Step 5: Line Your Eyes

Using a dark matte eyeshadow, line your eyes to create a subtle, natural-looking effect. Use a small brush to apply the eyeshadow along your lash line, starting from the outer corner and working your way in.

Step 6: Mascara

Finish off your look with mascara. Apply a few coats of mascara to your lashes to create volume and length. This will make your eyes appear more defined and complete your look.

Tips for Applying Matte Eyeshadow

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect matte eyeshadow look:

  • Use a fluffy brush to blend your eyeshadow shades.
  • Apply a small amount of eyeshadow and build it up gradually to avoid a heavy look.
  • Use a light hand when applying eyeshadow to prevent fallout.
  • Always start with a primer to create a smooth base for your eyeshadow.
  • Use a dark matte eyeshadow to line your eyes for a natural-looking effect.

FAQ about Best Matte Eyeshadow for Over 60

Q: Can I wear shimmer eyeshadow if I'm over 60?

A: Shimmer eyeshadows can accentuate wrinkles and fine lines, making them unsuitable for mature skin. It's best to stick to matte eyeshadows for a more natural-looking finish.

Q: What are the best matte eyeshadow shades for over 60?

A: Neutral shades like taupe, brown, and pink are perfect for everyday wear. For a bolder look, try shades like burgundy, plum, and navy.

Q: How can I prevent my eyeshadow from creasing?

A: Use an eyeshadow primer to create a smooth base for your eyeshadow. Apply a small amount of primer all over your eyelids before applying eyeshadow.

Hair Care for Over 60

As we age, our hair also changes. It becomes thinner, drier, and more brittle. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair if you're over 60:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and flat irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals like hair dyes and bleaches, as they can weaken your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

Conclusion

Matte eyeshadows are perfect for mature skin as they create a smooth, natural-looking finish that won't accentuate wrinkles and fine lines. When choosing a matte eyeshadow, consider your skin tone, texture, and pigmentation. Apply your eyeshadow using a light hand and blend well to avoid a heavy look. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve a flawless matte eyeshadow look that's perfect for any occasion.
