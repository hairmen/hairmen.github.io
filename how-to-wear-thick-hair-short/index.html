How To Wear Thick Hair Short A Relaxed Guide

Are you tired of dealing with long, thick hair that takes forever to style? If so, it might be time to consider cutting your hair short. Not only will a shorter cut be easier to manage, but it can also help you feel more confident and refreshed.

Description

Thick hair can be difficult to manage, especially when it's long. It can take hours to style and can be heavy and hot in the summer. By cutting your hair short, you can make it more manageable and easier to style. Plus, short hair can be incredibly stylish and trendy.

How to Cut Thick Hair Short

If you're ready to make the chop, there are a few things you should consider before heading to the salon. First, think about the shape of your face and what type of haircut would flatter your features. Second, consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair each day. Finally, think about the texture of your hair and what style would work best with it.

Types of Short Haircuts for Thick Hair

There are several types of short haircuts that work well for thick hair, including:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob
  • Layered cut
  • Asymmetrical cut

Step-by-Step Guide

Once you've decided on a short haircut, it's time to head to the salon. Here's what you can expect during the process:

  1. Consult with your stylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Wash and dry your hair.
  3. Section your hair into manageable sections.
  4. Cut your hair to the desired length.
  5. Style your hair using a blow dryer, flat iron, or curling iron.
  6. Apply hair product to help hold your style.
  7. Enjoy your new short haircut!

Tips for Styling Short, Thick Hair

Styling short, thick hair can be easy with these tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to create waves or curls.
  • Apply a styling product to help hold your style in place.
  • Accessorize with headbands, clips, or barrettes to add interest to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

Yes! You can still wear your hair up with a short haircut. Try using bobby pins or hair clips to secure your hair in place.

How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

You should get your short haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

What hair products should I use for short, thick hair?

You should use hair products that are designed for your hair type, such as volumizing mousse, texturizing spray, or pomade.

Hair Care for Short, Thick Hair

To keep your short, thick hair healthy and shiny, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these tips and tricks, you can rock a short haircut with confidence and ease. Say goodbye to long, heavy hair and hello to a fresh, new look!
