Mens Hairstyles Fade Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish haircut, look no further than the mens hairstyles fade sides long top. This trendy haircut combines a short fade on the sides with longer hair on top, creating a look that's perfect for any occasion. In this guide, we'll show you how to get the perfect fade sides long top hairstyle, along with some tips and tricks for maintaining it.

Description

The mens hairstyles fade sides long top is a haircut that's been popular for several years now. This haircut features short hair on the sides that gradually fades into longer hair on top. The longer hair can be styled in a variety of ways, from a slicked-back look to a messy, textured style.

How to Get the Look

Getting the mens hairstyles fade sides long top haircut is a bit more complicated than a traditional haircut, but it's still easily achievable. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step is to decide on the length of your hair. The sides should be shorter than the top, but the exact length is up to you. You can choose a short fade or a medium fade, depending on your preference.

Step 2: Cut the Sides

Next, your stylist will start cutting the sides of your hair. They'll use clippers to create a fade, gradually cutting the hair shorter as they move towards the top. The exact technique will depend on the style you're going for.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Once the sides are done, it's time to cut the top. Your stylist will use scissors to cut the hair to the length you want. They may also add layers or texture to the hair to create a more interesting look.

Step 4: Style the Hair

Finally, it's time to style your hair. You can use a variety of products, such as pomade or wax, to create the look you want. Experiment with different styles until you find one that works for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've got the mens hairstyles fade sides long top, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it looking clean and healthy. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.

Tip 2: Use the Right Products

Use the right products for your hair type. If you have thick hair, use a pomade or wax to keep it in place. If you have fine hair, use a lightweight product to avoid weighing it down.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh. Your stylist can trim the sides and top to keep the length and shape just right.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair every day. If your hair is dry or damaged, you may want to wash it less frequently.

Q: Can I style my hair in different ways?

A: Yes! The mens hairstyles fade sides long top is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different products and techniques to find a look that works for you.

Q: How long does the haircut last?

A: The mens hairstyles fade sides long top can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months, depending on how quickly your hair grows and how often you get trims.

Hair Care

Maintaining your mens hairstyles fade sides long top requires a little bit of hair care. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools, such as a blow dryer or flat iron. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

Tip 2: Don't Overbrush Your Hair

Brushing your hair too much can cause damage and breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead.

Tip 3: Use a Hair Mask

Use a deep conditioning hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Look for a mask that's specifically designed for your hair type.

Now that you know everything about the mens hairstyles fade sides long top, it's time to try it out for yourself. With the right cut and care, you'll have a stylish and versatile haircut that's perfect for any occasion.
