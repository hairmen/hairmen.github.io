How Long Does It Take To Start Dreads

Are you thinking about starting dreadlocks but wondering how long it will take to cultivate them? Starting dreadlocks is a big decision that requires patience, commitment, and the right techniques. In this article, we will discuss how long it takes to start dreads, the process involved, and tips to care for your hair to achieve healthy and long-lasting dreadlocks.

How to Start Dreads

Starting dreadlocks involves a process of allowing hair to grow and lock naturally. There are different methods to begin the locking process, including the backcombing technique, twist and rip, and the neglect method. The choice of method depends on your hair texture, length, and desired outcome.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to start dreadlocks using the backcombing technique:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any residue, dirt, or oils.
  2. Section your hair into small, equal parts using a comb or your fingers.
  3. Backcomb each section of hair from the root to the tip using a fine-toothed comb.
  4. Roll each section of hair between your palms in a clockwise motion to form a cylindrical shape.
  5. Secure each dreadlock with a hair elastic or clip.
  6. Allow your hair to dry naturally or use a hairdryer on a low heat setting.
  7. Remove the hair elastics or clips once your hair is completely dry.
  8. Use a crochet hook to tidy up any loose hair or frizz.
  9. Repeat the process every four to six weeks until your hair locks completely.

Tips for Starting Dreads

Here are some tips to help you start successful dreadlocks:

  • Be patient and allow your hair to lock naturally.
  • Avoid using wax, gel, or other products that can attract dirt and prevent locking.
  • Maintain a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.
  • Avoid frequent washing, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and frizz.
  • Protect your hair from harsh weather conditions and excessive heat.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about starting dreadlocks:

How long does it take to start dreadlocks?

The time it takes to start dreadlocks varies depending on your hair texture, length, and the method used. On average, it takes three to six months for hair to begin locking, and up to a year for mature dreadlocks.

Can I start dreadlocks with short hair?

Yes, you can start dreadlocks with short hair. However, the process may take longer, and you may need to wait for your hair to grow to a certain length before you can lock it.

Do I need to cut my hair to start dreadlocks?

No, you don't need to cut your hair to start dreadlocks. You can start the locking process with any hair length, texture, or type.

Hair Care for Dreadlocks

Once you have started your dreadlocks, proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy and long-lasting locks. Here are some tips on how to care for your dreadlocks:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo and avoid over-washing.
  • Use a light oil or spray to moisturize your hair and prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using heavy products or conditioners that can cause build-up and prevent locking.
  • Maintain your dreadlocks by palm-rolling, interlocking or crocheting to keep them neat and tidy.
  • Protect your hair from sun, wind, and water damage by wearing a hat or scarf.

In conclusion, starting dreadlocks is a personal choice that requires patience, commitment, and the right techniques. The time it takes to start dreadlocks varies depending on your hair type, texture, and the method used. By following the tips and care techniques outlined in this article, you can achieve healthy and long-lasting dreadlocks.
