How To Trim Your Own Hair Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 14, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking to trim your own hair yourself, you're in the right place. Whether you're looking to save money, can't make it to the salon, or just want to try something new, trimming your own hair can be a great way to change up your look.

Description

Trimming your own hair can be a bit intimidating at first, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve great results. This step-by-step guide will walk you through the process, from choosing the right tools to finishing touches.

How to Trim Your Own Hair Yourself

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, you'll need to gather a few tools. You'll need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a mirror. If you have long hair, you may also want to use hair clips to keep your hair out of the way while you work.

Step 2: Choose Your Style

Before you start cutting, you'll need to decide on the style you want. Look for inspiration online or in magazines, and choose a style that you feel confident you can achieve.

Step 3: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you begin cutting, it's important to start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to see what you're doing and ensure that your hair is evenly cut.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use your comb to section your hair into manageable sections. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you don't miss any spots.

Step 5: Start Cutting

Begin by trimming the ends of your hair. Take small sections at a time and trim them evenly. Be sure to cut at an angle, rather than straight across, to create a more natural look.

Step 6: Check Your Progress

As you cut, be sure to check your progress in the mirror. This will help you ensure that your hair is even and that you're achieving the style you want.

Step 7: Finish Up

Once you've trimmed all of your hair, use your comb to check for any stray hairs that need to be trimmed. You can also use your scissors to add texture or layers, if desired.

Tips

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up
  • Be patient and take your time
  • Don't cut too much at once
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Take breaks if you need to

FAQ

Can I trim my own hair if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can trim your own curly hair. Just be sure to cut your hair while it's dry and use a technique called "twist cutting" to ensure that your curls are evenly cut.

Can I trim my own hair if I have short hair?

Yes, you can trim your own short hair. Just be sure to use small, precise cuts and take your time to ensure that your hair is even.

Hair Care

After you've trimmed your hair, it's important to take care of it. Be sure to use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat tools too frequently. Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Trimming your own hair may seem daunting at first, but with these tips and techniques, you can achieve great results. Give it a try and see how it goes!
