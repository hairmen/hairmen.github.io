How To Make Straight Hair Messy Guys

Written by Daniel Oct 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of having perfectly straight hair? Do you want to give your hair a messy, tousled look that is both stylish and easy to maintain? In this article, we'll show you how to make straight hair messy guys. From the right haircut to styling techniques, we'll cover everything you need to know to achieve the perfect messy hairstyle.

Description

Messy hair is a popular look for men right now, and it's easy to see why. It's a low-maintenance style that looks effortlessly cool. But if you have naturally straight hair, it can be challenging to achieve the messy look without looking unkempt. That's where this guide comes in. We'll show you how to create a messy hairstyle that looks intentional and stylish.

How to

Step 1: Get the Right Haircut

The first step to achieving a messy hairstyle is to get the right haircut. You'll want a cut that is long enough to be styled but short enough to avoid looking too messy. A layered cut is a great choice, as it will add texture and movement to your hair. Talk to your stylist about what type of cut would work best for your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

The key to a messy hairstyle is using the right products. You'll want to use products that add texture and volume to your hair. A texturizing spray or a sea salt spray is a great choice. Apply the product to damp hair and scrunch it with your fingers to create a tousled look. You can also use a matte styling paste to add definition to your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Next, use a blow dryer to dry your hair. Use your fingers to tousle your hair as you dry it. This will help create a messy, undone look. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to add volume to your hair.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your look with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold your style in place without making it look too stiff. Be sure to choose a flexible hold hairspray so that your hair still has movement.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you achieve the perfect messy hairstyle:

  • Don't overdo it with the product. Too much product can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Use your fingers to style your hair. A comb or brush will make your hair look too neat.
  • Experiment with different partings. A messy hairstyle looks great with a side part or a center part.
  • Don't be afraid to leave some pieces of hair sticking out. This will add to the messy, undone look.

FAQ

Can I achieve a messy hairstyle with short hair?

Yes, you can! Just be sure to get the right cut and use the right products to add texture and volume to your hair.

Do I need to wash my hair every day to achieve a messy hairstyle?

No, you don't. In fact, washing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils and make it harder to style. Try washing your hair every other day and using a dry shampoo on the days in between.

Hair Care

To keep your messy hairstyle looking great, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often. Also, be sure to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

With the right haircut, styling products, and techniques, you can easily achieve a messy hairstyle that looks great on guys with straight hair. Just remember to keep it simple and don't overdo it with the product. With a little practice, you'll be able to create the perfect messy look in no time.
