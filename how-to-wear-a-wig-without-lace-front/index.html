How To Wear A Wig Without Lace Front

Written by Daniel Aug 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Wearing a wig is a great way to change up your hairstyle or cover up hair loss, but not everyone wants or needs a lace front wig. In fact, many people prefer wigs without lace fronts for a more natural look or for ease of wear. In this article, we’ll go over how to wear a wig without lace front and give you some tips for a comfortable and secure fit.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Wig Without Lace Front
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Wear a Wig Without Lace Front

First, let’s go over what a lace front wig is. A lace front wig has a sheer lace panel along the front hairline. This panel is designed to blend in with your skin tone and give the illusion of a natural hairline. However, not everyone needs or wants this feature. If you have a wig without a lace front, here’s how to wear it:

1. Prep Your Hair

Before you put on your wig, make sure your hair is clean and flat. If your hair is long, braid it or put it in a low bun to keep it from getting in the way. If your hair is short, you can use a wig cap to keep it flat.

2. Put on the Wig Cap

A wig cap is a stretchy cap that fits snugly over your head. It helps keep your hair flat and provides a base for the wig to sit on. To put on the wig cap, simply stretch it over your head and tuck in any loose hairs.

3. Adjust the Wig

Put the wig on over the wig cap, starting at the front and working your way back. Adjust the wig so that it sits comfortably on your head, covering the wig cap completely. Use the ear tabs to position the wig correctly and make sure it’s straight.

4. Secure the Wig

Most wigs without lace fronts come with adjustable straps at the back. Use these straps to tighten the wig and make it more secure. You can also use bobby pins to secure the wig to your hair or wig cap.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide to wearing a wig without lace front:

  1. Prep your hair by washing and flatting it
  2. Put on a wig cap
  3. Put on the wig, starting at the front and working your way back
  4. Adjust the wig so that it sits comfortably on your head
  5. Use the ear tabs to position the wig correctly
  6. Use the adjustable straps at the back to tighten the wig
  7. Use bobby pins to secure the wig to your hair or wig cap

Tips

Here are some tips to help you wear a wig without lace front:

  • Use a wig cap to keep your hair flat
  • Adjust the wig so that it sits comfortably on your head
  • Use bobby pins to secure the wig to your hair or wig cap
  • Consider using hair clips to keep the wig in place
  • If the wig feels too tight, use the adjustable straps to loosen it

FAQ

Do I need to use a wig cap?

While you don’t need to use a wig cap, it can help keep your hair flat and make the wig more comfortable to wear.

How do I make sure the wig stays in place?

You can use adjustable straps, bobby pins, and hair clips to keep the wig in place. Make sure it feels comfortable and secure before you go out.

Hair Care

To keep your wig looking great, it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your wig every 6-8 wears
  • Use a wig shampoo and conditioner
  • Let the wig air dry
  • Use a wig stand to store the wig when you’re not wearing it

With these tips, you should be able to wear a wig without lace front comfortably and confidently. Remember to take care of your wig properly to keep it looking great for longer.
