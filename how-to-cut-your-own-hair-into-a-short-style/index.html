How To Cut Your Own Hair Into A Short Style A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Nov 29, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking to cut your own hair into a short style, you might be feeling a little intimidated. After all, cutting your own hair can be daunting, especially if you’ve never done it before. But fear not! With a little patience and some helpful tips, you can achieve a stylish and trendy short haircut from the comfort of your own home.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Cut Your Own Hair into a Short Style
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair
  4. FAQs

How to Cut Your Own Hair into a Short Style

Before you start cutting your hair, it’s important to have a plan in place. Think about what kind of short style you want to achieve, and gather the necessary tools. You’ll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Mirror

Once you have your tools ready, it’s time to get started.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your own hair into a short style:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, it will be harder to see where you’re cutting.
  2. Section off the hair at the crown of your head and clip it up.
  3. Using your comb, create a guide for your cut by parting your hair at the back of your head and cutting along the guide.
  4. Release the clipped hair and create a guide for the sides of your hair by parting your hair at the temples and cutting along the guide.
  5. Using your clippers (if you have them), trim the back and sides of your hair to your desired length.
  6. Use your scissors to trim the top of your hair, using your fingers to hold the hair and cut it at an angle for added texture.
  7. Blend the top and sides of your hair together using your scissors.
  8. Check the back of your hair using a hand mirror or a second mirror to make sure it’s even.
  9. Make any final adjustments and trim any stray hairs.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind as you cut your own hair:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more hair off, but you can’t put it back on!
  • Don’t rush. Take your time and be patient.
  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to cut.
  • Don’t cut your hair too short. It’s better to start with a longer length and work your way down.
  • Watch YouTube tutorials for inspiration and guidance.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair into a short style:

Q: Can I cut my own hair if I’ve never done it before?
A: Yes, but start with small cuts and be patient. Q: Do I need clippers to cut my own hair?
A: No, but they can be helpful for trimming the back and sides. Q: How often should I cut my own hair?
A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is a good rule of thumb.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you’ve cut your own hair into a short style, it’s important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet to prevent breakage.

Cutting your own hair into a short style can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and a little patience, you can achieve a stylish and trendy haircut without leaving your house. Just remember to start with small cuts, be patient, and take care of your hair afterwards!
