I Look Younger With My Hair Up The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're like most women, you've probably tried every trick in the book to look younger. From anti-aging creams to botox injections, there's no shortage of options when it comes to turning back the clock. But did you know that one of the simplest ways to take years off your appearance is by simply putting your hair up? Yes, that's right - I look younger with my hair up, and so can you! In this comprehensive guide, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect updo, answer some common questions, and offer some helpful tips to keep your hair looking its best.

Why Putting Your Hair Up Makes You Look Younger

There are several reasons why wearing your hair up can make you look younger. First, it exposes your face, which can help to highlight your best features and draw attention away from any wrinkles or imperfections. Second, it creates the illusion of a longer neck, which is a classic sign of youth and vitality. Finally, an updo can help to lift your face, giving you a more youthful, refreshed appearance.

How to Achieve the Perfect Updo

Now that you know why putting your hair up can make you look younger, let's talk about how to achieve the perfect updo. Whether you're going for a sleek bun or a messy topknot, there are a few key steps to follow:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you start styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help your updo to stay in place and prevent any frizz or flyaways. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you might want to consider straightening it before you begin.

Step 2: Choose your style

Next, decide on the type of updo you want to create. There are countless options to choose from, so take some time to browse through Pinterest or Instagram for inspiration. Some popular styles include:

  • Sleek bun
  • Messy topknot
  • French twist
  • Braided updo

Step 3: Gather your tools

Once you've chosen your style, gather all the tools you'll need to create your updo. This might include bobby pins, hair ties, a comb, and hairspray.

Step 4: Start styling

Now it's time to start styling! Depending on the type of updo you're going for, you might need to tease your hair, twist it into a bun, or braid it into a crown. Take your time and don't be afraid to experiment until you find a style that you love.

Tips for Keeping Your Hair Looking its Best

Now that you know how to achieve the perfect updo, here are a few tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage your strands and lead to breakage.
  • Invest in a good hairspray or styling product to help your updo stay in place all day.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions that women ask about wearing their hair up:

Q: Will wearing my hair up damage it?

A: Wearing your hair up occasionally is unlikely to cause any damage. However, if you wear your hair up too tightly or too often, it can lead to breakage and hair loss.

Q: What if I have short hair?

A: If you have short hair, you can still achieve an updo by using bobby pins and hair ties to create a faux bob or a sleek pixie cut.

Q: Can I wear my hair up for formal occasions?

A: Absolutely! A chic updo is a classic choice for weddings, proms, and other formal events.

Hair Care in 2023

In 2023, hair care will continue to be a top priority for women of all ages. From natural, organic products to cutting-edge styling tools, there will be no shortage of options when it comes to keeping your hair looking its best. Whether you prefer a sleek, polished look or a more relaxed, bohemian vibe, there will be a hair care regimen that's perfect for you. So go ahead and experiment with different styles, and remember - I look younger with my hair up, and so can you!
