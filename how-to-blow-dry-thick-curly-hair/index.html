How To Blow Dry Thick Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 10, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're someone with thick curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to style it. One of the most common hairstyle choices for those with curly hair is to blow dry it. But, how do you go about blow drying thick curly hair? In this article, we'll show you how to blow dry your hair step-by-step, along with some tips and FAQs to help you achieve the best results.

How to Blow Dry Thick Curly Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's first understand the basics of blow drying thick curly hair. The key to achieving the best results is to use the right tools and techniques. Here are some tools you'll need:

  • Blow dryer
  • Diffuser attachment
  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hair serum or oil

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to blow dry your thick curly hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while it's still wet.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from the heat of the blow dryer.
  4. Attach the diffuser to your blow dryer.
  5. Flip your head upside down and place your hair into the diffuser.
  6. Turn on the blow dryer on a low setting and start drying your hair.
  7. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair as you blow dry it.
  8. Once your hair is about 80% dry, flip your head back up and use a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.
  9. Continue blow drying your hair until it's completely dry.
  10. Finish off with a blast of cool air to set your style.

Voila! You now have beautifully blow-dried hair.

Tips for Blow Drying Thick Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when blow drying your thick curly hair:

  • Always use a diffuser attachment to prevent your curls from getting frizzy.
  • Use a low heat setting to prevent your hair from getting damaged.
  • Never blow dry your hair when it's soaking wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Apply a hair serum or oil after blow drying to add shine and reduce frizz.
  • Don't touch your hair too much while blow drying, as this can cause frizz.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about blow drying thick curly hair:

Q: How often should I blow dry my hair?

A: It's best to limit blow drying to once or twice a week to prevent your hair from getting damaged.

Q: Can I blow dry my hair without a diffuser?

A: While you can blow dry your hair without a diffuser, it's not recommended for those with curly hair as it can cause frizz.

Q: Should I use a heat protectant spray?

A: Yes, always use a heat protectant spray to prevent your hair from getting damaged from the heat of the blow dryer.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind if you have thick curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautifully blow-dried hair that's healthy and full of life.
