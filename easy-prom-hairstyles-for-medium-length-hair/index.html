Easy Prom Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

If you have medium length hair, you might be wondering what kind of prom hairstyles you can rock. Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this blog post, we'll show you some easy prom hairstyles for medium length hair that you can do at home without needing a professional stylist. So, let's get started!

Description

Prom night is a special occasion that calls for a perfect hairstyle. If you have medium length hair, you might be wondering what kind of hairstyle you can do. There are many easy prom hairstyles that you can do at home without needing a professional stylist. These hairstyles are perfect for medium length hair and will make you look stunning on your prom night.

How To

Before we start with the hairstyles, it is important to prepare your hair properly. Wash your hair the day before prom and use a moisturizing conditioner. Blow dry your hair and use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage. Now, let's move on to the hairstyles.

The Half-Up Half-Down Hairstyle

The half-up half-down hairstyle is a classic prom hairstyle that works well for medium length hair. Start by sectioning off the top half of your hair and securing it with a hair tie or clip. You can leave some hair out at the front to frame your face. You can either leave the bottom half of your hair straight or curl it with a curling iron. This hairstyle is simple yet elegant and will make you look stunning on prom night.

The Low Bun Hairstyle

The low bun hairstyle is perfect for a more sophisticated look. Start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail and securing it with a hair tie. Twist your hair around the hair tie to create a bun and secure it with bobby pins. You can leave some hair out at the front to frame your face. This hairstyle is perfect for medium length hair and will make you look elegant on your prom night.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions for the half-up half-down hairstyle and the low bun hairstyle.

Half-Up Half-Down Hairstyle

  1. Section off the top half of your hair and clip it up.
  2. Curl the bottom half of your hair with a curling iron.
  3. Take down the top section of your hair and tease the crown for volume.
  4. Pull the top half of your hair back and secure it with a hair tie or clip.
  5. Use bobby pins to secure any loose hair.

Low Bun Hairstyle

  1. Pull your hair back into a low ponytail.
  2. Twist your hair around the hair tie to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Leave some hair out at the front to frame your face.
  5. Use hairspray to keep your bun in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing your prom hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Use bobby pins to secure your hairstyle in place.
  • Don't overdo it with hairspray â you don't want your hair to look stiff.
  • Practice your hairstyle before prom night to make sure you get it right.

Hair Care

It is important to take good care of your hair before prom night. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair the day before prom.
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Get a trim a few weeks before prom to get rid of split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair â let your hair air dry whenever possible.
