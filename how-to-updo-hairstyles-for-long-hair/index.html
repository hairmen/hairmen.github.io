Description

Updo hairstyles for long hair are perfect for formal events, weddings, and even everyday wear. They are elegant, sophisticated, and can change your entire look. Whether you have straight, wavy or curly hair, there is an updo hairstyle that will suit you. In this article, we will share tips, tricks and a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect updo hairstyle for your long hair.

How to Updo Hairstyles for Long Hair

Before we get started with the step-by-step guide, here are some basic supplies you will need:

  • Bobby pins
  • Hair elastics
  • Hairspray
  • Texturizing spray
  • Comb or brush

Now let's get started on creating your perfect updo hairstyle.

Step 1: Choose Your Updo Hairstyle

There are countless updo hairstyles for long hair, from braids to buns, twists to knots. Choose a style that suits your hair type and the occasion you are attending. You can browse online for inspiration or consult with your hairstylist.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Apply a volumizing conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to style. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry to remove excess water.

Step 3: Apply Texturizing Spray

Apply texturizing spray to your hair to add grip and hold. This will help your hair stay in place throughout the day or night. Use your fingers to distribute the spray evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Brush Your Hair

Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to style your hair and create the perfect updo.

Step 5: Create Your Updo

Now it's time to create your updo hairstyle. Follow the step-by-step guide below for your chosen hairstyle.

Step by Step Guide

Braided Updo

  1. Brush your hair and gather it into a ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Braid your ponytail and secure the end with an elastic band.
  3. Wrap the braid around the base of the ponytail to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold the style.

Twisted Updo

  1. Brush your hair and gather it into a low ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a rope-like shape.
  3. Wrap the twisted ponytail around the base of the ponytail to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold the style.

Braided Crown Updo

  1. Part your hair down the middle and braid each side into a French braid.
  2. Wrap each braid over the top of your head like a crown.
  3. Secure the braids with bobby pins.
  4. Tuck the ends of the braids under the opposite braid and secure with bobby pins.
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold the style.

Tips for Perfect Updo Hairstyles

  • Use hair accessories such as hairpins or flowers to add a touch of elegance to your updo hairstyle.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and smoothness to your hair.
  • Experiment with different updo hairstyles to find the perfect one for you.
  • Practice your updo hairstyle before the event to ensure you are comfortable with the style.

FAQs

How long does it take to create an updo hairstyle?

The time it takes to create an updo hairstyle depends on the complexity of the style. Simple styles can take as little as ten minutes, while more intricate styles can take up to an hour.

Can I create an updo hairstyle on my own?

Yes, you can create an updo hairstyle on your own with practice and patience. Start with simple styles and work your way up to more complex styles.

Hair Care Tips for Updo Hairstyles

  • Avoid washing your hair on the same day as the event to ensure your hair has enough texture and grip to hold the style.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any product buildup from your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can create the perfect updo hairstyle for your long hair. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and accessories to make the look your own. Remember to take care of your hair before and after styling to keep it healthy and strong.
