How To Style Layered Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 14, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have layered curly hair, you’ve likely experienced the joys and challenges of managing your locks. While curly hair can be stunningly beautiful, it can also be a bit finicky when it comes to styling. But don’t worry, with the right techniques and tools, you can easily style your layered curly hair to perfection. In this article, we’ll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style your layered curly hair, along with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Layered Curly Hair

Styling layered curly hair can be a bit tricky, but with the right techniques and tools, you can achieve a beautiful, defined, and voluminous look.

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it’s important to note that different hair types and textures require different styling techniques. So, while the following steps may work for some, they may not work for everyone. It’s important to experiment and find what works best for your hair.

Step 1: Start with Clean, Conditioned Hair

The first step to styling your layered curly hair is to start with clean, conditioned hair. Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and a nourishing conditioner to cleanse your hair and provide it with moisture.

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz and damage.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Once your hair is damp, apply a styling product that works for your hair type and texture. This can be a mousse, gel, cream, or any other product that provides hold and definition.

Work the product through your hair from roots to tips, making sure to distribute it evenly. Be careful not to use too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and cause them to look greasy.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair into manageable sections. This will make it easier to style your curls and ensure that each section is evenly coated with product.

You can use clips, hair ties, or your fingers to section your hair. If you have a lot of hair, you may want to section it into four or more sections.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser

Now it’s time to dry your hair. If you have a diffuser attachment for your hair dryer, use it to dry your curls. A diffuser helps to distribute heat evenly and prevent frizz.

Start by placing a section of your hair into the diffuser and holding it in place for a few seconds. Then, move on to the next section, making sure to dry each section thoroughly before moving on to the next.

Step 5: Scrunch Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, scrunch your curls to help define them and add volume. You can do this with your fingers or a microfiber towel.

Gently scrunch each section of your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

Step 6: Finish with a Hair Oil or Serum

To add shine and smoothness to your curls, finish with a hair oil or serum. Apply a small amount to your palms and work it through your hair, focusing on the ends.

Be careful not to use too much oil or serum, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your layered curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, conditioned hair.
  2. Apply a styling product that works for your hair type and texture.
  3. Section your hair into manageable sections.
  4. Use a diffuser to dry your curls.
  5. Scrunch your hair to help define your curls and add volume.
  6. Finish with a hair oil or serum to add shine and smoothness.

Tips for Styling Layered Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your layered curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a nourishing conditioner to cleanse and moisturize your hair.
  • Be gentle when towel drying your hair to avoid frizz and damage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Section your hair into manageable sections to ensure that each section is evenly coated with product.
  • Use a diffuser to dry your curls and prevent frizz.
  • Scrunch your hair to help define your curls and add volume.
  • Finish with a hair oil or serum to add shine and smoothness.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my layered curly hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and texture. Some people with curly hair can go several days without washing their hair, while others may need to wash their hair every other day. Experiment to find what works best for your hair.

Q: Can I use a regular hair dryer to dry my curls?

A: Yes, you can use a regular hair dryer to dry your curls, but make sure to use a diffuser attachment to prevent frizz.

Q: How can I control frizz in my layered curly hair?

A: To control frizz, avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, use a diffuser when drying your hair, and finish with a hair oil or serum.

Hair Care for Layered Curly Hair

In addition to styling your hair, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a nourishing conditioner to cleanse and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to provide it with extra moisture.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily style your layered curly hair to perfection. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair, and don’t be afraid to have fun with your curls!
