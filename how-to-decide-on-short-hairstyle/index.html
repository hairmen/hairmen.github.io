How To Decide On Short Hairstyle A Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Nov 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing a new hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you're looking to go short. Short hair can be a bold and edgy statement, but it can also be versatile and low-maintenance. In this guide, we'll go over how to decide on a short hairstyle that suits your style and face shape. Whether you're looking for a pixie cut, bob, or something in between, we've got you covered.

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in deciding on a short hairstyle is to determine your face shape. Your face shape will help you decide which hairstyles will look best on you. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and long. To determine your face shape, look in the mirror and measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline.

If your face is round, a short bob or pixie cut can help elongate your face. If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any short hairstyle. Square faces look great with textured crops or layered pixie cuts. Heart-shaped faces look great with short, wispy bangs or chin-length bobs. Diamond-shaped faces look great with short, textured hairstyles with volume on top. If you have a long face, a short bob or textured pixie cut can help balance your features.

Consider Your Hair Type

Another factor to consider when deciding on a short hairstyle is your hair type. If you have thick hair, a layered bob or a short shag can help thin out your hair. If you have fine hair, a blunt bob or a pixie cut with lots of texture can help add volume. If you have curly hair, a short bob or pixie cut can help showcase your natural curls. If you have straight hair, a blunt bob or a sleek pixie cut can help accentuate your features.

Lifestyle Factors

Your lifestyle can also play a role in deciding on a short hairstyle. If you're someone who likes to wash and go, a short hairstyle can be low-maintenance and easy to style. If you're someone who likes to experiment with different looks, a short hairstyle can be versatile and easy to switch up. If you're someone who works in a professional setting, a short hairstyle can look polished and sophisticated.

Personal Style Preference

Ultimately, your personal style preference should be the deciding factor in choosing a short hairstyle. Do you want to make a bold statement with a dramatic pixie cut? Or do you prefer a more subtle look with a chin-length bob? Consider your personal style and what will make you feel confident and comfortable.

Consult with a Hairdresser

If you're unsure about which short hairstyle to choose, it's always a good idea to consult with a hairdresser. They can help you determine your face shape, hair type, and personal style to find a short hairstyle that suits you. They can also offer advice on how to style and maintain your new haircut.

Styling Tips for Short Hair

Styling short hair can be easy and fun. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Invest in quality styling products like texturizing spray, pomade, and hairspray.
  • Experiment with different partings and styling techniques to switch up your look.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural texture.
  • Accessorize with headbands, clips, and barrettes for a fun and stylish look.

FAQs

1. Will a short hairstyle suit me?

Short hairstyles can suit almost anyone, as long as you choose a style that complements your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

2. How often do I need to get a haircut with a short hairstyle?

Short hairstyles require more frequent haircuts to maintain their shape and style. You should plan on getting a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

3. Can I still style my short hair for special occasions?

Absolutely! Short hair can be styled in a variety of ways for special occasions, from sleek and sophisticated to bold and edgy.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires a different hair care routine than longer hair. Here are some tips to keep your short hair looking healthy:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to detangle your hair without causing damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your hairstyle and prevent split ends.

Choosing a short hairstyle can be a fun and exciting change. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find a short hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Remember to consult with a hairdresser if you're unsure, and have fun experimenting with different styling techniques!
