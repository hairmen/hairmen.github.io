Men s Hairstyle For Thinning Hair On Top Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Jul 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're a man with thinning hair on top, you're not alone. In fact, up to 85% of men will experience some degree of hair loss by the age of 50. But just because your hair is thinning doesn't mean you can't still have a great hairstyle. Here are some tips and tricks for men's hairstyle for thinning hair on top.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for thinning hair on top is to choose a style that complements your face shape. If you have a round face, you should avoid hairstyles that make your face look even rounder, such as a buzz cut. Instead, opt for a longer style that adds height to your hair, such as a messy top with short sides.

If you have a square face, you can pull off a buzz cut or a short, textured crop. For an oval face, you have more flexibility and can choose from a variety of styles.

Step-by-Step Guide

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle, here's how to get the look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as a wax or pomade, to your hair.
  3. Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair into the desired shape.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Thinning Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling thinning hair:

  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look even thinner.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair in the opposite direction from where it naturally falls to add volume.
  • Consider getting a shorter haircut, as shorter hair can create the illusion of thickness.
  • Embrace your thinning hair and consider going bald or shaving your head.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's hairstyle for thinning hair on top:

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have thinning hair on top?

A: Yes, you can still have long hair if you have thinning hair on top. However, you should avoid wearing your hair slicked back, as this can make your hairline more noticeable. Instead, opt for a messy, textured style that adds volume to your hair.

Q: Should I use hair extensions or a toupee to make my hair look thicker?

A: It's up to you, but keep in mind that hair extensions and toupees can be expensive and require maintenance. Plus, they may not look natural. Instead, consider embracing your thinning hair and choosing a hairstyle that complements your face shape.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips for men with thinning hair:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, as pulling on your hair can cause it to break.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a scalp sunscreen.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, iron, and vitamin C, which are all important for healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, men's hairstyle for thinning hair on top doesn't have to be a challenge. With the right hairstyle, styling tips, and hair care routine, you can look and feel your best.
