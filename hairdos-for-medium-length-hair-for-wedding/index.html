10 Stunning Hairdos For Medium Length Hair For Wedding

Written by Christine Jun 29, 2023 · 6 min read
10 Stunning Hairdos for Medium Length Hair for Wedding | Hair Care Tips

If you’re a bride-to-be with medium length hair, you know that finding the perfect wedding hairstyle can be a challenge. Luckily, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ve compiled a list of 10 stunning hairdos for medium length hair that are perfect for weddings. From classic updos to romantic braids, these hairstyles are sure to make you feel beautiful and confident on your big day.

1. Classic Updo

The classic updo is a timeless hairstyle that’s perfect for weddings. It’s simple, elegant, and it keeps your hair out of your face all day long. To create this hairstyle, start by pulling your hair into a low ponytail. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Finish the look by adding some hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips:

  • For a more romantic look, leave a few strands of hair loose around your face.
  • Accessorize with a beautiful hairpin or tiara for some added sparkle.

2. Romantic Braid

A romantic braid is a perfect hairstyle for a bohemian or rustic wedding. It’s simple, yet elegant, and it’s a great way to show off your medium length hair. To create this hairstyle, start by braiding your hair from the crown of your head down to the nape of your neck. Secure the braid with a clear elastic band and loosen it up for a more relaxed look.

Tips:

  • Add some fresh flowers or greenery to your braid for a more natural look.
  • For a more polished look, use a curling iron to add some loose waves to your hair before braiding it.

3. Half Up Half Down

The half up half down hairstyle is a classic look that’s perfect for medium length hair. It’s simple, yet elegant, and it’s a great way to show off your hair while still keeping it out of your face. To create this hairstyle, start by pulling the top half of your hair back and securing it with a clear elastic band. You can leave the bottom half of your hair down or add some loose waves for a more romantic look.

Tips:

  • Add some braids or twists to the top half of your hair for a more intricate look.
  • Accessorize with a flower crown or hairpin for a bohemian touch.

4. Chignon

A chignon is a classic hairstyle that’s perfect for weddings. It’s elegant, timeless, and it keeps your hair out of your face all day long. To create this hairstyle, start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Finish the look by adding some hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips:

  • Add some loose waves to your hair before creating the chignon for a more romantic look.
  • Accessorize with a beautiful hairpin or tiara for some added sparkle.

5. Vintage Waves

Vintage waves are a classic hairstyle that’s perfect for a vintage or retro-inspired wedding. It’s simple, yet elegant, and it’s a great way to show off your medium length hair. To create this hairstyle, start by parting your hair down the middle. Use a curling iron to create loose waves and finish the look by adding some hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips:

  • Add a deep side part and tuck one side behind your ear for a more elegant look.
  • Accessorize with a vintage hairpin or headband for a retro touch.

6. Side Swept

A side swept hairstyle is a simple and elegant look that’s perfect for a modern wedding. It’s a great way to show off your medium length hair and it’s easy to create. To create this hairstyle, simply sweep your hair to one side and secure it with bobby pins. You can leave your hair straight or add some loose waves for a more romantic look.

Tips:

  • Add some braids or twists to the side of your hair for a more intricate look.
  • Accessorize with a beautiful hairpin or statement earrings for some added sparkle.

7. Fishtail Braid

A fishtail braid is a trendy and elegant hairstyle that’s perfect for a beach or bohemian wedding. It’s a great way to show off your medium length hair and it’s easy to create. To create this hairstyle, start by dividing your hair into two sections. Take a small piece of hair from the outside of one section and cross it over to the inside of the other section. Repeat this process until you reach the end of your hair and secure the braid with a clear elastic band.

Tips:

  • Loosen up the braid for a more relaxed, bohemian look.
  • Add some fresh flowers or greenery to your braid for a more natural look.

8. Low Bun

A low bun is a simple and classic hairstyle that’s perfect for a formal wedding. It’s a great way to show off your medium length hair and it’s easy to create. To create this hairstyle, start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Finish the look by adding some hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips:

  • Add some braids or twists to the bun for a more intricate look.
  • Accessorize with a beautiful hairpin or tiara for some added sparkle.

9. Twisted Updo

A twisted updo is a modern and elegant hairstyle that’s perfect for a chic wedding. It’s a great way to show off your medium length hair and it’s easy to create. To create this hairstyle, start by twisting two sections of hair from the front of your head towards the back. Secure the twists with bobby pins and pull the rest of your hair back into a low bun. Finish the look by adding some hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips:

  • Loosen up the twists for a more relaxed look.
  • Accessorize with a beautiful hairpin or statement earrings for some added sparkle.

10. Boho Braid

A boho braid is a trendy and relaxed hairstyle that’s perfect for a bohemian wedding. It’s a great way to show off your medium length hair and it’s easy to create. To create this hairstyle, start by braiding your hair from the crown of your head down to the nape of your neck. Secure the braid with a clear elastic band and loosen it up for a more relaxed look.

Tips:

  • Add some fresh flowers or greenery to your braid for a more natural look.
  • Accessorize with a flower crown or hairpin for a bohemian touch.
