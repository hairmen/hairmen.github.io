How To Find Out What Hairstyle Looks Best On You

Written by Robby Oct 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of always wearing the same hairstyle and wondering if there's a better option out there? Figuring out what hairstyle suits you best can be overwhelming, but don't worry, we're here to help. In this article, we'll give you tips on how to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Considering Your Hair Type
  • Matching Your Personal Style

Determining Your Face Shape

Your face shape plays a big role in determining what hairstyles flatter you. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and measure the widest part of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

If all three measurements are about the same, you have an oval face. If your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you have a heart-shaped face. A round face has a wider forehead and rounded chin, while a square face has a strong jawline and a forehead of similar width. A diamond-shaped face has a narrow forehead and jawline with wide cheekbones, and an oblong face has a longer, narrow shape.

Considering Your Hair Type

Your hair type is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. Different hairstyles work better on certain hair types, so it's important to know yours. Here are some common hair types:

  • Straight
  • Wavy
  • Curly
  • Kinky

If you have straight hair, you can go for a sleek, straight look or add some waves for texture. Wavy hair looks great with layers or a beachy, tousled look. Curly hair can be worn in its natural state or styled into defined curls. Kinky hair can be styled into a variety of braids or twists.

Matching Your Personal Style

Your personal style is what sets you apart from everyone else, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that reflects that. Are you more of a classic, sophisticated type or do you prefer a more edgy, daring look? Do you like to wear your hair up or down? These are all questions to consider when choosing a hairstyle.

If you like to keep things simple, a classic bob or long layers might be a good choice. If you prefer a more daring look, consider a pixie cut or asymmetrical bob. If you like to wear your hair up, a messy bun or sleek ponytail might be your go-to.

Exploring Haircut Options

Now that you've determined your face shape, hair type, and personal style, it's time to explore different haircut options. Here are some popular hairstyles to consider:

  • Bob
  • Layered
  • Pixie
  • Bangs
  • Updo
  • Long and straight

Your hairstylist can help you determine which style will work best for you based on your hair type and face shape. Don't be afraid to bring in pictures of hairstyles you like to help give your stylist an idea of what you're looking for.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine

FAQ

How do I know if a hairstyle will look good on me?

Consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style when choosing a hairstyle. Your hairstylist can also offer suggestions based on your features.

What is the most flattering haircut?

The most flattering haircut is one that complements your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

What should I do if I don't like my new haircut?

If you're not happy with your new haircut, communicate with your hairstylist and let them know what you don't like. They may be able to make adjustments or offer suggestions for a different style.

How can I keep my hair healthy?

Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited for your hair type, avoid using hot tools too often, get regular trims, and protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.

With these tips, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features and suits your personal style. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
