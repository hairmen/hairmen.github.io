How To Do A 40 S Hair Roll A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you've ever wanted to achieve the classic, vintage look of the 1940s, then learning how to do a 40's hair roll is a must. This iconic hairstyle is perfect for special occasions or for everyday wear. In this blog post, we'll show you how to create the perfect roll, from start to finish.

Description of a 40's Hair Roll

A 40's hair roll is a classic hairstyle that was popular during the 1940s. It's a vintage look that's become iconic over the years. The hair is rolled up into a tight, cylindrical shape, usually at the front or side of the head. It's a versatile style that can be worn with any hair length and texture.

How to Do a 40's Hair Roll

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to do a 40's hair roll:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair, and then use a curling iron or hot rollers to create small curls all over your head.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into two parts, the front and the back. Use a comb to create a deep side part on the front section.

Step 3: Create the Roll

Take the front section of your hair and brush it back towards the crown of your head. Hold the hair in place with one hand, and then use the other hand to roll the hair up into a tight, cylindrical shape. Pin the roll in place with bobby pins.

Step 4: Secure the Roll

Use bobby pins to secure the roll in place. Make sure to tuck in any loose ends of hair.

Step 5: Style the Back

Take the back section of your hair and style it however you like. You can leave it down, or you can create more curls or waves.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish the look with a spritz of hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips for Creating the Perfect 40's Hair Roll

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect 40's hair roll:

  • Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create small curls all over your head.
  • Brush your hair back towards the crown of your head before creating the roll.
  • Use bobby pins to secure the roll in place.
  • Tuck in any loose ends of hair before securing the roll.
  • Style the back section of your hair however you like.
  • Finish the look with hairspray to keep everything in place.

FAQs About 40's Hair Rolls

Q: Can I do a 40's hair roll with short hair?

A: Yes, you can still create a 40's hair roll with short hair. You'll just need to use more bobby pins to secure the roll in place.

Q: Is a 40's hair roll difficult to create?

A: It can take some practice to get the hang of it, but with a little patience and some bobby pins, anyone can create a 40's hair roll.

Q: Can I wear a 40's hair roll for everyday wear?

A: Absolutely! A 40's hair roll is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn for special occasions or for everyday wear.

Hair Care for 40's Hair Rolls

To keep your 40's hair roll looking its best, make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools. Also, use a hairspray to keep everything in place. If you have dry or damaged hair, consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Now that you know how to do a 40's hair roll, you can create this iconic, vintage look whenever you want. Practice makes perfect, so don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with this classic hairstyle.
