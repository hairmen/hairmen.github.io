Wedding Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair With Tiara

Description

Shoulder length hair is a popular choice for brides on their wedding day. It is versatile, easy to style, and looks great with a tiara. A tiara is a beautiful accessory that can add an extra touch of elegance to your bridal look. In this article, we will explore different wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair with tiara, and provide you with some tips and advice on how to achieve the perfect look for your big day.

How to

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, it is important to consider the type of tiara you will be wearing. The tiara should complement your dress and overall bridal look. Once you have chosen your tiara, you can start thinking about the different hairstyles you can wear with it.

Updo

An updo is a classic hairstyle that works well with a tiara. You can opt for a sleek and polished bun or a more relaxed and tousled style. A low bun at the nape of the neck is a popular choice, as it allows the tiara to sit comfortably and securely on your head. You can also add some braids or twists to your updo for a more intricate look.

Half Up Half Down

The half up half down hairstyle is another great option for shoulder length hair with a tiara. This style combines the elegance of an updo with the simplicity of wearing your hair down. You can pull back the top section of your hair and secure it with some hairpins or a clip, leaving the rest of your hair loose and flowing. This style works well with both curly and straight hair.

Side Swept

The side swept hairstyle is a romantic and feminine look that works well with a tiara. You can sweep your hair to one side and secure it with some bobby pins or a clip. You can also add some curls or waves to your hair for a more textured and voluminous look.

Step by step

Updo

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it into a low bun.
  3. Secure the bun with some hairpins or a clip.
  4. Add some braids or twists to your bun for a more intricate look.
  5. Place the tiara on top of your head, just above the bun.
  6. Secure the tiara with some bobby pins.
  7. Spray some hairspray to hold the style in place.

Half Up Half Down

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take the top section of your hair and pull it back.
  3. Secure the section with some hairpins or a clip.
  4. Leave the rest of your hair loose and flowing.
  5. Place the tiara on top of your head, just above the pinned section.
  6. Secure the tiara with some bobby pins.
  7. Spray some hairspray to hold the style in place.

Side Swept

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Sweep your hair to one side and secure it with some bobby pins or a clip.
  3. Add some curls or waves to your hair for a more textured and voluminous look.
  4. Place the tiara on top of your head, just above the pinned section.
  5. Secure the tiara with some bobby pins.
  6. Spray some hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

  • Choose a tiara that complements your dress and overall bridal look.
  • Consider the shape of your face when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Practice the hairstyle before the wedding day to ensure that it looks the way you want it to.
  • Use hair accessories, such as hairpins and clips, to help secure the tiara.
  • Apply hairspray to hold the style in place and prevent flyaways.

FAQ

Can I wear a tiara with shoulder length hair?

Yes, shoulder length hair works well with a tiara. There are many different hairstyles you can wear with a tiara, including updos, half up half down, and side swept.

How do I choose a tiara for my wedding?

When choosing a tiara for your wedding, consider the shape of your face, the style of your dress, and your overall bridal look. Try on different tiaras to see which one looks best on you.

How do I secure the tiara to my head?

You can use bobby pins or hairpins to secure the tiara to your head. Make sure to place the pins discreetly so that they are not visible.

Hair Care

To ensure that your hair looks its best on your wedding day, it is important to take care of it in the weeks leading up to the wedding. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using hair masks to hydrate and nourish your hair, and getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

On the day of the wedding, make sure to wash and dry your hair before styling it. Use heat protectant spray when using hot tools, and apply hairspray to hold the style in place. Remember to also bring some extra pins and hairspray with you in case you need to touch up your hairstyle throughout the day.

By following these tips and advice, you can achieve the perfect wedding hairstyle for shoulder length hair with a tiara. Whether you opt for an updo, half up half down, or side swept hairstyle, you are sure to look stunning on your special day.
