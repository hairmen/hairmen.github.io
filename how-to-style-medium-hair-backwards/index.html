How To Style Medium Hair Backwards A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Medium hair can be a challenge to style, especially when you're looking for something different. One style that never goes out of fashion is the classic backwards look. In this post, we'll show you how to style medium hair backwards in a relaxed, easy-to-follow way. From prepping your hair to adding finishing touches, we'll cover everything you need to know. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Blow-Drying Your Hair
  • Choosing the Right Products
  • Brushing Your Hair
  • Styling Your Hair Backwards
  • Adding Finishing Touches
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Medium Hair

Prepping Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair backwards, you need to make sure it's properly prepped. This means washing and conditioning your hair with products that suit your hair type.

Once your hair is clean and conditioned, towel-dry it to remove excess moisture. Avoid using a rough towel as this can damage your hair. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to blot your hair gently.

Blow-Drying Your Hair

Once your hair is towel-dried, it's time to blow-dry it. Use a hair dryer with a concentrator nozzle to blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to go. This will help to create a smooth, sleek finish.

Make sure you don't over-dry your hair, as this can cause damage. Use the lowest heat setting on your hair dryer and keep it a few inches away from your hair.

Choosing the Right Products

Choosing the right products is key to getting the perfect backwards hairstyle. Start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools. Next, use a medium-hold styling cream or gel to create the shape you want. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and tips.

Brushing Your Hair

Now it's time to brush your hair. Use a paddle brush or a round brush to smooth your hair and create volume. Brush your hair backwards, starting from the front and working your way towards the back. Make sure you brush your hair in the direction you want it to go.

Styling Your Hair Backwards

Once your hair is brushed, it's time to style it backwards. Use your fingers to create the shape you want, starting from the crown of your head and working your way down to the nape of your neck. Use a comb to smooth out any bumps or tangles.

Adding Finishing Touches

Finally, add some finishing touches to your backwards hairstyle. Use a hairspray to set your hair in place and add shine. You can also use a pomade or wax to create texture and definition.

Tips and Tricks

  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Don't overuse styling products, as this can make your hair greasy and weighed down.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before brushing it.
  • Blow-dry your hair in sections to make it easier to style.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to create more volume.

FAQs

Q: Can I style my medium hair backwards if it's curly?

A: Yes, you can still style your hair backwards if it's curly. Use a curl-defining cream instead of a styling gel or cream, and scrunch your hair gently to create texture.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or you exercise regularly, you may need to wash your hair every day. If your hair is dry or you have a less active lifestyle, you may only need to wash your hair once or twice a week.

Q: Can I style my hair backwards without using heat?

A: Yes, you can create a backwards hairstyle without using heat. Simply apply a styling cream or gel to damp hair, brush it backwards, and let it air dry.

Hair Care for Medium Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, shiny hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your medium hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of protein and vitamins to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your medium hair backwards like a pro. Don't be afraid to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
