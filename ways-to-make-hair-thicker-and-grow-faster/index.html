Ways To Make Hair Thicker And Grow Faster

Written by Christine Jun 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for ways to make your hair thicker and grow faster, you've come to the right place! Whether you have thinning hair or just want to improve the overall health of your hair, there are several things you can do to promote hair growth and thickness. In this article, we'll cover everything from how to care for your hair to natural remedies that can help your hair grow faster and thicker.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • Diet

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for promoting hair growth and thickness. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Make sure to wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free of dirt and oils. However, avoid washing your hair too frequently as this can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and breakage.

Avoid Heat Styling

Using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and lead to breakage. Try to avoid using these tools as much as possible, or use them on a low heat setting. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can lead to breakage. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Diet

Your diet can also play a role in promoting hair growth and thickness. Here are some foods that can help:

Eat More Protein

Protein is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure to include plenty of protein-rich foods in your diet, such as eggs, chicken, fish, and legumes.

Get Your Vitamins

Vitamins like biotin, vitamin D, and vitamin E can help promote hair growth and thickness. Make sure to include foods that are rich in these vitamins in your diet, such as nuts, seeds, and leafy greens.

Natural Remedies

There are several natural remedies that can help promote hair growth and thickness. Here are some of the most effective:

Castor Oil

Castor oil is rich in fatty acids and vitamin E, which can help promote hair growth and thickness. Massage a small amount of castor oil into your scalp and hair, then leave it on for at least 30 minutes before washing it out.

Aloe Vera

Aloe vera contains enzymes that can help promote hair growth and thickness. Apply aloe vera gel to your scalp and hair, then leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it out.

FAQ

How long does it take for hair to grow?

On average, hair grows about half an inch per month. However, this can vary depending on factors like genetics, age, and health.

Can hair grow back after thinning?

Yes, hair can grow back after thinning. However, it's important to address any underlying health issues or hair care habits that may be contributing to hair thinning.

Are there any supplements that can help promote hair growth?

Yes, there are several supplements that can help promote hair growth, such as biotin, vitamin D, and iron. However, it's important to talk to your doctor before taking any supplements to make sure they're safe for you.

Conclusion

There are several things you can do to promote hair growth and thickness, from proper hair care to natural remedies. By incorporating these strategies into your hair care routine, you can improve the overall health of your hair and promote faster, thicker growth.
