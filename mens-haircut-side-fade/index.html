Mens Haircut Side Fade A Complete Guide For 2023

Written by Aprilia Sep 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are looking for a stylish and trendy haircut, a side fade is a great option to consider. This haircut features a fade on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top. In this article, we will provide a complete guide to mens haircut side fade, including how to get it, step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Mens Haircut Side Fade
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Haircut Side Fade
  • FAQs

Description

The mens haircut side fade is a modern and stylish haircut that features a fade on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in many ways, making it a popular choice for men of all ages and hair types. The side fade can be low, medium, or high, depending on the desired look. It can also be combined with other haircuts, such as an undercut or a pompadour, to create a unique and personalized style.

How to Get a Mens Haircut Side Fade

Getting a mens haircut side fade is a simple process that can be done at a barbershop or hair salon. To get this haircut, you will need to have at least 2-3 inches of hair on top, and the sides and back of your head will be trimmed. The barber or stylist will use clippers to fade the hair on the sides and back, gradually blending it into the longer hair on top. The fade can be low, medium, or high, depending on your preference. The barber or stylist will also trim and shape the hair on top to create the desired style.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are the step-by-step instructions for getting a mens haircut side fade:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Decide on the length of the fade and the style of the hair on top.
  3. Show the barber or stylist a picture of the desired haircut.
  4. Sit in the barber chair and let the barber or stylist trim your hair.
  5. The barber or stylist will use clippers to fade the hair on the sides and back.
  6. The barber or stylist will trim and shape the hair on top to create the desired style.
  7. After the haircut, the barber or stylist will apply styling products, such as pomade or wax, to create the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Haircut Side Fade

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens haircut side fade:

  • Visit the barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.
  • Apply styling products, such as pomade or wax, to create the desired look.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the hair and make it dry and brittle.

FAQs

Q: Can I get a mens haircut side fade if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, the mens haircut side fade can be done on curly hair. However, you may need to find a barber or stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair to get the best results.

Q: How long does a mens haircut side fade last?

A: A mens haircut side fade can last anywhere from 4-6 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain it.

Q: Can I style my mens haircut side fade in different ways?

A: Yes, the mens haircut side fade is versatile and can be styled in many ways. You can try slicking it back, combing it to the side, or spiking it up, depending on your preference.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Haircut Side Fade

Here are some hair care tips for mens haircut side fade:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the hair and make it dry and brittle.
  • Apply styling products, such as pomade or wax, to create the desired look.
  • Visit the barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

In conclusion, the mens haircut side fade is a stylish and trendy haircut that is versatile and easy to maintain. By following the tips and instructions in this article, you can get a great mens haircut side fade and keep it looking its best for weeks to come.
