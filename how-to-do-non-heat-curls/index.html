How To Do Non Heat Curls A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of using heat on your hair to achieve curls? Not only does it damage your hair, but it can also be time-consuming. Luckily, there are non heat curling methods that are easy to do and gentle on your hair. In this article, we’ll show you how to do non heat curls in a relaxed English language.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Do Non Heat Curls
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Non Heat Curls
  4. FAQs

How to Do Non Heat Curls

Non heat curls are achieved by using techniques that don’t require the use of heat styling tools like curling irons, straighteners, or hot rollers. Here are some popular non heat curling methods:

  • Braids
  • Bantu knots
  • Twists
  • Rag curls
  • Foam rollers

Each method has its own unique benefits and results, so it’s important to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look. In this article, we’ll focus on the braid method.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s how to do non heat curls using the braid method:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. You can either wash your hair or wet it with a spray bottle.
  2. Part your hair into sections. The more sections you create, the tighter your curls will be.
  3. Apply a curl-enhancing product to each section. This will help define your curls and keep them in place.
  4. Begin braiding each section. You can do a standard three-strand braid or a more intricate braid, like a fishtail braid.
  5. Secure the end of each braid with a hair tie.
  6. Let your hair air dry overnight or for several hours.
  7. Take out the hair ties and gently unravel each braid.
  8. Use your fingers to separate and fluff your curls.
  9. Apply a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.
  10. Optional: Use a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

And that’s it! You now have beautiful, bouncy curls without using any heat.

Tips for Non Heat Curls

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best non heat curls:

  • Apply a leave-in conditioner before braiding your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Use a silk scarf or bonnet to protect your curls while you sleep.
  • Experiment with different curl-enhancing products to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • If you have naturally straight hair, you may need to use a stronger curl-enhancing product or leave your braids in longer to achieve curls.
  • Don’t brush or comb your curls too much, as this can cause frizz.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about non heat curls:

Can I do non heat curls on short hair?

Yes, you can do non heat curls on short hair. However, you may need to use smaller sections and tighter braids to achieve the desired curl.

How long do I need to leave my braids in?

The longer you leave your braids in, the tighter your curls will be. Overnight is usually sufficient, but you can leave them in for several hours if you want tighter curls.

Can I use non heat curls on color-treated hair?

Yes, non heat curls are safe to use on color-treated hair. However, be sure to use a curl-enhancing product that is color-safe and won’t fade your hair color.

Hair Care for Non Heat Curls

To keep your non heat curls looking their best, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep your curls looking healthy.
  • Avoid using hair elastics with metal clasps, as these can cause breakage.

With these tips, you can enjoy beautiful, healthy non heat curls without any damage to your hair.
