Long Length Hairstyles For Over 50 With Glasses

Written by Oliver Sep 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're over 50 and wear glasses, finding a hairstyle that flatters your face and complements your glasses can be a challenge. However, there are plenty of long length hairstyles that can be perfect for you. In this article, we'll discuss how to choose the right hairstyle, step-by-step instructions for creating it, tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and of course, your glasses. For those with a round face, a long layered cut can help elongate the face. A square face can be softened with beachy waves or loose curls. If you have a heart-shaped face, try a side-swept bang or layers that hit at the cheekbones. And for those with an oval face, you can pretty much rock any long length hairstyle.

When it comes to your hair texture, those with straight hair can pull off a sleek and simple look, while those with curly hair can embrace their natural texture with layers and volume. As for glasses, consider the frame shape and size. If you have small frames, go for a more voluminous hairstyle. If you have larger frames, a sleek and simple look may be more flattering.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that cater to your specific hair type.


2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent any damage from hot tools.


3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.


4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or curls to your hair. Alternatively, you can leave your hair straight.


5. Apply a small amount of hair serum to add shine and tame any frizz.


6. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

1. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.


2. Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.


3. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and always use a heat protectant spray.


4. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.


5. Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair up if I have glasses?

A: Absolutely! A messy bun or low ponytail can look great with glasses.


Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type, but generally every 2-3 days is sufficient.


Q: Can I still have bangs if I wear glasses?

A: Of course! Just make sure the bangs hit at the right spot on your face to flatter your glasses.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Get regular trims to prevent split ends and consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect long length hairstyle that flatters your face and complements your glasses. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you!
