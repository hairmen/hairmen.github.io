Does Having Short Hair Make You Look Older

Written by Daniel Jul 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Many people believe that having short hair can make you look older. This is a common misconception that has been around for years. In this article, we will explore whether or not having short hair really does make you look older. We will also provide some tips on how to maintain short hair and keep it looking youthful.

Description

Short hair has been a popular trend for many years. It is a low-maintenance hairstyle that can be flattering for many face shapes. However, some people believe that having short hair can make you look older. This is because shorter hairstyles are often associated with maturity and professionalism.

But does having short hair really make you look older? The answer is no. Your hairstyle does not determine your age. There are many factors that contribute to how old you look, such as your skin, makeup, and clothing.

How to

If you are considering getting a short haircut, there are some things you should keep in mind to make sure it looks youthful and flattering.

Consider Your Face Shape

Short hairstyles can be flattering for many face shapes, but it is important to choose a style that complements your features. If you have a round face, a short bob with layers can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can soften your features.

Experiment with Color

Adding some highlights or lowlights to your short hair can help add dimension and make your hair look more youthful. Consider adding some warm tones to your hair, such as caramel or honey blonde.

Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can help keep your short hair looking healthy and shiny. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair, and a texturizing spray to add texture and definition.

Step by Step

Here are some steps you can follow to maintain your short hair and keep it looking youthful.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can make it look dull and dry.

Step 2: Apply Product

Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair while it is still damp. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and lift. Use the cool setting on your blow dryer to set your style.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a texturizing spray to add definition and texture to your hair. Use your fingers to style your hair into your desired shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling short hair.

Invest in Regular Haircuts

Short hair requires more frequent haircuts to maintain its shape and style. Consider getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh.

Accessorize

Adding some accessories to your short hair can help add some personality and style. Consider adding a headband, scarf, or hair clip to your hair.

FAQ

Can Short Hair Make You Look Older?

No, your hairstyle does not determine your age. There are many factors that contribute to how old you look.

What Short Hairstyles Are Flattering for Older Women?

Short hairstyles can be flattering for women of all ages. Consider a short bob with layers or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair, regardless of the length. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools, such as a blow dryer or flat iron, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Avoid Overwashing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can make it look dull and dry. Try to wash your hair every other day, or use a dry shampoo in between washes.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. Consider wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray when you are outside for extended periods.

In conclusion, having short hair does not make you look older. It is important to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Follow these tips and tricks to maintain your short hair and keep it looking youthful.
