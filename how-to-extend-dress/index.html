How To Extend Dress Tips Steps And Faq

Written by Daniel Jul 13, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a favorite dress that you love to wear, but it’s starting to feel too short or tight, don't worry! There are several ways to extend the life of your dress and make it more comfortable to wear. In this article, we will provide you with tips, step-by-step instructions, and answer some frequently asked questions on how to extend dress.

How to Extend Dress: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Add a Hemline

If your dress is too short, adding a hemline can give it a few extra inches. You can either take it to a tailor or do it yourself if you know how to sew. First, measure the desired length of your dress and add an additional 1-2 inches for the hem. Cut off the excess fabric, fold it over twice, and sew it in place.

Step 2: Add a Lining

If your dress is too tight, adding a lining can help you to extend it. You can either take it to a tailor or do it yourself if you know how to sew. First, measure the dimensions of the lining that you need, cut the fabric, and sew it onto the dress. This will help you to add an extra layer of fabric and give you more room to move around.

Step 3: Add a Belt

If your dress is too loose or baggy, adding a belt can help you to adjust the fit. You can either use a belt that matches the dress or choose a contrasting color to add some visual interest. Simply wrap the belt around your waist and adjust it until you feel comfortable.

Step 4: Add Fabric

If your dress is too tight, adding fabric can help you to extend it. You can either take it to a tailor or do it yourself if you know how to sew. First, measure the dimensions of the additional fabric that you need, cut the fabric, and sew it onto the dress. This will help you to add an extra layer of fabric and give you more room to move around.

Tips to Extend the Life of Your Dress

Here are some tips to help you extend the life of your dress:

  • Keep it clean by following the care instructions on the label.
  • Store it properly by hanging it up or folding it neatly in a drawer.
  • Rotate it with other dresses in your wardrobe to prevent wear and tear.
  • Avoid wearing it in harsh weather conditions or environments where it could get damaged.

FAQs on How to Extend Dress

Q: Can I still wear a dress that is too short?

A: Yes, you can still wear a dress that is too short if you feel comfortable in it. However, be mindful of the occasion and environment that you will be in.

Q: Can I add fabric to a dress that is too short?

A: Yes, you can add fabric to a dress that is too short. However, it might be more challenging to do so depending on the design and fabric of the dress.

Hair Care Tips for Wearing Your Dress

When you wear a dress, you want to look and feel your best. Here are some hair care tips to help you do just that:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements the dress and your face shape.
  • Use the right hair products to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, which can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using accessories, like headbands or hair clips, to add some extra flair to your look.

By following these tips and steps, you can extend the life of your dress and feel confident and comfortable when you wear it. Whether you're dressing up for a special occasion or just enjoying a casual day out, a well-maintained dress can help you look and feel your best.
