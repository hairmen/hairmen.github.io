High And Tight Haircut A Classic Look That Never Fades

Written by Christine Jul 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that’s both timeless and modern? Look no further than the high and tight haircut. This style has been popular for decades, and it’s still going strong in 2023. In this blog post, we’ll cover everything you need to know about the high and tight haircut, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • What is a High and Tight Haircut?
  • How to Get a High and Tight Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to High and Tight Haircut

What is a High and Tight Haircut?

The high and tight haircut is a military-inspired hairstyle that’s short on the sides and back and slightly longer on top. The hair is usually tapered, with the shortest length at the bottom and gradually getting longer as it goes up towards the top. The hair on top is typically around 1-2 inches long, and it’s styled to stand up or forward. The high and tight haircut is versatile and works well for most face shapes and hair types.

How to Get a High and Tight Haircut

The high and tight haircut is a relatively simple style that you can get at most barbershops or hair salons. To get the look, tell your stylist or barber that you want a high and tight haircut. They’ll use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair short, leaving a bit more length on top. You can also bring in a photo of the style to give your stylist a better idea of what you’re looking for.

Step-by-Step Guide to High and Tight Haircut

If you’re feeling adventurous, you can also try cutting your hair at home. Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting a high and tight haircut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a clipper with a #1 guard to cut the sides and back of your hair, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  3. Switch to a #2 or #3 guard to blend the hair on the sides and back into the longer hair on top.
  4. Use scissors to trim the hair on top to your desired length.
  5. Style your hair with a pomade or wax, using your fingers to create volume and texture.

Tips for Styling High and Tight Haircut

Styling a high and tight haircut is easy, but there are a few tips to keep in mind to make sure your hair looks its best:

  • Use a high-quality pomade or wax to style your hair. Cheap products can make your hair look greasy or stiff.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as slicking your hair back or creating a messy, textured look.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask your stylist for advice on how to style your hair. They can recommend products and techniques that work well with your hair type and face shape.

Frequently Asked Questions about High and Tight Haircut

What face shape does the high and tight haircut work best for?

The high and tight haircut works well for most face shapes, but it’s especially flattering for those with square or oval faces.

How often do I need to get a high and tight haircut?

You’ll need to get a high and tight haircut every 2-4 weeks to maintain the shape and length of the style.

Can I style a high and tight haircut with longer hair on top?

Yes, you can adjust the length of the hair on top to create a variety of different styles, including a longer, more textured look.

Hair Care Tips for High and Tight Haircut

To keep your high and tight haircut looking sharp, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy or weighed down.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of the style.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.

The high and tight haircut is a classic style that’s perfect for anyone who wants a low-maintenance, versatile look. Whether you’re headed to the office or the gym, this style will keep you looking sharp and stylish. With a little bit of styling know-how and some basic hair care tips, you can rock the high and tight haircut like a pro.
