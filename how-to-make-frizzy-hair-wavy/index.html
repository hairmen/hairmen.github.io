How To Make Frizzy Hair Wavy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Frizzy hair can be a nightmare to manage, but with the right technique, you can transform those unmanageable locks into beautiful waves. In this article, we’ll be discussing how to make frizzy hair wavy and provide tips on how to keep your hair looking healthy and fabulous.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Make Frizzy Hair Wavy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Products for Frizzy Hair

How to Make Frizzy Hair Wavy

Making frizzy hair wavy can be a great way to change up your look. Here are some ways to do it:

1. Braid your hair

Braiding your hair is a simple way to create waves overnight. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it’s slightly damp. Then, divide your hair into sections and braid each one tightly. You can do one large braid or multiple smaller braids, depending on the look you want. In the morning, undo the braids and gently finger comb your hair to create soft waves.

2. Use a curling iron

If you’re short on time, using a curling iron can be a quick and easy way to create waves. Start by sectioning your hair and applying a heat protectant spray. Then, wrap each section of hair around the curling iron and hold it for a few seconds. Release the hair and repeat the process until all sections have been curled. Once done, gently comb through your hair with your fingers for a natural wave look.

3. Apply a hair mousse

Using a hair mousse can help enhance the natural wave in your hair. Apply a small amount to your damp hair and scrunch it gently. Then, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help create natural-looking waves and reduce frizz.

4. Use a salt spray

A salt spray is a great option for creating beachy waves. Simply spray the product onto damp hair and scrunch it. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser to dry it. The salt spray will help create natural-looking waves and add texture to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’re looking for a more detailed guide on how to make frizzy hair wavy, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help hydrate your hair and reduce frizz.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant spray

If you’re using a curling iron, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent heat damage.

Step 3: Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to curl or braid your hair.

Step 4: Start braiding or curling your hair

Start braiding your hair or curling it with a curling iron. Make sure to hold the curling iron for a few seconds to create a defined curl.

Step 5: Finish and style

Once you’ve finished curling or braiding your hair, gently comb through it with your fingers to create a natural wave. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair

Now that you’ve made your hair wavy, here are some tips on how to maintain it:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to keep them hydrated.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.

FAQs

Q: Can I make my hair wavy without heat?

A: Yes, you can make your hair wavy without heat by using braids or a salt spray.

Q: How long will my wavy hair last?

A: How long your wavy hair lasts depends on how you maintain it. If you follow the tips above, your wavy hair can last for several days.

Q: Can I use a hair mousse and a salt spray together?

A: Yes, you can use a hair mousse and a salt spray together to create more defined waves.

Hair Care Products for Frizzy Hair

Here are some hair care products that can help manage frizzy hair:

  • Moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hair mousse
  • Salt spray
  • Hair oil or serum

By following these tips and techniques, you can transform your frizzy hair into beautiful waves. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and avoiding heat damage. With a little love and care, you’ll have gorgeous wavy hair in no time!
