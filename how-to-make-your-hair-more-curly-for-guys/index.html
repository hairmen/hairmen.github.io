How To Make Your Hair More Curly For Guys

Written by Frank Aug 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy with straight hair, you might be wondering how to get those luscious curls that seem to come so naturally to others. Luckily, there are a few tricks and tips that can help you achieve the curly look you desire. In this article, we'll cover everything from how to make your hair more curly for guys to hair care and FAQs.

  2. Step by Step Guide

How to Make Your Hair More Curly for Guys

One of the easiest ways to make your hair more curly for guys is by using a curling iron or wand. However, this method can be time-consuming and may cause damage to your hair if not used correctly. Instead, opt for more natural methods that will give you those perfect curls without damaging your hair.

One of the best ways to make your hair more curly is by using a sea salt spray. This spray is perfect for adding texture and volume to your hair, making it easier to style into curls. Simply spray the sea salt spray onto damp hair and scrunch your hair with your hands to create waves and curls.

Another great way to make your hair more curly for guys is by using a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer. This attachment helps to evenly distribute heat and air, allowing your curls to form naturally. Simply apply a heat protectant spray to your hair, attach the diffuser, and use a low heat setting to dry your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair more curly for guys:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a sea salt spray.
  3. Scrunch your hair with your hands to create waves and curls.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting.
  5. Finish with a curl defining cream or gel to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining and styling curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a curl defining cream or gel to help hold your curls in place.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your hair more curly for guys:

Can guys with straight hair get curly hair?

Yes, guys with straight hair can use various methods to make their hair more curly.

What is the best way to make your hair more curly naturally?

The best way to make your hair more curly naturally is by using a sea salt spray or diffuser attachment on your hairdryer.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

You should wash your curly hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying out your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as this can cause dryness and frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid damaging your hair while you sleep.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your hair more curly for guys and achieve the perfect curly hairstyle. Remember to take care of your hair by using sulfate-free products and avoiding heat tools too often to keep your curls looking healthy and bouncy.
