Mid Long Hairstyles A Guide To Styling Your Hair In 2023

Written by Luffy Jun 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's not too short or too long, then mid long hairstyles might be the perfect choice for you. These hairstyles are versatile, easy to maintain, and can be styled in various ways to suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about mid long hairstyles, including how to style them, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care.

How to Style Mid Long Hairstyles

Mid long hairstyles are perfect for those who want a hairstyle that's not too short or too long. These hairstyles typically fall between the chin and the shoulders and can be styled in various ways to suit your personal style. Here are some of the most popular ways to style mid long hairstyles:

1. Beach Waves

Beach waves are a popular hairstyle that's perfect for mid long hair. To achieve this look, start by applying a texturizing spray to your hair. Then, curl your hair using a curling iron or wand. Once you've curled your hair, gently brush through it to create loose waves. Finish the look by applying a sea salt spray to add texture and volume.

2. Half Up, Half Down

The half up, half down hairstyle is perfect for mid long hair. To achieve this look, start by pulling the top half of your hair back and securing it with a hair tie or clip. Then, leave the bottom half of your hair down. You can leave your hair straight or add curls or waves for added texture.

3. Braids

Braids are a great way to add texture and personality to mid long hairstyles. There are many different types of braids, including fishtail braids, Dutch braids, and French braids. You can braid your entire head or add braids to just a section of your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Mid Long Hair

If you're new to styling mid long hair, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type. If you have dry or damaged hair, consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask to hydrate and nourish your hair.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Step 3: Apply Styling Products

Apply styling products to your hair, such as a heat protectant spray, texturizing spray, or mousse. These products will help to add volume, texture, and hold to your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a hair dryer, curling iron, or straightener to style your hair. You can add curls, waves, or keep it straight, depending on the look you're going for. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've styled your hair, finish the look with a hairspray to hold it in place. Choose a hairspray that's suitable for your hair type and texture.

Tips for Maintaining Mid Long Hairstyles

Here are some tips for maintaining mid long hairstyles:

1. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are important for maintaining healthy hair and preventing split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

2. Use Heat Protectant Spray

Heat protectant spray is essential for preventing heat damage to your hair. Be sure to apply it before using any hot tools on your hair.

3. Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils and cause damage. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed.

4. Use Hair Masks and Treatments

Hair masks and treatments are great for nourishing and hydrating your hair. Use them once a week or as needed to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Frequently Asked Questions about Mid Long Hairstyles

1. What face shape is best for mid long hairstyles?

Mid long hairstyles are versatile and can suit various face shapes, including oval, round, square, and heart-shaped faces.

2. How do I choose the right mid long hairstyle for my hair texture?

Choose a mid long hairstyle that's suitable for your hair texture. If you have fine hair, opt for a layered cut to add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, consider a blunt cut to keep your hair looking polished and sleek.

3. Can I wear mid long hairstyles to formal events?

Yes, mid long hairstyles can be styled in various ways to suit formal events. Consider adding curls, braids, or an updo to elevate your look.

Hair Care Tips for Mid Long Hairstyles

Here are some hair care tips for mid long hairstyles:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet. This will help to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray. Sun exposure can cause damage and dryness to your hair.

3. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Use a silk pillowcase to prevent friction and damage to your hair while you sleep. Silk is gentle on your hair and can help to prevent breakage and frizz.

Mid long hairstyles are a great choice for those who want a versatile and easy-to-maintain hairstyle. With the right styling products, tools, and techniques, you can achieve the perfect mid long hairstyle that suits your personal style and face shape. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products, getting regular trims, and avoiding heat damage.
