Does A Short Bob Suit Me

Written by Robby Aug 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering getting a short bob haircut but wondering if it will suit you? Look no further, as we’ll guide you through everything you need to know about whether or not a short bob will work for your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Description

A short bob haircut is a classic, chic hairstyle that falls anywhere between the chin and the shoulders. It can be cut bluntly or with layers, and styled sleek and straight or with tousled waves. The beauty of the short bob is that it can be customized to suit any face shape or hair type.

How to

When considering whether a short bob will suit you, it’s important to take into account your face shape. If you have a round face, a blunt bob that falls just below the chin can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a layered bob with soft waves can soften your strong jawline. For those with a heart-shaped face, a chin-length bob with layers around the face can balance out a wider forehead. And for those with an oval face, any type of bob will suit you!

Next, consider your hair type. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob can help control the volume, while those with thin hair can benefit from a layered bob that adds texture and body. If you have curly or wavy hair, a bob with layers can help enhance your natural texture.

Finally, think about your personal style. A short bob can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and elegant to edgy and tousled. Choose a style that suits your personality and lifestyle.

Step by Step

If you’ve decided that a short bob is right for you, here are the steps to take:

  1. Find a reputable hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts.
  2. Bring in pictures of bob haircuts that you like to show your stylist.
  3. Discuss your face shape, hair type, and personal style with your stylist to determine the best bob haircut for you.
  4. Trust your stylist to create a custom bob haircut that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.
  5. Maintain your bob haircut with regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep the shape and style looking fresh.

Tips

  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your bob haircut to add dimension and depth.
  • Invest in high-quality hair care products to keep your bob looking healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Q: Will a short bob haircut make my face look wider?

A: It depends on your face shape and the length and style of the bob. A blunt bob that falls at chin-level can actually help elongate a round face, while a layered bob with soft waves can balance out a wider forehead on a heart-shaped face.

Q: Can I still style my hair in a ponytail with a short bob?

A: Yes, a short bob can be styled in a low ponytail or half-up style. You can also use bobby pins or hair clips to create a mini updo.

Hair Care

To keep your short bob looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day, and use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair to keep them hydrated.
  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your bob haircut.

In conclusion, a short bob can suit anyone, as long as it’s customized to your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Follow our tips and advice to find the perfect bob haircut for you, and maintain it with proper hair care to keep it looking fabulous!
