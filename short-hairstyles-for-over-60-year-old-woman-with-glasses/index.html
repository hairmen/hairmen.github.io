Short Hairstyles For Over 60 Year Old Woman With Glasses

Written by Frank Jul 30, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you a woman over 60 years old who wears glasses and is looking for a new short hairstyle? You’ve come to the right place! In this article, we will provide you with a variety of short hairstyles that will suit you perfectly. Keep reading to learn more!

Description

Short hairstyles are perfect for women over 60 who want to look stylish and chic. They are easy to maintain and can help you feel younger and more confident. If you wear glasses, you need a hairstyle that complements your face and enhances your features. The right short hairstyle can do just that!

How To

The first step in finding the perfect short hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are four basic face shapes: round, square, heart, and oval. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.

Next, consider the texture of your hair. If you have thin hair, you may want to choose a hairstyle that adds volume and thickness. If you have thick hair, you may want to choose a hairstyle that reduces bulk and weight.

Finally, think about your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend styling your hair each day. Short hairstyles are low-maintenance, but some require more styling than others. Choose a hairstyle that fits your lifestyle and makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape.

2. Consider the texture of your hair.

3. Think about your lifestyle.

4. Choose a short hairstyle that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.

5. Take a picture of your new hairstyle to your hairstylist and ask for their advice.

Tips

- Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

- Use hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type.

- Don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQ

Q: What short hairstyles are best for women over 60 with glasses?

A: Short pixie cuts, bobs, and layered hairstyles are all great options for women over 60 with glasses.

Q: Can I still have a stylish short hairstyle if I have thin hair?

A: Yes! There are many short hairstyles that add volume and thickness to thin hair.

Q: How often do I need to get my short hairstyle trimmed?

A: It depends on the style and how fast your hair grows. Generally, short hairstyles need to be trimmed every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Short hairstyles require minimal maintenance, but it's still important to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type. Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair. Instead, let your hair air dry and use styling products like mousse or gel to add volume and hold.

In conclusion, there are many short hairstyles that will suit women over 60 with glasses. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your features, enhances your natural beauty, and makes you feel confident and beautiful!
