How To Wear Long Hair With A Round Face

Written by William Jun 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a round face, you might think that long hair isn't for you. However, with the right styling and techniques, anyone can rock long locks no matter their face shape. In this article, we'll show you how to wear long hair with a round face, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

Description

A round face is characterized by soft, rounded features, such as a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The goal of styling long hair with a round face is to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. This can be achieved through a variety of techniques, including layering, face-framing, and parting your hair in a flattering way.

How to

The first step in styling long hair with a round face is to consider the length of your hair. Ideally, you want to aim for hair that falls past your shoulders, as shorter hair can make your face appear wider. From there, you can experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. Here are some tips:

1. Add layers

Layering your hair is a great way to add volume and movement to your locks, which can help to elongate your face. Opt for long layers that start around your chin or cheekbones, as this will help to create a more angular look.

2. Face-framing pieces

Adding face-framing pieces to your hair can also help to elongate your face. Ask your stylist to cut longer pieces around your face, as this will help to draw attention away from your cheeks and towards your eyes and cheekbones.

3. Part your hair in the middle

If you have a round face, parting your hair in the middle can help to create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face. This will also help to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones, rather than your cheeks.

4. Try a side part

If you don't want to part your hair in the middle, a side part can also be a flattering option. Aim for a deep side part that starts at the arch of your eyebrow, as this will help to create a more angular look.

5. Avoid blunt cuts

Blunt cuts can make your face appear wider, so it's best to avoid them if you have a round face. Instead, opt for layered cuts that add movement and volume to your hair.

6. Experiment with updos

An updo can also be a great option for those with a round face. Try a high bun or top knot, as this will help to elongate your face and draw attention upwards towards your eyes and cheekbones.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling long hair with a round face:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair using your preferred method. It's best to start with clean, dry hair, as this will help you to achieve your desired style.

Step 2: Add volume

Use a volumizing product, such as mousse or root-lifting spray, to add volume to your hair. Apply the product to your roots and work it through your hair, focusing on the crown of your head.

Step 3: Blow dry

Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume and create a more angular look. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to pull your hair away from your face.

Step 4: Add face-framing layers

If you want to add face-framing layers, ask your stylist to cut longer pieces around your face. This will help to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones, rather than your cheeks.

Step 5: Part your hair

Part your hair in the middle or try a deep side part to create a more angular look.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

Finish your style with a light mist of hairspray to hold your hair in place and add shine.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style long hair with a round face:

  • Avoid styles that add bulk to the sides of your face.
  • Experiment with different partings to find what works best for you.
  • Avoid overly tight hairstyles, such as slicked-back ponytails, as these can make your face appear wider.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add bulk to the sides of your face, such as blunt cuts or heavy bangs. It's also best to avoid overly tight hairstyles, such as slicked-back ponytails.

What hairstyles are best for a round face?

Hairstyles that elongate the face are best for a round face. Try long layers, face-framing pieces, and parting your hair in the middle or with a deep side part.

Can I still wear my hair long if I have a round face?

Absolutely! With the right styling and techniques, anyone can wear long hair no matter their face shape.

Hair care

To keep your long hair healthy and looking great, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
