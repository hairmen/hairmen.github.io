How To Make Curly Weave Less Poofy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have curly weave, you may have noticed that it can look a little poofy at times. While some people like the fullness that comes with curly hair, others prefer a more relaxed and defined look. If you fall into the latter category, don't worry! There are several things you can do to make your curly weave less poofy. In this article, we'll walk you through the process step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Curly Weave Less Poofy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Make Curly Weave Less Poofy

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's take a look at some of the things you can do to make your curly weave less poofy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry

Now that you know what to do, let's get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in making your curly weave less poofy is to wash your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any product buildup.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and reduce frizz. Make sure to apply the conditioner evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Dry Your Hair

Use a microfiber towel to gently squeeze out the excess water from your hair. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously as this can cause frizz. Once you have removed the excess water, use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair. This will help to distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a styling product to define your curls. You can use a curl cream, gel, or mousse depending on your preference. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, scrunching your curls as you go.

Tips

  • Avoid using too much product as this can weigh down your curls
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair
  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf at night to prevent frizz
  • Trim your ends regularly to keep your hair healthy

FAQ

How often should I wash my curly weave?

You should aim to wash your hair once a week to avoid drying it out. However, if your hair gets particularly dirty or sweaty, you can wash it more frequently.

Can I straighten my curly weave?

Yes, you can straighten your curly weave if you want to. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using too much heat to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your curly weave looking its best, make sure to take care of it properly. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, apply a leave-in conditioner, and avoid using too much heat. Trim your ends regularly and wrap your hair in a silk scarf at night to prevent frizz. With the right care, your curly weave can look beautiful and defined!
