Description

Heart shaped faces are defined by a wider forehead and a narrower chin. Long hair is a great choice for heart shaped faces because it can help balance out the proportions of the face. There are a variety of hairstyles that work well for heart shaped faces with long hair, from sleek and straight to voluminous curls.

How to Style Long Hair for Heart Shaped Faces

When styling long hair for heart shaped faces, the goal is to create balance and draw attention away from the forehead. Layers can help soften the look of a wider forehead, while volume at the ends of the hair can balance out a narrower chin. Consider the following hairstyles:

Long Layers

Long layers can help soften the look of a wider forehead and add movement to long hair. They can be cut in a variety of lengths and styles, from subtle face framing layers to more dramatic layers throughout the hair.

Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs can help draw attention away from a wider forehead and create a more balanced look. They can be cut in a variety of lengths and styles, from long and textured to shorter and blunt.

Voluminous Curls

Voluminous curls can help balance out a narrower chin and add movement and texture to long hair. They can be achieved with a curling iron or hot rollers, and can be styled in a variety of ways, from loose waves to tight curls.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Heart Shaped Faces

Follow these steps to style long hair for heart shaped faces:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant to the hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools
  3. Section the hair and curl with a curling iron or hot rollers, focusing on adding volume at the ends of the hair
  4. Brush out the curls for a more natural look
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to set the style

Tips and Tricks for Styling Long Hair for Heart Shaped Faces

Consider the following tips when styling long hair for heart shaped faces:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the crown of the head, as this can make the forehead appear wider
  • Consider adding subtle highlights or lowlights to the hair to add depth and movement
  • Try parting the hair in the center or to the side to create balance
  • Consider using a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the roots of the hair
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find what works best for you

Frequently Asked Questions about Long Hair Styles for Heart Shaped Faces

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a heart shaped face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the crown of the head, as this can make the forehead appear wider. Also avoid blunt bangs that sit at the eyebrows, as this can create a boxy look.

Should I keep my hair long or cut it shorter if I have a heart shaped face?

Long hair can work well for heart shaped faces, but shorter hair can also be flattering. Consider a chin-length bob or a layered shoulder-length cut to create balance.

Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a heart shaped face?

Yes, a middle part can work well for heart shaped faces. It can help create balance and draw attention away from a wider forehead.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair Styles for Heart Shaped Faces

Follow these tips to keep long hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash hair 2-3 times per week to avoid stripping the hair of natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently to prevent damage to the hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair and avoid breakage
  • Protect hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or swim cap

With these tips and hairstyle suggestions, you can rock long hair with a heart shaped face with ease. Experiment with different styles and lengths to find the perfect look for you!
