Short Pixie Style Human Hair Wigs The Perfect Choice For A Bold Look

Written by Aprilia Nov 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our blog post about short pixie style human hair wigs! In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about this trendy hairstyle. From how to wear it to the best ways to care for it, we’ve got you covered.

  • Description
  • How to Wear a Short Pixie Style Human Hair Wig

Description

Short pixie style human hair wigs are a great way to change up your look without committing to a drastic haircut. They are perfect for those who want a bold and edgy style that is both modern and classic at the same time. These wigs are made from 100% human hair, which means they look and feel just like your natural hair.

Short pixie style human hair wigs come in a variety of colors and styles, so you can choose the one that best suits your personality and style. They are also low maintenance and easy to style, making them a great choice for those who are always on the go.

How to Wear a Short Pixie Style Human Hair Wig

Wearing a short pixie style human hair wig is easy and requires no special skills. The first step is to choose the right wig for your face shape and skin tone. You can do this by trying on different wigs at a wig store or by using online wig try-on tools.

Once you have chosen the perfect wig, it’s time to put it on. First, make sure your natural hair is clean and flat against your head. Then, put on a wig cap to keep your natural hair in place. Next, place the wig on your head and adjust it until it feels comfortable and secure.

Step by Step Guide to Wearing a Short Pixie Style Human Hair Wig

If you’re new to wearing wigs or just need a refresher, here is a step by step guide to wearing a short pixie style human hair wig:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Put on a wig cap to keep your natural hair in place.
  3. Place the wig on your head and adjust the straps until it feels secure.
  4. Tuck any stray hairs under the wig cap.
  5. Style the wig using a wig brush or your fingers.
  6. Use hairspray or styling products to keep the wig in place.

Tips for Wearing a Short Pixie Style Human Hair Wig

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your short pixie style human hair wig:

  • Choose a wig that complements your face shape and skin tone.
  • Use a wig cap to keep your natural hair in place and prevent the wig from slipping.
  • Use a wig brush or your fingers to style the wig.
  • Use hairspray or styling products to keep the wig in place.
  • Take care when washing and drying the wig to prevent damage.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a short pixie style human hair wig if I have long hair?

A: Yes, you can wear a short pixie style human hair wig even if you have long hair. Simply put your hair in a braid or bun and put on a wig cap to keep it in place.

Q: How long does a short pixie style human hair wig last?

A: With proper care, a short pixie style human hair wig can last up to a year or more.

Q: Can I dye a short pixie style human hair wig?

A: Yes, you can dye a short pixie style human hair wig. However, it’s best to have a professional do it to avoid damaging the wig.

Hair Care Tips for Short Pixie Style Human Hair Wigs

Proper hair care is essential to keeping your short pixie style human hair wig looking great. Here are some tips to help you care for your wig:

  • Wash the wig every 7-10 wears using a mild shampoo and cool water.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on the wig, as this can damage the hair.
  • Store the wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to keep its shape.
  • Avoid exposing the wig to direct sunlight, as this can cause the hair to dry out and become brittle.

We hope you found this article helpful in learning about short pixie style human hair wigs. Whether you’re looking for a bold new look or just want to switch things up, a short pixie style human hair wig is a great choice. With proper care, your wig can last for months or even years, giving you a stylish and versatile look that you’ll love.
