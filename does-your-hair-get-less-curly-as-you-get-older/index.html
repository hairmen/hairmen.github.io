Does Your Hair Get Less Curly As You Get Older

Written by Frank Oct 10, 2023 · 3 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing or a curse, depending on how you look at it. Some people love their curly locks and wouldn't trade them for anything, while others spend countless hours and dollars trying to straighten their hair. But one question that seems to come up a lot is whether your hair gets less curly as you get older. In this article, we'll explore the answer to that question and provide some tips for taking care of curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Does Your Hair Get Less Curly as You Get Older?
  • Why Does Your Hair Get Less Curly as You Get Older?
  • How to Care for Curly Hair
  • Tips for Curly Hair
  • FAQ

Why Does Your Hair Get Less Curly as You Get Older?

The short answer is that yes, your hair can get less curly as you get older. This is because as you age, your body produces less of the hormones that are responsible for curling your hair. Additionally, your hair may become drier and more brittle with age, which can make it less curly.

This doesn't mean that everyone will experience a significant change in their hair's curliness as they get older, but it is a possibility.

How to Care for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair while it's wet, and avoid brushing it when it's dry.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to help define your curls.

Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Try plopping your hair with a t-shirt or microfiber towel to help define your curls.
  • Avoid using products that contain sulfates or alcohol, as these can dry out your hair.
  • Consider getting a haircut that is specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Sleep with your hair in a pineapple or a satin bonnet to help preserve your curls overnight.

FAQ

Does everyone's hair get less curly as they get older?

No, not everyone experiences a significant change in their hair's curliness as they get older. It depends on a variety of factors, including genetics, hormones, and hair care habits.

Can you make your hair more curly?

While you can't change your hair's natural texture, there are things you can do to enhance your curls. Using curl-enhancing products and avoiding heat styling tools can help your curls look their best.

Can you make your hair less curly?

If you have naturally curly hair, there's not much you can do to make it less curly. However, using chemical straighteners or heat styling tools can temporarily straighten your hair.

In conclusion, yes, your hair can get less curly as you get older, but it's not a guarantee. By taking good care of your curly hair and using the right products, you can help your curls look their best at any age.
