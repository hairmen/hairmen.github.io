Long on Top Short on Sides Men's Haircut

Looking for a stylish and versatile haircut? You might want to try the long on top short on sides men's haircut. This haircut has been trending for quite some time now and for good reason. It's a classic hairstyle that adds a touch of sophistication to any man's look. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to get the Long on Top Short on Sides Men's Haircut?
  • Step by step guide
  • Tips for styling and maintaining the haircut
  • FAQ

How to get the Long on Top Short on Sides Men's Haircut?

Getting this haircut is relatively easy. All you need to do is ask your barber or stylist to cut the sides and back of your hair short while keeping the top long. You can also ask for a fade or undercut on the sides for a more edgy look. It's important to communicate with your stylist to get the exact look that you want.

Step by step guide

1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
2. Towel dry your hair and comb it to remove any tangles.
3. Section your hair into three parts â the top, sides, and back.
4. Use clippers or scissors to cut the sides and back of your hair short.
5. Gradually blend the sides into the top to create a seamless transition.
6. Style the top of your hair using a hairdryer and a styling product of your choice.
7. Use your fingers or a comb to create your desired look.
8. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for styling and maintaining the haircut

- Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
- Use a good quality styling product that works well with your hair type.
- Visit your stylist regularly to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
- Avoid washing your hair too often as it can strip away natural oils.
- Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools.

FAQ

Q: How long should the top of my hair be for this haircut?
A: It depends on your preference, but typically, the top should be at least 2-3 inches long.

Q: Can I still get this haircut if I have curly hair?
A: Absolutely! In fact, this haircut works well with curly hair as it adds volume and texture to the top.

Q: How often should I visit my stylist to maintain the haircut?
A: It's recommended to visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

Hair care tips

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
- Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
- Avoid using hot tools too often as it can cause heat damage.
- Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.
- Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
