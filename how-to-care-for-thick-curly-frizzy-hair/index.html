How To Care For Thick Curly Frizzy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Sep 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Thick, curly, and frizzy hair can be a challenge to manage. Without the proper care, it can frizz up and become unmanageable. In this article, we'll be discussing some tips and tricks on how to care for thick curly frizzy hair, including how to prevent frizz and maintain your hair's health.

Table of Contents

  • Tip 1: Shampoo Less Often
  • Tip 2: Use a Deep Conditioner
  • Tip 3: Dry Your Hair with a T-Shirt
  • Tip 4: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb
  • Tip 5: Apply an Anti-Frizz Serum
  • FAQs

Tip 1: Shampoo Less Often

One of the biggest mistakes people with curly hair make is washing their hair too often. Shampooing your hair strips your scalp of its natural oils, leaving your hair dry and frizzy. Instead, try washing your hair every two to three days.

Tip 2: Use a Deep Conditioner

Deep conditioning is an essential step in caring for thick curly frizzy hair. It helps to moisturize and nourish your hair, leaving it soft and manageable. Use a deep conditioner once a week or as often as needed. Apply the conditioner to your hair and leave it on for 20-30 minutes before rinsing it off thoroughly.

Tip 3: Dry Your Hair with a T-Shirt

After washing your hair, avoid using a towel to dry your hair. Towels can cause frizz and damage to your hair. Instead, use a soft t-shirt to dry your hair. Simply wrap the t-shirt around your hair and squeeze gently to remove excess water.

Tip 4: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage and damage to your hair. Start by combing your hair from the ends and work your way up to the roots. Avoid using a brush, as it can cause your hair to become frizzy.

Tip 5: Apply an Anti-Frizz Serum

Using an anti-frizz serum is a great way to tame frizz and keep your hair looking smooth and healthy. Apply a small amount of serum to your hair after washing it, and comb it through your hair with your fingers or a wide-tooth comb.

FAQs

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every two to three days to prevent it from becoming dry and frizzy.

Can I use a brush instead of a wide-tooth comb?

No, using a brush can cause your hair to become frizzy and damaged. Use a wide-tooth comb instead to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

Can I use an anti-frizz serum every day?

Yes, you can use an anti-frizz serum every day to tame frizz and keep your hair looking smooth and healthy.

Is it necessary to use a deep conditioner?

Yes, using a deep conditioner is essential for caring for thick curly frizzy hair. It helps to moisturize and nourish your hair, leaving it soft and manageable.

Caring for thick curly frizzy hair can be a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. Remember to shampoo less often, use a deep conditioner, dry your hair with a t-shirt, use a wide-tooth comb, and apply an anti-frizz serum to tame frizz and keep your hair looking smooth and healthy.
