Men s Haircuts Long On Top Short On Sides And Back A Versatile And Stylish Choice

Written by Eveline Nov 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish haircut, men's haircuts long on top short on sides and back could be the perfect choice for you. This haircut has been popular for years and continues to be a favorite among men of all ages. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, tips for maintaining it, and the best hair care products to use.

Description

Men's haircuts long on top short on sides and back are characterized by longer hair on the top of the head that gradually tapers down to shorter hair on the sides and back. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, from a slicked-back look to a messy, textured style. It's a great choice for men who want a stylish and modern look that's easy to maintain.

How to

If you're interested in getting a men's haircut long on top short on sides and back, here's what you need to know:

Step 1: Choose the right length

The first step in getting this haircut is to decide how long you want your hair to be on top. This will depend on your personal style and the look you're going for. Some men prefer a longer, more dramatic look, while others opt for a shorter, more subtle style.

Step 2: Find a skilled barber

Once you've decided on the length you want, it's important to find a skilled barber who can give you the perfect cut. Look for a barber who has experience cutting men's hair and who has a good reputation in the community. You can also ask friends or family members for recommendations.

Step 3: Communicate with your barber

When you go in for your haircut, it's important to communicate clearly with your barber. Let them know the specific length you want on top and how you want the sides and back to be tapered. You can also bring in a photo of the style you're going for to give your barber a better idea of what you want.

Step 4: Style your hair

Once you've got your new haircut, it's time to style your hair. There are a variety of styling products you can use, including pomade, wax, and gel. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and the style you're going for.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your men's haircut long on top short on sides and back:

  • Get regular trims to keep the style looking fresh
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you
  • Don't be afraid to switch up your style and try something new

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's haircuts long on top short on sides and back:

Q: Will this haircut work for me?

A: This haircut can work for most hair types and face shapes. Talk to your barber to see if it's a good choice for you.

Q: How often do I need to get a trim?

A: You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking fresh.

Q: What styling products should I use?

A: It depends on your hair type and the style you're going for. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care products that can help you maintain your men's haircut long on top short on sides and back:

  • Shampoo and Conditioner: Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Pomade: Pomade is a great styling product for this haircut. It provides a medium hold with a shiny finish.
  • Hairspray: Hairspray can help keep your style in place all day long.

With the right styling products and regular trims, you can easily maintain your men's haircut long on top short on sides and back. This versatile and stylish haircut is a great choice for men who want a modern look that's easy to maintain.
