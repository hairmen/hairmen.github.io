Why Is My Hair Curlier In The Summer

Written by Bardi Jul 18, 2023 · 3 min read

Summer is a season that comes with a lot of changes, and one of the most noticeable changes is the way our hair behaves. Many people have noticed that their hair becomes curlier in the summer. This can be both exciting and frustrating, depending on your hair type and the look you’re going for. In this article, we’ll explore why your hair is curlier in the summer and what you can do to embrace your newfound curls.

Description

During the summer, the humidity in the air increases, and this can have a significant impact on your hair. Humidity is the amount of moisture in the air, and when there’s more moisture in the air, it can affect the hydrogen bonds in your hair. Hydrogen bonds are what give your hair its shape, and when they’re disrupted, your hair can become frizzy, curly, or wavy.

How to

If you want to embrace your curly hair in the summer, there are a few things you can do to enhance your curls. First, use a shampoo and conditioner that’s specially formulated for curly hair. These products will help keep your curls defined and moisturized. Second, avoid brushing or combing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet. Third, use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair, as this will help enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step tips for embracing your curly hair in the summer:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner formulated for curly hair
  • Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers when it’s wet
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it’s dry
  • Use a satin pillowcase to reduce frizz while you sleep
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz

Tips

Here are some additional tips for embracing your curly hair in the summer:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair and make it frizzy
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with UV protection
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids or buns, to keep your hair off your face and reduce frizz

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about why hair is curlier in the summer:

Q: Why does humidity make hair curly?
A: Humidity affects the hydrogen bonds in your hair, which can cause it to become curly or frizzy. Q: Can I make my hair less curly in the summer?
A: While you can use products to help keep your curls defined and reduce frizz, it’s difficult to completely change your hair’s natural texture. Q: Does the length of my hair affect how curly it is in the summer?
A: Yes, the longer your hair is, the more it will be affected by humidity and become curlier.

Hair Care

Embracing your curly hair in the summer requires a bit of extra care, but it’s worth it to have beautiful, defined curls. Remember to use products that are formulated for curly hair, detangle your hair when it’s wet, and avoid using heat styling tools. With a little bit of effort, you can have gorgeous, curly hair all summer long.
