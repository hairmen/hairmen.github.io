How To Make Guys Hair Curly With Rubber Bands

Written by Oliver Aug 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to try something new? Do you want to make your hair curly without using any heat or chemicals? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will teach you how to make guys hair curly with rubber bands. This method is easy, affordable, and safe for your hair. Read on to learn more.

Description

Curly hair is a popular hairstyle among guys. It gives a cool and trendy look to your personality. However, not everyone is blessed with naturally curly hair. That's where rubber bands come in handy. By using rubber bands, you can make your hair curly without damaging it. Rubber bands are easily available, and you can find them in any local store or online.

How to

Follow these simple steps to make your hair curly with rubber bands:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup from your hair, making it easier to work with.

Step 2: Apply mousse or gel

Apply a small amount of mousse or gel to your hair. This will help to hold your curls in place and make them last longer.

Step 3: Divide your hair into sections

Divide your hair into small sections, depending on how tight you want your curls to be. The smaller the section, the tighter the curl will be.

Step 4: Wrap rubber bands around each section

Take a rubber band and wrap it around the base of each section of hair. Make sure the rubber band is tight enough to hold your hair in place but not too tight that it causes discomfort.

Step 5: Leave the rubber bands in overnight

Once you have wrapped all the sections of your hair, leave the rubber bands in overnight. This will give your hair enough time to set and form curls.

Step 6: Remove the rubber bands

The next morning, carefully remove the rubber bands from your hair. Use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create volume.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your hair curly with rubber bands:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair before wrapping it with rubber bands.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair before wrapping it with rubber bands to prevent frizz.
  • For tighter curls, wrap your hair with smaller sections and use more rubber bands.
  • For looser curls, wrap your hair with bigger sections and use fewer rubber bands.
  • Experiment with different types of mousse or gel to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making guys hair curly with rubber bands:

1. Will this method damage my hair?

No, this method is safe and will not damage your hair if done correctly. However, it's important not to wrap the rubber bands too tightly, as this can cause discomfort and breakage.

2. How long will the curls last?

The curls can last for a few days, depending on how well you maintain them. To make them last longer, avoid touching your hair too much and use a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

3. Can I use this method on any hair type?

Yes, this method works on all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips to maintain healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or products on your hair.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and strong, and try out different hairstyles without damaging it.

Conclusion

Making guys hair curly with rubber bands is an easy and affordable way to try out a new hairstyle. By following the steps and tips mentioned in this article, you can achieve perfect curls without damaging your hair. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong, and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
