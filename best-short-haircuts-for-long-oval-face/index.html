Description

Having a long oval face can be tricky when it comes to choosing the right haircut. But there are plenty of short haircuts that can flatter your face shape and highlight your best features. In this article, we will be discussing the best short haircuts for long oval face.

How to

The first step in choosing the right short haircut for your long oval face is to determine your face shape. A long oval face has a lengthened shape with slightly wider cheekbones and a narrower chin and forehead. Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for short haircuts that will complement your features.

Step by step

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Step 2: Look for short haircuts that complement your features

Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect haircut for you

Tips

When choosing a short haircut for your long oval face, it's important to consider your hair texture and thickness. If you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a layered haircut to add volume and movement. If you have fine hair, a blunt bob or pixie cut can work well to create the illusion of fuller hair.

FAQ

Q: What short haircuts are best for a long oval face?

A: Some of the best short haircuts for a long oval face include a bob, a pixie cut, a layered cut, and a side-swept fringe.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen the perfect short haircut for your long oval face, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut, as well as using the right products for your hair type. A good shampoo and conditioner can help keep your hair healthy and shiny, while styling products can help you achieve your desired look.
