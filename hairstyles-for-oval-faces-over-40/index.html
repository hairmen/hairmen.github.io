Hairstyles For Oval Faces Over 40 A Guide To Looking Your Best

Written by Bardi Aug 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you over 40 and struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your oval face shape? Look no further! In this guide, we'll cover everything from how to determine your face shape to the best hairstyles for oval faces over 40. Let's get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into hairstyles, it's important to determine your face shape. Oval faces are characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, with prominent cheekbones. If this sounds like you, then you have an oval face shape!

Best Hairstyles for Oval Faces Over 40

1. Shoulder-length Lob

The shoulder-length lob is a great option for oval faces over 40. It's a versatile style that can be worn straight or with loose waves. This style also helps to elongate the face, making it look more youthful.

2. Layered Bob

A layered bob is another great option for oval faces over 40. The layers add texture and volume, which can help to balance out the face. This style can be worn straight or with waves for a more relaxed look.

3. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling daring, a pixie cut can be a great option for oval faces over 40. This style can help to accentuate your cheekbones and jawline, while also giving you a more youthful look.

4. Long Layers

If you're not ready to make a big chop, long layers can be a great option for oval faces over 40. This style adds movement and texture to your hair, while still keeping the length you love.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Now that you have some hairstyle ideas, here are a few tips for styling your hair:

1. Use Heat Protectant

If you're using heat tools on your hair, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

2. Invest in Quality Products

Investing in quality hair products can make a big difference in the health and appearance of your hair. Look for products that are specifically formulated for your hair type.

3. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every other day, or even every three days if possible.

FAQs

1. Can I still wear bangs with an oval face?

Yes! Oval faces can wear almost any type of bangs, from blunt to side-swept.

2. What if I have thinning hair?

If you have thinning hair, opt for styles that add volume and texture, like a layered bob or long layers.

3. How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips

Lastly, here are a few general hair care tips to keep in mind:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When brushing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage and damage.

2. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles like ponytails and braids, as they can cause tension and breakage.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Just like your skin, your hair can also be damaged by the sun. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from UV rays.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you'll be looking and feeling your best in no time!
