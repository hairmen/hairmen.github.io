Why Does My Hair In The Back Stick Up

Written by Petter Jun 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are someone who has experienced the frustration of having your hair in the back stick up, you are not alone. Many people wonder why this happens and what they can do to fix it. In this article, we will explore the reasons behind this phenomenon, as well as provide some tips and tricks for taming your unruly hair.

Description

Hair that sticks up in the back can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, hair type, styling techniques, and hair care habits. In some cases, the hair may simply be too short to lay flat against the scalp, while in other cases, it may be too thick or textured to be easily managed.

How To

If you are looking for ways to address this issue, there are a few things you can try:

1. Adjust your hair care routine

Make sure you are using the right products for your hair type and texture. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy, and avoid using too much heat or chemicals on your hair.

2. Try different styling techniques

If your hair tends to stick up in the back, experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you. You may want to try using a hairbrush to smooth your hair down, or using a styling product like gel or mousse to help hold your hair in place.

3. Get a haircut

If your hair is too short to lay flat against your scalp, consider getting a haircut that is longer in the back than in the front. This can help to create a more balanced look and make it easier to style your hair.

Step by Step

If you are unsure how to address your hair sticking up in the back, follow these steps:

1. Identify the cause

Take a close look at your hair and try to identify what is causing it to stick up in the back. Is it too short? Too thick? Too textured? Once you know what is causing the problem, you can start to address it.

2. Adjust your hair care routine

Make sure you are using the right products for your hair type and texture. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy, and avoid using too much heat or chemicals on your hair.

3. Try different styling techniques

Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you. You may want to try using a hairbrush to smooth your hair down, or using a styling product like gel or mousse to help hold your hair in place.

4. Get a haircut

If your hair is too short to lay flat against your scalp, consider getting a haircut that is longer in the back than in the front. This can help to create a more balanced look and make it easier to style your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for taming your unruly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too frequently
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help smooth your hair
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment or other professional hair smoothing treatment

FAQ

Q: Is hair that sticks up in the back a sign of damage?

A: Not necessarily. Hair that sticks up in the back can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, hair type, styling techniques, and hair care habits. However, if your hair is dry, brittle, or damaged, it may be more prone to sticking up.

Q: Can I use hairspray to hold my hair down?

A: Yes, hairspray can be a great way to hold your hair in place. Just be sure to use a light hold hairspray and apply it sparingly to avoid weighing down your hair or creating a stiff, unnatural look.

Q: Should I brush my hair when it's wet or dry?

A: It's best to avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and damage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair when it's damp, and wait until it's dry to brush it.

Hair Care

If you are struggling with hair that sticks up in the back, don't despair. With the right hair care routine, styling techniques, and a little patience, you can achieve a sleek and polished look that will leave you feeling confident and beautiful.
