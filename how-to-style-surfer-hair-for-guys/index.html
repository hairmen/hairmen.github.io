How To Style Surfer Hair For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 12, 2023 · 5 min read

Surfer hair is a laid-back, beachy style that's perfect for guys who want to look effortlessly cool. Whether you're hitting the waves or just hanging out with friends, surfer hair is a great way to show off your laid-back vibe. In this guide, we'll show you how to style surfer hair for guys, as well as provide some tips and tricks for keeping your hair looking great.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Surfer Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Surfer Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Style Surfer Hair

Surfer hair is all about that effortless, beachy look. The key is to keep your hair looking natural and relaxed, with just the right amount of texture and volume. Here's how to get the surfer hair look:

1. Grow out your hair

The first step to achieving surfer hair is to grow out your hair. You want your hair to be long enough to create some waves and texture, but not so long that it becomes unmanageable. Aim for hair that's around 3-4 inches long.

2. Get the right haircut

Once your hair is long enough, you'll want to get a haircut that's tailored to your hair type and texture. For surfer hair, you'll want to keep the sides and back of your hair shorter, while leaving the top longer. A layered cut can add some texture and movement to your hair, which is perfect for the surfer look.

3. Use the right products

When it comes to styling surfer hair, less is more. You want to use products that will enhance your hair's natural texture and create some volume, without weighing it down. A sea salt spray or texturizing spray can help create those beachy waves, while a light hold styling cream or wax can add some definition and hold.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling surfer hair:

1. Start with clean, damp hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, then towel dry until your hair is damp but not dripping wet.

2. Apply sea salt spray

Spray a sea salt spray or texturizing spray all over your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. Use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair, creating some volume and texture.

3. Blow-dry your hair

Use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to dry your hair, scrunching and tousling as you go. You want to create some volume and movement, so don't worry too much about getting your hair perfectly smooth.

4. Add some styling cream or wax

Once your hair is dry, add a small amount of styling cream or wax to your fingertips and work it through your hair, focusing on the ends. This will add some definition and hold to your style.

5. Finish with a light hairspray

Finish off your look with a light hairspray to hold everything in place. You don't want your hair to look too stiff or crunchy, so choose a hairspray with a light hold.

Tips for Maintaining Surfer Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your surfer hair:

1. Don't over-wash your hair

Surfer hair looks best when it's a little bit dirty, so don't wash your hair too often. Aim for washing your hair every other day, or even every few days.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Since you'll be spending a lot of time in the sun and salt water, your hair can become dry and damaged. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about surfer hair:

What hair type is best for surfer hair?

Surfer hair looks best on hair that's naturally wavy or has some texture. If you have straight hair, you can still achieve the surfer look with the right products and styling techniques.

How do I keep my surfer hair from looking too messy?

The key to surfer hair is to keep it looking natural and relaxed, but not too messy. Use a light hold styling product and don't be too heavy-handed with the sea salt spray or texturizing spray.

Can I still achieve surfer hair if I have short hair?

While surfer hair looks best on medium-length hair, you can still achieve the look with shorter hair. Ask your stylist for a layered cut that will add some texture and movement to your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that's gentle and won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for products with natural ingredients like aloe vera and coconut oil.

2. Don't over-style your hair

While styling products can help enhance your hair's natural texture and hold, don't overdo it. Too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

3. Get regular haircuts

Regular haircuts can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve the perfect surfer hair look. Remember, the key is to keep it relaxed, natural, and effortless. So grab your board and hit the waves with confidence!
