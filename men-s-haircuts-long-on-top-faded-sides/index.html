Description

Men's haircuts long on top faded sides has been the latest trend in 2023. This haircut features a long top with faded sides, creating a sleek and modern look. The length on top allows for versatility in styling, while the faded sides add a touch of edge and masculinity. This haircut is perfect for men who want to look stylish and put-together without sacrificing their masculinity.

How to Get the Look

The men's haircuts long on top faded sides requires a skilled barber or stylist who can create the perfect fade and blend it seamlessly into the longer hair on top. It's important to communicate your desired length and style with your barber or stylist to ensure that you get the look you want.

Choosing the Right Length

The length on top can vary depending on personal preference, but generally, it should be longer than the sides. This allows for more styling options and creates a more dramatic contrast with the faded sides. Consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing the length on top.

Choosing the Right Fade

The fade on the sides can also vary depending on personal preference. A low fade starts near the ear and gradually fades into the longer hair on top, while a high fade starts closer to the temples and creates a more dramatic contrast. It's important to choose the right fade for your face shape and hair texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting the men's haircuts long on top faded sides:

  1. Communicate your desired length and style with your barber or stylist.
  2. Start with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to prepare your hair.
  3. Use clippers to create the desired fade on the sides.
  4. Blend the fade into the longer hair on top using scissors or clippers.
  5. Style the hair on top using your preferred product.

Tips and Tricks

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and manageable.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly to maintain the fade and length on top.

FAQs

Is the men's haircuts long on top faded sides suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut can work for most hair types, but it's important to choose the right length and style for your hair type and texture.

How often do I need to visit my barber or stylist to maintain this haircut?

You should visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the fade and length on top.

Hair Care for Men

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips for hair care for men:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Overall, the men's haircuts long on top faded sides is a stylish and modern haircut that can work for most men. It's important to choose the right length and style for your hair type and face shape and to maintain the fade and length with regular visits to your barber or stylist.
