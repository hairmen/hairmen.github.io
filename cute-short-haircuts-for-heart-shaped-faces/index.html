Cute Short Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces 2023 Trending Styles

Written by Eveline Oct 13, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, congratulations! You have one of the most coveted face shapes out there. With its wider forehead and narrower chin, it’s no wonder why many celebrities and models have this face shape. However, finding the perfect haircut to complement your features can be a challenge. That's why we’ve put together a list of the cutest short haircuts for heart-shaped faces that you can try out in 2023.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling

Description

Heart-shaped faces are characterized by a wider forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The goal is to balance out the proportions of your face and highlight your best features. Short haircuts are perfect for heart-shaped faces because they accentuate the cheekbones and draw attention away from the forehead. We’ve rounded up the cute short haircuts for heart-shaped faces that are trending this year.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

Before choosing a haircut, it’s important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Heart-shaped faces look best with haircuts that are chin-length or shorter. Pixie cuts, bobs, and asymmetrical cuts work well for this face shape. If you have thick hair, a layered bob or shaggy cut can help to thin it out. If you have fine hair, a blunt bob or pixie cut can add volume.

Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in the best haircut for your face shape. If you have curly hair, a bob or shaggy cut can help to define your curls. If you have straight hair, a blunt bob or pixie cut can add dimension.

Think About Your Personal Style

Your personal style is also important when choosing a haircut. If you prefer a more edgy look, an asymmetrical cut or choppy layers can fit your style. If you prefer a classic look, a blunt bob or pixie cut can be a great choice.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have an idea of what to look for in a haircut, here’s a step-by-step guide on how to achieve some of the cutest short haircuts for heart-shaped faces.

Pixie Cut

To achieve a pixie cut:

  1. Start with dry hair.
  2. Create a side part.
  3. Use clippers to buzz the sides and back of your head.
  4. Use scissors to trim the top and front of your hair to the desired length.
  5. Add texture by using a texturizing spray or wax.

Blunt Bob

To achieve a blunt bob:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Create a center part.
  3. Use scissors to cut your hair to chin-length.
  4. Add texture by using a texturizing spray or wax.

Asymmetrical Cut

To achieve an asymmetrical cut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Create a side part.
  3. Use scissors to cut one side of your hair shorter than the other.
  4. Add texture by using a texturizing spray or wax.

Tips for Styling

Now that you have your cute short haircut, here are some tips for styling:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume.
  • Use a texturizing spray or wax to add texture.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to add waves or curls.

FAQs

What are the best short haircuts for heart-shaped faces?

Pixie cuts, bobs, and asymmetrical cuts work well for heart-shaped faces. The goal is to balance out the proportions of your face and highlight your best features.

How do I know if I have a heart-shaped face?

You have a heart-shaped face if you have a wider forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow chin.

Hair Care Tips

Short haircuts require less maintenance than longer hair, but it’s still important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.

Now that you have some ideas for cute short haircuts for heart-shaped faces, it’s time to book an appointment with your hairstylist. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a haircut. With the right haircut and styling, you can enhance your best features and feel confident in your new look.
