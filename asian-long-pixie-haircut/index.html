Asian Long Pixie Haircut A Stylish And Chic Look

Written by Frank Jun 04, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut that is trendy and stylish? Look no further than the Asian long pixie haircut. This haircut is perfect for those who want a shorter style but still want to keep some length. In this blog post, we will show you how to achieve this look, step-by-step, give you tips, and answer frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

Description

The Asian long pixie haircut is a short hairstyle that features longer length on top and shorter sides and back. It is a modern and trendy haircut that is perfect for those who want a low maintenance style. This haircut is great for all hair types, but it looks particularly stunning on straight and fine hair.

How To

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Here are the steps to achieve the Asian long pixie haircut:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the top, left side, right side, and back. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 2: Cut the Sides and Back

Using hair clippers or scissors, cut the sides and back of your hair to your desired length. For a classic Asian long pixie look, keep the sides and back short, but not too short. You want to keep some length on top for a more feminine look.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Using scissors, cut the top of your hair to your desired length. The length should be longer than the sides and back but not too long. You want to create a contrast between the shorter sides and the longer top.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Using scissors, blend the hair on the sides and top. Make sure the hair is evenly layered and there are no harsh lines.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Apply a small amount of hair product to your hair and style as desired. You can add volume to the top by blow drying your hair with a round brush. For a sleeker look, use a straightening iron.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting an Asian long pixie haircut:

  • Bring a picture of the haircut you want to your hairstylist.
  • Be open to your hairstylist's suggestions.
  • Use a high-quality hair product to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the Asian long pixie haircut:

What face shapes does the Asian long pixie haircut suit?

The Asian long pixie haircut suits oval, heart, and square-shaped faces.

Can I dye my hair after getting an Asian long pixie haircut?

Yes, you can dye your hair after getting an Asian long pixie haircut. However, make sure to use a high-quality hair dye and consult with your hairstylist before making any drastic changes.

Is the Asian long pixie haircut suitable for all ages?

Yes, the Asian long pixie haircut is suitable for all ages. It is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your personal style.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping the natural oils from your hair.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Now that you know everything about the Asian long pixie haircut, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and get this stylish and chic look!
