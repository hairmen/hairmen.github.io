Long Hair For Chubby Round Face Male A Guide To Flattering Hairstyles

Written by Aprilia Aug 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Long hair can be a great style choice for men with chubby round faces. It can help balance out the face and create a more elongated look. However, not all long hairstyles are created equal. In this guide, we'll take a look at how to choose the best long hair for your face shape, as well as some tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

Men with chubby round faces often struggle to find hairstyles that suit them. Long hair can be a great option, as it can create the illusion of a longer, more angular face. However, it's important to choose the right style to avoid making the face appear even rounder.

There are a few key factors to consider when choosing a long hairstyle for a chubby round face. These include the length of the hair, the cut and style, and the texture and thickness of the hair.

How to Choose the Right Long Hair for Chubby Round Face Male

When choosing a long hairstyle for a chubby round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. Here are some tips:

Length

The length of the hair is important when it comes to creating a flattering look for a chubby round face. Generally speaking, hair that falls past the shoulders is best, as it can help elongate the face. However, if you have very thick hair, you may want to consider a shorter length to avoid overwhelming the face.

Cut and Style

The cut and style of the hair is also important. Layers can help add texture and movement to the hair, which can be flattering for a chubby round face. Avoid blunt cuts and straight styles, as these can make the face appear even rounder.

Texture and Thickness

The texture and thickness of the hair can also play a role in determining the best long hairstyle for a chubby round face. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider thinning it out to avoid overwhelming the face. If you have fine hair, adding texture and volume can help create a more balanced look.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Chubby Round Face Male

Once you've chosen the right long hairstyle for your face shape, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step by step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suited to your hair type. Be sure to rinse thoroughly to avoid any product build-up.

Step 2: Apply Products

Apply any styling products you like to use, such as mousse or gel, to damp hair. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout the hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair, using your fingers or a brush to create the desired style. If you have very thick hair, you may want to use a diffuser attachment to help distribute the heat evenly.

Step 4: Finish

Once your hair is dry, use a styling product to create the final look. You may want to use a hair spray to help hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair for Chubby Round Face Male

Long hair can be a bit more high maintenance than shorter styles, so it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

Deep Conditioning Treatments

Deep conditioning treatments can help keep your hair soft and shiny. Use a deep conditioner once a week or as needed.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, can damage your hair over time. Try to limit your use of these tools, and always use a heat protectant spray.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun's rays can damage your hair, so be sure to wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF when spending time outdoors.

FAQs

Can men with chubby round faces wear long hair?

Yes, men with chubby round faces can wear long hair. However, it's important to choose the right style to create a flattering look.

What is the best length for long hair on a chubby round face?

Hair that falls past the shoulders is generally the most flattering length for men with chubby round faces.

What styling products are best for long hair on a chubby round face?

Styling products that add texture and movement to the hair, such as mousse or gel, can be helpful. Be sure to choose products that are suited to your hair type.

Hair Care for Long Hair for Chubby Round Face Male

In addition to regular trims and deep conditioning treatments, there are a few other things you can do to take good care of your long hair:

Use a Wide Tooth Comb

Using a wide tooth comb can help prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles, such as braids or ponytails, can put stress on your hair and lead to breakage. Try to avoid these styles or wear them loosely.

Wash Your Hair Less Frequently

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and damage. Try to wash your hair no more than every other day.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently rock long hair as a male with a chubby round face. Experiment with different styles and find the one that works best for you.
