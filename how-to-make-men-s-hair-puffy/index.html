How To Make Men s Hair Puffy A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of flat, lifeless hair? Do you want to achieve that voluminous, puffy look that many men are sporting these days? Look no further! In this article, we'll show you how to make men's hair puffy in just a few easy steps.

Description: What is Puffy Hair?

Puffy hair is a hairstyle that has volume and height. It's a popular trend for men that gives the appearance of a fuller head of hair. It's achieved by adding volume to the roots, which lifts the hair and creates a puffy effect.

How to Make Men's Hair Puffy: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to achieving puffy hair is to start with clean hair. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair extra body and lift. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry.

Step 2: Apply Mousse

Apply a golf ball-sized amount of mousse to your hair. Focus on the roots, applying the mousse in an upward motion. This will give your hair lift and volume.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair in an upward motion. Use your fingers to lift your hair at the roots as you blow dry. This will create even more volume.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Using a fine-toothed comb, tease your hair at the roots. This will add even more volume and lift to your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your puffy hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold your hair in place and prevent it from falling flat throughout the day.

Tips for Making Men's Hair Puffy

Here are a few additional tips to help you achieve the perfect puffy hairstyle:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add extra body and lift to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair in an upward motion to create even more volume.
  • Tease your hair at the roots for added lift and volume.
  • Use a light mist of hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.
  • Consider using a round brush to add even more volume to your hair.

FAQs About Making Men's Hair Puffy

Q: Will puffy hair work for all hair types?

A: Puffy hair works best for those with medium to long hair. If you have short hair, it may be more difficult to achieve the puffy look.

Q: How long will my puffy hairstyle last?

A: Your puffy hairstyle should last throughout the day with the help of hairspray. However, if you have particularly fine or oily hair, it may fall flat more easily.

Q: Can I make my hair puffy without using heat?

A: Yes! You can achieve a puffy hairstyle without using heat by applying mousse to your damp hair and letting it air dry. Once your hair is dry, tease it at the roots for added volume.

Hair Care for Puffy Hair

To keep your puffy hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, as this will cause less damage than a brush.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and frizzy.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Now that you know how to make men's hair puffy, it's time to give it a try! With a little bit of effort and the right products, you can achieve the puffy, voluminous hairstyle you've been dreaming of.
