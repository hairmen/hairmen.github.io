How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers Step By Step

Written by Robby Aug 15, 2023 · 5 min read

Welcome to the ultimate guide on how to cut men's hair with clippers step by step. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned barber, this article will provide you with all the information you need to give a great haircut using clippers. In this article, we'll cover the basics of how to use clippers, the different types of cuts you can create, and some tips for maintaining healthy hair.

How to Use Clippers

Before you start cutting hair, it's important to know how to use clippers properly. Here are some basic steps:

  1. Choose the right clippers for the job. You'll need to choose clippers with the right blade length, motor power, and cord length. Make sure you choose a high-quality clipper that will last.
  2. Attach the right blade guard. Choose a blade guard that will give you the desired length of hair. The longer the guard, the longer the hair will be.
  3. Start with clean, dry hair. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut, and can cause the clippers to rust.
  4. Comb the hair to remove any knots or tangles. This will make it easier to cut the hair evenly.
  5. Turn on the clippers and start cutting. Start at the back of the head and work your way forward, using even strokes.
  6. Trim the sides and back of the head first, then move to the top of the head. Use a slightly longer blade guard when cutting the top of the head.
  7. Clean the clippers and blade guards after each use. Use a brush to remove any hair and oil the blades to prevent rust.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know how to use clippers, let's go over a step by step guide for cutting hair with clippers:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

Choose clippers that are high-quality and have a powerful motor. Look for clippers with adjustable blade guards and a long cord. Make sure the clippers are comfortable to hold and easy to use.

Step 2: Decide on the Haircut

Before you start cutting, decide on the type of haircut you want to give. Look at pictures of different haircuts for inspiration. You can choose from buzz cuts, fades, crew cuts, and more.

Step 3: Prepare the Hair

Start with clean, dry hair. Comb the hair to remove any knots or tangles. Use a spray bottle to moisten the hair if it's too dry.

Step 4: Choose the Right Blade Guard

Choose a blade guard that will give you the desired length of hair. The longer the guard, the longer the hair will be. Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want the hair to be shorter.

Step 5: Start Cutting

Turn on the clippers and start cutting. Start at the back of the head and work your way forward, using even strokes. Use a shorter guard on the sides and back of the head, and a slightly longer guard on the top of the head.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Use a blending tool to blend the hair between different lengths. This will create a smooth, even transition between the different lengths of hair.

Step 7: Clean Up

Clean up any stray hairs around the ears and neckline. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that were missed by the clippers.

Different Types of Haircuts

Here are some of the most popular haircuts that can be created using clippers:

  • Buzz Cut: A short, even cut all over the head.
  • Fade: A gradual transition from short to long hair.
  • Crew Cut: Short hair on the sides and back, longer hair on top.
  • Undercut: Short hair on the sides and back, longer hair on top that is slicked back.
  • Textured Crop: Short hair on the sides and back, longer hair on top that is styled with texture.

Tips for Cutting Hair with Clippers

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer blade guard and work your way down if the hair needs to be shorter.
  • Use a blending tool to create a smooth transition between different lengths of hair.
  • Clean the clippers and blade guards after each use to prevent rust.
  • Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that were missed by the clippers.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting hair with clippers takes practice.

FAQs

Can I use clippers to cut my own hair?

Yes, you can use clippers to cut your own hair. However, it can be difficult to cut the back of your head, so you may need someone to help you.

Can I use clippers to cut wet hair?

It's best to cut hair when it's dry, as wet hair can be more difficult to cut and can cause the clippers to rust.

How often should I clean my clippers?

You should clean your clippers after each use to prevent rust and keep them in good condition.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair with clippers step by step, you're ready to give your first haircut. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. With practice, you'll be able to give great haircuts in no time.
