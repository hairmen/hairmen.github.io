Description

The slicked back long haircut has been a popular style for men for generations. This timeless look is achieved by combing the hair backwards and using a styling product to keep it in place. The length of the hair can vary, but it is typically longer on top and shorter on the sides and back. This style is perfect for men who want to look stylish and put-together without spending too much time on their hair.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve the slicked back long haircut, you will need to have longer hair on top and shorter hair on the sides and back. You can either grow your hair out or ask your barber for a cut that will give you the desired length. Once you have the right length, you will need to use a styling product to keep your hair in place.

Styling Products

There are a variety of styling products that you can use to achieve the slicked back look. Here are some of the most popular options:

  • Pomade: Pomade is a popular choice because it provides a strong hold and a shiny finish. It is perfect for achieving a classic, retro look.
  • Gel: Gel is another option for achieving the slicked back look. It provides a strong hold, but can make your hair look crunchy if you use too much.
  • Wax: Wax is a good choice if you want a more natural look. It provides a medium hold and a matte finish.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide for achieving the slicked back long haircut:

  1. Wash your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner to remove any buildup and make sure your hair is clean.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it is damp, but not wet.
  3. Apply a styling product to your hair. Start with a small amount and add more as needed. Make sure to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair.
  4. Use a comb to comb your hair backwards. Start at the front and work your way back.
  5. Use your fingers to smooth out any bumps or lumps in your hair.
  6. Use a hair dryer on low heat to dry your hair. Use a comb to guide your hair back as you dry it.
  7. Apply a final layer of styling product to hold your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

Here are some tips for maintaining your slicked back long haircut:

  • Visit your barber regularly to keep your hair trimmed and in good condition.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and style.

FAQs

How long does my hair need to be for a slicked back long haircut?

Your hair should be at least 4-6 inches long on top to achieve the slicked back look.

Can I achieve the slicked back long look with curly hair?

Yes, you can achieve the slicked back look with curly hair. However, you may need to use a stronger styling product to keep your curls in place.

How long will the slicked back style last?

The slicked back style can last all day if you use a good styling product and avoid touching your hair too much.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips for maintaining a healthy and stylish head of hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get plenty of sleep and exercise to reduce stress, which can lead to hair loss and other hair problems.

With these tips and a little bit of practice, you can achieve and maintain the slicked back long haircut for a timeless and stylish look that will never go out of fashion.
