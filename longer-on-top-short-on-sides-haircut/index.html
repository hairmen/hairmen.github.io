Longer On Top Short On Sides Haircut The Trendy Style Of 2023

Are you tired of the same old haircut? Want to try something new and trendy? Then you should go for the longer on top short on sides haircut. It's the latest style that is taking the world by storm. In this blog post, we will discuss everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, tips, and hair care.

Description

The longer on top short on sides haircut, also known as the undercut, is a trendy and stylish haircut that involves leaving the hair longer on the top while keeping the sides short. It's a versatile haircut that suits almost all face shapes and hair types. This haircut is perfect for those who want to experiment with their hairstyle and want to stand out from the crowd.

How to get the Longer on Top Short on Sides Haircut

To get the longer on top short on sides haircut, you need to visit a professional hairstylist who is experienced in this style. Before getting the haircut, make sure you have a clear idea of how you want your hair to look. You can bring pictures of the style you like to show your hairstylist.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to getting the longer on top short on sides haircut:

  1. Wash and dry your hair before going to the salon.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist about the style you want.
  3. Section the hair on top and clip it away from the sides.
  4. Start cutting the sides with a clipper, leaving the hair short. The length depends on your preference.
  5. Blend the sides with the top by using scissors or clippers with guards.
  6. Texturize the hair on top by using scissors or a razor.
  7. Style the hair on top as desired.
  8. Apply a hair product to hold the style in place.

Tips to Maintain the Haircut

Here are some tips to maintain your longer on top short on sides haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep the haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Apply a hair product such as wax or pomade to style the hair on top.
  • Use a comb or brush to style the hair on top.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about the longer on top short on sides haircut:

Q: Is the longer on top short on sides haircut suitable for all hair types?
A: Yes, it's suitable for all hair types. Q: How often do I need to visit the hairstylist to maintain the haircut?
A: You need to visit the hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the haircut. Q: Can I style the hair on top in different ways?
A: Yes, you can style the hair on top in different ways, depending on your preference.

Hair Care for the Longer on Top Short on Sides Haircut

Here are some hair care tips for the longer on top short on sides haircut:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair.

Now that you know everything about the longer on top short on sides haircut, it's time to book your appointment with your hairstylist and try out this trendy style.
