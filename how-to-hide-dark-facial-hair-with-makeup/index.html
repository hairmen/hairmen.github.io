How To Hide Dark Facial Hair With Makeup

Written by Petter Jun 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of feeling self-conscious about dark facial hair? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle with this issue, but there's a solution. With a few makeup tricks, you can easily hide your facial hair and feel confident in your own skin. In this article, we'll share with you how to hide dark facial hair with makeup, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Hide Dark Facial Hair with Makeup
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

How to Hide Dark Facial Hair with Makeup

The first step to hiding dark facial hair is to choose the right makeup products. You'll need a color corrector, foundation, and concealer. Look for a color corrector that is opposite to the color of your facial hair. For example, if you have dark facial hair, you'll need a peach or orange color corrector. If you have light facial hair, you'll need a green color corrector.

Once you have your color corrector, foundation, and concealer, you can start the application process. Here's how:

Step by Step

  1. Apply the color corrector directly onto the area with dark facial hair. Use your fingers or a brush to blend it in.
  2. Apply foundation over the color corrector. Blend it in with a makeup sponge or brush.
  3. Apply concealer on top of the foundation. Use a shade that matches your skin tone. Blend it in with a makeup sponge or brush.
  4. Set your makeup with a translucent powder. This will prevent it from smudging or rubbing off throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you hide dark facial hair with makeup:

  • Make sure your skin is clean and moisturized before applying makeup. This will help the makeup go on smoothly and last longer.
  • Use a primer to help your makeup stay in place.
  • If you have very thick or coarse facial hair, you may need to trim it before applying makeup.
  • Don't overdo it with makeup. Use a light hand and build up coverage gradually.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hiding dark facial hair with makeup:

Can I use a regular concealer instead of a color corrector?

You can, but a color corrector will provide better coverage. Regular concealer may not be able to completely cover dark facial hair.

Will the makeup rub off throughout the day?

If you set your makeup with a translucent powder, it should stay in place throughout the day. However, if you sweat or touch your face a lot, it may rub off.

Is it safe to use makeup to hide facial hair?

Yes, it is safe to use makeup to hide facial hair. Just make sure you're using high-quality, non-irritating products and removing your makeup at the end of the day.

Hair Care

In addition to using makeup to hide dark facial hair, you may also want to consider hair removal methods. Here are some options:

  • Shaving: This is a quick and easy method, but the hair will grow back quickly.
  • Waxing: This method removes hair from the root, so it lasts longer than shaving. However, it can be painful.
  • Laser hair removal: This method is more permanent than shaving or waxing, but it can be expensive and may require multiple sessions.

Ultimately, the method you choose will depend on your personal preference and budget. Just remember, there's no one right way to deal with dark facial hair. If you're comfortable with it, embrace it! If you're not, there are plenty of options available to help you hide it.
