Best Hairstyles For Big Square Faces

If you have a big square face, you might find it challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your features. However, worry no more as we have curated a list of the best hairstyles that will flatter your beautiful face shape. Let's dive right in!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's essential to determine your face shape to ensure that you get the right haircut. Here's how to determine your face shape:

  • Measure your forehead's width, cheekbones, and jawline with a measuring tape.
  • If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are relatively the same width, you have a square face shape.
  • Your jawline is sharp and angular, and your face is symmetrical.

Best Hairstyles for Big Square Faces

Now that you have determined your face shape, let's look at the best hairstyles that flatter big square faces:

Soft Waves

Soft waves soften the sharp angles of a square face, making it look more feminine. You can achieve this look by using a curling iron or hot rollers, then combing your hair with your fingers to loosen the curls.

Long Layers

Long layers add volume and texture to your hair, making it look more voluminous and bouncy. This hairstyle also draws attention away from your jawline, making your face look more balanced.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are an excellent option for big square faces as they soften the sharp angles and make them look more feminine. You can sweep the bangs to the side or part them in the middle, depending on your preference.

Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a versatile hairstyle that flatters a square face shape. You can opt for a long bob, a chin-length bob, or a layered bob, depending on your preference. This hairstyle also draws attention away from your jawline, making your face look more balanced.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and edgy hairstyle that flatters square faces. This hairstyle accentuates your cheekbones and draws attention away from your jawline. You can opt for a classic pixie cut or a textured pixie cut, depending on your preference.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you have chosen your hairstyle, here are some tips to help you maintain it:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hair color flatters a square face?

Warm hair colors like honey blonde, caramel, and auburn flatter square faces. You can also opt for highlights or balayage to add dimension and depth to your hair.

Can I wear a high ponytail with a square face?

A high ponytail accentuates your jawline, making it more prominent. Instead, opt for a low ponytail or a side ponytail to draw attention away from your jawline.

Hair Care Tips for Big Square Faces

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to ensure that your hair gets the essential nutrients it needs to grow healthy and strong.

With these hairstyles and hair care tips, you can rock your big square face with confidence. Remember to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel comfortable and confident, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one for you. Happy hairstyling!
