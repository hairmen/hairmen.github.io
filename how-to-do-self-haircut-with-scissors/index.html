How To Do Self Haircut With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to save some money and time, cutting your own hair might be the way to go. But if you're not sure how to do it or where to start, don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to do self haircut with scissors.

Description

Many people opt for a self haircut with scissors because it's a convenient way to keep their hair looking neat without having to go to a salon or barbershop. It's also a great way to save some money and time. However, cutting your own hair can be a bit tricky, especially if you've never done it before. But with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to master the art of self haircut with scissors in no time.

How to

Step 1: Gather your tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather your tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Make sure that your scissors are sharp because dull scissors can damage your hair and make it look uneven.

Step 2: Wash and dry your hair

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to wash and dry it. This will help you get rid of any dirt or oils that might be on your hair. Make sure that your hair is completely dry before you start cutting it. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut and can result in uneven hair.

Step 3: Decide on the length

Decide on the length that you want for your hair. You can use a ruler or a measuring tape to measure the length of your hair. Once you've decided on the length, use your comb to separate your hair into sections. This will help you cut your hair evenly.

Step 4: Start cutting

Start cutting your hair by holding a section of hair between your fingers and pulling it straight. Use your scissors to trim the ends of the hair. Make sure that you cut the hair straight across and not at an angle. Repeat this process with each section of hair until you've cut all of your hair.

Step 5: Check your work

Once you've finished cutting your hair, use your mirror to check your work. Make sure that your hair is even and that there are no uneven spots. If you notice any uneven spots, you can use your scissors to trim them.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when doing a self haircut with scissors:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up to the desired length.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once.
  • Be patient and take your time.
  • Use a comb to separate your hair into sections.
  • Use a mirror to check your work as you go.

FAQ

Is it difficult to cut your own hair?

It can be a bit tricky, especially if you've never done it before. But with the right tools and some patience, you can master the art of self haircut with scissors.

Can I use any type of scissors to cut my hair?

No, you should use a pair of sharp scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair. Using dull scissors can damage your hair and make it look uneven.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't worry. It's just hair and it will grow back. You can always trim it again or go to a salon to have it fixed.

Hair Care

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as hair dryers or flat irons.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great after a self haircut with scissors.
