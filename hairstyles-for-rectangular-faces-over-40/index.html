Are you over 40 and struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your rectangular face shape? Look no further! In this guide, we will be discussing various hairstyles that will flatter your face shape and make you look and feel your best.

Description

Rectangular face shapes are characterized by a long, straight jawline, and forehead. The length of the face is longer than the width, and the cheekbones are not very prominent. If you have a rectangular face shape, you want to choose a hairstyle that adds width to the face and softens the angles of the jawline and forehead.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for a rectangular face shape, it's important to consider the following:

  • Length: Short hairstyles are not recommended for rectangular faces, as they can make the face look even longer. Medium to long hairstyles are the best option, as they add width to the face.
  • Layers: Layers can add volume and movement to the hair, which can help soften the angles of a rectangular face.
  • Bangs: Side-swept or curved bangs can help shorten the forehead and balance out the length of the face.
  • Curls: Soft curls or waves can add volume and width to the face, making it look more oval-shaped.

Step by Step Guide

Here are some hairstyles that are perfect for rectangular faces over 40:

1. Shoulder-Length Layers

Shoulder-length layers are a great option for rectangular faces. They add volume and movement to the hair, which can help soften the angles of the jawline and forehead. To style, blow-dry the hair with a round brush and use a curling iron to add soft waves.

2. Long Bob

A long bob, or "lob," is a trendy and stylish option for rectangular faces. This hairstyle adds width to the face and frames the jawline. To style, use a flat iron to create loose waves and add texture to the hair.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help shorten the forehead and balance out the length of the face. This hairstyle works well with medium to long hair lengths. To style, blow-dry the hair with a round brush and use a flat iron to create soft waves.

4. Soft Curls

Soft curls can add volume and width to the face, making it look more oval-shaped. To achieve this look, use a curling iron to create loose curls and brush them out for a soft, natural look.

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips for styling your hair if you have a rectangular face shape:

  • Use volumizing products: Products like mousse and volumizing spray can help add volume to the hair and create a fuller look.
  • Avoid center parts: Center parts can make the face look longer, so it's best to stick with side parts.
  • Blow-dry with a round brush: Blow-drying your hair with a round brush can help add volume and movement to the hair.
  • Avoid sleek, straight hairstyles: Straight hairstyles can make the face look longer and more angular, so it's best to add some texture or waves to the hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair if you have a rectangular face shape:

  • Get regular trims: Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment: Deep conditioning treatments can help nourish and hydrate your hair, keeping it healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid over-styling: Over-styling your hair with heat tools can cause damage and breakage, so it's best to limit your use of these tools.

By following these tips and trying out some of the hairstyles mentioned above, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your rectangular face shape and feel confident and beautiful at any age!
