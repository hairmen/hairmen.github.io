How To Make My Short Curly Hair Look Longer

Written by Daniel Aug 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have short curly hair, you may often wonder how to make it look longer. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make your short curly hair look longer. We will also give you tips on how to maintain your hair and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Content

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

There are several ways to make your short curly hair look longer. You can use hair extensions, straighten your hair, or use a curly hair diffuser. However, the easiest and most effective way to make your short curly hair look longer is by stretching your curls.

Stretching Your Curls

Stretching your curls involves pulling them down to make them longer. Here is how to stretch your curls:

Step by Step

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into four sections.
  4. Take one section and apply a hair oil or cream to it.
  5. Twist the section of hair tightly.
  6. Pull the twisted section of hair downward gently.
  7. Repeat steps 4-6 on the other three sections of hair.
  8. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser to dry it.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make your short curly hair look longer:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair as it can damage your curls.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your hair from tangling while you sleep.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment on your hair once a week.
  • Use a hair mask to moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol as they can dry out your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making short curly hair look longer:

  • Can I use hair extensions to make my short curly hair look longer? Yes, you can use hair extensions to make your hair look longer. However, you should make sure that the extensions match your hair color and texture.
  • How often should I stretch my curls? You should stretch your curls once a week or as needed.
  • How do I maintain my stretched curls? You can maintain your stretched curls by using a curl-defining cream or gel.

Hair Care

To keep your short curly hair healthy and looking great, you should follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can make your short curly hair look longer and keep it healthy and beautiful.
