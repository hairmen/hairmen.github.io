How To Layer The Back Of Your Hair By Yourself

Written by Eveline Dec 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to add some dimension and volume to your hair, layering is the way to go. While it's always recommended to go to a professional stylist for a haircut, it's possible to layer the back of your hair by yourself. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps on how to achieve a layered look at home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Layer the Back of Your Hair by Yourself
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Layering Your Hair
  • FAQs

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Once it's dry, use the comb to create a middle part. Then, use the comb to section your hair into two sections - left and right.

Step 3: Create the First Layer

Starting with the bottom layer, take a small section of hair from the back and comb it straight down. Hold the section between your fingers and use the scissors to cut at an angle. This will create a V-shape. Repeat this process on both sides.

Step 4: Create the Second Layer

Once you've finished the first layer, move up to the next layer. Take a small section of hair from the back, just above the first layer, and repeat the same process as before. Continue working your way up until you've reached your desired length.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Once you've finished layering, use the scissors to blend the layers together. This will create a more natural look.

Tips for Layering Your Hair

Layering your hair can be a bit tricky, especially if you're doing it for the first time. Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much off.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take breaks to step back and assess your progress.
  • Be patient and take your time.

FAQs

Can I layer my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can layer curly hair. However, it's recommended to go to a professional stylist who has experience working with curly hair.

How often should I layer my hair?

It's recommended to layer your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and volume.

Hair Care Tips

After layering your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Layering the back of your hair by yourself can be a fun and rewarding experience. However, if you're not confident in your skills, it's always best to go to a professional stylist. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a layered look that complements your face shape and hair type.
