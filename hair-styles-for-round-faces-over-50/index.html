Hair Styles For Round Faces Over 50 Look Gorgeous And Confident

Written by Eveline Jul 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you over 50 and looking for some new hair styles to flatter your round face shape? Look no further! In this blog post, we will be sharing some of the best hair styles for round faces over 50. These hair styles are easy to maintain and will make you look gorgeous and confident.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Hair Style
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Style
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Description

As we age, our hair tends to thin out and lose its luster. This can make it difficult to find a hair style that flatters our face shape and makes us feel confident. Round faces are characterized by full cheeks and a rounded jawline. The key to finding a hair style that works for this face shape is to create angles and volume.

How to Choose the Best Hair Style

When choosing a hair style for a round face, it's important to consider the following factors:

  • Length of hair
  • Layers
  • Bangs
  • Texture

Long hair tends to elongate the face, while short hair can make it appear wider. Layers add volume and create angles, while bangs can help to balance out the face. Texture can also play a role in creating volume and adding interest to the hair style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your hair for a round face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair to one side to create an angle.
  3. Use a round brush to add volume at the roots.
  4. Use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls.
  5. If you have bangs, use a straightening iron to smooth them out.
  6. Finish with a light hair spray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Style

Here are some tips to help you maintain your hair style:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hair style.
  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different products, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to achieve your desired look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the best hair length for a round face over 50?

A: Shoulder-length hair is a great option for round faces over 50, as it creates angles and elongates the face.

Q: Should I have bangs if I have a round face?

A: Bangs can help to balance out a round face, but they are not necessary. If you do opt for bangs, choose a style that is side-swept or angled.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these tips and trying out some of the hair styles we've shared, you can look and feel your best at any age. Remember to choose a hair style that makes you feel confident and comfortable, and don't be afraid to experiment with different looks until you find the perfect fit.
