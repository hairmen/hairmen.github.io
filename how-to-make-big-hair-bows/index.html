How To Make Big Hair Bows A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to add some pizzazz to your hairdo, there's nothing quite like a big hair bow to do the trick. Not only are they cute and stylish, but they're also incredibly easy to make at home. In this guide, we'll show you how to make big hair bows step-by-step, along with some tips and tricks to make the process even easier. Let's get started!

Description

A big hair bow is a hair accessory that adds a touch of flair to any hairstyle. They come in a variety of styles, colors, and sizes, so you can choose the one that best suits your personal style. Making big hair bows at home is an easy and cost-effective way to add some variety to your hairdo without breaking the bank.

How to Make Big Hair Bows

Here's what you'll need to get started:

  • Grosgrain ribbon (2.25 inches wide)
  • Scissors
  • Hot glue gun
  • Barrette or hair tie

Now, let's get started:

Step 1: Cut the ribbon

Start by cutting a piece of grosgrain ribbon that is approximately 26 inches long. This will give you enough length to create a big bow without making it too bulky.

Step 2: Make the loops

Take one end of the ribbon and fold it over to create a loop. Pinch the center of the loop to create a crease, then use the hot glue gun to secure it in place. Repeat this process with the other end of the ribbon to create a second loop.

Step 3: Create the center loop

Take another piece of ribbon that is approximately 5 inches long and fold it into a loop. Use the hot glue gun to secure the loop in place.

Step 4: Assemble the bow

Take the two larger loops that you created in steps 2 and 3 and place them on top of each other, with the creases facing each other. Then, take the smaller center loop and place it on top of the larger loops, securing everything together with hot glue. Use the hot glue gun to attach a barrette or hair tie to the back of the bow.

Step 5: Fluff and shape

Once the bow is assembled, use your fingers to fluff and shape the loops until you're happy with the way it looks.

Tips for Making Big Hair Bows

Making big hair bows is a fun and creative process, but there are a few tips and tricks that can make the process even easier:

  • Use wired ribbon to make the bow easier to shape and manipulate
  • Experiment with different colors and patterns to create a unique look
  • Use a hair tie instead of a barrette for a more secure hold
  • Trim any excess ribbon to make the bow look neater and more polished

FAQ

How do I attach a big hair bow to my hair?

You can attach a big hair bow to your hair using a barrette or a hair tie. Simply slide the barrette or hair tie through the back of the bow and clip or tie it into your hair.

Can I use a different type of ribbon to make a big hair bow?

While grosgrain ribbon is the most commonly used ribbon for making big hair bows, you can use other types of ribbon as well. Just make sure that the ribbon you choose is sturdy enough to hold its shape.

How do I care for my big hair bow?

To keep your big hair bow looking its best, store it in a cool, dry place when you're not wearing it. If the bow becomes wrinkled or misshapen, you can use a low heat setting on an iron to smooth out any wrinkles.

Hair Care

When wearing a big hair bow, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your locks healthy and happy:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free from buildup
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf

With a little practice, you'll be a pro at making big hair bows in no time. Whether you're dressing up for a special occasion or just want to add some flair to your everyday look, a big hair bow is the perfect accessory. Happy crafting!
