How To Do A High Ponytail With Hair Extensions

Written by Eveline Nov 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your short hair not being able to make a high ponytail? Or maybe you just want to add some length and volume to your ponytail? Hair extensions are the solution! In this step-by-step guide, we will show you how to do a high ponytail with hair extensions.

Description

A high ponytail is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you are going to work or a party, a high ponytail can make you look stylish and elegant. With the help of hair extensions, you can create a more voluminous and longer ponytail that will make you stand out from the crowd.

How to

Follow these steps to create a high ponytail with hair extensions:

Step 1: Choose the Right Hair Extensions

Choose hair extensions that match your hair color and texture. Clip-in hair extensions are the easiest to use and are available in different lengths and textures. You can also use tape-in or sew-in hair extensions if you prefer.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Brush your hair to remove any tangles and make sure it is dry. Apply some hair serum or oil to add shine and prevent frizz.

Step 3: Clip in the Hair Extensions

Clip in the hair extensions starting from the bottom of your head and working your way up. Make sure to clip the hair extensions close to your scalp and secure them tightly.

Step 4: Gather Your Hair

Gather your hair into a high ponytail using a brush or comb. Make sure to smooth out any bumps or lumps.

Step 5: Secure the Ponytail

Secure the ponytail with a hair tie. You can also wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie and secure it with bobby pins for a more polished look.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your high ponytail with hair extensions look even better:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less visible.
  • Tease the crown of your head to add more volume to the ponytail.
  • Apply hairspray to keep the ponytail in place and prevent flyaways.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing a high ponytail with hair extensions:

Can I do a high ponytail with short hair?

Yes, you can use hair extensions to add length and volume to your short hair and create a high ponytail.

How long does it take to do a high ponytail with hair extensions?

It usually takes around 10-15 minutes to do a high ponytail with hair extensions.

Can I sleep with a high ponytail with hair extensions?

No, it is not recommended to sleep with a high ponytail with hair extensions as it can damage your hair and the extensions.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to take care of your hair extensions:

  • Brush your hair extensions gently to remove any tangles.
  • Wash your hair extensions with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair extensions.
  • Store your hair extensions in a cool and dry place.

Now that you know how to do a high ponytail with hair extensions, you can rock this hairstyle anytime, anywhere!
