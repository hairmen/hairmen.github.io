How To Change Your Hair Style In Animal Crossing New Horizons

Written by Oliver Jul 01, 2023 · 3 min read

Animal Crossing New Horizons is one of the most popular games out there right now, and it's easy to see why. It's a fun and relaxing game that allows you to create your own island paradise filled with cute animal friends. One of the best things about this game is that you can customize your character's appearance, including their hair style. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of changing your hair style in Animal Crossing New Horizons.

Table of Contents

  • How to Change Your Hair Style
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Changing Your Hair Style
  • FAQs About Changing Your Hair Style
  • Hair Care in Animal Crossing New Horizons

How to Change Your Hair Style

Changing your hair style in Animal Crossing New Horizons is a simple process that can be done at any time. Here are the steps:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Go to the Resident Services building on your island.
  2. Talk to Tom Nook or Isabelle at the front desk.
  3. Select "I want to change my look."
  4. Select "Hairstyle."
  5. Choose from the available hair styles.
  6. Confirm your choice and pay 1,800 Bells.
  7. Your character's hair style will be changed.

That's it! You now have a new hair style in Animal Crossing New Horizons.

Tips for Changing Your Hair Style

Here are some tips to keep in mind when changing your hair style:

  • Make sure you have enough Bells to pay for the change.
  • Try on different wigs to see how they look before you commit.
  • Don't worry about making the wrong choice - you can always change your hair style again later.

FAQs About Changing Your Hair Style

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing your hair style in Animal Crossing New Horizons:

  • Can I change my hair color? No, you can only change your hair style in Animal Crossing New Horizons.
  • Can I change my hair style more than once? Yes, you can change your hair style as many times as you want.
  • Do I need to unlock anything to change my hair style? No, you can change your hair style from the beginning of the game.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing New Horizons

While you can't change your hair color in Animal Crossing New Horizons, you can take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly by swimming in the ocean or river.
  • Use a hair dryer to dry your hair after swimming.
  • Avoid wearing hats or helmets for extended periods of time.
  • Visit Harriet at Shampoodle for more advanced hair care options.

That's it! Now you know how to change your hair style in Animal Crossing New Horizons. Have fun customizing your character's appearance!
