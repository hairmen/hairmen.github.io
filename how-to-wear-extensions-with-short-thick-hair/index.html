How To Wear Extensions With Short Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Extensions are a fantastic way to add length and volume to your hair, but if you have short and thick hair, you may be wondering how to wear extensions without them looking fake or unnatural. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve natural-looking extensions with short thick hair.

How to Choose the Right Extensions for Short Thick Hair

The first step in wearing extensions with short thick hair is choosing the right type of hair extensions. Here are some important factors to consider:

Length and Volume

The length and volume of the extensions you choose should match your natural hair. If you have short hair, you'll want to choose extensions that are similar in length. If you have thick hair, you'll want to choose extensions with enough volume to blend in seamlessly.

Texture and Color

The texture and color of your extensions should also match your natural hair. If you have curly hair, you'll want to choose extensions that are curly as well. If you have straight hair, you'll want to choose straight extensions. It's also important to choose the right color. If you have highlights or lowlights, try to find extensions that match those colors.

Quality

Investing in high-quality extensions is important when you have short and thick hair. Cheap extensions can look fake and may not blend in well with your natural hair. Look for extensions made from real human hair for the most natural look.

How to Wear Extensions with Short Thick Hair: Step by Step

Now that you've chosen the right extensions, it's time to learn how to wear them with short thick hair. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Prepare Your Natural Hair

Start by washing and blow-drying your natural hair. Brush it out to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Prepare Your Extensions

Clip your extensions onto a hanger or stand to prepare them for application. This will make it easier to apply them to your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, create a horizontal part at the back of your head, about an inch above your nape. Clip the top section of your hair up and out of the way.

Step 4: Apply the Extensions

Starting at the bottom of your head, clip the first weft of extensions onto your natural hair. Make sure the weft is as close to your scalp as possible. Continue clipping wefts onto your hair, working your way up to the top of your head.

Step 5: Blend the Extensions

Once all of the wefts are clipped in, use a comb to blend your natural hair with the extensions. This will create a seamless look.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

You can style your hair as desired, but be careful not to tug on the extensions too much. Use a hair straightener or curling iron on low heat to style your hair.

Tips for Wearing Extensions with Short Thick Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when wearing extensions with short thick hair:

Tip 1: Use Enough Extensions

If you have thick hair, you'll need more extensions to achieve a natural look. Make sure you have enough wefts to blend in with your natural hair.

Tip 2: Don't Tug on the Extensions

Be gentle when styling your hair to avoid pulling on the extensions. Tugging on the extensions can cause them to come loose or even damage your natural hair.

Tip 3: Use Heat Protectant

Protect your natural hair and extensions by using a heat protectant before using a hair straightener or curling iron.

FAQ

Can I Wear Extensions with Short Thick Hair?

Yes! You can wear extensions with short thick hair. Just make sure to choose the right type of extensions and follow the steps above to achieve a natural look.

How Long Do Extensions Last?

Extensions can last up to six months with proper care. To make them last longer, avoid sleeping in them and be gentle when styling your hair.

Hair Care for Extensions with Short Thick Hair

To keep your extensions looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

Tip 1: Wash Your Extensions

Wash your extensions every six to eight weeks to remove any buildup. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out the extensions.

Tip 2: Brush Your Extensions

Brush your extensions gently with a wide-tooth comb to avoid pulling on them. Start at the bottom and work your way up to the roots.

Tip 3: Store Your Extensions Properly

When you're not wearing your extensions, store them in a cool, dry place. You can also put them in a hair extension hanger to keep them organized.

By following these tips and steps, you can confidently wear extensions with short thick hair and achieve a natural look.
