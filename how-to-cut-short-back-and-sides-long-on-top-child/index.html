How To Cut Short Back And Sides Long On Top Child

Written by Oliver Oct 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a stylish haircut for your child? The short back and sides long on top hairstyle has become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. It's a versatile haircut that can be tailored to suit any child's face shape and hair type. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve this trendy look.

Description

The short back and sides long on top haircut is a classic style that has been around for decades. It's a stylish and practical choice for kids as it's easy to maintain and can be adapted to suit any occasion. The haircut is characterized by short hair on the back and sides, while the top is left long and styled in a variety of ways.

How to

Before you start cutting your child's hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clippers with various attachments.

Here's how to cut short back and sides long on top child:

Step 1: Preparation

Wash your child's hair and towel dry it. Use a comb to ensure the hair is tangle-free.

Step 2: Clipper Cut

Using the hair clippers, start cutting the back and sides of your child's hair. Use a number 2 attachment for a short cut, or a number 3 for a slightly longer cut. Start at the nape of the neck and work your way up, moving the clippers in an upward motion. Use a comb to keep the hair in place.

Step 3: Scissor Cut

Once you've finished cutting the back and sides, it's time to move on to the top. Use the scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length. Start by cutting small sections at a time and use the comb to hold the hair in place.

Step 4: Blending

Blend the top with the sides and back using the clippers without any attachment. Move the clippers up in a circular motion to blend the hair.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

Once you've finished cutting, use the scissors to trim any stray hairs. Use the clippers to trim the hair around the ears and the back of the neck.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short back and sides long on top hairstyle for your child:

  • Start with a slightly longer length on top, so you have room to adjust the length as needed.
  • Use a mirror to check the haircut from all angles.
  • Make sure the hair is cut evenly on both sides.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits your child best.

FAQ

Can I cut short back and sides long on top child at home?

Yes, you can cut short back and sides long on top child at home with the right tools and techniques.

How often should I cut my child's hair?

It depends on how fast your child's hair grows. On average, you should cut your child's hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

To maintain the short back and sides long on top hairstyle, use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and healthy. Use a hair gel or wax to style the hair on top.

Cutting short back and sides long on top child is a simple and stylish haircut that can be easily achieved at home. With the right tools and techniques, you can give your child a trendy new look that they'll love.
