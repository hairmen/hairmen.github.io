How Will I Know My Hair Type

Written by Luffy Jul 14, 2023 · 3 min read

Do you ever wonder what your hair type is? Understanding your hair type is important in determining what products and hairstyles work best for you. In this article, we’ll walk you through how to determine your hair type and provide some tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Hair Type
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Type
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Recommendations

How to Determine Your Hair Type

There are several factors that determine your hair type, including strand thickness, curl pattern, and porosity. The most common hair types are straight, wavy, curly, and coily. Here are some key characteristics of each:

  • Straight hair: hair that falls flat and lacks curl or wave
  • Wavy hair: hair that has a slight wave or curl pattern
  • Curly hair: hair that has a defined curl pattern
  • Coily hair: hair that has a tight, springy curl pattern

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’re unsure of your hair type, follow these steps to determine your hair’s characteristics:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Allow your hair to air dry without any products.
  3. Examine your hair’s texture, curl pattern, and porosity.
  4. Use the chart below to determine your hair type:
Hair TypeTextureCurl PatternPorosity
StraightFineStraightLow
WavyMediumSlight waveMedium
CurlyCoarseDefined curlsHigh
CoilyCoarseTight, springy curlsHigh

Once you determine your hair type, you can start tailoring your hair care routine to your specific needs.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Type

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair based on your hair type:

  • Straight hair: avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Wavy hair: use a curl-enhancing product to bring out your hair’s natural wave. Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Curly hair: use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and defined. Avoid using heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Coily hair: use a moisturizing product to keep your hair hydrated and soft. Avoid using harsh chemicals, as this can damage your hair and cause breakage.

FAQs

How often should I wash my hair?

This depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with oily hair may need to wash their hair every day, while others with dry hair may only need to wash their hair once a week.

Can I change my hair type?

No, your hair type is determined by genetics and cannot be changed. However, you can change the appearance of your hair through styling and products.

Hair Care Recommendations

Here are some hair care product recommendations based on your hair type:

  • Straight hair: use a lightweight shampoo and conditioner, like Herbal Essences Lightweight Clean Shampoo and Conditioner.
  • Wavy hair: use a curl-enhancing product, like Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl Enhancing Smoothie.
  • Curly hair: use a leave-in conditioner, like Cantu Shea Butter Leave-In Conditioning Repair Cream.
  • Coily hair: use a moisturizing product, like Ouidad Curl Quencher Moisturizing Gel.

Remember, understanding your hair type is the first step in maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. With these tips and recommendations, you can create a hair care routine that works best for you.
