Man Bun Haircut Styles

Written by Luffy Aug 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, then you might want to consider the man bun. This versatile hairstyle has been around for a few years now and has become increasingly popular among men of all ages. In this article, we'll discuss different man bun haircut styles that you can try out.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The man bun is a popular hairstyle that involves tying your hair into a bun at the top of your head. There are different types of man bun haircut styles, including the full bun, half bun, low bun, and undercut bun. Each style has its own unique look and can be adapted to suit your personal style.

How to

To achieve the man bun haircut style, you'll need to grow your hair to a certain length, usually around 6-8 inches. Once your hair is long enough, you can start tying it into a bun. The easiest way to do this is to gather your hair into a high ponytail, twist it around, and secure it with an elastic band or hair tie.

Full Bun

The full bun is the most popular man bun haircut style. To achieve this look, you'll need to gather all of your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with an elastic band or hair tie. You can also use bobby pins to hold any loose strands in place.

Half Bun

The half bun is a more relaxed version of the full bun. To achieve this look, gather the top half of your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with an elastic band or hair tie. Leave the bottom half of your hair down.

Low Bun

The low bun is a more casual man bun haircut style. To achieve this look, gather all of your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with an elastic band or hair tie.

Undercut Bun

The undercut bun is a more edgy version of the man bun. To achieve this look, shave the sides and back of your head and leave the top long. Gather the top section of your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with an elastic band or hair tie.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the full bun man bun haircut style:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather all of your hair into a high ponytail at the top of your head.
  3. Twist the ponytail around to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with an elastic band or hair tie.
  5. Use bobby pins to hold any loose strands in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect man bun haircut style:

  • Use a comb to create a sleeker look.
  • Apply some hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.
  • Experiment with different types of man bun haircut styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Can anyone wear a man bun?

Yes, anyone can wear a man bun. However, you'll need to have enough hair length to achieve the look.

How long does my hair need to be for a man bun?

Your hair should be at least 6-8 inches long to achieve the man bun haircut style.

Do I need to use hair products to achieve the man bun?

No, you don't need to use hair products to achieve the man bun. However, using hair gel or pomade can help keep your hair in place.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking good, you should follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner, using a hair mask once a week, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. You should also trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

With these man bun haircut styles, you can easily switch up your look and stay on-trend. Whether you opt for a full bun, half bun, low bun, or undercut bun, there's a man bun haircut style to suit everyone's personal style.
