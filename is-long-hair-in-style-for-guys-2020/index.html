Is Long Hair In Style For Guys 2020

Written by Petter Nov 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair has been a trend for men for the past few years. In 2020, many men started to grow their hair out, and it became a popular style for guys. But, is long hair still in style for guys in 2020? In this post, we will discuss the current trend for long hair in men's fashion.

Description

The long hair trend for men started in the 1960s and 1970s when rock stars such as The Beatles, Led Zeppelin, and The Rolling Stones made it popular. Since then, long hair has been a symbol of rebellion, freedom, and creativity. Nowadays, many men are embracing this trend again and growing their hair out.

How to Grow Long Hair

Growing long hair requires patience and dedication. Here are some tips to help you grow your hair:

  1. Start with a clean scalp: Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to keep your scalp clean and healthy.
  2. Eat a balanced diet: A diet rich in vitamins and minerals such as protein, iron, and biotin can help promote hair growth.
  3. Avoid heat styling: Heat styling tools such as blow dryers and flat irons can damage your hair and slow down growth.
  4. Trim your hair regularly: Regular trims can prevent split ends and breakage, which can make your hair look shorter than it is.

Step by Step Guide

If you want to grow your hair out, here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Decide on your hair length: Determine how long you want your hair to be and set a goal.
  2. Start growing your hair: Let your hair grow out for several months without cutting it.
  3. Maintain your hair: Keep your hair clean and healthy by washing it regularly and using a conditioner. Avoid using harsh products that can damage your hair.
  4. Style your hair: Once your hair is long enough, you can experiment with different styles such as a ponytail, bun, or braid.

Tips for Styling Long Hair

If you want to style your long hair, here are some tips:

  • Use hair products: Products such as hair gel, wax, or pomade can help keep your hair in place and add shine.
  • Experiment with different styles: You can try a messy look, slicked-back style, or a half-up, half-down style.
  • Accessorize: You can add accessories such as a headband, bandana, or hat to your long hair to add more style.

FAQ

Q: Is long hair only for younger men?

A: No, long hair can be worn by men of any age. It's a matter of personal preference and style.

Q: Can men with curly hair grow long hair?

A: Yes, men with curly hair can grow long hair. However, it may take longer to grow and require more maintenance.

Q: How long does it take to grow long hair?

A: It can take several months to a year or more to grow long hair, depending on how fast your hair grows and how long you want it to be.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Long hair requires more care than short hair. Here are some tips:

  • Don't over-wash your hair: Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and damaged.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb: A wide-tooth comb can help detangle your hair without causing breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF.

Conclusion

In conclusion, long hair is still in style for guys in 2020. It's a timeless trend that has been embraced by many men over the years. If you want to grow your hair out, be patient and take care of it. With the right care and styling, long hair can be a great look for any man.
