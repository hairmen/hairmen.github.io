How To Match Natural Hair Color With Dye

Written by Eveline Jul 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your natural hair color and want to try something new? Dyeing your hair can be a great way to switch things up and add some excitement to your look. However, finding the right hair color that matches your natural hair can be a daunting task. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to match natural hair color with dye.

Table of Content

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in matching your natural hair color with dye is to determine your natural hair color. Look at the roots of your hair to get a better idea of your natural hair color. Once you know your natural hair color, you can choose a hair dye that is closest to it.

When choosing a hair dye, it is important to consider the undertones of your natural hair color. If your natural hair color has warm undertones, choose a hair dye with warm undertones. If your natural hair color has cool undertones, choose a hair dye with cool undertones.

If you are unsure about which hair dye to choose, consult with a professional hair colorist. They can help you choose the perfect hair dye that will match your natural hair color.

Step by step

Here are the steps you should follow to match your natural hair color with dye:

  1. Determine your natural hair color
  2. Choose a hair dye that is closest to your natural hair color
  3. Consider the undertones of your natural hair color when choosing a hair dye
  4. If you are unsure, consult with a professional hair colorist

Tips

Here are some tips to help you match your natural hair color with dye:

  • Choose a hair dye that is one shade lighter or darker than your natural hair color for a subtle change
  • Do a strand test before dyeing your entire head to ensure that the hair dye matches your natural hair color
  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to preserve the color of your hair
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can cause the hair dye to fade faster

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about matching natural hair color with dye:

Can I dye my hair if it is damaged?

If your hair is damaged, it is best to wait until it is healthy before dyeing it. Dyeing damaged hair can cause further damage and breakage.

How often should I touch up my roots?

This depends on how fast your hair grows. Generally, it is recommended to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

If you have sensitive skin, it is important to choose a hair dye that is gentle and formulated for sensitive skin. You may also want to do a patch test before dyeing your entire head to ensure that you do not have an allergic reaction.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it is important to take good care of it to maintain the color and health of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can cause the color to fade faster

By following these tips and steps, you can easily match your natural hair color with dye and achieve the desired look for your hair. Remember to take good care of your hair after dyeing it to maintain its color and health.
