What Haircut Best Suits A Round Face

Written by Bardi Jun 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a round-faced beauty looking for the perfect haircut? Look no further! In this article, we'll be discussing the best haircuts for round faces, along with tips and tricks to keep your hair looking fabulous. Read on to discover which hairstyles will flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face typically has a width and height that are roughly equal, with soft, rounded features. The goal with any haircut for a round face is to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more angular shape. This can be achieved through a variety of techniques, including layers, angles, and volume.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect haircut for your round face is to determine your face shape. Stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the width and height of your face. If these measurements are roughly equal, you likely have a round face.

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to start exploring different haircuts that will flatter your features. Look for styles that incorporate layers, angles, and volume to create the illusion of a more angular face shape. Some popular haircuts for round faces include:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help to elongate the face and create a more angular shape. Ask your stylist for layers that start at the jawline and gradually get longer towards the ends of your hair.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to balance out the proportions of a round face. Ask your stylist for bangs that start at the side of your forehead and gradually get longer towards the other side of your face.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can help to create the illusion of a more angular face shape. Ask your stylist for a cut that incorporates layers and volume at the crown of your head.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape by measuring the width and height of your face.

2. Explore different haircuts that incorporate layers, angles, and volume.

3. Choose a style that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.

4. Bring a photo of your desired haircut to your stylist to ensure they understand your vision.

5. Maintain your haircut with regular trims and styling to keep it looking fresh and fabulous.

Tips

- Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline, as these can emphasize the roundness of your face.

- Experiment with different parting styles to create the illusion of a more angular face shape.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension in your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a bob if I have a round face?

A: Absolutely! Just make sure to ask your stylist for a layered bob that incorporates angles and volume to elongate your face.

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: Not necessarily. A well-executed pixie cut can actually help to create the illusion of a more angular face shape.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to invest in quality hair care products that are tailored to your specific hair type and needs. Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup and keep your hair looking fresh. And don't forget to schedule regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect haircut to flatter your round face and enhance your natural beauty. So go ahead and embrace your unique features - you're beautiful just the way you are!
