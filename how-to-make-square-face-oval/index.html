How To Make Square Face Oval A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Oct 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a square face shape, you may have noticed that your face appears more angular and less oval. While there is nothing wrong with having a square face, many people prefer a more oval shape, as it is considered more symmetrical and balanced. Fortunately, there are several ways to make your square face appear more oval, without resorting to cosmetic procedures or surgery. In this article, we will show you how to make square face oval in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Make Square Face Oval
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Oval Face

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and a straight line from the forehead to the jaw. This face shape is often seen as bold and angular, but it can also be softened with a few simple tricks. By making your face appear more oval, you can create a more balanced and symmetrical look. This can be achieved through makeup, hairstyles, and even accessories.

How to Make Square Face Oval

There are several ways to make square face oval, including:

  • Contouring with makeup
  • Choosing the right hairstyle
  • Wearing the right accessories

Step-by-Step Guide

Contouring with Makeup

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using different shades of makeup to create the illusion of shadows and highlights on the face. By using darker shades on the areas you want to recede, and lighter shades on the areas you want to bring forward, you can create the illusion of an oval face shape. Here's how:

  1. Start by applying foundation to your face, as you normally would.
  2. Apply a darker shade of foundation, bronzer, or contour powder to the sides of your forehead, temples, and jawline. Blend well.
  3. Apply a lighter shade of foundation, concealer, or highlighter to the center of your forehead, nose, cheeks, and chin. Blend well.
  4. Finish with a setting powder to lock everything in place.

Choosing the Right Hairstyle

The right hairstyle can also make a big difference in creating the illusion of an oval face shape. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid blunt bangs or hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Opt for long layers or soft waves that frame your face and soften your jawline.
  • Consider a middle part or deep side part, as this can help elongate your face.

Wearing the Right Accessories

Accessories can also play a role in creating the illusion of an oval face shape. Here are some tips:

  • Wear earrings that elongate your face, such as long dangles or hoops.
  • Avoid chokers or short necklaces that sit at your collarbone, as this can make your face appear more square.
  • Opt for longer necklaces or scarves that create a vertical line and elongate your face.

Tips and Tricks

  • Remember that there is nothing wrong with having a square face shape. It is simply a matter of personal preference.
  • Experiment with different makeup techniques and hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for advice from a professional makeup artist or hairstylist.
  • Remember that confidence is key. If you feel good about yourself, it will show in your appearance.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my square face shape permanently oval?

A: No, there is no permanent way to change the shape of your face. However, by using makeup, hairstyles, and accessories, you can create the illusion of an oval face shape.

Q: Can men make their square face shape oval?

A: Yes, the same techniques can be used for men as well as women.

Q: Is it necessary to make my square face oval?

A: No, it is not necessary to change the shape of your face. However, if you prefer a more oval shape, these techniques can help you achieve that look.

Hair Care for Oval Face

If you have an oval face shape, congratulations! This face shape is considered the most symmetrical and balanced. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles and lengths, as most styles will look great on an oval face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head, as this can elongate your face too much.
  • Consider a side part or no part, as this can help balance your features.
  • Don't be afraid to try bold colors or highlights, as they can add dimension to your face.

By following these tips, you can make the most of your oval face shape and create a look that is uniquely you. Remember, beauty comes in all shapes and sizes, so embrace your individuality and let your confidence shine through!
