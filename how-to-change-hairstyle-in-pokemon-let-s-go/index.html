Welcome to our guide on how to change your hairstyle in the popular game, Pokemon Let's Go! Whether you're tired of your current look or just want to switch things up, changing your hairstyle can be a fun way to breathe new life into your game. How to Change Your Hairstyle

Before we get into the specifics of changing your hairstyle, it's important to note that you'll need to have progressed far enough into the game to reach Celadon City. This is where you'll find the salon where you can change your hairstyle. Once you're in Celadon City, follow these simple steps:

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Head to the salon in Celadon City. It's located on the second floor of the department store.
  2. Talk to the hairstylist and choose the "Change Hairstyle" option.
  3. Select the hairstyle you want from the list of available options.
  4. Confirm your choice and pay the fee.
  5. Enjoy your new look!

That's it! Changing your hairstyle is quick and easy, so feel free to experiment with different looks until you find the perfect one.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle

With so many hairstyles to choose from, it can be difficult to know where to start. Here are a few tips to help you select the perfect hairstyle:

  • Consider your character's personality. Are they bold and daring, or more reserved and conservative?
  • Think about your character's outfit. Certain hairstyles may complement certain outfits better than others.
  • Try out different hairstyles in the salon before committing to one. This will give you a better idea of how the hairstyle looks on your character.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are a few of the most commonly asked questions about changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Let's Go:

How much does it cost to change your hairstyle?

The fee for changing your hairstyle is 4000 Poke Dollars.

Can you change your hairstyle as many times as you want?

Yes! You can change your hairstyle as many times as you want, so feel free to experiment with different looks.

Do different hairstyles have any gameplay effects?

No, changing your hairstyle is purely cosmetic and has no effect on gameplay.

Hair Care Advice

Finally, it's important to take care of your character's hair to keep it looking great. Here are a few tips for maintaining your new hairstyle:

  • Visit the salon regularly to keep your hairstyle looking fresh and vibrant.
  • Use hair products like gel or mousse to help your hair hold its shape.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dull.

With these tips in mind, you're ready to change your hairstyle and take on the Pokemon world with a fresh new look. Good luck!
