How To Remove Red And Orange Tones From Hair

Written by Oliver Jul 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you've recently dyed your hair and noticed red or orange tones that you weren't expecting, don't panic! There are several ways to remove these tones and get back to the color you wanted. In this article, we'll share how to remove red and orange tones from hair, step by step.

Description

Red and orange tones can appear in hair for several reasons. One common cause is using a hair dye with warm undertones, which can result in brassy or orange hues. If you have dark hair, red or orange tones can also appear after bleaching or lightening your hair. Whatever the cause, it's possible to remove these tones and achieve the color you want.

How to

There are several methods you can use to remove red and orange tones from hair. Here are some of the most effective ones:

Use a Color-Correcting Shampoo

Color-correcting shampoos are specially formulated to neutralize unwanted tones in hair. Look for a shampoo that's designed to remove red or orange tones, depending on the color you want to correct. Use the shampoo regularly until you achieve the desired result.

Try a Toner

Toners are another option for removing red and orange tones from hair. They work by depositing pigment onto the hair to neutralize the unwanted hues. Choose a toner that's the opposite color of the unwanted tones â for example, a blue toner for orange hair or a green toner for red hair. Apply the toner according to the instructions and rinse it out thoroughly.

Use a Vinegar Rinse

Vinegar is a natural acid that can help remove unwanted tones from hair. Mix equal parts water and apple cider vinegar and apply the solution to your hair. Leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to removing red and orange tones from hair using a color-correcting shampoo:

  1. Wet your hair thoroughly.
  2. Apply the color-correcting shampoo to your hair, focusing on the areas with the unwanted tones.
  3. Massage the shampoo into your hair and scalp for a few minutes.
  4. Rinse out the shampoo thoroughly.
  5. Repeat the process if necessary, until you achieve the desired result.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying to remove red and orange tones from hair:

  • Be patient. It may take a few treatments to achieve the desired result.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can further damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment regularly to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQ

Can I use bleach to remove red and orange tones from my hair?

Bleach can be effective for removing red and orange tones from hair, but it's also very damaging. We recommend trying other methods first, and only using bleach as a last resort.

How often can I use a color-correcting shampoo?

You can use a color-correcting shampoo as often as needed, but we recommend using it no more than once or twice a week to avoid drying out your hair.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment regularly.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.

By following these tips and using the methods we've outlined, you can effectively remove red and orange tones from your hair and achieve the color you want.
