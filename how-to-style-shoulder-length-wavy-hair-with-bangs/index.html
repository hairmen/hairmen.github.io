How To Style Shoulder Length Wavy Hair With Bangs

Are you looking for a new hairstyle for your shoulder length wavy hair with bangs? Look no further! In this article, we will show you how to style your hair in a way that complements your face shape and enhances your natural waves. We will also provide you with some tips on how to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Table of Contents

  • Choosing the Right Haircut
  • Styling Your Wavy Hair with Bangs

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in styling your hair is to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a haircut that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty. There are several different face shapes, including oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Use a washable marker to outline the shape of your face on the mirror. Then, compare your outline to the different face shapes to determine which one best matches your features.

Choosing the Right Haircut

Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to choose the right haircut for your shoulder length wavy hair with bangs. The right haircut can enhance your features and bring out your natural beauty. Here are some haircuts that are perfect for shoulder length wavy hair with bangs:

Layered Haircut

A layered haircut is perfect for shoulder length wavy hair with bangs. Layers add texture and movement to your hair, while bangs frame your face and bring out your eyes. This haircut is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance style that looks great every day.

Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is another great option for shoulder length wavy hair with bangs. This haircut is timeless and versatile, and it can be styled in many different ways. You can wear it sleek and straight or tousled and messy.

Styling Your Wavy Hair with Bangs

Now that you have chosen the right haircut for your shoulder length wavy hair with bangs, it's time to style it. Here are some tips for styling your hair:

Use a Heat Protectant

Before you style your hair, it's important to use a heat protectant. This will help protect your hair from heat damage and keep it healthy and shiny. Apply the heat protectant to your damp hair before blow-drying or using a curling iron or flat iron.

Blow-Dry Your Hair

To enhance your natural waves, blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help create volume and texture while keeping your waves intact. Use a low heat setting and scrunch your hair while blow-drying to enhance your waves.

Curl Your Hair

If you want more defined waves, curl your hair using a curling iron or wand. Wrap small sections of hair around the barrel of the curling iron and hold for a few seconds. Then, release the curl and let it cool before touching it. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

To keep your shoulder length wavy hair with bangs looking healthy and shiny, follow these tips:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.

Use a Hair Mask

Once a week, use a hair mask to nourish your hair and keep it healthy. Apply the mask to your damp hair and leave it on for 10-15 minutes before rinsing it off.

Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using heat on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and make your hair look dull and lifeless. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant and a low heat setting.

FAQ

Can I straighten my wavy hair with bangs?

Yes, you can straighten your wavy hair with bangs using a flat iron. Apply a heat protectant to your hair before using the flat iron and use a low heat setting to avoid heat damage.

What kind of bangs are best for wavy hair?

Side-swept bangs are best for wavy hair, as they blend in with your natural waves and frame your face nicely. Avoid straight bangs, as they may look too blunt and require more maintenance.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length wavy hair with bangs looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends. This will also help your hair grow longer and stronger.

Avoid Chemical Treatments

Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dye. These can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to keep your hair healthy and strong. Include plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins in your diet.

Now that you know how to style your shoulder length wavy hair with bangs, it's time to try it out for yourself! Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
