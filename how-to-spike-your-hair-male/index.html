How To Spike Your Hair Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Nov 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's edgy and easy to maintain, spiking your hair might just be the way to go. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, spiky hair can give you a cool and confident vibe. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to spike your hair male:

How To:

Step 1: Choose the Right Hair Product

The first step to achieving the perfect spiky hair is to choose the right hair product. Look for a hair gel or wax that can hold your hair in place for hours without making it look greasy or stiff. You can also opt for a hair spray if you want a more natural look.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure to wash it thoroughly to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and rinse it off with cold water to help seal the hair cuticles and add shine.

Step 3: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently pat it dry with a towel to remove excess water. Don't rub your hair vigorously as this can cause damage and frizz.

Step 4: Apply Hair Product

Take a small amount of hair gel or wax and rub it between your palms to warm it up. Apply it evenly to your hair, focusing on the roots and tips. Use more product if you have thick or long hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Using your fingers or a comb, style your hair upwards to create the spikes. Start at the front and work your way towards the back, making sure to create even spikes throughout. You can also use a blow dryer on a low setting to help set the hair in place.

Step 6: Finish Off with Hair Spray

Once you've styled your hair, spray it with a light-hold hair spray to help keep the spikes in place. Don't use too much hairspray as this can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Tips:

  • Experiment with different hair products and find the one that works best for your hair type and style.
  • Use a hair dryer on a low setting to help set the hair in place and add volume.
  • Don't touch your hair too much throughout the day as this can cause the spikes to flatten out.
  • Try adding some color or highlights to your hair to make your spikes stand out even more.

FAQ:

How long will my spikes stay in place?

This will depend on the type of hair product you use and how well you've styled your hair. Generally, spiky hair can last for several hours to a whole day.

Can I spike my hair if it's short?

Yes, you can still spike your hair if it's short. Just use less hair product and create smaller spikes.

Can I spike my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can still spike your hair if it's curly. Just make sure to use a hair product that can hold your curls in place without making them look too stiff.

Hair Care:

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to follow a proper hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a conditioner, and avoiding harsh chemicals or heat styling tools. You can also try using natural hair care products like coconut oil or aloe vera to nourish your hair and scalp.

Now that you know how to spike your hair male, it's time to try it out for yourself. With a little practice and the right hair products, you can rock the spiky hair look like a pro.
