How To Keep Your Hair Nice In Humidity

Written by Frank Oct 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you live in a high humidity area, you know the struggle of trying to keep your hair looking good. Humidity can cause frizz, limpness, and an overall lack of volume. But fear not! With a few tips and tricks, you can keep your hair looking great no matter the weather. Here are some ways to keep your hair looking nice in humidity:

How to:

1. Keep your hair hydrated

Humidity can cause your hair to dry out, leading to frizz and breakage. To combat this, make sure to keep your hair hydrated. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and consider adding a leave-in conditioner to your routine. You can also use a hair mask once a week to give your hair an extra boost of hydration.

2. Use anti-frizz products

Anti-frizz products are your best friend in humidity. Look for products that contain ingredients like argan oil, coconut oil, or shea butter, as these can help tame frizz and flyaways. Apply the product to your hair before styling, and use a small amount throughout the day as needed.

3. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz. Instead, opt for air-drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. If you must use a straightener or curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

4. Keep your hair up

One of the easiest ways to keep your hair looking nice in humidity is to keep it up. A messy bun, braids, or a sleek ponytail can all look great and keep your hair out of your face. Plus, this can help prevent frizz and keep your hair looking neat.

Step by Step:

1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed to hydrate your hair. Look for ingredients like argan oil, coconut oil, or shea butter.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair.

A leave-in conditioner can help keep your hair hydrated throughout the day. Apply a small amount to your hair, focusing on the ends.

3. Use an anti-frizz product.

Apply an anti-frizz product to your hair before styling. Make sure to distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

4. Air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

Avoid using a straightener or curling iron, as these can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz.

5. Keep your hair up.

Consider wearing your hair in a bun, braids, or a ponytail to keep it out of your face and prevent frizz.

Tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can transfer oils from your hands and make your hair greasier.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase, as these can help prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQ:

Q: What causes hair to frizz in humidity?

A: Humidity can cause hair to frizz because it makes the hair shafts swell, which disrupts the cuticle and causes it to become rough and frayed.

Q: Can I use hairspray to prevent frizz?

A: Yes, hairspray can help prevent frizz, but make sure to use a lightweight formula and apply it sparingly to avoid a sticky or crunchy look.

Q: How often should I use a hair mask?

A: It depends on your hair type and the level of damage. If your hair is very dry or damaged, you may want to use a hair mask once a week. If your hair is relatively healthy, once a month should be sufficient.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair looking nice in humidity can be a challenge, but it's not impossible. By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair looking great no matter the weather. Remember to keep your hair hydrated, use anti-frizz products, avoid heat styling, and keep your hair up. With a little bit of effort, you can have beautiful, frizz-free hair all year round.
