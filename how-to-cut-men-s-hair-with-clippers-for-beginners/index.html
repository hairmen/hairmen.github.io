How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers For Beginners A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 31, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a beginner looking to cut men's hair with clippers, you're in the right place. This guide will walk you through the process step-by-step, with tips and tricks along the way. By the end, you'll have the knowledge and confidence to give a great haircut.

Description

Cutting men's hair with clippers can be intimidating, especially if you've never done it before. But with a bit of practice and patience, it's a skill that anyone can learn. This guide is designed for beginners, so don't worry if you're a complete novice. We'll start with the basics and work our way up.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Clippers for Beginners: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, you'll need to make sure you have all the necessary tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Clipper guards (various sizes)
  • Scissors (optional)
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape or towel
  • Trash can (for hair clippings)

Make sure your clippers are clean and oiled before you begin.

Step 2: Prep Your Client

Before you start cutting, your client should be seated comfortably and wearing the haircutting cape or towel. Ask them what kind of haircut they want, and have them show you pictures if possible.

Wet the hair with a spray bottle or by running it under the sink. This will make it easier to cut.

Step 3: Choose Your Guard Size

Clipper guards come in various sizes, from 0 (no guard) to 8 (the longest guard). The size you choose will depend on the length of the hair you're cutting.

For a short buzz cut, start with a size 1 or 2 guard. For longer hair, start with a larger guard and work your way down if needed.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start at the bottom of the hairline and work your way up. Use the clipper guard to guide the clippers through the hair, moving in an upward motion. Take small sections at a time, and be sure to use a comb to keep the hair in place.

Continue cutting in sections, moving from the back of the head to the sides and then the top. Use a larger guard for the sides and a smaller guard for the top.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the hair to the desired length, it's time to blend the hair. This is where scissors come in handy.

Take a small section of hair between your fingers and use the scissors to blend the hair from one guard size to another. Work your way around the head, blending the hair as needed.

Step 6: Clean Up the Neckline

Use the clippers without a guard to clean up the hairline at the back of the neck. Use a comb to make a straight line across the bottom of the hairline, and then use the clippers to trim any hair below the line.

Step 7: Trim the Sideburns

Use the clippers or scissors to trim the sideburns to the desired length. Make sure they're even on both sides.

Step 8: Finish with Detailing

Use the clippers or scissors to clean up any stray hairs or uneven spots. Pay close attention to the hairline and around the ears.

Step 9: Clean Up

Use a brush or towel to remove any hair clippings from the client's neck and shoulders. If possible, have them take a quick shower to remove any remaining hair.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Clippers

  • Start with a larger guard and work your way down if needed
  • Take small sections at a time
  • Use a comb to keep the hair in place
  • Blend the hair with scissors for a more natural look
  • Pay close attention to the hairline and around the ears

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. However, it can be difficult to achieve a professional-looking cut without help.

How often should I oil my clippers?

You should oil your clippers before and after each use. This will help to maintain the blades and prevent them from rusting.

Can I use clippers to cut hair wet?

Yes, you can use clippers to cut hair when it's wet. In fact, wet hair is easier to cut than dry hair.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo to remove any hair clippings
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy

Now that you know how to cut men's hair with clippers, it's time to give it a try. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don't be too hard on yourself if it doesn't turn out perfectly the first time. With a bit of practice, you'll be giving great haircuts in no time.
