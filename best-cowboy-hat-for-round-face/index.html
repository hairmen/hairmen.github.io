Best Cowboy Hat For Round Face How To Choose The Perfect One

Written by William Nov 10, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for the perfect cowboy hat to complement your round face? Look no further! In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of choosing the best cowboy hat for your face shape, including tips, how-tos, and FAQs. Whether you’re a seasoned cowboy or just starting out, finding the perfect hat is essential to complete your look.

Description

If you have a round face, you may find it challenging to find the right cowboy hat that flatters your features. The key is to look for hats that balance out your face shape and provide a slimming effect. The right hat can make all the difference in your look, and finding the perfect one is essential.

How to

When choosing a cowboy hat for a round face, it’s important to consider the crown height and the brim width. The crown height should be high enough to elongate your face, while the brim width should be wide enough to balance your features. A medium brim is a good choice, as it provides a balance without overwhelming your face.

Another important factor to consider is the shape of the hat. A hat with a square or oval shape can help balance out your round features, while a round hat can accentuate them. It’s also important to consider the material of the hat, as some materials can be more flattering than others.

Step by Step

1. Measure your head to determine your hat size.

2. Choose a hat with a high crown to elongate your face.

3. Look for a medium brim width to balance your features.

4. Consider the shape of the hat, opting for a square or oval shape.

5. Choose a material that flatters your features.

Tips

When trying on cowboy hats, be sure to look at yourself from all angles to get a complete view of how the hat looks on your face. Don’t be afraid to try on multiple hats until you find the perfect one. It’s also important to consider the occasion and your personal style when choosing a cowboy hat.

FAQ

Q: What materials are best for cowboy hats?

A: Felt and straw are popular materials for cowboy hats, as they are durable and provide good sun protection. However, leather is also a good choice for a more traditional look.

Q: How do I measure my head for a cowboy hat?

A: Use a tape measure to measure the circumference of your head, just above your eyebrows and ears.

Q: How do I care for my cowboy hat?

A: To care for your cowboy hat, avoid getting it wet and store it in a cool, dry place. Use a soft brush to remove any dirt or dust, and avoid using harsh chemicals or cleaners.

Hair Care

When wearing a cowboy hat, it’s important to consider your hair. To avoid hat hair, try styling your hair in loose beachy waves or a messy bun. Avoid tight hairstyles that can create dents in your hair. You can also use a dry shampoo or texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.

Overall, choosing the best cowboy hat for a round face requires considering the crown height, brim width, shape, and material. By following these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to find the perfect hat that flatters your features and completes your cowboy look.
