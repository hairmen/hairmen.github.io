What Shape Glasses Look Best On A Heart Shaped Face

Welcome to our guide on what shape glasses look best on a heart shaped face. If you have a heart shaped face, you know that finding the perfect glasses can be a daunting task. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the best glasses for your face shape, how to choose the right frame, and some tips on how to style your hair to complement your glasses.

What is a Heart Shaped Face?

A heart shaped face is characterized by a broad forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. If you have a heart shaped face, you may find that glasses can accentuate your features in a way that is not always flattering. However, with the right frame, you can balance your facial proportions and enhance your natural beauty.

Best Glasses for Heart Shaped Faces

The best glasses for heart shaped faces are those that balance out the width of the forehead and the narrowness of the chin. The following frames are ideal for heart shaped faces:

  • Round glasses: Round glasses soften the angles of the face and draw attention to the eyes.
  • Cat-eye glasses: Cat-eye glasses accentuate the cheekbones and add definition to the face.
  • Aviator glasses: Aviator glasses balance out the forehead and the chin and provide a classic look.
  • Rectangular glasses: Rectangular glasses add structure to the face and bring attention to the eyes.

Choosing the Right Frame

When choosing a frame, it's important to consider the size and shape of your face. Heart shaped faces look best with frames that are wider at the top and narrower at the bottom. This helps to balance out the face and draw attention to the eyes.

Additionally, you should consider the color of the frame. If you have warm undertones in your skin, opt for frames in warm colors like gold, brown, or tortoise. If you have cool undertones, choose frames in cool colors like silver, black, or blue.

Hair Style Tips

Your hair can make a big difference in how your glasses look on your face. Here are some tips on how to style your hair to complement your glasses:

  • If you have long hair, consider wearing it down and straight. This will help to balance out the width of your forehead.
  • If you have short hair, consider adding volume to the crown of your head. This will help to balance out the narrowness of your chin.
  • A side part can also help to balance out the proportions of your face.

FAQs

What should I avoid when choosing glasses for a heart shaped face?

Avoid frames that are too narrow or too small for your face. These can make your forehead appear even wider and your chin even narrower. Additionally, avoid frames that are too round, as these can make your face appear even more heart shaped.

Can I wear oversized glasses with a heart shaped face?

Yes, oversized glasses can work well with a heart shaped face. Just make sure that the frame is wider at the top and narrower at the bottom to balance out your facial proportions.

What if I don't like any of the recommended frames?

Ultimately, the most important thing is that you choose a frame that you feel comfortable and confident in. If none of the recommended frames work for you, try on different styles and shapes until you find the perfect pair.

Choosing glasses for a heart shaped face can be a challenge, but with the right frame and a few styling tips, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your glasses. We hope that this guide has been helpful in your search for the perfect glasses.
