Can You Pull Off Baby Bangs

Written by Oliver Oct 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are looking for a change in your hairstyle and want to try something new, baby bangs might be the perfect choice for you. But the question is, can you pull off baby bangs? In this article, we will answer that question and provide you with all the information you need to know about baby bangs.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Baby bangs, also known as micro bangs or short bangs, are bangs that are cut above the eyebrows, giving a bold and trendy look. This hairstyle has been popularized by celebrities such as Emma Watson, Bella Hadid, and Rooney Mara.

However, not everyone can pull off baby bangs. It depends on various factors such as face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In the next sections, we will guide you through the process of determining whether you can pull off baby bangs or not.

How to

The first step to determining whether you can pull off baby bangs is to consider your face shape. Baby bangs tend to look good on people with oval or heart-shaped faces. If you have a round or square face, it might not be the best choice for you.

The next factor to consider is your hair texture. Baby bangs work best on straight or slightly wavy hair. If you have curly hair, it might be difficult to style your bangs and make them look even.

Finally, think about your personal style. Baby bangs are a bold and edgy look, so if you prefer a more traditional or classic style, this might not be the best choice for you.

Step by step

If you have determined that you can pull off baby bangs, here are the steps to achieving the perfect look:

  1. Start by sectioning off the front part of your hair that you want to cut into bangs. Use a comb to create a straight line across your forehead.
  2. Cut the hair above the line you created, making sure to cut it straight across.
  3. Trim the ends of your bangs to your desired length. Keep in mind that baby bangs are usually shorter than regular bangs.
  4. Style your bangs with a straightening iron or a blow dryer to make them look sleek and polished.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your baby bangs:

  • Use a good quality straightening iron to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Apply some hair serum or cream to your bangs to keep them smooth and shiny.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the look that suits you best.

FAQ

1. Will baby bangs suit me if I have a round face?

Baby bangs tend to work better on people with oval or heart-shaped faces. However, if you have a round face and still want to try baby bangs, you can ask your hairstylist to cut them slightly longer than usual.

2. Can I style my baby bangs with a curling iron?

It might be difficult to style your baby bangs with a curling iron, as they are usually too short. However, you can try using a smaller barrel curling iron to create a slight wave.

3. How often do I need to trim my baby bangs?

Since baby bangs are shorter than regular bangs, they might need more frequent trimming. You can trim them every 2-3 weeks to keep them looking neat.

Hair Care

To keep your baby bangs looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your bangs.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

With these tips and tricks, you can pull off baby bangs and rock a bold and trendy look. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style before making the decision to cut your hair.
