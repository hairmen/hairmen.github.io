How To Wear Curly Hair At The Gym Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jun 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that it can be a challenge to keep it looking great while working out at the gym. Sweat, humidity, and intense exercise can all cause your curls to lose their shape and become frizzy. But with the right techniques and products, you can keep your curls looking fabulous during and after your workout.

How to Wear Curly Hair at the Gym

The first thing to consider is the type of workout you'll be doing. If you'll be doing low-impact exercises like yoga or Pilates, you can leave your hair down and let it air dry. But if you'll be doing high-intensity cardio or strength training, you may want to tie your hair up to keep it out of your face and prevent it from getting sweaty.

The best way to keep your curls looking great at the gym is to use a combination of the right products and styling techniques. Here are some tips to help you get started:

1. Start with a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

Before you hit the gym, make sure to wash your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair. This will help to keep your curls hydrated and frizz-free. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter or coconut oil, which are great for moisturizing curly hair.

2. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

To help keep your curls hydrated during your workout, apply a leave-in conditioner before you hit the gym. This will help to prevent your curls from becoming dry and frizzy. Make sure to apply the conditioner evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

3. Tie Your Hair Up

If you're doing high-intensity cardio or strength training, you'll want to tie your hair up to keep it out of your face and prevent it from getting sweaty. Use a hair tie that won't tug or pull on your hair, and avoid pulling your hair too tightly. A loose ponytail or bun is a great option for curly hair.

4. Use a Sweatband

If you're really concerned about sweat getting in your hair, consider wearing a sweatband. This will help to absorb sweat and prevent it from dripping down onto your curls. Look for a sweatband that is made from a moisture-wicking fabric, like polyester or spandex.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to wearing curly hair at the gym:

1. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair.

3. Tie your hair up in a loose ponytail or bun.

4. Put on a sweatband to absorb sweat.

5. After your workout, take your hair down and fluff it up.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair at the Gym

Here are some additional tips to help you maintain your curls at the gym:

1. Don't brush or comb your hair while it's wet. This can cause breakage and damage to your curls.

2. Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help enhance your curls.

3. Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons on a daily basis.

4. Apply a hair oil or serum to your ends to help keep them moisturized.

FAQs

Q: Can I leave my curly hair down while working out at the gym?

A: If you're doing low-impact exercises like yoga or Pilates, you can leave your hair down and let it air dry. But if you're doing high-intensity cardio or strength training, you may want to tie your hair up to keep it out of your face and prevent it from getting sweaty.

Q: What's the best way to tie up curly hair at the gym?

A: Use a hair tie that won't tug or pull on your hair, and avoid pulling your hair too tightly. A loose ponytail or bun is a great option for curly hair.

Curly Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your curly hair:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

2. Avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

3. Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz.

4. Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week.

With these tips and tricks, you can keep your curls looking great at the gym and beyond. Happy exercising!
