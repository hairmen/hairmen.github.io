How To Lengthen Hem Pants A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 29, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of wearing pants that are too short? Do you need to lengthen your hem pants but don't want to spend a fortune at the tailor? Look no further! In this guide, we'll show you the best way to lengthen your hem pants at home without any sewing skills required. Let's get started!

How to Lengthen Hem Pants

Before we dive into the step-by-step process, let's first discuss the tools you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Fabric glue
  • Measuring tape

Step 1: Measure the Desired Length

Start by putting on your pants and measuring how much longer you want them to be. Use the measuring tape to ensure accuracy.

Step 2: Cut the Hem

Once you've determined the desired length, use the scissors to cut off the existing hem. Make sure to cut as straight as possible.

Step 3: Add Extra Fabric

Measure the length you just cut off and add an extra inch to it. Cut a strip of fabric that matches the width of the hem you just cut. Use fabric glue to attach the strip of fabric to the bottom of the pants, making sure to leave a little bit of extra fabric at the ends to fold over.

Step 4: Fold and Glue

Once the extra fabric has been attached, fold it over to create a new hem. Use fabric glue to secure the new hem in place. Make sure to apply enough glue to ensure that the new hem stays in place.

Step 5: Let it Dry

After you've glued the new hem in place, let it dry completely before wearing your pants. This will ensure that the glue has fully set and that the hem won't come undone.

Tips for Lengthening Hem Pants

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when lengthening your hem pants:

  • Use fabric glue specifically designed for hemming. This will ensure that the glue sets properly and that the hem doesn't come undone.
  • Make sure to cut the extra fabric strip to the same width as the hem you just cut off. This will ensure that the new hem looks seamless.
  • Iron the new hem after it has dried to ensure that it looks neat and tidy.

FAQs

Can I Lengthen Hem Pants Without Sewing?

Yes! Using fabric glue is a great way to lengthen your hem pants without any sewing required.

How Long Will the Glue Hold?

The length of time the glue will hold depends on the type of fabric glue you use. Make sure to choose a high-quality fabric glue specifically designed for hemming to ensure that the hem stays in place for as long as possible.

Can I Lengthen Hem Pants More Than Once?

Yes, you can lengthen hem pants more than once. However, keep in mind that each time you lengthen the pants, the hem will become thicker and may start to look a bit bulky.

Hair Care Tip

After lengthening your hem pants, why not treat yourself to a hair mask? Mix together equal parts coconut oil and honey, massage into your hair, and leave for 30 minutes before washing out. Your hair will be left soft, shiny, and nourished.

Now that you know how to lengthen hem pants, you can update your wardrobe without breaking the bank. Happy hemming!
