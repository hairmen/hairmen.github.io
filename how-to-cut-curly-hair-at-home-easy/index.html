How To Cut Curly Hair At Home Easy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 20, 2023 · 3 min read

Curly hair can be notoriously difficult to manage, and finding the right stylist who understands your hair can be a challenge. But what if we told you that you could cut your own curly hair at home, easily and without any stress? In this guide, we’ll take you through the steps to cutting curly hair at home, providing you with tips, tricks and answers to all your questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Curly Hair at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Keep in Mind
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Curly Hair

How to Cut Curly Hair at Home

The first thing you need to do when cutting curly hair at home is to gather the necessary tools. You’ll need a good pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair. It’s important to use sharp scissors, as dull scissors can cause split ends and frizz.

Next, make sure your hair is clean, dry, and detangled. It’s best to cut curly hair when it’s dry, as wet hair can appear longer than it actually is. Additionally, it’s important to work with your natural curls, so avoid straightening your hair before cutting it.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by parting your hair down the middle and clipping one side out of the way.

Step 2: Take a small section of hair from the unclipped side and twist it. This will help you see the length that you’ll be cutting off.

Step 3: Cut the twisted section of hair, making sure to cut in a straight line. Avoid cutting at an angle, as this can cause uneven layers.

Step 4: Repeat this process, working your way towards the back of your head. Remember to cut small sections of hair at a time, as this will help you achieve a more even cut.

Step 5: Once you’re finished with one side, repeat the process on the other side.

Tips to Keep in Mind

- Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can’t put hair back once it’s been cut.

- Use the point-cutting technique to add texture to your hair. This involves cutting into the ends of your hair at an angle, rather than straight across.

- Don’t try to achieve a perfect, straight line when cutting curly hair. Your hair will naturally form different lengths and layers, so embrace your natural texture.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my curly hair when it’s wet?

A: It’s best to cut curly hair when it’s dry, as wet hair can appear longer than it actually is.

Q: How often should I trim my curly hair?

A: It’s recommended to trim your curly hair every 8-12 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care After Cutting Curly Hair

After cutting your curly hair, it’s important to take care of it to maintain its health and natural texture. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair, as these will help to enhance your curls and prevent frizz. Additionally, avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair and cause it to become more frizzy.

Now that you know how to cut curly hair at home easily, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. Remember to take your time, be patient, and embrace your natural curls.
