How To Style Curly Hair Toddler A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 30, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re a parent of a toddler with curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to style their hair on a daily basis. Between the tangles, frizz, and stubborn curls, it’s easy to feel overwhelmed. But fear not! With a few tips and tricks, you can learn how to style your toddler’s curly hair like a pro.

How to Style Curly Hair Toddler

The first step in styling your toddler’s curly hair is to understand their hair type. Curly hair can range from loose waves to tight coils, and each type requires a different approach to styling. Once you’ve identified your toddler’s hair type, you can start experimenting with different styles and products to find what works best for them.

Some popular styles for curly hair toddler include:

  • Pigtails
  • Braids
  • Buns
  • Twists
  • Cornrows

When it comes to products, there are a few key items you’ll need to keep your toddler’s curly hair looking its best:

  • Shampoo and conditioner
  • Detangling spray
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Curl-defining cream
  • Hair gel

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics of styling curly hair toddler, let’s dive into a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by washing your toddler’s hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid sulfates and other harsh chemicals that can strip their hair of its natural oils. Instead, look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair.

Step 2: Detangle

After washing, use a detangling spray and a wide-tooth comb to gently remove any tangles. Be patient and gentle, as curly hair can easily break and become damaged.

Step 3: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your toddler’s hair to help lock in moisture and prevent frizz. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout their hair.

Step 4: Define Curls

Next, apply a curl-defining cream to your toddler’s hair. Use your fingers to scrunch their curls and define their natural texture. For tighter curls, you may want to use a hair gel to help hold the style in place.

Step 5: Style

Finally, it’s time to style your toddler’s hair. Whether you’re doing pigtails, braids, or a bun, be sure to use gentle hair ties and avoid pulling too tight. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to help hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair Toddler

Here are a few additional tips to help you style your toddler’s curly hair:

  • Use a satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz and tangles while your toddler sleeps
  • Try different styles and products to find what works best for your toddler’s hair type
  • Be gentle when combing or brushing their hair to avoid breakage
  • Encourage your toddler to embrace their natural curls and love their unique hair

FAQs About Styling Curly Hair Toddler

How often should I wash my toddler’s curly hair?

It depends on your toddler’s hair type and lifestyle. Some toddlers may only need to wash their hair once a week, while others may need to wash more frequently. Look for signs of dryness or oiliness to determine how often to wash their hair.

What should I do if my toddler’s hair is extremely frizzy?

Try using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help lock in moisture and tame frizz. You can also apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of their hair to help smooth and add shine.

Can I use adult hair products on my toddler’s curly hair?

No, it’s important to use products that are specifically designed for children’s hair. Adult hair products can contain harsh chemicals that can be damaging to your toddler’s hair and scalp.

Hair Care for Curly Hair Toddler

In addition to styling, it’s important to take good care of your toddler’s curly hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid brushing their hair when it’s dry, as this can cause breakage
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle their hair
  • Trim their hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Protect their hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or swimming cap

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently style your toddler’s curly hair and help them embrace their natural beauty.
