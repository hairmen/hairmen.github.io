Is Hair Relaxer Haram Understanding The Muslim Perspective

Written by Petter Jun 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As a Muslim, it is important to understand what is halal (permissible) and haram (forbidden) in our daily lives. This includes the products we use, such as hair relaxers. In this article, we will explore whether or not hair relaxers are haram and provide tips on how to care for your hair in a halal way.

What is Hair Relaxer?

Hair relaxers are a type of chemical treatment used to straighten curly or kinky hair. They work by breaking down the protein bonds in hair, which allows it to be reshaped and styled. Hair relaxers typically contain chemicals such as sodium hydroxide, calcium hydroxide, or guanidine hydroxide.

Is Hair Relaxer Haram?

There is no clear answer to whether or not hair relaxers are haram. Some Muslims believe that any chemical treatment that alters the natural state of hair is haram, while others believe that it is permissible as long as the ingredients are halal. Ultimately, it is up to each individual to decide for themselves.

How to Use Hair Relaxer in a Halal Way

If you choose to use hair relaxer, there are some steps you can take to ensure that you are doing so in a halal way:

1. Choose Halal Ingredients

Look for hair relaxers that use halal ingredients. This may require some research and reading ingredient lists carefully. Some brands may also have halal certification.

2. Perform Wudu Before Applying

Performing wudu (ablution) before applying hair relaxer can help ensure that you are doing so in a pure state. This is especially important if you will be praying after using the product.

3. Use in a Well-Ventilated Area

Hair relaxers contain strong chemicals that can be harmful if inhaled. Use the product in a well-ventilated area to minimize exposure.

4. Follow Instructions Carefully

Follow the instructions on the hair relaxer carefully to ensure that you are using it safely and effectively.

Tips for Halal Hair Care

If you choose not to use hair relaxer or are looking for alternative ways to care for your hair in a halal way, here are some tips:

1. Use Halal Shampoo and Conditioner

Look for shampoos and conditioners that use halal ingredients. These may be labeled as such or require some research into the ingredients.

2. Oil Your Hair Regularly

Oiling your hair regularly can help keep it healthy and shiny. Look for halal oils such as coconut oil, olive oil, or argan oil.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons can damage hair and alter its natural state. Try to avoid using them as much as possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I use hair relaxer if I am fasting?

There is no clear answer to this question. Some scholars believe that using hair relaxer while fasting is permissible, while others believe that it breaks the fast. It is best to consult with a knowledgeable scholar or imam for guidance.

2. Is hair relaxer safe to use?

Hair relaxers can be safe to use if used properly. However, they do contain strong chemicals that can cause damage to hair and skin if not used carefully. It is important to follow the instructions carefully and use the product in a well-ventilated area.

3. Can men use hair relaxer?

Yes, men can use hair relaxer if they choose to. The same halal guidelines apply.

Conclusion

Whether or not hair relaxers are haram is a matter of personal belief and interpretation. If you choose to use hair relaxer, there are steps you can take to ensure that you are doing so in a halal way. Alternatively, there are many halal ways to care for your hair without the use of chemicals. Ultimately, it is up to each individual to decide what is best for them.
