How To Trim A Pixie Cut With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a pixie cut, you know how important it is to keep it looking fresh and stylish. One of the best ways to do this is by trimming it regularly. But, what if you don't have access to a salon or don't want to spend money on a trim? The answer is simple: trim your pixie cut with clippers! In this article, we'll show you how to do it yourself, step-by-step.

Description

Trimming a pixie cut with clippers is a great way to save money and maintain your hairstyle at home. It's a simple process that can be done in just a few minutes, and with a little practice, you'll be able to do it like a pro.

How to Trim a Pixie Cut with Clippers

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, a comb, and a mirror. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

It's important to start with clean, dry hair so that you can see exactly where you need to trim. Wash and dry your hair as you normally would, and make sure it's completely dry before you start.

Step 2: Section your hair

Using your comb, section your hair into manageable pieces. Start with the top section and clip the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Choose your clipper guard

Choose the clipper guard that corresponds to the length you want to trim your hair to. If you're not sure, start with a longer guard and work your way down.

Step 4: Start at the back

Starting at the back of your head, use the clippers to trim your hair to the desired length. Move the clippers in an upward motion, against the direction of the hair growth. Be sure to keep the clippers flat against your head to ensure an even cut.

Step 5: Move to the sides

Once you've trimmed the back, move to the sides. Use the same technique as before, moving the clippers in an upward motion. Be sure to blend the back and sides together for a seamless look.

Step 6: Trim the top

Unclip the top section of your hair and trim it to the desired length using the same technique as before. Be sure to blend the top with the sides and back for a natural look.

Step 7: Clean up the edges

Using the clippers without a guard, clean up the edges around your ears and the nape of your neck. This will give your pixie cut a polished look.

Step 8: Check your work

Check your work in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments. If you notice any uneven spots, use the clippers to even them out.

Step 9: Style as desired

Once you're happy with your trim, style your hair as desired. You can use a styling product to add texture and definition to your pixie cut.

Tips for Trimming a Pixie Cut with Clippers

Here are a few tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid trimming too much off at once.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from all angles.
  • Be patient and take your time. It's better to go slow and get it right than rush and make a mistake.
  • Practice makes perfect! The more you trim your pixie cut with clippers, the easier it will become.

FAQ

Can I trim my pixie cut with scissors?

Yes, you can trim your pixie cut with scissors, but clippers are often easier to use and give a more even cut.

How often should I trim my pixie cut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your hairstyle loses its shape. As a general rule, it's a good idea to trim your pixie cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape.

Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

Keeping your pixie cut looking fresh and stylish requires a little bit of maintenance. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Use a styling product to add texture and definition to your pixie cut.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and a little bit of practice, you'll be able to trim your pixie cut with clippers like a pro. Give it a try and see how easy it can be!
