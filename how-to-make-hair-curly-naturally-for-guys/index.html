How To Make Hair Curly Naturally For Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Jul 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy with straight hair and want to switch up your look, curly hair may be the way to go. While there are several ways to achieve curly hair, not all of them are natural or healthy for your hair. In this article, we'll go over how to make hair curly naturally for guys and some tips for maintaining healthy curls.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Hair Curly Naturally for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Curls
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Make Hair Curly Naturally for Guys

Before we get started, it's important to note that not all hair types can achieve curly hair naturally. If you have naturally curly hair, you can enhance your curls with the tips we'll provide. However, if you have straight hair, you can try these methods to achieve curly hair:

  • Twist hair into sections and let it air dry
  • Braid hair into sections overnight
  • Use a curling cream or mousse and scrunch hair

While these methods can work, they may not produce long-lasting or defined curls. For those looking for more defined and long-lasting curls, we recommend using a perm or getting a curly haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're interested in trying natural methods to achieve curly hair, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dry and frizzy hair.
  2. After washing, gently towel dry your hair. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair. This will help define your curls and keep them moisturized.
  4. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section tightly. The tighter the twist, the tighter the curl will be.
  5. Let your hair air dry. If you're short on time, you can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to speed up the drying process.
  6. Once your hair is completely dry, gently unravel the twists. Do not comb or brush your hair, as this can cause frizz.
  7. Apply a lightweight hair oil to your hair to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Curls

Now that you have curly hair, it's important to maintain its health to keep your curls looking their best:

  • Avoid sulfates and other harsh chemicals in your hair products
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair and cause frizz
  • Invest in a satin or silk pillowcase to reduce frizz while you sleep
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or swim cap

FAQs

Can I make my hair curly without using heat?

Yes, you can use the natural methods we listed earlier to achieve curly hair without using heat.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

You should aim to wash your hair once or twice a week. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dry and frizzy hair.

Can I still use gel or hairspray with curly hair?

Yes, you can still use styling products with curly hair. Just be sure to look for products that are specifically formulated for curly hair and avoid those with harsh chemicals.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair requires a bit of extra effort, but the results are worth it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful curls:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from the elements by wearing a hat or scarf
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can dry it out

With these tips and methods, you can achieve and maintain natural curls for guys. Remember to be patient and gentle with your hair, and you'll have healthy, bouncy curls in no time!
