How To Pull Back Thin Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Nov 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thin hair, you may feel like you're limited in the hairstyles you can do. But fear not! There are plenty of ways to pull back thin hair that will leave you feeling confident and stylish. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for pulling back thin hair, step-by-step instructions, and answers to some frequently asked questions.

How to Pull Back Thin Hair: Step-by-Step

Before we get into the specific styles, let's go over some general tips for pulling back thin hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair some body.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume.
  • Tease the roots of your hair to create more volume.

Now let's get into some specific styles:

1. Low Ponytail

The low ponytail is a classic and easy hairstyle that can work for any occasion. To create a low ponytail, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair back and gather it at the nape of your neck.
  2. Tie your hair with a hair tie.
  3. Use a comb to tease the crown of your head for some added volume.
  4. Take a small section of hair from the underside of your ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to conceal it.
  5. Secure the wrapped hair with a bobby pin.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down style is a great way to show off some length while still pulling back some of your hair. To create a half-up half-down style, follow these steps:

  1. Take a section of hair from the top of your head and pull it back, leaving the rest of your hair down.
  2. Tie the section of hair with a hair tie.
  3. Tease the crown of your head for some added volume.
  4. Use a bobby pin to secure the section of hair to the back of your head.

3. Braided Crown

The braided crown is a beautiful and elegant style that can work for weddings, proms, or any special occasion. To create a braided crown, follow these steps:

  1. Part your hair down the middle and braid each side of your hair.
  2. Bring each braid over the top of your head and secure them with bobby pins.
  3. Use a comb to tease the crown of your head for some added volume.

Tips for Pulling Back Thin Hair

Here are a few additional tips for pulling back thin hair:

  • Avoid using elastic hair ties, which can cause breakage. Instead, use fabric-covered hair ties or scrunchies.
  • Use a dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I still pull back my hair if it's very thin?

A: Yes! There are many ways to pull back thin hair that will look great. Just remember to use volumizing products and avoid heavy products that can weigh your hair down.

Q: What if my hair is too short to pull back?

A: If your hair is too short to pull back, try using bobby pins to pin back any loose strands. You can also try a half-up half-down style, which can work for shorter hair.

Q: Can I still use hair accessories if my hair is thin?

A: Absolutely! Just be sure to choose hair accessories that won't weigh your hair down, such as small clips or headbands.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair

Finally, here are a few tips for keeping your thin hair healthy:

  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Limit your use of hair products, as too much product can weigh your hair down.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.
  • Consider using a volumizing mousse or spray to give your hair some extra lift.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to confidently pull back your thin hair in a variety of styles. Don't be afraid to experiment and find what works best for you!
