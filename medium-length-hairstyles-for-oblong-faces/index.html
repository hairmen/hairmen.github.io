Medium Length Hairstyles For Oblong Faces

Written by Bardi Sep 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, you may find it challenging to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. A medium-length haircut is an excellent choice for oblong faces because it adds volume and dimension to the face. In this blog post, we'll go over some of the best medium length hairstyles for oblong faces, how to style them, and hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and shiny.

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow shape, with a high forehead and prominent chin. The goal of any hairstyle for this face shape is to add width and balance to the face. A medium-length haircut is perfect because it frames the face without making it appear too long or too short.

How To

When choosing a medium length hairstyle for an oblong face, it's important to consider the shape of your features. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Opt for layers to add volume and texture to the hair.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that can make the face appear longer.
  • Consider adding side-swept bangs to balance out a high forehead.
  • Experiment with curls or waves to add width to the face.

Consult with your hairstylist to find the best medium length hairstyle for your unique face shape and hair type.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a medium length hairstyle for oblong faces:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.
  3. Use a curling iron or flat iron to add curls or waves to your hair.
  4. Apply a volumizing spray or mousse to the roots of your hair for added lift.
  5. Use a boar bristle brush to brush out your curls or waves and create a natural look.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling medium length hair for oblong faces:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can make the face appear longer.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length hairstyles for oblong faces:

What is the best haircut for an oblong face?

The best haircut for an oblong face is a medium-length haircut with layers and side-swept bangs.

Can oblong faces pull off short hair?

Oblong faces can pull off short hair, but it's important to choose a style that adds width and volume to the face, such as a bob or pixie cut with layers.

Can I wear my hair straight if I have an oblong face?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have an oblong face, but consider adding layers or waves to add dimension to the face.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and shiny is crucial when styling medium length hair for oblong faces. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too frequently, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently rock a medium length hairstyle that flatters your oblong face shape. Remember to consult with your hairstylist for personalized advice and recommendations.
