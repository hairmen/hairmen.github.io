How Do You Hide The Top Of Thinning Hair In 2023

Written by Bardi Jun 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thinning hair can be distressing, especially for women. It can make you feel self-conscious, and you may start avoiding certain hairstyles or events. However, there are ways to hide the top of thinning hair and boost your confidence. In this article, we will discuss how to hide the top of thinning hair, step-by-step, and also provide hair care tips to maintain healthy hair.

Table of Contents

1. How to hide the top of thinning hair
2. Tips to hide the top of thinning hair
3. FAQ about hiding the top of thinning hair
4. Hair care tips for thinning hair

How to Hide the Top of Thinning Hair

There are several ways to hide the top of thinning hair. Here are some options:

1. Hair Fibers

Hair fibers are small fibers that attach to your hair and create the illusion of fuller hair. They come in different colors to match your hair color. To apply hair fibers, simply shake the bottle over the thinning area and pat gently. You can also use a hairspray to secure the fibers in place.

2. Hair Extensions

Hair extensions can add volume and length to your hair, making it appear fuller. You can use clip-in extensions or sew-in extensions. Clip-in extensions are easy to apply and remove, while sew-in extensions last longer. Make sure to choose extensions that match your hair color and texture.

3. Hair Toppers

Hair toppers are hairpieces that cover the top of your head, where the thinning is most visible. They come in different sizes and colors to match your hair. Hair toppers are easy to apply and can be styled like your natural hair.

4. Hairstyles

Hairstyles can also help to hide the top of thinning hair. You can try a side part, a messy bun, or a layered haircut. Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair tightly, such as high ponytails or braids.

Tips to Hide the Top of Thinning Hair

Here are some tips to help you hide the top of thinning hair:

1. Use a Volumizing Shampoo

A volumizing shampoo can add volume to your hair and make it appear fuller. Look for shampoos that contain keratin or biotin, which are known to strengthen hair.

2. Blow-Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow-drying your hair upside down can add volume to the roots and make your hair appear fuller. Use a round brush to lift your hair and create volume.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it appear thinner. Avoid using flat irons or curling irons too often. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and set the temperature to low.

FAQ about Hiding the Top of Thinning Hair

1. Can hair fibers damage my hair?

No, hair fibers do not damage your hair. They are made of natural fibers and are safe to use. However, make sure to wash your hair thoroughly to remove the fibers.

2. Can hair toppers fall off?

No, hair toppers are designed to stay in place. They come with clips or combs that attach to your natural hair. You can also use hairspray to secure the topper in place.

3. Can hair extensions cause hair loss?

No, hair extensions do not cause hair loss. However, if they are not applied correctly or if they are too heavy, they can damage your hair. Make sure to have your extensions applied by a professional and to remove them properly.

Hair Care Tips for Thinning Hair

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet can promote hair growth and prevent hair loss. Eat foods that are rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals, such as eggs, nuts, spinach, and salmon.

2. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can improve blood circulation and stimulate hair growth. Use your fingertips to massage your scalp for a few minutes every day.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair and make it appear thinner. Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

We hope these tips will help you hide the top of thinning hair and boost your confidence. Remember to take care of your hair and seek professional help if you experience excessive hair loss. With the right care and styling, you can have beautiful, healthy hair.
