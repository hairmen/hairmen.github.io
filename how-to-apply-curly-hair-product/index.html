How To Apply Curly Hair Product A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair can be both beautiful and frustrating to manage. One way to make your curls look their best is by using the right hair products. But with so many types of products on the market, it can be overwhelming to figure out how to properly use them. In this blog post, we'll walk you through the steps of how to apply curly hair product.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair
  • Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner
  • Step 3: Add a curl defining product
  • Step 4: Scrunch and diffuse
  • Tips for Applying Curly Hair Product
  • FAQs About Applying Curly Hair Product
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

The first step to applying curly hair product is to start with clean, damp hair. Shampoo and condition your hair as you normally would, then gently towel dry it so that it's still slightly damp. Avoid using a regular towel to dry your hair, as the rough texture can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently blot your hair dry.

Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner

Once your hair is damp, apply a leave-in conditioner to help moisturize and detangle your curls. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends. Be careful not to apply too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.

Step 3: Add a curl defining product

Next, apply a curl defining product to help enhance your natural curls. There are many types of curl defining products on the market, including creams, gels, and mousses. Choose a product that works best for your hair type and texture. Apply the product in small sections, starting at the back of your head and working your way forward. Use your fingers to scrunch your curls as you apply the product, making sure to coat each strand evenly.

Step 4: Scrunch and diffuse

Once you've applied your curl defining product, it's time to scrunch your curls and diffuse your hair. Use your hands to gently scrunch your curls, lifting them up towards your scalp. This will help enhance your curls and create volume. Then, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on low or medium heat. Hold the diffuser close to your scalp, and move it around in a circular motion to help dry your curls evenly.

Tips for Applying Curly Hair Product

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove buildup from your hair.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your curls.
  • Apply product in small sections, starting at the back of your head and working your way forward.
  • Scrunch your curls with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to help enhance your curls.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help dry your curls evenly.

FAQs About Applying Curly Hair Product

What type of curl defining product should I use?

There are many types of curl defining products on the market, including creams, gels, mousses, and serums. Choose a product that works best for your hair type and texture.

How often should I apply curl defining product?

You should apply curl defining product every time you wash your hair. However, be careful not to use too much product, as this can weigh down your curls.

Can I apply curly hair product to dry hair?

It's best to apply curly hair product to damp hair, as this helps the product distribute evenly throughout your curls. However, if your hair is dry, you can still apply product to help tame frizz and enhance your curls.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

In addition to using the right hair products, it's important to take good care of your curly hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls when they're damp.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can cause damage and dry out your curls. Instead, opt for air drying or diffusing your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help moisturize your curls.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swimming cap.

By following these tips and using the right hair products, you can keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful.
