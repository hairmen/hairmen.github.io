Best Hairstyle For Square Shaped Face Male

Written by William Jun 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a man with a square shaped face and struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that will complement your facial structure? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for men with square shaped faces, along with tips and tricks to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.

Description

A square shaped face is characterized by a wide forehead, strong jawline, and a square chin. The aim of selecting a hairstyle for this face shape is to soften the angles and create a more balanced look.

How To

When choosing a hairstyle, it is important to consider the length, texture, and thickness of your hair. Here are some of the best hairstyles for square shaped face male:

Short Haircuts

A short haircut can work wonders for a square shaped face. A crew cut or buzz cut can help to highlight the strong jawline and create a more balanced look. You can also try a short textured cut or a messy fringe, which will add volume and texture to your hair.

Medium Length Haircuts

If you prefer a medium length haircut, try a side swept undercut or a layered cut. These hairstyles will soften the angles and create a more rounded look. You can also experiment with different partings to find the one that suits you best.

Long Haircuts

For men with long hair, a layered cut or a messy bun can help to soften the angles of a square shaped face. You can also try a half up half down hairstyle, which will add volume to the top of your head and create a more balanced look.

Step By Step

Here are the steps to achieve the perfect hairstyle for square shaped face male:

  1. Determine the length, texture, and thickness of your hair.
  2. Choose a hairstyle that will complement your facial structure.
  3. Consult with your hairstylist to ensure that the hairstyle suits your face shape and hair type.
  4. Use the right hair products to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking healthy and stylish.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when selecting a hairstyle for square shaped face male:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face look more angular.
  • Experiment with different haircuts and styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use hair products such as pomade, wax, or hairspray to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking fresh.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or flat, as they can accentuate the squareness of your face.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a square shaped face?

The best hairstyle for a square shaped face is one that softens the angles and creates a more balanced look. Short haircuts such as crew cuts or buzz cuts, medium length haircuts such as side swept undercuts or layered cuts, and long haircuts such as layered cuts or messy buns are all great options for men with square shaped faces.

How do I know if I have a square shaped face?

A square shaped face is characterized by a wide forehead, strong jawline, and a square chin. If you have these features, you likely have a square shaped face.

Can I still have a beard if I have a square shaped face?

A beard can help to balance out a square shaped face, but it is important to choose the right style. A short boxed beard or a goatee can work well with a square shaped face, but avoid long or bushy beards, as they can make your face appear more angular.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential to achieving the perfect hairstyle for square shaped face male. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy appearance.
  • Use hair products such as hair oil or leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your square shaped face and keep your hair looking healthy and stylish. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and experiment with different haircuts and styles to find the one that suits you best. Happy hairstyling!
