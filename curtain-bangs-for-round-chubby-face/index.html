Curtain Bangs For Round Chubby Face A Complete Guide

If you have a round chubby face and are looking for a new hairstyle that will flatter your features, then curtain bangs may be the perfect option for you. In this article, we'll provide you with everything you need to know about curtain bangs, including how to style them, tips for maintaining them, and more. So, without further ado, let's dive in!

  • How to Style Curtain Bangs for Round Chubby Face

Description

Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle that has been trending for a while now. They are a type of bangs that are parted in the middle, framing the face on either side. Curtain bangs are perfect for those who want to switch up their look without committing to a full-on fringe. They are also great for round chubby faces, as they help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look.

How to Style Curtain Bangs for Round Chubby Face

Styling curtain bangs is relatively easy once you have them cut. Here's how to style them:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair before using any heat styling tools.
  3. Blow-dry your hair, making sure to dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs if necessary.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair oil to your bangs to add shine and tame any flyaways.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Curtain Bangs

If you're considering getting curtain bangs, it's important to get them cut by a professional. However, if you're feeling brave and want to cut them yourself, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by sectioning off the hair you want to cut into bangs.
  2. Divide the hair into two equal sections, one on each side of the part.
  3. Hold the hair in front of your face and decide on the length you want your bangs to be.
  4. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the middle and working your way outwards.
  5. Repeat on the other side, making sure to cut at the same angle.
  6. Blend the bangs by cutting into them with scissors, creating a feathered effect.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips for maintaining your curtain bangs:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to prevent them from getting too long.
  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs from getting oily.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can cause your bangs to become greasy.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them look messy.

FAQ

Can anyone pull off curtain bangs?

Yes, anyone can pull off curtain bangs. However, they are particularly flattering on those with round chubby faces, as they help to elongate the face.

Do curtain bangs require a lot of maintenance?

Curtain bangs do require some maintenance, as they need to be trimmed regularly to prevent them from getting too long. However, they are relatively low-maintenance compared to other types of bangs.

Can I style curtain bangs differently?

Yes, you can style curtain bangs in a variety of ways. For example, you can braid them, pin them back, or curl them.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair from becoming dry and damaged.
  • Apply a weekly hair mask to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause your hair to become dry and brittle.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

So, there you have it - everything you need to know about curtain bangs for round chubby faces. Whether you're looking for a new hairstyle or just want to switch up your look, curtain bangs are a great option for those with round chubby faces. Follow our tips and tricks, and your hair will be looking fabulous in no time!
