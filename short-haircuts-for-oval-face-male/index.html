Short Haircuts For Oval Face Male A Guide To Choose The Best Style

Written by William Aug 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any haircut style. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect haircut that complements your face shape. In this article, we'll guide you through the best short haircuts for oval face male and provide tips on how to maintain them.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircuts for Oval Face Male

The oval face shape is characterized by a slightly rounded jawline, high cheekbones, and a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin. The key to choosing the best haircut for this face shape is to create balance by not making the face appear too long or too wide. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Consider Your Hair Texture

Before choosing a haircut, consider your hair texture. Short haircuts work best for oval face shapes with straight or wavy hair. If you have curly hair, you can still pull off short haircuts, but make sure to leave some length on top to avoid the "mushroom" effect.

2. Opt for a Side Part

A side part is a great option for oval face shapes as it helps to create balance by making the face appear slightly narrower. If you have a receding hairline, a side part can also help to conceal it.

3. Avoid Bangs

While bangs can work for some face shapes, they can make oval faces appear shorter. If you want to add some texture to your hairstyle, consider a textured crop or a messy quiff instead.

4. Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a short haircut, consider your lifestyle. If you have an active lifestyle, a low-maintenance haircut like a buzz cut or a crew cut is a great option. If you prefer a more polished look, consider a classic taper or a slicked-back undercut.

Short Haircuts for Oval Face Male

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a short haircut for oval face male, let's take a look at some of the best styles:

1. Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic low-maintenance haircut that looks great on oval face shapes. It works best on straight or wavy hair and can be customized to your preferred length. This haircut is perfect for men who want a fuss-free look.

2. Crew Cut

The crew cut is another classic short haircut that looks great on oval faces. This style features short hair on the sides and back with slightly longer hair on top. It works best on straight or wavy hair and can be styled with a side part or slicked back.

3. Textured Crop

The textured crop is a trendy short haircut that features textured hair on top and short hair on the sides and back. It works best on straight or wavy hair and can be styled with a side part or messy fringe.

4. Slicked-Back Undercut

The slicked-back undercut is a polished short haircut that features short hair on the sides and back with longer hair on top. This style works best on straight or wavy hair and can be styled with a side part or slicked back for a more polished look.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Oval Face Male

Now that you have chosen the perfect short haircut for your oval face shape, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

1. Visit Your Barber Regularly

To maintain the shape of your haircut, it's important to visit your barber every 4-6 weeks for a trim. This will also prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

2. Use the Right Products

Use the right products to style your hair. If you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture. If you have thick hair, use a matte paste or clay to add definition.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

If you use heat styling tools like a blow dryer or straightener, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

What is the most popular short haircut for oval face male?

The buzz cut and crew cut are popular short haircuts for oval face male.

Can oval face shapes pull off bangs?

Oval face shapes can pull off bangs, but they can make the face appear shorter.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts for Oval Face Male

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair 2-3 times a week with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Protect Your Scalp from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a scalp sunscreen to protect your scalp from the sun's harmful rays.

3. Avoid Overwashing

Avoid washing your hair too often as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness.

In conclusion, choosing the perfect short haircut for oval face male requires considering your hair texture, lifestyle, and face shape. The buzz cut, crew cut, textured crop, and slicked-back undercut are great options for this face shape. Remember to visit your barber regularly, use the right products, and protect your hair from heat to maintain your short haircut.
