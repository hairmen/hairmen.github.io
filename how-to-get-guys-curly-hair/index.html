How To Get Guys Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair is often associated with women, but men can rock those curls too! If you're a guy with straight hair and want to switch things up, or if you have natural curls but want to enhance them, this guide is for you. In this article, we'll go over how to get guys curly hair, from styling tips to hair care. Let's get started!

  • How to Get Guys Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Get Guys Curly Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over some basics. There are a few ways to get curly hair if you don't have it naturally:

  • Use a curling iron or hot rollers
  • Get a perm
  • Use styling products to enhance natural curls

For this guide, we'll focus on the third option: using styling products to enhance natural curls. This is the most low-maintenance and least damaging option.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. Look for products that are moisturizing and sulfate-free, as sulfates can be drying to hair. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently towel-dry, without rubbing too hard.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your damp hair, such as a curl cream, mousse, or gel. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly through your hair. Be careful not to use too much product, as this can weigh down your curls.

Step 3: Diffuse or Air-Dry

Next, you can either diffuse your hair with a blow dryer or let it air-dry. If you choose to diffuse, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer and scrunch your hair upwards towards your scalp. If you're air-drying, don't touch your hair too much as it dries to avoid frizz.

Step 4: Scrunch Out the Crunch

Once your hair is dry, it may feel crunchy from the styling product. To get rid of this crunch, gently scrunch your hair upwards towards your scalp again, using your fingers or a soft towel.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a bit more maintenance than straight hair. Here are some tips to help you keep those curls looking great:

  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip natural oils from your scalp
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and frizz
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends from weighing down your curls

FAQs

Can I get curly hair if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can use styling products such as curl creams or mousses to enhance natural curls or create curls with a curling iron or hot rollers. You can also get a perm, but this is a more drastic and potentially damaging option.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It's best to wash curly hair no more than once or twice a week, as washing too often can strip natural oils from your scalp and hair.

What's the best way to dry curly hair?

The best way to dry curly hair is by air-drying or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a bit more TLC than straight hair. Here are some hair care tips specifically for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair regularly to keep it hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends from weighing down your curls
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and frizz

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock those curly locks in no time. Remember to be patient with your hair, as it may take some time to figure out what products and techniques work best for you. Good luck!
