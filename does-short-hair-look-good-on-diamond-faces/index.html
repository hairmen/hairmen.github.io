Does Short Hair Look Good On Diamond Faces

Written by Bardi Jul 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you may wonder whether short hair will suit you or not. The answer is yes, short hair can look great on diamond faces! However, there are some things to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle that flatters your diamond face shape. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about short hair and diamond faces.

Description

Diamond-shaped faces are characterized by wide cheekbones, narrow forehead, and jawline that is narrower than the cheekbones. This face shape is considered to be one of the most unique and striking, but it can be challenging to find hairstyles that complement it. Short hair is a popular choice for diamond faces as it can accentuate the cheekbones and balance the overall shape of the face.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Diamond Faces

When choosing a short haircut for your diamond face, there are a few things to consider:

1. Length

Short haircuts that end at or above the jawline are ideal for diamond faces. This length helps to balance the width of the cheekbones and the narrowness of the forehead and jawline. You can also opt for a pixie cut or a cropped bob that ends just below the ears.

2. Texture

Adding texture to your short haircut can help soften the angles of a diamond face. Consider getting layers, choppy ends, or a messy fringe to create volume and movement.

3. Parting

The parting of your hair can help to balance your face shape. If you have a diamond face, try a side parting to create the illusion of width at the forehead.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Diamond Faces

Here's how to style short hair for diamond faces:

1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.

Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type to keep your locks healthy and shiny.

2. Towel dry your hair.

Gently pat your hair with a towel to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair as it can cause damage and frizz.

3. Apply a styling product.

Apply a styling product such as mousse, gel, or pomade to your hair to add texture and hold.

4. Blow-dry your hair.

Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends. This will add volume to your hair and create a sleek finish.

5. Style your hair.

Once your hair is dry, style it according to your desired look. You can use a curling iron or straightener to add curls or waves or use a hairspray to keep your locks in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Diamond Faces

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hair:

1. Visit a stylist regularly.

Short hair requires regular maintenance, so make sure to visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

2. Protect your hair from heat damage.

If you use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Use the right products.

Use products that are suitable for your hair type and style. For example, use a volumizing mousse if you want to add volume to your hair.

FAQs

1. Can diamond-faced women wear bangs?

Yes, diamond-faced women can wear bangs. However, it's best to choose a style that complements the face shape, such as side-swept bangs or curtain bangs.

2. What short hairstyles are best for diamond faces?

Short hairstyles that end at or above the jawline, such as a pixie cut or a cropped bob, are ideal for diamond faces. Adding texture and volume can also help to soften the angles of the face.

3. How can I tell if I have a diamond-shaped face?

A diamond-shaped face is characterized by wide cheekbones, a narrow forehead, and a jawline that is narrower than the cheekbones. To determine if you have a diamond-shaped face, measure the widest point of your face at the cheekbones and compare it to the width of your forehead and jawline.

Hair Care for Diamond Faces

When it comes to hair care for diamond faces, it's essential to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained. Regular trims, deep conditioning treatments, and using the right products for your hair type can help keep your locks looking their best. Additionally, protecting your hair from heat damage and avoiding harsh chemicals can help prevent damage and breakage.

In conclusion, short hair can look great on diamond faces, as long as you choose the right length, texture, and parting for your face shape. With the tips and tricks mentioned in this article, you can rock a short hairstyle that accentuates your unique features and flatters your diamond-shaped face.
