What Vitamins To Take On Keto For Hair Loss A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our comprehensive guide on what vitamins to take on keto for hair loss. If you are on a keto diet and experiencing hair loss, you are not alone. Many people on keto diets experience hair loss due to the lack of nutrients that are essential for hair growth. In this article, we will provide you with a detailed guide on the vitamins that you need to take to prevent hair loss while on a keto diet. We will also provide you with some tips on how to take care of your hair while on this diet.

What Causes Hair Loss on Keto Diet?

Before we dive into the vitamins that you need to take to prevent hair loss, let us first understand what causes hair loss on a keto diet. Hair loss on a keto diet is usually caused by a lack of essential nutrients that are required for hair growth. When you are on a keto diet, you restrict your carbohydrate intake, which can lead to a deficiency in vitamins and minerals such as biotin, vitamin D, and iron. These vitamins and minerals are essential for hair growth and maintenance.

What Vitamins to Take on Keto for Hair Loss?

Now that we know what causes hair loss on a keto diet, let us look at the vitamins that you need to take to prevent hair loss:

1. Biotin

Biotin is a B vitamin that is essential for hair growth. It plays a vital role in the production of keratin, which is a protein that makes up your hair, skin, and nails. Biotin also helps to improve the strength and elasticity of your hair. You can take biotin supplements or eat foods that are rich in biotin such as eggs, almonds, and spinach.

2. Vitamin D

Vitamin D is essential for hair growth as it helps to stimulate hair follicles. It also helps to improve the absorption of calcium, which is essential for hair growth. You can get vitamin D from sunlight, but if you are not getting enough sunlight, you can take vitamin D supplements. Foods that are rich in vitamin D include fatty fish, egg yolks, and mushrooms.

3. Iron

Iron is essential for hair growth as it helps to carry oxygen to the hair follicles. If you are deficient in iron, your hair follicles may not receive enough oxygen, which can lead to hair loss. You can get iron from foods such as spinach, beef, and lentils. If you are not getting enough iron from your diet, you can take iron supplements.

Tips for Taking Care of Your Hair on Keto Diet

Aside from taking the vitamins mentioned above, there are some tips that you can follow to take care of your hair while on a keto diet:

1. Drink Plenty of Water

Drinking plenty of water is essential for hair growth as it helps to keep your hair hydrated. Make sure that you drink at least 8 glasses of water per day.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair. Avoid using them as much as possible.

3. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is free from harsh chemicals. Harsh shampoos can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to hair breakage and hair loss.

FAQs

Q. Can a keto diet cause hair loss?

A. Yes, a keto diet can cause hair loss due to the lack of essential nutrients that are required for hair growth.

Q. How long does hair loss last on a keto diet?

A. Hair loss on a keto diet can last for a few months. However, if you take the necessary vitamins and follow the tips mentioned above, you can prevent hair loss.

Conclusion

Hair loss on a keto diet can be prevented by taking the necessary vitamins and following the tips mentioned above. Make sure that you take biotin, vitamin D, and iron supplements or eat foods that are rich in these vitamins. Additionally, drink plenty of water, avoid heat styling tools, and use a gentle shampoo that is free from harsh chemicals. By following these tips, you can prevent hair loss and maintain healthy hair while on a keto diet.
