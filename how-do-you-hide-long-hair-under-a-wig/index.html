How Do You Hide Long Hair Under A Wig Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Christine Sep 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Wearing a wig is a great way to change up your hairstyle or to cover up hair loss. However, one of the biggest challenges when wearing a wig is hiding your natural hair underneath. If you have long hair, this can be especially difficult. Don't worry, though, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll show you how to hide long hair under a wig, step-by-step. We'll also provide you with tips, tricks, and hair care advice to help you maintain healthy hair while wearing a wig.

  • How to Hide Long Hair Under a Wig
  • Tips for Hiding Long Hair Under a Wig

How to Hide Long Hair Under a Wig

Before you put on your wig, it's important to prepare your hair properly. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles and knots. This will make it easier to work with and prevent any lumps or bumps under the wig.

Step 2: Tie Your Hair in a Low Ponytail

Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and tie it in a low ponytail. Make sure it's not too tight or too loose. Use a snag-free elastic band to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 3: Twist and Pin Your Hair

Twist your ponytail into a tight bun and secure it with bobby pins. Make sure the bun is flat against your head, and the pins are hidden underneath. This will prevent any bulkiness under the wig.

Step 4: Wear a Wig Cap

Put on a wig cap over your hair bun. A wig cap helps to keep your hair in place and provides a smooth surface for the wig to sit on. Tuck any loose strands of hair under the wig cap.

Step 5: Put on Your Wig

Now you're ready to put on your wig! Adjust the wig to fit comfortably on your head, making sure it covers the wig cap and hair bun completely. Style the wig as desired, and you're good to go!

Tips for Hiding Long Hair Under a Wig

Here are some additional tips to help you hide your long hair under a wig:

  • Use a wig grip or wig tape to keep the wig in place. This will prevent any slipping or shifting of the wig, which can reveal your natural hair underneath.
  • Choose a wig with a full or partial lace front. This will give you a more natural-looking hairline and make it easier to blend in your natural hair.
  • Opt for a wig with a higher density or thicker hair. This will help to conceal your natural hair underneath.
  • Consider cutting your hair shorter or getting a trim. This will make it easier to hide your hair under a wig and reduce bulkiness.

FAQs About Hiding Long Hair Under a Wig

Can I wear a wig if I have long hair?

Yes, you can wear a wig if you have long hair. Just make sure to follow the steps outlined above to hide your hair properly.

Do I need to wash my hair before wearing a wig?

It's not necessary to wash your hair before wearing a wig, but it's recommended. Clean hair will help to prevent any oil or dirt buildup on the wig cap, which can cause irritation and discomfort.

How often should I wash my wig?

You should wash your wig every 6-8 wears, or when it starts to look dirty or oily. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for wigs, and let it air dry on a wig stand to maintain its shape.

Hair Care Tips for Wearing a Wig

Wearing a wig can be a fun and convenient way to change up your look. However, it's important to take care of your natural hair underneath to prevent damage and maintain healthy hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, such as blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons. These can cause damage and make it more difficult to hide your hair under a wig.
  • Protect your hair from the elements, such as sun, wind, and cold weather. Wear a hat or scarf to cover your hair when outdoors.
  • Take breaks from wearing your wig to give your hair and scalp a chance to breathe. This will help to prevent any irritation or discomfort.

By following these tips and tricks, you can successfully hide your long hair under a wig and maintain healthy hair at the same time. Have fun experimenting with different wig styles and colors!
