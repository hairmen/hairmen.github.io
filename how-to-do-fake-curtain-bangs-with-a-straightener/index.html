How To Do Fake Curtain Bangs With A Straightener In 2023

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and want to switch things up? Curtain bangs are a trendy and effortless way to change your look without having to commit to a drastic haircut. Plus, you can create fake curtain bangs with just a straightener! In this article, we'll guide you through the steps to achieve the perfect curtain bangs with a straightener.

How to Do Fake Curtain Bangs with a Straightener

Fake curtain bangs are a great way to switch up your hairstyle without committing to a permanent change. Here are the steps to achieve the perfect curtain bangs with just a straightener:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and blow-drying it straight. This will make it easier to work with and ensure your bangs lay flat. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, use a straightening iron to smooth it out before starting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, part your hair down the middle and separate your bangs from the rest of your hair. Clip the rest of your hair away and focus on your bangs.

Step 3: Curl Your Bangs

Using a straightener, curl your bangs away from your face. Start by placing the straightener at the roots of your bangs, then twist it away from your face. Glide the straightener down the length of your bangs, twisting it slightly as you go. Repeat this process until all of your bangs are curled.

Step 4: Comb Your Bangs

Once you've curled your bangs, use a comb to gently brush them out. This will give them a natural look and help them blend in with the rest of your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Bangs

If you want more volume, use a teasing comb to tease the roots of your bangs. You can also use hairspray to hold them in place.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a detailed step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect fake curtain bangs:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and blow-drying it straight. This will ensure your bangs lay flat and are easy to work with.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, part your hair down the middle and separate your bangs from the rest of your hair. Clip the rest of your hair away and focus on your bangs.

Step 3: Heat Your Straightener

Turn on your straightener and let it heat up. Make sure it's set to the right temperature for your hair type.

Step 4: Curl Your Bangs

Starting at the roots of your bangs, take a small section and place the straightener at the base. Twist it away from your face and glide it down the length of your hair. Repeat this process until all of your bangs are curled.

Step 5: Comb Your Bangs

Using a comb, gently brush out your bangs to create a natural look.

Step 6: Style Your Bangs

If you want more volume, use a teasing comb to tease the roots of your bangs. You can also use hairspray to hold them in place.

Tips for Perfect Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curtain bangs:

  • Make sure your straightener is set to the right temperature for your hair type. Too much heat can damage your hair.
  • Start with a small section of hair and work your way up. This will ensure you don't miss any sections and your bangs are evenly curled.
  • Brush out your bangs after curling them to create a natural look.
  • Use a teasing comb to add volume to your bangs.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and angles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Can I use a curling iron instead of a straightener?

Yes, you can use a curling iron to create curtain bangs. However, a straightener is more versatile and can also be used to straighten your hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's best to wash your hair every two to three days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils. However, if your hair gets oily quickly, you may need to wash it more often.

How do I maintain my curtain bangs?

To maintain your curtain bangs, trim them every four to six weeks. You can also use hairspray or dry shampoo to keep them in place between washes.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips to keep your curtain bangs looking their best:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a straightener or curling iron.
  • Trim your bangs every four to six weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Use a dry shampoo or hairspray to keep them in place between washes.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

And that's it! Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect fake curtain bangs with a straightener. Whether you're looking for a new look or just want to switch things up, curtain bangs are a great choice. Happy styling!
