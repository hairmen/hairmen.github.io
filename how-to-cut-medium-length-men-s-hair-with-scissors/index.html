How To Cut Medium Length Men s Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to cut your medium length men's hair with scissors? Look no further! In this guide, we'll go through how to cut your hair at home with scissors in a relaxed and easy-to-follow language. This guide is perfect for those looking to save money on haircuts or simply want to take matters into their own hands. Let's get started!

How to Cut Medium Length Men's Hair with Scissors

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before starting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair. It's also helpful to have a mirror and a spray bottle filled with water to keep your hair damp while cutting.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before cutting, it's best to wash and dry your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that your hair is clean and free of any product buildup. Once your hair is dry, comb it to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, section your hair into manageable parts. Start by dividing your hair in half, from ear to ear. Clip the top section away and focus on the bottom section first. Then, divide the bottom section into smaller sections, depending on how thick your hair is.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Starting with the bottom section, take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the section between your index and middle fingers and use your scissors to cut the ends. Remember to cut slowly and carefully, making small snips to avoid cutting too much hair.

Continue cutting the bottom section, taking small sections at a time. Once you're finished with the bottom section, unclip the top section and repeat the same process.

Step 5: Blend Your Hair

After cutting both sections, it's time to blend your hair. To do this, take a small section of hair from the top and bottom sections and comb them together. Hold the section tightly between your fingers and cut the ends, making sure to blend the hair together.

Continue blending small sections of hair until your hair is even and blended.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Once you're finished cutting, it's important to check your work. Use a mirror to check the back and sides of your head to ensure that your hair is even and blended. If you notice any uneven areas, go back and make small snips to even it out.

Tips for Cutting Medium Length Men's Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to keep in mind while cutting your hair:

  • Start with small snips to avoid cutting too much hair
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or frustrated
  • Use a spray bottle to keep your hair damp while cutting
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as this can lead to uneven cuts
  • Use a mirror to check your work

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut curly hair with scissors. Just make sure to cut it when it's dry and use a technique called "twist and snip" to avoid creating uneven layers.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake while cutting, don't panic. You can always go back and make small snips to even it out. If you're unsure, it's best to leave it and seek professional help.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat styling tools

And there you have it! A step-by-step guide on how to cut medium length men's hair with scissors. Remember to take your time and follow the tips provided for the best results. Happy cutting!
