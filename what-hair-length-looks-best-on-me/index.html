What Hair Length Looks Best On Me A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Length

Written by Petter Oct 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly trying out different hair lengths, only to end up feeling unsatisfied with the results? It's time to put an end to the guesswork and discover what hair length looks best on you. In this guide, we'll take you through the process of finding your perfect hair length, from determining your face shape to choosing the right style. Let's get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before deciding on a hair length, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a style that flatters your features and complements your overall look. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Using a measuring tape, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Write down each measurement.

Step 2: Determine Your Face Length

Measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.

Step 3: Analyze Your Measurements

Compare your measurements to the following guidelines to determine your face shape:

  • Round: Your face length is similar to your face width, and your jawline is soft and rounded.
  • Oval: Your face length is greater than your face width, and your jawline is softly rounded.
  • Square: Your face length, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar in width.
  • Heart: Your forehead is wider than your cheekbones, and your chin comes to a point.
  • Diamond: Your cheekbones are wider than your forehead and jawline, and your chin comes to a point.
  • Oblong: Your face length is greater than your forehead and jawline, and your chin is pointy.

Choosing the Right Hair Length Based on Your Face Shape

Now that you've determined your face shape, it's time to choose the right hair length for you. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

  • Round: Longer hair lengths, such as shoulder-length or longer, can help elongate your face and create a more oval shape.
  • Oval: Lucky you! Oval faces can pull off just about any hair length, from short pixie cuts to long, flowing locks.
  • Square: Soften your strong jawline with a shoulder-length or longer cut with layers that frame your face.
  • Heart: Avoid short, choppy cuts that accentuate your pointy chin. Opt for longer styles with layers and volume around your cheekbones.
  • Diamond: Add volume to the sides of your face with a layered cut that falls just below your chin.
  • Oblong: Create the illusion of a shorter face with a chin-length bob or lob.

Tips for Maintaining Your Perfect Hair Length

Once you've found your perfect hair length, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in quality styling tools and products to help you achieve your desired look.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and lead to dryness and breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about hair length:

  • Q: Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?
  • A: It depends on your face shape. Short haircuts can actually elongate round faces if they are styled with volume at the top.
  • Q: Can I pull off a pixie cut?
  • A: Pixie cuts look great on oval and heart-shaped faces, but may not be as flattering on square or round faces.
  • Q: Does my hair texture affect what length looks best on me?
  • A: Yes, your hair texture can play a role in determining what length looks best on you. Fine hair may look better with shorter cuts, while thicker hair can handle longer lengths.

Hair Care for Your Perfect Length

Regardless of your hair length, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental stressors by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

Now that you have a better understanding of what hair length looks best on you, it's time to find your perfect style. Whether you opt for a short bob or long, flowing locks, the key is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Happy styling!
