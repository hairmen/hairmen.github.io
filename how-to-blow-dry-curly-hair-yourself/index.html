How To Blow Dry Curly Hair Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to style it. Blow drying curly hair can be tricky, but with the right technique and tools, you can achieve a salon-worthy blowout at home. In this article, we’ll walk you through how to blow dry curly hair yourself, from start to finish.

Table of Contents

  • How to Blow Dry Curly Hair Yourself
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Blow Drying Curly Hair
  • FAQs about Blow Drying Curly Hair

How to Blow Dry Curly Hair Yourself

Blow drying curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right technique and tools, you can achieve a smooth, frizz-free finish. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • A diffuser attachment for your blow dryer
  • A heat protectant spray
  • A wide-tooth comb
  • A round brush
  • A leave-in conditioner or serum

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to blow dry your curly hair:

Step 1: Start with clean, towel-dried hair

Blow drying curly hair when it’s wet can cause damage and frizz. So, start by gently towel-drying your hair to remove excess water.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant spray

Spray a heat protectant on your hair to protect it from heat damage.

Step 3: Detangle your hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.

Step 4: Attach the diffuser to your blow dryer

Attach the diffuser to your blow dryer. The diffuser will help distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz.

Step 5: Start blow drying your hair

Start blow drying your hair on a low heat setting. Use the diffuser to dry each section of your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a scrunching motion to enhance your curls.

Step 6: Use a round brush to smooth your hair

Once your hair is mostly dry, switch to a round brush to smooth your hair and create volume at the roots.

Step 7: Finish with a leave-in conditioner or serum

Apply a leave-in conditioner or serum to your hair to add moisture and shine.

Tips for Blow Drying Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect blowout:

  • Use a diffuser attachment to reduce frizz and enhance your curls.
  • Blow dry your hair on a low heat setting to prevent damage.
  • Use a round brush to add volume at the roots.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or serum to add moisture and shine.
  • Avoid touching your hair while blow drying to prevent frizz.

FAQs about Blow Drying Curly Hair

Can you blow dry curly hair without a diffuser?

While it’s possible to blow dry curly hair without a diffuser, using a diffuser can help enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

What’s the best heat setting for blow drying curly hair?

The best heat setting for blow drying curly hair is low or medium. High heat can damage your hair and cause frizz.

Can you blow dry curly hair every day?

Blow drying curly hair every day can cause damage and dryness. It’s best to limit blow drying to a few times a week.

Curly Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you care for your curly hair:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of moisture.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to add moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and maintain your curls.

With these tips and techniques, you can blow dry your curly hair yourself and achieve a beautiful, frizz-free finish. Remember to use a diffuser, low heat, and a leave-in conditioner or serum to protect your hair and enhance your curls.
