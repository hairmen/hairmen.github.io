How To Figure Out What Hairstyle Is Best For You

Written by Bardi Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're feeling stuck with the same old hairstyle, it may be time for a change. But with so many options out there, it can be hard to figure out what hairstyle is best for you. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of finding the perfect hairstyle for your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type
  • Step 3: Decide on Your Personal Style
  • Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. This will help you narrow down your options and find a hairstyle that flatters your features. There are several common face shapes, including:

  • Round
  • Square
  • Oval
  • Heart-shaped
  • Diamond-shaped

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, compare the measurements to the following guidelines:

  • Round: forehead and jawline are approximately the same width, with full cheeks
  • Square: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all approximately the same width
  • Oval: forehead is slightly wider than the jawline, with gently rounded hairline and jawline
  • Heart-shaped: forehead is wider than the jawline, with a pointy chin
  • Diamond-shaped: high cheekbones, narrow forehead and jawline

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that complement your features.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

The next step in choosing the perfect hairstyle is to consider your hair type. Different hairstyles work better for different hair types, so it's important to take this into account when making your decision. Some common hair types include:

  • Straight
  • Wavy
  • Curly
  • Thick
  • Thin
  • Fine

If you have straight hair, you may want to consider a blunt cut or a sleek, layered look. If you have curly hair, you may want to embrace the curls and go for a voluminous, layered look. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a shorter cut to help manage the volume. If you have thin hair, you may want to go for a longer, layered look to add volume and dimension.

Step 3: Decide on Your Personal Style

The final step in choosing the perfect hairstyle is to decide on your personal style. Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and make you feel confident and comfortable. Some common styles include:

  • Classic
  • Edgy
  • Bohemian
  • Chic
  • Sporty

Think about the type of clothes you wear, the activities you enjoy, and the overall vibe you want to give off. This will help you choose a hairstyle that fits your personal style.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle

  • Bring pictures to your stylist to help them understand the look you're going for
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on styling your hair
  • Try on wigs or use virtual makeover tools to get an idea of what different hairstyles will look like on you
  • Don't be afraid to experiment and try something new!

FAQs About Choosing a Hairstyle

How often should I get my hair cut?

This depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Will a short hairstyle work for me?

Short hairstyles can work for anyone, regardless of face shape or hair type. However, it's important to choose a style that flatters your features and fits your personal style.

How do I choose a hair color?

When choosing a hair color, consider your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. It's also important to choose a color that complements your personal style and fits your lifestyle.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Hairstyle

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy and prevent color fading
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair

By following these tips and considering your face shape, hair type, and personal style, you can find the perfect hairstyle for you. Whether you go for a classic bob or a trendy pixie cut, your new look is sure to turn heads!
