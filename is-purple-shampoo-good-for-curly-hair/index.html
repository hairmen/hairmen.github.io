Is Purple Shampoo Good For Curly Hair Everything You Need To Know

Written by Daniel Sep 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Curly hair can be challenging to maintain and keep healthy. It requires a lot of effort, care, and attention, and the use of the right hair care products can make a significant difference. One of the most popular and talked-about hair care products in recent times is purple shampoo. But is purple shampoo good for curly hair? In this article, we will explore everything you need to know about purple shampoo and its impact on curly hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To Use Purple Shampoo on Curly Hair
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips for Using Purple Shampoo on Curly Hair
  5. FAQs

Description

Purple shampoo is a type of shampoo that is specifically designed for blonde or silver hair. It contains purple pigments that neutralize brassy or yellow tones in the hair, leaving it looking brighter, shinier, and more vibrant. However, purple shampoo is not just limited to blonde or silver hair. It can also be used for other hair types, including curly hair.

How To Use Purple Shampoo on Curly Hair

Using purple shampoo on curly hair is quite simple. Here is a step-by-step guide:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Wet your hair thoroughly.
  2. Apply a small amount of purple shampoo to your palms and massage it into your scalp and hair.
  3. Let the shampoo sit for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water.
  5. Apply conditioner to your hair and leave it in for a few minutes.
  6. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.
  7. Repeat the process once or twice a week, depending on how often you wash your hair.

Tips for Using Purple Shampoo on Curly Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when using purple shampoo on curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free purple shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment after using purple shampoo to prevent your hair from becoming dry or brittle.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing it with purple shampoo.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized and healthy.
  • Don't use purple shampoo too often, as it can dry out your hair and cause damage.

FAQs

1. Can purple shampoo be used on all hair types?

Yes, purple shampoo can be used on all hair types, but it is most commonly used on blonde or silver hair.

2. Will purple shampoo turn curly hair purple?

No, purple shampoo will not turn curly hair purple. It is designed to neutralize brassy or yellow tones, not add color to your hair.

3. How often should I use purple shampoo on curly hair?

You should use purple shampoo on curly hair once or twice a week, depending on how often you wash your hair.

Curly Hair Care Tips

Here are a few tips to keep your curly hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to avoid frizz and maintain your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your hair from becoming tangled or frizzy while you sleep.

In conclusion, purple shampoo can be a great addition to your curly hair care routine, but it is important to use it correctly and in moderation. Follow the tips and guidelines mentioned in this article to keep your curly hair healthy, vibrant, and beautiful.
