What Colour Suits My Skin Tone

Written by Luffy Oct 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Welcome to our guide on what colour suits your skin tone. Choosing the right colours can make a huge difference in how you look and feel, and it's important to choose shades that complement your skin tone. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about what colours work best for your skin tone.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Colours
  4. Tips for Choosing Colours That Suit Your Skin Tone
  5. FAQs

Description

Your skin tone is determined by the amount of melanin in your skin, and it can be classified as warm, cool, or neutral. Warm skin tones have yellow, peach, or golden undertones, while cool skin tones have pink, red, or blue undertones. Neutral skin tones have a mixture of both warm and cool undertones.

Knowing your skin tone is important when choosing clothing, makeup, and hair colours that will look best on you. Wearing colours that complement your skin tone can make your complexion look brighter and more vibrant, while the wrong colours can make you look washed out or sallow.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

There are a few different ways to determine your skin tone:

  • Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones, and if they appear green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you probably have neutral undertones.
  • Try on clothing in different colours to see which ones make your skin look brighter and more vibrant. If you look best in warm colours like red, orange, and yellow, you have warm undertones. If you look best in cool colours like blue, green, and purple, you have cool undertones. If you look good in both, you have neutral undertones.
  • Ask a makeup artist or stylist to help you determine your skin tone.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Colours

Once you've determined your skin tone, follow these steps to choose colours that will suit you:

  1. Choose colours that are opposite your skin tone on the colour wheel. For example, if you have warm undertones, look for cool colours like blue, green, and purple. If you have cool undertones, look for warm colours like red, orange, and yellow.
  2. Choose colours that are in the same family as your skin tone. For warm undertones, look for shades of red, orange, and yellow, while for cool undertones, look for shades of blue, green, and purple.
  3. Avoid colours that are too close to your skin tone, as they can make you look washed out. Instead, choose colours that provide contrast.
  4. Experiment with different shades to find the ones that look best on you. For example, if you have warm undertones, try different shades of red to see which one looks best on you.

Tips for Choosing Colours That Suit Your Skin Tone

Here are some additional tips for choosing colours that will flatter your skin tone:

  • Consider the occasion. Bright, bold colours can be great for a night out, but you may want to choose more muted shades for work or other professional settings.
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match. Combining different colours and patterns can create a unique and stylish look.
  • When in doubt, stick with neutral colours like black, white, and grey.
  • Remember that your skin tone can change throughout the year. For example, you may have a warmer skin tone in the summer when you have a tan, and a cooler skin tone in the winter when you're paler.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing colours for your skin tone:

Q: Can I wear colours that don't suit my skin tone?

A: Of course! While it's important to choose colours that flatter your skin tone, ultimately you should wear what makes you feel comfortable and confident.

Q: Can my skin tone change over time?

A: Yes, your skin tone can change due to factors like sun exposure, aging, and hormonal changes.

Q: Can I wear colours that are out of season?

A: Yes! While certain colours may be more popular in certain seasons, there are no hard and fast rules when it comes to fashion.

Hair Care Tips for Your Skin Tone

Your hair colour can also have a big impact on how well you suit certain colours. Here are some hair care tips for different skin tones:

  • Warm skin tones look great with warm hair colours like golden blonde, caramel, and copper.
  • Cool skin tones look great with cool hair colours like ash blonde, platinum, and cool brown.
  • Neutral skin tones can wear a variety of hair colours, but may want to avoid extremes like very light or very dark shades.

Remember, choosing colours that suit your skin tone is all about finding what works best for you. Don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your wardrobe!
