What Type Of Haircut Will Suit Me

Written by Christine Jun 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for a change? Finding the right haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture can be a daunting task. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of finding the perfect haircut that complements your features and enhances your overall look.

Description

Before we dive into the different types of haircuts, let's first understand what goes into finding the right one. Your face shape, hair texture, and personal style are the three main factors that determine which haircut will suit you best. Your face shape can be oval, round, square, or heart-shaped, while your hair texture can be curly, wavy, or straight. Your personal style is also important, as it defines your overall look and can influence the type of haircut you choose.

How to Find Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right haircut is to determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and examine your face in the mirror. Here's a breakdown of the different face shapes and the haircuts that suit them:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck. This face shape is considered to be the most versatile, and almost any haircut will suit you. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, an oval face shape can pull off any style. However, if you have a long forehead, consider getting bangs to balance out your features.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to create the illusion of length and angles. A layered haircut that frames your face will help elongate your features. Avoid blunt cuts and center parts, as they can make your face appear rounder.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you'll want to soften your angles with a layered haircut that adds movement to your hair. Side-swept bangs and long layers that fall around your face will help balance out your features.

Heart-Shaped Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to draw attention away from your forehead and emphasize your cheekbones. A layered bob or a long, layered haircut with side-swept bangs will suit you best.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you've determined your face shape, it's time to find the perfect haircut. Here's a step by step guide:

  1. Research different types of haircuts that suit your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Find inspiration photos of haircuts you like and bring them to your hairstylist.
  3. Consult with your hairstylist and ask for their professional opinion on which haircut will suit you best.
  4. Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair.
  5. Choose a haircut that complements your personal style and enhances your features.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the perfect haircut:

  • Don't be afraid to try something new. Hair grows back, so if you don't like your new haircut, it's not permanent.
  • Consider getting a trim every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Invest in quality hair products that will help you achieve the look you want.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding the right haircut:

What if I have thinning hair?

If you have thinning hair, consider getting a layered haircut that adds volume and movement to your hair. Avoid blunt cuts, as they can make your hair appear thinner.

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair, consider getting a layered haircut that enhances your natural curls. Avoid heavy bangs, as they can be difficult to style with curly hair.

What if I have a busy lifestyle?

If you have a busy lifestyle, consider getting a low maintenance haircut that requires minimal styling. A bob or a pixie cut are great options for those who don't have a lot of time to style their hair.

Hair Care

Now that you've found the perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling tools and let your hair air dry whenever possible.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Now that you know what type of haircut will suit you, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and transform your look. Remember to have fun with your hair and experiment with different styles until you find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
