Best Short Haircut For Square Face A Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Nov 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a square face, you may be wondering what the best short haircut is for you. A square face has a strong jawline and a broad forehead, which can make it challenging to find a hairstyle that flatters your features. In this guide, we'll explore the best short haircuts for square faces, how to cut and style them, and tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The best short haircut for a square face is one that softens your features and balances your proportions. This can be achieved through layers, texture, and strategic shaping around the face. Some of the most popular short haircuts for square faces include the pixie cut, the bob, and the lob (long bob).

When choosing a short haircut for a square face, it's important to consider your hair type and texture. Fine hair may benefit from a shorter, layered cut, while thick hair may require more weight and structure to prevent it from looking bulky. Your stylist can help you determine the best haircut for your hair type and face shape.

How to

The best way to get a short haircut for a square face is to visit a professional stylist. They can assess your face shape, hair type, and personal style to create a customized haircut that flatters your features. Before your appointment, it's a good idea to gather inspiration photos and communicate your preferences to your stylist.

During your appointment, your stylist will likely start by washing and conditioning your hair. They may also apply a heat protectant and styling product to help your hair hold its shape. Then, they'll use scissors and/or clippers to cut your hair to the desired length and shape. They may also add layers or texture to create movement and volume.

Once your haircut is complete, your stylist will likely blow dry and style your hair to show you the finished look. They may also offer tips for styling your hair at home.

Step by Step

Here are the basic steps for getting a short haircut for a square face:

  1. Choose a professional stylist who has experience cutting hair for square faces.
  2. Communicate your preferences and show inspiration photos to your stylist.
  3. Wash and condition your hair before your appointment.
  4. Let your stylist cut your hair to the desired length and shape.
  5. Add layers and texture to create movement and volume.
  6. Blow dry and style your hair to show you the finished look.

Tips

Here are some tips for getting and maintaining a short haircut for a square face:

  • Choose a haircut that flatters your features and complements your personal style.
  • Consider your hair type and texture when choosing a haircut.
  • Communicate your preferences and show inspiration photos to your stylist.
  • Use styling products to help your hair hold its shape.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for a square face?

The best short haircut for a square face is one that softens your features and balances your proportions. Some popular options include the pixie cut, the bob, and the lob (long bob).

How do I know if I have a square face?

A square face has a strong jawline and a broad forehead. To determine your face shape, measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If they are roughly equal in size, you likely have a square face.

Can I still have bangs with a square face?

Yes, you can still have bangs with a square face. However, it's important to choose a style that flatters your features. Soft, side-swept bangs can help balance a strong jawline, while blunt bangs may accentuate it.

Hair Care

To maintain your short haircut for a square face, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. This may include washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using styling products to help your hair hold its shape, and visiting your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.

You may also want to consider using a heat protectant before using styling tools such as flat irons or curling irons. This can help prevent damage and breakage.

Overall, the best short haircut for a square face is one that flatters your features and complements your personal style. By working with a professional stylist and following a regular hair care routine, you can achieve a look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
