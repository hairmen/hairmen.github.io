What Does Rain Do To Your Hair

Written by Bardi Aug 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you worried about how rain affects your hair? Well, you're not alone. Rain can wreak havoc on your hair, especially if you have chemically processed hair or a relaxed hair type. In this article, we’ll explore what rain does to your hair and give you tips on how to protect your tresses from the rain.

Description

Rainwater contains impurities and minerals that can harm your hair. When your hair gets wet, it absorbs the minerals that are present in the water, which can cause dryness, frizz, and damage. As a result, your hair may lose its shine and softness. This is especially true for relaxed hair, which is already weakened by chemicals.

How to Protect Your Hair from Rain

Here are some tips on how to protect your hair from rain:

1. Cover Your Hair

The best way to protect your hair from rain is to cover it with a hat or a scarf. This will prevent the rainwater from coming into contact with your hair, reducing the risk of damage.

2. Use a Water-Resistant Spray

You can use a water-resistant spray to protect your hair from the rain. These sprays contain a barrier that repels water, keeping your hair dry and protected. Apply the spray to your hair before you go out in the rain.

3. Avoid Using Heat Tools

Using heat tools on wet hair can cause damage. Avoid using blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons on wet hair, as they can cause breakage and split ends.

4. Moisturize Your Hair

Moisturizing your hair is crucial to keep it healthy and protected from the elements. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and soft.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to protecting your hair from rain:

Step 1: Cover Your Hair

Wear a hat or a scarf to protect your hair from the rain.

Step 2: Apply a Water-Resistant Spray

Apply a water-resistant spray to your hair before you go out in the rain.

Step 3: Avoid Heat Tools

Avoid using heat tools on wet hair.

Step 4: Moisturize Your Hair

Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you protect your hair from rain:

1. Don't Brush Your Hair When It's Wet

Wet hair is more fragile and prone to breakage. Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent damage.

2. Use a Satin Pillowcase

Sleeping on a satin pillowcase can help reduce friction and prevent breakage.

3. Wash Your Hair Less Frequently

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it more vulnerable to damage. Wash your hair less frequently to keep it healthy and protected.

FAQ

What Should I Do If My Hair Gets Wet in the Rain?

If your hair gets wet in the rain, gently pat it dry with a towel and apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep it moisturized.

Can Rainwater Damage Relaxed Hair?

Yes, rainwater can damage relaxed hair. The minerals and impurities present in rainwater can cause dryness, frizz, and damage.

How Often Should I Use a Water-Resistant Spray?

You should use a water-resistant spray every time you go out in the rain to protect your hair from damage.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair is essential to keep it healthy and protected from the elements. Using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil can help keep your hair moisturized and prevent damage. Avoid using heat tools on wet hair, and cover your hair with a hat or a scarf when it's raining. By following these tips, you can protect your hair from the harmful effects of rainwater.
