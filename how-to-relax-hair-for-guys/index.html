How To Relax Hair For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy looking to relax your hair, you've come to the right place! Relaxing your hair can be a great way to achieve a straight, smooth look. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of relaxing your hair, from preparation to aftercare. We'll also provide some tips and answer some frequently asked questions to help you achieve the best results possible.

Table of Contents

  • Preparation
  • Application
  • Processing
  • Rinsing
  • Aftercare
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Preparation

Before you begin the relaxing process, there are a few things you'll need to do to prepare:

  • Choose the right relaxer: There are different types of relaxers available, so it's important to choose the one that's right for your hair type. Look for a relaxer formulated for men's hair.
  • Protect your skin: Apply petroleum jelly or a protective cream around your hairline and ears to prevent the relaxer from getting on your skin.
  • Detangle your hair: Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before applying the relaxer.
  • Read the instructions: Make sure you read the instructions carefully before you begin.

Application

Now that you're prepared, it's time to apply the relaxer:

  • Divide your hair into sections: Use clips to divide your hair into four sections.
  • Apply the relaxer: Starting at the back of your head, use a brush to apply the relaxer to each section of hair. Be careful not to get the relaxer on your scalp.
  • Smooth the relaxer: Use the back of the comb to smooth the relaxer through each section of hair.

Processing

After you've applied the relaxer, it's time to let it process:

  • Check the time: Follow the instructions to determine how long you should let the relaxer process.
  • Check your hair: Every few minutes, check your hair to make sure it's not over-processing.

Rinsing

Once the relaxer has processed, it's time to rinse it out:

  • Rinse thoroughly: Use warm water to rinse the relaxer out of your hair.
  • Apply neutralizing shampoo: Use a neutralizing shampoo to stop the relaxing process and restore the pH balance of your hair.
  • Rinse again: Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.

Aftercare

After you've relaxed your hair, it's important to take care of it:

  • Avoid heat styling: Don't use heat styling tools on your hair for at least a week after relaxing it.
  • Moisturize: Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles: Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Don't over-process: Follow the instructions carefully to avoid over-processing your hair.
  • Ask for help: If you're not comfortable relaxing your own hair, ask a professional stylist for help.
  • Be patient: Relaxing your hair takes time and patience, so don't rush the process.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about relaxing hair for guys:

  • How often should I relax my hair?
  • It's best to wait at least 6-8 weeks between relaxer applications to avoid over-processing your hair.

  • Can I relax my hair if it's already damaged?
  • No, it's not recommended to relax damaged hair. Wait until your hair is healthy before relaxing it.

  • Can I still color my hair after relaxing it?
  • Yes, but it's best to wait at least a week after relaxing your hair before coloring it.

Hair Care

Relaxing your hair can be a great way to achieve a straight, smooth look. However, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling, and be gentle when brushing and styling your hair. With the right care, your relaxed hair can look great for weeks to come!
