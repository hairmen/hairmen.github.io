Shoulder Length Hairstyles Easy To Maintain

Written by Robby Nov 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's easy to maintain and doesn't require too much fuss, then a shoulder-length hairstyle might be perfect for you. Shoulder length hair is versatile, easy to style, and can be worn in a variety of different ways. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best shoulder length hairstyles that are easy to maintain, as well as some tips for keeping your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Description

Shoulder length hairstyles are a great option for those who want a low maintenance hairstyle that still looks great. They're perfect for those who don't have a lot of time to spend on their hair, but still want to look put together. Shoulder length hair is also versatile, so you can wear it in a variety of different styles.

How to

If you're interested in getting a shoulder length hairstyle, the first step is to find a good stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Once you've found a stylist, you can discuss the different options available to you. Some popular shoulder length hairstyles include the bob, the lob, and the shag.

The Bob

A bob is a classic shoulder length hairstyle that's easy to maintain. It's a simple cut that's perfect for those who want a low maintenance hairstyle. The bob can be worn straight or with waves, and can be styled in a variety of different ways.

The Lob

A lob is a longer version of the bob, and is another great shoulder length hairstyle option. The lob is a great choice for those who want a bit more length, but still want a low maintenance hairstyle. Like the bob, the lob can be worn straight or with waves.

The Shag

The shag is a more layered shoulder length hairstyle that's perfect for those who want a bit more texture in their hair. The shag can be worn straight or with waves, and is a great choice for those who want a more relaxed, bohemian look.

Step by Step

To maintain your shoulder length hairstyle, there are a few things you can do to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny. Here are some steps you can follow:

  1. Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized.
  3. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  4. Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.
  5. Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
  6. Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your shoulder length hairstyle:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Try out different hairstyles to keep your look fresh and interesting.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • If you have thin hair, consider using volumizing products to add more body.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length hairstyles:

Q: Is shoulder length hair easy to maintain?

A: Yes, shoulder length hair is relatively easy to maintain compared to longer hairstyles.

Q: Can shoulder length hair be worn in different styles?

A: Yes, shoulder length hair can be worn in a variety of different styles, including straight, wavy, and curly.

Q: What are some popular shoulder length hairstyles?

A: Some popular shoulder length hairstyles include the bob, the lob, and the shag.

Q: How often should I get my shoulder length hair trimmed?

A: It's a good idea to get your hair trimmed every six to eight weeks to keep it looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some general hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of protein and vitamins to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.

By following these tips, you can keep your shoulder length hairstyle looking great and easy to maintain.
