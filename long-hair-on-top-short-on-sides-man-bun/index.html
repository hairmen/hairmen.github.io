Long Hair On Top Short On Sides Man Bun A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 15, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy hairstyle, the long hair on top short on sides man bun is a great option. This hairstyle has become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be worn for formal or casual occasions, and it's relatively easy to maintain.

Description

The long hair on top short on sides man bun is a hairstyle where the hair is longer on the top and shorter on the sides. The hair on the top of the head is usually about 6 to 8 inches long, while the hair on the sides is about 1 to 2 inches long. The longer hair on top is then tied into a bun, while the shorter hair on the sides is left to hang naturally.

How to

To achieve the long hair on top short on sides man bun, you'll need to have long hair on top and shorter hair on the sides. Here's how to do it:

  1. Grow out your hair on the top of your head to about 6 to 8 inches in length.
  2. Keep the hair on the sides short, about 1 to 2 inches in length.
  3. Decide where you want to position your man bun. The bun can be positioned at the crown of your head or at the back.
  4. Gather the hair at the top of your head and tie it into a bun with an elastic hair tie.
  5. Leave the hair on the sides hanging naturally.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the long hair on top short on sides man bun:

Step 1: Grow Your Hair Out

The first step is to grow out your hair on the top of your head. This will take time, so be patient. You'll need to wait until your hair is about 6 to 8 inches in length before you can tie it into a bun.

Step 2: Keep the Sides Short

While you're growing out the hair on the top of your head, make sure to keep the hair on the sides short. This will create a contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides.

Step 3: Decide on Bun Placement

Decide where you want to position your man bun. You can position it at the crown of your head or at the back. The placement will depend on your personal preference.

Step 4: Gather Hair and Tie into a Bun

Gather the hair at the top of your head and tie it into a bun with an elastic hair tie. Make sure the bun is tight and secure.

Step 5: Leave the Sides Natural

Leave the hair on the sides hanging naturally. You can apply a small amount of hair gel or wax to keep the sides in place if you prefer.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your long hair on top short on sides man bun:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Trim the hair on the sides regularly to maintain the contrast with the longer hair on top.
  • Apply hair oil or leave-in conditioner to keep the hair on top soft and shiny.
  • Experiment with different bun styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Can I still wear a man bun if I have thinning hair?

Yes, you can still wear a man bun if you have thinning hair. Just make sure to tie the bun loosely and use a hair tie that won't pull on your hair.

Do I need to have straight hair to wear a man bun?

No, you don't need to have straight hair to wear a man bun. This hairstyle can be worn by people with curly, wavy, or straight hair.

How often should I wash my hair if I have a man bun?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days if you have a man bun. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.

Hair Care

Maintaining a long hair on top short on sides man bun requires proper hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid using a brush.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can maintain a healthy and stylish long hair on top short on sides man bun.
