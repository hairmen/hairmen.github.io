How To Cut Your Own Hair Face Frame A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking to change up your look without spending a lot of money or going to a salon, cutting your own hair can be a great option. One popular style is the face frame, which can help accentuate your facial features and provide a more youthful appearance. In this guide, we'll go over how to cut your own hair face frame in easy-to-follow steps.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Face Frame

Before getting started, it's important to gather the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair. It's also recommended to cut your hair while it's dry, as wet hair can appear longer and cause you to cut off more than intended.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to cut your own hair face frame:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle and then divide it into two sections.
  2. Take one section of hair and comb it forward over your shoulder.
  3. Starting at the bottom of your hair, take a small section and hold it between your fingers.
  4. Using the comb as a guide, cut the hair at a slight angle towards your face. Be sure to cut small sections at a time to avoid cutting off too much hair.
  5. Continue cutting the hair in small sections, gradually working your way up to the top of your head.
  6. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  7. Once both sides are cut, blend the two sections together by taking a small section from each side and cutting it at the same angle.
  8. Check your work in a mirror and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind while cutting your own hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take your time and cut in small sections to avoid cutting off too much hair.
  • Make sure your hair is dry before cutting to avoid any surprises once it dries.
  • Consider watching tutorial videos or practicing on a mannequin head before cutting your own hair.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about cutting your own hair:

Can I cut my own hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut your own hair if it's curly. However, it's important to cut it while it's dry and to take into account that curly hair shrinks when it dries.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my own hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting your own hair, don't panic. Depending on the severity of the mistake, you may be able to fix it by cutting off a little more hair or by styling it in a way that hides the mistake.

How often should I cut my own hair?

How often you cut your own hair will depend on your personal preference and hair type. Generally, it's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and maintain its shape.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair

After you've cut your own hair, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heating tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Cutting your own hair face frame can be a fun and rewarding experience. By following these simple steps and tips, you'll be able to achieve a new look without breaking the bank. Happy cutting!
