Best Haircut For Long Face Over 60 A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Jun 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a long face and are over 60, finding the right haircut can be challenging. You may have noticed that some hairstyles make your face look longer, while others flatter your features. In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for long faces over 60, how to style them, and tips for taking care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Haircut for Long Face Over 60
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Haircut

  • Hair Care Products for Long Face Over 60

Description

As we age, our hair becomes thinner, drier, and more brittle. This can make it challenging to find a haircut that flatters our face shape and suits our hair type. If you have a long face, you may want a haircut that adds volume and width to your hair. A layered haircut with bangs can frame your face and make it appear fuller. On the other hand, a sleek, straight haircut can elongate your face even more.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Long Face Over 60

When choosing a haircut for a long face over 60, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to create the illusion of width and fullness around your face. This can be achieved through layers, curls, and waves. Second, you want to avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short. Hair that is too long can drag down your face, while hair that is too short can make your face appear longer.

Here are some haircut ideas for long faces over 60:

1. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a great haircut for long faces over 60. It adds volume and movement to your hair and frames your face. You can add choppy layers for a more modern look or soft layers for a more elegant look.

2. Shoulder-Length Haircut with Bangs

A shoulder-length haircut with bangs can add fullness and width to your hair. The bangs can also frame your face and draw attention to your eyes. You can opt for side-swept bangs or blunt bangs, depending on your preference.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a great option for those who want a low-maintenance haircut. It can also add volume and fullness to your hair. You can add layers and texture to your pixie cut for a more modern look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Haircut

Once you have chosen the best haircut for your long face over 60, you may want to know how to style it. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your haircut:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash and condition your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and fullness.

2. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add waves or curls.

If you have a layered haircut, you can use a curling iron or hot rollers to add waves or curls to your hair. This will add volume and fullness to your hair.

3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair.

If you have a sleek, straight haircut, you can use a flat iron to straighten your hair. This will make your hair appear longer and sleeker.

4. Use a volumizing spray or mousse.

Apply a volumizing spray or mousse to your hair to add volume and fullness.

5. Finish with hairspray.

Spray your hair with hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Taking Care of Your Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

1. Use products that are suitable for your hair type.

Choose products that are designed for your hair type, whether it is fine, thick, curly, or straight.

2. Protect your hair from heat damage.

Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair to prevent heat damage.

3. Get regular trims.

Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your haircut.

4. Use a hair mask once a week.

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the best haircut for a long face over 60?

The best haircut for a long face over 60 is a layered bob, shoulder-length haircut with bangs, or a pixie cut.

2. How do I style my hair if I have a long face over 60?

You can style your hair using a curling iron or hot rollers to add waves or curls, or a flat iron to straighten your hair. Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add volume and fullness.

3. How often should I get my hair trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and health of your haircut.

Hair Care Products for Long Face Over 60

Here are some hair care products that are suitable for long faces over 60:

1. Volumizing Spray

A volumizing spray can add volume and fullness to your hair.

2. Heat Protectant Spray

A heat protectant spray can prevent heat damage when using hot tools on your hair.

3. Hair Mask

A hair mask can nourish and hydrate your hair.

Now that you know the best haircut for a long face over 60, how to style it, and tips for taking care of your hair, you can confidently rock a new hairstyle. Remember to choose a haircut that flatters your features and suits your hair type. With the right haircut and hair care products, you can have healthy, beautiful hair at any age.
