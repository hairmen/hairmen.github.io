Spiky Haircut Fade A Stylish Look For Men

Written by Petter Oct 01, 2023 · 3 min read

Description

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that adds texture and depth to your hair, then the spiky haircut fade is the perfect choice for you! This haircut involves creating a fade on the sides and back of your head, while leaving the hair on top longer and styled into spikes. It's a popular look among men of all ages, and it's easy to maintain with the right products and styling techniques.

How to Get a Spiky Haircut Fade

Getting a spiky haircut fade is a simple process that can be done at most barbershops or hair salons. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get this stylish look:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step to getting a spiky haircut fade is to decide how long you want your hair to be on top. This will depend on your personal preference and your hair type. Generally, the hair on top should be at least 2-3 inches long to create the spikes.

Step 2: Create a Fade

Next, your barber will create a fade on the sides and back of your head. This involves using clippers to gradually shorten the hair from the bottom up, creating a seamless transition from longer hair on top to shorter hair on the sides and back.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once the fade is complete, it's time to style your hair into spikes. Use a strong hold hair gel or pomade to create the spikes, and use your fingers or a comb to shape them into the desired style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Spiky Haircut Fade

To keep your spiky haircut fade looking fresh and stylish, here are a few tips to follow: - Use a high-quality hair gel or pomade to create the spikes, and avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy. - Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to keep it clean and healthy. - Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain the fade and keep your hair looking sharp.

FAQ

Q: Is a spiky haircut fade suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, a spiky haircut fade can be adapted to suit all hair types, whether you have thick, curly, or straight hair.

Q: How long does a spiky haircut fade last?

A: A spiky haircut fade can last for up to 4-6 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain it.

Q: Can I create the spiky look at home?

A: Yes, with the right products and styling techniques, you can create the spiky look at home. However, it's recommended to visit a professional barber for the initial haircut and fade.

Hair Care for a Spiky Haircut Fade

To keep your hair healthy and looking great with a spiky haircut fade, here are a few tips: - Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized. - Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers or straighteners, as these can damage your hair and make it harder to style. - Apply a hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to add moisture and prevent split ends. - Use a hair mask once a week to deep condition your hair and keep it healthy. With these tips and techniques, you can rock a stylish and trendy spiky haircut fade that will turn heads wherever you go!
