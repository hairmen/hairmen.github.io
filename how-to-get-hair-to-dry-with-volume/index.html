How To Get Hair To Dry With Volume A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to add some volume to your hair, you might think that the only way to achieve this is by using styling products or getting a fancy blowout at the salon. However, there's a simple trick that you can use to get your hair to dry with volume naturally. In this article, we'll show you how to get hair to dry with volume, step-by-step.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Hair to Dry with Volume
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Adding Volume to Your Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care Tips for Volume

How to Get Hair to Dry with Volume

The key to getting your hair to dry with volume is to flip your head upside down while it's drying. By doing this, you allow the roots of your hair to dry in an upward direction, which creates volume and lift. Here are the steps to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add body and fullness to your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  3. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your damp hair, focusing on the roots. This will help to add even more volume and hold to your hair.
  4. Flip your head upside down and use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to dry your hair. Use your fingers to lift and separate your hair at the roots as you dry it.
  5. Once your hair is about 80% dry, flip your head back up and continue to dry your hair until it's completely dry. Use a round brush to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

Tips for Adding Volume to Your Hair

In addition to the above steps, here are some additional tips for adding volume to your hair:

  • Avoid using heavy conditioners or styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush with natural bristles to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to add natural waves and volume.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as they can cause damage and breakage to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this method on all hair types?

Yes, this method can work on all hair types. However, if you have very thick or curly hair, you may need to use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help evenly distribute heat and speed up the drying process.

Do I need to use a special blow dryer?

No, you don't need to use a special blow dryer to get your hair to dry with volume. However, using a blow dryer with a nozzle attachment can help to concentrate the airflow and create more lift at the roots.

How often can I use this method?

You can use this method as often as you like, but be sure to give your hair a break from heat styling every once in a while to prevent damage and breakage.

Hair Care Tips for Volume

In addition to the above tips, here are some hair care tips for maintaining volume in your hair:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth and strength.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as these can cause damage and breakage.

By following these tips and using the upside down drying method, you can add volume and body to your hair without having to spend a lot of time or money on styling products or salon treatments. Give it a try and see the difference it can make!
