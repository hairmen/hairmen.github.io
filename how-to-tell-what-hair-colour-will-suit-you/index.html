How To Tell What Hair Colour Will Suit You

Written by Christine Aug 09, 2023 · 5 min read

Trying out a new hair colour can be an exciting process, but it can also be a daunting one. With so many shades to choose from, it can be difficult to determine which one will suit you best. In this article, we will provide you with some tips on how to tell what hair colour will suit you, so you can make an informed decision before heading to the salon.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Tell What Hair Colour Will Suit You
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Description

Before we dive into the specifics of choosing a hair colour, it's important to understand that there are a few different factors that can influence which colour will suit you best. These include your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour. By taking these factors into consideration, you can narrow down your options and choose a hair colour that will complement your natural features.

How to Tell What Hair Colour Will Suit You

One of the easiest ways to determine which hair colour will suit you is to look at your skin tone. There are generally three categories of skin tone: warm, cool, and neutral. If you have a warm skin tone, you will look best in warm hair colours, such as golden blonde, warm brown, or copper red. If you have a cool skin tone, you will look best in cool hair colours, such as ash blonde, cool brown, or burgundy. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can experiment with both warm and cool hair colours.

Another factor to consider when choosing a hair colour is your eye colour. If you have blue or green eyes, you will look best in cool hair colours, while if you have brown or hazel eyes, you will look best in warm hair colours. If you have a mix of both warm and cool colours in your eyes, you can experiment with both types of hair colours.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to help you determine which hair colour will suit you:

  1. Determine your skin tone. You can do this by looking at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell, you may have a neutral skin tone.
  2. Consider your eye colour. If you have blue or green eyes, you have cool undertones. If you have brown or hazel eyes, you have warm undertones.
  3. Take a look at your natural hair colour. If you have warm or golden tones in your hair, you have warm undertones. If you have ash or cool tones in your hair, you have cool undertones.
  4. Compare your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour to a hair colour chart. This will help you determine which shades will suit you best.
  5. If you're unsure, consult with a professional stylist. They can provide you with personalized advice based on your unique features.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair colour:

  • Consider the maintenance involved. Some hair colours require more upkeep than others, so make sure you choose a shade that fits with your lifestyle.
  • Think about your personal style. Your hair colour should complement your overall look and personality.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment. Hair colour is a fun way to express yourself, so don't be afraid to try something new.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a hair colour:

What if I have multiple skin tones?

If you have multiple skin tones, you may have a neutral skin tone. In this case, you can experiment with both warm and cool hair colours.

What if I have grey hair?

If you have grey hair, you can still experiment with hair colour. Just keep in mind that grey hair can be more porous, which means it may absorb colour differently than other hair types. It's best to consult with a professional stylist to ensure you achieve the desired result.

What if I don't like the colour?

If you don't like the colour, you can always change it. Keep in mind that some colours may be more difficult to remove than others, so it's best to consult with a professional stylist before making any drastic changes.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen a hair colour, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for coloured hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the colour.
  • Use a heat protectant before using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to sunlight for extended periods of time, as this can cause the colour to fade.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your hair colour looks great and lasts as long as possible.
