How To Treat Androgenic Alopecia Naturally

Written by Daniel Jul 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Androgenic alopecia, also known as male or female pattern baldness, is a condition that affects both men and women. It is characterized by thinning hair and baldness in certain areas of the scalp. While there are several treatments available to help manage androgenic alopecia, some people prefer to try natural remedies first. In this post, we will discuss how to treat androgenic alopecia naturally.

How to Treat Androgenic Alopecia Naturally

There are various natural remedies that can help to manage androgenic alopecia. These include:

1. Essential Oils

Essential oils such as lavender, peppermint, rosemary, and cedarwood have been shown to promote hair growth and reduce hair loss. To use, mix a few drops of essential oil with a carrier oil such as coconut oil and massage into the scalp. Leave on for at least 30 minutes before washing out.

2. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera contains enzymes that can help to unclog hair follicles and promote hair growth. Apply aloe vera gel directly to the scalp and leave on for at least an hour before washing out.

3. Saw Palmetto

Saw palmetto is a natural DHT blocker, which can help to reduce hair loss. Take a saw palmetto supplement daily or apply saw palmetto oil directly to the scalp.

4. Scalp Massage

Massaging the scalp can help to improve blood flow to the hair follicles and promote hair growth. Use your fingers to massage the scalp for at least 5 minutes daily.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when using natural remedies to treat androgenic alopecia:

  1. Choose a natural remedy that works for you.
  2. Apply the remedy to the scalp according to the instructions.
  3. Leave on for the recommended amount of time.
  4. Wash out the remedy with shampoo and water.
  5. Repeat the process regularly.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you manage androgenic alopecia:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on the hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Reduce stress levels through exercise, meditation, or other relaxation techniques.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on the hair.
  • Get enough sleep to promote overall health and wellbeing.

FAQs

Can natural remedies really help with androgenic alopecia?

While natural remedies may not work for everyone, they can be effective in promoting hair growth and reducing hair loss in some people.

How long will it take to see results?

It can take several weeks or even months to see results when using natural remedies for androgenic alopecia. Be patient and consistent with your treatment.

Can androgenic alopecia be cured?

Androgenic alopecia cannot be cured, but it can be managed with the right treatment and care.

Hair Care

In addition to using natural remedies, it is important to take good care of your hair to promote healthy growth and prevent further hair loss. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is free from harsh chemicals.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons.
  • Trim the ends of your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as it can cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

By following these tips and using natural remedies, you can manage androgenic alopecia and promote healthy hair growth.
