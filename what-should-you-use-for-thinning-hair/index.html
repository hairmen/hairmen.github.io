What Should You Use For Thinning Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Jun 22, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're experiencing thinning hair, you're not alone. It's a common issue that affects both men and women. But what can you do about it? In this article, we'll explore some of the best products and treatments for thinning hair. From shampoos to supplements, we've got you covered.

Shampoos

One of the easiest ways to treat thinning hair is by using a shampoo that's specifically designed for that purpose. Look for shampoos that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and niacin, which can help to strengthen hair and encourage growth.

Some popular options include:

  • Nioxin System 1 Cleanser: This shampoo is designed to remove follicle-clogging sebum, fatty acids, and environmental residues from the scalp, leaving hair looking fuller and thicker.
  • OGX Thick & Full Biotin & Collagen Shampoo: Infused with biotin, collagen, and wheat protein, this shampoo helps to thicken and strengthen hair.
  • Paul Mitchell Tea Tree Scalp Care Anti-Thinning Shampoo: This shampoo contains a blend of tea tree oil, peppermint, and lavender to help soothe the scalp and promote healthy hair growth.

When using a thinning hair shampoo, be sure to massage it gently into the scalp to stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth. Rinse thoroughly and follow up with a conditioner.

Conditioners

Conditioners can help to moisturize and nourish the hair, making it look fuller and healthier. Look for conditioners that contain ingredients like argan oil, keratin, and biotin.

Some popular options include:

  • Renpure Biotin & Collagen Conditioner: This conditioner is infused with biotin and collagen to help strengthen and thicken hair.
  • Moroccanoil Hydrating Conditioner: This conditioner contains argan oil to hydrate and nourish hair, leaving it looking shiny and healthy.
  • TRESemmé Keratin Smooth Conditioner: Infused with keratin and marula oil, this conditioner helps to smooth and strengthen hair, reducing breakage and preventing thinning.

Apply conditioner to the ends of your hair, avoiding the scalp. Leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing thoroughly.

Serums

Serums can help to nourish and protect the hair, reducing breakage and promoting growth. Look for serums that contain ingredients like argan oil, keratin, and biotin.

Some popular options include:

  • Moroccanoil Treatment: This serum contains argan oil to nourish and protect hair, leaving it looking shiny and healthy.
  • OGX Biotin & Collagen Weightless Healing Oil Treatment: This serum is infused with biotin, collagen, and hydrolyzed wheat protein to help strengthen and thicken hair.
  • Kérastase Initialiste Advanced Scalp & Hair Serum: This serum contains a blend of ceramides, peptides, and green tea extract to help strengthen hair and promote growth.

Apply serum to damp hair, focusing on the ends. Avoid applying too much, as this can weigh hair down.

Oils

Oils can help to moisturize and protect the hair, reducing breakage and promoting growth. Look for oils that contain ingredients like argan oil, castor oil, and coconut oil.

Some popular options include:

  • Moroccanoil Treatment: This oil contains argan oil to nourish and protect hair, leaving it looking shiny and healthy.
  • Castor Oil: This oil is rich in fatty acids and vitamin E, which can help to moisturize and strengthen hair, reducing breakage and promoting growth.
  • Coconut Oil: This oil is rich in lauric acid, which can help to moisturize and protect hair, reducing breakage and promoting growth.

Apply oil to damp or dry hair, focusing on the ends. Avoid applying too much, as this can weigh hair down.

Supplements

Supplements can help to nourish and strengthen hair from the inside out. Look for supplements that contain ingredients like biotin, vitamin D, and iron.

Some popular options include:

  • Viviscal Extra Strength Hair Growth Supplement: This supplement contains a blend of biotin, vitamin C, and iron to help nourish hair and promote growth.
  • Nature's Bounty Optimal Solutions Hair, Skin & Nails Gummies: These gummies contain biotin, vitamin C, and vitamin E to help support healthy hair, skin, and nails.
  • HUM Nutrition Hair Sweet Hair: This supplement contains biotin, folic acid, and zinc to help promote healthy hair and prevent breakage.

Be sure to follow the recommended dosage for any supplement you take, and talk to your doctor before starting any new supplement regimen.

Hair Transplant

If you're experiencing significant hair loss, you may want to consider a hair transplant. This involves removing hair follicles from one part of your scalp and transplanting them to another part of your scalp that's experiencing thinning or balding.

Hair transplants can be expensive and may not be covered by insurance, but they can be a highly effective way to restore hair growth.

Tips for Thinning Hair

In addition to using the right products and treatments, there are some simple lifestyle changes you can make to help prevent further hair loss and promote growth:

  • Eat a healthy diet: A diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean protein, and healthy fats can help to nourish your hair and promote growth.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles: Pulling your hair back tightly into a bun or ponytail can cause breakage and damage to the hair follicles.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling wands can cause damage to the hair, leading to breakage and thinning.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: UV rays can damage hair and cause it to become brittle and break. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray.

FAQs

Q: Can stress cause hair loss?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair loss. When you're under stress, your body releases hormones that can affect the hair growth cycle.

Q: Can medication cause hair loss?

A: Yes, certain medications can cause hair loss as a side effect. Talk to your doctor if you're concerned about hair loss related to medication.

Q: Can hair loss be reversed?

A: In some cases, hair loss can be reversed with the right treatment. But it's important to address the issue as soon as possible, as the longer you wait, the harder it can be to restore hair growth.

Hair Care for Thinning Hair

In addition to using the right products and treatments, it's important to take good care of your hair to prevent further damage and breakage:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Keeping your scalp clean and free of buildup can help to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals: Chemical treatments like relaxers and perms can damage hair, leading to breakage and thinning. Avoid these treatments if possible.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb: Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet
