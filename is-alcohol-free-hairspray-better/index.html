Is Alcohol Free Hairspray Better For Your Hair

Written by Frank Sep 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to hairspray, many people don't realize the impact that alcohol can have on their hair. Alcohol is a common ingredient in hairspray, but it can also be very drying and damaging to your hair. In recent years, alcohol-free hairspray has become more popular as people become more aware of the importance of using hair products that are gentle and nourishing. In this article, we'll explore the question of whether alcohol-free hairspray is better for your hair and provide some tips on how to use it effectively.

What Is Alcohol-Free Hairspray?

Alcohol-free hairspray is a type of hairspray that does not contain alcohol as an ingredient. Instead, it may contain other ingredients such as natural oils, plant extracts, and other gentle and nourishing ingredients. Alcohol-free hairspray is designed to provide hold and control for your hair without causing damage or dryness.

How to Use Alcohol-Free Hairspray

Using alcohol-free hairspray is very similar to using regular hairspray. Here are some tips on how to use it effectively:

Step 1: Choose the Right Hairspray

When selecting an alcohol-free hairspray, look for one that is designed for your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Some hairsprays are formulated for fine hair, while others are better for thick or curly hair. Make sure to read the label and choose a product that is suitable for your hair.

Step 2: Apply the Hairspray

Hold the hairspray bottle about 6-8 inches away from your hair and spray lightly. Avoid spraying too much at once, as this can make your hair look stiff and unnatural. If you need more hold, you can always apply additional layers of hairspray as needed.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you've applied the hairspray, you can style your hair as desired. Alcohol-free hairspray can help to hold your hair in place throughout the day without causing dryness or damage.

Tips for Using Alcohol-Free Hairspray

Here are some additional tips for using alcohol-free hairspray:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to distribute the hairspray evenly through your hair.
  • Don't apply hairspray to wet hair, as it can cause your hair to become sticky and clumpy.
  • If you're using a curling iron or flat iron, apply hairspray before styling to help hold the shape and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much after applying hairspray, as this can cause your hair to become oily and lose its shape.
  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any build-up of hairspray and other products.

FAQs

Is alcohol-free hairspray better for your hair?

Yes, alcohol-free hairspray is generally better for your hair than hairspray that contains alcohol. Alcohol can be very drying and damaging to your hair, so using an alcohol-free hairspray can help to keep your hair healthy and nourished.

Can alcohol-free hairspray provide the same hold as regular hairspray?

Yes, alcohol-free hairspray can provide the same hold and control as regular hairspray. However, you may need to apply more layers of hairspray to achieve the same level of hold, as alcohol-free hairspray is generally less stiff and sticky than regular hairspray.

Is alcohol-free hairspray suitable for all hair types?

Yes, alcohol-free hairspray is suitable for all hair types. However, you may need to choose a hairspray that is specifically formulated for your hair type, such as fine hair or curly hair.

Hair Care Tips

Using alcohol-free hairspray is just one part of a healthy hair care routine. Here are some additional tips for keeping your hair healthy and nourished:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals, such as vitamin E and biotin, which are essential for healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dyes.

Overall, alcohol-free hairspray can be a great choice for anyone who wants to keep their hair healthy and nourished while still achieving the hold and control they need. By following these tips and incorporating alcohol-free hairspray into your hair care routine, you can enjoy healthy, beautiful hair every day.
