Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Diy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Oct 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that you can do at home? Look no further than the mens haircut short sides long top DIY! This versatile hairstyle has been popular for years and shows no signs of going out of style anytime soon. In this article, we’ll provide a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look, as well as some tips for maintaining it and answering some frequently asked questions about the style.

  • How to Achieve the Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Look

How to Achieve the Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top Look

The mens haircut short sides long top is a hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on the top. This look can be achieved at home with the right tools and some patience. You’ll need a set of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror to see the back of your head.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these instructions to achieve the mens haircut short sides long top:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use the clippers to shave the sides and back of the head, starting with a longer guard and gradually going shorter until you reach the desired length.
  3. Blend the sides into the top using scissors, cutting at an angle to create a natural-looking transition.
  4. Trim the top to the desired length, leaving it longer towards the front of the head.
  5. Use scissors to add texture to the top, cutting into the hair at an angle.
  6. Style the hair using a product of your choice, such as pomade or wax.

Remember to take your time and go slowly when cutting your hair. You can always cut more off, but you can’t put it back on!

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Here are some tips to keep your mens haircut short sides long top looking great:

  • Visit a professional stylist for a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking fresh.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Consider adding a fade to the sides for a more dramatic look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about the mens haircut short sides long top:

Q: Will this style work for all hair types?
A: Yes, the mens haircut short sides long top can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How long does my hair need to be to achieve this look?
A: Your hair should be at least 2-3 inches long on top to achieve the mens haircut short sides long top look.

Q: Can I achieve this look without clippers?
A: While clippers are recommended for achieving the short sides, you can use scissors for the entire cut if you prefer a longer look.

Hair Care for the Mens Haircut Short Sides Long Top

Proper hair care is vital for maintaining the mens haircut short sides long top. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to deep condition your hair.

With these tips and a little practice, you can achieve the mens haircut short sides long top DIY and keep it looking great for weeks to come.
