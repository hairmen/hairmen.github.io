Easy Hairstyles For Round Chubby Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of styling your hair the same way every day? Do you have a round chubby face and struggle to find hairstyles that flatter your features? Well, you're in luck because we've put together a comprehensive guide on easy hairstyles for round chubby faces. Read on for our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round chubby face is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wide forehead. When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to opt for styles that balance out the fullness of your face and create the illusion of length. The key is to choose hairstyles that elongate your face and draw attention away from the roundness of your cheeks.

How to

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's discuss some basic guidelines for choosing hairstyles for round chubby faces:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long.
  • Choose hairstyles with layers that frame your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles with blunt bangs that cut across your forehead.

Step by Step

Now that you know what to look for when choosing a hairstyle, here are some easy hairstyles that flatter round chubby faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for round chubby faces as they add length and create movement. Ask your stylist to add long layers that frame your face and create texture.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add dimension to your face and draw attention away from your cheeks. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle so that they sweep across your forehead.

3. Bob with Layers

A bob with layers is a great option for a round chubby face as it adds height and creates angles. Ask your stylist to cut your bob at chin-length and add layers that frame your face.

4. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a quick and easy hairstyle that's perfect for a round chubby face. Pull your hair back into a high ponytail and twist it into a messy bun. Leave some hair loose around your face to create a soft, romantic look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing hairstyles for round chubby faces:

  • Experiment with different parts to create different looks.
  • Opt for hairstyles with volume at the crown of your head.
  • Avoid hairstyles with blunt ends that accentuate the roundness of your face.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and texture.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round chubby face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short, too long, or too blunt. Also, avoid hairstyles with blunt bangs that cut across your forehead.

What hairstyles flatter a round chubby face?

Hairstyles with height at the crown of your head, layers that frame your face, and side-swept bangs are all great options for a round chubby face.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for any hairstyle. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you have our comprehensive guide on easy hairstyles for round chubby faces, you can experiment with different styles and find the perfect one for you. Remember to consider your face shape and choose styles that balance out the fullness of your face. With the right hairstyle, you can feel confident and beautiful every day.
