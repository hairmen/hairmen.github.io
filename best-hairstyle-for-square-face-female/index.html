Best Hairstyle For Square Face Female A Complete Guide

Written by William Aug 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a square face, you may find it challenging to choose a hairstyle that complements your features. But with the right knowledge and guidance, you can find the perfect hairstyle that enhances your natural beauty. In this article, we will explore the best hairstyle for square face female, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve your desired look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Square Face Female
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Description

First, let's define what a square face shape looks like. Square faces have a prominent jawline, wide forehead, and angular features. The best hairstyles for square face female are those that soften the angular features and complement the jawline. These hairstyles can be short, medium, or long, and can include layers, side-swept bangs, and textured waves.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Square Face Female

When choosing a hairstyle, consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the best hairstyle for square face female:

  • Opt for hairstyles that soften the jawline, such as layers and waves.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, such as blunt bobs and straight-across bangs.
  • Consider side-swept bangs, which can help balance the width of the forehead.
  • Choose hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head, such as updos and ponytails.
  • Experiment with different hair lengths and textures to find what works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, let's explore some step-by-step guides to help you achieve your desired look.

Short Hair

For short hair, try a layered pixie cut with side-swept bangs. Here's how to style it:

  1. Start with freshly washed hair.
  2. Blow-dry hair with a round brush, focusing on adding volume to the crown of the head.
  3. Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or flyaways.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to the ends of the hair for added texture.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep the style in place.

Medium Hair

For medium hair, try a layered bob with side-swept bangs. Here's how to style it:

  1. Start with freshly washed hair.
  2. Blow-dry hair with a round brush, focusing on adding volume to the crown of the head.
  3. Use a curling iron to create loose waves throughout the hair.
  4. Sweep the bangs to the side and pin them in place with bobby pins.
  5. Finish with a medium-hold hairspray to keep the style in place.

Long Hair

For long hair, try a layered cut with loose waves. Here's how to style it:

  1. Start with freshly washed hair.
  2. Blow-dry hair with a round brush, focusing on adding volume to the crown of the head.
  3. Use a curling iron to create loose waves throughout the hair.
  4. Sweep the hair to one side and pin it in place with bobby pins.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's essential to maintain it properly. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands and hair clips, to add variety to your hairstyles.

FAQs

What are the best hairstyles for square face female?

The best hairstyles for square face female are those that soften the angular features and complement the jawline. These hairstyles can be short, medium, or long, and can include layers, side-swept bangs, and textured waves.

Can square face females have bangs?

Yes, square face females can have bangs. Side-swept bangs can help balance the width of the forehead and complement the jawline.

Can square face females have short hair?

Yes, square face females can have short hair. A layered pixie cut with side-swept bangs can be a flattering option.

Hair Care Advice for Square Face Females

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to help keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can lead to hair breakage and damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

In conclusion, the best hairstyle for square face female is one that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty. By following these tips and experimenting with different hairstyles, you can find the perfect look for you.
