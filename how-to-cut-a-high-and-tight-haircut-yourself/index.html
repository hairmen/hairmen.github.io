How To Cut A High And Tight Haircut Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for an easy and low-maintenance hairstyle, the high and tight haircut might be just what you need. This classic military-inspired cut is characterized by short sides and back with a slightly longer top. The high and tight is a great option for anyone who wants a clean and sharp look without spending too much time on styling. And the best part is, you can easily cut it yourself at home! In this article, we'll show you how to cut a high and tight haircut yourself in just a few simple steps.

Description: What is a High and Tight Haircut?

The high and tight haircut is a popular men's hairstyle that originated in the military. It's a short and low-maintenance cut that's easy to maintain and looks great on most face shapes. The haircut is characterized by short sides and back with a slightly longer top that's usually styled forward. The high and tight haircut is a classic style that never goes out of fashion and is perfect for anyone who wants a sharp and clean look.

How to Cut a High and Tight Haircut Yourself: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting a high and tight haircut yourself is to gather all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's important to make sure your clippers are sharp and well-oiled to ensure a smooth and even cut.

Step 2: Start with the Sides

Begin by using the clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair. Use a #1 or #2 guard to achieve the desired length. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using short and even strokes. Make sure to blend the hair at the sides and back to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 3: Trim the Top

Next, use scissors to trim the top of your hair. Cut the hair to the desired length, leaving it slightly longer in the front than in the back. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly. Make sure to blend the hair at the top with the sides and back to avoid any sharp lines.

Step 4: Clean Up the Neckline

Use the clippers to clean up the neckline, trimming any stray hairs. Use a #0 or #1 guard to achieve a clean and sharp look.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Once the haircut is complete, you can style the hair as desired. Use a small amount of pomade or wax to add texture and hold to the hair. Style the hair forward or to the side, depending on your preference.

Tips for Cutting a High and Tight Haircut Yourself

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect high and tight haircut:

  • Invest in a good pair of clippers to ensure a smooth and even cut.
  • Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to achieve the desired length.
  • Use scissors to blend the hair at the top and avoid any sharp lines.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure the haircut is even.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process. A high and tight haircut requires precision and attention to detail.

FAQs

Can I cut a high and tight haircut myself?

Yes, you can cut a high and tight haircut yourself with the right tools and techniques.

What tools do I need to cut a high and tight haircut?

You'll need clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

What length should I use for the sides and back?

Use a #1 or #2 guard to achieve the desired length for the sides and back.

How do I blend the hair at the top with the sides and back?

Use scissors to blend the hair at the top and avoid any sharp lines.

Hair Care for a High and Tight Haircut

A high and tight haircut is a low-maintenance style that requires minimal hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down the hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and the elements by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant.

With these simple steps and tips, you can easily cut a high and tight haircut yourself at home. This classic and timeless style is perfect for anyone who wants a sharp and clean look without spending too much time on styling. So go ahead and give it a try!
