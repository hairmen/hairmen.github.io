Best Haircut For Petite Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a petite round face, you know how challenging it can be to find the perfect haircut that flatters your features. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll walk you through the best haircut for petite round face, how to achieve it, and tips to maintain it. So, let's get started!

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips

Description

When it comes to finding the best haircut for petite round face, the key is to create an illusion of length and height to balance out the roundness of your face. You want a haircut that elongates your face, adds volume to the crown, and frames your face in a flattering way. The ideal haircut for a petite round face is one that has layers, texture, and movement. Below, we'll discuss the different types of haircuts that work best for petite round faces.

How to

Before we dive into the different types of haircuts, it's essential to determine your face shape. The best way to do this is to pull your hair back and examine your face's shape in the mirror. If your face is as wide as it is long, you likely have a round face. Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to choose a haircut that flatters your features. Below are the best haircuts for petite round faces.

Step by step

The Bob

The bob is a classic haircut that works well for petite round faces. The key is to keep the length above the shoulders and add layers to create volume and movement. To achieve this look, follow these steps:

  1. Ask your stylist to cut your hair into a bob that falls above your shoulders.
  2. Add layers throughout your hair to create volume and movement.
  3. Style your hair with a round brush and blow dryer for a sleek finish or use a curling iron for more texture.

The Pixie

The pixie haircut is another great option for petite round faces. This short and sassy haircut adds height to your crown, elongates your face, and draws attention to your eyes. To achieve this look, follow these steps:

  1. Ask your stylist to cut your hair into a short pixie cut.
  2. Add layers to the crown of your head to create volume and height.
  3. Style your hair with a texturizing spray for a messy, edgy look, or use a comb and pomade for a sleek finish.

The Lob

The lob, or long bob, is a trendy and versatile haircut that works well for petite round faces. This haircut falls just above the shoulders and adds length to your face while creating volume and movement. To achieve this look, follow these steps:

  1. Ask your stylist to cut your hair into a lob that falls just above your shoulders.
  2. Add layers throughout your hair to create volume and movement.
  3. Style your hair with a curling iron for loose waves or a straightener for a sleek finish.

Tips

Now that you know the best haircuts for petite round faces, here are some tips to help you maintain your new look:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that can make your face look rounder.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

FAQ

What are the best haircuts for round faces?

The best haircuts for round faces are those that add height and length to your face, such as the bob, pixie, and lob. These haircuts create volume and movement, which helps to balance out the roundness of your face.

What should I avoid when choosing a haircut for a round face?

Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that can make your face look rounder. Also, avoid haircuts that are too short, as they can make your face look wider.

How often should I get a trim?

You should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential, no matter what your face shape. Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

So there you have it, everything you need to know about the best haircut for petite round face. With the right haircut and proper hair care, you can rock any hairstyle and feel confident in your own skin. Remember to consult with your stylist to find the best haircut for your face shape and hair type. Happy styling!
