Is Fringe Good For Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you might be wondering if fringe (also known as bangs) is a good option for you. The answer is yes, but it depends on a few factors. In this article, we will explore everything you need to know about fringe and round faces, including how to choose the right fringe for your face shape, step-by-step instructions on how to cut your own fringe, tips on styling your fringe, and more. So, let's get started!

What is Fringe?

Fringe is a popular hairstyle where hair is cut and styled to hang over the forehead, creating a frame around the face. Fringe can be cut in different lengths and styles, such as blunt, side-swept, or parted. It can be worn by people of all ages and hair types.

Is Fringe Good for Round Face?

Yes, fringe is good for round face, but it depends on the type of fringe you choose. Round faces have soft, curved lines and equal width and length, so the goal of fringe is to create angles and length to balance the face shape. The best fringe for round face is a long, side-swept fringe that starts from the side of the forehead and angles down towards the cheekbones.

Another good option is a curtain fringe, which is a parted fringe that falls in the middle and frames the face. Avoid blunt, straight-across fringe that cuts the forehead horizontally and makes the face appear wider.

How to Choose the Right Fringe for Round Face

When choosing the right fringe for round face, consider the following factors:

  • Length: Choose a long fringe that falls below the brow bone and angles towards the cheekbones.
  • Shape: Choose a side-swept or curtain fringe that creates angles and lengthens the face.
  • Texture: Choose a textured fringe that adds volume and movement to the hair.
  • Face shape: Consider other facial features, such as eyes, nose, and lips, to determine the best length and style of fringe.

How to Cut Your Own Fringe

If you want to cut your own fringe at home, follow these steps:

  1. Start with dry hair and section off the hair you want to cut into fringe. Use a comb to create a triangle shape that starts from the middle of the forehead and ends at the temples.
  2. Hold the hair between two fingers and cut at an angle, starting from the outer corner of the eyebrows and angling down towards the cheekbones. Cut a little at a time and check the length frequently.
  3. Repeat on the other side and trim any uneven pieces.
  4. Style the fringe as desired.

Tips for Styling Your Fringe

Here are some tips for styling your fringe:

  • Blow-dry: Use a round brush and blow-dry your fringe in the direction you want it to fall.
  • Straighten: Use a flat iron to straighten your fringe for a sleek look.
  • Curl: Use a curling iron to add texture and volume to your fringe.
  • Accessorize: Use bobby pins, clips, or headbands to pin your fringe back or add some flair to your hairstyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of fringe is best for round face?

The best type of fringe for round face is a long, side-swept fringe that angles towards the cheekbones or a curtain fringe that falls in the middle and frames the face.

Can blunt fringe work for round face?

Avoid blunt, straight-across fringe that cuts the forehead horizontally and makes the face appear wider.

How often should I trim my fringe?

You should trim your fringe every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.

Hair Care Tips for Fringe

Here are some hair care tips for fringe:

  • Wash: Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Condition: Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Trim: Trim your fringe regularly to maintain the shape and length.
  • Avoid heat: Limit your use of heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

Conclusion

Fringe can be a great option for round faces, as long as you choose the right length and style. A long, side-swept fringe or a curtain fringe can create angles and length to balance the face shape. If you want to cut your own fringe at home, follow the step-by-step instructions and use the styling tips to keep your fringe looking great. Remember to trim your fringe regularly and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
