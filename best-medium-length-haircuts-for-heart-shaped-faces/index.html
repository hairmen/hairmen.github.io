Best Medium Length Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces

Written by William Oct 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for the perfect medium length haircut to flatter your heart shaped face? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the best medium length haircuts for heart shaped faces to help you find your next perfect style. From bobs to layers, there is a style for everyone.

Description

Heart shaped faces are characterized by a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The goal of a good haircut for this face shape is to balance out the proportions, and draw attention away from the forehead. Medium length haircuts work well for this face shape, as they can add volume and create the illusion of a wider chin.

How To

When choosing a medium length haircut for a heart shaped face, it is important to consider the length of the hair. Hair that falls just below the shoulders is perfect, as it creates the illusion of a wider chin. Layers and bangs can also be added to soften the look and add volume.

Step by Step

1. Determine the length of hair that works best for you.
2. Consider adding layers and bangs to soften your look.
3. Consult with your stylist to find the perfect style for your face shape.

Tips

- Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the crown of your head.
- Keep the length of your hair at or just below your shoulders.
- Consider adding side-swept bangs to create the illusion of a wider chin.

FAQ

Q: Can heart shaped faces pull off short haircuts?
A: Yes, heart shaped faces can pull off short haircuts, but they should avoid styles that add volume to the crown of the head.
Q: What hair color works best for heart shaped faces?
A: Hair color is subjective, but warm tones and highlights work well to soften the look of a heart shaped face.

Hair Care

Regardless of your face shape, it is important to take care of your hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid over-styling with heat tools. Regular trims will also help to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Overall, medium length haircuts are a great option for heart shaped faces. With the right styling and care, you can have a look that flatters your features and makes you feel confident.
