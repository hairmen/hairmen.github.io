What Can You Use To Slick Your Hair Back A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle? Look no further! In this guide, we'll take you through everything you need to know about what you can use to slick your hair back.

Description

Slicked-back hair has been a popular hairstyle for decades. It's a classic, timeless look that works well for both casual and formal occasions. However, achieving the perfect slicked-back hairstyle can be challenging, especially if you don't know what products to use. In this guide, we'll give you all the information you need to achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle.

How to Slick Your Hair Back: Step by Step

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to achieving the perfect slicked-back hairstyle is to start with clean hair. Make sure you wash your hair thoroughly and remove any excess oil or product buildup. This will help your hair look sleek and shiny.

Step 2: Choose the Right Product

Choosing the right product is essential to achieving the perfect slicked-back hairstyle. There are a variety of products you can use, including hair gel, pomade, and wax. Each product has a different level of hold and shine, so make sure you choose the product that works best for your hair type and desired look.

Step 3: Apply the Product

Once you've chosen your product, it's time to apply it to your hair. Start with a small amount and work your way up. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to distribute it from the roots to the tips.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair

After you've applied the product, it's time to comb your hair. Use a fine-toothed comb to comb your hair straight back from your forehead. Make sure to comb your hair evenly and avoid creating any bumps or lumps.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've combed your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help your hair stay in place and prevent any flyaways.

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle:

  • Use a hairdryer to help set your hair in place
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you
  • Don't use too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy
  • Make sure to comb your hair evenly to avoid creating any bumps or lumps
  • Finish with a light mist of hairspray to help your hair stay in place

FAQs

What products can I use to slick my hair back?

You can use a variety of products to slick your hair back, including hair gel, pomade, and wax. Each product has a different level of hold and shine, so make sure to choose the product that works best for your hair type and desired look.

How much product should I use?

Start with a small amount of product and work your way up. Using too much product can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

How do I avoid creating bumps or lumps when slicking my hair back?

Make sure to comb your hair evenly and avoid pulling your hair too tightly when slicking it back. This will help prevent any bumps or lumps from forming.

Hair Care Tips

Slicking your hair back can be a great hairstyle option, but it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any excess oil or product buildup
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers

By following these hair care tips, you can help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Conclusion

Slicking your hair back is a classic, timeless hairstyle that works well for both casual and formal occasions. By choosing the right product, applying it correctly, and following our tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect slicked-back look. Remember to take care of your hair and follow our hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great!
