How To Layer Short Hair On Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Short hair can be a challenge to style, especially if you want to add some volume and texture to your locks. One of the best ways to achieve this is by layering your hair on top. Layering not only adds volume, but it also gives your hair a more polished look. In this article, we’ll show you how to layer short hair on top in a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Prepare Your Hair
  2. Sectioning Your Hair
  3. Layering Your Hair
  4. Tips for Maintaining Layered Short Hair
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Layered Short Hair

How to Prepare Your Hair

Before you start layering your short hair on top, it’s important to prepare your hair properly. This means washing your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner. Use a volumizing shampoo to add body to your hair. Once you’ve washed your hair, towel dry it gently. Avoid using a hairdryer as it can damage your hair.

Sectioning Your Hair

The next step is to section your hair. Use a comb to part your hair into two sections, one at the front and one at the back. The front section should be from the hairline to the crown of your head. The back section should be from the crown to the nape of your neck. Use hair clips to secure the sections in place.

Creating Subsections

Once you have your two sections, create subsections. Use a comb to part your hair horizontally, starting from the back section. The subsections should be about 1 inch wide. Use hair clips to secure each subsection in place.

Layering Your Hair

Now it’s time to layer your hair. Start with the front section. Hold a subsection of hair between two fingers and cut it at an angle. The angle should be between 45 and 90 degrees, depending on the length of your hair. Cut the hair in a downward motion. This will create a layered effect. Repeat this process for each subsection in the front section.

Once you’ve finished the front section, move on to the back section. Repeat the same process of holding a subsection of hair between two fingers and cutting it at an angle. Cut the hair in a downward motion. Make sure to cut each subsection at the same angle as the front section. This will create a uniform look.

Tips for Maintaining Layered Short Hair

Layered short hair requires frequent maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hair.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Use a volumizing mousse to add body to your hair.

FAQs

Can you layer short hair on top?

Yes, you can layer short hair on top. Layering adds volume and texture to short hair and makes it look more polished.

How do you layer short hair on top?

To layer short hair on top, you need to section your hair into two parts, front and back. Then, create subsections and cut each subsection at an angle to create a layered effect.

Can you layer your hair at home?

Yes, you can layer your hair at home. However, it’s important to be careful and use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care for Layered Short Hair

Layered short hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Use a volumizing mousse to add body to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hair.

Layering short hair on top is an easy way to add volume and texture to your locks. By following these simple steps, you can create a polished look that’s easy to maintain. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. With the right hair care, your layered short hair will look great for months to come.
