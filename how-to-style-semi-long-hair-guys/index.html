How To Style Semi Long Hair Guys A Guide To Relaxed Hair

Written by Petter Jun 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a guy with semi long hair, you might find it challenging to style your hair. You don't want to cut it too short, but you don't want it too long either. Luckily, there are many ways to style semi long hair guys that can give you a relaxed, casual look. In this article, we're going to give you a step-by-step guide to styling your semi long hair, as well as some tips and tricks to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Semi Long Hair Guys

Styling your semi long hair can be easy if you know what to do. Here are some popular hairstyles that you can try:

1. The Messy Look

The messy look is perfect for guys with semi long hair who want a relaxed, casual look. To achieve this look, all you need to do is apply a small amount of styling cream or pomade to your hair, then mess it up with your fingers. This hairstyle works best for guys with wavy or curly hair.

2. The Slicked Back Look

The slicked back look is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. To achieve this look, apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair, then comb it back with a fine-tooth comb. This hairstyle works best for guys with straight hair.

3. The Half-Up Look

The half-up look is perfect for guys with semi long hair who want to keep their hair out of their face. To achieve this look, pull the top half of your hair back and secure it with a hair tie or clip. This hairstyle works best for guys with thick hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your semi long hair:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt and oil buildup. This will make it easier to style your hair and keep it looking healthy.

2. Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling cream, pomade, gel or wax to your hair. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

3. Style Your Hair

Use your fingers, a comb or brush to style your hair the way you want it. You can try different hairstyles until you find one that suits you best.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray to help hold it in place. This will also help to prevent frizz and flyaways.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style your semi long hair:

1. Use the Right Products

Choose styling products that are designed for your hair type. If you have curly hair, use a product that enhances your curls. If you have straight hair, use a product that adds volume and texture.

2. Experiment with Different Hairstyles

Don't be afraid to try different hairstyles until you find one that you like. You can also get inspiration from magazines, TV shows or movies.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends. You don't have to cut your hair too short if you don't want to.

4. Protect Your Hair from Heat

If you use heat styling tools like a blow dryer or flat iron, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

1. How often should I wash my semi long hair?

You should wash your semi long hair every two to three days to keep it clean and healthy. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Can I use a hair mask on semi long hair?

Yes, you can use a hair mask on semi long hair. A hair mask can help to nourish and moisturize your hair, making it look and feel healthier.

3. How can I prevent frizz and flyaways?

You can prevent frizz and flyaways by using a smoothing serum or oil on your hair. You can also use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.

Hair Care for Semi Long Hair Guys

Here are some tips for taking care of your semi long hair:

1. Use a Good Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair. You can make a DIY hair mask using ingredients like avocado, coconut oil, and honey.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays. Too much sun exposure can cause your hair to become dry and brittle.

4. Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts every six to eight weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

Styling semi long hair guys doesn't have to be difficult. With the right hair care products and a little bit of practice, you can achieve a relaxed, casual look that suits your style. Try out different hairstyles and don't be afraid to experiment until you find the one that works best for you.
