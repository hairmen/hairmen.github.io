How to Tone Your Hair at Home with Wella

Toning your hair at home with Wella is a simple process that involves using a toner to neutralize any unwanted tones in your hair. Wella toners come in a variety of shades, including Ash, Beige, and Gold, and can be mixed with a developer to achieve your desired result.

What You'll Need

  • Wella toner
  • Developer
  • Gloves
  • Applicator brush
  • Bowl
  • Sectioning clips
  • Shampoo
  • Conditioner

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to tone your hair at home with Wella:

  1. Start by shampooing your hair and towel drying it.

  2. Mix the Wella toner and developer in a bowl according to the instructions on the packaging.

  3. Put on your gloves and use your applicator brush to apply the toner mixture to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

  4. Use sectioning clips to divide your hair into sections, making sure to apply the toner evenly throughout.

  5. Leave the toner on for the recommended time, then rinse it out thoroughly with water.

  6. Follow up with a conditioner to nourish and hydrate your hair.

  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Toning Your Hair at Home

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when toning your hair at home:

  • Choose the right toner for your hair color and desired result.
  • Follow the instructions on the packaging carefully.
  • Wear gloves to protect your hands from the toner.
  • Use an applicator brush to apply the toner evenly.
  • Divide your hair into sections to ensure that the toner is applied evenly throughout.
  • Leave the toner on for the recommended time, but don't exceed the maximum time.
  • Rinse the toner out thoroughly with water.
  • Follow up with a conditioner to nourish and hydrate your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about toning your hair at home with Wella:

How long should I leave the toner on my hair?

The recommended time will vary depending on the toner and developer you're using. Be sure to read the instructions carefully and leave the toner on for the recommended time.

Can I use Wella toner on colored hair?

Yes, you can use Wella toner on colored hair. Just be sure to choose the right toner for your hair color and desired result.

How often can I tone my hair?

You should wait at least 4-6 weeks between toning your hair to avoid over-processing it.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and vibrant after toning:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can cause breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you can confidently tone your hair at home with Wella and achieve your desired result. Happy toning!
