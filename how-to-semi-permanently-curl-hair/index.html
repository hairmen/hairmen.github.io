How To Semi Permanently Curl Hair

Written by Eveline Nov 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to add some texture and volume to your hair, semi-permanent curls might be the solution you've been searching for. This process uses chemicals to alter the structure of your hair, leaving it bouncy and curly for months at a time. In this article, we'll guide you through how to semi-permanently curl hair, step-by-step, along with some tips and tricks to make sure your curls last as long as possible.

How to Semi Permanently Curl Hair: Step by Step

Step 1: Preparation

The first step to semi-permanently curl hair is to prepare it properly. You should start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any build-up or residue. After that, apply a deep conditioning treatment to nourish and protect your hair. Rinse out the conditioner and towel-dry your hair until it's damp, but not dripping wet.

Step 2: Sectioning

Next, divide your hair into several sections using hair clips. The number of sections you'll need will depend on the thickness and length of your hair, but four sections is usually sufficient. Make sure each section is equal in size and thickness.

Step 3: Applying the Perm Solution

Now it's time to apply the perm solution. Put on gloves to protect your hands, and start at the nape of your neck. Take a small section of hair and apply the perm solution, making sure to saturate it completely. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way up towards the crown of your head. Once you've applied the perm solution to all sections, cover your hair with a plastic cap and leave it for the recommended time, usually around 20-30 minutes.

Step 4: Rinsing

After the recommended time has passed, remove the plastic cap and rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water. Make sure to rinse out all of the perm solution, as any residue can damage your hair. Once you've rinsed your hair, apply a neutralizer to each section, working from the nape of your neck to the crown of your head.

Step 5: Final Rinse and Styling

After you've applied the neutralizer, rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water. This will help to close the hair cuticles and seal in the curls. Once your hair is rinsed, use a towel to gently blot out any excess water. Don't rub your hair, as this can cause frizz. You can now style your hair as desired, using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance the curls.

Tips for Semi Permanent Hair Curling

Here are some tips to ensure your semi-permanent curls last as long as possible:

  • Don't wash your hair for at least 48 hours after the treatment, as this can cause the curls to loosen.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause the curls to fall out.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and nourished.

FAQ About Semi Permanent Hair Curling

Q: How long do semi-permanent curls last?

A: Semi-permanent curls can last anywhere from 2-6 months, depending on how well you care for your hair and how quickly it grows.

Q: Can I color my hair after getting semi-permanent curls?

A: It's best to wait at least a week after getting semi-permanent curls before coloring your hair, as the chemicals can cause damage and dryness.

Q: Can I swim with semi-permanent curls?

A: Yes, you can swim with semi-permanent curls, but it's best to wear a swim cap to protect your hair from chlorine and salt water.

Hair Care After Semi Permanent Curling

After getting semi-permanent curls, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or relaxers, as these can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a detangling brush to avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.
  • Apply a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.
  • Keep your hair hydrated by using a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can enjoy beautiful, bouncy curls for months at a time with semi-permanent curling.
