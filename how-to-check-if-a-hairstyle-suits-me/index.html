How To Check If A Hairstyle Suits Me

Written by Christine Sep 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Trying out a new hairstyle can be exciting as it can change your entire look. However, not every hairstyle suits everyone. It is important to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape, hair type, and personal style. In this article, we will discuss how to check if a hairstyle suits you.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Understanding Your Hair Type
  • Identifying Your Personal Style
  • Trying Out the Hairstyle
  • FAQs

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a hairstyle that suits you is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each face shape has its own unique features and requires a specific type of haircut to complement those features.

To determine your face shape, you can use a measuring tape or ruler. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin. Once you have these measurements, you can compare them to the following guidelines:

  • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width.
  • Round: Prominent cheekbones and a rounded chin, with the length and width of the face being roughly equal.
  • Square: Strong jawline and forehead, with the width of the face being roughly equal to its length.
  • Heart: Wide forehead and cheekbones, with a narrow chin.
  • Diamond: Prominent cheekbones and narrow forehead and chin.
  • Oblong: Long and narrow face, with the length being more than one and a half times the width.

Once you have determined your face shape, you can search for hairstyles that complement your features.

Understanding Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair type. Different hair types require different haircuts to look their best. For example, if you have curly hair, you may want to avoid a haircut that is too short as it can make your hair look frizzy and unmanageable.

Some common hair types include straight, wavy, curly, and kinky. You can determine your hair type by looking at its texture and thickness. Thin hair will appear more see-through, while thick hair will have more volume.

Once you have identified your hair type, you can search for hairstyles that work well with it.

Identifying Your Personal Style

Your personal style is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and the image you want to project. For example, if you have a conservative personality, you may want to avoid a bold haircut that stands out too much.

Some common personal styles include classic, trendy, edgy, and bohemian. Consider your clothing style, makeup preferences, and overall aesthetic when choosing a hairstyle.

Trying Out the Hairstyle

Once you have identified a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair type, and personal style, it's time to try it out. You can use online hairstyle simulation tools or try on wigs to get an idea of how the hairstyle will look on you.

If you're not ready to commit to a drastic haircut, you can start with a small change, such as adding bangs or layers. This will give you an idea of how the hairstyle will look on you without making a permanent change.

FAQs

What if I have multiple face shapes?

If you have a combination of face shapes, you can choose a hairstyle that complements the most prominent features of each shape. For example, if you have a heart-shaped face with a prominent chin, you can choose a haircut that adds volume to the top of your head to balance out your face.

What if I have thinning hair?

If you have thinning hair, you can choose a haircut that adds volume and texture to your hair. Layers and bangs can also help create the illusion of thicker hair.

Hair Care Tips

Once you have chosen a hairstyle that suits you, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and choosing a hairstyle that complements your face shape, hair type, and personal style, you can achieve a look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
