Short Bob Hairstyles For Fine Hair Over 70

Written by Bardi Oct 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're over 70 and have fine hair, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. However, a short bob is a great option that can work wonders for adding volume and texture to your hair. In this article, we'll go over some of the best short bob hairstyles for fine hair over 70, as well as tips for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair.

Description

Short bob hairstyles are perfect for those with fine hair, as they can add volume and texture to your hair without weighing it down. This is especially important for those over 70, as hair tends to thin and lose volume as we age. A short bob can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style, and is easy to maintain and style.

How to

If you're considering a short bob hairstyle, it's important to work with a stylist who is experienced with fine hair. They can help you choose the right length and style that will work best for your hair type and face shape. Once you have your new haircut, it's important to use the right hair products and styling tools to keep your hair looking healthy and voluminous.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a short bob hairstyle:

  1. Consult with your stylist to choose the right length and style for your hair type and face shape.
  2. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for fine hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  4. Use a lightweight styling product to add hold and texture to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short bob hairstyle:

  • Choose a length that works best for your hair type and face shape.
  • Use products designed for fine hair to add volume and texture.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and texture.
  • Use a lightweight styling product to add hold and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short bob hairstyles for fine hair over 70:

What is the best length for a short bob hairstyle?

The best length for a short bob hairstyle will depend on your hair type and face shape. Your stylist can help you choose the right length that will work best for you.

What products should I use to add volume and texture to my hair?

Use lightweight products designed for fine hair, such as volumizing mousses and texturizing sprays. These products can help add volume and texture to your hair without weighing it down.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It's recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your short bob hairstyle and keep your hair looking healthy.

Hair Care

To keep your short bob hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair with products designed for fine hair.
  • Use a lightweight styling product to add hold and texture to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and texture.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair looking healthy.

With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can rock a short bob hairstyle at any age. Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles to find the perfect look for you.
