What Hair Color Is Attractive To Guys

Written by Petter Dec 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to hair color, women often wonder what shade will make them look more attractive to men. While hair color is not the only factor that determines attractiveness, it can make a difference. In this article, we will discuss what hair color is attractive to guys and provide some tips on how to achieve it.

Table of Content

  • What hair color do guys prefer?
  • How to choose the right hair color for you?
  • Step by step guide to achieve attractive hair color
  • Tips on maintaining your hair color
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

What hair color do guys prefer?

The answer to this question varies from person to person, and there is no one-size-fits-all answer. However, according to surveys, the majority of men prefer women with natural hair colors such as brown, blonde, and black. These colors are considered attractive because they are classic and timeless.

However, there are men who prefer more unique hair colors such as red, purple, and blue. These colors can be attractive if they complement the woman's skin tone and overall look.

How to choose the right hair color for you?

The key to choosing the right hair color is to consider your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. If you have warm undertones, you should opt for warm hair colors such as golden blonde, caramel brown, and copper red. If you have cool undertones, you should choose cool hair colors such as ashy blonde, platinum blonde, and jet black.

You should also consider your eye color when choosing a hair color. If you have blue or green eyes, you can opt for blonde or light brown hair. If you have brown eyes, you can choose darker hair colors such as brown or black.

Lastly, you should consider your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, you may want to lighten it gradually to avoid damaging your hair. If you have light hair, you can experiment with darker shades.

Step by step guide to achieve attractive hair color

Here is a step by step guide to achieving attractive hair color:

  1. Choose the right hair color for your skin tone and eye color.
  2. Consult with a professional hairstylist to ensure that the hair color will complement your features.
  3. Prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  4. Apply the hair color according to the instructions on the box or as directed by your hairstylist.
  5. Leave the hair color on for the recommended amount of time.
  6. Rinse the hair color out with warm water.
  7. Apply a hair mask or conditioner to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips on maintaining your hair color

Here are some tips on maintaining your hair color:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair color from fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as this can strip the color from your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons as these can damage your hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQs

Q: Does hair color really matter?

A: Hair color is just one factor that contributes to a person's overall attractiveness. While it can make a difference, it is not the only thing that matters.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: It is possible to dye your hair at home, but it is recommended that you consult with a professional hairstylist to ensure that you choose the right hair color and apply it correctly.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it is important to take care of it. Here are some tips on hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons as these can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

In conclusion, while there is no one hair color that is universally attractive to guys, natural hair colors such as brown, blonde, and black are generally considered attractive. However, if you want to try a more unique hair color, make sure that it complements your skin tone and eye color. And remember, the most important thing is to feel confident and comfortable in your own skin.
