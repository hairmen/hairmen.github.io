How Do You Know If Bangs Will Suit You

Written by Aprilia Nov 16, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but not sure if they will suit you? Bangs can be a great way to change up your look, but choosing the right style can be tricky. In this article, we’ll walk you through how to know if bangs will suit you.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Texture
  • Face Shape
  • Forehead Size
  • Hair Type
  • Lifestyle
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Bangs

Hair Texture

One important factor to consider when deciding if bangs will suit you is your hair texture. If you have fine or thin hair, wispy bangs can add volume and texture to your hair. On the other hand, if you have thick or curly hair, you may want to opt for heavier, fuller bangs.

Another thing to keep in mind is how your hair behaves in humidity. If you live in a humid climate, your bangs may frizz or stick to your forehead, making them difficult to manage.

Face Shape

Your face shape is another important consideration when deciding if bangs will suit you. Different face shapes suit different types of bangs:

  • Oval: Almost any type of bangs will suit an oval face shape.
  • Round: Side-swept bangs or long, layered bangs can help elongate a round face shape.
  • Square: Soft, wispy bangs can soften a square jawline.
  • Heart: Side-swept bangs that start at the temple can complement a heart-shaped face.

Forehead Size

Your forehead size can also play a role in whether or not bangs will suit you. If you have a larger forehead, bangs can help balance out your face and make your forehead appear smaller. If you have a smaller forehead, however, bangs may overwhelm your face.

Hair Type

The type of hair you have can also impact whether or not bangs will suit you. If you have naturally straight hair, bangs can be easier to manage and maintain. If you have curly or wavy hair, bangs may require more styling and upkeep.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another important consideration when deciding if bangs will suit you. If you’re someone who enjoys working out or spends a lot of time outdoors, bangs may not be the best choice for you. Sweating or getting caught in the rain can cause your bangs to stick to your forehead or become frizzy.

Tips for Choosing the Right Bangs

Here are some tips to help you choose the right type of bangs:

  • Consider your face shape and forehead size
  • Think about your hair texture and type
  • Consult with a stylist to find the best style for you
  • Start with longer bangs and gradually go shorter
  • Consider how much maintenance and styling your bangs will require

FAQ

Q: What if I don’t like my bangs?

A: If you don’t like your bangs, you can always grow them out or have them professionally trimmed.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with bangs?

A: Yes! Bangs can look great with a ponytail, bun, or updo.

Hair Care for Bangs

To keep your bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your bangs daily or as needed
  • Blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to lay
  • Use a small amount of styling product to tame any flyaways
  • Get regular trims to keep your bangs looking fresh

Now that you know how to know if bangs will suit you, you can confidently choose the right style for your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Happy styling!
