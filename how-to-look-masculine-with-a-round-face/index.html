How To Look Masculine With A Round Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Sep 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Do you have a round face and want to look more masculine? It's possible with a few simple tips and tricks. In this article, we'll show you how to contour your face, choose the right haircut, and style your facial hair to create a more masculine look. Let's get started!

How to Contour Your Face

Contouring is a makeup technique that can help create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones. To contour your face, you'll need a contouring powder or cream that is a few shades darker than your skin tone. Apply the contouring product along your jawline, under your cheekbones, and on the sides of your forehead. Blend well with a makeup brush to avoid any harsh lines.

Tips:

  • Choose a matte contouring product, as shimmery products can make your face look rounder.
  • Use a light hand when applying the product and build up gradually.
  • Blend well to avoid any visible lines.

Choose the Right Haircut

Your haircut can have a big impact on the shape of your face. If you have a round face, avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head. Instead, opt for a haircut that is shorter on the sides and longer on the top. This will create the illusion of a longer face and a more defined jawline.

Tips:

  • Try a buzz cut, crew cut, or undercut.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or have a center part.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice on the best haircut for your face shape.

Style Your Facial Hair

If you can grow facial hair, styling it can also help create a more masculine look. A beard or goatee can help add definition to your jawline and make your face appear longer. However, if you have a round face, avoid styles that are too full or bushy, as they can make your face look even rounder.

Tips:

  • Try a goatee or soul patch if you have a small chin.
  • Avoid styles that are too full or bushy.
  • Keep your facial hair well-groomed and trimmed.

FAQ

Q: Can I still look masculine if I don't have facial hair?

A: Absolutely! Contouring and choosing the right haircut can both help create a more masculine look, even if you don't have facial hair.

Q: What should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, as well as styles that are too slicked back or have a center part. Also avoid facial hair styles that are too full or bushy.

Q: How often should I trim my facial hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and the style you're going for. As a general rule, trim your facial hair every 1-2 weeks to keep it looking well-groomed.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type, and avoid using too much product, as it can weigh your hair down. If you have a beard or goatee, use beard oil or balm to keep it soft and moisturized.

By following these tips and tricks, you can create a more masculine look, even if you have a round face. Remember to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape, contour your face to create more definition, and style your facial hair to add to the overall effect. With a little effort, you can look and feel more confident and masculine.
