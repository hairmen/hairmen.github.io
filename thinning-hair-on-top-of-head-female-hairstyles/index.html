Thinning Hair On Top Of Head Female Hairstyles How To Style With Confidence

Written by Robby Jun 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Thinning hair can be a frustrating problem for many women, especially when it occurs on the top of the head. However, there are plenty of hairstyles that can help you feel confident and beautiful, no matter what stage of hair loss you're experiencing. In this article, we'll go over some of the best hairstyles for women with thinning hair on top of their head, as well as some hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and full.

Description

Thinning hair on top of the head is a common problem for many women, and it can be caused by a variety of factors such as genetics, aging, hormonal changes, and certain medical conditions. While there's no cure for hair loss, there are plenty of hairstyles that can help you feel confident and stylish, no matter what stage of hair loss you're experiencing.

How to Style Thin Hair on Top of the Head

If you're dealing with thinning hair on top of your head, there are plenty of hairstyles that can help you look and feel your best. Here are a few ideas:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can add volume and movement to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker. Ask your stylist to add long, textured layers throughout your hair, focusing on the top and crown area.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a great option for women with thinning hair on top of their head. This short, cropped style can give the illusion of fullness and volume, while also being easy to maintain.

3. Bob Cut

A classic bob cut can also be a great choice for women with thinning hair on top of their head. This style can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture, and can be worn straight or with waves for added volume.

4. Updo

An updo can be a great way to disguise thinning hair on top of the head. Try a messy bun or a braided updo to add volume and texture to your hair, while also keeping it off your face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Thin Hair on Top of the Head

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling thin hair on top of the head:

Step 1: Choose the Right Products

Choose hair products that are specifically designed for thinning hair, such as volumizing shampoos and conditioners. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and collagen, which can help strengthen and thicken your hair.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and lift to the roots. Focus on the crown area, using the brush to lift the hair up and away from the scalp.

Step 3: Add Texture

Add texture to your hair by using a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or curls. This can help give the illusion of fullness and volume.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair in your desired hairstyle, such as a bob or pixie cut. Use hair accessories like headbands or hair clips to add extra volume and texture to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Here are a few tips for maintaining healthy hair:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals can help keep your hair healthy and strong. Focus on foods that are high in protein, iron, and vitamin B12, such as lean meats, fish, and leafy greens.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Try to limit your use of these tools, or use a heat protectant spray before styling.

3. Use Gentle Hair Products

Avoid using harsh chemicals and sulfates in your hair products, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage. Look for products that are gentle and nourishing, and avoid over-washing your hair.

FAQs

Q: Is it normal to have thinning hair on top of the head?

A: Yes, thinning hair on top of the head is a common problem for many women, especially as they age. It can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, hormonal changes, and medical conditions.

Q: Can hairstyles really make a difference for thinning hair?

A: Yes, choosing the right hairstyle can help make your hair look fuller and thicker. Long layers, pixie cuts, and bobs are all great options for women with thinning hair on top of their head.

Q: Are there any hair care products that can help with thinning hair?

A: Yes, there are plenty of hair care products that are specifically designed for thinning hair, such as volumizing shampoos and conditioners, hair growth serums, and scalp treatments. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and collagen, which can help strengthen and thicken your hair.

Hair Care for Thinning Hair on Top of the Head

If you're dealing with thinning hair on top of the head, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are a few tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Less Often

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause damage. Try to wash your hair every other day, or even less often if possible.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb can help prevent breakage and damage to your hair. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots, being gentle and avoiding tugging or pulling.

3. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid wearing your hair in tight hairstyles like ponytails or braids, as these can cause damage and breakage to your hair. Instead, opt for loose, gentle styles that won't pull on your hair.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Wear a hat or use a hair sunscreen to protect your hair when you're outside.

5. Be Gentle

Above all, be gentle with your hair. Avoid using harsh chemicals or over-styling your hair, and embrace your natural texture and style.

Thinning hair on top of the head doesn't have to be a source of stress or anxiety. With the right hairstyles and hair care tips, you can feel confident and beautiful no matter what stage of hair loss you're experiencing. Try out some of the styles and tips we've shared here, and see what works best for you!
