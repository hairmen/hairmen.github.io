How To Relax Hair For Black Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Relaxing hair is a common practice among black guys who want to achieve a straighter and more manageable look. However, the process can be tricky and even damaging if not done correctly. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of how to relax hair for black guys in a safe and effective manner. We'll also provide some tips and answer frequently asked questions to help you achieve your desired look while maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Prepare Your Hair
  • Step 2: Apply the Relaxer
  • Step 3: Rinse and Neutralize
  • Tips for Relaxing Hair
  • FAQs about Relaxing Hair for Black Guys
  • Hair Care after Relaxing

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before applying a relaxer, it's important to prepare your hair so that it can withstand the chemical treatment. Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or oils. This will allow the relaxer to penetrate your hair more effectively. After washing, towel-dry your hair and detangle it gently with a wide-tooth comb. Avoid using a brush as it can cause breakage.

Next, apply a protective base to your scalp and hairline using petroleum jelly or a similar product. This will prevent the relaxer from burning your skin. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties to make the application process easier.

Step 2: Apply the Relaxer

Follow the instructions on the relaxer kit carefully. Wear gloves to protect your hands from the chemical. Begin applying the relaxer to the back of your head, working your way up to the top and front. Use a brush or your fingers to distribute the product evenly, making sure to cover each section thoroughly.

Be careful not to apply the relaxer to your scalp or hairline, as this can cause irritation and damage. Leave the product on for the recommended time, usually around 15-20 minutes, depending on your hair type and the strength of the relaxer. Check your hair every few minutes to make sure it's not over-processing.

Step 3: Rinse and Neutralize

Once the time is up, rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water until the relaxer is completely removed. Apply a neutralizing shampoo to stop the chemical process and restore the pH balance of your hair. Leave the shampoo on for a few minutes, then rinse it out with cool water. Follow up with a deep conditioner to moisturize and strengthen your hair.

Tips for Relaxing Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when relaxing your hair:

  • Choose a relaxer that's appropriate for your hair type and texture. There are different strengths available, so make sure to read the labels carefully.
  • Don't overlap the relaxer on previously relaxed hair, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Protect your hair from heat and avoid using styling tools that can cause further damage.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.

FAQs about Relaxing Hair for Black Guys

Can I relax my hair if it's already damaged?

No, it's not recommended to relax damaged hair as it can cause further breakage and damage. Wait until your hair is healthy before attempting to relax it.

How often should I relax my hair?

It's best to wait at least 6-8 weeks between relaxer applications to avoid over-processing and damage. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and texture.

Can I color my hair after relaxing it?

It's not recommended to color your hair immediately after relaxing it, as this can cause further damage. Wait at least 2 weeks before coloring your hair.

Hair Care after Relaxing

After relaxing your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain its health and prevent breakage. Here are some tips for post-relaxer hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible and use a heat protectant when necessary.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and strong.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause tension and breakage.

By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a relaxed hairstyle that looks great and is healthy. Remember to always prioritize the health of your hair over achieving a certain look.
