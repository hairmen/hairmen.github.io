Short Haircut Men 2021 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's both stylish and easy to maintain, then a short haircut might be just what you need. In this article, we'll explore the latest trends in short haircuts for men in 2021. From the buzz cut to the French crop, we'll cover everything you need to know to get the perfect short haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Description

Short haircuts for men have been trending for the past few years, and their popularity shows no signs of slowing down in 2021. These haircuts are versatile, low-maintenance, and can be customized to suit any face shape and hair texture. Whether you're looking for a classic or modern look, there's a short haircut that will fit your style.

How to

Getting a short haircut is relatively simple, but there are a few things you should keep in mind before you visit your barber. First, decide on the type of haircut you want. Look for inspiration online, and save pictures of haircuts that you like. This will give your barber a clear idea of what you want.

Next, consider your face shape and hair type. Certain short haircuts work better on different face shapes and hair textures. For example, a buzz cut might look great on someone with a square face, but not on someone with a round face. Similarly, a French crop might work better on someone with thick, straight hair, but not on someone with curly hair.

Once you've decided on the type of haircut you want, book an appointment with your barber. During the appointment, be sure to communicate clearly with your barber. Explain what you want, and ask for their advice if you're not sure about something. Remember, your barber is a professional, and they can help you get the perfect haircut for your style and hair type.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a short haircut:

  1. Decide on the type of haircut you want
  2. Consider your face shape and hair type
  3. Book an appointment with your barber
  4. Communicate clearly with your barber
  5. Sit back and enjoy your new haircut!

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your short haircut:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Invest in a good quality styling product to help you achieve the look you want
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your haircut looking sharp
  • If you're not sure about a particular haircut, ask your barber for their opinion

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for men:

Q: What short haircut is best for a round face?

A: A short haircut with volume at the top, such as a pompadour or quiff, can help elongate a round face.

Q: What short haircut is best for thick, curly hair?

A: A short haircut that's layered and textured, such as a curly crop or messy fringe, can work well with thick, curly hair.

Q: How often should I get a trim for my short haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your short haircut looking sharp.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep your short haircut looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straightening irons, as this can damage your hair
  • Invest in a good quality styling product to help you achieve the look you want
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your haircut looking sharp

Overall, a short haircut can be a stylish and low-maintenance option for men in 2021. By following these tips and communicating clearly with your barber, you can achieve the perfect short haircut for your style and hair type.
