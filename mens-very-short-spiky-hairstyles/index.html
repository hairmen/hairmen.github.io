Mens Very Short Spiky Hairstyles The Trending Look For 2023

If you're looking for a hairstyle that screams edgy and daring, mens very short spiky hairstyles might be the answer. This look is perfect for men who want to stand out from the crowd and show off their confidence. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about mens very short spiky hairstyles, from how to get the look to hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Description

Mens very short spiky hairstyles are characterized by short, sharp spikes that are styled using a strong-hold hair product. This look is perfect for men who want to show off their confidence and individuality. Mens very short spiky hairstyles can be worn in a variety of ways, from a messy, textured look to a more refined, sleek style.

How to Get the Look

The first step to getting mens very short spiky hairstyles is to find a skilled barber or hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. You'll also need to have the right hair length and texture. Mens very short spiky hairstyles work best on hair that is less than an inch long and has a naturally thick or coarse texture.

Tools You'll Need

  • Strong-hold hair product
  • Fine-tooth comb
  • Scissors or clippers

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting mens very short spiky hairstyles:

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Apply a strong-hold hair product to your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.
  3. Using a fine-tooth comb, create spikes by pulling your hair upward and away from your scalp.
  4. Trim any stray hairs with scissors or clippers.
  5. Style your hair as desired, using your fingers to create a messy, textured look or a comb for a more refined style.
  6. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to keep your spikes in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Mens very short spiky hairstyles require regular maintenance to keep their shape and style. Here are some tips for maintaining the look:

  • Visit your barber or hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking sharp.
  • Use a strong-hold hair product to keep your spikes in place.
  • Apply a weekly hair mask to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.
  • Invest in a good quality comb or brush to help you style your hair.

FAQ

What face shapes suit mens very short spiky hairstyles?

Mens very short spiky hairstyles can work on a variety of face shapes, but they tend to look best on men with square, oval, or heart-shaped faces.

Can mens very short spiky hairstyles be worn in a professional setting?

Mens very short spiky hairstyles can be adapted to suit a professional setting, but it's important to keep them neat and tidy. Avoid messy or unkempt spikes, and opt for a more refined style.

What hair types are best for mens very short spiky hairstyles?

Mens very short spiky hairstyles work best on hair that is naturally thick or coarse. If you have fine or thinning hair, this look might not be the best choice for you.

Hair Care Tips

Mens very short spiky hairstyles require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair product that will help you achieve and maintain your desired style.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock mens very short spiky hairstyles with confidence and style. Whether you're looking for a bold and daring look or a more refined style, this trend is sure to turn heads.
