Spiked Hair For Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Dec 01, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re looking to switch up your hairstyle and are considering going for a spiky look, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll give you a complete guide on how to achieve spiked hair for guys, including step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Spike Your Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Instructions
  4. Tips for Spiking Your Hair
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Spiked Hair

Description

Spiked hair is a popular hairstyle among guys, especially those who prefer a more edgy or punk look. The style involves using product to create a spiky, textured look on top of the head, while keeping the sides and back shorter.

How to Spike Your Hair

To achieve spiked hair, you’ll need a few products and tools, including:

  • Hair gel
  • Hairspray
  • Comb or brush
  • Blow dryer (optional)

Once you have these items, you can follow the steps below to create your spiky hairstyle.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Start with clean, dry hair. Wash and dry your hair, making sure it’s completely dry before styling.

2. Apply hair gel. Take a small amount of hair gel and rub it between your hands to warm it up. Apply the gel to your hair, focusing on the top of your head.

3. Style your hair. Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair into spikes. If you want more height or texture, use a blow dryer on low heat to dry your hair while styling it.

4. Set your style. Once you’ve achieved the desired look, use hairspray to set your hair in place.

5. Touch up as needed. Throughout the day, you may need to touch up your hair to maintain the spiky look. Simply add a bit more gel or hairspray as needed.

Tips for Spiking Your Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when spiking your hair:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed. Too much product can make your hair look greasy or weighed down.
  • Experiment with different types of gel to find one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • If you have thin or fine hair, consider using a volumizing product before adding gel to create more lift and texture.
  • Don’t be afraid to try different styles and looks until you find one that works for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about spiked hair for guys:

How long does spiked hair last?

The longevity of your spiked hair depends on a few factors, including the type of product you use and the weather conditions. Generally, spiked hair can last anywhere from a few hours to a full day.

Can you spike short hair?

Yes, you can still achieve a spiky look with short hair. Simply focus on styling the top of your head, and use a bit less product to avoid weighing down your hair.

Is spiked hair suitable for all face shapes?

Spiked hair can work for a variety of face shapes, but may be more flattering on those with square or oval-shaped faces.

Hair Care for Spiked Hair

To keep your spiked hair looking its best, it’s important to take care of your hair and scalp. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove product buildup and keep your scalp healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and make your hair harder to style.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your desired length and shape.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can achieve a stylish and edgy spiky hairstyle that’s sure to turn heads. Whether you’re going for a bold, punk look or just want to switch up your hairstyle, spiked hair is a great choice for guys of all ages and styles.
