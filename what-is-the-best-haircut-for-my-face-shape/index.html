Determining Your Face Shape

Before you can choose the best haircut for your face shape, you need to determine what your face shape is. There are six main face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Rectangle

Best Haircuts for Each Face Shape

Round Face

If you have a round face, the best haircuts will add angles and length to your face. This can be achieved with layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical styles. Avoid blunt cuts and middle parts, as they can make your face appear rounder.

Oval Face

An oval face is considered the most versatile face shape, as it can pull off a variety of haircuts. However, the best haircuts will showcase your facial symmetry, such as long layers or a bob. Avoid styles that cover your face, as they can detract from your natural beauty.

Square Face

If you have a square face, the best haircuts will soften your jawline and add length to your face. This can be achieved with layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and textured styles. Avoid blunt cuts and straight styles, as they can accentuate your square features.

Heart Face

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and a narrower chin. The best haircuts will balance out these features, such as long layers or a side-swept bob. Avoid styles that add volume to the top of your head, as they can make your forehead appear even wider.

Diamond Face

If you have a diamond-shaped face, the best haircuts will balance out your cheekbones and jawline. This can be achieved with chin-length bobs or layered cuts with volume at the crown. Avoid styles that add volume to your cheekbones, as they can make your face appear wider.

Rectangle Face

A rectangle face is characterized by a longer face length and a squared jawline. The best haircuts will add width to your face, such as layered cuts with volume at the sides. Avoid styles that are too long, as they can make your face appear even longer.
