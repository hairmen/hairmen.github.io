How To Know Which Hair Color Suits You The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be tricky. With so many options available, it’s easy to feel overwhelmed and unsure of what will suit you best. However, finding the perfect hair color doesn’t have to be difficult. In this article, we’ll show you how to determine which hair color is right for you, so you can feel confident and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Color

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step to choosing the right hair color is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone is the underlying color of your skin, and it can be warm, cool, or neutral. Here’s how to determine your skin tone:

Warm Skin Tone

If you have a warm skin tone, your skin will have a yellow or golden undertone. You may also have green veins on the inside of your wrist. People with warm skin tones look best in warm hair colors, such as golden blonde, caramel, copper, and auburn.

Cool Skin Tone

If you have a cool skin tone, your skin will have a pink or blue undertone. You may also have blue or purple veins on the inside of your wrist. People with cool skin tones look best in cool hair colors, such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, cool brown, and black.

Neutral Skin Tone

If you have a neutral skin tone, your skin will have a mix of warm and cool undertones. People with neutral skin tones can wear a variety of hair colors, but should avoid extremes in either direction.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Color

Once you’ve determined your skin tone, it’s time to choose the right hair color. Here’s a step by step guide:

Step 1: Determine Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color is a good starting point for choosing a new hair color. If you’re not sure what your natural hair color is, look at the roots or ask your hairstylist.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also help you choose the right hair color. For example, if you have blue eyes, you may look best in a cool blonde or brunette shade.

Step 3: Look at Your Skin Tone

Refer back to the section on determining your skin tone. Choose hair colors that complement your undertones.

Step 4: Consider Your Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hair color. If you’re low maintenance, choose a color that will grow out gracefully. If you’re adventurous, try a bold new shade.

Step 5: Consult with a Hairstylist

A hairstylist can help you choose the perfect hair color. They can also help you make any necessary adjustments to ensure that the color looks great with your skin tone, eye color, and lifestyle.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips for choosing the right hair color:

  • Choose a color that complements your overall style.
  • Consider any skin conditions or sensitivities you may have.
  • Try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to get an idea of what a color will look like on you.
  • Take inspiration from celebrities or social media influencers with similar skin tones and eye colors.
  • Remember that hair color can affect your mood and confidence, so choose a color that makes you feel good.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I have multiple skin undertones?

If you have multiple skin undertones, choose a hair color that complements the majority of your undertones.

What if I want to try a bold new color?

If you want to try a bold new color, consider doing a temporary or semi-permanent dye first to make sure you like the color.

What if I don’t like my new hair color?

If you don’t like your new hair color, don’t panic. Most hair colors can be adjusted or corrected with the help of a professional hairstylist.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Color

Once you’ve chosen the perfect hair color, it’s important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip the color.
  • Use a color-safe dry shampoo between washes.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, which can also fade color.
  • Get regular touch-ups to keep your color looking fresh.

Choosing the right hair color doesn’t have to be difficult. By following these tips and consulting with a hairstylist, you can find a color that complements your skin tone, eye color, and lifestyle. Remember to take care of your hair to keep your new color looking great.
