How Short Is A Number 3 And 4 Haircut

Written by Aprilia Jul 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're thinking about getting a haircut, you may have heard the terms "number 3" and "number 4" thrown around. But what do these numbers actually mean? In this article, we'll explain how short a number 3 and 4 haircut is, and give you some tips on how to take care of your hair after getting one.

Description

A number 3 haircut is usually cut to around 3/8 of an inch, while a number 4 haircut is slightly longer at around 1/2 an inch. These measurements apply to the guard on the clippers that your barber or stylist will use. So, if you ask for a number 3 or 4 haircut, they will use that specific guard to cut your hair to that length all over your head.

How To

If you want to get a number 3 or 4 haircut, the first step is to find a good barber or stylist who has experience with these types of cuts. You can ask for recommendations from friends or family members, or look for reviews online. Once you find someone you trust, you can show them a picture of the style you want, or simply ask for a number 3 or 4 all over.

During the haircut, your barber or stylist will use clippers with the appropriate guard to cut your hair to the desired length. They may also use scissors to trim the hair around your ears and neckline, and to add some texture to the top of your hair. The whole process should take around 20-30 minutes.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step breakdown of what to expect during a number 3 or 4 haircut:

Step 1: Wash and Dry

Your barber or stylist will start by washing your hair to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup. They will then towel-dry your hair and comb it out to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Clipper Cut

Using clippers with the appropriate guard, your barber or stylist will cut your hair to the desired length. They will start at the back of your head and work their way up to the top, making sure to cut evenly all around.

Step 3: Scissor Trim

Your barber or stylist will use scissors to trim the hair around your ears and neckline, and to add some texture to the top of your hair. They may also use thinning shears to remove some of the bulk.

Step 4: Style and Finish

Finally, your barber or stylist will style your hair using a comb, brush, or their fingers, depending on the look you want. They may also apply some product, such as pomade or wax, to add hold and shine.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair after getting a number 3 or 4 haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, which can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about number 3 and 4 haircuts:

Q: Will a number 3 or 4 haircut look good on me?

A: It depends on your face shape, hair type, and personal style. It's always a good idea to consult with a barber or stylist before getting a new haircut.

Q: How often do I need to get a number 3 or 4 haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your haircut. As a general rule, you should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I style my hair differently after getting a number 3 or 4 haircut?

A: Yes, you can style your hair in a variety of ways after getting a number 3 or 4 haircut. Talk to your barber or stylist about different styling options that would work for your hair type and face shape.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and attractive appearance. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat or styling products, which can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your haircut.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking and feeling its best, whether you have a number 3 or 4 haircut or any other style.
