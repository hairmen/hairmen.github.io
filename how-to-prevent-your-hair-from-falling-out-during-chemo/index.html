How To Prevent Your Hair From Falling Out During Chemo

Written by Eveline Jul 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Dealing with cancer can be a challenging experience, especially when undergoing chemotherapy. One of the most common side effects of chemotherapy is hair loss. Losing your hair can be distressing and can impact your self-esteem. Fortunately, there are ways to prevent hair loss during chemo. In this blog post, we will discuss how to prevent your hair from falling out during chemo and some tips on how to care for your hair during this time.

Table of Contents

  • How to Prevent Hair Loss During Chemo
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Hair Care During Chemo
  • Tips to Avoid Hair Loss During Chemo
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Prevent Hair Loss During Chemo

The first step in preventing hair loss during chemo is to talk to your doctor about your concerns. Your doctor may be able to suggest treatments that can help reduce hair loss. Some of the treatments that may help prevent hair loss during chemo include:

  • Scalp cooling: This involves using a special cap that cools the scalp and reduces blood flow to the hair follicles. This can help reduce hair loss during chemo.
  • Topical treatments: Some topical treatments, such as minoxidil, may help reduce hair loss during chemo.
  • Chemotherapy drugs: Some chemotherapy drugs are less likely to cause hair loss than others. Your doctor may be able to prescribe a drug that is less likely to cause hair loss.

It's important to note that not all of these treatments will be effective for everyone. Your doctor can help you determine the best course of action for your individual situation.

Step-by-Step Guide to Hair Care During Chemo

If you do experience hair loss during chemo, there are steps you can take to care for your hair and scalp. Here is a step-by-step guide to hair care during chemo:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Use a soft-bristled brush or wide-toothed comb to avoid pulling on your hair.
  3. Avoid using heat styling tools, such as hair dryers and curling irons.
  4. Avoid coloring or perming your hair during chemo.
  5. Wear a hat or scarf to protect your scalp from the sun.
  6. Avoid tight hairstyles that may pull on your hair.
  7. Consider getting a shorter haircut to make hair loss less noticeable.
  8. Use a satin pillowcase to reduce friction on your hair while sleeping.
  9. Avoid using products that contain alcohol or harsh chemicals.
  10. Consult with your doctor before using any new hair care products or treatments.

Tips to Avoid Hair Loss During Chemo

In addition to the treatments and hair care tips mentioned above, there are some other things you can do to help prevent hair loss during chemo:

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Avoid smoking and limit your alcohol intake.
  • Get plenty of rest and manage stress through activities such as yoga or meditation.
  • Consider wearing a cold cap during chemotherapy treatments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does everyone lose their hair during chemo?

No, not everyone loses their hair during chemo. The likelihood of hair loss depends on the type of chemotherapy drugs you are receiving.

Is there anything I can do to prevent hair loss during chemo?

Yes, there are some treatments and hair care tips that may help prevent hair loss during chemo. Talk to your doctor about your concerns and they can help you determine the best course of action for your individual situation.

Will my hair grow back after chemo?

Yes, in most cases your hair will grow back after chemo. However, it may take several months for your hair to return to its normal thickness and texture.

In conclusion, losing your hair during chemo can be distressing, but there are ways to prevent hair loss and care for your hair and scalp during this time. Talk to your doctor about your concerns and follow the hair care tips mentioned in this post to help prevent hair loss during chemo.
