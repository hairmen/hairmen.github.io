Best Hairstyles For Big Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Oct 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the best hairstyles for a big round face? If yes, you have come to the right place. A round face can be pretty challenging to style as it lacks angles and definition. However, with the right haircut and styling, you can make your big round face look slimmer and more defined. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for big round faces, how to style them, tips, and hair care. Let's dive in!

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips

Description

A big round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a round chin. The key to styling a big round face is to add height and volume at the crown and create the illusion of angles and definition. The best hairstyles for big round faces are those that have layers, angles, and volume. Long hairstyles with layers, angled bobs, and pixie cuts are the most flattering for big round faces.

How to

The best way to style a big round face is to add height and volume at the crown and create angles and definition. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  2. Create layers at the crown to add height and volume.
  3. Style your hair with a side part to create angles and definition.
  4. Use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls to add texture and definition.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair for a big round face:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair upside down using a round brush.
  4. Create layers at the crown using a razor or scissors.
  5. Style your hair with a side part and tuck one side behind your ear.
  6. Use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls.
  7. Spray your hair with a texturizing spray for added volume and texture.
  8. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair for a big round face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts as they can make your face look rounder.
  • Avoid center parts as they can make your face look wider.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume at the roots.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add height and volume.
  • Create layers at the crown to add height and volume.
  • Use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls to add texture and definition.
  • Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyles for big round faces:

Q. What are the best hairstyles for a big round face?

A. The best hairstyles for a big round face are those that have layers, angles, and volume. Long hairstyles with layers, angled bobs, and pixie cuts are the most flattering for big round faces.

Q. What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a big round face?

A. You should avoid blunt cuts, center parts, and hairstyles that add width to your face. Instead, opt for hairstyles that have layers, angles, and volume.

Q. How can I add height and volume to my hair?

A. You can add height and volume to your hair by blow-drying it upside down, creating layers at the crown, and using a volumizing mousse or spray.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when styling your hair for a big round face:

  • Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as it can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or flat iron.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its health.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily style your hair to flatter your big round face. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that suits you best. With the right haircut and styling, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident and beautiful.
