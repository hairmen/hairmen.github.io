How To Style Shoulder Length Hair With Bangs

Written by Luffy Jun 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have shoulder length hair with bangs, you might be wondering how to style it in a way that's cute and trendy. Fortunately, there are many different ways to style this type of hair, whether you want to wear it up, down, or somewhere in between. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for styling shoulder length hair with bangs, including how to create a cute updo, how to add volume to your hair, and more.

Description

Shoulder length hair with bangs is a popular hairstyle for women of all ages. It's versatile, easy to maintain, and allows for a range of styling options. Many women love this hairstyle because it's not too short, but not too long either. It's also a great way to showcase your facial features, especially if you have a cute set of bangs.

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair with Bangs

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

The first step to styling shoulder length hair with bangs is to start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet or damp, it will be difficult to style and may not hold the shape you want. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Step 2: Add Volume

If you have fine or thin hair, you may want to add some volume to your hair before styling it. There are many different ways to do this, including using a volumizing mousse or spray, blow drying your hair upside down, or using a round brush to create volume at the roots.

Step 3: Decide on Your Style

Once your hair is clean and dry, it's time to decide on your style. Do you want to wear your hair up, down, or somewhere in between? There are many different styles to choose from, including a messy bun, a sleek ponytail, or loose waves.

Step 4: Create Your Style

Once you've decided on your style, it's time to create it. Use a curling iron, straightener, or other styling tool to create the look you want. If you're wearing your hair up, be sure to secure it with bobby pins or hair ties.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling shoulder length hair with bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage when using hot styling tools.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Add accessories like headbands or hair clips to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

FAQ

Q: What's the best way to add volume to shoulder length hair with bangs?

A: There are many different ways to add volume to your hair, including using a volumizing mousse or spray, blow drying your hair upside down, or using a round brush to create volume at the roots.

Q: Can I wear my hair up if I have shoulder length hair with bangs?

A: Yes, there are many different updo styles that work well with shoulder length hair with bangs, including a messy bun, a sleek ponytail, or a braided updo.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length hair with bangs looking healthy and shiny, be sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair. Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking its best.

Styling shoulder length hair with bangs doesn't have to be difficult. With a little practice and some styling tools, you can create a cute and trendy look that's perfect for any occasion.
