How To Stop Your Hair From Falling Out After Covid 19

Written by Bardi Jul 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

COVID-19 is a disease that has affected millions around the world. While it is known to primarily affect the respiratory system, it has also been known to cause hair loss in some patients. Hair loss can be a traumatic experience for many people, especially those who have already been struggling with their mental health during the pandemic. If you are one of those people, here's how you can stop your hair from falling out after COVID-19.

Causes of Hair Loss After COVID-19

There are several causes of hair loss after COVID-19. One of the main causes is stress. The pandemic has been a stressful time for everyone, and stress can cause hair loss. Another cause is the virus itself. COVID-19 is known to cause inflammation in the body, which can affect the hair follicles and cause hair loss.

Additionally, many COVID-19 patients have been hospitalized and have undergone treatment such as chemotherapy, which can also cause hair loss.

Tips for Preventing Hair Loss After COVID-19

If you are experiencing hair loss after COVID-19, there are several things you can do to prevent further hair loss:

  • Reduce stress: Try to reduce your stress levels by practicing relaxation techniques such as meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises.
  • Eat a healthy diet: A well-balanced diet can help keep your hair healthy. Make sure you are getting enough protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals: Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair such as hair dyes and relaxers.
  • Be gentle with your hair: Be gentle when washing and brushing your hair. Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.
  • Stay hydrated: Drinking enough water can help keep your hair healthy.
  • Consult a doctor: If you are experiencing severe hair loss, consult a doctor. They may be able to prescribe medication or suggest other treatments.

FAQs About Hair Loss After COVID-19

Q: How long does hair loss after COVID-19 last?

A: Hair loss after COVID-19 can last for several months. It may take some time for your hair to grow back.

Q: Will my hair grow back after COVID-19?

A: In most cases, hair will grow back after COVID-19. However, it may take several months for your hair to fully grow back.

Q: Can I prevent hair loss after COVID-19?

A: While you cannot completely prevent hair loss after COVID-19, you can take steps to reduce your risk. Follow the tips listed above.

Hair Care After COVID-19

After recovering from COVID-19, it is important to take care of your hair to prevent further hair loss. Here are some tips:

  • Use a mild shampoo: Use a mild shampoo to avoid further irritation to your scalp.
  • Avoid heat styling: Avoid using heat styling tools such as hair dryers and straighteners as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles: Avoid tight hairstyles such as braids, ponytails, and buns as they can pull on your hair and cause damage.
  • Trim regularly: Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb: Use a wide-tooth comb to avoid pulling on your hair.

By following these tips, you can stop your hair from falling out after COVID-19 and promote healthy hair growth.
