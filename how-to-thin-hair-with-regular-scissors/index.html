How To Thin Hair With Regular Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Thinning hair with regular scissors can be a cost-effective way to achieve a more manageable and lighter hairstyle. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on how to thin hair with regular scissors. From the equipment you need to the step-by-step process, you will find everything you need to know to achieve the desired result.

What You Will Need

Before you start, you will need to gather the following equipment:

  • Regular scissors
  • Comb
  • Claw clips
  • Water spray bottle

How to Thin Hair with Regular Scissors

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Start by wetting your hair with a water spray bottle. This will make it easier to work with and prevent split ends.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into four sections: left, right, back, and top. Use claw clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Start Thinning Your Hair

Take a small section of your hair and comb it straight. Hold the section between your fingers and start cutting into your hair with the regular scissors. Cut small sections at a time and avoid cutting too much hair at once.

Make sure to cut at an angle instead of cutting straight across. This will create a more natural look and avoid harsh lines.

Step 4: Repeat the Process

Repeat the process on each section of your hair until you achieve the desired result.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Start with small sections of hair
  • Use the comb to keep the hair straight and avoid any uneven cuts
  • Work slowly and take your time
  • Don't cut too much hair at once
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about thinning hair with regular scissors:

Can I use any scissors to thin my hair?

It's best to use hairdressing scissors, but regular scissors will work as long as they are sharp.

Can I thin my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can. Just make sure to cut your hair when it's dry instead of wet.

How often should I thin my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. As a general rule, it's recommended to get a haircut every six to eight weeks.

Hair Care

Once you have thinned your hair, it's essential to take care of it to maintain the results. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

With these tips, you can maintain the results of your thinned hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

Thinning hair with regular scissors can be a simple and cost-effective way to achieve a lighter and more manageable hairstyle. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the desired result and maintain healthy hair.
