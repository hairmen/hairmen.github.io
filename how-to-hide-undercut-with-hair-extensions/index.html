How To Hide Undercut With Hair Extensions

Written by Christine Jun 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you have an undercut that you want to hide? Hair extensions can be a great solution for those who want to cover up their undercut without having to grow it out. In this article, we will show you how to hide your undercut with hair extensions, step by step. We will also provide you with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve the best results. Let's get started!

How to Hide Undercut with Hair Extensions

Hair extensions are a great way to add length and volume to your hair. They also come in different colors and textures to match your natural hair. Here's how you can use hair extensions to hide your undercut:

Choose the Right Hair Extensions

The first step is to choose the right type of hair extensions. Clip-in hair extensions are the easiest to use and can be removed easily. We recommend using human hair extensions as they blend well with your natural hair and can be styled easily.

Choose the Right Color

Choosing the right color is important to make sure that your hair extensions blend well with your natural hair. You can either choose the same color as your hair or a slightly lighter shade to add dimension to your hair.

Section Your Hair

Section your hair horizontally from the nape of your neck. Clip the top section of your hair away from the bottom section. This will make it easier for you to apply the hair extensions.

Apply the Hair Extensions

Start by applying the hair extensions at the back of your head. Clip the hair extension close to the roots of your hair. Make sure that the hair extension is secure and does not slip off.

Continue to apply the hair extensions in small sections until you reach the top section of your hair. Make sure that the hair extensions are evenly distributed and blend well with your natural hair.

Blend Your Hair

Once you have applied all the hair extensions, gently comb your hair to blend the hair extensions with your natural hair. You can also use a straightener to blend the hair extensions with your natural hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to hide your undercut with hair extensions:

  1. Start by choosing the right type of hair extensions. We recommend using clip-in human hair extensions.
  2. Choose the right color that matches your natural hair.
  3. Section your hair horizontally from the nape of your neck.
  4. Clip the top section of your hair away from the bottom section.
  5. Apply the hair extensions at the back of your head.
  6. Continue to apply the hair extensions in small sections until you reach the top section of your hair.
  7. Blend the hair extensions with your natural hair using a comb or straightener.

Tips for Hiding Undercut with Hair Extensions

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when hiding your undercut with hair extensions:

  • Choose the right type of hair extensions that match your natural hair.
  • Choose the right color to match your natural hair.
  • Apply the hair extensions in small sections to ensure even distribution.
  • Gently comb your hair to blend the hair extensions with your natural hair.
  • Use a straightener to help blend the hair extensions with your natural hair.

FAQs

Can I use synthetic hair extensions to hide my undercut?

We recommend using human hair extensions as they blend well with your natural hair and can be styled easily. Synthetic hair extensions can look unnatural and may not blend well with your natural hair.

How long can I wear my hair extensions?

You can wear your hair extensions for up to 8 hours a day. It's important to remove them before going to bed to avoid damage to your natural hair.

Can I wash my hair extensions?

Yes, you can wash your hair extensions. We recommend using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep them looking healthy and shiny.

Hair Care for Undercut and Hair Extensions

It's important to take care of your hair and hair extensions to ensure that they last long and look healthy. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair and hair extensions:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair and hair extensions looking healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair extensions as they can damage them.
  • Remove your hair extensions before going to bed to avoid damage to your natural hair.
  • Brush your hair extensions gently to avoid pulling and tugging.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products on your hair extensions as they can weigh them down.

With these tips, you can easily hide your undercut with hair extensions and achieve a natural-looking hairstyle. Remember to choose the right type and color of hair extensions, apply them correctly, and take care of your hair and hair extensions to keep them looking healthy and shiny.
