How To Do Makeup For Diamond Shaped Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Aug 16, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a diamond shaped face, you might be wondering how to make the most of your unique features with makeup. With a wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin, diamond shaped faces can be both striking and challenging to style. In this article, we’ll take a look at some tips and tricks for doing makeup that flatters your diamond shaped face.

Step 1: Start with a Clean Face

Before you begin applying makeup, it’s important to start with a clean and moisturized face. Use a gentle cleanser and apply a light moisturizer to ensure that your skin is smooth and hydrated. This will help your makeup to go on smoothly and last longer.

Step 2: Apply Foundation

Apply a foundation that matches your skin tone to even out your complexion. Be sure to blend it well, especially around your jawline and hairline. You can use a sponge or a brush to apply foundation, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Contour Your Cheekbones

Use a contouring powder or cream to define your cheekbones. Apply it just below your cheekbones, blending it upwards towards your temples. This will help to accentuate your high cheekbones and add definition to your face.

Step 4: Highlight Your Brow Bone

Use a highlighter or a light eyeshadow to highlight your brow bone. Apply it just under your eyebrow, blending it down towards your eyelid. This will help to draw attention to your eyes and balance out your face shape.

Step 5: Shape Your Brows

Use an eyebrow pencil or powder to shape your brows. Diamond shaped faces look best with brows that are slightly arched and angled towards the temples. Be sure to blend the color well and use a brow gel to keep your brows in place.

Step 6: Define Your Eyes

Use eyeliner and mascara to define your eyes. Diamond shaped faces look best with eyeliner that is applied slightly thicker towards the outer corner of the eye. You can also use eyeshadow to add some depth and color to your eyelids.

Step 7: Add Some Color to Your Lips

Finish off your look with a pop of color on your lips. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone and apply it with a lip brush for precision. You can also add a bit of gloss to make your lips look fuller and more hydrated.

Tips for Makeup on Diamond Shaped Faces

  • Keep your makeup simple and natural, focusing on one or two features at a time.
  • Avoid heavy or dark makeup that can make your face look smaller.
  • Use a matte finish for your foundation and powder, as a shiny finish can make your face look wider.
  • Experiment with different eyeliner styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use a bronzer to warm up your skin tone and add a bit of color to your face.

FAQs

What kind of blush should I use on a diamond shaped face?

Use a blush that is applied just below your cheekbones, blending upwards towards your temples. Stick to peach or pink shades, which complement most skin tones.

Can I wear bold lipstick on a diamond shaped face?

Absolutely! Bold lipstick can be a great way to add some color and personality to your look. Just be sure to keep the rest of your makeup simple and natural.

What kind of eyeshadow should I use on a diamond shaped face?

Experiment with different shades and textures to find what works best for you. Diamond shaped faces look great with both light and dark colors, so feel free to play around with different combinations.

Hair Care for Diamond Shaped Faces

When it comes to hair care, diamond shaped faces look best with hairstyles that add volume and width to the chin area. This can be accomplished with layers, curls, or waves. Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as they can make your face look longer and narrower.

With these tips and tricks, you can create a makeup look that flatters your diamond shaped face and highlights your unique features. Remember to experiment and have fun with different colors and styles to find what works best for you!
