How To Install A Frontal Wig With Glue

Written by Frank Nov 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you ready to switch up your look with a frontal wig? Installing a wig with glue may seem intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a simple process. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to install a frontal wig with glue, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Install a Frontal Wig with Glue
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Installing a Frontal Wig with Glue
  4. FAQs about Frontal Wig Installation with Glue
  5. Hair Care for Your Frontal Wig

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before installing your frontal wig with glue, prepare your hair by braiding it or putting it into a bun. Make sure your hair is flat to your scalp so that the wig lays smoothly. If you have long hair, consider using a wig cap to help keep your hair in place.

Step 2: Cut the Lace

Take your frontal wig and cut the lace along the hairline using scissors. Be sure to cut as close to the hairline as possible without cutting the hairs.

Step 3: Apply the Glue

Apply a thin layer of wig glue to your hairline using a brush or applicator. Be careful not to apply too much glue, as it can make the wig look bulky or unnatural. Wait a few seconds for the glue to become tacky.

Step 4: Place the Wig

Starting from the middle of your forehead, place the wig onto your head and press down firmly. Use a comb or your fingers to smooth out any bumps or lumps in the wig. If necessary, adjust the wig so that it sits comfortably on your head.

Step 5: Secure the Wig

Using a rat tail comb or your fingers, press the wig firmly onto your scalp, starting from the front and working your way back. Make sure the wig is securely in place and that there are no gaps between the wig and your scalp.

Step 6: Style Your Wig

Once the wig is secure, you can style it as desired. Use a flat iron or curling iron to create the look you want. Be careful not to apply too much heat, as it can damage the wig.

Tips for Installing a Frontal Wig with Glue

  • Use a wig cap to help keep your hair in place.
  • Apply the glue in thin layers to avoid a bulky look.
  • Wait for the glue to become tacky before placing the wig on your head.
  • Smooth out any bumps or lumps in the wig before securing it onto your scalp.
  • Be gentle when styling your wig to avoid damage.

FAQs about Frontal Wig Installation with Glue

Q: How long does the glue last?

A: The glue can last anywhere from a few days to a few weeks, depending on how well it is applied and how often you wash your hair. Be sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner when washing your wig to help prolong its life.

Q: Can I swim or shower with a frontal wig installed with glue?

A: It is not recommended to swim or shower with a wig installed with glue, as it can cause the glue to loosen and the wig to shift or fall off. If you need to wash your wig, remove it first and then wash it separately.

Hair Care for Your Frontal Wig

To keep your frontal wig looking its best, be sure to take good care of it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to wash it, and avoid using hot styling tools too often. Store it in a cool, dry place when not in use, and avoid exposing it to direct sunlight or heat. With proper care, your wig can last for many wears.

Now that you know how to install a frontal wig with glue, you can switch up your look whenever you want. Follow these simple steps and tips for a seamless and natural-looking wig installation.
