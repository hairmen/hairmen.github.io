How to Get Great Waves in Your Hair - Step by Step Guide

Have you ever wanted to achieve those perfect beachy waves, but didn’t know where to start? Look no further! In this step-by-step guide, we will show you how to get great waves in your hair. From using the right products to perfecting your technique, we’ve got you covered.

How to Get Great Waves in Your Hair

First, you’ll want to start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat styling tools.

Next, section your hair into manageable pieces. For looser waves, use larger sections. For tighter waves, use smaller sections. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Now it’s time to use your chosen heat styling tool. You can use a curling iron, wand, or straightener. For beachy waves, we recommend using a curling wand with a larger barrel size. Take each section of hair and wrap it around the wand, holding for 10-15 seconds before releasing. Repeat this process for all sections of hair.

Once you’ve finished curling all of your hair, it’s time to loosen up those waves. Run your fingers through your hair to break up the curls and create a more natural wave. For added texture, use a sea salt spray or texturizing spray.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Clean and Dry Hair

Make sure your hair is clean and dry before styling. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

Step 2: Section Hair

Section your hair into manageable pieces using hair clips. Use larger sections for looser waves and smaller sections for tighter waves.

Step 3: Heat Styling Tool

Use a curling iron, wand, or straightener to create your waves. For beachy waves, we recommend using a curling wand with a larger barrel size.

Step 4: Curl Hair

Take each section of hair and wrap it around the wand, holding for 10-15 seconds before releasing. Repeat this process for all sections of hair.

Step 5: Loosen Waves

Run your fingers through your hair to break up the curls and create a more natural wave. Use a sea salt spray or texturizing spray for added texture.

Tips for Perfect Waves

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  • Section your hair into manageable pieces for easier styling.
  • Use a curling wand with a larger barrel size for beachy waves.
  • Loosen up your waves with your fingers for a more natural look.
  • Use a sea salt spray or texturizing spray for added texture.

FAQs

How long will my waves last?

Your waves can last anywhere from 1-3 days depending on your hair type and how well you care for them.

Can I use a straightener to create waves?

Yes, you can use a straightener to create waves. Simply twist your hair as you run the straightener down the length of your hair.

How do I care for my waves?

Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your waves looking healthy. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Hair Care for Waves

Keeping your hair healthy is key to achieving great waves. Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Now that you know how to get great waves in your hair, it’s time to give it a try. Remember to use a heat protectant spray and take your time sectioning your hair for the best results. With a little practice, you’ll be able to achieve those perfect beachy waves in no time!
