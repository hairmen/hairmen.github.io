How To Cut Men s Hair To Go Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a classic and timeless hairstyle, the slicked-back look is a great option. It's versatile, easy to maintain, and suits almost any face shape. But achieving this look requires a specific haircut and styling technique. In this article, we'll show you how to cut men's hair to go back in relaxed English language, with detailed instructions and tips on hair care.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Cut Men's Hair to Go Back

Description

The slicked-back hairstyle has been around for decades and continues to be a popular choice for men of all ages. The style involves combing the hair back and away from the face, creating a sleek and polished look. To achieve this look, you'll need to cut the hair to a specific length and style it with pomade or gel. Cutting men's hair to go back requires a bit of skill and knowledge, but with the right tools and techniques, anyone can do it.

How to Cut Men's Hair to Go Back

To cut men's hair to go back, you'll need a few essential tools, including:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape or towel
  • Hair product (pomade or gel)

Step-by-Step Guide:

Follow these steps to cut men's hair to go back:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Wet hair can make it harder to achieve the desired length and shape.
  2. Section the hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Use clips to hold each section in place.
  3. Begin with the back section. Use clippers to trim the hair to the desired length, usually around 1/2 inch to 1 inch. Use the longest guard for the first pass, then switch to a shorter guard to clean up the edges.
  4. Move on to the sides. Use the same technique as the back, but taper the hair slightly shorter towards the bottom. Use scissors to blend the top and sides together for a seamless look.
  5. Finally, move to the top section. Use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length, usually around 2 to 3 inches. Cut in a straight line from front to back, then use thinning shears to remove bulk and add texture.
  6. Once the hair is cut, style it with pomade or gel. Start with a small amount and work it through the hair, starting at the roots and combing it back. Use a comb to create a smooth, even finish.
  7. Experiment with different styles and techniques to find the perfect slicked-back look for you. You can try a side part or a quiff, or add volume to the top with a blow dryer.

Tips for a Perfect Slicked-Back Hairstyle:

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle:

  • Choose the right product for your hair type. Pomade is best for thick or curly hair, while gel works better for fine or straight hair.
  • Don't use too much product. Start with a small amount, and add more as needed.
  • Comb your hair back while it's still wet to create a sleeker look.
  • Experiment with different combing techniques to find the best style for you.
  • Don't forget to trim and shape your sideburns and neckline for a clean finish.

FAQ:

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair to go back:

How often should I cut my hair for a slicked-back look?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. On average, you should get a haircut every 4 to 6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.

Can I achieve a slicked-back look with long hair?

Yes, but it will require more styling and product. You'll need to use a stronger hold product, like wax or clay, and comb the hair back tightly to create a sleek look.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go to a professional stylist to fix it, or try to blend the mistake with the rest of the hair using scissors or clippers.

Hair Care Tips for Slicked-Back Hair:

To keep your slicked-back hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or straighteners too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep the hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the length and shape.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle and keep your hair looking healthy and polished. Whether you're going for a classic look or a modern twist, the slicked-back hairstyle is a great choice for any man.
