Can You Train Hair To Slick Back

Written by Christine Aug 31, 2023 · 3 min read

Have you ever wondered if it’s possible to train your hair to slick back? Well, the answer is yes! With the right techniques and products, you can achieve a sleek and polished look that will last all day. In this article, we’ll guide you through how to train your hair to slick back, step by step.

Description

Slicked-back hair has been a popular hairstyle for decades, and it’s not going away anytime soon. It’s a versatile look that can be worn for both casual and formal occasions, and it’s easy to maintain once you’ve trained your hair to stay in place.

How To

Here’s how to train your hair to slick back:

Step 1: Start With Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup. This will help your hair to stay in place better.

Step 2: Use a Comb

Use a fine-toothed comb to comb your hair back. Start at the front and work your way to the back of your head. Make sure to comb your hair as straight back as possible.

Step 3: Apply Product

Apply a styling product to your hair while it’s still damp. A pomade or gel works best for slicked-back hair. Start at the roots and work your way to the ends.

Step 4: Blow Dry

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while combing it back. This will help to set the product and keep your hair in place.

Step 5: Train Your Hair

Repeat this process every day until your hair starts to stay in place on its own. Over time, your hair will become trained to stay slicked back.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you train your hair to slick back:

  • Use a strong hold styling product
  • Comb your hair back every day, even on days when you don’t plan to slick it back
  • Avoid touching your hair throughout the day to keep it in place

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about training hair to slick back:

1. How long does it take to train hair to slick back?

It can take anywhere from a few days to a few weeks to train your hair to stay slicked back. The key is to be consistent with your styling and use a strong hold product.

2. Can I slick back thin hair?

Yes, you can slick back thin hair, but you may need to use additional styling products to achieve the look you want. A volumizing mousse can help to add body to thin hair.

Hair Care

When training your hair to slick back, it’s important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day
  • Limit your use of styling products to prevent product buildup
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

Conclusion

Training your hair to slick back is a process that requires patience and consistency. With the right techniques and products, you can achieve a sleek and polished look that will last all day. Remember to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain healthy hair.
