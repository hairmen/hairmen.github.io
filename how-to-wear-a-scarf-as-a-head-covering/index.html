How To Wear A Scarf As A Head Covering Easy And Chic

Written by Oliver Jun 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Scarves are not only for keeping you warm during winter or adding a pop of color to your outfit. They can also be used as a stylish head covering, which is perfect for bad hair days, protecting your hair from the sun, or simply adding a touch of elegance to your look. In this post, we will show you how to wear a scarf as a head covering and provide you with some tips and tricks to make it look effortless.

How to Wear a Scarf as a Head Covering

Wearing a scarf as a head covering may seem intimidating at first, but it's actually quite easy. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Choose the Right Scarf

The first step is to choose the right scarf. You want a scarf that is long enough to wrap around your head and tie at the nape of your neck. A lightweight, silk scarf is perfect for warmer weather, while a thicker, woolen scarf is great for colder days.

Step 2: Fold the Scarf

Fold the scarf in half, so it forms a triangle.

Step 3: Place the Scarf on Your Head

Place the scarf on your head, with the longest edge of the triangle at the front.

Step 4: Tie the Scarf

Take the two ends of the scarf and tie them at the nape of your neck. You can tie the scarf in a knot, a bow, or a simple loop.

Tips for Wearing a Scarf as a Head Covering

Here are some tips to make your scarf head covering look effortless:

Tip 1: Choose the Right Style

There are many different ways to wear a scarf as a head covering. Try out different styles to see which one suits you best.

Tip 2: Coordinate with Your Outfit

Coordinate the color of your scarf with your outfit. A printed scarf can add a pop of color to a plain outfit, while a solid-colored scarf can tone down a busy outfit.

Tip 3: Use Hairpins

Use hairpins to secure the scarf in place. This will prevent the scarf from slipping off your head.

FAQ

Can I wear a scarf as a head covering in the summer?

Yes, you can wear a scarf as a head covering in the summer. Choose a lightweight, breathable fabric like cotton or silk to keep you cool.

Can I wear a scarf as a head covering if I have short hair?

Yes, you can wear a scarf as a head covering even if you have short hair. Simply tie the scarf at the nape of your neck, leaving your hair visible.

Hair Care

Wearing a scarf as a head covering can help protect your hair from damage caused by the sun or harsh weather conditions. However, it's important to still take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Moisturize Your Hair

Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.

Tip 2: Cover Your Hair at Night

Use a silk scarf or bonnet to cover your hair at night. This will prevent your hair from rubbing against your pillowcase and causing damage.

Tip 3: Don't Wear the Scarf Too Tight

Make sure the scarf is not too tight around your head, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Now that you know how to wear a scarf as a head covering, it's time to try it out for yourself! Remember to choose the right scarf, coordinate with your outfit, and take care of your hair.
