What Product Should I Use To Slick Your Hair Back

Written by Christine Jul 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to rock a slicked-back hairstyle, you'll need the right product to achieve the look. In this article, we'll explore the best products for slicking back your hair, as well as tips for achieving the perfect slicked-back style. Read on to learn more!

Description

Slicked-back hair is a classic look that has been popular for decades. It's a versatile style that works well for both casual and formal occasions. The key to achieving the perfect slicked-back look is using the right product. There are a variety of products on the market that can be used to slick back your hair, including pomades, gels, and waxes.

How to Slick Your Hair Back

Before you choose a product, it's important to know how to slick your hair back properly. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

For best results, start with clean, dry hair. This will help the product adhere better and prevent any greasiness or buildup.

Step 2: Apply the product

Apply your chosen product to your palms and rub them together to warm it up. Then, run your hands through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 3: Comb your hair back

Using a comb, comb your hair back in the direction you want it to stay. For a more polished look, you can use a fine-toothed comb.

Step 4: Style as desired

Once your hair is slicked back, you can style it as desired. You can leave it as is for a sleek look, or add some texture by using your fingers to separate and tousle your hair.

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back style:

  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed. You don't want to overdo it and end up with greasy, weighed-down hair.
  • Use a comb to distribute the product evenly and create a neat, polished look.
  • If you have long hair, consider using a hair tie or clip to keep it in place while you slick it back.

FAQ

What is the best product for slicking back hair?

The best product for slicking back hair depends on your hair type and texture. Pomades, gels, and waxes are all good options. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you.

How much product should I use?

Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed. You don't want to overdo it and end up with greasy, weighed-down hair.

Can I slick back long hair?

Yes, you can slick back long hair. Consider using a hair tie or clip to keep it in place while you slick it back.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by washing it regularly and using a conditioner to keep it healthy and hydrated. Avoid using too much product, as this can cause buildup and make your hair look greasy. If you have any concerns about hair loss or damage, consult with a hair care professional.

Conclusion

Now that you know what product to use to slick your hair back and how to achieve the perfect slicked-back style, you're ready to rock this classic look. Remember to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you, and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish.
