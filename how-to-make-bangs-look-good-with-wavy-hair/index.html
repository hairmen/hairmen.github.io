How To Make Bangs Look Good With Wavy Hair

Written by Petter Jul 25, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have wavy hair, you may have wondered whether bangs would suit your hair type. With the right styling techniques, bangs can look great with wavy hair. In this article, we’ll show you how to make bangs look good with wavy hair, including tips on how to style them and take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Bangs with Wavy Hair

How to Make Bangs Look Good with Wavy Hair

The key to making bangs look good with wavy hair is to choose the right type of bangs and to style them correctly. Here are some tips:

Choose the Right Type of Bangs

When choosing bangs for wavy hair, it’s important to consider the natural texture of your hair. Here are some types of bangs that work well with wavy hair:

  • Side-swept bangs: These are great for wavy hair as they can be easily blended in with the rest of your hair.
  • Curtain bangs: These are longer and more layered, making them ideal for wavy hair as they can be styled to frame the face.

Style Your Bangs Correctly

Once you’ve chosen the type of bangs that work best for your wavy hair, it’s important to style them correctly. Here are some tips:

  • Blow dry your bangs while they’re wet to smooth out any kinks or curls.
  • Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs and create a curved shape.
  • Apply a small amount of styling product to your bangs to help them hold their shape.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to make bangs look good with wavy hair:

  1. Choose the type of bangs that work best for your natural hair texture.
  2. Blow dry your bangs while they’re still wet.
  3. Use a round brush to create a curved shape.
  4. Apply a small amount of styling product to your bangs.
  5. Use a curling iron or straightener to style your hair.
  6. Apply a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Bangs with Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling bangs with wavy hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.
  • Blow dry your hair on a low heat setting to avoid frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair.
  • Don’t be afraid to trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh.

FAQs

Can I still wear bangs if my hair is very curly?

Absolutely! You may need to choose a different type of bangs, such as longer, more layered curtain bangs, but bangs can still look great with curly hair.

How often should I trim my bangs?

It’s a good idea to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair with Bangs

If you have wavy hair with bangs, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can make bangs look great with your wavy hair. Experiment with different styles and products to find the ones that work best for you, and don’t be afraid to try something new!
