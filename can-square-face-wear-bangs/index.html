Can Square Face Wear Bangs Tips And Tricks For A Flawless Hairstyle

Written by Bardi Jul 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having the same hairstyle for years? Do you want to change your look but don't know where to start? If you have a square face, you might be wondering if bangs are a good option for you. In this article, we will answer your question: can square face wear bangs? We will also provide you with tips on how to style your bangs and take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Wear Bangs with a Square Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Bangs
  • Tips for Perfect Bangs
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Bangs

Description

Square faces have a strong jawline and a broad forehead, which can make them look masculine. Bangs can soften the features and add a touch of femininity to the face. However, not all types of bangs are suitable for square faces. You need to choose the right length and style to flatter your face shape.

How to Wear Bangs with a Square Face

If you have a square face, you can wear bangs as long as you choose the right style. The best types of bangs for square faces are:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Long, wispy bangs
  • Curtain bangs

Avoid blunt bangs or straight-across bangs, as they can accentuate the squareness of your face. The length of your bangs should also be longer than your eyebrows to create a more balanced look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Bangs

Styling your bangs can be challenging, especially if you're new to bangs. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you style your bangs:

  1. Wash your hair and blow-dry it.
  2. Divide your bangs into small sections.
  3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs, starting from the roots and pulling them downwards.
  4. When your bangs are dry, use a flat iron to straighten them or a curling iron to add volume or waves.
  5. Apply hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Tips for Perfect Bangs

Here are some tips to help you achieve perfect bangs:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Avoid washing your bangs every day, as they can become greasy and lose their shape.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your bangs between washes.
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bangs for square faces:

  • Q: Can short bangs work for square faces?
    A: Short bangs can make your face look even squarer. It's best to choose longer, wispy bangs that frame your face.
  • Q: Can I wear bangs if I have a big forehead?
    A: Yes, bangs can help balance out your forehead and make your face look more proportional.
  • Q: Can I wear side-swept bangs if I have curly hair?
    A: Yes, side-swept bangs can work for curly hair. However, you might need to straighten them with a flat iron to keep them in place.

Hair Care for Bangs

Proper hair care is essential to keep your bangs looking their best. Here are some tips to take care of your bangs:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution by wearing a hat or using a hair serum with UV protection.

In conclusion, square faces can wear bangs as long as they choose the right style and length. Side-swept bangs, long, wispy bangs, and curtain bangs are the best options for square faces. With the right styling techniques and hair care, you can achieve a flawless hairstyle that flatters your face shape.
