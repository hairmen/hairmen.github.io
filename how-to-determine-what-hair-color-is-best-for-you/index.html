How To Determine What Hair Color Is Best For You

Written by Bardi Aug 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking about changing your hair color but not sure which one to choose? It's essential to pick a hair color that suits your skin tone, eye color, and personality. In this article, we will talk about how to determine what hair color is best for you. We'll go through the steps, tips, and frequently asked questions about hair color selection. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color and Natural Hair Color
  • Step 3: Think About Your Personality and Lifestyle
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right hair color is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral. Warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones, while cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones. Neutral skin tones have a balance between warm and cool undertones.

To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If you can't tell, you may have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color and Natural Hair Color

Once you have determined your skin tone, you should consider your eye color and natural hair color. If you have warm undertones and green or hazel eyes, warm hair colors like golden blonde or copper would complement your features. If you have cool undertones and blue or gray eyes, cool hair colors like platinum blonde or ash brown would suit you.

If you have a warm skin tone and dark hair, you can go for warm highlights like caramel or honey. If you have a cool skin tone and dark hair, cool highlights like ash or platinum would work well. If you have a neutral skin tone, you have more flexibility in choosing hair colors.

Step 3: Think About Your Personality and Lifestyle

Your hair color should also reflect your personality and lifestyle. If you're outgoing and adventurous, you might want to try bold, bright colors like red or purple. If you prefer a more natural look, you might opt for subtle highlights or lowlights.

You should also consider your lifestyle when choosing a hair color. If you have a busy schedule and don't have much time for upkeep, you might want to choose a low-maintenance hair color. If you're willing to spend time and money on upkeep, you can choose a high-maintenance hair color.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Don't go too far from your natural hair color. It will be easier to maintain and won't require as much upkeep.
  • Consider your skin's undertones and eye color when choosing a hair color.
  • Think about your personality and lifestyle when choosing a hair color.
  • Consult with a hairstylist if you're unsure about which hair color to choose.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair if you have sensitive skin, but you should choose a hair dye that is gentle and doesn't contain harsh chemicals. You can also do a patch test before dyeing your hair to make sure you don't have an allergic reaction.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair color?

A: It depends on the type of hair color you choose and how fast your hair grows. Generally, permanent hair color needs to be touched up every 6-8 weeks, while semi-permanent hair color can last up to 4 weeks.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but it's important to follow the instructions carefully and choose a hair dye that is appropriate for your hair type and color. If you're unsure, consult with a hairstylist.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

After dyeing your hair, it's essential to take care of it properly to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for colored hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent color fading.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine if you're swimming.

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, but by following these steps and tips, you can find a hair color that suits your skin tone, eye color, personality, and lifestyle. Don't be afraid to consult with a hairstylist if you're unsure about which hair color to choose. Remember to take care of your colored hair properly to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy.
