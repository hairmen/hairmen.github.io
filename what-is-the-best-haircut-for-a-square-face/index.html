What Is The Best Haircut For A Square Face

Written by Robby Oct 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a square face shape, you may be wondering what the best haircut is for you. A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, broad forehead, and angular features. The right haircut can help soften these features and balance out your face shape.

Description

Before we dive into the best haircuts for square faces, it's important to understand what a square face shape is. As mentioned above, a square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, broad forehead, and angular features. The goal of a haircut for a square face is to soften these features and create balance.

When choosing a haircut for a square face, you want to avoid styles that emphasize your strong jawline, such as bobs that end at the jaw or blunt bangs. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add softness and create movement.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for a Square Face

When choosing a haircut for a square face, there are a few key factors to consider:

  • Length: A longer length can help elongate your face and soften your features. Avoid hairstyles that end at your jawline.
  • Layers: Layers can add movement and softness to your hair. Avoid blunt cuts that create a boxy shape.
  • Side-swept bangs: Side-swept bangs can help soften a broad forehead and balance out your face shape. Avoid blunt bangs that accentuate your angular features.
  • Volume: Adding volume to the crown of your head can help balance out your jawline. Avoid styles that are flat or sleek.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut

Now that you know what to look for in a haircut for a square face, follow these steps to get the perfect cut:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine your face shape and discuss your desired look.
  2. Choose a length that elongates your face and adds movement.
  3. Add layers to soften your features and create texture.
  4. Consider side-swept bangs to balance out your face shape.
  5. Add volume to the crown of your head to create balance.
  6. Use styling products to maintain your desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Square Face Haircut

To maintain your square face haircut, follow these tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and movement.
  • Avoid flat ironing your hair, as this can make your face appear even more angular.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add movement and create a soft, tousled look.
  • Visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks for regular trims to maintain your desired length and shape.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear a bob with a square face?

A: Yes, but you'll want to choose a longer bob that falls below your jawline and add layers to create movement and softness.

Q: Should I avoid blunt cuts with a square face?

A: Yes, blunt cuts can create a boxy shape and emphasize your angular features. Opt for layers instead.

Hair Care for Square Faces

To keep your hair looking its best with a square face shape, make sure to use high-quality hair products that add volume and texture. Avoid flat ironing your hair, as this can make your face appear even more angular. Regular trims every 6-8 weeks will help maintain your desired length and shape.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut for your square face shape, you can enhance your natural beauty and create balance in your overall look.
