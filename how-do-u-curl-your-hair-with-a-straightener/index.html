How To Curl Your Hair With A Straightener In 2023

Written by William Jun 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to switch up your hairstyle, curling your hair with a straightener is a great option. Not only is it efficient, but it also creates soft and natural-looking curls. Keep reading to learn how to curl your hair with a straightener in 2023.

Description

Curling your hair with a straightener is a quick and easy way to achieve bouncy, voluminous curls. It's a popular technique that's easy to master with a little bit of practice.

How to Curl Your Hair with a Straightener

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to curl your hair with a straightener:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling your hair, it's important to prep it properly. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Then, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage. Blow-dry your hair until it's completely dry.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips or ties. Start with the bottom layer, and work your way up. The smaller the section, the tighter the curl will be.

Step 3: Heat Your Straightener

Once your hair is sectioned, heat up the straightener. Use a high heat setting for thick hair, and a lower heat setting for fine or damaged hair. Wait for the straightener to heat up completely before you start curling your hair.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Take a section of hair and clamp the straightener at the root. Twist the straightener away from your face, and pull it down to the end of the hair. Hold it for a few seconds, and release. Repeat this process until all sections are curled.

Step 5: Finish Your Look

Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently loosen the curls. Apply a hairspray to hold the curls in place, and you're done!

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curls:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Start with a lower heat setting and gradually increase it if necessary.
  • Don't clamp the straightener too tightly to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Twist the straightener away from your face for a natural-looking curl.
  • Use a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

FAQs

Can you curl your hair with a straightener?

Yes, you can curl your hair with a straightener. It's a quick and easy way to achieve bouncy, voluminous curls.

What is the best straightener for curling hair?

The best straightener for curling hair is one with rounded edges, such as the BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium-Plated Straightening Iron or the ghd Platinum+ Professional Performance Styler.

How long do curls last when you curl your hair with a straightener?

Curls can last up to two days when you curl your hair with a straightener, depending on your hair type and the products you use.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day, or every three days if you have dry hair.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Now that you know how to curl your hair with a straightener, it's time to try it out for yourself. With a little bit of practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect curls in no time!
