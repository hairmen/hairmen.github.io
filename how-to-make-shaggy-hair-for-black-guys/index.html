How To Make Shaggy Hair For Black Guys

Written by Eveline Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read
If you're a black guy looking to add some texture and dimension to your hair, a shaggy hairstyle might be just what you need. Shaggy hair is a popular style that can be achieved with different hair types and lengths. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of making shaggy hair for black guys step by step.

Description

Shaggy hair is a relaxed hairstyle that features uneven layers and choppy ends. This style is perfect for black guys who want to add volume and texture to their hair. Shaggy hair is versatile and can be worn in different ways, from messy to sleek. With the right products and techniques, you can achieve the perfect shaggy hairstyle for your hair type and face shape.

How to Make Shaggy Hair for Black Guys

To make shaggy hair for black guys, follow these steps:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup. This will help your hair to absorb the styling products better. Towel-dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 2: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer on a low heat setting, dry your hair. Use a round brush to add volume to your hair as you blow dry it. Once your hair is completely dry, use a flat iron to straighten it.

Step 3: Create Layers

Using a pair of hair cutting scissors, create layers in your hair. Start by sectioning your hair and cutting it into uneven layers. This will give your hair a shaggy look.

Step 4: Add Texture

To add texture to your hair, use a texturizing spray or wax. Apply the product to your hair and use your fingers to create a messy look. This will give your hair a shaggy and relaxed appearance.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you've added texture to your hair, style it as desired. You can sweep your hair to the side, create a messy look, or slick it back. Use a hair gel or pomade to hold your hair in place.

Tips

- Use a heat protectant before blow-drying or using a flat iron to prevent heat damage to your hair. - Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before blow-drying it. - Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type. - Use a hair spray to hold your hair in place and prevent frizz.

FAQ

What products should I use to make shaggy hair for black guys?

You can use a texturizing spray, wax, gel, or pomade to create a shaggy hairstyle for black guys. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too dry or greasy. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup and a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat damage.

Can I make shaggy hair with short hair?

Yes, you can make shaggy hair with short hair. Use a pair of hair cutting scissors to create uneven layers and add texture to your hair. Style your hair as desired using a texturizing spray or wax.

Hair Care

To maintain your shaggy hairstyle, follow these hair care tips: - Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming too dry. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair. - Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant before styling it.

With these tips and techniques, you can make shaggy hair for black guys that looks stylish and effortless. Experiment with different products and styles to find the one that suits your hair type and face shape. With a little bit of practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect shaggy hairstyle.
