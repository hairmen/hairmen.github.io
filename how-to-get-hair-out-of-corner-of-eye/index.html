How To Get Hair Out Of Corner Of Eye A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you've ever had a hair get stuck in the corner of your eye, you know how uncomfortable and irritating it can be. Fortunately, there are several easy ways to remove the hair and relieve your discomfort. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to get hair out of corner of eye, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and frequently asked questions.

How to Get Hair Out of Corner of Eye: Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow when you have a hair stuck in the corner of your eye:

Step 1: Wash Your Hands

Before touching your eye or the area around it, make sure your hands are clean to avoid any risk of infection.

Step 2: Rinse Your Eye with Water

Use lukewarm water to rinse your eye to help flush out the hair. Tilt your head to the side and let the water flow from the inner corner of your eye to the outer corner. Repeat this a few times until you feel some relief.

Step 3: Use a Cotton Swab

If the hair is still stuck in your eye, use a cotton swab to gently sweep it out. Be careful not to press too hard or scratch your eye. Use a clean cotton swab for each eye to avoid transferring any bacteria.

Step 4: Blink Your Eye

If the hair is still stuck, try blinking your eye rapidly. This may help dislodge the hair and allow it to come out.

Step 5: Use Eye Drops

If your eye is still uncomfortable, you can try using eye drops to lubricate your eye and help the hair slide out. Choose a gentle, saline-based eye drop to avoid any irritation.

Tips for Removing Hair from Your Eye

Here are some tips to help you remove hair from your eye:

  • Try not to rub your eye, as this can make the hair more difficult to remove and cause irritation.
  • Make sure your hair is tied back or clipped away from your face to prevent it from falling into your eyes.
  • If you wear contact lenses, remove them before trying to remove the hair.
  • If you have long hair, try wearing a headband or hairband to keep your hair out of your face.

FAQs About Removing Hair from Your Eye

Q: Can a hair in your eye cause an infection?

A: While a hair in your eye can be uncomfortable, it's unlikely to cause an infection unless you scratch your eye while trying to remove it. However, if your eye becomes red, swollen, or painful, see a doctor.

Q: Can I use tweezers to remove a hair from my eye?

A: No, you should never use tweezers to remove a hair from your eye. This can be dangerous and may cause injury to your eye or the surrounding area.

Q: How can I prevent hair from getting into my eyes?

A: To prevent hair from getting into your eyes, keep your hair tied back or clipped away from your face. You can also wear a headband or hairband to keep your hair in place.

Hair Care Tips to Prevent Hair from Falling into Your Eyes

Here are some hair care tips to help prevent hair from falling into your eyes:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage that can cause hair to fall into your eyes.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down and cause it to fall into your eyes.
  • Brush your hair regularly to remove any loose hairs that may fall into your eyes.
  • Consider wearing a hat or using a hair tie to keep your hair in place on windy days.

With these tips and tricks, you can quickly and easily remove hair from your eye and prevent it from happening again in the future. Remember to always wash your hands and be gentle when working around your eyes to avoid any injury or irritation.
