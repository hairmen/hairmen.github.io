How To Manage Short Hair In Lockdown Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 15, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

During the lockdown period, many people have been forced to manage their hair at home. For those with short hair, this can be a challenge. However, with the right techniques and tools, you can keep your hair looking great even during these difficult times. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to manage short hair in lockdown.

Table of Contents

  • How to Manage Short Hair in Lockdown
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Short Hair Care
  • FAQs

How to Manage Short Hair in Lockdown

Managing short hair during lockdown can be a bit challenging, especially if you're used to getting it done at the salon. However, there are many ways you can keep your hair looking neat and tidy at home.

One of the best ways to manage short hair is to keep it well-groomed. This means regularly trimming your hair to keep it at your desired length. You can use a pair of sharp scissors or clippers to do this. Be sure to trim your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can appear longer than it actually is.

Another way to manage short hair is to experiment with different hairstyles. You can try slicking your hair back with gel or pomade, or you can add some texture by using a salt spray. It's also a good idea to invest in some hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add some variety to your look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to manage short hair in lockdown:

  1. Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  2. Trim your hair every few weeks to keep it at your desired length.
  3. Experiment with different hairstyles to keep your look fresh and interesting.
  4. Invest in some hair accessories to add some variety to your look.
  5. Be patient with yourself and your hair. It may take some time to get used to managing your hair at home.

Tips for Short Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of short hair during lockdown:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Be patient with your hair. It may take some time to find the right products and techniques that work for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about managing short hair in lockdown:

How often should I trim my short hair?

You should trim your hair every few weeks to keep it at your desired length.

What products should I use on my short hair?

You should use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. You can also experiment with different styling products, such as gel, pomade, or salt spray, to achieve different hairstyles.

How can I add some variety to my short hair?

You can add some variety to your short hair by experimenting with different hairstyles and hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips.

What should I do if I make a mistake while trimming my hair?

If you make a mistake while trimming your hair, don't panic. You can always trim it a bit shorter to even it out, or you can wait for it to grow out.

Managing short hair in lockdown may seem daunting at first, but with the right techniques and tools, you can keep your hair looking great. Remember to be patient with yourself and your hair, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products.
