Which Hair Style Suits Me A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Aug 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. In this guide, we'll help you figure out which hair style suits you best. We'll cover everything from how to determine your face shape to different haircuts and styles that will flatter your features. Let's get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into different hairstyles, it's important to determine your face shape. Your face shape will play a big role in which hairstyles will suit you best. Here are the different face shapes and how to determine which one you have:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, your face is longer than it is wide. Your forehead is slightly wider than your jaw, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face. Lucky for you, almost any hairstyle will suit an oval face shape.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, your face is as wide as it is long. Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your jawline is rounded. To elongate your face, opt for hairstyles that add height to the top of your head.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar in width. Your jawline is more angular than rounded. To soften your features, choose hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, your forehead is wider than your jawline. Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face. To balance your features, choose hairstyles that add volume to the bottom half of your face.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, your forehead and jawline are narrower than your cheekbones. To balance your features, opt for hairstyles that add width to the forehead and chin.

Different Hairstyles for Different Face Shapes

Now that you know your face shape, let's take a look at different hairstyles that will suit you best.

Oval Face Shape Hairstyles

As we mentioned earlier, almost any hairstyle suits an oval face shape. However, if you want to highlight your features, try a long bob or a side-swept fringe.

Round Face Shape Hairstyles

For a round face shape, try a long layered haircut or a side-parted bob. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs, as they can make your face appear rounder.

Square Face Shape Hairstyles

For a square face shape, try a layered bob or a side-swept fringe. Soft curls and waves can also help to soften your features.

Heart Face Shape Hairstyles

For a heart face shape, try a chin-length bob or a layered haircut that falls just below your shoulders. Avoid heavy bangs, as they can make your forehead appear larger.

Diamond Face Shape Hairstyles

For a diamond face shape, try a chin-length bob or a layered haircut with side-swept bangs. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the cheekbones, as they can make your face appear wider.

Hair Care Tips

No matter what hairstyle you choose, taking care of your hair is important. Here are some hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and looking their best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as they can cause heat damage.
  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair health.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip your scalp of its natural oils.

FAQ

How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Can I pull off a pixie cut?

If you have an oval or heart face shape, you can pull off a pixie cut. If you have a round or square face shape, you may want to opt for a longer hairstyle.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have curly hair?

Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs if you have curly hair, as they can be difficult to style. Instead, opt for layered haircuts and side-swept bangs.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a fun and exciting process. By determining your face shape and choosing a hairstyle that suits your features, you can feel confident and stylish. Remember to take care of your hair and try out different hairstyles until you find the one that feels like you.
