How To Cut My Own Long Hair Youtube A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to cut your own long hair but don't know where to start, YouTube can be a great resource. With a plethora of tutorials available, you can easily find a video that suits your needs and learn how to cut your own hair like a pro. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own long hair using YouTube as your guide.

Description

Before you start cutting your own hair, it's important to understand the basics. Cutting your own hair can be tricky, especially when it comes to long hair. However, with the right tools and techniques, it can be done. In this guide, we'll walk you through the entire process, from choosing the right tools to cutting your hair at the right angle.

How to Cut My Own Long Hair YouTube: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting your own hair is to gather your tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair ties. It's also a good idea to have a handheld mirror on hand so you can see the back of your head.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to wash and dry it. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any tangles or knots. Once your hair is dry, comb it out and separate it into sections using hair ties.

Step 3: Watch YouTube Tutorials

Now it's time to turn to YouTube. Search for tutorials on how to cut your own long hair and watch a few videos to get an idea of the techniques that are commonly used. Pay attention to the angle at which the hair is cut and the sections that are being worked on.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Once you've watched a few tutorials and feel confident in your abilities, it's time to start cutting. Start at the bottom of your hair and work your way up, cutting small sections at a time. Be sure to cut at an angle to create layers and texture.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

As you cut your hair, be sure to check your progress in the mirror. This will help you ensure that you're cutting your hair evenly and that you're achieving the look you want.

Tips

- Start with small sections and work your way up
- Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
- Cut at an angle to create layers and texture
- Check your progress in the mirror often

FAQ

Q: Is it difficult to cut your own long hair?
A: Cutting your own long hair can be tricky, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done.

Q: What tools do I need to cut my own long hair?
A: You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair ties. It's also helpful to have a handheld mirror.

Q: How do I know if I'm cutting my hair evenly?
A: Check your progress often in the mirror to ensure that you're cutting your hair evenly.

Hair Care

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. This means using a good shampoo and conditioner, as well as regular trims to keep your hair healthy. If you're unsure about how to care for your hair, consult with a stylist or do some research online.

With this step-by-step guide, you'll be able to cut your own long hair like a pro using YouTube as your guide. Remember to take your time, start with small sections, and check your progress often. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve the look you want and save money on salon visits in the process.
