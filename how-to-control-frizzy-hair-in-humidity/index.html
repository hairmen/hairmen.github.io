How To Control Frizzy Hair In Humidity Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jun 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you live in an area with high humidity, you know how frustrating it can be to deal with frizzy hair. No matter how much you style it, the moisture in the air can cause your hair to puff up and become unmanageable. Luckily, there are several ways to control frizzy hair in humidity. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you keep your hair looking sleek and smooth, no matter the weather.

How to

Here are some steps you can take to control frizzy hair in humidity:

Step 1: Use a Smoothing Shampoo and Conditioner

Start your hair care routine with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that contain ingredients like argan oil, keratin, or coconut oil, which can help to smooth and hydrate your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help lock in moisture and prevent frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly through your hair.

Step 3: Use a Heat Protectant

If you plan to use heat styling tools, like a flat iron or curling iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray first. This will help to protect your hair from heat damage, which can lead to even more frizz.

Step 4: Style Your Hair with a Serum

To help keep your hair smooth and frizz-free, try using a smoothing serum or oil. Apply a small amount to your hair before styling, focusing on the ends and any areas that tend to get frizzy.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you control frizzy hair in humidity:

Tip 1: Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it more prone to frizz. Try to limit your hair washing to every other day, or even every few days if possible.

Tip 2: Use a Microfiber Towel

When drying your hair after washing, use a microfiber towel instead of a regular towel. Microfiber towels are gentler on your hair and can help to reduce frizz.

Tip 3: Avoid Touching Your Hair

The more you touch your hair, the more likely it is to become frizzy. Try to avoid touching your hair throughout the day, and if you need to adjust your style, use a styling product instead of your hands.

FAQ

Q: Can Humidity Cause Frizzy Hair?

A: Yes, humidity can cause frizzy hair. When there is moisture in the air, your hair can absorb it, causing it to swell and become frizzy.

Q: What Causes Frizzy Hair?

A: Frizzy hair can be caused by several factors, including genetics, damage from heat styling tools, and humidity.

Hair Care

Taking good care of your hair can help to reduce frizz and keep it looking healthy and shiny. Be sure to use products that are designed for your hair type, and avoid using too many styling products or heat styling tools. Additionally, try to eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair looking sleek and smooth, even in the most humid of climates.
