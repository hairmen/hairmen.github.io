How To Trim My Short Hair Myself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of visiting the salon every time you need to trim your short hair? Well, worry no more! With the right tools and techniques, you can learn how to trim your short hair yourself. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to trim your short hair at home with ease. Let's get started.

What You Will Need

Before we delve into the steps, let's first gather the necessary tools. You will need:

  • Sharp hair scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Mirror
  • Hair ties or clips

Once you have gathered these tools, you are ready to start trimming your short hair yourself.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to trim your short hair yourself:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help you to get a better view of your hair and ensure that it is clean and free from any products or oils that may interfere with your trimming process.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Using a comb, detangle your hair and ensure that it is smooth and free from knots. You can also use hair ties or clips to section your hair and make it easier to trim.

Step 3: Trim the Sides

Using your clippers, trim the sides of your hair. Start from the bottom and work your way up, ensuring that you trim evenly. Use a mirror to check that both sides are even.

Step 4: Trim the Back

Trim the back of your hair using the same technique as the sides. Use the clippers to trim the hair from the bottom and work your way up. Again, use a mirror to check that the back is even.

Step 5: Trim the Top

Using your scissors, trim the top of your hair. Start by sectioning your hair and trim the ends evenly. Take your time and ensure that you trim bit by bit until you achieve your desired length.

Step 6: Check for Any Unevenness

Check your hair for any unevenness and trim if necessary. Use a mirror to check all angles of your hair to ensure it is even.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Once you are satisfied with the length and evenness of your hair, style it to your desired look.

Tips

  • Always use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take your time and trim slowly to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use a mirror to check your hair from all angles.
  • Trim your hair when it is dry for better accuracy.

FAQ

Can I use kitchen scissors to trim my hair?

No, it is not advisable to use kitchen scissors as they are not sharp enough and may damage your hair.

How often should I trim my short hair?

You should trim your short hair every four to six weeks to maintain its shape and avoid split ends.

Do I need to use hair products after trimming my hair?

Using hair products after trimming your hair is optional. However, it can help to style your hair and keep it in place.

Hair Care

After trimming your short hair, it is essential to take care of it to maintain its health and shine. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair and avoid over-washing. You can also use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it moisturized.

In conclusion, learning how to trim your short hair yourself is easy and can save you time and money. Follow our step-by-step guide, and you will have a perfectly trimmed short hair in no time.
