Why Do Cats Shed Hair?

Cats shed hair as part of their natural grooming process. As they groom themselves, loose hair gets caught in their tongue and swallowed. This hair then passes through their digestive system and comes out in their feces. However, not all hair is ingested and some can shed throughout the day.

Additionally, cats shed more hair in the spring and fall as they adjust to changing temperatures and daylight hours. This is known as seasonal shedding and is completely normal.
