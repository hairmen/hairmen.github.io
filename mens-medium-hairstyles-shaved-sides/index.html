Mens Medium Hairstyles Shaved Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Sep 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh new hairstyle that is both stylish and low maintenance, then you should definitely consider the mens medium hairstyles shaved sides. This popular haircut has been gaining popularity in recent years, and it's not hard to see why. With its sleek and edgy look, it's perfect for any occasion - whether you're going out on a date or just hanging out with friends.

Description

The mens medium hairstyles shaved sides is a haircut that features short to medium-length hair on top, and shaved sides. The length of the hair on top can vary, but it's usually around 2 to 4 inches. The sides are shaved down to the skin, creating a sharp contrast between the two lengths. This haircut is also sometimes called the "undercut" or the "faded haircut."

How to

If you're interested in getting the mens medium hairstyles shaved sides, here's how you can do it:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step is to determine how long you want your hair to be on top. You can choose a length that suits your face shape and personal style. You can also consult with your barber to get their opinion on what length would look best on you.

Step 2: Shave the Sides

The next step is to shave the sides of your head. You can use clippers or a razor to do this. It's important to be careful when shaving to avoid any nicks or cuts. You can also ask your barber to do this step for you.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once the sides are shaved, you can style your hair on top however you like. You can use pomade, gel, or wax to give your hair some texture and hold. You can also experiment with different styles, such as a slicked-back look or a messy, textured look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your mens medium hairstyles shaved sides:

  • Visit your barber regularly to keep the sides shaved and the top trimmed
  • Use a quality hair styling product to keep your hair looking fresh and styled
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens medium hairstyles shaved sides:

Is this haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this haircut can work for all face shapes. However, you may want to consult with your barber to determine the best length and style for your particular face shape.

How often do I need to get this haircut?

The frequency of your haircuts will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain the sides. You may need to get a trim every 2-4 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh.

Can I still style my hair on top even if it's not that long?

Yes, you can still style your hair on top even if it's not that long. You can use a hair styling product to create texture and hold, even with shorter hair.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair with mens medium hairstyles shaved sides:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as these can damage your hair
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as these can also cause damage

With these tips and tricks, you can rock the mens medium hairstyles shaved sides with confidence and style. Whether you're looking for a new haircut for work or play, this edgy and versatile hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
