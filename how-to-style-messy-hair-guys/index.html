How To Style Messy Hair Guys

Written by William Sep 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our blog post on how to style messy hair guys. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the perfect messy hair look. We will also provide you with some tips and tricks to make sure your style lasts all day. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Messy Hair Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

How to Style Messy Hair Guys

Before we begin, it's important to note that messy hair is trendy and does not require perfection. In fact, imperfect hair is what makes this style unique. To achieve the perfect messy hair look, you'll need the right tools and products. Here's what you'll need:

  • Comb
  • Hairbrush
  • Hairdryer
  • Pomade or hair wax
  • Hairspray

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have all the necessary tools and products, let's get started with the step-by-step guide on how to style messy hair guys:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of pomade or hair wax to your fingertips and run it through your hair, focusing on the ends. This will create texture and definition in your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair on low heat using your fingers to create volume and texture. You can also use a hairbrush to create a more polished look.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a comb to create a deep side part or simply part your hair with your fingers for a more natural look.
  5. Use your hands to tousle your hair and create a messy, textured look. You can also use your fingers to create individual spikes or twists for added texture.
  6. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

That's it! You now have the perfect messy hair look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to make sure your messy hair style lasts all day:

  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to absorb excess oil and refresh your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to add accessories like hats or beanies to your messy hair look.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling to prevent breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and to keep your style looking fresh.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to style messy hair guys:

  • Can I achieve a messy hair look with short hair? Yes, you can achieve a messy hair look with short hair. Simply use a small amount of pomade or hair wax to create texture and tousle your hair with your hands.
  • How often should I wash my hair to achieve a messy hair look? It's recommended to wash your hair every other day to achieve a messy hair look. This will help maintain the natural oils in your hair and prevent it from becoming too oily or greasy.
  • Is messy hair appropriate for formal occasions? Messy hair can be appropriate for formal occasions if done correctly. Stick to a more polished messy hair look and avoid going too wild with your style.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair straighteners or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and to keep it looking healthy.

We hope you found this article on how to style messy hair guys helpful. Remember, messy hair is all about embracing imperfection and having fun with your style. Don't be afraid to experiment and try new things.
