How To Know If You Will Look Good With A Haircut

Written by Frank Jun 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you thinking about getting a new haircut but not sure if it will look good on you? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle with making the decision to change their hairstyle. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to know if you will look good with a haircut. Let's get started!

  2. How to
  3. Step by Step

Description

Changing your hairstyle can be a big decision, especially if you have had the same haircut for a long time. It's only natural to wonder if a new haircut will suit your face shape or if it will make you look older, younger, or unprofessional. Before you make an appointment with your hairdresser, there are some key factors you should consider to ensure you end up with a haircut that flatters your features and enhances your style.

How to

There are a few things you can do to determine if a particular haircut will look good on you or not. Let's take a look at some of them:

1. Look for Inspiration

Start by looking for inspiration from magazines, social media, or celebrity hairstyles. Look for people with a similar face shape, hair texture, and skin tone as you. This will give you an idea of what styles will work well on you.

2. Consider Your Face Shape

Another important factor to consider is your face shape. Different hairstyles suit different face shapes. For example, if you have a round face, a long bob or layered cut can help elongate your features. If you have a square face, a wavy or curly style can soften your jawline.

3. Think About Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important consideration. If you have fine hair, a layered cut can add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut can help control bulkiness. If you have curly hair, a layered cut can help define your curls.

4. Consult with Your Hairdresser

Your hairdresser is an expert in hair and can help guide you on what styles will work best for you. They can also advise you on the latest trends and techniques that will suit your hair texture and face shape.

Step by Step

Here are the steps you can follow to determine if a new haircut will look good on you:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Identify your face shape by pulling your hair away from your face and looking in the mirror. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. Once you determine your face shape, you can look for hairstyles that suit your features.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Think about your hair texture, whether it's fine, thick, curly, or straight. This will help you determine what styles will work for you.

Step 3: Look for Inspiration

Search for inspiration from magazines, social media, or celebrity hairstyles. Look for styles that suit your face shape and hair texture.

Step 4: Consult with Your Hairdresser

Book an appointment with your hairdresser and bring along pictures of the styles you like. They can advise you on what will suit your face shape and hair texture.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you determine if a new haircut will look good on you:

  • Consider your lifestyle when choosing a new hairstyle. Will it be easy to maintain and style?
  • Look for styles that flatter your best features, such as your eyes, cheekbones, or jawline.
  • Choose a style that complements your personality and fashion sense.
  • Experiment with different haircuts by using virtual try-on tools or trying on wigs.
  • Be open to your hairdresser's suggestions and advice.

FAQ

Q1: How do I know if a short haircut will suit me?

A: Short haircuts can be flattering on many face shapes, but it's important to consider your hair texture and lifestyle. If you have fine hair, a short bob or pixie cut can add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut or layered bob can help control bulkiness. Consult with your hairdresser to find the best short haircut for you.

Q2: Will a fringe suit me?

A: Fringes can be a great way to change up your look, but it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. If you have a round face, a side-swept fringe can help elongate your features. If you have curly hair, a wispy or textured fringe can complement your curls. Ask your hairdresser for advice on the best type of fringe for you.

Hair Care

Once you have a new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause breakage or damage to your hair.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your new haircut looks great and stays healthy. Remember, confidence is key when it comes to rocking a new hairstyle. Embrace your new look and enjoy the compliments!
