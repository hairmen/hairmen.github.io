How To Keep Curly Hair Slicked Back A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Nov 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks beautiful and voluminous, it can be difficult to manage and style. One of the most popular hairstyles for curly hair is slicked back, but it can be a challenge to keep it in place. In this article, we will discuss how to keep curly hair slicked back, step by step.

  • How to Keep Curly Hair Slicked Back
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Keeping Curly Hair Slicked Back
  • FAQs

How to Keep Curly Hair Slicked Back

To keep curly hair slicked back, you will need the right products and tools. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you begin styling your hair, make sure it is clean and free of any products or build-up. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any residue and give your hair a fresh start.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product that will help hold your curls in place. Look for a gel or mousse that is specifically designed for curly hair. Apply the product to damp hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 3: Comb your Hair Back

Using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers, comb your hair back and away from your face. Make sure to smooth out any bumps or knots in your hair.

Step 4: Secure with Bobby Pins

Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place. Start at the sides of your hairline and work your way back, pinning your hair in place as you go. Make sure to use enough bobby pins to keep your hair in place.

Step 5: Use Hairspray

Finish off your look with a strong hold hairspray. Spray your hair from a distance to avoid any stiffness or crunchiness. Use a light mist of hairspray to keep your hair in place without weighing it down.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to keeping your curly hair slicked back:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  2. Apply a styling product that will help hold your curls in place.
  3. Comb your hair back and away from your face.
  4. Secure your hair in place with bobby pins.
  5. Finish off your look with a strong hold hairspray.

Tips for Keeping Curly Hair Slicked Back

Here are some tips for keeping your curly hair slicked back:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any build-up.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to avoid damaging your curls.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to avoid them being visible.

FAQs

Can I use a hairbrush instead of a comb?

No, it is not recommended to use a hairbrush on curly hair as it can cause frizz and damage your curls. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers instead.

How can I make my hair look less greasy?

Use a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil and refresh your hair. Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can transfer oils from your hands to your hair.

Can I use a regular hairspray?

No, it is recommended to use a hairspray that is specifically designed for curly hair. Regular hairspray can be too heavy and weigh down your curls.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can keep your curly hair slicked back and looking great.
