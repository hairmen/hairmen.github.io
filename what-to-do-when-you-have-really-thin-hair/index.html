What To Do When You Have Really Thin Hair

Written by Luffy Jul 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have really thin hair, you may feel self-conscious about your locks. But don't worry, there are plenty of things you can do to make your hair look fuller and more voluminous. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for dealing with thin hair.

Table of Contents:

  • Haircuts for Thin Hair
  • Products for Thin Hair
  • Styling for Thin Hair
  • Diet for Thin Hair
  • FAQ About Thin Hair

Haircuts for Thin Hair

One of the best ways to make thin hair look fuller is to get the right haircut. Layered haircuts can help add volume and texture to thin hair. A bob that hits at the chin can also be a good option, as it creates the illusion of fullness. Avoid long, straight haircuts, as they can make thin hair look limp and lifeless.

Another option is to add bangs to your haircut. Bangs can help create the illusion of fullness at the front of your hair, making your locks look thicker overall.

Products for Thin Hair

Choosing the right hair products can also help make your thin hair look fuller. Look for volumizing shampoos and conditioners, which can help add body to your hair. Avoid heavy conditioners, which can weigh down your locks.

You can also use hair mousse or root-lifting sprays to add volume to your hair. Apply these products to your roots, then blow dry your hair upside down for maximum volume.

Styling for Thin Hair

When styling your hair, avoid using heavy products like hair wax or gel. These products can weigh down your hair and make it look even thinner. Instead, opt for lightweight styling sprays or creams.

You can also try blow drying your hair with a round brush to create volume. Start by blow drying your hair upside down, then switch to a round brush to lift your roots and create volume.

Diet for Thin Hair

Believe it or not, your diet can also impact the health of your hair. Eating a diet that's rich in protein and vitamins can help promote healthy hair growth. Foods like eggs, nuts, and leafy greens are all great for promoting hair health.

Make sure you're also drinking plenty of water, as dehydration can lead to dry, brittle hair.

FAQ About Thin Hair

Q: Can thin hair be thickened?

A: While you can't change the thickness of your individual hair strands, you can use styling techniques and products to make your hair look fuller and thicker.

Q: Can coloring thin hair make it look thicker?

A: Yes, adding color to your hair can help create the illusion of fullness. Highlights, lowlights, and balayage can all help add dimension and texture to thin hair.

Q: Should I avoid heat styling tools if I have thin hair?

A: Heat styling tools can be damaging to any hair type, but especially to thin hair. If you do use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and don't apply too much heat.

Conclusion

Having thin hair can be frustrating, but there are plenty of things you can do to make your locks look fuller and more voluminous. From getting the right haircut to using the right hair products, these tips and tricks can help you feel more confident about your hair.
