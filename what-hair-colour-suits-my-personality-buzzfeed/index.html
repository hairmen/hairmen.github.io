What Hair Colour Suits My Personality Buzzfeed A Guide To Finding The Perfect Hair Colour For You

Written by Aprilia Sep 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a change in your appearance, changing your hair colour can be an excellent option. But with so many colours to choose from, it can be challenging to know which one will suit your personality best. That's where Buzzfeed comes in, with their "what hair colour suits my personality" quiz. In this article, we'll explore how to find the perfect hair colour for you based on your personality.

Description

Buzzfeed has become a popular platform for quizzes that help people determine their personalities and preferences. Their "what hair colour suits my personality" quiz has been a hit, providing users with personalised hair colour suggestions based on their answers to a series of questions. This article will delve into the science behind the quiz and provide additional tips on how to choose a hair colour that complements your personality.

How to Take the Buzzfeed Quiz

The Buzzfeed quiz consists of a series of questions that aim to determine your personality type. The questions cover topics such as your favourite colours, hobbies, and the kind of music you listen to. Once you've answered all the questions, the quiz generates a personalised result that suggests the best hair colour for you.

Step by Step Guide to Taking the Buzzfeed Quiz

Step 1: Go to the Buzzfeed "what hair colour suits my personality" quiz page

Step 2: Answer all the questions truthfully

Step 3: The quiz generates a personalised result

Step 4: Read the result and take note of the suggested hair colours

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

While the Buzzfeed quiz is an excellent starting point, there are other factors to consider when choosing a hair colour that suits your personality. Consider the following tips:

1. Skin Tone: Your skin tone plays a crucial role in determining the best hair colour for you. If you have warm-toned skin, consider hair colours with warm undertones such as copper, caramel, or honey. If you have cool-toned skin, consider hair colours with cool undertones such as platinum blonde, ash brown, or black.

2. Natural Hair Colour: Your natural hair colour is another factor to consider. If you have dark hair, going for a lighter shade may not suit your personality. Consider going for a hair colour that is only a few shades lighter or darker than your natural hair colour.

3. Lifestyle: Your lifestyle can also influence the best hair colour for you. If you have an active lifestyle, consider a low-maintenance hair colour that won't require frequent touch-ups. If you work in a conservative environment, consider a natural hair colour that won't attract too much attention.

FAQ

Q: Will the suggested hair colour from the Buzzfeed quiz suit me?

A: The suggested hair colour from the Buzzfeed quiz is based on your personality, but it may not always be accurate. Consider other factors such as skin tone and natural hair colour before making a decision.

Q: Can I dye my hair myself?

A: While it's possible to dye your hair at home, it's best to leave it to a professional. They can ensure that the hair colour is applied correctly and prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect hair colour for you, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its vibrancy. Consider the following tips:

1. Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading

2. Avoid washing your hair too frequently as it can strip away the colour

3. Use a heat protectant when using hot styling tools such as straighteners and curling irons

Changing your hair colour can be a fun and exciting way to refresh your look. By considering your personality, skin tone, natural hair colour, and lifestyle, you can find the perfect hair colour that suits you. And, with the right hair care routine, you can maintain your vibrant new hair colour for longer.
