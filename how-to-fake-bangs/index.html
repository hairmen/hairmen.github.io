How To Fake Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 21, 2023 · 2 min read

Do you want to try out bangs but aren't ready to commit to a haircut? Or maybe you have bangs, but you want to switch things up without cutting your hair. Whatever your reason, you can fake bangs with just a few simple steps. In this guide, we'll show you how to fake bangs and give you some tips on how to make them look natural.

How to Fake Bangs

Here's what you'll need:

  • Bobby pins
  • A brush
  • Hairspray

Follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to the side that you want your bangs to be on.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and twist it.
  3. Pull the twisted section of hair across your forehead and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Spray your new bangs with hairspray to keep them in place.

And that's it! You now have fake bangs.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your fake bangs look more natural:

  • Use a brush to blend your fake bangs with the rest of your hair.
  • Choose a hair color that matches your natural hair color.
  • Trim your fake bangs if they're too long.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about fake bangs:

Can I use a hair tie instead of bobby pins?

No, a hair tie won't work as well because it won't keep your fake bangs in place as securely as bobby pins.

Can I fake bangs with curly hair?

Yes, you can. Just make sure to straighten the section of hair that you're using to create your fake bangs before twisting and pinning it across your forehead.

How long will my fake bangs last?

Your fake bangs should last all day if you use enough bobby pins and hairspray to keep them in place.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair while you have your fake bangs. Here are some tips:

  • Don't use too much hairspray, as it can dry out your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you're using heat to straighten your hair for your fake bangs.
  • Take out your bobby pins gently to avoid pulling out hair.

Now that you know how to fake bangs, you can try out a new look without committing to a haircut. Have fun experimenting!
