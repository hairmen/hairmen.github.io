How To Undercut Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

The undercut haircut has been gaining popularity over the past few years for its versatility and edginess. This haircut involves shaving the hair on the sides and back of the head while leaving the hair on top longer. It can be styled in various ways, from slicked back to messy and textured. If you're interested in getting an undercut haircut, keep reading for a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers with different attachments, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will ensure that your hair is clean and easy to work with. Dry your hair with a towel or blow dryer until it's completely dry.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section your hair. You'll want to separate the hair on the top of your head from the hair on the sides and back. Clip the top section of your hair up and out of the way.

Step 3: Choose Your Clipper Attachment

Decide how short you want the hair on the sides and back to be. Choose a clipper attachment that corresponds to the desired length. Start with a longer attachment and work your way down if you want shorter hair.

Step 4: Start Shaving

Begin shaving the hair on the sides and back of your head with the clippers. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Use the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to shave it off.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Use a shorter clipper attachment or scissors to blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 6: Cut the Top

Unclip the hair on top of your head and decide on the length you want it to be. Use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. You can also add layers or texture if you want.

Step 7: Style

Once your hair is cut, you can style it in various ways. You can slick it back with pomade, create a messy look with texturizing spray, or comb it over to one side.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting an undercut haircut:

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture.
  • Be patient and take your time when cutting your hair.
  • Ask a friend or family member for help if you're not confident in your cutting skills.
  • Experiment with different products to find the best one for your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about undercut haircuts:

How long does an undercut haircut last?

An undercut haircut can last anywhere from 2 to 4 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows. You'll need to get it touched up regularly to maintain the look.

Can I still wear my hair up with an undercut?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with an undercut. The longer hair on top will give you plenty of styling options.

Hair Care

To keep your undercut looking fresh, you'll need to take good care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. You can also use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect undercut haircut. Whether you're going for a classic or modern look, this haircut is sure to turn heads.
