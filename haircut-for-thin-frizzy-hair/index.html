Once youâve gotten your new haircut, there are several things you can do to maintain your style and keep your hair looking its best.

1. Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make a big difference in how your hair looks and feels. For thin frizzy hair, look for lightweight products like mousses, sprays, and serums that can add volume and texture without weighing your hair down. 3. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.
