Description

Heart shaped faces are one of the most versatile face shapes when it comes to hairstyles. With their wider forehead and narrow chin, heart shaped faces are perfect for long hairstyles that frame the face and soften the chin. In this guide, we will take a look at some of the best long hairstyles for heart shaped faces in 2023.

How to Choose Long Hairstyles for Heart Shaped Faces

When it comes to choosing long hairstyles for heart shaped faces, it's important to keep in mind that the goal is to balance out the wider forehead and narrow chin. Some key factors to consider when choosing a hairstyle include:

  • Length: Longer hairstyles that hit around the collarbone or below work best for heart shaped faces.
  • Layers: Layers can add volume and movement to a hairstyle, which is great for heart shaped faces.
  • Bangs: Side-swept bangs can help balance out a wider forehead.
  • Face-framing layers: Layers that frame the face can help soften the chin.

Step-by-Step Guide for Long Hairstyles for Heart Shaped Faces

Here is a step-by-step guide for achieving some of the best long hairstyles for heart shaped faces:

1. Long Layers

Start by cutting long layers into the hair, focusing on framing the face and adding volume. This look works especially well with side-swept bangs.

2. Beach Waves

Create loose, beachy waves throughout the hair using a curling iron or wand. Add some texturizing spray or sea salt spray for added volume and texture.

3. Half-Up, Half-Down

Take the top half of the hair and pull it back, securing with a hair tie or clip. Leave the bottom half down and add some loose waves or curls for added texture.

4. Side Part with Face-Framing Layers

Create a deep side part and add some face-framing layers to soften the chin. Style with loose waves or curls.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hairstyles for Heart Shaped Faces

Here are some tips for maintaining long hairstyles for heart shaped faces:

  • Regular trims: Keep the ends of the hair trimmed to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.
  • Use heat protectant: When using heat tools like curling irons or straighteners, always use a heat protectant to prevent damage to the hair.
  • Use a hair mask: Once a week, use a deep conditioning hair mask to keep the hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQs

Long hairstyles that hit around the collarbone or below work best for heart shaped faces.

What type of bangs work best for heart shaped faces?

Hair Care for Heart Shaped Faces

When it comes to hair care, it's important to keep your specific hair type and concerns in mind. However, some general tips for maintaining healthy hair include:

  • Wash hair every 2-3 days.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash hair.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing hair, especially when wet.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often.

By following these tips and choosing the right long hairstyle for your heart shaped face, you can achieve a look that is both flattering and easy to maintain.
