What Haircut Looks Good On A Round Fat Face

Written by Robby Aug 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round fat face, you might be wondering what haircut will flatter your features. Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be sharing some helpful tips on what hairstyle will look great on your face shape.

Description

A round fat face is typically characterized by a fullness in the cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wider forehead. The key to finding a flattering haircut for this face shape is to choose a style that balances out the roundness of the face.

How to

When it comes to choosing a haircut for a round fat face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of the face, as this will only make your face look wider. Instead, opt for a style that adds height to the top of the head, which will create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face.

Another important factor to consider is the length of your hair. Generally, shorter haircuts are more flattering on round faces, as they help to emphasize the cheekbones and jawline. However, if you prefer longer hair, you can still make it work by adding layers and volume to the top of your head.

Step by Step

Here are some specific steps you can follow when choosing a haircut for a round fat face:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Before you can choose a flattering haircut, you need to determine your face shape. If you're not sure whether you have a round face, use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If all three measurements are roughly the same, you likely have a round face.

Step 2: Look for inspiration

Once you know your face shape, start looking for inspiration for your haircut. Look for celebrities or models who have a similar face shape to yours and see what hairstyles they've worn that look great on them.

Step 3: Consult with a stylist

When you have some ideas for your haircut, it's a good idea to consult with a stylist to get their opinion. They can help you choose a style that will flatter your face shape and work with your hair texture and thickness.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a round fat face:

- Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as this can make your face look wider. - Consider adding side-swept bangs to your haircut, as this will help to create the illusion of a longer face. - Opt for layers throughout your hair, as this will add volume and movement to your style. - Avoid center parts, as this can emphasize the roundness of your face.

FAQ

What length of hair is best for a round fat face?

Generally, shorter haircuts are more flattering on round faces, as they help to emphasize the cheekbones and jawline. However, if you prefer longer hair, you can still make it work by adding layers and volume to the top of your head.

What kind of bangs look good on a round fat face?

Side-swept bangs are a great option for a round fat face, as they help to create the illusion of a longer face. Avoid straight-across bangs, as these can emphasize the roundness of your face.

Hair Care

To keep your haircut looking great, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type, and avoid using heat tools too often. Additionally, consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
