Why Is My Hair Straight On Top And Curly Underneath

Written by Luffy Jul 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have ever looked in the mirror and wondered why your hair is straight on top and curly underneath, you are not alone. This is a common hair type that many people have, and it can be a bit frustrating to style. In this article, we will explore the reasons behind this hair type and provide some tips on how to manage it.

Description

Straight on top and curly underneath hair type is when the hair on the crown of your head is straight, while the hair underneath is curly or wavy. This hair type is often referred to as "mixed texture" or "mixed curl pattern" hair. It can be challenging to manage because the different textures require different styling techniques and products.

How to

If you have straight on top and curly underneath hair, here are some tips on how to manage it:

1. Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, or argan oil. These ingredients will help to moisturize your curls and keep your hair healthy.

2. Use a Diffuser When Blow Drying

If you want to blow dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help to prevent frizz and maintain your curls' shape. Always use a heat protectant spray before blow drying to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Use Different Styling Techniques

When styling your hair, use different techniques for the straight and curly sections. Use a flat iron or straightener to style the straight sections and a curling wand or rollers to style the curly sections. This will help to maintain the different textures and prevent frizz.

4. Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type. Look for products that are formulated for mixed texture hair, such as curl creams, gels, or mousses. These products will help to define your curls and keep your hair moisturized.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to manage straight on top and curly underneath hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are designed for mixed texture hair.

Step 2: Apply Products

Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair while it is still damp. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help to maintain your curls' shape and prevent frizz.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a flat iron or straightener to style the straight sections of your hair. Use a curling wand or rollers to style the curly sections. Apply a finishing spray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for managing straight on top and curly underneath hair:

1. Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy. Use a hair mask or hot oil treatment to nourish your hair.

2. Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often. This can cause damage and breakage. If you must use heat, always use a heat protectant spray.

3. Sleep on a Satin Pillowcase

Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and maintain your curls' shape.

4. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about straight on top and curly underneath hair:

1. Is this hair type common?

Yes, this hair type is common. Many people have mixed texture hair.

2. Can I wear my hair straight or curly?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight or curly. Just be sure to use the right products and styling techniques for each section of your hair.

3. Can I get rid of the straight on top and curly underneath hair type?

No, this hair type is determined by your genetics. You can manage it, but you cannot change it.

Hair Care

Managing straight on top and curly underneath hair can be challenging, but with the right products and techniques, it can be done. Be sure to use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, deep condition your hair regularly, and avoid heat damage. Use different styling techniques for the straight and curly sections of your hair, and use products that are designed for mixed texture hair. With these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
