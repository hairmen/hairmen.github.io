How To Keep Your Long Hair Back For Guys The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy with long hair, you know how hard it can be to keep it out of your face. Whether you're working out, playing sports, or just going about your day, having your hair constantly falling in your eyes can be annoying and distracting. Fortunately, there are several ways to keep your long hair back and under control. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to keep your long hair back for guys.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Keep Your Long Hair Back for Guys
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Keeping Your Long Hair Back

How to Keep Your Long Hair Back for Guys

There are several ways to keep your long hair back, depending on your hair type and personal preference. Here are some of the most popular methods:

1. Ponytail

The ponytail is a classic hairstyle that works well for guys with long hair. To create a ponytail, gather your hair at the back of your head and secure it with an elastic band. You can wear your ponytail high or low, depending on your preference.

2. Bun

If you want a more sophisticated look, try a bun. To create a bun, gather your hair into a ponytail and twist it into a coil. Then, wrap the coil around the base of the ponytail and secure it with bobby pins.

3. Headband

A headband can be a great way to keep your hair back and out of your face. Choose a headband that's wide enough to hold your hair back, and wear it across your forehead.

4. Braid

If you want a more intricate hairstyle, try a braid. To create a braid, divide your hair into three sections and weave them together. You can braid your hair down your back or to the side, depending on your preference.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a simple ponytail:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the back of your head with your hands.
  3. Secure your hair with an elastic band.
  4. Adjust the ponytail so it's centered on the back of your head.
  5. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a bun:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a high or low ponytail.
  3. Twist the ponytail into a coil.
  4. Wrap the coil around the base of the ponytail.
  5. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

Tips for Keeping Your Long Hair Back

Here are some tips for keeping your long hair back and under control:

  • Use an elastic band that's strong enough to hold your hair in place, but not so tight that it causes discomfort.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.
  • Use a headband to keep your hair back and out of your face.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to keep your long hair back for guys:

Can I use hair gel to keep my hair back?

Yes, you can use hair gel to keep your hair back. Apply a small amount of gel to your hair and style it as desired.

How often should I wash my long hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more often. If you have dry hair, you may be able to wash it less frequently.

Hair Care for Long Hair

If you have long hair, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for keeping your long hair healthy:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners, which can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can keep your long hair back and under control. Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find the ones that work best for you. With a little practice, you'll be able to keep your long hair looking great in any situation.
