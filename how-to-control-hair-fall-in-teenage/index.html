How To Control Hair Fall In Teenage

Written by Oliver Jul 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are a teenager and experiencing hair fall, it is quite common. Many teenagers face hair fall due to various reasons like hormonal changes, stress, poor diet, and lack of hair care. But don't worry; there are several ways to control hair fall in teenage. Here are some effective ways to keep your hair healthy and prevent hair fall.

Step by Step Guide

Here are some effective steps to control hair fall in teenage:

1. Maintain a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet is essential for healthy hair growth. Include protein-rich foods like eggs, fish, and dairy products in your diet. Also, consume foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals like iron, zinc, and vitamin E.

2. Avoid Hairstyles that Pull Your Hair

Avoid tight hairstyles like braids, ponytails, and buns that pull your hair. These hairstyles can damage the hair follicles and lead to hair fall.

3. Use a Mild Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Avoid using harsh chemicals and hot water on your hair, as it can damage your hair and lead to hair fall.

4. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can improve blood circulation and promote healthy hair growth. Use warm oil like coconut or almond oil and massage your scalp for a few minutes before washing your hair.

5. Avoid Stress

Stress can lead to hair fall, so try to avoid stress as much as possible. Practice stress-reducing activities like yoga and meditation to keep your mind and body relaxed.

Effective Tips to Control Hair Fall

Here are some effective tips to control hair fall in teenage:

1. Use Natural Hair Masks

Use natural hair masks like aloe vera, onion juice, and fenugreek to promote healthy hair growth. These hair masks are easy to make and are effective in reducing hair fall.

2. Drink Plenty of Water

Drinking plenty of water is essential for healthy hair growth. It keeps your scalp hydrated and promotes healthy hair growth.

3. Avoid Chemical Treatments

Avoid chemical treatments like coloring, perming, and straightening, as they can damage your hair and lead to hair fall.

4. Use Silk Pillow Covers

Use silk pillow covers to reduce hair fall. Silk pillow covers are gentle on your hair and reduce friction, which can lead to hair fall.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much hair fall is normal for a teenager?

It is normal to lose 50-100 strands of hair per day. If you are experiencing more than that, you may need to take steps to control hair fall.

2. Can stress cause hair fall in teenagers?

Yes, stress can lead to hair fall in teenagers. It is essential to manage stress to prevent hair fall.

3. Can hair fall be reversed?

Yes, hair fall can be reversed with proper hair care and a healthy diet. It may take some time, but with consistent effort, you can promote healthy hair growth.

Healthy Hair Care Routine

Here is a healthy hair care routine that you can follow to prevent hair fall:

1. Wash your hair twice a week with a mild shampoo.

2. Use a conditioner after shampooing to keep your hair moisturized.

3. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.

4. Avoid using hot water on your hair.

5. Massage your scalp with warm oil once a week.

6. Avoid using harsh chemicals and treatments on your hair.

Follow these simple steps to keep your hair healthy and prevent hair fall.

By following these tips and maintaining a healthy hair care routine, you can control hair fall in teenage. Remember, healthy hair requires consistent effort and a healthy lifestyle. So, take care of your hair and enjoy healthy, luscious locks!
